

These are the points that we need to make that are very important when we have those level 5 pages, which is a page for an image, and that image is in that page. 

Important Points:
* The image is on the page. It's not anchored to the browser window to scroll up and down. Make sure it's on the page. If there's a lot of writing on it, three pages of writing, when you scroll down, the image is on the page and scrolling up. 

* Next is that the image is not transparent. In some cases, we've seen content underneath it because the image is transparent. We don't want the image transparent, opaque, and above anything else. 

* The text should wrap around it. Nothing should really flow underneath it or over it.
