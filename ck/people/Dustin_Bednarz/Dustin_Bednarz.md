# Dustin Brett Bednarz — Research Profile

Last updated: 2026-04-05


## Dustin Brett Bednarz

* **Full Name:** Dustin Brett Bednarz
* **Date of Birth:** January 9, 1987
* **Birthplace:** Lubbock, Texas
* **Family Base:** Lubbock, Texas
* **Parents:** Darrell Ray Bednarz and Cheryl Deneise "Bunny" Meadows Bednarz

Dustin Brett Bednarz, who commonly goes by his middle name Brett, was born on
January 9, 1987, in Lubbock, Texas. He is the middle child of Darrell Ray
Bednarz and Cheryl "Bunny" Meadows Bednarz, a well-known family in the
Lubbock and Slaton area with deep roots in the West Texas community.

Brett graduated from Texas Tech University with a Bachelor's degree in
Petroleum Engineering (2007-2010). He has built a career in the oil and gas
industry, holding engineering positions at several companies including Energen
Resources Corporation, Mewbourne Oil Company, and RAW Oil & Gas, Inc. He
currently serves as Vice President of Operations at Smith Energy Operating
Company in Lubbock, Texas.

Brett is married to Kacy Welch Bednarz (born May 25, 1986). They currently
reside at 12002 Topeka Ave, Lubbock, TX 79424, in the Vintage Township
neighborhood. They previously owned homes at 4405 105th St and 3803 139th St
in Lubbock. Kacy is an active tennis player who participates in USTA Serve
Tennis leagues and tournaments in the Lubbock area.

Brett's older brother, Douglas Blake Bednarz, became an independent citizen
investigator of the Charlie Kirk case and was active on X/Twitter under
@BlakeBednarz before deleting his accounts. Blake is married to Jennifer Sipes
Bednarz, which connects the Bednarz family to the Sipes family documented
further below in this profile.

Brett's younger sister is Brittney Nicole Bednarz Fritz (born March 8, 1994),
who played women's basketball at West Texas A&M University and is married to
Cole Gistin Fritz.

The Bednarz family operates Bednarz & Associates Insurance Agency at 12505
Quaker Ave in Lubbock, a locally owned agency since 1976 run by Brett's parents,
Darrell and Bunny Bednarz. Blake also works at the family insurance business.

The family has a rich heritage in West Texas, with strong Moravian and Polish
Catholic roots in the Slaton farming community. The family tree traces back
through Walter Lee Bednarz (1933-1981) and Edwin Frank Bednarz (1907-1996) to
early settlers Rudolph John Bednarz (1884-1958) and Adelheid Mary Kahlich in
the Fayette County and Lubbock County areas.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Investigation

Dustin Brett Bednarz's name entered the Charlie Kirk investigation through two
channels:

* **William Cumby's allegation:** Cumby publicly alleged on X/Twitter that
  Dustin was "the person in the van" at the rear of the scene on September 10,
  2025, identifying him in part by what Cumby described as "double earrings."
  This remains an unverified claim by a third-party commentator. No
  corroborating evidence has been publicly presented.

* **Blake Bednarz's citizen investigation:** Brett's brother Blake became an
  active independent investigator of the Charlie Kirk case, analyzing drone
  footage, producing flight-path reconstructions, acoustic studies, 3D site
  models, and frame-by-frame video analyses. Blake was subsequently accused by
  @JG_CSTT (CrowdSource the Truth) of fabricating and manipulating evidence.
  He reportedly received death threats, including being told he was "lucky he's
  not already in a box" and "Israel always wins." Blake deleted both his
  X/Twitter and YouTube accounts following these threats.

**No direct connection** between Dustin Brett Bednarz and Utah, UVU, or TPUSA
has been found through public records or web searches.
