# Tucker Carlson

**Tucker Carlson** is a conservative media figure and commentator who features in the Charlie Kirk investigation due to his political alignment with Kirk's evolving positions, his presence at key [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events in 2024-2025, and his role as a flashpoint in **donor-pressure** commentary.

## Background

Carlson is one of the most recognized figures in American conservative media, having hosted a prime-time show on Fox News before launching an independent media operation. He produces long-form interviews and commentary across multiple platforms.

## Connection to the Investigation

### AmericaFest 2024 (December 2024)
Carlson spoke at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) AmericaFest event and discussed topics including the Epstein case and foreign intelligence operations.

### TPUSA Student Action Summit (July 14, 2025)
Carlson spoke at the Tampa event shortly after [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)'s **July 13 debate with Dave Smith**. Commentary threads (including **[@based_sam_parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** and **[@TuckerCarlson](/Influencers/podcasts-tucker-carlson)** posts compiled in research notes) treat Carlson's TPUSA presence as central to Kirk's **mid-2025 Israel pivot** and the **donor revolt** that followed.

### Donor Pressure Over Carlson Bookings
Kirk's **reportedly** attributed private messages reference **donor friction connected to Tucker Carlson's presence** at TPUSA events:

- *"Just lost another huge Jewish donor"* over not cancelling Tucker Carlson
- *"Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes"*
- *"I do NOT appreciate being emotionally blackmailed"*

Commentary ties this donor pressure to the reported **Hamptons intervention** (August 4–6, 2025), where pro-Israel figures **allegedly** confronted Kirk about platform decisions including Tucker bookings. See [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) and [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).

### Kirk's Late Israel Skepticism — Carlson as Context
Tucker Carlson posts and interviews are frequently cited in **foreign-policy assassination motive theories** as evidence that Kirk was moving toward skepticism of pro-Israel donor orthodoxy. Carlson himself has provided **post-assassination commentary** on narrative gaps in the official account. These are **commentary and opinion**, not established facts about who ordered violence.

In a video circulated by **[@Partisangirl](/Influencers/x/partisangirl)** (June 20, 2026) and transcribed in the master investigation file, Carlson stated: *"All of us who knew Charlie Kirk, believe he was murdered because his views on Israel were changing."* This is Carlson's **attributed personal opinion**, not a verified finding about motive or perpetrator. Separately, former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that Kirk's disputes with pro-Israel donors were among the foreign-nexus leads his center was ordered to stop pursuing once Tyler Robinson was in custody.

### "Neutralized" Rhetoric Directed at Carlson
The master file also records commentary that **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) publicly called for Tucker Carlson to be "neutralized"** in the pre-assassination period, and separately references reluctance among some figures to see Carlson arrested. These are documented as part of the **hostile-rhetoric backdrop** to the summer 2025 Israel split — attributed claims recorded for context, not adjudicated facts.

### Post-Assassination Threats
According to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), threats were directed at Carlson, Owens, and [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll) immediately after the assassination. This has been cited as evidence that the investigation was perceived as a threat by unknown parties — an **allegation**, not a verified finding.

### July 20 AMFEST Context
A **July 20, 2025** AMFEST military/intelligence meeting **reportedly preceded** the Hamptons gathering by approximately four days. Donor pressure over Carlson's TPUSA appearances sits within this escalating summer timeline documented in [Motive Overview](/Motive/overview).

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

*(Attributed claims and public commentary — not court findings. No living person is found guilty here.)*

- According to Candace Owens’ circulating accounts of private chats (summarized widely on X in 2026), Charlie Kirk allegedly said roughly **48 hours** before his death that he had “no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause,” after losing a major donor for **refusing to cancel Tucker Carlson**. These are Owens-attributed claims about Kirk’s messages, not authenticated court exhibits on this page.
- A June 2026 video transcription retained in investigation notes attributes to **Tucker Carlson** the statement that people who knew Kirk believe he was murdered because his views on Israel were changing — opinion commentary by a public figure, not a judicial finding.
- **Ben Shapiro**, at TPUSA AmericaFest (Dec 2025), publicly criticized Carlson and Megyn Kelly by name for failing to denounce Candace Owens’ theories about the assassination, calling their silence “cowardice” (discussed again on X in July 2026). That is a public political dispute over speech norms, not evidence of a crime by Carlson.
- Citizen investigators treat Carlson’s post-assassination commentary and pre-event platforming at TPUSA events as part of the **motive / donor-pressure** map (see [Israel Motive Timeline](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israel_Motive_Timeline)). Competing voices call the Israel-hit narrative conspiracy and point to the Robinson prosecution.
