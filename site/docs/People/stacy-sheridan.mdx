# Stacy Sheridan

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only. Ms. Sheridan has **no official role** in the events of that day and is **not accused in any legal proceeding**.

**Stacy Sheridan** (sometimes spelled **Stacey Sheridan**) is a senior executive at [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview), reported to serve as **Senior Advancement Director** — a chief fundraising and development role. She is a longtime TPUSA colleague of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and is publicly described as being close to his widow, [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk). She features in the investigation primarily through her tribute at Charlie Kirk's memorial and through online commentary; there is **no credible evidence linking her to the assassination**.

## Background

Public materials describe a career in higher-education advancement and philanthropy before her TPUSA role:

- Reported to have served as an **assistant vice chancellor at Pepperdine University**
- Reported work with the **Ronald Reagan Presidential Foundation**
- Credited with helping launch a **charitable foundation with actor Gary Sinise**
- Currently reported as **Senior Advancement Director at [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)**, one of the organization's top fundraising positions

These biographical details are drawn from public reporting and social-media commentary and are documented here for reference.

## Connection to the Kirk Family

Sheridan is described in public discussion as a **longtime friend of the Kirk family** and as particularly close to [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk). Some online accounts characterize her informally as a "de facto mom" or "momager" figure to Erika, and note that she is said to **live in the same apartment complex** as Erika. Others emphasize a **genuine, mutual friendship** — noting that Charlie and Erika Kirk reportedly supported Sheridan during her own husband's illness.

These are **characterizations from public commentary**, not findings of this investigation.

## Memorial Tribute (September 21, 2025)

Sheridan delivered an emotional tribute at [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s memorial service on **September 21, 2025**, at State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona:

- She spoke about how Charlie **personally supported her during her husband Mike's cancer battle**. Her husband is reported to have died in **2024**.
- According to her account, Charlie **anonymously sent critical medication and an oxygen machine** to their doorstep during that illness.

This tribute is her most prominent public appearance connected to the case and is frequently cited by those who defend her as evidence of a sincere personal relationship with the Kirks.

## Online Scrutiny (Attributed Claims)

Following [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' commentary on perceived inconsistencies in TPUSA's public accounts of September 10, Sheridan became a focus of **online speculation**. The items below are **reported claims and questions circulating on X**, presented as attributed commentary — **not** as findings or accusations of wrongdoing:

- **Whereabouts on September 10:** Some posts question where Sheridan was on the day of the shooting, noting that — as a top fundraiser said to live near Erika — commentators expected her to be present during the initial aftermath. Candace Owens is described as having put out an "APB" asking about her location.
- **"Handler" framing:** Some users characterize her as being closely attached to Erika Kirk at memorials and events, framing her as a "handler" or damage-control figure. This is **interpretive commentary**, not established fact.
- **Fundraising at the memorial:** Some posts, referencing a Candace Owens episode, allege donation solicitation of wealthy attendees around the memorial. These allegations are **unverified** and are disputed.
- **Broader conspiracy claims:** The most extreme online theories speculate about TPUSA insider involvement in the killing for financial or political motives. These claims are **unsubstantiated**, and no evidence has been presented tying Sheridan to any plot.

**Defenses also circulate:** many observers point to the Kirks' reported support of Sheridan during her husband's illness and describe her relationship with Erika as a real friendship. This page treats the speculation as **unproven** and the sympathetic account as equally part of the public record.
