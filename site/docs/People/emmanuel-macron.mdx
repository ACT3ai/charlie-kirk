# Emmanuel Macron

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Emmanuel Macron is a living person and a sitting head of state. We document only what a named commentator has publicly alleged, with attribution, and note that those allegations are disputed and unproven. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

> **Reported context only — not findings of wrongdoing.** Everything on this page is attributed commentary from one public figure. It has not been established in any court and is contested.

## Investigative Value

Emmanuel Macron, the President of France, appears in this investigation solely because commentator Candace Owens publicly alleged a plot she attributes to "the Macrons." According to Owens, an unnamed French government official warned her that the Macron family had paid for and green-lit an assassination attempt against her, and further claimed that Charlie Kirk's alleged killer "trained with the French Legion's 13th Brigade." These are Owens's characterizations of what she says a source told her — not verified facts, and the French presidency has been reported to dispute the underlying premises. See [People](/People/overview) and the [Cover-Up overview](/CoverUp/overview) for how this thread fits the broader commentary.

There is no evidence in the public record tying President Macron to the events at Utah Valley University, and no charges exist. This page exists to record that his name was raised in reported allegations, not to endorse them.

## What is reported

- According to a public statement by Candace Owens (@RealCandaceO), an unnamed "high-ranking employee of the French Government" warned her that "the Macrons have executed upon and paid for" an assassination plan targeting her.
- Owens further claimed the same source said Charlie Kirk's alleged assassin "trained with the French legion 13th brigade with multi-state involvement." This is presented as her account of a source's claim, not as established fact.
- Separate citizen-research commentary (retained in the project's private investigation file) speculated about El-Sisi–Macron diplomatic contacts around September 2025 and a French state company's Tennessee investment. These items are speculation and do not establish any connection to the shooting.
- **Counterpoint:** These are attributed, unverified allegations from a single commentator relaying an anonymous source. They have not been proven, no court has found them true, and reporting indicates the French side disputes the premises. Presence of Macron's name here is not evidence of any act by him.
