# Connor Dority

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, ethical violation, or participation in any crime by Connor Dority or any other living person. This site documents questions and claims that have circulated in public commentary — not findings of fact. All persons named are presumed innocent. Connor Dority is named in compiled online research about the emergency-medical
response at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. According to that
commentary, he was one of the EMS crew members present at the scene. No
wrongdoing is alleged against him; the accounts describe him in the role of a
medic seeking to render aid.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to research compiled on the Charlie Kirk investigation site, dedicated
EMS crew members — reportedly including Connor Dority and a partner — were on
scene "dressed in full gear with walkie-talkies and professional equipment" and
approached security personnel asking to be let in to help. The same accounts
state that security reportedly turned the medics away. These are attributed,
unverified claims, and the framing casts Dority as someone who sought to assist,
not as a subject of suspicion.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Online commentary reports that Dority and his partner were EMS personnel
  present at UVU on September 10, 2025.
- The same sources allege that security kept trained medics from immediately
  reaching [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) — a claim about others' decisions, not about Dority.
- None of these accounts have been independently confirmed, and there is no
  suggestion that Dority did anything improper.
