# Baron Coleman

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Baron Coleman is a living person. We document his publicly stated commentary only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Baron Coleman is described in the source material as a radio host / podcaster who has publicly stated that he has evidence pointing to a multi-agency cover-up that, in his view, framed [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) for the shooting. These are his reported claims, attributed to him and not asserted as fact here.

According to a cited video, Coleman argues that timeline inconsistencies — including differences between when a sheriff's department reportedly had a suspect in custody and public statements about the arrest time — point to what he characterizes as a cover-up. See the [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) overviews for the broader debate these claims sit within.

## What is reported

* According to a cited video, Coleman states he is "convinced" the case reflects a "multi-agency cover-up designed to frame Tyler Robinson." This is his stated opinion, not a court finding.
* He reportedly points to discrepancies about custody times between different officials as the basis for his concern.
* He is also referenced in commentary threads discussing "shadow analysis" of a doorbell-camera timeline; these are attributed claims.
* **Counterpoint:** Coleman's assertions are commentary and have not been established in any court. Officials have offered their own accounts of the timeline, and nothing here proves a cover-up occurred.

## Open Questions

1. What specifically is the "evidence" Coleman references, and can any of it be independently examined?
2. Do the custody-time discrepancies he cites hold up against the official record?
3. How do his claims compare with statements from the officials involved?
