# Andrew Kolvet

**Andrew Kolvet** is the Chief Communications Officer and spokesman for [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview) and the executive producer of The Charlie Kirk Show. He was one of Charlie Kirk's closest professional associates.

## Background

Kolvet graduated from the University of Washington's Foster School of Business in 2006 with a B.A. in Business Marketing. Before joining TPUSA, he had a career in communications and media production:

- Founded and served as CEO of The ATK Company, a PR and brand strategy firm
- Held VP-level roles at The Kairos Company (a PR firm that represented TPUSA as a client)
- VP at UAMG/LightWorkers Media, working on faith-based media projects under Mark Burnett's production umbrella
- Earlier positions included Development Producer at Signet Media Group and Financial Analyst at Washington Mutual

Kolvet transitioned to [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) around 2019, moving from The Kairos Company into a direct communications role. After Charlie Kirk's death, he became one of the most visible public faces of the organization, appearing on Fox News (Martha MacCallum's "The Untold Story," November 2025) and NewsNation.

## Role in the Investigation

Kolvet has publicly confirmed the authenticity of Charlie Kirk's donor-related text messages, according to reporting by News.com.au. He has appeared on multiple news programs to discuss Charlie Kirk's legacy and the future of TPUSA.
