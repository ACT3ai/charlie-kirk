# Laine Schoenberg

This site documents questions and claims that have circulated in public commentary — not findings of fact. All persons named are presumed innocent. > **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Laine Schoenberg** is identified in the investigation file and security rosters as a **close-proximity response team member** who reportedly **helped carry [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** from the UVU courtyard stage area in the immediate aftermath of the September 10, 2025 shooting. He is part of the **carry team** named alongside [Alex Faria](/People/alex-faria) and [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff). Public information about Schoenberg outside the event remains thin — which makes his **eyewitness testimony**, if ever released, potentially decisive for reconstructing the seconds between the shot and the SUV departure.

## Background

The investigation file lists Schoenberg in the **close proximity / carry** group under the broader [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) footprint for the UVU event. Research notes also cross-reference a similarly spelled name (**[Laine Schoneberger](/People/laine-schoneberger)**) in crowd-identification threads describing a **dark blue shirt, Husky Square sunglasses** figure — a possible alternate spelling or misidentification in citizen footage; **not confirmed** as the same individual on this page.

Separately, unrelated public records describe a **Laine Schoenberg** in finance (Chief Investment Officer roles). **No verified link** between that professional profile and the UVU security roster has been established in the investigation file; treat any such connection as **unverified** unless documented by official sources.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to the master witness list and grok-compiled security threads:

- Schoenberg **helped carry Kirk** from the stage area immediately after the ~12:23 PM shooting.
- He is grouped with Faria and Haruff as the **physical carry team** before Kirk reached the black SUV occupied by [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek).
- The file notes **very limited** mainstream news coverage of his specific actions beyond roster inclusion — his value is primarily **positional eyewitness** and **sequence-of-events** reconstruction.
- Defense-witness commentary in the file lists Schoenberg among personnel [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s team **should call** if offering a complete account — **legal opinion**, not a finding about Schoenberg's conduct.

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

Per posts compiled in the People grok fan-out (Query 5) and investigation-file witness lists:

- **Close-proximity carry team:** Schoenberg named with Faria and Blake ("Gibby") as responders who moved Kirk from stage toward transport.
- **Identification ambiguity:** Some threads attempt to match courtyard figures to Schoenberg via clothing (dark blue shirt, sunglasses). **Crowd-sourced IDs are unverified** and should not be treated as official roster confirmation.
- **Yrefy / sponsor-rep confusion:** The file notes a separate thread comparing a courtyard figure to a **TPUSA sponsor representative** named Schoneberger — **possible misidentification**. Documented here as commentary only.
- **Low public statement volume:** Unlike Harpole or Turek, Schoenberg has **few attributed interviews** in compiled research — increasing the weight of any future **FBI 302** or deposition.

## Investigative Questions

- What was Schoenberg's **assigned security role** on September 10 — close protection, perimeter, or ad hoc response?
- What is his **step-by-step account** of the carry from stage to SUV?
- Does any **official roster** confirm his presence versus crowd-sourced visual matching?
- Was he **interviewed by FBI or Utah AG** investigators, and are those records sealed?
- How does his timeline **align** with blood-visible carry-team footage involving Faria and Haruff?
