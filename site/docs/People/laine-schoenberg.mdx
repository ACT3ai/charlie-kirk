## Connection to September 10, 2025

Laine Schoenberg is listed in the master investigation file among the group that **helped carry Charlie Kirk** from the stage area in the immediate aftermath of the shooting, alongside [Alex Faria](/People/alex-faria) and [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff) ("Blake aka Gibby").

## Reported Identification (Attributed)

Citizen research in the file proposes that the **"Husky Square sunglasses, dark blue shirt" man** seen in event footage **resembles Laine Schoneberger** (name variant), described as the **Chief Investment Officer and Managing Partner of Yrefy** and a **TPUSA sponsor representative** for that firm. The identification cites his [LinkedIn profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/laine-schoneberger-501b4710/). This is a **visual, citizen-sourced identification** — not an official TPUSA roster confirmation — and carries **no allegation of wrongdoing**. It is documented so the sponsor-rep angle can be cross-referenced against [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) sponsorship records and other close-proximity figures.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As an eyewitness to the immediate aftermath and carrying efforts, Schoenberg's testimony would help establish the sequence of events, positioning of personnel, and observations during the critical moments following the shooting. If the sponsor-rep identification is accurate, his account could also clarify TPUSA's on-site sponsor presence at the event.

**Status:** Alive
