# Laine Schoenberg

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Close proximity/response team member

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Laine Schoenberg helped carry Charlie Kirk from the stage area in the immediate aftermath of the shooting.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As an eyewitness to the immediate aftermath and carrying efforts, Schoenberg's testimony would help establish the sequence of events, positioning of personnel, and observations during the critical moments following the shooting.
