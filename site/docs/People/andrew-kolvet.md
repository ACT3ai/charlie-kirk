# Andrew Kolvet

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Andrew Kolvet.** All claims are attributed to their sources. Andrew Kolvet is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Andrew Kolvet** is the Chief Communications Officer and spokesman for Turning Point USA and the executive producer of The Charlie Kirk Show. He was one of Charlie Kirk's closest professional associates and has become a central figure in the post-assassination investigation due to information he reportedly communicated and his proximity to key events.

## Background

Kolvet graduated from the University of Washington's Foster School of Business in 2006 with a B.A. in Business Marketing. Before joining TPUSA, he had a career in communications and media production. He founded and served as CEO of The ATK Company, a PR and brand strategy firm. He held VP-level roles at The Kairos Company (a PR firm that represented TPUSA as a client) and at UAMG/LightWorkers Media, where he worked on faith-based media projects under Mark Burnett's production umbrella. Earlier positions included Development Producer at Signet Media Group and Financial Analyst at Washington Mutual.

Kolvet transitioned to TPUSA around 2019, reportedly moving from The Kairos Company into a direct communications role. He became one of the most visible faces of the organization after Charlie Kirk's death, appearing on Fox News (Martha MacCallum's "The Untold Story," November 2025) and NewsNation to discuss TPUSA's future and Kirk's legacy.

## Role in the Investigation

Kolvet is significant to the investigation for multiple reasons: his disclosure about a threat list, his confirmation of Charlie's donor-related text messages, timeline discrepancies involving his movements on September 10, and specific statements he made after the assassination.

## Key Connections (as reported)

### "The List" Disclosure
According to Candace Owens, when Kolvet arrived at the hospital after Charlie Kirk's shooting on September 10, he told Candace that she and Tucker Carlson "were on a list." This claim, if accurate, suggests that someone in the broader political circle was aware of specific targeting. The source and nature of this list remain an open investigative question.

### Confirmation of Charlie's Text Messages
TPUSA spokesman Andrew Kolvet has publicly confirmed the authenticity of Charlie Kirk's donor-related text messages, according to reporting by News.com.au and Wikipedia. These are the messages in which Kirk reportedly expressed frustration about losing donors over his Israel stance and said he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."

Charlie reportedly sent threat-related messages via Signal to Kolvet and via text to Dan Flood. The message to Kolvet reportedly said: "They will kill me." Multiple other witnesses — including Frank Turek, Harrison Smith, and Candace Owens — have corroborated that Charlie expressed fear for his life in the days before September 10.

### September 10 Timeline Discrepancy
According to Candace Owens' investigation, the timeline of Kolvet's arrival at the hospital contradicts Erika Kirk's account. Frank Turek — who was in the SUV performing CPR on Charlie — stated on the record that Erika Kirk arrived at the hospital around 4:00 PM. TPUSA and Erika reportedly claimed she met Kolvet on the runway when his plane landed from Santa Barbara.

However, according to flight records, that plane did not land in Provo until 4:40 PM. With a 20-minute drive to the hospital, Erika could not have both met Kolvet at the airport and arrived at the hospital by 4:00 PM. Candace Owens has stated she believes both Frank Turek and Andrew Kolvet are telling the truth, and that Erika must have arrived on an earlier flight — possibly Charlie Kirk's own plane, which landed at 3:31 PM.

### "Processing Their Guilt"
According to commentary compiled in the investigation, Kolvet reportedly said that Erika Kirk and the team were "processing their guilt" — rather than "grief" — after Charlie's death. This word choice was noted by investigators.

### "How Much Do You Know?"
According to investigation sources, Kolvet stated that a few hours after Charlie was shot, Erika Kirk received a phone call from an unidentified important individual who asked her: "How much do you know?" She reportedly answered: "Everything."

### Post-Assassination Media Activity
According to compiled commentary, Kolvet and Ben Shapiro were scheduling an upcoming show together on the same day as the assassination. Some commentators have questioned the timing of these activities relative to the shooting.

## Open Questions

- What was the source of the "list" Kolvet referenced at the hospital?
- When did Kolvet first learn of this list — before or after the shooting?
- What plane did Kolvet fly on to reach Provo on September 10, and what was the exact arrival time?
- Where was the "tarmac hug" between Kolvet and Erika Kirk, and at what time did it occur?
- What is the full content of the text messages Charlie sent to Kolvet via Signal?
- Who was the "important individual" who called Erika asking "how much do you know"?
