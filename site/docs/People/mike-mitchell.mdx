# Mike Mitchell

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Mike Mitchell is a living person. We document a named role from an official document only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Mike Mitchell is named in a government document quoted in the project's notes as a former Washington County Sheriff's Office deputy who, according to that document, received a phone call from the accused shooter's father and relayed the report up the chain, helping to coordinate the accused's turn-in at the Washington County Sheriff's Office. This is described as an intermediary, coordinating role.

For the surrounding proceedings, see [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview) and [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview).

## What is reported

- Per a government document quoted in the file, Sheriff Brooksby stated he received a call from a former deputy, identified as Mike Mitchell, who had received a call from the accused's father.
- Per the same document, Mitchell reportedly relayed that the accused had confessed to his father, and Mitchell "coordinated with law enforcement" so the accused could turn himself in at the Washington County Sheriff's Office without confrontation.
- **Counterpoint:** Serving as an intermediary to help arrange a peaceful, non-confrontational turn-in is a lawful, cooperative act. Nothing in the source material shows that Mitchell did anything improper; the account is drawn from an official document describing his coordinating role.
