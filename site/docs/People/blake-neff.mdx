# Blake Neff

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Producer of The Charlie Kirk Show and TPUSA team member.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Called his mom post-shooting (per Candace Owens). Publicly responded to Candace Owens' accusations against TPUSA. Organized/participated in livestreams addressing "false accusations" about the family/organization. Involved in leadership/succession disputes.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

TPUSA internal communications, knowledge of event planning, and post-incident statements.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* TPUSA internal communications around the event and cell records of post-shooting calls and knowledge of event security planning are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
