# Blake Neff

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Blake Neff** is a producer of The Charlie Kirk Show and a [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) team member who was on-site at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. He has been a focal point in post-shot **timeline reconstruction** because of his public statements about witnessing [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)'s calls — set against video that appears to show Mikey alone seconds later.

## The Witness Gap: Blake's Account vs. 12:24:02 Footage

Candace Owens' timeline analysis highlights a tension between Blake Neff's **witness claim** and a **12:24:02** video still cited in CK_FILE:

| Element | Blake Neff's public account | Video / timeline critique |
|---------|----------------------------|---------------------------|
| Mikey's call to **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** | Blake has said he **witnessed** this call | Erika says Mikey called at **12:23 PM Utah** (11:23 AM Arizona) |
| Mikey's call to **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** | Blake has said he **witnessed** this call | — |
| **12:24:02** position | Implied proximity to Mikey during calls | Footage shows **Mikey past SUV alone** — Blake **absent from frame** |

Investigators frame this as an **open timeline question**: if Blake was not beside Mikey at 12:24:02, how precisely did he witness both calls? The project does **not** assert deception, foreknowledge, or improper conduct — only that **public accounts diverge** and deserve documentary resolution.

See [Timeline overview](/Timeline/overview) and [TPUSA Staff — Sept 10](/key_individuals/TPUSA_Staff_Sept_10).

## Post-Shot Actions (Per Public Commentary)

Beyond the call-witness dispute, commentary and social posts have attributed the following to Blake Neff on September 10:

- Called **his mother** shortly after the shooting (referenced in Candace Owens coverage)
- Participated in **livestreams** and threads addressing what TPUSA described as false accusations against the organization and Kirk family
- Engaged publicly in **leadership and succession disputes** following [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death

**No accusation** in this project holds that Blake Neff knew of any assassination plan.

## Role at TPUSA

Blake Neff works as a **producer** on The Charlie Kirk Show — the daily media arm of [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview). He was listed among AV/team members in **close proximity** to the stage at the UVU event. He is part of the **Mikey–Blake–Kolvet** cluster central to Owens' coverage of TPUSA's internal post-shot response alongside [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet).

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- On-site TPUSA staff at the **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** UVU courtyard event
- Named in **call-timing reconstruction** with [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) and [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)
- Public **response to Candace Owens** accusations and investigative threads
- Involved in organizational **messaging** during succession discussions after September 10

## Potential Evidentiary Value

- **TPUSA internal communications** around the event and immediate aftermath
- **Cell records** of post-shooting calls (including call to his mother, per commentary)
- Knowledge of **event planning** and AV setup
- Corroboration or clarification of **who was standing where** in the first minute after the shot
