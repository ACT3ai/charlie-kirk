# Hunter Kozak

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Hunter Kozak is a living person. Online commentary has raised questions about his credentials, affiliations, and on-camera behavior. This page reports those claims with attribution only. We do **not** assert that Kozak knew of any attack in advance, staged the shooting, or acted with criminal intent.

## Investigative Value

**Hunter Kozak** is among the highest-value neutral eyewitnesses in the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination timeline because he reportedly asked the **final audience question** to Kirk on stage **moments before the shot**. That position places him at the center of the last exchange Kirk had with the crowd — a window investigators use to reconstruct final words, crowd dynamics, and stage orientation.

Citizen commentary on X.com describes Kozak as a **liberal UVU student and TikToker** who later spoke publicly calling for peace. His interviews and any retained phone or stage-area footage could corroborate or challenge the official rifle-shot narrative, especially when compared against [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) accounts describing a loud pop rather than a conventional rifle crack. See also [Henry Dells](/People/henry-dells), listed in some witness rosters as an early questioner, for bookend context on the Q&A sequence.

## Witness Role — September 10, 2025

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Described role | UVU student; final Q&A participant |
| Proximity | On stage / in the Q&A line at the UVU courtyard event |
| Status | **Alive** |

According to witness lists compiled from X.com / Twitter research (Query 6, peopleglyph), Kozak:

- Asked the **last question** Charlie Kirk took from the audience before the shooting.
- Was present in the [UVU](/UVU/overview) courtyard during the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) "Prove Me Wrong" event.
- Gave post-event interviews about being the final questioner, including a widely discussed **September 12, 2025** [Channel 5](/Videos/overview) interview with Andrew Callaghan.
- Publicly appealed for peace in media appearances after the killing.

The investigation file also notes Kozak's reported affiliation with the **Unf\*ck America** counter-narrative tour — a separate political organization from TPUSA that describes itself as a counter to Turning Point USA. Commentary threads further claim his **September 12, 2025** interview was with **Andrew Callaghan** on **Channel 5**. Some posts question how he obtained event access given that he and his **"Unf\*ck America" affiliates reportedly had their credentials revoked** from Charlie's events; those are **unverified access-control claims**, not established facts.

Separate commentary (attributed, unverified) states that Kozak's **father is [Steve Kozak](/People/steve-kozak), reported to be a director/executive associated with the Jimmy Kimmel show**. This is logged as a **reported family-connection claim** that has circulated online; it is **not** verified here and implies **no wrongdoing** by Kozak or any relative.

## Background

Kozak is described in the master file as an active UVU-area content creator with a liberal political profile — distinct from the TPUSA staff and AV roster documented on pages such as [Jeb Jacobi](/People/jeb-jacobi) and [Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown). That political distance is why multiple commentators treat his eyewitness value as comparatively **neutral**: he was not on Kirk's production or security team.

## Public Commentary (Attributed Claims Only)

Social-media threads have circulated claims that Kozak was seen "practicing being surprised" before the event, that bullet casings bore "transgender terms," and that his roommate was searched in connection with Israel-related Google queries. **None of these claims are verified on this page.** They are documented because they circulated publicly and because sworn testimony, FBI 302 reports, or unreleased stage footage could confirm or refute them.

We do **not** claim Kozak conspired with any "false-flag unit" or had foreknowledge of the attack. See [Possible Distraction People](/distraction_people/overview) for how witness-adjacent figures enter broader distraction debates.
