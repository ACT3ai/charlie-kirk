# Blake Haruff

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Blake Haruff is a living person. We document only his reported role in the security/carry response. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Blake Haruff** is listed in the master file among **"other security in close proximity"** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event, paired with [Pierce Beck](/People/pierce-beck). The file cross-references the responder recorded on some carry lists as **"Blake (aka Gibby)"** — the man seen helping carry Kirk with **significant blood visible** on him — and states this "likely refers to Blake Haruff." He appears in frame-by-frame footage analyses of the chaos in the seconds after the shot.

Like [Alex Faria](/People/alex-faria) and [Laine Schoenberg](/People/laine-schoenberg), Haruff is named on the master file's proposed **defense witness roster** for the [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) trial, on the argument that every close-proximity responder should be put on the stand to fix the response timeline and positioning discussed under [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Blake Haruff helped carry Charlie Kirk after the shooting. Significant blood was visible on him during the response, as documented in event footage and X posts. He appears in footage analyses of the chaos immediately after the shot was fired, in the **carry cohort** rather than the five-man SUV group ([Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)).

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Haruff's testimony would establish security actions and positioning, details of carrying Kirk, and direct scene observations. Possible **blood/DNA evidence**, phone footage, or scene observations could corroborate or clarify the sequence of events.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* Haruff's FBI Form 302 interview report and untested blood and DNA from the response and removed hospital and UVU camera footage are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
