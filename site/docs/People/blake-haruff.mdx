# Blake Haruff

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Security in close proximity (also known as "Gibby" / "Blake")

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Blake Haruff helped carry Charlie Kirk after the shooting. Significant blood was visible on him during the response, as documented in event footage and X posts. He appears in footage analyses of the chaos immediately after the shot was fired.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Haruff's testimony would establish security actions and positioning, details of carrying Kirk, and direct scene observations. Possible blood/DNA evidence or scene observations could corroborate or clarify the sequence of events.
