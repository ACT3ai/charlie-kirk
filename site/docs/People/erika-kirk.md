# Erika Kirk

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Erika Kirk.** All references to alleged decisions or actions are drawn from unverified public commentary and must be treated as unproven allegations. Erika Kirk is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Erika Kirk** (née Frantzve) is the widow of Charlie Kirk. Following his death on September 10, 2025, she became one of the most discussed figures in the investigation due to her role in TPUSA leadership succession, timeline discrepancies in her reported movements on the day of the shooting, and questions raised by citizen investigators about post-event decisions.

## Background

- **Maiden name**: Erika Frantzve
- **Marriage**: Wife of Charlie Kirk
- **Mother**: Lori Frantzve, who according to TPUSA financial records reviewed by commentators, served on the TPUSA board
- **Public profile**: Limited public presence prior to September 2025
- **Post-event role**: Reportedly assumed leadership responsibilities at TPUSA following Charlie's death

## TPUSA Leadership (as reported)

Following Charlie Kirk's death, Erika Kirk was associated with organizational leadership at TPUSA. According to Andrew Kolvet's public statements (on Fox News and NewsNation), Charlie had told him that Erika could handle leading TPUSA. The exact nature, scope, and formal title of her role has been described differently across sources.

According to commentary compiled in the investigation:
- A Google search for "Erika Kirk Vice President" was conducted from Arizona in April 2025 — several months before Charlie's death
- A search for "vancekirk2028.com" was conducted from Arizona in the same period
- TPUSA's board of directors before September 10 reportedly included: Doug DeGroote, Charlie Kirk, Erika Kirk, David Engelhardt, Mike Miller, and Tom Sodeika

## Key Points from the Investigation

### "Evil Do-ers" Speech
According to citizen investigators, Erika used the phrase "evil do-ers" (plural) approximately 55 hours after Charlie's death in a public statement. This was followed by an approximately 8-day gap before a TPUSA CEO announcement, during which some commentators allege board negotiations were taking place.

### September 10 Timeline Discrepancies
Multiple timeline discrepancies have been identified regarding Erika's movements on September 10:

- **Phone call timing**: Erika stated (in a Megyn Kelly interview) that Mikey McCoy called her at 12:23 PM Utah time (11:23 AM Arizona time) — the same time as the shot. Blake Neff reportedly said he witnessed this call, but at 12:24:02 on video, Mikey is visible alone and Blake is nowhere in frame.
- **Hospital arrival**: Frank Turek stated on the record that Erika arrived at the hospital around 4:00 PM. TPUSA and Erika reportedly claimed she met Andrew Kolvet on the runway when his plane landed from Santa Barbara.
- **Flight timing**: The Santa Barbara plane did not land in Provo until 4:40 PM. With a 20-minute drive to the hospital, Erika could not have both met Kolvet at the airport and arrived at the hospital by 4:00 PM. Investigators note that Charlie Kirk's own plane landed at 3:31 PM — the only flight that fits the 4:00 PM hospital arrival timeline.

### Egyptian Plane Overlap
According to flight tracking analysis compiled in the investigation, the Egyptian-registered plane SU-BTT reportedly had approximately 73 location overlaps with Erika's movements, compared to approximately 23 with Charlie's. The significance of this claim remains an open question.

### Denial of Charlie's Threat Messages
On Glenn Beck's show, Erika stated: "He was NOT messaging people the day before saying 'they're going to kill me.' I have his cell phone. It never happened." Multiple witnesses have contradicted this account:

- **Frank Turek**: Charlie texted him saying "I know they want me dead. I'm under the gun right now please pray for me"
- **Andrew Kolvet**: Confirmed the same story, per Candace Owens
- **Harrison Smith** (Infowars): Went public that Charlie told him days before that he feared Israel would take him out
- **TPUSA spokesman Andrew Kolvet**: Publicly confirmed the donor-related texts as genuine

Investigators note that Charlie reportedly sent messages via Signal and Telegram, not iMessages — which may not appear on a standard review of his iPhone.

### Life Insurance
According to commentary compiled in the investigation, a life insurance policy was taken out through GGLF 2023 LLC. TPUSA reportedly paid a $350,000 loan toward it. Estimated payout figures cited in commentary range from $20M to $50M. The policy was reportedly taken out in 2023, after Charlie began questioning Israel's Oct 7 narrative. These figures are unverified.

### TPUSA Financial Questions
According to financial analysis by citizen investigators:
- Erika's mother Lori Frantzve reportedly served on the TPUSA board, steering what commentators claim is over $15.2M to insider-controlled companies
- Stacy Sheridan (TPUSA Senior Advancement Director) allegedly pulled $2.7M+ through shell companies while salaried for the same role
- These claims are drawn from public commentary and have not been independently verified

### Candace Owens' "The Bride of Charlie"
Candace Owens produced a multi-part investigative documentary series titled "The Bride of Charlie" examining Erika Kirk's role in the aftermath, TPUSA succession, and unanswered questions. The series is available on Owens' YouTube channel.

## Open Questions

- What plane did Erika fly on to reach Provo on September 10, and what time did she arrive?
- Why does her account of the timeline contradict Frank Turek's on-record statement about her hospital arrival time?
- Why did she deny Charlie's threat messages when multiple witnesses have gone on record confirming them?
- What is the full structure of GGLF 2023 LLC and the life insurance arrangements?
- What is the nature of the relationship between the SU-BTT flight path and Erika's travel patterns?
