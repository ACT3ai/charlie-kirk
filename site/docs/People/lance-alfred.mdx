Sgt. Lance Alfred is identified as a spokesperson for the Washington County, Utah Sheriff's Office. He enters the record as an official source for public statements about the surrender of [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), the man charged in the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). This page documents his public-affairs role only and alleges no wrongdoing.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Per the research file, statements attributed to Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) and spokesperson Sgt. Lance Alfred, together with court documents and consistent outlet reporting (Fox News, ABC News, AP), form the basis of the official surrender narrative. See [Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff).
