# Sgt. Lance Alfred

Sgt. Lance Alfred is identified as a spokesperson for the Washington County, Utah Sheriff's Office. He enters the record as an official source for public statements about the surrender of Tyler Robinson, the man charged in the September 10, 2025 shooting of Charlie Kirk. This page documents his public-affairs role only and alleges no wrongdoing.
