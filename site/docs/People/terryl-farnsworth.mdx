# Terryl Farnsworth

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

TPUSA AV/videographer who controlled audio & video at the UVU event.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Filmed a selfie video seconds after the shot saying "They shot Charlie. He's dead. God help him." while standing on rocks (instead of immediately aiding or calling 911, per critics). Removed SD cards from the camera behind Kirk's head (accused by Candace Owens and others of tampering with crime scene footage; TPUSA claimed police instructed it or to prevent theft). An old 2011 Facebook video surfaced showing him and friends acting out shooting scenes. Cousin (Michael Burt) reportedly linked to Tyler Robinson's defense team in some posts. Heavy focus in conspiracy discussions (Candace Owens highlighted as one of "10 TPUSA verifiable lies").

## Potential Evidentiary Value

AV footage handling, timeline of events, what cameras captured, and any audio/video anomalies.
