# Terryl Farnsworth

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Terryl Farnsworth is a living person. Social-media commentary has accused him of evidence tampering and raised questions about SD-card removal. This page reports those **attributed allegations** only. We do **not** assert that Farnsworth knew of any attack in advance or committed any crime.

## Investigative Value

**Terryl Farnsworth** is the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) **AV/videographer** who reportedly **controlled audio and video** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) courtyard event — the highest-leverage AV witness for chain-of-custody questions. Query 6 research groups him with [Anthony West](/People/anthony-west), [Robert Golo](/People/robert-golo), and [Jayden Wilson](/People/jayden-wilson) on the **sound/AV close-proximity** roster.

Farnsworth filmed a public selfie seconds after the shot saying **"They shot Charlie. He's dead. God help him."** while standing on rocks — a statement used in timeline reconstructions alongside master-file timestamps (00:52–01:06). Allegations that he **removed SD cards** from the camera behind Kirk's head are widely discussed in [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) threads and [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) pages; TPUSA has offered alternate explanations (police instruction or theft prevention). **No tampering finding is established here.**

According to the master file and Query 6 research:

- Farnsworth was **already in position filming** before the shot (timeline entry ~00:27).
- He recorded the widely circulated **post-shot selfie** declaration.
- He reportedly worked with [Phillip Goldsberry Jr.](/People/phillip-goldsberry-jr) on audio issues during the event.
- An old 2011 Facebook video surfaced showing him and friends acting out shooting scenes — **context cited in commentary**, not proof of wrongdoing.
- A cousin ([Michael Burt](/People/michael-burt)) was reportedly linked in some posts to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s defense team — **unverified association**.

## AV Footage & SD-Card Controversy

The camera behind Kirk's head is a focal point for investigators comparing official bullet narrative against [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) audio claims. What that camera recorded — and when its media was removed — intersects [Videos](/Videos/overview), [cameras](/cameras/overview), and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video).

Citizen investigators argue complete TPUSA AV logs and raw files should be released to test whether any edit or early removal obscured the true mechanism of injury. Farnsworth's FBI 302 report and SD-card chain of custody are central [Fix](/Fix/overview) disclosure items.

## Public Commentary (Attributed Allegations Only)

Candace Owens and other commentators highlighted Farnsworth among "TPUSA verifiable lies" lists. **We document allegations; we do not adopt them as findings.** Farnsworth remains a witness whose testimony and footage handling are investigative subjects — not a person convicted or charged on this page.
