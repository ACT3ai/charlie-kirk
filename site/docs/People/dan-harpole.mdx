Dan Harpole is a name that appears in compiled online commentary about the
emergency-medical response at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025.
According to that commentary, he is associated with a public remark about
whether EMS personnel were present at the scene.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to research aggregated on the Charlie Kirk investigation site, Dan
Harpole reportedly claimed that "EMS personnel were nowhere to be found" at the
scene. The same compiled notes state that this characterization is contradicted
by video evidence showing dedicated EMS crew members on scene. This is presented
as attributed commentary and a disputed statement, not as any allegation of
wrongdoing on Harpole's part.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Online commentary attributes to Dan Harpole a statement that EMS personnel
  were absent from the UVU scene.
- The same commentary asserts that video appears to show EMS crew members were
  present, framing his remark as mistaken rather than culpable.
- An innocent explanation is that any such comment could reflect an honest,
  vantage-limited impression rather than an attempt to mislead. None of this
  has been independently verified.
