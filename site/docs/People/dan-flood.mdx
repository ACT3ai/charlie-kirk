# Dan Flood

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Dan Flood** was **Head of Security** for [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and Charlie Kirk. He was in the **SUV response chain** on September 10, 2025 — among the first to shield Kirk after the shot alongside [Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole). Citizen investigators focus on Flood because he reportedly sat on the **death-threat message chain** the night before the assassination.

## Why This Person Matters

According to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) coverage (attributed, not independently verified), Charlie Kirk sent messages on **Signal/Telegram** the night before September 10 saying he thought he would be **"executed tomorrow"** — and Flood was reportedly among the recipients alongside [Andrew Kolvet](./andrew-kolvet). If authenticated, Flood's testimony would establish whether TPUSA security treated those warnings as actionable before the [UVU](/UVU/overview) event.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- In the **GMC Yukon Denali SUV** with Kirk during and after the shooting; jumped on Kirk to shield him with Harpole per eyewitness accounts.
- Responsible for coordinating **private security posture** at the event — a hybrid model with [UVU campus police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police) that [Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole) later criticized for **rooftop failures**.
- Commentary threads note Flood was **not** among early misidentified figures (bald/plaid/square-glasses variants in confused OSINT).

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

- **@CandaceOwens**: Displayed death-threat texts; Flood as security recipient; Erika-attributed variant that "The Left will kill me" per Owens reporting on a Flood message.
- **Cattle Barons Ball thread** (childhood-friend post cited in CK_FILE): Flood reportedly ran security for **Dallas billionaires** at a Texas gala while TPUSA had **no finalized plan** for Kirk's next event — timing contrast raised as a planning question, not a crime allegation.
- **Ray-Ban glasses recording** claim appears in research notes — unverified hardware detail.
