# Dan Flood

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

TPUSA Security Director

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Dan Flood was in the SUV with Charlie Kirk during and after the shooting. He jumped on Kirk to shield him from further harm alongside Brian Harpole. As TPUSA Security Director, he was responsible for coordinating the security posture at the event.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Flood's testimony would be critical for establishing the exact timeline of events inside the SUV, Kirk's condition immediately after being shot, security decisions made before and during the event, and his direct observations of the shooting and response. His role as Security Director means he would have knowledge of pre-event security planning and coordination with UVU police.
