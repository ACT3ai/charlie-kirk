# Dan Flood

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Dan Flood** is described in the investigation file and public reporting as **TPUSA's Head of Security** — the senior security director assigned to protect [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at Utah Valley University. He was one of five occupants in the black SUV that rushed Kirk to Timpanogos Regional Hospital and, according to [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)'s public accounts, **physically shielded Kirk's body** immediately after the shot. Flood also appears in pre-event **death-threat message** threads as a named recipient on Signal and Telegram. Investigators treat him as a **core timeline witness** for everything that happened inside the SUV and in security planning before the event.

## Background

Flood is identified in research notes and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) documentation as Charlie Kirk's **security director**, working under the hybrid model that combined [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) private close protection with UVU campus police for perimeter duties on September 10, 2025. Crowd-sourced footage descriptions (not official roster releases) characterize him in a **dark blue button-up shirt** and **Ray-Ban-style glasses**, with some threads speculating the glasses recorded video — an **unverified** equipment claim.

He is listed alongside [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) (Integrity Security Solutions / Rockhouse Integrity), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) as the **SUV immediate-response team**. See [Dan Flood — Head of Security](/Security_Team/dan-flood) for the parallel Security Team profile.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to Brian Harpole's interviews (Shawn Ryan Show, Salt Lake Tribune) and the master witness list in the investigation file:

- Flood was **in the SUV with Kirk** when the shooting occurred and during the emergency transport to Timpanogos Regional Hospital (~2.6 miles from UVU).
- He reportedly **jumped on top of Kirk** alongside Harpole to shield him from further harm immediately after the shot.
- As TPUSA Security Director, he would have held responsibility for coordinating the **private security posture** with campus law enforcement — a lighter hybrid setup than prior Kirk events, with **no bodycams** documented that day per the investigation file.
- [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has **reportedly** displayed texts in which Charlie Kirk told donors, TPUSA members, and Flood he feared he would be **"executed tomorrow"** the night before September 10. The file also records Signal/Telegram recipients including [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), with a related thread referencing [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) and a message that **"The Left will kill me"** — presented as **reported commentary**, not independently verified.
- Commentary threads distinguish Flood from **misidentified figures** in early X posts (bald man, plaid shirt, square-glasses man) — attributing those mis-IDs to crowd confusion, not to Flood's confirmed appearance.

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

Per posts compiled in the People grok fan-out (Query 5) and the investigation file:

- **@CandaceOwens:** Flood received **death-threat messages** alongside Kolvet; **"executed tomorrow"** recipient; **Cattle Barons Ball** Dallas billionaires moonlighting thread (childhood-friend post); Ray-Ban glasses recording claim; in-SUV shielding role; **not** the misidentified bald/plaid/square-glasses figures.
- **Cattle Barons Ball thread:** An anonymous post described as a **childhood friend** claimed the last time they saw Flood, he was "running security for some Dallas billionaires at the **Cattle Barons Ball**" — a Texas charity gala. The same commentary links Flood to claims his team had **no security plan** for Kirk's next scheduled stop (Therese Bible College, September 11). These raise **open questions** about scheduling and client load; **no conflict of interest has been established**.
- **June 2026 SUV interior photos:** Owens reportedly surfaced SUV interior images that other researchers tied to [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) shaped-charge debris hypotheses. Flood is named in seating-diagram threads but the site does **not** assert any criminal act by named SUV occupants.

## Investigative Questions

- What did Flood observe **inside the SUV** regarding Kirk's condition, sounds heard, and decisions made during transport?
- What **pre-event security planning** did TPUSA coordinate with UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long) — and what did Flood know about **rooftop sweep failures** Harpole later criticized publicly?
- What is the full content of Charlie's **Signal/Telegram messages** to Flood the night before the event?
- Did Flood have **other security clients** (e.g., Cattle Barons Ball commentary) that affected staffing or planning for September 10–11? **No allegation of wrongdoing** — question only.
- Will Flood's **FBI Form 302** (if released under investigation laws) corroborate or revise public SUV timeline accounts?
