Christopher D. Ballard (also referred to as **Christopher Ballard**) is a Deputy
Utah County Attorney identified on the prosecution team in **State of Utah v.
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)**, and also listed in some materials as General Counsel / Public
Information Officer.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Ballard is a named prosecutor whose **Notice of Appearance for the State** is
recorded in the case docket (noted as 9/30/25). In **June 2026**, according to
court coverage, Judge **[Tony F. Graf Jr.](/court/judge-tony-graf)** found Ballard in **civil contempt**
after media statements (reportedly to TMZ, Fox, and USA Today) describing
"ample evidence" of guilt, at a time when an **ATF ballistics result was
inconclusive**. Per the site's legal notes, the contempt finding addresses
**pretrial-publicity rules — not guilt or innocence** on the underlying charges,
and Ballard is **not accused of any crime**.

## Reported / Documented Facts

- **Court record:** June 2026 civil-contempt finding regarding pretrial media
  statements *(attribution: [AP News](https://apnews.com/article/7b3e65b0c7b12a06828e4f8f0337cb29),
  [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/government/utah-judge-finds-prosecutor-contempt-court-kirk-case-2026-06-26/)).*
- **Context:** A civil-contempt finding concerns compliance with publicity rules
  and is distinct from any allegation of criminal conduct.
