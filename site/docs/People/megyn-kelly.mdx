## Investigative Value

Megyn Kelly is a broadcaster and journalist. Her relevance to the investigation is as the host of an August 6, 2025 appearance in which Charlie Kirk reportedly spoke about pressure from donors — a data point cited on the timeline of Kirk's public statements in the weeks before his death. Her platform is treated as one of several pre-event public moments, not as any involvement in the event itself.

The appearance is referenced in the [Motive](/Motive/overview) and [Israel](/Israel/overview) threads, where commentators track Kirk's reported public break with pro-Israel donors.
