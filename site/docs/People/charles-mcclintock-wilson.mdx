# Charles McClintock Wilson

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Charles McClintock Wilson is a living person. This page documents his professional photojournalism role only. We do **not** assert that Wilson knew of any attack in advance or participated in any illegal act.

## Investigative Value

**Charles McClintock Wilson** is a **ZUMA Press** documentary photojournalist whose still images from the [UVU](/UVU/overview) event are among the most widely cited visual records of Charlie Kirk's **final moments** and the **immediate aftermath** of the September 10, 2025 shooting. Unlike TPUSA production cameras — some of which became the focus of SD-card and chain-of-custody disputes — Wilson's independent press work offers a parallel photographic timeline investigators can compare against [Videos](/Videos/overview), [Photos](/Photos/overview), and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video).

His frames are used in citizen timelines to anchor exact body position, crowd reaction, and post-shot scene geometry. Original full-resolution files with embedded EXIF metadata and sequential timestamps could be decisive for synchronizing photographic evidence with audio anomalies discussed under [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview). See also [Andrew Feraci](/People/andrew-feraci), the TPUSA staff photographer positioned feet from Kirk.

According to witness and media rosters from X.com research (Query 6, peopleglyph):

- Wilson reportedly **captured the last moments** of Charlie Kirk on stage and the chaos that followed.
- He attended with his **wife** and was **not previously familiar** with Kirk, per public commentary — suggesting routine press assignment rather than TPUSA insider access.
- His stills are **widely referenced** in investigation threads for timeline documentation and frame-by-frame analysis.
- His work sits alongside other visual documentarians at the event, including [Brayden Glascock](/People/brayden-glascock), [Mathew Franke](/People/mathew-franke), and [Moyo Dasaolu](/People/moyo-dasaolu).

## Photographic Documentation Value

Professional press photographers at breaking-news scenes typically shoot in burst sequences with time-stamped metadata. If preserved unedited, Wilson's complete ZUMA assignment folder could:

- Establish millisecond-level ordering between Kirk's final gestures and the audible shot.
- Show vantage points not covered by fixed [cameras](/cameras/overview) or TPUSA production angles.
- Provide independent corroboration for [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) statements about crowd movement after the impact.

The investigation file treats Wilson's images as **documentary evidence**, not proof of any particular shooter theory.
