# Charles McClintock Wilson

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

ZUMA Press/documentary photojournalist

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Captured the last moments and aftermath of Charlie Kirk. Brought wife; not previously familiar with Kirk. Photos widely referenced for investigation/timeline.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Photographic documentation of exact moments before/during/after the shot.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* His original full-resolution unedited photo files and the embedded metadata and timestamps on those images and his FBI Form 302 interview report are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
