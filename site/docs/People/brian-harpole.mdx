# Brian Harpole

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Brian Harpole** was Charlie Kirk's **security chief** and founder of **Rockhouse Integrity / Integrity Security Solutions**. He was **in the SUV** when Kirk was shot at [UVU](/UVU/overview) on September 10, 2025, reported hearing **"two distinct sounds,"** and shielded Kirk with [Dan Flood](./dan-flood). His public interviews became a primary source on **rooftop security failures** — and a counterweight to online conspiracy theories he explicitly refuted.

## Why This Person Matters

The official narrative places the shooter on the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop** for nearly an hour. Harpole had **texted UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](./jeff-long)** before the event about rooftop concerns, then criticized the department after the shooting for not delivering the promised sweep. His account bridges **private TPUSA security** and **campus police gaps** documented in [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police).

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- **SUV eyewitness**: Heard two sounds at the shot; shielded Kirk with Flood; firsthand transport timeline to [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital).
- **Pre-event warnings**: Texted Long about rooftop security per Shawn Ryan Show and Salt Lake Tribune reporting cited in citizen threads.
- **Public pushback**: Refuted conspiracy theories alleging TPUSA orchestrated the killing — while still criticizing UVU police coordination.
- **Security model**: CK_FILE notes private security that day was **not** Shaffer Security Group (Greg Schaffer alumni); **no bodycams** on the detail.
