# Brian Harpole

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Charlie Kirk's security chief and head of contracted private security (Integrity Security Solutions / Rockhouse Integrity)

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Brian Harpole was in the SUV with Charlie Kirk when the shooting occurred. He reported hearing "two distinct sounds" when the shot was fired. He jumped on Kirk alongside Dan Flood to shield him from further harm. In subsequent interviews (Shawn Ryan Show, Salt Lake Tribune), Harpole publicly criticized UVU police for failing to secure rooftops as they had promised. He had texted UVU Police Chief Jeff Long about rooftop security concerns prior to the event. Harpole has also publicly refuted online conspiracy theories regarding the shooting.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Harpole's testimony is critical for establishing security planning failures, the nature and extent of police coordination (or lack thereof) with UVU, the exact timeline and positions of personnel, and his firsthand account of the shooting and immediate response. His pre-event communications with UVU Police Chief Jeff Long regarding rooftop concerns are potentially key evidence of foreknowledge of vulnerabilities.
