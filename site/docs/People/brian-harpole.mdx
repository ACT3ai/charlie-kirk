# Brian Harpole

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Brian Harpole** was [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s **security chief** on September 10, 2025 — head of contracted private protection through **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC (DBA Integrity Security Solutions)**. He was **in the SUV** when Kirk was shot, reported hearing **"two distinct sounds,"** and **shielded Kirk's body** with [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood). Harpole then became one of the most **public-facing critics** of UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)'s **rooftop security failures**, citing **pre-event texts** he sent Long about roof concerns. He has also **publicly refuted conspiracy theories** about the shooting. **This page does not assert Harpole knew of any assassination plan or committed any wrongdoing.**

## Background

Harpole is a **former police officer** who founded Integrity Security Solutions / Rockhouse Integrity. The investigation file places his company — not Shaffer Security Group — as the **September 10 vendor**, in a hybrid model with UVU campus police (~six officers, ~25% of department) and **no bodycams** documented that day.

He is linked historically to **Shaffer Security Group** alumni networks ([Greg Schaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer)) but the file states SSG **had not worked with TPUSA since 2022** while familiar protective-detail styling still appeared in crowd footage. See [Security Team — Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security).

His father **Bobby Harpole** appears in Security Team documentation as a related industry figure — family context only, not an event-day assignment claim on this page.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to Harpole's public interviews and the investigation file:

- He was **in the black SUV** with Kirk immediately after the ~12:23 PM shooting alongside Flood, [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek).
- He described hearing **"two distinct sounds"** when the shot occurred — a detail cited in [Mic](/Mic/overview) and ballistics commentary as potentially significant. **Interpretation disputed**; treated here as **his attributed observation**.
- He and Flood **jumped on Kirk** to shield him from further harm during transport.
- Cutler reportedly **held Harpole's shirt** to keep him stable in the cabin while Harpole administered **first aid** en route to Timpanogos Regional Hospital.
- Before the event, Harpole **texted UVU Police Chief Jeff Long** about **rooftop security concerns** — texts referenced in Shawn Ryan Show and Salt Lake Tribune coverage as evidence vulnerabilities were flagged.
- In those interviews, Harpole **criticized UVU police** for **not securing rooftops as promised** despite crowd size exceeding estimates — the shooter used a **Losee Center rooftop** line of fire.
- He has **publicly refuted** online conspiracy narratives about the event and security team conduct.
