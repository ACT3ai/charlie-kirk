# Brian Harpole

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, ethical violation, or participation in any crime by Brian Harpole or any other living person. This site documents questions and claims that have circulated in public commentary — not findings of fact. All persons named here are presumed innocent. Allegations referenced here are unproven and have not been established in any court.
:::

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Brian Harpole** was [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s **security chief** on September 10, 2025 — head of contracted private protection through **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC (DBA Integrity Security Solutions)**. He was **in the SUV** when Kirk was shot, reported hearing **"two distinct sounds,"** and **shielded Kirk's body** with [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood). Harpole then became one of the most **public-facing critics** of UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)'s **rooftop security failures**, citing **pre-event texts** he sent Long about roof concerns. He has also **publicly refuted conspiracy theories** about the shooting. **This page does not assert Harpole knew of any assassination plan or committed any wrongdoing.**

## Background

Harpole is a **former police officer** who founded Integrity Security Solutions / Rockhouse Integrity. The investigation file places his company — not Shaffer Security Group — as the **September 10 vendor**, in a hybrid model with UVU campus police (~six officers, ~25% of department) and **no bodycams** documented that day.

He is linked historically to **Shaffer Security Group** alumni networks ([Greg Schaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer)) but the file states SSG **had not worked with TPUSA since 2022** while familiar protective-detail styling still appeared in crowd footage. See [Security Team — Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security).

His father **[Bobby Harpole](/Security_Team/bobby-harpole)** appears in Security Team documentation as a related industry figure — family context only, not an event-day assignment claim on this page.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to Harpole's public interviews and the investigation file:

- He was **in the black SUV** with Kirk immediately after the ~12:23 PM shooting alongside Flood, [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek).
- He described hearing **"two distinct sounds"** when the shot occurred — a detail cited in [Mic](/Mic/overview) and ballistics commentary as potentially significant. **Interpretation disputed**; treated here as **his attributed observation**.
- He and Flood **jumped on Kirk** to shield him from further harm during transport.
- Cutler reportedly **held Harpole's shirt** to keep him stable in the cabin while Harpole administered **first aid** en route to Timpanogos Regional Hospital.
- Before the event, Harpole **texted UVU Police Chief Jeff Long** about **rooftop security concerns** — texts referenced in Shawn Ryan Show and Salt Lake Tribune coverage as evidence vulnerabilities were flagged.
- In those interviews, Harpole **criticized UVU police** for **not securing rooftops as promised** despite crowd size exceeding estimates — the shooter used a **Losee Center rooftop** line of fire.
- He has **publicly refuted** online conspiracy narratives about the event and security team conduct.

## Harpole v. Owens Defamation Suit (Reported)

Citizen investigators and legal-watch accounts on X have tracked a **civil defamation action** captioned roughly as **Harpole v. Owens** (Middle District of Tennessee, docket references such as **3:26-cv-00556** appear in public posts). According to those posts:

- Harpole **allegedly** sued [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), associated companies, and others over claims that he conspired in Kirk's assassination — claims this site **does not adopt** as findings.
- In **July 2026**, accounts including **@inion_De_1893** and **@MargoinWNC** reported that **all district and magistrate judges** in the Middle District of Tennessee **recused**, with the **Sixth Circuit** expected to assign an out-of-district judge. Recusal orders and case status should be verified against PACER/court records; social-media summaries can be incomplete.
- Posts also referenced motions to **preserve electronic communications** on multiple platforms. Preservation fights are common in high-profile defamation suits and do not, by themselves, prove any underlying criminal theory.

**Counter-narrative on X:** Accounts such as **@mandasue85** argue the shooter is [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) and reject lists that name Harpole, TPUSA, or [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk). Those posts are also **opinion**, not court findings.

## Rooftop-Text Debate on X (Both Sides)

- **[@ShawnRyanShow](/Influencers/podcasts-shawn-ryan)** (Nov 2025) amplified Harpole's attributed line that security told UVU's chief about student roof access and was told **"I got you covered."**
- **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** and allied threads argue the same text chain **undercuts** a pure "police-only rooftops" narrative and instead raises questions about **whether private security failed to staff roofs after authorization** — framed by those posters as negligence. **No court has found criminal negligence** against Harpole or Integrity Security.
- Other posters (**@The1stIvoryDove**, **[@BDPelton](/Influencers/x/bdpelton)**) emphasize UVU's failure to secure rooftops and ask whether Harpole **verified** the campus commitment — again as commentary, not adjudication.

Readers should treat text-message screenshots and podcast clips as **primary materials to evaluate**, not as substitute for a completed security after-action report. See [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long) and [Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures).

## Google Image Search Claim (July 2026)

In **July 2026**, [Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters) claimed that **Google image results for Harpole's name
disappeared** the day after his show discussed [Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) — reporting
that his name, a **misspelling probe**, and the exact-match string `"Brian Harpole security"` all returned nothing,
while people the show had **not** named still appeared.

**Harpole was searched for by other people. That is not an accusation and implies nothing about him.** The claim is
about **Google's results**, not his conduct — and if he or anyone else **requested removal of their own imagery**,
that is an ordinary right that would produce the identical result. Full detail and the counterarguments:
