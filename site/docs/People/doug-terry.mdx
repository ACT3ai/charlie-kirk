Doug Terry is a Utah attorney whose name appears in the record of the criminal case arising from the September 10, 2025 shooting at Utah Valley University. According to court materials, the accused, [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), named an attorney by this name when invoking his right to counsel.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to a defense motion reproduced in this site's timeline materials, during the Miranda encounter (recorded at roughly 6:26 PM, September 11, 2025), Robinson stated:

> "Uh, I would like to speak with my, uh, legal counsel. I'm hoping for a fellow in Utah named Doug Terry."

This is a statement attributed to the defendant, not an assertion by prosecutors. Being named by an arrestee as requested counsel does not, by itself, establish any prior attorney-client relationship or any connection to the underlying events.

## Background

Public information identifies the name as belonging to a Utah attorney. Further biographical detail is omitted where it cannot be reliably confirmed, and it is not established that the individual Robinson referred to ultimately represented him.
