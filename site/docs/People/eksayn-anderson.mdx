> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** EksAyn Anderson is treated as a living person on this page. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**EksAyn Anderson** is on the Query 6 list of **general witness accounts** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) courtyard event. Attendee witnesses collectively help investigators test claims about crowd reaction, evacuation paths, and whether the shot sounded like a rifle — themes central to [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and shaped-charge commentary.

Anderson appears alongside [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak), [Henry Dells](/People/henry-dells), and [Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown) in related-people grids. Any retained phone video could be compared with [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) and official [Videos](/Videos/overview) releases.
