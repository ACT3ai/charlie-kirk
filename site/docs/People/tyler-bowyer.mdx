# Tyler Bowyer

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to Turning Point USA and to discussions surrounding the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Tyler Bowyer** is the **chief operating officer of Turning Point Action / [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview)** and a longtime Arizona Republican operative who serves as a **Republican National Committeeman for Arizona**. For nearly a decade he has been one of the organization's most senior executives and a close professional associate of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).

Because of that seniority, his name recurs across several strands of the investigation — organizational, financial, security-related, and a circulated social-media exchange. This page separates two very different kinds of material: his **documented, legitimate role** at Turning Point (verifiable and sourced), and a set of **attributed, unproven items** raised by independent investigators and commentators. **No wrongdoing by Mr. Bowyer in connection with the events of September 10, 2025 is alleged or established anywhere on this page.**

In it, according to the screenshot, **[Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)** described being sent by the military *"down to [Ft Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) couple times for training,"* and **Tyler Bowyer** replied: *"jack stop going to huachuca."* Bowyer's reply is the circled portion of the circulated image.

The exchange reads as **banter** between two public figures; nothing in it connects Bowyer to the events of September 10, 2025. ## Role at Turning Point USA

Bowyer's connection to Charlie Kirk is long-standing and a matter of public record. The timeline below is drawn from Turning Point's own biographies and mainstream reporting.

- **Met Kirk in 2014.** According to [reporting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turning_Point_Action), the two met in 2014 while Bowyer was **chairman of the Maricopa County Republican Committee** in Arizona.
- **Joined TPUSA in 2015** and rose through the organization to become its **chief operating officer in 2017**, overseeing the field program and large-scale activist events (per [TPUSA](https://tpusa.com/staff/?uid=1440) and [Turning Point Action](https://www.tpaction.com/bio/tylerbowyer)).
- **In 2022 he shifted to full-time COO of Turning Point Action** — TPUSA's political arm — and helped launch the **Turning Point PAC** with Kirk.
- He serves as an elected **Republican National Committeeman for Arizona** and is described in reporting as a **7th-generation Arizonan** with a long history in state Republican politics, including county-chair and precinct-committeeman roles.

As Turning Point Action's chief operating officer, Bowyer is effectively the organization's top day-to-day operating executive beneath its founder — which is why questions about the organization's [structure, finances, security, and succession](/TPUSA/overview) after Kirk's death repeatedly surface his name.

According to a screenshot circulated on X on July 11, 2026 by **[@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles)**, Tyler Bowyer wrote in a post dated **September 18, 2025** — eight days after the assassination — describing how Turning Point handles event security:

> "We do have a team. We can only do as much as police on campus will allow and also working with the police or private security in a given venue.
>
> We run everything like a military op, all of our people are from that background."

The post visible in the screenshot shows 9 replies, 10 reposts, 61 likes, and 4.4K views. **[@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles)** asks whether Bowyer subsequently deleted the post; this site cannot independently confirm the deletion. The quote is documented here because commentators have highlighted the phrase *"all of our people are from that [military] background"* in the context of the case's [intelligence-services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview) and [day-of security](/Charlie/Day_Of_Security) themes. It is Bowyer's own reported description of TPUSA's security operation and is **not, by itself, evidence of any wrongdoing** in connection with September 10, 2025.

## Appearances in Investigation Discussions (Attributed / Unproven)

Bowyer's name appears in several strands of investigation commentary. **In every case these are reported claims and open questions — no wrongdoing by Mr. Bowyer is alleged or established:**

- **DOGE / internal-audit claims:** Per commentary catalogued in the [Motive](/Motive/overview) section, some accounts assert Bowyer *"reportedly would have been moved aside 8 days before"* the assassination in connection with an internal financial audit. This is **reported commentary, unverified**.
- **Flight-tracking discussion:** The tail number [N40JD](/Planes/N40JD) has, *"according to commentary circulated on X,"* been connected by some independent investigators to Bowyer. The [N40JD page](/Planes/N40JD) states plainly that *"there is no evidence in the public record tying Mr. Bowyer, the aircraft, its crew, or its passengers to the event, and no wrongdoing by him is alleged or established."*
- **Superfeed / data claims:** Certain compilations referenced in the [Cover-Up](/CoverUp/overview) material name Bowyer among figures tied to a data firm; those materials **explicitly note no wrongdoing** by him is alleged.
- **Fake-electors case:** Bowyer was **indicted on nine charges by an Arizona grand jury on April 24, 2024** in the state's 2020 alternate-elector case, and **pleaded not guilty** at a May 21, 2024 arraignment ([reporting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arizona_prosecution_of_fake_electors)). In **May 2025**, a Maricopa County judge **sent the case back to the grand jury** after finding prosecutors had not shown grand jurors the full text of the Electoral Count Act. In **November 2025**, President Trump **pardoned** Bowyer and ten other Arizona figures; reporting notes that a presidential pardon reaches federal exposure and that the status of the **state** charges brought by Arizona Attorney General Kris Mayes was still being litigated ([Arizona Mirror](https://azmirror.com/2025/11/10/trump-pardons-11-arizona-fake-electors-as-attorney-general-faces-deadline-to-refile-her-case/)). This is a **separate legal matter unrelated to the Charlie Kirk case** — Bowyer has contested the charges and there has been **no conviction**. It is noted only for completeness, not as evidence of anything regarding September 10, 2025.

## The DOGE Audit and the "Moved Aside" Claim

One of the most-cited threads connecting Bowyer's name to the timing of the assassination is a **governance-friction** theory — a *non-violent* motive narrative that remains **unverified**.

According to commentary circulated by **[@RealGeorgeWebb1](https://x.com/RealGeorgeWebb1/status/1990483831238037771)** and echoed in the [Motive](/Motive/overview) material, some accounts assert that Charlie Kirk had **"demanded a DOGE audit days before he died,"** that Bowyer *"reportedly would have been moved aside about 8 days before"* the assassination in connection with that internal financial review, and that a **September 2, 2025 memo named Justin Streiff as a new COO**. Investigation notes summarize the framing bluntly: *"Killing Charlie eight days after he moved Bowyer out for not doing the DOGE outside audit is a big deal."*

**None of this is established.** It is a chain of attributed claims — the existence of the memo, the reason for any personnel change, and any link between an internal audit dispute and the shooting are all **unconfirmed**. It is catalogued here as one of the open, non-violent motive threads on the review list, not as a finding, and **no wrongdoing by Mr. Bowyer is alleged**.

## The TPUSA Drone-Footage Contradiction

Independent commentators have highlighted an apparent **contradiction between public statements that drones were barred at the UVU event and later-released TPUSA footage.** After September 10, some Turning Point representatives said campus police did not allow preventive drones (see [UVU Security & No Drones](/Drones/UVU_Security_No_Drones)); yet TPUSA's own **October 14, 2025 birthday-tribute video** for Kirk reportedly included overhead aerial/drone-style shots from the event (see [TPUSA Production Drone](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone)).

Circulated posts framed this pointedly at Bowyer — one widely shared item was headlined *"TPUSA's Tyler Bowyer DOUBLY EXPOSED — Birthday Vid DROPS DRONE FOOTAGE."* Those posts also allege that TPUSA has withheld fuller camera feeds, citing an FBI request not to release footage during the investigation.

This is **attributed commentary**, not an established finding. The apparent discrepancy is a **documented open question** about TPUSA's public messaging and footage release; it is **not evidence** that Bowyer had any role in the assassination, and Turning Point may have legitimate explanations (different drone operators, media-production vs. security drones, or investigative constraints). It is logged for transparency and cross-referenced with the [Drones](/Drones/overview) and [Cover-Up](/CoverUp/overview) sections.

## Superfeed Technologies Board

A separate financial thread notes that **Erika Kirk's mother, [Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve), was reportedly placed on the board of a data firm, Superfeed Technologies, alongside Turning Point Action COO Tyler Bowyer.** One circulated post claims *"money from TP Action has been routed into Superfeed"* and describes the two as **"effectively business partners."**

These are **unverified allegations from social-media commentary**, referenced in the [Cover-Up](/CoverUp/overview) material. The underlying facts — board membership, any fund transfers, and their purpose — are **not independently confirmed here**, and the source material **explicitly notes no wrongdoing** by Mr. Bowyer is established. It is included as an OSINT item to be checked against corporate filings, not as a finding.

## Public Remembrances and Denials

For balance, Bowyer's **public conduct after the assassination** is also part of the record and cuts against the theories above:

- He gave **on-the-record interviews remembering Charlie Kirk**, including to Arizona outlet [KTAR](https://ktar.com/arizona-news/tyler-bowyer-remembers-charlie-kirk/5750465/) and national programs, and publicly recounted Turning Point's early interest in **hiring Erika Kirk**.
- Press items and X discussion note that Bowyer **refuted the theory that TPUSA approved the UVU stop as part of a murder plot**, pointing to an earlier-July approval timeline for the event.
- As COO he has continued in his operating role through the organization's transition, during which **Erika Kirk** stepped into leadership of Turning Point.

These statements are his own public positions and are presented so readers see **both** the attributed suspicions **and** Bowyer's documented responses to them.
