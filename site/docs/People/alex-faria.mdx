# Alex Faria

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Alex Faria is a living person. We document only his reported role in the security/carry response. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Alex Faria** is grouped in the investigation file among the **security / close-proximity** personnel who **helped carry Charlie Kirk** away from the stage after the shot, alongside [Laine Schoenberg](/People/laine-schoenberg) and "Blake (aka Gibby)" — cross-referenced in the master file as likely [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff). Per event-footage analyses and X posts cited in the file, Faria is described as one of the responders with **visible blood on his clothing**, indicating direct physical contact during the emergency response.

The master file lists Faria on a proposed **defense witness roster** for the [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) trial — the file argues that "if Tyler Robinson's defence team doesn't call up" the SUV and carry-team members "I don't believe they're offering the best defence available." As a hands-on responder, Faria's account bears on positioning, sequence, and the handling of Kirk in the seconds after the shot discussed under [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Medical](/Medical/overview).

- He is distinguished from the five-man **SUV group** ([Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)) — he is in the **carry cohort** instead.
- Mainstream coverage naming him specifically remains **limited**; he appears mostly in citizen-response analyses.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As a direct eyewitness to the shooting, response, and physical handling/carrying of Kirk, Faria's testimony would establish positioning, sequence of events, and observations during the immediate aftermath. Forensic testing of the **blood on his clothing** and any phone or camera footage from the carry could corroborate or clarify the response timeline.
