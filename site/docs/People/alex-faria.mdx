# Alex Faria

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Security/close proximity team member

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Alex Faria was part of the team that helped carry Charlie Kirk after the shooting. Visible blood on his clothing has been noted in event footage analyses and X posts, indicating direct physical contact during the emergency response.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As a direct eyewitness to the shooting, response, and physical handling/carrying of Kirk, Faria's testimony would establish positioning, sequence of events, and observations during the immediate aftermath.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* His FBI Form 302 interview report and the carry-team's phone and camera footage from the transport and forensic testing of the blood on his clothing are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
