# Alex Faria

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Alex Faria** is listed in the investigation file and security witness rosters as a **close-proximity team member** who reportedly **helped carry [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** from the stage area immediately after the September 10, 2025 shooting. Event footage analyses and X posts note **visible blood on his clothing**, indicating direct physical contact during the emergency response. Faria is part of the **carry team** alongside [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff) ("Gibby") and [Laine Schoenberg](/People/laine-schoenberg) — figures whose positioning and clothing may hold **forensic and timeline value** if testimony or DNA testing is ever released.

## Background

Faria appears in the master witness list under the **security / close proximity** grouping — distinct from the five-person **SUV core** ([Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)) but part of the same [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security) protective footprint described for the UVU event.

Limited mainstream news biographical detail is summarized in the file; his investigative significance rests on **eyewitness proximity** and **documented blood transfer** visible in public footage rather than on published interviews.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to the investigation file and grok-compiled security threads:

- Faria was part of the team that **helped carry Kirk** from the courtyard stage zone after the ~12:23 PM shot.
- **Visible blood on his clothing** has been noted in event footage analyses and X posts — consistent with direct handling of Kirk's wound during the carry sequence.
- He is grouped with [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff) and [Laine Schoenberg](/People/laine-schoenberg) in defense-witness lists arguing the full carry team should be available for formal testimony.
- The file does not attribute major independent conspiracy accusations to Faria beyond his documented **physical response role**.

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

Per posts compiled in the People grok fan-out (Query 5) and investigation-file footage breakdowns:

- **Carry team with blood visible:** Faria named alongside Blake Haruff ("Gibby") as responders with **significant blood** on clothing in chaos-aftermath clips.
- **Footage identification threads:** Citizen investigators use clothing markers and positioning to map who reached Kirk first versus who joined the carry to the SUV — Faria appears in those **attributed visual analyses**, not official charge documents.
- **Forensic speculation:** Commentary asks whether blood on carry-team clothing was **tested or preserved** — the file lists untested DNA/blood as a potential [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure item. **No allegation** that Faria tampered with evidence.
- **Lower accusatory volume:** Compared to [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) or [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), Faria attracts **less** direct conspiracy targeting in compiled grok results — focus stays on **witness value**.

## Investigative Questions

- What was Faria's **exact position** before the shot and during the carry to the SUV?
- What did he **see and hear** in the first 30–60 seconds after the shooting?
- Was blood on his clothing **collected or tested** by investigators — and are chain-of-custody records public?
- Does Faria's account **align or conflict** with Haruff, Schoenberg, and SUV-team timelines?
- Will an **FBI Form 302** or sworn statement surface under investigation-law disclosures?
