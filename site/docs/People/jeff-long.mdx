# Jeff Long

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Jeff Long** was **Chief of UVU Police** during the Charlie Kirk event on September 10, 2025. Investigators cite his department's **failure to secure rooftops** — especially the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) edge where charging documents place the shooter — as a central security gap. [Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole) reportedly texted Long about rooftop concerns **before** the event, then criticized the outcome publicly.

## Why This Person Matters

The charging narrative depends on a shooter reaching a **rooftop overlook** for nearly an hour. If campus police did not sweep that roof despite promises and pre-event warnings, the question becomes whether the failure was **resource limits**, **coordination breakdown** with [TPUSA private security](/Security_Team/overview), or something else — without implying Long intended harm.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- Led a **small campus force** (~six officers, ~25% staffing cited in commentary) at a high-profile outdoor event.
- **No rooftop sweep** documented in threads Harpole amplified post-shooting.
- **Drone sweep failure** claims appear in [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police) material — attributed commentary.
- Hybrid security: TPUSA private detail plus lighter campus police footprint than some prior Kirk events per CK_FILE research notes.

## Investigative Questions

- What did Long's department promise Harpole in pre-event texts?
- Why was the Losee rooftop accessible during the event window?
- Departure/resignation timing relative to [UVU President Astrid Tuminez](/Law_Enforcement/official-resignations) resignation threads.
