# Andrea Burkhart

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Burkhart's role here is as a **publisher and analyst of source documents**, not as a subject of the investigation. Several pages on this site reference documents hosted on her Substack (for example, the "Charlie Kirk legal documents" post) when discussing ballistics and forensics questions.
