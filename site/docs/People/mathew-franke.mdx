# Mathew Franke

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Mathew Franke is a living person. We document his reported videography role only. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Mathew Franke** is on the Query 6 videographer/photographer roster for the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event — described as a **UVU review videographer and content creator** in **close proximity** to the courtyard stage. Campus media witnesses collectively provide alternate angles to TPUSA-controlled feeds and institutional surveillance discussed under [cameras](/cameras/overview).

Franke is listed with [Moyo Dasaolu](/People/moyo-dasaolu) and [Brayden Glascock](/People/brayden-glascock). His footage and eyewitness account, if obtained, could support or challenge [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) claims about shot sound and immediate crowd motion, and could be compared with [Videos](/Videos/overview) and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video).
