# Pierce Beck

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Security in close proximity, member of Shaffer Security Group with executive protection experience

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to citizen-investigator footage breakdowns compiled in the master investigation file, Pierce Beck appears in footage running back toward the scene after the shooting, and has been identified as a **bearded figure in a striped shirt** in those analyses. Research notes also place Beck among the **security detail in close proximity** to Charlie Kirk, listing him with [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis) as "other people in the security company" — with the open question of whether they were on-site that specific day still noted in the file.

Some research notes speculate that Beck may be the person referred to in commentary as "the Blake with blood on him who helped carry Kirk," though this identification is **unconfirmed** and treated in the file as an open question, not a finding.

## Shaffer Security Group Background

Beck is described as a member of **Shaffer Security Group** with **executive protection experience**. That professional background distinguishes his page from the general-attendee witness roster: an experienced protective agent would be trained to read perimeters, sightlines, and threat posture. His observations — if obtained through sworn testimony — could speak to how the UVU courtyard security was arranged relative to the [Losee Center rooftop](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) firing position discussed elsewhere on this site.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Beck's testimony would establish security positioning prior to the shot and his immediate response actions. His executive protection background means he would have professional observations about the security environment and any anomalies. Compare his vantage point with that of [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), and [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), who were inside the SUV during the emergency transport.

**Status:** Alive
