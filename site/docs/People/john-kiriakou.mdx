## What is reported

- On the Julian Dorey Podcast, John Kiriakou reportedly described a CIA MK-Ultra experiment in which a "walk-in" source in London was placed under hypnosis by agency psychologists and a licensed hypnotist, and was reportedly controlled to the point of keeping an arm raised through a roughly 3-hour session while revealing details he could not consciously recall.
- This account describes a **historical program**, and there is a minor discrepancy in the recalled duration; no public reporting has been found tying Kiriakou to this specific claim in connection with the Kirk case.
- **Counterpoint:** This is offered strictly as **historical background** on documented government capabilities. Nothing connects Kiriakou or the MK-Ultra material to the actual events at UVU; the relevance is thematic and speculative, not evidentiary.
