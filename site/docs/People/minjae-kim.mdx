# Minjae Kim

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Minjae Kim is treated as a living person on this page. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Minjae Kim** is listed among **general witness accounts** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) courtyard event (Query 6, peopleglyph). Kim appears in the same attendee cluster as [Michael Dutsun](/People/michael-dutsun), [Jeremy King](/People/jeremy-king), and [Henry Dells](/People/henry-dells). Collective attendee testimony helps investigators test whether official [Videos](/Videos/overview) and [cameras](/cameras/overview) releases match what multiple independent observers saw and heard.

Any FBI 302 entry or retained phone footage from Kim could be synchronized with [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) front-row audio claims and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video). Roster placement is documentary only — not an accusation.
