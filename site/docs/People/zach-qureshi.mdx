# Zach Qureshi

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or participation in any crime by Zach Qureshi or any other living person. He was reportedly detained and released and was **never charged** in connection with the shooting. All persons named are presumed innocent. Claims that have circulated in public commentary are unproven and have not been established in any court.

**Zachariah "Zach" Qureshi** is a young man whose name surfaced in the first hours after the September 10, 2025 shooting at [Utah Valley University](/campus_university/overview) because, according to circulated reports, he was **detained near the event and then released**. He was **never charged** as the shooter. He is the **brother of [Zane Qureshi](./zane-qureshi)** and a **son of [Ahmed Qureshi](./ahmed-qureshi)**. The image at right accompanied a July 18, 2026 X post that named both brothers.

## Names he goes by

Public commentary refers to him under several spellings and forms. To keep the site to **one profile for one person**, all of these point here:

- **Zachariah Ahmed Qureshi** — the fullest form seen in public discussion.
- **Zachariah Qureshi**, **Zach Qureshi**, **Zach Kureshi**, **Zach Quershi** — common short and misspelled variants seen in posts and scanner discussion.
- His public **LinkedIn** handle is reported as `zach-qureshi`.

Other **unverified "possible name" guesses** have been floated in early threads. Those are speculative and are **not** treated as confirmed identities here.

## Background as reported

These biographical details come from social-media summaries and a report attributed to journalist Karol Markowicz. They are **not independently confirmed** here:

- A man in his **mid-twenties**, described as an **Arabic speaker**.
- Reported work as a **finance/VC analyst** and a past **Heritage Foundation** association.
- Reported ties to **Utah** (Salt Lake City), with schooling references including **Utah State University** and **BYU** in various threads.

## Where he was and what happened

The verifiable, non-accusatory arc — the part favorable to Qureshi — is that he was **reportedly detained and then released, and never charged as the shooter**:

- Commentary referencing local press describes him as **detained at or near the event** on September 10, 2025, then **released**, with a brief hold connected to an **obstruction** question.
- Federal leadership reportedly named a suspect **"in custody"** and then stepped that back roughly **90 minutes later**. Whether Qureshi was the person referenced in those earliest statements is **not established**. Commentators (including Brig. Gen. Blaine Holt) have asked whether that 90-minute window slowed the manhunt — an **open question, not a proven fact**. See the detailed [FBI "in custody" page](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi).
- His name also appears in a **police-scanner "hospital to hospital" story** whose details (vehicle color, who went where, whether anyone did) reportedly **keep changing**. The full breakdown, with attribution, lives on the [Distraction People profile](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi). None of it establishes that Qureshi did anything wrong.

## Family

- **Father:** [Ahmed Qureshi](./ahmed-qureshi) — described in commentary as a U.S. Navy Captain (O-6) and co-founder of Bilt Inc. Inclusion is **context only**; no wrongdoing is asserted.
- **Brother:** [Zane Qureshi](./zane-qureshi) — named alongside Zach in the July 18, 2026 X post below.
- There is a **third brother** referenced in public commentary, and a father who anchors the family; a fuller family page may be added over time.

## Status

**Status:** Alive.
