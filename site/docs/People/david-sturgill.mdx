David Sturgill is identified in court and investigation material as a Deputy
Utah County Attorney listed among the prosecution team in the criminal case
arising from the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at Utah Valley
University.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to the Charlie Kirk investigation site's court records, Sturgill
appears as one of the deputy county attorneys named in prosecution materials for
the State of Utah v. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) case, alongside others such as [Chad
Grunander](/People/chad-e-grunander), [Ryan McBride](/People/ryan-mcbride), [Lauren Hunt](/People/lauren-hunt), and [Christopher Ballard](/People/christopher-d-ballard). This is an
ordinary prosecutorial role in a publicly filed criminal matter and carries no
implication of wrongdoing.

## Background

Sturgill is described in the compiled materials as holding the title of Deputy
Utah County Attorney. Beyond that official designation, further biographical
detail is not established in the investigation material.
