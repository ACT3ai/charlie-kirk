# Josh Hammer

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Josh Hammer or any other living person. Hammer is **not accused** of involvement in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death. **Josh Hammer** is a conservative legal analyst, commentator, and opinion editor at Newsweek. He is an Orthodox Jew and a prominent pro-Israel voice in American conservative media. His name is among the most frequently cited in commentary about the reported **Hamptons intervention** of early August 2025.

## Background

### Education and Legal Career
Hammer holds a B.A. from Duke University and a J.D. from the University of Chicago Law School. He clerked on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit before moving into media and commentary.

### Media Career
Hammer served as an editor at large for The Daily Wire before becoming the opinion editor at Newsweek (starting around 2020-2021). He is a senior editor-at-large at Newsweek, hosts a podcast, and is a regular commentator across conservative media. He has affiliations with the Edmund Burke Foundation and the national conservatism movement.

### Stance on Israel
Hammer has consistently advocated for strong U.S.-Israel relations, defended Israeli military operations, and publicly debated figures on the right who question U.S. foreign policy toward Israel.

## Connection to the Investigation

### TPUSA Debate with Dave Smith (July 13, 2025)
Hammer debated Dave Smith (libertarian comedian and podcaster) at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Florida, on **July 13, 2025**. The debate centered on U.S. foreign policy toward Israel and non-interventionism. Commentary threads (including **[@based_sam_parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)**) treat this as a significant moment in the **intra-right policy debate** Charlie Kirk was navigating — part of the same mid-2025 pivot that triggered **donor revolt** narratives.

### July 20 AMFEST Military/Intel Meeting
A **July 20, 2025** AMFEST military/intelligence meeting **reportedly preceded** the Hamptons gathering by approximately **four days**, per commentary compiled in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons). That session sits in the pre-UVU timeline alongside the **July 31 White House** strategy meeting (Kirk and TPUSA **reportedly absent**) and the **August 4–6 Hamptons intervention**.

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to multiple compiled sources and X/Twitter threads:

- Hammer was one of several pro-Israel figures **reportedly** present at a meeting in the Hamptons in **early August 2025** where Charlie Kirk's evolving stance on Israel was discussed.
- Other **allegedly** reported attendees include [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).
- Commentators describe the gathering as confrontational — an **intervention** with explicit asks regarding Israel policy, platform bookings, and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)'s TPUSA appearances.
- Charlie Kirk **reportedly** later described the weekend to Megyn Kelly as an intervention rather than a friendly retreat.
- Prime Minister Netanyahu **reportedly** called Charlie separately to offer **financial support tied to an Israel trip**, which Kirk **reportedly declined**, per the same commentary compilations.

Per CK_FILE notes, the timeline lists this "intervention" as occurring around **August 4** (roughly four days after the July 31 White House strategy session on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel").

**No primary source documents or sworn transcripts have independently verified attendee lists or meeting content.**

### Donor Pressure Over Tucker Carlson
Kirk's **reportedly** attributed private messages reference losing donors over not cancelling Tucker Carlson at TPUSA events, including lines such as *"Just lost another huge Jewish donor"* and *"I do NOT appreciate being emotionally blackmailed."* Hammer's Hamptons presence is cited within this **donor-pressure layer** documented in [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) — not as evidence of involvement in violence. Kirk's disputes with pro-Israel donors are also one of the unexplored lead areas former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his team was ordered to abandon once Tyler Robinson was in custody.

### Reported September 9 Repost (Attributed)
CK_FILE timeline notes state that on **September 9, 2025** — the day before the assassination — Hammer reportedly reposted a Trump-related item with the comment **"Based."** This is logged in project research notes as a dated social-media observation; it is reproduced here as **reported commentary**, not as evidence of any involvement in the shooting.

### Post-Death Commentary (Attributed, Contested)
A commentator quoted in CK_FILE alleges that "Seth Dillon, like Josh Hammer, LIED in the immediate aftermath of Charlie's death to cover his own contribution to the pressure Charlie was under regarding his shifting positions on Israel." This is one commentator's **opinion and unproven allegation** about a living person; it is presented strictly as reported commentary. Hammer has not been charged with, or found by any court to have committed, any wrongdoing in connection with the case.
