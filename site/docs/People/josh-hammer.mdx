# Josh Hammer

**Josh Hammer** is a conservative legal analyst, commentator, and opinion editor at Newsweek. He is an Orthodox Jew and a prominent pro-Israel voice in American conservative media.

## Background

### Education and Legal Career
Hammer holds a B.A. from Duke University and a J.D. from the University of Chicago Law School. He clerked on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit before moving into media and commentary.

### Media Career
Hammer served as an editor at large for The Daily Wire before becoming the opinion editor at Newsweek (starting around 2020-2021). He is a senior editor-at-large at Newsweek, hosts a podcast, and is a regular commentator across conservative media. He has affiliations with the Edmund Burke Foundation and the national conservatism movement.

### Stance on Israel
Hammer has consistently advocated for strong U.S.-Israel relations, defended Israeli military operations, and publicly debated figures on the right who question U.S. foreign policy toward Israel.

## Connection to the Investigation

### TPUSA Debate with Dave Smith (July 13, 2025)
Hammer debated Dave Smith (libertarian comedian and podcaster) at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Florida, on July 13, 2025. The debate centered on U.S. foreign policy toward Israel and non-interventionism. It was a significant moment in the intra-right policy debate that Charlie Kirk was navigating in mid-2025.

### Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)
According to multiple compiled sources, Hammer was one of several pro-Israel figures at a meeting in the Hamptons in early August 2025 where Charlie Kirk's evolving stance on Israel was discussed. Other reported attendees include [Bill Ackman](./bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](./seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](./rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](./natasha-hausdorff). Per CK_FILE notes, the timeline lists this "intervention" as occurring around August 4 (roughly four days after the July 31 White House strategy session on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel"), with Kirk later describing the weekend to Megyn Kelly as an intervention rather than a friendly retreat.

### Reported September 9 Repost (Attributed)
CK_FILE timeline notes state that on **September 9, 2025** — the day before the assassination — Hammer reportedly reposted a Trump-related item with the comment **"Based."** This is logged in project research notes as a dated social-media observation; it is reproduced here as reported commentary, not as evidence of any involvement in the shooting.

### Post-Death Commentary (Attributed, Contested)
A commentator quoted in CK_FILE alleges that "Seth Dillon, like Josh Hammer, LIED in the immediate aftermath of Charlie's death to cover his own contribution to the pressure Charlie was under regarding his shifting positions on Israel." This is one commentator's **opinion and unproven allegation** about a living person; it is presented strictly as reported commentary. Hammer has not been charged with, or found by any court to have committed, any wrongdoing in connection with the case, and no evidence ties him to the shooting.
