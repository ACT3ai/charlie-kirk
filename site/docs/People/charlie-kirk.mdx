# Charlie Kirk

**Charles James Kirk** was a nationally influential conservative political activist, founder of [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) (TPUSA), and the victim of an assassination on September 10, 2025, at Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah. He was shot at approximately 12:23 p.m. MDT while speaking at an outdoor campus event and was pronounced deceased at Timpanogos Regional Hospital.

## Role in the Investigation

Charlie Kirk is the central figure of this entire investigation. Every thread — timeline, motive, suspects, cover-up allegations, and institutional responses — connects back to understanding why he was targeted and by whom.

## Background

Kirk built TPUSA into one of the largest conservative youth organizations in the United States, with significant operational assets and a national campus presence. He was a regular media figure, podcast host, and political advisor with direct White House access. His influence extended across youth politics, donor networks, and conservative media.

**Personal life:** Charlie Kirk was married to **Erika Kirk**. No credibly sourced mainstream reporting has established the existence of a separate romantic partner. Fringe commentary has made unverified claims about his personal life beyond his marriage; this investigation does not endorse or validate those claims.

## Key Events Before September 10

According to public reporting and sources compiled in this investigation:

- **Mid-2025 shift on Israel**: Kirk was reportedly reassessing his long-standing pro-Israel positioning, hosting figures critical of Israeli policy (including Dave Smith and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) at TPUSA events), and privately expressing frustration with donor pressure.
- **Hamptons intervention (early August 2025)**: Pro-Israel figures including [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff) **reportedly** confronted Kirk about his evolving stance. Netanyahu reportedly called to offer financial support tied to an Israel trip, which Kirk declined. Kirk **reportedly** rejected a **$150 million** funding offer; **billion-dollar offer** rumors circulate in commentary sourced through [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens).
- **July 20 AMFEST**: Military/intelligence meeting preceded Hamptons by ~4 days per commentary threads.
- **September 1-5**: Kirk reportedly wrote in group chats that he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."
- **September 8**: Posted publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement.
- **September 9**: Appeared on [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s show and directly criticized Israeli policy. Multiple sources allege he told confidants during a high-pressure meeting: "THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."
- **Night before Sept 10**: [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) **reportedly displayed texts** in which Charlie wrote his life was in danger and used language including **"executed tomorrow"** — sent to donors, TPUSA members, and Head of Security [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood); Signal/Telegram recipients **reportedly** include [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet).
- **September 10, 12:23 PM MDT**: Assassinated at UVU.
