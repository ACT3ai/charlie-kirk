# Charlie Kirk

**Charles James Kirk** was a nationally influential conservative political activist, founder of Turning Point USA (TPUSA), and the victim of an assassination on September 10, 2025, at Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah. He was shot at approximately 12:23 p.m. MDT while speaking at an outdoor campus event and was pronounced deceased at Timpanogos Regional Hospital.

## Role in the Investigation

Charlie Kirk is the central figure of this entire investigation. Every thread — timeline, motive, suspects, cover-up allegations, and institutional responses — connects back to understanding why he was targeted and by whom.

## Background

Kirk built TPUSA into one of the largest conservative youth organizations in the United States, with significant operational assets and a national campus presence. He was a regular media figure, podcast host, and political advisor with direct White House access. His influence extended across youth politics, donor networks, and conservative media.

**Personal life:** Charlie Kirk was married to **Erika Kirk**. No credibly sourced mainstream reporting has established the existence of a separate romantic partner. Fringe commentary has made unverified claims about his personal life beyond his marriage; this investigation does not endorse or validate those claims.

## Key Events Before September 10

According to public reporting and sources compiled in this investigation:

- **Mid-2025 shift on Israel**: Kirk was reportedly reassessing his long-standing pro-Israel positioning, hosting figures critical of Israeli policy (including Dave Smith and Tucker Carlson at TPUSA events), and privately expressing frustration with donor pressure.
- **Hamptons intervention (early August 2025)**: Pro-Israel figures including Josh Hammer, Bill Ackman, Seth Dillon, Rabbi Pesach Wolicki, and Natasha Hausdorff **reportedly** confronted Kirk about his evolving stance. Netanyahu reportedly called to offer financial support tied to an Israel trip, which Kirk declined. Kirk **reportedly** rejected a **$150 million** funding offer; **billion-dollar offer** rumors circulate in commentary sourced through Candace Owens.
- **July 20 AMFEST**: Military/intelligence meeting preceded Hamptons by ~4 days per commentary threads.
- **September 1-5**: Kirk reportedly wrote in group chats that he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."
- **September 8**: Posted publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement.
- **September 9**: Appeared on Ben Shapiro's show and directly criticized Israeli policy. Multiple sources allege he told confidants during a high-pressure meeting: "THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."
- **Night before Sept 10**: [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) **reportedly displayed texts** in which Charlie wrote his life was in danger and used language including **"executed tomorrow"** — sent to donors, TPUSA members, and Head of Security Dan Flood; Signal/Telegram recipients **reportedly** include Andrew Kolvet.
- **September 10, 12:23 PM MDT**: Assassinated at UVU.
