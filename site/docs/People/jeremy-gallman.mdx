## Investigative Value

**Jeremy Gallman** appears on the Query 6 **videographer/photographer roster** for the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event. According to his LinkedIn profile (as cited in citizen research), he reportedly worked as a **Camera Operator and Video Editor** for [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) in Phoenix, Arizona. Production-team members may have had access to multi-angle feeds, camera placement logs, and behind-the-scenes operations not visible in public [Videos](/Videos/overview) releases.

If Gallman was on the UVU crew, his testimony and any retained production files intersect directly with SD-card and chain-of-custody debates involving [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) and [cameras](/cameras/overview). Compare with independent documentarians [Mathew Franke](/People/mathew-franke) and [Andrew Feraci](/People/andrew-feraci).
