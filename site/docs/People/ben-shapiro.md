# Ben Shapiro

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Ben Shapiro.** All claims are attributed to their sources. Ben Shapiro is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Ben Shapiro** is the co-founder and editor emeritus of The Daily Wire, a conservative commentator, author, and one of the most prominent pro-Israel voices in American media. He appears in the investigation because of a pivotal interaction with Charlie Kirk on September 9, 2025, and because of post-assassination activities that have drawn scrutiny from citizen investigators.

## Background

Shapiro graduated from UCLA at age 20 and Harvard Law School at 23. He founded The Daily Wire in 2015 and built it into one of the largest conservative media companies. He is an Orthodox Jew and has been one of the most vocal defenders of Israel in American conservative media, frequently debating critics of Israeli policy within the right-wing movement.

## Key Connections to the Investigation

### September 9, 2025 — Kirk on Shapiro's Show
Charlie Kirk appeared on Ben Shapiro's show on September 9, 2025 — less than 24 hours before his death — and directly criticized Israeli policy to Shapiro's face. According to public reporting and video, this was one of Kirk's most public breaks from the pro-Israel consensus within the conservative movement. The appearance is considered a pivotal moment in the timeline.

### Post-Assassination Activity
According to commentary compiled in the investigation, Shapiro and Andrew Kolvet were scheduling an upcoming show together on the same day as the assassination (September 10, 2025). Some commentators have compared this to Tyler Hollyhand (described as Shapiro's friend) appearing upbeat on Fox News within one hour of the shooting.

According to investigation sources, in a clip from around this period, Kolvet said "an army was rising" and Shapiro said "we're willing to put boots on the ground" and "to do anything Charlie would have wanted to be done here."

Commentators critical of Shapiro's post-event posture note that this promise of continuing Kirk's work was quickly followed by decisions they consider contrary to Kirk's trajectory — including reportedly inviting Utah Governor Cox (described by commentators as "pro-trans, pro-Antifa, pro-censorship, pro-open borders") to speak at TPUSA events.

### Bullet Trajectory and Kolvet
According to investigation sources, Andrew Kolvet reportedly told others that the bullet entered at the C2 vertebra from a downward angle of approximately 5-9 degrees. Some investigators have questioned this account, arguing that a shot from the Losee Center rooftop at that angle would have entered through the face or jaw, not the neck.
