# Josh Hammer

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Josh Hammer.** All claims are attributed to their sources and must be treated as unproven allegations. Josh Hammer is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Josh Hammer** is a conservative legal analyst, commentator, and opinion editor at Newsweek. He is an Orthodox Jew and one of the most outspokenly pro-Israel voices in American conservative media. He appears in the investigation as a participant in key events preceding the assassination and as a figure whose public statements have drawn scrutiny from citizen investigators.

## Background

### Education and Legal Career
Hammer holds a B.A. from Duke University and a J.D. from the University of Chicago Law School. He clerked on the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit before moving into media and commentary.

### Media Career
Hammer served as an editor at large for The Daily Wire before becoming the opinion editor at Newsweek (starting around 2020-2021), where he shaped the publication's opinion section. He is a senior editor-at-large at Newsweek, hosts a podcast, and is a regular commentator across conservative media. He has affiliations with the Edmund Burke Foundation and the national conservatism movement.

### Stance on Israel
Hammer has consistently advocated for strong U.S.-Israel relations, defended Israeli military operations, and publicly criticized figures on the right — including populist and libertarian voices — who question U.S. aid to Israel or Israeli government policy. This positioning is central to his relevance in the Charlie Kirk investigation.

## Key Connections to the Investigation

### TPUSA Debate with Dave Smith (July 13, 2025)
Hammer debated Dave Smith (libertarian comedian and podcaster) at the TPUSA Student Action Summit in Tampa, Florida, on July 13, 2025. The debate centered on U.S. foreign policy toward Israel, the Epstein/Mossad connection, and non-interventionism. It became a significant flashpoint in the intra-right divide between pro-Israel hawks and the emerging non-interventionist wing that Charlie Kirk was reportedly moving toward.

### Hamptons Intervention (Early August 2025)
According to multiple sources compiled in the investigation, Hammer was one of several pro-Israel figures who allegedly confronted Charlie Kirk about his evolving stance on Israel at an "intervention-style" meeting in the Hamptons. Other reported attendees include Bill Ackman, Seth Dillon, Rabbi Pesach Wolicki, and Natasha Hausdorff. The meeting has been described as occurring around August 4-6, 2025.

This meeting is cited by investigators as evidence of coordinated pressure on Kirk in the weeks before his death. At this meeting or a similar gathering, according to Erika Kirk's account on Megyn Kelly, a donor or attendee asked Charlie: "What would happen to TPUSA if you DIED?" — approximately 40 days before his assassination.

### September 9 Retweet
On September 9, 2025 — the day before the assassination — Hammer retweeted a Trump post referencing a "12 year old execution" with the single word "Based." The timing and content of this post have been noted by citizen investigators.

### Tucker Carlson "Neutralize" Statement
According to investigation sources, Hammer reportedly made public statements calling for Tucker Carlson to be "neutralized." The exact context, date, and platform of this statement are tracked in the investigation files.
