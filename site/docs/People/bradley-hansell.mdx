# Bradley D. Hansell

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page exists to record **what other people have publicly said** about him in connection with September 10, 2025. This site has established no connection between Mr. Hansell and those events, and asserts none.

> **Living Person Notice:** Bradley D. Hansell is a living person. This page records his public office and what other people have said, with attribution. **This website makes no claim of its own** that he was aboard any aircraft, attended any meeting, or was in any particular place. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Bradley D. Hansell holds the public office of Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security, to which the Senate confirmed him on July 22, 2025. That confirmation is a matter of public record and is the only fact about him this site states in its own voice.

He enters this investigation solely through claims made by others. Commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has alleged that he was one of a small number of passengers on a Department of Defense VIP flight (callsign SAM-702) said to have departed [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) the day before the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting. **This site does not state that he was on that flight, and does not state that he was at Fort Huachuca or at any meeting.** No released manifest, visitor log, or official record cited anywhere on this site confirms his presence, and the significance attached to the flight is the commentator's inference rather than an established fact.

For the aircraft context see [Planes](/Planes/overview); for the intelligence-services discussion see [Proof: Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview). On the intelligence-community front more broadly, former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that his center's parallel review of potential foreign ties to the assassination was ordered to stop once a suspect was in custody.

## What others have alleged

Everything in this section is someone else's statement. It is recorded here because the claims are in public circulation, not because this site finds them to be true.

- **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) alleges** that one of the passengers on the SAM-702 VIP flight said to have left Fort Huachuca on the morning of September 9, 2025 — the day before the shooting — was Bradley D. Hansell. **That is her allegation and hers alone.** No released passenger manifest confirms it, and **this site does not assert it, adopt it, or treat it as established.**
- **The same account describes** his publicly available résumé (Army Special Forces, Boston Consulting Group, and National Security Council service) and says he reports to [Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth). Reporting lines within a department are ordinary organizational structure and carry no implication of any kind.
- **Commentators draw inferences** from his prior employer's past contracts. Those inferences are the commentators' own juxtapositions. **This site does not adopt them** and knows of no proven connection between them and the shooting.
- **Counterpoint:** A senior defense official traveling on an official aircraft, and visiting the Army's intelligence headquarters, is a routine part of that job. None of the cited material shows any link between Mr. Hansell and the events at Utah Valley University. No charge, indictment, or court finding exists against him.
