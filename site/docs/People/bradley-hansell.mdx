# Bradley Hansell

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Bradley Hansell is a living person. We document his public office and what a commentator reports only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Bradley D. Hansell is described in the source material as the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security. He enters this investigation through a single cited claim: commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reportedly named him as one of a small number of passengers on a Department of Defense VIP flight (callsign SAM-702) that departed Fort Huachuca the day before the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting. The significance placed on that flight is the commentator's inference, not an established fact.

For the aircraft context see [Planes](/Planes/overview); for the intelligence-services discussion see [Proof: Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).

## What is reported

- According to a cited account attributed to Candace Owens, Bradley D. Hansell was identified as one of the passengers on the SAM-702 VIP flight that left Fort Huachuca on the morning of September 9, 2025 — the day before the shooting.
- The same account describes Hansell's public résumé (Army Special Forces, Boston Consulting Group, and National Security Council service) and states that he "reports directly to [Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth)." These are presented as biographical and organizational facts.
- Commentary in the source draws inferences from his prior employer's past contracts; those inferences are the commentator's allegations and juxtapositions, not proven connections to the shooting.
- **Counterpoint:** A senior defense official traveling on an official aircraft is routine, and none of the cited material shows any link between Hansell and the events at Utah Valley University. No charge or court finding exists.
