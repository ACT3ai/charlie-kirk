Ryan McBride is a Deputy Utah County Attorney. According to public court records in *State of Utah v. [Tyler Alexander Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)* (Case No. 251403576), he is listed among the prosecutors representing the State in the criminal case arising from the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at Utah Valley University.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Court filings, including a Notice of Appearance dated September 30, 2025, list Ryan McBride for the State. Site notes and the site's prosecution-team page identify him as one of the Deputy Utah County Attorneys on the trial team, alongside others such as [Lauren Hunt](/People/lauren-hunt) and [David Sturgill](/People/david-sturgill). His role, as reflected in the public docket, is that of a government attorney of record.

## Background

Publicly available information identifies him as a Deputy Utah County Attorney working out of the Utah County Attorney's Office. Further biographical detail is not established in the source material collected here.
