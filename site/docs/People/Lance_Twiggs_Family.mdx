# The Lance Twiggs Family

This page collects publicly available records concerning the family of [Lance Twiggs](./lance-twiggs) — the partner and roommate of [Tyler Robinson](./tyler-robinson), the man charged in the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](./charlie-kirk) at Utah Valley University. The records below were surfaced by X user **@1MrsWhistledown** and consist of a Texas nonprofit filing and two county property-ownership histories. Forming a charitable organization and acquiring property are entirely lawful activities, and no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of any crime in connection with the case. The documents are reproduced here as primary records, with the questions they raise left open.

## What @1MrsWhistledown Reported

In a June 17, 2026 post, **@1MrsWhistledown** wrote:

> "Lance Twiggs flees to Texas post-shooting, while his parents suddenly acquire two new homes and start a 'Faith and Family' charity. The timing of these events is interesting."

The characterizations "flees" and "suddenly acquire" are the poster's words. What follows are the underlying public records the post attached, summarized as they appear in those documents.

## Timeline of the Records

Laid against the case timeline, the documented dates are:

* **September 10, 2025** — Shooting of Charlie Kirk at UVU.
* **October 16, 2025** — A property later held under the "13 Branches Family Trust and Twiggs" name is listed for sale.
* **December 4, 2025** — That property closes.
* **March 9, 2026** — The Faith and Family Foundation Charity is filed with the Texas Secretary of State.

The timing of these events is interesting. At the same time, these are records of lawful transactions — buying a home and registering a nonprofit are ordinary legal acts. The dates alone do not establish any connection to the events at UVU, and no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of wrongdoing in relation to the case. The records are presented so readers can examine the primary documents directly.

**Status:** Lance Twiggs — Alive. Family members named in the records (Jerry T Twiggs, Amy R Twiggs, Mikayla R Twiggs) — presumed Alive.
