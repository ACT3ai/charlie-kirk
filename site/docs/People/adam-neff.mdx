# Adam Neff

**Captain Adam Neff**, described in commentary as being from **Cookeville, Tennessee**, is named in whistleblower-sourced reporting amplified by **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** as the officer who reportedly led an interrogation at **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca)** on September 9, 2025 — the day before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at Utah Valley University. Everything here is presented as an attributed public claim, not as established fact.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to investigation notes circulated on X and reporting attributed to Candace Owens, a military whistleblower said he walked into a high-clearance meeting of roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels at Fort Huachuca — the US Army's intelligence hub — and was then held for a roughly seven-hour interrogation reportedly led by a "Captain Neff." The base and the meeting appear in the timeline because a Special Air Mission VIP jet was tracked through Fort Huachuca on September 8-9, 2025. None of this has been confirmed by any official source, and no crime has been established.

## Background

Publicly available detail is limited to what appears in the circulating commentary: the name **Adam Neff**, the rank **Captain**, and the town **Cookeville, TN**. Commentators have separately noted that a **Lt. Col. [Daniel Neff](/People/daniel-neff)** is described as a cousin of **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**, a longtime Charlie Kirk producer and TPUSA figure — a connection raised as a question, not a proven link.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- That a "Captain Neff" led a multi-hour interrogation of a whistleblower at Fort Huachuca on September 9, 2025 — attributed to Candace Owens' reporting; unconfirmed.
- That the Fort Huachuca meeting was a "final op planning session" — the whistleblower's characterization, not a verified description. An innocent explanation is that any meeting on an intelligence base could be a routine, unrelated briefing.
- That Adam Neff is related to Blake Neff through Lt. Col. Daniel Neff — raised by commentators as a question; no genealogical proof is offered here.

## Open Questions

1. Can any official record confirm that a Captain Adam Neff was present at Fort Huachuca on September 9, 2025?
2. What, if anything, connects any meeting on the base to events at UVU the next day?
