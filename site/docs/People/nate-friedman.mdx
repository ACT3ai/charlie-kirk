# Nate Friedman

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Nate Friedman or any other living person. Friedman is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Friedman or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Nate Friedman** is a pro-Israel social-media influencer named among the reported participants in the **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025** — the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Friedman is described in commentary as an **outspoken pro-Israel influencer**, known for confronting pro-Palestine activists in public settings. He operates within the same advocacy ecosystem as several figures cited in the [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) accounts.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to *The Grayzone* sourcing and attendee lists circulated on X:

- Friedman is **listed among the participants** at the gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- **Limited specific public comment** from Friedman about the meeting itself appears in the X discussions.
- Other reported attendees include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), [Arynne Wexler](/People/arynne-wexler), and [Ory Rinat](/People/ory-rinat).

His specific role and anything said at the gathering remain **unconfirmed** and rest on **secondary sourcing**, not primary documents.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Friedman is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Friedman or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

His mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 gathering, where he appears on attendee lists — not any accusation of wrongdoing.
