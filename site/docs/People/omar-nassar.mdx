# Omar Nassar

"Omar Nassar" (reported as "Eng. Omar Nassar") is a name that **surfaced online** as one of two possible passengers associated with the Egyptian-registered Dassault Falcon 7X, tail **SU-BTT**, said to have sat at Provo Municipal Airport from September 4 to 10, 2025. The name is entirely unverified. No passenger manifest confirms it, and no connection to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination has been established.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Per the [Egyptian Jets — Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo) page, citizen commentary and a reported aviation-source claim floated two possible names — Maj. [Ahmed Khalil](/People/ahmed-khalil) and Eng. Omar Nassar — in connection with passengers said to have boarded Egyptian aircraft. The same notes stress that the flight reportedly had **no passenger manifest recorded** and that "all of this remains" unverified. The name therefore functions as an online lead, not a confirmed identity.

## Background

There is no verifiable public biography attached to this name in the case record. It is described in notes as possibly an Egyptian Army engineer, but this is unconfirmed.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Online discussion names "Omar Nassar" as a possible SU-BTT passenger. This originates in citizen research and an unnamed aviation source; it is not supported by any released manifest or official record.
