# Rob McCoy

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Rob McCoy.** All claims are attributed to their sources. Rob McCoy is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Rob McCoy** (born August 10, 1964, Coronado, California) is an evangelical pastor, former politician, and co-founder of Turning Point USA Faith. He appears in the investigation because of his deep connection to the Kirk family, his son Mikey McCoy's role as Charlie Kirk's Chief of Staff, and timeline questions surrounding events on September 10, 2025.

## Background

### Pastoral Career
McCoy served as senior pastor of Godspeak Calvary Chapel in Thousand Oaks, California from 2001 until July 2025, when he transitioned to Pastor Emeritus. Under his leadership, Sunday attendance grew from approximately 50 to 1,500. He was ordained in 1995 and holds a B.A. in History from California State University, Fresno, with seminary training from the Mennonite Brethren Biblical Seminary. He and his wife Michelle have five children.

McCoy gained national attention during COVID-19 for defying California gathering restrictions, resulting in fines and legal challenges. This positioned him as a prominent figure in the intersection of faith and conservative politics.

### Political Career
McCoy was elected to the Thousand Oaks City Council in 2015 (Republican) and served as mayor from 2018 to 2019. He resigned from the council in 2020. He previously ran for the California state legislature in 2014.

### TPUSA Connection
McCoy co-founded Turning Point USA Faith with Charlie Kirk in 2021 and served as its co-chair. He was described as Charlie Kirk's personal pastor. His son, **Mikey McCoy**, became Charlie Kirk's Chief of Staff at TPUSA. Charlie reportedly recruited Mikey after a drive to the airport following a church event. Mikey described Kirk as "my boss, mentor, close friend."

### Church Funding
According to reporting by People For the American Way and the Thousand Oaks Acorn, the Heavenly Father's Foundation — a Texas-based nonprofit tied to fracking billionaire **Dan Wilks** — purchased a former YMCA facility for $2.85 million and planned to lease it as Godspeak's new home. McCoy publicly called Wilks a "very dear friend." Dan Wilks and his brother Farris had previously donated thousands to McCoy's 2014 campaign. McCoy reportedly met Wilks through connections in Israel.

## Key Connections to the Investigation

### Pastoral Resignation (July 2, 2025)
McCoy formally resigned as lead pastor of Godspeak Calvary Chapel on July 2, 2025 — approximately two months before Charlie Kirk's assassination. This was reportedly a pre-planned retirement announced in January 2025. Some investigators note the timing relative to subsequent events.

### Mikey McCoy at UVU (September 10, 2025)
Rob McCoy's son Mikey was at the UVU event on September 10. According to video timeline analysis compiled in this investigation:

- **12:24:02** (approximately 33 seconds after the shot): Mikey McCoy is visible on camera well past the SUV, by himself — Blake Neff, who reportedly was with him moments before, is nowhere in frame.
- **Phone calls**: According to Erika Kirk's account (in a Megyn Kelly interview), Mikey called Erika at 12:23 PM Utah time (11:23 AM Arizona time) to inform her of the shooting. Blake Neff reportedly said he witnessed this call and a subsequent call to Rob McCoy.
- **Video contradiction**: At 12:24:02 on video, Mikey is alone and Blake is not visible, raising questions about how Blake witnessed these calls.

### Mikey McCoy's Hospital Movements
According to an eyewitness account compiled in the investigation, Mikey McCoy arrived at the hospital in a second SUV shortly after Charlie's SUV. He reportedly did not enter the ER but instead drove to physician parking, picked up an unidentified individual, and departed. The identity of this individual and the purpose of this trip remain unknown.

### Mikey McCoy's Mic Video
According to investigation notes, Mikey McCoy filmed a close-up video of Charlie getting mic'd up before the event. Investigators have called for the release of this video, which has not been made public.

## Open Questions

- What was Mikey McCoy's complete timeline on September 10, from the moment of the shot through the rest of the day?
- How did Mikey call Erika at 12:23 PM if the shooting happened at 12:23 PM — was the call placed before or simultaneous with the shot?
- Where was Blake Neff when the calls to Erika and Rob McCoy were reportedly made?
- Who did Mikey pick up from physician parking at the hospital?
- What does the mic'd-up video that Mikey filmed show?
- What is Rob McCoy's complete account of the calls he received on September 10?
