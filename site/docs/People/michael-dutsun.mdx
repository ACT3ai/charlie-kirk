## Investigative Value

**Michael Dutsun** appears on the Query 6 roster of **general witness accounts** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event. Dutsun is listed with [Jeremy King](/People/jeremy-king), [Minjae Kim](/People/minjae-kim), and [Adam Bartholomew](/People/adam-bartholomew) — the attendee layer that broadens sample size for acoustic, motion, and timeline reconstruction beyond staff and press witnesses.

Unreleased FBI 302 material, phone video, or [cameras](/cameras/overview) metadata naming Dutsun could corroborate [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) claims about the shot's sound character and crowd panic. Compare with [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) and [Videos](/Videos/overview) already public.
