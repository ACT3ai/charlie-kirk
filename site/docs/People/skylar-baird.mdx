# Skyler Baird

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents a publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Skyler Baird is a living person. He has not been charged with anything, no court has found that he did anything wrong, and he has never been shown to be anyone other than who he says he is. Everything below is either a matter of broadcast record or an attributed claim made by other people online, labelled as such.

**Skyler Baird** is one of the eyewitnesses who gave on-camera interviews in the days after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). His name reached the public through a broadcaster's own graphic: a split-screen news frame whose lower chyron reads **SKYLER BAIRD / WITNESS**, opposite anchor [Josh Breslow](/Influencers/x/joshbreslo). That frame is the anchor for everything else on this page.

He appears in several other images that circulated afterwards — outdoor frames in a white shirt and striped tie, a selfie taken inside the arena at Kirk's memorial, and a Facebook screenshot in which an account under his name states that he was present when Kirk was shot. Those images are collected below, and the full set with individual write-ups is in the [Skyler Baird photo cluster](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/overview).

## What Is On the Record

- He was presented to viewers as an eyewitness in at least one televised split-screen interview, with the broadcaster's own lower-third graphic naming him. That is the strongest documented fact on this page.
- A Facebook account under his name posted a comment stating, in the account holder's words, that he was there when Charlie Kirk was shot, alongside a remark about the plaza [pavers](/UVU/Pavers). Holding and posting political opinions is lawful and unremarkable, and nothing in the posts indicates knowledge of or involvement in anything.
- Several still frames show him in the same white shirt and striped tie across different settings, which is how researchers have chained the images into an attempted movement timeline. That timeline remains unfinished and unverified — no image in the set fixes its own date or place.

## Questions Raised Online (Claims, Not Findings)

Citizen commentators on X have raised several questions about him. These are **claims posted by others**, reproduced here so readers can see what is circulating and judge it. None has been established:

- **Position in the crowd.** Posters assert he moved from further back in the crowd toward the front rows shortly before the shot. *(Poster framing. Moving within a public audience is not evidence of anything.)*
- **Memorial access.** Posters note he later appeared on the arena floor at the memorial with credentialed access after saying he probably could not get in. *(Attending a public memorial is not evidence of anything, and credential processes are not published.)*
- **Deleted footage.** Commentary in the investigation file alleges he was asked to delete phone video. **Unverified** — it is listed on [Witness Footage Pressure](/Censorship/Witness_Footage_Pressure) only because influencers cite it as part of a pattern.
- **Search-trend spikes.** [@SamParkerSenate](/Influencers/x/samparkersenate) circulated Google Trends panels showing regional interest in the name. Trends data is relative and noisy; a spike is not a finding.

**Counterpoint:** No primary record — no court filing, no document, no authority — ties Skyler Baird to planning, to the shooting, or to any crime. Resemblance arguments, social-media profiling and search-trend graphics are commentary, not proof. He is a private living person entitled to the presumption of innocence, and this site does not adopt any of the accusations made about him.

## Open Questions

1. What did he actually witness, and does his account match the other [eyewitness video](/Witnesses/overview) and the [testimony discrepancies](/Timeline/witness-testimony-discrepancies) already catalogued?
2. Who arranged his media interviews, and were other witnesses handled the same way?
3. Was any witness in fact asked to delete phone footage, and by whom — the question that matters far more than any one name?

## A Separate Person — Robbie Parker

The images in this second section are of **a different man, Robbie Parker**. He is documented separately here because his photographs were swept into this investigation's image files by online commentary. Nothing on this site connects him to the events of September 10, 2025 at Utah Valley University, and this site makes no claim about his identity beyond the public record summarised below.

**Robbie Parker** is the father of Emilie Parker, who was six years old when she was killed at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Newtown, Connecticut on December 14, 2012. In the years that followed he was the target of a sustained online harassment campaign by people who insisted the shooting had been staged, and he took legal action over it. In 2025 he published a memoir about that experience, *A Father's Fight: Taking on Alex Jones and Reclaiming the Truth About Sandy Hook*, and discussed it in an interview on the PBS program *Think Tank with Steve Adubato*. The screenshots below are captures of that PBS episode page.

Every image in this section is a screenshot of publicly published PBS material. They are reproduced so that a reader who encounters the surrounding commentary can go and read what the underlying source actually says.
