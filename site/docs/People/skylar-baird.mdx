# Skylar Baird

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Skylar Baird is a living person. We document only that his name appears in online witness commentary, and we do so with attribution. The "crisis actor" claims described below are **unproven allegations made by social-media posters**. We do **not** assert that Baird did anything wrong, staged anything, or is anyone other than who he says he is.

## Investigative Value

**Skylar Baird** (also spelled "Skyler Baird" in some posts) is named in citizen commentary as one of the eyewitnesses who gave on-camera interviews after the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting at Utah Valley University. His name is documented here strictly because it recurs in the [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) discussions online — not because any wrongdoing has been established.

Some X.com posters have gone further and speculated, without proof, that Baird is a so-called "crisis actor." **Those claims are unverified allegations, not court findings and not established fact.** They are noted only to document that such commentary exists; no evidence presented in the file ties Baird to the shooting or to any wrongdoing, and he has not been charged with anything.

## What is reported

- According to posts on X.com, Baird was seen near the stage area and gave several eyewitness interviews after the shooting. *(Reported by posters; not independently confirmed here.)*
- Some posters **allege**, without proof, that Baird resembles an unrelated person from a 2012 news event and label him a "crisis actor." **This is an unproven online allegation, not a fact**, and this page does not adopt it.
- **Counterpoint:** No evidence in the file connects Baird to planning, to the shooting, or to any crime. Resemblance claims and social-media profiling are commentary, not proof. He is a private living person entitled to the presumption of innocence.
