# Rabbi Pesach Wolicki

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Rabbi Pesach Wolicki or any other living person. Rabbi Wolicki is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Rabbi Wolicki or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Rabbi Pesach Wolicki** is a religious leader who has been mentioned in public commentary in connection with pro-Israel outreach and discussions about the so-called **"Hamptons intervention"** — a reported meeting in summer 2025 involving various figures in pro-Israel circles and [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).

## Background

Wolicki is known in conservative and pro-Israel media circles as a rabbi and commentator on **Jewish-Christian relations** and **Israel advocacy**. Commentary threads compiled from X/Twitter (including material attributed to **@based_sam_parker**) describe him as part of a **pro-Israel rabbi intervention** layer — figures who allegedly joined donors and policy advocates in pressing Charlie Kirk on his evolving public stance.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to commentary that has circulated on X/Twitter, in independent media, and in compiled research notes:

- Some accounts reference Rabbi Wolicki as one of several individuals **reportedly** present at or associated with a Hamptons-area meeting in **early August 2025** (commonly dated **August 4–6**).
- Other names **allegedly** cited in the same accounts include [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).
- Commentators describe the gathering as confrontational — an **intervention** in which pro-Israel figures **allegedly** asked Charlie to adjust platform decisions, Israel coverage, and bookings of critics such as [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson).
- Charlie Kirk **reportedly** later described the weekend as an intervention and vented about pressure from "friends," per accounts summarized in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

The nature, content, and significance of any such meeting are based entirely on **third-party accounts**; no primary sources have been independently verified.

### Pre-Hamptons Political Context

The Hamptons gathering **reportedly** followed two other pressure points in the summer 2025 timeline:

| Approx. date | Event | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **July 20, 2025** | AMFEST military/intel meeting | Preceded Hamptons by ~4 days per commentary |
| **July 31, 2025** | Off-the-record White House session | Strategy meeting on "woke-right defamation" of Israel; Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) **reportedly absent** |
| **Aug 4–6, 2025** | Hamptons intervention | Wolicki **reportedly** among pro-Israel figures confronting Kirk's evolving stance |

These timelines are reconstructed from **commentary and reporting**, not released transcripts.

### Charlie Kirk's Reported Response

Attributed statements from Kirk in the weeks after the reported Hamptons weekend (treat as **alleged**):

- *"Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"* (group chats, **Sept 1–5**)
- Public post on **September 8** about leaving the pro-Israel movement
- **September 9** appearance on [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s show with direct criticism of Israeli policy
- Variants of *"if I go against Israel, I think they will kill me"* and the attributed **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** statement to confidants

Researchers connect these attributed statements to **escalating donor friction** — not as proof of who ordered violence.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Rabbi Wolicki is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a strategy or donor meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Rabbi Wolicki or any Hamptons attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.
