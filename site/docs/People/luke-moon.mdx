# Luke Moon

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Luke Moon or any other living person. Moon is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. All references to meetings are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Luke Moon** is associated with the **Philos Project**, an organization focused on positive Christian engagement with the Holy Land and pro-Israel advocacy. His name appears in public commentary about a reported late-July 2025 off-the-record White House meeting.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (Late July 2025)

According to reporting and commentary compiled in [2025 Political Pressure and Meetings](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025):

- A late-July 2025 meeting was reportedly co-organized by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project.
- The stated focus was countering "woke-right defamation" of Israel and rising antisemitism within the MAGA movement.
- Moon is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside Ralph Reed, Mario Bramnick, Yehuda Kaploun, and Yair Netanyahu.
- Charlie Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were reportedly noted as **absent** despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel alignment.

These details come from secondary sources; no public transcript has been independently verified. Moon's mention reflects the pre-event political context — not any accusation of involvement in the September 10 shooting.
