# Luke Moon

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Luke Moon or any other living person. Moon is **not accused** of involvement in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Moon or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Luke Moon** is associated with the **Philos Project**, an organization focused on positive Christian engagement with the Holy Land and pro-Israel advocacy. His name appears in public commentary about a reported **late-July 2025 off-the-record White House meeting** and in threads describing **TPUSA-adjacent donor meetings** in the summer 2025 pressure map.

## Background

Moon's Philos Project affiliation places him within the **faith-based pro-Israel organizing** layer that commentators tie to pre-UVU strategy sessions. CK_FILE notes describe the Philos Project as **funded by Paul Singer** in research compilations — a detail cited in network-overlap analysis, not as a finding of wrongdoing.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (July 31, 2025)

According to reporting and commentary compiled in [2025 Political Pressure and Meetings](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025):

- A **July 31, 2025** meeting was **reportedly co-organized** by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project.
- The stated focus was countering **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel and rising antisemitism within the MAGA movement.
- Moon is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu).
- Charlie Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were **reportedly noted as absent** despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel alignment.

These details come from **secondary sources**; no public transcript has been independently verified.

### TPUSA-Adjacent Donor Meeting Context

Commentary attributed to **@based_sam_parker** and related threads cite Moon in the broader **donor-meeting ecosystem** surrounding Kirk's mid-2025 Israel pivot:

- Kirk was **reportedly** hosting critics of Israeli policy (Dave Smith, [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)) at TPUSA events while facing **donor revolt** threads.
- The **July 20 AMFEST** military/intel meeting **reportedly preceded** both the July 31 White House session and the **August Hamptons intervention** by days.
- Kirk **reportedly** rejected a **$150 million** funding offer; **billion-dollar offer** rumors circulate in commentary sourced through [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens).

Moon's name documents **network proximity** to these sessions — not participation in violence.

### Hamptons Layer (Indirect)

Moon is **not** among the names most commonly cited for the **August 4–6 Hamptons intervention** roster ([Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)). Researchers nonetheless compare July 31 attendees to Hamptons figures to map **escalating pre-event pressure** documented in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) and [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).
