Pam Bondi is the **U.S. Attorney General**. Her name appears in the project's research record in two reported contexts: an observation that she reportedly flew on a government aircraft months before the case surfaced in investigator interest, and a reported meeting with commentator [Benny Johnson](/Influencers/podcasts-benny-johnson) in the days after the September 10, 2025 assassination of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). As a senior public official, she is discussed here with attribution; nothing on this page asserts wrongdoing.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

- According to a tipster cited on the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page, Bondi **reportedly** flew on tail 99-0404 roughly five months earlier, from the Tampa area to Washington, DC. The source presents this as an observation from public flight-tracking data, "not as evidence of any wrongdoing."
- Per notes on the [WhiteHouse Halt IC](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC) and [Benny Johnson](/Influencers/podcasts-benny-johnson) pages, Johnson reportedly met with FBI Director [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) and Attorney General Bondi the day after publicly addressing death threats against him and his family. This is described as Johnson's own account.

## Background

Bondi is a former Florida Attorney General who serves as U.S. Attorney General, heading the Department of Justice. As the nation's chief federal law-enforcement officer, she would have an official role in any federal aspect of the case — an ordinary function of the office.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- The flight observation is a tipster's reading of public tracking data and does not establish any connection to the events at UVU. Government officials routinely use government aircraft.
- The reported meeting with Johnson is characterized as being about death threats, an ordinary law-enforcement matter, not evidence of any concealment. Commentators who advance a "halt" theory juxtapose it with other accounts; that juxtaposition is not proof.

The "halt" discussion gained a first-hand data point when former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) stated on the record that his center's parallel review of possible foreign ties was ordered to stop once Tyler Robinson was in custody — a claim that raises questions about how federal law enforcement, including DOJ, scoped the investigation.

## Open Questions

1. What, if any, official DOJ role did the Attorney General's office take in the federal handling of the case?
2. Are the reported flight and meeting details accurately characterized, and are they relevant to the investigation at all?

## Post-Assassination Speech Enforcement Commentary

Clips of Attorney General **Pam Bondi** discussing "hate speech" versus free speech after Charlie Kirk's murder circulated widely (e.g. critical posts framing her remarks as a threat to arrest speakers). Free-speech advocates argue the rhetoric chilled legitimate debate about the case; supporters argue she targeted true threats and celebration of violence.

**No public evidence** places Bondi in the operational events of September 10, 2025. This page concerns **post-event federal messaging** only. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview), [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).
