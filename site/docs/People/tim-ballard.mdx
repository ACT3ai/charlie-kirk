## Investigative Value

Tim Ballard is described in the investigation file as the **owner of a security company** and is cited as a **security expert commenting on the response** to the September 10, 2025 shooting. His relevance to the case is limited and indirect: according to the file, Ballard **publicly stated that people should not believe the theory that this was a lone shooter**. That is presented here as **his attributed opinion**, offered as an outside expert view — not as a proven fact about the case. See [Killer](/Killer/overview) and [Proof — Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview).

The file also notes, for completeness and context only, that Ballard was **sued in 2023 by [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler)**, who reportedly previously worked with him. That civil matter is unrelated to the shooting and is included only because both names appear in the surrounding source material.
