# Trigger Smart

Trigger Smart is the name used by an independent online commentator credited within citizen-investigator circles for research and analysis of theories about the September 10, 2025 shooting of Charlie Kirk at Utah Valley University. The name appears to be an online handle rather than a confirmed legal identity.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to the [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll) profile on this site, Carroll is credited "alongside Jon Bray, Baron Coleman, Trigger Smart, and Stew Peters for deep investigation work into multiple theories about the shooting, including close-range theories and alternative shooter hypotheses." Trigger Smart's involvement is therefore as one of several independent researchers publishing commentary and theories, not as a witness or participant in the events.

## Background

Publicly verifiable biographical information about the person behind the "Trigger Smart" name is limited. The name circulates primarily through independent social-media commentary about the case. This site does not have confirmed details on the commentator's identity, background, or credentials.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Independent commentary credits Trigger Smart with analysis supporting close-range and alternative-shooter theories. These are contested interpretations, not established findings, and are disputed by official accounts. Readers should weigh them as one set of opinions among many.
