# Chris Martenson

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Chris Martenson is a living person. We document his publicly stated analysis only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Chris Martenson is described in the source material as an analyst associated with Peak Prosperity (@peakprosperity) who produced a ballistics breakdown of the wounding pattern. According to that cited analysis, he argues the injury is more consistent with a high-velocity 9mm +P round than with the .30-06 rifle round in the official narrative. This is his reported analytical opinion, attributed to him and not asserted as fact here.

His breakdown is referenced by other commentators in support of alternative-weapon theories, and it intersects with medical-posture discussion. See the [Medical](/Medical/overview) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) overviews for the wider debate.

## What is reported

* According to a cited post, Martenson's breakdown describes a "high-speed cavitation event" and "overpressure" consistent, in his view, with a fragmenting 9mm +P round rather than a .30-06.
* His analysis is invoked by commentators arguing the official weapon account does not fit the observed injury.
* **Counterpoint:** This is independent commentary, not an official autopsy or forensic finding. Ballistics interpretation from video and photos is contested, and Martenson's conclusion has not been confirmed by any official examination.
