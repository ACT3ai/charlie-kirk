Grace Ashford is a journalist who posts on X as [@IGraceAshford](/Influencers/podcasts-baron). Her reporting is cited on this site chiefly for coverage of the *[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) v. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)* defamation litigation and related case commentary.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Ashford is referenced as a reporting source, not as a participant in events. According to her posts, she reported that "every single district and magistrate judge in the Middle District of Tennessee" recused from Harpole's defamation case against Candace Owens, and she attached a clip of Owens discussing the public-figure question. This site treats those procedural specifics as her account, pending verification against the official court docket.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Ashford reported an extraordinary mass judicial recusal in the Harpole v. Owens case. As noted on the site's coverage, these specifics had not been independently confirmed against the docket at the time of writing and should be treated as her reporting.
- She offered her own interpretation of *why* the district's judges stepped aside, tying it to unrelated, intelligence-adjacent claims. This site flags that framing as her opinion about motive — speculation, not evidence — and notes there are ordinary, non-conspiratorial reasons an entire district can recuse.
- Separate photo/video posts under the same handle (e.g., SUV-interior images) are preserved elsewhere as primary-source material that has not been independently authenticated.
