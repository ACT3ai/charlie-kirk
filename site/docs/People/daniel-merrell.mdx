# Daniel Merrell

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Daniel Merrell is a living person. We document only what he is reported to have said about the paving work he performed, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing by him.

## Investigative Value

**Daniel Merrell** is named in the investigation file as a **hardscape worker who says he installed pavers at the UVU tent/courtyard area on September 14, 2025** — four days after the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting. His account is investigatively relevant because the rapid re-paving of the courtyard where the shooting occurred is discussed in the [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) material as a question about **why the site changed so quickly**.

Merrell is presented as a **witness/worker describing a job he was called to do**. Nothing accuses him of any wrongdoing; he is described as having been directly contacted to perform the work.

## What is reported

- According to the file, Merrell (associated with a Utah hardscape company) **says he installed pavers on September 14, 2025**, and that he was **"called by … men who work for the state of Utah."** *(Reported statement.)*
- The work reportedly happened on a Sunday, which the file notes is unusual for fast campus/government projects. *(Commentary observation.)*
- Local reporting (KUTV/KATV) is said to note the courtyard changes but not to name a contractor or authorizer. *(Attributed to reporting.)*
- **Counterpoint:** The file explicitly states that a rumor blaming a suspect's father's company was checked and that **reporting found no credible link**. Being called to do paving work is ordinary employment and implies nothing improper about Merrell. Questions here concern **who authorized the rush job**, not Merrell's conduct.

## Open Questions

1. Who authorized and scheduled the paving, and why on the timeline reported?
2. Was any evidence preservation considered before the courtyard surface was changed?
3. Does documentary record (work orders, invoices) corroborate the account of a direct state call?

## Status

**Status:** Alive. No crime is alleged against Daniel Merrell on this page. His account of the paving work is reported with attribution; defamation-safe framing applies.
