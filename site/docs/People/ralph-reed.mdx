# Ralph Reed

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Ralph Reed or any other living person. Reed is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. All references to meetings are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.

**Ralph Reed** is the founder and chairman of the **Faith & Freedom Coalition**, a major Christian conservative political organization. His name appears in public commentary about a reported late-July 2025 off-the-record White House meeting focused on Israel-related politics within the MAGA movement.

## Background

Reed has been a prominent figure in Republican and evangelical political organizing for decades, with a long record of advocacy for strong U.S.-Israel relations.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (Late July 2025)

According to articles and X/Twitter threads compiled in the [Before](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) section:

- A meeting in late July 2025 was reportedly organized by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project.
- The stated focus was countering what attendees saw as "woke-right defamation" of Israel and rising antisemitism in the MAGA movement.
- Reed is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside Luke Moon, Mario Bramnick, Yehuda Kaploun, and Yair Netanyahu.
- Charlie Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were reportedly noted as **absent** despite Kirk's long-time pro-Israel profile.

The meeting's exact agenda and tone are reconstructed from secondary sources rather than a public transcript. Reed's presence in these accounts is cited as part of the broader narrative about strategy sessions occurring while Charlie was reportedly reassessing his Israel positioning.
