# Ralph Reed

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Ralph Reed or any other living person. Reed is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Reed or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Ralph Reed** is the founder and chairman of the **Faith & Freedom Coalition**, a major Christian conservative political organization. His name appears prominently in public commentary about a reported **late-July 2025 off-the-record White House meeting** focused on Israel-related politics within the MAGA movement — and as an organizer figure in the pre-Hamptons pressure map.

## Background

Reed has been a prominent figure in **Republican and evangelical political organizing** for decades, with a long record of advocacy for strong U.S.-Israel relations. Commentary threads (including **[@based_sam_parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** compilations) cite the Faith & Freedom Coalition as a **co-organizer** of the July 31 strategy session.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (July 31, 2025)

According to articles and X/Twitter threads compiled in the [Before](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) section:

- A meeting on **July 31, 2025** was **reportedly organized** by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project.
- The stated focus was countering what attendees saw as **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel and rising antisemitism in the MAGA movement.
- Reed is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu).
- [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were **reportedly noted as absent** despite Kirk's long-time pro-Israel profile.

The meeting's exact agenda and tone are reconstructed from **secondary sources** rather than a public transcript.

### Relationship to Hamptons Intervention (~Aug 4–6)

The July 31 White House session **reportedly preceded** the Hamptons intervention by approximately **four days**. Reed is most closely tied to the **July 31** layer in available commentary; the Hamptons roster in research notes more commonly cites [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).

Researchers compare both sessions as part of a single **pre-UVU political map**:

| Session | Approx. date | Reed's cited role |
|---|---|---|
| AMFEST military/intel meeting | **July 20, 2025** | Part of escalating summer pressure context |
| White House strategy session | **July 31, 2025** | **Reportedly** attendee / organizer figure |
| Hamptons intervention | **Aug 4–6, 2025** | Separate roster; donor-pressure layer |

### Donor-Pressure and Tucker Carlson Context

Commentary ties the July 31 and Hamptons layers to **donor pressure** over Kirk's platform decisions, including hosting [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) at TPUSA events. Kirk's **reportedly** attributed private messages reference losing donors over Tucker bookings. These threads are documented in [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and [Motive Overview](/Motive/overview) as **motive-layer context**, not proof of who ordered violence.

- **Reported presence** at a strategy meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Reed or any meeting participant knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.
