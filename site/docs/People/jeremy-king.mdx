## Investigative Value

**Jeremy King** is listed among **general witness accounts** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event (Query 6, peopleglyph). The master investigation file groups King with [Henry Dells](/People/henry-dells), [EksAyn Anderson](/People/eksayn-anderson), and [Michael Dutsun](/People/michael-dutsun) — attendees whose pooled testimony could support or challenge official timeline releases.
