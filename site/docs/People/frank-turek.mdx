> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Frank Turek** is a prominent Christian apologist, author, and longtime mentor to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) — described publicly as Kirk's **"friend and brother in Christ."** He was **feet from the stage** when Kirk was shot and **in the SUV** during emergency transport. Turek has given extensive on-record interviews (Greg Laurie, Megyn Kelly, Cissie Graham Lynch, and others) while **actively refuting conspiracy theories** that falsely accuse him of signaling the shooter or alleging [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) involvement. Investigators treat him as both a **close eyewitness** and a **narrative anchor** whose precise timeline statements — especially hospital arrival time — are central to post-event disputes.

## Background

Turek is a nationally known Christian apologist and speaker whose public ministry intersected with Kirk's political and faith outreach for years. The investigation file describes him as a **former Navy officer** and notes commentators who characterize his public statements as **unusually precise on timing** — a trait [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) cites when weighing his hospital-arrival account against TPUSA tarmac-hug timelines.

He is not a professional executive-protection operator; his presence at UVU was as Kirk's **mentor and event companion**, not as contracted security chief (roles held by [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) and [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)).

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to Turek's public interviews and the investigation file:

- He was **on stage or immediately adjacent** — "feet away" — when the shot was fired at approximately **12:23 PM**.
- He entered the **black SUV** with Kirk and the security team ([Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis)) for transport to Timpanogos Regional Hospital.
- He has **recounted Kirk's last moments** in memorials, podcasts, and media appearances.
- He has **strongly refuted** online theories accusing him of hat-touch **signaling**, TPUSA complicity, or other baseless claims — condemning accusations that cause "dissension" and urging evidence-based truth-seeking.
- The file records Turek stating Charlie **texted him** in the weeks before the event: **"I know they want me dead"** / **"they're going to take me out"** — presented as **Turek's attributed reporting** of Charlie's fears, not evidence Turek knew of any attack plan.
- Turek has **reportedly confirmed** TPUSA operated a **drone** before the event; he and Charlie reviewed footage en route and Turek **warned of too many buildings** — per commentary compiled in the file. Charlie reportedly shrugged it off.
- He gave a **hospital-scene interview** cited in timelines as among the more detailed public accounts of arrival and Kirk's condition.

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

Per posts compiled in the People grok fan-out (Query 5) and investigation file:

- **Conspiracy refutation:** Turek publicly counters theories implicating him in signaling or TPUSA orchestration — a stance repeated across his media tour.
- **Infrared laser reflection:** Some citizen analyses claim an **infrared laser reflection** appeared on Turek's **phone screen** at the moment of the shot — framed as a "smoking gun" in [Mic](/Mic/overview) threads. **UNVERIFIED**; not an official forensic finding.
- **Hat-touch signaling theory:** Early threads linked his **white shirt and white hat** to alleged shooter signaling. Turek and mainstream-adjacent commentators treat this as **debunked speculation**.
- **4:00 PM hospital arrival:** Owens' timeline analysis treats Turek as a **consistent narrator** who stated [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) arrived at the hospital around **4:00 PM** — a timestamp that collides with other TPUSA flight-landing accounts. Owens frames Turek as **telling the truth** while suggesting discrepancies point elsewhere. **Attributed commentary**, not a court finding.
- **TPUSA birthday tribute drone footage (Oct 2025):** Commentary notes official TPUSA video included **overhead drone shots** from September 10 despite FBI release restrictions cited elsewhere — with Turek named as having reviewed drone feed with Charlie.

## Investigative Questions

- What is Turek's **complete stage-to-SUV sequence** — positioning, sounds, and first seconds after the shot?
- Does **phone footage** he captured exist in full, and has any **laser-reflection** claim been independently analyzed?
- How does his **4:00 PM hospital arrival** statement align with flight logs, SUV GPS data, and hospital intake records?
- What **pre-event texts** from Charlie to Turek exist beyond public paraphrases — and were they shared with investigators?
- Did Turek observe **drone or rooftop security gaps** he reported to Kirk or TPUSA staff before the speech?
