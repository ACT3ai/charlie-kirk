# Frank Turek

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Christian apologist and author, mentor to Charlie Kirk ("friend and brother in Christ")

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Frank Turek was in the SUV with Kirk and was feet away on stage when the shooting occurred. He recounted the last moments in numerous interviews, memorials, and podcasts (Greg Laurie, Megyn Kelly, Cissie Graham Lynch). Turek has strongly refuted conspiracy theories, including those falsely accusing him of signaling the shooter or alleging TPUSA involvement. He has emphasized evidence-based truth-seeking and condemned baseless accusations as causing "dissension." The master file describes Turek as a **former Navy officer** and notes that he administered **CPR to Kirk inside the SUV** during the emergency transport.

## Death-Threat Texts Turek Reported (Attributed)

According to the investigation file, Turek has stated publicly that Charlie Kirk **texted him** in the weeks before the assassination with words to the effect of *"I know they want me dead. I'm under the gun right now — please pray for me."* Turek also **reportedly** said that **the day before the event** Charlie told him directly *"they're going to take me out."* These are Turek's attributed public recollections; they parallel the *"executed tomorrow"* death-threat texts [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reported were sent to donors and [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood).

## Drone Footage Reviewed En Route (Attributed)

Turek has **reportedly confirmed** that TPUSA had a **drone up** over the UVU event and that he and Charlie **reviewed the drone footage en route** to campus, with Turek warning there were **"too many buildings"** — a caution Kirk reportedly shrugged off. This attributed account is cited across commentary threads (and referenced against the [Drones](/Drones/overview) analysis); it contradicts claims that no drones were present.

## The Infrared-Laser Claim (Unverified)

Some commentary threads (e.g. tagging **@DrFrankTurek**) allege that an **infrared laser reflection** appeared on Turek's phone screen a split second before impact — framed as a possible sighting artifact of a concealed weapon. This page does **not** adopt that interpretation as fact; it is **unverified citizen analysis**, logged only because it circulates in the [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) and shaped-charge record. Turek is **not** accused here of any role in the shooting.

## Hospital Timeline (Candace Owens Analysis)

[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has cited Turek's precision as a "military man" in her analysis of the post-shooting timeline: Turek stated **on the record** that the group arrived at **Timpanogos Regional Hospital around 4:00 PM**. Owens says she believes both Turek **and** [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) are telling the truth about the sequence, using Turek's stated 4:00 PM arrival as a fixed anchor for reconstructing the "tarmac hug" and transport timeline. This is **reported analysis**, not an accusation against Turek.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As a close eyewitness who was feet away on stage, Turek's detailed account of the shooting and immediate response is highly valuable. His public statements addressing and countering conspiracy narratives could help clarify or address narrative issues in the investigation.

**Status:** Alive
