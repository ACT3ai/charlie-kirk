# Frank Turek

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Christian apologist and author, mentor to Charlie Kirk ("friend and brother in Christ")

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Frank Turek was in the SUV with Kirk and was feet away on stage when the shooting occurred. He recounted the last moments in numerous interviews, memorials, and podcasts (Greg Laurie, Megyn Kelly, Cissie Graham Lynch). Turek has strongly refuted conspiracy theories, including those falsely accusing him of signaling the shooter or alleging TPUSA involvement. He has emphasized evidence-based truth-seeking and condemned baseless accusations as causing "dissension."

## Potential Evidentiary Value

As a close eyewitness who was feet away on stage, Turek's detailed account of the shooting and immediate response is highly valuable. His public statements addressing and countering conspiracy narratives could help clarify or address narrative issues in the investigation.
