This section profiles the key individuals connected to the September 10, 2025 event at Utah Valley University. Each profile page provides biographical information, the person's connection to Charlie Kirk or [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), and links to the deeper topical sections where their role is discussed. The profiles cover the victim, the accused, family members, organizational leaders, media figures, and citizen investigators who have covered the case.

The people here fall into distinct categories. Charlie Kirk and Tyler Robinson are the central figures — victim and accused. Erika Kirk, Rob McCoy, and Andrew Kolvet are connected through [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and Charlie's inner circle. Candace Owens and Ian Carroll are independent journalists and investigators who have produced extensive coverage of the case. Tucker Carlson, Josh Hammer, and Ben Shapiro are conservative media figures connected to the political dynamics Charlie Kirk was navigating in mid-2025. Rick Cutler and Lance Twiggs are individuals connected to the event and the accused respectively.

Start with Charlie Kirk and Tyler Robinson for the full picture. For the media and political context, see Tucker Carlson, Josh Hammer, and Ben Shapiro. For the independent investigation coverage, see Candace Owens and Ian Carroll. The category pages (Central Figures, Families, Officials, Witnesses) provide broader groupings for individuals not yet profiled individually. For the organizational context, see the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) section.
