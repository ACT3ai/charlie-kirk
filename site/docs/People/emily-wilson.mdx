# Emily Wilson

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Emily Wilson or any other living person. Wilson is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Wilson or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Emily Wilson** — known online as **"Emily Saves America"** — is a conservative influencer and podcaster named among the reported participants in the **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025**, the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Wilson produces conservative commentary as a podcaster and social-media personality. She operates within the same influencer circle as several other figures cited in the [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) accounts.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to photos and *The Grayzone* reporting:

- Wilson is placed in the Hamptons around **August 8, 2025** via photographs, and is named among the **reported attendees** of the gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- She is pictured with other attendees including [CJ Pearson](/People/cj-pearson) and [Xaviaer DuRousseau](/People/xaviaer-durousseau).
- When contacted by *The Grayzone*, she **declined to comment**.

Her specific role and anything said at the gathering remain **unconfirmed** and rest on **photographic placement and secondary sourcing**, not primary documents.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Wilson is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Wilson or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why Her Name Appears in This Investigation

Her mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 gathering — she appears in attendee photos — not any accusation of wrongdoing.
