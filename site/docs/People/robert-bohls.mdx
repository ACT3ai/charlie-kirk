## Investigative Value

Robert Bohls is described in project notes as the FBI Special Agent in Charge (SAIC) of the Salt Lake City field office, having taken over the office in August 2025. That timing — a change in the field-office lead about a month before the September 10, 2025 shooting — is noted by citizen-researchers as part of a broader pattern of Utah leadership transitions they find worth examining. See the [FBI](/FBI/overview) section.

Per project notes, Bohls led the September 11, 2025 press conference on the shooting and was among the officials in attendance that evening. His prior role is not detailed in the file. Nothing here alleges wrongdoing; these are descriptions of a public official's stated duties.
