# Connor Henderson

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Connor Henderson is a living person. We document only what is reported in an unverified coincidence argument, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

> **⚠️ This is an unverified "coincidence" argument, not a court finding.** Connor Henderson's name appears only inside a chain-of-coincidence thread raised in commentary (attributed to remarks by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and social-media researchers). **The site does not claim that Connor Henderson, or the Henderson or Qureshi families, had any knowledge of or role in the assassination.** No charge, conviction, or evidence of wrongdoing is asserted.

## Investigative Value

Connor Henderson is named in a widely circulated **"coincidence thread"** connecting the Bilt Inc. / Romney family to Timpanogos Regional Hospital. According to that commentary, Connor is the **son of [Nathan Henderson](/People/nathan-henderson) and [Gina Romney Henderson](/People/gina-romney-henderson)** (executives at Bilt Inc.), reportedly **attended BYU at the same time as [Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi)**, and, per his own reported LinkedIn profile, worked as an **Emergency Room Scribe at Timpanogos Regional Hospital during summer 2025** — the hospital where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was taken.

The thread's authors themselves close by inviting readers to "draw your own conclusions" and describe these as **potential coincidences**. This page treats every element as **unverified, attributed commentary**. There is **no evidence that Connor Henderson did anything wrong**, and simply having held a hospital job is not evidence of any involvement in the events of September 10. See [Medical](/Medical/overview).

## What is reported

- Per the coincidence thread, Connor is the **son of Nathan and Gina Romney Henderson**, executives at **Bilt Inc.**
- Reportedly **attended BYU at the same time as Zachariah Qureshi**, whom the FBI briefly detained and then released.
- According to his own reported LinkedIn profile, he was an **ER Scribe at Timpanogos Regional Hospital in summer 2025**.
- The thread juxtaposes this with unrelated claims (an allegedly "empty" ER, a DoD contract to Bilt Inc.) to build a **pattern-of-coincidence argument**.
- **Counterpoint (important):** The authors label these as coincidences and ask readers to speculate. **No evidence ties Connor Henderson to the shooting.** Working as a hospital scribe is an ordinary job; presence near an event is not participation. **This is commentary, not a court finding.**
