# Lori Frantzve

Lori Frantzve is the mother of Erika Kirk (widow of Charlie Kirk). Her name appears in unverified online commentary following the September 10, 2025 assassination, tied to a reported corporate-board appointment that citizen commentators have questioned. This site does not endorse those allegations and treats them as speculation.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Frantzve is connected to the case only through family — as Erika Kirk's mother — and through online claims about post-assassination corporate governance around Turning Point USA (TPUSA) and affiliated entities.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- According to posts circulated on X, Frantzve was "recently put on the board" of a technology company (Superfeed Technologies) alongside a Turning Point Action officer. The posts speculate this board activity is linked to money moving toward "insider-controlled companies."
- These are **unverified allegations**, not established facts. No charges, findings, or independent reporting are cited to substantiate any improper payment.
- **Innocent explanation.** Board appointments of a founder's or executive's relatives are common and lawful; a family member joining a company board is not, by itself, evidence of wrongdoing. The claimed dollar figures and "insider" framing originate in social-media commentary and have not been verified.
