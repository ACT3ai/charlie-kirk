# Tom Sodeika

Tom Sodeika is named in this investigation as one of the individuals reportedly listed among Turning Point USA (TPUSA) board members serving prior to September 10, 2025. His name appears in discussions of the organization's governance and leadership succession.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to a board roster cited in investigation notes, Sodeika is listed alongside [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), and other named board members in the period before the shooting. Serving on a nonprofit board is a normal governance role and carries no implication of any involvement in the events of that day.

## Background

- Reportedly listed among TPUSA board members (roster cited as pre-September 10, 2025), together with figures such as [Doug DeGroote](/People/doug-degroote), [David Engelhardt](/People/david-engelhardt), and [Mike Miller](/People/mike-miller).
