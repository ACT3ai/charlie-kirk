# Bill Ackman

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Bill Ackman or any other living person. Ackman is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Ackman or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Bill Ackman** is a prominent hedge fund manager and activist investor. His name has appeared extensively in public commentary in connection with discussions about pro-Israel donor dynamics and a reported **Hamptons intervention** in summer 2025 involving Charlie Kirk and various figures in pro-Israel circles.

## Background

Ackman is the founder and CEO of **Pershing Square Capital Management**. He is a vocal public figure on business, politics, and U.S.-Israel relations, with a large following on X/Twitter and in financial media. Commentators frequently cite him as emblematic of the **donor-pressure layer** that Charlie Kirk reportedly navigated in mid-2025.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to accounts that have circulated in independent media, on X/Twitter, and in compiled research threads (including commentary attributed to **@based_sam_parker**):

- Some commentators reference a reported gathering in the Hamptons, sometimes called an **"intervention,"** in which Ackman and others **allegedly** expressed concerns about Charlie Kirk's evolving rhetoric on Israel-related topics.
- The meeting is commonly dated to **August 4–6, 2025**, roughly **four days after** the reported **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House strategy session (see [2025 Political Pressure and Meetings](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025)).
- Other names **reportedly** cited in the same Hamptons accounts include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).
- Charlie Kirk later **reportedly** described the weekend to Megyn Kelly as an **intervention** rather than a friendly retreat, per accounts summarized in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

The nature, content, and significance of any such meeting are based entirely on **third-party accounts** in public commentary; no primary source documents or sworn transcripts have been independently verified by this project.

### Donor Pressure Context (Mid-2025)

Commentary threads tie Ackman's reported Hamptons presence to a broader **donor revolt** narrative:

- Kirk was **reportedly** reassessing his long-standing pro-Israel positioning, hosting figures critical of Israeli policy (including Dave Smith and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) at [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events).
- Kirk **reportedly** rejected a **$150 million** funding offer and faced **billion-dollar offer** rumors in commentary sourced through [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and related threads.
- Prime Minister **Benjamin Netanyahu** **reportedly** called Charlie to offer **financial support tied to an Israel trip**, which Kirk **reportedly declined**, per the same commentary compilations.
- Kirk's private messages **reportedly** referenced losing donors over not cancelling Tucker Carlson's TPUSA appearances, including attributed lines such as *"Just lost another huge Jewish donor"* and *"I do NOT appreciate being emotionally blackmailed."*

These are **alleged statements and reported events**, not independently verified facts.

### July 20 AMFEST Military/Intel Meeting

Commentators note that a **July 20, 2025** AMFEST military/intelligence meeting **preceded** the Hamptons gathering by approximately four days. That session is part of the pre-UVU political map researchers use to reconstruct escalating intra-conservative friction over Israel policy. Ackman's name is most closely tied to the **Hamptons donor-pressure** layer rather than the July 20 session in available commentary.

### N1098L Address Speculation (Unverified)

Some online commentators noted that a New York address (888 7th Ave) reportedly tied to LASAI Aviation filings matched Pershing Square's offices, prompting speculation that Ackman "owned" the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) HADES aircraft. This claim is **unverified** and conflicts with FAA registration records pointing to a government ISR contractor. The address overlap alone establishes nothing.

### Unverified OSINT: Search-Traffic Spikes

Some citizen researchers cite **Google search spikes** on Hamptons attendee names in the days before September 10, 2025. These observations are **unverified OSINT** and do not establish attendance, intent, or any connection to the shooting.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Ackman is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a donor meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Ackman or any Hamptons attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

Ackman's name appears solely because commentators have cited him in discussions about potential **pre-event context** and **donor dynamics** within the conservative movement's Israel-policy split. His mention documents what commentators have said — it does not validate those claims or imply any accusation.
