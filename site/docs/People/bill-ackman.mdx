# Bill Ackman

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Bill Ackman or any other living person. Ackman is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. All references to meetings or interactions are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Bill Ackman** is a prominent hedge fund manager and activist investor. His name has appeared in public commentary in connection with discussions about pro-Israel donor dynamics and a reported meeting in summer 2025 involving Charlie Kirk and various figures in pro-Israel circles.

## Background

Ackman is the founder and CEO of Pershing Square Capital Management. He is a vocal public figure on business, politics, and U.S.-Israel relations, with a large following on X/Twitter and in financial media.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to accounts that have circulated in independent media and on X/Twitter:

- Some commentators reference a reported gathering, sometimes called an "intervention," in which Ackman and others allegedly expressed concerns about Charlie Kirk's evolving rhetoric on Israel-related topics.
- The nature, content, and significance of any such meeting are based entirely on third-party accounts in public commentary; no primary source documents have been independently verified.
- These reports do not establish that Ackman had any knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk or any connection to events on September 10, 2025.

**There is no established evidence linking Bill Ackman to any wrongdoing related to this case.**

### N1098L Address Speculation (Unverified)

Some online commentators noted that a New York address (888 7th Ave) reportedly tied to LASAI Aviation filings matched Pershing Square's offices, prompting speculation that Ackman "owned" the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) HADES aircraft. This claim is unverified and conflicts with FAA registration records pointing to a government ISR contractor. The address overlap alone establishes nothing.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

Ackman's name appears solely because commentators have cited him in discussions about potential pre-event context and donor dynamics. His mention documents what commentators have said — it does not validate those claims or imply any accusation.
