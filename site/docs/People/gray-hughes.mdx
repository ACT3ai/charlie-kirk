# Gray Hughes

Gray Hughes is a citizen investigator who posts on X as [@GrayHuze](https://x.com/GrayHuze) ("Gray Hughes Investigates"). He is cited on this site for open-source analysis of surveillance timing around the September 10, 2025 shooting at Utah Valley University.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Hughes is referenced as an independent analyst, not a witness or participant. According to his posts, he mapped movement "from shooting position to North East corner of Losee Center building in 25 seconds," using timestamped surveillance stills, and separately questioned discrepancies in the reported manhunt duration ("30 hours... or 33?").

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Hughes's 25-second egress timeline is his own reconstruction from publicly circulated surveillance frames. It is presented as citizen analysis and has not been independently validated on this site.
- His questions about timeline inconsistencies are framed as open questions raised by a commentator, not as established findings.
- As with all open-source analysis, the underlying frames could have alternate interpretations (camera angle, timestamp sync, frame selection); readers should weigh his conclusions against the primary footage.

## Open Questions

1. Do independent reviews of the same surveillance stills reproduce his 25-second timing?
2. What is the authoritative, sourced timeline for the manhunt duration he questioned?
