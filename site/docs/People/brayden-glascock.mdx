# Brayden Glascock

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Brayden Glascock is a living person. We document his reported videography role only. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Brayden Glascock** is listed in X.com witness research (Query 6, peopleglyph) as a **UVU-area videographer and content creator** — also described as a **Phoenix-area photographer** — who was in **close proximity** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event. Independent campus-adjacent footage fills gaps left by TPUSA production cameras and unreleased institutional [cameras](/cameras/overview).

Glascock appears in event photo and footage discussions alongside [Moyo Dasaolu](/People/moyo-dasaolu) and [Mathew Franke](/People/mathew-franke) on the videographer roster. His raw files, if preserved, could be compared against [Charles McClintock Wilson](/People/charles-mcclintock-wilson)'s ZUMA stills and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) cited under [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
