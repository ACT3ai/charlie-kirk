Tyler Robinson is **charged, not convicted**; [his profile](./tyler-robinson) and [Lance Twiggs](./lance-twiggs) connect to Miranda-timing disputes ([Nate Brooksby](./nate-brooksby)) and the [trial section](/court/overview).

**September 10 staffing** is its own puzzle: [Mikey McCoy](./mikey-mccoy) and [Blake Neff](./blake-neff) sit at the center of a **12:23–12:24 post-shot call timeline** that citizen investigators compare against footage. The [SUV security crew](./brian-harpole) reported **two distinct sounds**, criticized [UVU rooftop failures](./jeff-long), and appear in shaped-charge and [mic theories](/Mic/overview) as **attributed questions only** — not findings against any living person.
