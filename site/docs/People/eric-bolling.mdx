Eric Bolling is a well-known conservative television and radio broadcaster. His name appears in unverified online commentary that lists figures allegedly searched online in the weeks before September 10, 2025.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to unverified online "search-spike" commentary, Bolling's name is grouped with other [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) insiders and allies (such as [Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown), [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek), and others) said to have been searched in July–August 2025. In that framing, he is cited as part of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s broader media orbit — that is, as a possible person of interest to whoever conducted the searches, **not** as someone accused of any wrongdoing.

This is unverified commentary. The claim, even if accurate, would describe Bolling as an associate or potential subject of others' searches, not as a participant in any event.
