**Natasha Hausdorff** is a UK-based lawyer and pro-Israel advocate who has been mentioned in public commentary in connection with the reported **"Hamptons intervention"** — a meeting in early August 2025 involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and various pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Hausdorff is known for **legal commentary** and **advocacy on Israel-related issues** in UK and international media. Commentary threads (including material attributed to **@based_sam_parker**) cite her as bringing a **UK lawyer / Israel policy** perspective to the reported donor-pressure circle that allegedly confronted Charlie Kirk in summer 2025.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to social-media compilations, podcasts, and X/Twitter threads:

- Hausdorff is among the names commonly cited as a **reported participant** in the Hamptons-area meeting, dated **August 4–6, 2025** in most timelines.
- Other names **allegedly** mentioned in the same accounts include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), and [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki).
- Commentators describe the meeting as **confrontational**, with explicit asks for Charlie to adjust his public positioning on Israel-related topics, platform bookings, and donor expectations.
- Charlie Kirk **reportedly** described the weekend as an **intervention** rather than a retreat, per accounts in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

Attendee lists and specific statements remain **disputed** and are not confirmed by primary documents available to the public.
