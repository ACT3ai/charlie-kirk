# Natasha Hausdorff

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Natasha Hausdorff or any other living person. Hausdorff is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. All references to meetings are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Natasha Hausdorff** is a UK-based lawyer and pro-Israel advocate who has been mentioned in public commentary in connection with the reported "Hamptons intervention" — a meeting in early August 2025 involving Charlie Kirk and various pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Hausdorff is known for legal commentary and advocacy on Israel-related issues in UK and international media.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to social-media compilations and podcast discussions:

- Hausdorff is among the names commonly cited as a reported participant in the Hamptons-area meeting.
- Other names mentioned in the same accounts include Josh Hammer, Seth Dillon, Bill Ackman, and Rabbi Pesach Wolicki.
- Commentators describe the meeting as confrontational, with explicit asks for Charlie to adjust his public positioning on Israel-related topics.
- Attendee lists and specific statements remain disputed and are not confirmed by primary documents available to the public.

Her mention in this investigation reflects coverage of alleged pre-event donor and policy friction — not any accusation of wrongdoing.
