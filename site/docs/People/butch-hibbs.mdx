# Butch Hibbs

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Butch Hibbs is a living person. Online threads have discussed "cleanup crew" theories and pocket objects at the event. This page reports those claims with attribution only. We do **not** assert that Hibbs knew of any attack in advance, planted any device, or participated in evidence destruction.

## Investigative Value

**Butch Hibbs** is listed in Query 6 research among witnesses tied to **aftermath and response** discussions at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event — grouped with [Jason Behunin](/People/jason-behunin) and [Phillip Goldsberry Jr.](/People/phillip-goldsberry-jr) in "cleanup-crew" commentary threads. The master file emphasizes these figures as **witnesses to aftermath**, **not accused participants**.

Hibbs also appears in pre-event photos near Charlie Kirk. Investigation-file notes describe him as the brother of Pastor **Jack Hibbs** and a **Heber City, Utah** resident with long drone-industry experience — background context only, not proof of wrongdoing. Any sworn testimony on what Hibbs saw during response and tent-area activity could intersect [Tent](/Tent/overview), [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview), and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) timelines.
