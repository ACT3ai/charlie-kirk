**Zane Qureshi** is named in public commentary as the **brother of [Zach (Zachariah) Qureshi](./zach-qureshi)** and a **son of [Ahmed Qureshi](./ahmed-qureshi)**. He is a **different person from his brother Zach** — the two are frequently mentioned together. Very little about Zane is confirmed, and **no wrongdoing of any kind is asserted** on this page. The image at right accompanied the July 18, 2026 X post that named both brothers.

## How his name surfaced

- On **July 18, 2026**, [@MuppetMasher on X](https://x.com/MuppetMasher/status/2078404214313857083) posted: *"Zach and Zane Qureshi will become relevant again very soon. They were always relevant but it's coming around again. National guard just won't go away."* This is **the poster's own commentary** teasing future relevance — not an accusation and not established fact.
- Zane appears in this post specifically as **Zach's brother**. Where his brother Zach was **detained near the UVU event and released without charge** (see [Zach Qureshi](./zach-qureshi)), the public record around **Zane is far thinner**.

## What is (and is not) known

- **Confirmed relationship:** named publicly as a Qureshi brother, in the same family as [Ahmed Qureshi](./ahmed-qureshi).
- **Unconfirmed drone note.** An unverified note in circulation — **explicitly flagged "NEED TO DOUBLE CHECK"** — claims that a Qureshi brother (recorded as **"Tor"**) reportedly **specialized in drones at Fort Huachuca**. Whether that note refers to Zane, to a third brother, or to no one is **unverified**. It is noted here only so the open question is visible, **not** as a fact about Zane. Fort Huachuca also appears in a separate, unrelated [image-search claim](/GoogleSearches/fort-huachuca-image-searches).
- **No detention, charge, or evidence** of any role in the events of September 10, 2025 is reported for Zane.

## Family

- **Father:** [Ahmed Qureshi](./ahmed-qureshi) — described in commentary as a U.S. Navy Captain (O-6) and Bilt Inc. co-founder. Context only; no wrongdoing is asserted.
- **Brother:** [Zach Qureshi](./zach-qureshi) — the brother briefly named "in custody" on September 10 and then released.
- A dedicated **father / family** page may be expanded over time as the two brothers are documented.
