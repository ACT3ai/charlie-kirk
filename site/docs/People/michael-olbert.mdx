# Michael Olbert

Michael Olbert is described in this site's records as a person associated with the audio-visual / equipment setup at the September 10, 2025 [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) event at Utah Valley University where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to notes summarized on this site's [AV team page](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup), Olbert is reported to have had **equipment responsibility** at the event and is described as the brother-in-law of [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth). Citizen commentators have flagged that family connection as a "coincidence."

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- The AV/equipment role and the family relationship are drawn from **online commentary and investigation notes**, not from any official finding. The "coincidence" framing is speculation, not evidence.
- This site makes **no** claim that Michael Olbert had foreknowledge of, or any role in, the shooting. **No evidence** and **no charges** support any such suggestion.
- **Innocent explanation.** Handling event AV equipment is a normal job function, and being related to another staffer is not evidence of wrongdoing. The proposed [investigation laws](/Fix/overview) list AV personnel among those whose sworn statements and equipment logs should be reviewed — an investigative step, not an accusation.
