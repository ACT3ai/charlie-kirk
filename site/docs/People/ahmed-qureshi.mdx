# Ahmed Qureshi

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Ahmed Qureshi is a living person. We document his publicly reported role and an unverified coincidence argument only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

> **⚠️ This is an unverified "coincidence" argument, not a court finding.** Ahmed Qureshi's name appears inside a chain-of-coincidence thread raised in commentary (attributed to remarks by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and social-media researchers). **The site does not claim that Ahmed Qureshi, or the Qureshi or Henderson/Romney families, had any knowledge of or role in the assassination.** No charge, conviction, or evidence of wrongdoing is asserted.

## Investigative Value

Ahmed Qureshi is described in the commentary as an **active-duty U.S. Navy Captain (O-6)** and **co-founder of a Dallas-based technology company, Bilt Inc.**, which the thread says specializes in "3D interactive immersive intelligence" applications. He is also identified as the **father of [Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi)** — the young man the FBI, per the file, briefly named as a suspect and then released about ninety minutes later.

The coincidence thread adds that, according to federal spending records cited by a commentator, **Bilt Inc. reportedly received Department of Defense contract payouts in June 2025**. The thread strings these facts together and asks whether they are "staggering coincidences." This page treats all of it as **unverified, attributed commentary**. **No evidence ties Ahmed Qureshi to the shooting**, and holding a military commission or a defense contract is not evidence of any crime. See [Distraction People](/distraction_people/overview) and [US Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview).

## What is reported

- Per commentary, Ahmed Qureshi is an **active Navy Captain (O-6)** and **co-founder of Bilt Inc.** (with [Nathan Henderson](/People/nathan-henderson)).
- He is the **father of two brothers named in the case — [Zach (Zachariah) Qureshi](./zach-qureshi) and [Zane Qureshi](./zane-qureshi)**. Zach was **detained and released** during the manhunt, per the file; Zane is named in public commentary as Zach's brother, with little else confirmed.
- According to federal-spending records **cited by a commentator (Baron Coleman)**, Bilt Inc. reportedly received **DoD/Air Force payouts in June 2025**.
- The thread juxtaposes the payout, the family relationship, and a hospital connection to argue a "pattern," while explicitly telling readers to "draw your own conclusions."
- **Counterpoint (important):** These are described by their own authors as **possible coincidences**. **No evidence ties Ahmed Qureshi to the events at UVU.** A defense contract and a briefly-detained-then-released son are not proof of involvement. **This is commentary, not a court finding.**
