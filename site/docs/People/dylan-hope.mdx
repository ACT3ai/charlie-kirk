## Investigative Value

**Dylan Hope** is described in public reporting as a **26-year-old electrician working at a property next to the university** on the day of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting. He matters as a potential neutral eyewitness: he and his crew were reportedly shown a suspect photo by officers, which connects to the [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) and [Suspects](/Suspects/overview) discussions about whether the person initially sought matched the man later publicized.

Hope is described purely as a witness. Nothing accuses him of any wrongdoing.

## What is reported

- According to the reported account, Hope's crew described a suspect wearing black sunglasses, a black mask, a black trench coat, black cargo pants, a small backpack, and long dark hair. *(Attributed to the reported witness account.)*
- According to reporting, **Mr. Hope said the photo officers showed the crew on Wednesday did not appear to match the image the FBI released publicly on Thursday.** *(Reported statement.)*
- **Counterpoint:** A witness recollection of a suspect's appearance is an observation, not a legal conclusion, and eyewitness descriptions can vary or be mistaken. The account describes what Hope reports seeing; it makes no accusation against Hope himself.
