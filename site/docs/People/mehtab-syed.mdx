# Mehtab Syed

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Mehtab Syed is a living person. We document the publicly reported FBI personnel timeline only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Mehtab Syed is described in public commentary and reporting as the former FBI Special Agent in Charge (SAIC) of the Salt Lake City field office, appointed in February 2025 and replaced in August 2025 — roughly a month before the September 10, 2025 shooting. Citizen-researchers flag this leadership turnover as part of a set of Utah personnel changes they consider worth examining, alongside the arrival of successor [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls). See the [FBI](/FBI/overview) section.

One characterization of the departure ("forced out amid internal FBI changes") is an attributed reading drawn from public commentary, not a confirmed account of why the change occurred. Nothing here alleges any wrongdoing by Syed.

## What is reported

- According to public commentary, Syed was "appointed in February 2025" as SAIC of the Salt Lake City field office and was "replaced in August 2025," with successor Robert Bohls taking over.
- Public commentary describes the change as occurring "amid internal FBI changes under Director [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)"; some commentary characterizes it as being "forced out." That characterization is attributed commentary, not an established fact.
- The timing of the transition is noted by citizen-researchers as of interest given its proximity to the shooting; this is presented as an open question, not a conclusion.
- **Counterpoint:** Personnel turnover in a field office is common and can have entirely routine explanations. No public evidence connects Syed to the shooting or to any misconduct.

## Leadership Transition Context

Public research notes place Syed as FBI SAC Salt Lake City from approximately **February 2025** until departure in **August 2025**, succeeded by [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls). Citizen investigators flag the **proximity of that change to September 10** as a question about continuity of federal leadership in Utah — an **institutional timeline fact pattern**, not an accusation that Syed committed a crime.

Open question: What was the documented reason for the SAC change, and what pending case knowledge transferred?

## Who Said What (Attributed)

- Official FBI Salt Lake City communications after the shooting featured successor **Robert Bohls** as the public SAC face; Syed's earlier tenure is therefore mainly relevant as **pre-event leadership context**.
- Citizen investigators note the **August 2025 SAC change** under Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)'s broader FBI reorganization. Some ask whether Utah field-office priorities, tip-line handling, or liaison patterns shifted in the weeks before UVU — a **process question**, not a personal accusation.
