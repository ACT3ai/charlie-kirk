# Mehtab Syed

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Mehtab Syed is a living person. We document the publicly reported FBI personnel timeline only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Mehtab Syed is described in project notes as the former FBI Special Agent in Charge (SAIC) of the Salt Lake City field office, appointed in February 2025 and replaced in August 2025 — roughly a month before the September 10, 2025 shooting. Citizen-researchers flag this leadership turnover as part of a set of Utah personnel changes they consider worth examining, alongside the arrival of successor Robert Bohls. See the [FBI](/FBI/overview) section.

The file's framing of the departure ("forced out amid internal FBI changes") is an attributed characterization drawn from commentary, not a confirmed account of why the change occurred. Nothing here alleges any wrongdoing by Syed.

## What is reported

- According to project notes, Syed was "appointed in February 2025" as SAIC of the Salt Lake City field office and was "replaced in August 2025," with successor Robert Bohls taking over.
- Per the file, the change is described as occurring "amid internal FBI changes under Director Kash Patel"; one note characterizes it as being "forced out." That characterization is attributed commentary, not an established fact.
- The timing of the transition is noted by citizen-researchers as of interest given its proximity to the shooting; this is presented as an open question, not a conclusion.
- **Counterpoint:** Personnel turnover in a field office is common and can have entirely routine explanations. No evidence in the file connects Syed to the shooting or to any misconduct.
