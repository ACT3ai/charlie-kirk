# Matthew St Baker

Matthew St Baker (posting on X as **@matt82704417**) is a citizen investigator whose name appears on this site because he **reshared** a theory about the death of Charlie Kirk — not because he is a subject of suspicion.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to this site's [exploding-pendant page](/Mic/exploding-pendant-shaped-charge), a June 21, 2026 repost by St Baker amplified a "shaped-charge pendant" variant of the exploding-microphone theory. The original argument was advanced in a December 2025 thread by a different user identifying himself as a former Marine Corps explosive breacher.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- St Baker is described as an **investigator who reshared** the pendant theory. The theory itself — that a concealed explosive was in Charlie Kirk's crucifix pendant rather than his lapel mic — is presented on this site as the authors' analysis and **opinion, not established fact**.
- Resharing a theory is a commentary act; it does not make the underlying claim true, and this site does not adopt it as a conclusion.
