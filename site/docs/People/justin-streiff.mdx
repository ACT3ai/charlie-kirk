## Investigative Value

Justin Streiff is identified in public reporting as the **new Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Turning Point USA**, per a TPUSA memo dated **September 2, 2025** — about a week before the shooting. His name became a subject of commentary because **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) publicly alleged** that Streiff **"allegedly had a phone call right after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot"** with a person she believes to be a French-American named "[Pierre Du Pont](/People/pierre-du-pont)," and that a **potential large business partnership was on the line with TPUSA**.

That allegation is **attributed to Candace Owens and is contested**. It is presented here strictly as **her reported claim**, not as an established fact. The site makes **no assertion that Streiff had any knowledge of, or role in, the shooting**. See [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and [Motive](/Motive/overview).

## What is reported

- According to reporting, a **TPUSA memo dated September 2, 2025** named Streiff as the organization's **new COO**.
- According to a claim **attributed to Candace Owens**, Streiff **allegedly had a phone call shortly after the shooting** with someone she believes to be a French-American named "Pierre Du Pont," reportedly tied to a possible large partnership. This is **her allegation, not a verified event**.
- The surrounding commentary situates this within broader (and highly speculative) France/Israel-linked theories on the site; those theories are **disputed and unproven**.
- **Counterpoint:** No evidence has been presented tying Streiff to the shooting itself, and no charge or finding exists against him. The phone-call claim is **uncorroborated commentary attributed to a single source**, and the identity of the alleged other party is described as Owens's belief, not confirmed fact.

## TPUSA COO — Contested Phone-Call Claim

Justin Streiff is named in contested, attributed [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) claims about a phone call after the shooting. These claims are presented only as reported allegations attributed to their source, and this website makes no finding of any crime or wrongdoing.

The contested claim, as reported in public commentary, holds that Candace Owens said Streiff, the COO of TPUSA, allegedly had a phone call shortly after Charlie Kirk was shot with a person she believes to be a French-American. Related material has circulated alongside references to a TPUSA memo dated September 2, 2025 naming Streiff as the new COO and discussion of a DOGE-style outside audit. The phone-call allegation remains attributed to a single source and is unverified.

The **September 2, 2025** memo that named Streiff as COO is the same memo in which [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly announced an internal **DOGE-style financial audit** of TPUSA — eight days before his death. Some investigator accounts (e.g., [George Webb](/Influencers/x/george-webb)) frame the reshuffle as Kirk moving prior COO [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) out of that role over the audit. Streiff's appointment therefore sits at the center of the [COO-change](/TPUSA/charlie-replace-coo) and [DOGE-audit](/TPUSA/doge-audit) timeline.

**None of this implies wrongdoing by Streiff.** He is documented here only as the named incoming COO. The financial allegations covered in the video concern the organization's filings and are **disputed by TPUSA** (see [Results of the DOGE Audit](/TPUSA/doge-audit-results)).
