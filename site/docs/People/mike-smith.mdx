# Mike Smith

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Mike Smith is a living person. We document his public role as sheriff only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Mike Smith is named in the project's notes as the Utah County Sheriff. According to a government document quoted in the file, on September 11, 2025 Sheriff Smith received a phone call at approximately 8:04 PM from Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) of Washington County relaying that a former deputy had passed along a report from the accused's family — the account behind the reported "turn-in." Smith is also listed among the officials in attendance at the September 11 press event in Utah County.

Because parts of the reconstructed timeline are disputed by commentators, this page describes his named procedural role and flags the open questions as questions, not conclusions. See [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).

## What is reported

- Per a government document quoted in the file, Sheriff Smith received a call at ~8:04 PM (2004 hours) on September 11 from Sheriff Brooksby regarding the accused's reported turn-in.
- The project's notes list Smith among officials present at the September 11 press conference (alongside the governor, lieutenant governor, FBI leadership, and others).
- Some commentators have raised a timeline question — noting Smith was reportedly standing at the press event when the 8:04 PM call is said to have occurred, and asking how the sequence fits together. **This is a question raised by online commentary, not a proven claim, and the file's author expressly frames it as opinion.**
- **Counterpoint:** Officials commonly receive phone calls while attending events, and reconstructed public timelines can contain errors that are later clarified. Nothing in the source material shows that Sheriff Smith did anything improper.

## Open Questions

1. What is the precise, documented sequence of calls and notifications on the evening of September 11?
2. How do the press-event timing and the reported 8:04 PM call reconcile in the official record?
3. What contemporaneous records (call logs, reports) corroborate the government document's account?
