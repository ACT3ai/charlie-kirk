# Mark Harlan

Mark Harlan (also referred to as **Mark Harlin**) is a name that surfaces in online commentary about Utah Valley University and about search-trend activity around the September 10, 2025 assassination of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). He is listed among campus-associated names and among "TPUSA insiders and allies" whose names citizen researchers say were searched in mid-2025.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

His inclusion rests entirely on **unverified online lists** — a name appearing on a since-removed UVU society profile roster and among reported search-spike names. This site does **not** endorse those lists and makes **no** claim that he did anything wrong. If anything, the "in the crosshairs" framing used by some commentators casts listed names as possible targets of interest, not perpetrators.

There is **no evidence** of any involvement in the assassination and **no charges**.
