# Ben Shapiro

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Ben Shapiro or any other living person. Shapiro is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. References to meetings, donor dynamics, or media conflicts are drawn from **publicly reported commentary** and must be treated as unverified where not independently confirmed. **We do not claim** that Shapiro knew of or planned the assassination.

**Ben Shapiro** is the co-founder and editor emeritus of The Daily Wire, a conservative commentator, author, and one of the most prominent pro-Israel voices in American media.

## Background

Shapiro graduated from UCLA at age 20 and Harvard Law School at 23. He founded The Daily Wire in 2015 and built it into one of the largest conservative media companies. He is an Orthodox Jew and a vocal advocate for strong U.S.-Israel relations.

## Connection to the Investigation

### September 9, 2025 — Kirk on Shapiro's Show
Charlie Kirk appeared on Ben Shapiro's show on **September 9, 2025** — less than 24 hours before his death — and discussed Israeli policy. According to public reporting and video, this was one of Kirk's more direct public statements on the topic within the conservative movement.

Commentary threads (including **@based_sam_parker** compilations) place this appearance at the end of a rapid public escalation:

| Date | Kirk's reported public trajectory |
|---|---|
| **Aug 4–6, 2025** | Hamptons intervention — pro-Israel figures **reportedly** confront evolving stance |
| **Sept 1–5** | Group-chat statements: "no choice but to leave pro-Israel cause" |
| **Sept 8** | Public post leaving the pro-Israel movement |
| **Sept 9** | Shapiro show — criticized Israeli policy on air |
| **Sept 10, 12:23 PM** | Shot at UVU |

On the same day as the Shapiro appearance, sources **allege** Kirk told confidants during a high-pressure meeting: **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."** These are **attributed statements**, not independently verified facts.

### Hamptons Context (Indirect)
Shapiro is **not** among the names most commonly cited as a **reported attendee** of the August Hamptons intervention ([Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)). His relevance is primarily as the **September 9 broadcast platform** where Kirk's Israel-policy break became nationally visible — the day after his public exit post and the day before the assassination.

The Hamptons layer is documented separately in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) and [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).

### Intra-Conservative Media Friction
Via a **Megyn Kelly** clip circulated in X threads, Shapiro **reportedly** called [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'s questioning of the FBI version **"evil"** — an example of **intraconservative media conflict** over the investigation narrative. This is **reported commentary** about a living person, not a finding of wrongdoing.

Shapiro is a prominent figure within the broader conservative media ecosystem that intersects with [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and the donor networks discussed in the investigation's motive analysis sections.
