# Ben Shapiro

**Ben Shapiro** is the co-founder and editor emeritus of The Daily Wire, a conservative commentator, author, and one of the most prominent pro-Israel voices in American media.

## Background

Shapiro graduated from UCLA at age 20 and Harvard Law School at 23. He founded The Daily Wire in 2015 and built it into one of the largest conservative media companies. He is an Orthodox Jew and a vocal advocate for strong U.S.-Israel relations.

## Connection to the Investigation

### September 9, 2025 — Kirk on Shapiro's Show
Charlie Kirk appeared on Ben Shapiro's show on September 9, 2025 — less than 24 hours before his death — and discussed Israeli policy. According to public reporting and video, this was one of Kirk's more direct public statements on the topic within the conservative movement.
