Michael Powell is named in the project's research record and in citizen threads as a **reported owner of Hardscape Specialties LLC**, the corporate entity discussed in unverified claims about paving work in the Utah Valley University courtyard/tent area after the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). He is described online as a **Utah Attorney General special agent**. This page documents reported claims only; it does not assert that he knew of the event in advance, obstructed anything, or committed any crime.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to research notes and citizen commentary, Powell is listed as the owner of **Hardscape Specialties LLC** — an entity said to have been dormant for roughly twenty years and revived in February 2025 (see [Hardscape Specialties LLC](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties)). Investigators raise questions about who authorized courtyard changes near the shooting location within days of the event. Nothing in the public record establishes that Powell personally directed or altered any crime scene.

## Background

Publicly verifiable biographical detail is limited. He is described in the notes as affiliated with the Utah Attorney General's office as a special agent; this has not been independently confirmed on this site. Forming or reviving a limited-liability company and performing contracted paving work are ordinary, lawful activities.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Citizen threads describe Powell as owner of **Hardscape Specialties LLC**, alongside a co-owner said to be former UVU operations staff (per the [Tent](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties) page). This is a reported claim, not a confirmed filing summary.
- Commentators distinguish this LLC from the on-camera paver **Daniel Merrell / Hardscape Utah**; the relationship between the two names is an open question that business filings would resolve.
- Innocent explanation: a revived LLC and post-event paving may reflect routine campus repair and standard estate/business practice, with no connection to the investigation.
