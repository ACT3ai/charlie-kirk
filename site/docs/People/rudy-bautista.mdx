# Rudy Bautista

Rudy Bautista is a defense attorney who, according to a KUTV 2News Investigates segment that circulated in November 2025, was quoted raising concern about the reported absence of publicly released video of [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s surrender and booking at the Washington County, Utah Sheriff's Office.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to the reporting, Bautista commented on the records-response controversy discussed on the site's [Sheriff Video Withheld](/CoverUp/Sheriff_Video_Withheld) page. He is described as calling such footage "crucial for the defense work of mitigation," and reportedly noted that if the video had been lawfully transferred to Utah County investigators, he would have expected the records letter to say so rather than report no responsive records at all.

## Background

Bautista is identified in the coverage as a defense attorney offering professional commentary. This site treats his remarks as reported legal opinion, not as findings of fact.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- According to the KUTV 2News reporting, Bautista characterized the missing surrender/booking footage as important to mitigation. This is his stated professional view; the Sheriff's Office reportedly attributed the absence of footage to a routine 30-day retention/overwrite policy — an innocent explanation that remains on the table.
- The reported absence of footage does not, by itself, establish that anything improper occurred.

## Open Questions
