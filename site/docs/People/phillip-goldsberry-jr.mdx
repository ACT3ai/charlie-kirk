> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Phillip Goldsberry Jr. is a living person. Commentary has discussed microphone placement and "cleanup crew" threads. We report attributed claims only. We do **not** assert that Goldsberry knew of any attack in advance, rigged any device, or destroyed evidence.

## Investigative Value

**Phillip Goldsberry Jr.** is an **AV team member** who reportedly **mic'd up Charlie Kirk** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event — placing him at the center of [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) and audio-forensics debates. He worked alongside [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) and other close-proximity AV staff ([Robert Golo](/People/robert-golo), [Anthony West](/People/anthony-west), [Jayden Wilson](/People/jayden-wilson)).

Query 6 research also lists Goldsberry with [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs) and [Jason Behunin](/People/jason-behunin) in **cleanup-crew discussion threads**, explicitly as **aftermath witnesses, not accused participants**. His testimony on microphone setup, feedback issues, and real-time stage audio could be compared with [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) accounts and [Videos](/Videos/overview) / [cameras](/cameras/overview) releases.
