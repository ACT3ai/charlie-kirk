# Mike Miller

Mike Miller is named in the project's research record as a **board member of Turning Point USA (TPUSA)**, the organization Charlie Kirk co-founded and led. His name appears in the context of TPUSA governance and the organization's leadership succession after Kirk's death on September 10, 2025.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to notes summarized on the [Memorial and TPUSA Succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession) page, Miller is listed among TPUSA board members in the period leading up to and following the assassination. Serving on a nonprofit board is an ordinary governance role. Nothing in the public record ties him to the events at UVU beyond his organizational affiliation.
