# Yair Netanyahu

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Yair Netanyahu or any other living person. Yair Netanyahu is **not accused** of involvement in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death. All references to alleged presence, meetings, or connections are drawn from **unverified public commentary** and must be treated as unproven claims. **We do not claim** that Yair Netanyahu or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Yair Netanyahu** is the son of Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)**. His name has appeared in public commentary and citizen-investigation threads in connection with the Charlie Kirk case — primarily as a **reportedly cited attendee** of a July 2025 White House strategy session. **Hamptons presence claims exist only in unverified commentary and are NOT asserted here as confirmed attendance.**

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (July 31, 2025)

According to reporting and commentary compiled in the [Before](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) section:

- A **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House meeting is described as focused on countering **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel and rising antisemitism within MAGA.
- Yair Netanyahu is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), and [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun).
- Charlie Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were **reportedly noted as absent** despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel profile.
- These details come from **secondary sources**; no public transcript has been independently verified.

### Hamptons Claims — Unverified Only

Some social-media threads have **additionally claimed** Yair Netanyahu was present at or near the **August Hamptons intervention**. Per research notes (including **QUERY 7** compilations):

> **Presence claims are unverified commentary only; NOT asserted as confirmed attendance.**

The Hamptons roster most commonly cited in commentary includes [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff) — not Yair Netanyahu.

### Netanyahu Family Outreach Context (Reported)

Separately from Yair's cited meeting role, commentary threads **report** that Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu **called Charlie Kirk** to offer **financial support tied to an Israel trip**, which Kirk **reportedly declined**. This is attributed to **@based_sam_parker** and related donor-pressure compilations documented in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) and [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive). It is a **reported claim**, not an established fact about Yair Netanyahu personally.

### Other Claims in Commentary (Unverified)

Some commentators and citizen investigators have additionally claimed or speculated that Yair Netanyahu was allegedly present at or near certain other events connected to Charlie Kirk or associated figures in 2025. These claims originate from **social media posts and independent video analyses** — not from official sources or verified reporting.

**No official investigation has established or alleged that Yair Netanyahu had any connection to the events of September 10, 2025.**

## What We Do Not Claim

- Yair Netanyahu is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a strategy meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Yair Netanyahu or any person cited in meeting commentary knew of or planned the assassination.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

Commentators have cited his name in the context of broader theories about **Israeli political connections** and **pre-event meetings**. This page documents that his name appears in such commentary — it does **not** validate those theories or make any accusation against him.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

*(Attributed meeting/social claims — not findings of assassination involvement.)*

- @DefiyantlyFree (Jul 5, 2026) and related posts note that **Yair Netanyahu** publicly confirmed a **lunch with the Kirks in Jerusalem** during an Israel visit, framing it as ordinary pro-Israel networking in TPUSA-adjacent circles and rejecting “ongoing secret friendship” conspiracy framing. Photo/social confirmation is still **not** evidence of a crime.
- Investigation notes list Yair among attendees at a reported **July 31, 2025** White House–adjacent Israel strategy session with Ralph Reed, Luke Moon, Mario Bramnick, and [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun); Charlie Kirk / TPUSA were **absent** in those notes.
- Hamptons threads often discuss a **Benjamin Netanyahu phone call** narrative separately from Yair’s name; readers should not collapse father and son into one unproven “hit order” claim. No court has found either involved in the UVU killing.
