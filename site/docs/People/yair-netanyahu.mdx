**Yair Netanyahu** is the son of Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu. His name has appeared in some public commentary and citizen-investigation threads in connection with the Charlie Kirk case.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported White House Meeting (Late July 2025)

According to reporting and commentary compiled in the [Before](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) section:

- A late-July 2025 off-the-record White House meeting is described as focused on countering "woke-right defamation" of Israel and rising antisemitism within MAGA.
- Yair Netanyahu is among the names **reportedly** cited as an attendee, alongside Ralph Reed, Luke Moon, Mario Bramnick, and Yehuda Kaploun.
- Charlie Kirk and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) were reportedly noted as **absent** despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel profile.
- These details come from secondary sources; no public transcript has been independently verified.

### Other Claims in Commentary (Unverified)

Some commentators and citizen investigators have additionally claimed or speculated that Yair Netanyahu was allegedly present at or near certain events connected to Charlie Kirk or associated figures in 2025. These claims originate from social media posts and independent video analyses — not from official sources or verified reporting.

**No official investigation has established or alleged that Yair Netanyahu had any connection to the events of September 10, 2025.**
