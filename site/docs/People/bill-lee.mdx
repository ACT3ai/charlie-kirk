# Bill Lee

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Bill Lee is a living person. We document his public role and what commentators juxtapose only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Bill Lee is the Governor of Tennessee, the state where commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) resides. He appears in this investigation only because online commentators juxtapose his ordinary public activities — an economic-development trip to France and a meeting with an Israeli diplomat — with the timeline of a reported threat against Owens. These are the commentators' associations; the file itself notes the underlying events are routine gubernatorial economic-development and diplomatic activity.

For related context see [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [Motive](/Motive/overview).

## What is reported

- Per a cited timeline, on June 25, 2025 Governor Lee traveled to France in connection with a Howmet aerospace-engineering investment in Tennessee — described in the source as one of only a few international trips he had made in office.
- According to commentary, Governor Lee held a November 19 meeting with an Israeli Consul General in Nashville, framed publicly as continued support for Israel.
- The same commentary alleges these activities are suspicious in light of a reported (and disputed) threat against Candace Owens; the source explicitly presents this as connecting "dots" and asks readers to "demand answers" — i.e., it is opinion and advocacy, not a finding of fact.
- **Counterpoint:** Economic-development trips and meetings with foreign diplomats are standard, publicly announced functions of a state governor. Nothing in the file links Governor Lee to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shooting or to any threat, and no charge or court finding exists.
