# Apostle Mario Bramnick

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by any living person named here. All meeting references are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Apostle Mario Bramnick** is a Florida faith leader active in conservative political organizing. His name appears in **secondary reporting** about the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House session co-organized by the [Faith & Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), Latino Coalition for Israel, and Philos Project.

## Reported July 31 attendance

According to timelines compiled in [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) and [2025 Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025), Bramnick is among names **reportedly** listed alongside:

- [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)
- [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)
- [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun)
- [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)

Charlie Kirk and TPUSA were reportedly **absent**. No public White House transcript in this project confirms attendance.

## Why the name appears

Bramnick is cited as part of the **pre-UVU political map** — not as a suspect in the shooting. Researchers compare July 31 attendee networks to later **Hamptons** and **donor-pressure** threads ([Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons)).
