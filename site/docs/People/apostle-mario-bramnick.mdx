# Apostle Mario Bramnick

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by any living person named here. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Bramnick or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Apostle Mario Bramnick** is a Florida faith leader active in conservative political organizing. His name appears in **secondary reporting** as a **religious-political figure** cited in meeting claims about the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House session and the broader pre-UVU pressure map.

## Background

Bramnick operates at the intersection of **evangelical political organizing** and **pro-Israel advocacy**, placing him within the same ecosystem as the [Faith & Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition) and related groups that **reportedly** co-organized the July 31 session.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported July 31 Attendance

According to public commentary and the timelines in [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA):

- The **July 31, 2025** meeting was described as an off-the-record White House gathering organized by the **Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project** (**reportedly** funded by Paul Singer).
- The stated purpose described in that reporting was to discuss **"confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people"** and to combat **"growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement."**
- Bramnick is among names **reportedly** listed alongside [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu).
- **Charlie Kirk and [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) representatives were reportedly absent** from this "high-level strategy session, despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel alignment."

No public White House transcript confirms attendance.

### Pre-Hamptons Timeline

The July 31 meeting **reportedly preceded** the Hamptons intervention by about **four days** (early August 2025):

| Event | Approx. date | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| AMFEST military/intel meeting | **July 20, 2025** | Precedes both July 31 and Hamptons |
| White House strategy session | **July 31, 2025** | Bramnick **reportedly** among attendees |
| Hamptons intervention | **Aug 4–6, 2025** | Separate roster; Kirk **reportedly** confronted |
| Kirk public exit posts | **Sept 8, 2025** | Breaking point after summer friction |

Bramnick is primarily tied to the **July 31** layer in available commentary. The Hamptons roster more commonly cites [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).

### Donor-Pressure Context

Commentary threads tie the July 31 session to escalating **donor pressure** over Kirk's platform decisions — including hosting [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) and reassessing pro-Israel positioning. These threads are documented in [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and [Motive Overview](/Motive/overview) as **motive-layer context**.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Bramnick is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a strategy meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Bramnick or any meeting participant knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

Bramnick is cited as part of the **pre-UVU political map** — not as a suspect in the shooting. Researchers compare July 31 attendee networks to later **Hamptons** and **donor-pressure** threads ([Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons)).
