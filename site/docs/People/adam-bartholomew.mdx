# Adam Bartholomew

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Adam Bartholomew is treated as a living person on this page. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Adam Bartholomew** is listed among **general witness accounts** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event in X.com research (Query 6, peopleglyph). He belongs to the large attendee cohort whose collective testimony could corroborate crowd density, acoustic character, and post-shot movement — especially when compared with front-row accounts in [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video).

Bartholomew is grouped with [EksAyn Anderson](/People/eksayn-anderson), [Jeremy King](/People/jeremy-king), [Michael Dutsun](/People/michael-dutsun), and [Minjae Kim](/People/minjae-kim). Even witnesses without viral clips may hold FBI 302 material or phone video subject to the deletion-pressure threads documented under [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).
