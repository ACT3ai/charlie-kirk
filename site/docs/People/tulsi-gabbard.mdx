## Investigative Value

Tulsi Gabbard is the Director of National Intelligence (DNI). Her connection to the case in the project file is thin and entirely second-hand: she is named in a cited account attributed to Russell Brand, who said two unnamed sources described a meeting where the investigation's direction was discussed. This page is deliberately modest because the underlying material is limited and unverified. See the [US Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview) section for the broader context.

Separately, project notes referencing Candace Owens's commentary place Gabbard among officials reportedly interested in the case. All of this is attributed commentary, not established fact; Gabbard is not accused of any wrongdoing.
