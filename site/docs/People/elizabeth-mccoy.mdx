## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

The only basis for the name's appearance is uncorroborated online commentary, which lists her among people reportedly contacted by phone in the minutes after the shooting. Being named as someone who received a phone call is not evidence of any knowledge of, or involvement in, the event, and has ordinary innocent explanations.

There is **no evidence**, and there are **no charges**, connecting this individual to the assassination or to any wrongdoing.
