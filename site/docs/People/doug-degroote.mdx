# Doug DeGroote

Doug DeGroote is reported to be a board member of Turning Point USA (TPUSA). His name appears in online commentary about the organization's internal governance in the period after Charlie Kirk's death on September 10, 2025.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to a reported pre-September 10 board roster, DeGroote is listed as a TPUSA board member alongside Charlie Kirk, Erika Kirk, David Engelhardt, Mike Miller, and Tom Sodeika. His name subsequently appears in commentary — much of it associated with the [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) *Bride of Charlie* ecosystem — discussing succession and governance questions at TPUSA.

These are **contested commentary and questions, not court findings or proven facts**. Every figure named in that commentary is living and has not been accused of any crime.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Online commentary alleges that, after Charlie Kirk's death, DeGroote sought to identify TPUSA members sympathetic to Candace Owens internally. This is an **unverified allegation** about internal organizational politics — not a crime, and not independently confirmed.
- Innocent explanation: routine post-leadership-transition personnel and governance reviews are ordinary in any organization, and no wrongdoing is established by such an allegation.
- No response or denial from DeGroote or TPUSA is documented here; readers should treat the claim as one side of a contested narrative.
