# Ory Rinat

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Ory Rinat or any other living person. Rinat is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Rinat or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Ory Rinat** is a communications executive and former Trump-administration official named among the reported participants in the **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025** — the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Per commentary and reporting, Rinat is described as a **former Special Media Advisor to Jared Kushner** and a **former White House chief digital officer** under President Trump. He is reported to be the **CEO of Urban Legend**, a PR firm said to work with a large network of influencers. Commentary threads describe him as having **ties to the Netanyahu government**. These are **reported descriptions** of his professional background.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to *The Grayzone* sourcing and attendee lists circulated on X:

- Rinat is **cited in attendee lists** for the gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- **Limited direct public commentary** from Rinat about the event itself appears in the X discussions.
- Other reported attendees include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), [Nate Friedman](/People/nate-friedman), and [Xaviaer DuRousseau](/People/xaviaer-durousseau).

His specific role and anything said at the gathering remain **unconfirmed** and rest on **secondary sourcing**, not primary documents.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Rinat is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Rinat or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

His mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 gathering, where he appears on attendee lists — not any accusation of wrongdoing.

2. **Alleged foreign-government adjacency:** some commentary threads claim ties to the **Netanyahu government**. Those are **unverified network allegations**, not court findings.
