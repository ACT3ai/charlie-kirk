# Arynne Wexler

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Arynne Wexler or any other living person. Wexler is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Wexler or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Arynne Wexler** is a former **Goldman Sachs** trader turned conservative and pro-Israel influencer, named among the reported participants in the **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025** — the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving Charlie Kirk and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Wexler moved from a finance career at Goldman Sachs into political commentary and social-media influence, where she has been a **vocal Zionist voice**. She has publicly **praised [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)'s activism**. She sits within the same advocacy circle as several figures cited in the [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) accounts.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to *The Grayzone* reporting and attendee lists circulated on X:

- Wexler is cited among the **reported attendees** of the gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- She has offered **no detailed public statement** about the event and **did not respond** to *The Grayzone*'s inquiries.
- Other reported attendees include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), [Emily Wilson](/People/emily-wilson), and [Nate Friedman](/People/nate-friedman).

Her specific role and anything said at the gathering remain **unconfirmed** and rest on **secondary sourcing**, not primary documents.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Wexler is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Wexler or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why Her Name Appears in This Investigation

Her mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 gathering and her public support for Ackman's advocacy — not any accusation of wrongdoing.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

*(The following are unverified claims from public commentary, not findings of fact. Wexler is a living person; no court has found her guilty of any crime related to this case.)*

On X, **Arynne Wexler** has drawn **more accusatory mention volume** than most other reported Bridgehampton attendees. A recurring series from account **@Bayer76L** (late 2025–early 2026) repeatedly named her as part of an "Israel-First" group that allegedly **pressured or blackmailed** Charlie Kirk at the Hamptons weeks before his death. Those posts are **inflammatory, unproven allegations** by a private social-media user; this site documents that they circulated, it does **not** adopt them.

A still more extreme December 2025 post (**@vonSinwar313**) grouped Wexler with [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon) and [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) and claimed that after an unsuccessful intervention the group "gave the go-ahead" for a foreign kill decision. That is a **conspiracy accusation without court support**. It is recorded here only as an example of how far public commentary has gone — and it is explicitly **not** this site's finding.

More measured investigator discourse (Owens, Grayzone-linked threads, Project Constitution summaries) keeps Wexler in the **attendee / pro-Israel influencer** category: reported presence at an Ackman-associated Bridgehampton gathering, vocal Zionist advocacy, public praise of Ackman's activism, and **no detailed public statement** to *The Grayzone*. Investigators who want primary evidence typically ask for:

- Contemporaneous guest lists, hotel folios, and payment trails
- Any recorded remarks or texts from the gathering about Kirk's platforming of Israel-skeptical guests
- Wexler's own account of the meeting, if she chooses to give one

**Counter-narrative:** Friendly post-event WhatsApp/texts published by Ackman are used by his defenders to argue the gathering was **not** a blackmail session. That dispute is political and factual-claim dispute — it is **not** a criminal adjudication of any attendee.
