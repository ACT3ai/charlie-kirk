# Lori Robinson

Lori Robinson is named in online commentary as the owner of a general-contracting company (Crew General Contractors Inc.) that citizen researchers say was associated with construction work near the September 10, 2025 event location at Utah Valley University.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Her name appears in unverified online speculation only. One social-media post noted the shared surname with the accused (Tyler Robinson) while itself acknowledging the two "may or may not be related." This site does **not** endorse, adopt, or detail that speculation.

There is **no evidence** connecting Lori Robinson to the assassination, and **no charges** have been filed against her. The surname coincidence alone establishes nothing.
