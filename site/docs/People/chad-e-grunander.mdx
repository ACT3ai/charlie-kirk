Chad E. Grunander (also referred to as **Chad Grunander**) is a Utah County
prosecutor identified in the prosecution of **State of Utah v. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)**.
The county's press materials list him as **Deputy / Chief Deputy Utah County
Attorney**.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Grunander appears as a named prosecutor on the Utah County Attorney's team
handling the capital aggravated-murder case against the accused. A **Notice of
Appearance** for Chad Grunander for the State is recorded in the case docket
(dated in the investigation notes as 9/30/25). His documented role is a **public
prosecutorial function**; this page records public roles and court actions only
and makes no claim of wrongdoing.
