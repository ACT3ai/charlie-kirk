# Jason Behunin

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Jason Behunin is a living person. "Cleanup crew" framing appears in social-media commentary. This page reports that framing with attribution only. We do **not** assert that Behunin knew of any attack in advance or destroyed evidence.

## Investigative Value

**Jason Behunin** is named in Query 6 research within **cleanup-crew conspiracy discussions** alongside [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs) and [Phillip Goldsberry Jr.](/People/phillip-goldsberry-jr). The source material explicitly classifies these individuals as **witnesses to aftermath**, **not accused participants** — a distinction this page preserves for defamation safety.

Behunin's investigative value lies in potential testimony about **response timing**, tent-area activity, and who was present during early scene control — topics that overlap [Tent](/Tent/overview), [(Possible) Cover-up](/CoverUp/overview), and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview). Unreleased FBI 302 reports and [cameras](/cameras/overview) footage of the cleanup window could confirm or refute viral claims without naming Behunin as a suspect.
