# Yehuda Kaploun

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by any living person named here. All meeting references are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Yehuda Kaploun** appears in project timelines as a **reported attendee** of the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House meeting on Israel-related politics within the MAGA movement.

## Reported meeting role

Sources aggregated on [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons), [Timeline/Charlie_Kirk](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk), and [July 31 Meeting](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) list Kaploun among participants when **Charlie Kirk and TPUSA were absent**. Per CK_FILE's "WhiteHouse" note, the other reported attendees included **Ralph Reed, Luke Moon, Apostle Mario Bramnick, and Yair Netanyahu** (Benjamin Netanyahu's son).

According to CK_FILE, the session was **organized by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project** (described in the notes as **funded by Paul Singer**). Meeting themes in those sources include:

- Countering **"woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people"**
- Combating **"growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement"**

The same notes state the July 31 gathering **preceded the Hamptons intervention by about four days** (early August 2025). Details are reconstructed from **commentary and reporting**, not a released transcript.
