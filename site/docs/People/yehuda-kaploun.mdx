# Yehuda Kaploun

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by any living person named here. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Kaploun or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Yehuda Kaploun** appears in project timelines and citizen-research threads as a figure with **Israeli connections** cited in commentary about pre-UVU political strategy sessions. He is most prominently listed as a **reported attendee** of the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House meeting on Israel-related politics within the MAGA movement.

## Background

Public commentary and CK_FILE research notes describe Kaploun in connection with **pro-Israel advocacy networks** operating at the intersection of faith-based conservative organizing and foreign-policy messaging. His professional biography in mainstream sources is thinner than figures such as [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed); this page documents **why his name appears in the investigation**, not a comprehensive biography.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported July 31 White House Meeting

Sources aggregated on [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons), [Timeline/Charlie_Kirk](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk), and [July 31 Meeting](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) list Kaploun among participants when **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and TPUSA were absent**. Per CK_FILE's "WhiteHouse" note, other **reportedly** cited attendees included:

- [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)
- [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)
- [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick)
- [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu) ([Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)'s son)

The session was **reportedly organized** by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project (described in notes as **funded by Paul Singer**). Meeting themes in those sources include:

- Countering **"woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people"**
- Combating **"growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement"**

Details are reconstructed from **commentary and reporting**, not a released transcript.

### Network Overlap With Hamptons Pressure Layer

The July 31 gathering **reportedly preceded** the Hamptons intervention by about **four days** (early August 2025). Researchers compare attendee networks across both sessions to map **pre-event political pressure** documented in [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive):

| Session | Approx. date | Kirk/TPUSA presence (reported) |
|---|---|---|
| AMFEST military/intel meeting | **July 20, 2025** | Kirk at AMFEST ecosystem |
| White House strategy session | **July 31, 2025** | Kirk and TPUSA **reportedly absent** |
| Hamptons intervention | **Aug 4–6, 2025** | Kirk **reportedly** confronted by pro-Israel figures |

Kaploun is primarily tied to the **July 31** layer in available commentary. His **Israeli connections** are cited in citizen research as part of the broader foreign-policy motive map — not as evidence of involvement in violence.

### Charlie Kirk's Later Public Break

After these reported summer sessions, Kirk's attributed trajectory (treat as **alleged**):

- **Sept 1–5**: Group-chat statements about having "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"
- **Sept 8**: Public post leaving the pro-Israel movement
- **Sept 9**: [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) show appearance with direct Israel-policy criticism

Researchers connect these to **escalating donor friction** — not as proof of who ordered violence.

- **Reported presence** at a strategy meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Kaploun or any meeting participant knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.
