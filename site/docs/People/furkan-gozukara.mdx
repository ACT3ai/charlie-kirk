Furkan Gözükara, who posts on X as **@FurkanGozukara**, is a social-media user whose posts appear among the sources that circulated online commentary about the September 10, 2025 events at Utah Valley University. He is referenced here only as a poster who shared content, also referred to as **@FurkanGozukara**.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to this site's source notes, posts from the @FurkanGozukara account shared and amplified video clips tied to the online discussion — including a segment attributed to Stew Peters and clips referenced in discussion of a Buckley Carlson video. In that role he is a distributor of third-party commentary, not a subject of the investigation.

Sharing or reposting clips does not imply endorsement of, or responsibility for, the underlying claims, which are themselves unverified.
