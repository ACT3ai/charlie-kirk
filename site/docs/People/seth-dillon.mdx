# Seth Dillon

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Seth Dillon or any other living person. Dillon is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Dillon or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Seth Dillon** is the CEO of **The Babylon Bee**, a conservative Christian satire publication. His name has been mentioned extensively in public commentary in connection with the reported **"Hamptons intervention"** — a meeting in early August 2025 where pro-Israel figures and donors **allegedly** confronted [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) about his evolving rhetoric and platform decisions.

## Background

Dillon has been a prominent voice in **conservative media** and **pro-Israel advocacy** circles. The Babylon Bee operates within the same ecosystem as [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview). Commentary threads (including **[@based_sam_parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** compilations) cite Dillon as part of the **donor network** layer that allegedly pressed Kirk in summer 2025.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to social-media threads, podcasts, and compiled timelines:

- Dillon is among the names commonly mentioned as a **reported attendee** at the Hamptons-area meeting, dated **August 4–6, 2025** in most accounts.
- Other names **allegedly** cited in the same accounts include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff).
- Commentators describe explicit asks for Charlie to adjust Israel-related positioning, platform bookings, and relationships with critics such as [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson).
- Charlie Kirk **reportedly** later characterized the weekend as an **intervention**, per accounts in [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

The tone, agenda, and specific statements from any such meeting are reconstructed from **secondary sources**; attendee lists have not been adjudicated in court or confirmed by primary documents.

### Deposition Shift (Reported)

A significant thread in the research record concerns how Dillon's public characterization of the Hamptons weekend **reportedly changed**:

| When | Dillon's account (as reported) |
|---|---|
| **Sep 16, 2025** (X post) | No "angry confrontation"; described as an enjoyable summer retreat |
| Later, under oath | **"Heated and at times spirited debate"** admitted, per accounts attributed to [Carrie Prejean Boller](/People/carrie-prejean-boller) questioning Dillon |

Commentary **alleges** that Dillon and [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) initially **misrepresented** the pressure Charlie faced after his death. Dillon is a **living person**; this page attributes the contrast to **reported deposition testimony and public posts**, not a court finding of dishonesty or wrongdoing.

### Donor-Pressure Context

Dillon's reported Hamptons role sits within the broader narrative in [Charlie — Israel, Donors, and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive):

- Kirk **reportedly** rejected a **$150 million** funding offer and faced **billion-dollar offer** rumors in commentary.
- Kirk's attributed private messages reference donor friction over Tucker Carlson's TPUSA appearances.
- The Hamptons gathering **reportedly** followed the **July 20 AMFEST** military/intel meeting and the **July 31 White House** strategy session by roughly four days.

These accounts illustrate **intensifying private disagreements** over Israel policy in summer 2025. They do **not** establish that Dillon had any knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk. Notably, former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that Kirk's disputes with pro-Israel donors were among the foreign-nexus lead areas his center was still examining when its parallel review was ordered to stop.

## What We Do Not Claim

- **Reported presence** at a donor meeting is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Dillon or any Hamptons attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.
