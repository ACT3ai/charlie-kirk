# Seth Dillon

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Seth Dillon or any other living person. Dillon is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. All references to meetings are drawn from publicly reported commentary and must be treated as unverified claims.
:::

**Seth Dillon** is the CEO of **The Babylon Bee**, a conservative Christian satire publication. His name has been mentioned in public commentary in connection with the reported "Hamptons intervention" — a meeting in early August 2025 where pro-Israel figures and donors allegedly confronted Charlie Kirk about his evolving rhetoric and platform decisions.

## Background

Dillon has been a prominent voice in conservative media and pro-Israel advocacy circles. The Babylon Bee is a widely followed outlet within the same ecosystem as [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Hamptons Meeting (Early August 2025)

According to social-media threads, podcasts, and compiled timelines:

- Dillon is among the names commonly mentioned as a reported attendee at the Hamptons-area meeting.
- Other names cited in the same accounts include Josh Hammer, Bill Ackman, Rabbi Pesach Wolicki, and Natasha Hausdorff.
- The tone, agenda, and specific statements from any such meeting are reconstructed from secondary sources; attendee lists have not been adjudicated in court or confirmed by primary documents.

These accounts are used in motive narratives to illustrate intensifying private disagreements over Israel policy in summer 2025. They do not establish that Dillon had any knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk or any connection to September 10, 2025.
