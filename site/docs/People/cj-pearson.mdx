# CJ Pearson

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by CJ Pearson or any other living person. Pearson is **not accused** of involvement in Charlie Kirk's death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that Pearson or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.

**CJ Pearson** is a conservative political activist and **chair of the Republican National Committee Youth Advisory Council**, photographed among the reported participants in the **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025** — the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

Pearson is a young conservative influencer and organizer who chairs the **RNC Youth Advisory Council**. He operates within the same influencer and organizing ecosystem as [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and other figures cited in the [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) accounts.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to photos and *The Grayzone* reporting:

- Pearson appears in **photos with other attendees**, including [Emily Wilson](/People/emily-wilson) and [Xaviaer DuRousseau](/People/xaviaer-durousseau), placing him at the gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- When questioned by *The Grayzone*, he **referred the inquiry to a communications director**.
- Other reported attendees include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), and [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon).

His specific role and anything said at the gathering remain **unconfirmed** and rest on **photographic placement and secondary sourcing**, not primary documents.

## What We Do Not Claim

- Pearson is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that Pearson or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

His mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 gathering — he appears in attendee photos — not any accusation of wrongdoing.
