## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

The sole basis for Daniel Neff's appearance in the investigation material is
online commentary that describes him as a relative of Blake Neff, a longtime
Charlie Kirk producer and TPUSA figure. Commentators have raised this family
connection as a question, not as any proven link to events at Utah Valley
University. There is no evidence that Daniel Neff had any involvement in, or
knowledge of, the September 10, 2025 assassination, and no charges or official
findings connect him to the case. Being related to another person is not, by
itself, evidence of anything.
