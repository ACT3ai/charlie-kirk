# Rick Cutler

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Rick Cutler** is a retired law enforcement and military veteran who was present at the UVU event on September 10, 2025.

## Background

### Military Service
Cutler served as a U.S. Army military police officer for six years, including active duty and reserves, prior to his law enforcement career.

### Law Enforcement Career
Cutler served with the Irving, Texas Police Department for approximately 29 years, retiring in April 2019 with the rank of tactical sergeant. He was assigned to Special Operations and served on the SWAT/tactical team for 22 of those 29 years, eventually leading the team. He also worked as an undercover officer executing search warrants and narcotics operations.

### Post-Retirement Humanitarian Work
After retiring in 2019, Cutler moved to Guatemala. According to his biography on the Kabod International website, he works as a missionary focused on aftercare for human trafficking victims and a community feeding ministry. He has also worked with Operation Underground Railroad (OUR), the anti-trafficking organization founded by Tim Ballard, conducting undercover operations to intercept trafficking networks. He was additionally involved with United Rescue Alliance and disaster relief training (including in the Bahamas after Hurricane Dorian).

## Presence at UVU Event

Cutler was present at the UVU event where Charlie Kirk was killed on September 10, 2025. According to official reports, he assisted in transporting Kirk to the hospital. Citizen investigators have referred to him as the "arm puller" based on video footage from the event. See [Rick Cutler analysis video (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tx84ViFZkaA) for the publicly available footage.
