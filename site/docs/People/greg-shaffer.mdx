# Greg Shaffer

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Greg Shaffer is a living person. We document his public role and his own reported statements only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Greg Shaffer (the file also spells the name "Schaffer") is described as a principal of **Shaffer Security Group (SSG)**, a protective-detail firm founded, per the investigation file, by a twenty-year FBI Special Agent together with [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole). His value to the case is as a **first-hand voice on how Charlie Kirk's security arrangement came together**. The file notes that SSG "didn't since 2022" work with TPUSA, and that the September 10 detail was instead run through Integrity Security Solutions — so Shaffer's public account is treated here as **context about a hand-off**, not an accusation.

According to citizen-research summaries of his remarks, Shaffer said he was **"stabbed in the back"** and offered "incredible new details about how Integrity Security Solutions came to guard Kirk in the first place." This is **his attributed characterization**; the site does not treat it as an established finding. See [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Cover-Up](/CoverUp/overview).

## What is reported

- Per the file, Shaffer is a principal of **Shaffer Security Group**, described as founded by a former FBI Special Agent and Brian Harpole, recruiting from U.S. Tier 1 operator and federal-agency backgrounds.
- Reportedly, SSG had **not worked with TPUSA since 2022**, and the September 10 detail was provided through **Integrity Security Solutions**, not SSG.
- According to summaries of his own statements, Shaffer said he was **"stabbed in the back"** and gave details on **how Integrity came to guard Kirk** — presented here as his attributed account.
- A September 2025 AP report referenced in the file reportedly suggests **last-minute rehiring for "specialized security,"** which commentators say raises transparency questions.
- **Counterpoint:** None of this establishes that Shaffer had foreknowledge of, or any role in, the shooting. The file frames his input as testimony about a business/security hand-off, not as evidence of a crime.

## Open Questions

1. What exactly did the "hand-off" from Shaffer Security Group to Integrity Security Solutions involve, and when did it occur?
2. What did Shaffer mean by being "stabbed in the back," and who does he say was responsible for the change?
3. Does an official record support the reported last-minute rehiring for "specialized security"?
