# Rick Cutler

:::caution
This page compiles publicly reported information and commentary. **Nothing here constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Rick Cutler.** No charges have been filed against him. All claims are attributed to their sources. Rick Cutler is presumed innocent of any wrongdoing.
:::

**Rick Cutler** is a retired law enforcement and military veteran who was present at the UVU event on September 10, 2025. Citizen investigators have identified him as the "arm puller" — an individual in a brown shirt and sunglasses whose actions before and during the shooting have drawn significant scrutiny.

## Background

### Military Service
Cutler served as a U.S. Army military police officer for six years, including active duty and reserves, prior to his law enforcement career.

### Law Enforcement Career
Cutler served with the Irving, Texas Police Department for approximately 29 years, retiring in April 2019 with the rank of tactical sergeant. He was assigned to Special Operations and served on the SWAT/tactical team for 22 of those 29 years, eventually leading the team. He also worked as an undercover officer executing search warrants and narcotics operations.

### Post-Retirement
After retiring in 2019, Cutler moved to Guatemala. According to his biography on the Kabod International website, he works as a missionary focused on aftercare for human trafficking victims and a community feeding ministry. He has also worked with Operation Underground Railroad (OUR), the anti-trafficking organization founded by Tim Ballard, conducting undercover operations to intercept trafficking networks. He was additionally involved with United Rescue Alliance and disaster relief training (including in the Bahamas after Hurricane Dorian).

## Role at the UVU Event

Cutler was present at the UVU event where Charlie Kirk was killed on September 10, 2025. According to official reports, he reportedly assisted in transporting Kirk to the hospital. Citizen investigators have focused on his behavior before the shooting.

## Key Observations (as reported from video analysis)

### Pre-Shooting Actions
According to citizen investigators who have analyzed event footage:

- Cutler was observed near the stage area in a brown shirt and sunglasses prior to the shooting.
- He reportedly set up or manipulated equipment on the left side of the stage area, described by some investigators as creating a "mechanical issue."
- He is alleged to have taken down or interfered with a camera positioned behind Charlie Kirk.
- Video from the SUV transport shows Kirk's head visible in the window — investigators note that Cutler was not yet in the vehicle at that point.

### Hand Device
According to investigation notes and video analysis, Cutler appeared to have a device around his hand that some investigators compared to a Pero Gesture Control Device — a wearable smart mouse that executes actions by gesture. The significance of this observation is debated among investigators.

### Google Searches
According to Google Trends analysis compiled in the investigation, the search term "Rick Cutler" spiked in July 2025, the same period that other searches related to the UVU event and key political figures were also elevated.

### "Barrel in Hand" Claims
Some citizen investigators (including @WeAreNotGTM) have published video analysis claiming to show Cutler concealing a barrel-like object in his hand. These claims are highly contested and should be treated as unverified theories.

## Open Questions

- What was Cutler's official role or reason for being at the UVU event?
- Was he part of the event security team, TPUSA staff, or present in another capacity?
- What specific actions did he take with equipment near the stage?
- What happened to the camera that was reportedly positioned behind Charlie Kirk?
- What was the device observed in or on his hand?
- Has he been interviewed by law enforcement, and has he made any public statements?
