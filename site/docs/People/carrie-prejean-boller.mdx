# Carrie Prejean Boller

Carrie Prejean Boller is a public figure whose name enters case-adjacent
discussion through online commentary reporting that she questioned
[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon) under oath about a so-called "Hamptons
meeting."

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to circulating online commentary, Prejean Boller "asked Seth Dillon,
while he was under oath, about the infamous Hamptons meeting with Charlie Kirk."
This site documents that reported exchange as **part of the broader
donor-meeting and Israel-friction discussion**, not as evidence of any
wrongdoing by any party. The nature, forum, and accuracy of the reported
questioning are **not independently verified here**.
