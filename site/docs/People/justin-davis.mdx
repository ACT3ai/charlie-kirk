# Justin Davis

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Justin Davis** is described in the investigation file as the **trained emergency driver** of the black SUV that carried [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) from UVU to Timpanogos Regional Hospital on September 10, 2025. While [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) and [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood) shielded Kirk and [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) stabilized the cabin during first aid, Davis controlled the **route and elapsed time** of the most scrutinized transport in the case. His testimony — or any unreleased GPS, dashcam, or phone data from the drive — could anchor or challenge competing hospital-arrival timelines cited by [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek), [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), and TPUSA spokespeople.

## Background

Davis is listed in the master witness roster and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) notes as part of **Integrity Security Solutions / Rockhouse Integrity** personnel associated with Kirk's protective detail. The investigation file groups him with [Pierce Beck](/People/pierce-beck) as security-company figures whose **on-site presence September 10** is documented in witness lists but whose public statements remain limited compared to Harpole or Turek.

His described specialty is **emergency driving** — cutting through traffic and traversing intersections under crisis conditions rather than a public-facing security chief role.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

According to the investigation file and grok-compiled security threads:

- Davis was the **designated emergency driver** in the SUV immediately after the ~12:23 PM shooting.
- He was one of **five SUV occupants** with Kirk: [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek), and Davis.
- The file describes him **cutting through traffic and breaking intersections** while rushing Kirk approximately **2.6 miles** to Timpanogos Regional Hospital in Orem — the facility Kirk was taken to rather than the closer Utah Valley Hospital in Provo (a routing choice discussed in [Medical](/Medical/overview) threads).
- No major independent media interview catalog is summarized in the file beyond his driving role; citizen timelines treat the SUV run as a **black-box interval** between courtyard chaos and hospital intake.

## X.com / Citizen Commentary (Attributed)

Per posts compiled in the People grok fan-out (Query 5) and investigation-file witness lists:

- **SUV core team listing:** Davis named consistently alongside Flood, Harpole, Cutler, and Turek as the **immediate transport crew** — a roster repeated in defense-witness commentary arguing [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s team should call SUV occupants.
- **Hospital routing debates:** Citizen threads question why Timpanogos Regional was chosen and whether drive time supports or undermines **4:00 PM hospital arrival** claims attributed to Frank Turek. Davis's route is central to that math — **attributed analysis only**, not a finding against Davis.
- **June 2026 SUV interior photos:** [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reportedly published SUV interior images analyzed for [Mic](/Mic/overview) debris theories. Davis is not the focus of those threads but sits in the same vehicle compartment under scrutiny.
- **Limited conspiracy targeting:** Unlike Cutler or Harpole, Davis has attracted **less** direct accusatory commentary in compiled grok results — his role is primarily **logistical** in public discourse.

## Investigative Questions

- What **route and elapsed time** did Davis take from UVU to Timpanogos Regional — and does it match GPS, dashcam, or phone data if released?
- What did Davis **hear or observe** from the driver's position during transport (sounds, statements, Kirk's condition)?
- How does SUV timing align with **hospital intake records** and public **4:00 PM vs 4:40 PM** tarmac-hug timeline disputes?
- Will Davis's **FBI Form 302** (if obtained) corroborate the emergency-driving account in the master file?
- Was any **alternate hospital** considered en route, and who made that decision?
