# Justin Davis

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

Emergency driver in the SUV

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Justin Davis was the trained emergency driver who cut through traffic and broke intersections rushing Charlie Kirk to the hospital after the shooting. He was part of the core SUV response team alongside Dan Flood, Brian Harpole, Rick Cutler, and Frank Turek.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Davis's testimony would establish the timeline and observations during the high-speed transport to the hospital and the immediate post-shooting chaos. His account of the route taken, time elapsed, and Kirk's condition during transport would be valuable for the investigative record.
