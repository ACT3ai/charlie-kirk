Steven DeNoon is an online commentator who posts under the handle @stevendenoon and describes himself as a former CIA analyst. He is cited in this investigation as a producer of YouTube content analyzing lasers, casings, ballistics, and alternative shooter-location theories.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to posts by DeNoon and collaborators, he has publicly presented what he describes as evidence pinpointing an alleged shooter location, including analysis of an infrared laser said to appear in footage from the event. These are the interpretations of independent online researchers and have not been confirmed by official investigators.
