# Pete Hegseth

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Pete Hegseth is a living person. We document his public office and what is reported by commentators only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Pete Hegseth is the U.S. Secretary of Defense. He appears in this investigation only through aircraft-timeline commentary: online researchers name a Department of Defense flight (callsign SAM702) as being "in his orbit" as the head of the Department, and note that the under secretary said to have been aboard a related flight reports to him. Nothing in the file connects Secretary Hegseth personally to the events at Utah Valley University; his relevance is entirely a function of the chain of command being discussed.

For the aircraft angle see [Planes](/Planes/overview); for the intelligence-agency discussion see [Proof: Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).

## What is reported

- According to a cited account by commentator Stew Peters, a DoD aircraft (tail number 99-0404, callsign SAM702) is described as "Pete Hegseth's plane." This is the commentator's characterization, presented as such.
- Per the same commentary, that flight is discussed in connection with a Department of Defense order and a later factory explosion; the source presents these as its own allegations and a timeline to investigate, not as proven facts.
- A separate cited account states that Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence Bradley Hansell "reports directly to Pete Hegseth" — a plain description of the Defense Department reporting structure.
- **Counterpoint:** Being the cabinet secretary at the top of a department's org chart does not, by itself, imply knowledge of or involvement in any specific flight or event. No evidence in the file ties Secretary Hegseth to the shooting, and no charge or court finding exists.
