# Joshua Peterson

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Joshua Peterson is a living person. We document his publicly stated account only, with attribution. We do **not** assert wrongdoing.

## Investigative Value

Joshua Peterson (posting as @RagingPatriot20) is described in the source material as a former Turning Point USA representative who came forward with an account from his time at a TPUSA training event. According to a reporting account (Project Constitution / @ProjectConstitu), Peterson alleges he was approached by individuals who claimed to be affiliated with a foreign intelligence service.

This is a single, contested, uncorroborated allegation by one former representative. It is explicitly **not** established fact, and — as the source file itself states — it is not a claim that [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), its leadership, or any employee knew of, approved, or carried out anything alleged. It is summarized here only to note the call for a trustworthy investigation.

## What is reported

* According to the reporting account, Peterson alleges that during a training event, established representatives questioned new hires about their views and that some later described themselves as affiliated with a foreign intelligence service.
* The claim is presented in the file with an explicit disclaimer that it is a single, unverified allegation.
* **Counterpoint (stated in the source):** No evidence establishes that TPUSA, its leadership, or any employee knew of or approved anything alleged, and no one is asserted to be a foreign-intelligence officer. The account is contested and calls for investigation rather than proving anything.
