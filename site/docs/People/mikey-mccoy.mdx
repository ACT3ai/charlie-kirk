# Mikey McCoy

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

## Role

TPUSA Chief of Staff, one of Charlie Kirk's closest friends.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

Standing feet away from Kirk on stage. Walked away immediately after shot (phone to ear, per viral footage and Candace Owens critiques). Called Erika Kirk, Elizabeth McCoy, and Rob McCoy. Spoke at memorials and on CrossExamined podcast about continuing Kirk's legacy. Subject of conspiracy theories about his reaction/walking away.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

Close eyewitness, calls made post-shot, and TPUSA internal response.
