# Mikey McCoy

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Mikey McCoy** is Chief of Staff at [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview) and one of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s closest friends. He was standing feet from the stage when Kirk was shot on September 10, 2025, and is central to citizen-investigator reconstruction of the **first-minute post-shot call chain**.

## Post-Shot Call Timeline (12:23–12:24)

The minute after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was struck at approximately **12:23 PM MDT** is among the most scrutinized intervals in the case. Public accounts from [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), and video stills cited in timeline compilations do not fully align — the project records this as an **open reconstruction question**, not a finding of wrongdoing.

| Time / claim | Source | Notes |
|--------------|--------|-------|
| **~12:23 PM** — shooting | Affidavit / widespread reporting | Kirk struck at UVU courtyard event |
| **12:23 PM Utah / 11:23 AM Arizona** — Erika says Mikey called her | Erika's public account | She has stated the call came at the moment of the shooting |
| Immediately post-shot — Mikey walks away, phone to ear | Viral footage; [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) timeline analysis | Widely circulated video shows him departing stage area with phone raised |
| **12:24:02** — Mikey past SUV **alone** | Video still cited in CK_FILE | Blake Neff is **not visible** in this frame despite his later witness claim |

### Calls Reported in Public Accounts

According to accounts that have circulated in media and commentary, Mikey placed or attempted calls to:

- **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** — timing disputed against video stills; see [Timeline overview](/Timeline/overview)
- **[Elizabeth McCoy](/People/elizabeth-mccoy)** — family contact (Mikey's mother)
- **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** — Arizona pastor; [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) has said he witnessed this call

**No accusation** in this project holds that Mikey McCoy knew of any assassination plan. Questions here concern **timeline reconstruction** and **organizational response** only.

## Role at TPUSA

Mikey McCoy serves as **Chief of Staff** — Kirk's top operational aide inside [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview). He is the son of [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy), co-founder of TPUSA Faith and Kirk's described personal pastor. Mikey has publicly described Kirk as his "boss, mentor, and close friend."

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- Standing **feet from Kirk** on stage at the UVU outdoor event
- **Immediate departure** from the stage area after the shot, phone to ear per widely shared footage
- **Memorial speaker** and appearances on CrossExamined podcast discussing continuation of Kirk's legacy
- Named alongside [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) and [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) in Candace Owens coverage of TPUSA's post-shot internal response and succession narrative

## Mikey–Blake–Kolvet Triangle

Citizen timelines treat **Mikey McCoy**, **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**, and **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** as a connected cluster in the minutes and days after the shooting: call-timing disputes (Mikey vs. Blake), Kolvet's public communications role, and TPUSA leadership messaging. See also [TPUSA Staff — Sept 10](/key_individuals/TPUSA_Staff_Sept_10).

## The Mic'ing Video

According to citizen-investigator commentary compiled in the master file, **Mikey McCoy reportedly filmed a close-up video of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) being mic'd up** before the event — the microphone setup that is itself a focus of the [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) analysis and the AV chain-of-custody questions surrounding [Phillip Goldsberry Jr.](/People/phillip-goldsberry-jr) and [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth). Commentators have publicly asked where that close-up footage is ("Mikey, where's that video?"), treating it as a potential documentary record of how the wireless mic was placed. This is an **open evidentiary question**, not an allegation of wrongdoing.

Public footage also **reportedly** shows Mikey driving or exiting an SUV that arrived at the stage area moments after the shot — cited in [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) timeline commentary alongside the call-timing dispute above.

## Potential Evidentiary Value

- Close **eyewitness** to the shooting and immediate stage response
- **Post-shot call records** — cell timestamps, duration, and tower data could resolve Erika/Mikey/Blake timeline gaps
- The **close-up mic'ing video** Mikey reportedly filmed — relevant to microphone-placement questions
- **TPUSA internal communications** in the first hour after the event
- Testimony on **event security posture** and who was notified when

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X — Call Timeline Controversies

Independent analysts continue to treat the **first post-shot minute** as a reconstruction problem. The items below are **attributed commentary**, not findings of wrongdoing by McCoy.

### Immediate Departure Footage

**[@KeliRabon](/Influencers/x/kelirabon)** (October 24, 2025) circulated a widely viewed analysis clip arguing that McCoy, standing near Kirk, **moved away with a phone to his ear within a split second** of the shot, without appearing to render aid. Rabon framed this as a question about **who was called and why**, not as courtroom proof of conspiracy.

### "Seconds" vs Minutes — Conflicting Public Accounts

- **[@baroncoleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)** (December 19, 2025) compared Erika Kirk's Jesse Watters interview language ("within seconds") to a timeline associated with [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) commentary suggesting a longer interval (~three minutes).
- **[@5149jamesli](/Influencers/x/5149jamesli)** (November 7, 2025) highlighted tension between Erika's Fox News account and statements attributed to [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) / TPUSA figures about whether McCoy was on the phone.

### Defensive / Clarifying Threads

Other users (e.g. July 2026 replies in hearing-related threads) assert McCoy called Erika **so she would not hear first from media**, and/or coordinated a **joint call with his wife**. Those are also **attributed explanations**, not adjudicated fact.

### Site Position

Questions about call logs, phone metadata, and video timestamps are **legitimate transparency issues**. They do **not** establish that Mikey McCoy had advance knowledge of any plot. See [Timeline](/Timeline/overview) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
