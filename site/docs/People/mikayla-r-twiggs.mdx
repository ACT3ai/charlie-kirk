Her name appears in publicly surfaced records: a Texas nonprofit filing and county property-ownership histories. She is a private individual. No member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of any crime in connection with the case.

Per records collected on the [Lance Twiggs Family](./Lance_Twiggs_Family) page (surfaced by an X user), Mikayla R Twiggs is listed as one of the **directors** of a Texas "Faith and Family" nonprofit and appears as a **co-seller** of a residential property transferred into a family trust in late 2025.
