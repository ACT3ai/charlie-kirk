# Bryan Starbuck

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing by Bryan Starbuck or any other living person. This page documents his publicly posted open-source analysis and advocacy — not criminal accusations against third parties as site findings.
:::

Bryan Starbuck is a citizen investigator who publishes independent analysis
under the online handle **HolonCitizen**. His work is cited on this site in
connection with the "Stairs Guy" video comparison — an open-source examination
of Utah Valley University surveillance imagery.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Starbuck's video analysis is referenced in this investigation's notes as the
basis for the [Stairs Guy vs Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy)
comparison. According to that analysis, the morning-arrival figure and the later
"stairs" figure differ across backpack fill, hat and clothing, and the
practicality of carrying a scoped rifle — arguments offered as an identity
challenge to the official narrative. These are presented as **open-source
analytical claims for public scrutiny**, not as forensic conclusions.

## Background

- Publishes investigative video content on YouTube as
  [HolonCitizen](https://youtube.com/@HolonCitizen).
- Compiles findings into public repos and the private working notes underpinning this site (including GitHub material under the BryanStarbuck namespace referenced in research notes).
- Active in X threads on Miranda timing, ballistics energy comparisons (.30-06 vs 9mm framing in his posts), and disclosure legislation.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Attributed)

According to **[@HolonCitizen](/Influencers/x/holoncitizen)** posts captured in July 2026 research:

- Starbuck **publicly claims authorship** of draft **Charlie Kirk Forced Disclosure** legislation and promotes four-law frameworks discussed on this site under [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview). In a long-form video post (approx. July 9, 2026), he argued Law #4 would empower "trusted investigators" and listed names including [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll), [Jon Bray](/People/jon-bray), Dave Smith, [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent), and others — **advocacy**, not enacted statute.
- He frames disclosure bills as a path to foreign-intel, aircraft, [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview), and AES-factory records — claims about what laws *could* surface, not proven conclusions about those topics.
- Other posters reply to HolonCitizen on Miranda/confession-timing debates and "lost" station-entry video questions; those threads are **commentary clusters**, not court findings.
