# Steven Crowder

Steven Crowder is a well-known conservative commentator and media host. His name enters the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) discussion through online commentary about the flow of case information in the days following the September 10, 2025 shooting.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to commentary cited in this investigation, Crowder was described as having received or aired information about the case. Reporting on and receiving leaked or early information is a normal part of journalism and commentary and, on its own, implies no wrongdoing.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Online commentary references Crowder as one of several media figures who discussed leaked or early details about the shooter's asserted motive. The accuracy, timing, and source of any such information are not established here.
- No claim on this page asserts that Crowder had foreknowledge of the event or engaged in any misconduct; that is not alleged.
