# Xaviaer DuRousseau

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or ethical violation by Xaviaer DuRousseau or any other living person. DuRousseau is **not accused** of involvement in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death. Meeting attendance and motive allegations cited in commentary are **reported claims**, not court findings. **We do not claim** that DuRousseau or any other person named here knew of or planned the assassination.
:::

**Xaviaer DuRousseau** is a conservative influencer and **PragerU** personality who has been named as a participant in the reported **Bridgehampton gathering of August 4-5, 2025** — the event [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others later called the **"Hamptons intervention"** involving Charlie Kirk and pro-Israel figures.

## Background

DuRousseau is known for content produced through **PragerU** and for commentary across conservative media. He works within the same influencer ecosystem as [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and other figures cited in the [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) accounts.

## Connection to the Investigation

### Reported Bridgehampton Gathering (Aug 4-5, 2025)

According to X/Twitter threads, photos, and *The Grayzone* reporting:

- DuRousseau is named as a **reported attendee** at the Bridgehampton gathering convened by [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman).
- He later released a statement **recalling Charlie Kirk complaining about "moral blackmail"** during an argument over Israel — while **contextualizing the event** in line with other attendees who described normal, robust discussion rather than a coordinated intervention.
- Other reported attendees include [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), [Emily Wilson](/People/emily-wilson), and [CJ Pearson](/People/cj-pearson).

The tone and specific statements from any such meeting remain **disputed** and are reconstructed from **secondary sources**.

### Reported Israel-Related Trip

Commentary notes that DuRousseau later received an **all-expenses-paid Israeli government trip** to a Gaza aid hub, where he filmed content disputing famine claims and attributing blame to the UN and Hamas. Some X posts criticized this framing; others defended it. This is **reported context** about his public activity, not evidence of any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.

## What We Do Not Claim

- DuRousseau is **not** identified by any official investigation as a suspect.
- **Reported presence** at a gathering is **not** evidence of knowledge of threats against Charlie Kirk.
- **We do not claim** that DuRousseau or any attendee knew of or planned the assassination on September 10, 2025.

## Why His Name Appears in This Investigation

His mention reflects coverage of the reported summer 2025 donor-and-policy gathering — including his own on-record recollection of Kirk's "moral blackmail" remark — not any accusation of wrongdoing.

- Viral Hamptons / Bridgehampton X threads in late 2025–2026 focus heavily on **Bill Ackman**, **Josh Hammer**, and alleged **Netanyahu call** pressure on Charlie Kirk; DuRousseau appears less often as a lead name and more as a listed PragerU / conservative-influencer attendee in the same social map.
