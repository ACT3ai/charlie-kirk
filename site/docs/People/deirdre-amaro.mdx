# Dr. Deirdre Amaro

Dr. Deirdre Amaro is identified in investigation material as Utah's chief medical
examiner, reportedly having begun in the role in mid-2025. Her name appears in
notes about the autopsy of Charlie Kirk following the September 10, 2025 shooting
at Utah Valley University. Holding a statewide medical-examiner post is an
ordinary official role and, as the source material itself states, implies no
wrongdoing.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to compiled research on the Charlie Kirk investigation site, Dr. Amaro
is described as Utah's chief medical examiner who "reportedly started working
full-time" in that position on or around July 1, 2025 — roughly two months
before the shooting. Utah autopsies are handled through the statewide Office of
the Medical Examiner. Because commentators have raised general questions about
institutional continuity given her recent start date, her name is catalogued;
the site explicitly notes this is an ordinary role that implies no wrongdoing.

## Reported Claims (Unverified)

- Investigation notes state Dr. Amaro reportedly began full-time as Utah's chief

  uncorroborated commentary and open questions, not as established fact, and an
  innocent explanation — routine name searches around a new appointment — is at
  least as consistent with any such data.
- No evidence, charge, or official finding connects Dr. Amaro to any wrongdoing.
