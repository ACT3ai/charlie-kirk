## Note on framing

Legacy trash files used the title **"Girlfriend Investigation"** — an **inaccurate framing**. **Charlie Kirk was married to [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk).** No credibly sourced mainstream reporting has established a separate romantic partner. This page exists to **document that fringe claims circulated** while preventing false implications against unnamed individuals.

## What public reporting establishes

- Kirk was **married to Erika Kirk** at the time of his death
- Erika Kirk appears in **memorial reporting**, **Medal of Freedom** coverage, and **TPUSA succession** discussions ([TPUSA Replacement Timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline))
