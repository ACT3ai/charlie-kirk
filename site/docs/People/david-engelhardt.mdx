David Engelhardt is named in investigation material as one of the reported
members of the [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) (TPUSA) board of directors as it stood before
September 10, 2025. His inclusion reflects that governance role and does not
carry any allegation of wrongdoing.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

According to compiled reporting on the Charlie Kirk investigation site, TPUSA's
board of directors prior to September 10 reportedly included [Doug DeGroote](/People/doug-degroote),
[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), David Engelhardt, [Mike Miller](/People/mike-miller), and [Tom Sodeika](/People/tom-sodeika).
Because much of the post-September 10 commentary concerns questions about TPUSA
governance and succession, board members of that period are catalogued for
completeness. The site notes that these succession questions are debates and
open questions, not adjudicated findings, and nothing here suggests Engelhardt
acted improperly.
