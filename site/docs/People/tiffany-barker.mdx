# Tiffany Barker

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Tiffany Barker is a living person. We document only that her name appears in early-witness commentary, with attribution. Any "crisis actor" or coordination framing that appears in that commentary is an **unproven allegation by posters and hosts**, not a fact. We do **not** assert that Barker did anything wrong.

## Investigative Value

**Tiffany Barker** is named in commentary — notably a [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) segment — as one of the early witnesses who spoke to the media and described [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) as having been shot in the **chest / near the heart**. That description matters to the case because it differs from the reported neck-wound account, and it is discussed in the [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) material.

Commentary also notes that Barker was **reportedly associated with a local political campaign** ([Phil Lyman](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman)'s). This is documented only as a reported association. **It is not evidence of wrongdoing**, and no crime is alleged against her here.

## What is reported

- According to a Candace Owens segment summarized in posts, Barker was among witnesses who told news outlets that Kirk was hit in the chest/heart. *(Attributed commentary.)*
- The same commentary notes she was **reportedly working on Phil Lyman's campaign**. *(Reported association only.)*
- The host **speculates** that early witnesses may have been "coordinated." **This is the host's opinion and an unproven allegation**, not a finding, and this page does not adopt it.
- **Counterpoint:** Witnesses under stress frequently misdescribe wounds; a differing account is not proof of a plan. A campaign job is lawful, ordinary work and implies nothing improper. No evidence in the file ties Barker to the shooting, and she has not been charged.
