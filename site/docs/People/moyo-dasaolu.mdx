# Moyo Dasaolu

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

> **Living Person Notice:** Moyo Dasaolu is a living person. We document his reported videography role only. We do **not** assert foreknowledge of the attack or any criminal involvement.

## Investigative Value

**Moyo Dasaolu** is listed among the **videographer/photographer roster** at the September 10, 2025 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event in X.com research (Query 6, peopleglyph). Described as a **UVU review videographer and content creator** involved in global-engagement campus media, Dasaolu represents the student-documentary layer of evidence that may survive outside TPUSA-controlled SD cards.

Footage from Dasaolu could be synchronized with [Brayden Glascock](/People/brayden-glascock), [Mathew Franke](/People/mathew-franke), and press stills from [Charles McClintock Wilson](/People/charles-mcclintock-wilson). Investigators compare such material against [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) accounts and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) when official [Videos](/Videos/overview) releases are incomplete.

UVU global-engagement and review videography programs place student creators at high-visibility campus events. That positioning yields both opportunity (multiple angles) and risk (devices subject to the same deletion-pressure threads documented for other attendees).
