## Investigative Value

"Pierre Du Pont" is a name that appears in this investigation only through commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'s account. According to Owens, a TPUSA executive allegedly placed a phone call shortly after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot to a person she believes to be a "French-American named Pierre Du Pont," and she suggested a large business partnership was reportedly at stake. The investigation notes themselves flag uncertainty about whether this name — or an associated company name — is even accurate. See [People](/People/overview) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) for context on the surrounding commentary.
