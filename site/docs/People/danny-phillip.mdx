# Danny Phillip

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Danny Phillip** is a [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) team member listed among staff in **close proximity** to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at the Utah Valley University event on September 10, 2025. He is part of the broader on-site roster citizen investigators map against the **12:23–12:24** post-shot timeline dominated by [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), and [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet).

## Staff Periphery & the First Minute After the Shot

The highest-scrutiny interval begins at **~12:23 PM MDT** when Kirk was struck. Public accounts attribute these actions to other staff — not Phillip directly — but his **proximity** matters for reconstruction:

| Event | Principal figures | Phillip's roster position |
|-------|------------------|------------------------|
| **12:23** — Erika says Mikey called her | [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) | Close-proximity staff — potential corroborating witness |
| **12:24:02** — Mikey past SUV alone | Mikey; Blake **absent from frame** | May clarify where other staff were standing |
| Post-shot organizational response | [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), Blake Neff | Part of broader TPUSA team presence |

Phillip is not named in the Blake **witness gap** dispute. His value is as a potential **eyewitness** to stage-area movements in the seconds before and after the shot. **No accusation** holds that he knew of any assassination plan. See [Timeline overview](/Timeline/overview) and [TPUSA Staff — Sept 10](/key_individuals/TPUSA_Staff_Sept_10).

## Role at TPUSA

Danny Phillip is identified in investigative rosters as **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) team / close proximity** — meaning he was among the national staff physically near the stage and AV setup at the UVU courtyard event. He appears alongside [Andrew Sypher](/People/andrew-sypher), [Anthony West](/People/anthony-west), and other field and operations personnel in September 10 staff compilations.

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- On-site at the UVU outdoor event when [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot
- Listed in **close-proximity** staff rosters cited in citizen timeline work
- Part of the **broader TPUSA staff presence** that investigators compare against video stills and public statements
- Potential witness to **who moved where** in the first minute — independent of the Mikey/Erika call-timing debate

## Relationship to the Mikey–Blake–Kolvet Triangle

[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**, and **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** anchor post-shot **call and communications** scrutiny. Phillip belongs to the **peripheral ring** — staff close enough to see the stage response but not central to published call-witness claims. Documenting the full periphery helps resolve whether Blake could have witnessed Mikey's calls from a position consistent with **12:24:02** footage.
