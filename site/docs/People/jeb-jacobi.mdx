## Investigative Value

**Jeb Jacobi** is a close-range eyewitness because he reportedly stood **approximately 10 feet from [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** when the shot occurred at [UVU](/UVU/overview) on September 10, 2025. As a [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) volunteer who helped bring Kirk to campus, Jacobi bridges event logistics and firsthand perception — two categories investigators cross-check against [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) audio descriptions and [eyewitness mobile video](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video).

His public descriptions of the moment as "haunting" place him among the inner-circle volunteers whose testimony could address shot direction, immediate crowd reaction, and whether the sound matched a conventional rifle report. Compare his proximity with [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak) (final questioner) and [Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown) (post-shot chaos accounts).

According to X.com witness research (Query 6, peopleglyph):

- Jacobi **helped bring Kirk to UVU** for the courtyard event.
- He was standing **about 10 feet away** when Kirk was hit.
- He has **spoken publicly** about the experience, describing the memory as haunting.
- Investigation-file commentary links him and Cooper Brown in TPUSA volunteer contexts; separate posts have discussed Fort Huachuca associations — those threads are **unverified** and not stated as fact here.

## Background

Jacobi appears in the master witness roster alongside other TPUSA-adjacent figures such as [Caleb Chilcutt](/People/caleb-chilcutt) (UVU chapter president) and [Andrew Feraci](/People/andrew-feraci) (staff photographer). His volunteer status means he likely had backstage or near-stage access without being on the AV crew documented under [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth).

Any phone video, photos, or sworn statement from ~10 feet could materially affect trajectory and sound-source analysis debated in [Real Killer](/Killer/overview) threads.
