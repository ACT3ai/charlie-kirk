He is a longtime figure in the online conservative ecosystem around [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview) and [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), and he appears in the investigation only because of his **publicly documented U.S. Navy intelligence background** and a **widely-shared social-media exchange** that touches on the case's [intelligence-services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview) theme.

A screenshot resurfaced in July 2026 by **[@ArtifexMemor](/Influencers/x/artifexmemor)** — an account whose bio lists prior work with **[@tpusa](/Influencers/x/tpusa)** and **[@charliekirk11](/Influencers/x/charliekirk11)** — captures a February 4, 2022 exchange on X. In it, according to the screenshot, **Jack Posobiec** wrote: *"Military sent me down to [Ft Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) couple times for training. We used to walk over the border to get lunch in Nogales,"* and added, *"Then there's that time I took Tanya to Juarez. That was a great livestream and we almost got deported (her fault not mine)."*

**Fort Huachuca**, Arizona is the home of the **U.S. Army Intelligence Center of Excellence** and the Military Intelligence Corps — the Army's primary intelligence-training installation. In the circled reply, **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)**, chief operating officer of Turning Point Action/TPUSA, wrote: *"jack stop going to huachuca."*

The exchange reads as **banter** between two public figures, and nothing in it connects either man to the events of September 10, 2025. It is documented here only because [@ArtifexMemor](/Influencers/x/artifexmemor) and other commentators have **circulated it as a pointer to Posobiec's intelligence background** — a background that Posobiec himself has spoken about publicly.

## Documented Naval Intelligence Service

Posobiec's military-intelligence service is a matter of public record and has been reported by mainstream outlets and acknowledged by Posobiec himself:

- He **enlisted in the U.S. Navy Reserve** in 2010 and was commissioned as an officer in 2013, reaching the rank of **lieutenant junior grade** before leaving in 2017 (per [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/right-winger-jack-posobiec-retweeted-trump-navy-intel-officer-n793191) and [Task & Purpose](https://taskandpurpose.com/news/jack-posobiec-controversial-conservative-navy-intel-officer-opens-military-service/)).
- From roughly **2014 to 2017** he was assigned to a Reserve Intelligence unit at the **Office of Naval Intelligence's** Naval Maritime Intelligence Center in Washington, reportedly working at the Kennedy Irregular Warfare Center.
- His file reportedly lists a **10-month posting at Guantánamo Bay, Cuba**, beginning in September 2012, where he served as a **HUMINT (human-intelligence) analyst** in the interrogation cell.
- His **security clearance was suspended in February 2017** before he left his civilian post at the Office of Naval Intelligence, according to [The Daily Beast](https://www.thedailybeast.com/new-pentagon-defense-pool-reporter-jack-posobiec-once-had-naval-intelligence-security-clearance-suspended/).

These are **publicly reported facts about his career** — not allegations of wrongdoing. They are relevant to the investigation only insofar as the case examines [intelligence-service connections](/US_Intelligence/overview) among public figures in Charlie Kirk's broader orbit.
