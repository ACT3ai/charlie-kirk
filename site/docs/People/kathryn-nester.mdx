Kathryn N. Nester is the **lead capital defense attorney** for [Tyler Robinson](./tyler-robinson)
in *State of Utah v. Tyler Robinson*. She is a Salt Lake City-based attorney of
record with a long criminal-defense career.

## Connection to the Charlie Kirk Case

Nester was appointed lead defense counsel in the capital case against Tyler
Robinson, the man charged in the September 10, 2025 shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at
Utah Valley University. She works through **Nester Lewis PLLC**.

## Background

* Salt Lake City-based defense attorney with **30+ years in criminal defense**.
* Reported experience across **multiple capital / aggravated-murder cases** in
  several states.
* Firm: **Nester Lewis PLLC**.
