# Andrew Sypher

> **Disclaimer:** Inclusion on this page does not imply that this individual had knowledge of, participated in, or committed any illegal or immoral act. This page documents their publicly known connection to the events of September 10, 2025 for investigative reference only.

**Andrew Sypher** is Vice President of Field Operations / Chief Field Officer at [Turning Point USA (TPUSA)](/TPUSA/overview), a longtime employee since 2016. He oversaw national field teams and student chapters and was listed among AV/TPUSA staff in **close proximity** to the stage when [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot on September 10, 2025.

## Event Operations Context (12:23 PM)

While post-shot timelines focus on [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)'s **12:23** call to [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) and the **12:24:02** footage showing Mikey alone (with [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) absent from frame), Sypher represents the **field-operations layer** of TPUSA's on-site presence:

| Function | Sypher's role | Investigative relevance |
|----------|--------------|------------------------|
| **Field team coordination** | Chief Field Officer since 2016 | Who was positioned where on the courtyard perimeter |
| **AV / event crew** | Listed in close-proximity staff rosters | Stage setup, mic routing, crew movements post-shot |
| **Chapter network** | Oversees student chapters nationally | Links to [Caleb Chilcutt](/People/caleb-chilcutt) UVU chapter coordination |

Sypher is not named in the Mikey/Blake call-witness dispute but is part of the **broader staff periphery** citizen investigators map against the [Timeline overview](/Timeline/overview). **No accusation** holds that he knew of any assassination plan.

One X thread in public commentary speculates that Sypher and TPUSA AV lead [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) used similar language suggesting **"the blast came from inside Charlie's shirt,"** attributing it either to a shared "reaction briefing" or simply to independent perception. That is **unverified interpretive commentary**, not established fact, and is noted only because it recurs in the [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview) discussion.

## Role at TPUSA

Andrew Sypher has been with [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) since **2016**, rising to **Vice President of Field Operations** and **Chief Field Officer**. His portfolio includes:

- National **student chapter** network and campus activations
- **Field team** logistics for TPUSA events
- Coordination with chapter presidents such as [Caleb Chilcutt](/People/caleb-chilcutt) at UVU

## Connection to September 10, 2025

- On-site at the UVU **"American Comeback"** courtyard event
- Listed among **AV/TPUSA team** members in close proximity to Kirk on stage
- Involved in **event operations** — staging, field staff, and chapter coordination
- Part of the staff roster referenced alongside [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), and [Danny Phillip](/People/danny-phillip) in September 10 reconstructions
