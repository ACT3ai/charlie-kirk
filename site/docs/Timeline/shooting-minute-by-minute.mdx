# The Shooting: 12:23 PM MDT Minute-by-Minute Timeline

This page covers **66 seconds**. The master investigation file logs a reconstruction that starts at **12:23:30 PM MDT** — the instant of the shot — and ends at **12:24:36 PM MDT**. Everything inside that window is timed to the second against video frames, and almost every second inside it is contested. Times below are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT / Utah clock)**, with elapsed offsets shown as `mm:ss` from the 12:23:30 start mark.

For the wider window either side of these 66 seconds, see [the ten minutes before the shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot) and [the ten minutes after](./ten-minutes-after-shot).

## The Timeline

### Setup — 12:15 to 12:23:29 PM MDT

* **12:15 PM MDT** — Per the charging documents summarized on the [affidavit of probable cause page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause), the accused entered the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) from the southeast side and crossed a railing onto the roof — an account tracked in detail on the [pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus) and disputed by the [stairs-guy identity gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap).

* **12:17 PM MDT** — The same filings place the accused climbing a short wall and crouching on the north side of the roof, the moment the [roof-jumping claims](./roof-jumping-timeline) and the [camera-sightings record](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson) both hinge on.

* **12:22 PM MDT** — Filings describe a run west across the roof to a prone firing position roughly **430 feet** from the stage; commentators note the distinctive limp seen in earlier footage is [no longer visible in this stretch](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/roof-run-limp-disappears).

* **~12:23 PM MDT** — [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak) asks the last audience question of the event; the FBI questioned and cleared him, and his on-camera reaction is treated by officials as an ordinary startle response, though it is [discussed among crowd-figure threads](/distraction_people/Hunter_Kozak).

* **12:23:28 PM MDT (−00:02)** — Citizen analysts working from the released rooftop clip calculate that the running figure **began an all-out sprint two seconds before the shot**, an argument set out on [the runner-sprint-before-shot page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/runner-sprint-before-shot) and used by [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) researchers to say the timing does not close.

### The 66 seconds — 12:23:30 to 12:24:36 PM MDT

* **12:23:30 PM MDT — 00:00** — The shot. [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is struck in the neck mid-answer; official accounts and the master file agree on this second, while a competing **12:27 PM** figure circulates in some compilations and is unpacked on [the shot-time dispute page](./12-23-shooting-time) and [12:23 vs 12:27](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/shot-time-1223-vs-1227).

* **12:23:30 PM MDT — 00:00** — In a separate reconstruction preserved in the master file, **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)** — TPUSA chief of staff — is described as calling his wife **[Elizabeth McCoy](/People/elizabeth-mccoy)** at the same second the shot lands, the claim examined on [McCoy Reportedly Phones His Wife as the Shot Lands](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife).

* **12:23:34 PM MDT — 00:04** — The publicly released rooftop video **begins here**, four seconds after the shot; researchers argue the trim point conceals roughly 60 feet of the runner's path, a complaint filed under [withholding rooftop video](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/withholding-rooftop-video) and [selective 4K release](/Suspicious/FBI/selective-4k-release).

* **12:23:48 PM MDT — 00:18** — **Security begins to move Charlie.** This is the first entry in the master file's second-by-second log; the [security team](/Security_Team/overview) response and the absence of a staged medical plan are covered on [six officers, no ambulance](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/six-officers-no-ambulance).

* **12:23:55 PM MDT — 00:25** — A second reconstruction in the master file states Kirk **"is carried into the SUV"** at this second — six seconds earlier than the 00:31 load time in the primary log. Both figures are recorded here; neither has been authenticated against a released, timecoded master video.

* **12:23:57 PM MDT — 00:27** — **[Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is already in position on the rock pile, filming.** The speed with which he reached that vantage is the whole subject of [Farnsworth Already Filming From the Rock Pile](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile), and his later handling of camera media is separately covered under [SD cards removed from the scene](/CoverUp/SD_Cards_And_Cameras_Removed).

* **12:24:01 PM MDT — 00:31** — **Charlie is loaded into the SUV.** The vehicle itself, its blurred windows, and what happened to it afterwards are documented on [the SUV transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie) and [SUV blurred windows](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/suv-blurred-windows).

* **12:24:02 PM MDT — 00:32** — In the frame at this second, **Mikey McCoy is alone**; [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) is nowhere in view. This single frame is the anchor of the [McCoy alone in frame, Neff nowhere](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict) conflict described below.

* **12:24:03 PM MDT — 00:33** — **Mikey McCoy is well past the SUV, without Blake Neff.** The master file's author states plainly that he makes no accusation and is only trying to reconstruct the timeline; the same caution applies on this page.

* **12:24:12 PM MDT — 00:42** — **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) gets into the SUV.** Turek is one of the small group in the vehicle alongside [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) per accounts collected on [the hospital-trip page](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital).

* **12:24:13 to 12:24:35 PM MDT — 00:43–01:05** — **The SUV departs for the hospital.** The 22-second spread is the master file's own range, not a single logged departure second; the destination choice is analysed on [hospital choice and transport](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport) and [trauma center bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **12:24:22 PM MDT — 00:52** — **"They just shot Charlie"** — Terryl Farnsworth, on his own audio. This is the first spoken confirmation captured in the reconstruction window.

* **12:24:36 PM MDT — 01:06** — **"He's dead"** — Terryl Farnsworth. Sixty-six seconds after the shot, a bystander with a camera states the outcome that the hospital would not announce for another forty minutes or more. The reconstruction **ends here**.

### Immediately outside the window

* **12:24 PM MDT** — A second reconstruction places the **SUV beginning transport** at this minute, and researchers note that **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** flew a **335 ft AGL** low pass over Orem at 12:24 PM, addressed on [N1098L low-pass timing](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/n1098l-low-pass-timing) and [N1098L HADES over UVU](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU).

* **~12:24 PM MDT** — Filings describe a **dark item — the rifle wrapped in a dark towel — being placed on the rooftop** before the figure drops to the grass, the sequence handled on [rifle and towel on the roof](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/rifle-towel-on-roof) and [the gun-discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

* **12:26 PM MDT** — Per one reconstruction, **Mikey McCoy three-ways [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) into a call**; per police audio summarized in the master file, the **first radio report of a shooting** also lands around 12:26, covered on [the dispatch and scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **12:29–12:35 PM MDT (estimated)** — Kirk arrives at [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital); everything from here forward belongs to [the ten-minutes-after timeline](./ten-minutes-after-shot) and [the medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point in dispute | Version A | Version B | Who asserts each |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Second of the shot | **12:23:30 PM** | **12:27 PM** | The master file's second-by-second log and official summaries give 12:23:30; a 12:27 figure appears in some media and citizen compilations — see [12:23 vs 12:27](./12-23-shooting-time) |
| Second Kirk entered the SUV | **00:31 (12:24:01)** | **12:23:55** | Two separate reconstructions inside the master file; neither is tied to a released authenticated master video |
| Who witnessed McCoy's calls | Blake Neff reportedly says he **witnessed** the call to Erika Kirk and the follow-on call to [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) | At **12:24:02** McCoy appears **alone in frame** with Neff not visible | Erika Kirk's account, as reported, versus the frame itself — laid out on [McCoy alone in frame](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict) |
| When the runner started running | **12:23:28**, two seconds *before* the shot | Official framing has the figure running *after* firing | Citizen video analysts calculating from the 12:23:34 trim point; see [runner sprint before shot](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/runner-sprint-before-shot) |
| Why released video starts at 12:23:34 | Ordinary clip selection | Deliberate trim hiding ~60 ft of path | Prosecutors have not publicly explained the trim; the complaint is documented on [withholding rooftop video](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/withholding-rooftop-video) |
| Farnsworth's position at 00:27 | Coincidence of where he happened to be filming | Too fast to be reactive | Compared side by side on [Farnsworth rock pile](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile) |

## Open Questions

1. Is there a single, continuous, timecoded master video of the courtyard covering 12:23:00 to 12:25:00 — and if so, why has only a trimmed excerpt starting at 12:23:34 been released?
2. Which SUV-load second is correct, 12:23:55 or 12:24:01, and what frame does each derive from?
3. What do carrier records show for the [McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) call sequence — outbound at 12:23:30, and who was actually on each leg?
4. Where was Blake Neff standing between 12:23:30 and 12:24:36, and does any frame place him beside McCoy?
5. Does the courtyard audio captured by the [AV team](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup) contain a second acoustic event, as argued on [the two-event split page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Acoustic_Two_Event_Split)?
6. What is the exact second the SUV crossed the campus boundary, and does any traffic or [campus camera](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) confirm it inside the 00:43–01:05 range?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The untrimmed rooftop and courtyard master video with authenticated timecodes, the full [AV team](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup) audio recordings for 12:23–12:25, the carrier call-detail records for the calls placed from the courtyard in the first ninety seconds, the SUV's own telematics and departure log, and the chain-of-custody record for every camera and SD card taken from the scene are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
