# Timeline by Year

This is the routing page. Each year of this case has a different character — 2024 is search
data and nothing else, 2025 is a pressure campaign that ends in a killing, 2026 is a
courtroom — so the year you enter determines what kind of evidence you are about to read.
Below is the anchor chronology for each year with links into the detailed timelines. Times
are Mountain Time. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.

## 2024 — Searches Without Events

* **Aug 2024** — Research notes describe searches for "Utah Valley University buildings" and
  similar terms, interpreted by some analysts as early reconnaissance interest and by
  skeptics as ordinary campus traffic
  ([UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches)).

* **June–Nov 2024** — Trends compilations report sustained interest in "Tyler Robinson
  Discord / Reddit / FBI / LDS / TPUSA / Israel" search strings, months before any public
  connection existed ([Tyler Robinson and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches)).

* **Dec 2024** — Google searches begin appearing for key people and locations later tied to
  the case, per citizen research notes
  ([pre-event searches and recon](/Before/PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon)).

* **Dec 8–14, 2024** — A one-week spike, peaking at 100 interest, for attorney
  [Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester) — later Robinson's defense counsel — reportedly
  originating entirely from Israel. This is the single most-cited 2024 anomaly
  ([Kathryn Nester searches](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches),
  [Nester and Google Trends](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/nester-google-trends)).

* **Dec 2024 – Sept 2025 (continuous)** — Searches for Lance Twiggs' townhouse address in
  St. George reportedly run continuously across the whole nine-month span
  ([Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns)).

* **2024 (counterpoint)** — Trends data cannot identify who searched. A single VPN exit node,
  a news aggregator, or a research firm can produce an identical geographic signature
  ([Trends deletion and suppression](/GoogleSearches/trends-deletion-suppression)).

## 2025 — Pressure, Then the Shot

* **June 18, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) goes to the White House to advise
  **against** regime change in Iran ([Kirk against the Iran war](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war)).

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister
  [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) visits the United States
  ([Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm)).

* **July 11–14, 2025** — At the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Kirk
  platforms anti-Israel-war voices and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) tells the
  audience to "go all the way" ([Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction)).

* **July 18, 2025 (claim)** — Some commentators assert a decision to target Kirk in Utah was
  made around this date; **no public official record supports it**
  ([the July 18 decision claim](./july-18-decision)).

* **July 20, 2025** — The Egyptian/Israeli Air Force jet [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) reportedly
  makes its first U.S. trip, to an Army base in Nebraska, on the same day as an alleged
  military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil
  ([foreign leaders meeting](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20),
  [Nebraska Army base](./nebraska-army-base-july-20)).

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting on "confronting the woke-right
  defamation of Israel," organized by the Faith &amp; Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition
  for Israel and the Philos Project; Kirk and TPUSA were **not** present
  ([July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA)).

* **~Aug 4, 2025** — The "Hamptons intervention," where pro-Israel figures reportedly
  confronted Kirk ([Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting));
  [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon) later publicly denied any "angry confrontation."

* **Aug 6, 2025** — Kirk speaks on Megyn Kelly's program about donor pressure
  ([donor pressure as motive](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy)).

* **Aug 8, 2025** — Israel-origin search interest reportedly focuses on the ISR jet
  [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview), 32 days before the shooting
  ([plane search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns)).

* **Sept 1–5, 2025** — In group chats Kirk reportedly says he has "no choice but to leave the
  pro-Israel cause" ([reported quotes and warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings)).

* **Sept 4, 2025** — SU-BTT flies France → Provo and remains parked until the morning of the
  assassination ([SU-BTT movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline)).

* **Sept 8, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement; the same day,
  roughly twelve lieutenant colonels are said to have gathered at **Fort Huachuca**, with
  [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) departing at 12:49 AM on Sept 9
  ([Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline)).

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Kirk criticizes Israel to [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s face on
  air, attends a high-pressure meeting, and reportedly texts
  ["they are going to kill me"](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text)
  ([the Sept 9 meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 7:08 AM** — SU-BTT departs Utah for Wilmington, hours before the shot.

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 8:07–11:49 AM** — A figure the State identifies as Robinson is captured
  on Ring, WYZE and UVU cameras near campus, with a reported clothing change
  ([camera sightings](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — ~11:47 AM** — N1098L is reported at low altitude near Utah Lake; the
  exact minute varies by source
  ([N1098L activity](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 12:23 PM** — Kirk is shot at the UVU courtyard event; the first police
  report is logged at 12:26 PM, and some compilations use 12:27 PM for the shot itself
  ([shooting time disputes](./12-23-shooting-time),
  [minute by minute](./shooting-minute-by-minute)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — ~12:24–12:48 PM** — N1098L reportedly executes a second low pass; sources
  cite either 12:24 or 12:48 PM.

* **Sept 10, 2025 — evening** — [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) departs Provo and its ADS-B goes
  dark mid-flight before it returns ([other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline)).

* **Sept 11, 2025** — The surrender, the disputed **6:25 PM** Miranda timestamp, the 7:57 PM
  Discord messages and the 10:00 PM booking-sheet arrest time
  ([custody and surrender timeline](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline)).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — evening** — SU-BTT departs the United States for Egypt.

* **Sept 12–16, 2025** — Booking under **#460956**, the "33-hour manhunt" characterization,
  and the Sept 16 charging announcement with a death-penalty notice
  ([press conferences and statements](./press-conferences-statements-timeline)).

* **Sept 14, 2025** — The UVU courtyard is torn up and paved, destroying the scene
  ([paving timeline](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14),
  [scene changes](./scene-changes-paving-timeline)).

* **Oct 15, 2025** — A later-2025 anchor tracked separately
  ([October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025)).

* **Dec 16, 2025** — Judge Graf issues a broad gag order on the court's own motion, closing
  the year and setting the terms of 2026
  ([gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing)).

## 2026 — The Court Year

* **Jan 21, 2026** — A citizen-assembled discovery list is delivered to counsel
  ([discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes)).

* **Mar 10, 2026** — The defense moves to seal the four-page **ATF report** finding the
  autopsy bullet-jacket fragment comparison **inconclusive**; a media coalition opposes the
  seal ([ATF ballistics](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla)).

* **Mar 27, 2026** — Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) resigns without public
  explanation ([Brooksby resignation](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation)).

* **Apr–May 2026** — The discovery stall: roughly 600,000 files produced while the contested
  ballistics, DNA and chain-of-custody items remain hard to isolate
  ([FBI discovery delays](/FBI/Discovery_Delays)).

* **Jun 2026** — A deputy prosecutor is found in civil contempt over public statements about
  the strength of the evidence ([prosecution team](/court/prosecution-team)).

* **July 2026** — A four-day preliminary hearing presents surveillance video, DNA and officer
  testimony, with the bind-over decision deferred
  ([Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview),
  [2026 timeline](./2026)).
