# Timeline by Year

This is the routing page. Each year of this case has a different character — 2024 is search
data and nothing else, 2025 is a pressure campaign that ends in a killing, 2026 is a
courtroom — so the year you enter determines what kind of evidence you are about to read.
Below is the anchor chronology for each year with links into the detailed timelines. Times
are Mountain Time. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.

## Where to Go

| --- | --- | --- |

## 2024 — Searches Without Events

* **Aug 2024** — Research notes describe searches for "Utah Valley University buildings" and
  similar terms, interpreted by some analysts as early reconnaissance interest and by
  skeptics as ordinary campus traffic
  ([UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches)).

* **June–Nov 2024** — Trends compilations report sustained interest in "Tyler Robinson
  [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) / Reddit / [FBI](/FBI/overview) / LDS /
  [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) / [Israel](/Israel/overview)" search strings, months before any public
  connection existed ([Tyler Robinson and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches)).
  Sceptic analyses counter that "Tyler James Robinson" shows **near-zero** 2024 Trends interest —
  an unreconciled data conflict laid out on [2024 Pre-Event Timeline](./Year_2024).

* **Sept 17–18, 2024** — **Operation Grim Beeper**: thousands of pagers detonate across Lebanon and
  Syria. Documented mainstream history, entirely separate from this case, cited only as a
  **capability** argument ([Operation Grim Beeper](/Israel/Operation_Grim_Beeper),
  [capability context](/Suspicious/Israel/grim-beeper-capability)). Capability is not use.

* **Dec 2024** — Google searches begin appearing for key people and locations later tied to
  the case, per citizen research notes
  ([pre-event searches and recon](/Before/PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon)).

* **Dec 8–14, 2024** — A one-week spike, peaking at 100 interest, for attorney
  [Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester) — later a member of Robinson's
  [defense team](/court/defense-team) — reportedly originating entirely from Israel. This is the
  single most-cited 2024 anomaly, and no mainstream outlet has reproduced its absolute volumes
  ([Kathryn Nester searches](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches),
  [Nester and Google Trends](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/nester-google-trends)).

* **Dec 2024 – Sept 2025 (continuous)** — Searches for [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)'
  townhouse address in St. George reportedly run continuously across the whole nine-month span
  ([Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns)). Twiggs is a living
  private individual accused of nothing; his [phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) becomes a central
  exhibit at the 2026 hearing.

* **2024 (counterpoint)** — Trends data cannot identify who searched. A single VPN exit node,
  a news aggregator, or a research firm can produce an identical geographic signature
  ([Trends deletion and suppression](/GoogleSearches/trends-deletion-suppression)).

## 2025 — Pressure, Then the Shot

* **June 18, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) goes to the White House to advise
  **against** regime change in Iran ([Kirk against the Iran war](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war)).

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister
  [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) visits the United States
  ([Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm)).

* **July 11–14, 2025** — At the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Kirk
  platforms anti-Israel-war voices, the Dave Smith / [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) debate airs
  the split, and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) tells the audience to "go all the way"
  ([Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction),
  [Carlson on evolving Israel views](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel)).

* **July 18, 2025 (claim)** — Some commentators assert a decision to target Kirk in Utah was
  made around this date; **no public official record supports it**
  ([the July 18 decision claim](./july-18-decision)).

* **July 20, 2025** — The Egyptian/Israeli Air Force jet [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) reportedly
  makes its first U.S. trip, to an Army base in Nebraska, on the same day as an alleged
  military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil
  ([foreign leaders meeting](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20),
  [Nebraska Army base](./nebraska-army-base-july-20)).

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting on "confronting the woke-right
  defamation of Israel," organized by the [Faith &amp; Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition),
  the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel) and the
  [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding); Kirk and TPUSA were **not** present
  ([July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA),
  [why TPUSA was absent](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31)).

* **~Aug 4, 2025** — The "Hamptons intervention," where pro-Israel figures reportedly
  confronted Kirk ([Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting),
  [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons),
  [the Hamptons intervention and the death question](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention));
  [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon) later publicly denied any "angry confrontation." In the same
  window, commentators allege a [funding call from Netanyahu](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call) that
  Kirk declined — figures in circulation run from unspecified to ~$120–150 million and the
  sourcing is disputed ([the billion](/Israel/billion)).

* **Aug 6, 2025** — Kirk speaks on [Megyn Kelly](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly)'s program about
  donor pressure ([donor pressure as motive](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy)).

* **Aug 17–24, 2025** — [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) runs Paris → Omaha/Lincoln → Wilmington → Paris.
  Some compilations call *this*, not July 20, the jet's first U.S. arrival — see the conflicts
  table below.

* **Aug 8, 2025** — Israel-origin search interest reportedly focuses on the ISR jet
  [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview), 32 days before the shooting
  ([plane search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns)).

* **Sept 1–5, 2025** — In group chats Kirk reportedly says he has "no choice but to leave the
  pro-Israel cause" ([reported quotes and warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings)).

* **Sept 4, 2025** — SU-BTT flies France → Provo and remains parked until the morning of the
  assassination ([SU-BTT movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline)).

* **Mid–late summer 2025 (claimed search ladder)** — Beyond the single Sept 8 Fort Huachuca bullet,
  citizen investigators (**[@StewPetersNet](/Influencers/x/stewpetersnet)** / Ana Escobar materials) publish a **July–September 2025**
  sequence of geo-associated image searches for security, TPUSA-communications, and
  intelligence-community names. Treat as **unverified search-trend claims**, not a proven meeting
  roster; local residents near the base have publicly dismissed grand-plot narratives as disconnected
  from on-the-ground talk ([Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline)).

* **Sept 8, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement; the same day,
  roughly twelve lieutenant colonels are said to have gathered at
  **[Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9)**, with
  [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) — flown against tail [99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) — departing at
  00:49 on Sept 9 per one note and ~11:43 AM / 12:31 PM per the ADS-B figures
  ([Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline),
  [the JTF meeting](/Suspicious/US_Intel/fort-huachuca-jtf-meeting),
  [records reportedly withheld](/CoverUp/Huachuca_Records_Withheld)).

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Kirk criticizes Israel to [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s face on
  air, attends a **high-pressure meeting** (distinct in the record from the ~Aug 4 Hamptons
  intervention — discourse sometimes merges the two), and reportedly texts
  ["they are going to kill me"](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text)
  ([the Sept 9 meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 7:08 AM** — SU-BTT departs Utah for Wilmington, hours before the shot.

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 8:07–11:49 AM** — A figure the State identifies as Robinson is captured
  on Ring, WYZE and UVU cameras near campus, with a reported clothing change
  ([camera sightings](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — ~11:47 AM** — N1098L is reported at low altitude near Utah Lake; the
  exact minute varies by source
  ([N1098L activity](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 12:23 PM** — Kirk is shot at the UVU courtyard event; the first police
  report is logged at 12:26 PM, and some compilations use 12:27 PM for the shot itself
  ([shooting time disputes](./12-23-shooting-time),
  [minute by minute](./shooting-minute-by-minute)).

* **Sept 10, 2025 — ~12:24–12:48 PM** — N1098L reportedly executes a second low pass; sources
  cite either 12:24 or 12:48 PM.

* **Sept 10, 2025 — evening** — [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) departs Provo and its ADS-B goes
  dark mid-flight before it returns ([other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline)).

* **Sept 11, 2025** — The surrender, the disputed **6:25 PM** Miranda timestamp, the 7:57 PM
  [Discord messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) and the 10:00 PM booking-sheet arrest time
  ([custody and surrender timeline](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline),
  [6:25 PM custody and the Discord confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession)).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — evening** — SU-BTT departs the United States for Egypt.

* **Sept 12–16, 2025** — Booking under **#460956**, the "33-hour manhunt" characterization —
  other compilations count **30 hours** — and the Sept 16 charging announcement with a
  death-penalty notice ([press conferences and statements](./press-conferences-statements-timeline),
  [Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline)).

* **Sept 18, 2025** — A **FARA filing** for Brad Parscale's
  [Clock Tower X LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clock-tower-x-llc) covers Israeli-government messaging routed
  through [Salem Media](/TPUSA/Companies/salem-media), which already carried the Kirk show —
  eight days after the assassination. A disclosure filing is not evidence of a crime
  ([FARA and the show's distribution](/CoverUp/FARA_Salem_Capture)).

* **Sept 14, 2025** — The UVU courtyard is torn up and paved, destroying the scene
  ([paving timeline](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14),
  [scene changes](./scene-changes-paving-timeline)).

* **Oct 9–10, 2025** — The **AES** explosives plant at McEwen, Tennessee explodes, killing 16 —
  the firm holding the miniaturized-charge defense contract at the centre of the exploding-mic
  thread ([AES Tennessee explosion](/CoverUp/AES_Tennessee_Explosion),
  [DoD contract](/Mic/DoD_Contract)). Timing coincidence is not a link.

* **Oct 15, 2025** — A later-2025 anchor tracked separately
  ([October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025)).

* **Oct 27 – Dec 9, 2025** — A continuous run of decorum orders, sealing motions, transcript
  classifications and a news-media intervention moves through the Fourth District Court
  ([case overview](/court/case-overview), [discovery timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline)).

* **Dec 16, 2025** — Judge Graf issues a broad gag order on the court's own motion, closing
  the year and setting the terms of 2026
  ([gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing)).

* **Dec 2025 — Year in Search** — Google's **Year in Search 2025** lists Charlie Kirk as the
  **#1 trending** U.S. query after the assassination (widely reported, e.g. **@FoxNews**).

## 2026 — The Court Year

* **Jan 9, 2026** — The defense objects to the **conditions of bullet-fragment testing**, including
  the FBI's planned Virtual Comparison Microscopy and the denial of any right to observe or record
  it ([ATF ballistics](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla),
  [the banned CBLA bullet-lead test](/Suspicious/FBI/cbla-bullet-lead-test)).

* **Jan 21, 2026** — A citizen-assembled discovery list is delivered to counsel
  ([discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes)).

* **Mar 10, 2026** — The defense moves to seal the four-page **ATF report** finding the
  autopsy bullet-jacket fragment comparison **inconclusive**; a media coalition opposes the
  seal ([ATF ballistics](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla),
  [what is still under seal](/Legal/Evidence-Sealing-2026)).

* **Mar 27, 2026** — Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) resigns without public
  explanation ([Brooksby resignation](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation)).

* **Apr 15, 2026** — A **GRAMA release** of roughly 50 documents / 112 pages via the Daily Caller
  News Foundation puts the September 9 UVU warnings on the record
  ([UVU GRAMA records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records),
  [pre-shooting warnings](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings)).

* **Apr–May 2026** — The discovery stall: roughly 600,000 files produced while the contested
  ballistics, DNA and chain-of-custody items remain hard to isolate
  ([FBI discovery delays](/FBI/Discovery_Delays)).

* **Jun 2026** — Deputy Utah County Attorney [Christopher Ballard](/People/christopher-d-ballard)
  is found in **civil contempt** over public statements about the strength of the evidence; the
  judge orders fee-shifting and declines to strike the death penalty as a sanction
  ([prosecution team](/court/prosecution-team)). Civil contempt of a prosecutor is a court event,
  not a criminal finding about the murder.

* **July 6–10, 2026** — A **five-day** preliminary hearing — not four, as early summaries said —
  presents surveillance video, DNA and officer testimony, ending around noon MDT on Friday
  July 10. The judge **deferred** an immediate bind-over and set a briefing ladder
  (**State July 28, defense reply Aug 11, State rebuttal Aug 18**) with oral argument on
  **September 1, 2026** ([Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview),
  [2026 timeline](./2026)).

* **As of mid-July 2026** — Court-watchers note [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) had
  **not yet entered a plea**; arraignment would follow only if the case is bound over. Older
  "pleaded not guilty" copy is superseded ([case overview](/court/case-overview)).

* **Jul 9–16, 2026** — Parallel evidentiary threads: [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite) extraction of
  [Lance Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) confirmed in open court, and an anonymous
  Axon-user email challenging the "body camera died on the roof" account
  ([Officer Bagley](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam),
  [missing bodycam and the GRAMA admission](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage)).

* **July 2026 — records and citizen field work** — Parallel to the courtroom: **Judicial Watch**
  university clean-up document releases and ongoing GRAMA fights
  ([Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline)); citizen on-site interviews reopening
  Sept 10 evening clocks (e.g. **Panguitch** Cowboy's Smokehouse staff/owner accounts —
  [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape)).
