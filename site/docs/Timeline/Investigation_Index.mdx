# Timeline Investigation Index

This is the **routing map** for the Timeline section. Every entry below is a real dated event with a one-line description, hyperlinked to the page that owns it in depth. Read it top to bottom for the whole arc, or jump to the window you came for. The master anchor is **12:23:30 PM MDT, September 10, 2025** at [UVU](/UVU/overview). [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**. Where sources conflict, this page points at both.

## The Timeline

### 2024 and earlier — background signals

* **2019–2020 (reported)** — The "Groyper wars" establish the intra-right factional fight that later reappears in motive analysis; background is on the [Charlie Kirk personal timeline](./Charlie_Kirk) and [pre-event political pressure](./pre-event-political-pressure).

* **December 2024 (claimed)** — Researchers report **search-trend spikes for the UVU location** more than eight months before the event, the earliest entry on [Year 2024](./Year_2024) and [pre-event searches and recon](/Before/PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon).

### Spring and early summer 2025

* **April 2025 (claimed)** — Searches for a Kirk-succession domain appear from Arizona; the succession-timing argument runs through the [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **May 27 – June 2, 2025 (claimed)** — [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) and other Egyptian-registered aircraft move into and out of Provo, beginning the pattern tracked on the [SU-BTT movement timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline), [other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline) and the [Egyptian plane armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada).

* **June 13, 2025 (reported)** — The [Israel–Iran twelve-day war](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war) begins, the geopolitical backdrop for [Israel events and pressure](./israel-events-pressure-timeline).

* **June 18, 2025 (reported)** — Kirk goes to the White House specifically to argue [against regime change in Iran](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war) — the position donor-pressure researchers treat as the origin of the rift.

### July 2025 — the pivot month

* **July 11, 2025 (reported)** — Kirk urges [Tucker Carlson](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) to go all the way against Israel in a speech, the moment that opens the donor war; see [Year 2025](./Year_2025).

* **July 13–14, 2025 (reported)** — [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Florida hosts the [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)–Smith debate and the Carlson speech, escalating the public fight.

* **July 18, 2025 (claimed)** — The assertion that a decision to target Kirk in Utah was made around this date; sourcing is examined at [the July 18 decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025 (claimed)** — [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) makes its first-ever U.S. arrival at a **Nebraska Army base** — [Nebraska Army base, July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **July 20, 2025 (claimed)** — A **military meeting with foreign leaders** on U.S. soil, split across [military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20) and [foreign leaders meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

* **July 22, 2025 (reported)** — Senate confirmation of the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security whose office later booked the September VIP routing — [Fort Huachuca and military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and the [Libby Army Airfield field page](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca).

* **July 31, 2025 (reported)** — An off-the-record White House meeting on countering "woke-right" criticism of Israel takes place **without** Kirk or TPUSA present — [political pressure and meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).

* **July–early September 2025 *(claimed)*** — Citizen datasets ([Stew Peters Network](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters) and others) allege [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)–area [image-search prep](/GoogleSearches/fort-huachuca-image-searches) for named military and Kirk-orbit figures culminating Sep 6–9 — [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline); the wipe allegation is at [image results reportedly wiped](/Suspicious/US_Intel/image-results-wiped). Unverified.

### August 2025 — the intervention window

* **August 2–25, 2025 (reported)** — French legionnaires train in California; the later assassination allegation is unsupported and is held at [French Foreign Legion claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims).

* **August 4–6, 2025 (claimed)** — The [Hamptons intervention](/Israel/israel-hamptons) — [Hamptons intervention meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [the Hamptons death question](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention). Some sources date the same confrontation to September 9 instead.

* **August 6, 2025 (reported)** — Kirk speaks publicly on [Megyn Kelly's show](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly) about donor pressure — [Year 2025](./Year_2025).

* **August 2025 (reported)** — At a donor meeting, someone asks what would happen to [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) if Kirk died; the exchange resurfaces later in [the Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

### September 1–9, 2025 — the final week

* **September 1–5, 2025 (reported)** — Group-chat messages in which Kirk writes he has "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."

* **September 3, 2025 (documented)** — Orem PD emails UVU the event ticket link; the department later says it was "not involved" — [UVU security and surveillance timeline](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline).

* **September 4, 2025 (claimed)** — SU-BTT flies France to Provo; the counter-UAS testing window at Provo airport is argued at [Provo airport counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS).

* **September 8, 2025 (reported)** — Kirk posts about leaving the pro-Israel movement; the **authenticated** WhatsApp donor texts date from this day (some later commentary moves the "leave the cause" private message to **Sep 9** after [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) clarifications) — [Charlie Kirk personal timeline](./Charlie_Kirk) and [Year 2025](./2025).

* **September 8–9, 2025 *(reported texts)*** — Hammer/Wolicki chat partners → emergency Zoom → reported [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) "kill me" recipient sequence — [kill-me text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) and [high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9).

* **September 8, 2025 (claimed)** — Roughly twelve lieutenant colonels are reported at Fort Huachuca — [Fort Huachuca and military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and the [Fort Huachuca JTF meeting](/Suspicious/US_Intel/fort-huachuca-jtf-meeting).

* **September 8–9, 2025 *(contested)*** — [Mitch Snow](/People/mitch-snow)'s contested identification of specific living people at [Fort Huachuca on September 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9), the day before the shooting — [Fort Huachuca](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline); the identifications are disputed and are the subject of civil defamation litigation involving [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens). Records on the meeting are [reportedly withheld](/CoverUp/Huachuca_Records_Withheld).

* **September 9, 2025 (reported)** — Kirk appears on [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s show and criticizes Israeli policy to his face — [high-pressure meeting, September 9](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9).

* **September 9, 2025 (claimed)** — The **combative private meeting** and the alleged **"they are going to kill me"** text — [the kill-me text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text). An associate has publicly denied the text exists.

* **September 9, 2025, 2:23 PM MDT (documented)** — A redacted email asks UVU police to relay a message to Kirk — [UVU pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **September 9, 2025, 10:35 and 11:19 PM MDT (documented)** — Two men come to a UVU office to talk to police about "a plan"; 44 minutes later they are recharacterized as GOP reps who overstated their knowledge — the underlying [GRAMA records](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings) were released only in redacted form ([UVU records redacted](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records)).

### September 10, 2025 — the day

* **8:07–11:49 AM MDT (reported)** — [Ring doorbell](/cameras/Ring) and campus camera sightings attributed to Robinson, with a clothing change in between — [camera sightings](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson), [Tyler on surveillance cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras) and [pre-shot campus movements](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus). He is charged, not convicted, and the identifications are contested.

* **~11:47 AM MDT (claimed)** — The first reported [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) low pass near UVU — [N1098L activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline).

* **12:13–12:23 PM MDT (claimed)** — The final approach window — [ten minutes before the shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot).

* **12:15–12:22 PM MDT (prelim testimony / court video, reported)** — Roof walk on the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) ~12:15; approach/prone ~12:21–12:22 before the shot — [12:23 shooting time](./12-23-shooting-time) and [roof jumping](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **12:23:30 PM MDT (reported)** — **The shot.** Full day-of clock at [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025); the second-by-second reconstruction at [shooting minute by minute](./shooting-minute-by-minute); the time dispute at [12:23 vs 12:27](./12-23-shooting-time). Early Sept 12 graphics also floated a **12:20** shot stamp.

* **12:23:30 PM MDT +milliseconds (claimed)** — The acoustic arrival-order argument, built on the [Canon XA55 broadcast audio](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) and the [RØDE Wireless PRO](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic) on Kirk's shirt — [audio, acoustic and mic timeline](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline).

* **12:23:48–12:24:35 PM MDT (reported)** — Security moves Kirk, loads the SUV, and departs — [ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot).

* **12:23–12:35 PM MDT *(claimed)*** — Notification-order dispute (who received real-time updates from the transport chain, and when — including alleged Shapiro-before-Erika race) — [12:23 shooting time](./12-23-shooting-time) and [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

* **12:26 PM MDT (reported)** — First police report logged — [dispatch and radio scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **~12:42 PM MDT (reported)** — UVU student alerts reportedly announced a suspect **"in custody,"** later reversed — [UVU security and surveillance](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline) / [distraction events](./distraction-events-timeline).

* **12:29–12:35 PM MDT (estimated)** — Arrival at [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital); pronouncement follows — [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline) and [autopsy and forensic timeline](./autopsy-forensic-timeline). Researchers dispute why the [regional trauma center was bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **Afternoon (reported)** — Detentions and releases at and near the scene (including same-day POI detain-and-release), plus the roof-egress claims — [distraction events](./distraction-events-timeline) and [roof jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **Afternoon *(claimed)*** — Dual [N1098L](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline) low-pass readings (~12:24 vs ~12:48).

* **Afternoon into evening (claimed)** — Post-shot movement and vehicle questions — [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape) and [rental cars and vehicle movements](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).

* **~8:55–9:47 PM MDT *(contested)*** — Cowboy's Smokehouse, Panguitch, Utah dinner claim re the charged man — [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape) / [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13). Owner clarifications and citizen "100% ID" claims conflict.

* **Evening (claimed)** — [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) departures, the [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) ADS-B gap, and the wider aircraft picture — [other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline) and the [September 10 flight timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).
