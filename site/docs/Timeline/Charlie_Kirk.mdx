# Charlie Kirk Timeline

This is the chronology of **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** himself — not of the investigation into his death. It runs from his 1993 birth through the founding of [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview), the Groyper confrontations of 2019–2020, the donor rupture of summer 2025, and then slows to hours for the final 72 hours of September 8–10, 2025. Read it when you want to know what *he* was doing and saying, in order; the day-of forensic sequence lives on [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025) and the [minute-by-minute](./shooting-minute-by-minute) reconstruction.

## The Timeline

### Early life and the founding years

* **October 14, 1993** — Charles James Kirk is born in Illinois, per widely published biography, and grows up in the northwest Chicago suburbs. Basic biographical facts about a deceased person can be stated plainly, unlike the contested claims elsewhere on this site.

* **2012** — At eighteen, straight out of high school, Kirk co-founds **Turning Point USA** with the older conservative organizer Bill Montgomery. The founding story sits on [TPUSA founder Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Founder-Charlie) and the corporate entity map on [Turning Point USA (nonprofit)](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-usa).

* **2012–2016** — TPUSA scales from a startup into a national campus network on donor money, and Kirk becomes the movement's most recognizable young face. That base is what later makes both his platform and his donor dependence central to [motive analysis](/Motive/overview).

* **2016** — Kirk aligns TPUSA's energy behind the [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) campaign, beginning the direct White House access that continues through 2025 and that gives weight to his June 2025 intervention on Iran.

### The Groyper wars, 2019–2020

* **September 30, 2019** — TPUSA severs ties with a brand ambassador after she appears alongside Nick Fuentes and others. The fallout helps trigger Fuentes's campaign to send "groypers" to TPUSA's fall campus tour — the internal-right fracture summarized at [TPUSA opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie).

* **October 29, 2019 — Ohio State University** — Groypers dominate the Q&A, reportedly asking **11 of 14 questions**, with hostile prompts aimed at Kirk and his co-host. This is the first notable incident of the campaign.

* **November 8, 2019 — Arizona State University** — At a TPUSA "Prove Me Wrong" event featuring a sitting member of Congress, protesters aligned with the groyper campaign confront the forum.

* **November 10, 2019 — UCLA** — Donald Trump Jr., Kirk and Kimberly Guilfoyle appear for a book promotion. The Q&A is cancelled in anticipation of groyper questions, heckling follows, and the event ends early after roughly 20–30 minutes. It is the most-cited moment of the whole campaign.

* **November 25, 2019 — University of Houston** — A "Prove Me Wrong" stop devolves as a hostile crowd chants "America First."

* **December 2019 — West Palm Beach** — Fuentes hosts a Groyper Leadership Summit opposite TPUSA's Student Action Summit; groyper figures are removed after attempting to enter the TPUSA venue.

* **Late 2019 – early 2020** — The in-person disruptions wind down as TPUSA bans known groypers and tightens event security. There is no formal end date. The episode belongs in this timeline for one reason: it established, five years early, that **the intra-right fight over Israel was the fault line running through Kirk's own audience** — context for [the MAGA antisemitism debate](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate) and [woke-right defamation](/Before/Woke-Right-Defamation).

### 2023–2024

* **2023** — A life insurance policy is reportedly taken out, held through **[GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc)**, with a TPUSA loan of roughly $350,000 attached and payout estimates circulating in commentary. Researchers flag the timing relative to Kirk's late-2023 questions about Israel; the policy's existence and its interpretation are two separate things. See [GGLF life insurance](/Suspicious/Israel/gglf-life-insurance).

* **August 2024 (claim)** — Researchers report Google Trends activity for **"Utah Valley University buildings"** and related campus terms. These are aggregate trend graphics, not identified searchers. Handled in full on the [2024 pre-event timeline](./Year_2024) and at [UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches).

* **December 8–14, 2024 (claim)** — A reported one-week Google Trends spike for **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** — later [defense counsel](/court/defense-team) for the man charged — attributed by commentators to Israeli IP addresses. Treated as an unresolved data curiosity at [Nester Google Trends](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/nester-google-trends) and [the December 2024 spike](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches).

### 2025 — the year the alignment breaks

* **April 3, 2025** — Kirk posts a long warning against a new Middle East war, arguing that war with Iran would be "a catastrophic mistake" and that America has "a golden opportunity to pull away from Middle East quagmires for good." This is the earliest 2025 marker researchers use for his foreign-policy divergence; the full record of what he said is at [Kirk's Iran warnings](/Iran/kirk-warnings-quotes).

* **June 13, 2025** — The [Iran–Israel twelve-day war](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war) commences, sharpening every position Kirk had taken in April.

* **June 18, 2025** — Kirk goes to the White House specifically to **advise against regime change in Iran** — documented lobbying against the policy his largest donors favoured. See [Charlie's stand against the Iran war](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war).

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits the United States. Researchers place the visit immediately before the July TPUSA events, though no meeting with Kirk is established; see [Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm).

* **July 11–13, 2025 — TPUSA Student Action Summit, Tampa** — Kirk appears with [Megyn Kelly](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly). Per the master file he was rattled by having allowed Israel-critical speakers on his stage, and pro-Israel figures were rattled that he had.

* **July 13, 2025** — [Dave Smith](/Influencers/podcasts-dave-smith) debates [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) at the Florida event — the Israel argument staged inside Kirk's own conference.

* **July 14, 2025** — **[Tucker Carlson](/Influencers/podcasts-tucker-carlson)** speaks, telling the TPUSA audience to "go all the way." Platforming Carlson is the act researchers most often name as the one donors would not forgive; Carlson's own shifting position is traced at [Tucker Carlson on evolving Israel views](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel).

* **July 16, 2025** — The *Ami's House* podcast episode "Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout" airs, putting the donor friction on the public record for the first time.

* **July 18, 2025 (claim)** — Commentators assert a decision to target Kirk in Utah was made around this date. **No public official document or court finding confirms any such decision**, and this site does not endorse it; the claim and its evidentiary basis are examined on [July 18 decision claims](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — Aircraft **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** makes its first-ever U.S. trip, to an army base in Nebraska, on the same day researchers place a military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil. See [military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20) and [Nebraska army base July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting on "confronting woke-right defamation of Israel" is reported, organized by the [Faith and Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel) and the [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding). Reported attendees include [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun) and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu). **TPUSA is not present** — the absence itself is the finding, covered at [July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA).

* **~August 4, 2025 — the Hamptons intervention** — Pro-Israel figures reportedly confront Kirk about his platforming and rhetoric. Names circulating in commentary include [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman) and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff). Attendance lists are **reported claims, not court findings**, and no attendee is alleged here to have known of or planned anything. See [Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons). Secondary narratives about the **purpose** of this meeting (funding/TPUSA deal vs other alleged purposes) have **shifted over time** in commentator accounts — treat purpose as unresolved claimed variants, not established fact.

* **~August 4–6, 2025 (claim)** — Netanyahu reportedly calls Kirk and offers funding tied to an Israel trip, which Kirk declines. Reported figures range from **$150 million** in some accounts to a **billion-dollar** framing sourced through [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) commentary; the discrepancy is itself unresolved. Dollar figures and call purpose remain **unresolved claimed variants**. See [the billion claim](/Israel/billion) and [rejected Israeli funding claims](/Iran/funding-rejection-claims). Owens has separately described a **final** [Netanyahu call about Iran](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call) — a different claim, unconfirmed by any named participant, and not to be merged with this one.

* **August 6, 2025** — In a Megyn Kelly appearance Kirk publicly "lashes out about donors pressuring him" — the first time the private friction becomes visible to his own audience.

* **August 2025 (reported)** — A donor reportedly asks **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)**, in her own later recollection, *"What would happen to TPUSA if you died?"* — dated by that recollection to roughly **40 days** before the assassination. It is a recollection, not a record.

* **September 1–5, 2025** — In private group chats Kirk reportedly writes that he has **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."** The leaked text set is catalogued at [leaked Kirk texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts) and [donor texts in the final 48 hours](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours).

* **September 4, 2025** — SU-BTT flies from France into Provo, Utah, and remains through the event window — the aircraft thread carried on [SU-BTT plane movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **September 4–10, 2025** — Counter-UAS equipment is reportedly tested at [Provo Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) across this window; see [counter-UAS at Provo](/Suspicious/US_Intel/counter-uas-provo) and [Provo Airport Counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS). A separate claim that the airport's **badge access list was modified on 9/11/25** is at [Provo badge list modified](/Suspicious/US_Intel/provo-badge-list-modified).

### The final 72 hours — September 8 to 10, 2025

* **September 8 (Monday)** — Kirk **posts publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement**. This is the moment the private rupture becomes an on-the-record public position, and every donor-pressure motive theory hinges on it. See [Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and [his recorded Israel quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

* **September 8–9 *(attributed private texts)*** — Circulating packages (e.g. @AdameMedia summary of Owens-shared material) place a compressed sequence: (1) a group-chat message about losing a big donor and **"leaving the pro-Israel cause"** involving chats with figures including **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** and **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**; (2) an **emergency Zoom** the next day with Kirk, the rabbi, and Hammer; (3) a text immediately afterward to **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)**: **"they are going to kill me."** These are attributed private messages; wording, recipients, and timing remain disputed, and at least one associate has denied key variants. TPUSA spokesman Kolvet has been widely reported as **confirming authenticity** of the leaked WhatsApp/group frustration texts. Separate commentators stress those texts were **private vents, not a public renunciation** of Kirk's prior Israel positions.

* **September 9 *(date correction, reported)*** — Per commentary on Josh Hammer's later statements (@itsmorganariel and others), the **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** message may date to **September 9** rather than September 8 — less than 24 hours before the UVU shooting. Cross-check against any authenticated export before treating either day as settled.

* **September 8** — Roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels are reported at **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca), Arizona** with flight **[SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702)**, departing at 00:49 on September 9. Tracked at [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline), [the Sept 9 meeting](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and [SAM 702](/Suspicious/US_Intel/sam-702-fort-huachuca). Contested witness claims about other named living people at the base the same window belong on that page and the [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline), not as facts about Charlie's presence.

* **September 9 (Tuesday)** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s** show and criticizes Israel **to Shapiro's face**. Donor-pressure researchers treat this as the point of no return; it is the last major public act of the divergence.

* **September 9** — A **high-pressure meeting** takes place. During it Kirk reportedly texts others: **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."** The text, its recipients and its provenance are examined on [the "they are going to kill me" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) and the [high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) page.

* **September 9** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) later says three separate sources told her Kirk was worried "they" would kill him. Additional reported texts use stronger language including **"executed tomorrow"**, reportedly sent to donors, TPUSA figures and head of security [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood), with [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) among reported recipients. These are **attributed accounts**, gathered at [reported quotes and warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

* **September 9** — Researchers report a Google Trends spike in Utah for the tail number **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** — a search pattern that precedes the aircraft's own activity the following evening.

* **September 10, ~7:08 AM MT** — SU-BTT departs Utah for Wilmington, hours before the event.

* **September 10, morning** — Kirk travels to [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) for [an outdoor campus event](/Locations/Public-Speaking-Event-UVU). The morning's security arrangements — including the absence of a staged ambulance — are examined at [day-of security](/Charlie/Day_Of_Security).

* **September 10, 12:23 PM MDT** — **The shot.** Kirk is struck while speaking under a canopy in the [UVU courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward) west of the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center). Some compilations place it at 12:27; the dispute is on [12:23 vs 12:27](./12-23-shooting-time). For second-by-second movement of Kirk after impact, see the citizen reconstruction popularized by @BasedSamParker and that dedicated shot-time page; this personal timeline only marks the shot and SUV transport decision.

* **September 10, 12:23–12:31 PM** — Security begins moving him within seconds and he is loaded into an SUV rather than an ambulance. The [ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot) page covers this window and [scoop and run](/Suspicious/Medical/no-ambulance-scoop-and-run) the transport decision. Citizen claims about who received real-time condition updates from the transport chain (including alleged Shapiro-before-Erika notification order) are tracked on [12:23 shooting time](./12-23-shooting-time) and [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline) — attribution only.

* **September 10, afternoon** — He is taken to [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) and pronounced deceased. Researchers ask why a nearer Level II facility was not used — [was a trauma center bypassed?](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed). The medical sequence and its disputes are on the [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

* **September 10, evening** — Private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departs Provo, **disables ADS-B in flight**, and later returns — one of the aircraft anomalies carried on [other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline).

* **September 11, 2025** — SU-BTT leaves the United States for Egypt via Wilmington. Everything past this point belongs to the investigation rather than to Kirk's own chronology: see [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13), the [Discord messages attributed to the accused](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), and the [custody vs Discord conflict](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline). [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.

All three are living people accused of nothing; these are **citizen posts**, not filings. ## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | One account | The competing account |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Date of the "intervention" | ~August 4, in the Hamptons | Some accounts place a confrontation on **September 9** instead; the master file preserves both readings without resolving them |
| Size of the declined offer | **$150 million** | A **billion-dollar** figure circulating through Candace Owens sourcing. Neither has a supporting document |
| The "they are going to kill me" text | Sent September 9 during a combative meeting; reported recipient Andrew Kolvet after Zoom with Hammer/Wolicki | Variants place similar language across several days with different recipients and wording — "executed tomorrow", "they want me dead"; associate denial exists |
| Date of "leave the cause" message | **Sep 8** in early viral packages | **Sep 9** after Josh Hammer clarification reports (@itsmorganariel et al.) |
| Private texts vs public stance | Authenticated private vents + public leave-cause post | Private frustration ≠ public renunciation; some say he remained Israel-supportive until death |
| Time of the shot | **12:23 PM MDT** | **12:27 PM** in several media compilations |
| The July 18 "decision" | An inflection point in citizen chronologies | Unsupported by any official record; critics call it retrofitted narrative |
| Kirk's trajectory on Israel | A genuine break with the pro-Israel movement | Others argue he was a lifelong Zionist who was merely frustrated and would never have turned — a reading his own recorded words do not fully settle |
| Hamptons / Netanyahu purpose | Funding / TPUSA deal narratives | Later alternate purpose theories in commentator accounts (unstable secondary narratives) |

## Open Questions

1. Who received the **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** text, and does an authenticated export of the Signal or Telegram threads exist?
2. What was Kirk's actual published schedule for September 8–10, and does it match his documented movements?
3. Who attended the ~August 4 Hamptons meeting, and what was actually said there?
4. Was a funding offer made and declined, and if so at what figure and on what condition?
5. Why was no ambulance staged at an outdoor event for a figure who had reportedly told his own security he feared for his life?
6. What did the June 18 White House meeting on Iran policy record, and who else was in the room?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The full authenticated export of the Signal and Telegram threads containing the "they are going to kill me" and "executed tomorrow" messages together with the identity of every recipient, the attendance records and communications from the ~August 4 Hamptons confrontation and the July 31 White House meeting, the terms of any funding offer made and declined, the GGLF 2023 LLC life insurance policy and its beneficiary, and the flight log for N888KG covering the period after ADS-B went dark are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
