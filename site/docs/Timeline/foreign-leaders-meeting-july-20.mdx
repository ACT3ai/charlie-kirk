# Foreign Leaders Meeting on U.S. Soil (July 20, 2025, Claims)

July 20, 2025 is the earliest date citizen researchers treat as an operational anchor —
the day an alleged **military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil** is said to have
occurred, the day the Israel/Egyptian Air Force jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** is said to
have made its first-ever U.S. arrival, and the date around which Israel-origin Google Trends
activity clusters. This page is the dated chronology of that window. Nothing here is
established fact: no attendee roster, agenda, or government statement has been released, and
no named living person is accused of a crime.

:::caution
Every item below is either public flight-tracking data or investigative commentary. This
website does not assert that any named official attended any meeting, ordered violence, or
had advance knowledge of the September 10 assassination.
:::

:::note Disambiguation — not [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)
The **July 20, 2025 "foreign leaders on U.S. soil"** claim is **not** the same event as the
**September 8–9, 2025 [Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) / [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) VIP visit**
described by [Mitch Snow](/People/mitch-snow) and others. Keep
the clocks separate: [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and
[military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20).
:::

## Before July 20 — Building the Anchor

* **June 18, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) goes to the White House
  specifically to advise against regime change in Iran, the position
  [war-motive researchers](/Iran/iran-war-motive) treat as the origin of the friction
  ([Kirk against the Iran war](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war)).

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister
  [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) visits the United States, thirteen days
  before the July 20 anchor ([Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm)).

* **July 11–13, 2025** — Kirk hosts [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa,
  where anti-Israel-war speakers appear on his stage for the first time, a programming
  choice pro-Israel donors reportedly reacted badly to
  ([Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction)).

* **July 14, 2025** — [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) speaks at the same TPUSA event
  and tells the audience to "go all the way"; researchers note this is the same week Israel
  and DC-origin search activity spikes ([Tucker Carlson and Israel](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel)).

* **July 14, 2025** — Per Google Trends screenshots compiled by citizen researchers, an
  Israel-origin search hits Timpanogos surgeon [Richard Rasmussen](/People/richard-rasmussen),
  the first of seven emergency surgeons at Kirk's future hospital searched inside a two-week
  window ([Timpanogos medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches)).

* **July 16, 2025** — DC-origin searches hit
  [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) itself — the hospital
  Kirk was later taken to instead of the region's
  [Level I trauma center](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed) — per the
  same compilation ([Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns)).

* **July 17, 2025** — [Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason) is appointed Utah
  DPS Commissioner, replacing Jess Anderson; researchers note DC searches for his name dated
  July 16, the day before ([FBI and DPS searches](/GoogleSearches/fbi-dps-law-enforcement-searches)).

* **July 18, 2025** — Some commentators assert a decision to target Kirk in Utah was made
  around this date. This is **speculative interpretation**, unsupported by any public
  official record, and is treated separately on
  [the July 18 decision claim](./july-18-decision). The same day, an Israel-origin search
  reportedly hits surgeon [Steven Neeleman](/People/steven-neeleman).

* **July 19, 2025** — Israel-origin searches reportedly hit surgeon
  [Lee Patterson](/People/lee-patterson), per the Trends compilation.

## July 20, 2025 — The Anchor Day

* **July 20, 2025** — Flight-tracking compilations state that **SU-BTT made its first trip
  to America ever**, landing at a **U.S. Army base in Nebraska**, with reported legs in the
  **Omaha → Lincoln** corridor (trackers have named **Eppley Airfield, Omaha** on later
  logs of the same airframe). The specific Army installation varies by source and is not
  confirmed in any Kirk-case filing summarized on this site
  ([Nebraska Army Base, July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20)).

* **July 20, 2025** — Master-file notes state that **a military meeting with foreign leaders
  took place on U.S. soil**. No attendee list, host unit, or agenda has been published; the
  claim rests on commentary chains and discovery requests, not a manifest
  ([July 20 military meeting](./military-meeting-july-20)).

* **July 20, 2025** — Google Trends researchers describe July 20 as the date "it all lights
  up": the heaviest clustering of Israel- and DC-origin searches across figures, locations
  and aircraft later central to the case
  ([the July 20, 2025 anchor](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor)).

* **July 20, 2025 (window)** — Within the same period, searches reportedly touch
  [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) himself, the
  [UVU campus layout](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches) including the
  [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop, and
  the [HADES](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU) ISR jet
  [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)
  ([plane search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns)).

* **July 20, 2025 (commentary)** — Some threads tie the alleged meeting to
  **counter-UAS (anti-drone) coordination**, later echoed in claims about SU-BTT's September
  stop at Provo ([Provo counter-UAS claims](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS)).
  These are interpretive inferences, not minutes.

* **July 20, 2025 (counterpoint)** — Aviation skeptics note that foreign VIP and government
  jets routinely make training, maintenance and diplomatic stops at U.S. military airfields,
  and that Omaha's corridor hosts substantial defense infrastructure independent of any Utah
  connection ([Egyptian armada context](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)).

* **October 12, 2025 (public origin of the mass-circulation claim)** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)
  ([@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)) stated that Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT** made its **"first trip to America
  EVER" on July 20** to **"an army base in Nebraska,"** contrasting that with the jet's presence
  at Provo around September 10 and inferring that [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) "must
  have had some powerful friends." That post (~2M views) became the **primary mass-audience
  source** for the July 20 framing used across citizen timelines. **Owens's inference is
  speculative; no government confirmation of a foreign-leaders meeting that day has been
  produced.** Amplifiers the same day included [@ninoboxer](/Influencers/x/ninoboxer).

## After July 20 — The Rest of the Summer

* **July 22–30, 2025** — Israel-origin searches reportedly hit three more Timpanogos surgeons —
  [Brian Gill](/People/brian-gill) (July 22), [Robert Patterson](/People/robert-patterson)
  (July 23–24) and [William Pugh](/People/william-pugh) (July 30) — closing the two-week
  surgeon cluster that brackets the July 20 anchor. The cluster itself belongs to the medical
  layer and is set out in full at
  [the Timpanogos surgeon search-trends frenzy](/Suspicious/Medical/surgeon-search-trends).
  All seven are living physicians; nothing here suggests any of them did anything wrong.

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting organized by the
  [Faith &amp; Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), the
  [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel) and the
  [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding) discusses "confronting the woke-right
  defamation of Israel"; reported attendees include [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed),
  [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick),
  [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun) and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu).
  Kirk and TPUSA were **absent** ([July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA)).

* **August 2, 2025** — French legionnaires reportedly arrive in the U.S. for a three-week
  California training rotation ending Aug 25
  ([French Foreign Legion claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims)).

* **~August 4, 2025** — The "Hamptons intervention," where pro-Israel figures reportedly
  confront Kirk; names reported include [Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki),
  [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon),
  [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman) and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)
  ([Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting),
  [Israel–Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons)).

* **~August 4–6, 2025** — Netanyahu [reportedly calls Kirk](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call) and offers
  funding; Kirk reportedly declines a trip to Israel
  ([donor texts](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours)).

* **August 8, 2025** — Israel-origin search interest reportedly focuses on **N1098L**,
  32 days before the shooting ([N1098L HADES low passes](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n1098l-hades-low-passes)).

* **August 17–24, 2025** — A separate SU-BTT U.S. visit is logged in the same master file:
  Paris → Omaha/Lincoln → Wilmington, DE → Paris. **This directly conflicts with the "first
  U.S. trip on July 20" framing** — see the conflicts table below
  ([SU-BTT movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline)).

* **Undated within the July–Aug 2025 window (flight tracking)** — Aviation tracker
  **@sipjack1776** has logged **SU-BTT** (Dassault Falcon 7X, ADS-B hex **#0101D3**) on
  **Paris → Eppley Airfield (Omaha)** and **Lincoln → Wilmington** legs. These logs support an
  **Omaha/Lincoln corridor** presence but do **not**, by themselves, establish "first ever"
  status, name a specific Army installation, or prove a meeting agenda.

* **September 4, 2025** — SU-BTT flies France →
  [Provo, Utah](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo) and stays until the morning of
  the assassination, the sequence that turned July 20 into a retroactive anchor
  ([2025 context timeline](./Year_2025), [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13)). Citizen investigators
  ([@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) / [@nihilox](/Influencers/x/nihilox)) further describe a **Paris-Le Bourget** coincidence of
  **[T7-ELL](/Planes/T7ELL-EJM36)**, **N360BE**, and **SU-BTT**, with SU-BTT then associated with a **Minot AFB**
  intermediate leg before Provo. A competing technical summary frames the Provo stop as
  **Duncan Aviation maintenance**, with departure the morning of September 10 for
  **Wilmington, DE (KILG)** then Cairo. Both the multi-jet framing and the maintenance framing
  belong as **competing after-July-20 context**; detail lives on
  [SU-BTT movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SU-BTT's first U.S. arrival | **July 20, 2025**, to a Nebraska Army base — Owens Oct 12, 2025 mass-circulation framing | **Aug 17, 2025**, Paris → Omaha/Lincoln, per a separate routing block in the same master file marked "1st time in USA" |
| The destination base | A **U.S. Army base in Nebraska** | Trackers name **Eppley (Omaha)** and **Lincoln**; no specific Army installation is confirmed |
| Provo stop (Sept 4–10) | Part of an operational SU-BTT chain after multi-jet Paris meetup / Minot leg | **Duncan Aviation maintenance** at Provo; depart morning of Sept 10 for Wilmington then Cairo |
| The meeting itself | A **military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil** occurred July 20 | No government body has confirmed such a meeting, and no host unit has responded publicly |
| Conflation risk | July 20 foreign-leaders claim is an independent anchor | **Not** the [Sept 8–9 Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) / SAM VIP visit — different clocks, different page |
| The July 18 link | Commentators pair July 20 with an alleged July 18 targeting decision | The July 18 claim has **no supporting public record** and is flagged speculative on its own page |
| Search-trend significance | July 20 is the peak of foreign-IP interest in case figures | Trends data cannot establish who searched, and single-VPN artifacts can produce identical patterns |

## Open Questions

   base arrival record exist?
2. Which of the two "first U.S. trip" dates — July 20 or Aug 17 — is the tracker record, and
   which is a transcription error?
3. Did any host unit's public affairs office confirm or deny a foreign-delegation visit that
   week?
4. Do CBP entry records show foreign nationals entering on July 20 consistent with the
   claimed delegation?
5. Why does the surgeon-search cluster fall inside a two-week band centered on July 20 rather
   than being spread across the summer?
6. Is there any documented link at all between July 20 activity and Utah, or is the
   connection entirely retroactive?
7. Do ADS-B exports confirm the Paris multi-jet (T7-ELL / N360BE / SU-BTT) coincidence and any
   Minot AFB intermediate before Provo?
8. What is the FOIA/CBP/FAA status of requests for July 20 logs (requested, denied, pending)?
