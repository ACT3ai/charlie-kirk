# Foreign Leaders Meeting on U.S. Soil (July 20, 2025, Claims)

Citizen-research timelines and flight-tracking compilations allege that **July 20, 2025** included a **military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil**, overlapping the reported first U.S. arrival of the Israel/Egyptian Air Force jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** at a **Nebraska Army base**. This page records **what those sources claim**; it does not assert that any named official ordered violence or that the meeting is established fact.

## Reported same-day pairing

| Element | As claimed in project sources |
| --- | --- |
| **Date** | **July 20, 2025** |
| **Foreign-leaders meeting** | Alleged gathering of **international officials** with **U.S. military hosts** on domestic soil |
| **Aviation anchor** | **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** described as making its **first-ever U.S. trip** the same day — see [Nebraska Army Base (July 20)](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) |
| **Evidence type** | ADS-B screenshots, commentary chains, and *Laws* discovery requests — **not** a public attendee manifest |

Mainstream outlets have **not**, at time of writing, publicly confirmed a foreign-leaders roster tied to the Kirk case. The connection is drawn primarily in investigative commentary and master research notes ([White House Halt of IC](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC)).

## What sources allege about participants

Trash-source *Laws* blocks and foreign-intelligence write-ups preserved in retired directories describe the meeting in general terms:

- **Foreign leaders or military delegations** — specific identities are **alleged but not publicly verified** on this site.
- **U.S. military hosts** — described as base-level or joint-service personnel; no official roster has been released in Kirk-case filings summarized here.
- **Diplomatic entry** — commentators cite **CBP entry records** and **visitor logs** as records that could confirm or refute foreign-national presence; none are public in the docket as summarized on this site.

Living persons are not described here as criminals; any attendee names circulating online remain **unverified allegations** unless confirmed in official proceedings.

## Alleged purpose (commentary only)

Secondary summaries link the July 20 gathering to several **interpretive** themes — none adjudicated in court here:

- **Counter-UAS (anti-drone) coordination** — tied by some threads to later claims about SU-BTT's September Provo stop ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)).
- **Special-forces or ISR coordination** — some compilations pair July 20 with **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** HADES activity reported later in the summer.
- **Middle East security planning** — framed in broader Israel/Egypt aircraft narratives ([Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)).

These purposes are **commentary inferences**, not official meeting minutes.

## Placement in the summer 2025 sequence

Citizen timelines often order July 20 between:

- **[July 18 "decision" claim](./july-18-decision)** — speculative commentary about a Utah targeting decision; **not** supported by public official records.
- **July 31 White House session** — Faith & Freedom / Latino Coalition for Israel / Philos Project meeting ([2025 Context Timeline](./Year_2025)).
