This is the **master chronology hub** for [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) across the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case: what the charging documents say he did, what the surveillance record is said to show, and where the custody and confession sequence stops making internal sense. Level 4 sub-pages carry the hour-by-hour detail. **All times are Mountain Time (MT).**

:::caution Charged, not convicted
Tyler Robinson is **charged** in *[State of Utah v. Tyler Alexander Robinson](/court/case-overview)*, Case No. 251403576, at the [Fourth District Court in Provo](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo). He has **not been convicted of anything**. Every item below sourced to the affidavit or the charging documents is an **allegation by the State**, not a finding of fact. Every item sourced to citizen research is an unproven claim. He is presumed innocent.
:::

## The Timeline

### Before September 10, 2025

* **Before Sept 10** — Charging documents allege a **dinner conversation** in the weeks before the shooting in which Robinson discussed Kirk; the pre-event background is collected on [Tyler Robinson before the event](/Tyler_Robinson/Before) and his household and relationships on [Family](/Tyler_Robinson/Family) and [Girlfriend / roommate](/Tyler_Robinson/Girlfriend/overview).

* **Before Sept 10 (claim)** — Citizen researchers allege that **unusual visitors with out-of-state plates** were seen at the residence before September 10. This is an **unverified claim** from compiled X threads, recorded on [the "recruited" question page](/Tyler_Robinson/Recruited).

* **Sept 9, 2025** — A **high-pressure meeting** involving Charlie Kirk, during which Kirk reportedly texted others: **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."** This is on Kirk's side of the case, not Robinson's, but it anchors the day before — see [the September 9 meeting page](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and [the text itself](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

### September 10, 2025 — day of the shooting

*Camera sightings below are broken down by device on [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview): [Ring](/cameras/Ring), [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE), [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance), with the full list on [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).*

* **7:08 AM** — [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), an Egyptian Air Force Gulfstream, departs Utah for Wilmington — a separate thread carried on [the SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **8:07 AM** — Robinson is recorded on a **[Ring camera](/cameras/Ring)** on S 800 W in a maroon shirt and light shorts, heading toward campus. The full morning is reconstructed on [the pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).

* **8:29 AM** — Robinson arrives at UVU in a **gray Dodge Challenger**. **No backpack is visible** — a detail central to [the backpack-rifle impossibility argument](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility).

* **9:57 AM** — Robinson appears on a **[WYZE camera](/cameras/WYZE)**; the outfit has **changed** to jeans and a long-sleeved shirt. The clothing changes across the morning are catalogued on [Robinson clothing changes](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/robinson-clothing-changes).

* **11:30 AM (claim)** — Citizen research notes a vehicle associated with a local political figure parked at a house on the walking route; the claim, and the caution attached to it, are on [the Phil Lyman page](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman).

* **11:49–11:50 AM** — Robinson is recorded on [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) near Campus Drive in a **black eagle-graphic shirt, dark jeans, Chuck Taylors and a dark backpack**, with a distinctive **stiff right-leg gait**. The gait is the single most-argued identification detail in the case.

* **Morning sequence (hearing framing, claimed)** — Preliminary-hearing coverage circulating on X (e.g., summaries amplified by @BubbaLostAtSea and court-watch accounts) describes the State’s video package as placing the charged individual on campus **multiple times** that day — some summaries say **four visits** — across the Ring / Challenger / WYZE / Campus Drive chain above. Allegations only; charged, not convicted. Camera-by-camera detail: [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **12:02 PM** — Robinson enters the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** through the southeast doors, per the affidavit.

* **12:15 PM** — The affidavit describes him crossing a railing from a public walkway onto the roof — the segment disputed on [the stairs-guy identity gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) and [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy).

* **12:17 PM** — He climbs a short wall and crouches on the north side of the roof, per the affidavit; the feasibility argument is on [the roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **12:22 PM** — He runs west across the roof and lies prone at the edge, roughly **430 feet** from the stage. Commentators note the **limp is no longer visible** in this stretch — see [roof run, limp disappears](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/roof-run-limp-disappears).

* **12:23:30 PM** — **Charlie Kirk is shot in the neck.** The State alleges Robinson fired one round from the Losee roof, then stood and ran north. The sixty-six seconds that follow in the courtyard are reconstructed on [the minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute); a competing **12:27 PM** shot time is examined on [the shot-time dispute page](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **12:24 PM** — Per the affidavit, a **dark item — a rifle wrapped in a dark towel — is placed on the rooftop**; the figure lowers himself to the grass and runs north across Campus Drive into the wooded area. See [the post-shot escape timeline](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape) and [rifle and towel on the roof](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/rifle-towel-on-roof).

* **12:35 PM (official account)** — A **Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle** is reported found wrapped in a dark towel in the [wooded area north of campus](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery), with inscribed cartridge casings nearby. See [the Mauser page](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) and [the weapon page](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle). A competing account places the recovery **the next day, after K-9 and officer searches failed** — [the gun-discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

* **12:39 PM** — **First [FBI](/FBI/overview) agents arrive** on scene — see [the FBI involvement timeline](./fbi-involvement-timeline).

* **4:21 PM** — FBI Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) publicly announces a suspect in custody. **This was incorrect** — the false-custody news cycles of that afternoon are on [the distraction events timeline](./distraction-events-timeline) and [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings).

* **~6:00–7:00 PM (claimed)** — Neighbor/citizen interviews amplified by @billiewebbb and @brandisiciliani place rifle recovery **between 6 PM and 7 PM** on September 10 — an intermediate clock between the affidavit’s early-afternoon framing and the “next day after failed K-9” account. Unverified citizen claim only.

* **Evening** — Texts attributed to Robinson are cited in the charging documents, including *"My dad wants photos of the rifle"* and *"I had enough of his hatred."* Their authentication is contested — see [phones and devices in the court record](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices) and [Cellebrite extractions](/court/Cellebrite).

* **~8:55–9:00 PM (claimed / disputed)** — An employee at **Cowboy’s Smokehouse in Panguitch, Utah** reportedly told media and citizen interviewers he was “100%” certain he served a diner matching Robinson (steak and beers; distinctive voice), with a receipt near **8:55 PM**, roughly **3+ hours** south of UVU ([the receipt itself](/Tyler_Robinson/cowboys-smokehouse-receipt)). Amplification included [@HustleBitch_](/Influencers/x/hustlebitch) and others; **owner later clarifications**, as summarized in secondary commentary, say it was **not** Robinson and that a circulating receipt was not the tip document given to investigators. Contested travel-timeline data only — see [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape) and the competing [Dairy Queen Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) placement.

### September 11, 2025 — the surrender day

* **7:15–7:17 AM** — The gray Dodge Challenger is captured on surveillance at the **[Maverik gas station in Cedar City](/Locations/Cedar_City_Maverik)**, roughly 215 miles south, on the route toward Washington County. Payment reportedly by credit card. See [Travel](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel).

* **9:58 AM** — The FBI releases its **first photos of a person of interest** — low-resolution stills criticised at the time; see [enhanced suspect photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).

* **10:44 AM** — The FBI announces a **$100,000 reward**.

* **Daytime/evening** — Per reporting compiled in the master file, **Robinson's parents recognised him in the released photos** and persuaded him to come to their residence in Washington City, near St. George. He reportedly expressed fear of a SWAT raid at both the parents' house and his own [apartment in St. George](/Locations/Tyler_Apartment).

* **6:25 PM** — Per a Bates-stamped defense exhibit analysed on [the Mirandizing page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing), Robinson is **Mirandized**, invokes the right to counsel — naming attorney **[Doug Terry](/People/doug-terry)** — and invokes the right to remain silent. **No questioning occurs.** The date on the exhibit is not printed; the September 11 attribution rests on the recorded remark that the lawyer's "office is closed for the night." Defense-side amplifiers (e.g., @SteveCameronPr1) state the prosecution has not disputed the time stamp in public citizen recounts.

* **~7:00 PM (family-confirm claim)** — Citizen reporting amplified by @DiligentDenizen claims **family confirmation** of a roughly **7 PM** turn-in window and **immediate phone seizure**, placing a third clock between the 6:25 PM Miranda exhibit and the booking sheet’s **10:00 PM** arrest stamp. This is a family-sourced citizen-media claim, not a court finding.

* **7:55 PM** — Per an AP-sourced timeline, the FBI states publicly that it is still seeking a **"person of interest"** and releases photos and rooftop video.

* **7:57 PM** — A **Discord post attributed to Robinson** reads: *"It was me at UVU yesterday. Im surrendering through a sheriff friend in a few moments."* If the 6:25 PM Miranda time is correct, this **post-dates** the invocation of silence by ninety minutes — the core conflict on [custody vs Discord confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline), [the Discord custody timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline), and [Discord messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages). **Version B media framing** (e.g., @Liz_Wheeler, July 2026): physical surrender ~**9 PM**, so Discord is a full hour **before** body presence at the sheriff’s office; formal arrest may be as late as **4:00 AM** September 12.

* **8:02 PM** — A **retired Washington County detective**, named [Mike Mitchell](/People/mike-mitchell) in prosecution inventories and contacted by the family, telephones Sheriff [**Nate Brooksby**](/People/nate-brooksby) to arrange a voluntary surrender. Brooksby later resigned — see [the Brooksby resignation page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation) and [Miranda vs the Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account). Commenters note “turning himself in ~8:30” language can mean **process/call chain**, not physical presence (arrived later).

* **~9:50 PM** — Robinson, his father, and the retired detective drive to the **[Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff)** in Hurricane, Utah.

* **10:00 PM** — The Utah County **inmate booking sheet lists the arrest date as 9/11/2025 at 10:00 PM**, with the arresting officer named as **[Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis)** of the State Bureau of Investigation. That is a southern-Utah arrest by a northern-Utah officer, and it is the documentary spine of the [arrest-time discrepancy](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy). Full document conflicts: [Arrest & Affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline).

* **10:26 PM** — Robinson arrives at the Washington County Sheriff's Office, is placed **uncuffed on a couch with his parents**, and waits roughly **2.5 hours** for Utah County investigators. The Sheriff's Office has stated it **did not interview or question him**. No surrender footage has been produced — see [missing surrender footage](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/missing-surrender-footage) and [Surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender).

* **After arrival at WCSO (phone, hearing summaries)** — Preliminary-hearing coverage states **Washington County deputies confiscated Robinson’s phone** at the facility after surrender and **before** Agent Davis / FBI arrival — a chain-of-custody constraint for Discord and text authentication. See [phones and devices](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices).

### September 12, 2025 — booking

* **~12:30 AM** — Utah County investigators arrive; Robinson is transferred to FBI custody.

* **4:00 AM** — **Formal arrest and booking** at [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail), Spanish Fork, booking #460956. Charges: **Aggravated Murder (1st-degree felony), Felony Discharge of a Firearm (1st-degree felony), Obstruction of Justice (2nd-degree felony)**. Held without bail.

* **7:18 AM** — **Probable Cause entered** by Officer Brian Davis, Probable Cause ID 189904 — the document analysed on [the affidavit page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause).

* **5:00 PM** — Utah County issues the **public press release** confirming the arrest.

* **Sept 12 (media / corporate statement)** — TMZ-era reporting that **Discord denied** the platform was leveraged for planning or admitting the killing remains in circulation as a counterpoint to charging exhibits that still present Discord content. Corporate statement versus State exhibits — present as conflict, not resolved fact.

### September 16, 2025 — charging

* **Sept 16** — **Formal charges filed.** A **Notice of Intent to Seek the Death Penalty** is filed — six days after the shooting, a speed questioned on [the death-notice page](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/death-notice-six-days).

* **Sept 16** — A **Pretrial Protective Order** is issued, beginning the sequence of gag and publicity orders documented on [gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and [the breadth of the publicity order](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/publicity-order-breadth).

* **Sept 16** — The **Affidavit of Probable Cause** is submitted by Officer Brian Davis.

### Pre-trial, 2025–2026

* **Ongoing** — [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf) presides; the [defense team](/court/defense-team) has litigated discovery, Miranda, and publicity issues. The [preliminary hearing days](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) are logged individually.

* **Ongoing** — Discovery disputes over device extractions, [Twiggs phone material](/court/Twiggs_Phone), and Brady obligations run through [discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes) and [the discovery/Brady timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

* **2026** — Attorney [**Baron Coleman**](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) publicly argues that Robinson was in Washington County custody **well before 6:30 PM on September 11**, and that the official "33-hour manhunt" framing cannot be reconciled with the booking sheet. His running commentary is tracked on [the Baron Coleman timeline](./baron-coleman-timeline) and [the fake-confession custody analysis](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession).

* **July 2026 — preliminary hearing (reported)** — Public coverage of Agent [**Brian Davis**](/People/brian-davis) testimony (amplified by [@SteveCameronPr1](/People/steve-cameron), [@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)): he arrived **near midnight / ~12:00 AM September 12**, conflicting with the booking sheet’s **10:00 PM September 11** stamp in his name. Commentators also note the defense **did not cross-examine** Davis after testimony covering surrender, roommate materials, Discord, warrants, and physical evidence — significance contested (strategy vs concession). Hearing packages circulating online also emphasize **palm/forearm prints** alleged on the roof and State video of multiple campus appearances. Robinson remains **charged, not convicted**. See [Arrest & Affidavit](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline) and [Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview).

* **2026 (ballistics caveat, reported)** — Circulating hearing summaries state the fatal bullet **fragment analysis was class-characteristic only / inconclusive** as to individualization to the recovered rifle — a hub-level caveat also tracked on [audio and mic](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline) and [ATF fragment](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive).

* **2026** — Case status: **pre-trial proceedings ongoing**, capital exposure intact. See [Case & Trial](/Tyler_Robinson/Trial/overview) and [the trial press page](/Media/tyler-robinson-trial-press).

## Open Questions

1. What is the **date stamp** on the Bates-marked Miranda video, and why has it not been made public?
2. How could an officer based in northern Utah effect a **10:00 PM arrest in Washington County** unless he departed several hours before the surrender call at 8:02 PM?
3. Where is the **Washington County Sheriff's Office lobby video** for the evening of September 11?
4. What device forensics authenticate the Discord posts and the text messages, and who performed the [extractions](/court/Phone_Extraction)?
5. Does any continuous, timecoded footage show the same individual from Campus Drive at 11:50 AM through the roof and back down — or does the chain break?
6. Why was the **death-penalty notice filed within six days**, before discovery had substantially begun?
