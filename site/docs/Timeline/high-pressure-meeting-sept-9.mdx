# High-Pressure Meeting (September 9, 2025, Claims)

This is the **last full day** [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was alive, tracked hour by hour in **Mountain Daylight Time**. Its centre is a **contentious private meeting** with pro-Israel advocates that citizen-research compilations say took place that day, and the alleged **"they are going to kill me"** text reportedly sent during it. The day also carries two **independently documented** UVU records — a 2:23 PM email and a 10:35 PM office visit — that exist in a [GRAMA release](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) and do not depend on the meeting claim at all. Read this page for the eve-of-event pressure layer; the shot itself is on [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025).

## The Timeline

### Overnight and early morning

* **~00:49 MDT (claimed)** — Master-file flight notes record the **[SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702)** VIP aircraft departing [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) after the reported **Sept 8 gathering of roughly twelve lieutenant colonels**; the military layer of this same day is developed on the [Fort Huachuca and military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

* **Early morning (claimed)** — A source described publicly as a **military whistleblower** says he walked into a closed meeting at Fort Huachuca that morning and was questioned at length before military police were called; the account, the responding incident report, and the pushback against it are summarized at [Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and on the [Mitch Snow profile](/People/mitch-snow).

* **~7:30 AM local (claimed)** — Master-file notes state that meeting **concluded at 7:30 AM local time**, with a [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) executive reportedly among those seen leaving. Identification of anyone in that group is **unverified** and disputed by the same sources that report it.

### Daytime: the public appearance

* **Daytime (reported)** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s** show and presses criticism of Israeli policy directly to Shapiro. This is the day's **publicly verifiable anchor** — it is on tape — and is the point that donor-pressure researchers treat as the escalation, tracked at [Israel events and pressure](./israel-events-pressure-timeline) and [reported Charlie Kirk quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel). Some later commentators call the same-window pairing of **"leave the pro-Israel cause"** private texts with a public Shapiro appearance **"nonsensical"**; others treat it as deliberate confrontation. Exact show start time is not fixed on this page from archive clocks.

* **Daytime (reported, dating conflict on donor texts)** — WhatsApp group messages — *"Just lost another huge Jewish donor. $2 million a year because we won't cancel Tucker"* and *"Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"* — were **confirmed as genuine** by TPUSA spokesman [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) (clips recirculated as *"That was a text grab… that I had shared"*). Early retellings and this site's prior placement put those messages on **September 8**. A widely shared February 2026 claim asserts **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** fixed the leave-pro-Israel message on **September 9** — less than 24 hours before the shooting. **Authenticated content ≠ settled calendar date** until chat metadata is public. See [political pressure and meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).

* **Anti-conflation note** — Public posts routinely collapse the **Kolvet-authenticated** "$2M donor / leave pro-Israel cause" texts and the **unauthenticated** **"they are going to kill me"** phrase onto a single **"9/9 text."** They are **different claim clusters** with different evidence status. Do not treat them as one message.

* **Daytime (claimed)** — [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) is reported to have retweeted a [Trump](/People/donald-trump) post about a twelve-year-old execution with the word **"Based."** The post is cited in motive compilations; it is a public post, not evidence of foreknowledge, and no allegation of wrongdoing is made here.

* **Daytime (claimed)** — Google Trends screenshots circulated by researchers show searches for **n888kg** — the tail number of the jet that would leave Provo the following evening — originating in **Utah on September 9**. See [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) and [planes search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns).

### The session itself — call vs in-person (structure disputed)

* **Structure A — debate-prep call (claimed; Owens track)** — According to **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** (clips amplified e.g. Oct 2025), on **September 9** Kirk joined a **call** to prepare for anti-Israel debates involving **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**, and a few unnamed **TPUSA producers**. This is a **debate-prep call** description — not a documented multi-person in-person venue. **No public recording** of any such call has been released.

* **Structure B — intervention-style meeting (claimed; timeline compilations)** — Other compilations describe a **combative in-person high-pressure meeting** / "intervention," not routine scheduling. **No source in the public record** fixes a start time, a location, or a duration. **No agency has confirmed** any such meeting occurred.

* **Participants commonly named (alleged)** — Wolicki, Hammer, [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff). **None of these individuals has been charged with or accused by any authority of any offence.** The names appear here only because they recur in public commentary. The call-track names a **narrower** set (Wolicki, Hammer, TPUSA producers).

* **Attendee / date overlap risk** — The same names appear on the early-August [Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) lists (including Ackman's Bridgehampton account) — background on the venue and the donor setting is at [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons) and [the Hamptons intervention and the death question](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention). Commentators read an **escalating private-pressure track**; secondary timelines often **conflate** August Hamptons with September 9. The master file itself carries both datings.

* **During the day / meeting (claimed — kill-me cluster)** — Kirk **reportedly texted others** saying **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** / **"I think they're going to kill me."** Owens has said **three separate people** received written warnings that day, one of them a donor. Separate commentary asserts **Kolvet** privately relayed knowledge of such a text to Owens within ~48 hours after Sept 10 and/or that security lead **[Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood)** was a recipient (Flood has not been charged with or accused by any authority of any offence). **No screenshot or carrier record** has been published. Full sourcing chain and variants: [the "they are going to kill me" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text); Owens track: [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **Denials (part of the record)** — An associate path states the kill-me message **"never happened"** and that the speaker **holds Kirk's phone**. Secondary summaries describe **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** as denying a fear-driven final night (phone in her possession; final night framed as prayer, not fear). These denials belong on this page alongside the claims.

* **Same day (claimed)** — A separate reported exchange has a friend writing *"I hope you improved your security"* and Kirk answering *"I did"* and *"I know they want me dead."* Sourcing is second-hand; see [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) and [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group) for the security-posture question this raises.

* **Same week (2026 Candace expansion)** — Later Candace segments describe pressure from **"Christian Zionist donors"** in addition to the September donor-chat cluster. Treat as **her characterisation of sources**, not an authenticated roster.

* **Same day (claimed)** — Master-file notes record Kirk texting that he would **invite Candace Owens to AmFest**, a detail proponents use to argue he was actively realigning in his final 48 hours.

### Evening: the documented UVU record

* **2:23 PM MDT (documented)** — An email moves through **UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)'s** communication chain. Subject line: **FYI**. Body: *"He wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk. His phone number is REDACTED."* The requester's name and the number are **redacted**; whether the message was ever delivered is not documented anywhere in the 112 released pages. See [UVU pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **10:35 PM MDT (documented)** — A UVU staff message states that **two men showed up at offices asking to talk to police about "a plan,"** and that staff escalated it because two senators were expected the next day. The message asks whether anyone has more information. Document reference **UVU 25-330:0023**.

* **11:19 PM MDT (documented)** — **44 minutes later**, a follow-up message states the two men were **not** from a senator's office but were **"GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did."** Nobody in the chain asks what the plan was; nobody documents what the two men told police; all names are redacted under Utah exemptions. See [UVU administration response](/UVU/UVU-Administration-Response).

* **Overnight (reported)** — Fourteen hours after the 10:35 PM visit, the shooting occurs. Also in the same [GRAMA set](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records): **[Orem PD](/Law_Enforcement/orem-police-response)** had sent UVU the event ticket link on **Sept 3**, then said after the shooting that it was **"not involved"** — despite UVU paying Orem PD annually for a joint Public Order Unit that was never activated. See [campus security gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps) and [no counter-snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers).

## Open Questions

1. Does any calendar entry, travel record, or venue booking corroborate that a September 9 meeting happened, and where?
2. Who sent the 2:23 PM email asking UVU police to relay a message to Kirk, and was it delivered?
3. Who were the two men who came to a UVU office at 10:35 PM, and what "plan" did they describe?
4. If the "they are going to kill me" text exists, what device, carrier record, and timestamp authenticate it?
5. Why did the joint UVU–Orem Public Order Unit never activate for an event Orem PD had circulated tickets for a week earlier?
6. Are the Sept 9 private-pressure track and the Sept 8–9 Fort Huachuca activity connected in any documentary way, or only in sequence?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The attendee list and contemporaneous communications from the September 9 session, the unredacted UVU GRAMA pages including the names behind the 2:23 PM email and the 10:35 PM office visit, the additional UVU documents the university has acknowledged exist but refused to release, the carrier and forensic records that would authenticate or disprove the "they are going to kill me" message, and the donor-network financial records tied to the pressure campaign are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
