# High-Pressure Meeting (September 9, 2025, Claims)

Citizen-research compilations and long-form summaries describe a **contentious meeting on September 9, 2025** between [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and pro-Israel advocates — the day before the UVU shooting. The meeting is often paired with Kirk's alleged **[\"they are going to kill me\" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text)** and with **UVU security communications** that same night.

## Participants commonly named (alleged)

Sources cited on this site (e.g., comprehensive examination summaries in retired trash analysis docs, [Before: Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025)) frequently list:

- **[Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**
- **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**
- **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**
- **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**
- **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)**

These names overlap the **[Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting)** attendee lists from early August — suggesting to commentators an **escalating private pressure track**, not a single isolated event.

## Same-day public and private activity

| Time / item | Source basis |
| --- | --- |
| **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) show (reported)** | [2025 Context Timeline](./Year_2025) — Kirk appeared Sept 9 with sharper Israeli-policy criticism (public media anchor) |
| **Alleged warning text** | [They Are Going to Kill Me Text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) — claimed message to confidants during or after the meeting |
| **2:23 PM UVU email (documented)** | [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) — GRAMA record of someone asking UVU police to relay a message to Kirk (name redacted) |
| **10:35 PM UVU visit (documented)** | Same GRAMA release — two men asked to speak with police about **"a plan"**; identities redacted |

The GRAMA items are **independently documented** through DCNF's release; the **September 9 private meeting** itself is **not** confirmed by those records.

## What commentators infer

- The meeting is framed as **high-pressure** — an intervention-style confrontation rather than routine scheduling.
- Timeline videos place it **after** Kirk's **September 8** post about leaving the pro-Israel movement ([Timeline Overview](./overview)).
- Motive theorists link it to **[Israel](/Israel/overview)** and **[Motive](/Motive/overview)** threads; this page only records **alleged sequencing**.
