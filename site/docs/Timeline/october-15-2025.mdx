# October 15, 2025 — Post-Assassination Developments

**October 15, 2025** falls roughly **35 days** after the September 10 UVU shooting — a mid-October checkpoint when memorial politics, citizen-investigator threads, and pretrial court scheduling were all moving in parallel. The retired trash page *October 15th 2025* contained mostly generic boilerplate; this page anchors **documented or widely reported developments in the surrounding October window** (approximately **October 10–16**), with clear separation between **public record**, **media reporting**, and **unverified allegations**.

## Immediate context (late September → early October)

Before mid-October, project timelines already note:

- **Sep 11, 2025:** [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) reported U.S. departure toward Egypt ([Post-Assassination Investigations](./post-assassination-investigations)).
- **Sep 19, 2025:** *Demystifying the Mossad* interview episode widely cited in commentary ([Year_2025](./Year_2025)).
- **Sep 15, 2025 (claims):** Research notes describe an **El-Sisi video conference** at Macron's invitation — an **alleged** diplomatic huddle in the immediate post-assassination window; not confirmed here as Kirk-case-related.

## October 10, 2025 — AES factory explosion

On **October 10, 2025** — **30 days** after the assassination — **Accurate Energetic Systems (AES) Building 602** in Tennessee was destroyed in a massive blast. Site coverage treats this as a major post-shot development because online investigators had already tied a **DoD munitions contract** and an **exploding-microphone theory** to AES ([AES Factory Explosion](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory)). The explosion killed **16 workers** per public reporting; proponents and skeptics disagree on whether timing is suspicious or coincidental.

## October 16, 2025 — investigator commentary

On **October 16, 2025**, citizen investigator **Jon Bray** reportedly framed the AES destruction in stark terms online — suggesting, in his posted characterization, that destroying a building could eliminate evidence ([AES Tennessee Explosion](/CoverUp/AES_Tennessee_Explosion)). That is **commentary**, not a verified finding about intent or perpetrators.

## Court and prosecution milestones (October window)

Public court reporting cited on this site includes:

- **Oct 30, 2025 (scheduled):** Tyler Robinson reportedly set to appear **in person** for a **scheduling hearing** before Judge **Tony Graf** ([Prosecution & Court Proceedings](/Law_Enforcement/court-proceedings)).
- **Ongoing pretrial posture:** Large-volume discovery, protective orders, and later **December 2025 gag-order** reporting — see [Post-Assassination Investigations](./post-assassination-investigations) and [Evidence Sealing 2026](/Legal/Evidence-Sealing-2026).

Tyler Robinson remains the **charged defendant** and has **not** been convicted; court dates are procedural milestones, not findings of guilt.

## Developments within days (late October preview)

Several major post-assassination threads **accelerated just after** mid-October:

- **Late October 2025:** *New York Times* reporting (c. **Oct 28**) on an alleged **White House dispute** over counterterrorism review of foreign leads ([White House Halt of IC](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC)).
- **Oct 25–29, 2025:** Viral amplification of claims that the **FBI asked a witness to delete video** ([FBI Asked Delete Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video)).

These are included because citizen timelines often treat **mid-to-late October** as one continuous post-shot phase; they are **not** dated to October 15 specifically unless sourced.
