# Funeral, Memorial & Immediate Aftermath Timeline

This timeline runs from the **moment of notification on September 10, 2025** through the memorial service, the succession announcement, the posthumous honors, and the institutional resignations that followed. It is the *ceremonial and institutional* aftermath. The criminal-investigation track is on [the post-assassination investigation timeline](./post-assassination-investigations); the organizational track is on [the TPUSA internal timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

:::caution Attribution notice
Every living person named here — including **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)**, **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)**, and **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** — is accused of no crime and has been found by no court to have done anything wrong. Contested items below are **what named speakers said in public**, recorded with the ordinary explanation alongside.
:::

## September 10, 2025: notification

* **~12:23 PM MDT** — Erika Kirk has publicly said that **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)** called her at the moment of the shooting; the separate reported call to his wife in the same second is at [McCoy phones his wife](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife), and the account and its timing are catalogued on [memorial and TPUSA succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession) and [the Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

* **~11:23 AM Arizona time (12:23 PM MDT)** — Per a witness account attributed to **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**, Mikey McCoy walked away with a phone to his ear and called **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** in Arizona. The viral post-shot footage of a man walking away on a phone is the visual attached to this claim; it is a **reconstruction from public accounts**, not a phone record. Compare [McCoy alone in frame](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict).

* **Afternoon, September 10** — Kirk is transported to [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) — a choice disputed at [was a nearer trauma center bypassed?](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed) — where the medical, autopsy, and death-certificate questions begin; those belong to [the medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline), [the autopsy report page](/Medical/autopsy_report_not_public), and [the death certificate page](/Medical/Autopsy/death_certificate).

* **Evening, September 10** — Aircraft departures from [Provo](/Locations/Provo_Airport) the same evening, including the private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)**, are tracked on [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025) and [the other-planes activity timeline](./other-planes-activity-timeline). Family travel after a death is unremarkable; the flight records are logged because they are checkable.

* **September 11, 2025** — Kirk's **next scheduled event** had, per public accounts, **no security plan in place**, against a normal practice reported as roughly twelve days of preparation per event. See [next event, no plan](/Security_Team/next-event-no-plan) and [day-of security](/Charlie/Day_Of_Security).

## September 11–13, 2025: the casket flight, the speech, and memorial announcement

* **September 11, 2025** — **Air Force Two** transports the casket from **Salt Lake City to Phoenix**, with Vice President [JD Vance](/People/jd-vance) accompanying Erika Kirk — see [the casket flight page](/Planes/Casket-Flight) and [Vance mission continuation](/Media/Vance-Mission-Continuation).

* **September 11, 2025 — the tarmac account (disputed)** — Kolvet publicly described Erika running into his arms on the tarmac. Owens has questioned the sequencing against FlightRadar data she says shows Erika's aircraft arriving more than an hour earlier. Both are **public commentary**; see [the disputed succession page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed) and [the Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

* **September 12, 2025 — approximately 9:09 PM ET (~55 hours after the shot)** — Erika Kirk delivers the speech in which she refers to **"evil do-ERs" — plural**. The plural pronoun is the whole of the observation, and it is an observation about **wording**, nothing more. See [the Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline) and the corresponding discovery item in [Law 3: require the investigation](/laws/Require_to_Investigate/Law_3_Require_to_Investigate).

* **September 11–13, 2025** — In parallel, the surrender and custody sequence unfolds in Washington County; that track is on [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) and [the Sept 11 surrender and custody page](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody).

* **September 13, 2025** — Turning Point USA publicly announced plans for a stadium memorial service, according to contemporary reporting later summarized in mainstream timelines (including Wikipedia's memorial-service entry). A memorial planning site (**fightforcharlie.com** and successors) circulated in the same window per LiveNowFox-style coverage.

## The memorial service

* **Mid-September 2025 — production** — **Mosaic Pro Events**, TPUSA's long-standing production partner, handled the stadium build — hundreds of crew, trucks, and lighting rigs — the same vendor that produced AmericaFest and the campus tour, including the September 10 event's [production footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage). Program elements included stadium-scale lighting, sound, and **pyrotechnics** that later became a recurring subject of online commentary about tone (production aesthetics = **opinion**, not evidence).

* **September 17, 2025 (satellite memorials)** — Smaller campus and community memorials (e.g. CSU and other local events noted on X) ran in parallel with State Farm planning — the ceremonial arc was **not stadium-only**.

* **September 20, 2025 (reported security incident)** — An **armed man posing as law enforcement** was **arrested** at the Arizona stadium complex where the memorial was to be held the next day, per **New York Post** reporting. Logged as a **security incident**, not as a finding about the assassination itself.

* **September 21, 2025 — 11:00 a.m. MST** — The memorial service is held at **State Farm Stadium** in Glendale, Arizona, with reporting of **Desert Diamond Arena** overflow and a crowd on the order of **100,000+**. Doors were advertised around **8:00 a.m.** President [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump), the Vice President, and movement figures spoke; coverage and political framing are on [Trump support after the death](/TPUSA/Trump-Support-After-Death) and [the Trump votes speech page](/Media/Trump-Votes-Speech).

* **September 21, 2025 — speeches (public record)** — Per national coverage (e.g. ABC key takeaways): President Trump framed Kirk as a **martyr/warrior** figure; Vice President Vance called him a **hero to the United States**; Erika Kirk said he died with **"incomplete work but not with unfinished business"** and publicly **forgave** the accused. Later opinion pieces and influencers disputed whether the eulogy and production read more like **fundraising or worship spectacle** than private grief — **opinions about tone**, not legal findings.

* **September 21, 2025 — notable attendees** — In addition to the President and Vice President, coverage and clip discourse place figures including **Elon Musk** in the stadium (including widely shared VIP-section footage later disputed as "empty seats for security"). The earlier page note about a September 10 **scene eyewitness** seated with a **media badge** remains a **circumstantial oddity**, not an accusation.

* **The eulogy dispute** — Per **[@godfatherofchao](/Influencers/x/godfatherofchao)** (July 6, 2026), Owens argued Erika's eulogy read more like a fundraising appeal than a personal tribute. That is an **opinion about tone**, not a legal claim; recorded on [memorial and TPUSA succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession). A July 2026 **Napolitan News** poll summary circulated claiming a majority sided with Erika's forgiveness posture versus harder Trump-era rhetoric — **public-opinion beat only**.

* **The VIP-eyewitness question** — A man who gave prominent eyewitness interviews from the September 10 scene was reportedly seated on the main floor at the memorial with a **media badge**, after publicly doubting he could get in at all. Commentators — including, per the master file, his own teenage son — called it odd. This is **circumstantial oddity, not an accusation**; see [witnesses, security, and citizen investigators](/People/Witnesses_Security_And_CitizenInvestigators) and [witness testimony discrepancies](./witness-testimony-discrepancies).

* **The attendee-data claim (attributed, unverified)** — Citizen posts allege that a data broker connected to **[Superfeed Technologies](/TPUSA/Companies/superfeed-technologies)** geotracked memorial attendees. The claim appears in commentary tied to [Israel-related motive threads](/Israel/overview); no filing, contract, or audit has been produced, and it is recorded here only because it is repeatedly asserted.

## Succession and honors

* **September 18, 2025** — **Erika Kirk is announced as TPUSA CEO**, eight days after the killing and six days after the 55-hour speech. Kolvet stated Charlie had told him she could lead; Owens has questioned the corroboration for the underlying Aspen statement. See [the TPUSA replacement timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline) and [the TPUSA internal timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **Same-week media** — A TPUSA figure appeared on **Fox News** within hours of the killing, a speed several commentators found notable; see [the TPUSA replacement Fox interview](/Media/tpusa-replacement-fox-interview) and [successor on Fox](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/successor-on-fox).

* **October 14, 2025 — Kirk's 32nd birthday** — The **Presidential Medal of Freedom** is awarded posthumously ([page](/Media/Presidential-Medal-of-Freedom)) and a **National Day of Remembrance** is proclaimed ([page](/Media/National-Day-Remembrance)). TPUSA posts a tribute video the same day that contains September 10 drone footage — a detail that later cut against the "no drones" account, per [the drone contradiction page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cease-desist-drone-contradiction).

* **2026 — anniversary memorial culture** — Artist **Sergio Furnari** publicized a **Charlie Kirk Memorial Monument** project aimed at a **September 10, 2026** Times Square unveiling, extending the ceremonial aftermath past the stadium service.

* **Ongoing — grave and memorial access** — Debate over pilgrimage access versus family privacy recurs in commentary without any single published policy; see [the After overview](/After/overview) and [memorial and TPUSA succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession).

## Backlash and institutional exits

* **September 2025 onward** — **Visa revocations** followed online celebration posts by foreign nationals ([page](/After/Visa-Revocation-Celebrations)), and Reuters-reported **600+ workplace actions** followed Kirk-related posts, covered on [media narratives and censorship after](/After/Media_Narratives_And_Censorship_After).

* **2026** — **[Washington County Sheriff Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** — who received the call that led to [the surrender](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff) — resigned; see [the Brooksby resignation page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation) and [official resignations](/Law_Enforcement/official-resignations). Resignations happen for many ordinary reasons and none is asserted here.

* **2026** — **[UVU](/UVU/overview) President [Astrid S. Tuminez](/People/astrid-s-tuminez)** announced her resignation; recorded as a documented leadership transition on [institutional resignations](/After/Institutional_Resignations) and [the UVU institutional response](/UVU/UVU-Institutional-Response).

* **2026 — the security-detail lawsuits** — A defamation suit filed by a member of the private security detail against a commentator moved through federal court, with an unusual recusal pattern noted; see [the Brian Harpole page](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [the Candace Owens profile](/People/candace-owens).

## Open Questions

2. What **board actions** occurred between September 12 and September 18, 2025, before the CEO announcement?
3. Who issued the **media credential** that seated a scene eyewitness on the memorial floor?
4. Was attendee location data collected at the memorial by any vendor, and under what contract?
5. What were the stated reasons for the **2026 resignations** of the sheriff who took the surrender call and the university president?
6. What is the full public record on the **September 20 armed impersonator** arrest (identity, charges, disposition)?
7. What are exact AF2 casket-flight departure/arrival times if ADS-B or mission logs exist?
