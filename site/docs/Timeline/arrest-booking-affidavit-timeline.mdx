# Arrest, Booking & Probable Cause Affidavit Timeline

This timeline is anchored to **paper, not testimony**. Four documents describe the same arrest — the **Affidavit of Probable Cause**, the **Utah County inmate booking sheet**, the **police computer system arrest record**, and the **charging documents** — and they do not agree with each other on when the arrest happened. This page walks the documents in order and marks every interval where they diverge. **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted**, and every assertion below is an allegation of the State of Utah untested at trial.

## The Documents

| Document | Who produced it | What it fixes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Affidavit of Probable Cause | Agent **[Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis)**, Utah State Bureau of Investigation, signed "/S/ Davis, Brian" | The 9/10 surveillance narrative and the basis for charges — [full text page](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit) |
| Inmate Booking Sheet & PC Statement | Utah County Sheriff's Office, booking #460956 | **Arrest date 09/11/2025 10:00 PM**, arresting officer Davis |
| Police computer system record | Sworn via Utah Attorney General ORI UT018015A | **Arrest date/time 09/12/2025 04:00**, PC entered 09/12/2025 07:18 |
| Charging documents / information | [Utah County Attorney's Office](/court/prosecution-team) | Aggravated murder and related counts, filed Sept 16 |

## September 10, 2025 — What the Affidavit Asserts

* **~0829 hours** — The affidavit states investigators identified Robinson arriving on the UVU campus in a **grey Dodge Challenger**, in a maroon T-shirt, light shorts, black hat with a white logo, and light shoes.

* **~1150 hours** — A UVU surveillance camera "first captured Suspect walking across a grassy area north of Campus Drive at approximately 800 West" — note the affidavit begins its tracked sequence here, more than three hours after the 0829 arrival, with the intervening period unaddressed. Camera-by-camera detail is on [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).

* **~1153 hours** — The Suspect "paused at the top of the stairs and pulled out what appeared to be a cellphone from his right pocket," then replaced it — a detail that matters for the [cell tower and phone data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline).

* **~1202 hours** — Recorded walking on the north side of the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) and entering through the southeast corner doors.

* **~1215 / 1217 / 1222 hours** — The rooftop intervals: up the stairs, over the short wall, then running and going prone near the west edge, with the affidavit itself noting "the notable limp from previous surveillance was absent." These four minutes are unpacked on [10 Minutes Before the Shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot).

* **~1223 hours** — "Charlie Kirk was shot." The affidavit gives no second-level time, which is the root of the [12:23 versus 12:27 dispute](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **~1224 hours** — The Suspect placed "a dark colored item" on the rooftop, lowered himself to the grass, and ran north across Campus Drive; post-shot movement is on [Tyler Robinson Post-Shot Escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **Later** — Investigators "discovered a bolt action rifle wrapped in a dark colored towel," a **Mauser Model 98 .30-06** with a scope, plus engraved casings and a Converse-consistent shoe impression at the northeast rooftop edge — see [Mauser .30-06 Rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) and [Wooded Area — Gun Recovery](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery).

## September 11, 2025 — The Contested Evening

* **~6:25 PM MDT** — A defense filing quotes a video exhibit (**Bates 3996-R1/R2**) whose timestamp reads 6:25 PM and captures a full Miranda advisement, with Robinson responding that he would like counsel and that "my folks are trying to get a hold of him — I think his office is closed for the night." The date is not printed on the excerpt. Analysis on [Mirandized September 11](/court/mirandize/mirandized-sept-11-image) and the [Mirandizing timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **~7:17–7:58 PM MDT** — A second set of stairwell stills was released publicly and a Utah County press conference presented **enhanced suspect photos** and rooftop video — see [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos) and the [press conferences timeline](./press-conferences-statements-timeline).

* **~7:57 PM MDT** — The **Discord messages** attributed to Robinson appear, including the statement that he would be turning himself in. If the 6:25 PM Miranda reading occurred on this date, those messages post-date custody by roughly ninety minutes — the core of [Discord & Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline) and [Custody vs Discord Confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

* **~2004 hours (8:04 PM)** — A government document states Sheriff **[Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith)** of Utah County received a call from Sheriff **[Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** of Washington County, who had been contacted by former deputy **[Mike Mitchell](/People/mike-mitchell)**, who had been called by Robinson's father. Researchers note Smith is described as physically present at the press conference at that moment — [Miranda vs Brooksby Account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account).

* **~8:30 PM MDT (claim)** — A citizen researcher asserts Agent Davis was ordered to fly to St. George around this time, and frames the sequencing as suspicious. This is that researcher's stated opinion, not an established fact; it is presented on [Baron Coleman — Early Turn-In](/court/mirandize/baron-coleman-early-turn-in).

* **10:00 PM MDT** — The **Utah County inmate booking sheet** records **ARREST DATE 09/11/2025 10:00 PM**, arresting officer **DAVIS**, arrest agency "Other," arrest location "Outside Ut Cnty."

* **~2226 hours (10:26 PM)** — The same government narrative states Robinson "arrived at the Washington County Sheriff's Office accompanied by his parents at approximately 2226 hours," wearing a dark hat, maroon T-shirt, jeans, and white/grey Converse-style shoes. **This is 26 minutes after the booking sheet says he was already arrested.** Location detail on [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff) and [Tyler Robinson Surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender).

## September 12, 2025 — Booking and Formalization

* **01:58** — The booking sheet records the date-in entry at **09/12/2025 01:58**, roughly four hours after the 10:00 PM arrest stamp — consistent with the drive time from Hurricane to Spanish Fork, which is itself the argument for why the 10:00 PM stamp cannot be the moment Davis first arrived.

* **04:00** — The police computer system lists **Arrest Date/Time: 09/12/2025 04:00**, with SID: NONE and FBI ID: NONE. This is the time federal and state spokespeople used publicly, and it is six hours later than the booking sheet's own arrest stamp.

* **Early morning hours** — The affidavit states that "when encountered in-person by investigators in Washington County on September 12, early morning hours," Robinson was in clothing consistent with the surveillance images.

* **07:18** — **Probable Cause Entered: 09/12/2025 07:18**, Probable Cause ID **189904**, booking agency Utah County Sheriff Office, agency inmate ID **460956**, ORI UT0250000, OTN 70090584 — the electronic record of the affidavit reaching the system.

* **On or about Sept 12** — Robinson was booked into the **Utah County Jail** in Spanish Fork under restrictive housing; custody conditions are on [Utah County Jail Custody](/court/utah-county-jail-custody) and [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail).

* **Charges listed** — Utah Code **76-5-202** aggravated murder (capital/first-degree felony), **76-8-306** obstruction of justice, and **76-11-210** felony discharge of a firearm causing serious bodily injury.

## September 16, 2025 and After — Charging Documents

* **September 16, 2025** — First court appearance by video before Judge **[Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** in Provo; held without bail on aggravated murder. Venue detail on [Fourth District Court — Provo](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo).

* **September 16, 2025** — The charging documents and the affidavit of probable cause **omit the screwdriver** that FBI leadership had described publicly on national television the day before as carrying the defendant's DNA and being found on the roof. It was the only claimed physical link to the roof left out of both documents — [Confession Fabrication](/CoverUp/Confession_Fabrication) and [Kash Patel Briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) cover the shift.

* **Late 2025 onward** — Discovery volume, sealing, and Brady disputes moved through the court; see [Discovery & Brady Disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes) and the [discovery timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

* **March 27 filing** — An addendum states the Utah State Bureau of Investigation has had **28 different law enforcement agencies** produce evidence in the case, which is one measure of how large the unreleased record is.

* **July 2026** — The preliminary hearing put surveillance video, DNA, and officer testimony on the record for the first time, including testimony in which Agent Davis is reported to have said he did not arrive until **near midnight** on September 11 — directly contradicting the 10:00 PM arrest stamp bearing his name. See [Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Interval | Document A | Document B | Gap |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Time of arrest | Booking sheet: **09/11 10:00 PM** | Police computer system: **09/12 04:00** | **6 hours** |
| Arrest vs arrival at the sheriff's office | Arrested **10:00 PM** | Arrived at WCSO **10:26 PM** | Arrest precedes arrival by **26 min** |
| Arresting officer's own account | Booking sheet names **Davis** as arresting at 10:00 PM | Davis's reported hearing testimony: arrived **near midnight** | ~2 hours |
| Miranda timing | Defense exhibit timestamp **6:25 PM**, date not printed | Official framing places advisement on **9/12** after transport | Up to **~14 hours** |
| Confession sequencing | Discord messages timestamped **7:57 PM 9/11** | Official account has him not yet in custody at that time | Contested — [Custody Confession Timeline](/CoverUp/Custody_Confession_Timeline) |
| Screwdriver evidence | Described publicly as key roof-linking DNA evidence | **Absent** from both charging documents and the affidavit | Total omission |

A counterargument deserves to be stated plainly: Utah permits an **investigative hold**, so a delay between contact and formal arrest is not by itself unlawful, and clerical variation between a jail booking sheet and a statewide computer record is common. That counterargument is developed on [Investigative Hold Counterpoint](/court/mirandize/investigative-hold-counterpoint). What it does not explain is an arrest stamped 26 minutes before the arrival it describes.

## Open Questions

1. Which document controls — the booking sheet's 09/11 10:00 PM or the computer system's 09/12 04:00 — and who entered each?
2. What date is printed on the unredacted Bates 3996-R1/R2 video exhibit that carries the 6:25 PM Miranda timestamp?
3. Why does the arresting officer's name appear on a 10:00 PM arrest he reportedly testified he could not have made?
4. Where is the Washington County Sheriff's Office lobby and interview-room video from the afternoon and evening of September 11, and under what retention policy was it destroyed?
5. Why was the screwdriver, described publicly as the key roof-linking DNA evidence, omitted from both the affidavit and the charging documents?
6. What is in the roughly three-hour gap between the 08:29 campus arrival and the 11:50 start of the affidavit's tracked sequence?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The unredacted booking sheet and police computer system audit trail showing who entered each arrest time and when, the complete Washington County Sheriff's Office video from September 11 including the Bates 3996 exhibit with its date field intact, the transport and custody logs between Hurricane and Spanish Fork, and the investigative file explaining the screwdriver's disappearance from the charging documents are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
