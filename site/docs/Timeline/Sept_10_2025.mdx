# September 10, 2025 – Day-of Timeline (Reported Facts and Claims)

This is the single-day clock for **September 10, 2025**, midnight to midnight, **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT)** — the Utah clock every other page on this site references. It runs from the pre-dawn aircraft picture, through the camera sightings attributed to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) between **8:07 and 11:49 AM**, to the **12:23:30 PM** shot, the SUV run to [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Medical/Timpanogos_Facility), the death pronouncement, and the evening manhunt and flight activity. [Tyler Robinson is charged, not convicted](/court/case-overview); every claim below is labeled **reported** (documented in filings, agency statements, or mainstream coverage) or **claimed** (citizen-investigator compilation, not adjudicated).

## The Timeline

### Overnight and morning (00:00 – 11:00 AM MDT)

* **~00:49 MDT (Sept 9 into Sept 10 boundary, claimed)** — Citizen flight-trackers place the **[SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702) / [99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)** VIP departure out of [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) in the small hours around the Sept 9 date line; the day-before military layer is tracked separately on the [Fort Huachuca and military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and at [Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9).

* **~7:08 AM MDT (claimed)** — Researchers tracking the Egyptian-registered [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) say it departed Utah for Wilmington roughly five hours before the shot, a movement discussed alongside the rest of the [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada), the [SU-BTT claim page](/Suspicious/Israel/egyptian-jet-su-btt) and the [Egyptian jets at Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo) thread.

* **~8:07 AM MDT (reported)** — A **Ring** doorbell camera records a man identified in reports as Tyler Robinson walking on **S 800 W** toward campus, the first entry in the [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson) and on the [Ring device page](/cameras/Ring).

* **~8:23 AM MDT (reported)** — A **WYZE** camera on **W 880 S** captures him driving in the area, per the [WYZE camera breakdown](/cameras/WYZE) and the compiled list at [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).

* **~8:29 AM MDT (reported)** — [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) footage reportedly shows his vehicle entering campus, an anchor time that later collides with the 9:24 AM arrival claim below.

* **~9:24 AM MDT (claimed, conflicting)** — A clip circulated in July 2026 states police put the campus arrival at **9:24 AM**, roughly an hour after the 8:29 AM camera entry; the conflict is unresolved and is carried on the [pre-shot campus movements page](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).

* **~9:57 AM MDT (reported)** — Another WYZE clip shows the same individual walking on **W 880 S**, the last morning sighting before the reported clothing change tracked at [Robinson clothing changes](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/robinson-clothing-changes).

* **~10:00 AM MDT (claimed, unverified)** — The same July 2026 clip asserts the arriving man went to the **quad** and made contact with **representatives of [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview)**; no TPUSA employee has been named and no agency has confirmed it, and ordinary event-staff interaction is the baseline explanation — see [TPUSA and Tyler Robinson Chat](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat).

* **10:45 AM MDT (claimed)** — Master-file notes record that French president **[Emmanuel Macron](/People/emmanuel-macron)** called **[Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump)** that morning, a data point motive researchers pair with the [French Foreign Legion claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims) and the [foreign-leaders meeting thread](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

### Late morning (11:00 AM – 12:20 PM MDT)

* **~11:17 AM MDT, Sept 9 analog (reported, adjacent day)** — Bomb dogs logged in the [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) incident report belong to **Sept 9**, not Sept 10; the [FBI bomb-dog pattern](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs) is a separate strand and is not part of this day's clock.

* **~11:44 AM MDT (reported)** — A Ring camera records the man on **W 800 S**, now described in media summaries as wearing **jeans and a long-sleeve shirt** — an outfit different from the morning clips.

* **~11:47 AM MDT (claimed)** — ADS-B screenshots cited by aviation researchers show the U.S. Army-linked Bombardier Global 6500 **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)**, associated with the **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)** ISR program, making a **low-altitude, low-speed pass** near Utah Lake and the UVU area about 36 minutes before the shot; see [N1098L HADES over UVU](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU) and the [low-pass timing page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/n1098l-low-pass-timing).

* **~11:49 AM MDT (reported)** — Ring footage shows him again on **S 800 W**, reportedly heading back toward campus — the **last** camera sighting before the shot and the outer edge of the feasibility argument on the [roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **~12:00 – 12:20 PM MDT (reported)** — Kirk takes the stage under the event tent in the [UVU courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward); [security staffing](/Security_Team/overview) that day was **six campus officers** for a crowd the chief later described as **3,000**, with [no counter-snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) and [no ambulance on site](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/six-officers-no-ambulance).

* **12:13 – 12:23 PM MDT (claimed)** — The final ten-minute window — runner sprints, the [all-black figure](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/all-black-construction-worker), and the [stairs guy with a backpack](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/stairs-guy-backpack) — is reconstructed on the sibling page [ten minutes before the shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot).

### The shot and the first 90 seconds (12:23 PM MDT)

* **12:23:30 PM MDT (reported)** — **The shot.** Most compilations, including those built from police-audio timestamps, place it here; a competing **12:27 PM** figure appears in other summaries and is argued out on [12:23 vs 12:27 shooting time](./12-23-shooting-time) and [shot time 12:23 vs 12:27](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/shot-time-1223-vs-1227).

* **+00:00 (12:23:30, claimed)** — Acoustic analysts working the [Canon XA55](/cameras/Canon_XA55_Broadcast) [audio track](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) argue the recording contains **three separate low-frequency events**, not one; the split is examined on the [audio and mic timeline](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline) and at [Acoustic Two-Event Split](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Acoustic_Two_Event_Split).

* **+00:00 (12:23:30, claimed)** — [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) is said to call his wife [Elizabeth](/People/elizabeth-mccoy) at the instant of the shot, a call sequence disputed on [McCoy calls wife](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife).

* **12:23 PM MDT (state claim via 2026 hearing coverage)** — Commentary on the [preliminary hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) reports the State argued phone **geolocation data pings the charged defendant's device on the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof at 12:23 PM**. The underlying records have not been published, no independent analyst has examined the ping radius, and [Robinson is charged, not convicted](/court/case-overview).

* **+00:18 (12:23:48, reported)** — Security begins moving Kirk, per the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **+00:27 (12:23:57, claimed)** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is described as already in position on the rock pile filming, the subject of [Farnsworth rock pile](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile).

* **+00:25 – +00:31 (12:23:55 – 12:24:01, reported)** — Kirk is carried into and loaded into the **SUV**; the vehicle itself and its later contents are covered at [the SUV transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).

* **+00:33 (12:24:03, claimed)** — [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) is well past the SUV **without** [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) in frame — the core of the [McCoy/Neff call conflict](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict).

* **+00:42 (12:24:12, claimed)** — [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) gets into the SUV.

* **+00:52 (12:24:22, claimed)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying **"They just shot Charlie."**

* **+00:43 – +01:05 (12:24:13 – 12:24:35, reported)** — The SUV departs for the hospital; the choice of a **scoop-and-run** with no ambulance is examined at [no ambulance / scoop and run](/Suspicious/Medical/no-ambulance-scoop-and-run) and [hospital choice and transport](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport).

* **+01:06 (12:24:36, claimed)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying **"He's dead"** — roughly **38 minutes** before any surgeon is reported to have pronounced death.

### Transport, hospital, and death (12:24 – 1:15 PM MDT)

* **12:24 PM MDT (reported)** — SUV begins transport toward [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Medical/Timpanogos_Facility), about **2.6 miles** away — not the region's Level I trauma center, a routing question raised at [trauma center bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **~12:24 PM MDT onward (claimed)** — Commentators assert [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) began receiving **real-time condition updates** from the SUV en route; this is a contested inference and is not established in any filing.

* **12:26 PM MDT (reported)** — **First police report** of the shooting is logged in dispatch audio, roughly three minutes after the shot — the gap analyzed at [first police report gap](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/first-police-report-gap) and on the [dispatch and radio scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **12:26 PM MDT (claimed)** — [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) is said to have three-wayed [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) into a call; the call chain is one of the day's most disputed items and appears on the [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

* **12:29 – 12:35 PM MDT (estimated, reported)** — Kirk arrives at Timpanogos Regional Hospital; the scene inside is described at [events at hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital) and [hospital scene and EMS](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems).

* **~12:38 PM MDT (claimed, disputed)** — An early version of the **[Dairy Queen](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem)** claim placed a person said to be Robinson at **813 E 800 N, Orem** only 15 minutes after the shot; a later version moves the same photo to **6:38 PM**. Both versions circulate; see the evening entry below and [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **~12:24 – 12:48 PM MDT (claimed)** — A second **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** low pass is reported in this window, after the shot; exact times vary by source and are cataloged with the rest of the [ISR operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations) picture.

* **~12:56 – 1:13 PM MDT (claimed window)** — Citizen reconstructions build a minimum arrival-to-OR-to-pronouncement chain of **33–50 minutes**, landing death in this range; one master-file note gives **1:03:30 PM MDT** as the pronouncement. The comparison against the **Jerusalem Post** publication time is the load-bearing dispute — see [OR timeline vs Jerusalem Post](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post) and the [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

* **~1:00 PM MDT onward (claimed)** — A surgeon is reported to have been **blocked from returning to the room** by federal personnel, and hospital interior footage was reportedly seized; see [FBI hospital footage seizure](/FBI/Hospital_Footage_Seizure) and [hospital footage and NDAs](/CoverUp/Hospital_Footage_And_NDAs).

* **~1:00 PM MDT onward (claimed)** — Observers describe the emergency room as unusually **empty** and note [personnel changes](/Suspicious/Medical/empty-er-personnel-changes) around that week, including a CEO who had started weeks earlier.

### Afternoon: crowd, scene, and first custody claims

* **~12:42 PM MDT (reported)** — The first campus alert goes out roughly **19 minutes** after the shot and states **"suspect in custody"** — which was not true; the alerting failures are documented in the [GRAMA release](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) summarized at [UVU pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) and [campus security gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps).

* **~1:02 PM MDT (documented)** — President **[Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump)** posts news of the shooting on Truth Social — the first federal-level public acknowledgement, roughly **39 minutes** after the shot and before any official confirmation of death. Statement sequencing is tracked on the [press conferences and statements timeline](./press-conferences-statements-timeline).

* **~1:37 PM MDT (reported)** — UVU **closes campus** and urges evacuation — see the [UVU institutional response](/UVU/UVU-Institutional-Response).

* **~1:51 PM MDT (reported)** — A **Run-Hide-Fight** alert goes out about **88 minutes** after the shot still containing placeholder text reading **"Shooter in INSERT location"** — per the same [GRAMA documents](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings).

* **~2:01 PM MDT (reported)** — UVU reverses the evacuation instruction and tells remaining persons to **secure in place**. The close-then-secure sequence inside 25 minutes is part of the alerting-failure record at [campus security gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps).

* **~2:40 PM MDT (documented)** — Trump **confirms Charlie Kirk's death** on Truth Social. Researchers weigh this against the disputed hospital/OR clock and the [Jerusalem Post announcement question](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post) on the [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

* **~4:00 PM MDT (documented)** — Governor **[Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox)** holds a press conference calling the killing a **"political assassination."** See the [Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline).

* **Afternoon (reported)** — **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** is detained at the scene after shouting; witnesses later said they recognized him from prior TPUSA events. He was not charged in the shooting. Distraction-actor theories are collected at [patsies and distraction actors](/Killer/Patsies_Distraction_Actors), the [list of distraction people](/CoverUp/Distraction_People) and the [distraction events timeline](./distraction-events-timeline).

* **Afternoon (reported)** — **[Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi)** is publicly announced as a person in custody and released roughly **90 minutes** later with no explanation ([arrest record](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi)); researchers argue the announcement paused the search. He has not been charged.

* **Afternoon (claimed)** — The **[Mauser .30-06](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle)** is reported found in wooded ground near campus only after **a third search**: an arm-to-arm sweep found nothing, dogs found nothing, and the weapon surfaced after federal personnel directed junior officers to search again — see [gun discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence), [gun dropped location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and the [rifle chain of custody](/Law_Enforcement/rifle-chain-of-custody).

* **Afternoon (state theory via July 2026 prelim inventory)** — Attorney [Andrea Burkhart](/People/andrea-burkhart) ([@aburkhartlaw](/Influencers/x/aburkhartlaw)) publicly listed day-of items the State later argued at the [preliminary hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview): a **screwdriver** (DNA mixture discussed in court), a **sniper pad** on the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof, and **shoe impressions/scuffs** at the northeast drop corner, alongside the Mauser recovery already on this clock. Robinson is charged, not convicted.

* **Afternoon (claimed)** — An executive is described removing an **SD card** from a camera at the scene; the account and the counterargument are at [SD card removed](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed).

### Evening (5:00 PM – midnight MDT)

* **~6:38 PM MDT (claimed photo; not a state exhibit)** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and amplifiers (e.g. [@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra)) circulated a previously unseen photo of a person they identify as Robinson at the **Orem Dairy Queen**, timestamped **6:38 PM** — about six hours after the shot and roughly 15–17 minutes' drive from campus. Some commentary frames the stop as a **planted-clothing drop point** rather than proof of presence. Multiple independent summaries state the **prosecution did not introduce** that photo at the July 2026 preliminary hearing. See the [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **~7:12 PM MDT (claimed)** — Aviation trackers say the private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departed **[Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport)**, about a 10–12 minute drive from UVU. A competing version in the same master-file notes says it left **45 minutes after the assassination**; the two departure times are not reconciled.

* **~7:44 – 7:58 PM MDT (claimed)** — The same trackers say **N888KG disabled its ADS-B transponder** roughly 30–46 minutes into the flight, going dark over the Arizona/Nevada corridor. Aviation commentators note disabling ADS-B mid-flight is not routine; no agency has publicly described this flight as connected to the shooting.

* **~8:30 – 9:00 PM MDT (claimed)** — N888KG is said to reappear on public radar reversing course and later lands back at Provo, with **no publicly filed flight plan**. A separate strand claims a landing at **Wendover** and a leg to **Page, Arizona**; the accounts conflict and are held together at [other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline) and [foreign flight records](/CoverUp/Foreign_Flight_Records).

* **~8:55–9:47 PM MDT (claimed)** — Citizen investigators ([@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen-2) and others) say a **Panguitch, Utah** steakhouse receipt (Cowboy’s / Smokehouse Grill) and staff place a person identified as Robinson dining roughly three hours after the DQ photo and ~8–9 hours after the shot, on the southbound route toward Washington County. The stop is **not** part of the official charging narrative summarized publicly; geography debates compare it to alleged late-night Orem activity. See [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **~11:00 PM MDT onward (alleged texts; state theory)** — Preliminary-hearing inventories and TPUSA-adjacent summaries of Agent Hull testimony ([@BlakeSNeff](/Influencers/x/blakesneff)) describe a longer **text thread with roommate [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)** — the device examined on [Lance Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) — in which the account attributed to Robinson allegedly admitted the shooting, said the rifle was left **wrapped in a towel in a bush**, and discussed a failed retrieval — plus a note photographed under a keyboard and later described as partially burnt. Authenticity and authorship remain contested by citizen investigators who date custody earlier. See [Tyler: Sept 8–13](./Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept).

* **Evening (claimed)** — A further flight, **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)**, is described running Scottsdale to Provo and back out via Santa Barbara the same day — the [Sept 10 Scottsdale-Provo turnaround claim](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n560tw-turnaround) — and it is one of the day's least-examined aircraft entries.

* **Evening (reported)** — The **manhunt** framing begins. Officials later described a **"33-hour manhunt"** ending Sept 11; researchers who date custody earlier dispute that arithmetic on the [Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline), in the [Patel press briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) and at [Miranda vs Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account).

* **Evening into midnight (claimed)** — Some early summaries place an arrest **around midnight** at a nearby apartment; official filings place the surrender on **Sept 11**. The surrender chain is on [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) and [Sept 11 surrender and custody](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody).

* **~12:30–12:38 AM Sept 11 (hearing testimony, disputed occupancy)** — [Agent David Hull](/People/david-hull) testimony clips (@JLRINVESTIGATES, [@BlakeSNeff](/Influencers/x/blakesneff)) and state inventories describe a **gray Dodge Challenger** near the gun-recovery neighborhood (Noble Ring, W 925 / Campus Drive). An officer reportedly logged the plate to Robinson. Defense-focused commentary notes a homeowner report of a **bald driver and three passengers**, not a confirmed solo Robinson exit. This overnight bridge is expanded on [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) and [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **Same evening (reported)** — Within roughly one to four hours of the death being reported, a successor spokesman was on national television; the succession fight is tracked at [Erika CEO succession disputed](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed) and the [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

## Open Questions

1. Which single authenticated clock — camera EXIF, CAD dispatch log, or hospital intake record — is the site of record for the 12:23 versus 12:27 dispute?
2. Why does the campus arrival time move by roughly an hour between the UVU camera timestamp and the police account repeated in 2026?
3. What is the certified time the surgeon pronounced death, and how does it compare to the first published death report?
4. Was the N888KG transponder gap an equipment issue, an authorized suppression, or something else — and who were the passengers?
5. Why did the recovered rifle surface only on a third search, and what is the unbroken chain of custody from discovery forward?
6. Who took the SD card at the scene, what was on it, and where is it now?
7. Can the Panguitch steakhouse receipt window and the ~12:30 AM Campus Drive / Noble Ring Challenger contact both be true for the same driver?
8. Were the ~11:00 PM Twiggs texts authenticated as originating from Robinson’s device, and when was that phone last in his physical control?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The certified death certificate and operating-room record with the exact pronouncement time, the full synchronized UVU camera set with authenticated timecodes, the complete CAD dispatch log for September 10, the N1098L HADES mission orders and flight manifest, the N888KG passenger list and the technical record of its ADS-B gap, the seized hospital interior footage, and the chain-of-custody file for the recovered rifle and the removed SD card are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
