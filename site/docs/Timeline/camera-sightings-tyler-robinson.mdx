# Surveillance Cameras: Tyler Robinson Sightings Timeline

This page traverses **one evidence class**: every timestamped camera frame that investigators or commentators have attributed to **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** across September 10–11, 2025. Neighborhood [Ring](/cameras/Ring) and [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE) doorbells, [UVU campus surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance), the stairwell and rooftop stills, merchant CCTV, and — just as important — the **intervals where a camera should exist and no footage has been released**.

**Robinson is charged, not convicted.** Cameras record movement, not identity: every "Robinson" below is an attribution made by a human reading footage, and on several frames that attribution is [actively disputed](/cameras/Tyler_Placeholder).

## The Timeline

### Morning — neighborhood cameras (8:07 to 9:57 AM MDT)

* **8:07 AM MDT** — A **[Ring](/cameras/Ring) doorbell** on **S 800 W** captures a figure walking toward campus in a **maroon shirt and light-colored shorts**; one widely circulated version of this clip, sourced to TMZ, describes the shirt as "plum" and the shorts as dark, an early wardrobe discrepancy carried through the whole [camera chain](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).

* **8:23 AM MDT** — A **[WYZE](/cameras/WYZE) camera** on **W 880 S** captures the figure **driving**, one of only two vehicle frames in the entire morning sequence — the other being the campus arrival six minutes later, per the [vehicle chronology](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).

* **8:29 AM MDT** — **[UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance)** records arrival on campus in a **grey Dodge Challenger**, wearing a maroon t-shirt, light shorts, light shoes, and a **black hat with a white logo**. The probable-cause affidavit cites this frame; notably it records **no backpack** and **never states when the vehicle left the lot** — see [Affidavit of Probable Cause](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause).

* **9:57 AM MDT** — A **[WYZE](/cameras/WYZE) camera** on **W 880 S** captures the figure again, now **on foot**. This is the last frame before the reported clothing change.

* **~10:00 to 11:44 AM MDT — no released footage** — Roughly **one hour and forty-five minutes** with no publicly released camera frame, the interval in which the [clothing change](/Tyler_Robinson/clothing-change-agent-david-hull) must have occurred. Where it happened, and whether any camera recorded it, is not in the public record — see the [gaps timeline](./open-questions-gaps-timeline). Citizen posts in July 2026 also allege a mid-morning **Chick-fil-A on campus** stop with **no released stills** despite dense cameras — still only an allegation.

### Late morning — the return (11:44 to 11:53 AM MDT)

* **11:44 AM MDT** — A **[Ring](/cameras/Ring)** camera on **W 800 S** captures a figure now in **jeans and a long-sleeve shirt**, the first frame on the far side of the wardrobe seam.

* **11:49 AM MDT** — A **[Ring](/cameras/Ring)** camera on **S 800 W** captures the figure walking toward campus again. Citizen researchers note this is the same street and the same approach direction as the 8:07 frame, and that **[Phil Lyman's](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman) car was reportedly parked at that house from 11:30 AM** — a coincidence the site records without asserting meaning.

* **~11:50 AM MDT** — The **first UVU surveillance frame** the affidavit uses: a figure crossing a **grassy area north of Campus Drive at roughly 800 West**, in a **black long-sleeve shirt with an American flag and eagle**, dark jeans, dark cap, large sunglasses, dark backpack, walking with a **stiff right leg** and a pronounced limp.

* **11:53 AM MDT** — The figure **pauses at the top of the tunnel stairs**, removes what appears to be a **cellphone** from the right pocket with the right hand, and replaces it. This is the most-analyzed frame in the sequence because it is one of the few showing hand detail — and because a phone visible that clearly in a tight pocket is used in the [Backpack Rifle Impossibility](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility) argument.

### Midday — the Losee Center sequence (12:02 to 12:23 PM MDT)

* **12:02 PM MDT** — UVU cameras record the figure on the **north side of the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)**, same gait, entering the building through the **southeast corner doors**.

* **12:15 PM MDT** — The figure is observed **walking up the stairs** to the common area adjacent to the Losee Center — the frames later released publicly as the "**stairwell photos**," and the frames [Kash Patel described on Fox News](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) as "**enhanced**," which the defense argues means they are not fair and accurate representations of the original scene: see [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/CoverUp/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).

* **12:17 PM MDT** — The figure **climbs the short concrete wall** onto the Losee Center roof and crouches on the north side of it, out of most camera lines.

* **12:22 PM MDT** — The figure **stands and runs across the rooftop** — and the affidavit itself notes that "**the notable limp from previous surveillance was absent**." That single sentence is the strongest official-document support for the [Stairs Guy identity gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) argument that the morning figure and the rooftop figure may not be the same person.

* **12:22 PM MDT** — The figure **lies prone** near the west edge of the roof facing the courtyard, in the [shooting position](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) the state's case is built on.

* **12:23 PM MDT** — **The shot.** Surveillance stills at **12:23:29, 12:23:34, 12:23:45, and 12:23:54** trace movement from the shooting position to the **northeast corner** of the Losee Center — about **25 seconds** across the roof, per frame-by-frame work summarized on the [minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute). Some media timelines still use **12:27 PM** for the shot; see [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time). Critically, these are **posture/movement stills around the shot window** — they are **not** universally accepted as a clear public video of the trigger pull itself (see July 2026 prosecutor colloquy below).

* **12:23–12:24 PM MDT** — The figure **places a dark item on the roof**, lowers himself off the northeast edge onto the grass, and runs north — the sequence at the center of the [roof-jumping feasibility dispute](./roof-jumping-timeline) and the [post-shot escape timeline](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **12:24 PM MDT** — The figure **crosses Campus Drive** northbound carrying an unidentified item, and the released footage ends. The [rifle was recovered](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) in the wooded area beyond, but **no camera frame shows the item being placed there**.

### Afternoon and evening — merchant cameras and overnight gap

* **~12:25 PM to 6:38 PM MDT — no released footage** — Just over **six hours** with no publicly released frame of the figure anywhere, despite the density of cameras between campus and Orem; see [UVU HD Footage Withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld).

* **6:38 PM MDT** — A photo released by **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** reportedly shows Robinson at a **[Dairy Queen in Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem)** (813 E 800 N), about **17 minutes from campus**, appearing calm, in a maroon shirt, blue jeans, hat, and sunglasses. The clothing here resembles the **morning** wardrobe, not the rooftop wardrobe. **Authenticity later becomes a two-way public fight** (July 2026 — below).

* **8:55 to 9:47 PM MDT (contested)** — A circulated **Cowboy's Smokehouse receipt** (Check 211) is dated 9/10/25 with an order time of 8:55 PM and card payment at 9:47 PM; the poster claims wait staff identified the customer as Robinson and that the location is "3-plus hours from UVU." The receipt is **unauthenticated**, the location is unconfirmed, and the drive-time claim is the poster's — see [Cowboy's Smokehouse Receipt](/Tyler_Robinson/cowboys-smokehouse-receipt). Hard drive-time math later used online argues **Orem DQ 6:38 PM → Panguitch Cowboy's ~8:55 PM** cannot both be true without extreme speeds (~200+ miles / ~3h+ drive vs ~2h17m window).

* **~11:00 PM MDT, Sept 10, 2025 (prosecution timeline as described at prelim)** — According to hearing summaries recirculated online, texts attributed to the charged subject describe remaining near campus to recover a rifle, then abandoning it under police presence ("alright im gonna have to leave it…"). Treat message content as **contested evidence**, not established fact — see [cell-tower and phone-data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline).

* **~12:30–1:30 AM MDT, Sept 11, 2025 (hearing claim)** — UVU or nearby cameras reportedly capture a vehicle **attempting to re-enter a campus road**, with a brief interaction with an officer on security detail, before departure south. KSL reported **Ring doorbell** footage of late-night residential parking introduced at the prelim. These frames are **not yet itemized** on the public affidavit-era map on this page; they partially fill the post-6:38 gap. See also [post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

### September 11 — travel and the release problem

* **7:15–7:17 AM MDT, September 11** — **Maverik gas station CCTV** in **[Cedar City](/Locations/Cedar_City_Maverik)** captures the grey Dodge Challenger; the driver pays at the pump by credit card and **does not approach any trash cans**, contradicting the early alert to check bins for dumped evidence — see [Merchant and Travel CCTV](/cameras/Merchant_And_Travel_CCTV).

* **~7:17 AM to evening, September 11 — no released footage** — The entire day in southern Utah is uncovered by any released camera frame, up to the surrender described on the [custody and surrender timeline](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline).

* **7:17 PM Central / 6:17 PM MDT, September 11** — The **second set of stairwell photos** is released to the public. [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline) argues Robinson was **already in custody and being Mirandized at 6:25 PM MDT**, making a public "help us find him" release logically inverted — see [Custody Timeline vs Fake Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession).

* **7:58 PM MDT, September 11** — At the Utah County press conference, officials release the "**enhanced**" stairwell stills and a **video of a tiny figure** running on the Losee Center roof. Critics including Coleman argue the rooftop figure is too few pixels for a parent to identify a child from — the identification the FBI's account depends on; see [Rooftop Video Trimmed](/CoverUp/Rooftop_Video_Trimmed).

* **Ongoing** — The released suspect video is repeatedly described by investigators as "**1973-quality VHS**" — resolution so low that gait, build, and clothing detail cannot be independently checked. UVU is documented to hold **higher-definition footage** that has not been released: [UVU HD Footage Withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld). Separately, LE **bodycam** non-capture / non-release in the shooting window is tracked on [bodycam GRAMA](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage) and [UVU security timeline](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline). The broader **GRAMA** redaction fight over UVU's own records is at [UVU records redacted](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) and [UVU GRAMA pre-shooting warnings](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings).

### July 2026 preliminary hearing — new video, no shot tape, authenticity wars

* **July 7, 2026 (approx.)** — Prosecutors play **previously unreleased campus surveillance** of the charged suspect at the preliminary hearing. Fox and other reporters describe an officer narrating a **limp** and a climb onto the building believed to be the sniper position. Viral clips (@CinemaShogun, @MattFinnFNC, @MJTruthUltra and others) frame the package as answering influencers who said "Tyler was never there." **Identification remains an allegation by the State**; Robinson is **charged, not convicted**.

* **July 2026 (court colloquy as reported)** — When **[Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** asked whether there was footage of the shooting itself, a prosecutor is quoted answering **"There is not, Judge."** That admission sits in tension with some media claims (e.g. [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec) on [the Megyn Kelly Show](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly) that he had seen surveillance of Robinson **taking the shot**). Attribute both; the court colloquy is the cleaner negative-evidence clock for this page.

* **July 2026 (testimony summaries)** — SBI Agent **[David Hull](/People/david-hull)** is widely described as the primary sworn identifier of the campus video subject by appearance, clothing, and timeline (including Losee climb ~12:22, prone, then flee). Useful for the Conflicts table: **who** is doing the identification under oath.

* **July 2026 (technical claim)** — Posters assert UVU analysis used **BriefCam** video analytics (vendor HQ often described as Israel) to trace the subject across cameras. Confirm against discovery before elevating beyond **"claimed"**; corporate HQ ≠ state involvement — see also [Israeli tech in the investigation](/Israel/israeli-tech-in-investigation) and [cell/phone data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline).

* **July 17–18, 2026** — Authenticity of the **6:38 PM Dairy Queen** image becomes a public fight: @DiligentDenizen and @BasedSamParker argue **shoe/hat/jeans mismatches** (e.g. white shoes vs grey shoes on 9/10 frames), **prosecutor omission** from the official timeline, and that the still may **not be Sept 10**; others treat it as proof the charged suspect was still local hours after the shot. Some claim the DQ still was leaked to Candace to **discredit** the Panguitch / Cowboy's alibi thread. See [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **July 17–19, 2026** — Competing staff-ID vs owner-denial accounts around **Cowboy's Smokehouse** peak; @DiligentDenizen amplifies an interview claiming staff identified Tyler and that **neither state nor defense** contacted them after they said they turned information into the FBI. Drive-time math vs DQ remains the sharpest internal inconsistency on this page.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 8:07 clothing | Maroon shirt, light shorts (affidavit-aligned compilations) | Plum shirt, dark shorts, backpack visible (TMZ-sourced description) |
| Backpack at 8:29 | Affidavit records no backpack at campus arrival | Later frames show a full dark backpack; when it was acquired is unrecorded |
| Same person all day | One continuous subject across all frames; Hull prelim narration | The morning "waif-like" figure and the afternoon "linebacker-shouldered" figure look different (Coleman) |
| The limp | Distinctive stiff-right-leg gait identifies the subject; hearing packages re-emphasize limp | The affidavit itself says the limp is absent on the rooftop run |
| Stairwell photos | Legitimate enhancement for clarity | "Enhanced" means altered; visible overlays and non-anatomical facial lines (Coleman, defense argument) |
| Photo release timing | Standard manhunt procedure | Released after the suspect was already Mirandized and in custody |
| Footage of the shot itself | Some media personalities claim they saw "taking the shot" tape | Prosecutor to Judge Graf (as reported): **"There is not, Judge"** |
| July 2026 "never-before-seen" campus package | Proves presence on campus / climb sequence | Still LE attribution of identity; public may still lack HD exports |
| 6:38 PM Dairy Queen | Places Robinson in Orem hours after the shot (Owens release) | Shoe/hat/jeans mismatches; wrong date; prosecutor omitted it (Denizen / Parker, July 2026) |
| Cowboy's Smokehouse receipt | Potential alibi with a witness identification | Unauthenticated receipt; drive-time vs DQ impossible; owner/staff accounts conflict |

## Open Questions

1. Where are the **original camera exports** — Ring and WYZE cloud files, UVU DVR files — with device clocks and hash values, rather than re-posted social clips?
2. When did the **grey Dodge Challenger leave the UVU parking lot**? The affidavit records the arrival and never the departure.
3. What camera, if any, covers the **9:57 to 11:44 AM** window in which the clothing change occurred — including any **Chick-fil-A** or merchant stop?
4. Why does the affidavit note the **limp disappearing** on the roof without resolving it?
5. What exactly was **"enhanced"** in the stairwell stills, by what process, and does an unaltered original still exist?
6. Why were **suspect photos released after** the subject was in custody, and who authorized that release?
7. Does UVU's **high-definition footage** — including any better copy shown in the July 2026 prelim — show the rooftop sequence at a resolution that permits independent identification?
8. What is the chain of custody and date stamp for the **Dairy Queen** still, and can **DQ and Cowboy's** both be true on drive-time math?
9. Will the **overnight (~12:30–1:30 AM)** vehicle/Ring frames from the prelim be released with hashes and officer IDs?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The original unenhanced UVU surveillance exports with device timestamps and chain-of-custody logs, the raw Ring and WYZE cloud files for all six morning sightings, the full high-definition rooftop and stairwell footage UVU has acknowledged holding, the unreleased footage covering the 9:57 to 11:44 AM clothing-change window and the six-hour afternoon gap, and the technical record of what was done to the "enhanced" stairwell images and by whom are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
