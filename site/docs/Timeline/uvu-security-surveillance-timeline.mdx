# UVU Campus Security, Surveillance & Camera Timeline

This is the **institutional layer** of September 10, 2025: what [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) was told before the event, what it did and did not deploy, which cameras were recording, and how footage was released, trimmed, or withheld afterward. It is not a suspect timeline — for the movements attributed to the accused see [the pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus). Nothing here establishes wrongdoing by any UVU employee; the record is drawn from GRAMA releases, sworn hearing testimony, and attributed public commentary.

## Before the event: planning and warnings

* **September 3, 2025** — **[Orem Police Department](/Law_Enforcement/orem-police-response)** emails UVU the Charlie Kirk ticket link, subject line "Charlie Kirk Tickets," per the **Daily Caller News Foundation** GRAMA production. After the shooting Orem PD publicly said it was "not involved," even though UVU pays Orem PD annually for a joint **Public Order Unit** built for exactly this kind of event. The unit was never activated — see [the UVU GRAMA records page](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **September 8, 2025 (claim)** — A UVU maintenance eyewitness reported seeing a crew testing non-standard **drone technology** near campus engineering buildings and described it as "not normal." This is an unverified single-witness account logged alongside [the UVU no-drones dispute](/Drones/UVU_Security_No_Drones) and [the Provo counter-UAS thread](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS).

* **September 9, 2025 — 2:23 PM MDT** — An email moves through **UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)'s** communication chain with the subject line "FYI," reading: "He wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk. His phone number is [REDACTED]." The sender's name and number are redacted, and whether the message was delivered is not documented anywhere in the 112 pages — see [pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **September 9, 2025 — 10:35 PM MDT** — Document **UVU 25-330:0023** records that **two unidentified men** came to UVU offices asking to speak with police about **"a plan."** Staff escalated it to the **university cabinet**, the president's office, per [the GRAMA warnings page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings).

* **September 9, 2025 — 11:19 PM MDT** — Forty-four minutes later someone replies in the same chain that the two men "are NOT from a senator's office. They are GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did!" **Nobody in the chain asks what the plan was.** Both men's names remain redacted under Utah exemptions for "security of public property" and "privacy" — see [the UVU administration response](/UVU/UVU-Administration-Response).

* **Pre-event posture (as later documented)** — Six campus police officers, about a quarter of the department, were assigned to a crowd the chief later described as roughly 3,000. Faculty had never been trained on active-shooter protocols, no ambulance was staged on site, and no outside law enforcement was coordinated — see [six officers and no ambulance](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/six-officers-no-ambulance) and [campus security gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps).

* **Rooftops and counter-snipers** — No counter-sniper element was posted and nearby rooftops were reportedly not swept, a decision examined on [no counter-snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) and [perimeter and rooftop failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures).

* **Harpole–Long text exchange (later public)** — Per **[Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole)** of the private detail and screenshots circulated from his **Shawn Ryan Show** interview (~Nov 2025), he texted **UVU Police Chief Jeff Long** about **student-accessible roofs** near the setup and asked for controlled access or one of his people on the roof. Long replied to the effect of **"I got you covered."** Harpole later said rooftops were **not** secured as he understood. UVU has pointed to crowd size exceeding estimates and a six-officer allocation. Analytical replies often frame private detail as responsible for **stage/close protection** and campus LE for **distant roofs after warning** — a follow-through dispute, not an adjudicated finding. Campus Safety Magazine and Salt Lake Tribune–style coverage of the security-chief critique recirculated into 2026.

* **Competing staffing claim (2026)** — **[@brandisiciliani](/Influencers/x/brandisiciliani)** reported an anonymous source saying **local officers offered** extra personnel and rooftop surveillance before the event and were told the detail **already had everything covered** — the reverse of Harpole's public narrative. This is an **uncorroborated second-hand claim**; both sides should be shown without asserting that either party lied.

## Day of: the camera picture

* **Morning, September 10, 2025** — UVU's institutional CCTV network records the parking, tunnel, garage-stairwell, and building-edge angles later stitched into the state's narrative; the camera map is on [the UVU surveillance page](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance).

* **Pre-event, September 10** — [TPUSA production](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage) ran its own broadcast cameras including a **[Canon XA55](/cameras/Canon_XA55_Broadcast)** (whose [audio track](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) drives the acoustic dispute), and — per a TPUSA birthday tribute video posted **October 14, 2025** — at least one **drone shot** overhead, which citizen investigators contrast with the "campus police banned drones" explanation. See [the TPUSA production drone page](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone) and [the cease-and-desist drone contradiction](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cease-desist-drone-contradiction).

* **12:15–12:22 PM MDT** — UVU cameras record a figure crossing a railing from a **public walkway** onto the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** roof, about **430 feet** from the stage — the sightline at the center of the whole security question. See [the Losee Center page](/UVU/Losee_Center) and [the UVU campus maps](/UVU/maps).

* **12:23 PM MDT — the shot.** By the affidavit's own narration, the campus footage **"does not capture the act of firing."** For the second-by-second reconstruction see [the minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute) and [the shooting-time dispute](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **12:23:34 PM MDT (release start point, disputed)** — The rooftop-view clip that was eventually released begins at 12:23:34, after a running figure is already well away from the alleged perch. Critics call the start point a deliberate trim; defenders note briefing clips are routinely cut to the segment officials consider relevant. Both readings are laid out on [Rooftop Video Trimmed](/CoverUp/Rooftop_Video_Trimmed).

* **~12:42 PM MDT (19 minutes post-shot)** — UVU's first campus alert falsely states **"suspect in custody."** Roughly 10,000 students stayed on campus believing it was safe — documented in [the GRAMA record](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **~1:51 PM MDT (88 minutes post-shot)** — The **Run-Hide-Fight** alert goes out still carrying **placeholder text**: "Shooter in [INSERT location]." See [pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) and [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025).

* **Afternoon, September 10** — An SD card is reportedly removed from event production equipment by a TPUSA executive who, per the master file, had never attended a university event before or after; see [SD card removed](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed) and [executive SD cards](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/executive-sd-cards). This is an attributed claim about a living person's presence, not an accusation of any crime.

* **Afternoon, September 10** — Separately, hospital security-camera footage handling at [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) is disputed on [hospital security cameras removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed) and [the FBI hospital-footage seizure page](/FBI/Hospital_Footage_Seizure); why the regional [trauma center was bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed) is argued separately.

## After: release, withholding, and the scene itself

* **September 11, 2025 — 7:55 PM MDT** — The FBI releases person-of-interest photos (hat, sunglasses, long-sleeve black shirt, backpack) plus video of a person crossing the roof and walking off campus. The stills were later described as **"enhanced"** — see [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos) and [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings).

* **September 14, 2025** — Contractor **[Daniel Merrell](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah)** of [Hardscape Specialties](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties) states he was called by men working for the State of Utah to install **pavers over the courtyard** where Kirk was shot. Grass at the shooting spot was excavated and covered. See [courtyard paved over](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/courtyard-paved-over), [the pavers page](/UVU/Pavers), [the paving timeline](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14), and [FBI crime-scene paving](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving). Merrell's account of who authorized the urgency is **his statement**, not a documented order.

* **Fall 2025 onward — the release posture** — Only compressed, re-encoded derivatives of campus footage have appeared publicly; original DVR exports with embedded per-frame timestamps, and the tunnel, rooftop, and building-edge angles referenced but never shown, remain unreleased. See [UVU HD footage withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld) and [selective 4K release](/Suspicious/FBI/selective-4k-release).

* **~November 2025 — behind-tent GRAMA denial** — **[@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** reported that UVU **denied** a GRAMA request for the camera feed **from behind Charlie's tent** — the angle that would show post-shot actions near the stage area involving staff such as [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), and [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth). The denial cited public-safety, enforcement-proceeding, and fair-trial concerns. This is a **specific denied feed**, not only the generic "HD withheld" complaint — [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **December 16, 2025** — Judge **Tony Graf** issues a broad **gag order on his own motion**, which citizen investigators argue further narrows what campus witnesses and counsel can say about the security and camera record. See [gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and [the judge's page](/court/judge-tony-graf).

* **April 15, 2026** — **[@danksterintel](/Influencers/x/danksterintel)** publishes the **Daily Caller News Foundation** GRAMA production: **50 documents, 112 pages**, containing the September 9 warnings, the Orem PD ticket email, and the alert failures. UVU acknowledges **additional documents exist** and declines to release them. See [the UVU GRAMA records page](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **July 2026 — Judicial Watch clean-up docs** — **@JudicialWatch** released university documents on the **clean-up of the assassination site**, overlapping the facilities chronology on the [Utah officials / Cox timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline) (Fri hose-down narrative → deeper excavate → Sunday pavers). Harpole separately said the paving speed looked suspicious and urged FOIA on who ordered the tear-up while rejecting tunnel/trap-door theories.

* **July 2026 — Bagley roof response** — Former UVU officer **[Chris Bagley](/People/chris-bagley)** (later Spanish Fork PD) testified he responded to the Losee Center roof, confirmed a **clear line of sight** from the far south edge to the courtyard tent, and located a **red-and-black screwdriver** on the rooftop gravel later woven into the state's evidence narrative — see [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence) and [Bagley's bodycam gap on the roof](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam).

* **July 2026 — preliminary hearing testimony (Hull)** — State agent **[David Hull](/People/david-hull)** walks the court through UVU surveillance covering the parking structure, courtyard, and Losee Center roof, plus chain of custody and the rooftop search; see [Preliminary Hearing Day 2](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_2) and [the Hull clothing-change page](/Tyler_Robinson/clothing-change-agent-david-hull). Coverage describes **enhanced / composite** material presented in court; the judge reportedly **restricted some zoomed versions**, and native full DVR exports remain largely unreleased to the public. Influencer claims of "undeniable HD of the shot itself" are disputed (affidavit still: footage **"does not capture the act of firing"**).

* **July 2026 — the abandoned-items testimony** — **Sergeant Jennifer Faumuina** of UVU Public Safety testified that officers recovered a **backpack, jacket, and gloves** along the route away from the Losee Center, near the **Fulton Library**, that the items went to the FBI for DNA analysis, and that testing **stopped after the items were linked to Kirk security personnel** and returned (per hearing coverage). Asked who ordered the stop, she testified she could not clearly recall. See [discovery withholding](/Suspicious/FBI/discovery-withholding) and [discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes).

* **Ongoing** — A **rooftop eyewitness** who has not been called to testify is tracked on [the uncalled roof eyewitness page](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/uncalled-roof-eyewitness), and the fight over the rooftop video itself on [withholding rooftop video](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/withholding-rooftop-video).

* **2026 — institutional aftermath** — UVU President **[Astrid S. Tuminez](/People/astrid-s-tuminez)** announced her resignation; the site records this as a documented leadership transition and does **not** assert any connection to the events above. See [the UVU institutional response](/UVU/UVU-Institutional-Response) and [institutional resignations](/After/Institutional_Resignations).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | One account | The competing account |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drones over the event | Campus police banned drones, per the explanation given after the shooting | A TPUSA-posted tribute video appears to contain a drone shot from the event ([page](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone)) |
| Rooftop responsibility | Harpole texts Long about roofs; Long: **"I got you covered"**; Harpole says roofs not secured ([page](/Security_Team/brian-harpole)) | UVU points to crowd size / six officers; anonymous reverse claim says locals offered help and were told security already had coverage ([page](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps)) |
| Camera quality | Released stills were "enhanced" because source imagery was poor; judge limited some zoomed court versions | UVU runs an institutional HD system; native exports and the **behind-tent** feed remain unreleased / GRAMA-denied ([page](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld)) |
| The 12:23:34 clip start | A routine editorial trim to the relevant segment | A start point chosen after the running begins, hiding the decisive seconds ([page](/CoverUp/Rooftop_Video_Trimmed)) |
| Orem PD involvement | Orem PD said it was "not involved" | Orem PD emailed UVU the ticket link on Sept 3 and is paid for a joint Public Order Unit ([page](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records)) |
| Paving the courtyard | Campus restoration after a traumatic event | Excavation of a crime scene four days after the killing ([page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/courtyard-paved-over)) |
| Abandoned-items testing | Items later linked to **security detail** and returned; testing stopped | Testing stopped without a clearly recalled decision-maker ([page](/Suspicious/FBI/discovery-withholding)) |

## Open Questions

1. Who were the **two men** at 10:35 PM on September 9, and what was the "plan" they wanted to describe to police?
2. Why was the joint UVU–Orem **Public Order Unit** never activated for an event both agencies knew about a week in advance?
3. How many **UVU cameras** recorded the Losee Center roof and its approaches, and how many of those feeds have been shown to anyone outside the prosecution?
4. Who authorized the **19-minute "suspect in custody"** alert, and on what information?
5. Who decided to **stop DNA testing** the backpack, jacket, and gloves recovered near the Fulton Library?
6. What is in the **additional GRAMA documents** UVU has acknowledged exist but declined to release?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The unredacted September 9 GRAMA communications naming the two men and the person who wanted a message delivered to Charlie Kirk, the full UVU camera inventory and original DVR exports for September 10, the untrimmed rooftop-view files with chain-of-custody logs, the internal decision record for the 19-minute and 88-minute campus alerts, the authorization chain for the September 14 courtyard paving, and the order to stop testing the recovered backpack, jacket, and gloves are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
