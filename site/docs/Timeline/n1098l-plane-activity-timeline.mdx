# N1098L (HADES) Plane Activity Timeline — Pre & Post Shot

This is a single-aircraft chronology. **N1098L** is a Bombardier Global 6500 flown for the US Army's **HADES** program (High Accuracy Detection and Exploitation System), registered to **LASAI Aviation II LLC**, and it is reported to have made two unusually low, unusually slow passes within a few miles of [UVU](/UVU/overview) on September 10, 2025 — one before the shot and one after it. Everything here is aircraft-tracking data and the arguments built on it; the aircraft profile is on [N1098L Spy Plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview), and every other tail number is on [Other Planes Activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline).

## Before September 10, 2025

* **2021** — LASAI Aviation, the operator of record, came under **Leidos** control according to corporate-history research summarized on [LASAI Aviation II LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/lasai-aviation-ii-llc); the same operator's aircraft were reported circling Washington DC on and immediately after January 6, 2021.

* **Prior registration** — The airframe reportedly carried the tail number **N911PV** before being re-registered as N1098L, a change researchers flag when reconstructing older track history — see [ISR Program Context](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

* **2024–2025 (program context)** — Open trade reporting described Army plans to test **air-launched drones from a Global Express business jet**, the same airframe family as the HADES aircraft; that context is the basis for the hypotheses on [N1098L Air-Launched Drones](/Drones/N1098L_Air_Launched_Drones) and [Switchblade & DARPA Drone Claims](/Drones/Switchblade_DARPA_Claims).

* **Program leadership** — [Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell) is identified in the master file as responsible for the HADES program, run out of **Biggs Army Airfield** in El Paso, Texas — the field the September 10 flight is said to have departed, profiled on [Biggs Army Airfield (KBIF)](/Planes/Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield).

* **August 8, 2025** — Researchers report a spike of **searches for "N1098L" from Israeli IP ranges**, 32 days before the shooting; the method and its limits are discussed on [Israeli Search Patterns (Claims)](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims) and [Planes Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns). This is a Google Trends inference, not a subpoenaed record.

## September 10, 2025 — The Two Passes

{/* Times below are Utah local (MDT) unless marked UTC. Sources disagree.
    Both versions are shown deliberately — see the conflicts table. */}

* **~07:48 MDT** — One tracking summary states the aircraft's **first and only flight of the day** departed **Biggs Army Airfield (KBIF)**, El Paso, and arrived at **Glacier Park International (KGPI)**, Kalispell, Montana at **10:38 MDT** — a leg that, taken alone, would not put the jet over Utah Valley at all.

* **~09:16 MDT / 15:16:01 UTC** — A second tracking account places the **first low pass** here: a descent to roughly **650 ft** at low speed near Utah Lake, described by researchers as consistent with a release, roughly three hours before the shot.

* **~11:47 MDT** — A third account places the first low pass at **11:47 AM at about 600 ft AGL**, still roughly two miles from campus. The 09:16 and 11:47 versions cannot both describe the same event, and this page does not choose between them — see the conflicts table.

* **~11:47 AM–12:23 PM MDT** — In every version the jet climbs back toward high altitude (reported cruise near 41,000 ft) between the two passes, which is why the low segments stand out so sharply on an altitude ladder plot.

* **12:23 PM MDT** — Kirk was shot in the [UVU courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward); the ten minutes leading to that second are on [10 Minutes Before the Shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot).

* **~12:24 PM MDT** — One account places the **second low pass** here — about **335 ft AGL at 129 knots**, roughly one minute after the shot. This is the version quoted inside the "drone retrieval training" explanation.

* **~12:48 PM MDT / 18:48 UTC** — A competing account places the second pass at **12:48 PM**, at about **203–204 ft** and **116–118 knots** (near stall speed for the type), lasting on the order of 28 seconds and roughly 25 minutes after the shot; this is the version most cited on [N1098L HADES Over UVU](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU).

* **Afternoon** — Citizen video from the courtyard and surrounding streets is claimed to show small fast-moving objects overhead; those clips are examined on [Military Drones on Video](/Drones/Military_Drones_On_Video) and [Butch Hibbs & Crowd Drones](/Drones/Butch_Hibbs_Crowd_Drones). No link between those objects and this aircraft has been established.

* **Same day, nearby airspace** — [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) counter-drone questions and the other tails on the ramp that week are covered on [Provo Airport Counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS) and the [September 10 flight timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).

## After September 10, 2025

* **September 11–17, 2025** — No public accounting of the aircraft's tasking for the shooting week has been released; the withholding pattern is tracked on [Airborne Surveillance Records](/CoverUp/Airborne_Surveillance_Records) and [Foreign Flight Records](/CoverUp/Foreign_Flight_Records).

* **September 18, 2025** — A researcher states in the master file that a contact at the **National Counterterrorism Center** was, as of that date, **unaware of N1098L's flight path** before or after the shooting. That is an assertion about an unnamed source, not a documented briefing; NCTC's role is on [NCTC](/US_Intelligence/nctc), and the later halt to its foreign-involvement inquiry on [Joe Kent — Foreign Inquiry Shutdown](/Proof_Intel_Services/Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown).

* **Late September 2025** — Public attention to the tail number grew as flight-tracking screenshots circulated; the parallel drawn to unexplained drone activity at Butler, Pennsylvania is on [Butler PA Drone Parallel](/Drones/Butler_PA_Drone_Parallel).

* **October 6, 2025** — Tracking data records the aircraft departing an Army airfield at **14:18 local** and arriving at **Corpus Christi International (CRP)** at **15:35 local**, a 1 hour 17 minute leg, with no further flights scheduled that day; it remained on the ground at CRP.

* **Ongoing** — The sister ship **N2100L**, also a LASAI-operated Global 6500, is tracked on [N2100L LASAI Sister Ship](/Planes/N2100L) as a control case for what routine HADES tasking looks like.

## Records Requests Attempted

* **FAA registry** — Lists LASAI Aviation II LLC at 10660 Aviation Lane, Manassas, Virginia. The operator is described as an ISR contractor to the US Army, which is the stated reason crew details are not public.
* **NTSB database** — A search for N1098L on the date returns no incident, so no post-flight report is reachable by that route.
* **FBO and handling records** — Requests for handling-crew details tied to any September 10 arrival or departure have not produced a public result.
* **Ownership claims** — Some commentators have asserted an address overlap between the LASAI corporate filing and offices associated with **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**. That is an online-researcher claim, it is disputed, and no ownership of this aircraft by any private individual has been established.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point in dispute | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| First low pass time | **~09:16 MDT** (15:16:01 UTC), ~650 ft | **~11:47 MDT**, ~600 ft AGL |
| Second low pass time | **~12:24 PM MDT**, 335 ft, 129 kt | **~12:48 PM MDT**, 203–204 ft, 116–118 kt |
| Was the jet over Utah at all | One summary says the only flight that day was **KBIF → KGPI** (El Paso to Kalispell) | Multiple tracking captures place two low segments near **Utah Lake**, about two miles from UVU |
| Purpose of the passes | **Drone drop and recovery training** — the innocent explanation offered publicly | An operational release and recovery timed to the event — the [HADES over UVU](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU) argument |
| Can this airframe launch drones | One commentator argues the jet **was not built out** for drone launch | Army program reporting describes exactly this test capability for the type |
| Crew on board | Two mutually incompatible crew lists circulate online | **Neither list is verified.** No named individual should be treated as confirmed to have been aboard |

Note on names: this page deliberately does not reproduce the circulating crew rosters as fact. They contradict each other, no primary document supports either, and the people named are living private individuals.

## Open Questions

1. Which ADS-B archive segments are authoritative, and can the 09:16 and 11:47 accounts be reconciled from raw data rather than screenshots?
2. If the only logged flight on September 10 was El Paso to Kalispell, what is the origin of the tracked low segments near Utah Lake?
3. Who tasked the two low passes, and does a mission order exist naming a training objective and the date it was scheduled?
4. Was anything released or recovered on either pass, and if so what, and where is the recovery log?
5. Who was on board, and why is even a redacted crew manifest unavailable if the flight was routine training?
6. Why does an Israeli-IP search spike for this exact tail number appear a month before the event, and is that signal real or an artifact of how Trends aggregates rare terms?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The unreleased mission tasking orders, crew manifests, sensor logs, and any drone release or recovery records for N1098L on September 10, 2025, together with the raw ADS-B and radar archive for Utah Valley airspace that day and the contract terms between the Army, Leidos, and LASAI Aviation II, are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
