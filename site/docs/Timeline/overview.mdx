The **master anchor** is [12:23 PM MDT on September 10](./12-23-shooting-time): Tyler Robinson camera sightings from **8:07–11:49 AM**, [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) low passes, the shot, SUV transport, and evening [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) ADS-B gaps. Read the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute), [ten minutes before](./ten-minutes-before-shot), and [ten minutes after](./ten-minutes-after-shot) when you need the day as clocks rather than as a single page. The [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) chain carries through surrender: media/Fox-style manhunt timelines often fix booking near **~10 PM Sept 11**, while court-document readings track **Miranda ~6:25 PM**, Discord **~7:57 PM**, and booking-time conflicts — hold both on [Custody & Surrender](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline) and [Custody vs Discord](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline). Tyler depth: [Sept 8–13 hour-by-hour](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept), [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

**Before the shot**, the [2025 context arc](./Year_2025) runs July 20 [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) + foreign-meeting claims, August [Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) (attendee list and even whether the meeting occurred remain disputed), and September 9 [pressure session](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) with the ["they are going to kill me" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text). The most-shared X path compresses to **Sept 8** donor/Israel texts (sometimes naming Hammer/Wolicki recipients and a **$150M** Netanyahu-offer rumor) → **Sept 9** high-pressure meeting → UVU; unpack on [Political Pressure](./pre-event-political-pressure) and [Israel Events](./israel-events-pressure-timeline). The hero image above is an Israel-related events collage — a claim map, not a court finding. [2024 pre-event signals](./Year_2024) are OSINT claims only.

**After the shot and into 2026**, start at [After](/After/overview) and [post-assassination investigations](./post-assassination-investigations): federal takeover, the halted foreign-nexus inquiry, sealed evidence, discovery fights, a June 2026 prosecutor contempt finding, and the multi-day **July 2026 preliminary hearing** (DNA/Twiggs testimony, rooftop video **without** a verified trigger-pull frame, ATF ballistics disputes, lead investigator **not consulted** on courtyard paving). Parallel branches: aircraft stack on [Other Planes](./other-planes-activity-timeline) (N888KG, SAM flights, Egyptian Provo cluster, N560TW/N102DZ/N872RA — plus N1098L and SU-BTT deep dives); sealed autopsy, transport, and mic/acoustic disputes on [Medical & Death](./medical-death-timeline), [Autopsy & Forensics](./autopsy-forensic-timeline), and [Audio & Mic](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline); holes rather than events on [Open Questions & Gaps](./open-questions-gaps-timeline). Tyler Robinson remains **charged, not convicted**.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Flight manifests for N1098L, SU-BTT, N888KG, SAM/Huachuca legs, and related tails; Kirk's "they are going to kill me" texts and recipients; cell-tower data filling gaps in Robinson's movements; sealed autopsy and ATF/FBI raw ballistics files; SD cards pulled from the camera behind Kirk; unredacted UVU GRAMA warnings; and the paving work-order chain are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.

Timeline readers on X most often arrive via the compressed Sept 8–10 story (@AdameMedia donor texts and “they are going to kill me”; Frank Turek “they want me dead” threads) and then fan out into July 20 flight/meeting claims, Hamptons pressure, Egyptian/N888KG planes, roof-jump feasibility, Mirandize/surrender bodycam disputes, and 2026 prelim gaps (no shot footage on the record; paving authorization). Keep documented clocks (12:23 MT shot; charging affidavit intervals) in one hand and attributed political-pressure narratives in the other. This hub routes to both without declaring a conspiracy proven.
