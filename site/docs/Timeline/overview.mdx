The **master anchor** is [12:23 PM MDT on September 10](./12-23-shooting-time): Tyler Robinson camera sightings from **8:07–11:49 AM**, [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) low passes, the shot from the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop, [SUV transport](/Suspicious/Medical/no-ambulance-scoop-and-run), and evening [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) ADS-B gaps. Read the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute), [ten minutes before](./ten-minutes-before-shot), and [ten minutes after](./ten-minutes-after-shot) when you need the day as clocks rather than as a single page. The [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) chain carries through surrender: media/Fox-style manhunt timelines often fix booking near **~10 PM Sept 11**, while court-document readings track **Miranda ~6:25 PM**, [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) **~7:57 PM**, and [booking-time conflicts](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy) — hold both on [Custody & Surrender](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline) and [Custody vs Discord](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline). Tyler depth: [Sept 8–13 hour-by-hour](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept), [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

The most-shared X path compresses to **Sept 8** [donor/Israel texts](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours) (sometimes naming [Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)/Wolicki recipients and a **$150M** [Netanyahu-offer](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call) rumor) → **Sept 9** high-pressure meeting → UVU; unpack on [Political Pressure](./pre-event-political-pressure) and [Israel Events](./israel-events-pressure-timeline). The 2023 [GGLF life-insurance policy](/Suspicious/Israel/gglf-life-insurance) on Kirk sits behind the [TPUSA internal timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline). The hero image above is an Israel-related events collage — a claim map, not a court finding. [2024 pre-event signals](./Year_2024) are OSINT claims only.

Parallel branches: aircraft stack on [Other Planes](./other-planes-activity-timeline) (N888KG, SAM flights, Egyptian Provo cluster, [N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)/N102DZ/N872RA — plus N1098L and SU-BTT deep dives); sealed autopsy, transport, and mic/acoustic disputes on [Medical & Death](./medical-death-timeline), [Autopsy & Forensics](./autopsy-forensic-timeline), and [Audio & Mic](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline); holes rather than events on [Open Questions & Gaps](./open-questions-gaps-timeline). Flight manifests for [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview), [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG), [SAM](/Planes/SAM-702)/Huachuca legs, and related tails; Kirk's "they are going to kill me" texts and recipients; [cell-tower data](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline) filling gaps in Robinson's movements; [sealed autopsy](/CoverUp/Autopsy_Sealed_Examiner) and ATF/FBI raw [ballistics files](/Suspicious/FBI/cbla-bullet-lead-test); [SD cards](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed) pulled from the camera behind Kirk; unredacted [UVU GRAMA warnings](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records); the [GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc) life-insurance file; and the paving work-order chain are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.

This hub routes to both without declaring a conspiracy proven.
