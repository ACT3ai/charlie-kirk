# Hamptons Intervention Meeting (Early August 2025, Claims)

Social-media threads, podcasts, and long-form commentary describe a **Hamptons-area gathering in early August 2025** — often labeled an “intervention” — where pro-Israel donors and advocates allegedly confronted Charlie Kirk about his recent rhetoric and platform choices. This page records **what those sources claim**; it does not assert that any attendee ordered or carried out violence.

## Reported date and setting

- **Timing (claims):** Most compilations place the meeting **around August 4, 2025**, shortly after the late-July White House strategy session described on [2025 Context Timeline](./Year_2025) and [Before: Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).
- **Location (claims):** Accounts refer to a **Hamptons-area** private meeting, not a public venue with an official agenda on record.

## Names commonly cited

Commentary and secondary reporting frequently name the following as participants or organizers (all **alleged**; none confirmed in public court filings at time of writing):

- **Pesach Wolicki**
- **Josh Hammer**
- **Seth Dillon**
- **Bill Ackman**
- **Natasha Hausdorff**

For individual profiles, see [People](/People/overview) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

## What sources say happened

- Attendees allegedly pressed Kirk to **adjust his public positioning** on Israel-related topics after he hosted or aligned with voices critical of Israeli policy (e.g., discussions involving **Tucker Carlson** and **Dave Smith** cited in [Before: Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025)).
- Some accounts describe the tone as **confrontational** — an “intervention” rather than a routine donor check-in.
- Kirk is said to have **resisted parts of what was asked**, according to second-hand retellings on X and talk-show segments.

## How this fits the wider 2025 sequence

Citizen-research timelines often place the Hamptons meeting **after** the reported **July 31 White House** session (where Kirk/TPUSA were noted as absent) and **before** Kirk’s early-September posts about leaving the pro-Israel movement and the **[September 9 high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9)**. That ordering is **interpretive**, not established by official records.
