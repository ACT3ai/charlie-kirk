# Hamptons Intervention Meeting (Early August 2025, Claims)

This timeline covers a **five-week band running from late July to mid-September 2025**, organised around one reported weekend: a Hamptons-area gathering in early August that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is said to have afterwards described as an **"intervention."** A reader enters here to see the meeting placed in sequence — what preceded it, what is reported to have been said and offered, and what Kirk did in the weeks after.

:::caution Attributed claims only, no accusation of any crime
Every person named on this page is a living private citizen engaged in lawful political advocacy. **None has been charged with, or found responsible for, anything relating to Charlie Kirk's death.** Nothing here asserts that any attendee knew of, planned, funded, or participated in any crime. Arguing forcefully with an ally is lawful. Offering funding is lawful. Asking a founder about succession is ordinary fiduciary diligence. Known denials are included in full.
:::

## The Timeline

### Before the weekend — June and July 2025

* **June 18, 2025** — Per the master investigation file, Kirk went to the **White House specifically to advise against regime change in Iran**, the position tracked on [Charlie's Israel, donors and motive page](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and against the backdrop of [the 12-day war and Operation Midnight Hammer](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war).

* **July 14, 2025** — [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) speaks at a TPUSA event in Florida and, per the file, tells the audience to **"go all the way"** — a moment cited in the [MAGA antisemitism debate](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate) and on [Tucker Carlson and Israel](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) as the point the intra-coalition argument went public.

* **July 18, 2025** — Citizen timelines assert that a decision to kill Kirk in Utah was made around this date; that assertion is **unproven and rests on inference**, and is examined on its own page, [the July 18 decision claims](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — A reported military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil, and the first-ever U.S. arrival of the aircraft [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) at a Nebraska army base — separate threads carried on [the July 20 military meeting page](./military-meeting-july-20) and [the Nebraska army base page](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** convened, per the file, by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project, to discuss "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel." Attendees named include [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), Luke Moon, Apostle Mario Bramnick, [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu). **Kirk and TPUSA representatives were absent.** See [2025 political pressure and key meetings](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) and [the woke-right defamation framing](/Before/Woke-Right-Defamation).

* **August 2, 2025** — French legionnaires arrive in the United States for a three-week training rotation in California, an item logged in the master file's 2025 sequence and carried on [the 2025 context timeline](./Year_2025).

### The weekend itself — August 4–6, 2025

* **August 4, 2025 (or August 5–6)** — The **Hamptons gathering takes place.** The master file itself is uncertain on the date, recording it as "Aug 4th (or 5th-6th)." No venue, guest list, or agenda has been published from a primary source. Background on the setting is on [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **August 4–6, 2025** — Named in commentary as attendees or as participants in the wider pressure effort: **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**, **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**, **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**, and **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)**. All five are living private citizens; the attendee list is **alleged**, not established in any public filing. See [the Hamptons intervention claim page](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention).

* **August 4, 2025 (or August 5–6)** — Per the master file, **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) telephones Kirk and offers money**, and Kirk **declines to make the trip to Israel.** The file's own line reads: "Bibi Netanyahu calls Charlie offers the money. Charlie says not to trip to Israel." Netanyahu is a sitting head of government; the call, its content, and any funding figure are **reported claims with no released transcript**. Context is on [Israel's Prime Minister](/Israel/israel-pm) and [the reported final Netanyahu call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call).

* **August 4–6, 2025 (reported framing)** — A widely circulated list of case claims summarises the sequence as: Netanyahu **requests Kirk visit Israel**, Netanyahu **offers TPUSA large funding**, Kirk **refuses the offer**, and Kirk becomes disaffected with Netanyahu. One anonymous-donor account circulated in 2026 puts a figure of **$150 million** on the offer, and another describes [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman) as pushing Kirk to "take the cash and fall in line." **Both are unverified secondhand claims from unnamed sources**, presented here as claims only. See [the billion-dollar influence page](/Israel/billion).

* **August 2025 (date unspecified) — the donor-meeting question** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) reportedly told Megyn Kelly that at an August 2025 donor meeting someone asked what would happen to **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) if Kirk died**. This was within roughly forty days of the assassination. The ordinary reading is key-person and succession diligence; the succession dispute itself is covered on [the disputed CEO succession page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed) and the insurance angle on [GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc).

* **August 6, 2025** — Kirk appears on **Megyn Kelly's** program and, in the file's words, **"lashes out about donors pressuring him."** This is the first public trace of the private pressure and is logged on [Charlie's reported quotes and warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

### After the weekend — September 2025

* **September 1–5, 2025** — In private group chats later published by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), Kirk writes that he has **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** and that "Jewish donors really live up to all the stereotypes." The messages were treated as a hoax until published. See [Charlie's quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel) and [leaked Kirk texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts).

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly about **leaving the pro-Israel movement**, the break analysed on [Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and in [the Israel motive timeline](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israel_Motive_Timeline).

* **September 9, 2025** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s** show and criticises Israel to Shapiro's face — treated by [donor-pressure researchers](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) as the point of no return.

* **September 9, 2025** — A **combative high-pressure meeting**, during which Kirk reportedly texted others: **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."** That day has its own chronology on [the September 9 high-pressure meeting page](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and the text itself on [the "they are going to kill me" text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **September 10, 2025 — 12:23:30 PM MDT** — Kirk is shot at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). The 66 seconds that follow are reconstructed on [the minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **September 16, 2025** — Per the investigation file, **Seth Dillon publicly states there was "no angry confrontation"** in the Hamptons and presents the weekend as an enjoyable summer retreat. **This denial is part of the record and belongs on this page.**

* **Later (date not fixed in the file)** — Under questioning by **[Carrie Prejean Boller](/People/carrie-prejean-boller)**, Dillon is reported to have acknowledged there had been a **"heated and at times spirited debate."** Critics read that as a contradiction. Dillon's position on its face — that a robust argument among friends is not an "angry confrontation" — is a coherent distinction and is **not an admission of anything**.

* **2026 (recirculation)** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) states in broadcast segments that Kirk's final phone call was an intervention by Netanyahu pushing for war with Iran, and that Kirk was killed after refusing. This is **her characterisation**, unsupported by any released record; it is tracked on [the reported final Netanyahu call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call) and [donor texts in the final 48 hours](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours).

## Open Questions

1. What is the actual date and venue of the gathering, from a calendar, invitation, or travel record rather than from retellings?
2. Who attended? No primary attendee list has ever been published, and every name on this page comes from commentary.
3. Does any record exist of a call between Netanyahu and Kirk in early August 2025 — a readout, a log, a message confirming it?
4. If a funding offer was made, what was the amount, who conveyed it, and what conditions attached to it?
5. What is the full transcript of Seth Dillon's questioning by Carrie Prejean Boller, so his September 16 statement and his later acknowledgement can be read side by side rather than paraphrased?
6. Who asked the "what happens to TPUSA if you died" question, in what setting, and did the same meeting include key-person insurance or board-governance discussion that would explain it outright?

The calendars, invitations, and travel records fixing the date and attendance of the August 2025 Hamptons weekend, any record or readout of a call between Kirk and a foreign head of government in that window, the complete donor communications pressuring Kirk on Israel across August and early September 2025, the unedited transcripts of the Dillon statements, and the minutes of the August donor meeting where the succession question was reportedly asked are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
