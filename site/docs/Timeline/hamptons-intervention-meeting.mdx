# Hamptons Intervention Meeting (Early August 2025, Claims)

This timeline covers a **five-week band running from late July to mid-September 2025**, organised around one reported weekend: a Hamptons-area gathering in early August that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is said to have afterwards described as an **"intervention."** A reader enters here to see the meeting placed in sequence — what preceded it, what is reported to have been said and offered, and what Kirk did in the weeks after.

:::caution Attributed claims only, no accusation of any crime
Every person named on this page is a living private citizen engaged in lawful political advocacy. **None has been charged with, or found responsible for, anything relating to Charlie Kirk's death.** Nothing here asserts that any attendee knew of, planned, funded, or participated in any crime. Arguing forcefully with an ally is lawful. Offering funding is lawful. Asking a founder about succession is ordinary fiduciary diligence. Known denials are included in full.
:::

## The Timeline

### Before the weekend — June and July 2025

* **June 18, 2025** — Per the master investigation file, Kirk went to the **White House specifically to advise against regime change in Iran**, the position tracked on [Charlie's Israel, donors and motive page](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and against the backdrop of [the 12-day war and Operation Midnight Hammer](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war).

* **July 14, 2025** — [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) speaks at a TPUSA event in Florida and, per the file, tells the audience to **"go all the way"** — a moment cited in the [MAGA antisemitism debate](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate) and on [Tucker Carlson and Israel](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) as the point the intra-coalition argument went public.

* **July 18, 2025** — Citizen timelines assert that a decision to kill Kirk in Utah was made around this date; that assertion is **unproven and rests on inference**, and is examined on its own page, [the July 18 decision claims](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — A reported military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil, and the first-ever U.S. arrival of the aircraft [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) at a Nebraska army base — separate threads carried on [the July 20 military meeting page](./military-meeting-july-20) and [the Nebraska army base page](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** convened, per the file, by the [Faith & Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel), and the [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding) (reportedly funded by [Paul Singer](/TPUSA/Paul_Singer_Funding)), to discuss "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel." Attendees named include [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu). **Kirk and TPUSA representatives were absent.** See [2025 political pressure and key meetings](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) and [the woke-right defamation framing](/Before/Woke-Right-Defamation).

* **August 2, 2025** — French legionnaires arrive in the United States for a three-week training rotation in California, an item logged in the master file's 2025 sequence and carried on [the 2025 context timeline](./Year_2025).

### The weekend itself — August 4–6, 2025

* **August 4–5, 2025 (Ackman account — venue and framing)** — In a later 2026 podcast interview (clips recirculated as *"The Western Spirit"* with Ariel Whitman), investor **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)** says he hosted a **Bridgehampton hotel** gathering of roughly **35 influencers** (combined audience described as over **100 million** followers), covering travel and lodging. He says **Kirk initiated** the idea to convene young conservative influencers on marriage, kids, economics, and Israel; that he **"really just went to listen"**; that he **missed the Israel session** for a board meeting; and that **"at no time was [Kirk] ever threatened by me or anyone else."** This is **Ackman's primary-party account**. It is **disputed** by Candace Owens, Grayzone-sourced reporting, and others. Background on the setting: [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **August 4, 2025 (or August 5–6) — date still multi-sourced** — The master file is uncertain, recording "Aug 4th (or 5th-6th)." Ackman's later Bridgehampton account **pins Aug 4–5** if accepted; no published primary invitation or hotel guest list has fixed the date independently. See [the Hamptons intervention claim page](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention).

* **August 4–6, 2025 — core names in commentary (alleged)** — **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**, **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**, **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**, and **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)**. All are living private citizens; the attendee list is **alleged**, not established in any public filing.

* **Additional names in commentary (alleged, unproven)** — X and video commentary has also named **[Xaviaer DuRousseau](/People/xaviaer-durousseau)**, **[Emily Wilson](/People/emily-wilson)** ("Emily Saves America"), and — per **[Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** — **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)** as present (McCoy's own conduct on September 10 is a separate thread: [McCoy reportedly phones his wife as the shot lands](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife)). Attendance remains **alleged**; no primary guest list has been released.

* **August 4–5, 2025 (claimed — Grayzone / Owens track)** — Reporting attributed to **The Grayzone / Max Blumenthal** and amplified by **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** describes the same weekend as an **"intervention"** in which Ackman allegedly "hammered" Kirk over platforming Israel critics, presented a list of perceived offenses, pressed funding and an Israel trip, and — per one anonymous source — demanded Kirk **rescind Tucker Carlson's [AmFest](/Israel_Main_Suspect/control-amfest-conferences) invitation**. One source said Kirk walked away feeling an attempt at **blackmail**. **No public filing corroborates blackmail or threats**; Ackman has called threat claims **false**.

* **August 4, 2025 (or August 5–6)** — Per the master file, **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) telephones Kirk and offers money**, and Kirk **declines to make the trip to Israel.** The file's own line reads: "Bibi Netanyahu calls Charlie offers the money. Charlie says not to trip to Israel." Netanyahu is a sitting head of government; the call, its content, and any funding figure are **reported claims with no released transcript**. Context is on [Israel's Prime Minister](/Israel/israel-pm) and [the reported final Netanyahu call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call). In post-shooting podcast appearances, Netanyahu publicly stated he had **called Kirk weeks earlier and invited him to Israel** — contemporaneous confirmation of *contact*, not of a funding transcript. See also the [May 2025 Kirk letter to Netanyahu](./israel-events-pressure-timeline) on the Israel pressure year-arc.

* **August 4–6, 2025 (reported framing)** — A widely circulated list of case claims summarises the sequence as: Netanyahu **requests Kirk visit Israel**, Netanyahu **offers TPUSA large funding**, Kirk **refuses the offer**, and Kirk becomes disaffected with Netanyahu. One anonymous-donor account circulated in 2025–2026 puts a figure of **$150 million** on the offer, and another describes [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman) as pushing Kirk to "take the cash and fall in line." Early amplifiers included clips featuring **Nick Fuentes**; later critics attacked the **anonymous source's credibility**. **Both the figure and the "fall in line" line remain unverified secondhand claims**, presented here as claims only. See [the billion-dollar influence page](/Israel/billion).

* **August 2025 (date unspecified) — the donor-meeting / succession question** — In a **November 24, 2025** [Megyn Kelly](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly) interview (clips recirculated by Project Constitution and others), **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** reportedly said that at an August 2025 Hamptons-linked donor setting, one of the first questions was what would happen to **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)** **"if something happens to you,"** and that Charlie answered the organization was vision-led and that **Erika would do a great job running it**. This was within roughly forty days of the assassination. The ordinary reading is key-person and succession diligence; the succession dispute itself is covered on [the disputed CEO succession page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed) and the insurance angle on [GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc).

* **Immediately after the weekend (claimed)** — **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)**, as relayed in 2026 clips with **[Carrie Prejean Boller](/People/carrie-prejean-boller)**, said Kirk came to Tucker's home and described the Hamptons episode in detail shortly after it occurred. Treat as **secondhand recollection** pending a primary recording. Prejean later confronted Dillon publicly ("You were at the Hamptons and you were bullying Charlie Kirk"); Dillon disputes a bullying framing.

* **~August 5–6, 2025 (claimed dating)** — A video segment by **[Harrison Smith](/People/harrison-smith)** is circulated (recirculated October 7, 2025 by **[@KAGdrogo](/Influencers/x/kagdrogo)**, ~419k views) with the claim it was recorded **"the day after"** the Hamptons weekend, and is offered as contemporaneous evidence of Kirk's mood in the fallout. The **day-after dating is the poster's assertion**, not a verified recording date.

* **August 6, 2025** — Kirk appears on **Megyn Kelly's** program and, in the file's words, **"lashes out about donors pressuring him."** Commentators treat this as public evidence the private weekend "did not go well." This is the first public trace of the private pressure and is logged on [Charlie's reported quotes and warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

### After the weekend — September 2025

* **September 1–5, 2025** — In private group chats later published by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), Kirk writes that he has **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** and that "Jewish donors really live up to all the stereotypes." The messages were treated as a hoax until published. See [Charlie's quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel) and [leaked Kirk texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts).

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly about **leaving the pro-Israel movement**, the break analysed on [Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and in [the Israel motive timeline](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israel_Motive_Timeline).

* **September 9, 2025** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s** show and criticises Israel to Shapiro's face — treated by [donor-pressure researchers](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) as the point of no return.

* **September 9, 2025** — A **combative high-pressure meeting**, during which Kirk reportedly texted others: **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."** That day has its own chronology on [the September 9 high-pressure meeting page](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and the text itself on [the "they are going to kill me" text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **September 10, 2025 — 12:23:30 PM MDT** — Kirk is shot at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). The 66 seconds that follow are reconstructed on [the minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **~September 15–16, 2025 (public dispute)** — Owens publicly said **"threats were made"** at the Hamptons gathering and urged Ackman and Dillon to **release text messages**. Major clip accounts carried Ackman's contemporaneous denial (often framed as **"totally false"**). Per the investigation file, **Seth Dillon publicly states there was "no angry confrontation"** and presents the weekend as an enjoyable summer retreat. **These denials are part of the record and belong on this page.**

* **Later (date not fixed in the file)** — Under questioning by **[Carrie Prejean Boller](/People/carrie-prejean-boller)**, Dillon is reported to have acknowledged there had been a **"heated and at times spirited debate."** Critics read that as a contradiction. Dillon's position on its face — that a robust argument among friends is not an "angry confrontation" — is a coherent distinction and is **not an admission of anything**. Circulating secondhand Prejean-chain material attributes further quoted concessions to Dillon about the **cost of criticizing Israel**; those lines remain **unverified** against a primary recording.

* **2026 (recirculation and claim evolution)** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) states in broadcast segments that Kirk's final phone call was an intervention by Netanyahu pushing for war with Iran, and that Kirk was killed after refusing. Later 2026 critics note she has also floated variants in which the Hamptons/Netanyahu contact track **"could've been about"** harming her — which they treat as **inconsistent** with earlier "TPUSA money deal" packaging. All of this is **her characterisation and critics' meta-commentary**, unsupported by any released official record; tracked on [the reported final Netanyahu call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call), [donor texts in the final 48 hours](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours), and the [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

Kirk's disputes with pro-Israel donors are also one of the unexplored lead areas former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has pointed to on the record, saying his center's parallel foreign-nexus review was ordered to stop before leads like these could be run to ground.

## Open Questions

1. What is the actual date and venue of the gathering from a calendar, invitation, hotel folio, or travel record — does any primary document corroborate Ackman's **Bridgehampton hotel / Aug 4–5 / ~35 influencers** account?
2. Who attended? No primary attendee list has ever been published; core five plus Xaviaer, Emily Wilson, Mikey McCoy, and others remain **commentary names**.
3. Does any record exist of a call between Netanyahu and Kirk in early August 2025 — a readout, a log, a message confirming content beyond Netanyahu's public "invited to Israel" remark?
4. If a funding offer was made, what was the amount, who conveyed it, and what conditions attached to it — and can the **$150M** anonymous source be independently vetted?
5. What is the full transcript of Seth Dillon's questioning by Carrie Prejean Boller, so his September 16 statement and his later acknowledgement can be read side by side rather than paraphrased?
6. Who asked the "what happens to TPUSA if something happens to you" question, in what setting, and did the same meeting include key-person insurance or board-governance discussion that would explain it outright?
7. Does any primary recording support the Tucker "living-room debrief" claim and the Grayzone-sourced AmFest-rescind / blackmail-feeling allegations?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The calendars, invitations, and travel records fixing the date and attendance of the August 2025 Hamptons weekend, any record or readout of a call between Kirk and a foreign head of government in that window, the complete donor communications pressuring Kirk on Israel across August and early September 2025, the unedited transcripts of the Dillon statements, and the minutes of the August donor meeting where the succession question was reportedly asked are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
