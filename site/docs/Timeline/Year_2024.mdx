# 2024 Pre‑Event Timeline (Searches and Early Signals – Claims)

This page covers the **calendar year 2024** — a full year before the September 10, 2025 shooting — and collects everything citizen researchers point to as the earliest visible signal in the case. Almost all of it is one evidence class: **Google Trends screenshots**. Trends is aggregate, normalized and carries no user identifiers, so nothing on this page shows who searched for anything. Read it as a map of what researchers claim to see in 2024 data, not as a catalog of proven pre-planning.

## What 2024 evidence actually is, and is not

Every search-pattern entry below rests on graphs of **relative topic interest**, sometimes filtered by region. Trends can make a handful of absolute queries look like a spike of 100. It cannot tell you whether one person searched a hundred times or a hundred people searched once, and it cannot attribute a query to a person, an organization, or a country beyond a coarse regional label. Both facts are why researchers who advance these claims describe them as suggestive rather than probative, and why the [trends deletion and suppression](/GoogleSearches/trends-deletion-suppression) dispute matters — if the underlying graphs later changed, the screenshots become the only record. The full search corpus is indexed at [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/investigation-index).

## The Timeline

### First half of 2024 — context, not signals

* **April 2024** — **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)**, TPUSA Action's COO, is indicted in an unrelated Arizona fake-electors matter. He has **not been convicted** and contests the charges. It appears here only because his name returns in the 2025 [DOGE audit timing](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/doge-audit-bowyer-timing) thread; it has no established connection to the Kirk case.

* **May 2024** — Flight-history compilations note that the jet later tracked as **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** was flying an ordinary schedule at this point, including trips to Cancun. Researchers cite the clean prior history precisely to argue the September 2025 ADS-B behaviour was anomalous.

* **June 30, 2024** — TPUSA's fiscal year closes. Filings covering this year are where researchers locate the **$350,000 loan** attached to the [GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc) life insurance arrangement, examined at [GGLF life insurance](/Suspicious/Israel/gglf-life-insurance). The policy's existence and its interpretation are two different things.

* **June–November 2024 (claim)** — Compilations assert search interest across the term cluster **"Tyler Robinson Discord / Reddit / FBI / LDS / TPUSA / Israel"** during this window. If real, it would mean the man later charged was a search subject roughly a year before the event. Tracked at [Tyler Robinson and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches). **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** is charged, not convicted.

### August 2024 — the UVU building searches

* **August 2024 (claim)** — Researchers report Google Trends activity for **"Utah Valley University buildings"** and, across the broader compilation, terms including UVU rooftop, gun, parking, CCTV, layout and the Losee Center. This is the earliest entry treated as possible **site reconnaissance**. See [UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches) and [pre-event searches and recon](/Before/PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon).

* **August 2024 (claim)** — The same compilation reports a search from Israeli IP addresses for **Greg Gunn**, the drafting attorney behind what became Utah's autopsy-law change — months *before* the bill was introduced, with a second reported spike in May 2025 the same week the new law took effect. The law itself and why it matters to this case are on [autopsy law SB0082](/Medical/Autopsy/autopsy_law_sb0082) and [Utah autopsy law](/CoverUp/Utah_Autopsy_Law).

* **Caveat that applies to both** — Neither entry identifies a searcher. A rise in interest in "Utah Valley University buildings" is also what a construction bid cycle, a student intake, or a local news story produces.

### September 2024 — capability and threat context

* **September 10, 2024** — Per federal filings later cited in this investigation (New Jersey District Court, Case No. 2:25-cr-00299), the Department of Justice documented a threat to kill **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** and her family. The defendant in that matter is charged; **charges are not convictions**. It sits in this timeline exactly one year before the shooting and is cited by researchers as evidence the threat environment around Kirk's circle predated 2025.

* **September 17–18, 2024** — **Operation Grim Beeper**: thousands of pagers and hundreds of walkie-talkies intended for Hezbollah detonate across Lebanon and Syria after a supply-chain infiltration. This is well-documented mainstream history, entirely separate from the Kirk case, and appears here only because it is the reference point researchers use when arguing about **device-based capability** — see [Operation Grim Beeper](/Israel/Operation_Grim_Beeper), [Beepers](/Israel/Beepers) and [Grim Beeper capability](/Suspicious/Israel/grim-beeper-capability). Citing a capability is not evidence it was used.

### November 2024 — the political backdrop

* **November 2024** — The U.S. general election concludes and the political temperature that frames every 2025 event is set. TPUSA's turnout operation is at peak visibility; the resulting White House access is what makes Kirk's mid-2025 dissent consequential. Background at [heightened political tensions](/Before/Heightened-Political-Tensions).

* **2024 (parallel, unrelated)** — Nick Fuentes runs a second, online-only "Groyper War," this time aimed at the Trump campaign rather than at campus events. It is **not** a continuation of the 2019–2020 in-person disruptions that targeted Kirk directly; the earlier episode is on the [Charlie Kirk timeline](./Charlie_Kirk).

### December 2024 — the entries the whole page turns on

* **December 2024 (claim)** — The master investigation file records, as a single line in its own timeline, **"Google Searches for the location."** Researchers describe early search activity in this month from certain regions — including Washington, D.C. and foreign IPs — for locations and people later central to the case. It is a compressed note, not a dataset.

* **December 8–14, 2024 (claim)** — A **one-week Google Trends spike peaking at an interest value of 100** for **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)**, attributed by commentators including [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) to Israeli IP addresses. Nester later becomes defense counsel for the man charged. Proponents call this advance interest in a future defense attorney; skeptics note Trends normalizes to 100 regardless of absolute volume, so a rare name with almost no baseline traffic will show a dramatic spike from a handful of queries. Both readings are laid out at [Nester Google Trends](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/nester-google-trends) and [Kathryn Nester searches](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches).

* **December 2024 onward (claim)** — Compilations assert that a residential address in **St. George, Utah** associated with [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) was searched continuously from December 2024 through September 2025. This site does not publish the address. Twiggs is a living private individual and no wrongdoing is alleged. See [Tyler Robinson and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches).

* **Late 2024 (claim)** — The same threads assert search interest beginning to cluster around Utah hospitals, medical examiners and certain legal actors, later presented as a pre-planning layer for post-incident handling. Collected at [Timpanogos and medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches) and [Israeli search patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Proponents say | Skeptics say |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The Dec 8–14 Nester spike | A one-week, single-region surge for an obscure attorney a year before she was appointed is not coincidence | Trends normalizes to 100. For a name with near-zero baseline, a few queries produce an identical-looking graph |
| The August 2024 UVU searches | Early site reconnaissance — rooftop, CCTV, layout, parking | Aggregate topic interest with no searcher attribution; ordinary campus activity produces the same curve |
| Regional attribution ("Israeli IPs", "D.C.") | Trends' region filter shows where interest originated | The region filter reflects coarse geolocation and is trivially confounded by VPNs and by low absolute volume |
| Are the screenshots reliable? | They were captured before any alleged scrubbing | If the underlying graphs cannot now be reproduced, the screenshots are unverifiable — see [Google Trends scrubbing](/Censorship/Google_Search_Trends_Scrubbing) |
| What 2024 proves | The event was not spontaneous — attention to these people and places ran for a year | Even taken at face value, none of it identifies a searcher, an intent, or a plan |

## Open Questions

1. Can the December 8–14, 2024 Nester spike be independently reproduced in Google Trends today, and what were the **absolute** query volumes behind the normalized value of 100?
2. Do Google's underlying query logs for the 2024 spikes resolve to identifiable subscribers or IP ranges?
3. What exactly does the master file's one-line "December 2024: Google Searches for the location" refer to — which locations, which regions, and from which screenshots?
4. Why would a specific Utah attorney's name draw regionally concentrated interest in December 2024, months before any of the events that made him relevant?
5. Are there any **non-Trends** 2024 records — flights, filings, communications — that independently support a 2024 preparation phase?
6. Which 2024 search screenshots still match live Trends output, and which no longer do?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Google's underlying search logs behind the 2024 regional spikes together with subscriber and IP attribution, the full 2024 Trends corpus in a form that can be independently reproduced rather than screenshotted, the TPUSA filings covering the fiscal year ended June 30, 2024 including the GGLF 2023 LLC insurance arrangement and its $350,000 loan, and any [NSA](/US_Intelligence/nsa) signals record touching the 2024 activity described here are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
