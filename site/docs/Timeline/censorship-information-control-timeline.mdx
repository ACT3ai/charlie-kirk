# Censorship, Platform Activity & Information Control Timeline

:::caution Read this before the timeline
Two very different things appear below and must not be conflated. The **search-and-Trends items are contested open-source interpretation** — Google Trends is volatile, its geographic attribution is coarse, and low-volume terms produce noisy graphs. The **gag order, the archive subpoena and the cease-and-desist letters are documented actions**. Nothing here asserts that any named person, platform, or government committed a crime. Living persons are presumed innocent.
:::

This timeline tracks a single layer: **who tried to control what the public could see, search, or say about this case — and when**. It begins more than a year before the shooting with the search anomalies citizen researchers flag as foreknowledge, and runs through platform actions, witness-footage pressure, legal threats and the court's own gag order. For the search-pattern evidence in depth see [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview); for the section-level treatment see [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).

## The Timeline — Search and Trends Anomalies (Contested)

Every item in this section rests on **Google Trends screenshots preserved by citizen researchers**. Trends reports relative interest, not identities, and researchers preserve archives precisely because live graphs shift. See [Trends Deletion & Suppression Claims](/GoogleSearches/trends-deletion-suppression).

* **November 2023** — Searches for **[Jaxson Fox](/court/jaxson-fox-informant-order)** are claimed to originate from Israel, the earliest anomaly in the compiled set.

* **June – November 2024** — Search terms pairing **"Tyler Robinson"** with [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), Reddit, FBI, LDS, [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and Israel are claimed to appear well before the assassination — see [Tyler Robinson & Lance Twiggs Searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches).

* **August 2024** — Searches for **"Utah Valley University buildings"** are logged, which researchers read as campus reconnaissance and skeptics read as ordinary interest in a large public university. See [UVU Campus & Location Searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches).

* **Dec 8–14, 2024** — A **one-week spike peaking at 100 interest** for **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** — later lead counsel on the accused's [defense team](/court/defense-team) — is claimed to trace entirely to an **Israeli** geography. This is the single most-cited Trends item in the case. The counterweight is documented at [Nester and the Google Trends Spike](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/nester-google-trends); the detail page is [Kathryn Nester — Dec 2024 Israel Spike](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches).

* **Dec 2024 – Sept 2025** — The **St. George townhouse address** later associated with [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) is claimed to be searched continuously across this period. Twiggs' device and its contents became their own dispute later — see [Lance Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone).

* **May 15, 2025 and July 26, 2025** — Unique searches from Israeli IP geographies are claimed for **Judge [Robert Lunnen](/court/judge-robert-lunnen)** and **Judge [Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** respectively, with **no Utah-based searches on either until after the killing**. See [Judges Graf & Lunnen Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/judges-graf-lunnen-searches) and the [counterweight](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/judge-trends-claims).

* **May 18–24, 2025** — A YouTube-specific search spike for **[Jaxson Thomas Fox](/distraction_people/Jaxson_Thomas_Fox)** is claimed from Israel; a further worldwide hit from **Namibia on August 23, 2025** is logged in the same thread.

* **May – July 2025** — Searches for **UVU rooftop, gun, parking, CCTV, layout and the Losee Center** are claimed across this window — the cluster researchers treat as the strongest recon signal. See [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center).

* **July 3, 16 and 18–30, 2025** — Searches are claimed for **[Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Medical/Timpanogos_Facility)**, the Utah medical examiner, and **seven named emergency surgeons** at that hospital — the same facility later at the centre of the [bypassed-trauma-center question](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed). See [Timpanogos, Examiner & Surgeon Searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches), [the surgeon search-trends frenzy](/Suspicious/Medical/surgeon-search-trends), and [Israel Google Searches for Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people).

* **July 20, 2025 — the anchor date** — The date around which the largest cluster of pre-event search activity is claimed to concentrate, two months before the assassination and the same week [Tucker Carlson](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) was platformed at a Turning Point event. See [July 20, 2025 — Anchor Window](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor) and [Foreign Leaders Meeting, July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

* **August 8, 2025** — The surveillance-capable aircraft **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** is claimed to be searched from Israel, 32 days before the shooting. See [Planes Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns) and [N1098L Plane Activity Timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline).

* **Mid-2025** — A **new Utah law criminalizing the sharing of autopsy photographs** takes effect months before the assassination — a legal change researchers pair with the absence of a full autopsy release. See [the Utah autopsy-photo law](/CoverUp/Utah_Autopsy_Law), [Utah autopsy law changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes), and [Autopsy & Forensic Timeline](./autopsy-forensic-timeline).

* **September 9, 2025 — the day before** — **Google Trends shows the tail number `n888kg` being searched in Utah.** The aircraft [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) departed the area after the shooting the following day. This is the tightest pre-event search anomaly in the entire set and the hardest to explain away by ordinary curiosity, because almost nobody searches a private tail number before that aircraft is newsworthy. See [Other Planes Activity Timeline](./other-planes-activity-timeline).

* **September 10–11, 2025** — DC-geography searches for **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** and Timpanogos Regional Hospital spike, which is exactly what would be expected of ordinary news interest — the researchers' argument rests on the **pre-event** hits, not these. See [DC & Metro Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/dc-metro-search-patterns).

## The Timeline — Footage, Platforms and Legal Pressure (Documented and Alleged)

* **September 10, 2025 — hours after the shot** — Witness **Ryne Simmons** says he recorded **close-up 4K 60fps video** and sent it to the FBI; he says the Bureau then called **multiple times**, escalating above the local field office, to ensure the file was **deleted** from his device. He says he preserved a copy anyway. See [Ryne Simmons — FBI Video Deletion](/Censorship/Ryne_Simmons_FBI_Video) and [the claim page](/Suspicious/FBI/simmons-video-deletion).

* **September 12–16, 2025** — High-engagement posts claim **YouTube removed** a Charlie Kirk clip questioning whether **Israel issued a stand-down order on October 7**. Platform moderation of the **victim's own Israel-critical content** becomes part of the early information-control narrative — **not proof of culpability** for any government or living person.

* **September 2025 (reported)** — A second eyewitness account describes federal personnel **urging deletion of phone footage**, framed to the witness as being for his own wellbeing. See [FBI Asked a Witness to Delete Their Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video) and [Witness Footage Pressure](/Censorship/Witness_Footage_Pressure). Some later threads name **Skylar Bard** as a witness who says feds urged deletion of phone video ("PTSD cure" framing in amplifiers' words) — attribute as witness claim; living person.

* **September 2025 onward** — TPUSA reportedly withheld certain camera angles at the FBI's request while releasing other 4K clips in tribute material — the tension examined at [Selective 4K Release Versus the FBI Gag Claim](/Suspicious/FBI/selective-4k-release) and [TPUSA Production Footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage).

* **October 13, 2025** — The widely-amplified post claiming **multiple attendees found their footage deleted or altered before they got home** is published. **No device-forensics report, filing or chain-of-custody record supports a remote wipe** — and no ordinary explanation has been excluded either. See [Witness Videos Deleted Remotely](/CoverUp/Videos_Deleted_Remotely).

* **October 14, 2025** — [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/x/project-constitution) publishes a large **Google Trends compilation** alleging Israeli and DC-geography interest in Twiggs' address, Robinson-linked search strings, UVU recon terms, TPUSA figures, and medical facilities, clustered around **July 20, 2025**. Trends remain **relative-interest screenshots**, not identified searchers — a dated public dump of the corpus already itemized above.

* **On or about October 24–25, 2025** — Citizen reporters claim Judge **Tony Graf** closed hearing access and ordered transport of inmate **[Jaxson Thomas Fox](/court/jaxson-fox-informant-order)** into the proceedings, which they interpret as a potential jailhouse-informant move under a broader secrecy regime later including the Dec 16 gag. Treat as **process reporting by advocates**, not a finding that Fox testified falsely or has any proven connection to the Kirk killing beyond Trends anomalies already listed.

* **Autumn 2025** — Users report **suspensions, 12-hour locks, shadowbans and "limited visibility" warnings** for liking, replying to or reposting [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) content about the case. Whether this is coordinated reporting, automated enforcement, or ordinary policy at scale is unresolved. See [X — Candace Owens Engagement Bans](/Censorship/X_Candace_Engagement_Bans) and [X — Deboosting Investigators](/Censorship/X_Deboosting_Investigators).

* **November 2025** — The **FBI subpoenaed the domain registrar behind Archive.is / Archive.today**, seeking identifying information about its operator — reported by Ars Technica, 404 Media, The Verge and others, with **no crime specified in the public reporting**. Investigators who rely on archived pages treat this as an existential threat to the evidentiary record; the ordinary explanation is an unrelated criminal probe. See [FBI Subpoena of Archive.today](/CoverUp/Archive_Is_Purge).

* **November 12, 2025** — Archive.today / Archive.ph "under siege" posts intensify after the registrar-subpoena wave; some threads restate the witness-deletion narrative and name **Skylar Bard** alongside Ryne Simmons as accounts of federal deletion pressure.

* **Late 2025 (reported)** — A **TPUSA cease-and-desist letter** was sent to an X poster over drone-footage claims. Per Candace Owens' account the letter conceded that a **media production drone flew for B-roll before the event** while maintaining that security drones were denied by the university — a distinction that is coherent, not a confession. **The letter itself has never been published.** See [The Cease-and-Desist and the Drone Contradiction](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cease-desist-drone-contradiction) and [TPUSA Legal Threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats).

* **Late 2025 – mid-2026 (reported / published by recipients)** — Cease-and-desist pressure expands beyond the drone poster. **Project Constitution** (@ProjectConstitu) says it received **multiple** TPUSA / Erika legal-team C&Ds and later **displays letter images** online (see June 2026 third letter). Treat published letter images as recipient claims of authenticity; this site takes **no position** on trademark merits — see [Project Constitution](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) and [TPUSA legal threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats).

* **December 16, 2025 — the gag order** — Judge **Tony Graf** issued a sweeping order **on his own motion**, requested by neither prosecution nor defense, binding **all witnesses, future counsel, staff, and even non-case attorneys in the same firms**. Gag orders are routine in high-profile cases; the objections here are its **breadth**, its **sua sponte** origin, and that it silenced the witnesses whose accounts most needed testing. See [Court Gag Orders](/Censorship/Court_Gag_Orders), [Gag Orders & Sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and [Sweeping Gag Order and Sealed Proceedings](/CoverUp/Gag_Order_Sealed_Court).

* **December 2025 onward** — Reporting describes **witnesses and attorneys declining to speak** and closed hearings, with press challenges to the order not materializing. See [Legal Process, Gag Orders and Secrecy](/After/Legal_Process_Gag_Orders_And_Secrecy) and [Evidence Sealing 2026](/Legal/Evidence-Sealing-2026).

* **January 21, 2026** — Per **Will Sommer** reporting, **TPUSA sent Candace Owens a cease-and-desist** invoking a **non-disparagement** clause over her case coverage. A civil demand is not a ruling on her claims — see [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **June 7, 2026** — @AnaEscobarShow's claim that **~17 Google Trends spikes align with Miriam Adelson private-jet locations** (Israel/Cyprus same days as Trends geographies for Timpanogos hospital, Utah hotels, etc.) is amplified by Project Constitution and others. **Correlation claim only** — living donor aircraft; Trends geography ≠ identified searchers; methodology is fragile and contested. No crime is asserted.

* **June 11–12, 2026** — @ProjectConstitu posts what it says is a **third** TPUSA / Erika legal-team C&D (attorney **Louis T. Perry**, Faegre Drinker, dated **June 11, 2026**) demanding it stop using Charlie Kirk's name, image, likeness, voice, video and audio and remove posts/banners using Charlie. The account claims Candace and other investigators also received letters and that **it received three**. Report C&D existence as claimed; no position on merits.

* **2026** — Readers report that **this site is hard to surface through Google search**, with both suppression and ordinary algorithmic explanations presented side by side. See [Google Search Censorship of This Website](/CoverUp/Google_Censorship) and [Google Search & Trends Scrubbing](/Censorship/Google_Search_Trends_Scrubbing).

* **2026** — Parallel claims describe **YouTube and X accounts belonging to this investigation appearing demonetized or algorithmically throttled** — see [YouTube throttling claims](/CoverUp/YouTube_HolonCitizen_Censored), [X throttling claims](/CoverUp/X_HolonCitizen_Censored), and [Platform — YouTube & Meta](/Censorship/Platform_YouTube_Meta).

* **July 2026** — The **1:17 on-camera video of the witness describing the FBI deletion requests** surfaced on X with a full transcript, moving that account from secondhand summary to a recorded first-person statement. See [Censorship of Citizen Investigators](/CoverUp/Censorship_Investigators) and [Media vs Citizen Investigation Timeline](./media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline).

* **July 19–20, 2026** — **@TheKirkArchive** launches **kirkarchive.com** as an independent digital archive preserving public records of the assassination, investigation, and trial — a citizen response to link-rot, takedown, and Archive.is pressure fears.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | The suppression reading | The ordinary reading |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-event Trends spikes | Foreign geographies researched the case's figures months early | Trends is noisy at low volume; geography is coarse and small samples mislead |
| Trends ↔ private-jet correlation | Adelson aircraft tracks match Trends geographies (Escobar/Project Constitution) | Coarse geographies + ADS-B coincidence; not identity of searchers |
| The Sept 9 `n888kg` search | Someone knew about the aircraft before it mattered | Tail numbers do get searched by aviation hobbyists; the sample size is tiny |
| YouTube removal of Kirk Israel clip | Scrub of victim's Israel criticism | Ordinary platform moderation / copyright / community guidelines |
| FBI asking a witness to delete video | Evidence suppression | Investigators asking a civilian not to circulate homicide footage is not unusual — though asking for **deletion** rather than preservation is |
| Vanishing witness footage | Remote wipe | No forensics exist; app behavior, storage limits and user action are unexcluded |
| The Archive.is subpoena | Erasing the archived record of the case | An unrelated criminal probe, with no crime specified in reporting |
| Closed hearings / Fox transport | Secrecy + jailhouse-informant play | Ordinary capital-case security / unproven snitch theory |
| The Dec 16 gag order | A blackout on justice | Standard protection of the jury pool in a capital case |
| Cease-and-desist letters (Owens, Project Constitution, drone poster) | Chilling investigators | Ordinary trademark, non-disparagement, and defamation enforcement |
| This site's Google visibility | Deliberate suppression | A young site with thin authority signals ranks poorly by default |
| Kirk Archive launch | Necessary counter to scrubbing | Ordinary citizen archiving; no proven mass deletion of the public record |

## Open Questions

1. Will **Google** produce, under legal process, the query and geographic records behind the disputed Trends spikes — the only thing that can confirm or destroy that entire evidence class?
2. Did the **FBI** ever put in writing a request that any witness **delete** rather than **preserve** footage, and does a policy exist authorizing that?
3. What footage did the FBI ask **TPUSA** to withhold, when, and in what document?
4. What crime, if any, underlies the **Archive.is registrar subpoena**, and does it touch this case at all?
5. Why was the **December 16 gag order** issued on the court's own motion when neither party requested it, and what record supports its breadth — and have any **2026 press challenges** materialized?
6. Will the **cease-and-desist letters** (Owens Jan 2026; Project Constitution letters including the claimed June 11 third letter; drone poster) be authenticated in full so their actual language replaces paraphrases?
7. Has any **device forensics** ever been performed on a phone whose owner says footage vanished?
8. What is the chain of custody and scope of **@TheKirkArchive / kirkarchive.com** materials relative to Archive.is?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The **Google query and geographic records** behind the disputed Trends spikes, any **written federal request** to a witness to delete rather than preserve footage, the **FBI's withholding instructions to TPUSA** and the corresponding camera logs, the **full text of the cease-and-desist letters** sent to citizen investigators, the **sealed docket and the record supporting the December 16 gag order**, and the **device forensics** on every phone whose footage is said to have vanished are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
