# September 8–13, 2025: Full Timeline

Six days carry almost the entire dispute in this case. Everything before Sept 8 is
context and everything after Sept 13 is litigation — but inside this window sit the
public break with pro-Israel donors, a military flight, a shot, a surrender, a Miranda
warning, a Discord confession, and two irreconcilable arrest times. All clock times are
Mountain Time (MT/MDT) unless stated. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is
**charged, not convicted**, and every attribution below names who asserts it.

## September 8, 2025 — The Break and the Flight

* **Sept 8, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) posts publicly about distancing
  himself from the pro-Israel movement, the culmination of the Sept 1–5 group-chat
  messages in which he reportedly said he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel
  cause," a sequence [donor-pressure researchers](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) treat as
  the trigger for everything that follows.

* **Sept 8–9, 2025 (widely circulated claim)** — Viral timelines (e.g. [@AdameMedia](/Influencers/x/adamemedia)) assert
  Kirk texted a group including **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** and **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**
  about leaving the pro-Israel cause, held an emergency Zoom on Sept 9, then texted
  **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)**
  "they are going to kill me." Treat as attributed claim pending authenticated messages;
  living persons named only as chat participants, not as perpetrators
  ([kill-me text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text);
  [Sept 9 high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9)).

* **Sept 8, 2025** — Master-file notes place roughly **twelve lieutenant colonels** at
  **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview), Arizona**, the U.S. Army Intelligence Center, in a gathering reported by
  a claimed military witness and amplified by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens); the
  claim is unverified by the Army and is tracked on
  [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and the
  [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

* **Sept 8, 2025 (evening)** — The VIP jet **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** is logged at the
  [Fort Huachuca airfield](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca); Owens later named
  [Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell), Under Secretary of War for Intelligence and
  Security, as one of four passengers, an identification the Pentagon has not confirmed
  ([SAM-702 to Fort Huachuca](/Suspicious/US_Intel/sam-702-fort-huachuca)).

* **Sept 8, 2025** — Pro-Israel commentators and donors react publicly to Kirk's post; the
  reaction chain is catalogued on [Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction)
  and in the [Israel pressure timeline](./israel-events-pressure-timeline).

## September 9, 2025 — Shapiro, the Meeting, and the Text

* **Sept 9, 2025 — 12:49 AM** — SAM-702 departs Fort Huachuca (logged as 0:49 in the master
  file), a departure citizen trackers tie to the same
  [ISR and VIP flight cluster](/Planes/ISR-Operations) that includes
  [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview).

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Kirk appears on [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s show and criticizes
  Israel to Shapiro's face — the most public form the break had yet taken, and the moment
  [motive researchers](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) treat as the point of no return.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Kirk attends what sources describe as a **high-pressure meeting**;
  during or immediately after it he reportedly texted associates
  ["they are going to kill me"](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text). The meeting itself is
  reconstructed on the [Sept 9 high-pressure meeting page](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9).

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Owens says three separate sources told her Kirk had said he was worried
  "they" would kill him; the corroborating quotes are collected on
  [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) and disputed by
  associates who describe the remarks as private venting.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Google Trends notes in the master file report Utah-origin searches for
  the tail number **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** — a day before that aircraft's disputed
  evening ADS-B gap ([plane search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns)).

## September 10, 2025 — 12:23 PM and After

* **12:23 PM (12:23:30 by the frame count)** — Charlie Kirk is shot while speaking into a
  microphone in the UVU courtyard, hit in the neck and falling to his left; some
  compilations use **12:27 PM** instead, a conflict traced on
  [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time) and reconstructed second-by-second on the
  [minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **12:23 PM +00:18 to +00:31** — Security begins moving Kirk at 18 seconds and he is loaded
  into the SUV at 31 seconds, per the frame reconstruction; the
  [SUV transport question](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie) and the
  [medical timeline](./medical-death-timeline) pick up from there.

* **12:23 PM (immediately after)** — The Suspect stands, sprints north across the rooftop,
  lowers himself off the northeast corner and drops to the grass — the sequence contested on
  [Roof Jumping Timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline) and
  [roof run limp disappears](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/roof-run-limp-disappears).

* **12:24 PM** — The Suspect runs north across Campus Drive; investigators later recover a
  **Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle** wrapped in a dark towel in the
  [wooded area north of campus](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery)
  ([Mauser rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle)).

* **12:24 PM (evidence)** — Cartridge casings with engraved inscriptions are found with the
  rifle; per Deseret News quoting Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) and LiveNOW
  from FOX, the **fired casing** read *"Notices, bulges, OwO what's this?"* and the three
  **unfired casings** read *"Hey fascist! CATCH!"*, *"O Bella ciao, Bella ciao, Bella ciao,
  Ciao, ciao!"*, and *"If you read this, you are gay LMAO."* Defense filings note the
  engravings are consistent with a rotary tool such as a Dremel.
  ([Deseret News](https://www.deseret.com/utah/2025/09/12/charlie-kirk-shooting-arrest-tyler-robinson-arrest-shell-casing-inscriptions-fascist/),
  [LiveNOW from FOX](https://www.livenowfox.com/news/messages-found-tyler-robinsons-bullet-casings-what-said))

* **~12:24–12:48 PM** — N1098L reportedly executes a second low-altitude maneuver after the
  shot; sources cite either 12:24 or 12:48 PM
  ([N1098L activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline)).

* **12:26 PM** — Regional law enforcement logs the first reports of the shooting — three
  minutes after the shot, a gap examined on
  [first police report gap](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/first-police-report-gap).

* **12:35 PM** — An officer broadcasts a suspect description: jeans, black shirt, black mask,
  long rifle ([dispatch and scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline)).

* **12:39 PM** — The first FBI agents arrive and secure the scene
  ([SLC Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office)).

* **12:48 PM** — UVU issues a campus emergency alert about a shot fired toward a visiting
  speaker ([UVU administration response](/UVU/UVU-Administration-Response)).

* **Afternoon** — Kirk is transported to
  [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) in Orem and
  pronounced deceased; the bypassing of closer trauma capacity is disputed on
  [trauma center bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **1:02 PM** — [President Trump](/People/donald-trump) shares news of the shooting on Truth
  Social, ahead of any Utah podium statement
  ([press conferences timeline](./press-conferences-statements-timeline)).

* **1:37 PM** — UVU closes campus and urges evacuation.

* **2:01 PM** — UVU instructs remaining persons to secure in place.

* **2:40 PM** — Trump confirms Kirk's death on Truth Social.

* **4:00 PM** — Governor Cox holds a press conference calling the killing a "political
  assassination" ([Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline)).

* **4:21 PM** — FBI Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) announces a subject is in custody — premature and
  incorrect; two unrelated individuals had been briefly detained
  ([Kash Patel briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)).

* **5:59 PM** — Patel corrects the earlier statement and the detained subject is released
  ([manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline)).

* **7:59 PM** — The FBI releases video footage showing the alleged shooter jumping off the
  rooftop and fleeing campus, in a quality critics call implausibly poor for a campus with
  HD coverage ([UVU HD footage withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld)).

* **Evening** — The private jet [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG) departs
  [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport); trackers report its ADS-B transponder
  goes dark mid-flight before it reappears and returns to PVU
  ([other planes activity](./other-planes-activity-timeline)).

* **~6:38 PM (claimed photo; not a state exhibit)** — Candace Owens amplifiers circulate an
  Orem **[Dairy Queen](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem)** image of a person they identify as Robinson; public hearing summaries
  say the State did not introduce the photo at the July 2026 prelim
  ([day-of evening clock](./Sept_10_2025)).

* **~8:55–9:47 PM (claimed)** — Citizen investigators place a person identified as Robinson
  at a **Panguitch steakhouse** (Cowboy’s / Smokehouse Grill) with a receipt window and staff
  accounts on the southbound route toward Washington County — a stop absent from early federal
  pressers ([post-shot escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape)).

* **~11:00 PM onward (alleged texts; state theory)** — Per public inventories of the State’s
  prelim package ([@aburkhartlaw](/Influencers/x/aburkhartlaw)), texts attributed to Robinson and roommate
  **[Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)** — later extracted from
  [Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) — begin ~11 p.m., allegedly admitting the shooting and describing a towel-wrapped rifle in a
  bush; a note photographed under a keyboard and later seen partially burnt is also described.
  Authorship remains contested by custody-first researchers
  ([Tyler: Sept 8–13](./Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept)).

## September 11, 2025 — Manhunt, Surrender, Miranda

* **6:00 AM** — Trump appears on Fox &amp; Friends saying "with a high degree of certainty"
  that the suspected killer is in custody — before any public surrender had occurred.

* **7:00 AM** — Officials announce overnight forensic results: the Mauser .30-06, footwear
  impressions, a palm print and a forearm impression
  ([ballistics and forensics](/FBI/Ballistics_Forensics)).

* **7:15 AM** — A gray Dodge Challenger appears on surveillance at the
  [Cedar City Maverik](/Locations/Cedar_City_Maverik), roughly 215 miles south of Orem;
  the driver pays by credit card and leaves within three minutes.

* **9:58 AM** — FBI Salt Lake City releases the first two photos of the person of interest
  ([enhanced suspect photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos)).

* **10:44 AM** — The FBI announces a **$100,000 reward** for information.

* **6:17 PM** — The FBI releases additional photos of the person of interest.

* **Afternoon–evening (contested reconstruction)** — Some researchers ([@VLuvMully](/Influencers/x/vluvmully) and others)
  argue Bates-stamped WCSO video starting Miranda at **03:36:53 / 6:25 p.m.** implies the
  soft-room recording began ~**3:00 p.m.**, hours before Brooksby’s 8:02 p.m. call narrative.
  Officials and prelim testimony summarized by others place voluntary arrival ~**9:00 p.m.**
  Full legal analysis on [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **Afternoon–evening (family-attributed claims, third-party video)** — Circulating interviews
  amplified by [@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen-2) and [@baroncoleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) claim Robinson’s parents say he **denied**
  killing Kirk, that they contacted a bishop/community figures from **fear**, and that the
  turn-in chain did not originate as a confession relay. Officials describe a family-arranged
  peaceful surrender ([did not surrender strand](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/robinson-did-not-surrender)).

* **6:25 PM** — Per a defense filing (Bates 003996-R2), a video of the encounter shows
  Robinson read his **Miranda rights** at the
  [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff); he answers
  each right affirmatively, then says *"I would like to speak with my legal counsel. I'm
  hoping for a fellow in Utah named [Doug Terry](/People/doug-terry)."* Full analysis on
  [Custody, Surrender and Mirandizing](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline) and
  [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **6:26 PM** — He states the attorney's office is "closed for the night" and invokes
  silence: *"Until I've had the opportunity to speak with someone, I would like to remain
  silent."* The phrase "for the night" — rather than "for the weekend" — is the anchor of
  [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline)'s argument that this happened on
  Thursday Sept 11, not Friday Sept 12
  ([Coleman: early turn-in and the refused surrender video](/court/mirandize/baron-coleman-early-turn-in)).

* **7:30–8:00 PM** — A press conference with Governor Cox reports over 7,000 tips and nearly
  200 completed interviews and shows suspect photos and surveillance video; attendees
  included Cox, Lt. Gov. [Deidre Henderson](/People/deidre-henderson), FBI Director Patel,
  Deputy Director [Dan Bongino](/People/dan-bongino), Utah County Sheriff
  [Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith) and SAC [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)
  ([official statements](/Legal/Official-Statements)). Citizen investigators later argue this
  podium still presented the suspect as at large if custody had already begun at 6:25 PM.

* **7:45 PM (approx.)** — Air Force Two carrying Kirk's casket lands at Phoenix Sky Harbor
  with [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), VP [JD Vance](/People/jd-vance) and Second Lady
  Usha Vance aboard ([casket flight](/Planes/Casket-Flight)).

* **7:57 PM** — Discord messages allegedly posted under the username *zealous_moneky_55095*
  read *"Hey guys, I have bad news for you all. It was me at UVU yesterday..."* — roughly
  **90 minutes after** the 6:25 PM Miranda timestamp, the core of the
  [Discord custody conflict](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline) and
  [Discord messages page](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages). Critics ask how messages were
  sent if a phone was confiscated at the soft room; the State points to Discord production
  under subpoena and roommate identification of the account.

* **8:02 PM** — Washington County Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) says he
  received a call from a retired detective family friend: *"I know who the Charlie Kirk
  shooter is. He's in Washington County now, and we're working on trying to get him to come
  in voluntarily"* ([Miranda vs Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account)).
  Brooksby’s later press (KSL/Fox13, ~Sept 17) added that Robinson was allegedly en route
  toward a remote part of Washington County with **suicidal ideation** concerns and that
  parents persuaded a **"peaceful, gentle"** surrender with the father driving. Brooksby
  resigned in March 2026; researchers link the two
  ([Brooksby resignation claims](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation)).

* **8:04 PM** — Per the probable-cause narrative, Brooksby calls Utah County Sheriff Mike
  Smith — who critics note was standing at the Utah County press conference at that moment
  ([Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff)).

* **~8:30 PM ([Sam Parker](/Influencers/sam-parker) analysis of Davis testimony)** — [@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker) argues investigators
  were ordered to **fly to St. George at 8:30 p.m.** — before the publicized ~9:00 p.m.
  surrender — chartering a plane with a Utah County attorney and FBI agents, and that
  [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) is listed as **10:00 p.m.** arresting officer while
  testifying to a **near-midnight** arrival
  ([arrest-booking-affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline)).

* **State package names (hearing inventory)** — Public inventories of the State’s prelim
  evidence ([@aburkhartlaw](/Influencers/x/aburkhartlaw)) name **[Mike Mitchell](/People/mike-mitchell)** as the family friend who helped coordinate
  surrender with WCSO; parents were interviewed; WCSO **interview-room video** of entry was
  described as existing (distinct from bodycam / lobby CCTV retention disputes).

* **~9:50–10:26 PM** — Robinson travels with his parents and the retired detective to the
  sheriff's office in Hurricane, Utah; the government document records arrival at
  **2226 hours (10:26 PM)** while media timelines and some hearing-watcher summaries of
  Davis/Hull testimony say **"just before 9 p.m." / around 9 p.m.**
  ([Tyler Robinson surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender)).

* **10:00 PM** — The Utah County inmate booking sheet records the **arrest time as
  09/11/2025 10:00 PM**, arresting officer [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) — a time that
  cannot be reconciled with a 3.25–4 hour drive from Utah County if the trip began after the
  8:02 PM call ([arrest time discrepancy](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy)).

* **~12:30–12:38 AM (bridge from Sept 10 night; hearing)** — Separately from the surrender
  clocks, Hull testimony clips describe a gray Challenger near the gun-recovery neighborhood
  earlier that night with a plate logged to Robinson; defense-focused commentary cites a
  homeowner report of a **bald driver + three passengers** ([Sept 10 day-of](./Sept_10_2025)).

* **Evening** — SU-BTT departs the United States for Egypt, one day after the assassination
  ([Egyptian jets in Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo)).

## September 12, 2025 — Transport, Booking, Charges

* **~12:30 AM–2:00 AM** — After roughly 2.5 hours at the Washington County Sheriff's Office —
  uncuffed on a couch with his parents, given water, and not questioned — Robinson is
  transferred to federal and state investigators for the drive north
  ([Utah County jail custody](/court/utah-county-jail-custody)).

* **1:58 AM** — The booking record shows "Date In 09/12/2025 01:58 AM" under
  **Booking #460956**, one of four separate timestamps competing to be the moment of
  arrest. Independent analysis (Chris Friel, Academia.edu) anchors this clock and related
  OTN workflow to argue a 10:00 p.m. Washington County arrest time cannot fit a 3+ hour
  northbound drive without earlier southbound departure
  ([arrest, booking and affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline)).

* **4:00 AM** — The Utah County affidavit lists the **formal arrest time as 09/12/2025
  04:00**, after transport to the [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail) in Spanish
  Fork. Charges: Aggravated Murder (F1), Felony Discharge of a Firearm Causing Serious
  Bodily Injury (F1), Obstruction of Justice (F2). Held without bail.

* **7:18 AM** — Probable Cause is formally entered by Officer Brian Davis of the Utah
  Attorney General's office (UTBDAVIS), Probable Cause ID **189904**
  ([probable cause affidavit](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause)).
  Commentators note Davis later described elements of the 9:00 p.m. turn-in as something he
  “was told,” and Friel flags possible **jail vs court copy** divergences on clothing and
  first-sighting details.

* **7:00–7:30 AM** — Director Patel publicly characterizes the case as a **"33-hour
  manhunt"** — a figure that, measured from 12:23 PM Sept 10, ends at 9:23 PM Sept 11, three
  hours after the 6:25 PM Miranda timestamp
  ([custody vs Discord confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline)).

* **Morning** — The Utah State Bureau of Investigation issues a records request to
  [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) covering the username and chat history
  ([Discord evidence in court](/court/discord-evidence-court)); the seized handsets go to
  [forensic phone extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction) on
  [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite) tooling
  ([Robinson's phone and devices](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices)).

* **5:00 PM** — Utah County issues its official press release announcing the arrest and
  states formal charges will be filed by noon on September 16
  ([case overview](/court/case-overview)).

## September 13, 2025 — The Quiet Day

* **Sept 13, 2025** — No major new law-enforcement developments are publicly confirmed in
  available sources; national coverage consolidates around the lone-shooter account
  ([media vs citizen investigation](./media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline)).

* **Sept 13, 2025** — Citizen compilations begin flagging the scene-alteration window that
  culminates in the Sept 14 courtyard work
  ([scene changes and paving](./scene-changes-paving-timeline),
  [paving timeline Sept 14](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14)).

* **Sept 13, 2025** — Requests for the Washington County surrender footage begin; the county
  later says the CCTV is gone under retention policy and that no bodycam was rolling
  ([missing surrender footage](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/missing-surrender-footage)).
