# Witness Testimony & Timeline Discrepancies

This timeline collects the moments where **what one witness said** does not line up with **what
another witness said**, or with **the official clock**. It runs from the September 9 warnings
through the September 11 custody hours and into the later interview and footage-handling disputes.
It does not allege that any witness lied or committed perjury; people under fire misremember,
clocks drift, and time zones get transposed. The value is in seeing which discrepancies survive
scrutiny. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged and has pleaded not guilty — he has
not been convicted**.

## The Timeline

### Before the shot

* **Sept 9, 2025** — UVU internal emails obtained through public records show **staff disagreeing
  about how seriously to treat threat information** ahead of the event. That is a documented
  institutional discrepancy, distinct from eyewitness shot geometry, but it belongs to the same
  "who knew what when" landscape — [Pre-Shooting Warnings at UVU](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) and
  [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **Sept 9, 2025 (claim)** — Kirk is said to have texted confidants that he feared for his life.
  The named recipients differ between retellings, and at least one associate says the messages
  never happened — the whole dispute is on
  [the "they are going to kill me" text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **Sept 10, morning** — Doorbell and campus camera sightings place a figure near UVU from roughly
  **8:07 AM to 11:49 AM MDT**, including a reported clothing change. Whether that trail leaves room
  for the charged sequence is disputed on
  [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras) and
  [the pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus). A separately cited **9:24 AM**
  quad-meeting description either implies a second arrival or contradicts one of the two times.

### The shot itself

* **12:23 vs 12:27 PM MDT** — The single most consequential discrepancy on the site. Police-audio
  reconstructions favor **12:23:30**; several early media compilations print **12:27**. Four
  minutes changes every downstream feasibility calculation —
  [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **Shot direction** — Witness accounts split on where the shot came from, which feeds directly into
  the acoustic analysis and the alternative-position theories on
  [Witness Audio Conflicts](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Witness_Audio_Conflicts),
  [the audio and acoustic timeline](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline), and
  [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations).

* **Number of events heard** — Some witnesses describe a single report; acoustic analysts arguing a
  two-event split describe more —
  [Acoustic Two-Event Split](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Acoustic_Two_Event_Split).

* **Wound location** — A significant cluster of first-hour witnesses described a **chest** or
  **heart** wound before the account settled on the **neck**. The shift is documented on
  [Shot in Chest Reversal](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal),
  [Shot to the Heart](/Witnesses/shot-to-the-heart), and
  [Chest Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses).

### The first two minutes

* **12:23:57 PM (T+00:27)** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is already filming from
  the rock pile. Researchers question the speed of that positioning —
  [the rock pile analysis](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile).

* **12:24:02–12:24:03 PM** — Video shows [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) alone, past the SUV,
  with [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) not in frame.

* **The conflict** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) says McCoy called her at **12:23 PM MDT /
  11:23 AM Arizona time**, and Neff says he **witnessed** that call and a subsequent call to
  [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy). The video at 12:24:02 does not show Neff present. The master
  investigation file records this explicitly as a reconstruction problem and explicitly makes no
  accusation — [McCoy/Neff call conflict](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict) and
  [McCoy calls his wife](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife).

* **12:24:36 PM (T+01:06)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying *"He's dead"* — about 39 minutes before
  the reported hospital pronouncement of 1:03:30 PM. Both statements are on the record; how to read
  the gap is contested — [the medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

### Afternoon of September 10

* **Transport accounts** — Eyewitnesses describe a private SUV with no ambulance and no sirens; a
  hospital-contractor account describes a second SUV going to physician parking rather than the ER.
  Both are single-source — [Events at Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital) and
  [Witness Account Conflicts](/Medical/Witness_Account_Conflicts).

* **Dairy Queen sighting (Orem)** — Social-media claims annotate the same photograph variously as
  **12:38 PM** and **6:38 PM MDT**. A six-hour spread on one image has never been publicly
  adjudicated — [Dairy Queen Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) and
  [the post-shot escape timeline](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **Roof egress vs the camera trail** — Compilations that use a 12:27 PM shot time place an
  immediate roof jump right after it, while the morning camera trail is used to argue the sequence
  is tight to the point of implausibility — [Roof Jumping Timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **A detained-then-released suspect** — [Zachariah Qureshi](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi) was publicly
  presented as in custody, then released about ninety minutes later without a public explanation,
  which changed what witnesses and the public believed was happening in real time.

* **Witnesses asked to delete footage (allegation)** — Eyewitness Skylar Bard and others have said
  federal agents asked them to delete phone video. These are witness allegations, not adjudicated
  findings — [FBI Asked to Delete Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video) and
  [Witness Footage Pressure](/Censorship/Witness_Footage_Pressure).

* **A contested witness identity** — Claims circulated that a televised eyewitness was a "crisis
  actor" appearing under a second name. Identity-swap claims of this type have a poor track record
  and are recorded here only because they shaped the witness discourse —
  [Wrong Witnesses](/Witnesses/wrong_witnesses).

### September 11 — the custody hours

* **6:25 PM MDT** — A Bates-stamped defense exhibit shows a Miranda reading, per attorney and
  commentator Baron Coleman. The document reportedly carries **no date**, which is the crux of the
  argument — [Baron Coleman's early turn-in case](/court/mirandize/baron-coleman-early-turn-in) and
  [the Mirandizing timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **Coleman's internal-evidence argument** — In the transcript, the person says his folks are trying
  to reach an attorney whose *"office is closed for the night."* Coleman argues that phrasing fits a
  **Thursday, September 11** evening rather than a **Friday, September 12** evening, when an office
  would be closed for the weekend, and that someone in custody nearly 24 hours would not describe
  parents as having just called. This is an inference from wording, not a court finding.

* **The counterargument** — Defenders of the official chronology place the Mirandizing on
  **September 12** in Utah County, after the overnight transport. The site keeps that counterweight
  visible on [the investigative-hold counterpoint](/court/mirandize/investigative-hold-counterpoint).

* **7:57 PM MDT** — The alleged Discord "confession" posts appear. Coleman states that Washington
  County protocol is to seize a suspect's phone once he is in custody and Mirandized, and that
  Robinson's phone was therefore held from 6:25 PM until Utah County officer
  [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) arrived to transport him — which, if accurate, would place the
  messages outside Robinson's hands. This is a claim advanced in citizen research, not an
  adjudicated finding — [Discord Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline) and
  [the custody vs Discord confession timeline](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

* **7:58–8:00 PM MDT** — The Utah County press conference proceeds on the premise that the suspect
  is not yet in custody, and releases the enhanced stairwell images —
  [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).

* **8:02 PM / 8:04 PM MDT** — Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) says he received a call
  from a retired deputy, then called Sheriff [Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith) — who was reportedly
  standing at the press conference at that moment. Both times come from official statements.

* **10:00 PM MDT** — Davis is listed on the booking sheet as arresting Robinson at the Washington
  County Sheriff's Office. Coleman's drive-time argument is that a 3.5-to-4-hour trip means Davis
  departed around 6:30 PM — before the 8:02 PM call that supposedly triggered everything. Davis's
  own account of when he was told has been quoted in several different verb tenses, which citizen
  investigators flag as a discrepancy of its own —
  [the arrest and booking affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline) and
  [Washington County Sheriff](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff).

* **Sept 12, ~7:00 AM MDT** — Officials describe a **"33-hour manhunt."** From 12:23 PM Sept 10 that
  lands at about 9:23 PM Sept 11; from a 6:25 PM custody start the figure is closer to **30 hours** —
  [the Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline).

### Later — interviews, records, and testimony

* **Sept 15, 2025** — A screwdriver is described on national television as having been on the
  rooftop with matching DNA, where September 11 reporting had placed it near the rifle; the
  September 16 charging documents reportedly omit it entirely —
  [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

* **Ongoing (allegation)** — Researchers allege that **FBI Form 302** interview reports may not match
  the public summaries of what witnesses said, that agents asked witnesses to delete video, and that
  UVU and traffic camera footage was withheld or released with inconsistent timecodes. These are
  **access and process allegations**, not direct witness quotes —
  [FBI Witness Coordination](/FBI/Witness_Coordination),
  [UVU HD Footage Withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld), and
  [Witness Interviews Blocked](/CoverUp/Witness_Interviews_Blocked).

* **Ongoing** — Sealed warrants and gag orders limit what the public can compare early witness
  statements against — [Gag Orders and Sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and
  [Post-Assassination Investigations](./post-assassination-investigations).

* **Preliminary hearing** — Sworn testimony is where several of these conflicts finally meet
  cross-examination; the day-by-day record is on
  [the preliminary hearing pages](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) and
  [hearing witnesses](/court/hearing-witnesses).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Discrepancy | Version A | Version B | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Shot time | 12:23:30 PM MDT | 12:27 PM MDT | Unresolved publicly; 12:23 is the site's working anchor |
| Wound location | Neck | Chest or heart, in many first-hour accounts | The neck account prevailed; the early cluster is unexplained |
| Erika Kirk's call | 12:23 PM MDT, witnessed by Neff | Video at 12:24:02 shows McCoy alone | Raised as a reconstruction problem; no accusation made |
| "He's dead" | Said at T+01:06 on scene | Pronounced 1:03:30 PM at the hospital | Both on the record |
| Dairy Queen photo | 12:38 PM | 6:38 PM | Never adjudicated publicly |
| Miranda date | Sept 11, 6:25 PM (Coleman's reading) | Sept 12, Utah County (official chronology) | The exhibit reportedly bears no date |
| Manhunt length | 33 hours | ~30 hours | Depends entirely on which custody time is correct |
| Discord confession | Posted by Robinson at 7:57 PM | Not possible if he was in custody without his phone from 6:25 PM | Contested; authorship has not been conceded by the defense |
| Screwdriver location | Rooftop, with matching DNA | Near the rifle | Charging documents reportedly omit it |
| Bystander video | Voluntarily provided | Deletion requested by agents | Witness allegation |

## Open Questions

1. Will the Form 302 reports be produced, and do they match the public summaries of witness
   statements?
2. Does a single harmonized witness-statement table with authenticated timestamps exist anywhere in
   the case file?
3. What are the phone-metadata records for the McCoy, Neff, Erika Kirk, and Rob McCoy call chain in
   the first two minutes?
4. Does the Bates-stamped Miranda exhibit carry a date anywhere in the unredacted original?
5. Which witnesses were asked to delete footage, by whom, and was any of it recovered?
6. Why did so many early accounts describe a chest wound, and did any of those come from the people
   physically closest to Kirk?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The unredacted FBI Form 302 interview reports for every witness, any records of requests that
witnesses delete video, the complete UVU and municipal camera footage with authenticated timecodes,
the unredacted and dated Miranda exhibit, the Washington County surrender video, and the phone
metadata for the first-two-minutes call chain are things that the
[Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that
aren't out yet.
