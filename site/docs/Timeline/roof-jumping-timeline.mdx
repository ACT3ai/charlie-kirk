# Roof Jumping Timeline (September 10, 2025, Claims)

:::caution Charged, not convicted
**[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted.** Everything below describes what the probable-cause affidavit, campus surveillance summaries and citizen compilations *allege* about the roof sequence. No court has adjudicated any of it, and this page takes no position on his guilt.
:::

This timeline covers one narrow layer: **the alleged ascent to the roof of the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center), the firing position, and the drop off the roof afterward** — and the feasibility arguments citizen investigators raise against it. If you want the whole day, use [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025); if you want what happened after the accused left campus, use [Post-Shot Escape](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape). This page is about the roof and only the roof.

## The Timeline

All times are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT/MT)**, the Utah clock. Times through 12:24 are drawn from the affidavit-derived movement summary and the campus surveillance compilation on this site.

### Morning approach (camera sightings)

* **8:23 AM MDT** — A neighborhood **WYZE camera** reportedly captures a vehicle driving on W 880 S, per the doorbell-and-camera compilation on [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras) and [WYZE footage](/cameras/WYZE).

* **8:29 AM MDT** — **UVU campus surveillance** logs a gray Dodge Challenger arriving on campus; the summary describes a plain maroon T-shirt, light shorts, light shoes and a black hat with a white logo, **no backpack**. Investigators note that the official account **never states when that vehicle left the lot** — see [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location).

* **9:57 AM MDT** — A **WYZE camera** reportedly records a person walking on W 880 S. Between this sighting and the next, the compilation places a **clothing change** into jeans and a long-sleeve shirt ([Clothing Change](/Tyler_Robinson/clothing-change-agent-david-hull)).

* **11:44 AM MDT** — A **Ring camera** reportedly records a person walking on W 800 S ([Ring footage](/cameras/Ring)).

* **11:49 AM MDT** — A **Ring camera** records a person walking on S 800 W back toward campus, now in a **black long-sleeve shirt with a US flag and eagle, dark jeans, dark hat, large sunglasses, and a dark backpack**. Citizen researchers point out that the backpack **appears and disappears** between adjacent entries in the official summary — see [Backpack Rifle Impossibility](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility).

### Ascent to the roof

* **11:50 AM MDT** — Seen walking in a grassy area north of Campus Drive near 800 West, **walking with a limp** (stiff right leg, limited bending), heading south through a parking lot toward a pedestrian tunnel.

* **11:52 AM MDT** — Movement tracked through stairwells and **up to the roof of the Losee Center**, roughly 150 yards from the courtyard.

* **11:53 AM MDT** — Paused at the top of stairs, **pulled out a cell phone**, put it back, then descended into the pedestrian tunnel and ascended a stairwell in the adjacent parking garage. That phone moment is why the [cell tower and phone data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline) matters here: a device visibly in hand is a device that should have left records.

* **12:02 PM MDT** — Walking on the north side of the Losee Center, **still limping**, entering the building through the southeast corner doors. The stairwell images from this window are the ones later released in "enhanced" form — see [Stairs Guy Identity Gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) and [Enhanced Photos After Custody](/Suspicious/FBI/enhanced-photos-after-custody).

* **12:15 PM MDT** — Ascended stairs beside the building, **crossed a railing from a public walkway onto the roof**, ran across it, and crawled into a shooting position.

* **12:17 PM MDT** — Climbed over a short wall and crouched on the **north side** of the roof.

* **12:22 PM MDT** — Stood, ran west, scooted near the edge — **the limp no longer noticeable** — and lay down facing the courtyard, roughly **430 feet (130 m)** from the stage. The disappearing limp is one of the most-cited discontinuities in the sequence.

### The shot and the drop

* **12:23:00 PM MDT** — A single shot is fired from the roof, per the affidavit summary. A **competing shot time of 12:27 PM** appears in some media compilations; the conflict is tracked in full on [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **12:23:30 PM MDT** — The timestamp used across this site as the **exact moment of impact** ([minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute)).

* **12:23:29 – 12:23:54 PM MDT** — Surveillance stills published by **Gray Hughes Investigates** are said to show movement **from the shooting position to the northeast corner of the Losee Center in about 25 seconds**, traced across four frames (12:23:29, 12:23:34, 12:23:45, 12:23:54). This is the tightest published sequence for the egress and is the anchor for most feasibility argument on both sides — see [UVU Surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance).

* **12:24 PM MDT** — Per the summary: a **dark-colored item (a rifle wrapped in a dark towel) is placed on the rooftop**, the figure **lowers himself onto the grass below**, and runs north toward the parking lot east of the Losee Center. Commentary widely describes this as a **roof "jump" of roughly 20 feet**; the affidavit language is *lowered himself*, not *jumped* — the two are not the same act and the site distinguishes them.

* **12:24+ PM MDT** — Seen running north across Campus Drive carrying an item, crossing a grassy area and into wooded ground north of campus. A shoe impression consistent with Converse-type sneakers is reported. A **Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle with scope** was later recovered in that wooded area — see [Mauser .30-06 Rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) and [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

### After the roof

* **Sept 10, 12:26 PM MDT** — Per police audio cited in the file, the **first report of the shooting** comes in — three minutes after the affidavit's shot time, which some researchers treat as ordinary dispatch lag and others read as a gap. See [Dispatch & Radio Scanner Timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **Sept 10, 6:38 PM MDT** — CCTV at an Orem **Dairy Queen** reportedly captures the accused about six hours after the shooting, back in what the summary describes as his **morning clothing** (maroon shirt and cap) — a second clothing change that the roof narrative has to accommodate. See [Merchant and Travel CCTV](/cameras/Merchant_And_Travel_CCTV).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — evening into night** — The surrender and custody chain begins, which is a **separate sequence from the roof narrative** and is covered on [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13) and [Custody, Surrender & Mirandizing](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — 7:17 PM Central (reported)** — A **second set of stairwell photos** was released to the public. [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline) argues the accused was **already in custody** at that moment, and asks why photos would be released to help find someone already caught. [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) separately described the images as **"enhanced"** on Fox News. Both points bear on the roof narrative because those images are what fixes the roof figure's identity in the public mind.

* **Undated, later** — The FBI's account holds that **Robinson's father recognized his son** from video of the figure dropping off the roof. Critics respond that the figure in the released frame is **so small it could be mistaken for an insect**, and that a parent could not identify anyone from it without having been shown the enhanced stills first. This is a dispute about **image quality**, not a claim that anyone lied.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | One version | The competing version |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Shot time | **12:23 PM MDT** (12:23:30 impact) — affidavit summary and this site's standard | **12:27 PM MDT** — some media timeline compilations ([details](./12-23-shooting-time)) |
| Roof exit | **"Lowered himself onto the grass"** — affidavit language | **"Jumped from a ~20 ft roof"** — media and commentary shorthand |
| The limp | Present at 11:50 AM and 12:02 PM, **gone by 12:22 PM** — same summary, same person | Investigators read the disappearance as evidence the figures may not be the same person |
| Egress speed | **~25 seconds** from firing position to NE corner — Gray Hughes frame analysis | Feasibility critics argue the run, the rifle placement and the drop cannot all fit that window |
| Video quality | Released roof-jump video is what the FBI had | Critics call it "1973 VHS quality" on a campus with high-definition coverage ([selective 4K release](/Suspicious/FBI/selective-4k-release)) |
| Arrest location | Some early media stubs implied an at-scene apprehension | Later accounts place the arrest in **Washington County on Sept 11**, hundreds of miles south ([arrest and booking](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline)) |
| Whether the shot came from the roof at all | The official accusation timeline | Tent, van and close-range theories ([Close-Range Theories](/Killer/Close_Range_Theories), [Van Man](/Suspects/Van_Man_Bednarz)) |

## Open Questions

1. Have the **physical roof-edge heights and drop zones** at the Losee Center ever been measured independently and published?
2. Is there **continuous camera coverage** of the alleged drop itself — one unbroken clip, not four stills — and if so, why has it not been released with authenticated timecodes?
3. Does any released footage show the **rifle being placed on the roof** and later being carried into the woods, or is that inferred from the recovery location?
4. Why does the **limp** appear at 11:50 AM and 12:02 PM and vanish at 12:22 PM, and does any single continuous clip cover that transition?
5. If the accused's phone was visibly in hand at 11:53 AM, what do the **carrier records** show for the roof window — and why have they not been produced? ([phone data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline))
6. What is the **chain of custody** for the recovered Mauser between 12:24 PM Sept 10 and its logging into evidence?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The unreleased **UVU rooftop and traffic camera footage** with authenticated timecodes and chain-of-custody logs, the **continuous clip** covering the alleged roof drop rather than selected stills, the **synchronized chain-of-custody timeline for the recovered Mauser rifle**, the **original unenhanced stairwell images** alongside whatever process produced the "enhanced" versions, and the **remotely wiped witness phone videos** from the scene are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
