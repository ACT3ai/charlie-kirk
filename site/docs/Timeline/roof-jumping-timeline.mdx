# Roof Jumping Timeline (September 10, 2025, Claims)

Several media timelines and citizen compilations state that **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** — the accused shooter — **jumped from a roof** in connection with the September 10, 2025 UVU shooting. Official public filings summarized on this site provide **less granular public detail** on exact roof egress timing. This page tracks the **alleged sequence** and why researchers dispute it.

## What commentary claims

| Claim element | Source basis on this site |
| --- | --- |
| Shot from **elevated position** / roof line | Widely repeated in media-response timeline stubs and [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025) movement discussions |
| **Immediate roof exit** after firing | Trash *timeline_compilation* lists "Robinson allegedly jumped from roof" right after a **12:27 PM** shot time (contrasts with **12:23 PM** compilations — see [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time)) |
| Same-minute roof activity | [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025) notes citizen compilations questioning whether roof-jump descriptions **square with official time-of-death ranges** |

## Morning movement context (reported)

Before the shot, [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025) documents **doorbell and campus camera sightings** of Robinson near UVU from roughly **8:07 AM through 11:49 AM MDT**, including a reported **clothing change**. Researchers debate whether those sightings leave enough time for **roof access, firing, and egress** without conflicting clips — a question tied to [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) and [Close-Range Theories](/Killer/Close_Range_Theories).

## Open feasibility questions

- **Camera coverage gaps:** Whether rooftop or sight-line footage has been **released** with authenticated timecodes.
- **Arrest timing:** Some summaries place Robinson's arrest around **midnight** at a nearby apartment ([comprehensive examination](/trash/analysis_documentation/comprehensive_examination) summary in retired trash) versus "at scene" language in early media stubs — another timeline tension.
- **Alternative shooter theories:** Roof narrative is central to the **official-accusation timeline** but disputed by tent/van theories on [Killer](/Killer/overview) pages.

## Records that could clarify

Trash *Laws* text preserved from the retired roof-jumping page:

- Unreleased **UVU rooftop and traffic camera** footage with chain-of-custody logs
- Synchronized timeline for the **recovered rifle**
- **Remotely wiped** witness phone videos from the scene

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The unreleased UVU rooftop and traffic camera footage and the synchronized chain-of-custody timeline for the recovered rifle and any remotely-wiped phone videos from the scene are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
