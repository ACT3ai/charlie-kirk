# Post-Shooting Scene Changes, Paving & Evidence Handling Timeline

This timeline follows **one physical object — the ground of the UVU courtyard** — from the moment of the shot on **September 10, 2025** until it was covered in pavers. In under five days a grass courtyard that was a homicide scene became a hardscape. The chronology below assembles the reported dates, the contractor's own on-camera account, and UVU's stated explanation, and keeps the documented parts separate from the unverified ones. Times are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT)**.

Nothing on this page asserts that any living person or organisation destroyed evidence or acted illegally. Rapid site restoration after a violent death on a campus has ordinary explanations, including biohazard remediation and grief-management. Those explanations are stated alongside the questions.

## The Timeline

### September 10 — the scene while it was still a scene

* **12:23:30 PM, Sept 10** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is shot in the courtyard beneath the event tent; the physical setting is described on [the tent structure page](/Tent/Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie) and [the UVU courtyard page](/UVU/Courtyward).

* **12:23:57 PM, Sept 10** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is already filming from the rock pile, per [the minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute) and [the rock-pile page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile).

* **Minutes after the shot, Sept 10** — Citizen research alleges that **camera media and SD cards were removed from the scene before it was secured**, an allegation set out on [cameras and SD cards removed](/CoverUp/SD_Cards_And_Cameras_Removed), [crime-scene camera actions](/cameras/Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions), and [the SD-card removal claim](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed). These are **allegations**, not adjudicated findings.

* **Afternoon, Sept 10** — Researchers allege that **bomb dogs were kept away from parts of the scene**, the claim on [bomb dogs kept away](/Suspicious/FBI/bomb-dogs-kept-away) and [the FBI bomb-dogs page](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs). It matters here only because the same theory — an explosive device rather than, or in addition to, a rifle round — is the reason soil composition later became contested. See [the exploding-pendant theory](/Mic/exploding-pendant-shaped-charge) and [PETN and soil absorption](/Israel/petn).

* **12:35 PM, Sept 10 (official account)** — A Mauser Model 98 .30-06 rifle is reported found wrapped in a dark towel in the wooded area north of campus, per the [affidavit summary](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause) and [the Mauser page](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle). A competing account places the find **the following day, after K-9 and officer searches had failed** — the conflict developed on [the gun-discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

* **12:39 PM, Sept 10** — First FBI agents arrive on scene, beginning the federal role described on [the FBI investigation page](/FBI/FBI_Investigation) and [crime-scene handling](/Law_Enforcement/crime-scene-handling).

### September 11 — the scene loses its perimeter

* **~24 hours after the shot, Sept 11** — [Russell Kennington](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington) is arrested **inside the taped scene**, reportedly with a long gun; charges were later dropped. Whatever the explanation, the arrest establishes that the perimeter was penetrable a day in. See [Kennington released](/CoverUp/Kennington_Released) and [the distraction events timeline](./distraction-events-timeline).

* **Sept 11 (claim)** — A widely circulated allegation states: *"They covered up the evidence THE NEXT DAY, demolishing and repaving the crime scene."* The **"next day"** framing conflicts with the contractor's own **September 14** paver date. Both are recorded on [the paving timeline](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14); the likeliest reconciliation is **two distinct work phases**, excavation first and pavers later.

### September 12–13 — the first team and the excavation

* **~Sept 12 (two days post-shooting)** — A **work order is issued**, per notes compiled on [Hardscape Specialties LLC](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties). No public bidding process has been identified.

* **Sept 12–13 (reported, unverified)** — An **unidentified first team** removes topsoil and grass around the shooting spot and power-washes the area. Figures cited for the depth range from **six to ten inches**. The identity and authority of this team have **never been confirmed by any official source** — see [six inches of dirt removed](/CoverUp/Dirt_Removed) and [state workers and groundskeepers](/Tent/State_Workers_Groundskeepers).

* **Sept 13 (approx.)** — Per the account on [the Hardscape Specialties page](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties), the company said to have performed the tent-area paving had been **dormant for about twenty years and was revived in February 2025**, with an owner described as a **Utah Attorney General special agent** and a co-owner described as **former UVU operations staff**. These are compiled research claims; the corporate record questions are also carried on [the TPUSA-adjacent LLC page](/TPUSA/LLCs/hardscape-specialties-llc) and [the UVU Hardscape page](/UVU/Hardscape-Specialties).

### September 14 — the paver install

* **Sunday, Sept 14** — **Daniel Merrell**, owner of Hardscape Utah, says he received an unexpected **"paver emergency"** call and assembled a crew within roughly ninety minutes. His account is the single most detailed first-hand source and is transcribed on [the Daniel Merrell page](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah).

* **Sunday, Sept 14 — on arrival** — Merrell states the excavation was **already complete when he got there**: *"Everything's dug up… They had taken out about **10 inches** of the dirt around the whole place."* He says he photographed it and that it did not strike him as odd at the time.

* **Sunday, Sept 14 — the quoted instruction** — Merrell quotes an on-site coordinator: *"This is above our pay grade. **The governor and the FBI said they want this done by Monday.** And we need to get it done."* He also says the crew were told **"we're not talking to anybody… not saying anything to the media."** These are **Merrell's characterisations of what he was told by a third party** — not statements by [Governor Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) or by the FBI, neither of whom has confirmed any such instruction. See [the Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline) and [FBI crime-scene paving](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving).

* **Sunday, Sept 14 — who was coordinating** — Merrell describes **two men in charge**: one said to manage a state-owned facility in American Fork, the other a maintenance groundskeeper at a Provo facility, with what he called a "ragtag crew." Sunday hardscape work on a university courtyard is described in citizen research as **unusual** for Utah County.

* **Monday, Sept 15 — the deadline** — The stated completion target. By this point the courtyard grass had been replaced with brick pavers over the area where Kirk had stood. Photographic before-and-after material is on [the UVU pavers page](/UVU/Pavers) and [site alteration evidence](/Tent/Site_Alteration_Evidence).

### After — the explanations and the disputes

* **Days following, Sept 2025** — **UVU characterised the work as pre-scheduled.** The university's president reportedly declined to give specifics, citing a **pending external review**, per AP-sourced reporting summarised on [the UVU institutional response page](/UVU/UVU-Institutional-Response). Local outlets noted the courtyard changes without naming a contractor or an authoriser.

* **Autumn 2025** — A rumour attributing the work to the charged suspect's father's company circulated widely; reporting found **no credible link**, and it is corrected on [other contractors and the dad rumour](/Tent/Other_Contractors).

* **Summer 2025 (retrospective claim)** — Citizen researchers claim Google Trends shows **Daniel Merrell's name searched from Israeli IP addresses in the summer of 2025**, in the same window as searches for the hospital and the state medical examiner. This is a **search-pattern inference**, not evidence of contact, and is logged on [misc figures searches](/GoogleSearches/misc-figures-searches) and [Israeli search patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims).

* **May 2026** — Merrell's interview clips **recirculate widely** and the "governor and FBI" quotation goes viral, prompting renewed questions about who authorised the work and when forensic collection was signed off. The recirculation is tracked on [the courtyard-paved-over page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/courtyard-paved-over) and [site changes and crime-scene handling](/After/Site_Changes_And_Crime_Scene_Handling).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point in dispute | Version A | Version B | Who asserts each |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| When the scene was altered | **"The next day"** — Sept 11 | **Four days later** — Sept 14 pavers | Viral claim versus Merrell's own date; likely two separate phases, excavation then pavers |
| Depth of soil removed | **Six inches** | **About ten inches** | Circulated figures versus Merrell's on-camera statement, "about 10 inches" |
| Why the soil was removed | **Paver over-excavation** — Merrell explains that doing pavers correctly means over-excavating to get a good base | **Biohazard remediation** after a fatal injury — routine and required | **Explosive-residue removal**, because compounds like PETN bind strongly to soil |
| Who did the excavating | Merrell's crew | An earlier, unidentified team already finished when he arrived | Merrell is explicit that he did **not** do it; no official has identified who did |
| Who ordered the deadline | The governor and the FBI, per the on-site coordinator quoted by Merrell | Neither has confirmed any such instruction | A **third-hand quotation** — Merrell quoting a coordinator quoting an unnamed authority |
| Was the work pre-scheduled? | UVU's stated position | Compiled research argues the timing and the emergency framing contradict a routine schedule | UVU versus citizen researchers; the work order and any prior schedule remain unreleased |
| Was forensic collection finished first? | Presumed yes | Unestablished — no public clearance record exists | Neither side has the document; this is the question the whole page turns on |

## Open Questions

1. What is the **dated work order**, who signed it, and does it predate or postdate September 10?
2. Who was the **first team** that excavated the soil, under whose authority, and where did the soil go?
3. Is there a **forensic clearance record** showing ballistics, blood-pattern, and residue collection were complete before the ground was disturbed?
4. Did anyone **test the removed soil** for explosive residue before disposal, and if so, what did the test show?
5. If the courtyard work was pre-scheduled, where is the pre-September-10 schedule, purchase order, or campus maintenance calendar entry showing it?
6. Who was the on-site coordinator Merrell quotes, and will that person confirm or deny the "governor and FBI" instruction on the record?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The dated work orders and emergency authorisations for the courtyard excavation and paving, the pre-September-10 campus maintenance schedule that would substantiate the pre-scheduled explanation, the forensic clearance record showing what was collected before the ground was disturbed, the disposal record for the removed soil and any testing performed on it, the identity of the first excavation team and the authority it acted under, and the chain-of-custody log for every camera and SD card taken from the scene are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
