# Tyler Robinson: Pre-Shot UVU Campus Movements Timeline

This page covers only the **morning of September 10, 2025**, from the first reported doorbell-camera sighting at **8:07 AM MDT** to the last pre-shot rooftop frame at **12:22 PM MDT**. Everything after the shot — the roof exit, the flight into the woods, the evening sightings, the surrender — sits on [the post-shot escape timeline](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape). **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted.** Every sighting below is a figure that law enforcement and media have *attributed* to him, and several of those attributions are actively disputed.

## Before the campus: neighborhood doorbell cameras

* **8:07 AM MDT** — A **Ring doorbell camera** on **S 800 W** records a man walking toward campus in a maroon (or "plum") shirt and light-colored shorts with a black hat, per the reconstruction preserved in the master investigation file and cross-checked on [the Ring doorbell camera page](/cameras/Ring); a TMZ-sourced description of the same 8:07 frame adds a **backpack** and a black hat with a triangle logo, which the law-enforcement-referenced description of the 8:29 arrival does not mention.

* **8:23 AM MDT** — A **WYZE camera** on **W 880 S** records the figure *driving*, per [the WYZE camera thread](/cameras/WYZE) — meaning the man walking toward campus at 8:07 is in a vehicle sixteen minutes later, a transition no released footage covers.

* **8:29 AM MDT** — **UVU surveillance** records a **gray Dodge Challenger** arriving on campus, the time used in [the probable-cause affidavit](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit) and repeated on [the affidavit timeline page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause). Clothing described: plain maroon T-shirt, light shorts, light shoes, black hat with a white logo. **No backpack is mentioned.**

* **~9:24 AM MDT (disputed)** — A video circulated by **@FaithDrivUr** in July 2026 characterizes police as saying the suspect arrived on campus at **9:24 AM** and went to the quad, where he made contact with **TPUSA representatives** — the claim tracked on [the TPUSA–Tyler Robinson chat page](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat) and on [the TPUSA internal timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline). This does **not** match the 8:29 AM arrival above; no TPUSA employee has been named, and no primary filing containing a 9:24 AM time has been located. The ordinary explanation — [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) staff were tabling and doing setup on the quad all morning, so a brief exchange is unremarkable — is the baseline this site records first.

* **9:57 AM MDT** — The **WYZE camera** on **W 880 S** records the figure again, this time **walking**, still in the morning clothing. Between 8:29 and 9:57 there is no published account of when the Challenger left the parking lot, a gap also flagged on [the camera-sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **~10:00–11:44 AM MDT — the unaccounted window** — Roughly **105 minutes** with no released camera frame, no published cell-site record, and no witness account. Citizen researchers treat this as the largest hole in the pre-shot chronology, and it is where [the clothing change](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/robinson-clothing-changes) is presumed to have happened.

* **Disputed: the "9:00 AM doorbell" reading** — Radio host and attorney **Baron Coleman** has publicly argued, from shadow analysis of the released doorbell frames, that the morning doorbell timeline does not sit where officials placed it. Those claims are tracked on [the Baron Coleman timeline](./baron-coleman-timeline) and on [the Ring camera page](/cameras/Ring); they are **his stated analysis, not a court finding**.

## The return to campus

* **11:44 AM MDT** — A **Ring camera** on **W 800 S** records a figure now in a **black long-sleeve shirt with an American flag and eagle**, dark jeans, a dark cap, large sunglasses, and a **dark backpack** — a complete change from the morning attire, per [the clothing-change record](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/robinson-clothing-changes) and [Tyler on surveillance cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).

* **11:49 AM MDT** — A **Ring camera** on **S 800 W** records the figure walking toward campus again. The master file notes that a car belonging to Utah legislator Phil Lyman had reportedly been parked on that street since about **11:30 AM**, placing an unrelated public figure's vehicle on the approach route — logged as a coincidence to be checked against [the vehicle-movements timeline](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline), not as an allegation about anyone.

* **11:50 AM MDT** — The figure reappears in a **grassy area north of Campus Drive near 800 West**, walking with a pronounced **limp** (stiff right leg, limited bending), then moves south through a parking lot toward a pedestrian tunnel. The affidavit narration at this point records **no backpack** — one of the discrepancies driving [the backpack-and-rifle impossibility argument](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility).

* **11:52 AM MDT** — Movements are tracked through stairwells toward the roof of the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)**, roughly **150 yards** from the courtyard where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was speaking, per [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025).

* **11:53 AM MDT** — The figure pauses at the top of a stairway, pulls out a **cell phone**, puts it back, descends into the **pedestrian tunnel**, and ascends a stairwell in the adjacent **parking garage** — the sequence that produced the contested stairwell stills discussed on [the stairs-guy identity page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) and [Tyler Robinson: Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy).

## The stairwell stills and the identity dispute

* **11:53 AM frames — kit as described by law enforcement on the day** — Converse-style sneakers, a **blue mesh baseball cap**, and a **dark blue, full backpack**, per the day-of description preserved in the master file and mapped on [the UVU surveillance page](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance).

* **The backpack-fullness argument** — Citizen video reconstructions argue the man who left the Challenger carried a **light blue pack roughly half empty**, while the stairwell figure carries a **different, near-full pack** — and that swapping shorts for pants would leave a pack *emptier*, not fuller. That reasoning is developed on [Stairs Guy and the backpack](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/stairs-guy-backpack); it is an **inference from compressed video**, not an established finding.

* **The "enhanced" image problem** — FBI Director Kash Patel described the released stairwell images as **"enhanced,"** even though UVU operates a high-definition campus system. The tension between an enhanced grainy still and an unreleased native HD original runs through [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos), [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings), and [UVU HD footage withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld).

* **Camera-label discrepancy** — **@Sunny61120** (July 9, 2026) alleged that parking-garage frame labels flip between **"level 2 NE"** and **"level 1 NE"** on what appears to be the same stairwell. Clerical labeling slips are common and mundane; this is logged as a question for [the UVU surveillance record](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) and [the campus security gaps page](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps), not as proof of editing.

* **The rooftop clothing question** — State investigating agent **David Hull** walked the July 2026 preliminary hearing through the campus video, including a rooftop clothing sequence that citizen investigators say received no public follow-up; see [the Hull clothing-change page](/Tyler_Robinson/clothing-change-agent-david-hull) and [Preliminary Hearing Day 2](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_2).

## The approach to the roof

* **12:02 PM MDT** — The figure is seen on the **north side of the Losee Center**, still limping, and enters the building through the **southeast corner doors**, per the affidavit narrative summarized on [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025).

* **12:15 PM MDT** — The figure ascends stairs beside the building, crosses a railing from a **public walkway** onto the roof, runs across it, and crawls into a firing position — the access route that anchors [the perimeter and rooftop failure analysis](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures) and [the UVU security and camera timeline](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline).

* **12:17 PM MDT** — Climbs over a short wall and crouches on the **north side** of the roof.

* **12:22 PM MDT** — Stands, runs west, scoots near the roof edge — **the limp is no longer visible** — and lies down facing the courtyard, roughly **430 feet (130 m)** from the stage. The disappearing limp is examined on [the roof-run limp page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/roof-run-limp-disappears).

* **12:23 PM MDT — end of this page's scope.** The shot, the rifle-and-towel placement, and the roof exit are covered on [the minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute), [the shooting-time dispute](./12-23-shooting-time), and [the roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Campus arrival time | **8:29 AM** — law-enforcement-referenced UVU surveillance, gray Dodge Challenger | **9:24 AM** with a quad meeting attached — a narrator's characterization of police statements in a @FaithDrivUr video ([page](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat)) |
| Backpack at 8:07 AM | Present, per the TMZ-sourced description | Not mentioned in the 8:29 AM law-enforcement clothing description |
| Backpack at the stairwell | Dark blue and **full**, per the day-of law-enforcement description | Light blue and **half empty** when leaving the car, per citizen video analysis ([page](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/stairs-guy-backpack)) |
| One subject or two | Official position: one continuous subject tracked across UVU cameras | Citizen position: the morning figure and the "stairs guy" are **different people** ([page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap)) |
| The limp | Present 11:50–12:02, gone by 12:22 — read by some as consistent with something carried in a pant leg | Read by others as evidence *against* the pant-leg theory, since stairwell frames show full leg bend ([page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility)) |
| Image quality | Stills were "enhanced" for release | Native HD exports exist and have never been released ([page](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld)) |

## Open Questions

1. When did the gray Dodge Challenger **leave** the parking lot, and which camera covers that moment? No public document states it.
2. Where was the subject between roughly **10:00 AM and 11:44 AM** — the 105-minute window in which the clothing change is presumed to have occurred?
3. Do **cell-site records** for the phone visible at 11:53 AM match the camera track, and why has no cell-tower map been released? See [the cell tower and phone data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline).
4. Who were the **TPUSA representatives** in the quad-contact claim, and does UVU or TPUSA footage of that exchange exist?
5. Is there a **continuous camera handoff** from the parking-garage stairwell to the Losee Center roof, or are there cuts between angles that no released exhibit bridges?
6. Did the rifle enter campus with the subject at all — and if so, on which frame is it visible? See [the gun discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The original UVU DVR exports with embedded per-frame timestamps for the 8:07 AM through 12:22 PM window, the parking-lot departure footage that was never released, the cell-site records covering the 105-minute morning gap, the unredacted camera-location labels on the parking-garage stairwell frames, and any TPUSA or UVU footage of the alleged quad contact are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
