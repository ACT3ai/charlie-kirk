# Rental Cars & Vehicle Movements Timeline

This timeline follows **the vehicles**, not the people. Six of them carry weight in this case: the **grey Dodge Challenger**, the **grey van parked behind the tent**, the **black SUV that carried Charlie Kirk to the hospital and was later destroyed**, the **minivan or SUV with the open back hatch**, the **monopod camera truck**, and the **rental cars issued to passengers off foreign aircraft at [Provo Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport)**. Each one has a chronology, and each one has a hole in it.

Tyler Robinson is **charged, not convicted**. Every person named below is alive unless stated otherwise, and no claim here asserts criminal conduct by anyone.

## The Timeline

### Before the event

* **May 27 to June 2, 2025** — Egyptian-registered **SU-BTU** lands at and departs [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) with passengers who, per aviation researchers, moved onward by ground vehicle; the [Egyptian crew hotel](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel) research is an attempt to reconstruct that ground leg.

* **September 4–10, 2025** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** sits at Provo through the event window. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) alleges that **rental cars were issued via Duncan Aviation** to unidentified passengers from those aircraft; Duncan Aviation operates a maintenance facility at Provo but is **not documented to offer car rental**, so who actually issued the vehicles is unresolved — see [Egyptian Jets at Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo).

* **September 10, 2025 — morning** — Event production vehicles, including the **[TPUSA production](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage) rigs** and the **camera truck** carrying the [monopod camera](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) setup, are staged around the courtyard; who parked which vehicle where has never been published as a diagram.

### September 10 — the day

* **8:23 AM MDT** — A **[WYZE](/cameras/WYZE) camera** on W 880 S records the **grey Dodge Challenger** being driven, the first vehicle frame of the day.

* **8:29 AM MDT** — **[UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance)** records the **grey Dodge Challenger** arriving on campus and parking. The probable-cause affidavit uses this frame — and **never records when the car left the lot**, an omission citizen researchers flag as conspicuous given how completely the morning is otherwise reconstructed on the [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **~11:30 AM MDT** — A car reportedly belonging to Utah legislator **[Phil Lyman](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman)** is parked at a house on the route the suspect figure walks past at 11:49 AM. The site records the coincidence and asserts nothing further; Lyman is a living person and has not been accused by any authority of anything.

* **~12:07 PM MDT (claims)** — Drone footage that researchers say syncs with parking-lot cameras and FBI-released clips is timestamped in the minutes before the shot; whether those "same cars" identifications hold up depends on footage nobody has released in full — see [Military Drones on Video](/Drones/Military_Drones_On_Video).

* **12:23:26 PM MDT, four seconds before the shot (claims)** — The **rear hatch of a grey van** parked behind and to the right of Kirk's position, tucked under the tunnel and partly obscured by the tent, reportedly **snaps open** — and slams shut immediately after the crack. This is the core of the [Grey Van Shooter](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter) theory, built on enhanced reflections and frame analysis; it is contested even among investigators sympathetic to alternative-shooter theories.

* **12:23:30 PM MDT** — The shot. Security begins moving Kirk at 00:18, per the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **12:24:01 PM MDT** — Kirk is **loaded into a black GMC Yukon Denali**, the transport SUV, at roughly 00:31 into that sequence.

* **12:24:12 PM MDT** — **Frank Turek** enters the SUV at roughly 00:42; the SUV departs for [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) between 00:43 and 01:05 — see [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).

* **~12:31 to 12:35 PM MDT** — The SUV arrives at the hospital. Researchers note the chosen hospital was **not the nearest facility**, a routing question examined on the [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline).

* **After the shot** — A **black SUV or minivan with the back hatch open** appears in courtyard footage; the person associated with it, described as wearing a baseball cap with curly hair, has been the subject of competing identification attempts, none confirmed. The site does not name a suspect — see [Curly Hair Man](/distraction_people/Curly_Hair_Man).

* **6:38 PM MDT** — A photograph released by Owens reportedly places Robinson at an **[Orem Dairy Queen](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem)**, about 17 minutes from campus by car — which requires the Challenger to have left the UVU lot at a time no record documents.

* **8:55 to 9:47 PM MDT (contested)** — The circulated **Cowboy's Smokehouse receipt** would require a substantial drive from Orem in the intervening hours. The receipt is **unauthenticated** and the restaurant location unconfirmed — see [Cowboy's Smokehouse Receipt](/Tyler_Robinson/cowboys-smokehouse-receipt).

* **~Midnight (claims)** — Text messages attributed to Robinson describe a **midnight encounter with an officer** and a **Challenger parked in front of the "Nobles" house** with a bald driver and three passengers, captured on a Ring camera. If the restaurant receipt is genuine these messages cannot be, and vice versa — one of the sharpest contradictions on the [gaps timeline](./open-questions-gaps-timeline).

### September 11 — the drive south

* **Overnight into early September 11** — The **grey Dodge Challenger** travels south on **I-15**, roughly **250 miles** from Orem toward the St. George and Washington City area, per news reconstructions summarized on [Tyler Robinson Travel](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel).

* **7:15–7:17 AM MDT** — The Challenger stops at the **[Cedar City Maverik](/Locations/Cedar_City_Maverik)**. The driver pays at the pump with a **credit card**, fuels up, and **does not approach any trash cans** — notable because authorities had specifically alerted stations to watch for dumped evidence.

* **Daytime, September 11** — No documented vehicle movement. The car and driver are placed at the **parents' residence in Washington City** by reporting, but no camera frame or record covers the interval.

* **8:02 PM MDT** — A retired Washington County detective, acting as a family friend, calls **Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** to arrange a surrender, per the sheriff's later account — a timeline [Baron Coleman disputes](./baron-coleman-timeline) as incompatible with a **6:25 PM Miranda time**.

* **Shortly after 8:02 PM MDT** — The retired detective **drives Robinson and his parents** from the Washington City residence directly to the **[Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff)** in Hurricane, a 15–20 minute local drive. **No stop at Robinson's St. George apartment** appears in any account — see [Tyler's Apartment](/Locations/Tyler_Apartment).

* **9:00 to 10:00 PM MDT (accounts vary)** — Arrival at the sheriff's office. Some reports say "around 9 PM," others "just before 10 PM"; the **10:00 PM** figure is the one that appears as the official arrest time, and the discrepancy is the hinge of the [custody and surrender timeline](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline).

### September 12 — the transport north

* **~2:00 AM MDT** — Federal and state investigators take custody at Hurricane and **drive Robinson north to Utah County** — roughly **250 miles, three and a half to four hours** — to the [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail) in Spanish Fork. Whether any interview occurred **during that drive** is not addressed in any public statement; Washington County explicitly said it did not question him.

* **Utah County officer Brian Davis** is recorded as making the arrest at the Washington County Sheriff's Office at **10:00 PM on September 11** — which would require him to have already driven the same 3.25–4 hours south. Coleman argues his presence fits a **6:25 PM** custody time far better than an **8:04 PM** notification call; see [Custody Timeline vs Fake Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession).

### After

* **Undated, post-event** — The **black GMC Yukon Denali** that transported Kirk is reported to have been **chopped up and melted down** rather than preserved as evidence — a vehicle that carried the victim, the wound, and any trace material from the [microphone assembly](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic). See [SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed).

* **Undated, post-event** — Photographs attributed to Owens showing the **SUV interior** circulate: staining on the second-row seats, debris on the floor, and a small dark object with a blue label some viewers describe as a **transmitter or windscreen**. The images are **not independently authenticated**, and the debris could have innocent explanations — see [Burnt Object / RODE Transmitter](/Mic/burnt-object-rode-transmitter).

* **Ongoing** — Investigators publicly ask anyone who saw **specific vehicles** in UVU lots or nearby streets that day to come forward, because **no comprehensive parking-lot camera release** has ever been made; see [Airborne Surveillance Records](/CoverUp/Airborne_Surveillance_Records) for the parallel problem in the air.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Challenger departure from UVU lot | Implicitly left sometime after 8:29 AM | No record exists; the affidavit documents arrival only |
| Grey van hatch | Opened four seconds pre-shot and closed after (frame analysis) | Rejected even by some alternative-theory investigators as over-read footage |
| 6:38 PM Dairy Queen vs 8:55 PM receipt | Both can be true if the restaurant is within ~2 hours | Incompatible if the "3-plus hours from UVU" claim holds |
| Midnight Ring-cam Challenger | Documented in the Twiggs text messages | Cannot coexist with a verified out-of-area restaurant receipt |
| Arrival at Hurricane sheriff's office | Around 9:00 PM | Just before 10:00 PM (the official arrest-time figure) |
| Officer Davis's presence | Drove down after the 8:04 PM call | Impossible in the time available; fits a 6:25 PM custody time (Coleman) |
| Transport SUV disposal | Routine disposal of a damaged vehicle | Destruction of a primary-evidence vehicle before independent examination |
| Provo rental cars | Issued via Duncan Aviation to foreign-aircraft passengers (Owens) | Duncan Aviation is not documented as a car-rental provider; the issuing party is unknown |

## Open Questions

1. What time did the **grey Dodge Challenger leave the UVU parking lot**, and which camera covers that lot?
2. Who **rented the vehicles** issued to passengers off the Egyptian aircraft at Provo, and under what names?
3. Where is the **grey van** now, who owned it, and was it ever searched?
4. Who owned or drove the **minivan or SUV with the open back hatch**, and what was placed in or taken from it?
5. Who authorized the **destruction of the transport SUV**, and was any forensic examination completed first?
6. Was Robinson **questioned during the four-hour transport drive** on September 12, and is there a recording?
7. Do UVU and Orem hold **license-plate reader data** for the campus lots on September 10, and has it been requested?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The complete UVU parking-lot camera footage covering the grey Dodge Challenger from 8:29 AM to its departure, the license-plate reader logs for campus and surrounding streets on September 10, the rental agreements and lessee names for every vehicle issued to passengers from foreign aircraft at Provo, the ownership and search records for the grey van and the open-hatch minivan, the full disposal authorization and forensic report for the black GMC Yukon Denali before it was destroyed, and any recording of the September 12 transport drive are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
