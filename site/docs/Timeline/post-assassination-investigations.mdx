# Post-Assassination Investigation Timeline

This page tracks **the investigations themselves** — local, state, federal, and intelligence — from the first dispatch call after **12:23 PM MDT on September 10, 2025** through the 2026 preliminary hearing. It is a chronology of *investigative acts*: who opened what, who took over, what got sealed, and what got closed. **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted**, and every official named below is a living person against whom no court has found wrongdoing.

## September 10, 2025: the first hours

* **12:23 PM MDT** — The shot. Whether the fatal moment is 12:23 or 12:27 is itself disputed across compilations; see [the shooting-time page](./12-23-shooting-time) and [the minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **12:26 PM MDT** — The first police report of the shooting comes in, per police audio summarized on [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025); the radio traffic itself is on [the dispatch and scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **Immediately after** — A man later identified as **George Zinn** is detained and briefly treated as a suspect before the FBI stated he was not the shooter; he was charged on unrelated counts. See [the George Zinn page](/distraction_people/George_Zinn) and [the distraction timeline](/distraction_people/Distraction_Timeline).

* **Afternoon** — Scene response, crowd control, and initial witness interviews proceed with **six campus officers** and no staged ambulance; see [six officers and no ambulance](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/six-officers-no-ambulance) and [the UVU security and camera timeline](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline).

* **~12:42 PM and ~1:51 PM MDT** — UVU's campus alerts — the false **"suspect in custody"** message at **19 minutes**, and the **Run-Hide-Fight** alert at **88 minutes** still containing placeholder text — are documented in [the UVU GRAMA records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) and [pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **Within hours** — The **FBI** joins and the case shifts toward federal coordination; see [the FBI involvement timeline](./fbi-involvement-timeline) and [the Salt Lake City field office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office). Local investigators later described being told not to pursue their own lines — an attributed claim on [locals told not to investigate](/Suspicious/FBI/locals-told-not-to-investigate).

* **Same afternoon** — K-9 units were reportedly kept away from parts of the scene during the initial search; see [bomb dogs kept away](/Suspicious/FBI/bomb-dogs-kept-away) and [the FBI bomb dogs page](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs).

* **Evening** — Aircraft departures from Provo, including **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)**, are logged on [the September 10 page](./Sept_10_2025) and [the other-planes activity timeline](./other-planes-activity-timeline).

## September 11, 2025: rifle, photos, and surrender

* **September 11 (daytime)** — A **[Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle)** is reported found in the [wooded area north of campus](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery), after K-9s and officers had not located a weapon in the initial search. The sequence is examined on [the gun discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence) and [rifle found after federal redirect](/Suspicious/FBI/rifle-found-after-federal-redirect).

* **September 11** — **Russell Kennington** is arrested inside the active crime scene carrying a long gun and wearing a lab coat, released the same day with no bail, and the charges were later dropped; the arrest affidavit reportedly makes no mention of a firearm. See [the Kennington page](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington), [Kennington released](/CoverUp/Kennington_Released), and [charges dropped](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/kennington-charges-dropped).

* **September 11 — 7:55 PM** — The FBI announces it is seeking a "person of interest" and releases stills (hat, sunglasses, long-sleeve black shirt, backpack) plus roof and off-campus video. The stills were later described as **"enhanced"** — see [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos) and [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings).

* **September 11 — 8:02–8:04 PM** — **Washington County Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** receives a call from a retired deputy, **[Mike Mitchell](/People/mike-mitchell)**, relaying that Robinson's father said his son had confessed; Brooksby then calls **Utah County Sheriff [Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith) at 2004 hours (8:04 PM)**, per the government document quoted in the master investigation file. Brooksby resigned in March 2026 without a stated explanation — [Brooksby's resignation](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation).

* **September 11 — 10:26 PM (2226 hours)** — Robinson arrives at the Washington County Sheriff's Office with his parents, per that same document, **not wearing clothing consistent with the surveillance imagery** — described instead in a dark hat, maroon T-shirt, jeans, and white/gray Converse-style shoes. See [the Sept 11 surrender and custody page](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) and [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **The arrest-time conflict** — Media summaries say "around 9 PM"; [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) said **10 PM**; the government document says **10:26 PM**; some long-form summaries place an arrest **around midnight at a nearby apartment**. All four circulate. See [the arrest-time discrepancy page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy) and [the arrest, booking and affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline).

* **The surrender-versus-arrest dispute** — Whether September 11 was a voluntary surrender at all is argued on [Robinson did not surrender](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/robinson-did-not-surrender) and [the custody vs Discord confession timeline](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

## Mid-September 2025: charging

* **September 12–15, 2025** — Digital evidence collection begins; the **Utah State Bureau of Investigation** seeks records from **[Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages)** relating to the chats later placed at the center of the case. See [Discord evidence in court](/court/discord-evidence-court) and [the Discord custody timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline).

* **September 14, 2025** — Contractor [Daniel Merrell](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah) states he was called to install **[pavers over the courtyard](/UVU/Pavers)** where Kirk was shot, four days after the killing. See [courtyard paved over](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/courtyard-paved-over) and [FBI crime-scene paving](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving).

* **September 15, 2025** — Patel states on national television that a **screwdriver** was found on the roof carrying Robinson's DNA. CNN had reported on September 11 that it was found near the rifle. Both accounts remain in circulation; see [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings).

* **September 16, 2025** — Robinson makes his **first court appearance** by video before **Judge [Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** in Provo and is held **without bail** on charges including aggravated murder; prosecutors later signal they will seek the **death penalty**. See [the case overview](/court/case-overview) and [the prosecution team](/court/prosecution-team). The charging documents and probable-cause affidavit **do not mention the screwdriver** — see [the probable-cause affidavit page](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit).

* **~September 29, 2025 (second-appearance clips)** — Viral courtroom audio ([@HustleBitch_](/Influencers/x/hustlebitch) and others) shows prosecutors describing discovery as "**voluminous**" and the defense refusing to waive the preliminary hearing because of "**massive**" digital production, while the court's gag posture is discussed on the record. See [discovery and Brady](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

* **September 19, 2025** — A *Demystifying the [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)* interview episode circulates widely in post-event commentary, logged on [Year 2025](./Year_2025).

* **September 11 onward — foreign departures** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** is reported departing the United States toward Egypt; see [the SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline) and [Egyptian jets at Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo).

## The foreign-nexus inquiry and its closure

* **Fall 2025** — **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)**, director of the **[National Counterterrorism Center](/US_Intelligence/nctc)** under DNI **[Tulsi Gabbard](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard)**, opened an inquiry into whether any foreign government had involvement. See [the Joe Kent foreign-inquiry shutdown page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown) and [Kent's NCTC probe halted](/Suspicious/US_Intel/kent-nctc-probe-halted).

* **Reported closure** — Per Daily Mail reporting cited in the master file, FBI Director **Kash Patel** "snuffed out" the Counterterrorism Center's effort, with administration officials reportedly concerned that a foreign-interference finding could let the defense argue more than one person was involved. Parallel commentary claims **DNI Tulsi Gabbard** and Kent were "**prohibited by DOJ**" from following foreign-involvement leads — a phrasing variant of the same attributed shutdown, not an independent court finding. See [Patel shut down the Kent probe](/Suspicious/FBI/patel-shut-down-kent-probe) and [FBI foreign leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads). This is **attributed reporting**, and prosecutors have legitimate institutional reasons to protect a single-defendant theory.

* **Leads no public investigative product addresses** — Foreign phones at UVU ([page](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU)), the Israeli-phones claim ([page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israeli_Phones_Bumblehive)), and the Provo counter-UAS testing window of September 4–10 ([page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS)).

## Late 2025: sealing and gag orders

* **September 23 and November 20, 2025** — Docket notices before Judge Graf appear in the research record; see [the case overview](/court/case-overview).

* **October 14, 2025** — The **[Presidential Medal of Freedom](/Media/Presidential-Medal-of-Freedom)** and a **[National Day of Remembrance](/Media/National-Day-Remembrance)** land on Kirk's 32nd birthday, alongside the mid-October developments consolidated on [October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025).

* **December 16, 2025** — Judge Graf issues a broad **gag order on his own motion** — neither prosecution nor defense requested it — binding parties, witnesses, and counsel. See [gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and [court gag orders](/Censorship/Court_Gag_Orders). Gag orders are routine in capital cases; the sua sponte scope is what commentators contest.

* **Late 2025** — A transport order for an unrelated inmate, **[Jaxson Fox](/distraction_people/Jaxson_Thomas_Fox)**, held in [the same jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail) draws jailhouse-informant concerns from citizen commentators; see [the Jaxson Fox informant order page](/court/jaxson-fox-informant-order) and [informant transport timing](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/informant-transport-timing).

* **Sealed into March 2026** — Search warrants and digital-forensics returns are described in examination summaries as sealed, limiting public comparison against [witness accounts](./witness-testimony-discrepancies).

## 2026: discovery fights and the preliminary hearing

* **Early 2026** — Discovery disputes over raw **ATF and FBI DNA and ballistics files** escalate; see [discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes), [discovery withholding](/Suspicious/FBI/discovery-withholding), and [FBI discovery delays](/FBI/Discovery_Delays).

* **March 14, 2026 (reported)** — According to widely shared news clips ([@GuntherEagleman](/Influencers/x/gunthereagleman), [@DerrickEvans4WV](/Influencers/x/derrickevans4wv), and others), Judge **Tony Graf** rejected a defense push to **seal records and ban cameras**, keeping most filings public and a subsequent hearing presumed open — a transparency milestone that sits beside the earlier **sua sponte gag order** on parties and counsel.

* **March 2026 filings** — Defense sought delay of the prelim, citing unfinished **ATF/FBI** bullet-fragment work and the volume of multi-agency material. Contemporaneous reporting (WDEF/CBS and others) said ATF could **not conclusively link** an autopsy fragment to the recovered rifle and that the FBI was still running further tests, including lead-composition work.

* **March 17, 2026 (citizen probe)** — Outside the state case, **[@baroncoleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)** published competing hour-by-hour reconstructions (including a "23-hour timeline of Tyler Robinson") — track as parallel citizen investigation, not an official act; see [Baron Coleman timeline](./baron-coleman-timeline) and [media vs citizen](./media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline).

* **March 27, 2026 filing** — An addendum records that the **Utah State Bureau of Investigation has had 28 different law enforcement agencies produce evidence** in this case — a scale figure that raises its own chain-of-custody questions across [ballistics and ATF CBLA testing](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla), [the banned CBLA bullet-lead test](/Suspicious/FBI/cbla-bullet-lead-test), and [phone extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction).

* **The prosecution's forensic-extraction position** — Opposing a defense continuance, prosecutors wrote that the state "does not intend to offer evidence obtained through forensic extractions of electronics seized in this case" — meaning the widely publicized messages may never be offered by the state at all. See [Robinson's phones and devices](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices) and [Cellebrite extraction](/court/Cellebrite).

* **April 15, 2026** — The **Daily Caller News Foundation** GRAMA production (50 documents, 112 pages) surfaces the September 9 warnings and the campus alert failures; see [the UVU GRAMA records page](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **April 2026 (media layer)** — Broadcast segments (e.g. [Newsmax](/Media/broadcast-tv-networks) guests including [Rob O'Neill](/People/rob-oneill) and Gen. Hall, as amplified by @InvestigateEar1) publicly challenged the lone-30-06 investigative product, citing SD-card removal and early multi-suspect confusion — pressure on investigative credibility **outside** the courtroom.

* **GRAMA appeal over the surrender bodycam** — Scripps News pressed Washington County for bodycam footage of Robinson walking in to surrender; the "no footage" response is on [bodycam GRAMA, no footage](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage).

* **June 2026** — Judge Graf held Deputy County Attorney **[Christopher Ballard](/People/christopher-d-ballard)** in **civil contempt** for media comments about bullet evidence that the court treated as gag-order violations (coverage cites outlets including Fox, TMZ, CNBC, and USA Today). Sanctions reportedly included fee payments to the defense while the **death-penalty option remained** on the table. Contempt is a court finding on pretrial speech, not a finding on the merits of the murder case. See [the case overview](/court/case-overview) and [preliminary hearing motions](/court/preliminary-hearing-motions).

* **July 2026 — the preliminary hearing** — Held before Judge Graf across [Day 1](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_1), [Day 2](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_2), [Day 3](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_3), and [Day 4](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/Day_4). State agent **[David Hull](/People/david-hull)** walked the court through UVU surveillance covering the parking structure, [courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward), and rooftop, plus chain of custody. Careful coverage notes the video shows **arrival, positioning, and flight — not a verified trigger-pull frame**. Coverage also notes **no eyewitness identification** of a firing act.

* **July 2026 — no shot footage (open court)** — Secondary posts claim that when Judge Graf asked whether any footage shows the shooting itself, a prosecutor answered to the effect of **"There is not, Judge"** — conflicting with media personalities (e.g. [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec), as cited by posters) who said they had seen a shot video. Attribute both statements; do not resolve. See [open questions](./open-questions-gaps-timeline).

* **July 2026 — Hull and the paved courtyard** — Hearing coverage records Hull saying he was **not consulted** on the post-shot courtyard paving and **did not know who authorized it** — an investigative preservation admission on the record. See [scene-changes timeline](./scene-changes-paving-timeline).

* **July 2026 — DNA and ballistics products** — Public summaries of hearing testimony cite extremely high DNA likelihood ratios (some coverage: on the order of **≥1 trillion** times more likely Robinson on the rifle) and name FBI examiner **Amanda Bakker** among experts. Testimony described DNA links to Robinson and mixtures involving **[Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)** on [a towel](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/rifle-towel-on-roof) and screwdriver (after re-testing, per some reports), plus related digital statements — see [Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) — and an engraving tool. Defense counsel including **[Michael Burt](/People/michael-burt)** challenged DNA protocols and **ballistics reliability**; coverage states bullet fragments from Kirk were **inconclusive** — could not confirm or exclude the recovered bolt-action rifle. Twiggs cooperated under limited immunity and is **not charged as the shooter**; see [hearing witnesses](/court/hearing-witnesses) and [autopsy-forensic timeline](./autopsy-forensic-timeline).

* **July 2026 — planning chronology tension** — Hearing discussion of bullet engraving "**about a month before**" (per Twiggs-related testimony summaries) sits beside [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)'s public UVU-event announcement around **August 27–28** — a motive-timing tension investigators note, not proof of multi-party planning.

* **July 2026 — the untested items** — **Sergeant Jennifer Faumuina** of UVU Public Safety testified that a **[backpack](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility), jacket, and gloves** recovered near the Fulton Library went to the FBI for DNA analysis and that testing was then **stopped**; she testified she could not recall who made that decision. See [forensic tests skipped](/CoverUp/Forensic_Tests_Skipped).

* **July 2026 — vehicle exhibit claim** — Some hearing commentary alleges the car seized by the FBI **does not match** the vehicle shown in Utah SBI's UVU video presentation — an exhibit-identity dispute; see [vehicle movements](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).

* **July 2026 — the defense posture** — Lead counsel **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** and co-counsel cross-examined chain of custody, the rooftop search, casings, and the informant-transport issue; see [the defense team](/court/defense-team) and [testimony](/court/testimony).

* **Calendar after the prelim (claimed)** — Multiple secondary summaries say the judge deferred a final **bind-over** decision pending arguments around **September 1, 2026**. Other secondary posts assert the case was already bound over. **Flag for primary docket check** before treating either as settled; track status on [2026](./2026).

* **Still open** — A roof-adjacent eyewitness has reportedly never been called ([page](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/uncalled-roof-eyewitness)); the rooftop video remains partially withheld ([page](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/withholding-rooftop-video)); no GSR or comparable physical testing has been publicly documented ([page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/No_GSR_And_Physical_Tests)); the ATF ballistics result has been characterized as inconclusive ([page](/Proof_Not_Tyler/ATF_Inconclusive_Ballistics)); and a public **28-agency evidence map** plus a **written NCTC closure order** remain unpublished.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Time of the shot | **12:23 PM MDT** | **12:27 PM MDT** in other compilations ([page](./12-23-shooting-time)) |
| Time of arrest | **10:26 PM** arrival per the government document; **10 PM** per Kash Patel | **~9 PM** in media summaries; **~midnight at an apartment** in long-form summaries ([page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy)) |
| The screwdriver | Found near the rifle — CNN, September 11 | Found on the roof with DNA — Patel, September 15; absent from the September 16 charging documents entirely |
| Foreign involvement | No credible foreign nexus; single-defendant theory | An NCTC inquiry existed and was reportedly closed from above ([page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown)) |
| Digital messages | Central to the public narrative | Prosecutors stated they do not intend to offer forensic-extraction evidence ([page](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices)) |
| Items near the library | Testing stopped for ordinary investigative reasons | Testing stopped with no recalled decision-maker ([page](/Suspicious/FBI/discovery-withholding)) |
| The gag order | Routine in a capital case | Issued sua sponte with unusually broad reach ([page](/court/gag-orders-sealing)) |
| Video evidence | Shows the accused's movements across campus; some media figures claim they saw a shot video | By the affidavit's own text and (per coverage) prosecutor open-court answer, does **not** capture the act of firing ([page](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld)) |
| Bind-over status | Arguments deferred ~**Sept 1, 2026** (secondary summaries) | Already bound over (other secondary posts) — docket not reconciled here |
| Ballard sanction | Routine gag enforcement | Civil contempt with fee sanctions after named-outlet media comments; death penalty retained |

## Open Questions

1. Which of the **28 agencies** produced which evidence, and does a single unified chain-of-custody log exist?
2. Who ordered the **NCTC foreign-nexus inquiry** closed, in writing, and on what stated grounds?
3. Who decided to **stop DNA testing** the backpack, jacket, and gloves recovered near the Fulton Library?
4. If the state will not offer **forensic extractions**, why were the extracted messages released into public view at all?
5. Why does the **screwdriver** appear in two contradictory public accounts and in neither charging document?
6. What is in the **search warrants and forensic returns** sealed into 2026, and when do they become public?
7. Why has the roof-adjacent eyewitness never been called, and does an interview record exist?
8. What is the **official bind-over status** after the July 2026 prelim, and what is on the September 2026 calendar?
9. Who authorized courtyard paving if the lead investigator was **not consulted**?
10. If the prosecutor told the court there is **no shot footage**, what video did public figures claim to have seen?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The written closure order for the National Counterterrorism Center's foreign-nexus inquiry, the unified chain-of-custody log across all 28 producing agencies, the sealed search warrants and forensic returns, the decision record for halting DNA testing on the items recovered near the Fulton Library, the untrimmed rooftop video with authenticated timecodes, and the surrender bodycam footage that Washington County says does not exist are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
