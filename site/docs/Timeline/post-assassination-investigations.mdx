# Post-Assassination Investigation Timeline

After the September 10, 2025 shooting at Utah Valley University, **local law enforcement, federal agents, and media** began parallel timelines that do not always agree. This page summarizes **reported and alleged** post-shot milestones from project sources — not a complete police blotter.

## September 10, 2025 (day of)

| Approx. time / event | Source basis |
| --- | --- |
| **12:26 PM MDT** | First dispatch / police report of shooting ([Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025)) |
| **Afternoon** | On-scene response, crowd control, initial interviews (general news pattern) |
| **Evening** | Private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departure claims from PVU ([Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025)) |
| **~Midnight (claim)** | Tyler Robinson arrest at nearby apartment cited in long-form examination summaries |
| **UVU alerts (documented)** | Misleading **"suspect in custody"** alert at **19 minutes** post-shot; **Run-Hide-Fight** placeholder text at **88 minutes** — [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) GRAMA review |

Shot-minute disputes: [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time).

## Immediate federal and media response (as summarized)

Trash *timeline_compilation* and legal stubs (boilerplate bodies, preserved sequence claims):

- **FBI arrival within hours**
- Initial **evidence collection** and **witness interviews**
- Early **media identification** of Robinson as suspect
- **Breaking-news** timelines that later diverge from citizen sync projects ([Witness Testimony Discrepancies](./witness-testimony-discrepancies))

## Sealed records and delayed disclosure

Multiple site sections note:

- **Search warrants and digital forensics** described as **sealed until March 2026** in examination summaries — limiting public comparison to [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) accounts.
- **[Legal](/Legal/overview)** and **[FBI](/FBI/overview)** child pages discuss obstruction and intimidation **allegations** separately.

## Alleged investigative limits (claims)

Trash *post_assassination_investigations* *Laws* block — not established in public court records here:

- **Leads shut down** before exhaustion
- **Evidence destroyed** or witnesses **pressured**
- **Foreign-nexus inquiry halted** by unknown officials

These claims appear in citizen-research narratives tying post-shot work to **[Israel](/Israel/overview)** and **[Cover-Up](/CoverUp/overview)** sections.

## October 15, 2025

Mid-October milestones — including the **Oct 10 AES explosion**, **Oct 14 Presidential Medal of Freedom**, and surrounding citizen-investigator and court-scheduling claims — are summarized on the dedicated page [October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025).

## September 11+

- **Sep 11:** [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) reported U.S. departure toward Egypt ([Year_2025](./Year_2025))
- **Sep 19:** *Demystifying the Mossad* interview episode widely cited post-event ([Year_2025](./Year_2025))

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The leads the FBI shut down before exhausting them and records of evidence destroyed or witnesses pressured and who ordered the foreign-nexus inquiry halted are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
