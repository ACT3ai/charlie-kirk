# Israel-Related Events & Pressure Timeline 2025

This is the **Israel layer** of 2025 laid out in date order — the war, the visits, the meetings, the donor pressure, and the twelve weeks in which **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** went from the most valuable non-Jewish pro-Israel voice in American politics to a man writing that he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause." Nothing on this page establishes that any government, organization, or person caused his death. It establishes **what happened, in what order**, so a reader can judge the sequence themselves. Every allegation is attributed. Living people named here have denied wrongdoing or have not been accused of any crime.

## The Timeline

### Spring 2025 — Iran becomes the fault line

* **April 3, 2025** — Kirk publishes a long warning against "an all-out regime change war against Iran," arguing depleted stockpiles, debt, and the scale of Iran make it a "catastrophic mistake" — a position squarely against the loudest pro-Israel voices in Washington ([Israel's Push for War With Iran](/Iran/israel-iran-war-push)).

* **May 27, 2025** — Egyptian-registered jet **SU-BTU** flies into Provo with Walid Mahmoud reportedly aboard; **June 2**, it departs. This is the opening of an Egyptian and Israel-linked aviation pattern over Provo that runs to September ([SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [Israel / Egyptian Plane Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)).

* **June 13, 2025** — Israel and Iran's **twelve-day war** commences, moving the American debate about Iran from theoretical to immediate ([Israel's Motivation](/Israel/motivation)).

* **June 18, 2025** — Kirk goes to the **White House specifically to advise against regime change in Iran**. Commentators describe President Trump pushing back hard. This is the point where a movement's most effective youth organizer is publicly on the opposite side of an active Israeli war aim ([Charlie Kirk vs Israel on the Iran War](/Israel_Main_Suspect/iran-war-charlie-vs-israel)).

### July 2025 — The visit, the speech, and the meeting without TPUSA

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits the United States ([Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm)).

* **July 9, 2025** — Researchers note the FBI's Salt Lake City Special Agent in Charge, **[Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed)**, is tied in circulating timelines to Israel activity in this same window; her replacement arrives from Israel on **September 1** ([Salt Lake City Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office), [Key Officials Swapped in the Weeks Before the Event](/CoverUp/Pre_Event_Personnel_Swaps)).

* **July 11, 2025** — Kirk allows **anti-Israel speakers** at his events, and the pro-Israel side of his donor base reacts sharply. The same day, **[Dustin Gard-Weiss](/People/dustin-gard-weiss)** is announced as the new CIA executive director, replacing **[John Ratcliffe](/People/john-ratcliffe)** — a coincidence of dates that citizen researchers flag without claiming causation ([TPUSA Before vs After Charlie](/Israel_Main_Suspect/tpusa-before-vs-after)).

* **July 13–14, 2025** — At TPUSA's Florida student event, **Dave Smith** debates **Josh Hammer**, and **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** takes the stage. Researchers describe Kirk telling Carlson to "go all the way" in a speech against Israel — the moment they say launched the open war with his top donors ([Tucker Carlson on Evolving Israel Views](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel), [MAGA Movement and Antisemitism Debate](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate)).

* **July 18, 2025** — Some citizen timelines assert a decision to kill Kirk in Utah was made around this date. This is an **allegation with no released document behind it**, tracked as a claim on [July 18 Decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — Egyptian jet **SU-BTT** makes its **first-ever trip to America**, to an army base in Nebraska, on the same day a military meeting with foreign leaders is said to have taken place on U.S. soil ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [Nebraska Army Base July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20), [Foreign Leaders Meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20)).

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** convened by the **Faith and Freedom Coalition**, the **Latino Coalition for Israel**, and the **Philos Project** (funded by Paul Singer) to discuss "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people" and to combat "growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement." Attendees reportedly included **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)**, **[Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)**, **[Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick)**, **[Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun)**, and Netanyahu's son **[Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)**. Kirk and TPUSA were **absent** ([July 31 White House Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA), [Why TPUSA Was Absent on July 31](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31), [Woke-Right Defamation Frame](/Before/Woke-Right-Defamation)).

### August 2025 — The Hamptons and the offer

* **August 2, 2025** — French legionnaires arrive in America for three weeks of training in California, scheduled to end **August 25** ([French Foreign Legion Claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims)).

* **August 4–6, 2025** — The **Hamptons meeting**. Kirk is confronted by pro-Israel figures reportedly including **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **Josh Hammer**, **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**, **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)** and **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)**. Kirk left describing it as an **"intervention."** Dillon initially said publicly on **September 16** that there was "no angry confrontation"; under oath he later acknowledged a **"heated and at times spirited debate"** ([Hamptons Intervention Meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting), [The Hamptons Intervention and the Death Question](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention), [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons)).

* **August 4–6, 2025** — In the same window, Netanyahu is reported to have **called Kirk and offered TPUSA funding**, and to have pressed for a trip to Israel; Kirk reportedly declined the trip ([The Reported Final Netanyahu Call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call), [Rejected Israeli Funding](/Iran/funding-rejection-claims)).

* **August 6, 2025** — Kirk appears with **Megyn Kelly** and vents about donors pressuring him ([The Megyn Kelly Show](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly)).

* **August 25, 2025** — An unrelated-looking but frequently paired date: the **AES** delivery deadline for a miniaturized anti-personnel charge contract, sixteen days before September 10 ([The DoD Contract](/Mic/DoD_Contract), [AES Heber City to Nashville Flight](/Mic/aes-heber-nashville-flight)).

### September 1–9, 2025 — The final nine days

* **September 1, 2025** — **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)** takes over the FBI's Salt Lake City field office, arriving from Israel per circulating accounts, weeks before the event he would help brief ([Salt Lake City Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office)).

* **September 1–5, 2025** — In private group chats Kirk writes that he has **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** ([Reported Charlie Kirk Quotes About Israel and Donors](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel)).

* **September 4, 2025** — **SU-BTT** flies from France into **Provo, Utah** ([Egyptian Jets — Provo Sept 4–10](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo)).

* **September 8, 2025** — **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** later publishes what she describes as authenticated WhatsApp messages from a nine-person chat on this date, in which Kirk writes *"Just lost another huge Jewish donor. $2 million a year because we won't cancel Tucker"* and *"Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes. I cannot and will not be bullied like this. Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."* TPUSA spokesman **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** has publicly confirmed the donor texts as genuine ([Leaked Kirk Texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts), [The Donor Texts in the Final 48 Hours](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours)).

* **September 8, 2025** — Roughly twelve lieutenant colonels are reported at **Fort Huachuca**, Arizona, with SAM702 departing **September 9 at 00:49** ([Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9), [Fort Huachuca and Military Timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline)).

* **September 8, 2025** — Egypt's **[Abdel Fattah El-Sisi](/People/abdel-fattah-el-sisi)** meets the USCENTCOM commander, two days before the shooting ([Egyptian Foreign Operations — Provo](/intelligence/Egyptian_Foreign_Ops)).

* **September 9, 2025** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)**'s show and criticizes Israel **to Shapiro's face**. Donor-pressure researchers treat this as the point of no return ([Charlie, Israel and the Donors Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive)).

* **September 9, 2025** — Owens states that three sources told her Kirk said he believed **"they're going to kill me."** The specific phrasing has never been published in a screenshot or confirmed by any filing ([They Are Going to Kill Me Text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text), [Charlie Said Israel Would Kill Him](/Israel_Main_Suspect/charlie-said-israel-would-kill-him)).

* **September 9, 2025** — A **high-pressure meeting** is described, during which Kirk reportedly texted others the same warning ([High-Pressure Meeting Sept 9](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9)).

### September 10–11, 2025 — The day and the phones

* **September 10, 7:08 AM MDT** — **SU-BTT** departs Utah for Wilmington, hours before the shot ([SU-BTT Plane Movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline), [The Egyptian Jet SU-BTT](/Suspicious/Israel/egyptian-jet-su-btt)).

* **September 10, 12:23:30 PM MDT** — The shot at Utah Valley University.

* **September 10, ~12:56 PM – 1:13 PM MDT (calculated)** — Working forward from the shot through transport, unloading, and 20–30 minutes in the OR, the earliest realistic pronouncement window lands here — and researchers assert the **Jerusalem Post had already announced his death** before it ([The OR Timeline Versus the Jerusalem Post](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post), [Media vs Citizen Investigation Timeline](./media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline)).

* **September 10 (claim)** — Investigator **@Cully_1985** and others assert raw GPS data showed **44 foreign-registered cellphones** near Kirk that day: **16 Israeli, 13 Chinese, 12 Russian, 3 Iranian**. This is an unverified private-dataset claim; no agency has confirmed it ([Sixteen Israeli-Registered Phones at UVU](/Suspicious/Israel/israeli-phones-at-uvu), [Foreign Phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU), [Israeli Phones and Bumblehive](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israeli_Phones_Bumblehive)).

* **September 11, 2025** — **SU-BTT** leaves the United States for Egypt via Wilmington ([Post-Assassination Investigations](./post-assassination-investigations)).

### After — where the Israel thread goes

* **Later 2025 (claim)** — Four of the alleged sixteen Israeli-registered devices are said to have surfaced at the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** during U.S.–Israel–Iran talks. Again: an unverified claim from the same private dataset ([Four of Those Phones Later at the Islamabad Serena](/Suspicious/Israel/phones-islamabad-serena), [Islamabad Serena Link](/intelligence/Islamabad_Serena_Link)).

* **Late October 2025** — *New York Times* reporting describes a White House dispute over counterterrorism review of **foreign leads**, and the counterterrorism inquiry led by **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)** being wound down ([FBI Halt of Joe Kent's Counterterrorism Leads](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt), [Israel Foreign Leads](/Israel/foreign-leads)).

* **2026** — The Jerusalem Post reports Israel's coalition approving a large **hasbara** public-diplomacy budget for 2026, which investigators cite when arguing narrative operations are a funded capability, not a theory ([Online Pro-Israel Narrative Control](/Israel_Main_Suspect/online-narrative-control)).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Date of the Hamptons intervention | Aug 4–6, 2025 | Some accounts place an "intervention" as late as Sept 9; the master file flags both |
| What happened in the Hamptons | Seth Dillon, Sept 16: "no angry confrontation," an enjoyable retreat | Dillon under oath: a "heated and at times spirited debate"; Kirk called it an intervention |
| "They are going to kill me" | Owens: three sources, said Sept 9 | No screenshot, filing, or TPUSA statement has ever shown the phrase |
| The donor texts | Confirmed genuine by TPUSA's spokesman | Their meaning — business frustration vs a break with a movement — is contested |
| The 16 Israeli phones | Presented as raw GPS telemetry | No agency, court filing, or independent audit has verified the dataset |
| Jerusalem Post timing | Death announced before the earliest possible pronouncement | Skeptics note wire desks routinely publish on official confirmation, and that pronouncement math is an estimate |
| The July 18 "decision" date | Asserted in citizen timelines | No document, source, or filing has ever been produced |

## Open Questions

1. What is the exact publication timestamp of the Jerusalem Post's first report of Kirk's death, and who was the source?
2. Who attended the July 31 White House meeting in full, and why was the largest youth organization in the movement not in the room?
3. What did Netanyahu and Kirk actually discuss on the reported August call, and does any record of the funding offer exist?
4. Have the "they're going to kill me" communications been preserved, and are they in the possession of any investigating agency?
5. Who holds the underlying telemetry behind the 44-foreign-phones claim, and can it be independently audited without exposing the seller?
6. Why was the counterterrorism review of foreign leads wound down, and on whose instruction?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The Jerusalem Post's first-publication timestamp and its source chain, the full attendee list and minutes of the July 31 White House meeting, the complete unredacted donor-chat record from the final month, the preserved communications behind the reported "they are going to kill me" warnings, the underlying telemetry and provenance for the foreign-registered-phone dataset, the flight and passenger manifests for SU-BTT and the other Egyptian-registered aircraft in Provo between July 20 and September 11, and the internal record of who ordered the foreign-lead counterterrorism review halted are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
