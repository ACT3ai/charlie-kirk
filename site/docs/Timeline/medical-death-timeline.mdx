# Medical & Death Timeline — Shot to Pronouncement

This timeline covers one narrow window: **12:23:30 PM MDT on September 10, 2025**, when
[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot at UVU, through the reported **1:03:30 PM MDT**
pronouncement of death at Timpanogos Regional Hospital in Orem. Roughly forty minutes. Almost
every minute inside it is contested — the transport method, the hospital chosen, who performed
CPR, and whether a foreign newspaper announced the death before the surgeon walked out of the
operating room. Suspect movement and the campus manhunt belong to
[the minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute) and
[the ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot); this page stays with the body.

## The Timeline

### 12:23 PM — The shot and the scene

* **Sept 10, 2025 — 12:23:30 PM MDT (T+00:00)** — The shot. Most compilations on this site anchor
  here, though a competing **12:27 PM** figure circulates in early media timelines — see
  [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time) for which sources assert which.

* **12:23:48 PM (T+00:18)** — Security begins moving Kirk from the tent, per the movement log
  reproduced on the [Medical section's death timeline](/Medical/Death_Timeline) and
  [the minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **12:23:57 PM (T+00:27)** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is already in position
  on the rock pile filming, a placement citizen researchers flag as unusually fast on
  [the rock pile analysis](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile) and in
  [crime-scene camera actions](/cameras/Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions).

* **12:24:01 PM (T+00:31)** — Kirk is loaded into a black GMC Yukon Denali — a **private SUV, not an
  ambulance**. That vehicle's interior later became evidence in its own right on
  [the SUV transport page](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie), and the vehicle was reportedly
  destroyed afterward per [SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed).

* **12:24:03 PM (T+00:33)** — [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) is well past the SUV, and
  [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) is not in frame — the seed of the
  [call-witness conflict](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict) noted below.

* **12:24:12 PM (T+00:42)** — [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) gets into the SUV. Witness accounts
  describe him performing CPR during transport; see
  [Chest Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) and
  [Shot to the Heart](/Witnesses/shot-to-the-heart) for how those descriptions vary.

* **12:24:22 PM (T+00:52)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying *"They just shot Charlie."*

* **12:24:13–12:24:35 PM (T+00:43–01:05)** — The SUV departs for the hospital, per
  [the Medical section's death timeline](/Medical/Death_Timeline) and
  [Charlie's Trip to Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital).

* **12:24:36 PM (T+01:06)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying *"He's dead"* — roughly 66 seconds after
  the shot and about 39 minutes before the reported pronouncement, a gap discussed on
  [Witness Account Conflicts](/Medical/Witness_Account_Conflicts).

### 12:25–12:40 PM — Transport

* **~12:25 PM** — Investigators model roughly **one minute** from shot to departure, then a
  **7–10 minute drive**. No ambulance and no sirens appear in the eyewitness accounts summarized on
  [Emergency Response](/Medical/Hospital/emergency_response) and
  [Hospital Scene EMS](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems).

* **~12:25–12:35 PM** — The destination is **Timpanogos Regional Hospital** in Orem, a **Level IV**
  trauma center about **5.8 miles / 12–15 minutes** away — not **Utah Valley Hospital**, the
  **Level II** center **4.2 miles / 8–12 minutes** away. Utah County protocol for serious gunshot
  wounds reportedly directs transport past Level IV and V facilities to the Level II center. That
  deviation is the core of [Hospital Choice & Transport](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport)
  and [the Timpanogos facility page](/Medical/Timpanogos_Facility).

* **Same window (unverified claim)** — Sourcing summarized on
  [Hospital People & Bullet Claims](/Medical/hospital_people_and_bullet_claims) reports a bomb scare
  at Intermountain Health Utah Valley Regional at approximately the same time, with staff
  reportedly asked to sign NDAs. This has not been independently confirmed; it is tracked alongside
  [Hospital Footage and NDAs](/CoverUp/Hospital_Footage_And_NDAs).

* **Disputed, same window** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has publicly asked why a
  medical file for Kirk reportedly exists at **Utah Valley Hospital** if he was taken to
  Timpanogos. The claim originates from an unnamed medical staffer and is unverified; it is logged
  on [Hospital People & Bullet Claims](/Medical/hospital_people_and_bullet_claims).

### 12:33–12:45 PM — Arrival and intake

* **~12:33–12:40 PM** — Arrival at Timpanogos. An eyewitness account circulated on
  [social platforms](/Media/social-media-platforms) describes bodyguards meeting staff **outside the
  surgery doors** rather than through the emergency entrance, and describes Kirk as already
  unresponsive on arrival. Recorded on [Events at Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital).

* **~12:33–12:43 PM** — Investigators model **5–10 minutes minimum** to unload, admit, and move a
  patient into an operating room. The [911 call record](/Medical/timpanogos_911_call) and
  [Emergency Response](/Medical/Hospital/emergency_response) pages track what is and is not public
  about this interval.

* **~12:35 PM (unverified claim)** — A second SUV is described arriving behind the first, reportedly
  driven by chief of staff [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), who — according to a single
  hospital-contractor account — drove to physician parking rather than the ER, picked up a
  passenger, and left. Single-source and unverified; see
  [Events at Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital).

* **Reported, same afternoon** — The FBI is described as moving quickly to seize interior hospital
  footage while the campus manhunt was still active, per
  [Hospital Footage Seizure](/FBI/Hospital_Footage_Seizure) and
  [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed). Commentators
  also describe the ER lobby as unusually empty; no official explanation of either point is public.

### 12:43–1:03 PM — Operating room and pronouncement

* **~12:43–1:03 PM** — Investigators model **20–30 minutes in the operating room** before a surgeon
  emerged. No hospital record establishing these intervals is public; they are reconstruction, as
  [the Medical section states plainly](/Medical/Death_Timeline).

* **1:03:30 PM MDT** — The reported pronouncement of death by the surgeon who came out of the OR.
  This is the single most-cited hard time in the medical window and appears in the master
  investigation file as **(1:03:30 MT)**.

* **Modeled total** — 1 minute to leave, plus 7–10 minutes driving, plus 5–10 minutes to reach the
  OR, plus 20–30 minutes in the OR = **33–50 minutes**, producing an end window of
  **12:56 PM – 1:13 PM MDT**. The 1:03:30 figure sits inside that window, which is why researchers
  treat the model as roughly sound and then ask what happened outside it.

* **The Jerusalem Post conflict** — The master file records that the **Jerusalem Post had already
  announced Kirk's death** before the modeled pronouncement window closed. If that reporting
  timestamp holds up, either the death was known outside the hospital before the official
  pronouncement, or the outlet worked from a different time source. Neither branch has been
  publicly reconciled; see [Media Timeline Compilation](/Media/Media-Timeline-Compilation) and
  [September 10 Breaking News](/Media/september-10-breaking-news).

### After the pronouncement

* **Afternoon of Sept 10** — The treating surgeon is publicly described as calling the wound an
  *"absolute miracle"* in the sense that the bullet did not exit and therefore did not endanger
  bystanders — a description that sits uneasily with front-neck-exit theories on
  [Wound Trajectory Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle) and
  [Trajectory Wound Mismatch](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Trajectory_Wound_Mismatch). No name was attached to
  the quote in the coverage summarized on [Doctors](/Medical/doctors).

* **Aug 19 / Aug 25, 2025 (background)** — **Andrew Zenger** was reported appointed CEO of
  Timpanogos Regional Hospital on August 19 and to have started August 25, weeks before the
  shooting. A further citizen claim that he joined the surgical team on August 28 is unverified.
  Tracked on [Hospital Leader](/Medical/hospital_leader) and
  [Medical Staff Changes](/Medical/medical_staff_changes). Recording this timing alleges no
  wrongdoing by any person named.

* **July 1, 2025 (background)** — **Dr. [Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** began full-time as
  Utah's chief medical examiner, roughly ten weeks before the shooting. Her post-death role is
  covered on [Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner) and in the sibling
  [autopsy and forensic timeline](./autopsy-forensic-timeline).

* **July 2025 (background, claim)** — Google Trends data cited by citizen researchers reportedly
  shows Israeli-IP searches for *Timpanogos Regional Hospital*, for Amaro, and for seven Timpanogos
  surgeons in a two-week window around July 20. Trends data does not identify individual searchers;
  see [Israel IP Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns) and
  [Israel Google Searches — Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people).

* **Later, date not fixed (disputed)** — The Yukon Denali used for transport was reportedly cut up
  and melted down. If accurate, that removes a blood-spatter and trajectory reference from any
  future forensic reconstruction. See [SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed) and
  [the SUV interior photographs](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B | Who asserts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Time of the shot | 12:23:30 PM MDT | 12:27 PM MDT | Police-audio and citizen reconstructions favor 12:23; some early media compilations print 12:27 — [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time) |
| When death was announced | Pronounced 1:03:30 PM at the hospital | Jerusalem Post reported the death before the modeled window closed | The master investigation file records both; no outlet has published its internal timestamp trail |
| Transport | Private SUV, no ambulance, no sirens | A standard EMS response | Eyewitness accounts and video support the SUV; no public EMS run sheet confirms or contradicts — [Hospital Scene EMS](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems) |
| Hospital chosen | Timpanogos Regional, Level IV, 5.8 mi | Utah Valley Hospital, Level II, 4.2 mi, is protocol for gunshot wounds | Citizen medical analysts raise the deviation; no official explanation is public — [Hospital Choice & Transport](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport) |
| CPR in the SUV | Frank Turek described performing CPR | Other accounts describe the wound as immediately unsurvivable, making CPR moot | Eyewitness retellings differ — [Chest Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) |
| Wound location described | Neck | Chest or heart in several first-hour statements | [Shot in Chest Reversal](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal) documents the shift |
| "He's dead" at T+01:06 | Farnsworth's on-camera statement | Official pronouncement ~39 minutes later | Both are on the record; commentators read the gap differently |
| Erika Kirk call time | [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) says McCoy called her at 12:23 PM MDT with Neff witnessing | Video at 12:24:02 shows McCoy alone, Neff not in frame | Raised in the master file as a reconstruction problem, not an accusation — [McCoy/Neff call conflict](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict) |

## Open Questions

1. What does the hospital's own arrival timestamp say, and does it fall inside the modeled
   12:33–12:40 PM window?
2. Who made the transport decision — the security detail or a dispatcher — and was Utah Valley
   Hospital considered and rejected?
3. What time did the Jerusalem Post publish, in what time zone, and from what source?
4. Which physician pronounced death at 1:03:30 PM, and does that physician's name appear on the
   [death certificate](/Medical/Autopsy/death_certificate)?
5. Was CPR performed in the SUV, by whom, and for how long?
6. Where is the interior hospital footage the FBI reportedly seized, and does it show the arrival
   and the described second SUV?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The hospital arrival log and the operating-room entry timestamp, the 911 and dispatch audio behind
the transport decision, the trauma-triage justification for choosing a Level IV center over a
Level II center, the seized interior hospital camera footage, the identity of the pronouncing
physician, and the publication metadata behind the earliest death announcements are things that
the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that
aren't out yet.
