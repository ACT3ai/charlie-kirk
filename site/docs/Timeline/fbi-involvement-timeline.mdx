# FBI Involvement & Announcements Timeline

This page follows one actor: the **FBI**, from the August 2025 change of leadership at the Salt
Lake City field office through the announcements of September 10–12, 2025, the halting of parallel
inquiries by other agencies, the ballistics transfer after the ATF returned an inconclusive result,
and the discovery and records fights that followed. Nothing here establishes that any named
official committed a crime; the entries are timestamps, public statements, and documented
disputes. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged and has pleaded not guilty — he has
not been convicted**, and every framing on this page should be read against that.

## The Timeline

### Before the shooting

* **February 2025** — [Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed) is appointed Special Agent in Charge of the FBI's Salt Lake City
  field office, the office that would later run the case — background on
  [the SLC Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office).

* **August 2025** — Syed is replaced by **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)** amid broader FBI
  leadership changes under Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel). The change lands roughly a month before the
  shooting, which is why it appears in [Pre-Event Personnel Swaps](/CoverUp/Pre_Event_Personnel_Swaps)
  alongside the [hospital](/Medical/hospital_leader) and
  [judicial](/court/judge-tony-graf) appointments made in the same window. Personnel timing is
  documented here as timing only. Some commentators (e.g. [@SeanMcCarthyCom](/Influencers/x/seanmccarthycom)) describe Syed as
  **"fired"** rather than reassigned; the page records the leadership change without adjudicating
  the label. Citizen accounts also note Bohls's reported **cyber** specialty and question how
  digital "chatter" later attributed to the case was not intercepted earlier — **timing commentary,
  not proof of misconduct.**

* **Sept 1, 2025 (claim)** — Citizen researchers report an Israeli-IP Google search spike on Bohls'
  name, and a July 9 spike on Syed's, from Google Trends data. Trends data cannot identify
  individual searchers; see [Israel IP Search Patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns).

### September 10, 2025 — Day of the shooting

* **12:23 PM MDT** — Kirk is shot at UVU. The FBI takes a lead federal role almost immediately; the
  scene response is covered on [FBI Investigation](/FBI/FBI_Investigation) and in
  [the ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot).

* **Afternoon (reported)** — The FBI reportedly seizes interior footage from
  [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) while the campus manhunt
  is still active — [Hospital Footage Seizure](/FBI/Hospital_Footage_Seizure)
  and [Hospital Footage and NDAs](/CoverUp/Hospital_Footage_And_NDAs). The
  [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline) covers what was happening inside the
  building at that hour, and the separate question of whether a nearer Level II facility was passed
  is on [trauma center bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **Afternoon — early POIs (reported)** — [Zachariah Qureshi](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi) is detained and, per multiple
  accounts, publicly presented as a suspect in custody, then **released roughly an hour and a half
  later** without a public explanation. Citizen reconstructions (e.g. [@TheCKFiles1](/Influencers/x/theckfiles1)) map a tighter
  window: Director Kash Patel reportedly characterizing a **"subject in custody" around ~4:21 PM**,
  a **weapon find around ~5:40 PM**, and a **release around ~5:59 PM** — timestamps that **need
  primary-clip verification**. Commentators argue the premature announcement may have
  paused the wider search; that is an inference, not an established fact. His profile as a
  distraction figure is on [Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi), and
  [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) collect the statements. **Qureshi was not charged.**

* **Afternoon — Qureshi / scanner layer (claims)** — Police-scanner breakdowns circulating online
  (e.g. [@iluminomoly](/Influencers/x/iluminomoly)) describe confusion over vehicle descriptions, including a **Nissan Armada**
  later associated in commentary with FBI presence, and name **Qureshi** and
  **[Andrew Piskadlo](/distraction_people/Andrew_Piskadlo)**
  among people detained or questioned. Separate reports claim Qureshi was seen **driving
  hospital-to-hospital** and that a **long rifle** was associated with a vehicle stop, and that
  local LE was told the **FBI would handle** the stop — **reported scanner/commentary claims, not
  adjudicated findings**. [Fox News](/Media/fox-news-coverage) and other outlets named Qureshi and
  **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** among early
  persons of interest who were **ruled out / released**; the wider reversal cluster is on
  [the distraction events timeline](./distraction-events-timeline).

* **Afternoon — George Zinn (parallel early POI)** — **George Zinn** was arrested after a public
  false claim of being the shooter. Later public accounts describe an **obstruction** case and a
  **January 29, 2026** sentencing on obstruction (with separate, non-assassination counts reported
  in secondary summaries). Officials treat the Zinn episode as **unrelated to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)
  as the charged shooter**. Logged here as an **FBI/local early-POI announcement layer**, not as an
  alternate-assassin finding.

* **Afternoon–evening — bomb dogs (expanded claims)** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has stated that the FBI did
  not want bomb dogs taken to certain areas of the scene. Beyond that single claim, citizen
  researchers (e.g. @MeOhMyTruth, @SallyResearch) add a micro-timeline: K9 activity near the
  **library ~3:04 PM**; dogs **not permitted near [the tent](/UVU/Tent)**; dogs **failing to alert on
  the [wooded rifle cache](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery)** later associated with the official
  find; and later courtyard / Fugel Gateway K9
  movement. The claim is documented on [Bomb Dogs](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs), on
  [bomb dogs kept away](/Suspicious/FBI/bomb-dogs-kept-away), and folded into
  [the six-item anomaly convergence list](/FBI/Rigged_Mic_Explains_Anomalies). **No public agency
  after-action report resolves these claims.**

* **Reported, same day** — Witnesses including [Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird) have described federal agents asking them
  to delete phone video. This is a witness allegation, not an adjudicated finding — see
  [FBI Asked to Delete Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video) and
  [Witness Footage Pressure](/Censorship/Witness_Footage_Pressure).

* **Reported** — The FBI's shooting-reconstruction team is described as delayed reaching the scene
  because agency aircraft were in use for the director's travel. Reported by
  [Steve Cameron](/People/steve-cameron) and
  [others on X](/Media/social-media-platforms); no official confirmation exists.
  Scene condition in those hours matters because of
  [the later paving of the site](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving).

### September 11, 2025 — Photos, press conference, and custody

* **6:25 PM MDT** — Court documents cited by attorney and commentator
  [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) place a
  Mirandizing of Tyler Robinson at the
  [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff). This is the pivot point
  for everything that follows on this page — [Baron Coleman's early turn-in
  argument](/court/mirandize/baron-coleman-early-turn-in) and
  [the Mirandizing timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).

* **7:17 PM CDT (6:17 PM MDT) — disputed** — Coleman argues the second set of stairwell photos was
  released while Robinson was already in custody and being Mirandized. The dispute is documented on
  [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos),
  [enhanced photos released after custody](/Suspicious/FBI/enhanced-photos-after-custody), and
  [the cover-up section's version](/CoverUp/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).

* **7:55 PM MDT** — Per an AP-based timeline, the FBI says it is looking for a **"person of
  interest"** and releases photos of a figure in a hat, sunglasses, long-sleeved black shirt and
  [backpack](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility), plus rooftop video —
  [Black Clothing Photo](/FBI/Black_Clothing_Photo) and
  [the Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center).

* **7:57 PM MDT** — The alleged [Discord "confession" messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages)
  appear — about ninety minutes after
  the 6:25 PM Mirandizing time. The conflict is the whole subject of
  [Discord Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline) and
  [the custody vs Discord timeline](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

* **Sept 11, daytime (Bohls podium)** — Widely shared video clips (e.g. @ThePatriotOasis, @gaye_gallops)
  show SLC SAC **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)** stating that investigators had found **the
  weapon**, **[palm prints](/Gun_Bullet/Rifle_Fingerprints_Mismatch)**, and **footprint
  impressions**, and calling for identification of the
  person responsible. Attribute as **public SAC remarks** during the manhunt messaging window —
  distinct from mere attendance at the evening conference.

* **7:58–8:00 PM MDT** — The Utah County press conference releases the "enhanced" stairwell images
  and the rooftop video. Attendees reported include Governor
  [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox), Lt. Gov. [Deidre Henderson](/People/deidre-henderson),
  Director Kash Patel, Deputy Director [Dan Bongino](/People/dan-bongino), Utah County Sheriff
  [Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith), and SAC [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls). Patel attended
  but did not speak — [FBI press conference media](/Media/fbi-press-conference-media). Public
  misidentification cascades from the enhanced photos (including internet "IDs" of living people
  later ruled out) are a documented side-effect of the release cycle; handle any named misID with
  care.

* **8:02 PM MDT** — Washington County Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) says he
  received a call from a retired deputy about the suspect's identity. Critics note the sheriff's
  office had, on Coleman's reading of the documents, already Mirandized Robinson 97 minutes
  earlier — [Washington County Sheriff](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff).

* **8:04 PM MDT** — Brooksby is said to have called Sheriff Mike Smith, who was reportedly standing
  at the press conference at that moment. Both times are drawn from official statements; the
  tension is raised by citizen investigators such as
  [Gray Hughes](/People/gray-hughes) and Coleman.

* **~9:00–10:00 PM MDT** — Robinson turns himself in with his parents; Utah County officer
  [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) is listed as the arresting officer at 10:00 PM. Coleman's
  drive-time argument — a 3.5-to-4-hour trip from Utah County — is on
  [the arrest and booking affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline).

### September 12–17, 2025 — The manhunt narrative

* **Sept 12, ~6:00 AM MDT** — President [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) announces a suspect is
  in custody.

* **Sept 12, ~7:00–7:30 AM MDT** — Patel publicly characterizes the search as a **"33-hour
  manhunt."** Measured from 12:23 PM Sept 10, 33 hours lands at about 9:23 PM Sept 11 — roughly
  three hours after the 6:25 PM Mirandizing time in the documents Coleman cites. From 6:25 PM the
  elapsed figure is closer to **30 hours**. See
  [the Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline).

* **Sept 15, 2025** — Patel states on [Fox News](/Media/fox-news-coverage) that DNA on
  [a towel around the rifle](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/rifle-towel-on-roof) and on a
  screwdriver found on the rooftop matched Robinson. Earlier CNN reporting on September 11 had
  placed the screwdriver near the rifle, and the September 16 charging documents reportedly omit
  the screwdriver entirely — [Official Narrative](/FBI/Official_Narrative) and
  [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

* **Sept 17, 2025** — Brooksby holds a press conference reaffirming the 33-hour manhunt and the
  8:02 PM and 8:04 PM calls. Coleman argues the account cannot be reconciled with the 6:25 PM
  document; the sheriff's office has not publicly responded to that argument.

* **Reported, "I'll see you in Valhalla"** — Patel's early podium remarks, including the Valhalla
  reference and the number 33, drew sustained criticism, including from Buckley Carlson —
  [the Buckley Carlson video](/Videos/buckley-carlson-kash-patel-valhalla) and
  [Valhalla as a location page](/Locations/Valhalla).

### Blocking other investigations

* **~Sept 16, 2025 (claim)** — Russell Brand says two cabinet-meeting sources told him that about
  six days after the death, Patel, [Tulsi Gabbard](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard) and Vice
  President [JD Vance](/People/jd-vance) said the administration was "not looking at foreign
  involvement" and "not looking at
  any accomplices domestically." Second-hand and unverified —
  [Russell Brand's cabinet claims](/Israel/Russell_Brand_Cabinet_Claims).

* **Fall 2025 (reported)** — [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent), then director of the
  [National Counterterrorism Center](/US_Intelligence/nctc), was reportedly investigating whether
  foreign powers were involved, and
  that inquiry was shut down. Reporting attributed to the Daily Mail describes Patel as angry that
  Kent went through FBI material on the case — [Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt),
  [Patel shut down the Kent probe](/Suspicious/FBI/patel-shut-down-kent-probe), and
  [Joe Kent Foreign Inquiry Shutdown](/Proof_Intel_Services/Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown).

* **Reported** — Local law enforcement was
  [told it could not conduct its own investigation](/Suspicious/FBI/locals-told-not-to-investigate),
  and parallel intelligence-community work was halted — [CIA Block](/FBI/CIA_Block),
  [CIA Blocked](/CoverUp/CIA_Blocked), and [White House Halt of IC work](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC).

* **Reported** — Witness interviews by other bodies were blocked or discouraged —
  [Witness Interviews Blocked](/CoverUp/Witness_Interviews_Blocked) and
  [FBI Witness Coordination](/FBI/Witness_Coordination).

* **Claim** — Owens has stated that the FBI's Connecticut office,
  which she describes as sharing space with the ADL, was sent to work the Utah investigation.
  Unverified — [FBI Connecticut](/FBI/Connecticut) and
  [the ADL floor-sharing claim](/Suspicious/FBI/suv-destroyed-adl-floor).

### Ballistics and forensics

* **Recovered at [autopsy](/Medical/Autopsy/overview)** — One bullet jacket fragment and four lead fragments.
  The jacket is identified as **.30 caliber class** —
  [ATF Fragment Inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive).

* **ATF result** — The jacket shares **class** characteristics with the
  [Mauser 98](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) attributed to
  Robinson, so the rifle **cannot be excluded**, but the fragment **lacks individual
  characteristics** permitting identification to one rifle. The ATF summary is, in its own words,
  **inconclusive** — [ATF Inconclusive Ballistics](/Proof_Not_Tyler/ATF_Inconclusive_Ballistics).
  The defense has signalled it will use the state's own ATF report as exculpatory.

* **Transfer to the FBI (disputed)** — Citizen researchers reading the filings allege the FBI took up
  **[Comparative Bullet Lead Analysis (CBLA)](/Suspicious/FBI/cbla-bullet-lead-test)** — a technique
  the FBI itself abandoned in 2005 after
  concluding it had "flawed scientific, statistical, and interpretive validity," and which
  underpinned wrongful convictions such as the Jimmy Yates case. Whether CBLA is actually being run
  in this case is contested, not established — [Ballistics ATF/CBLA](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla) and
  [Ballistics Sealed CBLA](/CoverUp/Ballistics_Sealed_CBLA).

* **Jan 9, 2026** — A defense motion objects to the State proceeding with **Virtual Comparison
  Microscopy** on the jacket fragment and unspecified metallurgical testing of a lead fragment,
  after requests to photograph the fragment in its current state and observe testing were
  [denied](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/denied-access-to-testing).
  An FBI analyst who received the fragment from the ATF noted part of it had become **detached in
  the packaging** — [FBI Ballistics Forensics](/FBI/Ballistics_Forensics).

* **July 2026 — preliminary hearing (open-court forensics)** — According to hearing summaries
  circulated on X, the medical examiner's autopsy (single GSW, homicide) was **admitted**, and the
  **ATF toolmark result remained inconclusive** on the jacket fragment versus the recovered Mauser
  — the fragment **cannot identify or exclude** that rifle at the individual level. Defense
  highlighted the state's own ATF report; the State relied on DNA, video, and alleged confessions.
  Expert and industry rebuttals (e.g. ammunition-tech commentary on Cor-Lokt jacket-separation
  theories) continue in public discourse. **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) remains
  charged and not convicted.** See [Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) and
  [ATF Inconclusive Ballistics](/Proof_Not_Tyler/ATF_Inconclusive_Ballistics).

* **Ongoing** — Standard tests researchers say were skipped, including gunshot-residue testing, are
  catalogued on [Forensic Tests Skipped](/CoverUp/Forensic_Tests_Skipped) and
  [No GSR and Physical Tests](/Proof_Not_Tyler/No_GSR_And_Physical_Tests).

### Records, discovery, and disclosure

* **Spring 2026** — The case stalls over discovery. Commentators argue the case should not proceed
  until material said to be withheld is produced — [FBI Discovery Delays](/FBI/Discovery_Delays),
  [FBI and ATF discovery withholding](/Suspicious/FBI/discovery-withholding),
  [discovery and Brady disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes), and
  [the discovery and Brady evidence timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

* **Ongoing** — Requests for the Washington County surrender video were refused, and the office
  later said the video had been deleted under a 30-day retention policy, while pleadings suggest
  footage does exist in law-enforcement hands — [Sheriff Video Withheld](/CoverUp/Sheriff_Video_Withheld)
  and [the bodycam GRAMA response](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage). The parallel campus
  records fight over redacted GRAMA productions is on
  [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **Ongoing (claim)** — Archive services and platform records relevant to the case are described by
  researchers as being purged or taken down. These are claims by investigators, not findings —
  [Archive.is Purge](/CoverUp/Archive_Is_Purge) and
  [Videos Deleted Remotely](/CoverUp/Videos_Deleted_Remotely).

* **March 27, 2026** — Sheriff Brooksby resigns without a stated public explanation. The timing is
  noted by researchers; no cause has been established —
  [Brooksby's resignation](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation) and
  [Institutional Resignations](/After/Institutional_Resignations).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Official version | Competing version | Who asserts the competing version |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Length of the search | "33-hour manhunt" (Patel, Sept 12) | ~30 hours, if custody began at 6:25 PM Sept 11 | Baron Coleman, [Gray Hughes](/People/gray-hughes), reading court documents |
| Why photos were released Sept 11 evening | To find an unidentified person of interest | Robinson was already in custody, so the release was narrative-building | [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos) |
| "Enhanced" images | Standard forensic enhancement | Enhancement means the images are not fair and accurate representations and should be excluded | Coleman, arguing on evidentiary grounds |
| First suspect / early POIs | Qureshi & Zinn released / ruled out; Robinson later charged | Premature "subject in custody" messaging (~4:21–5:59 PM clocks claimed) paused wider search | Citizen scanner analysts ([@TheCKFiles1](/Influencers/x/theckfiles1), [@iluminomoly](/Influencers/x/iluminomoly)); no full FBI minute log released |
| Ballistics | ATF could not exclude the Mauser 98; State relies on DNA/video/confessions | ATF was inconclusive under oath at prelim; CBLA/VCM disputes continue | Defense filings + July 2026 hearing summaries; citizen researchers for CBLA claim |
| Screwdriver | On the rooftop, with matching DNA (Patel, Sept 15) | Near the rifle (CNN, Sept 11); omitted from Sept 16 charging documents | Compare [Official Narrative](/FBI/Official_Narrative) |
| Foreign leads | Not pursued because there were none | An active NCTC inquiry was halted | Joe Kent's account and Daily Mail reporting — [FBI Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads) |
| Surrender video | Deleted under retention policy | Pleadings indicate footage exists | [Sheriff Video Withheld](/CoverUp/Sheriff_Video_Withheld) |

## Open Questions

1. What time, to the minute, did each FBI photo release go out, and what was the internal
   authorization chain for each?
2. Did the bureau know Robinson was in custody at the time of the 7:55–7:58 PM releases?
3. What exactly was done to the stairwell images, by whom, using what tool, and is the unaltered
   original preserved?
4. On whose authority was the NCTC foreign-involvement inquiry ended, and in what written form?
5. Is CBLA, or any successor lead-composition method, actually being applied to the recovered lead
   fragments?
6. What happened to the recovered bullet jacket fragment between ATF custody and FBI custody, and
   how did part of it become detached?
7. Can primary clips confirm the ~4:21 PM "subject in custody" / ~5:40 PM weapon / ~5:59 PM release
   clocks mapped to the Qureshi cycle?
8. Does any FBI after-action log address bomb-dog exclusion zones, the ~3:04 PM library K9 window,
   or the wooded rifle cache search?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The unredacted FBI case file and Form 302 witness interview reports, the internal authorization and
timestamp log for each suspect-photo release, the original unenhanced stairwell images and the
enhancement work product, the written order that ended the National Counterterrorism Center's
foreign-involvement inquiry, the complete ATF-to-FBI chain of custody for the bullet jacket
fragment, and the Washington County surrender video are things that the
[Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that
aren't out yet.
