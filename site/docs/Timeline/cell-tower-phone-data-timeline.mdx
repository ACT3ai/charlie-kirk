# Cell Tower, Phone Data & Movement Timeline (Claims)

:::caution What this page is and is not
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**. The foreign-device claims below come from a **private open-source analyst** and have **not** been corroborated by any agency, court filing, or published dataset. **Nothing here asserts that any named person or government carried out or knew of the killing.** Cell-tower data is also **coarse** — routinely overread as GPS-grade precision — and this page says so wherever it matters.
:::

This timeline follows one evidence class: **devices**. Where phones were, what they recorded, which messages carry timestamps, and — the recurring theme — **which device records demonstrably exist and have never been produced**. It runs from the morning of September 10, 2025 through the 2026 hearings and the Islamabad tracking claim. For the physical movement narrative see [Roof Jumping Timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline); for platform-side suppression see [Censorship & Information Control](./censorship-information-control-timeline).

## The Timeline

Times are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT/MT)** unless stated otherwise.

### September 10, 2025 — the device record of the day

* **8:29 AM MDT** — A gray Dodge Challenger is logged onto [UVU](/UVU/overview) campus by surveillance. Investigators note that the official summary **never states when the vehicle left the lot**, and ask why: a phone travelling that route would **ping every tower it passed**. See [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [Camera Sightings](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **11:53 AM MDT** — The movement summary records the figure on the stairs **pulling out a cell phone**, putting it back, then continuing toward the parking-garage stairwell. This is the single moment in the entire charged route where a device is **visibly in hand** — and no carrier record covering that minute has been made public. See [Roof Jumping Timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **12:23:30 PM MDT — the shot** — According to open-source analyst **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** (primary public post **April 11, 2026**, post ID **2042976110468542912**), raw GPS data shows **44 foreign-registered cellphones** in the immediate area of Charlie Kirk at that moment: **16 Israeli, 13 Chinese, 12 Russian, and 3 Iranian**. **No dataset, no methodology, and no independent verification has been published**, and the source's own graphic was labelled a recreation. The claim is catalogued at [Sixteen Israeli-Registered Phones at UVU](/Suspicious/Israel/israeli-phones-at-uvu), [Foreign Phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU) and [Chinese, Russian and Iranian presence claims](/intelligence/Chinese_Russian_Iranian_Presence). An earlier public wave (below) used a **~12 Israeli phones** figure before Seruga's 16/44.

* **12:23:30 PM MDT** — In the same second, [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) reportedly phones his wife [Elizabeth McCoy](/People/elizabeth-mccoy) — a call that, unlike almost everything else on this page, **would appear on an ordinary carrier record** if anyone subpoenaed it. See [the claim page on that call](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife).

* **12:23–12:26 PM MDT** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) says she was called at **12:23 PM MDT**; a competing citizen reconstruction places a **three-way call at 12:26 PM**. Carrier records would settle it in an afternoon and have never been publicly sought — see [Erika Kirk Timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline).

* **12:23–12:33 PM MDT** — Dozens of attendees are recording on phones through this window. Some of that footage is the material later alleged to have **disappeared from devices without the owners' action** — see [Ten Minutes After the Shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot) and [Witness Videos Deleted Remotely](/CoverUp/Videos_Deleted_Remotely).

* **Sept 10, 11:00 PM MDT** — Per **[Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite)** extraction testimony given in open court, the **first text message** of the charged Sept 10–11 exchange between Robinson and [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) was sent at this time. Note what that means: the messages entered the record through the **roommate's device**, not the defendant's — see [Lance Twiggs' Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) and [Tyler Robinson's Phone & Devices](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices). Hearing-era summaries also discuss the **content** of overnight texts (attempting to recover a rifle / abandoning it under police presence) and whether some messages were **scheduled/auto-sent** versus live typing — characterizations that matter for whether a human was at the keyboard; treat as **contested**.

### September 11–12, 2025 — custody, Discord, and timestamps

* **Sept 11, 2025 — afternoon arrival (family-report claim, published June 29, 2026)** — Per [@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen-2) / [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline) amplification of family statements, Robinson's **phone was confiscated as soon as he arrived** at the sheriff's office with parents and a retired sheriff. **Second-hand family reporting**, not a court finding — but it is a hard device-access datum if true.

* **Sept 11, 2025 — 6:25 PM (date disputed)** — A Bates-stamped defense exhibit (Bates 003996-R2, timestamp 03:36:53) records a Mirandizing at **6:25 PM** — but **the document prints no date**. [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline) argues from internal evidence — "my folks are trying to get a hold of him, I think his office is closed for the night" — that it is **Sept 11**, not Sept 12. See [Mirandizing — Court Evidence](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview) and [6:25 PM Custody vs the Discord Confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — ≈7:55–8:57 PM (inferred)** — If the 6:25 PM Mirandizing is Sept 11, the **Discord confession messages** would have been posted roughly **90 minutes after** (Coleman/secondary walkthroughs often cite **~7:55–8:57 PM**) the accused invoked counsel and declined to answer questions. That inference drives the whole [Discord Confession Timing](/Gov_Mind_Control/discord-confession-timing) thread — and it rests entirely on **a date the exhibit does not print**.

* **Sept 11, 2025 — 10:00 PM** — The Utah County inmate booking sheet lists the **arrest**, with the arresting officer identified as [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) of the State Bureau of Investigation. The booking-into-Utah-County stamp reads **Sept 12** — a roughly four-hour transport gap examined on the [arrest and booking timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline).

* **Undated, September 2025** — The Discord message set itself becomes evidence. Whether account activity, device attribution, and server-side logs align is contested at [Discord Evidence in Court](/court/discord-evidence-court) and [Tyler Robinson Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages).

### October 2025 – mid-2026 — extraction, foreign-phone waves, tracking claim

* **Oct 13, 2025** — The most-cited post alleging that **witness phone footage vanished before the owners got home** is published on X. There is **no device-forensics report, no filing, and no chain-of-custody record** supporting a remote wipe — and no ordinary explanation, such as app behavior or storage handling, has been excluded either. See [Witness Videos Deleted Remotely](/CoverUp/Videos_Deleted_Remotely).

* **November 5, 2025** — Accounts including [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) circulate that **~12 Israeli-registered phones** were active at UVU on Sept 10 and that **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** reportedly repeated the claim on air ([her statements timeline](./candace-owens-timeline)). No public dataset or carrier production accompanies the posts. This **pre-Seruga** figure is the wave most amplifiers first saw; Seruga's later **16/44** claim is separate.

* **Undated, 2025–2026** — Prosecutors filed that they did **not** intend to offer evidence obtained through **forensic extractions of the seized electronics** — and then an agent testified that a **completed Cellebrite extraction of Twiggs' phone** had been performed and personally reviewed. Both statements cannot describe the same case posture. See [the extraction contradiction](/CoverUp/Cellebrite_Extraction_Contradiction) and [Forensic Phone Extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction).

* **Undated, 2026** — Because **Cellebrite DI Ltd.** is an Israeli firm headquartered in Petah Tikva, its role became a conflict-of-interest argument in a case where Israeli involvement is itself a live theory. The decisive counterweight recorded on this site: **the defense is not challenging the extraction** — the party best positioned to expose a fabrication holds the full images and is not contesting authenticity. See [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite) and [Israeli Technology Inside the Investigation](/Israel/israeli-tech-in-investigation). Ordinary counterweight: UFED is **global LE standard equipment**; vendor nationality alone does not prove state direction of a case.

* **April 8, 2026** — [@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen-2) Space asks, if custody already holds, **"Did the cops let him use his phone?"** for evening Discord activity — the device-access question that June 29 family-report phone confiscation sharpens.

* **April 10–11, 2026** — During US–Iran talks at the **[Islamabad Serena Hotel](/intelligence/Islamabad_Serena_Link)** — attended per reporting by Vice President [JD Vance](/People/jd-vance), with the hotel requisitioned and locked inside a 3 km security zone — Seruga claimed **four of the sixteen Israeli-registered devices** from his UVU set pinged at that hotel. **The device identifiers have never been published**, so no one outside his system can check it. His **April 11** post packages the UVU 44-phone graphic with the Serena follow-on. See [Islamabad Serena Tracking](/Israel/Islamabad_Serena_Tracking) and [Four of Those Phones Later at the Serena](/Suspicious/Israel/phones-islamabad-serena).

* **April 12, 2026** — Seruga followed with the **Bumblehive** argument: because the NSA's Utah Data Center in Bluffdale ingests bulk phone data, and because "cross-pollination" of call, text, location, Wi-Fi and app metadata accumulates, a **burner phone can reportedly be linked to its real owner after roughly 30–60 days of normal use**. His conclusion is that any foreign devices present at UVU **are already identifiable to the US government** whether or not the public ever sees it. See [NSA Cellphones & Bumblehive](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive), [Israeli Phones & Bumblehive](/Proof_Intel_Services/Israeli_Phones_Bumblehive), [the NSA section](/US_Intelligence/nsa) and [the counterweight page](/Suspicious/US_Intel/nsa-bumblehive-phones).

Whether any agency ever ran these foreign-device leads to ground is exactly the question former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has raised on the record — he says his center's parallel review of potential international ties to the assassination was still in its early phases when it was ordered to stop.

* **June 29, 2026** — Device-access arguments intensify when family-report versions (via Coleman/Denizen) say the **phone was seized on afternoon arrival** while Discord timestamps sit in the **evening**. Alternatives discussed online — second device, delayed send, third-party access, or a wrong custody clock — are **none of them proven publicly**.

### July 2026 preliminary hearing — Cellebrite confirmed; tower map still missing

* **July 2026 (prelim)** — Testimony and reporting confirm the Robinson–Twiggs message set entered the case through a **Cellebrite extraction of Twiggs' phone**. Some amplifiers emphasize that messages were **deleted then recovered**; treat "deleted" language as **testimony-dependent** until the transcript is public. Commentators highlight Cellebrite's Israeli corporate domicile; the ordinary counter remains global LE standard use.

* **July 14, 2026 onward** — Investigators publicly ask why **no cell-tower map of Robinson's own handset** was shown at the multi-day prelim if it would place him at UVU. The absence is a **negative evidence** point — process gap, not proof that tower data does not exist.

* **July 2026 (technical claim)** — Posts allege UVU/investigators used **BriefCam** analytics (vendor HQ often described as Israel) to track the charged subject across cameras — complementary to handset forensics; **verify in discovery** before stating as fact. See [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **Ongoing, 2026** — Whether a **cell-site simulator (Stingray-type device)** operated at or near the campus that day is an open collection question tracked at [UVU Stingray Device](/UVU/Stingray-Device). If one did, it would have logged **every handset in range** — including any foreign ones.

* **Ongoing, 2026** — Separately, [Charlie Kirk's own phone](/Charlie/Phone_Surveillance_Claims) and its message history sit at the center of a public dispute over whether he sent warning texts in the days before his death — a device question with no forensic answer published. See ["They Are Going To Kill Me" Text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

## Open Questions

1. Where are the **carrier records** for the charged route on September 10 — the parking lot at 8:29 AM, the stairwell at 11:53 AM, the roof window at 12:15–12:24 PM — and why has **no tower map of Robinson's handset** been produced even after the July 2026 prelim?
2. What is the **margin of error** on every tower-based location claim in this case, and has any filing ever stated it?
3. Will Seruga publish **device identifiers, collection method and provenance** for the 44-phone dataset (and the earlier ~12-phone wave) so it can be independently checked or falsified?
4. Did any **cell-site simulator** operate at or near UVU on September 10, 2025, under whose authority, and does a log survive?
5. Does the prosecution's filing that it would not offer forensic-extraction evidence **still stand** after July 2026 Cellebrite/Twiggs testimony?
6. Has the **defense independently re-processed** the device images with a non-Cellebrite tool, and what did it find?
7. Do **server-side Discord logs** — held by the platform, not by any seized handset — confirm the posting times attributed to the confession messages, and were they introduced at prelim?
8. If the phone was **confiscated on afternoon arrival**, what device or account path produced evening Discord activity?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The **carrier and tower records** for every device on the charged route, the **cell-site simulator logs** for the UVU area on September 10, 2025, the **complete Cellebrite extraction reports** with full timestamps rather than courtroom screenshots, the **server-side Discord logs** independent of any seized handset, the **chain of custody** for each seized device, and whatever the **NSA's Bluffdale facility** already retains on the foreign-registered handsets alleged to have been present are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
