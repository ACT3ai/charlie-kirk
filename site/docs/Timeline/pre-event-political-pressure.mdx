# Pre-Event Political Pressure Timeline (July–September 2025)

This timeline covers **one layer only**: the political and donor pressure applied to Charlie Kirk between **July 11 and September 9, 2025**. No flights, no ballistics, no campus movement — those live on sibling pages. A reader enters here to answer one question: **what was being done to him, by whom, in the nine weeks before the shot**, and how much of that is documented versus retold.

Roughly half the entries below are **on-the-record** (broadcast segments, public posts, released records). The other half come from **secondary reporting and commentary**, and are labeled. Everyone named here is a living person; nothing below asserts that any of them committed a crime, and no court has found otherwise.

## The Timeline

### July 2025 — the rupture goes public

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits Washington, the trip that most [donor-pressure reconstructions](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) use as their starting marker for the summer's coalition fight.

* **July 11, 2025** — Kirk's decision to **platform anti-Israel speakers** at [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events reportedly alarms pro-Israel allies and donors; the friction is documented on [Charlie–Israel Friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and is the earliest entry most compilations treat as the start of the pressure arc.

* **July 13, 2025** — At **TPUSA's Student Action Summit in Florida**, the **[Dave Smith](/Influencers/podcasts-dave-smith) vs [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** debate puts the Israel split on the movement's own main stage, per the [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **July 14, 2025** — **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** speaks at the same summit and tells the audience to "**go all the way**," a line that [Israel-coverage researchers](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) read as permission for the base to break with pro-Israel orthodoxy — and that critics read as a generic applause line.

* **July 16, 2025** — A podcast episode titled "**Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout**" discusses internal tensions and donor disputes at TPUSA, useful because it is **contemporaneous** rather than retrospective; see [TPUSA Opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie).

* **July 18, 2025 (contested)** — Some citizen researchers assert a **decision to target Kirk in Utah** was made around this date, based on search-trend patterns and retrospective sequencing. There is **no document, filing, or court finding** supporting it — see [July 18 Decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** convenes on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people" and "growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement." It is organized by the **[Faith and Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition)**, the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel), and the [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding), reportedly funded by [Paul Singer](/TPUSA/Paul_Singer_Funding).

* **July 31, 2025** — Reported attendees include **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)**, **[Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)**, **[Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick)**, **[Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun)**, and **[Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)** — and **Kirk and TPUSA are not there**, despite Kirk being, until weeks earlier, the movement's loudest pro-Israel voice. The absence is the entry researchers treat as load-bearing: see [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) and [TPUSA Absence July 31](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31).

### August 2025 — the intervention

* **August 4, 2025 (or August 5–6; date disputed)** — The **Hamptons meeting**. Multiple accounts describe it as an "**intervention**" — sometimes framed as a **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**–linked Hamptons setting — at which pro-Israel figures confronted Kirk about his rhetoric and his platforming choices — see [Hamptons Intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons). By mid-2026, critics on X assert that **attendees deny the meeting occurred** at all.

* **August 4–6, 2025 (reported)** — Names circulated in those accounts include **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**, **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**, **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**, and **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)**. The attendee list comes from **secondary reporting and commentary**; it has never been confirmed in any filing, and none of these individuals has been accused of any crime by any authority.

* **August 4–6, 2025 (claims; amount disputed)** — In the same window, commentators recount an alleged **[phone call from Netanyahu](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call)** offering substantial funding tied to an Israel trip, which Kirk reportedly **declined** ([Rejected Israeli Funding](/Iran/funding-rejection-claims)). Alternative-media outlets including *The Grayzone* (amplified by @KitKlarenberg and high-engagement X posts) put the figure on the order of **$150 million** and frame a Jerusalem visit as a "**re-education**" tour. The claim also traces to [Candace Owens'](/People/candace-owens) sourcing and third-party retelling — see [the billion-dollar claim](/Israel/billion) and [Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm). No call recording or contract is public; treat as attributed reporting, not proven fact.

* **August 6, 2025 (primary)** — Kirk appears with **Megyn Kelly** and complains on the record that **donors are pressuring him** and that "**my integrity is being called into question**." This is the strongest **primary-source artifact** in the entire pressure arc — Kirk himself, on camera — and is distinct from third-party retellings of private calls or dollar figures. See [Megyn Kelly coverage](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly).

* **Mid-August 2025** — Follow-on media segments and podcast commentary widen the donor-pressure story inside the alternative-media ecosystem, tracked on [Mainstream vs Alternative Media](/Media/Mainstream-vs-Alternative-Media).

* **Late summer 2025 (claims)** — Commentators circulating **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)** remarks argue pro-Israel donors also pressured Kirk because he was urging President [Trump](/People/donald-trump) **against war with Iran** — an Iran-policy thread not limited to campus-speaker fights, developed on [Charlie's stand against the Iran war](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war) and [Iran War (Charlie vs Israel)](/Israel_Main_Suspect/iran-war-charlie-vs-israel). Attribute the video commentary; no donor is named here as a criminal actor.

### September 1–8, 2025 — the exit

* **September 1–5, 2025 (claims)** — Compilations claim Kirk told **group chats** he had "**no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause**," was "**thinking of inviting Candace**," and "**won't cancel Tucker**." The chats have not been authenticated in any public court filing and exist publicly only as screenshots — see [Leaked Kirk Texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts).

* **September 3, 2025** — **[Orem Police](/Law_Enforcement/orem-police-response)** email UVU the **Charlie Kirk ticket link**; after the shooting Orem PD stated it was "not involved," and the joint Public Order Unit UVU pays for annually was never activated, per the [GRAMA records release](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk **posts publicly about leaving the pro-Israel movement**, the clearest public marker of the break and the most-quoted single artifact on [Charlie's Israel Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel). Viral compilations ([@AdameMedia](/Influencers/x/adamemedia) and others) go further, quoting group-chat texts to **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** and **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)** about losing a "**big donor**," stereotypes language, and "**leaving the pro-Israel cause**." Screenshots remain unauthenticated in a public court file; living recipients are not accused of any crime.

* **September 8–9, 2025 (competing document)** — Rebuttal accounts point to a multi-page **letter from Kirk to Netanyahu** advising how Israel could better reach younger audiences and counter antisemitism (sometimes described as a ~7-page constructive note, not a break letter). Posters who emphasize the letter argue he had **not** formally abandoned the pro-Israel cause. Publish both the break narrative and the letter-interpretation narrative.

* **September 8, 2025** — Roughly **twelve lieutenant colonels** are reported at **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca)** aboard [SAM 702](/Planes/SAM-702), departing early on September 9 — a military-calendar entry, included here only because researchers place it inside the same week; see [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9).

### September 9, 2025 — the last day

* **September 9, 2025 — daytime** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro's](/People/ben-shapiro)** show and criticizes Israeli policy **to Shapiro's face**, the break [donor-pressure researchers](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) treat as the point of no return. Shapiro is a living person and nothing here implies involvement in what followed.

* **September 9, 2025 — 2:23 PM MDT** — An email in **[UVU Police Chief Jeff Long's](/People/jeff-long)** communication chain reads "**He wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk. His phone number is REDACTED**." The requester's name and number are redacted and delivery is undocumented across all 112 released pages — [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **September 9, 2025 (claims)** — Dominant X chronology ([@AdameMedia](/Influencers/x/adamemedia) and reprints) labels the day an "**emergency Zoom**" with the rabbi and Hammer, after which Kirk allegedly texted **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** that "**they are going to kill me**." Broader accounts still say he texted "others" during or after a combative high-pressure meeting. Sourcing is Owens citing three sources plus corroborating commentary; the texts have not been produced in a public filing — see [High-Pressure Meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and [the text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text). Living named parties are not accused of murder.

* **September 9, 2025 (parallel warning stream)** — [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) and security-adjacent commentary describe Kirk telling people close to him "**they want me dead**" / that Israel would kill him in the weeks before UVU — a multi-week warning stream that runs beside the single-night Owens/Kolvet text narrative. Attribute; no perpetrator is proven.

* **September 9, 2025** — [Candace Owens](./candace-owens-timeline) alleges three separate sources told her Kirk feared "they" would kill him. She also reports Kirk texted about **inviting her to [AmericaFest](/Israel_Main_Suspect/control-amfest-conferences)**, a detail that cuts against the reading that he was withdrawing from public life.

* **September 9, 2025 (unverified rumor)** — Some 2026 posts claim Hammer was "**taking calls from Israel**" the night before the shooting. No primary call log is public; listed only as an attributed rumor.

* **September 9, 2025** — **Google trend** researchers report Utah-origin searches for the tail number **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)**, a data point offered as foreknowledge evidence and rejected by critics as trend-geography noise — see [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview).

* **September 9, 2025 — 10:35 PM MDT** — **Two unidentified men** walk into a UVU office asking to speak with police about "**a plan**"; staff escalate to the university cabinet. Forty-four minutes later someone in the chat says they were "GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did." Nobody asked what the plan was and their names remain **redacted** — [pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **September 10, 2025 — 12:23 PM MDT** — The pressure arc ends. From here the chronology moves to the [day-of timeline](./Sept_10_2025) and the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute); for the year-scale view, see the [2025 Context Timeline](./Year_2025).

### Scope note for the same nine weeks

The same summer window includes **July 20** foreign-leader and military-calendar claims ([foreign leaders July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20), [military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20), [Nebraska Army base](./nebraska-army-base-july-20)) and **Sept 8–9** Fort Huachuca / SAM activity on sibling timelines. Those are **not** donor-pressure events, but viral "summer of pressure" graphics often mash them into this arc. Keep layers separate.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hamptons date | August 4 | August 5–6; some retellings conflate it with a September 9 meeting entirely |
| Hamptons attendees | The five names above, from secondary reporting (often framed as a Bill Ackman–linked setting) | No confirmed list exists; by mid-2026 critics on X assert **attendees deny the meeting occurred** |
| Netanyahu call / offer | Occurred early August; funding offered (sometimes **$150M** + Jerusalem trip); Kirk declined | Undocumented; exists only in third-party retelling; no contract or recording public |
| Primary vs secondary hierarchy | Aug 6 Megyn Kelly segment is on-camera Kirk describing donor pressure | $150M / Netanyahu call and group-chat screenshots are third-party or unauthenticated |
| "They are going to kill me" | Texted September 9 after an "emergency Zoom" / pressure meeting; dominant compilation names **Andrew Kolvet** as recipient | Not produced in any public filing; known only through commentary; Turek multi-week "want me dead" stream is a parallel claim |
| Kirk–Netanyahu letter | Part of a break / rupture narrative | Multi-page constructive letter on reaching young people / antisemitism — evidence he had **not** formally left the pro-Israel cause |
| Sept 9 UVU visitors | Genuine attempt to warn about a plan | Dismissed in the chat as GOP reps posturing |
| Kirk's Sept 8 post / texts | A formal break with the pro-Israel movement (group chat to Hammer/Wolicki) | A narrower criticism read as a break in hindsight; some Grok/skeptic replies say he never announced leaving |
| July 18 "decision" | A targeting decision was made | Inference only, from trend data and retrospective sequencing |
| Pressure motive | Campus-speaker and donor fights only | Also Iran-war lobbying pressure (Joe Kent commentary) |

## Open Questions

1. Who **called** the July 31 White House meeting, and was Kirk's exclusion a decision or an oversight?
2. Do the **September 1–5 group chats** exist in an authenticated form (including Hammer/Wolicki recipients), and are they in the discovery file?
3. Was the redacted **"get a message to Charlie Kirk"** request ever delivered, and by whom?
4. Who attended the **September 9 high-pressure meeting** (in-person or "emergency Zoom"), and where was it held?
5. Who were the **two men** at the UVU office at 10:35 PM on September 9?
6. Did any donor or organizational actor make a **documented financial threat**, as distinct from the disputed **$150M** / Netanyahu-offer reports?
7. What is the **date and full text** of the multi-page Kirk letter to Netanyahu, and how does it sit beside the Sept 8 "leaving" texts?
8. Who were the **recipients** of the "they are going to kill me" message(s), and are the originals in discovery?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The attendance record and minutes of the July 31 White House meeting, the authenticated originals of the September 1–5 group chats and the September 9 "they are going to kill me" text with its recipients (including any Kolvet message), the full Kirk–Netanyahu letter, the unredacted UVU GRAMA correspondence naming the two September 9 visitors and the sender of the "get a message to Charlie Kirk" email, and any contemporaneous record of the Hamptons meeting and the reported Netanyahu call (including any $150M figure) are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
