# Press Conferences &amp; Official Statements Timeline

Almost everything the public believes about September 10 was set at a podium, not in a
document. This timeline follows the official statements in order — who spoke, what they said,
and what the public timeline looked like immediately afterward. Twice within nine hours the
FBI told the country a suspect was in custody and then said he was not. Each entry notes what
changed, because the sequence of statements is itself contested evidence. No official named
here has been found by any court to have done anything wrong.

## September 10 — The First Six Hours

* **12:48 PM Sept 10** — [UVU](/UVU/overview) issues the first official communication of any
  kind: a campus emergency alert reporting a shot fired toward a visiting speaker. It named
  no victim and no suspect ([UVU administration response](/UVU/UVU-Administration-Response)).

* **1:02 PM Sept 10** — President [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) posts about the
  shooting on Truth Social — the first national-level acknowledgment, and it came from the
  White House rather than from Utah
  ([September 10 breaking news](/Media/september-10-breaking-news)).

* **~1:30 PM Sept 10** — Orem Police and UVU campus police provide the earliest on-scene
  characterizations to local media; the "one shot from an elevated position" framing enters
  circulation here ([Orem police response](/Law_Enforcement/orem-police-response),
  [UVU campus police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police)).

* **1:37 PM Sept 10** — UVU announces campus closure and urges evacuation.

* **2:01 PM Sept 10** — UVU reverses to a secure-in-place instruction, a change citizen
  timelines flag as evidence the threat picture was still unresolved
  ([UVU social media response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response)).

* **2:40 PM Sept 10** — Trump confirms [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death on Truth
  Social — again ahead of Utah officials and ahead of the medical examiner
  ([medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline)).

* **4:00 PM Sept 10** — Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) holds the first Utah
  press conference and calls the killing a **"political assassination,"** the phrase that
  framed all subsequent coverage ([Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline)).

* **4:21 PM Sept 10** — FBI Director Kash Patel announces on X that **"the subject for the
  horrific shooting is now in custody."** Two unrelated individuals had been briefly
  detained; the statement was wrong ([Kash Patel briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)).

* **5:59 PM Sept 10** — Patel retracts: the detained subject has been released and the
  investigation continues. The whiplash between 4:21 and 5:59 is the origin of much of the
  public's distrust of subsequent federal statements
  ([FBI's official narrative](/FBI/Official_Narrative)).

* **7:59 PM Sept 10** — The FBI releases rooftop and escape video of the alleged shooter. The
  low quality of the release, on a campus with high-definition coverage, became its own
  controversy ([UVU HD footage withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld),
  [selective 4K release](/Suspicious/FBI/selective-4k-release)).

## September 11 — Manhunt Messaging

* **6:00 AM Sept 11** — Trump tells Fox &amp; Friends he believes "with a high degree of
  certainty" the killer is in custody — a statement no Utah or FBI podium had made and that
  no public surrender yet supported.

* **7:00 AM Sept 11** — Officials announce overnight forensic recoveries: the
  [Mauser .30-06 rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle), footwear impressions, a palm print
  and a forearm impression ([FBI ballistics and forensics](/FBI/Ballistics_Forensics)).

* **9:58 AM Sept 11** — FBI Salt Lake City releases the first two person-of-interest photos
  ([SLC Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office)).

* **10:44 AM Sept 11** — The FBI announces a **$100,000 reward**, formally signaling that the
  suspect was still unidentified ([suspect manhunt](/Law_Enforcement/suspect-manhunt)).

* **6:17 PM Sept 11** — Additional person-of-interest photos are released
  ([enhanced suspect photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos)).

* **7:55–7:58 PM Sept 11** — Officials say they are looking for a "person of interest" and
  release "enhanced" stairwell photos plus rooftop video — eight minutes before the alleged
  Discord confession timestamp and roughly ninety minutes after the disputed **6:25 PM
  Miranda video** ([custody and surrender timeline](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline)).

* **~8:00 PM Sept 11** — The Utah County evening briefing. On the podium: Governor Cox, Lt.
  Gov. [Deidre Henderson](/People/deidre-henderson), Director Patel, Deputy Director
  [Dan Bongino](/People/dan-bongino), Sheriff [Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith), and FBI SAC
  [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls), who led it. Over **7,000 tips** and nearly **200
  interviews** are reported ([official statements](/Legal/Official-Statements)).

* **~8:00 PM Sept 11 (the silence)** — Utah reporters noted that Patel was present but did
  **not speak** at this briefing. Critics later argued the silence is meaningful given the
  Miranda timestamp; there is no public explanation either way
  ([FBI press conference media coverage](/Media/fbi-press-conference-media)).

* **8:04 PM Sept 11 (the collision)** — The probable-cause statement says Sheriff Smith
  received Brooksby's surrender call at 2004 hours — while Smith was standing at this
  briefing ([Miranda vs Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account)).

## September 12 — The Arrest Announcement

* **6:00 AM Sept 12** — Trump announces publicly that a suspect is in custody, before Utah's
  formal release ([broadcast TV networks](/Media/broadcast-tv-networks)).

* **7:00–7:30 AM Sept 12** — Patel characterizes the case as a **"33-hour manhunt."** Counted
  from the 12:23 PM shot, that ends at 9:23 PM Sept 11 — three hours after the Miranda
  timestamp and about 90 minutes after the Discord messages
  ([manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline)).

* **Sept 12 (framing)** — In the same period Patel used the phrases later criticized by
  commentators including Buckley Carlson — the "Valhalla" reference and an emphasis on the
  number 33 — remarks the FBI has never publicly explained
  ([Patel and the Valhalla remarks](/Videos/buckley-carlson-kash-patel-valhalla)).

* **5:00 PM Sept 12** — The Utah County Attorney's office issues its official press release
  announcing the arrest and stating that formal charges would be filed by **noon on September
  16** ([case overview](/court/case-overview)).

* **Sept 12 (casings)** — Governor Cox publicly describes the engraved cartridge
  inscriptions, quoted by Deseret News — the first time motive language entered the official
  record ([Deseret News](https://www.deseret.com/utah/2025/09/12/charlie-kirk-shooting-arrest-tyler-robinson-arrest-shell-casing-inscriptions-fascist/)).

## September 15–17 — Charging and Consolidation

* **Sept 15, 2025** — Patel appears on national television and revises the screwdriver
  account: originally reported by CNN on Sept 11 as found near the rifle, it is now described
  as found on the roof bearing DNA. The item then vanishes from the charging documents
  entirely ([FBI evidence footage](/FBI/Evidence_Footage)).

* **Sept 16, 2025** — Utah County Attorney [Jeffrey S. Gray](/People/jeffrey-s-gray)
  announces the charges — Aggravated Murder, Felony Discharge of a Firearm Causing Serious
  Bodily Injury, and Obstruction of Justice — and files the **Notice of Intent to Seek the
  Death Penalty** ([prosecution team](/court/prosecution-team)).

* **Sept 16, 2025** — [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) makes his first court
  appearance by video before Judge [Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf) in Provo and is held
  without bail. He is **charged, not convicted**
  ([Fourth District Court](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo)).

* **Sept 17, 2025** — Sheriff [Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) holds a press conference
  re-affirming the 33-hour manhunt and describing the 8:02 PM and 8:04 PM calls — the account
  investigators later contrast with the Miranda filing
  ([Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff)).

## Later Statements That Changed the Record

* **Late 2025** — A gag order and sealing regime narrows what any official may say, moving
  the information flow from podiums into filings
  ([gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing),
  [court gag orders](/Censorship/Court_Gag_Orders)).

* **Late 2025 – 2026** — Washington County tells records requesters the surrender CCTV no
  longer exists and, in a GRAMA appeal, that no bodycam was recording — a records position,
  not a podium statement, that contradicts the impression left by the September briefings
  ([missing surrender footage](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/missing-surrender-footage)).

* **July 2026 (preliminary hearing)** — Under oath, Gray corrected a location he had
  misstated at an official press conference, testifying he was at a budget meeting in Layton,
  Davis County — not Ogden ([Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview)).

* **2026 (press posture)** — Coverage of the trial consolidates around filings and hearings
  rather than briefings ([trial press](/Media/tyler-robinson-trial-press),
  [post-assassination investigations](./post-assassination-investigations)).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Statement | What was said | What conflicts with it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 4:21 PM Sept 10 | Patel: subject in custody | Retracted at 5:59 PM the same evening |
| 6:00 AM Sept 11 | Trump: killer in custody "with a high degree of certainty" | No agency had a suspect publicly identified until 9:58 AM |
| ~8:00 PM Sept 11 | Officials present the suspect as still at large | The defense filing's **6:25 PM** Miranda timestamp |
| 8:04 PM Sept 11 | Smith received the surrender call | Smith was at the podium at that moment |
| 33-hour manhunt | Patel, Sept 12; re-affirmed by Brooksby Sept 17 | ~30 hours if measured to the Miranda timestamp |
| Screwdriver | CNN Sept 11: near the rifle. Patel Sept 15: on the roof, with DNA | Absent from both the charging documents and the affidavit |
| Surrender footage | September briefings implied a documented voluntary surrender | County later says CCTV is deleted and no bodycam ran |

## Open Questions

1. What did the FBI have at 4:21 PM on Sept 10 that produced a custody announcement retracted
   98 minutes later?
2. Who drafted and approved the ~8:00 PM Sept 11 briefing script, and when were they told
   about the Washington County encounter?
3. Why did Director Patel attend the Sept 11 evening briefing without speaking?
4. What is the origin of the "33 hours" figure, and against which two timestamps was it
   calculated?
5. Why did the screwdriver move from "near the rifle" to "on the roof" and then out of the
   charging documents entirely?
6. Are transcripts and internal drafts of each briefing preserved, and will they be produced
   in discovery?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* Full transcripts, internal drafts and approval chains for every September 2025 briefing,
  the FBI's internal record of what supported the 4:21 PM custody announcement and its
  retraction, the calculation behind the "33-hour manhunt" figure, the communications
  scheduling the Sept 11 evening press conference, and the unedited source footage behind the
  7:59 PM Sept 10 video release are things that the
  [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out
  that aren't out yet.
