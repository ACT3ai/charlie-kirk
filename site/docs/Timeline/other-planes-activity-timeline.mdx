# Other Planes Activity Timeline (N888KG, SAM Flights, Cluster)

This timeline covers **every aircraft layer in the case except two**. The Army-contracted ISR jet has its own [N1098L activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline), and the Egyptian Falcon has its own [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline). What is left over is still substantial: a private jet that went dark after the shot, two U.S. government **Special Air Mission** flights in and out of an Army intelligence post, a five-jet Egyptian cluster parked at Provo, and a stack of business jets that landed at Provo Municipal in the hours after the shooting. Times are **MDT** unless marked otherwise, and every entry below is a **reported or tracked claim**, not a finding.

## The Timeline

### Spring and summer 2025 — the pattern before the week

* **May 27, 2025** — The Egyptian-registered jet **[SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU)** flies into **[Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)](/Locations/Provo_Airport)**, roughly seven miles from [UVU](/UVU/overview), reportedly carrying a passenger identified in compilations as "Walid" ([Walid Mahmoud](/People/walid-mahmoud)) — the earliest entry in the [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) sequence and the reason researchers argue the September stop was not a one-off.

* **June 2, 2025** — The same passenger reportedly **departs Provo**, closing a roughly six-day Egyptian presence at PVU three months before the shooting.

* **July 20, 2025** — Flight-tracking compilations date **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)**'s first-ever U.S. trip to a Nebraska Army installation, the anchor of the separate [July 20 military meeting timeline](./military-meeting-july-20) and the [Nebraska Army base page](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **Across 2025** — Raw flight logs circulated by citizen investigators claim **five** Egyptian Air Force jets touched Provo during the year: **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)**, **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)**, **[SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU)**, **[SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV)** and **[SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM)**. Screenshot tables (e.g. [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/x/project-constitution) / [@WeAre_TheElite](/Influencers/x/weare-theelite)) list additional Provo touch dates across **February–October 2025** beyond the May–September bullets below. Skeptics reply that VIP government jets routinely rotate through maintenance-capable fields and that the "armada" framing is a tally, not a finding.

* **August 22–27, 2025 (ADS-B claims)** — Commentators (@furbiddentruths and others) report Kirk's aircraft **[N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ)** sat on the ground in **Bozeman, Montana** between a late-August **Defense Intelligence Agency** leadership change (search-trend context on [DIA search patterns](/GoogleSearches/dia-search-patterns)) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)'s public announcement of the fall campus tour with UVU as the first stop. Correlation only; no public flight purpose is disclosed.

### September 4–9 — the pre-event week

* **Sept 4, 2025** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** arrives at Provo direct from France and **stays through the event window**, the fact that generated the [Egyptian jets at Provo](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo) page. Citizen compilations also place **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** in the same Provo window, with one jet departing before the shot and one after in some accounts.

* **Sept 4–10, 2025** — Sources described as connected to **Duncan Aviation** claim **4–6 subcontractors with Egyptian passports** boarded, that their badges read **"U.S. Department of Defense Liaison,"** and that **counter-UAS (anti-drone) equipment** was tested at Provo across this window — the allegation catalogued on the [counter-UAS at Provo page](/Suspicious/US_Intel/counter-uas-provo). No manifest confirming any of it has been released.

* **Sept 4–10, 2025 (unconfirmed)** — The same threads name passengers **"Maj. [Ahmed Khalil](/People/ahmed-khalil)"** and **"Eng. [Omar Nassar](/People/omar-nassar),"** allege **no-scan priority** Provo tower handling, and cite a UVU-area maintenance eyewitness of Egyptian-crew drone/tech testing on **Sept 8**. The posters themselves label the passenger names unconfirmed; none appear in released FAA or court records.

* **Sept 4–10, 2025** — The same threads place the visiting crew at a hotel in **Orem**, per the [Egyptian crew hotel page](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel), and allege four rental vehicles were issued through Duncan Aviation to unidentified passengers — the plates tracked on the [rental cars and vehicle movements timeline](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).

* **Sept 7, 2025** — The Air Force VIP jet **tail 99-0404** flies **[Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews) to Colorado Springs**, the first leg of the route reconstructed on the [SAM 99-0404 page](/Planes/SAM-99-0404).

* **Sept 8, 2025** — **99-0404** flies **Colorado Springs to Tucson**, then departs Tucson roughly five hours later, after which trackers say the **transponder appears to go off** — a gap the [SAM-702 Fort Huachuca claim page](/Suspicious/US_Intel/sam-702-fort-huachuca) treats as the most contested link in the chain.

* **Sept 8, 2025** — The investigation file places roughly **12 lieutenant colonels** at **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca)**, Arizona — the U.S. Army Intelligence Center — the gathering described on the [Fort Huachuca Sept 9 page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and in the [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

* **Sept 9, 2025 — 00:49** — Per the file's own timeline entry, **SAM702** departs. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) separately describes the **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** VIP flight as leaving [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) on the **morning** of September 9 with **four passengers**. Which clock reading is correct has never been reconciled.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Owens names **[Bradley D. Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** — described as Under Secretary of War for Intelligence and Security — as one of those four passengers. Hansell is a **living public official**; no wrongdoing by him is alleged or established, and travelling on a government VIP flight is ordinary for that office. His reported earlier career at **[Boston Consulting Group](/TPUSA/Companies/boston-consulting-group)** is why commentators fold the flight into the [Israel](/Israel/overview) thread.

* **Sept 9, 2025 — 12:31 PM** — **99-0404** flies **Fort Huachuca to El Paso**, then El Paso back to **[Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews)**, closing the loop.

* **Early September 2025** — The callsign **[SAM650](/Planes/SAM-650)** appears in the same compilations on a **Wilmington → Andrews → Nashville** itinerary with what posters call "Colorado Springs syncs." No tail number has ever been attached to it, which is the single largest gap on that page.

* **Sept 9–13, 2025 (claims)** — Compilations (e.g. [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution), crediting [@nihilox](/Influencers/x/nihilox)) track NetJets callsign **EJA685 (N685QS)** near the **El Paso / Biggs** window associated with the SAM chain and a short **Salt Lake City → Las Vegas** hop after the SU-BTT departure narrative — offered as a possible passenger-swap leg. No passenger manifest supports any swap; living NetJets operators are not accused of wrongdoing.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — **Google Trends** shows the tail number **n888kg** being searched **in Utah** — the day before the shooting, and before that aircraft had done anything newsworthy. The [planes search-pattern page](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns) records it; nothing establishes who searched, or why. Separate Fort Huachuca **Google image-search** prep claims (high-level names searched before the SAM window) live primarily on the [Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

### September 10 — morning

* **~7:00 AM (skeptic clock)** — Some posters assert an Egyptian-registered jet left Provo about **five hours before** the shot (@AuburnJPJ and others), arguing the Egyptian cluster proves presence and maintenance rotations, not operational timing around 12:23. Track this early-departure rebuttal beside the mid-morning power-up claims below.

* **~9:05 AM** — One of the parked **Egyptian jets** at Provo Municipal reportedly **powers up**, then drops off tracking — the start of the anomaly described on the [Sept 10 flight timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).

* **~9:30 AM** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s aircraft **[N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ)** is reported to land at **Salt Lake City International**, roughly 45 miles north of UVU, rather than at Provo seven miles away. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) flagged that diversion in her November 17, 2025 "Operation Mocking-Plane" episode.

* **Morning** — The contract survey aircraft **[N59906](/Planes/N59906)**, a Piper Navajo operated by MARC Inc., reportedly flies **four mapping passes** beginning and ending at Provo at roughly **19,000 feet MSL**. Early posts labelled these "low-altitude"; the [N59906 page](/Planes/N59906) corrects that — it is a standard high-altitude mapping band, not a treetop pass.

* **~10:06 AM** — **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)** lands at **Provo** from Scottsdale. Some posts instead give **~10:26 AM**; the [N560TW turnaround page](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n560tw-turnaround) preserves both readings.

* **~11:37 AM** — **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)** departs Provo for **Santa Barbara** after roughly ninety minutes on the ground — the turnaround that first drew [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' attention. Some posts treat the tail as almost exclusively TPUSA-associated travel; that ownership/use pattern is commentary, not a court finding.

* **~12:44 PM** — **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)** is reported back on the ground at **Scottsdale**, about twenty minutes after the shot at UVU.

* **Afternoon (disputed tarmac narrative)** — Citizen ADS-B readings ([@hurtfeelingzday](/Influencers/x/hurtfeelingzday) and others) claim **N560TW** left a **Santa Barbara** gate roughly **fourteen minutes** after the UVU shot — too soon, they argue, for a cold aircraft to be staged for a Provo recovery — and that [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)'s tarmac-hug account fits **Scottsdale**, not Provo. That conflicts with other Provo-arrival narratives for **[N872RA](/Planes/N872RA)** / **[N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ)**. Attribute flight-app disputes only; no wrongdoing is alleged.

### September 10 — afternoon, the Provo arrival stack

* **12:27 PM MST** — **[N40JD](/Planes/N40JD)**, a six-seat Beechcraft Premier 1, **departs Scottsdale**. The clock collision with the disputed **12:27 PM** shot time is a time-zone coincidence, not evidence — see [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **~1:11–1:20 PM** — **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)**, a Bombardier Challenger 300 registered to an LLC in **Lehi, Utah**, **departs Provo** heading southeast — roughly 45 minutes to an hour after the shot. The entity is profiled on the [N888KG LLC page](/TPUSA/LLCs/n888kg-llc), which alleges no wrongdoing by its owners.

* **~1:29 PM** — The Egyptian jet that powered up at 9:05 AM is described as **back on tracking and idling on the tarmac** to this point, having reactivated roughly **17 minutes after the shot**, per the [Sept 10 flight timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).

* **~1:43 PM** — **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)**'s **ADS-B transponder goes dark** near the northern Arizona border, opening a gap trackers report as 30 to 50-plus minutes.

* **~1:48 PM** — **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** is reported to land at **Page Municipal Airport (PGA)**, Arizona.

* **~2:29 PM** — **[N40JD](/Planes/N40JD)** lands at **Provo**, per the flight-record screenshots circulated by investigators. A competing figure of **~4:40 PM** appears elsewhere on this site — see the conflicts table.

* **~2:30 PM** — **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departs Page for the return leg. The owner is reported to have stated that **no one disembarked** at Page and that **seven company employees boarded** there for the trip home.

* **~3:06 PM** — **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** lands back at **Provo**. No public flight plan is associated with the dark leg, which trackers label a "ghost flight."

* **~3:31 PM** — **[N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ)**, Kirk's own aircraft, lands at **Provo** after a Scottsdale departure. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) argues this is the only tail whose timing lets [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) reach the hospital by the **4:00 PM** that [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) stated on the record.

* **~4:36 PM** — **[N872RA](/Planes/N872RA)**, a Hawker Beechcraft that left **Santa Barbara at ~2:19 PM**, lands at **Provo**. This is the Santa Barbara inbound the tarmac-hug account depends on, and the reason the "4:40 PM Kolvet plane" label is contested below. [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) is a living person and nothing here alleges wrongdoing by him.

* **Afternoon** — Aircraft are described as clustering on the **Duncan Aviation** ramp at Provo after the 3:30 PM landings, with **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** reported still parked and running.

* **Late evening Sept 10 – ~2:00 AM Sept 11 (claims)** — @PBI_CarrieQuinn and others flag air ambulance **N315NG** departing Provo late on the 10th and landing the Phoenix/Scottsdale area around **2:00 AM** on the 11th. Separate fringe body-preservation theories attach to this tail; ADS-B presence and purpose remain unconfirmed in official releases. Living persons named in those threads are not accused of wrongdoing here.

### September 11 and after

* **Sept 11, 2025** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** departs the United States for Egypt, per the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **Sept 15, 2025** — The [scene-changes timeline](./scene-changes-paving-timeline) records ground-level alterations at UVU; some posts also date an **[N59906](/Planes/N59906)** survey flight to this day rather than September 10, an unresolved date conflict noted on that page.

* **Late 2025** — Compilations claim two Egyptian jets appear near [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)'s travel **73 documented times**, the tally examined on the [following Charlie and Erika page](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika). Critics including [Liz Wheeler](/People/liz-wheeler) argue such overlap counts rely on loose matching criteria and on **UTC-versus-local time-zone errors**.

* **Nov 17, 2025** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) publishes "Operation Mocking-Plane," pulling the airport-diversion and flight-overlap arguments into wider circulation — tracked on the [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **2026** — The transport aircraft used after the funeral is documented on the [casket flight page](/Planes/Casket-Flight). No flight record for any aircraft on this page has been produced through the court process tracked on the [discovery and evidence timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

## Open Questions

1. Who filed, and who paid for, the **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** leg that carried no public flight plan, and do FAA strips confirm the owner's account of the Page stop?
2. What **tail number** flew under the **[SAM650](/Planes/SAM-650)** callsign, and what was its manifest?
3. Do Fort Huachuca **flight strips and visitor logs** confirm or refute the **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** passenger list, and who booked the trip?
4. What did the **[N59906](/Planes/N59906)** survey cameras record over the UVU campus, who tasked the flight, and on which date did it actually fly?
5. Do **Duncan Aviation** ground-handling, fuel and rental-car records exist for the Egyptian aircraft across **September 4–10**, and do they name the passengers?
6. Which aircraft carried **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** into Utah on September 10 — the ~3:31 PM [N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ) or the ~4:36 PM [N872RA](/Planes/N872RA)?
7. Was **n888kg** searched in Utah on **September 9** by a person with prior knowledge, or is the spike an artifact of how Trends aggregates rare queries?
8. Who flew **EJA685 / N685QS** near the El Paso and Salt Lake windows, and do any manifests support a passenger-swap claim?
9. Does ADS-B archive confirm **N315NG**'s late Sept 10 Provo departure, and what was its filed purpose?
10. Does any primary source confirm the unconfirmed Egyptian passenger names and **no-scan** tower claim?
