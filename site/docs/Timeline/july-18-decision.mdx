# July 18, 2025 "Decision" Claim

This page covers **June 13 through August 6, 2025** — the weeks on either side of a single unverified assertion: that around **July 18, 2025** a decision was made to target **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** in Utah. **No public official document, court filing or sworn testimony confirms any such targeting decision**, and this site does not endorse it. Citizen investigators do point to a **separate, non-criminal scheduling claim** the same day: [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)'s [UVU event](/Locations/Public-Speaking-Event-UVU) request for September 10, reportedly submitted the morning of July 18 (Fox 13 / [GRAMA-style](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) reading). What follows is the dated record on both sides of July 18 — booking claims, search-pattern claims, and aviation — so a reader can see exactly what the "decision" inference is built from and judge whether the gap it fills is real.

## What the claim actually asserts, and what it does not

The assertion appears in long-form citizen chronologies as a **story beat**: a midpoint between the July media appearances and the September shooting. It names no person. It cites no order, no minutes, no leak. Proponents reason backwards from later patterns — [Google Trends](/GoogleSearches/investigation-index) screenshots, flight scheduling, the density of events in the following fortnight — and place an inflection point on July 18. Critics call that retrofitting: picking a date because the surrounding narrative needs one. This site records the allegation **without endorsing it** and **without naming any living person as having made such a decision**.

## The Timeline

### The run-up — mid-June to mid-July

* **June 13, 2025** — The Iran–Israel [twelve-day war](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war) commences. Every foreign-policy position Kirk had staked out in April becomes live.

* **June 18, 2025** — Kirk goes to the White House specifically to **[advise against regime change in Iran](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war)**. Researchers treat this as the moment his divergence stopped being rhetorical and became lobbying — the substrate for [donors, Israel and foreign policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy).

* **June 25, 2025** — Governor [Bill Lee](/People/bill-lee) travels to France for an aerospace investment, one of only a handful of foreign trips in his tenure. Researchers list it as an adjacent oddity; it has no established connection to Kirk and is included only because citizen timelines cite it.

* **July 2, 2025** — [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) formally steps down as lead pastor of Calvary Church in Thousand Oaks, a retirement reported as pre-planned in January. Relevant to this page only because his name reappears in the day-of chronology.

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits the United States, immediately before the TPUSA summit; no meeting with Kirk is established. See [Israel PM](/Israel/israel-pm).

* **July 9, 2025 (claim)** — Researchers assert the outgoing Salt Lake City FBI Special Agent in Charge, [Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed), was in Israel on this date; her successor [Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls) is claimed to have arrived from Israel on September 1. Both are researcher assertions, tracked at [SLC Field Office](/FBI/SLC_Field_Office) and [FBI and DPS search patterns](/GoogleSearches/fbi-dps-law-enforcement-searches).

### The July 11–16 window — the friction goes public

* **July 11–13, 2025 — TPUSA Student Action Summit, Tampa** — Kirk appears with [Megyn Kelly](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly). Per the master file he was rattled at having platformed Israel-critical speakers, and pro-Israel figures were rattled that he had.

* **July 13, 2025** — [Dave Smith](/Influencers/podcasts-dave-smith) debates [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) on stage — the Israel argument staged inside Kirk's own conference, covered at [Charlie–Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction).

* **July 14, 2025** — **[Tucker Carlson](/Influencers/podcasts-tucker-carlson)** speaks and tells the audience to "go all the way." Some Google Trends compilations claim this was the **first time Carlson appeared on a Kirk stage**, and researchers who advance the July 18 claim treat this platforming — and [Carlson's Israel positions](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) — as its immediate trigger.

* **July 16, 2025** — The *Ami's House* podcast episode **"Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout"** airs, putting donor friction on the public record for the first time. This is the **last clearly dated public-media event before July 18** — and one of the two anchors the claim leans on hardest (the other is the UVU form clock below).

* **July 17, 2025 (personnel adjacency — claimed)** — Citizen Trends compilations note **[Utah DPS Commissioner Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason)**'s appointment around **July 17**, with search interest the prior day. **Adjacency is not evidence** of any targeting decision; it is recorded only because those compilations place it next to July 18.

### July 18 — what is claimed, and what is documented

* **July 18, 2025 ~10:00–10:08 a.m. (scheduling claim — local reporting / GRAMA-style records)** — According to citizen investigators amplifying **Fox 13 Utah** reporting and [UVU records](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings), **TPUSA submitted the venue / event request** for the **September 10** American Comeback Tour stop at [UVU](/UVU/overview) on **this morning**. The submitter name is reportedly **redacted**. Secondary posts describe a form lifecycle of **modified ~August 21**, **publicly advertised ~August 27**, questions about whether a **[security plan](/Security_Team/next-event-no-plan)** box was checked, and whether the event jumped a **waiting list**. A form date — **if the reporting holds** — is a **booking fact**, **not** a document that anyone decided to kill Kirk. Primary Fox 13 URL and form images should be pinned when available. Sequencing used by the community (e.g. @NikkiRosier4, [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)): form **~5 days after** the Josh Hammer / Dave Smith debate and **~2 days before** the July 20 Trends / aviation cluster.

* **July 18, 2025 (claim, unverified — "decision" language)** — Citizen chronologies assert a **decision to target Kirk in Utah** was made "around" this date. There is **no primary source for any such decision**: no order, no minutes, no authenticated leak, no filing. Once the UVU form date circulated (late Oct–Nov 2025), many timelines **fused** the booking clock with decision language — *"UVU was locked in the same morning the decision was made."* That fusion is **inference**. Critics correctly note the absence of any kill-order document.

* **July 18, 2025 (claim evolution / weak variants)** — Low-engagement posts sometimes place the "decision" **during a U.S. military meeting** on or around July 18 — a **conflation** with the separate **July 20** military-meeting claim. Do not merge the dates.

* **July 18 (what the public record candidates are)** — The date is no longer a pure narrative empty cell if the **Fox 13 / form** track holds: July 18 has a **concrete scheduling candidate**. It still has **zero** public document asserting a targeting decision. The claim's remaining weight is pattern reasoning: July 14–16 fallout behind it; form morning; July 20 aviation, meeting, and Trends activity in front of it.

### The fortnight after — why the date is placed here

* **July 18–20, 2025 (Trends cluster — claimed)** — [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline)–style / [Project Constitution](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) compilations place multi-term search spikes (including hospital/surgeon terms; one list names a **July 18** hit on a surgeon at [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) — the hospital Kirk was later taken to instead of the region's [Level I trauma center](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed)) with a **July 20 peak**, two days after the alleged form filing. The surgeon cluster is catalogued at [the Timpanogos surgeon search frenzy](/Suspicious/Medical/surgeon-search-trends). Google Trends is **coarse, normalized, and without user identifiers**; geolocation-to-Israel claims are **contested**. See [July 20 2025 anchor](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor) and [Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns).

* **July 20, 2025** — Aircraft **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** makes its **first-ever trip to the United States**, to an army base in Nebraska. Detailed on [Nebraska army base July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) and [Egyptian jet SU-BTT](/Suspicious/Israel/egyptian-jet-su-btt).

* **July 20, 2025 (claim)** — A **military meeting with foreign leaders** is reported to have taken place on U.S. soil the same day. See [military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20) and [foreign leaders meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

* **July 20, 2025 (search-pattern claim)** — Researchers state that Google Trends interest across their whole tracked term set **peaks around July 20, 2025** — the single strongest anchor point in the search-pattern argument.

* **July–August 2025 (claim)** — Compilations report search spikes across the same window for TPUSA figures and Utah political names, listed at [TPUSA and political figures searches](/GoogleSearches/tpusa-political-figures-searches). Trends data has no user identifiers, so this shows topic interest and nothing about who generated it.

* **May–July 2025 (claim)** — The same compilations report **UVU-specific** searches — rooftop, parking, CCTV, layout, the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) — across this window, catalogued at [UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches) and [pre-event searches and recon](/Before/PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon).

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting on "confronting woke-right defamation of Israel," reportedly organized by the [Faith and Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel) and the [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding). Reported attendees include [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun) and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu). **TPUSA is absent** — see [July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA).

* **August 2, 2025** — French legionnaires reportedly arrive in the United States for a three-week training rotation in California ending August 25. Included because citizen timelines list it; no connection to Kirk is established.

* **~August 4, 2025** — The **Hamptons intervention**, where pro-Israel figures reportedly confront Kirk. Attendance lists are reported claims, not court findings. See [Hamptons intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **~August 4–6, 2025 (reported)** — [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) reportedly telephones Kirk in the same window and an offer of funding is described; Kirk reportedly declines a trip to Israel. The call is a reported claim with no released recording or readout — see [the reported final Netanyahu call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call).

* **August 6, 2025** — Kirk publicly complains about donor pressure in a Megyn Kelly appearance — the first time the friction reaches his own audience directly, and the point at which the "decision" narrative stops needing inference because the conflict is visible.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Proponents say | Critics say |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Is there a source for a **targeting decision** on July 18? | Pattern reasoning: Tampa fallout → form morning → July 20 Trends/aviation | There is **no primary kill-order** — no order, minutes, or authenticated leak. A booking form is not a decision document |
| Is there a source for **anything** on July 18? | **UVU event request ~10:00–10:08 a.m.** (Fox 13 / GRAMA-style citizen reading) | Primary form PDF / Fox 13 URL still needs independent pin; redactions leave submitter unknown |
| Why that specific date? | Form date + midpoint between July 16 fallout and July 20 cluster | Even with a form, "decision" language is still retrofitted inference |
| Form + "decision" fusion | "UVU was set up the same time the plan was made" | Legitimate scheduling for a fall tour stop; correlation is not a conspiracy finding |
| Google Trends as evidence | Peaks around July 20 and UVU-specific / surgeon terms show scouting | Trends is coarse, normalized, has no user identifiers; IP-to-Israel claims are contested |
| The July 20 meeting | A foreign-leaders military gathering on U.S. soil days after the "decision" | The meeting itself is a researcher claim; official confirmation and any attendee list remain unreleased |
| Effect of publishing the claim | Names no living person, so it is safe to record | Even unnamed, it invites readers to fill in names — which is why this site states plainly the **decision** claim is unproven |

## Open Questions

1. Can the **Fox 13 Utah** report, UVU form images, exact submission timestamp, approver, and security-plan fields be produced and independently verified?
2. Who at TPUSA (redacted name) submitted the UVU request, and did the event jump a waiting list?
3. Is there any primary document — an order, a calendar entry, minutes, a communication — dated on or near July 18, 2025 that is **not** a venue form but that touches targeting, security stand-down, or hostile surveillance of Kirk?
4. What was the manifest and purpose of SU-BTT's July 20 arrival at the Nebraska base?
5. Who attended the reported July 20 foreign-leaders meeting, and does an official record of it exist?
6. Can the July 20 Google Trends peak and any July 18 surgeon-term hit be reproduced independently, and does the underlying query volume survive scrutiny?
7. Does any of the July 2025 search activity resolve to identifiable subscribers, or does it remain aggregate forever?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Google's underlying query logs behind the July 2025 trend peaks with their subscriber and IP attribution, the flight manifest and CBP records for SU-BTT's July 20 arrival, the attendance list and minutes of the reported July 20 foreign-leaders meeting on U.S. soil, the scheduling record showing when and by whom the September 10 UVU appearance was booked, and the communications of the parties alleged in commentary to have influenced that event's location or security are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
