# 2025 Context Timeline

This page walks the whole of 2025 in order — the year that ended at 12:23 PM MDT on September 10 at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). It mixes four strands that most compilations keep apart: **donor and Israel-policy friction** around Charlie Kirk, **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) internal politics**, **foreign and military aircraft movements**, and **the private meetings and messages** researchers cite as pressure points. Documented events and contested claims are labeled as such on every line.

All Utah clock times are **MDT (Mountain Daylight Time)**. Tyler Robinson is **charged, not convicted**, and every allegation about a living person below is reported here as an allegation with its source named.

## The Timeline

### Winter and spring 2025

* **December 2024 (carried into 2025)** — Researchers compiling [Google search trend data](/GoogleSearches/overview) claim location-specific searches for the eventual venue appear months ahead of the event; this is a [researcher-reported dataset](/Timeline/2025), not a government record, and is disputed on methodology.

* **February 2025** — **Hardscape Specialties LLC**, the firm that would later pave the courtyard, is revived after roughly twenty dormant years, per corporate-filing research summarized on [Hardscape Specialties](/UVU/Hardscape-Specialties); the reactivation date only became notable in hindsight after the [post-shooting paving](/CoverUp/Dirt_Removed).

* **May 27, 2025** — Egyptian-registered aircraft **SU-BTU** flies into [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport), with **Walid Mahmoud** reported aboard by open-source flight trackers cataloged under the [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada).

* **June 2, 2025** — The same Egyptian party departs Provo, closing the first of what aviation researchers count as repeated [Egyptian-crew visits](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel) to the Provo area in 2025.

* **June 13, 2025** — The **Iran–Israel twelve-day war** begins, the geopolitical backdrop that [motive researchers](/Motive/Geopolitical_Intel_Service_Theories) treat as the year's inflection point for American conservative [Israel politics](/Israel/overview).

* **June 18, 2025** — Kirk visits the White House, reportedly to argue **against regime change in Iran**, per accounts collected on [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) and discussed on the [Iran](/Iran/overview) pages.

* **June 25, 2025** — Tennessee Governor **Bill Lee** takes an unusual international trip to France tied to an aerospace investment, a data point cited by researchers connecting the [AES explosives factory](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) thread to Tennessee; the connection is inference, not an established link.

### July 2025 — the split becomes public

* **July 2, 2025** — **Rob McCoy** formally resigns as lead pastor of Calvary Church, a retirement reported as pre-planned in January and later re-examined because McCoy appears in the [day-of call chain](/Timeline/shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **July 7–9, 2025** — Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits Washington, the visit that anchors most [donor-pressure reconstructions](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) of what followed.

* **July 11, 2025** — Kirk's decision to platform **anti-Israel speakers** reportedly alarms pro-Israel allies, the first open rupture documented on [Charlie–Israel Friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and detailed on the [political pressure timeline](./pre-event-political-pressure).

* **July 13, 2025** — At **TPUSA's Student Action Summit in Florida**, the **Dave Smith vs [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** debate on Israel airs the split in front of the movement's own base, per the [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **July 14, 2025** — **Tucker Carlson** speaks at the same summit and tells the audience to "go all the way," a line [Israel-coverage researchers](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) treat as the moment the intra-coalition fight stopped being private.

* **July 16, 2025** — A podcast episode titled "Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout" discusses internal tensions and donor disputes around TPUSA, cited by commentators as contemporaneous evidence the [fallout was real](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie) rather than retrospective.

* **July 18, 2025 (contested)** — Some citizen researchers assert a **decision to target Kirk in Utah** was made around this date; there is **no public document or court finding** supporting it, and the claim rests on trend data and retrospective sequencing — see [July 18 Decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025 (contested)** — Egyptian Air Force Falcon **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** makes what trackers describe as its **first-ever trip to America**, reportedly to an [Army base in Nebraska](./nebraska-army-base-july-20), the same day researchers place a [military meeting with foreign leaders](./military-meeting-july-20) on U.S. soil; no manifest has been released, so the purpose is unknown.

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** organized by the Faith and Freedom Coalition, the [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel), and the [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding) convenes on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel"; reported attendees include **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)**, **[Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)**, **[Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick)**, **[Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun)**, and **[Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)** — and **Kirk and TPUSA are absent**, a fact treated as significant on [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA).

### August 2025 — the intervention month

* **August 2, 2025** — **French legionnaires** arrive in the United States for a reported three-week California training rotation ending August 25, a detail that later attached itself to [Candace Owens' French-source claims](/Media/Candace-Owens-Israel-Controversies) about who trained the shooter; the training rotation itself is unremarkable and the link is her source's assertion, not a documented one.

* **August 4–6, 2025 (date disputed)** — The **Hamptons meeting**, described by multiple accounts as an "intervention" in which pro-Israel figures — reportedly **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)**, **[Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon)**, **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**, and **[Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff)** — confront Kirk about his rhetoric and platforming; attendee lists come from secondary reporting and have never been adjudicated. See [Hamptons Intervention](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **August 4–6, 2025 (contested)** — In the same window, commentators recount an alleged **call from Netanyahu** offering substantial funding tied to an Israel trip, which Kirk is said to have **declined**; the funding figure and the call itself rest on [Candace Owens'](/People/candace-owens) sourcing and third-party retelling, not on documents — see [the billion](/Israel/billion).

* **August 6, 2025** — Kirk appears with **Megyn Kelly** and, in widely circulated clips, complains that **donors are pressuring him** and that "my integrity is being called into question," the most-cited on-the-record artifact of the pressure arc; see [Megyn Kelly coverage](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly).

* **August 8, 2025 (contested)** — Some timelines mark a spike in **Israel-origin searches for the tail number [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L)**; this is a researcher-reported [Google Trends](/GoogleSearches/overview) data point, not a government statement, and Trends geography is coarse enough that critics reject the inference.

### September 1–9, 2025 — the last nine days

* **September 1–5, 2025 (claims)** — Compilations amplified by Owens and others claim Kirk said in **group chats** that he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"; the chats have not been authenticated in any public court filing — see [Leaked Kirk Texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts).

* **September 3, 2025** — **Orem Police** email UVU the Charlie Kirk **ticket link**, subject line "Charlie Kirk Tickets," per the Daily Caller News Foundation's [GRAMA records release](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records); Orem PD later said it was "not involved," and the joint Public Order Unit UVU pays for annually was never activated.

* **September 4, 2025** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** flies **France to Provo** and remains in the area through the event, per ADS-B screenshots collected on the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly about **leaving the pro-Israel movement**, the most-cited public marker of the break; see [Charlie's Israel Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

* **September 8, 2025** — Roughly **twelve lieutenant colonels** are reported at **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca)**, Arizona, arriving on **[SAM 702](/Planes/SAM-702)** and departing September 9 at 00:49, per flight-log compilations on [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9); the meeting's subject is not public.

* **September 9, 2025** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro's](/People/ben-shapiro)** show and criticizes Israeli policy to Shapiro's face, the break [donor-pressure researchers](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) treat as the point of no return.

* **September 9, 2025 — 2:23 PM** — An email in **[UVU Police Chief Jeff Long's](/People/jeff-long)** communication chain reads "He wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk," with the sender's name and phone number **redacted**; whether the message was delivered is not documented in the 112 released pages — see [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **September 9, 2025 (claims)** — Kirk attends a **combative high-pressure meeting** after which he reportedly texted that "**they are going to kill me**"; the sourcing is Owens and other commentators citing three sources, and the texts have not been produced in a public filing — see [High-Pressure Meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and [the text itself](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **September 9, 2025 — 10:35 PM** — **Two unidentified men** walk into a UVU office asking to speak with police about "**a plan**"; staff escalate it to the university cabinet, and 44 minutes later someone in the chat describes them as "GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did." Their names are **redacted** and nobody in the chain documented what they said — [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

### September 10–13, 2025

* **September 10, 2025 — 7:08 AM MDT** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** departs Utah for Wilmington, hours before the shooting, per the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **September 10, 2025 — 8:07 to 11:49 AM MDT** — A figure identified by investigators as Robinson is captured on **[Ring](/cameras/Ring) and [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE)** neighborhood cameras and [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) across six timestamps with a reported clothing change between them — see the [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **September 10, 2025 — 12:23 PM MDT** — **The shot.** Security begins moving Kirk at 00:18 and he is loaded into the SUV at 00:31 per the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute); some compilations use **12:27 PM** instead — see [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time).

* **September 10, 2025 — 6:38 PM MDT** — A photo released by Owens reportedly places Robinson at an **Orem [Dairy Queen](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem)**, about 17 minutes from campus, which sits awkwardly against the [vehicle and travel chronology](./rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).

* **September 11, 2025 — 6:25 PM vs 10:00 PM MDT** — Court documents cited by [Baron Coleman](./baron-coleman-timeline) show Robinson **Mirandized at 6:25 PM**, while the official arrest is logged at **10:00 PM**; the gap drives the whole [custody dispute](./custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline).

* **September 11, 2025 — 7:57–7:58 PM MDT** — The alleged **Discord confession** timestamps and the **enhanced stairwell photos** press conference both land **after** the 6:25 PM Miranda time, the core of the [confession-fabrication argument](/CoverUp/Confession_Fabrication) and the [enhanced-photo dispute](/CoverUp/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).

* **September 11, 2025** — **SU-BTT** leaves the United States for Egypt via Wilmington, per the same [flight-log compilation](/Planes/SU-BTT).

* **September 12, 2025 — ~2:00 AM MDT** — Federal and state investigators take custody at the **[Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff)** and drive Robinson 250-plus miles north to the [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail).

* **September 14, 2025** — Crews **pave the courtyard** where Kirk was shot, on a Sunday, under what contractor Dan Merrell says he was told was an emergency instruction — see [Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) and the [scene-changes timeline](./scene-changes-paving-timeline).

### The rest of 2025

* **September 16, 2025** — The **indictment** text messages appear in a form that differs from the messages Lance Twiggs reportedly gave the FBI on September 12, the two-version discrepancy examined on [Discord Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline).

* **September 17, 2025** — Sheriff **[Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** holds a press conference re-affirming the "**33-hour manhunt**" figure that citizen researchers argue is arithmetically incompatible with a 6:25 PM September 11 custody time — see [Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline).

* **September 19, 2025** — An interview titled "Demystifying the Mossad" with a former Mossad chief airs and is widely re-circulated by commentators building [Israel-linked theories](/Israel/Mossad); its relevance is thematic, not evidentiary.

* **September 21, 2025** — The **memorial service** at State Farm Stadium, produced with the same live-events vendor TPUSA had used for years, covered on the [funeral and memorial timeline](./funeral-memorial-timeline).

* **October 15, 2025** — Court proceedings and evidence-handling disputes begin the pattern that would stall the case into 2026 — see [October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025) and the [discovery timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hamptons meeting date | August 4 | August 5–6, or (per some retellings) conflated with a September 9 meeting |
| Netanyahu funding call | Occurred in early August, offer declined | Never documented; exists only in third-party retelling |
| July 18 "decision" | A decision to target Kirk was made around this date | No document, no filing, no court finding — pure inference from trend data |
| Shot time | 12:23 PM MDT (affidavit, most compilations) | 12:27 PM MDT (some media timelines) |
| SU-BTT relevance | Presence in Provo Sept 4–10 is operationally meaningful | Egyptian aircraft visited Provo repeatedly through 2025; presence proves nothing alone |
| The two men at UVU on Sept 9 | Tried to warn about "a plan" | Characterized in the chat as GOP reps who "played like they knew more than they did" |
| N1098L search spike | Israel-origin interest on August 8 | Google Trends geography is too coarse to support the inference |

## Open Questions

1. What is the **primary source of record** for each contested 2025 node — is there a document, a filing, or only a retelling?
2. Who convened the **July 31 White House meeting**, and why were Kirk and TPUSA not invited to a session about defending Israel inside the MAGA coalition?
3. What was **SU-BTT** doing in Provo between September 4 and 10, and who were its passengers?
4. Were the **September 9 group-chat and text messages** ever collected by investigators, and if so, are they in discovery?
5. Who were the **two unidentified men** who walked into a UVU office at 10:35 PM on September 9, and what "plan" were they describing?
6. Does any record show whether the redacted **September 9 message for Kirk** was ever delivered?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The SU-BTT and Egyptian-armada flight manifests and passenger lists, the attendance record and minutes of the July 31 White House meeting, the unredacted UVU GRAMA correspondence from September 9 including the names of the two men and the sender of the "get a message to Charlie Kirk" email, the authenticated originals of the September 1–9 group-chat and text messages, and the Fort Huachuca visitor logs for September 8–9 are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
