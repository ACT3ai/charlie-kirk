# Shooting Time Disputes (12:23 vs 12:27 MDT)

This timeline covers one clock face: the minute the shot was fired at Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025**, and every competing timestamp that has been attached to it. All times are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT)** unless marked otherwise. Enter here if you want to see exactly where **12:23:30**, **12:26** and **12:27** each came from, and what each one does to the roof-egress window and the death-pronouncement arithmetic downstream.

## The Timeline

### Morning — the clocks that were already running

* **~8:23 AM** — A neighborhood **[WYZE camera](/cameras/WYZE)** logs a vehicle on W 880 S, the first of the residential-camera timestamps that later anchor the [pre-shot campus movement reconstruction](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus) attributed to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), who is **charged and not convicted**.

* **~8:29 AM** — **[UVU campus surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance)** picks up the same driving sequence, giving investigators a second independent clock to check against the WYZE device — the kind of cross-check the [UVU security and surveillance timeline](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline) says has never been published with authenticated timecodes.

* **~9:16 AM** — Citizen flight-trackers say the Army-contracted Bombardier **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)**, the aircraft associated with the [HADES ISR program](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU), was over the valley on what one thread calls a drone drop-off leg — the opening entry of the [N1098L activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline) that later has to be aligned against whichever shot time you accept.

* **~9:57 AM** — A **WYZE camera** records a figure walking on W 880 S; the [camera-sightings page](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson) notes a **clothing change** to jeans and a long-sleeved shirt between this sighting and the next.

* **~11:44–11:49 AM** — **[Ring doorbell cameras](/cameras/Ring)** on W 800 S and S 800 W log the last pre-shot residential sightings, closing the morning window used in [roof-jumping feasibility](./roof-jumping-timeline) arguments and in the [clothing-change claim](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/robinson-clothing-changes).

* **~11:47 AM** — Trackers log **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** descending to roughly **600 feet AGL** near Utah Lake, about 36 minutes before the video-anchored shot — one of the [low-pass timing claims](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n1098l-hades-low-passes) that only reads as "minutes before" if 12:23 is correct.

* **11:22 AM – 12:12 PM (citizen clock)** — A Project Constitution GoPro recording circulated with continuous timestamps from a **11:22 AM bike arrival** through **12:12 PM**, ending about eleven minutes before the video-anchored shot. It is a fourth independent public clock for the morning, useful only if its device time is ever checked against the campus system.

* **~11:52 AM (reported)** — Early TMZ / police-framing summaries circulated by @OliLondonTV placed the charged person's **campus arrival** at about **11:52 AM**, two to three minutes after the last Ring sighting at 11:49 — useful as a pre-shot police stamp even though it is not a shot-time source. Charged, not convicted.

### 12:15–12:22 PM — roof approach clocks (prelim + early graphics)

* **~12:15 PM MDT (prelim testimony, reported)** — According to citizen re-tellings of investigator testimony in the July 2026 [preliminary hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) (summarized by @Recklesstruth00 and related threads), a figure identified by the State as the charged man was described **walking on the Losee roof** around this time before later prone positioning. Not a court finding of guilt; Robinson remains charged, not convicted.

* **12:20 PM MDT (early graphic, disputed)** — An early Sept 12, 2025 subject-timeline graphic circulated by @MJTruthUltra listed **"12:20 Shot fired"** and **"12:23 SJ jumping off building,"** conflicting with the later **12:23:30** consensus. Preserved here as an early alternate stamp, not as settled fact.

* **12:21–12:22 PM MDT (court video, reported)** — Per @EricLDaugh courtroom reporting of [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)'s description, enhanced roof surveillance played at the preliminary hearing showed **timestamp progression into position at ~12:21–12:22** before the 12:23 shot ("you see him take the shot… 12-21, 12-22… don't get to 12-23" framing of the approach). This is courtroom reporting of a video exhibit, not a conviction.

* **12:22 PM** — Per the charging narrative summarized on [Tyler Robinson's day-of page](/Tyler_Robinson/Tyler_9_10_2025), a figure stands, crosses the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** roof to the west side and lies prone facing the courtyard, roughly **430 feet** from the stage. Citizen critics of the Hull/investigator timeline argue ~90 seconds from prone to a long-range shot is implausible under adrenaline; that is a feasibility argument, not a finding.

* **12:22:08–12:22:12 PM MDT (citizen claim)** — @AngelaBethMoore posts stills claiming **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** executed a **360° phone pan of the crowd** less than a minute before impact; significance is disputed and is an open question about recording orientation, not an allegation of wrongdoing.

### 12:23 PM — the disputed minute

* **12:23:00 PM** — The state's account places **a single shot** fired from the Losee roof; this is the minute used by the affidavit summarized on the [probable-cause page](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit).

* **12:23:30 PM** — The investigation file's settled figure for the **exact moment of impact**, converted as **18:23:00 UTC**, and the zero point of the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](./shooting-minute-by-minute). Every other second-count on this site is measured from here. Citizen reconstructions (notably @BasedSamParker, Dec 2025) anchor their full evacuation clock at this zero.

* **12:23:28–12:23:30 PM (disputed visual)** — @DailyPlanetoid and others claim CourtTV-captured roof footage shows a **second moving figure** near the shot second before an angle cut; the State focuses on a single roof figure. Unverified visual interpretation of grainy surveillance — see also [roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview).

* **12:23:29 – 12:23:54** — Four **surveillance stills** carry these embedded timestamps and trace movement from the firing position to the northeast corner of the Losee Center; the [cameras section](/cameras/overview) treats the stills as the closest thing to a public timecode, while [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) argues they have never been released as continuous footage.

* **12:23:30 PM (00:00)** — In the same second, **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)** is described as [calling his wife](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-calls-wife) — a call the [witness-testimony discrepancies page](./witness-testimony-discrepancies) flags because [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) and [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) give accounts that do not reconcile with what the frame shows.

* **12:23:48 PM (00:18)** — Security **begins to move** [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), the first physical event that can be counted off the video clock rather than off a report.

* **12:23:52 PM (00:22, alternate micro-stamp)** — @BasedSamParker later cites **load-start at 12:23:52** (32 seconds post-shot), slightly earlier than the page's common **12:24:01** load stamp — a micro-timing conflict, not a different shot time.

* **12:23:57 PM (00:27)** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) is already **in position on the rock pile** filming, per the [ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot) reconstruction and the [rock-pile claim page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile).

### 12:24 PM — the first minute after

* **12:24:01 PM (00:31)** — Kirk is **loaded into the black SUV**; the vehicle itself became its own dispute, documented on the [SUV destruction page](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed) and in the [discovery and evidence timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).

* **12:24:03 PM (00:33)** — Mikey McCoy is seen **well past the SUV without Blake Neff** in frame — the [McCoy alone in frame conflict](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/mccoy-neff-call-conflict), and the specific frame that drives the [witness discrepancy](./witness-testimony-discrepancies) argument.

* **12:24:12 PM (00:42)** — **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** gets into the SUV; his later on-record hospital timing is the anchor that makes the afternoon [aircraft arithmetic](./other-planes-activity-timeline) contested.

* **~12:24 PM** — On the roof, per the state summary on [Tyler Robinson's shooting page](/Tyler_Robinson/Shooting/overview), a **dark-wrapped item** is placed down and the figure lowers to the grass — the egress step whose feasibility inside a single minute is the whole subject of the [roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline).

* **12:24:22 PM (00:52)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying **"They just shot Charlie."** The [SD cards and cameras removed from the scene](/CoverUp/SD_Cards_And_Cameras_Removed) question begins in this same untimed stretch.

* **12:24:13 – 12:24:35 PM (00:43–01:05)** — The **SUV departs** for the hospital without an ambulance — an omission tracked on the [six officers, no ambulance page](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/six-officers-no-ambulance).

* **12:24:36 PM (01:06)** — Farnsworth is recorded saying **"He's dead"** — roughly **66 seconds** after the video-anchored shot, and about **39 minutes** before the arithmetic below places a surgeon's pronouncement.

* **~12:24 PM** — One reading of the tracking data puts a second **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** low pass at about **335 feet AGL** here; a competing reading puts it at **12:48 PM**, and the [low-pass timing page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/n1098l-low-pass-timing) does not resolve which.

### 12:26–12:27 PM — the dispatch clock and the orphan timestamp

* **12:26 PM** — Per **police audio**, the **first report of the shooting** comes in. This is a *call-received* time, not an impact time, and the [first police report gap page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/first-police-report-gap) argues the two are routinely conflated in later summaries. Contemporaneous @SLCScanner write-ups also fixed the incident "around 12:23 p.m. local time," consistent with the video consensus rather than the orphan 12:27.

* **12:26 PM** — Mikey McCoy is described as **three-waying in [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** — the [dispatch and scanner timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline) carries the radio side of the same two minutes — a call sequence the [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline) tracks separately.

* **Notification order (citizen claim, ~12:23–12:35)** — @NihiloX argues **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)** received **real-time condition updates from the transport chain before Erika Kirk was fully connected** at ~12:26. @LibertyLockPod and @BasedSamParker challenge the latency math against a roughly **10-minute hospital run**, arguing any "security update" claim before ~12:33 MT should be timestamp-proved. Attribution only — not established fact; living persons are not accused of wrongdoing by this site.

* **12:27 PM** — The **disputed alternative shot time**. It appears in parentheses in the investigation file and in some secondary media summaries, but **no outlet, dispatch log, or document has ever been named as its source** — the reason the [shot-time claim page](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/shot-time-1223-vs-1227) rates it THIN rather than treating it as a live official conflict. Some low-engagement posts also float **12:24 as a shot time**; that reading is usually a conflation with the first post-shot minute rather than a primary source.

* **12:27 PM MST** — A separate and unrelated **12:27** sits in the same day's record: the **Scottsdale departure** of the aircraft [N40JD](/Planes/N40JD). Time-zone collisions like this are exactly the mechanism critics cite when they argue [plane-overlap claims](./other-planes-activity-timeline) rest on UTC-versus-local errors.

### After — the death-pronouncement arithmetic

* **12:29–12:35 PM (estimated)** — Kirk reportedly arrives at **[Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital)**; the [medical and death timeline](./medical-death-timeline) notes the choice of a Level IV trauma center over the closer, higher-capability alternative, argued at [trauma center bypassed](/Suspicious/Medical/trauma-center-bypassed).

* **~12:42 PM MDT** — According to screenshots shared by @ZachCostello_, **UVU student alerts announced a suspect "in custody"** less than 20 minutes after the shot; a later alert reportedly warned the shooter had **not** been found (~+69 minutes). Early false-custody clock relative to 12:23 — see [UVU security and surveillance](./uvu-security-surveillance-timeline) and [distraction events](./distraction-events-timeline).

* **~12:56 PM – 1:13 PM** — A citizen reconstruction adds the segments (1 minute to leave, 7–10 minutes to drive, 5–10 minutes to unload and reach the OR, 20–30 minutes in the OR) and lands on this window as the **earliest possible pronouncement of death**. The same reconstruction claims a **Jerusalem Post** death announcement published before that window closed — a sequencing claim examined at [the OR timeline versus the Jerusalem Post](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post) and tracked on the [Israel events timeline](./israel-events-pressure-timeline). Commentators sharpen the math as JPost stamp **21:54 Israel time = 12:54 MT**, roughly **31 minutes** after a 12:23 shot ("before any U.S. outlet" claim per @JohnBWellsCTM) — media-timing claim only, not proof of foreign involvement.

* **~1:03:30 PM** — The midpoint of that arithmetic, quoted in the file as the moment **a surgeon came out and pronounced him deceased**. It is a *derived* time, not a released record; the certificate itself remains among the items on the [autopsy and forensic timeline](./autopsy-forensic-timeline) and the [sealed autopsy page](/CoverUp/Autopsy_Sealed_Examiner).

* **~5:42 PM MDT** — @SLCScanner contemporaneous radio traffic reported a **weapon recovered** on campus across from the Losee Building — the [Mauser .30-06](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) of the official narrative (day-of recovery clock, not shot time). Scanner accuracy is not guaranteed, and the [rifle chain of custody](/Law_Enforcement/rifle-chain-of-custody) starts from this stamp.

* **~8:12 PM** — Some citizen compilations instead cite this evening time as the pronouncement, in one framing "at the scene." It is irreconcilable with the hospital-transport sequence above and is preserved here as a **conflicting compilation entry**, not as a finding — see the [autopsy and death-certificate claim page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/autopsy-death-certificate).

* **~7:12 PM (disputed)** — The investigation file also carries an evening departure for the jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** leaving Provo, described elsewhere in the same file as "45 minutes" and "one hour" after the shot. Those descriptors point to the early afternoon, not the evening, which is why the [N888KG page](/Planes/N888KG) and the [other-planes timeline](./other-planes-activity-timeline) flag 7:12 PM as an internal inconsistency.

### Later — how the times hardened

* **Sept 11, 2025 — 6:25 PM** — A Washington County video exhibit (**Bates 003996-R2**) timestamps a **Miranda reading**, per the defense filing summarized on the [Miranda hub](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview) — a clock that collides with the official custody narrative.

* **Sept 11, 2025 — 8:02 PM** — Sheriff **[Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** states he received the identifying call at this time, an account the [Miranda-versus-Brooksby page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account) sets against the 6:25 PM stamp; he [later resigned](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation).

* **Sept 11, 2025 — ~9 PM / 10 PM** — Surrender and booking times diverge between the sheriff's account and FBI Director **[Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)**'s public "arrest at 10 p.m." framing, per the [Patel press briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings), the [Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline) and the [Sept 11 surrender page](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody).

* **Nov 17, 2025** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) publishes the "Operation Mocking-Plane" episode, pushing the day's timing questions — airport diversion, flight overlaps — into wider circulation, as tracked on the [Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **July 2026 hearings** — The **preliminary hearing** (about five court days ending ~July 10) presented enhanced roof video with continuous on-screen times through **12:21–12:23** and investigator testimony placing a roof walk earlier (~12:15) and prone at ~12:22. The [preliminary hearing pages](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) and [Timeline 2026](./2026) still record **no publicly released master slate** synchronizing campus cameras, livestream and dispatch onto one continuous timecode.

## Open Questions

1. Which specific outlet, log, or broadcast first published **12:27 PM**, and does that source still exist to be examined?
2. Has any authority released a **master slate** synchronizing UVU cameras, the livestream audio, and dispatch onto one continuous timecode?
3. What is the **clock drift** on each recording device — WYZE, Ring, campus CCTV, livestream, body cameras — and was any of it ever measured?
4. What time does the **death certificate** state, and does it match the ~1:03 PM arithmetic or the ~8:12 PM compilation figure?
5. Does the raw **Carbyne 911 / dispatch** record contain a call earlier than 12:26 PM that never reached the public summaries?
6. Do the tracking archives place the second **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** low pass at 12:24 or 12:48, and does the answer change the low-pass interpretation?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The unedited UVU camera files with embedded and authenticated timecodes, the raw 911 and Carbyne dispatch logs with clock-synchronization records, the sealed autopsy and the death-certificate timing, the original livestream master audio, and the unredacted flight-tracking archives for aircraft over Utah Valley that day are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
