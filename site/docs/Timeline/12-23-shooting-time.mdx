# Shooting Time Disputes (12:23 vs 12:27 MDT)

The most contested minute on September 10, 2025 is **when the fatal shot occurred** at Utah Valley University. Public sources cluster around **12:23 PM MDT** or **12:27 PM MDT**, with dispatch logs and time-of-death windows cited differently. This page maps the dispute without picking a single official verdict beyond what each source type claims.

## Timestamps in circulation

| Time (MDT) | How it appears in project sources |
| --- | --- |
| **~12:23 PM** | [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025), [Timeline Overview](./overview) — shot time from **police-audio timestamps** and many citizen compilations; used in analysis docs (e.g., 10-page write-up summaries) |
| **12:26 PM** | First **police report / dispatch** log in [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025) and overview summaries |
| **~12:27 PM** | Some **media response timelines** and suspect-profile summaries (e.g., trash *timeline_compilation*, *tyler_robinson* stubs) — "Kirk shot in throat area" |
| **12:23–12:27 window** | **Time-of-death range** noted on [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025) when sources disagree on the precise impact second |

## Why the four-minute spread matters

Citizen investigators argue that **synchronized audio, scanner feeds, and video** anchor the shot closer to **12:23**, while some mainstream or early media summaries rounded or reported **12:27**. A few compilations also cite **8:12 PM** as when Kirk was **pronounced dead at the scene** (citizen examination doc) — a separate question from **time of impact**.

Discrepancies affect:

- Whether **[roof-jumping timeline](./roof-jumping-timeline)** accounts are physically plausible in the same minute as the shot
- How **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** low passes (claimed ~11:47 AM pre-shot; ~12:24–12:48 PM post-shot on [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025)) align with impact time
- **[Witness testimony](./witness-testimony-discrepancies)** consistency across interviews

## Evidence types behind each time

- **12:23 anchor:** Police-radio compilations posted by journalists and commentators; multi-angle sync projects on X/YouTube (labeled **claims** on [Sept_10_2025](./Sept_10_2025)).
- **12:27 anchor:** Secondary media timelines without always citing primary audio timecodes.
- **12:26 anchor:** Dispatch "first report" time — not identical to time of injury, but often quoted in news briefings.

## What would resolve the dispute

Trash-source *Laws* blocks (merged from former `12_23` and `12_27` stubs) point to records not fully public:

- Unedited **UVU camera timestamps** synchronized with **raw 911 / dispatch** logs
- **Sealed forensic and autopsy** timeline entries
- **Carbyne 911 system** logs and **cell-tower** movement data for the accused

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The unedited UVU camera timestamps and the raw 911 and dispatch call logs and the sealed forensic and autopsy timeline and the police dispatch and Carbyne 911 system logs and cell-tower data fixing the suspect's exact movements are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

**12:23 PM MT** remains the public anchor time for the fatal shot in most reconstructions.

- Citizen multi-cam sync projects argue sub-second offsets between crack, mic artifacts, and body motion — relevant to [Mic](/Mic/overview) and acoustic placement theories.
- Official affidavit paths use the same minute window for rooftop flight after the shot.
- Disputes are less about the clock minute than about **what energy source** produced the wound inside that second.
