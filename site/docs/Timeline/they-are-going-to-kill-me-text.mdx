# "They Are Going to Kill Me" Text (September 9, 2025, Claims)

One alleged message, sent the day before the shooting, has become the most-cited pre-death artifact
in the case: [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly telling confidants **"they are going to
kill me."** This page is a chronology of that claim — what Kirk did publicly on September 8 and 9,
when each alleged recipient and each wording variant entered the public record, who disputes the
messages exist at all, and what would authenticate them. **No court has authenticated these
messages, and no court has found that any living person named in connection with them caused
Kirk's death.**

## The Timeline

### The months before

* **April 3, 2025** — Kirk posts publicly against a new Middle East war, warning that "the war drums
  are beating again in DC" — the earliest widely-cited marker of the friction that motive
  researchers point to. Context on [Charlie–Israel Friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and
  [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

* **Summer 2025** — Kirk pushes on the Epstein files and declines to cancel Tucker Carlson, both of
  which appear in donor-conflict accounts —
  [the Epstein files push](/Charlie/Epstein_Files_Push) and
  [Tucker Carlson and Israel](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel).

* **Aug 2025 (reported)** — A meeting in the Hamptons is described by researchers as a donor-pressure
  event. The date is uncertain in the source material — some accounts place it in early August,
  others tie the confrontation to September 9 —
  [the Hamptons intervention meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and
  [Israel and the Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **Before Sept 9 (leaked, later confirmed by TPUSA)** — A WhatsApp chat with donors in which Kirk
  writes that he has "just lost another huge Jewish donor" over refusing to cancel Carlson, that
  "Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes," and that this is *"leaving me no choice but to
  leave the pro-Israel cause."* **TPUSA has publicly confirmed this chat**, which is what
  distinguishes it from the "kill me" messages —
  [Leaked Kirk Texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts) and
  [Israel Donors and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).

### September 9, 2025 — the day itself

* **Sept 9 (reported)** — A confrontation described as an *"intervention"* in which Kirk is
  challenged by pro-Israel figures. Named attendees in citizen compilations include Rabbi Pesach
  Wolicki, Josh Hammer, Seth Dillon, Bill Ackman and Natasha Hausdorff. Attendance lists differ
  between retellings and none of the named attendees has been accused in any court of anything —
  [the September 9 high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) and
  [Political Pressure and Meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).

* **Sept 9 — publicly verifiable** — Kirk appears on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s** show
  and voices sharper criticism of Israeli policy to Shapiro directly. This is the one anchor of the
  day that requires no private sourcing — [2025 Context Timeline](./Year_2025) and
  [Charlie's Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

* **Sept 9, during or after the meeting (claim)** — The alleged text. The master investigation file
  records it as: *"During this combative meeting, Kirk reportedly texted others saying, 'THEY ARE
  GOING TO KILL ME.'"* The timing is placed inside or immediately after the confrontation in most
  retellings, but no retelling gives a clock time.

* **Sept 10, 12:23 PM MDT** — Kirk is shot at UVU, roughly 24 hours after the alleged messages —
  [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025).

### How the claim entered and grew in the record

* **Weeks after the shooting** — **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** publishes the donor
  WhatsApp texts, which had been dismissed as a hoax until she released them. That publication is
  what made subsequent private-message claims credible to a large audience —
  [Candace Owens' podcast coverage](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) and
  [the Candace Owens timeline](./candace-owens-timeline).

* **On Myron Gaines' podcast and in later interviews (claim)** — Owens reportedly states that a
  **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) staffer** possessed a text from Kirk the day before he died reading
  *"they are going to kill me,"* and that in additional September 9 communications with **three
  people** he said *"I think they're going to kill me."*

* **Aggregation phase** — Multiple outlets including Primetimer, Sportskeeda and Times of India
  repeat the quote, at which point it circulates far beyond its original sourcing —
  [Mainstream vs Alternative Media](/Media/Mainstream-vs-Alternative-Media).

* **Variant: "They will kill me"** — Attributed to a message said to have been sent to a recipient
  named **Andrew**. Some threads on X, including [@AdameMedia](/Media/social-media-platforms),
  identify that recipient as **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** after a September 9 Zoom.
  Kolvet has not been accused in any court of anything.

* **Variant: "I'm worried that Israel is going to kill me"** — Circulated in second-hand retellings.

* **Variant: "They will kill me if I break away from being pro-Israel"** — Attributed to second-hand
  sources including **[Harrison Smith](/People/harrison-smith)** segments and unnamed friends —
  [Political Pressure and Meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).

* **Variant: "executed tomorrow" language** — Attributed in the master file to messages involving
  **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** and security staff. Flood has not been accused in any court of
  anything.

* **Variant: "They want me dead"** — A thread attributed to **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)**
  describing a warning plus increased security. Circulated by
  [@Villgecrazylady](/Media/social-media-platforms) among others.

* **The denial** — At least one associate close to Kirk is quoted saying the messages never
  happened: *"He was NOT messaging people the day before saying 'they're going to kill me.' I have
  his cell phone. It never happened."* TPUSA has confirmed the **donor** texts but has **not**
  confirmed this specific message, and the alleged September 9 "kill me" messages have **not been
  released or independently verified**.

* **Counterweight reading** — Even taken at face value, the messages may be private hyperbole under
  extreme donor stress rather than a specific warning about a specific plot. The official case
  charges no text recipient with anything.

* **Ongoing** — Phone forensics that could settle it are contested territory in the litigation —
  [Phone Extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction) and
  [Phone Surveillance Claims](/Charlie/Phone_Surveillance_Claims).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point | Version A | Version B | Who asserts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Do the messages exist | Yes — a staffer had one, and three people received variants | No — "I have his cell phone. It never happened." | Candace Owens vs an associate quoted in rebuttal |
| Exact wording | "They are going to kill me" | "I think they're going to kill me" / "They will kill me" / "They want me dead" | Different retellings; no two agree exactly |
| Recipient | Andrew (reported as Andrew Kolvet) | Dan Flood, Frank Turek, unnamed staffer, three unnamed people | Each attribution has a different source chain |
| Trigger | The September 9 confrontation | Accumulated donor pressure over months | [High-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9) vs [donor motive](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) |
| Meaning | A specific warning about a specific threat | Private hyperbole under stress | Both readings are live; neither is established |
| Corroboration | TPUSA confirmed the donor texts, so these are plausible | TPUSA has pointedly not confirmed these | The distinction is the strongest fact on this page |

## Open Questions

1. Does the full unredacted thread exist in any forensic extraction of Kirk's phone, and who holds
   that device now?
2. Who are the three people Owens says received September 9 messages, and will any of them speak on
   the record?
3. What do carrier records show for outbound messages from Kirk's number on September 9?
4. Has any named alleged recipient confirmed or denied receiving such a message in a sworn or
   on-the-record statement?
5. Why has TPUSA confirmed the donor WhatsApp chat but not these messages?
6. Do the messages, if authenticated, name anyone, or are they generic?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The full unredacted text thread and the complete recipient list, the carrier records authenticating
send times and originating device, the forensic extraction of Kirk's phone, and any preserved
copies held by TPUSA staff or security personnel are things that the
[Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that
aren't out yet.
