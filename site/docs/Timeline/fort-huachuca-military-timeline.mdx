# Fort Huachuca & Military-Related Activity Timeline

**Fort Huachuca, Arizona** is the U.S. Army's Intelligence Center of Excellence and counterintelligence school. This page is the **military-adjacent layer** of the case chronology: the July 2025 meetings on U.S. soil, the French legionnaire training window, the VIP aircraft routing that put a government jet on that base on **September 8–9, 2025**, the reported gathering of roughly **twelve lieutenant colonels**, and the post-event photograph claims. Almost everything here is **circumstantial and disputed** — VIP visits to an intelligence installation are routine, and no agency has connected any of it to the September 10 shooting. The political-pressure layer of the same 48 hours is on the [September 9 high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9); the day-of clock is on [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025).

## The Timeline

### July 2025 — personnel, meetings, and first-ever arrivals

* **July 11, 2025 (reported)** — The same day Kirk publicly urged Tucker Carlson to go "all the way" in criticism of Israel, master-file notes record a Washington shuffle: **[Dustin Gard-Weiss](/People/dustin-gard-weiss)** moves from performing the duties of **Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security** to **Executive Director of the CIA**. The same notes elsewhere describe John Ratcliffe as remaining CIA Director, so the "replacing" language in circulation is internally inconsistent and should be treated with care.

* **July 18, 2025 (claimed)** — Master-file timeline asserts a decision to target Kirk in Utah "was made around July 18th." This is an **assertion without a named source** and is examined on its own page at [the July 18 decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025 (claimed)** — The Egyptian-registered [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) makes its **first-ever trip to America**, arriving at an **Army base in Nebraska** — the subject of [Nebraska Army base, July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) and the [SU-BTT movement timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **July 20, 2025 (claimed)** — A **military meeting with foreign leaders** is described as taking place on U.S. soil the same day; the two overlapping accounts are separated on [military meeting July 20](./military-meeting-july-20) and [foreign leaders meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20), with the search-trend anchor at [July 20, 2025 anchor](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor).

* **July 22, 2025 (reported)** — The Senate confirms **[Bradley D. Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** as **Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security**. His public résumé includes Army Special Forces, Boston Consulting Group, and the National Security Council. Confirmation to a Senate-confirmed post is a matter of public record and carries no implication of wrongdoing.

### August 2025 — training window

* **August 2 – August 25, 2025 (reported)** — **French legionnaires** arrive in the United States for a roughly **three-week training rotation in California** alongside U.S. Marines. The window closes about two weeks before September 10. Later commentary tying legionnaires to the assassination is an **attributed allegation with no named evidence** and is held, with its counterarguments, at [French Foreign Legion claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims).

* **August 2025 (claimed)** — Commentary alleges a payment originating with a Paris club of elites and routed through a Canadian bank; the figure is quoted inconsistently as **$1.5 million** and **$150 million** in the same source material. The internal inconsistency is itself the reason to treat the claim as unverified.

### September 7–8, 2025 — the aircraft routing

* **September 7, 2025 (claimed, flight-tracking)** — A U.S. government **C-37A Gulfstream V VIP jet, tail 99-0404**, flies from [Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews) to **Colorado Springs**. See [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404).

* **September 7–8, 2025 (reported)** — Public reporting cited in the master file places **Hansell** meeting **JTF South Border** leadership in **Colorado Springs** — the same unit a witness later says he encountered at Fort Huachuca the following day.

* **September 8, 2025 (claimed, flight-tracking)** — The same aircraft flies **Colorado Springs to Tucson**, then departs Tucson about five hours later with the transponder **apparently switched off**, entering [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) with the callsign changed from **RCH** to **SAM**. The tipster who compiled this notes it is the **only time** he has observed that aircraft at Fort Huachuca.

* **September 8, 2025 (claimed)** — Master-file notes state **Hansell's office booked** the Andrews–Colorado Springs–Tucson trip. The aircraft is described as holding **twelve**; who else was aboard, and who was picked up at Colorado Springs that triggered the callsign change, is unresolved.

* **September 8, 2025 (claimed)** — Roughly **twelve lieutenant colonels** are reported at Fort Huachuca. The callsign under which the trip is discussed varies across sources — **SAM702**, **SAM-000**, and **SAM-112** all appear — against the single tail number 99-0404. See [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702), [SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112), and [SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000).

* **September 8, 2025 (claimed)** — A separate note associates **[Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth)** with Fort Huachuca on this date and adds that the flight reduced altitude and speed over the **AES** plant in Tennessee. The AES connection is a proponent-side link to the [exploding-mic thread](./audio-acoustic-mic-timeline) and is disputed; see [AES Tennessee explosion](/CoverUp/AES_Tennessee_Explosion).

### September 9, 2025 — the meeting and the departures

* **~00:49 (claimed)** — Master-file timeline records **SAM702 departing** at this hour. This conflicts with the tipster's daytime departure figures below; the site has not reconciled them.

* **Early morning (claimed)** — **[Mitch Snow](/People/mitch-snow)** says he walked into a closed high-clearance meeting on base, was taken outside and questioned at length, and was then reported as a possible bomb threat.

* **~7:30 AM local (claimed)** — Master-file notes give the meeting's conclusion time. The person described as leading the roughly **seven-hour questioning** is named as **Captain [Adam Neff](/People/adam-neff)** of Cookeville, Tennessee. A **Lt. Col. [Daniel Neff](/People/daniel-neff)** is described as a cousin of **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**, Kirk's longtime producer — who was, per public record, a **wargames specialist at the Marine Corps War College from 2016 to 2020**, work that involved facilitating simulations with colonels and lieutenant colonels. **Shared surnames and adjacent careers are not evidence of coordination**, and no authority has alleged any of them did anything wrong.

* **~7:30 AM local (claimed, contested identification)** — Snow says he believes he saw a **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) executive** leaving with an unidentified grey-haired man. Candace Owens, who published his account, states explicitly that **this identification is the part that cannot be confirmed**. See [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole).

* **11:17 AM (documented)** — The **incident report** later obtained by Owens records **bomb dogs being called** at this time — corroborating that Snow was on base and that an incident occurred, while corroborating nothing about who he saw. See [FBI and bomb-dog patterns](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs) for the broader pattern.

* **~11:43 AM (claimed)** — The tipster's read of the flight data places the aircraft leaving at this time, which he argues lines up with a morning sighting of the group.

* **12:31 PM (claimed)** — The tipster's screenshots show **99-0404 flying Fort Huachuca to El Paso**, then **El Paso back to Andrews**. The El Paso leg matters because **Biggs Army Airfield** is there.

* **Undated within this window (claimed)** — Master-file notes state **Hansell is in charge of the HADES program**, run out of **[Biggs Army Airfield](/Planes/Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield)** — the field from which **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** departed on September 10. That is the single link that connects the Huachuca thread to the aircraft over UVU; see [N1098L HADES over UVU](/Proof_Intel_Services/N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU), the [N1098L activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline), and [ISR operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

* **Undated, "strange event" note (claimed, speculative)** — A master-file entry describes a **"JTF"** visit at Fort Huachuca involving a well-dressed-down woman described as a senior administration visitor, a **General Services Administration** presence, a Secret Service officer, and a group who "cared very much about the next 24 hours" and departed with **Rep. Mark Amodei (R-NV)**. The note floats **Karoline Leavitt** as a guess for the unnamed woman. This is **explicitly speculation in the source itself**, no identification has been made, and no wrongdoing is alleged against any person named or guessed at.

### September 10, 2025 and after

* **September 10, 2025 (claimed)** — [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) makes its reported low passes near UVU; the day-of aviation entries are on [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025).

* **After the event (claimed)** — An open-source account compares, in [the Cabot Phillips alibi analysis](/US_Intelligence/Cabot_alibi), the flooring, dark wall trim, and a distinctive yellow wall in **Cabot Phillips's** alibi video to interior photographs from Fort Huachuca, arguing the video may have been shot on or near the base. **A visual similarity in institutional carpet and trim is weak evidence** — these finishes are standard across federal facilities.

* **After the event (claimed, unresolved)** — Posts allege that **[Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown)**, described as handling the standing mic at the TPUSA event, and **[Jeb Jacobi](/People/jeb-jacobi)** appear in a Fort Huachuca photograph. The open question of whether "Cooper Brown" and Jeb Jacobi are the **same person or two people** is unresolved and asserted neither way. The counterpoint is strong: the referenced image is a Fort Huachuca **Morale, Welfare and Recreation** community post showing three young men in **Lawley Automotive Group** shirts at what looks like a dealership giveaway in a base recreation centre — which establishes no intelligence affiliation at all. See [Cooper Brown as witness](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cooper-brown-witness) and the [witness profile](/Witnesses/cooper-brown).

* **After the event (circulated screenshot, 2022 origin)** — A screenshot recirculates of a **2022** exchange in which **[Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)** mentions being sent to Fort Huachuca for training and **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)** replies "jack stop going to huachuca." It predates the case by three years and is offered as tone, not evidence.

* **After the event (claimed, uncorroborated)** — A note flags a Qureshi-family relative said to have specialized in **drones at Fort Huachuca**; the master file marks this entry **"need to double check"** and does not resolve which brother it refers to. See [Zach Qureshi](/People/zach-qureshi) and [Ahmed Qureshi](/People/ahmed-qureshi).

* **After the event (claimed)** — A post alleges the man filmed burgling Mitch Snow's home is **[Kyle Saltz](/People/kyle-saltz)**, described as Huachuca-trained counterintelligence. This is an **unproven allegation against a living person** repeated here only because it circulates; no charge or finding supports it.

* **After the event (claimed)** — Researchers say that **the day after** Fort Huachuca image-search data was published, results for every named figure — DIA, CENTCOM, Crane, and Kirk security figures — were **wiped**, while unnamed people still returned results. See [Fort Huachuca image searches](/GoogleSearches/fort-huachuca-image-searches), [DIA search patterns](/GoogleSearches/dia-search-patterns), [CENTCOM search patterns](/GoogleSearches/centcom-search-patterns), and [Crane search patterns](/GoogleSearches/crane-search-patterns).

* **Ongoing (reported)** — Records requests touching the base and the flight remain unfulfilled; the withholding is tracked at [Huachuca records withheld](/CoverUp/Huachuca_Records_Withheld) and [U.S. intelligence assisted — Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Which callsign | **SAM702** | **SAM-000** or **SAM-112**, all against tail 99-0404 | Master-file timeline vs the Hegseth note vs flight-tracking screenshots |
| Departure time from Huachuca | **00:49 on Sept 9** | **~11:43 AM** or **12:31 PM on Sept 9** | Master-file timeline vs the tipster's ADS-B reads |
| Meeting date | **Sept 8** (LTC gathering) | **Sept 9** (the meeting Snow walked into) | Two entries in the same file; possibly two events, possibly one |
| Snow's account | Corroborated by the incident report and bomb-dog log | The **identification of who he saw** is unconfirmed by anyone, including Owens | Owens' release vs Owens' own caveat |

## Open Questions

1. What is the complete **passenger manifest** for the 99-0404 / SAM trip, and who boarded at Colorado Springs?
2. What was the **stated purpose** of the September 8–9 gathering, and does a meeting agenda or attendance roster exist?
3. Why did the transponder go quiet on the Tucson leg, and was that an authorized suppression?
4. Was there **any** operational connection between the Huachuca visit and the HADES tasking that put N1098L over Utah on September 10?
5. Does the incident report name the officers who questioned Mitch Snow, and does a written statement from that questioning exist?
6. Who requested the removal of the Fort Huachuca-related image search results, if anyone did?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The full passenger manifest and trip-authorization file for the September 7–9 SAM routing through Colorado Springs, Tucson, Fort Huachuca and El Paso, the agenda and attendance roster for the September 8–9 gathering, the unredacted Fort Huachuca incident report and any statements taken during the questioning that morning, the HADES tasking orders that placed N1098L over Utah on September 10, and the records the Army has so far declined to release about who was on base that week are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
