# Nebraska Army Base Visit — SU-BTT (July 20, 2025, Claims)

**July 20, 2025** is the earliest date at which the Egyptian-registered Falcon 7X **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** is claimed to have set down on U.S. soil — reportedly in the **Omaha–Lincoln, Nebraska** corridor, at what open-source researchers describe as an Army base. The same date carries a separate allegation of a [foreign-leaders meeting on U.S. soil](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20). This page walks the chronology around that single day and states plainly which parts are documented, which are alleged, and which are neither.

:::caution What is actually established here
**No official source has connected the July 20 Nebraska stop to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death.** The installation has never been named in any document reproduced on this site. The entire item rests on ADS-B screenshots and aviation-spotter posts. Foreign government aircraft visit U.S. military airfields routinely for training, maintenance and diplomacy. Nothing below accuses any living person of any crime.
:::

## The Timeline

### The run-up — early July 2025

* **Jul 2, 2025** — [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) formally resigns as lead pastor of Calvary Church in Thousand Oaks, a retirement reported in January; sibling airframe [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV) logs a Provo local flight the same day.

* **Jul 7–9, 2025** — [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) visits the United States, the trip researchers place at the head of the summer's [Israel pressure sequence](./israel-events-pressure-timeline).

* **Jul 11, 2025** — Reporting describes pro-Israel figures reacting badly to Kirk's decision to platform Israel-critical speakers — the friction catalogued at [Charlie–Israel Friction Checklist](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction).

* **Jul 13–14, 2025** — At TPUSA's Florida events, Dave Smith debates Josh Hammer and **Tucker Carlson** tells the audience to "go all the way," escalating the donor conflict traced at [Charlie, Israel, Donors and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).

* **Jul 18, 2025** — The investigation file records the assertion that *"it looks like a decision to murder Charlie Kirk in Utah was made around July 18th."* That is an inference drawn backwards from later events, and it is treated as exactly that at [July 18, 2025 "Decision" Claim](./july-18-decision).

* **Jul 18, 2025 — Staging (tracker summary)** — Aviation OSINT accounts describe **SU-BTT** arriving **Paris from Cairo** before the U.S. leg — European staging, not yet Nebraska.

### July 20, 2025 — the day itself

* **Jul 20, 2025 — Primary OSINT** — **@Borrowed7Time** logged **SU-BTT (#0101D3) Paris → Omaha Eppley (KOMA)** the same day. The contemporaneous post names a **civil airfield** (Eppley Airfield), not a military ramp, and does **not** name Army bases, meeting attendees, or any Kirk-case link.

* **Jul 20, 2025** — Later project timelines describe SU-BTT making **its first trip to America ever**, landing in the **Omaha–Lincoln corridor**. Some OSINT posts describe an internal **Omaha → Lincoln** leg on the same visit; whether July 20 itself included that internal hop remains **compilation-dependent** (later Aug 2025 and Mar 2026 legs clearly use **both** fields).

* **Jul 20, 2025 — Label conflict** — Later citizen compilations characterize the destination as a **U.S. Army base**. Research for this page found **no primary tracker post naming Offutt, Camp Ashland, a Nebraska National Guard field, or any specific Army installation** for this landing. The Omaha area's best-known major base is **Offutt Air Force Base**, so the "Army base" label is either loose shorthand, a possible later ground move that is unproven, or simply wrong. See Conflicts below.

* **Jul 20, 2025** — A separate strand alleges a **military meeting with foreign leaders on U.S. soil** the same day. The two claims — a flight and a meeting — are asserted together but have never been tied to each other by any document; they are kept on separate pages at [Foreign Leaders Meeting](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20) and [July 20 Military Meeting](./military-meeting-july-20).

* **Jul 20, 2025 (alleged participants)** — Commentary describes foreign leaders or military delegations plus U.S. hosts. **No manifest, no attendee list and no visitor log has been produced.** The visitor and attendee logs are precisely what the [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) are drafted to compel.

* **Jul 20, 2025 (alleged purpose)** — Commentary links the stop variously to **counter-UAS testing**, special-forces coordination, or Middle East security discussions. These are competing guesses, not competing sources; none is documented.

* **Jul 20, 2025 (overlap claim)** — Research notes ask whether the Nebraska corridor overlaps installations associated with **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** HADES ISR testing. That overlap is asserted in commentary and is **not** documented here as verified co-location; no primary co-location of N1098L at Omaha on July 20 was found. Program context: [ISR Program Context](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

* **Around Jul 14–30, 2025** — Separately, search-pattern researchers report a cluster of **Israeli-IP searches for Timpanogos-area surgeons** across this same two-week span; that is a different evidence class entirely and is documented at [Israel search patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims).

### After July 20 — how the airframe kept moving

* **Aug 2, 2025** — French legionnaires reportedly arrive in the U.S. for a three-week training rotation in California ending August 25 — a strand tracked at [French Foreign Legion Claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims).

* **Aug 4–6, 2025** — The [Hamptons intervention meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) takes place, followed by the reported Netanyahu call and funding offer discussed at [Alleged $1 Billion Israeli Offer](/Israel/billion).

* **Aug 8, 2025** — Sibling airframe [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU) is logged Minot, ND → Provo, putting a second Egyptian tail into Utah.

* **Aug 17, 2025** — SU-BTT is logged **Paris → Omaha / Lincoln**, returning to the same Nebraska corridor a month after the July 20 stop.

* **Aug 23, 2025** — SU-BTT is logged **Omaha / Lincoln → Wilmington, Delaware** — the destination it would fly to again on September 10.

* **Aug 24, 2025** — SU-BTT is logged **Wilmington → Paris**, closing the August loop.

* **Sep 4, 2025** — SU-BTT is logged inbound from **Paris-Le Bourget via Minot, ND into Provo, Utah**, six days before the assassination, and stays. Full chronology: [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **Sep 10, 2025** — SU-BTT departs Utah for Wilmington on the day of the shooting; the departure time is itself disputed between a 7:08 AM log entry and a later powered-up-then-dark account.

* **Sep 11, 2025** — SU-BTT leaves the United States for Cairo, one day after the assassination.

* **Nov 18, 2025 — Innocent-reading primary** — Commentators (e.g. **@ConservativeDV**) argue Egyptian special forces have **trained in Utah and Nebraska for decades**, that TPUSA events overlap those geographies, and that **correlation is not causation** — an ordinary explanation that does not erase the flight log but refuses to treat it as proof of a plot.

* **March 2026 — Control case** — Trackers again posted **Paris → Eppley Omaha (KOMA)**, **Lincoln → Wilmington**, then **Wilmington → Cairo** for SU-BTT, documenting **KOMA + Lincoln as a repeat pair** and showing the Nebraska corridor as a **recurring** Egyptian VIP route rather than a one-off July anomaly ([SU-BTT movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline)).

* **2026** — July 20 is retained in citizen timelines as the **earliest anchor point** for this airframe's U.S. presence, and appears in the discovery lists behind the proposed disclosure laws.

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point in dispute | Version A | Version B | Who asserts each |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Was July 20 the first-ever U.S. trip? | Yes — first ever, per tracker tallies | August 17–24 is the first documented sustained visit; Mar 2026 reuses the same corridor; tally methods disputed | Project timelines vs. skeptics of the tally method |
| What kind of field was it? | A **U.S. Army base** | Primary same-day post: **KOMA / Eppley (civil)**; no named Army installation found; corridor's principal major base is **Air Force** (Offutt) | Later citizen compilations vs. @Borrowed7Time / absence of naming document |
| Omaha and Lincoln | Two legs of one July 20 visit | Paris→KOMA is primary; same-day Lincoln hop may be inferred from later patterns | OSINT compilations vs. ordinary tracker imprecision |
| The same-day foreign-leaders meeting | Occurred, and is connected to the flight | Two separate assertions never linked by any document | Commentary vs. the documentary record |
| Nebraska–N1098L installation overlap | The same airbase | Alleged only; no co-location record exists | Commentary vs. this site's own sourcing note |
| Why a foreign VIP jet was there at all | Coordination ahead of a political killing | Routine VIP routing + multi-decade Egyptian training in the corridor | Citizen investigators vs. ordinary reading |

## Innocent readings worth keeping in view

Fairness requires stating the ordinary explanations as plainly as the suspicious ones. **VIP government jets make training, maintenance and diplomatic stops at U.S. military *and civil* airfields constantly**, and doing so implies nothing covert. The best same-day primary for July 20 is **civil KOMA**, not a named Army ramp. **Nebraska appears in overlap spreadsheets partly because the Omaha corridor hosts substantial aviation and defense infrastructure** — repeat visits by any government aircraft are unremarkable there, and **2026 Paris–Omaha–Lincoln–Wilmington legs** prove corridor reuse. Commentators also note **long-running Egyptian unit training** in Utah/Nebraska. And the **"first U.S. trip ever"** framing comes from tracker tallies whose date-matching critics dispute; if the tally is wrong, the entire significance of July 20 evaporates. See the counterpoints section of [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT).

## Sourcing status

* **Documented (OSINT):** Same-day **@Borrowed7Time** post of SU-BTT **Paris → KOMA**; later public flight-tracking screenshots for Omaha/Lincoln/Wilmington legs across [Planes](/Planes/overview) coverage.
* **Alleged:** The exact **Army base** label, the passenger manifest, the meeting, and any relevance to the Kirk case.
* **Not established:** Any official statement connecting the July 20 Nebraska stop to the September 10 shooting; any primary naming of a specific Army installation for that landing.
