# Nebraska Army Base Visit — SU-BTT (July 20, 2025, Claims)

Open-source aviation research places **July 20, 2025** as the date the Israel/Egyptian Air Force jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** — a Dassault Falcon 7X class aircraft in site documentation — allegedly made its **first-ever trip to the United States**, with a reported destination in the **Nebraska Army base / Omaha–Lincoln corridor**. Commentators pair that flight with a same-day **[foreign leaders meeting](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20)** allegation on U.S. soil. Mainstream reporting has **not** publicly framed this visit as conclusively tied to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s case.

## Flight-tracking claims (July 20)

| Detail | As claimed in compilations |
| --- | --- |
| **Aircraft** | Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT** ([Planes: SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)) |
| **Date** | **July 20, 2025** — described as **first U.S. visit ever** in multiple project timelines |
| **Routing (claims)** | Some OSINT posts describe **Omaha → Lincoln** legs within Nebraska |
| **Destination type** | **U.S. Army base** — specific installation name **varies by source** and is **not confirmed** in official Kirk-case filings on this site |
| **Evidence type** | ADS-B / flight-tracking screenshots and aviation-spotter posts |

Research notes also ask whether the same Nebraska corridor overlaps installations associated with **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** HADES ISR testing — that overlap is **alleged in commentary**, not documented here as a verified co-location.

## Nebraska base meeting context (claims)

Trash-source *Laws* blocks and foreign-intelligence summaries describe a **Nebraska Army base meeting** overlapping the SU-BTT visit:

- **Participants (alleged):** Foreign leaders or military delegations plus U.S. hosts — see [Foreign Leaders Meeting (July 20)](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).
- **Purpose (alleged):** Commentary links the stop to **counter-UAS testing**, special-forces coordination, or broader Middle East security topics; **no official manifest** tying attendees to the later UVU event has been released.
- **Visitor logs:** The Nebraska base visitor and attendee logs are cited in project *Laws* sections as records that could confirm or refute the gathering.

## Relationship to later 2025 SU-BTT trips

July 20 is treated in many timelines as an **early anchor** before the September window:

- **Aug 17–24, 2025 (claims):** A separate U.S. visit — Paris → Omaha/Lincoln → Wilmington, DE → Paris ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)).
- **Sep 4, 2025:** Reported arrival from France to **Provo, Utah** ([Year_2025](./Year_2025)).
- **Sep 10–11, 2025:** Departure timelines around the UVU event ([September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025)).

Commentators treat July 20 as **prior presence** of the same airframe; that remains **interpretive**, not established by government statements on this site.

## Innocent vs. suspicious readings

Fairness requires noting routine explanations cited by skeptics:

- **VIP government jets** make training, maintenance, and diplomatic stops at U.S. military airfields without implying covert operations.
- **Nebraska** appears in overlap spreadsheets partly because **Omaha** and related corridors host **aviation and defense infrastructure** — repeat visits are not automatically surveillance.
- The **"first U.S. trip ever"** framing comes from tracker tallies that critics dispute on date-matching grounds ([SU-BTT counterpoints](/Planes/SU-BTT)).

## Sourcing status

- **Documented in compilations:** SU-BTT appears in **public flight-tracking** screenshots referenced across [Planes](/Planes/overview).
- **Alleged:** Exact base name, passenger manifest, and Kirk-case relevance.
- **Not established:** Any official statement connecting the July 20 Nebraska stop to the September 10 shooting.
