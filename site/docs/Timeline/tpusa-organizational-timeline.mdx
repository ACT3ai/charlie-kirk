# TPUSA Organizational & Internal Timeline 2025

This page tracks **[Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) as an organization** through 2025 — its events, its internal fights, its staff on September 10, its succession sequence, and its entity filings. It is not the [Erika Kirk personal timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline) and not the [funeral and memorial timeline](./funeral-memorial-timeline); both are linked where they intersect.

:::caution Attribution notice
TPUSA is a **litigious organization** and every person named below is **living and accused of no crime**. Financial and governance claims on this page are **allegations raised in public commentary**, not audited findings or court judgments. Ordinary explanations are given alongside every contested item.
:::

## Entity and funding groundwork (before 2025)

* **May 15, 2023 (entity record)** — **[GGLF 2023 LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/gglf-2023-llc)** is created, per the corporate-record notes compiled in the master investigation file.

* **2023 (reported)** — A **[life insurance policy](/Suspicious/Israel/gglf-life-insurance)** on Charlie Kirk is reportedly taken out and held by **GGLF 2023 LLC**, with **TPUSA reportedly paying a $350,000 loan** against it and an estimated payout figure circulating in the **$20M–$50M** range in citizen commentary. Researchers say the policy surfaces in **TPUSA's fiscal year ending June 30, 2024**, and flag the timing — reportedly shortly after Kirk began questioning aspects of the **October 7** narrative. **No underwriter has been publicly identified** and no filing has been produced in the posts reviewed; the master file records this as an **attributed claim**. Insuring a founder is ordinary nonprofit practice and no wrongdoing is established.

* **Through 2024** — Insider-payment allegations later compiled by researcher **@theleahfiles** cover roughly ten years, including **$9M+** to an entity characterized as a political direct-mail group (see [Moore DM Group / Resource One](/TPUSA/Companies/moore-dm-group)), **$999K** to **[Clocktower LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clocktower-llc)** (dissolved 2022) for a "research project," and **$2.7M+** routed through shell entities by an advancement director. See [insider payments and 990s](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/insider-payments-990s) and the [LLC index](/TPUSA/LLCs/overview). These are **contested financial allegations**, not findings.

## July 2025: the speaker fights

* **July 11, 2025** — Kirk platforms speakers critical of Israel, alarming pro-Israel donors and allies; the master file marks this as the day the donor conflict became open. See [Israel donor pressure as motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and [donors, Israel, and foreign policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy).

* **July 13, 2025 — TPUSA Florida** — The **[Dave Smith](/Influencers/podcasts-dave-smith) / [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** debate takes place at the Florida event, one of the flashpoints in the movement's internal argument over Israel policy. See [the MAGA antisemitism debate page](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate).

* **July 14, 2025 — TPUSA Florida** — **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** speaks, and Kirk reportedly encouraged him to "go all the way." Researchers treat [Carlson's evolving Israel position](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) and the platforming as the moment the donor conflict became irreversible; see [pre-event political pressure](./pre-event-political-pressure) and [political pressure and meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).

* **July 18, 2025** — Citizen timelines place a decision point here; the reasoning and the counterarguments are on [the July 18 decision page](./july-18-decision) and [the July 20 foreign leaders meeting](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20). Nothing in the public record establishes any decision was made.

* **July 31, 2025** — An off-the-record White House meeting organized by the **[Faith & Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition)**, the **[Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel)**, and the **[Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding)** convenes on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel." **TPUSA representatives were absent** from a strategy session on a subject central to their founder — see [the July 31 meeting without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) and [TPUSA's absence July 31](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31).

## August–early September 2025: governance friction

* **August 15–17, 2025 — Aspen donor retreat** — TPUSA's donor gathering, later central to the succession dispute: the claim that Kirk named **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** as his successor there is, according to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), uncorroborated by attendees she says she contacted. TPUSA-side accounts say Kirk did express that wish. Both versions are recorded on [the disputed CEO succession page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed).

* **September 2, 2025 (circulated memo claim)** — Citizen investigators (**[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/x/project-constitution)**, Finance Wolves and others, widely amplified Nov 16 2025) describe an internal Charlie Kirk memo that (1) named **[Justin Streiff](/People/justin-streiff)** (spelling also appears as Strife/Streiff) as the new COO, (2) moved **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)** off day-to-day COO duties toward **[Turning Point Action](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-action)**, and (3) ordered a **[DOGE-style](/TPUSA/DOGE-TPUSA-Connection)** organization-wide efficiency/financial review separate from the routine annual audit. The memo has circulated in activist media; a full authenticated original on TPUSA letterhead has **not** been produced in the posts reviewed. Ordinary governance change and "secret cancel to hide issues" readings both exist; neither is court-established. See [the DOGE audit page](/TPUSA/doge-audit), [DOGE audit results](/TPUSA/doge-audit-results), and [the DOGE-audit / Bowyer timing page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/doge-audit-bowyer-timing).

* **Early September 2025 (Bowyer reply claim)** — When asked whether the audit would proceed, Bowyer is widely quoted in screenshot threads (e.g. **@phantompain1984**) as saying the organization already audits yearly and was audited in July, pointing to Form 990s and the outside auditor ([Baker Tilly](/TPUSA/Companies/baker-tilly)). Critics argue that answer addresses a **different process** than a DOGE-style forensic review. Reassigning a COO is an ordinary governance act; missing-money figures circulating in advocate media (e.g. **~$10M** claims about Turning Point Action) are **allegations without a public court finding**.

* **Parallel thread** — The question of who would replace Kirk's COO, and what the org chart looked like going into September, is tracked on [Charlie's plan to replace the COO](/TPUSA/charlie-replace-coo) and [TPUSA's replacement timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline). Some investigators also claim Bowyer **introduced Erika Frantzve to Charlie** years earlier — relationship map, not proof of misconduct. A separate allegation about a call attributed to the incoming COO is logged at [the Streiff / "Pierre Du Pont" call page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/streiff-du-pont-call).

## September 10, 2025

* **Morning** — TPUSA staff work the **[UVU courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward)** ("the quad"), tabling and running setup, and TPUSA production crews run broadcast cameras and at least one drone; see [TPUSA production footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage) and [the TPUSA production drone](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone).

* **~9:24–10:00 AM MDT (claim)** — A July 2026 video by **@FaithDrivUr** characterizes police as saying the suspect met **TPUSA representatives** on the quad hours before the shooting. **No employee has been named and no primary document with that time has surfaced**; the baseline explanation is an unremarkable exchange with staff who were tabling. See [the TPUSA–Tyler Robinson chat page](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat) and [the pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).

* **12:23 PM MDT** — TPUSA staff positions at the moment of the shot — who was on stage, in the tent, at the mic stand, and in the SUV — are catalogued on [TPUSA staff on September 10](/key_individuals/TPUSA_Staff_Sept_10). Security that day was contracted through a private firm, not TPUSA employees; see [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) and [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security).

* **Immediately after** — Multiple 2026 posts restate that a TPUSA-associated person removed an **SD card** from a camera behind Kirk (some name **[Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth)**, who is separately placed [filming from the rock pile seconds after the shot](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile), and describe standing on a chair; earlier master-file notes describe an executive who had not attended a university event before or after). Ordinary explanation remains evidence preservation / theft prevention per police-instruction claims. Chain of custody is unpublished; later lore sometimes confuses **USB vs SD** when describing how the card was transferred. See [executive SD cards](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/executive-sd-cards) and [SD card removed](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed).

* **Hours after** — A TPUSA figure appears on **[Fox News](/Media/fox-news-coverage)** the same day, a speed several commentators found notable; see [the successor on Fox](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/successor-on-fox) and [the TPUSA replacement Fox interview](/Media/tpusa-replacement-fox-interview).

## The Cooper Brown video and the witness question

* **Within days of September 10** — A widely shared clip shows a man putting hands on a young Turning Point person to stop him giving witness testimony on camera, while covering his own ears. **@DiligentDenizen** identified the man as **"Cooper Brown"** and stated he is *not* a student or TPUSA staff member. See [Cooper Brown as witness](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cooper-brown-witness) and [the Cooper Brown profile](/People/cooper-brown).

* **The contradiction inside that identification** — Earlier posts in the same ecosystem described "Cooper Brown" as the **junior TPUSA volunteer standing about fifteen feet from Kirk** who was going to say on camera that he heard the mic **explode** — meaning the same name is attached to both the person silenced and the person doing the silencing. **2026** posts further claim the man labeled Cooper Brown who blocked a young witness was also presented on **Fox (Jesse Waters)** as a primary / "sole" eyewitness the same day, while the silenced student was not interviewed. See [the Cooper Brown witness page](/Witnesses/cooper-brown) and [the mic investigation index](/Mic/Investigation_Index). These viral identifications have shifted over time and are treated as **contested**, not established.

* **The Fort Huachuca photo question** — A circulated photo is claimed to show "Cooper Brown" alongside **[Jeb Jacobi](/People/jeb-jacobi)** at **Fort Huachuca**. Whether the two are the same person, and the original date and source of the photo, are **unresolved open questions** — see [Fort Huachuca September 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and [the Fort Huachuca military timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

## Succession: September 12–18, 2025

* **September 12, 2025 — approximately 9:09 PM ET (~55 hours after the shooting)** — Erika Kirk delivers the speech in which she refers to **"evil do-ERs" (plural)**. The plural is the entire observation; it is an **observation about wording**, not evidence of anything. See [the Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline) and the parallel discovery item in [Law 1: DOJ and FBI disclosure](/laws/DoJ_FBI/Law_1_DoJ_FBI).

* **September 12–18, 2025 — the eight-day gap** — Between the speech and the CEO announcement sits an eight-day interval during which board deliberations, donor conversations, and governance decisions occurred that have never been made public. See [the disputed succession page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed) and [memorial and TPUSA succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession).

* **September 18, 2025** — **Erika Kirk is announced as TPUSA CEO.** Spokesman **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** stated publicly that Charlie had told him Erika could lead the organization; Owens has questioned whether the Aspen statement is corroborated. Both accounts appear on [the TPUSA replacement timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline).

* **September 18, 2025 — same day** — Bowyer posts, "We run everything like a military op, all of our people are from that background." Commentators ask whether the post was later deleted. It is logged for the intelligence-and-security theme on [the Bowyer profile](/People/tyler-bowyer); **no wrongdoing is established or alleged**.

## Fall 2025 into 2026: filings, audits, and legal posture

* **October 14, 2025** — TPUSA posts a birthday tribute video containing **drone footage from the September 10 event**, which citizen investigators contrast against the "drones were banned" explanation. See [the cease-and-desist drone contradiction](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cease-desist-drone-contradiction) and [UVU security and no drones](/Drones/UVU_Security_No_Drones).

* **Late 2025** — Commentary flags **missing 990s** for **[Turning Point Endowment](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-endowment)**, **[Turning Point Action](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-action)**, and **[America's Turning Point](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/americas-turning-point)**. Late or delayed nonprofit filings are common; the claim is that the delay coincides with the audit dispute. See [insider payments and 990s](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/insider-payments-990s).

* **December 2025 – February 2026** — Advocate outlets claimed TPUSA/Bowyer reversed or **"lied"** about the fate of the DOGE review (e.g. **@SwordTruth** framing); others note nonprofits continued ordinary annual audits and that "secretly canceled to hide issues" lacks official confirmation. Keep both readings.

* **January 2026** — Commentary (**@ProjectConstitu** / Owens ecosystem) alleged internal **staff purges on the order of 30–40 people** during a leak crackdown, plus cease-and-desist activity against independent creators — part of the [TPUSA legal threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats) thread.

* **Late 2025 into 2026** — Board-adjacent allegations circulate, including that Erika Kirk's mother **[Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve)** sat on the board and appears on the board of **[Superfeed Technologies](/TPUSA/Companies/superfeed-technologies)** alongside Bowyer. Every person named is **living and accused of no crime**; see [the secret stock fund claim](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/secret-stock-fund) and [the Frantzve profile](/People/lori-frantzve).

* **July 20, 2026** — Researcher **@leahfiles** published Part 3 of a TPUSA financial series focusing on **[Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan)**, described as both Senior Advancement Director and owner of successive outside fundraising entities (**GSM Strategy → [Lionrock](/TPUSA/LLCs/lionrock-llc) → [Cloverstone](/TPUSA/LLCs/cloverstone-llc)**) that took custody of tens of millions in donations (poster figures include **>$88M** through custody and **~$5M+** private fees). The post frames Schedule L / private-inurement questions from public filings and states **no public government charge** — analysis of filings, not a verdict. See [insider payments and 990s](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/insider-payments-990s).

* **2026** — TPUSA's **legal threats** against investigators and commentators become their own thread; see [TPUSA legal threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats) and [censorship of investigators](/Influencers/Censorship_of_Investigators). July 2026 also saw a public Owens–Kolvet spat escalation (engagement attacks / "Karen" framing / affiliate disclaimers) that documents communications posture under the new CEO without establishing any crime.

* **2026 — sponsor and staff threads** — The Yrefy sponsorship connection to **[Laine Schoneberger](/People/laine-schoneberger)** is on [the Schoneberger / Yrefy page](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/schoneberger-yrefy-sponsor); the removal of security director **Dan Flood** from the TPUSA site is on [Flood scrubbed from site](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/flood-scrubbed-from-site); a whistleblower "stand-down" allegation is on [the stand-down whistleblower claim](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/stand-down-whistleblower-claim).
