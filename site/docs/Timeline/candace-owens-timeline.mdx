# Candace Owens Public Statements & Releases Timeline

This page tracks **what [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) said publicly and when she said it** — a release chronology, not an assessment of whether she was right. A large share of what the wider public believes about this case entered circulation through her podcast, so the order in which she disclosed things is itself a timeline worth having. The analytical treatment of her claims lives on her [profile page](/People/candace-owens) and her [podcast coverage page](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens).

:::caution Living people — attribution only
**Candace Owens is a living person. So are [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) and everyone else named below.** Nothing here states as fact that any of them committed a crime; none has been convicted of anything connected to this case. Statements attributed to Owens are **her published claims**, reported as claims. Where her account is contested — and several are — the counter-account is given alongside it. Fringe theories that name Owens herself are unsupported rumor and are not adopted here. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.
:::

## The Timeline

### Before September 10, 2025

* **2024** — In a filmed interview, Mike Huckabee — later U.S. Ambassador to Israel — reportedly remarks to Owens on camera that people could "make you disappear." Owens' supporters recirculated the clip in 2026 as context; it is a resurfaced interview moment, not a documented threat.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Owens states that **three sources** told her [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was worried "they" were going to kill him. This is the assertion that anchors the whole [pre-assassination warning](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) thread and connects to the disputed ["they are going to kill me" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **Sept 9, 2025 (as she later described it)** — Owens says Kirk told donors, TPUSA members and head of security [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) that he thought he would be "executed tomorrow." Erika Kirk has publicly disputed that any such messages exist, saying she has his phone — the direct conflict documented at [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

### September 10–30, 2025 — the first disclosures

* **Sept 10, 2025** — Owens says Kolvet told her at the hospital that she and **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** **"were on a list."** Single-source, unrecorded recollection — see [It Was Supposed To Be You](/Suspicious/Israel/it-was-supposed-to-be-you).

* **Days after Sept 10, 2025** — Owens says Kolvet told her, verbatim, **"It was supposed to be you."** Kolvet has not confirmed the words on the record; the ordinary reading — shock and relief rather than foreknowledge — is set out on the same page.

* **Immediately after Sept 10, 2025** — Owens reports that threats arrived for her, Tucker Carlson and [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll) in the days following the killing — Carroll's own posts on the case are catalogued at [Ian Carroll — X posts](/Influencers/x/ian-carroll).

* **September 2025** — Owens **publishes screenshots of Kirk's own messages** to donors, including *"Jewish donors really live up to all the stereotypes"* and language about having *"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."* These texts had been dismissed as a hoax until she published them; the donor-pressure reading is at [Charlie, Israel, Donors and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and [donor texts 48 hours before](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours). Later clips also circulate a **Christian Zionist donor text** condemning Kirk for platforming Tucker and Candace as "antisemites" — presented as a "final warning" framing; attribute to Owens' published screenshots only. Carlson's own trajectory on the subject is at [Tucker Carlson on evolving Israel views](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel).

* **October 6–7, 2025** — Owens' donor-text disclosures are amplified with named group-chat context (public discussion of **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** and **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** as participants when Kirk explained ditching Israel under donor pressure — living persons; no crime claim). Separately, clips circulate of **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) confirming** the authenticity of the donor messages Owens published. Confirmation of **texts** is not confirmation of any homicide theory.

* **September 2025** — Owens releases **a photograph she says shows Tyler Robinson at a Dairy Queen in Orem at 6:38 PM** on the day of the shooting. The venue is documented at [Dairy Queen — Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem), and the timing conflict it creates with the custody narrative is at [custody vs Discord confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline). **July 2026 authenticity disputes** are logged below and on the [camera sightings timeline](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

### Autumn 2025 — the episode arc

* **Ep. 235 — "They Are Lying About Charlie Kirk"** — Owens' first sustained attack on the official account, arguing early official statements were internally inconsistent.

* **Ep. 245 — "DEAD OR ALIVE: Who Betrayed Charlie Kirk?"** — turns the inquiry inward toward [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and the circle around Kirk.

* **Ep. 256 — "INFILTRATION: Charlie Kirk Was Being Tracked For Years"** — the episode that introduces the multi-year aircraft-overlap argument later expanded at [Planes Following Charlie or Erika](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika).

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens introduces the **color-coded plane shorthand**: [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) as "the yellow plane," [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND) as "the blue plane" — the naming that the entire [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) thread inherited. Her SU-BTT chronology is on the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens discloses the claim of roughly **twelve cell phones registered on Israeli plans** operating near UVU on September 10 — the [foreign phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU) thread. A public confirmation wave peaks around **November 5, 2025** (@ProjectConstitu and amplifiers saying Candace "just confirmed"); later analyst **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** publishes a **16 Israeli / 44 foreign** figure — see [cell-tower and phone-data timeline](./cell-tower-phone-data-timeline). **Unverified private analysis throughout.**

* **Autumn–Winter 2025 (clip wave; exact episode TBD)** — Owens states, as widely relayed on X, that **Benjamin Netanyahu offered major funding / elevation of TPUSA**, that **Kirk refused**, and that **Kolvet** was her source for the offer; she also says **Erika Kirk was unaware**. This is **her account of private offers and conversations**, unconfirmed by the named parties. Keep this **distinct** from her separate claim of a **final Netanyahu call about Iran** (below). No crime by any living leader is asserted here.

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens covers the flight **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)**, Scottsdale to Provo on September 10 and back — the [same-day turnaround claim](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n560tw-turnaround) — one of several tails she raised alongside the [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada).

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens reports that the **FBI did not want the bomb dogs taken to certain places** — the claim behind [Bomb Dogs Standdown](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs) and [bomb dogs kept away](/Suspicious/FBI/bomb-dogs-kept-away).

* **Nov 17, 2025 — "Operation Mocking-Plane: The Charlie Kirk Plot Thickens"** — Owens argues it is strange Kirk was routed into Salt Lake City, roughly 45 miles from UVU, rather than [Provo Municipal](/Locations/Provo_Airport) seven miles away, where SU-BTT was parked.

* **Ep. 287 — "The Past Is Prologue: Why Charlie Kirk Was Murdered"** — her most comprehensive single treatment: the lone-shooter narrative challenged, the Israeli-phone signal claim, possible accomplices, and TPUSA donor pressure.

* **Dec 2025 (relayed by @itsmorganariel)** — Owens publicly discusses a tip from police officers alleging that during the manhunt a white Toyota RAV4 was stopped for speeding and the driver **flashed an FBI badge**. Relayed tip, not confirmed by any agency — [RAV4 flashed badge](/Suspicious/FBI/rav4-flashed-badge).

### 2026 — succession, legal pressure, forensics, and litigation

* **January 21, 2026** — Per reporting by **Will Sommer** (@willsommer / The Bulwark), **TPUSA sent Owens a cease-and-desist** asserting violation of a **non-disparagement agreement** over her coverage of the case. A civil demand is **not a ruling** on the truth of her claims — see also [censorship and information-control timeline](./censorship-information-control-timeline) and [TPUSA Legal Threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats).

* **Feb 2026** — Backlash builds over the trailer for Owens' **"Bride of Charlie"** series examining Erika Kirk and the [TPUSA leadership succession](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline). This is media framing about a living widow, not a judicial finding of any kind.

* **2026** — Owens names **[Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** as a passenger on the **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** flight out of [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) on the morning of September 9, 2025 — the reported meeting there is covered at [Fort Huachuca — Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and [the JTF meeting claim](/Suspicious/US_Intel/fort-huachuca-jtf-meeting). Identifying a public official on a flight is not an accusation, and none is made here.

* **2026** — Owens reports a claim that a man named [George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn) told hospital staff he had been **paid to be a decoy**; unverified relayed account, connected to the [distraction people](/CoverUp/Distraction_People) thread.

* **2026** — Owens names **[VFX LA](/Suspicious/US_Intel/clock-tower-x-and-vfx-la)**, a voice-cloning and deepfake firm, as a company TPUSA personnel reportedly met with after Kirk's death, and questions the authenticity of the video in which Kirk appears to designate Erika Kirk as successor. Kolvet has reportedly acknowledged a meeting with deepfake creators while saying the offer was declined — see [Erika CEO succession disputed](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed).

* **2026** — Owens says she is **"99.9% sure Tyler Robinson did not shoot Charlie Kirk"** and disputes that he confessed or turned himself in, citing the absence of a signed confession and the account of a former deputy who allegedly urged him to go to the station. The messages themselves are at [Tyler Robinson Discord messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) and [Discord evidence in court](/court/discord-evidence-court); counterpoint material is at [Discord and Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline). Robinson is **charged, not convicted**.

* **2026** — Owens states that Kirk's **final phone call was an intervention by Benjamin Netanyahu** pressing for war with Iran. This is her account of a private call, unconfirmed by any participant — see [Netanyahu's final call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call). Keep **distinct** from the earlier **funding / elevate-TPUSA offer** claim.

* **Jun 22, 2026** — Owens airs a segment arguing that early on-camera witnesses described **blood pooling near Kirk's heart** — a wound that, in her account, never existed — and framing that as evidence of a chest-shot narrative prepared in advance. The competing witness accounts are at [Chest-Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) and [Shot in the Chest, Then the Neck](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal).

* **Jun 26, 2026** — Owens releases **photographs of the SUV interior**, watermarked "CANDACE," showing charred material and debris on the floor of the GMC Yukon Denali. Commentators read a fused, blackened object as melted shirt fabric plus a transmitter component; others read it differently. Reported material, not authenticated forensics — [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie), [burnt object and transmitter](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/burnt-object-transmitter).

* **Jul 10, 2026** — Owens publicly discusses the **public-figure question** in [Brian Harpole's](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) [defamation suit](/Defamation/Brian_Harpole_Candace_Defemation) against her, saying she had never covered him before his own podcast appearance. Separate July reporting describes **mass judicial recusal** in the Middle District of Tennessee. A civil defamation docket is not a criminal finding against either party, and this site takes no position on the merits.

* **July 14–16, 2026 (recirculation; verify original air date)** — Clips attribute to Owens a claim that during the post-shot **SUV transport**, someone **prioritized updating [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)'s security** rather than (or before) normal emergency focus. **Attribute only**; living persons; **no crime established**. Related clocks on [medical/death timeline](./medical-death-timeline) and [ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot).

* **Jul 16, 2026** — An **Axon Body Cam 4** user emails Owens' tips line challenging the account that an officer's body camera died on the rooftop, citing the model's roughly fourteen-hour battery and mandatory dock-and-upload cycle. Anonymous tip — see [Officer Bagley's body cam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam), [missing bodycam and the GRAMA admission](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage), and the wider [GRAMA records denials](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).

* **July 15–16, 2026** — High recirculation of Netanyahu **funding-offer / refusal** clips framed as confirming bombshells; meta-timeline of claim longevity rather than new primary evidence.

* **July 17–19, 2026** — The **Dairy Queen 6:38 PM** still becomes a two-way fight: critics say it **conflicts with** the [Cowboy's Smokehouse receipt](/Tyler_Robinson/cowboys-smokehouse-receipt) alibi timeline or is **not from Sept 10**; defenders treat it as proof the charged suspect was still local hours after the shot. Full clothing/authenticity detail on [camera sightings](./camera-sightings-tyler-robinson).

* **Ongoing 2026** — Owens' engagement metrics on X are reported by supporters to have been suppressed; the claim is documented at [X — Candace Owens Engagement Bans](/Censorship/X_Candace_Engagement_Bans). Her role in the proposed reform framework is at [Trusted Investigators](/Influencers/Trusted_Investigators).

6. What, if anything, did the **VFX LA** meeting produce, and does any document confirm what was discussed?
