# Candace Owens Public Statements & Releases Timeline

This page tracks **what [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) said publicly and when she said it** — a release chronology, not an assessment of whether she was right. A large share of what the wider public believes about this case entered circulation through her podcast, so the order in which she disclosed things is itself a timeline worth having. The analytical treatment of her claims lives on her [profile page](/People/candace-owens) and her [podcast coverage page](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens).

:::caution Living people — attribution only
**Candace Owens is a living person. So are [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) and everyone else named below.** Nothing here states as fact that any of them committed a crime; none has been convicted of anything connected to this case. Statements attributed to Owens are **her published claims**, reported as claims. Where her account is contested — and several are — the counter-account is given alongside it. Fringe theories that name Owens herself are unsupported rumor and are not adopted here. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.
:::

## The Timeline

### Before September 10, 2025

* **2024** — In a filmed interview, Mike Huckabee — later U.S. Ambassador to Israel — reportedly remarks to Owens on camera that people could "make you disappear." Owens' supporters recirculated the clip in 2026 as context; it is a resurfaced interview moment, not a documented threat.

* **Sept 9, 2025** — Owens states that **three sources** told her [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was worried "they" were going to kill him. This is the assertion that anchors the whole [pre-assassination warning](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) thread and connects to the disputed ["they are going to kill me" text](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

* **Sept 9, 2025 (as she later described it)** — Owens says Kirk told donors, TPUSA members and head of security [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) that he thought he would be "executed tomorrow." Erika Kirk has publicly disputed that any such messages exist, saying she has his phone — the direct conflict documented at [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

### September 10–30, 2025 — the first disclosures

* **Sept 10, 2025** — Owens says Kolvet told her at the hospital that she and Tucker Carlson **"were on a list."** Single-source, unrecorded recollection — see [It Was Supposed To Be You](/Suspicious/Israel/it-was-supposed-to-be-you).

* **Days after Sept 10, 2025** — Owens says Kolvet told her, verbatim, **"It was supposed to be you."** Kolvet has not confirmed the words on the record; the ordinary reading — shock and relief rather than foreknowledge — is set out on the same page.

* **Immediately after Sept 10, 2025** — Owens reports that threats arrived for her, Tucker Carlson and [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll) in the days following the killing.

* **September 2025** — Owens **publishes screenshots of Kirk's own messages** to donors, including *"Jewish donors really live up to all the stereotypes"* and language about having *"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause."* These texts had been dismissed as a hoax until she published them; the donor-pressure reading is at [Charlie, Israel, Donors and Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) and [donor texts 48 hours before](/Suspicious/Israel/donor-texts-48-hours).

* **September 2025** — Owens releases **a photograph she says shows Tyler Robinson at a Dairy Queen in Orem at 6:38 PM** on the day of the shooting. The venue is documented at [Dairy Queen — Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem), and the timing conflict it creates with the custody narrative is at [custody vs Discord confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).

### Autumn 2025 — the episode arc

* **Ep. 235 — "They Are Lying About Charlie Kirk"** — Owens' first sustained attack on the official account, arguing early official statements were internally inconsistent.

* **Ep. 245 — "DEAD OR ALIVE: Who Betrayed Charlie Kirk?"** — turns the inquiry inward toward [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and the circle around Kirk.

* **Ep. 256 — "INFILTRATION: Charlie Kirk Was Being Tracked For Years"** — the episode that introduces the multi-year aircraft-overlap argument later expanded at [Planes Following Charlie or Erika](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika).

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens introduces the **color-coded plane shorthand**: [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) as "the yellow plane," [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND) as "the blue plane" — the naming that the entire [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) thread inherited. Her SU-BTT chronology is on the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens discloses the claim of roughly **twelve cell phones registered on Israeli plans** operating near UVU on September 10 — the [foreign phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU) thread.

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens covers the flight **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)**, Scottsdale to Provo on September 10 and back, one of several tails she raised alongside the Egyptian jets.

* **Autumn 2025** — Owens reports that the **FBI did not want the bomb dogs taken to certain places** — the claim behind [Bomb Dogs Standdown](/FBI/Bomb_Dogs) and [bomb dogs kept away](/Suspicious/FBI/bomb-dogs-kept-away).

* **Nov 17, 2025 — "Operation Mocking-Plane: The Charlie Kirk Plot Thickens"** — Owens argues it is strange Kirk was routed into Salt Lake City, roughly 45 miles from UVU, rather than [Provo Municipal](/Locations/Provo_Airport) seven miles away, where SU-BTT was parked.

* **Ep. 287 — "The Past Is Prologue: Why Charlie Kirk Was Murdered"** — her most comprehensive single treatment: the lone-shooter narrative challenged, the Israeli-phone signal claim, possible accomplices, and TPUSA donor pressure.

* **Dec 2025 (relayed by @itsmorganariel)** — Owens publicly discusses a tip from police officers alleging that during the manhunt a white Toyota RAV4 was stopped for speeding and the driver **flashed an FBI badge**. Relayed tip, not confirmed by any agency — [RAV4 flashed badge](/Suspicious/FBI/rav4-flashed-badge).

### 2026 — succession, forensics, and litigation

* **Feb 2026** — Backlash builds over the trailer for Owens' **"Bride of Charlie"** series examining Erika Kirk and the [TPUSA leadership succession](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline). This is media framing about a living widow, not a judicial finding of any kind.

* **2026** — Owens names **[Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** as a passenger on the **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** flight out of [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) on the morning of September 9, 2025. Identifying a public official on a flight is not an accusation, and none is made here.

* **2026** — Owens reports a claim that a man named Zinn told hospital staff he had been **paid to be a decoy**; unverified relayed account, connected to the [distraction people](/CoverUp/Distraction_People) thread.

* **2026** — Owens names **VFX LA**, a voice-cloning and deepfake firm, as a company TPUSA personnel reportedly met with after Kirk's death, and questions the authenticity of the video in which Kirk appears to designate Erika Kirk as successor. Kolvet has reportedly acknowledged a meeting with deepfake creators while saying the offer was declined — see [Erika CEO succession disputed](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/erika-ceo-succession-disputed).

* **2026** — Owens says she is **"99.9% sure Tyler Robinson did not shoot Charlie Kirk"** and disputes that he confessed or turned himself in, citing the absence of a signed confession and the account of a former deputy who allegedly urged him to go to the station. Counterpoint material is at [Discord and Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline).

* **2026** — Owens states that Kirk's **final phone call was an intervention by Benjamin Netanyahu** pressing for war with Iran. This is her account of a private call, unconfirmed by any participant — see [Netanyahu's final call](/Iran/netanyahu-final-call).

* **Jun 22, 2026** — Owens airs a segment arguing that early on-camera witnesses described **blood pooling near Kirk's heart** — a wound that, in her account, never existed — and framing that as evidence of a chest-shot narrative prepared in advance. The competing witness accounts are at [Chest-Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) and [Shot in the Chest, Then the Neck](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal).

* **Jun 26, 2026** — Owens releases **photographs of the SUV interior**, watermarked "CANDACE," showing charred material and debris on the floor of the GMC Yukon Denali. Commentators read a fused, blackened object as melted shirt fabric plus a transmitter component; others read it differently. Reported material, not authenticated forensics — [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie), [burnt object and transmitter](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/burnt-object-transmitter).

* **Jul 10, 2026** — Owens publicly discusses the **public-figure question** in [Brian Harpole's defamation suit](/Defamation/Brian_Harpole_Candace_Defemation) against her, saying she had never covered him before his own podcast appearance. Separate July reporting describes **mass judicial recusal** in the Middle District of Tennessee. A civil defamation docket is not a criminal finding against either party, and this site takes no position on the merits.

* **Jul 16, 2026** — An **Axon Body Cam 4** user emails Owens' tips line challenging the account that an officer's body camera died on the rooftop, citing the model's roughly fourteen-hour battery and mandatory dock-and-upload cycle. Anonymous tip — [missing bodycam and the GRAMA admission](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage).

* **Ongoing 2026** — Owens' engagement metrics on X are reported by supporters to have been suppressed; the claim is documented at [X — Candace Owens Engagement Bans](/Censorship/X_Candace_Engagement_Bans). Her role in the proposed reform framework is at [Trusted Investigators](/Influencers/Trusted_Investigators).

## Conflicts and Disputed Points

| Point in dispute | Owens' account | The counter-account | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kirk's pre-death warning messages | Displayed on air; sent to donors, Kolvet and Dan Flood | Erika Kirk has said publicly she has his phone and it never happened | Unresolved; no forensic extraction has been published |
| "It was supposed to be you" | Kolvet said it days after the killing | Kolvet has not confirmed it; no recording or second witness exists | Single-source recollection |
| The Erika Kirk succession video | Questions its authenticity, citing donor accounts and AI analysis | TPUSA has treated the succession as legitimate | Unresolved; no independent authentication published |
| Tyler Robinson's culpability | 99.9% sure he did not fire the shot | The State's four-day preliminary-hearing case | In litigation — see [Preliminary Hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) |
| The SUV debris | Consistent with a shaped charge or transmitter fragmentation | Consistent with tissue, fabric and ordinary vehicle debris | No published forensic examination of the photographed items |
| Her coverage of the FBI account | Legitimate journalism | Ben Shapiro reportedly called the questioning "evil" | Public dispute between commentators, nothing more |
| Fringe claims naming Owens herself | — | Unsupported rumor circulated by low-engagement accounts | **Not adopted here**; no court has found her involved in anything |

## Open Questions

1. Can the **three sources** behind the September 9 claim be identified, even under seal, or corroborated by any second party?
2. Will a **forensic extraction of Charlie Kirk's phone** ever be published to settle the warning-message dispute in either direction?
3. Who took the **SUV interior photographs**, when, and where was the vehicle when they were taken?
4. Has **Andrew Kolvet** ever been asked on the record about either quoted remark?
5. Which of Owens' flight-log claims are **reproducible from raw ADS-B archives**, and which rest on screenshots alone?
6. What, if anything, did the **VFX LA** meeting produce, and does any document confirm what was discussed?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The forensic extraction of Charlie Kirk's phone and the underlying message logs, the subscriber records for the phones reported to be operating on Israeli plans near UVU, the FBI Form 302 reports covering the interviews behind Owens' disclosures, the chain-of-custody record for the SUV and the photographs taken of its interior, and the passenger manifests for the aircraft she named are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
