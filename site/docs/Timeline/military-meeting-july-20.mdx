# July 20, 2025 Military & Foreign-Leaders Meeting (Claims)

This is the **date hub for July 20, 2025** — the day on which two separate claims converge: that the Egyptian Air Force jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** made its **first-ever trip to the United States**, landing at a **Nebraska Army base**, and that a **military meeting with foreign leaders took place on U.S. soil**. The aircraft leg is detailed on the [Nebraska Army base page](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) and the attendee question on the [foreign leaders meeting page](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20); what this page adds is the **surrounding chronology** — everything in the weeks on either side that researchers use to argue July 20 is a hinge date rather than an ordinary Sunday.

:::caution
This website does not assert as fact that any living person named here committed a crime or had advance knowledge of or involvement in the assassination. The items below reflect **public flight-tracking data and public commentary**. Nothing here is a finding by this website, and mainstream reporting has **not** publicly framed these events as conclusively tied to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s case.
:::

## The Timeline

### June 2025 — the run-up

* **June 13, 2025** — The **twelve-day war** between Israel and Iran begins, the geopolitical backdrop against which every subsequent item on this page is read by commentators — see [Israel](/Israel/overview).

* **June 18, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly goes to the **White House** specifically to argue **against regime change in Iran**, the earliest dated instance of the position break that the [donor-pressure motive page](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) treats as the root of the story.

* **June 25, 2025** — Tennessee Governor **[Bill Lee](/People/bill-lee)** travels to **France** for a Howmet Aerospace investment — only his fourth international trip in office. Researchers connect it to the Tennessee manufacturing thread that later surfaces on the [AES pages](/Mic/AES); the connection is **interpretive**.

* **Late June 2025** — **Google Trends** activity attributed to Israeli and DC IP ranges begins registering on Utah figures and locations, per the [Israel IP search-pattern page](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns). Trends data shows interest, not identity; the attribution is a claim.

### Early July 2025 — the two weeks before

* **July 2, 2025** — Pastor **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** formally resigns as lead pastor of Calvary Church in Thousand Oaks, a retirement reported as pre-planned in January. It is noted only because his family appears elsewhere in the [ten minutes after the shot](./ten-minutes-after-shot) reconstruction.

* **July 7–9, 2025** — **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** visits the United States, the diplomatic event closest in time to the July 20 claim, tracked on the [Israel events and pressure timeline](./israel-events-pressure-timeline).

* **July 9, 2025** — A search spike from Israeli IP space is claimed for **[Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed)**, then the FBI Salt Lake City special agent in charge; her successor **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)** draws a comparable spike on **September 1**. See [FBI and DPS search patterns](/GoogleSearches/fbi-dps-law-enforcement-searches).

* **July 11, 2025** — Kirk is publicly criticized by pro-Israel figures for **platforming anti-Israel speakers**, an escalation in the pressure sequence documented on the [pre-event political pressure timeline](./pre-event-political-pressure).

* **July 13, 2025** — At the TPUSA Florida conference, **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** debates Dave Smith — the public face of the intra-coalition fight covered on the [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **July 14, 2025** — **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** speaks at the same conference and tells the audience to **"go all the way."** Commentators date the July 20 Trends anchor to **the same week Kirk platformed Carlson for the first time**, an argument developed on the [AmFest control page](/Israel_Main_Suspect/control-amfest-conferences).

* **July 14–19, 2025** — Per the search-pattern compilations, Israeli-IP searches hit a run of **Timpanogos Regional Hospital emergency surgeons** — Rasmussen (July 14), Neilman (July 18), a Patterson (July 19) — with more following on July 22, 23–24 and 30. The names and dates are catalogued on [Timpanogos medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches). These are living physicians; nothing here suggests any of them did anything wrong.

* **July 16–17, 2025** — DC-attributed searches for the incoming **Utah DPS Commissioner** appear on July 16; the appointment is announced **July 17**, replacing the prior commissioner. The sequencing claim — searches preceding the public announcement — is the whole of the allegation, and an ordinary explanation (a leak, a rumor, or an announced shortlist) has not been excluded.

* **July 18, 2025** — Commentary places a **"decision" to target Kirk in Utah** around this date. This is **speculation with no supporting public record**, and it is preserved as such on the [July 18 decision page](./july-18-decision).

### July 20, 2025 — the date itself

* **July 20, 2025** — Flight-tracking compilations record **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** — an Egyptian-registered Dassault Falcon 7X class jet — making what trackers call its **first-ever trip to the United States**, with a reported **Omaha → Lincoln** routing into the Nebraska Army-base corridor. The specific installation **varies by source** and is not confirmed in any Kirk-case filing. Full detail on the [Nebraska Army base page](./nebraska-army-base-july-20).

* **July 20, 2025** — The investigation file states, in a single line, that **"a military meeting with foreign leaders took place on July 20th on U.S. soil."** No attendee list, agenda, manifest or minutes has ever been produced. The attendee question is examined on the [foreign leaders meeting page](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

* **July 20, 2025** — Alleged purposes circulating in commentary include **counter-UAS (anti-drone) coordination**, special-forces or ISR coordination, and Middle East security planning. The counter-UAS thread later reappears at Provo in September, on the [counter-UAS page](/Suspicious/US_Intel/counter-uas-provo) and the [Egyptian jets at Provo page](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo). These are **inferences**, not meeting records.

* **July 20, 2025** — This is the date the Google-Trends compilations call their **anchor**: the point at which searches for case figures, UVU campus geography, and the eventual suspect reportedly cluster. See the [July 20 anchor page](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor) and the [UVU campus location searches](/GoogleSearches/uvu-campus-location-searches). Trends data is aggregate and low-volume queries are noisy; the anchor is a pattern claim, not proof of foreknowledge.

* **Around July 20, 2025** — The same compilations claim a **blip** for the eventual suspect's name two months before the shooting, tracked on [Tyler Robinson and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches). [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**, and the search claim establishes nothing about him.

### Late July and August 2025 — what followed

* **July 30, 2025** — The last of the Timpanogos surgeon search spikes in the two-week frenzy the compilations describe around July 20.

* **July 31, 2025** — An **off-the-record White House meeting** organized by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel and the **[Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding)** convenes on "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel." Attendees reportedly included **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)**, **[Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon)**, Apostle Mario Bramnick, Yehuda Kaploun and **[Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)**. Kirk and TPUSA were **absent** — the omission examined on [TPUSA's absence from July 31](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31) and [the July 31 meeting page](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA).

* **Aug 2, 2025** — **French legionnaires** reportedly arrive in the United States for a three-week California training rotation ending **Aug 25** — the claim examined on the [French Foreign Legion page](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims).

* **Aug 4–6, 2025** — The **Hamptons "intervention"**, at which Kirk was reportedly confronted by pro-Israel figures, and a reported call from **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)**. Detailed on the [Hamptons intervention meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and the [Hamptons claim page](/Suspicious/Israel/hamptons-intervention).

* **Aug 6, 2025** — Kirk publicly **"lashes out about donors pressuring him"** in a Megyn Kelly appearance — the on-record moment the [donor-pressure motive page](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) treats as confirmation the pressure was real.

* **Aug 8, 2025** — Israeli-IP searches are claimed for the ISR aircraft **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)**, 32 days before it flew low passes over Utah Valley — see [planes search patterns](/GoogleSearches/planes-search-patterns).

* **Aug 17–24, 2025** — Trackers describe a **second SU-BTT U.S. visit**: Paris → Omaha/Lincoln → **Wilmington, Delaware** → Paris, repeating the same Nebraska corridor. Detailed on the [SU-BTT movements timeline](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **Sept 4, 2025** — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** arrives at **Provo, Utah** direct from France and remains through the event window, closing the arc that began on July 20 — see [September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025).

## Open Questions

1. Does any **base visitor log, NOTAM, flight strip or public-affairs notice** for July 20, 2025 exist, and what does it show?
2. Who, by name and from what government, is alleged to have attended — and is there a **single primary source** for any name?
3. Do **CBP entry records** show foreign nationals entering on or around July 20 in connection with the aircraft?
4. What is **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)**'s complete registration and movement history before July 20, and does it support or refute the "first trip ever" framing?
5. Why does the same **Omaha–Lincoln corridor** recur on the August 17–24 leg, and is there a mundane operational reason such as maintenance or crew basing?
6. Is the **July 20 Google-Trends anchor** reproducible today from the raw Trends interface, or does it depend on screenshots that cannot be re-derived?
