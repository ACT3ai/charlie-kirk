# July 20, 2025 Military & Foreign-Leaders Meeting (Claims)

Open-source aviation research and investigative commentary place **July 20, 2025** as the date of two linked claims: (1) the Israel/Egyptian Air Force jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** made a reported **first U.S. trip**, landing at a **Nebraska Army base** — see [Nebraska Army Base (July 20)](./nebraska-army-base-july-20); and (2) a **military meeting with foreign leaders** occurred on U.S. soil the same day — see [Foreign Leaders Meeting (July 20)](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20). Mainstream reporting has **not** publicly framed these events as conclusively tied to Charlie Kirk's case.

## SU-BTT arrival (flight-tracking claims)

| Detail | As claimed in compilations |
| --- | --- |
| **Aircraft** | Egyptian Air Force Gulfstream **SU-BTT** (Dassault Falcon 7X class jet in site docs) |
| **Date** | **July 20, 2025** — described as first-ever U.S. visit in multiple project timelines ([Year_2025](./Year_2025), [Planes: SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)) |
| **Destination** | **Nebraska Army base** (specific base name varies by source; not confirmed in official Kirk-case filings on this site) |
| **Evidence type** | ADS-B / flight-tracking screenshots and aviation-spotter posts |

## Nebraska base meeting (claims)

Trash-source *Laws* blocks and conspiracy-theory summaries (e.g., foreign-intelligence write-ups in retired trash dirs) describe a **Nebraska Army base meeting** overlapping the SU-BTT visit:

- **Participants (alleged):** Foreign leaders or military delegations — attendee identities **not publicly verified** here.
- **Purpose (alleged):** Commentary links the meeting to counter-UAS testing, special-forces coordination, or broader Middle East security topics; **no official manifest** tying attendees to the UVU event has been released.
- **Same-day timing:** Citizen timelines pair the base visit with the **[July 18 "decision" claim](./july-18-decision)** and the **July 31 White House** session as a summer 2025 sequence.

## Foreign-leaders framing

The separate trash page *Foreign Leaders Meeting* contained only generic boilerplate. The substantive claim preserved on this site is narrower:

- Investigators allege **international officials** met with **U.S. military hosts** on or about July 20 while SU-BTT was in country.
- **CBP entry records** and **attendee logs** are cited in *Laws* sections as records that could confirm or refute participation — none are public in the Kirk investigation docket as summarized here.

## Relationship to September flights

July 20 is an **early anchor** in a longer 2025 aircraft narrative:

- **Sep 4:** SU-BTT reported arrival from France to **Provo, Utah** ([Year_2025](./Year_2025))
- **Sep 10–11:** Departure timelines around the UVU event ([September 10, 2025](./Sept_10_2025))

Commentators treat July 20 as **prior presence** of the same airframe; that remains **interpretive**.

## Sourcing status

- **Documented:** SU-BTT appears in **public flight-tracking** compilations referenced across [Planes](/Planes/overview).
- **Alleged:** Base meeting agenda, foreign-leader identities, and Kirk-case relevance.
- **Not established:** Any government statement connecting July 20 meetings to the September 10 shooting.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The Nebraska base visitor and attendee logs and [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)'s flight manifest and passengers from that July 20 visit and attendee records of the foreign leaders meeting and CBP entry records for the foreign nationals are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
