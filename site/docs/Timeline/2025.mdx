## The Timeline

### Winter and Spring — Groundwork

* **January 9, 2025** — Utah **SB0082**, the Autopsy Photo Amendments bill, was introduced in the state legislature, tightening the rules governing release of medical examiner records — [Utah Autopsy Law SB0082](/Medical/Autopsy/autopsy_law_sb0082).

* **May 1, 2025 (documented contract)** — NSWC Crane awarded **Accurate Energetic Systems** a $440,494 contract, N0016425PJ538, for "MINIATURIZED-XS DEMOLITION CHARGES, ANTI PERSONNEL-XS," with performance running **May 1 – August 25, 2025**. One master-file entry dates the signing to April 22 instead. This is the contract at the centre of the [exploding-mic theory](/Mic/DoD_Contract); the contract's existence is documented, the theory is not — [AES explosion factory](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory).
* **May 2, 2025** — Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) appointed **Tony F. Graf Jr.**, a former Deputy Utah County Attorney, to Utah's Fourth District Court; he would preside over the defendant's first appearance four months later — [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).


* **May 7, 2025** — SB0082 took effect. Researchers report unusual search interest in its drafting attorney from Israeli IP ranges that same week, an inference from trend data rather than from any record — [Utah Autopsy Law Changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes).

* **May 15, 2025 *(claims)*** — Citizen compilations report an Israeli-IP search for **[Judge Robert Lunnen](/court/judge-robert-lunnen)**, whose retirement (effective **August 1, 2025**) created the Fourth District vacancy [Judge Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf) filled — see [Judges Graf and Lunnen search patterns](/GoogleSearches/judges-graf-lunnen-searches). Aggregate Trends only; no searcher identification.

* **May 23, 2025 *(flight logs)*** — Egyptian-registered **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** landed at [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) and, per compilations, remained roughly four months, not leaving the country until September 13 — [Egyptian armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada).

* **July 1, 2025** — **[Dr. Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** began full-time as Utah's chief medical examiner, ten weeks before the shooting — [Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner).

### July — Meetings, Appointments, and First Flights

* **July 11–13, 2025** — [Megyn Kelly](/Influencers/podcasts-megyn-kelly) appeared with **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Florida, during the period when Kirk's public position on [Israel](/Israel/overview) was already under scrutiny ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccRNaLGavf8)). The **Dave Smith vs [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** debate (July 13) and **[Tucker Carlson](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel)**'s "go all the way" remark (July 14) put the split in front of the movement's own base — [TPUSA organizational timeline](./tpusa-organizational-timeline).

* **July 14–30, 2025 *(claims)*** — A compilation of alleged Israeli-IP searches runs through a roster of **[Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital)** surgeons on specific dates, plus DPS Commissioner **Beau Mason** (July 16, appointed July 17) and **Judge Tony Graf** (July 26). Aggregate Trends carries no searcher identity; every person named is living and accused of nothing — [surgeon search trends](/Suspicious/Medical/surgeon-search-trends) and [Timpanogos and medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches).

* **July 16, 2025** — The *Ami's House* podcast published "Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout," documenting the internal split in public ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8HkNMTNHsa8)).

* **~July 17, 2025** — **Beau Mason** was appointed Commissioner of the Utah Department of Public Safety, replacing Jess Anderson; one master-file entry dates the appointment to June instead — [Utah DPS — Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason) and the [Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline).

* **July 18, 2025 *(claims)*** — Some online commentators assert that a decision to "murder Charlie Kirk in Utah" was made around this date. This is a **speculative interpretation** read backward from later commentary; it has not been confirmed in any official proceeding — [July 18 Decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — The Israeli/Egyptian Air Force Gulfstream **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** reportedly made its first-ever U.S. trip, to a U.S. Army base in Nebraska; a military meeting with foreign leaders is reported on U.S. soil the same day — [Nebraska Army Base July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) and [Foreign Leaders Meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** as Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security; citizen researchers later tie his office to the booking of the September [SAM-702 flight](/Suspicious/US_Intel/sam-702-fort-huachuca). * **July–early September 2025 *(claims)*** — According to a [Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters) Network presentation ([@StewPetersNet](/Influencers/x/stewpetersnet)), geo-specific **Google Image searches from the [Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) area** for a roster of military, DIA, and Kirk-orbit figures (including, allegedly, [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) and [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole)) ramped from mid-July into a **Sept 6–9 cluster** ([Fort Huachuca image searches](/GoogleSearches/fort-huachuca-image-searches); [image results reportedly wiped](/Suspicious/US_Intel/image-results-wiped)). ### September 1–9 — The Cluster

* **September 1–5, 2025 *(claims)*** — Kirk reportedly told group chats he had "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"; the chats have not been authenticated in any public filing — [Leaked Kirk Texts](/Media/Leaked-Kirk-Texts).

Orem PD later said it was "not involved," and the joint Public Order Unit UVU pays for annually was never activated — [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records) and [UVU GRAMA warnings](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings).

* **September 4, 2025** — **SU-BTT** flew from France to **Provo, Utah**, and remained in the area — [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) and [SU-BTT plane movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline). One master-file entry gives the origin as **Minot, North Dakota** instead of France. Researchers also allege Egyptian "DoD liaison" subcontractors and [counter-UAS testing](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS) at Provo across September 4–10, with the crew reportedly housed in Orem — [Egyptian crew hotel](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel).

* **September 7–8, 2025 *(flight logs)*** — **[SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)**, a C-37A based at Andrews, flew Andrews → Colorado Springs, then Colorado Springs → Tucson, where compilations say the transponder went off and the callsign shifted from RCH to SAM. The same tail is reported as **SAM702**, **[SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000)** and **[SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112)** across sources.

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk posted publicly about distancing himself from the pro-Israel movement — [Charlie–Israel Friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13).

* **September 8–9, 2025 *(reported texts)*** — Circulating authenticated-group-chat narratives place Kirk's **"leave the pro-Israel cause"** vent in chats involving figures including **[Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer)** and **[Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki)**, an **emergency Zoom** with Kirk, the rabbi, and Hammer, and a subsequent **"they are going to kill me"** text (reported recipient **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)**) in a ~48-hour window. * **September 8–9, 2025 *(claims)*** — Researchers report a gathering of roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels at **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)**, the Army intelligence center, with an associated SAM flight — [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9), [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404), and the [Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

* **September 8–9, 2025 *(contested witness claim)*** — Army veteran **[Mitch Snow](/People/mitch-snow)** publicly alleged that people he believed to be **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)**, **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)**, and Rep. The claim is contested and is the subject of **[defamation litigation](/Defamation/Brian_Harpole_Candace_Defemation)** involving Harpole and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens); it is **not** a court finding — [Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and [Erika Kirk timeline](./erika-kirk-timeline). * **September 9, 2025** — During a contentious meeting the same period, Kirk reportedly texted **"They are going to kill me"** — [the text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text), the [high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9), and [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).
