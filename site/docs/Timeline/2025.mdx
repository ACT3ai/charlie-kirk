# Timeline 2025

This is the **whole calendar year in one scroll** — the single page to read if you want to see how January's legislative housekeeping, July's meetings and flights, September's ten-day cluster, and the autumn court fights sit on one line together. Deeper windows have their own pages and are linked at each event. Items marked *(claims)* are assertions by researchers or commentators that no official proceeding has confirmed.

## The Timeline

### Winter and Spring — Groundwork

* **January 9, 2025** — Utah **SB0082**, the Autopsy Photo Amendments bill, was introduced in the state legislature, tightening the rules governing release of medical examiner records — [Utah Autopsy Law SB0082](/Medical/Autopsy/autopsy_law_sb0082).

* **May 2, 2025** — Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) appointed **Tony F. Graf Jr.**, a former Deputy Utah County Attorney, to Utah's Fourth District Court; he would preside over the defendant's first appearance four months later — [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).

* **May 7, 2025** — SB0082 took effect. Researchers report unusual search interest in its drafting attorney from Israeli IP ranges that same week, an inference from trend data rather than from any record — [Utah Autopsy Law Changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes).

* **July 1, 2025** — **[Dr. Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** began full-time as Utah's chief medical examiner, ten weeks before the shooting — [Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner).

### July — Meetings, Appointments, and First Flights

* **July 11–13, 2025** — Megyn Kelly appeared with **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Student Action Summit in Tampa, Florida, during the period when Kirk's public position on Israel was already under scrutiny ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccRNaLGavf8)).

* **July 16, 2025** — The *Ami's House* podcast published "Inside the Turning Point USA Fallout," documenting the internal split in public ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8HkNMTNHsa8)).

* **~July 17, 2025** — **Beau Mason** was appointed Commissioner of the Utah Department of Public Safety, replacing Jess Anderson; one master-file entry dates the appointment to June instead — [Utah DPS — Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason) and the [Utah officials timeline](./utah-officials-cox-timeline).

* **July 18, 2025 *(claims)*** — Some online commentators assert that a decision to "murder Charlie Kirk in Utah" was made around this date. This is a **speculative interpretation** read backward from later commentary; it has not been confirmed in any official proceeding — [July 18 Decision](./july-18-decision).

* **July 20, 2025** — The Israeli/Egyptian Air Force Gulfstream **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** reportedly made its first-ever U.S. trip, to a U.S. Army base in Nebraska; a military meeting with foreign leaders is reported on U.S. soil the same day — [Nebraska Army Base July 20](./nebraska-army-base-july-20) and [Foreign Leaders Meeting July 20](./foreign-leaders-meeting-july-20).

* **July 31, 2025** — A White House meeting organized by the Faith & Freedom Coalition, the Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project addressed confronting "woke-right" narratives. Attendees reportedly included [Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu). **TPUSA was not present** — [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA) and [Why TPUSA Was Absent](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Absence-July-31).

### August — Pressure and Signals

* **Early August (~August 4), 2025** — The reported **"Hamptons intervention,"** in which pro-Israel figures confronted Kirk. Names reported present include [Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff) — [Hamptons Intervention Meeting](./hamptons-intervention-meeting) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons).

* **August 8, 2025** — Reported Israeli-IP search interest in the tail number **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)**, 32 days before the shooting, per Google Trends notes — [N1098L plane activity timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline).

* **August 25, 2025** — A flight from Heber City, Utah to Nashville is logged for an aircraft tied to **AES (Accurate Energetic Systems)**, the explosives firm later central to the mic theory — [AES Heber–Nashville Flight](/Mic/aes-heber-nashville-flight).

### September 1–9 — The Cluster

* **September 2, 2025** — Kirk issued a memo replacing TPUSA's chief operating officer, eight days before the shooting — [Charlie Replaces the COO](/TPUSA/charlie-replace-coo).

* **September 4, 2025** — **SU-BTT** flew from France to **Provo, Utah**, and remained in the area — [Provo Municipal Airport](/Locations/Provo_Airport) and [SU-BTT plane movements](./su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).

* **September 8, 2025** — Kirk posted publicly about distancing himself from the pro-Israel movement — [Charlie–Israel Friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction) and [Sept 8–13](./Sept_8_to_13).

* **September 8–9, 2025 *(claims)*** — Researchers report a gathering of roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels at **Fort Huachuca**, the Army intelligence center, with an associated SAM flight — [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9), [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404), and the [Fort Huachuca timeline](./fort-huachuca-military-timeline).

* **September 9, 2025** — Kirk appeared on **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)**'s show and pressed his criticisms of Israel directly, a break donor-pressure researchers treat as decisive — [Israel Donors Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive).

* **September 9, 2025** — During a contentious meeting the same period, Kirk reportedly texted **"They are going to kill me"** — [the text page](./they-are-going-to-kill-me-text), the [high-pressure meeting](./high-pressure-meeting-sept-9), and [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).

* **September 9, 2025 (night) *(claims)*** — Two men reportedly contacted UVU about the coming event the night before, an account examined on [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) and [UVU Sept 9 Foreknowledge](/Proof_Intel_Services/UVU_Sept_9_Foreknowledge).

### September 10, 2025 — Day of the Shooting

*Camera sightings below are broken out by device on [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview): [Ring](/cameras/Ring), [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE), and [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance), with the full list on [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras).*

* **8:07 AM MDT** — A Ring camera shows **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** — charged, not convicted — walking on S 800 W toward campus.
* **8:23 AM MDT** — A WYZE camera captures him driving on W 880 S.
* **8:29 AM MDT** — UVU surveillance records him driving onto campus in a grey Dodge Challenger; the [Affidavit of Probable Cause](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit) uses this time. A separate video claim asserts a **9:24 AM** campus arrival and a quad conversation with a TPUSA staffer; the two times do not reconcile — [TPUSA and Tyler Robinson Chat](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat).
* **9:57 AM MDT** — A WYZE camera captures him walking on W 880 S.
* **Late morning** — An outfit change is noted, to jeans and a long-sleeve shirt, before the late-morning sightings — [pre-shot campus timeline](./tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).
* **11:44 AM MDT** — Ring camera, walking on W 800 S.
* **11:49 AM MDT** — Ring camera, walking on S 800 W toward campus again.
* **~11:47 AM MDT** — **N1098L (HADES)** reported at low altitude near Utah Lake before the shooting; the exact minute is disputed between roughly 9:16 AM and 11:47 AM — [N1098L timeline](./n1098l-plane-activity-timeline).
* **12:13–12:23 PM MDT** — Stage, microphone, camera and security positions in the final ten minutes — [10 Minutes Before the Shot](./ten-minutes-before-shot).
* **12:23 PM MDT** — The shot. The reported time-of-death window is 12:23–12:27 PM MDT and the first report is logged at 12:26 PM — [Shooting Time Disputes](./12-23-shooting-time) and [Shooting Minute by Minute](./shooting-minute-by-minute).
* **12:24–12:48 PM MDT** — N1098L conducts a second low maneuver after the shot; accounts cite either 12:24 PM at 335 ft or 12:48 PM at roughly 204 ft.
* **Afternoon–evening** — Reports of a Dairy Queen sighting in Orem, with accounts conflicting between **12:38 PM and 6:38 PM MDT** — [Dairy Queen — Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem).
* **Evening** — Private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departs Provo Municipal Airport, its ADS-B transponder goes dark mid-flight, then reappears before it returns to PVU.

### September 11–30 — Custody, Charges, and the Scene

* **September 11, ~6:25 PM MDT *(disputed)*** — A defense video exhibit carries a **6:25 PM** Miranda advisement timestamp with no printed date; researchers place it on this evening, officials place formal arrest the next morning — [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).
* **September 11, ~7:57 PM MDT** — The **Discord messages** attributed to Robinson appear — [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) and [Custody vs Discord Confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).
* **September 11, ~7:58 PM MDT** — A Utah County press conference releases enhanced suspect photographs and rooftop video — [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos).
* **September 11, 10:00 PM / 10:26 PM MDT** — The booking sheet stamps the arrest at 10:00 PM; the government narrative has Robinson **arriving** at the Washington County Sheriff's Office at 10:26 PM — [arrest and affidavit timeline](./arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline).
* **September 11, 2025** — **SU-BTT departs the U.S. for Egypt.**
* **September 12, 01:58 / 04:00 / 07:18** — Booking entry, the police computer system's arrest time, and probable cause entry, in that order; he was housed at the [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail).
* **~September 14, 2025 (Sunday)** — Excavation and paving began in the tent and courtyard area, four days after the shooting — [Crime Scene Paving](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving) and the [scene changes timeline](./scene-changes-paving-timeline).
* **September 15, 2025** — FBI leadership stated on national television that a screwdriver found on the roof carried the defendant's DNA — [Kash Patel Briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings).
* **September 16, 2025** — Charging documents filed by the Utah County Attorney's Office; first appearance before Judge Graf, held without bail. **Neither the charging documents nor the affidavit mention the screwdriver.**
* **September 19, 2025** — "Demystifying the Mossad" with former Mossad chief Yossi Cohen published ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qpGPZHdil1E), [Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/uy/podcast/demystifying-the-mossad-with-ex-mossad-chief-yossi-cohen/id1589871008?i=1000727438458)) — [Mossad](/Israel/Mossad).
* **Late September 2025** — Public memorial services, and the TPUSA leadership succession that followed — [funeral and memorial timeline](./funeral-memorial-timeline), [Memorial and TPUSA Succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession), and [TPUSA Replacement Timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline).

### October–December — Aftermath and Court

* **October 2025** — An explosion at the **AES (Accurate Energetic Systems)** facility in Tennessee, roughly a month after the shooting, drew attention because of the company's miniaturized-charge defense contract — [AES Tennessee Explosion](/CoverUp/AES_Tennessee_Explosion) and [AES Explosion Factory](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory).
* **October 6, 2025** — N1098L is tracked landing at Corpus Christi International after a short Army-airfield leg, and remains on the ground there.
* **October 15, 2025** — A cluster of case developments and public disclosures dated to this day — [October 15, 2025](./october-15-2025).
* **Late October 2025 *(claims)*** — Reporting and commentary describe FBI leadership objecting to the National Counterterrorism Center's review of foreign-involvement leads; the inquiry is described as halted — [Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt) and [Joe Kent — Foreign Inquiry Shutdown](/Proof_Intel_Services/Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown).
* **Autumn 2025** — Gag orders, sealing motions, and discovery disputes moved through the Fourth District Court — [Gag Orders and Sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing) and the [discovery timeline](./discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).
* **Autumn 2025** — Citizen investigators reported takedowns and reach suppression across platforms — [censorship and information control timeline](./censorship-information-control-timeline).

Throughout 2025 the useful discipline is to keep **documented events** — published podcasts, filed documents, logged flights, the charged crime date — visually separate from **speculative targeting-decision language**. This page marks the latter with *(claims)*.
