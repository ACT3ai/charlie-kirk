# Distraction Events Timeline — September 10–11

Between the shot at **12:23:30 PM MDT on September 10, 2025** and the surrender that began around **9:00 PM MDT on September 11**, roughly **33 hours** elapsed. This timeline tracks what occupied those hours instead of the manhunt: one man's shouted false confession, at least two detentions that ended in release, two separate public "suspect in custody" claims that were both wrong, and a set of parallel weapon scares. Times are **Mountain Daylight Time (MDT)**.

The people named here are, with one exception, **living private citizens who were released without being charged as the shooter**. Nothing on this page asserts that any of them coordinated with anyone. The one documented admission — George Zinn's — is described below in the terms police themselves used.

## The Timeline

### September 10 — the first hour

* **12:23:30 PM** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is shot in the courtyard at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview); the 66 seconds that follow are reconstructed on [the minute-by-minute page](./shooting-minute-by-minute).

* **~12:24 PM** — The figure on the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof drops to the grass and moves north; the search has no confirmed direction of travel yet, a gap covered on [the post-shot escape timeline](./tyler-robinson-post-shot-escape).

* **Minutes after the shot** — **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)**, 71, shouts words to the effect of **"I shot him, now shoot me"** and is taken into custody — the **first person detained** in the case. Per the [Utah County Sheriff's Office](/Law_Enforcement/utah-county-sheriff), he later told investigators he made the claim **to draw attention away from the real shooter**. July 2026 prelim coverage summarizes campus-officer testimony that radio traffic reported a **suspect in custody** almost immediately — a **Zinn-driven false positive** on the channel, not a confirmed shooter capture. This is the one on-the-record admission that distraction at the scene was real and not merely theorised.

* **~12:26 PM** — Per police audio summarised in the master file, the **first radio report of a shooting** is logged, three minutes after the shot; the scanner record is on [the dispatch and radio timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).

* **~12:42 PM (+19 minutes)** — A **campus alert states a suspect is in custody.** It was **false**. Researchers tie the alert to the Zinn radio detention. Roughly ten thousand students stayed put on the strength of it; **who authorized the message remains unknown**. The GRAMA thread on campus messaging is documented at [UVU GRAMA warnings](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/uvu-grama-warnings), [the redacted UVU GRAMA records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records), and [pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

* **Same hour — Zachariah Qureshi** — **[Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi)**, 25, of Provo — a returned missionary who attended as a **supporter**, per DPS/NewsNation profiles — is reportedly detained near the scene. Commentary describes him as having been within roughly **25 feet** of Kirk; family said **mistaken detention**. He was **released and never charged as the shooter** — the documented arc is exonerating. Fox and others named Zinn and Qureshi as **ruled out** the same night. Scanner narrations (Coleman / **[@whokilledck](/Influencers/x/whokilledck)**) place a Qureshi-linked **hospital-to-hospital** hunt on the radio before release; hospital staff, via officers on air, denied any such confrontation ([dispatch / radio timeline](./dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline)).

### September 10 — afternoon

* **Early afternoon** — Utah DPS Commissioner **[Beau Mason](/distraction_people/Beau_Mason)** states publicly that Zinn was taken into custody but **cleared as the shooter the same day** ("Shortly after the shooting, we did have a suspect in custody, George Zinn…"), an account tracked on [the Utah DPS page](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason).

* **Afternoon** — A parallel weapon scare runs on the Provo scanner: **[Pellet Gun Man](/distraction_people/Pellet_Gun_Man)**, on a bicycle with an air rifle. Unrelated to the shooting, it nonetheless pulled units and airtime, as did the crowd threads around [Curly Hair Man](/distraction_people/Curly_Hair_Man), [USA-Chant Man](/distraction_people/USA_Chant_Man), and the [Badge Man "fake cop" claim](/distraction_people/Fake_Cop_Badge_Man).

* **Afternoon** — An unidentified **all-black-clothed figure** is reported crossing a construction site north of campus, telling an excavator operator he was "just trying to get home safe after a shooting." That figure — described with a black trench coat, black mask, sunglasses, long dark hair and a small backpack — has **never been identified**. See [Black Clothing Figure](/distraction_people/Black_Clothing_Figure) and [all-black construction worker](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/all-black-construction-worker).

* **4:21 PM** — **FBI Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) publicly announces a suspect is in custody.** It was **incorrect**. The announcement is documented on [Kash Patel's briefings](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) and, in the Qureshi context, on [the FBI's Qureshi page](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi).

* **~90 minutes later (approx. 5:50 PM)** — The person named in that announcement is **released without further public explanation**. Commentators ask whether the premature announcement **paused the search** and handed the actual shooter a ninety-minute window. That is an **allegation about consequence, not a finding** — **no public CAD log has confirmed a stand-down**; see [the suspect manhunt page](/Law_Enforcement/suspect-manhunt).

* **Evening / social-media afterlife** — The public narrative begins shifting toward the eventual charged suspect. Separately, after Qureshi's release some social threads continued a **"Muslim shooter"** frame despite same-night clearances — misdirection that is itself part of the distraction window. Coverage patterns for the day are catalogued on [September 10 breaking news](/Media/september-10-breaking-news) and [FBI press conference media](/Media/fbi-press-conference-media).

### September 11 — the second news cycle

* **7:15 AM** — A gray Dodge Challenger is captured on surveillance at the **Maverik station in Cedar City**, roughly 215 miles south, per the charging documents summarised on [the Tyler Robinson timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson) and [Cedar City Maverik](/Locations/Cedar_City_Maverik).

* **9:58 AM** — The FBI releases its **first photos of a person of interest** — low-resolution stills that drew immediate criticism, discussed on [enhanced suspect photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos) and [enhanced photos released after custody](/Suspicious/FBI/enhanced-photos-after-custody).

* **10:44 AM** — The FBI announces a **$100,000 reward**, keeping the "unknown shooter at large" frame alive through the middle of the second day.

* **~24 hours after the shot** — **[Russell Kennington](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington)**, 38, is arrested **inside the taped crime scene** at UVU, reportedly carrying a long gun, and charged with criminal trespass and felony obstruction. The charges were **later dropped**. This happened the **same day** the rifle attributed to the charged suspect was found after earlier K-9 and officer searches had failed — the sequencing question raised on [the gun-discovery sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence), [rifle found after federal redirect](/Suspicious/FBI/rifle-found-after-federal-redirect), [Kennington released](/CoverUp/Kennington_Released), and [Kennington charges dropped](/Suspicious/Trial_Legal/kennington-charges-dropped).

* **6:25 PM (disputed)** — Per the court record analysed on [the Mirandizing page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing), [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is Mirandized. Attorney **[Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)** has said publicly that Robinson was in Washington County custody "well before 6:30 PM." If that is right, the "still searching" news cycle continued for hours after the search had ended — the core of [the arrest-time discrepancy](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy).

* **7:55 PM** — Per an AP-sourced timeline preserved in the master file, the FBI says it is looking for a **"person of interest"** and releases photos plus rooftop video — again framing the case as unsolved.

* **7:57 PM** — [Discord posts attributed to Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) appear, saying "It was me at UVU yesterday." Whether those posts precede or follow custody is the whole dispute on [custody vs Discord confession](./custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline) and [the Discord custody timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline).

* **8:02 PM** — Washington County Sheriff **[Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)** reportedly receives a call from a retired deputy saying he knows the shooter's identity and is working with the family. Brooksby later resigned; the account and the questions about it are on [the Brooksby resignation page](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/brooksby-resignation) and [Miranda vs the Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account).

* **~9:00 PM** — Robinson reportedly turns himself in with his parents at the [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff). No surrender footage has been produced — see [missing surrender footage](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/missing-surrender-footage) and [the claim he did not surrender at all](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/robinson-did-not-surrender).

* **10:00 PM (as stated by the FBI Director)** — Patel states the arrest occurred at 10 PM Thursday, and later describes a **"33-hour manhunt"** — a figure that, worked backwards, lands at about **9:23 PM on September 11**, roughly three hours after the Miranda time in the court record. That arithmetic conflict is the closing item of this timeline and is developed on [the surrender and custody page](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) and [the Kash Patel manhunt timeline](./kash-patel-manhunt-timeline).

### Aftermath of the distraction actors (2026)

* **Jan 29, 2026** — **George Zinn** is sentenced on **obstruction** and **two counts of sexual exploitation of a minor** under Utah's indeterminate scheme (media report maximum range up to **15 years** concurrent). The CSAM counts are court facts about Zinn's separate criminal exposure; they do **not**, by themselves, prove who assigned him any role on Sept 10.

* **2026 reporting on payment / TPUSA claims** — [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others have reported Zinn told hospital staff he was **paid** to act as a decoy and had not been paid yet / did not know by whom; that payment claim remains **unverified** in official charging narratives, and commentary also notes Zinn denials or alternate trauma explanations. A **[@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen)** interview clip is cited online for Zinn saying he worked for **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)** — attributed claim only; TPUSA is a living organization and the employment claim is **not** established as fact here. Separately, **[@DiligentDenizen](/Influencers/x/diligent-denizen-2)** (July 2026) amplified prelim commentary that Agent [David Hull](/People/david-hull) described Robinson meeting **"TPUSA representatives"** on 9/10 — see [TPUSA and the Tyler Robinson chat](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat); do **not** treat that as proof of a Zinn–Robinson joint plot; it is a cross-link only.

## Open Questions

1. Who authorised the 12:42 PM campus "suspect in custody" alert, and on what information?
2. Who was the person the FBI Director referred to at 4:21 PM, and what were the grounds for the detention and the release ninety minutes later?
3. Do dispatch logs show units being pulled off the perimeter after either false custody claim, and for how long?
4. How many minutes did the Zinn arrest occupy responders and cameras during the window in which the shooter left the area?
5. Was the all-black figure described by the construction crew ever identified, and does any released footage show him?
6. Why has no surrender footage from the Washington County Sheriff's Office been produced, given the number of cameras normally covering such a lobby?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

The complete CAD and dispatch logs for September 10–11 showing unit assignments during and after each false custody claim, the detention and release paperwork for every person held that day, the FBI's internal record behind the 4:21 PM announcement, the UVU alert-authorisation record for the 12:42 PM message, the booking and interview file for George Zinn, and the Washington County Sheriff's Office lobby video for the evening of September 11 are things the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
