# N560TW's Sept 10 Scottsdale-Provo Turnaround (Claims)

:::caution Attributed claims only
**Tom Lewis and the T.W. Lewis Foundation are accused of nothing.** A private jet flying a multi-stop day trip is what private jets do. The person raising this is **asking for tips**, not stating a finding, and this page treats it that way.
:::

## What is alleged

Candace Owens said on her live broadcast that she is "now very interested in" tail **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)**. In her words: *"It departed on the morning of September 10th from Scottsdale, Arizona, and landed in Provo at 10:06 AM. It then immediately turned around and departed for Santa Barbara... then back to Scottsdale by 12:44 PM. Somebody saw something."* The aircraft is a **Cessna Citation XLS**, and the shape of the itinerary — land, sit briefly, leave — is what investigators describe as a **drop-off or pickup rather than a trip**.

The connective tissue is ownership. The aircraft is reported owned by **T.W. Lewis Aviation LLC**, tied to the **T.W. Lewis Foundation**, which publicly lists Turning Point USA as a partner on its own website. **Tom Lewis** reportedly shared a stage with Charlie Kirk at an ASU event in 2023. That places the aircraft inside the **donor-pressure thread** running through this investigation — the reporting that Kirk was losing major donors over his refusal to stay "pro-Israel" and over his refusal to cancel Tucker Carlson, and his own reported message that he had "no choice but to leave the pro Israel cause." Investigators also connect it to a same-day **Arizona-to-Provo pattern** they allege for the separate tail [N40JD](/Planes/N40JD).

Owens' actual framing matters here and is often lost in the retelling: she is **requesting tips**. She and the amplifying researcher are asking ground crew, pilots and witnesses in Arizona, Utah and California who was aboard. That is a question, not an accusation, and no answer has been produced.

## The ordinary explanation

A Citation XLS flying Scottsdale → Provo → Santa Barbara → Scottsdale across a single morning is an **utterly typical light-jet day-trip pattern**. That is precisely what private business jets are chartered and owned to do, and short ground times reflect passenger drop-offs on a multi-stop itinerary rather than evasion. Read the same track without the context and it is indistinguishable from a thousand other Western-US business days.

The timing works against the claim rather than for it. The aircraft **landed at 10:06 am** — more than two hours before Charlie Kirk was shot — and had departed Utah before it happened. An aircraft that leaves before the event is a poor candidate for a role in it. It was also **fully transponder-visible on public trackers throughout**, which is the entire reason anyone can discuss it; that is not how covert movement is conducted.

Finally, the ownership link is close to the least surprising fact in the investigation file. A conservative foundation that **openly and publicly** partners with TPUSA has an aircraft that visits Provo — home to a TPUSA event that day. That is what partnership looks like from the air. If donor-linked aircraft never appeared near TPUSA events, that would be the anomaly.

## What would settle it

1. The passenger manifest for N560TW for September 10, 2025 — the single question Owens is actually asking.
2. Provo Airport FBO records showing who was met, and by whom, at 10:06 am.
3. Whether anyone boarded or deplaned at Provo at all, or whether the stop was a fuel or crew-duty stop.
4. The purpose of the Santa Barbara leg, which the tracking data cannot supply.
5. Whether the alleged N40JD Arizona-to-Provo pattern is real and, if so, whether the two are connected in any way.
