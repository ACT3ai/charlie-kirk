# The COO's Alleged Call to "Pierre Du Pont" (Claims)

:::caution Attributed claims only
A chief operating officer **making and receiving calls in a crisis is his literal job**. Justin Streiff is a living private citizen accused of no crime. The identification below is explicitly **a belief, not a confirmation**, in the words of the person who made it, and the associated $1 billion figure has never been documented in any form.
:::

## What is alleged

**Candace Owens** alleges that **Justin Streiff** — named chief operating officer in a memo the investigation file dates **September 2, 2025**, eight days before the assassination — had a phone call *"right after Charlie Kirk was shot"* with a person **she believes** to be a French-American named **Pierre Du Pont**. She says a *"potential billion dollar partnership was on the line with TPUSA."*

This dovetails with a separate claim by **Stew Peters**, who states that *"this gentleman called Pierre who is in Israel"* offered Kirk a billion dollars that Kirk rejected, and that the money was *"accepted"* by Turning Point USA after his death. The investigation file's own defamation note on the Peters segment stresses that these claims are **unverified**, that **no court has adjudicated any of them**, that the "Pierre" identification should be attributed to Owens rather than asserted independently, and that Peters frames significant portions of his commentary as **his own opinion**. Peters himself says of the figure: *"I've reported loosely that there was a billion dollar offer."*

There is **no call record, no phone number, no confirmed identity for "Pierre," and no URL** for either allegation in the file. Owens hedges her own identification with the words *"who she believes is"* — she does not claim to have confirmed it.

## The ordinary explanation

**A newly installed COO making and receiving phone calls in the minutes after his founder is shot on camera is his job.** That is crisis management: notifying the board, reaching family, contacting counsel, standing up a response. Framing it as sinister requires assuming the conclusion first and reading a call log — which nobody has — second.

The identification is by its author's own wording a **belief**, not a confirmation, and no record supports that any such call occurred at all. On the money: the **$1 billion figure has never been documented in any form** — not as cash, a pledge, a grant agreement, or a contract. No donor entity has been named. TPUSA has not been shown to have received it, and Peters concedes he "reported loosely" on the number. Finally, Kirk's September 2 COO appointment was **a management decision Kirk made himself**, which is evidence of ordinary succession planning by a man who knew he was under threat — not evidence of a plot against him.

## What would settle it

1. Produce a phone record establishing that the call occurred, and when.
2. Establish whether a person named Pierre Du Pont exists in this context and confirm the identification, which Owens explicitly has not.
3. Produce any document — pledge, grant letter, wire, contract — reflecting a $1 billion offer to Charlie Kirk or a receipt by TPUSA.
4. Produce the September 2, 2025 memo naming Streiff as COO, which the file references but never reproduces.
