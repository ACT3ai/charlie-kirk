# Mic vs Pendant — Competing Internal-Device Theories

Citizen investigators largely agree Charlie Kirk was **not** killed by a clean .30-06 rooftop
shot. They disagree on **which internal device** delivered the fatal energy. The two leading
hypotheses both live under the Mic section because both reject the official bullet narrative.

## Side-by-side comparison

| Factor | [Lapel mic theory](./rode-wireless-mic) | [Crucifix pendant theory](./exploding-pendant-shaped-charge) |
|--------|----------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Device** | RØDE Wireless PRO TX under shirt | PETN cone in crucifix under "FREEDOM" lettering |
| **Anchor** | Belt clip / fabric (weaker) | Silver box chain around neck (stronger) |
| **Blast direction** | Left-side pack → upward pinch toward neck | Upward from chest toward neck |
| **Key video cue** | Fabric expansion from mic pack; clasp ejected | Shirt movement **after** wound would differ for external bullet |
| **Military comment** | O'Neill/Holt: "explosion on him" | USMC breacher: unanchored mic would push **left**, not observed |
| **Procurement link** | [AES mini-charges](./DoD_Contract) | Same AES / micro-charge ecosystem |
| **Candace thesis** | Primary — rigged **second mic** for **chest** | Pendant discussed in shaped-charge community |

## Why both beat the rifle story (per proponents)

- [Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Audio): stage detonation separate from 120 m muzzle blast
- [Video](./mic-explosion-video-analysis): spherical shirt motion, not rifle vector
- [Trajectory](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle): rooftop angle mismatch to neck entry
- No catastrophic .30-06 exit / tarp blood per [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview)

## Can both be true?

Some analysts treat mic and pendant as **redundant kill mechanisms** — either consistent with
foreign-intelligence "pager attack" style redundancy. Others treat them as **mutually exclusive**
physical explanations requiring one primary device.

No public forensic report has identified explosive residue on either object.

## Skeptic counterpoint

[@Trillion0x](https://x.com/Trillion0x/status/2070699686961520802) and [Demo_of_shape_charge](./Demo_of_shape_charge)
argue a real shaped charge produces a **large smoke signature** absent in UVU footage. Proponents
reply any field device would be a **micro-EFP**, not a breaching charge.

## Research path

1. Autopsy and clothing lab for residue and fragment metallurgy
2. Recover or photograph intact mic TX and pendant post-event
3. Reconcile [SUV fragments](./suv-transporting-charlie) (glass, blue-label black object) with
