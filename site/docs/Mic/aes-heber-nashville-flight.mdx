# AES — The Heber City → Nashville Flight (Aug 25, 2025)

:::caution Attributed questions, not findings
Everything below is an **open investigative thread** built from public records (flight data, photographs, a federal contract) and the interpretation independent researchers place on them. The flight, the contract, and the photographs are verifiable; the **inference that they connect** is a theory. No living person named here is accused of any crime, and no wrongdoing is asserted.
:::

This page isolates one distinct aspect of the [AES / exploding-mic thread](./aes-explosion-factory): the **courier question** — how a device allegedly manufactured at the [Accurate Energetic Systems](./AES) factory in McEwen, Tennessee could have traveled the ~1,600 miles to the [Utah Valley University](/campus_university/overview) stage. Researchers point to a single-date flight and a photograph. For the factory explosion itself see [AES Factory Explosion](./aes-explosion-factory); for the procurement see the [DoD Contract](./DoD_Contract).

## The date that lines up

The [DoD contract](./DoD_Contract) awarded to AES for "MINIATURIZED-XS DEMOLITION CHARGES, ANTI-PERSONNEL-XS" carried a **period of performance ending August 25, 2025** — the delivery deadline. According to independent researchers compiling the [Who Killed CK writeup](/People/jon-bray):

- On **August 25, 2025** — the exact contract delivery date — a **private aircraft departed Heber City, Utah** and landed at **John Tune Airport in Nashville, Tennessee**.
- **John Tune Airport is the closest airport to the AES facility**, less than **60 km** from McEwen, Tennessee.
- Heber City is a small town of roughly 17,000 people; researchers describe private flights from Heber City directly to Nashville as **extremely rare**.
- **No return flight to Heber City was logged.** Researchers note that if the trip involved collecting energetic material, flying home with explosives would carry obvious security risk — offered as their reasoning, not as proof.

Heber City is also the closest general-aviation field to the UVU event site, which is why researchers treat the Utah end of the flight as event-adjacent.

## The microphone-in-pocket photograph

The flight thread connects to the mic thread through **[Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs)** — a Heber City, Utah resident and, per investigation-file notes, the brother of pastor [Jack Hibbs](/People/jack-hibbs). **He is a living person and is not accused here of anything.** Researchers (attributed to a [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) investigation) highlight the following, framed as questions:

- Hibbs was **photographed near [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** as Kirk arrived at UVU on September 10, 2025 — sixteen days after the flight.
- In pre-event photos he is described as wearing a checkered shirt with a **square object in his front right pocket**, reportedly measuring **just under 2×2 inches** with rounded corners, protruding less than an inch.
- Researchers say those dimensions **match a RØDE Wireless-series receiver** (the [RØDE Wireless PRO / GO II](./rode-wireless-mic) class, ~1.8 × 1.8 × 0.7 in) — the same microphone family Kirk was wearing.
- He was reportedly photographed **standing next to the sound crew and the person who mic'd up Kirk** before the event.
- Photographs taken **after** Kirk was killed reportedly show **Hibbs's pocket empty**.

None of this establishes that Hibbs carried a device, knew of any plan, or did anything wrong. The [Butch Hibbs profile](/People/butch-hibbs) documents the "cleanup-crew" and pocket-object threads with the same attribution-only framing and explicitly asserts no wrongdoing.

## Questions researchers say require answers

The value of this thread is a short list of **verifiable** questions, not a conclusion:

1. Who was aboard the August 25, 2025 flight from Heber City to John Tune Airport, Nashville?
2. Was there truly no return leg, and what do the full FAA/ADS-B records show?
3. What was the square object in the pre-event pocket photograph, and where did it go?
4. Why was that person reportedly standing with the sound crew that mic'd up Kirk?

These are answerable through flight logs, photographs, and testimony — the kind of records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are designed to force into the open.

## Why this matters to the theory

If the [exploding-mic theory](./mic-explosion-video-analysis) is correct, the device had to get from a Tennessee factory to a Utah stage. A single-date, one-way private flight landing beside the AES plant on the **exact contract delivery date** is the courier step the theory needs — which is precisely why proponents emphasize it and why skeptics note that coincidences of date and geography are **not** the same as a documented chain of custody. This site records the **adjacency** and leaves the inference to the reader.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)
