the [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index), then follow video, audio, procurement, or

The exploding-mic thread matters because it attacks the government's physical premise directly:
if Charlie died from a **stage-origin detonation**, [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s attributed rooftop shot
becomes narratively optional. [Jon Bray's video work](./mic-explosion-video-analysis) argues shirt
fabric expands **before** the neck wound with **spherical** not rifle-vector motion; the
[Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Audio) recording reportedly separates a **120 m muzzle blast** from a
**louder 46 m tent detonation**; [Rob O'Neill](./rob-oneill-navy-seal-testimony) and Gen. [Blaine
Holt](/People/blaine-holt) told NEWSMAX they saw an **explosion**, not a rifle wound signature. June 2026
[SUV interior photos](./suv-transporting-charlie) added fragment and reported glass-debris readings
investigators tie to a fragmented transmitter.

Substance clusters into five paths. **Hardware and placement:** [Phillip Goldsberry](./AV_Team_Mic_Setup)
reportedly routed the mic **under** the shirt — unlike prior TPUSA outdoor events.
**Immediate aftermath:** [table jumpers](./Table_Jumpers_Device) and [chest-shot witnesses](./Chest_Shot_Witnesses)
raise chain-of-custody and scripted-narrative questions (framed as hypotheses).
**Procurement:** the [April 2025 AES contract](./DoD_Contract) for miniaturized anti-personnel
charges and the [October factory explosion](./aes-explosion-factory) sit on the same timeline as
the [Aug 25 Heber–Nashville courier flight](./aes-heber-nashville-flight). **Competing internal device:** [Mic vs Pendant](./Mic_vs_Pendant)
compares lapel and crucifix theories. **Skeptic balance:** [shaped-charge demo](./Demo_of_shape_charge)
documents smoke-signature objections.

Read [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index) first, then [Video](./mic-explosion-video-analysis)
+ [Canon audio](./Canon_XA55_Audio) if you want physics, [SUV photos](./suv-transporting-charlie)
for post-transport debris, and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) to connect to the
Tyler Robinson frame. [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) list explosive-residue
and AES disclosure as leverage for answers not yet public.


## Public commentary

As of mid-2026, public discussion on X around the microphone theory remains **polarized**:

1. **Device-rupture camp** — RØDE/Sennheiser + PETN/AES narratives (**[@jonaaronbray](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)**, **[@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)**, **[@richtidwell](/Influencers/x/richtidwell)**).
2. **Rifle camp** — autopsy GSW, fragments, crack-bang, physics of shaped charges (**[@alexboge](/Influencers/x/alexboge)**, hearing-watch accounts, some [@grok](/Influencers/x/grok) replies).
3. **Hybrid questions** — glass in SUV, under-shirt placement, AES contract timing, factory explosion adjacency.

This overview does not pick a winner; the child pages hold the evidence stacks. Related context appears in the [Video Analysis](./mic-explosion-video-analysis), [RODE mic](./rode-wireless-mic), [DoD Contract](./DoD_Contract), and [AES factory](./aes-explosion-factory) sections.

According to [@ZREIKMIESTER](/Influencers/x/zreikmiester) (Ali Zreik) on X, these annotated diagrams and a slow-motion clip attempt to lay out, step by step, a claimed sequence in which a "molten hot LiPO battery pierced into" Charlie Kirk's neck, "and how the PCB followed into his neck sleeve." In a companion post, the same author writes that "melted into the polyester shirt are battery fragments and Rode microphone clip parts," and asserts that "they cleaned up the body before delivering it to the hospital."

This is presented here as an **attributed, unverified proponent-side illustration** of the broader [exploding-mic theory](./mic-explosion-video-analysis) — the argument that a device associated with the [RØDE Wireless PRO](./rode-wireless-mic) transmitter, rather than a rooftop rifle round, caused the fatal neck injury. It is one interpretation among several. The **official finding is a rifle gunshot**, and skeptics of the mic theory (documented on the [shaped-charge demo](./Demo_of_shape_charge) and [Mic vs Pendant](./Mic_vs_Pendant) pages) dispute the physical premise. The author's "cleaned up the body" statement is his own assertion, names no individual, and is not independently verified here. Nothing on this page is a finding of fact.
