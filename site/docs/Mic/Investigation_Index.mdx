# Mic Investigation Index

This is the working memo for the **exploding microphone** thread in the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)
assassination. Citizen investigators argue Charlie was killed by an **internal device
detonation** at the stage — not a distant .30-06 from [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s attributed rooftop
position. Nothing here claims any named AV technician, TPUSA staffer, or AES employee
committed a crime or knew in advance that Charlie Kirk would die.

## Why this thread ranks high in the investigation

Four evidence layers stack:

1. **Video** — [Frame analysis](./mic-explosion-video-analysis): shirt moves before neck wound;
   magnetic clasp ejected; spherical not rifle-vector motion ([Jon Bray](https://x.com/jonaaronbray)).
2. **Audio** — [Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Audio): three separated booms — muzzle blast ~120 m
   **and** stage detonation ~46 m at the tent.
3. **Post-event physical** — [SUV photos](./suv-transporting-charlie): fragments, charred material,
   reported "shattered tempered glass" like a RØDE transmitter.
4. **Procurement timeline** — [AES DoD contract](./DoD_Contract) and [factory explosion](./aes-explosion-factory)
   tied to miniaturized anti-personnel charges.

[Military veterans](./rob-oneill-navy-seal-testimony) ([Rob O'Neill](/People/rob-oneill), Gen. [Blaine Holt](/People/blaine-holt)) publicly
rejected the shirt movement as rifle-caused on NEWSMAX.
