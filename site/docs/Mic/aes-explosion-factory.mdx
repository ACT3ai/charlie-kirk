# AES — Accurate Energetic Systems Factory Explosion

**Accurate Energetic Systems (AES)** is a Tennessee-based manufacturer of military-grade explosive devices and energetic materials. On **October 10, 2025** — approximately one month after Charlie Kirk's assassination at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025 — AES Building 602 was destroyed in a massive explosion involving approximately **24,000 pounds of explosive materials**.

## The $440,000 Payment and the Flight

Investigators and independent researchers tracking financial connections to the Kirk assassination identified a significant payment:

- **$440,000** reportedly reached AES, with a delivery date of **August 25, 2025** — two weeks before Kirk's death.
- On that same date, a rare private flight departed from **Heber City, Utah** (the closest regional airport to the Utah Valley University event site) to **Nashville, Tennessee** (the closest major airport to the AES facility in Tennessee).
- **No return flight** was logged for that trip.

The question raised by investigators: who was on that flight, and could it have been someone picking up a concealed explosive device — specifically, a shaped charge or EFP-style device small enough to be hidden inside a lavalier microphone transmitter?

## Building 602 and the Scale of the Explosion

AES Building 602, the structure destroyed on October 10, 2025, held approximately **24,000 lbs of explosives** at the time of the blast. The scale of the destruction raised questions among investigators:

- Was the explosion accidental, or was it a deliberate destruction of evidence?
- The explosion occurred 30 days after Kirk's assassination — within the window where physical evidence tying a device to AES could have been traced if the investigation moved quickly.
- The timing mirrors the pattern of other physical evidence in the case being destroyed or made inaccessible — including the paving over of the UVU scene within days of the assassination.

## Connection to the Microphone Theory

The AES connection becomes significant in the context of the exploding microphone theory. According to that theory:

- A small shaped charge or EFP (explosively formed penetrator) device was hidden inside or attached to Kirk's **RØDE Wireless PRO lavalier microphone transmitter**, which was clipped to his shirt near his left chest.
- The device was detonated at close range, producing an outward blast consistent with what multiple camera angles captured: omnidirectional fabric expansion from the mic position, not the directional kinetic transfer that a high-velocity rifle round produces.
- The magnetic clasp of the mic transmitter reportedly became a projectile, visible in multiple camera angles being ejected through Kirk's neck area — creating the rectangular wound seen in post-event descriptions.

AES specializes precisely in the type of small, precision energetic devices that could be miniaturized to fit inside commercial audio equipment. The company's product line includes shaped charges and custom energetic configurations for military and government customers.

## Phillip Goldsberry Jr. and the Mic Placement

**Phillip Goldsberry Jr.** was the AV team member who reportedly mic'd up Charlie Kirk at the UVU event. Investigators have noted an anomaly: Kirk's lavalier microphone was placed **under his shirt**, with the transmitter on his left belt — despite the RØDE Wireless PRO having a furry windscreen designed for outdoor placement **on top of clothing**. Multiple other outdoor debates featuring Kirk and TPUSA shows that the standard practice was to clip the mic to the outside of the shirt.

The decision to route the mic cable under Kirk's shirt — unusual for the venue and equipment setup — would have placed the transmitter in a concealed position against the body, where a shaped-charge device would produce maximum effect with minimal external visibility.

## The N1098L Flight and the Tennessee Corridor

Separately from the Heber City–Nashville flight on August 25, independent aviation analysts tracked flight **N1098L** — a surveillance-capable aircraft with a documented history of ISR (intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance) missions. That flight's altitude was reduced when passing over the AES factory/plant area in Tennessee, at reduced speed, in a pattern consistent with aerial observation of the facility. See the [Planes section](/Planes/overview) for the full N1098L analysis.

## What the AES Evidence Suggests

No single element proves that the AES facility produced the device used in Kirk's assassination. The convergence of the following, however, places AES at the center of a credible investigative thread:

1. $440,000 payment to AES, delivered August 25, 2025
2. A private flight Heber City, UT → Nashville, TN on the same date, with no logged return
3. AES Building 602 destroyed by explosion on October 10, 2025 — 30 days after Kirk's death
4. AES's product line includes precision energetic devices small enough to be concealed in audio equipment
5. Military veterans who reviewed the footage say the wound and shirt-movement pattern is consistent with a small explosion, not a rifle round

The destruction of Building 602 effectively eliminated any physical trail linking AES to a specific device or order — which would be consistent with evidence destruction if such a link existed.
