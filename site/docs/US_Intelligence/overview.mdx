The most **documented attributable thread** is [Joe Kent](./joe-kent)'s public account: as NCTC
director he says the FBI **halted** his inquiry before he could exhaust **foreign-nexus** leads.
That inquiry ran through [ODNI](./odni) under [Tulsi Gabbard](./tulsi-gabbard). Parallel reporting
alleges **[Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)** shut the NCTC effort and was upset Kent reviewed FBI material — presented as
reported allegations, not adjudicated obstruction.

The **signals lane** centers on the NSA's Utah Data Center (**Bumblehive**) minutes from UVU. An
admin-insider tip claims **12 Israeli-registered cellphones** at the scene reached the White House;
[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)'s posts expand to **44 foreign phones** and burner deanonymization. The [NSA](./nsa) page
holds the U.S.-agency framing; [Israel's NSA phones page](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive) holds
the foreign-device breakdown.

Operational claims include [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) HADES passes, [CIA](./cia) UVU National
Security Society profiles removed **9/24/2025**, and **Fort Huachuca** timing — SAM-702 VIP flight
with Under Secretary **[Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** on **Sept 9** plus the [Cabot Phillips](./Cabot_alibi) carpet
comparison. The FBI's role in blocking investigators is detailed in [FBI](/FBI/overview); foreign
state actors in [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).
