# Tulsi Gabbard

Tulsi Gabbard served as **Director of National Intelligence (DNI)** — head of [ODNI](./odni) — for more
than a year overlapping the Kirk case. The [NCTC](./nctc) foreign-nexus inquiry ran in her chain of command.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Role | Director of National Intelligence (DNI) |
| Oversaw | [ODNI](./odni) containing [NCTC](./nctc) |
| Connection to CK Case | DNI during reported NCTC halt; office reportedly replied to Macron allegation |
| Evidence Rating | MODERATE (mixed public accounts) |
| Status | Alive |

## Position in the chain

**[Joe Kent](./joe-kent)** (NCTC) → **ODNI (Gabbard)** → **President**

When [Joe Kent](./joe-kent) says his inquiry was stopped, oversight questions include what **ODNI leadership**
knew and whether the DNI acted to preserve intelligence or allow the halt.

## Macron allegation — only ODNI reply? (Owens account)

[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) reported that after she raised an alleged assassination
directive connected to **French President [Emmanuel Macron](/People/emmanuel-macron)**, she received **no responses** from the FBI,
DOJ, or White House — and that **only Tulsi Gabbard's office replied.**

This is **Owens's account** of engagement, not proof of Macron's involvement. See [Israel France/Macron threads](/Israel/overview).

## Russell Brand cabinet-source claim

**Russell Brand** stated (~June 2026) that **two sources** from a **cabinet meeting** (~six days after
Kirk's death) described **Kash Patel** arriving with **Tulsi Gabbard**, the **Vice President**, and others,
and directing: **no foreign involvement**, **no domestic accomplices**. Brand framed this as minimum
cover-up evidence. Full context: [Russell Brand Cabinet Claims](/Israel/Russell_Brand_Cabinet_Claims).

We do **not** assert Gabbard knew of or ordered the assassination.

## Kash Patel vs Kent/Tulsi (commentary)

X posts summarized in the master file describe Patel as upset that **Kent and Gabbard were "trying to do
their jobs"** on the murder case while Patel sought **sole point-person** control — a reported tension
between FBI case ownership and ODNI/NCTC foreign analysis.

## Criticism from investigators

Transcribed independent commentary argues Gabbard, on exiting the administration, produced social-media
disclosures that did not materially advance the Kirk case. That is **critic opinion**, included for balance.

## Key investigative questions

- What did ODNI record about the NCTC Kirk inquiry and its termination?
- What was the substance of Gabbard's office reply to Owens on the Macron allegation?
- Did ODNI task or receive [NSA](./nsa) phone-detection summaries?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

ODNI records of the NCTC inquiry, Macron-allegation correspondence, and NSA consolidation tasking are
among disclosures the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may compel.

## Tulsi Gabbard / DNI Claims on X

Alex Jones network posts (Oct 2025) assert that Gabbard’s ODNI was blocked by Patel from
investigating accomplices in the Kirk assassination, and that Gabbard’s office later confirmed
aspects of that account. @kangminlee ties the fight to Joe Kent’s foreign-assistance file review.

These are **high-profile political allegations** about investigative scope. They do not, by
themselves, prove foreign involvement or exonerate any charged defendant. Readers should seek
primary ODNI/FBI statements and congressional records.
