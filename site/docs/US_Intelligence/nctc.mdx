# NCTC — National Counterterrorism Center

The **National Counterterrorism Center (NCTC)** integrates terrorism-related intelligence across agencies.
Under director [Joe Kent](./joe-kent), it reportedly opened an inquiry into whether **foreign governments**
were behind Charlie Kirk's murder — then, per multiple accounts, was **stopped**.

## Chain of command

- **[Joe Kent](./joe-kent)** (Director) → **NCTC**
- **NCTC** → **[ODNI](./odni)** (Director of National Intelligence)
- **ODNI** → **President**

During the relevant window, [Tulsi Gabbard](./tulsi-gabbard) led ODNI.

## The shutdown allegations

Reporting summarized in `Charlie_Kirk.txt` and amplified on X (including
[@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO/status/1983328053519688164)) states:

1. **Joe Kent** was investigating foreign involvement in the Kirk murder.
2. **Kash Patel** "needed that shut down" and was **very mad** Kent **"went through FBI material"** on the case.
3. Commentary frames Patel as wanting to be the **sole point person** — raising why foreign-nexus work
   inside NCTC would be unwelcome.

These are **reported allegations** about living officials. Kash Patel has not been adjudicated to have
obstructed justice; we document what sources and commentators have stated.

## FBI blocking parallel tracks

The same master-file block notes:

- FBI allegedly told **local law enforcement they can't investigate** the case.
- That pattern sits beside the NCTC halt — multiple lanes reportedly closed simultaneously.

See [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [FBI Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads).

## Benny Johnson — "state actors" (on record)

In a video reshared by [@CatQuestionsAll](https://x.com/CatQuestionsAll/status/2071736376215920842)
(2026-06-29), **Benny Johnson** — describing himself as very close to Charlie's team — states:

> "There is some **considerable evidence that there were state actors involved** here. That's it. I
> don't want to go any further than that. I can't go any further than that."

The file notes Johnson reportedly met **Kash Patel and Pam Bondi** the **next day** over **death threats**.
This sits adjacent to the NCTC foreign-involvement shutdown in the same commentary ecosystem.

## The Butler connection (claim)

Investigative accounts claim the **NCTC believed the same group** behind the **Butler, Pennsylvania**
attempt on Donald Trump may have been involved in the Kirk case — citing similar **drones** and a
**"highly sophisticated"** signature. **Unverified**; included because it defines the foreign-terror
lane NCTC was built to examine.

## Russell Brand cabinet-source claim

**Russell Brand** stated that ~**six days after** Kirk's death, **two cabinet meeting sources** told him
**Kash Patel** entered with **Tulsi Gabbard**, the **Vice President**, and others and said they were
**"not looking at foreign involvement"** and **"not looking at any accomplices domestically."** Brand
presented this as bare-minimum cover-up evidence. See [Russell Brand cabinet claims](/Israel/Russell_Brand_Cabinet_Claims).
