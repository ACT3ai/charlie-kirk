# CIA

This page documents **claims and questions** about possible CIA involvement in public commentary
about the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination. The CIA has not been officially investigated in connection
with the killing. We do **not** assert as fact that the CIA ordered the assassination or that any
named employee participated in illegal acts.

## UVU National Security Society (claims)

Compiled investigation notes state that UVU's **National Security Society** online profiles were
**taken down on September 24, 2025** — two weeks after the shooting. Names listed in the master file
as associated with the society (a mix of claimed CIA-linked and campus-security figures) include the
names below. **Every entry is an unverified listing copied from investigation notes.** None of these
people is accused of anything here, membership in a campus society is not evidence of intelligence
employment, and a profile being removed from a website has many ordinary explanations.

| Name (as listed in CK) | Note in file (unverified) |

| [Rusty Needs](/People/rusty-needs) | Linked to **CNSSI** (CIA organization on campus, per claim) |

Expanded UVU context: [CIA UVU Profiles](/UVU/CIA-UVU-Profiles). UVU hosts a large regional
**Center for National Security Studies** program — a benign explanation for foreign-national
presence that [NSA phone claims](./nsa) also acknowledge.

## Utah State University enrollment claim

Commentary alleges **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** was briefly enrolled at **Utah State University**, described
in posts as a **CIA-affiliated research hub**. This is an **unverified** social-media claim used to
question gaps in Robinson's background; it is not confirmed by court filings on this site.

## HADES, N1098L, and military ISR

Public flight-tracking research ties the Army **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)** program to [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview),
a Bombardier Global 6500 operated by LASAI Aviation II LLC (Leidos subsidiary) with claimed SIGINT,
ELINT, and drone-deployment capabilities.

ADS-B data cited by investigators shows **two low-altitude passes** near the UVU/Orem corridor on
September 10, 2025 — roughly three hours before the shooting and again ~20–28 minutes after. Analysts
dispute whether these were drone deploy/retrieve missions or routine ISR training. See
[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and [ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

Defense intelligence overlap ([SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702), [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)) is covered on [Cabot alibi](./Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi) and
[Investigation Index](./US_Intelligence_Investigation_Index).

## Historical context — ARTICHOKE memo

Investigators cite republication of a **January 22, 1954 CIA Project ARTICHOKE** memo evaluating
whether a subject could be involuntarily induced to attempt assassination of a prominent official.
This is **historical document context** cited on X; it does not prove any 2025 operation.

## Rogue-faction theories

A prominent line of theory argues a **rogue faction** within the intelligence community — not a
formally sanctioned program — may share operational signatures with the Kirk case and the
**[Butler](/Drones/Butler_PA_Drone_Parallel)** attempt (see [NCTC](./nctc)). Elements include:

- Charlie's evolving criticism of intelligence overreach and foreign-policy consensus ([Charlie](/Charlie/overview))
- Coordinated optics: security posture, [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) timing, rapid [paving](/UVU/Pavers)
- Commentary that only **CIA or [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)**-scale services could kill and cover (investigator opinion, not proof)

Pattern-matching without classified discovery remains **hypothesis**.

## Living persons — high-risk allegations

Posts allege CIA links to **[Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler)** (palm-device commentary), **[Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird)** (staged-witness
claims), and security figures. These are **serious unproven allegations** against living people;
this site does not endorse them as fact. See [Mic](/Mic/overview) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).

## Key investigative questions

- What UVU National Security Society records existed before 9/24/2025 profile removals?
- What mission logs exist for N1098L on September 10, and who received ISR products?
- Has any congressional committee requested CIA posture on domestic political violence cases?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

N1098L mission crew and product recipients, UVU society membership records, and any domestic-operation
oversight files are among targets of the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview).

## Citizen Claims Involving CIA Equities (Attributed)

- Investigative notes tie **July 11, 2025** CIA executive-director changes
  (Dustin Gard-Weiss) and DoD intel undersecretary moves ([Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)) to flight/call-sign
  questions around Fort Huachuca — **chronology research**, not proof of operational involvement
  in the UVU killing.
- Online claims that CIA "ran the hit" are **unverified conspiracy assertions**; this site does
  not state them as fact.
- Legitimate open questions remain about which U.S. intelligence components touched the case
  after September 10 and what was scoped out.

See [Joe Kent / NCTC](./joe-kent), [ODNI / Tulsi](./tulsi-gabbard), [NSA](./nsa).
