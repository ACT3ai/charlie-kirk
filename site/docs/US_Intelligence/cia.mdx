# CIA

This page documents **claims and questions** about possible CIA involvement in public commentary
about the Charlie Kirk assassination. The CIA has not been officially investigated in connection
with the killing. We do **not** assert as fact that the CIA ordered the assassination or that any
named employee participated in illegal acts.

## UVU National Security Society (claims)

Compiled investigation notes state that UVU's **National Security Society** online profiles were
**taken down on September 24, 2025** — two weeks after the shooting. Names listed in the master file
as associated with the society (mix of claimed CIA-linked and campus-security figures) include:

| Name (as listed in CK) | Note in file |
|------------------------|--------------|
| Rusty Needs | Linked to **CNSSI** (CIA organization on campus, per claim) |
| Dr. Elena Vasquez | Profile removed with group |
| Mark Harlan | Profile removed |
| Sarah Klein | Profile removed |
| Jamal Reid | Profile removed |
| T. Ramirez | Profile removed |

Expanded UVU context: [CIA UVU Profiles](/UVU/CIA-UVU-Profiles). UVU hosts a large regional
**Center for National Security Studies** program — a benign explanation for foreign-national
presence that [NSA phone claims](./nsa) also acknowledge.

## Utah State University enrollment claim

Commentary alleges **Tyler Robinson** was briefly enrolled at **Utah State University**, described
in posts as a **CIA-affiliated research hub**. This is an **unverified** social-media claim used to
question gaps in Robinson's background; it is not confirmed by court filings on this site.

## HADES, N1098L, and military ISR

Public flight-tracking research ties the Army **HADES** program to [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview),
a Bombardier Global 6500 operated by LASAI Aviation II LLC (Leidos subsidiary) with claimed SIGINT,
ELINT, and drone-deployment capabilities.

ADS-B data cited by investigators shows **two low-altitude passes** near the UVU/Orem corridor on
September 10, 2025 — roughly three hours before the shooting and again ~20–28 minutes after. Analysts
dispute whether these were drone deploy/retrieve missions or routine ISR training. See
[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and [ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

Defense intelligence overlap (SAM-702, Fort Huachuca) is covered on [Cabot alibi](./Cabot_alibi) and
[Investigation Index](./US_Intelligence_Investigation_Index).

## Historical context — ARTICHOKE memo

Investigators cite republication of a **January 22, 1954 CIA Project ARTICHOKE** memo evaluating
whether a subject could be involuntarily induced to attempt assassination of a prominent official.
This is **historical document context** cited on X; it does not prove any 2025 operation.

## Rogue-faction theories

A prominent line of theory argues a **rogue faction** within the intelligence community — not a
formally sanctioned program — may share operational signatures with the Kirk case and the
**Butler** attempt (see [NCTC](./nctc)). Elements include:

- Charlie's evolving criticism of intelligence overreach and foreign-policy consensus ([Charlie](/Charlie/overview))
- Coordinated optics: security posture, [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) timing, rapid [paving](/UVU/Pavers)
- Commentary that only **CIA or Mossad**-scale services could kill and cover (investigator opinion, not proof)

Pattern-matching without classified discovery remains **hypothesis**.

## Living persons — high-risk allegations

Posts allege CIA links to **Rick Cutler** (palm-device commentary), **Skylar Baird** ("crisis actor"
pipeline), and security figures. These are **serious unproven allegations** against living people;
this site does not endorse them as fact. See [Mic](/Mic/overview) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
