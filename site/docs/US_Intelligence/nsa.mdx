# NSA

The National Security Agency (NSA) enters the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation for two reasons: it operates
the largest U.S. signals-collection facility **in the same state** as the assassination, and multiple
accounts place **foreign-registered phone detections** in the evidentiary chain that [Joe Kent](./joe-kent)
says he could not fully pursue.

## The Utah Data Center ("Bumblehive")

The NSA's Utah Data Center sits in **Bluffdale, Utah** — nicknamed **"Bumblehive"** — a short drive from
UVU. Investigator **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** ([@TonySeruga](https://x.com/TonySeruga) on X) describes **cross-pollination**:
merging call metadata, texts, location pings, app data, Wi-Fi associations, and device history so that
even a **burner phone**, after ~**30–60 days** of normal use, can be linked to its true owner and prior
devices with near-certainty (his claim, not an NSA public statement).

If accurate, any unattributed phones near UVU on September 10 are **identifiable in principle** — making
NSA custody of collection the pivotal disclosure question.

## The "12 Israeli cellphones" admin tip

A widely circulated **SCOOP** in the master file states:

> Got a tip … verified with someone inside the admin that the **NSA detected 12 Israeli registered
> cellphones** at the site of the shooting … The NSA picked the data up and handed it over to federal
> law enforcement officials who passed it along to the **WH**. Sources said those in the administration
> with this info are **"really freaked out."**

[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) is among those who amplified the count; the file
also notes the government **keeping secret** (`CK` item 32). This remains **unverified** without released
SIGINT summaries.

### Benign alternate explanations (acknowledged in source material)

The same SCOOP author listed non-malicious possibilities:

1. **UVU Center for National Security Studies** — largest regional national-security program; Israeli nationals may study there.
2. **Salt Lake City synagogues** (~30 miles north) — Israeli visitors or dual citizens in the area.

Benign presence does **not** disprove a foreign-nexus inquiry; it explains why investigators demand
**subscriber identities** rather than nationality alone.

## Tony Seruga — 44 foreign phones (claims)

Seruga's follow-up posts expand the dataset to **44 foreign-registered cellphones** allegedly in
Charlie's immediate vicinity on September 10:

| Nationality (claimed) | Count (claimed) |
|-----------------------|-----------------|
| Israeli | 16 |
| Chinese | 13 |
| Russian | 12 |
| Iranian | 3 |

Seruga's exact wording in the master file: *"Raw GPS data shows that 44 foreign-registered cellphones
were in the immediate area of Charlie Kirk on that sad day: 16 Israeli, 13 Chinese, 12 Russian, and 3
Iranian."* He further claims **4 of the 16 Israeli phones** later appeared at a **Pakistan hotel** during
high-level US–Israel–Iran peace talks — see [Islamabad Serena tracking](/Israel/Islamabad_Serena_Tracking).
Full Israel-angle breakdown: [NSA Cellphones & Bumblehive](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive).

## Link to the halted NCTC inquiry

If NSA passed foreign-phone detections to federal law enforcement and the White House, that data should
have fed the [NCTC](./nctc) **foreign-powers** thread [Joe Kent](./joe-kent) says was stopped. The
**custody and use** of those signals after receipt is a core [cover-up](/CoverUp/overview) question.

## Key investigative questions

- Did NSA capture cellular activity at/near UVU on September 10, 2025, and where are those records?
- Who in federal law enforcement and the White House received the 12-phone (or 44-phone) summaries?
- Has congressional oversight requested Bumblehive collection for that window?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

NSA SIGINT from the UVU window and subscriber identities behind Israeli and other foreign-registered
devices are among disclosures the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may compel.
