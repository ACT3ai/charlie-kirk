# Butch Hibbs & Crowd Drones

While [TPUSA Production Drone](./TPUSA_Production_Drone) threads focus on **official media B-roll**,
other posts highlight **Butch Hibbs** — described as a **Utah drone expert** (brother of Pastor **Jack Hibbs**)
— as a **white-bearded man trailing Charlie** in crowd footage, near an eyewitness image of someone
**holding drone equipment**. **Presence only** unless sourced to a primary interview.

## On-scene observations (citizen posts)

Compiled allegations from X research in the master file:

- **"Little old guy"** with white beard near Kirk's path,
- **Eyewitness photo** — man in crowd gripping what appears to be a **drone controller**,
- Hibbs described as **decades in Utah drone operations** — legal commercial work is plausible,
- Question raised: if TPUSA flew a **media drone**, why **deny** aerial assets while a **known operator**
  was on the ground?

**We do not claim** Hibbs operated an illegal or weaponized drone.

## Heber City — AES flight thread (post-event analysis)

**Candace Owens** (Jan 2026) connected **Heber City, Utah** — listed as **Hibbs's home area** — to a
reported **August 25, 2025** private flight **Heber City → Nashville, Tennessee**, near **Accurate Energetic
Systems (AES)**. That date aligns with a **DoD contract delivery** for **miniaturized XS demolition charges**
to AES (see [Mic](/Mic/overview) shaped-charge threads).

Speculative chain ( **unverified** ): flight carried **munitions prototypes** → **Sept 10** event uses
**mic/tent charge** rather than drone kill. Included here because **drone and explosive** theories share
**Hibbs** as a node — not because they are mutually exclusive.

## Frank Turek corroboration (non-Hibbs)

**Dr. Frank Turek** independently confirms **drone footage existed** before the event — separate from
identifying **who flew** it. [Crossexamined.org](https://crossexamined.org) podcast materials (Sept 2025)
discuss Kirk's final day in that context.
