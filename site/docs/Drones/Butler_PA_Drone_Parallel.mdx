# Butler PA Drone Parallel

Commentators draw a **pattern line** between **unexplained drone activity** at the **July 13, 2024**
**Butler, Pennsylvania** Trump rally shooting attempt and **drone-heavy theories** around **Charlie Kirk's
September 10, 2025** death. Mainstream outlets documented **airspace drone concerns** at Butler; UVU has
**no equivalent official drone bulletin** — only citizen claims and military-flight OSINT.

## Butler — documented drone concern

[Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/next-assassination-attempt-drone-pilot-secret-service-2024-8) and
other outlets reported **Secret Service** worries about **drone threats** to outdoor rallies after Butler.
**@BlakeBednarz** explicitly linked Butler's **"unexplained drone"** to his **"16 drones"** UVU post (see
[Military Drones on Video](./Military_Drones_On_Video)).

**Butler outcome:** Thomas Matthew Crooks named as shooter; **drone did not replace** the official rifle narrative
in court-facing accounts.

## NCTC "same group" allegation

Master-file notes from a reported **~45-minute call** summarize an **NCTC working theory** (third-hand in
this repo):

- **Rogue CIA faction** killed Kirk,
- **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** maneuvers + **@JG_CSTT** acoustic work are "too damning,"
- **Same sophisticated group** behind Butler — **"same drones, apparently,"**
- Modus operandi: **kill via drone (or advanced means), blame local patsy**, accept collateral damage.

**We have not independently verified** that NCTC holds this view. Treat as **investigator allegation** until
primary sourcing or FOIA emerges.

## Pattern vs. proof

| Element | Butler (2024) | UVU (2025) |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Official shooter story | Rooftop gunman | Rooftop gunman (Tyler Robinson charged) |
| Drone in public security discourse | Yes (threat framing) | Mostly citizen/OSINT |
| Army ISR jet low passes | Not central | N1098L central |
| Patsy + advanced means theory | Fringe on both events | Stronger in Kirk independent research |

Parallel **does not prove** shared perpetrators — only that **drone anxiety** now accompanies **political
outdoor shootings**.
