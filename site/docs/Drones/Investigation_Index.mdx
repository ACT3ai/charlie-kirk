# Drones — Investigation Index

Drone threads in the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case split into **three buckets**: (1) **military/government UAS**
tied to [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and Provo-week exercises, (2) **event-level camera drones**
([TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) production B-roll and crowd operators), and (3) **weaponized-drone theories** (Israeli quadcopter,
Switchblade, DARPA air-drop) that some citizen investigators propose as alternatives to the rooftop
**.30-06** story. **This site does not claim any person or agency used a drone to kill Charlie Kirk**
unless citing a court record or attributed allegation.
