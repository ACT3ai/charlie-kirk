# TPUSA Production Drone

Separate from **military ISR** claims, citizen investigators focus on a **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)-operated camera drone**
over the UVU crowd on **September 10, 2025**. The dispute is not whether **some** drone flew — TPUSA later
**admitted** a production flight — but whether leadership **misled** the public about drones while
**denying security** the same capability.

## The denial vs. the footage

[**Tyler Bowyer**](/People/tyler-bowyer) (TPUSA COO) and security figures including [**Brian Harpole**](/People/brian-harpole) publicly framed the event as
**no drones overhead** — university/campus policy allegedly blocked them. Then:

- **October 14, 2025** — TPUSA posted a **Charlie birthday tribute** video (his 32nd) with **overhead drone
  B-roll** from the Sept 10 event, including wide crowd shots **roughly an hour before** the shooting.
- **Dr. [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** (event speaker) reportedly told commentators he and Charlie **reviewed drone footage
  en route** and he warned about **too many buildings** for sniper exposure; Charlie proceeded.

A later **cease-and-desist** letter from TPUSA counsel to citizen investigator **[@FinanceWolves](/Influencers/x/financewolves)** (Zach)
**admitted**, per research notes: *"TPUSA media production drone briefly flew for B-roll BEFORE the event"*
— while **security was denied** drone overwatch. [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) (Jan 2026) highlighted that split as
**contradiction central**: media got aerial views; protective detail did not.

[**Andrew Kolvet**](/People/andrew-kolvet) offered an alternate explanation (wide-angle from a bridge, not drone). **We present
all accounts; we do not adjudicate which camera source each clip used.**

## Why investigators care

A production drone is **not proof** of a weaponized kill. It **is** evidence about:

- **What angles existed** that may never have been released to the public or defense,
- **Whether TPUSA statements** about "no drones" were **literally false**,
- **Security policy failures** — if aerial overwatch was available to media but blocked for protection.

Utah generally restricts drone flight over crowds without permits; commentators allege **illegal hover**
regardless of operator intent.

## Timestamp threads

Some posts sync drone-path analysis to **~12:07 PM** parking-lot cameras and FBI-released clips (same
vehicles, per claimants). **Independent verification** of timestamp alignment is ongoing.
