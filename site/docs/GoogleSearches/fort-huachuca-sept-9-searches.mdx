This page records the **original exposure claim** — the July 14, 2026 post whose data reportedly
["vanished" two days later](./fort-huachuca-image-searches). On **July 14, 2026**,
**[Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** claimed that **Google image-search data** showed
**repeated geo-specific spikes** for figures tied to **[Fort
Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9)** — the U.S. Army's intelligence and
counterintelligence base in Arizona — in the **weeks and days before September 9, 2025**, the day the
**[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** VIP flight arrived there and the day before [Charlie
Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at UVU.

This page records **an allegation about search-pattern data**. It makes **no claim about any named
individual's conduct**, and being the subject of someone else's searches is **not evidence of wrongdoing**.

## What the post says

The quoted post by [@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters), verbatim:

> "Fort Huachuca image search data shows repeated geo-specific spikes for top **[DIA](./dia-search-patterns)**,
> **[CENTCOM](./centcom-search-patterns)**, **[Crane](./crane-search-patterns)** weapons/tech & Kirk
> security/comms figures in the weeks/days before Sept 9.
>
> These are the exact people who'd need escort, ID & coordination at America's premier Army intel base."

**[@AmericaOnly76](https://x.com/AmericaOnly76/status/2079684519762993659)**, quoting the video on July 21, 2026,
added: "We clearly understand what happened to Charlie Kirk but they will never arrest themselves." The quote was
posted in a reply thread about the separate claim that [Tyler Robinson](/Killer/overview) was in **Panguitch, UT**
from 8:55–9:47pm on Sept 10, 2025.

## Why Sept 9 matters to the claim

The date anchors the claim to two threads already documented on this site:

- **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** — the government VIP flight into [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) on **Sept 9, 2025**, reportedly
  carrying senior intelligence figures, departing early Sept 9 with roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels reported
  on base Sept 8 ([Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9)).
- The **escort/ID/coordination argument** — Peters' framing is that the categories of people allegedly being
  searched (DIA, CENTCOM, Crane weapons/tech, and Kirk security/comms figures) are "the exact people who'd
  need escort, ID & coordination" for a visit to an Army intelligence base.

That framing is **an inference by the claimant**, not evidence: search interest in intelligence figures near a
publicized base visit can equally reflect news coverage, prior viral posts, or the researchers' own repeated queries.

## Methodology limits

The same limits documented across the [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview) section apply here:

- **Aggregate search data cannot identify who searched or why** — geography buckets are not people,
- **No archived before-state** of the claimed spikes has been published for third-party inspection,
- The claim comes from **the interested party's own broadcast**, without the exact query strings or date windows,
- Two days after this post, Peters claimed the underlying results [were wiped](./fort-huachuca-image-searches) —
  which means the claim now rests on **retained screenshots** that have not been independently examined.

**Being searched for is not an accusation.** Every category of person referenced (DIA, CENTCOM, Crane, and Kirk
security/comms staff) is recorded here because of a claim about **search behavior by unknown third parties** —
nothing on this page suggests any of them did anything improper.

## Transcript — key statements

The 32-minute video (automatic transcription; several names garbled, spellings unverified) is an interview with an
unnamed researcher who says she pulled **Google Trends image-search data for Sierra Vista, AZ** — the town serving
Fort Huachuca, which she describes as roughly 45,000 people, about 80% service members and families.

**The method claim.** The guest — a former Army OPSEC instructor by her own account — argues that **junior enlisted
soldiers routinely image-search VIPs** before picking them up at the airport and escorting them onto post. In her
words the searches are *"an appropriate action on the part of the service member"* — the claimed anomaly is **who**
was being searched from that geography and **when**, not the act of searching:

> "They were looking for images of individuals, with specific roles, using common military jargon. It's a very
> specific Google search... It's much more likely that a military person, most likely an escort, was conducting
> that search so that they'd be able to visually identify the person that they were charged with going to pick up."

**The claimed timeline.** According to the video, image searches from the Sierra Vista geography clustered as
follows (all reported claims, none independently verified): **mid-July** — Johnny Moore; **August** —
[CENTCOM](./centcom-search-patterns) and [Crane](./crane-search-patterns) figures including the Crane commander and
research director; **mid-to-late August** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s communications and
[security](/Security_Team/overview) staff, including [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) (Aug 18–19, 27–28);
**late August** — CENTCOM's commander and [DIA](./dia-search-patterns) staff; **September 6–9** — the DIA director
("multiple massive spikes"), CENTCOM's deputy commander, and on **September 8–9** the Integrity Security driver who
reportedly took the vehicle carrying Kirk's body to Timpanogos Regional Hospital.

The video also repeats claims documented elsewhere on this site: the [Crane](./crane-search-patterns) extra-small
shape-charge contract (May 25 – Aug 25, 2025), the **Accurate Energetic Systems explosion** of Oct 10, 2025, and the
**[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** flight of Sept 9 with Undersecretary [Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell) reported aboard.

**What the video does not establish.** Peters' closing monologue presents the pattern as proof of a coordinated
operation; that is **his characterization**, contested and unproven. The innocent explanation is contained in the
same video: routine escort lookups for a legitimately scheduled — even if undisclosed — VIP visit to a major Army
intelligence base. Every person named is either a serving official or a private citizen against whom this page makes
**no allegation**; [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) in particular has publicly denied the speculation
circulating about him and has filed a defamation suit over related claims.
