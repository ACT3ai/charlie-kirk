Search-pattern work exploded after Sept 10 because **aggregate Trends geographies** appeared to front-run names that
only later mattered in court — **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** (Dec 2024 Israel band), **[Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)’s St. George address** (Dec
2024–Sept 2025), **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** (Aug 2025 Israel), **[judges](./judges-graf-lunnen-searches)**
with **zero Utah pre-search** in one viral post. Commentators anchor many July–August spikes on **[July 20,
2025](./july-20-2025-anchor)** — the same week as **AMFEST/Tucker**, **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** Omaha routing, and
**Timpanogos surgeon** query frenzies. **DC metro** claims emphasize **[Timpanogos](./timpanogos-medical-searches)**
(June–July + **Sept 9** day-before), **[Beau Mason](./fbi-dps-law-enforcement-searches)** (July 16), and **Tyler
Robinson** (July 20 blip).

**Virginia** rarely appears as its own Trends bucket; **Northern Virginia** enters mainly via **DC hospital/doctor**
speculation ([Medical — DC phone call](/Medical/doctor_dc_phone_call)). The stronger anomaly — if screenshots hold — is
**foreign-geography interest with absent local Utah interest** on judges and medical staff. **[Deletion
claims](./trends-deletion-suppression)** allege Terms vanish after virality — archive screenshots early. Disclosure
laws may force **subscriber identities** behind geographic buckets.

## What Citizen Investigators Emphasize on X

The Google Searches section is defined by a fight over **method**: Baron Coleman-class investigators publish pre-event Trends screenshots (hospital, judges, planes, Israel geos); critics (**[@paramounttactcl](/Influencers/x/paramounttactcl)**, Tim Pool-class, **[@MaxNordau](/Influencers/x/maxnordau)**) say Trends cannot identify searchers and is statistical noise. This overview keeps both sides and routes to topic pages. Nothing here asserts a foreign state ran pre-event Google queries as operational planning. Worth noting: former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has cited online pre-knowledge of the assassination as one reason he says "more than likely there's something else at play" — and says his center's parallel foreign-ties review was halted before such leads were exhausted.

## The Fort Huachuca image-search cluster (July 2026)

A newer thread shifts from **Trends** to **image search**. On **July 14, 2026**, **[Stew
Peters](https://x.com/realstewpeters)** aired the original claim that Sierra Vista/Fort Huachuca geography showed
**[pre-Sept 9 image-search spikes](./fort-huachuca-sept-9-searches)** for DIA, CENTCOM, Crane, and Kirk
security/comms figures. On **July 16, 2026**, he claimed that image results for people his show named around **[Fort
Huachuca](./fort-huachuca-image-searches)** were **"completely wiped"** the next day — grouping them under
**[DIA](./dia-search-patterns)**, **[CENTCOM](./centcom-search-patterns)**, **[Crane](./crane-search-patterns)**, and
Kirk security figures — while unnamed people's results persisted. The **named-vs-unnamed contrast** is the only
control offered; there is **no archived before-state**, and **removal requests by private individuals** explain the
same appearance. These four pages record the claim and its limits; none of them allege wrongdoing by any named person.
