**Crane** is the third institution **[Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** named on **July 16, 2026** in his
claim that **Google image search results were "completely wiped"** for figures discussed alongside **[Fort
Huachuca](./fort-huachuca-image-searches)** — *"DIA, CENTCOM, Crane & Kirk security figures — all gone."*

The post does **not** define "Crane." In context, the near-certain referent is **NSWC Crane**, the Navy facility
already threaded through this investigation via a **munitions contract**.

## What NSWC Crane is

**Naval Surface Warfare Center, Crane Division** (Crane, Indiana) is a Navy warfare center specializing in
**electronic warfare, strategic missions, and expeditionary systems** — including **energetics and ordnance**
procurement. It is a **routine defense contracting agency** that awards thousands of ordinary contracts.

## Why Crane appears in this case

Crane's presence in the investigation comes entirely from a **federal contract record** in the master file, cited in
support of the **[exploding-mic theory](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic)** — the contention that Charlie Kirk's
microphone, not or in addition to a rifle round, caused the neck wound:

- **Award ID:** N0016425PJ538
- **Awarding agency:** Department of the Navy — **NSWC Crane**
- **Recipient:** **[Accurate Energetic Systems, LLC](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion)** (AES), McEwen, Tennessee
- **Requirement:** *"miniaturized-XS demolition charges and demolition charges, anti personnel-XS"*
- **Amount:** $440,494 · **Period:** May 1 – Aug 25, 2025 · **Delivery:** Aug 25, 2025
- **Codes:** NAICS 325920 (Explosives Manufacturing) · PSC 1375 (Demolition Materials)

Investigators emphasize the **delivery date — Aug 25, 2025**, sixteen days before the assassination — and the phrase
**"miniaturized"** as consistent with a small shaped charge.

**The counterweight is decisive and should be read first.** A **publicly posted, openly searchable, competitively
awarded contract** is close to the **worst possible artifact** of a covert operation. Miniaturized demolition charges
are **standard military inventory** with many legitimate uses, AES is an **established explosives manufacturer**, and
a **$440k** award is **routine**. Nothing in the record connects this contract to Utah, to UVU, or to Sept 10. The link
is **thematic adjacency**, not evidence.

## What the search claim adds

**Nothing evidentiary.** Peters alleges only that image results for Crane-adjacent names **stopped appearing** after
his broadcast, and that unnamed people's results did not. As on the [[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)
page](./fort-huachuca-image-searches), that observation was made **once**, with **no archived before-state**, and is
fully explained by **personalization, query drift, re-ranking, or ordinary removal requests**.

It is worth stating plainly: **a contract record and an image search are different things**. The contract is **public
and still verifiable today** at the award ID above — which itself cuts against the suppression narrative, since the
**actual documentary evidence** in this thread has **not** disappeared.

## What would move this

- **Chain-of-custody** for the AES delivery — where the Aug 25 units went,
- **Before/after archives** with exact query strings from a neutral session,
- **FOIA** to NSWC Crane on N0016425PJ538 disposition,
- Forensic support for the [mic theory](/Mic/overview) independent of contract adjacency.
