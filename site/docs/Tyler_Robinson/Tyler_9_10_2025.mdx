This page examines Tyler Robinson's claimed movements on the day of the Charlie Kirk assassination, **September 10, 2025**, at Utah Valley University. The timeline raises questions about whether Robinson could have physically carried out the shooting and reached the locations shown on surveillance footage in the time documented.

## Shooting Position to Losee Center: 25 Seconds

According to [@GrayHuze (Gray Hughes Investigates)](https://x.com/GrayHuze/status/2044131420956963292), surveillance camera footage shows movement from the shooting position to the **North East corner of the Losee Center building in 25 seconds**.

The surveillance stills show four timestamps:
- **12:23:29** — A figure near the event area at ground level
- **12:23:34** — Rooftop/elevated camera showing movement with a traced path (red arrows)
- **12:23:45** — Aerial surveillance view of the Losee Center building corner
- **12:23:54** — Building-level camera showing arrival at the NE corner of Losee Center

This 25-second window is a key data point in evaluating whether the claimed shooter could have traversed from the alleged shooting position to the documented location on the Losee Center building.

## Law Enforcement's Detailed Rooftop Timeline (as reported)

A minute-by-minute sequence compiled in Charlie_Kirk.txt from law-enforcement references and surveillance descriptions traces the movements attributed to the suspect. Each entry below is a **reported observation**, not an established fact against Robinson:

- **8:29 a.m. MT** — Arrived on the UVU campus driving a **gray Dodge Challenger**, reportedly wearing a plain **maroon T-shirt, light-colored shorts, light-colored shoes, and a black hat with a white logo**. No backpack noted.
- **11:49 a.m. MT** — Seen in a nearby neighborhood; a **clothing change** is reported to a **black long-sleeve shirt with a U.S. flag and eagle**, dark jeans, a dark hat, large sunglasses, and a **dark-colored backpack**.
- **11:50 a.m. MT** — Walking north of Campus Drive near 800 West, reportedly with a **limp (stiff right leg, slow pace)**.
- **11:52 a.m. MT** — Tracked through stairwells up to the roof of the **Losee Center** (about **150 yards** from the courtyard).
- **11:53 a.m. MT** — Paused at the top of the stairs, reportedly pulled out a cell phone, then descended into a pedestrian tunnel and up a parking-garage stairwell.
- **12:02 p.m. MT** — Walking on the north side of the Losee Center, still limping; entered through southeast-corner doors.
- **12:15 p.m. MT** — Ascended to the roof, crossed a railing from a public walkway, ran across the roof, and reportedly crawled into a shooting position.
- **12:17 p.m. MT** — Climbed over a short wall and crouched on the north side of the roof.
- **12:22 p.m. MT** — Stood, ran west near the edge (the limp "no longer noticeable"), and lay down facing the courtyard, reportedly about **430 feet (130 m)** away.
- **12:23 p.m. MT** — A single shot is reported fired from the roof; **12:23:30** is cited as the exact moment of the shot to Kirk's neck.
- **12:24 p.m. MT** — Reportedly placed a **dark-colored item (a rifle wrapped in a dark towel)** on the roof, lowered onto the grass, and ran north into a wooded area. A **Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle with scope** was later recovered there; a shoe impression was described as consistent with **Converse / Chuck Taylor** sneakers.

The "limp no longer noticeable" detail and the reported change **back** into the morning maroon shirt and cap after the shooting are two of the points citizen investigators cite when questioning whether one person made all these movements (see [Stairs Guy vs Tyler Robinson](./Stairs_Guy)).

## The Runner "Started Before the Shot" Claim

Charlie_Kirk.txt records an analysis arguing the rooftop figure began running **before** Kirk was struck, which the author frames as making the attributed shot "impossible":

- The claim states **Utah County DA Jeffrey Gray** and UVU are "hiding the rooftop-view video files," and that the released clip was "trimmed" to **start at 12:23:34**, when the runner was already about **60 feet from the perch position**.
- Using a measured **220 feet in 15 seconds**, the analysis back-calculates that the runner "began all-out run at **12:23:28**," while "Charlie was hit at **12:23:30**" — i.e., the figure was reportedly already fleeing at the moment of the shot.

This is a **reported reconstruction** by an online investigator, not a court finding; it is offered as an argument for releasing the full, untrimmed rooftop footage.

## The 8:07 AM "Same Clothes" Photo (claim)

A separate claim in the master file, attributed to a bystander photographer and TMZ footage, holds that Robinson was photographed at **8:07 a.m.** in a **"plum" (maroon) shirt, dark shorts, and a black hat with a triangle logo, carrying a backpack** — and was allegedly seen in the **same clothes** shortly after, which the source uses to argue "**NO WAY he could have been on the ROOF**" in the dark outfit. This is presented as a reported eyewitness claim requiring the underlying HD images to verify.

## Day-of Timeline (Compiled from Multiple Sources)

Based on publicly discussed doorbell cameras, surveillance footage, and law enforcement references (see [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview) for the breakdown by device — [Ring](/cameras/Ring), [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE), [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) — and [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras) for the full sighting list):

- **8:07 AM** — Walking on S 800 W toward campus (Ring camera)
- **8:23 AM** — Driving on W 880 S (WYZE camera)
- **8:29 AM** — Driving on campus (UVU surveillance)
- **9:57 AM** — Walking on W 880 S (WYZE camera)
- Outfit change reportedly occurred thereafter (to jeans and long sleeve shirt)
- **11:44 AM** — Walking on W 800 S (Ring camera)
- **11:49 AM** — Walking on S 800 W toward campus (Ring camera)
- **12:23 PM** — Time of shot (some references cite 12:27 PM; first radio report cited as ~12:26 PM)
- **12:23:29–12:23:54** — 25-second movement from shooting position to NE corner of Losee Center (per @GrayHuze analysis)
- **~6:38 PM** — Claimed sighting at a Dairy Queen in Orem (unverified)

## Key Questions About the 25-Second Window

- Is 25 seconds physically sufficient to traverse from the alleged rooftop shooting position to the NE corner of Losee Center?
- What is the distance and route between these two points?
- Does the figure in the surveillance stills match Robinson's described clothing at this time?
- Were there any obstructions, doors, or elevation changes (stairs, ladders) along this path that would affect transit time?
- Has any independent analysis confirmed or disputed the 25-second claim?
