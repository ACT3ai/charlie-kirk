# Rick Cutler — Arm Puller & Camera Crew

**Rick Cutler** is a **named [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)/event crew figure** who appears in post-shooting video analysis as the
**"brown shirt / arm puller"** who reportedly adjusted equipment near [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) — including taking
down a camera behind the stage. Citizen threads ask whether that role was **technical support** or
**operational distraction**. This page documents **presence and attributed speculation only**. We do
**not** claim Cutler knew of the assassination, triggered a device, or committed any crime.

## What footage threads describe (not adjudicated)

CK_FILE and [Mic — SUV transport](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie) research note:

- **Brown-shirt figure** labeled Cutler in annotated stills — **arm puller** / sunglasses in crowd clips
- Reportedly **pulled down or adjusted camera** behind Charlie before or around the shot window
- **SUV interior** seating chart includes Cutler among TPUSA staff transporting Kirk ([cropped public image with disclaimer on Mic page](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie))

## Pero "gesture control" speculation

[@RealTruthJustic](https://x.com/RealTruthJustic/status/2070597252133818558) (June 2026) compared Cutler's
hand to a **Pero** wearable smart-mouse **gesture trigger** device. That is **frame interpretation** —
not a product ID from law enforcement.

A separate [@DanCampbel44604](https://x.com) image added **severe accusations** (shape charge, strangulation).
CK_FILE records that text as **unverified** and **not published** on the public Mic page. **We do not repeat those accusations here.**

## Background notes (public record / posts)

- CK mentions Cutler **sued [Tim Ballard](/People/tim-ballard)** (Operation Underground Railroad) in **2023** — civil docket reference in research file
- Appears in **Google Trends** political-search clusters (July 2025) alongside other Utah figures in CK notes — unverified significance

## Why he is in Distraction People (not People/Killer)

Cutler is listed here because commentators slot **event insiders** who control **cameras and stage access**
into distraction/insider-op theories — separate from rooftop Tyler. **No charge** ties him to the shooting.

## Status

**Status:** Alive (per public commentary). **Not accused** by this site of assassination involvement.

## Defamation-safe note on extreme X accusations

Some X posts name **Rick Cutler** together with other TPUSA/security figures and assert collective responsibility for Charlie Kirk's death (example pattern: lists including Neff, Kolvet, Turek, Harpole, Cutler, Erika Kirk). **Those accusations are unverified public claims.** This site does **not** adopt them as findings. Cutler remains documented here for **camera-handling / proximity threads** already on the page and in [Crime-Scene Camera Actions](/cameras/Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions). Living-person criminal guilt requires court proof.
