# Black Clothing Figure — Unidentified

Citizen research describes an **unidentified person** in **all-black clothing** — trench coat, mask,
cargo pants, small backpack — seen near the UVU event and, in some threads, in a **prone position on a
roof** roughly an hour before the shooting. Commentary contrasts this silhouette with the later
**Tyler Robinson in T-shirt and jeans** packaging. **No reliable public identification** is established here.

## Description in research record

CK and [Proof Not Tyler — Witness Conflicts](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Witness_Audio_Conflicts) compile:

- **Black sunglasses, C-mask, trench coat, cargo pants, small backpack**
- **All black, blood on hands** in one crowd clip description — **not forensically verified**
- Ran **left across Charlie's level**, jumped down, stayed low while others hovered (CK note)
- **Prone black figure** on roof **~1 hour before** hit — then absent from narrative once Tyler frame set (allegation in posts)

## Stairs footage comparison (careful)

Some social posts compare **stairs/roof surveillance** to Tyler and speculate about **other matches**.
**Tyler's father** was reportedly quoted saying stairs footage **did not look like Tyler** (CK posts).
**This site does not name** any alternative individual as the shooter — comparisons are **unverified**
and defamation-risky.

See [Stairs Guy gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) and [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) if published.

## Construction crew photo mismatch

**Mr. Hope** reportedly said an FBI-shown photo **did not match** a person workers encountered —
overlaps [Gun Discovery](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence) thread.

## Status

**Status:** **Unidentified** on this site. No living person named; **no accusations**.
