# David Sprague

"David Sprague" is **one of several names that online citizen researchers have
proposed** for the [curly-haired man](./Curly_Hair_Man) seen in Utah Valley
University crowd footage on September 10, 2025. This page exists because those
names circulate publicly and readers search for them — **not** because any
identification has been confirmed and **not** to accuse anyone of anything.

:::caution Unverified identification
The link between this name and the man in the footage rests entirely on
**crowd-sourced facial matching and public-records guessing**. No reliable,
on-the-record source has confirmed it. Being present in a crowd is **not**
evidence of involvement in any crime. Nothing on this page should be read as a
finding of wrongdoing against any living person.
:::

## The "several names" claim

Citizen researchers have suggested the same individual may be associated with
more than one name. The names that circulate are:

* [Matt Taber](./Matt_Taber) — an early guess later set aside by online researchers
* [James Norman Rawlinson](./James_Norman_Rawlinson) — floated as a possible birth name
* **David Sprague** — described online as a name he reportedly uses now
  (reportedly a mother's maiden name)

These cross-connections are **allegations made by anonymous online accounts**, not
established fact. This site does not vouch for any of them.

## Publicly-searchable records cited online

Online commentators point to ordinary public records. None of these records
indicate any wrongdoing; they are listed only because they are repeatedly cited:

* A business reportedly operated under the name **Joseph Hasel LLC, DBA "Salon 21"**.
* That business reportedly received roughly **$115,200 in federal COVID-era loans** —
  a program used by hundreds of thousands of small businesses.
* A profile for a stylist doing locs at a Provo, Utah salon ("Dreadknotilus") under
  a similar name.

Running a salon and receiving a pandemic small-business loan are **normal, lawful
activities**. They are noted here only to document what online researchers have
pointed to, not to imply anything improper.

## What the footage actually shows

The underlying observation is limited: a curly-haired man in the crowd was
described as **turning toward the direction of the shot** and **standing when
others did not**. Commentators interpreted his demeanor in various ways. Those are
**subjective readings of short video loops**, not findings — a startle-and-stand
response is common and looks different in isolated clips than in full context.

## Open questions

1. Has any reliable, on-the-record source actually identified this man?
2. Do the circulated names refer to a real single person, or to mismatched records?
3. Does complete, time-synced video place his movements in ordinary context?

## Status

**Status:** Alive (private individual). No individual named on this page is
accused of any crime or of any role in the events of September 10, 2025.
