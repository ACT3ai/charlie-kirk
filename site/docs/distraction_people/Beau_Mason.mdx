# Beau Mason — DPS Commissioner

**Beau Mason** became **Utah Department of Public Safety Commissioner** in **mid-2025** (appointed by
Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox), replacing **Jess Anderson**). CK notes variously date the appointment to **June 2025**
and **July 17, 2025** — weeks before the assassination. He is the **on-record official** who
**cleared [George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** as the shooter the same day — making Mason the bridge between **documented
distraction** (Zinn) and the **official Tyler narrative**.

## Public statements tied to distraction thread

| Statement (reported) | Distraction relevance |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| Zinn **cleared as shooter** same day | Confirms first arrest was **wrong person** by design (per Zinn's admission to police) |
| **"High powered bolt action rifle"** recovered in wooded area | Locks weapon class early |
| **Enhanced photos** of suspected shooter released | Feeds [stairs guy vs Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) debates |

Sources: KSL/ABC4 reporting on Zinn; CK_FILE DPS press summaries.

## Why commentators scrutinize Mason

- **New appointee** weeks before assassination — timing noted in CK **Google Trends** threads (Washington DC searches around July 16, 2025)
- Named on a CK **"officials who knew"** list arguing at least a dozen officials — including the DPS
  commissioner — never acknowledged that [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) was reportedly in custody **~3.5 hours before**
  the official arrest time and **~90 minutes before** the alleged Discord messages posted. This is a
  commentator's timeline argument, not a proven claim against Mason personally.
- Mason is listed in CK **medical/hospital** disclosure wish-lists alongside Timpanogos figures — overlap only, not allegation of wrongdoing
- **Enhanced** imagery release while raw UVU feeds stay restricted fuels **selective transparency** claims ([FBI](/FBI/overview))

We do **not** claim Mason obstructed justice or knew of the killing in advance.
