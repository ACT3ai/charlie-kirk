# Matt Taber

"Matt Taber" was **an early name floated by online citizen researchers** for the
[curly-haired man](./Curly_Hair_Man) seen in Utah Valley University crowd footage
on September 10, 2025. Researchers later leaned toward other names. It is
documented here only because the guess circulated publicly.

:::caution Unverified identification
This was an **anonymous online guess**, not a confirmed identification, and online
researchers themselves moved away from it. Presence in a crowd is not evidence of
involvement in any crime. Nothing here is a finding of wrongdoing against any
living person.
:::

## The "several names" claim

The same individual has been linked online — all unverified — to more than one name:

* **Matt Taber** — an early guess, later set aside
* [James Norman Rawlinson](./James_Norman_Rawlinson) — floated as a possible birth name
* [David Sprague](./David_Sprague) — a name he reportedly uses now (see that page
  for the fuller record of what researchers cite)

These connections are **unverified allegations by anonymous online accounts**. This
site does not confirm any of them.

## Status

**Status:** Alive (private individual). No individual named on this page is accused
of any crime or of any role in the events of September 10, 2025.

## Sources
