# Possible Distraction People

Sophisticated operations may use **side figures** to pull police, cameras, and scanners toward false
leads while the real exit happens elsewhere. Start with the [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index)
and [Distraction Timeline](./Distraction_Timeline). **Most people here were cleared, released, or never
identified** — this site does **not** accuse them of the assassination.

This does not say that these people knew the assassination would happen or that they necessarily worked
to do anything. The word "distractions" just means law enforcement were tied up their time pursuing these.
We also point them out because they have interesting aspects to them that people often find worthy to
learn about.

The only **documented on-record distraction admission** in this section is **[George Zinn](./George_Zinn)** —
police say he shouted that he shot [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) to **draw attention from the real shooter** and cleared him
as the gunman within hours. That single fact justifies treating "distraction people" as a real phenomenon,
not only a conspiracy label. A coordinated multi-actor reading, if ever documented, would also bear on the
foreign-nexus review former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his team was ordered
to abandon once a suspect was in custody. **[Zachariah Qureshi](./Zachariah_Qureshi)** sits in the **early-custody**
bucket: detained and released, never charged as shooter, central to **[Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) "in custody"** timing debates
([FBI page](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi)).

Unidentified threads — **[pellet gun](./Pellet_Gun_Man)**, **[SMBB break man](./SMBB_Break_Man)**, **[curly hair](./Curly_Hair_Man)**,
**[USA chant](./USA_Chant_Man)**, **[black clothing](./Black_Clothing_Figure)** — rest on
scanner audio, single posts, or short clips. **[Kennington](./Russell_Kennington)** is disputed: booking screenshots vs
**GRAMA "no arrest."**

Prioritize **[Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index)** → **[Zinn](./George_Zinn)** + **[Timeline](./Distraction_Timeline)** for
documented facts, then **[Qureshi](./Zachariah_Qureshi)** and **[Kennington](./Russell_Kennington)** for false-lead timing, then
thin-ID pages only after you understand defamation limits. Companion: [CoverUp distraction list](/CoverUp/Distraction_People),
[Killer patsies](/Killer/Patsies_Distraction_Actors), [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview). Disclosure targets are in
[Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) **P15 — distraction persons**.
