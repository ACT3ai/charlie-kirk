# Hunter Kozak — Last Question

**Hunter Kozak** reportedly asked **the last audience question** before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot at UVU on
September 10, 2025. That timing places him in every **timeline** of the event — and in citizen threads
that ask whether questioners or reactions were **scripted distractions**. This page states **documented
role + attributed speculation** only. Kozak is **not accused** here of any crime or foreknowledge.

## Documented proximity

- Identified in public commentary as the student who **questioned Kirk moments before** the fatal injury
- Listed among **five people** in the SUV immediately after the shooting in research compilations (with event staff names)

## Citizen-thread claims (unverified)

Public commentary alleges:

- Video shows Kozak **"practicing being surprised"** before the event — **not independently verified** by this site
- Active in **"Unf#ck America"** tour — described as counter-narrative to TPUSA
- Father **[Steve Kozak](/People/steve-kozak)** reportedly is a **director at the Jimmy Kimmel show** and has
  "worked for Jimmy Kimmel for years," per biographical posts
- **Credentials reportedly revoked.** Commentary claims Kozak and his "Un-Eff America"
  affiliates had their **credentials revoked** to enter Charlie Kirk events — raising
  the poster's question of "who let him in line" to ask the final question

Commentators use these points to suggest **crisis-actor** or **narrative plant** theories. **No official source** supports that framing.

## The Sept 12, 2025 Channel 5 interview (attributed)

A widely shared clip is a **September 12, 2025 Channel 5 interview** in which Kozak
spoke with **Andrew Callaghan** (a streamer whom the poster ties to Destiny's
"UnF*ck America" tour and describes as "the son of a Jimmy Kimmel exec"). In the
clip, commentary alleges Kozak said words to the effect of "for some reason this
makes people think I was in on it... that I was a **MIC**," then added he did not
know what "MIC" meant and had "just learned about it two hours ago." Posters read
this as a slip referencing a shadowy intelligence "cell"; there is **no official
basis** for that interpretation, and an ordinary misspeak is at least as likely.
This is documented only because the clip circulates widely — not as evidence of
anything.

## Why distraction theorists care

Last-question positioning can:

- Fix cameras and audience attention on the **microphone line** (relevant to [Mic theories](/Mic/overview))
- Create a **memorable witness** for media packages after the killing
- Supply a **liberal student** archetype in early storyline (interpretation only)

None of that proves coordination.

## Status

**Status:** Alive (per public posts). **Not charged** in the shooting.

This website makes no claim that Hunter Kozak committed any crime, did anything wrong or illegal, or had any knowledge of or involvement in the assassination. Kozak's name appears here only in the context of public discussion. Nothing here is a finding by this website.

## Sources

- Public commentary and reporting on Kozak's last-question timing,
  the "practicing being surprised" clip, the Steve Kozak / Jimmy Kimmel biographical
  claim, the revoked-credentials claim, and the Sept 12, 2025 Channel 5 / Andrew
  Callaghan interview. All are attributed citizen commentary; no official source
  corroborates the crisis-actor reading.

## Public commentary

Kozak's name continues to appear in citizen **distraction / early-suspect** discussions of the UVU event. No court has adjudicated any criminal guilt related to the Kirk homicide, and "he was part of the hit" language circulating on X remains **unverified allegation**. Related context appears in the [Distraction Overview](/distraction_people/overview) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) sections.
