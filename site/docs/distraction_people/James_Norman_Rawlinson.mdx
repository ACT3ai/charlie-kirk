# James Norman Rawlinson

"James Norman Rawlinson" is **one of several names online citizen researchers have
floated** as a possible birth name for the [curly-haired man](./Curly_Hair_Man)
seen in Utah Valley University crowd footage on September 10, 2025. It is
documented here because the name circulates publicly — **not** because any
identification has been confirmed.

:::caution Unverified identification
This name-to-person link rests on **anonymous crowd-sourced research**, not a
reliable on-the-record source. Presence in a crowd is not evidence of involvement
in any crime. Nothing here is a finding of wrongdoing against any living person.
:::

## The "several names" claim

Online accounts have suggested the same individual may go by more than one name:

* [Matt Taber](./Matt_Taber) — an earlier guess
* **James Norman Rawlinson** — floated as a possible birth name
* [David Sprague](./David_Sprague) — a name he reportedly uses now (see that page
  for the fuller record of what researchers cite)

All three connections are **unverified allegations by anonymous online accounts**.
This site does not confirm any of them.

## Status

**Status:** Alive (private individual). No individual named on this page is accused
of any crime or of any role in the events of September 10, 2025.
