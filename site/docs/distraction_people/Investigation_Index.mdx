# Distraction People — Investigation Index

A sophisticated operation may need **noise** — people who claim to be the shooter, wave props,
tamper with scenes, or absorb early headlines — while the real mechanism and exit happen elsewhere.
This section catalogs **documented and alleged** distraction figures around UVU on **September 10, 2025**.
**No one here is accused by this site of the assassination** unless a court has said so on another matter.
