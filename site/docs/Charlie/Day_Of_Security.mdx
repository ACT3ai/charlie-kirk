# Day-of Security

[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s **personal protective posture** on September 10, 2025 is a standing question in this
investigation: who was responsible for close protection, what technology was denied, and whether
personnel had **conflicting loyalties**. This page summarizes Charlie-specific security claims;
institutional UVU failures are detailed in [UVU Campus Security Gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps).

## Integrity Security Solutions

Charlie's private detail that day was reportedly provided by **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC**
(DBA **Integrity Security Solutions**), founded by former police officer **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)**.

Identified close-protection figures in compiled video analysis include:

| Person | Role (claims) |
|--------|----------------|
| **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** | Head of security; dark blue button-up, Ray-Ban glasses |
| **Brian Harpole** | Firm founder |
| **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** | White shirt/hat; mentor, not uniformed detail |
| **[Laine Schoneberger](/People/laine-schoneberger)** | [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) sponsor rep (Yrefy CIO) — square sunglasses, dark blue shirt |

The setup was described as **lighter than prior Kirk events** — a hybrid of private detail plus
**six UVU campus police** (~3,000 attendees, no drone engagement, no activated Orem joint unit).

## Dan Flood — allegations and open questions

Commentary raises several **unproven** concerns about Flood:

- Prior service on **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)'s security team** (allegation in social posts)
- Charlie allegedly texted Flood the **night of Sept 9** that **"they"** would kill him **tomorrow**
  after a donor call ([quotes](./Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings))
- Flood was **standing beside Charlie** when he was hit; some videos show him stepping **right**
  a split second before impact — interpreted variously as reflex or suspicion
- **Cattle Barons Ball** allegation — Flood allegedly provided security for Dallas billionaires
  around the same period while TPUSA had **no written plan** for Charlie's **Sept 11** next stop
  (Therese Bible College)

We **do not** claim Dan Flood knew of the assassination in advance or failed to protect Charlie
criminally. We document why researchers scrutinize his role.

## UVU institutional layer

UVU Police Chief **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)** led six officers focused on crowd control; rooftop inspection was
reportedly inadequate. [GRAMA records](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) show pre-event warnings were
buried. Charlie's exposure on an **open courtyard dais** under a tent is analyzed in [UVU Tent](/UVU/Tent)
and [Mic](/Mic/overview) threads.

## TPUSA media drone vs security denial

A major contradiction surfaced in **January 2025** [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) commentary and TPUSA legal correspondence:

- TPUSA **admitted** a **media production drone** flew briefly for B-roll **before** the event
- **Security's counter-drone request** was **denied** by the university
- **Frank Turek** reported seeing drone footage with Charlie **en route**
- **Brian Harpole** stated no drones — university denied
- **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** claimed wide-angle from a bridge, not a drone

Researchers argue approved media drones could have doubled as **surveillance** or that denied
security drones left Charlie exposed. See [Drones](/Drones/overview).

## Night-before security message

Candace Owens alleges Charlie messaged security chief **"Dan"** after a donor call that **"they"**
would kill him **tomorrow**. If true, this is a **failure-to-escalate** question for any future
inquiry — why was the event not postponed or hardened?
