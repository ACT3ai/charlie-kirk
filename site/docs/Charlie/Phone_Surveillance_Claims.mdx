# Phone Surveillance Claims

A separate thread from the public kill-me quotes alleges **active surveillance** of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s
communications and a **direct donor threat** tied to his research into Israel-linked funding and
Epstein networks.

## Mega-donor account (allegation)

Commentary describing an **unnamed [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) mega-donor** (image and voice digitally altered in the
source material) claims:

- Charlie was warned: **"Back off or you won't make it to Christmas"**
- He was investigating **elite networks** — Israel ties, Epstein-linked funding, and donor flows
  he was not supposed to examine
- After the warning, an **"intervention"** in the **Hamptons** followed, with **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)**
  allegedly pressing Charlie to take cash and fall in line

We **do not** name the donor, **do not** assert the donor committed illegal surveillance or
threats, and **do not** claim Ackman knew of the assassination in advance.

## Phone anomalies Charlie reportedly described

According to the same donor narrative and compiled notes, Charlie told associates his phone:

- **Overheated** and drained rapidly with no explanation
- Produced **weird feedback** on calls
- Had a **second SIM card** in a backup phone he **never installed**

Charlie's friend **Mike** (per interview references in the master file) allegedly had a meeting
**sabotaged** because "they" knew about it ahead of time — cited as corroborating total surveillance.

These are **self-reported symptoms**, not forensic proof of hacking. Technical validation would
require carrier records, device images, and lawful discovery.

## Hamptons intervention context

The Hamptons meeting is documented in more detail on [Israel, Donors & Motive](./Israel_Donors_Motive)
and [Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction). Key claimed attendees include [Rabbi Pesach
Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff), and Bill Ackman.

**Seth Dillon** later said under oath there was a **"heated and at times spirited debate"** —
which commentators contrast with his earlier public statement that there was no angry
confrontation. Charlie reportedly described the
weekend as an **intervention** and vented to Megyn Kelly afterward.

## Connection to Epstein and DOGE threads

The donor account ties surveillance pressure to Charlie's **[Epstein files push](./Epstein_Files_Push)**
and a reported order to establish a **DOGE-style audit committee** of TPUSA finances approximately
**one week** before the shooting. Ex-employee **Joshua Peterson** separately alleged a hidden **$5M**
"stock market" TPUSA branch Charlie did not know about — see [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).

## Investigative value

If substantiated, phone compromise would explain how adversaries knew meeting schedules and could
amplify **psychological pressure** before September 10. It would also imply **insider access** to
TPUSA communications infrastructure — a separate question from rooftop ballistics.
