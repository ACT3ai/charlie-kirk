# Reported Quotes and Warnings

The weeks before Charlie Kirk's death produced a **dense cluster of alleged statements** about
personal danger, donor pressure, and breaking with pro-Israel politics. This page organizes them
by source tier. Unless marked otherwise, treat every quote as **alleged** pending primary-source
authentication.

## Tier 1 — Named witnesses on the record

| Source | Alleged statement | Channel / date (claims) |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| **Andrew Kolvet** (TPUSA) | "They will kill me" | Signal/Telegram; confirmed in commentary per Candace Owens |
| **Frank Turek** (mentor) | "I know they want me dead… under the gun… please pray for me" | Text weeks before; also day-before warning per public interviews |
| **Dan Flood** (head of security) | Charlie feared **"executed tomorrow"** night before event | Per Candace Owens reporting |
| **Harrison Smith** | Charlie feared **Israel** would be targeted / he would be taken out | Public commentary |
| **Candace Owens** | Showed **actual texts** on her program | Phone display; Sept 9 kill-me language |

Compiled notes state Charlie used **Signal and Telegram**, not iMessage, for some threat messages —
which matters for forensic recovery if devices are ever examined in litigation.

## Tier 2 — Widely circulated social quotes (unverified)

- **"If I go against Israel, I think they will kill me"** — allegedly to a friend **August 13, 2025**
- **"I'm worried that Israel is going to kill me"** — attributed **one day before** the assassination
- **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** — during **September 9** combative meeting (Instagram/X compilations:
  [@BalochRamzan2](https://x.com/BalochRamzan2/status/1965901874974044386), [@Partisangirl](https://x.com/Partisangirl/status/1965873642547589214), others)
- **Donor stereotype / leaving pro-Israel cause** — group-chat lines cited by Candace Owens and
  [@zebboykin Megyn Kelly compilation](https://x.com/zebboykin/status/1986806479718871495)
- **AIPAC treason** comment — amplified via [@AdameMedia](https://x.com/AdameMedia/status/1999306381623709957)

Israel-specific quote compilation also lives at [Israel: Charlie Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

## Tier 3 — Revolution / legacy texts (Candace thread)

See [Candace Owens Friendship](./Candace_Owens_Friendship) for the **"end of this revolution"** private
text and AmFest invitation — distinct from kill-me warnings but part of the same late-September
messaging pattern.

## Erika Kirk contradiction

On Glenn Beck's show, **Erika Kirk** stated Charlie was **not** messaging people the day before
saying "they're going to kill me," and that she had his cell phone and **"it never happened."**

Multiple public figures (Kolvet, Turek, Candace Owens, Harrison Smith) have **contradicted** that
denial in commentary. This investigation **does not** claim Erika Kirk lied to cover up a crime or
knew of the assassination in advance; we document the **conflicting accounts** because they affect
evidence preservation and witness credibility.

2. **Original video** — Megyn Kelly segments, Shapiro Sept 9 appearance
3. **Sworn testimony** — any future deposition of Kolvet, Flood, Turek
4. **Court discovery** — [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure targets
