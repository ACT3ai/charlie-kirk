# Reported Quotes and Warnings

The weeks before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death produced a **dense cluster of alleged statements** about
personal danger, donor pressure, and breaking with pro-Israel politics. This page organizes them
by source tier. Unless marked otherwise, treat every quote as **alleged** pending primary-source
authentication.

## Tier 1 — Named witnesses on the record

| Source | Alleged statement | Channel / date (claims) |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** ([TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)) | "They will kill me" | Signal/Telegram; confirmed in commentary per [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) |
| **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** (mentor) | "I know they want me dead… under the gun… please pray for me" | Text weeks before; also day-before warning per public interviews |
| **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** (head of security) | Charlie feared **"executed tomorrow"** night before event | Per Candace Owens reporting |
| **[Harrison Smith](/People/harrison-smith)** | Charlie feared **Israel** would be targeted / he would be taken out | Public commentary |
| **Candace Owens** | Showed **actual texts** on her program | Phone display; Sept 9 kill-me language |

Compiled notes state Charlie used **Signal and Telegram**, not iMessage, for some threat messages —
which matters for forensic recovery if devices are ever examined in litigation.

## Tier 2 — Widely circulated social quotes (unverified)

- **"If I go against Israel, I think they will kill me"** — allegedly to a friend **August 13, 2025**
- **"I'm worried that Israel is going to kill me"** — attributed **one day before** the assassination
- **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** — during **September 9** combative meeting (Instagram/X compilations:
  [@BalochRamzan2](https://x.com/BalochRamzan2/status/1965901874974044386), [@Partisangirl](https://x.com/Partisangirl/status/1965873642547589214), others)
- **Donor stereotype / leaving pro-Israel cause** — group-chat lines cited by Candace Owens and
  [@zebboykin Megyn Kelly compilation](https://x.com/zebboykin/status/1986806479718871495)
- **AIPAC treason** comment — amplified via [@AdameMedia](https://x.com/AdameMedia/status/1999306381623709957)

Israel-specific quote compilation also lives at [Israel: Charlie Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

Charlie's own warnings were not the only ones: former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that people posted online that Charlie Kirk would die that day — pre-knowledge he calls a strong indication "more than likely there's something else at play," and a lead area he says his center was ordered to stop examining.

## Tier 3 — Revolution / legacy texts (Candace thread)

See [Candace Owens Friendship](./Candace_Owens_Friendship) for the **"end of this revolution"** private
text and AmFest invitation — distinct from kill-me warnings but part of the same late-September
messaging pattern.

## Erika Kirk contradiction

On Glenn Beck's show, **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** stated Charlie was **not** messaging people the day before
saying "they're going to kill me," and that she had his cell phone and **"it never happened."**

Multiple public figures (Kolvet, Turek, Candace Owens, Harrison Smith) have **contradicted** that
denial in commentary. This investigation **does not** claim Erika Kirk lied to cover up a crime or
knew of the assassination in advance; we document the **conflicting accounts** because they affect
evidence preservation and witness credibility.

### Cross-cutting X signals that touch this topic

- **Pre-event donor pressure (as reported):** Candace Owens publicized private messages (authenticity discussed with TPUSA spokesman **Andrew Kolvet** in mainstream commentary) in which Charlie reportedly wrote ~**48 hours** before Sept 10 that donor pressure was "leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause," and that he had lost a major donor after refusing to cancel **Tucker Carlson**. These are **private-text claims**, not a formal public policy renunciation, and they do **not** establish who fired any shot.
- **July 2026 preliminary hearing:** Citizen live-notes and press describe defense challenges to **FBI DNA** methods and **ATF ballistics** (including **inconclusive** comparisons widely discussed online), plus multi-agency testimony on the [Sept 11 surrender chain](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody).
- **Information control:** Threads continue on [gag orders](/After/Legal_Process_Gag_Orders_And_Secrecy), [Ryne Simmons video](/Censorship/Ryne_Simmons_FBI_Video), platform deboosting, and rapid [crime-scene paving](/After/Site_Changes_And_Crime_Scene_Handling) (Lead Investigator **Hull** reportedly learned of paving via news, per circulating hearing notes).
- **Counter-claims:** Other accounts insist surveillance, DNA, texts, and admissions support a **lone-actor** charging narrative and treat foreign-intel theories as unsubstantiated. Present both sides; [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**.
