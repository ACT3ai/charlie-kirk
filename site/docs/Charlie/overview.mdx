If Tyler Robinson did not act alone — or did not fire the killing shot — motive analysis starts
with **who Charlie was becoming** in mid-2025. Compiled research describes a nationally visible
conservative leader who was **breaking with pro-Israel donor orthodoxy**, pushing **Epstein files**
release, platforming [Tucker Carlson](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel), and privately texting that
**"they will kill me."** Those claims are contested; this section organizes them for evaluation
alongside forensic and institutional evidence elsewhere on the site.

The substance is not biographical but **pressure-shaped**: a July 12 reported **Trump reprimand**
over Epstein coverage, a **Hamptons intervention**, alleged **phone surveillance**, a **DOGE audit**
order one week before the event, and a burst of **September 9** warnings and texts. Day-of context
includes a **lighter security detail**, **denied counter-drone** requests while a **media drone**
flew, and [UVU institutional gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps) that left six campus officers
covering thousands of attendees.
