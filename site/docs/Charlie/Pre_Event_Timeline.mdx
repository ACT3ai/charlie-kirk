# Pre-Event Timeline

This chronology focuses on **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s** reported actions and pressures from **July through
September 9, 2025**. Dates and quotes are **claims** drawn from compiled research unless linked to
primary reporting. For the full assassination timeline see [Timeline: Charlie Kirk](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk)
and [Before](/Before/overview).

## July 2025

| Date (claim) | Event |
|--------------|-------|
| **July 12** | [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) reportedly **called Charlie** and reprimanded him over **[Epstein files](./Epstein_Files_Push)** coverage; Charlie **refused** to back off |
| **July 31** | White House strategy session (Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, Philos Project) on combating "woke-right defamation" of Israel — Charlie and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) **notably absent** per notes |

## August 2025

| Date (claim) | Event |
|--------------|-------|
| **Early August** | **[Hamptons "intervention"](./Israel_Donors_Motive)** — pro-Israel figures and donors allegedly confronted Charlie over rhetoric and platforming |
| **Aug 4–6** | Prime Minister **Netanyahu** reportedly offered financial support tied to an Israel trip — **declined**; Charlie appeared on **Megyn Kelly** discussing donor pressure |
| **Aug 13** | Friend allegedly told to post: **"if I go against Israel, I think they will kill me"** |
| Ongoing | Platforming [Tucker Carlson](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel) and Dave Smith; donor pullbacks over Tucker ban refusal |

## Early September 2025

| Date (claim) | Event |
|--------------|-------|
| **Sept 1–5** | Group chats — Charlie reportedly said he had **"no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** |
| **~1 week before shooting** | Charlie allegedly ordered **DOGE-style committee** to audit TPUSA finances (friction checklist) |
| **Sept 8** | Public post about **leaving the pro-Israel movement** (per compilations) |
| **Sept 9 AM/PM** | **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)** appearance — sharper Israeli policy criticism |
| **Sept 9** | High-pressure meeting; texts **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** ([quotes page](./Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings)) |
| **Sept 9** | Text to **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** inviting her to **AmFest** ([Candace page](./Candace_Owens_Friendship)) |
| **Night of Sept 9** | Charlie allegedly told **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** and donors he would be **"executed tomorrow"** (claim) |

## September 10, 2025

| Time | Event |
|------|-------|
| **12:23 PM MDT** | Charlie Kirk shot at **[UVU](/UVU/overview)** outdoor event; pronounced dead at Timpanogos Regional Hospital |

## UVU warnings (institutional — not Charlie-authored)

The **night of Sept 9**, two men asked UVU police about **"a plan"**; a separate email sought to
deliver a **message to Charlie** — see [UVU Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

- **Pre-event donor pressure (as reported):** Candace Owens publicized private messages (authenticity discussed with TPUSA spokesman **Andrew Kolvet** in mainstream commentary) in which Charlie reportedly wrote ~**48 hours** before Sept 10 that donor pressure was "leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause," and that he had lost a major donor after refusing to cancel **Tucker Carlson**. These are **private-text claims**, not a formal public policy renunciation, and they do **not** establish who fired any shot.
