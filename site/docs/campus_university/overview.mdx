## Citizen investigator focus (UVU as crime scene institution)

Public commentary on X treats **UVU as both campus and crime scene**: institutional CCTV quality, rooftop access control, event-security coordination with TPUSA, and post-shot evidence handling. Citizen investigators repeatedly ask whether UVU preserved **all Losee Center / courtyard / parking** angles with verified clocks, and whether any campus-police bodycam or radio logs explain the **minutes after 12:23 p.m.** July 2026 preliminary-hearing coverage revived these questions when Agent **David Hull** was cross-examined on rooftop search and shell-casing absence. Campus-response threads also track **UVU statements** against what commentators describe as TPUSA's acknowledgment of media-drone B-roll while security was denied drones. Readers should use [UVU](/UVU/overview), [cameras](/cameras/overview), and [security](/security_law_enforcement/overview) pages for detail; this hub only orients the **institutional** slice of the investigation.
