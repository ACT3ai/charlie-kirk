The **strongest foreign-presence indicators** in open sources are **quantitative**: analyst **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)**'s claimed **44 foreign phones** at [UVU](/UVU/overview) ([Foreign Phones](./Foreign_Phones_UVU)), plus **Egyptian Air Force** tails at [Provo](/Locations/Provo_Airport) the week of the shooting ([Egyptian Foreign Ops](./Egyptian_Foreign_Ops)). The **strongest foreign-motive layer** is **Israel-related**: Kirk's late criticism of unconditional aid, alleged **Israeli IP search spikes** before July 20, 2025 ([Foreign Search IP Patterns](./Foreign_Search_IP_Patterns)), and **[Mossad](./Mossad)** recruitment allegations at [TPUSA](./TPUSA_Foreign_Recruitment).

**Linkage is not guilt.** Foreign registration on a phone or a government jet on ADS-B does not identify a shooter. Investigators still treat these threads as **pointers to records** — especially after reporting that [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)'s [NCTC foreign-nexus review was halted](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC) at the White House. April 2026 **Islamabad Serena** tracking claims added a **diplomatic tail** on four Israeli devices ([Islamabad link](./Islamabad_Serena_Link)). **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** introduced a separate **[French Foreign Legion](./French_Foreign_Legion_Claims)** training claim — unverified, but part of the foreign-service map.
