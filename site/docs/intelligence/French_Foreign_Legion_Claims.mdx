# French Foreign Legion Claims

A **non-Israeli foreign-service** thread entered the investigation through **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** broadcasts (2026), citing a **French government source**:

## Owens' attributed claims (Charlie Kirk angle)

Per [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) summaries:

1. A French official warned Owens that **President Macron** allegedly green-lit threats against her; the same network allegedly behind Kirk's **"public execution"**
2. The French source did **not** name [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) as the assassin — instead claimed Kirk's killer trained with the **French Foreign Legion 13th Brigade**
3. Owens clarified: Robinson is the **"patsy"** who **never stepped on campus** — his role was **dropping clothes** near [Dairy Queen](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) / cemetery to frame him
4. French government response reportedly denied only that **"Tyler Robinson was never in the Legion"** — interpreted by some as **not denying** a separate shooter existed

## Candace Owens French-service claims — and pushback

- Owens has publicly claimed **French Foreign Legion** / **French intelligence (DGSE)** involvement and, in wider commentary, **French/Israeli** hit-squad themes. **RT** and others amplified **Pavel Durov** saying French involvement is "entirely plausible."
- Critics (**[@MaxNordau](/Influencers/x/maxnordau)** and others, July 2026) call the story **unstable** (Legion, Egyptian planes, shifting cast) and demand reconciliation of claims.
- This page documents **attributed allegations and rebuttals**. No court has found France or the Legion responsible for Kirk's death.
