# Chinese, Russian & Iranian Presence Claims

Within [Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)'s alleged **44-phone** set at [UVU](/UVU/overview) ([Foreign Phones](./Foreign_Phones_UVU)), the **non-Israeli** registrations break down as:

| Claimed registration | Count |
|---------------------|-------|
| Chinese | **13** |
| Russian | **12** |
| Iranian | **3** |

Public sources have **not** released IMEI lists, subscriber names, or carrier contracts. This page documents **why each nationality appears in foreign-intel threads** — not who the owners were.

## Chinese threads

- **Student / trade delegation** benign readings (large international enrollment at Utah universities)
- [@RealGeorgeWebb1](https://x.com/RealGeorgeWebb1/status/1968002673510437301) theorized UVU **Global Engagement Center** money flows tied to political violence — **unverified**
- **Utah Chinese Civic Center** + **Younique** plane: three **Page, Arizona** round trips on the murder day per Webb ([Planes](/Planes/overview))

## Russian threads

- Twelve registrations appear only as a **Seruga aggregate** — few independent corroborating posts name Russian **operatives** or **embassy** links
- Some commentators group Russian count with **multi-polar** foreign-interference framing ([Motive — Geopolitical Intel](/Motive/Geopolitical_Intel_Service_Theories))

## Iranian threads

- Three registrations gain attention alongside **US–Iran diplomacy** monitoring theories
- Four **Israeli** phones later allegedly pinged **Islamabad Serena** during US–Iran talks ([Islamabad Serena Link](./Islamabad_Serena_Link)) — Iranian delegation was present at that hotel per news reporting; **coincidence vs coordination** is disputed

## Investigative discipline

National registration on a phone is **weak evidence** of state direction. Investigators demanding records should prioritize **subscriber deanonymization** (Bumblehive claims) and **cross-border travel** logs — not nationality alone.

We do **not** claim Chinese, Russian, or Iranian nationals committed crimes related to this case.

## Public commentary (multi-nation phone claims)

Tony Seruga-style **foreign-phone tallies** (Chinese / Russian / Iranian slices inside larger UVU device counts) continue to circulate beside **Israeli-phone** discourse. Benign explanations (students, tourists, dual-SIM travelers) coexist with **Bumblehive / deanonymization** claims. Treat country counts as **OSINT hypotheses** requiring raw device-location methodology, not as proof any nation-state ordered the killing.
