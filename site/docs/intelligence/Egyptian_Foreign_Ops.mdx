# Egyptian Foreign Operations — Provo (2025)

Among the clearest **foreign-government physical presence** indicators near the crime scene are **Egyptian Air Force** jets logged at **[Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)](/Locations/Provo_Airport)** in the days around **September 10, 2025**. This is **flight-record territory** — not proof of who fired a shot.

## Why foreign-intel investigators care

Citizen posts ask:

- Why **government VIP transports** sat **six days** before the UVU event?
- Whether **DoD badge / no-scan** entry logs at PVU match foreign-national access questions on the airport page
- Whether Egypt functions as a **logistics proxy** in multi-service theories ("Mossad proxies via Egypt" appears in X commentary — **speculative**, not established)

[@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) flight-tracking compilations are the primary public source; Egyptian government **denial or explanation** of the Provo visits has not been attached in this repository.

## Distinction from the official case

The Utah prosecution centers on **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** (presumed innocent until proven guilty). Egyptian jets are a **parallel foreign-presence thread** — relevant to **motive/capability** discussions, not a substitute for ballistics or eyewitness evidence.

We do **not** claim Egyptian pilots, diplomats, or contractors participated in the assassination.
