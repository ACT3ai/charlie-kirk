# CornerShot Technology (Israeli Hardware Claims)

Some citizen threads route the killing mechanism through **Israeli-made military hardware** rather than a **Losee Center .30-06** shot. The most repeated device is **CornerShot** — a real Israeli-developed system — described in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) and X posts as:

| Feature | Claimed relevance |
|---------|-------------------|
| **90° hinge** + camera feed | Fire around tent barriers without exposing shooter on rooftop |
| **IR laser scope** | Laser visible on **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)**'s phone screen before impact — invisible to naked eye |
| **Downward shell ejection** | Explains **casing on ground** without matching heavy rifle acoustics |
| **Glock 9mm mount** | Smaller report than .30-06 — fits "pop not crack" witness language |

## Relationship to foreign-intel theories

CornerShot is cited as evidence of **professional tradecraft** associated with **Israeli defense industry** exports — bridging [Mossad](./Mossad) motive threads and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) ballistics objections. It does **not** require Mossad ownership of the device; any purchaser could theoretically deploy it.

Parallel domestic-tech routes: [Mic — exploding microphone](/Mic/overview), [Proof Not Tyler — Alternative Mechanisms](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Alternative_Kill_Mechanisms), [Gun_Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview).

## Evidence status

- **CornerShot exists** as a product class
- **No public chain-of-custody** ties a CornerShot unit to UVU on Sept 10
- **No official source** replaced the Mauser rifle narrative with CornerShot

Treat this as a **hypothesis** demanding forensic imaging and wound-path analysis — not a finding.
