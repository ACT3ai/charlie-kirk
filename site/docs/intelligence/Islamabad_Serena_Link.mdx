# Islamabad Serena Link (Foreign Intel)

In **April 2026**, **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** ([@TonySeruga](/Influencers/x/tonyseruga)) and amplified X threads claimed **four** of the **16 Israeli-registered phones** allegedly present at [UVU](./Foreign_Phones_UVU) on **September 10, 2025** were later traced to the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** in Pakistan — the venue for high-stakes **US–Iran** negotiations ("Islamabad Talks").

> "The accident narrative is dead: You don't have four high-level Israeli-linked devices at a murder scene and then at a secret diplomatic summit by coincidence." — paraphrase of viral X framing ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/))

## Diplomatic context (public reporting)

News accounts described a secured hotel with US delegation figures (including **[JD Vance](/People/jd-vance)**; **Jared Kushner** and **Steve Witkoff** named in negotiation orbit) and Iranian leadership (**Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf**, foreign ministry officials). Israeli strategic interest in **any US–Iran deal** is the **motive layer** commentators attach — not proof any phone owner was an assassin.
