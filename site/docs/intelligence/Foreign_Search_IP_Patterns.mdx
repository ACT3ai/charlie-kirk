# Foreign Search IP Patterns (Claims)

A major **foreign-intel reconnaissance** thread alleges **Israeli IP addresses** (and some **Washington, DC** IPs) produced unusual **Google search spikes** for Charlie Kirk case figures **weeks or months before** the **September 10, 2025** shooting — with an anchor date of **July 20, 2025** in viral compilations ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) "ISRAELI IP MYSTERY" section).

## Recurring search targets (claimed)

| Target category | Examples named in threads |
|-----------------|---------------------------|
| **UVU layout** | "UVU Rooftop," "Gun," "Parking," "CCTV," "Losee Center" — May–July 2025 |
| **Medical / autopsy** | Timpanogos Regional Hospital; **seven surgeons** in a July frenzy; ME **[Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** |
| **Judiciary** | Judge **[Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** (July 26 spike); predecessor **[Robert Lunnen](/court/judge-robert-lunnen)** (May 15) — before Graf presided |
| **Defense** | **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** — Dec 8–14, 2024 Israeli IP spike |
| **Legislation** | Autopsy bill drafter **Greg Gunn** — Aug 2024 & May 2025 |
| **Political figures** | [Phil Lyman](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman), [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Derek Maxfield](/People/derek-maxfield) — July 2025 |

Deeper Israel-section treatment: [Israeli Search Patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims) · [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview).

## Foreign-intel interpretation (claims only)

Commentators argue the pattern looks like **advance mapping** of:

- Crime-scene geometry
- Hospital where Kirk would be transported
- Judges who would handle the case
- Autopsy path if one occurred

**Benign readings** also exist: international news interest, diaspora researchers, VPN artifacts mislabeled as "Israeli IP," or coordinated **citizen OSINT** feeding back into Trends.

## Evidence limits

- Google Trends **does not prove** who searched or why
- **No court exhibit** has authenticated IP logs in this repository
- Spikes **correlate** with public events (e.g., **AMFEST / [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** first joint appearance week cited near July 20)

We do **not** claim anyone who appeared in search data participated in violence.

Online pre-knowledge signals of this kind are among the reasons former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says "more than likely there's something else at play" — and he has stated on the record that his center's foreign-nexus review was halted before such leads were exhausted.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Raw search telemetry, VPN attribution studies, and IC assessments of foreign reconnaissance are among [Fix Law 2](/Fix/overview) targets.

## Reconnaissance OSINT claims (limits)

Israeli/foreign **Google search spike** claims about UVU, judges, and surgeons before September 10 remain **methodology-sensitive**. Without published query logs, time zones, and VPN filtering, treat spikes as **weak OSINT**. Useful as a research lead for FOIA to search platforms/agencies; insufficient alone to prove pre-attack recon by a state service.
