## What this area covers

This area is about *framing*, not about any single piece of physical evidence. In a fast-moving, heavily filmed public event, the words used to describe what happened — where the wound was, who was arrested, whether there was a confession, who was to blame — spread through television and social media long before any official finding. This section collects the pages where the investigation documents those early descriptions as-given and asks a narrow, recurring question: which framings appeared, how widely they were amplified, and which ones later receded once raw footage and official records circulated.

The clearest worked example is the "shot to the heart" description. Across the earliest on-camera interviews, multiple witnesses — several of them featured in national television segments — described Charlie Kirk as struck **directly in the heart**. Skeptics note that no imagery shows a chest entry wound or chest blood, that a high ratio of the earliest confident statements landed on TV news, and that the framing faded as videos of a **neck** wound dominated timelines. The dedicated catalog of X posts is [Shot in the Heart](./Shot_in_the_heart); full TV compilations and the shaped-charge question live on [Shot to the Heart](/Witnesses/shot-to-the-heart). The same pattern-question runs through the [confession-fabrication](/CoverUp/Confession_Fabrication) and [custody-timeline](/CoverUp/Custody_Confession_Timeline) disputes and the broader [narrative-control](/CoverUp/Narrative_Psyop) claims.

Start with [Shot in the Heart](./Shot_in_the_heart) for the complete clickable X archive of heart/chest claims, then [Shot to the Heart](/Witnesses/shot-to-the-heart) for embedded witness video. Read [Wrong Witnesses](/Witnesses/wrong_witnesses) and [Shot in Chest, Then Neck](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal) for the witness-account layer. For the online and media dimension — how framings were amplified or suppressed — see [Online Narrative-Control Operations](/CoverUp/Narrative_Psyop), [Media Framing & Coverage](/Media/overview), and [Censorship](/Censorship/overview). Throughout, every framing is recorded as a **question**, weighed against the ordinary explanations (genuine virality, lay shorthand, and coverage naturally tracking official findings), and never asserted as a coordinated operation.
