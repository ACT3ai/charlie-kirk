The **first 24 hours** set the pattern: false UVU **"suspect in custody"** alerts, the [Sept 11 surrender chain](./Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) with **6:25 PM Miranda** vs **8:02 PM** [Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby) call disputes, and Discord messages timestamped **~7:57 PM**. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted** — custody timing is evidence-process questions, not guilt findings.

**Days to months** brought [courtyard paving](./Site_Changes_And_Crime_Scene_Handling) within days, [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)'s **Dec 16, 2025** gag order, and resignations by Brooksby and UVU President **Tuminez** ([institutional exits](./Institutional_Resignations)). Citizen investigators parallel mainstream coverage with [censorship threads](./Media_Narratives_And_Censorship_After) — Ryne Simmons' 4K video, platform suspensions, sealed discovery.

**Political aftermath** runs through [memorial and TPUSA succession](./Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession) ([Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), donor threads, national honors) and [visa enforcement](./Visa-Revocation-Celebrations) against celebration posts — policy debates separate from ballistics.
