# Memorial and TPUSA Succession After Charlie Kirk's Death

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
**[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** and other living persons on this page are discussed only through **public reporting and attributed commentary**. We do **not** claim Erika Kirk or any other individual knew of, planned, or participated in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death.
:::

The hours and months **after 12:23 PM on September 10, 2025** reshaped [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and conservative movement politics — through memorial events, leadership succession, and intense media scrutiny.

## Immediate post-shot notification chain

Before memorial politics took shape, citizen investigators reconstructed who was notified first:

| Step | Time / account | Source |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **1** | **~12:23 PM MDT** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) says [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) called at shooting moment | Erika's public account |
| **2** | Mikey walks away, phone to ear — viral post-shot footage | Circulated video |
| **3** | Mikey calls [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) in **Arizona** (**11:23 AM** local) | [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) witness account |
| **4** | **Sept 11** — surrender timeline begins | [Sept 11 Surrender](./Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) |

Rob McCoy later appeared at memorial events and on the [CrossExamined](https://x.com) podcast with Mikey. See [Rob McCoy profile](/People/rob-mccoy) and [Handler Network Claims](/Gov_Mind_Control/handler-network-claims) — **speculation only**.

### TPUSA finances and governance questions (claims)

Independent researcher **[@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles)** published a chart of roughly **ten years of TPUSA insider payments** that citizen investigators tie to the succession question. Per that thread (**allegations, not findings**):

- **$9M+** reportedly paid to **"Resource One"** — characterized as a DBA for a large political direct-mail group rather than a printer.
- **$999K** reportedly paid to **Clocktower LLC** (dissolved 2022) for a "research project" with no public deliverables — framed as a possible slush fund.
- **[Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan)** (TPUSA advancement director) reportedly routed **$2.7M+** through shell entities while salaried for the same role.
- **Erika Kirk's mother, [Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve)**, reportedly sat on the board and is tied to **$15.2M+** steered to insider-controlled companies; a separate thread places her on the board of **Superfeed Technologies** alongside Turning Point Action COO **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)**.
- CK_FILE notes Charlie reportedly **"demanded a DOGE audit days before he died."**

Every figure named here is **living and not accused of any crime**; these are **contested financial allegations** raised in [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'s *Bride of Charlie* ecosystem, not audited findings. They matter to succession because they concern **who controlled donor flows and governance** after Sept 10.

## Citizen investigator claims on succession (attributed only)

:::caution High-sensitivity topics
Claims about **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** and TPUSA governance are among the highest defamation-risk materials on this site. Everything below is **what named speakers said in public**. The site does **not** adopt any claim that Erika Kirk or any living person planned, knew of, or participated in Charlie Kirk's death.
:::

- According to [**@ProjectConstitu**](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/2013787152883052889) (Jan 21, 2026; ~89k views), **Candace Owens** challenged the public succession story: (1) the claim that Charlie named Erika to lead TPUSA at an **Aspen donor retreat (Aug 15–17, 2025)** is **uncorroborated** by multiple attendees Owens says she contacted; (2) **audio** of related remarks played at AmFest exists without matching **video**, which Owens treats as a transparency problem in an AI era; (3) **Andrew Kolvet**'s "tarmac / ran into Erika's arms" account is compared to **FlightRadar** data allegedly showing Erika's plane arrived **over an hour earlier** than Kolvet's — Owens asks why wait if rushing to the hospital. These are **commentary questions**, not court findings.
- According to [**@Bubblebathgirl**](https://x.com/Bubblebathgirl/status/1999126572897341623) (Dec 11, 2025), Owens's line **"You don't just get to be widowed into chairman and CEO"** of TPUSA drew a counter-clip: Charlie wanted Erika to take over if he passed — **both sides** of the succession dispute circulate on X.
- According to [**@godfatherofchao**](https://x.com/godfatherofchao/status/2074164592109125991) (Jul 6, 2026), Owens argued Erika's **eulogy** felt more like a **fundraising appeal** than a personal tribute — an **opinion** about tone, not a legal claim.
- According to [**@phantompain1984**](https://x.com/phantompain1984/status/2074960567933247918) (Jul 8, 2026), some commentators allege that after Charlie's death the organization **elevated Ben Shapiro** while **Tucker Carlson** was sidelined, then layer **unverified** "network" theories. Record as **speculative commentary**; do not treat foreign-network claims as established facts.
