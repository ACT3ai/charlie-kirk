# Memorial and TPUSA Succession After Charlie Kirk's Death

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
**Erika Kirk** and other living persons on this page are discussed only through **public reporting and attributed commentary**. We do **not** claim Erika Kirk or any other individual knew of, planned, or participated in Charlie Kirk's death.
:::

The hours and months **after 12:23 PM on September 10, 2025** reshaped [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and conservative movement politics — through memorial events, leadership succession, and intense media scrutiny.

## Immediate post-shot notification chain

Before memorial politics took shape, citizen investigators reconstructed who was notified first:

| Step | Time / account | Source |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **1** | **~12:23 PM MDT** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) says [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) called at shooting moment | Erika's public account |
| **2** | Mikey walks away, phone to ear — viral post-shot footage | Circulated video |
| **3** | Mikey calls [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) in **Arizona** (**11:23 AM** local) | [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff) witness account |
| **4** | **Sept 11** — surrender timeline begins | [Sept 11 Surrender](./Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) |

Rob McCoy later appeared at memorial events and on the [CrossExamined](https://x.com) podcast with Mikey. See [Rob McCoy profile](/People/rob-mccoy) and [Handler Network Claims](/Gov_Mind_Control/handler-network-claims) — **speculation only**.

- Kolvet also publicly confirmed authenticity of certain donor-related messages in mainstream reporting — [Candace Owens Israel Controversies](/Media/Candace-Owens-Israel-Controversies).

## Public grief vs polarization

- Large memorial events and tributes shaped how supporters frame **justice** — themes in [After overview](./overview).
- **[Visa revocations](./Visa-Revocation-Celebrations)** and employer terminations over celebration posts — documented backlash pattern.
- Reuters-reported **600+** workplace actions over Kirk-related posts ([Media](/Media/overview) institutional backlash threads).
- Charlie Kirk **grave and memorial security** debates — privacy vs. public access — recur in citizen commentary without unified official policy.

### Grave site and memorial access

Commentary on [After overview](./overview) themes notes tension between supporters seeking pilgrimage sites and family or institutional efforts to limit location disclosure. No court order or TPUSA policy document reviewed here establishes a single public standard; debates remain in **social-media and podcast commentary**, not adjudicated findings.
