# Companies & Organizations

This page is the master index of every **company, non-profit, and government body** profiled anywhere in the investigation. It gathers the for-profit firms and LLCs, the non-profit and political entities, U.S. government agencies, and non-U.S. (foreign) government and intelligence bodies into one place so their roles can be compared side by side. Many entries are marked **(Claims)** — meaning the entity's link to the case is an attributed allegation from public reporting or social media, not an established fact.

**What this index is.** Each row above points to a full profile or section page elsewhere on the site. Grouping them here answers a recurring reader question — *"who are all the businesses, charities, and agencies named in this case, and how do they relate?"* — without forcing you to hunt across the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview), [FBI](/FBI/overview), and [Intelligence](/intelligence/overview) sections one at a time.

**How the four groups differ.** *Companies* are for-profit firms and single-purpose LLCs — security vendors, aviation and paving entities, media and consulting firms. *Non-Profit Organizations* are the 501(c) charities, foundations, endowments, and PACs, most in the Turning Point family. *US Government* covers federal agencies (FBI, CIA, NSA, DOJ) plus the state and county law-enforcement bodies that touched the response. *Non-US Government* covers foreign state and intelligence entities — led by Israel's Mossad — that appear in geopolitical motive theories.

**How to read the "(Claims)" tag.** Many entities are named only in attributed allegations from public reporting or social-media investigators. Their appearance here is **not** a finding that the company, charity, or agency did anything wrong. Follow each link for the sourcing, the counter-arguments, and the alive/dead-or-active status. For living people and active firms, this site uses attribution language throughout and states no crime as fact absent a court finding.
