# Preliminary Hearing — Day 4

*Verbatim record of public judicial proceedings in State of Utah v. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) (Case No. 251403576). Robinson is charged, not convicted; testimony below is quoted, not adopted as fact.*

The fourth and near-final day of the [preliminary hearing](/court/preliminary-hearing-motions) opened with argument over whether the state's [Exhibit 16 messages, Discord chats, and note](/court/discord-evidence-court) could be published in the courtroom and on the camera feed, with [Judge Tony F. Graf, Jr.](/court/judge-tony-graf) issuing a tiered ruling. [FBI/SBI Agent Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) then resumed, playing [Lance Twiggs'](/People/lance-twiggs) immunized interview and reading the [text thread](/court/Twiggs_Phone) in which the sender confesses. [Sergeant Jennifer Faumuina](/UVU/Losee_Center) walked through the rooftop "perch," the [wooded-area rifle recovery](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery), and the [ATF DNA and ballistics reports](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla). The defense called ATF firearm/toolmark examiner **Samantha Karner**, whose [bullet-jacket comparison was inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive). The state rested; the day ended with a bind-over briefing schedule and oral argument set for September 1.
