# Preliminary Hearing

The **preliminary hearing** in **State of Utah v. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** (Case No. **251403576**) ran across four sessions in **July 2026** before **[Judge Tony F. Graf, Jr.](/court/judge-tony-graf)** in the **[Fourth District Court in Provo](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo)**. This is the stage where the State must show *probable cause* to bind the capital case over for trial — and where the [inconclusive ATF ballistics](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla), the [rooftop perch](/UVU/Losee_Center), the [autopsy](/Medical/trial_and_autopsy_report), and the [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) [phone and video evidence](/court/Twiggs_Phone) were first tested under oath and cross-examination.

Each day below links to a **full, color-coded transcript** of that session's proceedings — every speaker in their own color, every named person, exhibit, and location hyperlinked to its investigation page. Robinson is **charged, not convicted**; the testimony is quoted, not adopted as fact.

## What the Hearing Decides

A preliminary hearing is not a trial. The judge does not decide guilt; he decides only whether the State has shown enough evidence for the case to proceed. That lower bar is why the defense spent four days attacking the **reliability** of the State's forensics rather than offering an alternative story — most sharply through ATF firearm examiner **Samantha Karner**, whose [bullet-jacket comparison to the seized Mauser was inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive), and through repeated challenges to the [chain of custody](/court/discovery-brady-disputes) on the [phone](/court/Phone_Extraction), the [Discord messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), and the [rooftop DNA](/UVU/Losee_Center).

The evidentiary spine the State built runs across the four days: the [rooftop shooting position](/UVU/Losee_Center) and [campus surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) (Day 1), the [DNA and surveillance forensics](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla) (Day 2), [Robinson's surrender and booking](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender) with the disputed [Twiggs interview](/court/discord-evidence-court) (Day 3), and the [confession texts, rifle recovery, and ballistics](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) (Day 4). Readers weighing the [case against Tyler Robinson](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) should read the transcripts alongside the [ATF inconclusive fragment](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla) and the [Miranda / Discord timing](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview) analyses.

After the State rested on Day 4, **[Judge Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** set a bind-over briefing schedule with oral argument in **September 2026** — meaning the decision on whether Robinson stands trial was deferred, not delivered, at the close of these sessions. Watch the [Court &amp; Trial index](/court/investigation-index) and the [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026) for the ruling.
