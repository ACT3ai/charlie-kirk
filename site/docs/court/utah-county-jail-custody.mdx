# Utah County Jail & Custody

Tyler Robinson has been held at the **Utah County Jail** (Utah County Security Center) in **Spanish
Fork** since shortly after his September 11–12, 2025 arrest. He is **charged, not convicted**.

## Jailhouse informant thread

Court commentary describes **Jaxson Thomas Fox** — held in the **same jail** on separate **child-related
charges** — receiving a **transport order** from Judge Graf at a Robinson preliminary hearing.
Commentators infer Fox may be positioned as a **jailhouse informant** claiming Robinson made
incriminating statements. That inference is **commentary**, not established fact.

Fox has **no documented connection** to the UVU shooting scene in the materials summarized here. See
[Jaxson Fox page](./jaxson-fox-informant-order) and [distraction profile](/distraction_people/Jaxson_Thomas_Fox).

We do **not** claim jail staff or prosecutors manufactured confessions or acted illegally.

## Remote appearances and security

Reporting on high-profile capital cases notes **heavy courthouse security** and lawyers declining
comment under gag orders ([gag orders](./gag-orders-sealing)). Robinson appeared **in person** for
some scheduling hearings (e.g., **Oct 30, 2025** per Fox 13 reporting cited in research notes) while
other dates used video.
