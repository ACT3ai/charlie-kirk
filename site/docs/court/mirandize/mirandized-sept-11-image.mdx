The OCR text above was extracted automatically from the screenshot using tesseract. Key details:

* The Bates exhibit number referenced is **003996-R2** — the bodycam / video exhibit showing the
  Miranda rights reading.

  approximately 6:25 PM MDT on September 11, 2025 (offset from midnight UTC).
* The AI summary distinguishes two separate events: (1) the Miranda encounter on the evening of
  September 11, and (2) the formal arrest recorded as September 12 at 4:00 AM.
* This distinction is central to the Mirandizing date controversy: the Miranda reading occurred
  on September 11, several hours before the official arrest booking timestamp.
