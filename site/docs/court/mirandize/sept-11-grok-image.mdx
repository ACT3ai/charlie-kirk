## Analysis Notes

Key facts confirmed by the Grok analysis and OCR extraction:

* **Exhibit identity:** Bates 003996-R2 is a discovery exhibit produced by the Utah County
  Attorney's Office — a video recording of Robinson's law enforcement encounter.
* **Miranda time:** The video shows Robinson being read his rights at approximately
  **6:25 PM MDT on September 11, 2025**.
* **Internal timestamp:** The video's internal clock shows approximately 03:36:53, consistent
  with a UTC-offset from the actual wall-clock time.
* **Significance:** Robinson allegedly invoked his right to counsel immediately after the
  Miranda reading — and the Discord messages attributed to him appeared hours later, after
  this Miranda encounter.
* **Coverage:** Investigator Baron Coleman has publicly discussed and quoted from this exhibit
  in his reporting on the case.
