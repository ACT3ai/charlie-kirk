# Judge Robert Lunnen — Prior Bench Vacancy

**Status: Alive (retired from bench).** **Robert Lunnen** was the **prior** Fourth District judge
whose retirement created the vacancy **[Tony F. Graf Jr.](/court/judge-tony-graf)** filled before **State of Utah v. [Tyler
Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** reached early pretrial stages. Lunnen had **no direct involvement** in the Robinson case
— he was off the bench by **August 1, 2025**, weeks before the September 10 UVU shooting.

We do **not** claim Lunnen knew of the assassination in advance or acted improperly.

## Retirement timeline

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **Aug 1, 2025** | Lunnen retirement effective (per reporting) |
| **May 2 / Aug 4, 2025** | Graf appointment and swearing-in (dates vary by source) |
| **Sept 10, 2025** | [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shot at UVU |
| **Sept 16, 2025** | Robinson first appearance before **Graf** |

The sequence is procedural: a new judge assumed the seat shortly before a high-profile capital case
entered the docket.

## Why investigators discuss Lunnen

Commentary focuses on **bench transition timing**, not Lunnen's merits as a jurist:

* Online posts tie Lunnen's retirement and Graf's appointment to **same-week FBI leadership changes**
  in Salt Lake City — presented as coincidence questions, not proof.
* **Google Trends** claims allege searches for Lunnen's name from **Israeli IPs** on **May 15, 2025**
  before the assassination, with Utah interest only afterward. See
  [judges Google Searches page](/GoogleSearches/judges-graf-lunnen-searches). These are
  **unverified** citizen-research observations.

## Distinction from presiding judge

All gag orders, discovery rulings, preliminary-hearing scheduling, and the **June 2026 Ballard
contempt** finding discussed on this site involve **Judge Graf**, not Lunnen. For active case
management see [Judge Tony Graf](./judge-tony-graf).

## Public discussion limits

Judge **Robert Lunnen** appears in citizen timelines mainly as the **prior occupant** of the Fourth District seat later filled by **Tony Graf** after Lunnen's **August 1, 2025** retirement. Unverified **Google Trends** claims about Lunnen circulating in OSINT threads should remain **attributed speculation only**. This page does **not** allege Lunnen had any role in the Kirk killing, the Robinson charging decisions, or any cover-up. The investigative value is **vacancy → appointment → case assignment chronology**, not personal culpability.
