# Goat Island

## Overview

**Goat Island** has become a point of investigative interest after it was reportedly found as a desktop photo on [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview)'s computer. Investigators also note that Goat Island is located just down the road from the [Valhalla Danish House](/Locations/Valhalla), a detail that has attracted attention given Kash Patel's use of the word "Valhalla" at the post-assassination press conference.

According to [@MJTruthUltra on X](https://x.com/MJTruthUltra/status/2046416354454990874?s=20):

> "Still my favorite Conspiracy Theory out there… • Kash: 'Rest now brother. We have the watch, and I'll see you in Valhalla' • Tyler Robinson photo on Desktop — Goat Island • Goat Island, Just down the road, Valhalla Danish House • There is no documented proof of any"

The poster notes there is no documented proof of any connection. These are investigative questions, not established facts.

Investigators report that a photo of Goat Island was found as the desktop background on Tyler Robinson's computer. The significance of this is unclear:

- **Personal connection?** Tyler Robinson may have simply liked the scenery, as Goat Island is a well-known natural landmark in Utah.
- **Geographic significance?** Goat Island is reportedly located just down the road from the Valhalla Danish House.
- **Operational significance?** Some researchers have speculated whether Goat Island was connected to any pre-event reconnaissance or meeting.

No documented evidence has been presented establishing any direct connection between Goat Island and the assassination.
