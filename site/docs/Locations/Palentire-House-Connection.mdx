# Palantir / "Palentire" Property Claims

Investigators flagged a **property-ownership thread** after commentators asked **who owns the house or land** near where the suspect's rifle was reportedly recovered. Project notes and researcher questions ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/), [Miscellaneous Evidence Leads](/Other/Miscellaneous_Evidence_Leads)) spell the name **"Palentire"** while some threads say **Palantir** — treat both as **unverified orthography** until title records are produced.

## What is established

- A weapon recovery location is discussed on [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) with building context from [@MuppetMasher](https://x.com/MuppetMasher/status/2070163736858694089)
- Citizen timelines question **gaps** between parking, movement, and drop-site geography

## What is disputed (claims only)

| Claim | Status in project |
|---|---|
| Adjacent property owned by **UVU** | Cited in researcher Q&A; not confirmed here by deed excerpt |
| Adjacent property tied to **Palantir / "Palentire"** | Recurring OSINT thread; **not verified** |
| Ownership explains **timeline anomalies** | Speculative |

**No public deed record** is attached in this repository proving Palantir corporate ownership of the gun-drop parcel.

## Why the lead persists

- **Spatial reconstruction** of suspect movement depends on **parcel boundaries**
- Some theorists connect **tech/intel contractors** to broader **surveillance-plane** threads ([Planes](/Planes/overview), [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview))
- Lawmakers' disclosure proposals request **property title and wire-transfer** records around relevant parcels

## Wooded recovery linkage

The parcel debate attaches to the [wooded area north of campus](./Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery) where the scoped **Mauser .30-06** was reportedly found. [@MuppetMasher](https://x.com/MuppetMasher/status/2070163736858694089) posted building/parcel context used in citizen maps. FBI agent [**Beau Mason**](/distraction_people/Beau_Mason) publicly referenced a **bolt-action rifle** recovered in a wooded area — the charging narrative's physical anchor ([Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location)).

## Open records questions

Several public records would help settle the property question. **Utah County recorder** deeds for the gun-drop coordinates would identify the parcel owner, and separating **UVU institutional property** from any private holdings would clarify who controlled access. No adjudicated evidence supports any claim that **Palantir or any company directed the shooting**.

We do **not** claim Palantir, UVU, or any property owner knew of or participated in the assassination.

See also [University House Ownership](/Locations/University-House-Ownership) for the parallel **university-owned** claim.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The property's title and ownership-transfer records, any financial wire transfers tied to its purchase, and who authorized access around the event are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Online OSINT continues to probe **parcel ownership and fence lines** near the **wooded recovery** area, including threads that name **Palentire**-related property interests as adjacent to the flee/recovery corridor. These remain **unverified property-research claims**. No living owner is accused here of participating in the assassination or planting evidence. The disciplined question is geographic: **who controlled access** to the wooded strip when K-9s and officers swept it? Related context appears in the [Wooded Area Gun Recovery](./Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery) section.
