# Dairy Queen — Orem (813 E 800 N)

The **Dairy Queen** at **813 E 800 N, Orem, UT 84097** is a recurring location in citizen-investigator threads because the accused, [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), was reportedly captured on **restaurant CCTV at approximately 6:38 PM MDT on September 10, 2025** — roughly six hours after the fatal shooting at [UVU](/UVU/overview).

## What reporting and posts describe

According to [@HustleBitch_ on X](https://x.com/HustleBitch_/status/1968828220859441591) and amplification by [@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens), an **unreleased or partially released photo** shows Robinson at this Dairy Queen in a **maroon shirt, blue jeans, hat, and sunglasses**, appearing calm. Project notes place the restaurant about **17 minutes' drive** from the campus crime scene ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) researcher notes).

The charging affidavit timeline includes a **6:38 PM** Dairy Queen entry after the **12:23 PM** shooting, roof escape, and **wooded-area gun drop** — which raises **timing and vehicle** questions reviewed on [Tyler Robinson — Travel](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel).

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Posts circulating on X place **charged suspect Tyler Robinson** on **Orem Dairy Queen CCTV around 6:38 PM on September 10, 2025** — roughly **six hours** after the UVU shooting. Timeline analysts use this pin to argue he remained in the **Orem/UVU orbit** long after the manhunt narrative began, and to demand full **release of the raw CCTV** with timestamps.

As with other fixed-camera stops, the claim is **reportage and charging-narrative geography**, not a conviction. Open questions: chain of custody of the DQ footage; whether clothing matches roof or surrender images; how this coheres with wooded-area rifle recovery timing.
