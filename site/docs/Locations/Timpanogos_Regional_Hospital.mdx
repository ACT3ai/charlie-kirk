# Timpanogos Regional Hospital (Orem)

**Timpanogos Regional Hospital** in **Orem, Utah** is the facility where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was **transported after the shooting** and where he was **pronounced dead** on **September 10, 2025**, per governor statements, FBI releases, and major news outlets ([@ian_carroll compilation](/Influencers/x/ian-carroll), AP/CNN live coverage).

## Distance and transport

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Distance from [UVU courtyard](./Public-Speaking-Event-UVU) | Roughly **2.6–5.8 miles** depending on route source |
| Drive time cited | **~12–15 minutes** in project notes |
| Transport vehicle | Witness threads describe a **black SUV**, not a lighted ambulance ([Medical/Hospital](/Medical/Hospital/overview)) |
| Trauma designation | **Level IV** trauma center per hospital classification threads |

## Debates raised on X (attributed claims)

Citizen investigators question whether transport to Timpanogos followed **standard gunshot-wound protocol** when **Utah Valley Hospital** (closer / more capable in some accounts) exists nearby — see [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) hospital-protocol notes and [Medical](/Medical/overview) section for wound analysis (not duplicated here).

Other **attributed** threads (not established facts):

- **Unusually empty ER** the afternoon of Sept 10 — observer claims on X
- **Andrew Zenger** appointed CEO **August 19, 2025**, started **August 25** — reportedly treated Kirk; Israel IP search-spike claims around hospital surgeons in July — **speculative OSINT**
- Conflicting reports of a staff file placing Kirk at **Utah Valley Hospital** instead — **unverified**

Memorial flowers and notes appeared at both **UVU** and Timpanogos per news compilations ([george_webb_posts.md](https://github.com/)).

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

**[@NateNewsNow](/Influencers/x/natenewsnow)** (**July 6, 2026**) summarized testimony: after the shot, Kirk was removed in a **"hasty transport"** vehicle to **Timpanogos Hospital**, pronounced dead there; a medical examiner later told investigator **Hull** that Kirk had died. **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** (**November 10, 2025**) pushed a hospital-contractor witness video claiming **Mikey McCoy** went to **physician parking**, picked someone up, and left — a claim McCoy and others have not been adjudicated on; treat as **unverified allegation**.

**Counter-framing:** **[@grok](/Influencers/x/grok)** notes the SUV run (~4 miles), an approaching ambulance they passed, and later doctor comments that the chaos call was reasonable with **no planned event ambulance**. Hospital-choice debates (Level IV Timpanogos vs closer higher-level options) live in [Medical / Hospital Choice](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport). Living persons named in pickup theories are **not** declared participants in any crime here.
