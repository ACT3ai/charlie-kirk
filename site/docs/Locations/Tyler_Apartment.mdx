# Tyler Robinson Residences

Two **southern-Utah residences** are tied to the accused, [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson). Both are roughly **215–250 miles south** of the UVU crime scene, along the I-15 corridor, and both were searched by law enforcement after his surrender.

## The apartment — St. George

Reporting and court records place Robinson's apartment in the **Fossil Hills / "Cottage at Fossil Hills" development in St. George**. This is described as where he lived at the time of the shooting. Police secured the apartment after the surrender was arranged. The unit's street address is withheld here — his former roommate, [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs), is a private individual who has not been charged with any crime, and the exact address adds nothing to the timeline.

## The family home — Washington City

Robinson's **parents' home** is in **Washington City** (often written "Washington, Utah"), near St. George. The street address is withheld here: his parents have not been charged with any crime and are private individuals. According to reporting, his parents recognized him in surveillance photos and convinced him to come to their home on September 11; while there he reportedly implied involvement before agreeing to surrender. Some posts noted the family home address appeared blurred on Google Maps.

## Why these locations matter

The residences anchor the southern end of the **escape-route timeline** — the drive from Orem down I-15 to the St. George/Washington area, including a gas-station stop along the way. They are also the staging point for the surrender that followed. Independent reviewers have asked which residence the surrender party departed from, whether the separate apartment was ever stopped at, and what was recovered in each search.

## I-15 corridor and fixed camera stops

After the [Dairy Queen — Orem](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) (~6:38 PM Sept 10) sighting, reporting places Robinson driving south on **I-15** overnight. The next **confirmed surveillance pin** is the [Cedar City Maverik](./Cedar_City_Maverik) (~7:15–7:17 AM Sept 11) in a **gray Dodge Challenger**. Reviewers ask whether one driver could cover Orem → southern Utah in that window and whether anyone else used the vehicle within 24 hours ([Travel timeline](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel)).

A **retired detective** reportedly accompanied the surrender party from the **Washington City** parents' home to [Washington County Sheriff](./Washington_County_Sheriff) in Hurricane — without a documented stop at the St. George apartment first (open question in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) notes).

**Tyler Robinson** is the accused and is presumed innocent; the account above reflects reporting and allegations, not proven facts. Addresses listed here have circulated in public reporting and court filings.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Citizen timeline maps distinguish **Robinson’s St. George-area apartment** from the **Washington City family home** where parents and **Mike Mitchell** reportedly arranged surrender. Some threads allege an **apartment stop** before WCSO; government-document summaries in research notes often describe a **direct** parents-home → **Hurricane WCSO** path. Resolving that fork matters for **phone/Discord** custody claims.
