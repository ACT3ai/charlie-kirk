# Fourth District Court — Provo

**Utah's Fourth District Court** in **Provo, Utah** is the judicial venue for **State v. Tyler Robinson**, the prosecution of the accused shooter in the Charlie Kirk assassination. [Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **presumed innocent**; this page documents the **court location** and public hearing record, not guilt.

## Judge and appointment context

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Presiding judge | [**Tony Graf**](/court/judge-tony-graf) (Tony F. Graf Jr.) — appointed **May 2, 2025** by Gov. [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) |
| Prior vacancy | [**Robert Lunnen**](/court/judge-robert-lunnen) retired effective **August 1, 2025** |
| First appearance | **September 16, 2025** — video feed from [Utah County Jail](./Utah_County_Jail); **no bail** on aggravated murder charges per reporting |

Subsequent motions — evidence release, discovery disputes, gag orders — are tracked on [Tyler Robinson — Trial](/Tyler_Robinson/Trial/overview) and [court/mirandize](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview).

## Custody chain to the courtroom

Robinson's physical path to Provo hearings:

1. [UVU crime scene](./Public-Speaking-Event-UVU) (Sept 10)
2. [Dairy Queen](./Dairy_Queen_Orem) / [I-15 south](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel) (Sept 10–11)
3. [Cedar City Maverik](./Cedar_City_Maverik) (Sept 11 morning)
4. [Washington County Sheriff — Hurricane](./Washington_County_Sheriff) (surrender Sept 11 evening)
5. [Utah County Jail — Spanish Fork](./Utah_County_Jail) (booked ~Sept 12)
6. **Fourth District Court — Provo** (hearings, often remote video)

## Judicial speech restrictions

Judge Graf's **December 16, 2025** gag order — issued on the court's own motion — is documented on [Censorship — Court Gag Orders](/Censorship/Court_Gag_Orders). That order affects **what witnesses and lawyers may say**, not the physical court address.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Unsealed hearing transcripts, discovery rulings, and gag-order text are among records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may bring into public view.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

**Fourth District Court in Provo** is the physical hub of the **gag-order vs cameras** fight. **March 14, 2026** posts (**[@DerrickEvans4WV](/Influencers/x/derrickevans4wv)**, **[@GuntherEagleman](/Influencers/x/gunthereagleman)**) celebrated rulings keeping **cameras and key documents public**. Other posts describe **Judge Tony Graf**’s gag as silencing parties and a huge potential witness pool (**[@xRevelationOne](/Influencers/x/xrevelationone)**).

**July 2026** prelim days produced the **Bagley body-cam**, **screwdriver**, and **SBI/FBI multi-agency** testimony summaries dominating X. The courthouse is therefore both a **legal venue** and an **information bottleneck**. See [Evidence Sealing 2026](/Legal/Evidence-Sealing-2026), [Trial Press](/Media/tyler-robinson-trial-press).
