# UVU Public Speaking Event (September 10, 2025)

[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot during an **outdoor TPUSA speaking event** in the **Utah Valley University courtyard** in Orem, Utah, on **September 10, 2025**, at approximately **12:23 PM MDT** (with **12:27 PM** variants in some media compilations).

## Event footprint

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Venue** | UVU courtyard / tent stage area |
| **Organizer** | [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) |
| **Confirmed fatal shooting** | Governor, FBI, and multiple news outlets |
| **Death transport** | [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Medical/Hospital/overview) |

Spatial detail: [UVU Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward), [Tent](/UVU/Tent), [Campus maps](/UVU/maps).

## Security and surveillance (claims)

Investigators question:

- **Hybrid security** — TPUSA private detail plus campus/local law enforcement ([Campus Security Gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps))
- **Camera and drone coverage** — what TPUSA/UVU captured vs released ([UVU Surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance), [Cameras overview](/cameras/overview))
- **Rooftop monitoring** of [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)

## Pre-event warnings

UVU **GRAMA emails** ([Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)) show staff discussing threat levels **September 9** — institutional context, not shooter identity.

## Post-event site changes

Rapid **courtyard paving** and landscaping changes are documented in [UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) and [After — Site Changes](/After/Site_Changes_And_Crime_Scene_Handling).

## Shot-origin debates

Competing theories evaluate lines of sight from rooftops, tent equipment, and adjacent structures ([Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations), [Ballistics Topic Analysis](/Topic-Analyses/Ballistics_And_Surveillance_Claims)).

[@HustleBitch_ on X](https://x.com/HustleBitch_/status/1968679380298703070) amplified claims that a UVU journalist **slipped** and said the killer was **"there and there"** — interpreted by some as multiple positions, not a single rooftop ([Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations)). Other posts flagged a **trap door** at the shot location and rapid **site rebuilding** ([@HustleBitch_](https://x.com/HustleBitch_/status/1968709782371541192)) — documented as **claims** on [After — Site Changes](/After/Site_Changes_And_Crime_Scene_Handling).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The unreleased UVU security-camera footage, records of who set the event's security posture and access control, and crime-scene chain-of-custody logs are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Event-footprint posts emphasize **under-staffed elevated security**: **six UVU PD** officers, private detail ~**12**, **rooftops unsecured**, and alleged **drone denial** by **Chief Jeff Long** (**[@JohnParson35771](/Influencers/x/johnparson35771)**, **[@BDPelton](/Influencers/x/bdpelton)**). Post-event, **paving / soil removal** claims (**[@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra)** / Dan Merrell; **Hardscape** ghost-crew posts by **[@HustleBitch_](/Influencers/x/hustlebitch)**) dominate location forensics. The speaking site is both a **crime scene** and a **rapidly altered landscape**. Related: [UVU Campus Police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police), [Crime Scene Handling](/Law_Enforcement/crime-scene-handling).
