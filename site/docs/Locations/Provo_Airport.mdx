# Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)

**Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)** is the airfield closest to Utah Valley University — roughly a 10–12 minute drive — which puts it at the center of several **flight-related questions** raised in the investigation. The items below are reported claims and open questions, not established facts.

## Reported points of interest

- **Badge-access list updated 9/11/25** — investigators and online researchers have asked why the airport's badge-access list was reportedly modified the day after the shooting (CK strange event **#8**). Commentary has also raised questions about **who badged in foreign nationals**, whether entry logs were complete, and whether any surveillance footage was deleted — **these are open questions, not established facts**.
- **Duncan Aviation** — a full-service maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) facility based at PVU. CK_FILE cites **Duncan Aviation sources** claiming **4–6 subcontractors** boarded the Egyptian jet with **Egyptian passports** but badges reading **"U.S. Department of Defense Liaison."**
- **Egyptian jet "SU-BTT"** — an Egyptian-registered Falcon that, per [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' flight-tracking threads, flew **direct from France (Paris) on September 4** and stayed at Provo until departing to **Wilmington, DE at ~7:08 MT on Sept 10**, then on to **Cairo Sept 11**. A companion tail, **SU-BTU**, flew into Provo **May 27** with [**Walid Mahmoud**](/People/walid-mahmoud) aboard (departed June 2). CK notes **every other** SU-BTT U.S. trip went to an **army base** — Provo was the anomaly.
- **"Counter-UAS" testing claim** — posts allege counter-drone (Counter-Unmanned Aerial Systems) gear was tested at the airport between roughly September 4 and 10, with a **"No-scan priority clearance"** federal override logged Sept 4. This is an **unverified claim**.
- **N888KG departure** — a white private jet (owner **N888KG LLC**, registered **Lehi, UT**; [**Derek Maxfield**](/People/derek-maxfield) listed in some databases) reportedly departed PVU at **~7:12 PM MDT**, roughly an hour after the shooting; its flight path is the subject of a drop-off theory toward **Page, Arizona**. The tail number **n888kg** was **searched on Google Trends in Utah on Sept 9** — the day before the killing.
- **SLC diversion question** — in her Nov 17, 2025 podcast, **Candace Owens** flagged as strange that Charlie was routed toward **Salt Lake City (~45 miles north)** rather than the much closer **PVU (~7 miles)** where SU-BTT was based.

## Why this location matters

Because PVU is the nearest airfield to the crime scene, it is the natural focal point for any theory involving rapid air movement before or after the event. The aviation questions here connect to the broader **Planes** and **Drones** threads of the investigation, where flight records, tail numbers, and transponder data are analyzed in detail.

Many of the specific claims above originate from social-media flight-tracking threads and have not been independently confirmed. They are presented as reported questions for further verification.

## X.com / flight-tracking threads

[@RealGeorgeWebb1](https://x.com/RealGeorgeWebb1/status/1972596043079655624) claimed a **Younique**-linked plane made **three round trips to Page, Arizona** on the day of the murder — tying PVU to the broader [N888KG departure theory](/Planes/overview). Israel IP search-spike posts (compiled in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/)) allege **"UVU Rooftop"** and campus layout searches **May–July 2025** — **OSINT claims**, not proof of foreknowledge.

Deeper dives: [Proof Intel — Egyptian Jets](/Proof_Intel_Services/Egyptian_Jets_Provo), [Proof Intel — Counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS), [Drones](/Drones/overview).


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Provo’s airport appears in citizen work as a **regional aviation node** for tails discussed in the [Planes](/Planes/overview) section (including **N1098L** threads). Separately, hearing notes on X say investigators used **St. George Airport** the night of the surrender — a different field, but part of the same **Utah air mobility** picture. Flight-tracking claims are **OSINT hypotheses** unless matched to ADS-B/primary logs. Do not treat airport proximity as proof of operational involvement by any pilot or passenger.
