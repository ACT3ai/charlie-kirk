# Utah County Jail (Spanish Fork)

The **Utah County Jail** — also called the **Utah County Security Center** — at **3075 N Main St, Spanish Fork, UT 84660**, is where the accused, [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), has been held since on or around **September 12, 2025**.

## What the records show

- Robinson was **booked** into the facility after federal/state investigators took custody overnight on September 12 (**Booking #460956**).
- Reporting describes him being held in a **high-security / special housing unit**, initially on **suicide watch** and under the most restrictive custody level.
- He has **appeared at Utah County court hearings — often by video feed — from this facility**, including his first appearance on September 16, 2025 before [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).

## Transport from Hurricane

Federal and state investigators took custody at the [Washington County Sheriff's Office](./Washington_County_Sheriff) around **2:00 AM on September 12, 2025** and drove north roughly **250+ miles** (3.5–4+ hours) to Utah County. Public reporting does **not** pin a formal police interrogation to a single location on Sept 12; Washington County explicitly said it did **not** question Robinson ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) custody notes).

## Why this location matters

The jail is the custody location for the duration of the pretrial and trial process, and the point from which Robinson participates in hearings before [Fourth District Court — Provo](./Fourth_District_Court_Provo). The conditions of confinement (isolation, restricted custody) and the use of remote video appearances are recurring topics in coverage of the proceedings.

**Tyler Robinson** is the accused and is presumed innocent. This page reflects booking records and reporting, not proven facts.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

X hearing notes place **formal detention ~4:00 AM September 12** after the overnight drive from **Washington County** to **Utah County** booking. Citizen analysts use the jail as the endpoint that makes **phone seizure**, **Miranda**, and **Discord** timing testable. See [Utah County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/utah-county-sheriff) for the officer chain.
