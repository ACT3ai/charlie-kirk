# Utah County Jail (Spanish Fork)

The **Utah County Jail** — also called the **Utah County Security Center** — at **3075 N Main St, Spanish Fork, UT 84660**, is where the accused, **Tyler Robinson**, has been held since on or around **September 12, 2025**.

## What the records show

- Robinson was **booked** into the facility after federal/state investigators took custody overnight on September 12 (**Booking #460956**).
- Reporting describes him being held in a **high-security / special housing unit**, initially on **suicide watch** and under the most restrictive custody level.
- He has **appeared at Utah County court hearings — often by video feed — from this facility**, including his first appearance on September 16, 2025 before Judge Tony Graf.

## Why this location matters

The jail is the custody location for the duration of the pretrial and trial process, and the point from which Robinson participates in hearings. The conditions of confinement (isolation, restricted custody) and the use of remote video appearances are recurring topics in coverage of the proceedings.

**Tyler Robinson** is the accused and is presumed innocent. This page reflects booking records and reporting, not proven facts.
