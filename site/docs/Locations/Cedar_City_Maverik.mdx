# Cedar City Maverik Gas Station (I-15)

The **Maverik** gas station in **Cedar City, Utah** (on **I-15**, roughly **215–250 miles south** of [UVU](/UVU/overview)) is a **confirmed surveillance point** on Tyler Robinson's southbound drive after the September 10 shooting.

## What the record describes

According to [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) researcher compilation and consistent reporting:

| Detail | Source type |
|--------|-------------|
| **Date/time:** Sept 11, ~**7:15–7:17 AM** MDT | Surveillance stills referenced in reporting |
| **Vehicle:** Gray **Dodge Challenger** | Same vehicle tied to southern-Utah residences |
| **Payment:** Credit card at pump | No approach to trash cans (initial dump-alert cleared) |
| **Direction:** Southbound on I-15 toward St. George / Washington City area | Fits overnight drive narrative |

This stop sits **between** the [Dairy Queen — Orem](./Dairy_Queen_Orem) (~6:38 PM Sept 10) sighting and Robinson's arrival at his family's **[Washington City](./Tyler_Apartment)** home before the evening surrender at [Washington County Sheriff](./Washington_County_Sheriff).

## Why investigators focus here

- **Hard timestamp** on a public camera — rare fixed point in a contested timeline
- **Vehicle identification** — links Orem-area events to southern-Utah custody chain
- **Trash-can alert** — authorities initially asked stations to check for dumped evidence; Robinson reportedly did not use trash cans

**Tyler Robinson** is the accused and is presumed innocent. We do **not** claim Maverik staff or other customers were involved in the assassination.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Pump video, card-authorization logs, and geofence dumps for this Maverik on September 11 are among records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may compel.
