## Utah — The Event Area

The immediate area in and around **Orem, Utah** (Utah County, Utah Valley region) where the shooting, the death, and the cover-up questions are anchored. Charlie Kirk was shot at **Utah Valley University** on September 10, 2025, at approximately 12:23 p.m. MDT during an outdoor [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) speaking event in the campus courtyard. Local law enforcement includes Orem Police, Utah County Sheriff's Office, and UVU campus police; the FBI took the federal lead on the investigation.

## How to use this index

This page is a navigation aid. The locations cluster into three stories: the **crime scene** (UVU campus, the courtyard, the Losee Center rooftop, the hospital), the **suspect's custody chain** (his residences, the Hurricane sheriff's office, the Spanish Fork jail, and the Provo court), and the **aviation questions** (Provo airport and the flights tied to it). Start with whichever story you came here to understand.

The confirmed locations are where the verifiable record lives — surveillance footage, charging documents, booking records, and the physical crime scene. The reported-claim locations come from open-source flight-tracking and research threads; they are included because they recur across the investigation, but each is flagged on its own page as unverified until records confirm it. Treat the two categories differently as you read.

What this section is ultimately testing is whether the official account holds together in physical space — whether the movements, response times, sight lines, and travel paths are consistent with the timeline. For the spatial detail behind these places, follow the links into [UVU](/UVU/overview), [Maps](/maps/overview), [Planes](/Planes/overview), and [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview).
