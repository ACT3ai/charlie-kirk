# Valhalla Danish House

## Overview

The **Valhalla Danish House** is a location that has come to the attention of investigators due to two converging threads: Kash Patel's striking use of the word "Valhalla" at the post-assassination press conference, and the discovery that [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) reportedly had a photo of nearby Goat Island on his computer desktop.

According to reporting by [@MJTruthUltra on X](https://x.com/MJTruthUltra/status/2046416354454990874?s=20), Goat Island is located just down the road from the Valhalla Danish House. The poster notes that there is no documented proof of any direct connection between these locations and the assassination — but the geographic proximity, combined with Kash Patel's statement, has generated significant investigative interest.

At the post-assassination press conference, Kash Patel made a statement that investigators have found unusual. According to the transcribed video, he said:

> "To my friend Charlie Kirk, rest now brother, we have the watch, and I'll see you in Valhalla."

Observers have raised several questions about this statement:

- **Why Valhalla?** "Valhalla" is a Norse mythological concept (the hall of slain warriors), not a phrase commonly used in law-enforcement press conferences.
- **"We have the watch"** — what does this phrase mean in this context?
- **The number 33** — Kash Patel reportedly referenced the "33-hour manhunt" in the same press conference, which some observers have flagged as Masonic signaling. No authoritative explanation of these phrases has been offered.

These are open investigative questions. No evidence has been presented that definitively connects Kash Patel to any wrongdoing. He was FBI Director at the time and attended the press conference in an official capacity.

### Key Statements from Transcript

From the transcribed video:

> "…the initial press conference held by the supposed head of the FBI, Kash Patel, who said a lot of nonsensical things behind that podium. And no one has ever explained it. What does it mean to see? 'I'll see you in Valhalla, Charlie.' What is the significance of the number 33? What does that mean? I don't know. No one's ever been compelled to answer that question."

On the quality of released evidence:

> "They released video of the supposed killer Tyler Robinson jumping off like a 20 foot roof. It was a very bad quality video, even [though] they had high quality video on an entire campus. They released this ridiculous absurd 1973 quality VHS tape video of the supposed killer."

On George Zinn:

> "We're supposed to believe that the guy in the crowd, George Zinn, had been in various other hotspots, like the Boston Marathon bombing. And he'd been a witness at 9-11."

These are claims made by a social media commentator. They represent investigative questions, not established facts.
