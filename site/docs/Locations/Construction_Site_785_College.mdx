# Construction Site — 785 College Dr, Orem

An active **construction site** at **785 College Dr, Orem, UT 84058** (north of the [UVU](/UVU/overview) campus area) appears in both the **official movement narrative** and **alternate-shooter** threads. Investigators map it because a worker there described seeing someone in **all-black tactical clothing** close to the time of the shooting — a description that diverged from the later focus on [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) in a T-shirt and jeans.

## Dylan Hope eyewitness account (attributed)

According to commentary compiled in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) and social posts, [**Dylan Hope**](/People/dylan-hope), a construction worker on site, described a man in a **black trench coat, black cargo pants, and long greasy hair** — matching early **police dispatch** language about a suspect in **all black with a tactical helmet and mask**.

Project notes also state that a person in black encountered the crew after the shooting; law enforcement reportedly showed workers a photo that **did not match** Tyler Robinson ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) — construction-site note).

We do **not** assert that Dylan Hope, any construction worker, or any person in black clothing committed a crime or knew of the assassination in advance.

## Geographic role

Researcher maps place this site on the **path Robinson may have walked** after leaving the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop sequence — connecting the construction zone to the [wooded gun-recovery area](./Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery) and [gun-drop property leads](./University-House-Ownership).

[Google Maps reference](https://maps.app.goo.gl/oSFBPJTg6tCgWJ787) — 785 College Dr, Orem.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Worker statements, site security cameras, and dispatch audio tying this address to the first-hour suspect description are records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may bring into public view.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

**@ProjectConstitu** and related threads amplify **Dylan Hope**’s account that a construction colleague spoke with a man in **all-black tactical clothing** who asked to pass through the site **immediately after the shooting**, saying he was trying to get home safe — reportedly **before sirens**. Citizen investigators claim officers later **canine-tracked** that path and that early **dispatch** described a **tactical helmet / mask** figure matching roof video, not later T-shirt imagery.

This location is therefore treated as a **competing-suspect geography** problem, not a decorative map pin. All accounts here are **attributed eyewitness/social claims**. Hope and the unnamed excavator operator are living private persons; no crime is alleged against them. See [Suspect Manhunt](/Law_Enforcement/suspect-manhunt).
