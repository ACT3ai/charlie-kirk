# Wooded Area — Gun Recovery (North of UVU)

The **wooded area north of the Utah Valley University campus** is where charging documents say a **Mauser Model 98 .30-06 bolt-action rifle with scope** was found — reportedly **wrapped in a dark towel** — after the suspect fled across [Campus Drive](/UVU/overview) and through trees at the campus edge.

## Affidavit movement (summary)

Per the probable-cause narrative summarized in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) and [Gun_Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) pages:

| Time (MDT) | Location action |
|------------|-----------------|
| ~12:24 PM | Suspect runs north across Campus Drive, enters wooded edge |
| Later | **Shoe impression** consistent with Converse/Chuck Taylor sneakers near recovery zone |
| Recovery | Scoped **Mauser .30-06** assembled in woods |

FBI agent [**Beau Mason**](/distraction_people/Beau_Mason) referenced a **"high powered bolt action rifle"** recovered in a wooded area at a press briefing (see [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview)).

## Property-boundary debates
