# Patrick Bet-David — PBD Podcast

**Patrick Bet-David** hosts the **PBD Podcast**, one of the highest-production-value independent political interview programs in America. Bet-David is the founder of Valuetainment Media and brings a business entrepreneur's analytical framework to political investigations. His program is known for hosting panels that actively debate alternative theories, government overreach, and contested explanations for major political events.

- **YouTube:** [PBD Podcast on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@PBDPodcast)

## Coverage Approach

The PBD Podcast, according to its published content, hosts panels that actively debate conspiracy theories, government overreach, and alternative motives behind major political events. For the Charlie Kirk investigation, this format is valuable — Bet-David tends to bring together guests with different perspectives and let them argue their cases, which can surface evidence and counter-evidence more effectively than single-perspective commentary.

Bet-David appeared at TPUSA's AmericaFest 2024 (December 19–24, 2024), giving him direct familiarity with the TPUSA network and its key personnel in the months before Kirk's death.

## Why This Program Matters

Bet-David's business audience skews toward professionals and entrepreneurs who may be skeptical of both mainstream media and partisan political commentary. The PBD Podcast can reach an audience that other programs in this list cannot — people who would dismiss InfoWars or Steve Bannon but will sit through a 3-hour structured debate format that presents evidence systematically.

For investigators, any PBD Podcast episode featuring guests with direct knowledge of the Kirk case, the TPUSA organization, or the intelligence angles of the investigation would be high-value source material.
