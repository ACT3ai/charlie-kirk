# Steve Bannon — War Room

**Steve Bannon** hosts **War Room**, a high-frequency political program that airs multiple episodes per day and has built one of the most engaged audiences in conservative independent media. Bannon has deep ties to the America First movement and a history of covering allegations that federal agencies and foreign actors are actively working to subvert that movement.

- **YouTube search:** [Steve Bannon War Room on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Steve+Bannon+War+Room)

## Coverage Angle

War Room, according to its published content, routinely hosts guests who argue that federal agencies and foreign actors are actively subverting the America First movement — the political movement that Charlie Kirk helped build through TPUSA. Bannon's program treats the Kirk assassination as part of a larger pattern of targeted political violence against the America First leadership class, rather than as an isolated event by a lone gunman.

Key themes covered by War Room in relation to the Kirk assassination:
- Federal agency weaponization against America First figures
- Foreign actor involvement in domestic political violence
- The suppression of investigative journalism and citizen research
- Political context of why Kirk was a high-value target

## Bannon's Unique Position

Bannon appeared at TPUSA's AmericaFest 2024 (December 19–24, 2024) alongside Tucker Carlson, Donald Trump, and others — giving him direct familiarity with the TPUSA network, its internal dynamics, and the political environment in the months before Kirk's death.

His experience running the Breitbart News network and advising the Trump White House gives him sources and institutional knowledge relevant to understanding who controls federal agencies and how political investigations are conducted or suppressed.
