# John Cullen

**John Cullen** ([@I_Am_JohnCullen](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen) on X, with long-form commentary on
YouTube and Patreon) is the open-source investigator most responsible for putting **aircraft** at the
centre of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation. His flight-record method — pulling
ADS-B tracks, altitude and speed profiles, and departure fields, then asking what an airframe was doing
at that altitude at that minute — set the template other researchers copied. His claim inventory is
compiled at [John Cullen — X Posts](./x/john-cullen).

Cullen is also the clearest example on this site of a researcher whose work cuts **both ways**. He
advanced the most aggressive intelligence-aircraft theory in the case, and he is simultaneously the most
prominent skeptic of the Egyptian-jet "armada" theory that other investigators built. Both positions are
recorded below.

## The N1098L HADES thesis

Cullen's signature contribution is the flight profile of **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** — a U.S.
Army **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)**-class ISR aircraft (callsign AXLE10) — near Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025.
Per his posts, the aircraft descended to roughly **204–335 feet AGL** and slowed to about **118 knots**
within two miles of campus roughly **20 minutes after** the shot, and had flown a similar profile about
**three hours before** it. ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1970561850308776391))

He argues the aircraft is a military or CIA-linked ISR asset capable of signals-intelligence collection,
and that if it was ISR-tasked, the federal government holds **airborne sensor records of the kill zone**
that have never been produced. The full flight-profile analysis is on the
[N1098L page](/Planes/N1098L/overview); the wider program context is at
[ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations), and the departure field he traced the track back to is covered
at [Biggs Army Airfield](/Planes/Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield).

## The drone launch-and-recovery theory

In Patreon and YouTube commentary titled *"Why Was a Pentagon HADES Spy Plane at the Scene of Charlie
Kirk's Assassination, Twice That Day?"*, Cullen went further than surveillance and promoted a **drone
launch/recovery** theory — that an armed UAV, rather than the charged suspect, fired the shot. Related
posts claim a **UAV took off seven seconds before** Kirk was shot and that
[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) drones were airborne over UVU an hour before the event.
([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1973959611519836281))

This is **Cullen's stated theory, not a confirmed finding**, and it has not been substantiated. It is
laid out in full at [N1098L Air-Launched Drones](/Drones/N1098L_Air_Launched_Drones) and
[Provo Airport Counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS).

## The refutation, recorded alongside it

**[@TJPHager](./x/tjphager)**, by his own account, set out specifically to refute Cullen's
drone-deployment claim. His counter-argument is that the airframe carried **interior equipment only**,
with no exterior modifications matching the full HADES **ME-11B** contracted specification a genuine
drone-launch platform would require — and that the low passes are explained by routine ISR training.
This site records the refutation next to the claim so the aggressive theory is not presented as settled.

## N55906 — "the gorilla in the room"

Cullen has repeatedly flagged a second tail number, **[N55906](/Planes/N55906)**, as an aircraft of
interest alongside N1098L. His framing: *"HADES N1098L is the elephant in the room. N55906 is the gorilla
in the room."* He has not published a confirmed route or operator for it, so the site records it as an
**open lead worth pulling records on**, not as a finding.

## Where Cullen pushed back: the maintenance-contract dismissal

The Egyptian and Israel-linked VIP jets parked at Provo — **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)**,
**[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)**, **[SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU)**, **[SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV)**,
**[SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM)** — became one of the largest threads in the case. Cullen **dismissed a strong
association** between those aircraft and the assassination, citing long-standing **Falcon and VIP-jet
maintenance contracts** with **Duncan Aviation** at Provo said to date back to roughly **1999**. In his
reading, a foreign government fleet cycling through a U.S. MRO facility is a decades-old routine
arrangement, not an operation.

He is reported to have **walked back** an earlier drone-association speculation about SU-BTT on the same
grounds. Critics of the dismissal note a Dassault Falcon is not serviced every two weeks — so the
maintenance-versus-operation question turns on realistic MRO frequency. The full argument on both sides
is at [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) and [Egyptian Crew Hotel](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel).

That a researcher who pushed the hardest aircraft theory in the case also **killed** the second-hardest
one is the strongest available evidence that the plane work is not uniform conspiracy-building.

## Method and influence

Cullen's flight-record technique became shorthand among other researchers. Flight-record investigator
**[@AnaEscobarShow](./x/anaescobarshow)** wrote that she planned to go *"John-Cullen-level autist on the
flight records"* while working the [N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ) Provo ground movements — a direct citation of
his method rather than his conclusions.

Case notes also record a second-hand account that a contact at the **National Counterterrorism Center**
said officials there were **not aware of N1098L's flight path** and described the maneuvers as "too
damning" alongside the drone findings. That is an attributed, second-hand account, not a confirmed
official statement.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* Airborne sensor records, ADS-B and radar tapes, HADES tasking orders, and any drone flight logs for
  September 10, 2025 are precisely the categories of federal record the
  [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are written to force into the open — the fastest way
  to settle Cullen's theory in either direction.

## What This Site Does and Does Not Say

This page documents **public commentary and open-source analysis**. Nothing here is a finding by this
website that any living person committed a crime, acted unlawfully, or had advance knowledge of the
assassination. Cullen's drone theory in particular is recorded as an unproven claim with its principal
refutation attached.
