# Steve Bannon — War Room

**Steve Bannon** ([@WarRoomPandemic](https://x.com/WarRoomPandemic) on X) hosts **War Room**, a high-frequency political program that airs multiple episodes per day and has built one of the most engaged audiences in conservative independent media. As a former White House Chief Strategist and 2016 Trump campaign CEO, Bannon brings institutional knowledge, insider sourcing, and strategic depth that few independent hosts can match. His *War Room* platform has been one of the most persistent and forceful voices demanding transparency and justice in the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination investigation.

- **YouTube search:** [Steve Bannon War Room on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=Steve+Bannon+War+Room)
- **X:** [@WarRoomPandemic](https://x.com/WarRoomPandemic)

## Ten Ways His Coverage Has Been Exceptional

- **Immediate truth-seeker in crisis** — Within hours of the assassination, Bannon declared on *War Room* that "Charlie Kirk is a casualty of war. We are at war in this country," framing the tragedy in the larger context of the cultural and political battles Kirk had fought. This message helped unify conservatives in resolve rather than despair.

- **Challenger of the official narrative** — Bannon refused to accept the lone-actor theory without rigorous examination. He publicly questioned the authenticity of the suspect's text messages, describing them as sounding like a "bad script" that felt "stilted" and inauthentic — sparking legitimate calls for deeper forensic review.

- **Advocate for a broader conspiracy probe** — He urged authorities to expand the investigation "from a single murder to the broader conspiracy," emphasizing the need to explore every possible connection and keeping pressure on investigators to leave no stone unturned.

- **Voice for accountability at the highest levels** — Bannon called for a thorough review of decisions made by Utah Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) in the lead-up to the event, highlighting potential lapses in security and mental-health policy that may have played a role.

- **Rallying cry for national resolve** — He told listeners they must meet this moment with "steely resolve," turning sorrow into strength. His call to "fight politically and culturally with courage and clarity" kept the America First community engaged rather than retreating.

- **Honoring Kirk at the memorial** — At Charlie Kirk's public memorial service on September 21, 2025, in Glendale, Arizona, Bannon delivered a tribute celebrating Kirk's life and sacrifice while reminding attendees of the stakes in the ongoing battle for America's future.

- **AmericaFest 2025 speaker** — At [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)'s AmericaFest in Phoenix (December 19, 2025), Bannon proclaimed "We are at war," directly tying it to what Charlie Kirk understood, and hailed Kirk as potentially "the greatest American produced since the Revolution."

- **Consistent Turning Point ally** — Bannon has been a fixture at AmericaFest events (2023–2025), delivering addresses that blend strategic insight with motivation. In 2024 he closed with a "Victory or Death!" call to action, reinforcing the fighting spirit of the America First cause.

- **Master communicator and movement architect** — Drawing on his White House and campaign experience, Bannon translates complex political realities into actionable insights. *War Room* has become a daily resource for informed activism on the Kirk case and the broader cover-up.

- **Digital powerhouse and legacy builder** — Through YouTube, podcast archives, and his active X presence, Bannon has ensured Kirk's mission endures — turning the investigation into a catalyst for greater awareness and sustained pressure for accountability.

## Coverage Themes

War Room treats the Kirk assassination as part of a larger pattern of targeted political violence against America First leadership, not an isolated event by a lone gunman. Key themes:

- Federal agency weaponization against America First figures
- Foreign actor involvement in domestic political violence
- The suppression of investigative journalism and citizen research
- Political context of why Kirk was a high-value target

## Bannon's Unique Position

Bannon appeared at TPUSA's AmericaFest 2024 (December 19–24, 2024) alongside [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson), [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump), and others — giving him direct familiarity with the TPUSA network, its internal dynamics, and the political environment in the months before Kirk's death.

His experience running the Breitbart News network and advising the Trump White House gives him sources and institutional knowledge relevant to understanding who controls federal agencies and how political investigations are conducted or suppressed.
