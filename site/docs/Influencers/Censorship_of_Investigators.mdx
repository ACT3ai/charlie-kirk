# Censorship of Investigators

Citizen investigators say suppression accelerated as **[Candace Owens](./podcasts-candace-owens)**
and others approached disclosures that would challenge the official narrative. This page compiles
**reported** censorship threads — not proven coordination by any named entity.

## Mass X suspensions (claims)

Compiled social posts describe **thousands of accounts** receiving suspensions, 12-hour locks,
**shadowbans**, and "limited visibility" warnings when users like, retweet, or reply to
[@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) posts about the Kirk assassination.

Commentary attributes the pattern to:

- Coordinated **bot-report** campaigns ("violent speech," "harassment" templates)
- Fear of receipts on **Egyptian planes**, **maroon shirts**, **Israeli Google Trends**, and
  **TPUSA donor** threads

We **do not** claim any specific company or donor **illegally** operated bot farms; we document the
allegation because it affects whether open-source evidence survives on X.

See also [Censorship section](/Censorship/overview) for statutory speech restrictions.

## FBI witness video deletion (claim)

Witness **Ryne Simmons** reportedly sent **4K 60fps close-up** footage to the FBI and was
**instructed to delete it** instead of it entering the investigative record, per amplification of
[@ninoboxer](https://x.com/ninoboxer/status/1982164944914162103).

If true, this is direct **federal pressure on a citizen witness** — distinct from platform moderation.
Simmons allegedly **preserved a copy** anyway (claim).

## TPUSA cease-and-desist (documented in commentary)

[Candace Owens](./podcasts-candace-owens) reported TPUSA legal threats to **[@FinaceWolves](/Influencers/x/financewolves) (Zach)**
over drone-footage contradictions — [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) is reported by Owens to have **stated** that a media
production drone flew before the event while **a counter-drone request from security was denied**. The letter itself
has not been published in full, so the wording remains an attributed characterization rather than a documented
admission. See [Charlie day-of security](/Charlie/Day_Of_Security).

[Project Constitution](./podcasts-project-constitution) received a **cease-and-desist** from TPUSA/Erika
side per their reporting — treat as allegation until the letter is published in full.

## Search and platform scrubbing (claims)

Commentary in the master file alleges **Google scrubbing** of Kirk–Israel searches, **fact-check**
laundering, and eyewitness pressure (e.g., [Skylar Baird](/People/skylar-baird) allegedly asked to delete phone footage).
Each item needs primary documentation; they are listed because influencers cite them as motive for
moving evidence to [IPFS](/IPFS/ipfs.txt) and this site.

## Why this matters to the investigation

Censorship does not prove a cover-up by itself — but the reported pattern is offered as an explanation for
**gaps in the public record**: the alleged deleted witness video, the alleged throttling of flight-track
threads, and the reported legal chill on people close to TPUSA. Each of those is an attributed claim, not
an established fact. The
[Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure framework is partly designed to recover what platform
suppression and gag orders keep offline. The suppression of citizen leads also has an inside-government
parallel: former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his center's own review of
potential foreign ties was ordered to stop while leads were still open.
