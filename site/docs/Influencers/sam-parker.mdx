# Sam Parker

**Sam Parker** posts as **[@BasedSamParker](https://x.com/BasedSamParker)** and
**[@SamParkerSenate](https://x.com/SamParkerSenate)**. He is one of the highest-volume citizen
investigators on the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case, and his output falls into four distinct
lanes: **second-by-second timeline reconstruction**, **Google Trends search-correlation graphics**, an
**Israel-motive thesis**, and **document-versus-testimony comparison** from the court record. His raw
claim inventories are compiled at [Based Sam Parker — X Posts](./x/based-sam-parker) and
[SamParkerSenate — X Posts](./x/samparkersenate).

Parker's work is used across this site more than almost any other single researcher's — and every one of
his findings below is recorded as an **attributed claim**, not as a site finding.

## The evacuation clock

The reconstruction most other timelines on this site anchor to is Parker's. He places the shot at
roughly **12:23:27–12:23:30 PM MDT** and measures every subsequent event as an offset from that zero
point — security reaching Kirk at about T+18 seconds, SUV load beginning at **12:23:52** in his later
stamp (about 32 seconds post-shot, slightly earlier than the common 12:24:01 figure used elsewhere).

That micro-timing disagreement is a **second-count dispute inside the same clock**, not a competing shot
time — the 12:23 versus 12:27 argument is separate and is worked through at
[Shooting Time Disputes](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time). The full minute-by-minute build is at
[Ten Minutes After the Shot](/Timeline/ten-minutes-after-shot).

## The 911 call inventory

Parker has stated he holds **all twenty 9-1-1 calls** from that day and claims **zero** originated from
TPUSA staff or Kirk's security detail. In parallel compilations he reports that
[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet),
[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), and [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) each indicated
they did not place a 911 call, while [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) has said security
called **on the way to the hospital**.

Carrier records have not been published, so this remains an attributed claim to verify against primary
audio releases. The open question is tracked at
[the 911 call that reportedly never came](/Medical/timpanogos_911_call).

## Document versus testimony

Parker's court-record work compares **what the paperwork says** against **what witnesses testified**.
Two examples recur across this site:

* **The Davis timing conflict.** The booking sheet lists Agent [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) as
  arresting at **10:00 PM on September 11**, while Davis testified he arrived **near midnight**. Parker
  also argues investigators were ordered to fly to **St. George around 8:30 PM** — before the publicized
  ~9:00 PM surrender window. See [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing).
* **The Mitchell statement.** Parker raised questions about **Mike Mitchell's** second sworn statement
  (dated 3/31/2026), filed after Sheriff Brooksby's resignation announcement, citing redactions and
  mismatches with booking-sheet testimony.

Both are presented as **reconstruction problems**, not as court findings and not as accusations against
any named official.

## The Google Trends graphics

Parker's most widely circulated artifacts are annotated **Google Trends** composites, usually carrying
the @SamParkerSenate watermark, arguing that specific names show search spikes **filtered to Israeli or
DC IP regions** at notable dates:

* **Utah's autopsy-photo law (S.B. 82)** — drafting attorney **Greg Gunn** shows Israel-filtered spikes
  in June 2024 and May 2025. Written treatment at
  [Utah Autopsy Law Changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes).
* **Medical examiner and surgeons** — Israeli and DC IP interest in
  [Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro), Timpanogos Regional Hospital, and Utah surgeons in mid-2025.
  See [Surgeon Search Trends](/Suspicious/Medical/surgeon-search-trends).
* **Court personnel** — isolated Israel-region spikes for **[Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)**
  and retiring judge Robert Lunnen in a mid-2025 window.
* **Names before the event** — [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak), Skyler Baird, George Zinn, and Beau
  Mason showing regionally filtered search interest before September 10.

**Methodological caution applies to every one of these.** Google Trends reports *relative, normalized*
interest. A peak of 100 in a low-volume region can rest on a handful of searches, and the tool does not
identify who searched or why. The correlation is recorded as Parker states it; the inference is his. The
broader dataset is at [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview).

## The Israel-motive thesis

Parker's most contested line of work argues Kirk had become a liability to pro-Israel political
interests — citing his role in defeating **HR 867**, his public conflict with the ADL, his opposition to
platform censorship of anti-Israel content, and reported claims that he turned down a large donation and
told people he believed he might be killed. The site treats this as a **motive hypothesis under active
dispute**, laid out with counterarguments at [Israel](/Israel/overview).

## Records and narrative work

* **GRAMA denials** — Parker reported that UVU **denied** a records request for the camera feed from
  **behind Charlie's tent**, citing public-safety and fair-trial grounds. See
  [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).
* **Paving sequencing** — working from Judicial Watch documents, Parker stresses that contractor
  [Daniel Merrell](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah) was the **install crew**, not the origin of the
  excavation decision — an important distinction when mapping who set the schedule.
* **The wound-description reversal** — his June 2026 synthesis documents early "shot in the chest"
  broadcast language flipping to **neck** within roughly an hour. See
  [Shot in Chest Reversal](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The 911 carrier records, the withheld behind-tent camera feed, unredacted sworn statements, and the
  complete booking and arrest paperwork are exactly the documents that would resolve Parker's
  document-versus-testimony conflicts — and are among the records the
  [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are designed to force into public view.

## What This Site Does and Does Not Say

Every Parker claim on this page is recorded as **his public analysis**. This website does not assert as
fact that any living person named here committed a crime, acted unlawfully, or had knowledge of the
assassination in advance. The Google Trends correlations in particular are recorded with their
methodological limits stated alongside them.
