# Ian Carroll

**Ian Carroll** is an independent researcher and content creator who operates the **Cancel This Clothing Co** channel and makes frequent appearances across multiple independent platforms. He is one of the most active independent investigators of the Charlie Kirk assassination, known for systematically dismantling the official FBI narrative with documented evidence.

## Key Coverage: Liberty Vault Playlist

Ian Carroll's most concentrated body of Charlie Kirk investigation coverage is archived in the Liberty Vault playlist on YouTube, which documents his repeated appearances challenging the official narrative.

- **Channel:** [Liberty Vault on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@LibertyVault)
- **Carroll's Channel:** [Cancel This Clothing Co on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@CancelThisClothingCo)

## Investigative Approach

Carroll, according to his published content, takes the position that the FBI's lone-shooter narrative does not hold up under scrutiny. He has, reportedly, collaborated with figures like Dave Smith to explore alternative data points and cover-up theories. His approach is data-driven: he works through the documented evidence systematically rather than relying on unnamed sources or speculation.

Key areas Carroll has reportedly covered:
- Inconsistencies in the official timeline of events on September 10, 2025
- Questions about Tyler Robinson's ability to have carried out the shooting alone
- Alternative data points about who was present at UVU that day
- Analysis of what law enforcement did and did not do in the immediate aftermath

## Hodgetwins Collaboration

Carroll has appeared alongside hosts on programs including the Hodgetwins podcast, where his research on Israeli theories and non-traditional motives was discussed in the context of the Kirk assassination. ([Related Hodgetwins episode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J2DKRfwSt94))
