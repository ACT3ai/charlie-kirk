# The Charlie Kirk Show

**The Charlie Kirk Show** was one of the most-listened-to conservative podcasts in America, hosted by Charlie Kirk before his death on **September 10, 2025**, at Utah Valley University. Kirk used the show to cover conservative politics, culture, and TPUSA events, building an audience of millions of subscribers across podcast platforms and YouTube.

## About the Show

The Charlie Kirk Show was distributed through the Turning Point USA media network and available on all major podcast platforms. It featured guest interviews, political commentary, debate coverage, and live event content from the TPUSA circuit. The show consistently ranked at the top of political podcast charts in the United States.

## Significance to the Investigation

Because Charlie Kirk was the host and subject of the show, his communications, guest relationships, and public statements in the months before his death are direct investigative material. Key areas of relevance:

- **Pre-death appearances** — Kirk's documented public appearances in mid-2025, including the TPUSA Student Action Summit in Tampa (July 11–13, 2025) and the Virginia Tech event (September 4, 2025), were covered through the show's media output. The Virginia Tech appearance was six days before his death.
- **Guest and associate network** — The show's guest history maps Kirk's network of political allies, potential adversaries, and intelligence-adjacent contacts in the period leading up to September 2025.
- **TPUSA internal dynamics** — The show's content reflects the state of TPUSA leadership and internal disputes in the months before September 10, 2025, context that is directly relevant to questions about who may have had motive or foreknowledge.

## Key Episodes and Documented Events

- **May 31, 2025:** Kirk hosted and appeared on stage at the Turning Point Action launch rally for Congressman Andy Biggs' campaign for Arizona governor.
- **July 11–13, 2025:** Kirk appeared at TPUSA's Student Action Summit (SAS) in Tampa, Florida. Megyn Kelly also appeared at this event. ([YouTube coverage](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccRNaLGavf8))
- **September 4, 2025:** Kirk appeared at a TPUSA event at Virginia Tech — his last documented major public appearance before his death six days later. ([YouTube coverage](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0pvEGO4W85k))

## After September 10, 2025

Following Kirk's death, the future of the TPUSA media operation and the Charlie Kirk Show archive became subjects of investigation. The rapid consolidation of TPUSA leadership and decisions about the show's content are open questions that bear on who controls the narrative of Kirk's legacy and what records may be accessible to investigators.
