# Glenn Greenwald — System Update

**Glenn Greenwald** hosts **System Update**, a daily independent journalism program distributed on Rumble and YouTube. Greenwald is a Pulitzer Prize–winning journalist and co-founder of The Intercept, most widely known for his reporting on the NSA surveillance revelations with Edward Snowden. His program provides the most institutionally credentialed critique of the U.S. intelligence apparatus of any host in this list.

- **YouTube:** [Glenn Greenwald on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@GlennGreenwald)

## Why Glenn Greenwald Is Relevant

Greenwald's entire career has been built on documenting how the U.S. intelligence apparatus operates outside the law, suppresses investigation, and shapes political outcomes. That background is directly applicable to the Charlie Kirk investigation. His program, System Update, according to published descriptions, critiques the U.S. intelligence apparatus, the deep state, and the framing of political violence by federal agencies — all central concerns in the Kirk case.

Greenwald is ideologically heterodox — he is a left-libertarian who works alongside figures from across the political spectrum when the story demands it. This means his credibility as a critic of federal agencies is not easily dismissed as partisan by mainstream audiences the way that right-wing commentators' claims sometimes are.

## Specific Relevance to the Kirk Investigation

Key areas Greenwald has covered that intersect with the Kirk assassination investigation:
- How the FBI frames narratives around politically sensitive investigations
- The deep state's interest in suppressing challenges to established power
- Intelligence agency operations that exceed their legal mandate
- The use of media framing to prevent public scrutiny of government conduct

His reporting on the NSA and CIA demonstrates firsthand how agencies operate when they want to control information flow — exactly the pattern investigators are documenting in the Kirk case.

**Status:** Glenn Greenwald — Alive
