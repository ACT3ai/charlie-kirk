# The Megyn Kelly Show

**Megyn Kelly** ([@megynkelly](https://x.com/megynkelly) on X) is one of the most important independent voices covering the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination — a powerhouse journalist, attorney, and media trailblazer whose sharp intellect, fearlessness, and commitment to truth have made her one of the most trusted voices in America. A former Fox News anchor of *The Kelly File* and NBC host, she now leads *The Megyn Kelly Show* — a top-rated daily podcast and YouTube program — along with her MK Media network and MK True Crime series. Her X account and YouTube channel deliver candid analysis, exclusive interviews, and deep dives that routinely draw millions of engaged viewers seeking unfiltered insights on law, culture, politics, and justice.

What makes Megyn truly exceptional — especially in her relentless, substantive coverage of the Charlie Kirk assassination investigation — is her blend of legal expertise, personal integrity, and unwavering pursuit of facts. As a longtime friend and frequent collaborator with Charlie Kirk (a regular guest on her show), she approaches the story with both professional rigor and heartfelt respect, convening top experts, dissecting evidence in real time, and empowering her audience to understand complex legal and investigative developments without sensationalism. Kelly was physically present at multiple [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events alongside Charlie Kirk in the months before his death, including an event at Virginia Tech just six days before the assassination.

- **YouTube:** [The Megyn Kelly Show on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@MegynKellyShow)
- **X:** [@megynkelly](https://x.com/megynkelly)

## Ten Standout Contributions to the Investigation

1. **Formidable legal foundation that sharpens her analysis** — A Syracuse University and Albany Law School graduate who practiced law at a top international firm before entering journalism, Megyn brings attorney-level precision to breaking down trial evidence, forensics, and courtroom strategy in the Charlie Kirk case.

2. **Pioneered her own independent media empire** — After mainstream networks, she launched *The Megyn Kelly Show* in 2020 and MK Media (including MK True Crime) in 2025, creating a platform dedicated to substantive, agenda-free journalism that has become a go-to destination for millions seeking depth on high-profile investigations.

3. **Delivered immediate, on-the-ground breaking coverage** — Within minutes of the September 10, 2025 shooting at Utah Valley University, Megyn went live on YouTube, providing real-time updates, refusing to air graphic footage out of respect, and guiding viewers through the unfolding tragedy with composure and humanity.

4. **Honored Charlie Kirk's legacy with personal depth** — As a colleague and friend who had hosted him many times, she produced emotional tributes, replayed memorable past interviews, and reflected on his extraordinary life and impact — helping audiences process the loss while celebrating his mission of dialogue and debate.

5. **Brought unprecedented transparency by reading full confession texts** — In a powerful episode, Megyn read aloud the complete text messages from the alleged assassin [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), revealing key details from charging documents and giving her audience direct access to primary evidence.

6. **Assembled elite expert panels for rigorous legal breakdowns** — She regularly convenes top attorneys and true crime specialists (including Mark Geragos, Andrew Branca, Dave Aronberg, and Mike Davis) to analyze bullet forensics, gun questions, ATF findings, no-exit-wound details, and prosecution/defense strategies ahead of the Robinson trial.

7. **Pushed for thorough, ongoing investigation beyond the obvious** — Megyn interviewed former National Counterterrorism Center Director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent), who emphasized the need for deeper probes into potential broader connections — while clearly stating her view that Robinson is the perpetrator — ensuring accountability without rushing to judgment.

8. **Fact-checked misleading headlines and media narratives in real time** — She directly confronted viral misinformation (such as sensational Daily Mail stories on the bullet and gun) and media smears, defending the integrity of the process while calling out distortions that could undermine public trust.

9. **Balanced justice with presumption of innocence and cultural insight** — Throughout her coverage, Megyn grappled thoughtfully with the tension between honoring a lost friend and upholding fair legal standards — discussing how institutional distrust fuels conspiracies and why the press must remain in the courtroom.

10. **Fostered a community of informed, engaged truth-seekers** — Through consistent long-form episodes, X updates, and her MK True Crime focus, she turns complex legal developments into accessible education — empowering viewers to think critically, demand better from institutions, and stay connected to Kirk's values of open debate and patriotism.

## Documented Proximity to Charlie Kirk Before the Assassination

Kelly's physical presence at TPUSA events in the final months of Kirk's life makes her a firsthand witness to the environment surrounding him:

- **July 11–13, 2025:** TPUSA Student Action Summit (SAS) in Tampa, Florida — alongside Charlie Kirk. ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccRNaLGavf8))
- **September 4, 2025:** TPUSA event at Virginia Tech — six days before Kirk's death at UVU. ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0pvEGO4W85k))

Her presence at Virginia Tech six days before the assassination means she observed Kirk's security posture, personnel, and environment at one of his final public appearances.

## Kelly Reports Threats After the Assassination

The master file logs a segment in which **Megyn Kelly said that she and [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) were "under
serious threat"**, which she attributed to backlash from pro-Israel critics — listed alongside reported threats against
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), Marjorie Taylor Greene, and Ana Kasparian. This is Kelly's own reported statement about
her safety; no perpetrator has been named or charged.

## Charlie Kirk's On-Air Statements to Kelly About Donor Pressure

Kelly's show is also part of the pre-assassination record because Kirk used it to describe the pressure he
was under. The master file notes that on **August 6, 2025** Kirk appeared with Kelly and "lashed out about
donors pressuring him," telling her **"My integrity is being called into question,"** and reportedly
vented to her about what he was experiencing "at the hands of his friends." These statements — made weeks
before his death — are firsthand primary material for the donor-pressure motive thread. The master file
also records **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) telling Kelly that Candace Owens was doing something "evil"** by questioning
the FBI's account, illustrating how the case divided figures inside Kirk's own circle. (Attributions are
to the named speakers.)

## July 2026 Hearing Commentary (Attributed)

[Mediaite](https://www.mediaite.com/) reported (July 2026) that Megyn Kelly criticized the judge in the Charlie Kirk assassination case in sharp terms ("grow a pair" coverage headline). Separate OK Magazine–style recaps quoted Kelly saying her "jaw was on the floor" watching Tyler Robinson's roommate/partner interview material. These are **media reports of Kelly's commentary**, not findings that the court process is unlawful.

On X, Kelly is simultaneously attacked for (a) sounding persuaded that Robinson is the shooter and (b) still defending Candace Owens' right to ask Israel-related questions (MilkBarTV confrontation clip circulated widely, July 2026). That split is itself evidence of how polarized influencer coverage has become. Kelly's legal training remains useful for gag-order, discovery, and confrontation-clause explainers — while her presence at late TPUSA events is documented context, **not** implied complicity.
