# YouTube Coverage

Long-form video remains the primary medium for **frame-by-frame forensics**, **expert interviews**,
and **hour-long timelines** the Kirk case demands. Podcast hosts often publish to YouTube simultaneously;
this page highlights **video-first** channels and playlists.

## Witness video suppression (claim)

Commentary alleges the FBI asked witness **Ryne Simmons** to **delete** 4K close-up footage after he
submitted it — see [Censorship of Investigators](./Censorship_of_Investigators). Preserved copies,
if they exist, are among the highest-value YouTube uploads investigators seek.
