# YouTube Coverage

Long-form video remains the primary medium for **frame-by-frame forensics**, **expert interviews**,
and **hour-long timelines** the Kirk case demands. Podcast hosts often publish to YouTube simultaneously;
this page highlights **video-first** channels and playlists.

## Witness video suppression (claim)

Commentary alleges the FBI asked witness **Ryne Simmons** to **delete** 4K close-up footage after he
submitted it — see [Censorship of Investigators](./Censorship_of_Investigators). Preserved copies,
if they exist, are among the highest-value YouTube uploads investigators seek.

## X ↔ YouTube pipeline

Many [X researchers](./x) post short clips that expand into full YouTube breakdowns — e.g.
[danksterintel](./x/danksterintel) GRAMA threads → long Candace episodes;
[Shadow of Ezra](./x/shadowofezra) Merrell clip → [UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) analysis.

## YouTube as Evidence Host (Attributed)

YouTube remains the long-form host for Harpole (Shawn Ryan), Owens interviews, Dore/Carroll episodes, and Project Constitution hearing uploads. X clips often compress claims without exhibits — prefer full episodes when citing. Account strikes and demonetization complaints are part of the [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) story when documented.
