# The Joe Rogan Experience

**Joe Rogan** hosts **The Joe Rogan Experience (JRE)**, the most widely listened-to podcast in the world with an estimated 11–14 million listeners per episode. Rogan's program is notable for its long-form interview format, which allows guests to make detailed arguments without the interruptions and framing constraints of traditional media. He has a documented history of hosting guests who argue for intelligence service involvement in historical and current political assassinations.

- **YouTube:** [Joe Rogan on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@joerogan)

## Significance to the Investigation

Rogan's audience size makes him the single largest potential amplifier of the Charlie Kirk investigation among all the podcasters listed here. A dedicated JRE episode or segment on the Kirk assassination — particularly one featuring credible guests such as Tucker Carlson, Dave Smith, or Ian Carroll — would reach a mainstream audience that has not yet been exposed to the evidence challenging the official narrative.

The JRE's history of platforming alternative assassination theories (covering JFK, MLK, and other political killings) establishes a precedent for this type of coverage. Rogan has, reportedly, hosted guests who propose that intelligence services have played active roles in shaping American political events through violence and cover-up.

## What to Watch For

Investigators should monitor the JRE feed for any episode addressing:
- The Charlie Kirk assassination directly
- TPUSA and its funding and personnel relationships
- Israeli intelligence operations in the United States
- The FBI's conduct in the investigation

If Rogan hosts guests with direct knowledge of the case, that episode would be the highest-reach single moment in the investigation's media history.
