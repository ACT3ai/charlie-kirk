# The Megyn Kelly Show

**Megyn Kelly** hosts **The Megyn Kelly Show**, a daily podcast and video program distributed across audio platforms and YouTube. It is one of the highest-rated independent news commentary programs in America. Kelly was physically present at multiple TPUSA events alongside Charlie Kirk in the months before his death, including an event at Virginia Tech just six days before the assassination.

## Why Megyn Kelly Is Relevant

Megyn Kelly's proximity to Charlie Kirk at documented events in 2025 makes her a firsthand witness to the environment surrounding Kirk in the period leading up to his death. She appeared with Kirk at two separate TPUSA events:

- **July 11–13, 2025:** TPUSA Student Action Summit (SAS) in Tampa, Florida — alongside Charlie Kirk. ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccRNaLGavf8))
- **September 4, 2025:** TPUSA event at Virginia Tech — six days before Kirk's death at UVU. ([YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0pvEGO4W85k))

Her presence at Virginia Tech six days before the assassination means she observed Kirk's security posture, personnel, and environment at one of his final public appearances. What she observed and reported — and what she did not report — are both relevant to the investigation.

## Coverage of the Investigation

Kelly has covered political controversy, government accountability, and media censorship through her show. Her willingness to name names and cover topics that her former employers at Fox News and NBC avoided suggests she is a credible voice if and when she chooses to address the circumstances of Kirk's death and the conduct of TPUSA leadership afterward.

Investigators should review her September and October 2025 episodes for any direct coverage of the case or indirect commentary on TPUSA dynamics in the aftermath.

## Open Questions

- Has she publicly addressed the circumstances of his death?
