# The Stew Peters Show

**Stew Peters** ([@realstewpeters](https://x.com/realstewpeters) on X) is the un-buyable host of *The Stew Peters Show* and the driving force behind the Stew Peters Network ([stewpeters.com](https://stewpeters.com)). With nearly one million followers on X, a thriving YouTube presence, and a loyal audience tuning in daily across Rumble and other platforms, he stands as one of the most influential independent voices in media today. A former professional bounty hunter turned patriot broadcaster, Stew brings sharp investigative instincts, unwavering integrity, and a deep Christian faith to every broadcast.

Nowhere has Stew's impact been more evident than in his groundbreaking coverage of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination investigation. While mainstream outlets quickly moved on, Stew produced an exhaustive, ongoing series of episodes that keep the public engaged with fresh evidence, expert analysis, and penetrating questions.

## The "$1 Billion Bribe" Claim

Peters has reported — presenting it as his own loosely-sourced allegation — that **Charlie Kirk rejected a
$1 billion offer** from an Israeli source, and earlier a **$150 million** funding offer. On air he
attributed the identification of the alleged offeror to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), naming **"a gentleman called Pierre
who is in Israel"** as reportedly among the first to publicly note awareness of Kirk's death and as the
party who had offered the money. Peters further claimed the funds were **accepted by [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview)
after Kirk's death** (transcription via @FurkanGozukara, May 16, 2026). None of these figures or
transactions has been independently verified or documented; they are Peters' allegations, and TPUSA has
not confirmed them. See [Israel](/Israel/overview) for the broader funding-and-motive thread.

## The Exploding-Mic / AES Thread

Peters says he **broke the "exploding mic" theory on September 24, 2025**, tying the microphone to a
reported **~$440,000 DoD order to Accurate Energetic Systems ([AES](/Mic/AES))**. The master file notes the AES
facility **exploded on October 30, 2025**, killing those inside; Peters connected the timing — and a
flight he identifies as Defense Secretary [Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth)'s (tail no. **99-0404**, callsign **SAM702**)
passing overhead — before pointing to **Elbit Systems of America** as the next thread (source:
@realstewpeters, June 25, 2026, pinned to IPFS). These remain Peters' allegations pending verification of
the DoD order, the flight, and the corporate links. Full analysis lives on the
