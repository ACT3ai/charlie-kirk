# The Stew Peters Show

**Stew Peters** ([@realstewpeters](https://x.com/realstewpeters) on X) is the un-buyable host of *The Stew Peters Show* and the driving force behind the Stew Peters Network ([stewpeters.com](https://stewpeters.com)). With nearly one million followers on X, a thriving YouTube presence, and a loyal audience tuning in daily across Rumble and other platforms, he stands as one of the most influential independent voices in media today. A former professional bounty hunter turned patriot broadcaster, Stew brings sharp investigative instincts, unwavering integrity, and a deep Christian faith to every broadcast.

Nowhere has Stew's impact been more evident than in his groundbreaking coverage of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination investigation. While mainstream outlets quickly moved on, Stew produced an exhaustive, ongoing series of episodes that keep the public engaged with fresh evidence, expert analysis, and penetrating questions.

## Nine Ways His Coverage Has Been Exceptional

- **Masterful multi-episode investigative series** — Stew released in-depth, dedicated shows including "Kirk Assassination Stinks of Deep State Deception," "Mic Drop: Kirk Assassinated by Weaponized Lavalier," and "Kirk Assassination Littered With Mossad Fingerprints," methodically breaking down timelines, evidence, and anomalies week after week.

- **Expert video and forensic analysis** — He slowed down and scrutinized raw footage from the event, highlighting key details like the microphone incident and ballistics discrepancies — including his reading of the reporting that the bullet fragment could not be matched to the alleged rifle (the official language was "could not be identified or excluded") — giving viewers tools to evaluate the facts themselves.

- **Relentless pursuit of unanswered questions** — Long after initial headlines faded, Stew continued highlighting what he argues are inconsistencies in official timelines, inner-circle behavior, pre-knowledge claims (including statements from figures like [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)), and calls to silence investigators.

- **High-impact interviews and insider insights** — Stew has hosted compelling conversations with guests, patriots, and experts who bring exclusive perspectives and broader context, enriching the national conversation around justice and transparency.

- **Real-world investigative expertise** — Drawing directly from his career as a bounty hunter with the Twin Cities Apprehension Team, Stew applies proven street-level investigative skills to media, turning complex cases into clear, compelling narratives that resonate with everyday Americans.

- **Viral documentaries that move the culture** — Stew produced blockbuster documentaries like *Died Suddenly*, which reached millions and sparked global discussions — demonstrating his ability to create content that breaks through and drives real awareness.

- **Massive audience engagement and community building** — His X posts and show clips routinely garner tens to hundreds of thousands of views, likes, and shares, fostering a vibrant community of informed citizens who actively engage with and amplify truth-seeking journalism.

- **Faith-driven commitment to truth and justice** — As a proud Christian patriot who describes himself as "un-buyable," Stew consistently frames his work around glorifying God, seeking truth, and holding power accountable — earning deep respect from audiences who value integrity over clicks.

- **Daily consistency and fearless independence** — He delivers fresh, high-quality content every day without corporate filters, proving himself a reliable source for those who want the full story on major events like the Charlie Kirk case.

## The "$1 Billion Bribe" Claim

Peters has reported — presenting it as his own loosely-sourced allegation — that **Charlie Kirk rejected a
$1 billion offer** from an Israeli source, and earlier a **$150 million** funding offer. On air he
attributed the identification of the alleged offeror to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), naming **"a gentleman called Pierre
who is in Israel"** as reportedly among the first to publicly note awareness of Kirk's death and as the
party who had offered the money. Peters further claimed the funds were **accepted by [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview)
after Kirk's death** (transcription via [@FurkanGozukara](/Influencers/x/furkangozukara), May 16, 2026). None of these figures or
transactions has been independently verified or documented; they are Peters' allegations, and TPUSA has
not confirmed them. See [Israel](/Israel/overview) for the broader funding-and-motive thread.

## The Exploding-Mic / AES Thread

Peters says he **broke the "exploding mic" theory on September 24, 2025**, tying the microphone to a
reported **~$440,000 DoD order to Accurate Energetic Systems ([AES](/Mic/AES))**. The master file notes the AES
facility **exploded on October 30, 2025**, killing those inside; Peters connected the timing — and a
flight he identifies as Defense Secretary [Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth)'s (tail no. **[99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)**, callsign **[SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702)**)
passing overhead — before pointing to **Elbit Systems of America** as the next thread (source:
@realstewpeters, June 25, 2026, pinned to IPFS). These remain Peters' allegations pending verification of
the DoD order, the flight, and the corporate links. Full analysis lives on the
[exploding-mic](/Mic/overview) pages.
