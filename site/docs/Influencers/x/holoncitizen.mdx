# HolonCitizen — X Posts

[@HolonCitizen](https://x.com/HolonCitizen) (**Bryan Starbuck**) is the citizen-investigator
account most closely tied to this site's own research. His analyses are published with sources and
pinned to IPFS; like every other account covered here, his claims are presented as attributed
citizen analysis, not adjudicated findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **.30-06 energy mismatch (April 17, 2026)** — published caliber-comparison charts (bullet size
  and muzzle energy: .30-06 ~3,950 J vs 9mm ~482 J) arguing the visible wound behavior fits poorly
  with a .30-06 impact — the analysis behind
  [Thirty-06 Energy Mismatch](/Gun_Bullet/Thirty_06_Energy_Mismatch) and
  [.30-06 Did Not Kill Charlie](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/thirty-06-did-not-kill-charlie).
  ([Primary thread](https://x.com/HolonCitizen/status/2045212282712703209))
- **Utah autopsy-law analysis** — advanced the argument (with supporters of Baron Coleman) that a
  2025 Utah law criminalizing public release of autopsy photos **and video**, arriving months
  before the shooting, would be "critical in a cover up" if anyone wanted a non-truthful autopsy
  kept from scrutiny — presented with point-by-point counter-threads on
  [Utah Autopsy Law](/CoverUp/Utah_Autopsy_Law).
- **"Stairs Guy" video comparison** — open-source examination of UVU surveillance imagery comparing
  campus-camera figures, cited on [Bryan Starbuck](/People/bryan-starbuck) and the
  [Losee Center page](/UVU/Losee_Center).
- **Platform-suppression documentation** — the account's own reported demonetization and reach
  throttling on X, and near-zero algorithmic traffic on YouTube, are documented as claims-with-
  indicators on [X @HolonCitizen Censored](/CoverUp/X_HolonCitizen_Censored) and
  [YouTube @HolonCitizen Censored](/CoverUp/YouTube_HolonCitizen_Censored).

## Investigative value
