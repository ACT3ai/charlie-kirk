# iluminomoly — X Posts

[@iluminomoly](https://x.com/iluminomoly) is a citizen-investigator account central to the **SUV
evidence thread** — the photographs, disposal claim, and scanner-audio breakdowns around the
vehicle that carried Charlie Kirk from UVU. All items are attributed claims; the photos are not
independently authenticated and no fragment from them has been publicly lab-tested.

## Claims from X posts

- **SUV interior photos (June 26, 2026)** — posted photo sets reportedly released by
  [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) (watermarked "CANDACE") showing the black GMC Yukon Denali
  interior: rear-seat staining, a pale fragment some commentators call possible bone or tissue, a
  small black object read as a mic windscreen/transmitter, and debris some describe as shattered
  tempered glass — the basis of [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie) and the
  [photo cluster](/Photos/Mic/Mic_SUV_Transporting/overview).
  ([Primary post](https://x.com/iluminomoly/status/2070601845697888339))
- **SUV destruction claim** — posted that the SUV "was chopped into a thousand pieces… melted
  down," attributing disposal to an unnamed "they" — no agency or person is named, a point the site
  states plainly on [SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed) and
  [SUV Destroyed / ADL Floor](/Suspicious/FBI/suv-destroyed-adl-floor).
  ([Post](https://x.com/iluminomoly/status/2070599972072874006))
- **Police-scanner breakdown (July 9, 2026)** — compiled scanner audio in which, per the poster,
  the story of who drove hospital to hospital looking for Kirk keeps changing between
  [Zach Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi),
  [Andrew Piskadlo](/distraction_people/Andrew_Piskadlo), or no one — the poster's
  characterization, not established fact.
  ([Post](https://x.com/iluminomoly/status/2075191642366054733))
- **Early-manhunt vehicle confusion** — described shifting vehicle descriptions (including a Nissan
  Armada later associated in commentary with FBI presence) on the
  [FBI involvement timeline](/Timeline/fbi-involvement-timeline).

## Investigative value

The account preserved and circulated the **only public interior imagery of the SUV**, which is why
the exploding-mic and evidence-preservation debates keep returning to its posts. Chain of custody,
authentication, and lab testing remain the open items that would move any of this beyond claim.
