# FinanceWolves (Zach) — X Posts

[@FinanceWolves](https://x.com/FinanceWolves) (**Zach**; some site references spell the handle
**@FinaceWolves**) is a citizen investigator best known in this record as the reported recipient
of a **TPUSA cease-and-desist letter** over drone-footage contradictions — a letter that, per
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' account, conceded the central point investigators had
raised. All items below are attributed claims, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts and related coverage

- **The cease-and-desist admission (reported)** — TPUSA counsel's letter to Zach allegedly stated
  a **"TPUSA media production drone briefly flew for B-roll BEFORE the event"** while
  **security's counter-drone request was denied** by the university — the contradiction treated as
  central on [the TPUSA production drone](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone) and
  [cease-and-desist drone contradiction](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/cease-desist-drone-contradiction).
  The letter itself has **not** been published; every quote is a secondhand characterization.
- The episode is logged as an example of **private legal pressure on investigators** — see
  [TPUSA legal threats](/Censorship/TPUSA_Legal_Threats) and
  [censorship of investigators](/Influencers/Censorship_of_Investigators).
- The account's drone-contradiction work sits beside conflicting statements attributed to
  **Brian Harpole** ("no drones — university denied"), **Andrew Kolvet** ("wide-angle from
  bridge, not drone"), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s reported en-route drone review —
  compiled on [TPUSA production footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage).
- **July 19, 2026** — Tagged in [@FaithDrivUr](./faithdrivur)'s viral clip asking which TPUSA
  representative allegedly met the assassin on the quad
  ([post](https://x.com/FaithDrivUr/status/2078917940993311136)).

## Investigative value

If the letter says what Owens reports, TPUSA's own counsel confirmed aerial media coverage
existed while protective drone overwatch was denied — the sharpest documented version of the
drone contradiction. Until the letter is published in full, the site treats it as an
**allegation with MODERATE evidence rating**.
