# Joshua_the_car — X Posts

preliminary-hearing notes** on the autopsy are cited across the site's [Medical](/Medical/overview)

  **entered into evidence** with cause of death **gunshot wound to the neck**, manner **homicide**,
  and **bullet fragments recovered** — while the judge ruled the full ME report **not available to
  the public** ([Trial and the Autopsy Report](/Medical/trial_and_autopsy_report)).
- **July 7, 2026** — Hearing-clip framing that Kirk was **pronounced dead at Timpanogos** hospital
  ([Events at the Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital)).

  ([Death Certificate](/Medical/Autopsy/death_certificate)).
