# Joshua_the_car — X Posts

[@Joshua_the_car](https://x.com/Joshua_the_car) is a court-watch account whose **July 2026
preliminary-hearing notes** on the autopsy are cited across the site's [Medical](/Medical/overview)
pages. These are court-watch notes from X — not official transcripts.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 6–7, 2026** — Reported (with **@alexboge** and **@stratagemmer**) that the autopsy was
  **entered into evidence** with cause of death **gunshot wound to the neck**, manner **homicide**,
  and **bullet fragments recovered** — while the judge ruled the full ME report **not available to
  the public** ([Trial and the Autopsy Report](/Medical/trial_and_autopsy_report)).
- **July 7, 2026** — Hearing-clip framing that Kirk was **pronounced dead at Timpanogos** hospital
  ([Events at the Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital)).
- His testimony/autopsy language is read by some as proof an **autopsy narrative exists even if
  the report is sealed** — a competing claim against the earlier "no ME autopsy / death certificate
  signed at hospital" stack from September 2025
  ([Death Certificate](/Medical/Autopsy/death_certificate)).
- The notes coexist with the **September 17, 2025 ATF "inconclusive"** fragment-to-Mauser
  comparison that citizen investigators say keeps the rifle narrative open.

## Investigative value

The account is one of the site's sources for what the **sealed autopsy** reportedly says — filling
the public-record gap between the sealed ME report and the courtroom fragments read into evidence.
Treat as attributed hearing reporting pending official transcripts.
