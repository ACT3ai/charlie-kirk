# DailyPlanetoid — X Posts

[@DailyPlanetoid](https://x.com/DailyPlanetoid) is the source of one of the most-discussed
**single-shooter challenges** from the July 2026 preliminary-hearing window: an alleged **second
moving figure** in roof footage at the second of the shot. This is an unverified visual
interpretation of grainy surveillance, recorded as an attributed claim.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026** — Claimed that CourtTV-captured roof footage from the preliminary hearing shows a
  **second person sprinting toward the claimed Losee Center shooter corner** as the shot occurs
  (~**12:23:28–12:23:30 PM**), just before an angle cut. The State's case focuses on a single roof
  figure. Logged on [12:23 shooting time](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) as a disputed visual and
  amplified via [Project Constitution's hearing coverage](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution).
- The claim is held in the site's conflicts table against the State-identified single roof figure —
  "if authentic and unedited," it raises single-shooter questions; nothing about it has been
  authenticated.

## Investigative value

One narrow but pointed contribution: a timestamped, checkable visual claim tied to a specific
public stream (CourtTV). It either survives frame-level analysis of the original footage or it
does not — which is exactly the kind of primary-source test the
[Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure proposals are designed to force.
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) remains charged, not convicted.
