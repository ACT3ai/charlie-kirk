# The General — X Posts

[@GeneralMCNews](https://x.com/GeneralMCNews) (**The General**) is a verified breaking-news
style account whose posts are preserved in the site's photographic evidence record at three
points — aircraft tracking, the FBI foreign-leads story, and an Israel-related threat claim.
All items below are attributed claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **N708JH tracking block** — A post alleging FBI Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)
  requested a halt to public tracking of a government-owned jet, paired with a FlightAware notice
  that aircraft **N708JH** "is not available for public tracking" — preserved as
  [Aircraft Photo 113](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_ea2521).
- **Joe Kent probe halt** — "BREAKING: FBI Director Kash Patel has **reportedly** stopped Tulsi
  Gabbard's closest advisor from investigating whether Charlie Kirk's assassination had ties to
  foreign intelligence operations" (132 replies / 28K views) — a one-sentence compression of the
  New York Times reporting on [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent), preserved as
  [FBI Photo 25](/Photos/FBI/Img_Photo_66aa52) and documented on [Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt)
  and [Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads).
- **"Aaron Levitsky" threat post** — A post reporting an alleged FBI situation involving an
  "Israeli sniper Aaron Levitsky" who threatened a "Christian influencer," with DM screenshots —
  preserved as [Israel Photo 39](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_44b5f4); the underlying claim is unverified.

## Investigative value

The account illustrates **story compression**: nuanced mainstream reporting condensed into
one-sentence claims that reached tens of thousands of readers within a day. The site keeps the
screenshots as records of how the foreign-leads and aircraft stories travelled, while pointing
readers to the documented versions on the FBI and Planes pages.
