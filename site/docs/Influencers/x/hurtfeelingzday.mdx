# hurtfeelingzday — X Posts

[@hurtfeelingzday](https://x.com/hurtfeelingzday) is one of the investigation's most-cited
**flight-tracking** accounts. Its ADS-B screenshots and tail-number timelines underpin several
Planes-section pages. All items below are attributed claims from X posts; sensitive military
flights lawfully fly with transponders off, and no flight pattern proves involvement in any crime.

## Claims from X posts

- **SAM [99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) / [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) legs** — described a C-37A Gulfstream (tail **99-0404**) flying
  Andrews → Colorado Springs (Sept 7), Colorado Springs → Tucson (Sept 8), going "dark for hours,"
  then reappearing Sept 9 flying Fort Huachuca → El Paso → Andrews — and tied the travel to
  **Bradley Hansell** per [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) confirmation claims. See
  [Bradley Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell), [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca)
  and [Huachuca Records Withheld](/CoverUp/Huachuca_Records_Withheld).
- **AES overflight claim (July 2026)** — claimed the same tail 99-0404 later overflew the
  **Accurate Energetic Systems** plant in Tennessee after Huachuca-related activity — unconfirmed;
  flagged for ADS-B-archive and FOIA verification on
  [AES Factory Explosion](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion).
- **N102DZ screenshots** — shared extensive Flightradar24 captures of **N102DZ**'s Scottsdale and
  Provo (PVU) movements; portions of that data were later said to be scrubbed or harder to find
  (a non-suspicious LADD-blocklist explanation is noted) — [N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ).
- **N1098L "AXEL10" Orem loops** — summarized: "ON 9/10/2025 air force jet N1098L mission call sign
  AXEL10 ... descended to 300 ft, slowed to 150k and looped around Orem," repeating the loop at
  11:27 MT — reproduced with its Army-ISR contracting-trail caveats on
  [N1098L overview](/Planes/N1098L/overview).
- **[N560TW](/Planes/N560TW) tarmac dispute** — claimed **N560TW** left a Santa Barbara gate roughly fourteen minutes
  after the UVU shot — too soon, the argument runs, for a staged Provo recovery — see the
  [other planes activity timeline](/Timeline/other-planes-activity-timeline).

## Investigative value

The account supplies the **capability-and-proximity half** of the intelligence-service theories
tracked on [Geopolitical / Intel Service Theories](/Motive/Geopolitical_Intel_Service_Theories):
who flew where, when, and with what transponder gaps. Screenshots are secondary evidence — primary
ADS-B archives and FOIA returns are the verification path.
