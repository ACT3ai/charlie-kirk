# ReturnOfKappy — X Posts

[@ReturnOfKappy](https://x.com/ReturnOfKappy) posts in the crime-scene-alteration lane of the
investigation, focused on the rapid landscaping work at the UVU courtyard after the shooting.
Claims below are attributed to the account's posts and echoes; the site treats them as
allegations, not findings of evidence destruction.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified the reports (alongside @JodyChaseTN and @ZachCostello_) that **dirt and ground
  material were removed** from the scene area — the core claim examined on
  [Dirt Removed](/CoverUp/Dirt_Removed).
- On **May 21, 2026**, described contractor **Dan Merrell** of Hardscape Utah in connection with
  the work — see [Daniel Merrell / Hardscape Utah](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah), where
  the account is cited for echoing an **"emergency directive"** narrative: cover the area quickly
  under claimed Governor-and-FBI urgency. The site marks the directive framing as an attributed
  narrative, and Merrell is not accused of wrongdoing.
- The same May 2026 posts (with @RealAlexJones the next day) feed the
  [Signs of Cover-Up](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/signs-of-cover-up) analysis.
- Circulated viral podium clips showing an official first saying **~36 hours** and then
  correcting the figure — logged on the
  [press conferences timeline](/Timeline/press-conferences-statements-timeline).

## Investigative value

The account is a primary amplifier of the **scene-hardscaping question** — why ground material at
a homicide scene was covered or removed quickly, and on whose instruction. The site treats the
underlying work orders and directives as the verifiable records to pursue.
