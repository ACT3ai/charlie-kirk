# AmericaOnlyBro — X Posts

[@AmericaOnlyBro](https://x.com/AmericaOnlyBro) contributed two distinct threads to the site's record: a widely seen **motive checklist** and an on-the-ground **UVU site-visit analysis**. Both are **commentators' claims**, not findings, and no living person named is accused of a crime.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026 motive "bill of particulars"** — a stacked list arguing: (1) a **donor break** — private texts about bullying and "leaving the pro-Israel cause" after losing major funding; (2) **youth-pipeline stakes** — TPUSA as the largest youth conservative pipeline that could be steered into stronger pro-Israel messaging after Kirk's death; (3) Kirk's **October 7 / Gaza language** on his own podcast. The site records these at [extreme motivation](/Israel_Main_Suspect/extreme-motivation) with the caveat that post-hoc "after" evidence is disputed and circular as motive proof.
- **July 2026 site-visit thread** — alleged that AV staffer [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) was moving toward a climbing vantage on the rock pile **before Kirk hit the ground**, extending the timing question documented at [Farnsworth already filming](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/farnsworth-rock-pile). A video-based citizen claim about living AV staff — not a court finding of wrongdoing.

## Investigative value

The motive checklist is useful precisely because readers will encounter it repeatedly — the site reproduces it in attributed form so each plank can be checked against primary sources rather than absorbed as a block. The Farnsworth timing claim feeds the [minute-by-minute reconstruction](/Timeline/shooting-minute-by-minute), where every second of the first half-minute after the shot is contested.
