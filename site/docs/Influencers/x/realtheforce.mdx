# RealTheForce — X Posts

[@RealTheForce](https://x.com/RealTheForce) posts in the foreign-involvement and rigged-mic lanes
of the investigation discourse. The site cites this account for **restated and expanded
narratives** — and explicitly flags where those expansions go beyond what any official has said
on the record.

## Claims from X posts

- Restated **remote-detonator / "rigged mic explosive"** language (alongside @martinez_j7902 and
  @NJDarling1977) about the RODE microphone — logged on
  [RODE Mic](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic) as **alleged acts by unnamed or unproven
  actors, not established facts**.
- Expanded the investigation-block narrative to claim **Tulsi Gabbard (as DNI) was also shut
  out** of foreign inquiry, in addition to [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)'s on-record
  account. The site's caution: this is a **separate claim requiring her own on-record
  confirmation** — see [Leads to Foreign Involvement](/Israel_Main_Suspect/leads-to-foreign-involvement).
- Viral posts asking why **Kash Patel** allegedly shut down both Gabbard and Kent from
  investigating foreign involvement — then leaping to a conclusion about Israel. The site
  records that leap as **commentator conclusion, not evidence**, on
  [Cover-Up of Foreign Involvement](/Israel_Main_Suspect/coverup-foreign-involvement), noting
  Gabbard has issued no matching "I was blocked" statement in the captured material.

## Investigative value

The account illustrates how **verified kernels (Kent's clips) get stacked with unverified
expansions** (Gabbard co-blocked framing). The site uses its posts to map the cast of officials
citizens believe were constrained, while keeping the evidentiary line at what is on record.
