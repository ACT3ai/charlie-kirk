# gatewood__ — X Posts

[@gatewood__](https://x.com/gatewood__) is cited in the investigation record for a narrow but
consequential footage argument during the July 2026 preliminary-hearing coverage: what the
court video actually shows versus what viral posts said it shows. Items below are attributed
claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **No trigger-pull footage** — During July 2026 preliminary-hearing discussion, asserted
  (alongside **@DerekImproves**) that there is still **no released footage of the trigger pull**,
  demanding full raw multi-camera dumps — logged on
  [TPUSA production footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage), where posts also claim the judge
  asked how TPUSA handled footage from the back of the tent.
- **Real-time dispute of "shows him taking the shot" claims** — Viral posts claiming court video
  "shows Robinson taking the shot" were disputed in real time by @gatewood__ (with
  @tommyborumjokes and local-news summaries): hearing coverage more carefully describes
  **positioning and flight**, not a documented trigger-pull frame — see
  [the Tyler placeholder camera analysis](/cameras/Tyler_Placeholder).

## Investigative value

The distinction the account pressed — positioning footage versus an actual firing frame — is
central to the identification dispute: if no frame shows the shot being fired, the rooftop
figure's link to the fatal round rests on inference and continuity, not direct capture.
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted**, and this site records
the footage gap as an open evidence question, not a conclusion.
