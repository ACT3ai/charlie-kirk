# James Li — X Posts

[@5149jamesli](https://x.com/5149jamesli) (**James Li**) is an independent journalist whose posts recur across the site's motive, timeline, and court threads — from amplifying the Hamptons donor-meeting narrative to the custody-versus-Discord timing argument. The bullets below are **claims attributed to his X posts and videos**, not confirmed findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **Sept 15, 2025 (~1.3M views)** — Amplified [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) describing a private **Hamptons** meeting with [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman) and pro-Israel mega-donors, with Owens claiming "threats were made" after Kirk turned down Israeli money — Owens's allegation, not a court finding. See [Donors, Israel &amp; Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy).
- **Nov 7, 2025** — Analyzed Erika Kirk's Fox interview and highlighted tension between her "called me within seconds" account and statements attributed to [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) / TPUSA figures about whether [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) was on the phone; kept the McCoy call-log demand alive. See the [Erika Kirk timeline](/Timeline/erika-kirk-timeline).
- **April 9, 2026** — Posted that the Discord message used to allege a confession was published **several hours after** Robinson was already arrested, "per court documents reviewed by @baroncoleman" — the post that spread the **"33-hour cover-up"** phrasing. Tracked at [narrative control](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/narrative-control) and [faked confession](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/faked-confession).
- **July–Aug 2025 Trends wave** — Popularized compilations reporting Israel-origin Google Trends interest in [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital), medical examiner [Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro), and associated surgeons. Aggregate Trends only; no searcher IDs. See [2025 Context Timeline](/Timeline/Year_2025).
- Posted a critical thread on a list of **250 state legislators** allegedly attending a "50 States 1 Israel" conference — preserved in the [Israel photo cluster](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_0e5e54).
- Circulated clips of Charlie Kirk's Gaza remarks (the "ethnically cleanse Gaza" paraphrase) used on [Charlie's Israel Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).
- **July 2026** — Relayed courtroom observers (e.g. @brandisiciliani) claiming there is **no high-quality zoomed-in shooter video**, countering [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)'s "most damning" courtroom-video characterization.

## Investigative value

Li operates as a bridge between attorney-sourced court analysis ([Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)) and mass X audiences — his April 2026 custody-before-Discord post was picked up by mainstream aggregators within a day. His threads are commentary and synthesis; verify against primary filings where available.
