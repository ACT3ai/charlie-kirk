# MrDiscordeous (Justin Ruh) — X Posts

[@MrDiscordeous](https://x.com/MrDiscordeous) (**Justin Ruh**, publishing under DISCOURSEDIALOGUE
LLC, analyst I.D. JR936M) produced some of the more rigorous independent **impact-location
reconstructions** in circulation — signed, dated forensic-style graphics rather than anonymous
memes. His work is an inference built on public video, explicitly conditional on a wound geometry
that has not been publicly released, and it names no shooter.

## Claims from X posts

- Published **"Impact Location 157° / Top Radius"** — a graphic (created 09/18/25) overlaying a
  high-resolution aerial of the plaza with a skull-bearing compass, ranking candidate firing origins:
  **most probable (trajectory/evasion)**, **second most probable (person seen in this location)**,
  and a third tier flagged as lacking video evidence. See the
  [aerial impact map analysis](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Img_Photo_1fe5c0_x) and
  [companion sheet](/Photos/Shot_Position/Img_Photo_1fe5c0).
- Published **"Impact Location 120° / Facial Radius"** — the companion measurement of the same
  reconstruction on a different radius, with an **"Exit Wound Ejection"** photographic inset he
  labels as interpretation. See
  [the 120-degree sheet](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Img_Photo_3b8027) and
  [its companion](/Photos/Shot_Position/Img_Photo_6b443d).
- The site's [Shot Position overview](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview) summarizes his conclusion:
  the impact bearing of roughly **120–157 degrees**, he argues, points **south of the officially
  named Losee Center perch** — one of several competing, unsettled readings of the geometry.

## Investigative value

The method is the value: treat the head as a sphere, express the impact as a bearing, work backward
to positions that could produce it, and grade each by available footage. It is **reproducible and
falsifiable in public** — rare in this space — but it depends on an assumed wound orientation that
remains contested while the autopsy record is sealed.
