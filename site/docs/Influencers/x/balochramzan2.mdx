# BalochRamzan2 — X Posts

[@BalochRamzan2](https://x.com/BalochRamzan2) is one of the X accounts most frequently cited as
a **carrier of Charlie Kirk's alleged final warnings** — the compilation posts through which the
reported "they are going to kill me" texts and Israel-related quotes spread. Everything below is
an **attributed social-media claim** requiring corroboration against primary materials, not an
established fact.

## Claims from X posts

- Circulated compilations of the reported **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** texts allegedly sent
  during Charlie's combative **September 9, 2025** meeting — cited alongside @Partisangirl on
  [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) and on
  [Political Pressure and Meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).
  ([Source](https://x.com/BalochRamzan2/status/1965901874974044386))
- Listed among the carriers (with @hodgetwins and @FactsVsZee) of the alleged
  **"If I go against Israel, I think they will kill me"** remark attributed to an August 13, 2025
  private conversation — cataloged on [Charlie's Israel Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).
- Cited in [Israel Donors Motive](/Charlie/Israel_Donors_Motive) as one of the source types for
  paraphrased remarks including "I'm going to have to stop supporting Israel" and the
  donor-pressure "leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause" line.

## Investigative value

The account is a **quote-compilation amplifier** rather than an originator of documents. Its
value is in preserving and spreading the screenshot/reel record of Charlie's alleged final
statements; its weakness is that those quotes rest largely on second-hand retellings. The site
treats them as claims requiring authentication — original video, authenticated messages, or
sworn testimony would move them from claim to record.
