# Rapid Response 47 — X Posts

[@RapidResponse47](https://x.com/RapidResponse47) (**Rapid Response 47**) is the official White
House rapid-response clip account. The site cites it as a **primary-source archive of
administration statements** in the days after the assassination — official messaging, not
citizen-investigation claims.

## Clips cited on the site

- **September 12, 2025 Fox & Friends interview** — President Trump urging supporters seeking
  "revenge" to pursue it **"at the voter box"**
  ([clip](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1966481904405725436)), analyzed on
  [Trump: Votes Speech](/Media/Trump-Votes-Speech).
- Vice President Vance: **"If it weren't for Charlie Kirk, I would not be the Vice President of
  the United States."** ([clip](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1967645677347057784)) — see
  [Vance: Mission Continuation](/Media/Vance-Mission-Continuation).
- Deputy Communications Director **Kaelan Dorr** on the mission to "continue to do his work
  because that's exactly what he'd want"
  ([clip](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1967632525016314212)).
- **Stephen Miller**'s DOJ pledge to "identify, disrupt, dismantle, and destroy" radical-left
  networks — "we will do it in Charlie's name"
  ([clip](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1967627728687157593)).

## Investigative value

These clips fix the **official narrative timeline**: what the administration said, when, and in
what framing. The site uses them as the baseline against which citizen-investigation claims about
narrative control and investigative scope are compared.
