# tsorama — X Posts

[@tsorama](https://x.com/tsorama) appears in the investigation record as the sharer of a single
consequential document: the anonymous **Axon Body Cam 4** tip email sent to the Candace Owens tips
line, which disputes the account that Officer **Chris Bagley's** body camera simply died on the
Losee roof. Bagley is a witness, not a suspect; he has not been charged with anything, and
body-worn cameras genuinely do fail.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 17, 2026** — Shared a screenshot (quoting **@TheCKFiles1**) of an **anonymous email** to
  the [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) tips line, sender redacted, describing how an
  **Axon Body Cam 4** buffers, records, and stops — technical behavior the emailer argues is
  inconsistent with the reported single ~27.5-minute recording ending on the roof.
  ([Post](https://x.com/tsorama/status/2078105368933142589))
- The email's author is unknown and nothing in it has been independently verified; it is preserved
  because its central claim is **testable** against Axon's own documentation — see
  [Chris Bagley](/People/chris-bagley) and the [photo exhibit](/Photos/People/People_Chris_Bagley/overview).

## Investigative value

One checkable technical claim. The tip enters the record not as an accusation but as a
falsifiable statement about a specific camera model's behavior, set against Bagley's reported roof
arrival around 12:44 p.m. and the screwdriver / disturbed-gravel testimony from the 2026
preliminary hearing. The bodycam question is developed at
[Officer Bagley Bodycam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam).
