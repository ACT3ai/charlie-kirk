# GoingStrategic — X Posts

[@GoingStrategic](https://x.com/GoingStrategic) is cited in the investigation record through
two preserved post screenshots in the photographic evidence archive — one in the cover-up
cluster and one in the distraction/patsy cluster. Items below are attributed claims from X
posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **Cover-up before/after graphic** — A post discussing an alleged "Cover Up in
  [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination," featuring a graphic comparing **"Before"
  and "After" media layouts** of the scene; the site preserves the post's analytics screenshot as
  [Cover Up Photo 6](/Photos/Cover_Up_Evidence/Img_Photo_08b425) — part of the wider
  [Cover Up (Possible)](/CoverUp/overview) record.
- **September 25, 2025 — Pellet Gun Man** — A post investigating the individual referred to as
  the **"Pellet Gun man"** arrested in Provo, questioning his identity and analyzing his weapon
  via a four-panel collage of arrest footage and a wooden-stock air rifle product image —
  preserved as [Pellet Gun Man Photo 1](/Photos/Patsy/Distraction_Pellet_Gun_Man/Img_Photo_3150fb)
  in the [Patsy Candidates cluster](/Photos/Patsy/overview).

## Investigative value

Both preserved posts belong to the **distraction-and-scene-integrity** question set: whether
same-day arrests of other armed individuals functioned as distractions, and whether the scene's
media presentation changed between early and later coverage. These are open questions the site
tracks; the pellet-gun individual was not charged in the assassination, and nothing here
asserts wrongdoing by any identified living person.
