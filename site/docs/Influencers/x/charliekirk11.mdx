# charliekirk11 — X Posts

[@charliekirk11](https://x.com/charliekirk11) was **Charlie Kirk's own X account** (Deceased 2025).
Unlike the other accounts in this section, it is not a citizen-investigator account — it appears in
the investigation record because Kirk's own final-week posts are themselves evidence, and because
other accounts cite an association with @charliekirk11 as a credential.

## Posts and references in the record

- **Sept 3, 2025** — One week before [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), Kirk posted:
  *"BREAKING: Epstein survivors announce that they will compile their own list of abusers who
  frequented his island and plane. 'We know the names. Many of us were abused by them.'"* The post
  amplified a survivors' press conference in Washington, D.C. A commentator (@Rothbard1776) later
  framed it: Kirk *"was publicly and privately working on the complete disclosure of the Epstein
  files… He had many enemies, both foreign AND domestic."* The Kirk post is a documented fact about
  his final week; the enemies inference is the commentator's. See the
  [preserved screenshot](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Img_Photo_3efcd3) and [Motive](/Motive/overview).
- **Credential reference** — The account **@ArtifexMemor**, which resurfaced the 2022
  [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec) / [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) "[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)"
  exchange in July 2026, lists prior work with **@tpusa** and **@charliekirk11** in its bio — one
  example of how proximity to Kirk's account is used as a marker of insider standing.

## Investigative value

Kirk's own posting record is primary-source material for the [Motive](/Motive/overview) and
[Before the Shooting](/Before/overview) threads — what he was publicly doing and saying in his final
weeks, from the Epstein survivors amplification to the donor-pressure arc documented elsewhere on
this site.
