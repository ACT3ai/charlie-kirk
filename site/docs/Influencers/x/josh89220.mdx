# Josh89220 — X Posts

[@Josh89220](https://x.com/Josh89220) is an aircraft-OSINT account cited across the site's
[Planes](/Planes/overview) section for **FAA/transponder analysis** of military surveillance
aircraft around UVU. These are correlation claims — flight logs do not prove any operational role
in the killing, and no pilot, passenger, or official is alleged here to have carried out the
assassination.

## Claims from X posts

- Cited **FAA/transponder logs** of an Army **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)-class ISR jet** (**N1098L** / AXLE10 in
  investigation notes) making low (~200 ft class) passes near **UVU/Orem before and after the
  shooting** ([N1098L Spy Plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview)).
- The claims feed the open question of whether ISR / counter-UAS capability at Provo/UVU that week
  was routine training or something investigators should subpoena
  ([ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations),
  [Provo Airport Counter-UAS](/Proof_Intel_Services/Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS)).
- The account's transponder work is cross-referenced with the airfield context pages, including
  [Biggs Army Airfield](/Planes/Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield) and the
  [Sept 10 flight timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).

## Investigative value

The account supplies the **primary-data layer** (transponder tracks, altitudes, timing) behind the
N1098L question — the strongest documented aircraft anomaly in the case record. The site's rule
applies: flight logs are not intent; the remedy is subpoenaed manifests and tasking orders via the
[Fix Laws](/Fix/overview).
