# Project Constitution — X Posts

[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) is among the citizen investigators posting flight data, ballistics questions, drone sightings, and agency-involvement theories about the UVU shooting. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings. Each line links to the cited post where available.

## Claims from X posts

- Hats were flying as the crowd cheered [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) during the Q&A session just before the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The Q&A at the UVU event dived into discussions about guns, foreshadowing the incident.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- There was one razor-sharp bang, like a firecracker, during the shooting of Charlie Kirk.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- Charlie Kirk's head snapped back in blood, resulting in an instant kill from the shot.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- After the shot, there was dead silence followed by frenzy, with the crowd dropping and surging to the back exit.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- An eyewitness was inches away from Charlie Kirk and snagged a hat mid-air during the incident.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- There was no echo from the shot due to the crowd and clothes absorbing the sound like a trap.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The sound felt like it rose upward, ruling out a roof or balcony origin because the canopy would block the view and angle.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The trajectory of the shot was at eye level with no ricochet or external noise.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- Outside the event, [George Zen](/distraction_people/George_Zinn), a broke devout LDS loner with no gun, was cuffed fast by plainclothes cops.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- George Zen is considered an obvious patsy in the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The UVU event was low-key with only 2-3 black-shirt guards and sketchy audio setup, tinny Q&A mics versus clear for Charlie Kirk.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- An eyewitness suggested a remote speaker rig for the shot sound.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- Theory: The shot was from a concealed remote device at the top inside the tent/canopy, triggered wirelessly for precision.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The mic could have blasted the pop through speakers, with no visible shooter and chaos covering escape.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- The eyewitness account supports internal tent tech based on direction, acoustics, and staging vibes.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- Called for forensics on tent fixtures before teardown to test the account's theory that a device was planted. No device has been found or documented, and no person is identified as having planted anything.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880))
- Hundreds of students, parents, and attendees were recording on their phones at the UVU event.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976059967816736801))
- Seeking footage of older men with long hair tied in a ponytail or bun from the UVU event.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976059967816736801))
- Footage from Charlie Kirk’s left side (facing the crowd) is a total black hole and missing.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976059967816736801))
- Seeking clips from 10 minutes before the shots or during the immediate chaos after.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976059967816736801))
- The account says [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) has not released its full 4K/5K recordings of the UVU event and characterizes that as a refusal. TPUSA has not been shown to be under any obligation to release them.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976059967816736801))
- A drone was overhead at Charlie Kirk’s UVU event on September 10, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1976299265547706374))
- fox13now.com CCTV footage showed the person of interest walking towards UVU campus at 11:49 AM, arriving on campus at 11:52 AM.  ([Source](https://x.com/OliLondonTV/status/1966467191752061003))
- The suspect confessed in a Discord group chat before surrendering.  ([Source](https://x.com/BNODesk/status/1967669847585853801))
