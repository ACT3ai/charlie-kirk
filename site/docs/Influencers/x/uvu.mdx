# UVU (Official) — X Posts

[@UVU](https://x.com/UVU) is the **official X account of Utah Valley University** — not a citizen
investigator. Its posts matter to the record because they are the institution's contemporaneous
public statements, and researchers cross-check them against FBI, prosecutor, and mainstream
timelines for framing differences.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 10, 2025** — First post: a shot fired at a visiting-speaker event at **~12:10 PM**,
  with initial language that a suspect was taken into custody.
  ([Post](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965856814320300326)) — the FBI/prosecutor timeline later put the
  shot at **12:23 PM**, a discrepancy tracked on [UVU Social Media Response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response).
- Campus **closed**, classes canceled, shelter-in-place until police escort.
  ([Post](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965868138228068793))
- Shared the **FBI digital media tip line** for shooting information.
  ([Post](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965969976184988136))
- Announced the **Vigil for Unity** — September 19, 2025, UCCU Center.
  ([Post](https://x.com/UVU/status/1968004319350841422))
- Researchers note @UVU posts **did not specify** shooter position or weapon, while official filings
  named the **Losee Center rooftop** and a .30-06 — framing differences that may reflect drafting lag,
  different clocks, or intentional vagueness.
- In censorship threads, investigators observed official @UVU posts **remained fully visible** while
  some eyewitness and rooftop-access threads saw reduced reach — an unverified reach-asymmetry
  observation on [X Deboosting Investigators](/Censorship/X_Deboosting_Investigators).

## Investigative value

The @UVU feed is a fixed institutional baseline: whatever else is contested, these posts are what the
university told ten thousand sheltering students and the public in real time. The **~12:10 vs 12:23**
shot-time gap and the early custody language feed directly into the
[false "suspect in custody" alert](/Timeline/distraction-events-timeline) analysis.
