# charlie_ward77 — X Posts

[@charlie_ward77](https://x.com/charlie_ward77) posted the single clearest example of the **"shot in
the heart"** framing arriving through **live television** — a clip now embedded, transcribed, and
IPFS-pinned at the head of the site's wound-location catalog. The witness statements are recorded
as-given; this site does not adjudicate them.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 13, 2026** ([post](https://x.com/charlie_ward77/status/2076465128329846991)) — Shared a
  live TV news segment in which an on-camera witness says the arrested man (whom he names as
  **George Zinn**) was the wrong guy, and that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was hit by *"one
  shot, right directly to the heart"* by *"a good shooter, a sharp shooter."* The broadcast cuts
  away to Jason Chaffetz, confirming it ran inside a live news program.
- The witness adds his opinion that Zinn was *"maybe a perfect person to pick and say that did
  it. But I don't think he did it at all."* The public record is that Zinn was not the shooter and
  faced unrelated charges; Mr. Zinn is a living person and nothing here asserts he committed any
  crime, nor is the witness accused of anything.
- The clip is the featured broadcast exhibit on [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart),
  the [Narrative overview](/Narrative/overview), and
  [Witnesses — Shot to the Heart](/Witnesses/shot-to-the-heart), because the witness independently
  uses the exact "directly to the heart" phrasing that recurs across the earliest TV interviews —
  before any footage or medical information was public.

## Investigative value

One artifact, heavily load-bearing: it ties the **heart/chest wound descriptions** to a live-TV
primary source rather than social-media retellings, and intersects the
[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn) distraction thread. Compare against the settled
official **neck** account on [chest versus neck](/Suspicious/Medical/chest-versus-neck-accounts).
