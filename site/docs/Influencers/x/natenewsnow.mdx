# NateNewsNow — X Posts

[@NateNewsNow](https://x.com/NateNewsNow) contributed **hearing-coverage notes** from the July 2026
Tyler Robinson preliminary hearing — specifically the testimony describing how Charlie Kirk reached
the hospital. These are a poster's summaries of reported testimony, not official transcripts.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 6, 2026** — Summarized testimony from investigator **[David Hull](/People/david-hull)**:
  after the shot, Kirk was removed in a **"hasty transport"** vehicle to **Timpanogos Regional
  Hospital** and pronounced dead there; a **medical examiner later told Hull** that Kirk had died.
  Recorded on [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital),
  [Charlie's Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital), and
  [Death Timeline](/Medical/Death_Timeline).
- The notes feed the site's minute-level disputes on
  [Events at the Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital) — whether death occurred en route, exact
  ER/OR minutes, and conflicting call logs — questions that matter for EMS-protocol and
  evidence-preservation analysis, not for declaring any named person a killer.
- The site pairs these hearing notes with counter-framing (an @grok summary noting the ~4-mile SUV
  run, a passed ambulance, and doctors calling the chaos call reasonable with no planned event
  ambulance) so both readings stay on the record.

## Investigative value

Hearing-notes accounts like this one are how testimony details reached the public while transcripts
lagged. The Hull "hasty transport" and medical-examiner-contact details anchor several hospital
pages; all of it should be re-checked against primary transcripts and audio as they are released.
