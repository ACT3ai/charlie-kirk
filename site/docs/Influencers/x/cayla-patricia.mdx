# Cayla_Patricia — X Posts

[@Cayla_Patricia](https://x.com/Cayla_Patricia) is one of the accounts carrying the
**foreign-phones-at-UVU** claim through 2026 X discourse. Their posts use the **"11 Israeli phones"**
count and pair it with separate allegations about day-of security communications. All items below
are attributed claims, not verified records.

## Claims from X posts

- Cited the **~11 Israeli-linked phones** figure for UVU on September 10 — one of several counts
  circulating (others say 12 or 16), a drift documented on
  [Foreign Phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU). Phone presence ≠ operational guilt, and
  no primary SIGINT or subscriber records have been publicly produced.
- By mid-2026, repeatedly **paired** the phones claim with a separate allegation that **Ben Shapiro**
  said his **IDF-connected security** was in contact with Charlie's team while Charlie was bleeding
  out in a vehicle. The site logs that pairing as **investigative rhetoric** — two disputed claims
  stacked to imply foreign coordination — on
  [Israelis present at UVU](/Israel_Main_Suspect/israelis-present-at-uvu). This site does **not**
  adopt the implication that IDF security or Shapiro caused the death.

## Investigative value

Useful mainly as a documented example of how the phone-count claim **mutates and combines** in
secondary retellings — the site's phones pages track exactly this count drift and claim-stacking.
Readers should weigh the independent skepticism recorded alongside: no documents, data dumps, or
official confirmation of the phones claim have been produced.
