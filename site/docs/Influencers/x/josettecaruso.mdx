# josettecaruso — X Posts

[@josettecaruso](https://x.com/josettecaruso) enters the site's record as the host account of a
widely cited **@PatriotVerity repost** concerning warnings issued on the day of the shooting. The
claim is **unverified** and recorded only as circulating commentary.

## Claims from X posts

- Hosted the repost claiming **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** and
  **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** received warnings on **September 10, 2025** that
  they were on an assassination-related **target list**
  ([post](https://x.com/josettecaruso/status/1968339454248337918),
  [Public Response](/Other/Public_Response)).
- The same item is logged in the site's [UVU social-media response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response)
  record among the day's viral claim clusters — alongside bystander-video claims and the
  [George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn) diversion theory — with the instruction to treat
  non-UVU items as unverified unless independently confirmed.

## Investigative value

A single-claim account in the site's record, but the target-list warning it carried is one of the
recurring **foreknowledge questions**: whether other commentators were warned on the day of the
shooting, and by whom. No primary confirmation of the warning call has surfaced.
