# Kahlissee — X Posts

[@Kahlissee](https://x.com/Kahlissee) (**Khalissee**) is a commentary account that enters the site's
record through **screenshot evidence pages** — captures of its threads filed in the photo clusters.
The claims inside those threads are the poster's own, reproduced with attribution rather than
adopted as conclusions.

## Posts in the site's record

- A composite screenshot of a Kahlissee thread containing serious, **unverified political
  allegations** linking Israeli-born US Attorney appointee **Sigal Chattah** to the protection of
  alleged child predators, paired with a LinkedIn capture of **Tom Alexandrovich** of Israel's
  National Cyber Directorate — filed as an artifact of what circulated, not endorsed
  ([Israel and Mossad — Photo 29](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_2deec4)).
- A mobile screenshot of a Kahlissee post featuring a video of
  **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** — documenting that Owens's clips were shared and
  discussed on the platform; nothing in the cluster alleges wrongdoing by Owens
  ([Candace Owens photo cluster](/Photos/People/People_candace_owens/overview)).

## Investigative value

A circulation-record account: the site preserves its threads as **screenshots** because they show
which allegations moved through the Israel-focused commentary ecosystem. The underlying allegations
about living persons remain unverified and are not findings of this site.
