# CrystalSmallen — X Posts

[@CrystalSmallen](https://x.com/CrystalSmallen) appears in the record as a contributor to two
documentation threads: the **event-setup questions** at UVU and the **press-briefing chronology**
after the shooting. Items below are attributed claims circulating on X, not proven findings.

## Claims from X posts

- Raised the **booth location change** question — that the September 10 setup differed from prior
  UVU events — logged among circulating claims (GRAMA pre-warnings, six officers vs ~3,000
  attendees, no ambulance, camera-placement debates) on
  [Free Speech Campus Issues](/Before/Free-Speech-Campus-Issues). The site marks these
  as discussion points, not established facts.
- **2026** — Recirculated behind-the-scenes / day-after briefing footage (tagging
  **@baroncoleman**) in which officials still present the suspect as **not in custody** — a data
  point in the site's press-conference chronology and its collision with the probable-cause
  statement's account of the surrender call. See
  [press conferences and statements timeline](/Timeline/press-conferences-statements-timeline).

## Investigative value

lane, and the briefing recirculation helps pin down **who said what, when** in the official
communications sequence — the same window where the "suspect in custody" alerts reversed.
