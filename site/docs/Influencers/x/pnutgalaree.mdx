# EddieSpaghetti (PnutGalaree) — X Posts

[@PnutGalaree](https://x.com/PnutGalaree) (**EddieSpaghetti**) is a citizen researcher whose
posts feed the site's mic-placement, dispatch-audio, and hospital-footage pages. Everything below
is an attributed claim from their X posts, not a confirmed finding.

## Claims from X posts

- An **October 8, 2025 (2:20 AM)** post with a video still ringing a man dubbed **"Mr. 1776"** —
  claiming he was the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) individual recorded placing the wireless lavalier
  mic on [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), and arguing a lawful autopsy would show evidence
  bearing on the mic question. The still credits [@PHIL_LYMAN](./phil-lyman) as source. The site
  reproduces this as **one poster's unverified claim about an unidentified person** —
  [Table 1776 Man](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Table_1776_Man/overview).
- **Scanner-audio analysis** identifying UVU Officer **Chris Bagley** as radio callsign "Bags":
  on archived traffic he reportedly calls in a **rooftop imprint** on the Losee Center but does
  **not** report a screwdriver or casing on air, and a dispatch call he later testified to is
  said to be **absent** from circulated archives — see the
  [dispatch radio scanner timeline](/Timeline/dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline). The site stresses
  this is **not** a finding of perjury.
- Repeated the claim, most-shared via [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), that the FBI seized
  all Timpanogos hospital surveillance footage —
  [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed).

## Investigative value

The account's scanner work gives the site one of its few **radio-versus-testimony** comparisons,
which underpins the [Bagley bodycam gap](/CoverUp/Bagley_Bodycam_Gap) questions. The Mr. 1776
identification remains an unverified crowd claim that this site does not adopt.
