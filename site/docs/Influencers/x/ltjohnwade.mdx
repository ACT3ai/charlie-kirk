# John Wade — X Posts

[@LtJohnWade](https://x.com/LtJohnWade) (**John Wade**) appears in the site's record through
screenshots preserved in the Israel photo-evidence cluster. His posts advance a
**Mossad-assassination theory** of the killing. These are **attributed opinions from X posts** —
conspiracy-theory content in the site's own description — not findings, and no foreign service
has been found by any court to be responsible for Kirk's death.

## Claims from X posts

- A self-reply post expressing the theory of a **"MOSSAD assassination"** and criticizing the
  [FBI](/FBI/overview)'s handling of the case
  ([Photo record](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_909d27)).
- An **October 18** post making a claim about an alleged "assassination" of
  [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), mentioning **Mossad and security teams**
  ([Photo record](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_960955)).

## Investigative value

The Wade screenshots are preserved as **primary-source records of the discourse** — examples of
how the Mossad theory circulated on X — rather than as evidence about the shooting itself. The
substantive treatment of foreign-involvement claims, with counterarguments, lives on the
