# Shaffer Security Group (@ShafferGroup) — X Posts

[@ShafferGroup](https://x.com/ShafferGroup) is the corporate X account of **Shaffer Security Group
(SSG)**, founded by [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer), described in reporting as a former
FBI agent. It is not a citizen investigator — it is a **first-party participant** whose since-deleted
posts are preserved as evidence of the dispute over which firm held the TPUSA security contract on
September 10, 2025.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 26, 2025, 11:33 PM** (deleted within hours, 13 views at capture) — posted that the
  security firm contracted by TPUSA when [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed "want[s] to
  remain in the shadows while my firm takes blame," stating **"SSG was not and had not been
  affiliated or contracted to do security for TPUSA since 2022,"** with the Texas business record
  for **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC** attached.
  ([Capture](/Photos/Security_Team/Security_shaffer_security_group/Img_Photo_31b790))
- **September 27, 2025** — a second post: *"My firm was NOT in Utah on 9/10/2025... The private
  security firm, hiding in the shadows, not taking responsibility for their actions is Rockhouse
  Integrity Group, dba: Integrity Security Solutions."* The attached record lists **Brian
  Harpole** as registered agent and manager.
  ([Capture](/Photos/Security_Team/Img_Photo_e5afed))
- The corporate registration core is a **public record** and independently checkable; the
  characterization of the other firm is one party's view in what reporting describes as a
  longstanding business dispute between former colleagues. Greg Shaffer repeated the substance to
  Headline USA.
- The account's **April 2017 join date** was separately captured to authenticate the deleted posts.
  ([Capture](/Photos/Security_Team/Security_shaffer_security_group/Img_Photo_b9ae00))

## Investigative value

The deleted posts are the clearest first-party statement on **which firm was actually responsible
for Kirk's private security** at UVU — a question central to the security-failure record. That the
posts were deleted within hours is itself part of the record. No wrongdoing by Brian Harpole,
Integrity Security Solutions, SSG, or anyone else named is established by these documents.
