# Rahul_EIWF — X Posts

[@Rahul_EIWF](https://x.com/Rahul_EIWF) circulates screenshots and text extracts claimed to be
from [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s private messages about donor pressure and the
pro-Israel movement. The site records these as **attributed, unauthenticated screenshot claims**
— none have been verified against original devices or court records.

## Claims from X posts

- Circulated extracts claimed to be Kirk's messages, including the quoted line that **"Jewish
  donors play into all the stereotypes"** and language about leaving the pro-Israel cause —
  indexed on the [Motive Investigation Index](/Motive/Motive_Investigation_Index) alongside
  [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'s disclosures.
- Amplified donor-pressure framing cited on
  [Donors, Israel & Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) alongside @RealCandaceO.
- Among the accounts (with @Jeannin55385803, @JustTheTweets17, @ImBreckWorsham) circulating
  screenshots presented as Charlie's own words on Israel —
  [Charlie's Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel), which cautions that the extracts
  are unverified.
- An [October 2025 post](https://x.com/Rahul_EIWF/status/1983573879650988408) is logged in the
  source trail on the [Harrison Smith](/People/harrison-smith) page.

## Investigative value

The account is part of the **screenshot-evidence lane** of the motive question: if the quoted
messages are authentic, they bear directly on donor-pressure motive theories; if not, they are a
case study in how unauthenticated text spreads. The site treats authentication as the open task.
