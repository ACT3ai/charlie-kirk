# 1MrsWhistledown — X Posts

[@1MrsWhistledown](https://x.com/1MrsWhistledown) is a public-records compiler whose **June 17, 2026** posts surfaced Texas corporate and county property filings concerning the family of [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) — the roommate and partner of [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), the man charged in the Charlie Kirk shooting. The records are lawful filings; no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of any wrongdoing. The bullets below are **claims and characterizations attributed to their X posts**, not findings.

## Claims from X posts

- Post framing (June 17, 2026): "Lance Twiggs flees to Texas post-shooting, while his parents suddenly acquire two new homes and start a 'Faith and Family' charity. The timing of these events is interesting." The words "flees" and "suddenly acquire" are the poster's characterization. ([Primary post](https://x.com/1MrsWhistledown/status/2067169575217799549))
- Attached a **Texas Secretary of State** entity record and company overview for a **"Faith and Family Foundation Charity"** — reproduced at [The Lance Twiggs Family](/People/Lance_Twiggs_Family).
- Attached two **county property ownership histories** referencing the **"13 Branches Family Trust and Twiggs"** — one property listed Oct 16, 2025, closed Dec 4, 2025 at a $350,000 list price.
- A follow-up post the same day showed the "Sold by" field: the property was sold by **Twiggs Amy R and Twiggs Mikayla R** to the family trust — an intra-family transfer consistent with ordinary estate planning. ([Follow-up post](https://x.com/1MrsWhistledown/status/2067170760238792731))
- The poster's argument is about **timing relative to [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** — a question, not evidence of any offense.

## Investigative value

The value of this account's contribution is that it is **documentary**: corporate filings and deed histories can be checked by anyone against state and county systems. The site reproduces the records with the questions left open at [The Lance Twiggs Family](/People/Lance_Twiggs_Family) and in the [Twiggs family photo cluster](/Photos/People/People_lance_twiggs_family/overview). The site's own reading notes the sold-by record reframes one "new home" as a family-trust transfer.
