# GoUncensored — X Posts

[@GoUncensored](https://x.com/GoUncensored) is cited in this investigation's coverage for
narrative-pressure commentary around the **Accurate Energetic Systems (AES) plant explosion** and
for speculative framing of how the Tyler Robinson confession story could evolve. Everything below
is an **attributed claim from their X posts**, not a finding of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **AES local-silence claim (June 25, 2026)** — alleged that a "prominent voice" on Kirk's
  assassination who lives less than an hour from McEwen, Tennessee did not mention the
  [AES factory explosion](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion) until much later — a
  narrative-pressure observation rather than a technical one.
- **Information-war confession framing (July 12, 2026)** — argued that actors who "killed Charlie"
  would need to convince the public [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is so guilty he would
  "kill himself" before those same actors "kill Tyler and fake a confession." The site records this
  only as circulating speculative language about how a cover story might evolve under trial stress —
  see [Faked Confession claims](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/faked-confession). No living person is
  found here to have faked anything; Robinson is charged, not convicted.

## Investigative value

The account contributes to the **US intelligence-assisted** cluster of theories: it does not supply
primary evidence, but its posts show how the AES/mic counter-narratives and confession-integrity
disputes are argued in public. Pair with [Discord evidence in court](/court/discord-evidence-court)
and the [Mic overview](/Mic/overview) before weighing any of it.
