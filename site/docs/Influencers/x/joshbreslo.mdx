# Josh Breslow — X Posts

[@JOSHBRESLO](https://x.com/JOSHBRESLO) (**Josh Breslow**, Status: Alive) is a television news
anchor — not a citizen investigator — who appears in the site's record through a **broadcast
artifact**: a split-screen breaking-news segment identifying an eyewitness on air. No claim of
wrongdoing is made about Breslow; his role is documentary.

## What the site records

- A phone capture of his **breaking-news split-screen segment** carries the banner **JOSH BRESLOW /
  @JOSHBRESLO** above an outdoor interview whose chyron reads **SKYLER BAIRD / WITNESS** — the
  frame that establishes, from a broadcaster's own graphic, that Baird was presented to a national
  audience as an eyewitness to the [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting
  ([the screenshot page](/Photos/People/Img_Photo_abea10)).
- The same frame anchors the [Skyler Baird photo cluster](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/Img_Photo_abea10_x):
  the interviews are real and on the record, and everything commentators later built on them —
  questions about Baird's position in the crowd and his memorial attendance — are **questions posed
  by posters, not findings**. Baird has not been charged with anything.

## Investigative value

The chyron is a small but load-bearing artifact: it fixes **who was presented as a witness, by
whom, and when** — the starting point for the witness-account record rather than any conclusion.
See [Skyler Baird's profile](/People/skylar-baird) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
