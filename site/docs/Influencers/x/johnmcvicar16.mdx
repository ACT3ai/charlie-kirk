# johnmcvicar16 — X Posts

[@johnmcvicar16](https://x.com/johnmcvicar16) is one of the amplifier accounts for the
**pre-event search-pattern analysis** attributed to [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron):
the claim that Google searches for the hospital, its doctors, and the medical examiner spiked from
**Israeli and Washington, DC IP ranges** before September 10, 2025. These are unverified search-data
claims, not confirmed forensic findings.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified (with **@MZakkiel**, **@malteselove67**, **@TheCKFiles1**, and others) the claim that
  DC-area and Israeli IPs searched the specific hospital, the emergency surgeon, and medical
  examiner **[Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** in the weeks before the shooting
  ([Israel Google Searches — Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people)).
- The broader claim set asserts foreign IPs "scouted EVERY angle pre-assassination," reportedly
  including non-medical figures — the Losee Center, [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), the
  public defender, judge [Tony F. Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf), and event questioners.
- The DC-origin search claims feed speculation about the **unidentified person picked up in the
  hospital's physician-parking area** — layered speculation the site presents only to explain the
  framing ([Doctor / DC Phone Call](/Medical/doctor_dc_phone_call)).

## Investigative value

The account documents the reach of the **search-analytics claim cluster** — one of the most
circulated pre-knowledge arguments. Its value is in mapping the amplification chain from Coleman's
analysis to X; the underlying Google Trends interpretations remain unverified.
