# Joe Kent — X Posts

[@joekent16jan19](https://x.com/joekent16jan19) (**Joe Kent**, Status: Alive) is the former U.S. Army
Special Forces officer and **National Counterterrorism Center** director whose on-record public
statements anchor the site's foreign-leads-halted record. Unlike most accounts in this section, Kent
is a **primary participant** speaking about his own halted review — his statements are attributed,
on-record claims, not independently confirmed findings, and the FBI has not endorsed his account.

## Claims from X posts and public statements

- Per **New York Times** reporting (**Oct 28, 2025**), a counterterrorism review Kent led into
  possible **foreign ties** to the assassination was reportedly halted
  ([White House Halt IC](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC)).
- Kent later told Tucker Carlson (March 2026) the NCTC was **"stopped from continuing to
  investigate"** ([Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt)).
- In a clip attributed to Stew Peters, Kent reportedly stated: *"Multiple people had pre-knowledge
  of Charlie Kirk's assassination and the FBI shut down my investigation before I was able to run
  down all of my leads."*
- Supporter accounts say Kent **stepped down in part to speak publicly** about information he
  believes is being withheld, and that when he pursued foreign-involvement leads,
  [FBI](/FBI/overview) Director Kash Patel stopped him — attributed characterizations Patel has not
  conceded.
- **July 2026** — On the **Julian Dorey Podcast** (circulated by [@juliandorey](./juliandorey)),
  Kent explained NCTC investigates assassinations **in parallel** with the FBI for foreign ties and
  stops only once none are established — and described his review being pulled off early
  ([Joe Kent Dorey Interview](/intelligence/Joe_Kent_Dorey_Interview)).
