# mmpadellan — X Posts

[@mmpadellan](https://x.com/mmpadellan) appears in the site's record as a **critic voice** — a
high-follower commentary account whose posts mocking the case's Valhalla theorizing are preserved
to show how the claims circulated and were received, not as investigative contributions.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 12, 2025** — Posted viral **mockery** of the "Valhalla" stack: Kash Patel's
  *"Rest now brother… see you in Valhalla"* tribute and the claims connecting it to a
  **Goat Island** desktop image on Tyler Robinson's computer. The site's
  [Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island) page records this alongside the proponent posts and the
  @grok counter-claim that no credible court record confirms any Valhalla/Goat Island wallpaper.
- On [Valhalla](/Locations/Valhalla), the account is listed with @aldamu_jo among those treating
  Patel's line as material for criticism or ridicule — against supporters who read it as warrior
  rhetoric and theorists who read it as a coded tell. The site asserts **no operational connection**
  between the Valhalla language and the UVU shooting.

## Investigative value

Minimal as evidence, useful as **reception history**. The site's method on contested theories is to
keep the proponents, the debunkers, and the mockers on the same page so a reader can see the full
public conversation. This account supplies the mockery data point in the Valhalla cluster.
