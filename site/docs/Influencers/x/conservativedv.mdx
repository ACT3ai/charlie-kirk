# ConservativeDV — X Posts

[@ConservativeDV](https://x.com/ConservativeDV) is a **rebuttal voice** in the flight-tracking lane:
the site cites their November 2025 posts as the leading **innocent-explanation** counterweight to
theories built on the Egyptian VIP airframe **SU-BTT** and its July 20, 2025 Nebraska stop.

## Claims from X posts

- **Nov 18, 2025** — Argued that [Egyptian special forces](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) have **trained
  in Utah and Nebraska for decades**, that TPUSA event travel overlaps those same geographies, and
  that **correlation is not causation** — an ordinary explanation that does not erase the SU-BTT
  flight log but refuses to treat it as proof of a plot. Logged on
  [Nebraska army base July 20](/Timeline/nebraska-army-base-july-20).
- The same argument is retained on [military meeting July 20](/Timeline/military-meeting-july-20)
  as the counterpoint that "severs automatic causation from geography."
- The site pairs their rebuttal with the **March 2026 control pattern** — SU-BTT again flying
  Paris → Omaha → Lincoln → Wilmington → Cairo — showing the Nebraska corridor is a **recurring**
  Egyptian VIP route rather than a one-off summer 2025 anomaly.

## Investigative value

This account matters because the site deliberately preserves **both sides** of the flight-data
argument. @ConservativeDV supplies the strongest publicly posted benign reading of the SU-BTT
timeline; readers should weigh it against the arc that ends with SU-BTT at Provo through the event
window and departing for Cairo the day after the assassination.
