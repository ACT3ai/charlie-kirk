# Leah Files — X Posts

[@theleahfiles](https://x.com/theleahfiles) (**Leah Files**) is the independent financial researcher behind the most-cited **TPUSA insider-payments analysis** in this investigation — a reading of the organization's public 990 filings that the Wolves And Finance YouTube channel built on. **Every bullet below is an attributed allegation from her threads, not a finding of fraud.** No person or company named is accused in any legal proceeding, and TPUSA's attorney Robert Barnes has broadly rejected the accusations as "false" with "no proof."

## Claims from X posts

- A chart of roughly **ten years of TPUSA insider payments**: **$9M+** to **"Resource One"** — described as a DBA for **[Moore DM Group](/TPUSA/Companies/moore-dm-group)**, a large political direct-mail company, while the 990 lists ~$1.35M for printing; the chain is linked in her threads to **[1Ten LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/1ten-llc)** ([TPUSA Succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession)).
- **$999K** reportedly paid to **[Clocktower LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clocktower-llc)** (dissolved 2022) for a "research project" with no public deliverables — framed by the thread as a possible slush fund.
- **[Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan)**, Senior Advancement Director, reportedly received **$2.7M+** through entities such as [Lionrock LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/lionrock-llc) and [Cloverstone LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/cloverstone-llc) "while salaried for the same job" — an allegation based on one investigator's reading of public filings ([insider payments and 990s](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/insider-payments-990s)).
- **July 20, 2026 (Part 3)** — The Sheridan fundraising-entity chain (**GSM Strategy → Lionrock → Cloverstone**) allegedly took custody of tens of millions in donations (poster figures include **greater than $88M** through custody and **~$5M+** private fees), framing Schedule L / private-inurement questions — **analysis of filings, not a verdict**, with **no public government charge** ([TPUSA organizational timeline](/Timeline/tpusa-organizational-timeline)).
- **Lori Frantzve** (Erika Kirk's mother) reportedly sat on the board while **$15.2M+** allegedly flowed to insider-controlled companies, with a separate thread placing her on the [Superfeed Technologies](/TPUSA/Companies/superfeed-technologies) board.
