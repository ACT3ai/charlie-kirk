# OutnumberedFNC — X Posts

[@OutnumberedFNC](https://x.com/OutnumberedFNC) is the X account of Fox News Channel's
*Outnumbered* program. It enters the site's record not as a citizen investigator but as a
**mainstream amplifier** of court-access developments in the Tyler Robinson proceedings — one of
the few establishment accounts whose posts the investigation pages cite.

## Claims from X posts

- **December 29, 2025** (~1.2M views) — Post covering the release of a **redacted transcript of a
  closed-door hearing** to media, with Judge **[Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** citing a
  **presumptive public right** to court records.
  ([Source](https://x.com/OutnumberedFNC/status/2005690225675403421)) Recorded on
  [Legal Process, Gag Orders, and Secrecy](/After/Legal_Process_Gag_Orders_And_Secrecy).
- Listed (with @EricLDaugh and @GuntherEagleman) among accounts amplifying rulings associated with
  Judge Graf **rejecting broad defense sealing efforts** and allowing **cameras** in key hearings —
  the "public access wins" thread of the case. See the
  [legal investigation overview](/legal_investigation/overview), which cautions that exact docket
  language should be checked against primary court files.

## Investigative value

These posts document the **transparency ledger** of the proceedings: what the court opened versus
what it sealed or gagged. The site sets them against the countervailing entries — the December 2025
gag order, exhibit-broadcast sanctions, and sealing fights — so readers can weigh access wins and
secrecy moves together.
