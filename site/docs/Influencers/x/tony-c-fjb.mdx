# Tony_C_FJB — X Posts

[@Tony_C_FJB](https://x.com/Tony_C_FJB) is a citizen commentary account cited on this site in two very different lanes: the **soft-point ballistics debate** and the **extreme-fringe end** of the Fort Huachuca allegations. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — the second item is expressly flagged as a severe unproven accusation.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 12, 2026** — Soft-point rounds *"do not fragment in 4 inches of human flesh"*; labs could not confirm the metal pieces as bullet fragments in the way headlines imply; and *"all tests were inconclusive"* — part of the vernacular energy-mismatch debate ([.30-06 did not kill Charlie](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/thirty-06-did-not-kill-charlie)). Active counters in the same feed argue a .30-06 **can** fail to exit under the facts presented.
- **Fort Huachuca fringe claims** — Alleged Erika Kirk was with **Cabot Phillips** at Huachuca on 9/8 and that Phillips met Brian Harpole on 9/9 to "get the mic." These are **severe accusations against living people circulating without any court finding** — recorded on [the Fort Huachuca page](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca) only to show what citizen investigators are saying, and **explicitly not adopted as fact** by this site. See [Cabot alibi](/US_Intelligence/Cabot_alibi).

## Investigative value

The account illustrates the **spectrum problem** in citizen coverage: a defensible technical question (fragmentation behavior of soft points) sitting next to unverified accusations against named living people. This site separates the two — the ballistics point joins the open forensic file; the Huachuca claims stay quarantined as attributed speech with innocent explanations unrefuted.
