# TriciaThom40938 — X Posts

[@TriciaThom40938](https://x.com/TriciaThom40938) contributes **security-failure analogy commentary**,
placing the UVU event inside the frame of the **Butler, Pennsylvania** Trump assassination attempt.
The comparison is commentary, not forensic proof that the same failure mode occurred at UVU.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026** — Compared UVU to **Butler, PA**: "signs were there" — cameras and known threats
  existed — yet security did not act before the shot. Catalogued on
  [No Counter-Snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) as analogy commentary.
- Placed front-of-house **screening failures** in the same Butler-style frame: known threats plus
  surveillance coverage, yet suspicious pre-shot behavior (in the official Robinson narrative) was
  not stopped — see [Screening and Credentials](/Security_Team/screening-credentials). The analogy
  does **not** prove any Q&A participant was part of a plot.

## Investigative value

The Butler analogy is one of the recurring frames citizen investigators use to argue the UVU
security posture was indefensible rather than merely unlucky: rooftops unsecured despite documented
death threats, six campus officers for roughly 3,000 attendees, and no counter-sniper overwatch.
This account's posts keep that comparative frame in circulation; the underlying staffing facts are
developed on the [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) pages.
