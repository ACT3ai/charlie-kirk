# MaxtheCrowhouse — X Posts

[@MaxtheCrowhouse](https://x.com/MaxtheCrowhouse) (associated with **The Crowhouse**) appears in
the site's record as one of the accounts circulating the **alleged final-days Israel quotes**
attributed to Charlie Kirk. These are **alleged, not confirmed, statements** — the underlying
message logs have not been authenticated in publicly available court records.

## Claims from X posts

- Circulated phrases attributed to Kirk including **"I'm going to have to stop supporting
  Israel…"** and **"You've left me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"** — quote
  extracts used to argue Kirk was breaking from prior pro-Israel positions in private
  communications ([Charlie Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel)).
- The same attributed line is cited on [Israeli-Agenda Criticism](/Media/Israeli-Agenda-Criticism)
  among the recurring lines circulating in the 48 hours before Kirk's death — where the site
  notes mainstream desks labeled these as **alleged** unless tied to Andrew Kolvet's limited
  confirmation of parts of the September 8 chat, while alternative media presented them as motive
  proof.

## Investigative value

The account is part of the amplification chain for the leave-the-cause quotes — the material
that turns Kirk's donor dispute into the central motive claim of the Israel-suspect lane. The
site's treatment: verify any quote against primary video or authenticated text before treating
it as established.
