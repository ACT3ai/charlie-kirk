# Willdbuckley — X Posts

[@Willdbuckley](https://x.com/Willdbuckley) appears in the record as a relay of **Losee Center
rooftop** witness material during the July 2026 preliminary-hearing window, drawing on Daily Caller
reporting. The items are hearing-adjacent recounts, not court transcripts.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 6, 2026** — Relayed witness recounts (via @DailyCaller) of a **screwdriver** and a
  **distinct gravel impression** found on the Losee roof, with line-of-sight to the courtyard — the
  position the charging narrative identifies as the shooter's perch. See
  [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center).
- The relays circulate alongside the **Officer Bagley bodycam** chain-of-custody flashpoints — the
  camera reportedly dying on roof arrival and an unnamed second badge-holder — tracked at
  [Officer Bagley Bodycam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam).
- Listed among the top rooftop-evidence threads in the
  [UVU Investigation Index](/UVU/UVU_Investigation_Index), and cited in the
  [Tyler on September 10](/Tyler_Robinson/Tyler_9_10_2025) evidence roundup.

## Investigative value

The screwdriver and gravel-impression recounts are physical-scene details that must reconcile with
Bagley's testimony, the evidence photos, and the archived dispatch record — analysts note no
screwdriver or casing call appears in the scanner audio. That reconciliation problem is why this
relay thread stays in circulation.
