# Fritz (diddlezs) — X Posts

[@diddlezs](https://x.com/diddlezs) (**Fritz**) is a flight-tracking contributor in the aircraft
lane of the investigation. The account supplies **Flightradar24 map data** in replies to
[John Cullen](./john-cullen)'s aircraft threads and receives an explicit hat-tip credit in the
site's [SU-BTT plane movements timeline](/Timeline/su-btt-plane-movements-timeline). All items
below are attributed tracker-data claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **Sept 10, 2025 — SU-BND same-day window** — Per **@I_Am_JohnCullen** (h/t **@diddlezs**),
  [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND) — the "blue plane," serial **1332** — had its transponder come online
  on the Provo tarmac about **21 minutes before** the shot, remained powered roughly **an hour
  after**, then went dark **without departing that day**. Aircraft presence is not involvement;
  the jet is separately described as leaving Utah on Sept 13.
- A reply from the account with a **Flightradar24 screenshot** of aircraft tracking data appears
  in the photographic record — see [Aircraft Photo 68](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_4e36fd), a
  capture of a [John Cullen](/Influencers/john-cullen) post about a military surveillance jet with Fritz's map data beneath it.

## Investigative value

The account illustrates how the aircraft thread was assembled collaboratively: Cullen's
narrative claims rest partly on tracker screenshots contributed by accounts like @diddlezs.
Transponder on/off windows are **tracker reconstructions**, not verified flight logs — the
records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) would compel into the open.
