# Camelot Jack (CamelotJack1963) — X Posts

[@CamelotJack1963](https://x.com/CamelotJack1963) (**Camelot Jack**) is one of the case's
working **GRAMA records-request filers** — an account that produces documents rather than
commentary. The site cites the account both for what its requests returned and for what was
refused. Claims drawn from those productions remain subject to verification against UVU's full
unredacted records.

## Claims from X posts

- Credited by [@ProjectConstitu](./project-constitution) (November 9, 2025) for the **GRAMA work**
  behind the UVU **paving texts**: redacted contractor/staff screenshots said to show paving
  kicked off **September 11** — one day after the killing — while the FBI still had the scene
  locked, with a uniformed officer posted to keep media away and a contractor writing workers
  were "well-trained in keeping quiet." Recorded on
  [Signs of Cover-Up](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/signs-of-cover-up) as among the strongest
  process-level documents citizen researchers cite.
- Filed and publicized a **GRAMA request for UVU security-camera footage** that was **denied**,
  posting "Request denied" and "Appeal will be filed" in a thread with @BasedSamParker —
  archived in the [Security Cameras photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Cameras/Img_Photo_ec6181)
  and the [UVU Venue cluster](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Img_Photo_10e148), where the site notes the
  footage has not been released and UVU acknowledged holding additional records.
- The @CamelotJack1963 / @ProjectConstitu records model is held up on
  [Your Actions Fix It](/Your_Actions_Fix_It/overview) as the template for lawful citizen
  pressure: file and litigate GRAMA/FOIA with specific record IDs.

## Investigative value

This is the **records lane** of the citizen investigation: requests, denials, appeals, and
partially redacted productions. The paving texts and the camera-footage refusal are exactly the
category of withheld material the proposed [disclosure laws](/Fix/overview) are written to
reach. Authenticity of redacted screenshots should be checked against UVU's own GRAMA
production log.
