# MuppetMasher — X Posts

[@MuppetMasher](https://x.com/MuppetMasher) (**Muppet Masher**) is one of the most-cited citizen
investigators on this site — nearly forty pages reference the account. Their work spans on-camera
**witness interviews**, spatial analysis of the **gun-drop location**, and **identification threads**
for people near the stage. All items below are attributed claims and citizen research, not sworn
testimony or court findings.

## Investigative value

MuppetMasher's interviews put **named, on-camera witnesses** into the record — primary material the
site can transcribe and cross-reference — while the drop-location and identification threads supply
checkable geography and names. The site verifies against court maps and records where possible and
flags everything else as OSINT.
