# TPV_John — X Posts

[@TPV_John](https://x.com/TPV_John) is a citizen commentary account working the **exploding-mic evidence lane** — frame-level debris readings and vehicle-handling allegations. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts**; they are visual interpretations and process allegations, not laboratory results or court findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **Fall-moment debris reading** — Described shards at the moment of the hit **resembling mic glass**, plus **battery-fragment claims** from the Yukon floor — visual interpretation only, part of the frame-by-frame record on [the audio/acoustic/mic timeline](/Timeline/audio-acoustic-mic-timeline).
- **SUV carpet removal** — Alleged (with [@TheDibsterX](./thedibsterx), June 30, 2026) that the transport SUV's **carpet** — claimed to hold mic particles or explosive residue — was removed and the vehicle **sold or shipped to a California car lot**, analogized by posters to rapid 9/11 steel removal ([Signs of Cover-Up](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/signs-of-cover-up)). Vehicle chain of custody remains an open FOIA target; no court has found evidence destruction.

## Investigative value

Both threads feed the site's [RØDE mic](/Mic/burnt-object-rode-transmitter) and SUV-debris pages, where the counterpoint is always recorded: an SUV floor during a chaotic evacuation is not a controlled forensic environment, and released chain-of-custody records would settle the vehicle question. Robinson is charged, not convicted, and the mic theory remains a contested hypothesis.
