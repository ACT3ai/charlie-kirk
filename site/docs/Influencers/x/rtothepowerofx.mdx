# RtothepowerofX — X Posts

[@RtothepowerofX](https://x.com/RtothepowerofX) (account name **David**) occupies an unusual place
in the coverage: it is both a poster cited in crowd-footage discussion and, per **anonymous online
researchers**, the alleged handle of the crowd figure himself. Every link between the handle and any
crowd figure is an **unverified allegation by anonymous accounts**. Being present in a crowd, or
reacting to a shooting, is not evidence of involvement in any crime, and nothing on this page is a
finding of wrongdoing against any living person.

## Claims and appearances from X posts

- The handle is cited in discussion of the [Curly Hair Man](/distraction_people/Curly_Hair_Man) —
  a crowd figure alleged to have turned toward the shooter's direction, stood when others ducked,
  and reacted approvingly; the same figure is elsewhere identified as the
  [USA-Chant Man](/distraction_people/USA_Chant_Man), who reportedly said the chant was meant as a
  calming distraction, not celebration.
- Crowd-sourced facial matching has tried to attach names to that figure —
  [David Sprague](/distraction_people/David_Sprague),
  [James Norman Rawlinson](/distraction_people/James_Norman_Rawlinson),
  [Matt Taber](/distraction_people/Matt_Taber) — **none confirmed** by any reliable on-the-record
  source, with @RtothepowerofX cited online in connection with him.
- The account posted a first-person statement responding to being identified in crowd footage —
  *"every lie about me brings me one step closer to becoming very dead... I'm terrified for my
  family and sad for Charlie's too"* — preserved at
  [The Crowd Man's Own Post](/Photos/Patsy/Img_KOP_27768a).
- The handle appears among the commentary sources for
  [The List of Distraction People (Claims)](/CoverUp/Distraction_People).

## Investigative value

This page exists mainly to keep the subject's **own words** next to the claims made about him. The
identification chain is entirely anonymous crowd-sourcing; innocent explanations for the filmed
behavior are at least as plausible, and the site does not vouch for any of the circulating names.
