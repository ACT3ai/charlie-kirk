# JenEllen773 — X Posts

[@JenEllen773](https://x.com/JenEllen773) is a citizen-investigator account posting on the
**post-assassination resignation wave** and the **George Zinn distraction question**. Everything
below is an attributed online claim — not a court finding of evidence destruction or coordination
by any living person.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 7, 2026** — Paired Sheriff **Nate Brooksby's** resignation with claims that Tyler
  Robinson's **surrender video was unavailable** and that UVU Officer **Christopher Bagley**
  (of the body-cam "died" testimony) resigned from UVU in **January 2026**
  ([post](https://x.com/JenEllen773/status/2074511433807593721),
  [Institutional Resignations](/After/Institutional_Resignations)).
- Argued that identifying **who (if anyone) contracted** [George Zinn's](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)
  "I shot him, now shoot me" spectacle would reveal organizers of a multi-actor operation — an
  **investigative hypothesis** the site records without adopting; no one has been shown to have
  hired Zinn.
- The equipment-and-personnel timing claims sit alongside the site's separate coverage of the
  [Bagley body-cam gap](/CoverUp/Bagley_Bodycam_Gap) and remain online allegations.

## Investigative value

The account connects **personnel departures** (Brooksby, Bagley, Tuminez-era UVU) to **evidence
availability questions** (surrender video, body-cam footage) — the linkage pattern the
[After](/After/Institutional_Resignations) section inventories. Useful as a map of which timing
questions citizen investigators consider unresolved.
