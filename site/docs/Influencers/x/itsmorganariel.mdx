# itsmorganariel — X Posts

[@itsmorganariel](https://x.com/itsmorganariel) is a citizen-investigator account cited for relay
and correction work across the timeline pages — moving Candace Owens material, date fixes, and
security-gap claims into wider circulation. Every bullet is an attributed claim from X posts, not a
confirmed finding.

## Claims from X posts

- **RAV4 / FBI-badge tip relay (Dec 2025)** — relayed [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'
  public discussion of a tip from multiple police officers alleging that during the manhunt a white
  Toyota RAV4 was stopped for speeding and the driver **flashed an FBI badge** — an attributed tip
  relay, not authenticated law-enforcement confirmation; tracked on the
  [Candace Owens timeline](/Timeline/candace-owens-timeline) and
  [RAV4 flashed badge](/Suspicious/FBI/rav4-flashed-badge).
- **Date correction: Sept 8 → Sept 9** — per commentary on Josh Hammer's later statements, argued
  Kirk's "no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause" message may date to **September 9** rather
  than September 8 — less than 24 hours before the shooting; logged on the
  [2025 timeline](/Timeline/2025) and the
  [Charlie Kirk personal timeline](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk).
- **Early "they are going to kill me" circulation** — among the first accounts (with @AdameMedia)
  circulating the reported text the same day it surfaced — see
  [the text page](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).
- **No-exit-wound skepticism** — among users calling the absence of an exit wound from a ~142-yard
  .30-06 shot "impossible" or highly suspicious — an opinion the site pairs with the forensic
  counterpoint that rifle rounds do sometimes fragment and lodge; see
  [Hospital People and Bullet Claims](/Medical/hospital_people_and_bullet_claims).
- **TPUSA security-plan claim** — cited on [No Security Plan for Sept 11](/TPUSA/no-security-plan-sept-11),
  where users linked the claim to other alleged lapses (rooftops, drones, the hospital car ride,
  911 calls).

## Investigative value

The account functions as a **timeline hygiene** contributor: its date-correction and relay posts
force reconciliation between viral packages and primary records. Its medical opinions sit in the
skeptic column and are balanced on-site by forensic counterpoints.
