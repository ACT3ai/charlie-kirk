# Harrison Smith — X Posts

[@HarrisonHSmith](https://x.com/HarrisonHSmith) (**Harrison Smith**) is a broadcaster whose single
most-cited contribution to this investigation is a **pre-assassination post**: a secondhand report
of Charlie Kirk's own fear, published four weeks before the shooting. It is a contemporaneous fear
claim about Kirk's belief — **not proof of who killed him**.

## Claims from X posts

- **August 13, 2025** — posted: "I'm not gonna name names, but I was told by someone close to
  Charlie Kirk that Charlie thinks **Israel will kill him if he turns against them**." The post
  pre-dates the September 9 "they are going to kill me" text reports and reached tens of thousands
  of likes — see the [2025 timeline](/Timeline/2025) and
  [Year 2025 timeline](/Timeline/Year_2025).
- **September 10, 2025 confirmation** — on the day of the assassination, the account **@ViceLitty**
  quoted the August post and asked "Is this true @HarrisonHSmith I only need a yes or no." Smith
  replied one hour later with a single word: "**yes**." The quoted post shows 2:34 PM, 10 Sep 25 and
  240K views — documented with the screenshot on
  [the Israel photo page](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_f18540).
- The post is cross-referenced against the reported September 9
  ["they are going to kill me" text](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) as an earlier,
  independent occurrence of the same fear claim.

## Investigative value

Because the post is **timestamped before the assassination**, it cannot be a retrofitted narrative —
which is why researchers treat it as one of the strongest contemporaneous fear artifacts. It remains
secondhand ("someone close to Charlie"), unnamed, and unverified against any primary message log.
