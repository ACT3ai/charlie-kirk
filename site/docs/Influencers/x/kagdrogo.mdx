# KAGdrogo — X Posts

[@KAGdrogo](https://x.com/KAGdrogo) is an amplifier account in the **Hamptons donor-meeting**
narrative — the reported August 2025 gathering at which Candace Owens alleges pressure was applied
to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) over Israel positions. All items are attributed claims and
rhetorical framings by the poster; no court has found that any attendee threatened or harmed Kirk.

## Claims from X posts

- Framed the reported Hamptons meeting as an aggressive **"intervention"**, and characterized the
  subsequent Netanyahu invitation to Israel as a **"last warning"** — rhetorical framing by the
  poster, recorded as such
  ([Donors, Israel and Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy)).
- **October 7, 2025** (~419k views) — Recirculated a **[Harrison Smith](/People/harrison-smith)**
  video segment with the claim it was recorded **"the day after"** the Hamptons weekend
  (~August 5–6, 2025), offered as contemporaneous evidence of Kirk's mood — the day-after dating is
  the poster's assertion, not a verified recording date
  ([Hamptons Intervention Meeting](/Timeline/hamptons-intervention-meeting)).
- Listed among the amplifications (with **@5149jamesli**, **@Uncommonsince76**) of Owens's account
  naming **[Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman)** in the meeting narrative — Owens's claim, not a
  court finding; the site does not state Ackman participated in or knew of any plan to harm Kirk.

## Investigative value

The account illustrates how the Hamptons narrative hardened in circulation: Owens's firsthand
allegation, plus dated video artifacts, plus poster-added framing language. The site separates
each layer — see the [timeline reconstruction](/Timeline/hamptons-intervention-meeting) for what is
claimed versus dated versus asserted.
