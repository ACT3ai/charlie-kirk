# Steven DeNoon — X Posts

[@stevendenoon](https://x.com/stevendenoon) (**Steven DeNoon**) is a YouTube and X commentator who
describes himself as a former CIA analyst — a personal claim this site does not independently
verify. His person profile is at [Steven DeNoon](/People/steven-denoon); this page compiles the
claims his posts and videos advance. All are **attributed analyst commentary**, contested by other
analysts and unconfirmed by official investigators.

## Claims from X posts and videos

- Presented what he describes as evidence pinpointing an **alternative shooter location**, built on
  an **infrared laser reflection** said to appear briefly on [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s
  phone screen at the moment of the shot — analyzed with @ProjectConstitu, @WeAreNotGTM, and others.
  See [Close Range Theories](/Killer/Close_Range_Theories).
- Advanced versions of the **cartridge-casing reading**: a case appearing at the top edge of the
  tent, rolling down the tarp — argued to fit a close-range weapon rather than a 142-yard rifle
  shot. See the [MonoPod camera cluster](/Photos/Truck_Tent/MonoPod_Camera/overview).
- Amplified the **CornerShot concept** — an Israeli-made hinged mount allowing a pistol to fire
  around a corner via camera — as a candidate mechanism
  ([YouTube breakdown](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zeioeWdmSIw)).
- His grey-van and laser trajectory work overlaps the [Grey Van
  Shooter](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter) thread — rear hatch opening seconds before the shot,
  enhanced stills said to show an occupant — documented as overlap, not as an assertion that a
  weapon was deployed from the van.
- Cited (with @ProjectConstitu) as a secondhand amplifier of **Dr. Chris Martenson**'s
  energy-mismatch analysis on [Cause of Death: Energy
  Mismatch](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/energy-mismatch), and on the
  [CIA](/US_Intelligence/cia) page as independent analyst commentary, not agency disclosure.

## Investigative value

DeNoon's content maps which alternative-mechanism theories reach large audiences. It is
**opinion/analysis media**: no corner-fired weapon has been recovered or entered into evidence, and
every technical claim should be cross-checked against [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and
multi-source video before being treated as fact.
