# Pete Hegseth — X Posts

[@PeteHegseth](https://x.com/PeteHegseth) (**Pete Hegseth**) is a senior U.S. defense official
whose X account appears in the investigation coverage indirectly: in **July 2026**, citizen
accounts (for example replies from **@hurtfeelingzday** quoting @PeteHegseth) restated claims
tying military aircraft movements to the sequence around the AES plant explosion. Hegseth himself
has made no statement connecting these events, and nothing on this site alleges wrongdoing by him.

## Claims from X posts (as cited in reply threads)

- Reply chains quoting @PeteHegseth restated the claim that jet tail **99-0404** overflew the
  **AES factory** site after activity at **Fort Huachuca** — see
  [AES Factory Explosion](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion).
- The same threads sit inside the wider reported sequence — senior defense travel to
  [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca), a JTF element described as fired or
  reassigned, then the plant explosion — a framing publicly advanced by commentator
  [Stew Peters](/Mic/Stew_Peters_Broke_AES_Mic), not by Hegseth.
- The site flags these overflight and sequence claims as **unconfirmed** items for verification
  via ADS-B archives and FOIA, not as findings.

## Investigative value

This page exists because reply-thread citations of a cabinet-level account gave the 99-0404
overflight claim unusual reach. The underlying aviation claims live on the
[Planes](/Planes/overview) pages; Hegseth's account is the amplification vector cited, not a
source of the theory.
