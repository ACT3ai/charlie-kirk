# Liz Wheeler — X Posts

[@Liz_Wheeler](https://x.com/Liz_Wheeler) (**Liz Wheeler**, conservative commentator and podcast
host — see her [People profile](/People/liz-wheeler)) is cited on this site as a
**mainstream-adjacent counterweight** to several citizen-investigator timeline claims. Items
below are her **attributed commentary**; she asserts no wrongdoing by anyone, and no living
person is accused of wrongdoing here.

## Claims from X posts

- **Version B surrender rebuttal (July 2026)** — Argued that Robinson's **physical surrender came
  ~9 PM MT**, so the 7:57 PM Discord confession is a full hour **before** body presence at the
  sheriff's office — with formal arrest possibly as late as 4:00 AM September 12. This is the
  main mainstream-side answer to the custody-before-confession argument
  ([Arrest & Affidavit Timeline](/Timeline/arrest-booking-affidavit-timeline),
  [Tyler Robinson Timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson)).
- **Preliminary-hearing coverage (July 2026)** — Among the commentators (with @BlakeSNeff and
  @JackPosobiec) describing the surveillance video prosecutors played: roughly **four campus
  visits / multi-loop scouting** the morning of September 10, including an on-campus Chick-fil-A
  stop before the clothing-change window
  ([Pre-Shot Campus Timeline](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus)).
- **Paving counter-explanation** — Associated with commentary arguing investigators **released**
  the UVU scene after multi-day processing, and that paving addressed biohazard cleanup and
  campus reopening, not evidence destruction — against the "Cox ordered it paved over" claim, for
  which no document has been produced ([Utah Officials / Cox Timeline](/Timeline/utah-officials-cox-timeline)).

## Investigative value

citizen claims: on surrender sequencing, campus scouting, and scene handling, her framings are
