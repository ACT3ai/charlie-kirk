# LionelMedia — X Posts

[@LionelMedia](https://x.com/LionelMedia) (the media commentator known as **Lionel**) amplified
one of the clearest **defense-side syntheses** of the Tyler Robinson case in July 2026. The items
below are **attributed legal commentary**, not court findings. Robinson is charged, not
convicted, and presumed innocent.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026** — Amplified the framing that the state's case is a stack of **association**
  evidence that skips the ultimate causal act: screwdriver on roof means contact with a
  screwdriver, not a trigger pull; DNA on rifle/towel/casing means handling, not necessarily the
  fatal shot; campus video means presence, not identity-proof of the shot; vehicle and shoe
  impressions mean movement, not wound mechanism
  ([More Reasons](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/more-reasons)).
- The missing element in this framing is the **"causal bridge"** — conclusive proof that Robinson
  fired the projectile that killed Kirk — which the ATF's **inconclusive** fragment comparison
  leaves open ([A .30-06 Did Not Kill Charlie](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/thirty-06-did-not-kill-charlie)).
- The site notes this framing's caveat in the other direction: association evidence can satisfy
  **probable cause** at a preliminary hearing without closing the causation gap — a preliminary
  finding is not a verdict either way.

## Investigative value

The "association is not causation" formulation became the shorthand for the entire
ballistics-skepticism lane — it is quoted across the Proof Not Tyler pages as the cleanest
statement of what the July 2026 preliminary hearing did and did not establish.
