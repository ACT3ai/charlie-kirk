# DanCampbel44604 — X Posts

[@DanCampbel44604](https://x.com/DanCampbel44604) is the original poster of the **annotated SUV
interior seating image** that drives the [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) discussion threads. The
image was quote-reposted by @RealTruthJustic on **June 26, 2026**
([post](https://x.com/RealTruthJustic/status/2070597252133818558)) with the comment "Could this be
it." Important: the original post's caption made **serious accusations against named living people**
that are **unverified**, were **not published** to this site, and are **not repeated here**.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 26, 2026** — Posted an annotated still of the SUV that transported
  [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), labeling seating positions with names including
  **Rick Cutler** and Brian Mericle. The identifications are unverified; presence in a transport
  vehicle would naturally include security, staff, and medical responders, and indicates no role in
  Kirk's death. See [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).
- The caption text made extreme accusations (shape charge, strangulation) against named SUV
  occupants. The investigation file's compiler judged that material **not fit to publish**, cropped
  the image to remove the caption, and published only the seating photo with a disclaimer — a
  handling decision the site documents on
  [Cutler hand device](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/cutler-hand-device), which rates the claim
  **THIN** and notes the post **cites no evidence** for its caption.
- The image also feeds the **Pero gesture-device** speculation about an object near Cutler's hand —
  an unverified suggestion with no official finding tying any device to the shooting. See
  [Rick Cutler — Person of Interest](/Suspects/Rick_Cutler_POI).

## Investigative value

The account's seating annotation is a useful **who-was-where** record *if accurate* — but its
accusatory framing is the site's canonical example of material that fails the publication bar. No
wrongdoing by Rick Cutler or any named SUV occupant is alleged or established; Mr. Cutler is
reportedly a retired police officer with 29 years of service.
