# Julian Dorey — X Posts

[@juliandorey](https://x.com/juliandorey) (**Julian Dorey**, Status: Alive) hosts the **Julian Dorey
Podcast** and is the account that put former NCTC director **[Joe Kent's](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)**
first-person account of the halted foreign-ties review into mass circulation in July 2026.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 20, 2026** — Posted the clip *"Joe Kent Addresses the Charlie Kirk Assassination
  STRANGENESS..."*, in which Kent explains that NCTC investigates assassinations of prominent
  figures **in parallel** with the [FBI](/FBI/overview), looking for foreign ties, and describes his
  center's review being **halted early**
  ([primary post](https://x.com/juliandorey/status/2079257528513499568),
  [Joe Kent Dorey Interview](/intelligence/Joe_Kent_Dorey_Interview)).
- The clip was quote-tweeted by **@ArtifexMemor** ("This is what an American patriot looks like...")
  and embedded on the site's home page and Kent profile
  ([amplification post](https://x.com/ArtifexMemor/status/2079560806690324910)).
- The interview is the fullest first-person account of the halt that reporting had previously
  described secondhand (NYT Oct 2025, Tucker Carlson interview March 2026) — Kent's statements
  remain attributed, on-record claims not endorsed by the FBI.

## Investigative value

Dorey's platform converted the NCTC-halt story from **secondhand reporting to first-person
testimony** on video. As podcast journalism rather than citizen OSINT, the account's value is
access: the clip is now the reference artifact for what Kent himself says happened.
