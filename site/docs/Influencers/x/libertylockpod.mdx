# Clint Russell — X Posts

[@LibertyLockPod](https://x.com/LibertyLockPod) (**Clint Russell**, host of the Liberty Lockdown
podcast) appears in the site's record both as a source of a widely shared timeline narrative and
as a **skeptical checker** of other citizen claims. All items below are **attributed claims from
X posts**; no living person named is accused of wrongdoing by this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **The Josh Hammer "based" timeline post** — A screenshot preserved in the site's photo evidence
  shows a Russell post alleging a timeline involving [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk),
  [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), and [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump), with an embedded
  dark-mode screenshot of Hammer replying "based" to a 2013 Trump tweet calling for public
  executions ([Photo record](/Photos/Suspicious_Anomalies/Img_Photo_6b657c)). The juxtaposition
  is the poster's framing, not proof of foreknowledge by anyone.
- **Notification-order latency challenge** — With @BasedSamParker, challenged @NihiloX's claim
  that [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro) received real-time condition updates before Erika Kirk
  was connected, arguing the math against a roughly **10-minute hospital run** means any
  "security update" claim before ~12:33 MT should be **timestamp-proved**
  ([12:23 shooting time](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time)).

## Investigative value

Russell's role illustrates both sides of the citizen-investigation coin: amplifying a suggestive
juxtaposition (the Hammer screenshot) while applying rigor to someone else's timing claim. The
latency challenge is one of the site's examples of investigators policing their own lane.
