# SelinaAnneXX — X Posts

[@SelinaAnneXX](https://x.com/SelinaAnneXX) is a frame-by-frame analyst whose zoomed composites of
the UVU tent scene surfaced physical-scene details that published coverage passed over. The bullets
below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — questions and observations, not findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 19, 2026** — posted a composite marking **crushed Kirkland water bottles** wedged under
  [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s sneakers on the low metal riser of the tent platform,
  asking what they were doing there and what security head **Dan Flood** was standing on (described
  in the thread as a separate riser on a hard-shell equipment case).
  ([Source](https://x.com/SelinaAnneXX/status/2078856527541362697)) Full write-up:
  [Platform Water Bottles](/Tent/Platform_Water_Bottles).
- Asked who held the **low, close-range vantage** the tight crop was shot from — *"who was standing
  in the perfect spot to take it?"* The images matter because the platform surface was
  **excavated and paved over on September 14, 2025**, so objects on it survive only in photographs.
  See [Paving Timeline September 14](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14).
- The same thread included an AI-chat screenshot ("What are his feet resting on?") and a four-panel
  composite advancing a speculative device theory that the site **does not endorse** and no
  published forensic examination has validated. Images preserved at
  [Tent Water Bottles cluster](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Tent_Water_Bottles/overview).
- **June 2026** — published detailed dossiers on [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), alleging a
  quick arm/hand motion under a sleeve at the moment of the hit, a wrist band some interpret as a
  wearable device, and little prior public footprint at TPUSA events — unverified video
  interpretation about a living person, reported as allegation only.

## Investigative value

The account's contribution is **scene preservation**: cataloguing surfaces and objects that were
physically removed days later. The evidentiary questions it raises — were the platform and riser
photographed and inventoried before removal — are answerable ones. The device theories attached to
the same threads remain speculation.
