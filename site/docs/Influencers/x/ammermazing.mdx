# Ammermazing — X Posts

[@Ammermazing](https://x.com/Ammermazing) is the OSINT account most credited across the site for the **Israeli-IP Google Trends** claim set — reported pre-event search spikes for medical, legal, and contractor figures who later touched the case. All of it is **attributed, unverified trend analysis**: Google Trends geography cannot identify who searched, and the living persons named are expressly not accused of anything.

## Claims from X posts

- **[Dr. Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)** — Utah's chief medical examiner (full-time from July 1, 2025) reportedly saw **sudden Israel-origin search interest** in summer 2025 while barely being searched in DC or Utah. See [Medical &amp; Legal Periphery](/key_individuals/Medical_Legal_Periphery).
- **[Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital)** — the receiving hospital reportedly searched from Israeli/DC IPs in the same July–August 2025 window. Collected at [Timpanogos and medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches).
- **[Daniel Merrell / Hardscape Utah](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah)** — the contractor who paved the scene on 9/14/25 reportedly searched from Israeli IPs earlier that summer. The commentary (relayed via [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll)) expressly states it is "not accusing Daniel Merrell of anything nefarious." See [Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) and [crime scene handling](/Law_Enforcement/crime-scene-handling).
- **Legal figures** — claims of unusual pre-event searches for names including Lunnen, Graf, Zenger, and Syed (e.g. a Lunnen search May 15, a Graf search July 26) — flagged as unverified on [Pre-Event Personnel Swaps](/CoverUp/Pre_Event_Personnel_Swaps).
- **[Kathryn Nester](/People/kathryn-nester)** — included in the site's index of Nester-related Trends spike attributions. See [Investigators and Media](/key_individuals/Investigators_And_Media).

## Investigative value

The Trends threads are the connective tissue of the "pre-planning layer" hypothesis — the argument that hospital, examiner, contractor, and judge names drew foreign search interest before they had any public connection to Charlie Kirk. The counterargument is methodological: Trends normalizes to 100, rare names spike from a handful of queries, and geography is not identity. The site holds both readings at [Israeli Search Patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims).
