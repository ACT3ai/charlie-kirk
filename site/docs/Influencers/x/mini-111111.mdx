# Mini_111111 — X Posts

[@Mini_111111](https://x.com/Mini_111111) is a citizen-investigator account cited on the site's
aircraft, evidence-custody, and confession-timeline pages. The bullets below are **claims attributed
to their X posts** — unverified public allegations, several of which are disputed in the same
threads where they appeared.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified the **N59906** thread (with @ProjectConstitu, @AnaEscobarShow, @leahfiles, and
  @StefaniWilcox8) alleging the MARC Inc. plane circling UVU during the event ties, through
  **Alpha Whiskey 2 LLC**, to a company where **Alexis Wilkins** — reported as Kash Patel's
  girlfriend — is registered agent. The claim is **unproven and disputed in the same threads**,
  including Grok rebuttals stating no public evidence connects the LLCs or people to the flight.
  See [N59906](/Planes/N59906).
- **July 6, 2026** — Amplified community notes that a person identified as **"Terryl"** gave Utah
  SBI a **new flash drive with only four selected clips** from the cameras behind Kirk, not the
  original full recordings — a **chain-of-custody / selective-clip** claim. See
  [FBI Witness Intimidation](/Legal/FBI-Witness-Intimidation).
- Same date — posts on **crime-scene seconds**, revisiting claims that a TPUSA tech lead removed a
  camera from behind Kirk, folded into the site's
  [FBI–Local Coordination](/Legal/FBI-Local-Coordination) coverage.
- **June 27, 2026** — Argued that if the Discord confession messages were **not produced by Tyler
  Robinson**, Baron Coleman's multi-agency cover-up framing follows — a citizen inference from the
  Bates-number and booking timeline, not a court ruling. See
  [Faked Confession](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/faked-confession).

## Investigative value

The account concentrates on **custody questions**: who holds the original footage, who owned the
surveillance aircraft, and whether the confession record matches the custody clock. Each claim is
recorded here as attribution only; the aircraft-ownership allegation in particular carries explicit
rebuttals on the referenced page.
