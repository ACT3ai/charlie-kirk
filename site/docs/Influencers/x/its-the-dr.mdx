# Johnny Midnight — X Posts

[@its_The_Dr](https://x.com/its_The_Dr) (**Johnny Midnight**) is cited in the official-narrative
coverage for one of the most-viewed early attempts to reconcile two incompatible witness accounts —
"shot in the heart" versus the neck wound visible on video. His theory is reproduced as the
speaker's stated opinion, not established fact.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 12, 2025 — armor-fragmentation theory** — posted a video arguing Kirk "was actually
  **shot in the heart** but he was wearing **body armor**, the bullet fragmented and hit his
  throat" — one of the more-viewed reconciliations (~61k+ views) of the Fox News heart-shot witness
  wording with the neck injury. Documented, with its strong-language caveat, on
  [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart) and in the
  [photo cluster](/Photos/Official_Narrative/Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart/Img_Photo_Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart_6).
  ([Primary post](https://x.com/its_The_Dr/status/1966293765158560167))
- The same video layers on further **unproven** allegations — a professional shooter, an
  intelligence operation, and Kirk's reported fear of being targeted — all recorded as the
  speaker's theory only. Note: [Based Sam Parker](/Influencers/sam-parker) later reported Kirk was **not** wearing body armor,
  a directly conflicting claim tracked on [Based Sam Parker](./based-sam-parker).

## Investigative value

The post marks the point where the heart-shot thread turned from **testimony to interpretation**:
witnesses said heart and chest, the footage shows neck bleeding, and this theory proposes a
mechanism producing both. No autopsy release has confirmed or excluded it; the wound-mechanism
dispute continues on the Medical pages.
