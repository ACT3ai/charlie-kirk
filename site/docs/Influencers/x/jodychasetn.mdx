# JodyChaseTN — X Posts

[@JodyChaseTN](https://x.com/JodyChaseTN) is one of the highest-volume amplifier accounts in the
site's **cover-up and scene-alteration** record, spanning the paving contractor story, the transport
SUV's fate, courtroom-video disputes, and resignation timing. All items are attributed claims and
questions, not findings of wrongdoing by any living person.
