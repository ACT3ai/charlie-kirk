# Gillx_AF — X Posts

[@Gillx_AF](https://x.com/Gillx_AF) is cited in the investigation record for pressing one
recurring question in the **scene-handling** debate: why the courtyard where
[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot was reportedly dug out and repaved within days.
Items below are attributed claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 12, 2026** — Argued that skeptics of the mic-device theory still have to explain why
  crews allegedly dug **~10 inches** of dirt and **repaved the scene in under 72 hours** — the
  scene-destruction argument logged on
  [explosive mic killed Charlie (claims)](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/explosive-mic-killed-charlie).
- Linked the 10-inch dig / rapid repave to people who dismiss device theories **without
  explaining scene destruction** — catalogued with the wider aftermath claims on
  [signs of cover-up](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/signs-of-cover-up).
- The account (with others) treats the reported dig-and-repave of the kill area within ~72 hours
  as the reason they reject "impossible explosive mic" scoffing — a **scene-control argument**
  connected to the [RØDE mic device thread](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic).

## Investigative value

The repave argument is **circumstantial support**, not mechanism proof: rapid repair can equally
be crisis logistics, as campus officials and mainstream coverage describe. The site records both
readings. The official prosecution remains a **rifle / gunshot-wound** case, and
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted.
