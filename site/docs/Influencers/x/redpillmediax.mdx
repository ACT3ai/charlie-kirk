# RedPillMediaX — X Posts

[@RedPillMediaX](https://x.com/RedPillMediaX) is cited on the site's timeline pages for
high-engagement posts in the first days after the assassination. The claims are attributed
screenshot assertions, not settled facts.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 11–13, 2025** posts with screenshots (alongside @jacksonhinkle, @AdameMedia, and
  @BGatesIsaPyscho) asserting that the **Jerusalem Post was the first major outlet** to confirm
  Charlie Kirk's death — logged on the
  [media vs citizen investigation timeline](/Timeline/media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline).
- Some of the same posts tied the early foreign-outlet clock to the afternoon custody-and-release
  of a different man; the site notes **no major outlet has published a full first-publication
  timestamp ledger** (time zone plus source desk) to settle the claim — see
  [OR vs Jerusalem Post](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post) and the
  [medical death timeline](/Timeline/medical-death-timeline).

## Investigative value

The JPost-first claim is one of the earliest and most persistent anomaly assertions in the case.
This account is among its primary circulators; resolving it requires the timestamp ledger the
site identifies as the missing verification step.
