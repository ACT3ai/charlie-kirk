of the accounts casting **George Zinn's** on-scene false confession as a designed element of a
coordinated operation. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — poster
framing, not adjudicated findings.

  obstruction as real but uncoordinated.

## Investigative value

Zinn's admitted obstruction is court record; whether it was coordinated is not. This account
documents the strongest form of the coordination reading, which the site keeps next to the official
position so readers can weigh both. Nothing here is a factual finding that any living, uncharged
individual participated in a crime.
