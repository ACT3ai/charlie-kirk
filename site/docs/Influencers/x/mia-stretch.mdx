# Mia_Stretch — X Posts

[@Mia_Stretch](https://x.com/Mia_Stretch) is a citizen researcher credited across the site's
hospital and Qureshi-family threads, working alongside the [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)
and Baron Coleman research ecosystem. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts and
credited research** — explicitly "draw your own conclusions" coincidence threads, not confirmed
findings, and not court findings about any living person.

## Claims from X posts

- Credited with the **Romney connection** in the Timpanogos Hospital thread — the claim that the
  hospital's ER was suspiciously empty on September 10, that the [FBI](/FBI/overview) seized interior
  footage quickly, and that newly installed staff had connections worth examining. The site rates
  this claim **THIN** — internally contradicted and partly self-retracted. See
  [Empty ER and Personnel Changes](/Suspicious/Medical/empty-er-personnel-changes).
- Credited (with Baron Coleman's USASpending records work) in the **Qureshi family** research thread:
  public-record data points around [Zachariah Qureshi](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi), described as the son
  of [Ahmed Qureshi](/People/ahmed-qureshi), reportedly a U.S. Navy O-6 and co-founder of a
  Dallas-based technology firm with reported **$8M DoD contracts** (Bilt Inc.). Presented by
  commentators as reported coincidences to weigh, not wrongdoing.
- Credited in the [Gina Romney Henderson](/People/gina-romney-henderson) and
  [Connor Henderson](/People/connor-henderson) coincidence threads — none of it independently
  verified.
- **October 7, 2025** — Posted the annotated escort photograph of **Russell Kennington**, noting his
  booking on **9/11/25 at 12:33 PM** for criminal trespass and felony obstruction related to the UVU
  crime scene, and asking whether an escorting officer was carrying a rifle. The booking time is the
  only independently checkable element. See the
  [image analysis](/Photos/Patsy/Img_Photo_a97388) and
  [Russell Kennington](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington).

## Investigative value

Mia_Stretch functions as a **records-and-connections researcher** inside larger commentary threads:
booking sheets, corporate profiles, LinkedIn inference, and family-tree work that others amplify.
The site carries the checkable facts and flags the rest as unverified — several of the credited
claims contradict themselves or were retracted in the underlying file.
