# jacksonhinkle — X Posts

[@jacksonhinkle](https://x.com/jacksonhinkle) (**Jackson Hinkle**) is a high-engagement X commentator whose
posts entered the site's record on the **media-timing question**: whether a foreign outlet announced
[Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) death before the modeled pronouncement window closed. All items
below are **claims attributed to his X posts**, not confirmed findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **Sept 11–13, 2025** — Posted or recirculated screenshots asserting the **Jerusalem Post was the
  first major outlet** to confirm Kirk's death, alongside **@RedPillMediaX**, **@AdameMedia**, and
  **@BGatesIsaPyscho** ([Media vs Citizen Timeline](/Timeline/media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline)).
- The claim collides with the **~12:56–1:13 PM MDT** earliest-realistic pronouncement window modeled
  from transport and operating-room time — if the JPost timestamp holds, the death was reportedly
  known outside the hospital before the official pronouncement
  ([Medical & Death Timeline](/Timeline/medical-death-timeline)).
- Some posts in this cluster also tied the early foreign-outlet clock to the afternoon
  **custody-and-release** of a different man ([Suspect Manhunt](/Law_Enforcement/suspect-manhunt)).
- No major outlet has published a full first-publication timestamp ledger (time zone + source desk)
  to settle the claim — the site treats it as an **open timestamp dispute**.

## Investigative value

Hinkle's amplification kept the **JPost-first** question in mass circulation during the critical
Sept 11–13 window. The underlying dispute is analyzed at
[The OR Timeline Versus the Jerusalem Post](/Suspicious/Medical/or-timeline-jerusalem-post); his posts
are amplification evidence of what circulated and when, not proof of foreknowledge by anyone.
