# John Cullen — X Posts

@I_Am_JohnCullen is among the citizen investigators posting flight data, ballistics questions, drone sightings, and agency-involvement theories about the UVU shooting. This page is the raw claim inventory; the narrative profile of his work is at **[John Cullen](/Influencers/john-cullen)**. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings. Each line links to the cited post where available.

## Claims from X posts

- [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was fatally shot in the neck during an event at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1965905334561616366))
- No suspect was in custody immediately after the shooting, and it was an active investigation involving Orem Police, Utah Valley University Police, the FBI, and the Utah Department of Public Safety.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1965905334561616366))
- The shooter was approximately 130 yards away from Charlie Kirk.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1966111044973244889))
- The bullet that killed Charlie Kirk was not recovered as of September 13, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1966671807617941891))
- There is no ballistic connection between the recovered rifle and the bullet that killed Charlie Kirk since the bullet was not found.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1966671807617941891))
- A .30-06 caliber rifle with a scope was found wrapped in a towel in a wooded area near UVU on September 11, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1967440635922612609))
- Forensic evidence, including the projectile, was recovered for analysis according to the Utah Medical Examiner's Office on September 12, 2025, but the full report is restricted.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1967953311094415453))
- A US Army [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) spy plane (N1098L) descended to 335 feet AGL and slowed to 118 knots near UVU 20 minutes after the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1969392393595347321))
- The same HADES spy plane performed a similar maneuver three hours before the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1969392393595347321))
- The HADES spy plane may have deployed UAVs to carry out the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1969392393595347321))
- The account claims [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) was running drones over UVU an hour before Charlie Kirk's event.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975953729636782490))
- Charlie Kirk feared assassination before the UVU shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975953729636782490))
- Charlie Kirk refused to wear a bulletproof vest the day he was killed.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1970460813769973872))
- A UAV took off 7 seconds before Charlie Kirk was shot.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1973959611519836281))
- An Egyptian government aircraft ([SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)) was in Provo, Utah, on the day Charlie Kirk was murdered.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1977321375376519463))
- [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), a Dassault Falcon 7X operated by the Egyptian Air Force, arrived at Provo on September 4, 2025, for maintenance and departed on September 10, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1977719896625529235))
- SU-BND, a Gulfstream IV-SP operated by the Egyptian Air Force, was in Provo for maintenance and departed on September 13, 2025.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1977720764921967095))
- N1098L, a HADES G6500 spy plane, was at 204 feet AGL and 118 knots two miles from UVU 20 minutes after Charlie Kirk was shot.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1970561850308776391))
- The same spy plane was involved in a similar maneuver three hours prior to the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1970561850308776391))
- Charlie Kirk was shot from behind.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1971391855901409656))
- No one in the vicinity reacted to a shot from close range, inconsistent with a nearby shooter.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975986026377248901))
- The angle from a nearby vehicle trunk would require the shot to originate over 6 feet high, with no evidence of that.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975988842412032103))
- A van/SUV with an open back hatch was present prior to Charlie Kirk taking the stage.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975965356125774106))
- No exit wound from a .30-06 round is physically impossible.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975984621008585034))
- Two shooters may have been involved: one close and one far away, possibly with a .22 pistol with suppressor.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1975982887012057295))
- The police response at UVU after the shooting was analyzed for inconsistencies.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1969866389730324951))
- [Skye Valadez](/People/skye-valadez) was a valid suspect due to uploading tracks like "Charlie Kirk Dead at 31" before the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1966539898917097806))
- Elbit units may have been involved in the killing.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1977328898544406852))
- The [CIA](/US_Intelligence/cia) killed Charlie Kirk using UAVs deployed from a spy plane.  ([Source](https://x.com/I_Am_JohnCullen/status/1971391855901409656))
