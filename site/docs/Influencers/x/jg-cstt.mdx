# JG CSTT — X Posts

@JG_CSTT is among the citizen investigators posting flight data, ballistics questions, drone sightings, and agency-involvement theories about the UVU shooting. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings. Each line links to the cited post where available.

## Claims from X posts

- [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot from behind by a low muzzle velocity round fired from a PCP gun.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1975543076161729003))
- A supersonic round was fired from in front of Charlie Kirk, but it did not hit him and was meant as a distraction.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977496256030085543))
- The kill shot on Charlie Kirk came from the tunnel or van behind him, possibly using a .50 caliber PCP gun or similar low-velocity weapon.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1970358585331982516))
- Drones were present at UVU during the shooting, conducting electronic warfare to disable unauthorized recordings.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1966932228912419210))
- An ISR aircraft owned by Lasai Aviation II LLC was loitering over Utah Lake near UVU during the assassination, possibly involved in surveillance or disruption.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1969174004490449313))
- A minivan's rear hatch opened seconds before Charlie Kirk was shot, potentially indicating the shooter's location.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1975689674795475050))
- Charlie Kirk planned a DOGE-style audit of [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) finances shortly before his death, which may have provided a motive for his assassination.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977246945111343188))
- A person was hiding behind the underpass at UVU in a position suitable for a rear shot on Charlie Kirk.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1970616915962769817))
- Unknown flying objects recorded at UVU were drones, not birds or insects, with up to three different ones present.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1967478416778887580))
- A drone disrupted the only known livestream of the event at the moment of the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1966863968330449139))
- The projectile that killed Charlie Kirk was something like an 8mm steel ball traveling 200-300 feet per second.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1970358585331982516))
- Anduril drones may have been on an ISR mission over UVU during the assassination.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1969455539521196369))
- A supersonic rifle round from the roof was fired to mislead witnesses, but the fatal shot came from behind using a tactical crossbow or similar.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1968509669929820216))
- An Egyptian Air Force jet departed Provo the morning of the shooting, but this is unrelated due to timing.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977350311741096353))
- Charlie Kirk was not wearing body armor at the time of the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1966863968330449139))
- A person resembling the assassin was seen jumping off the roof at UVU carrying an object, possibly a rifle.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1966537983810580819))
- The gun used in the shooting was left in the woods after the assassin protected it during escape.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1966352504838344858))
- An exploding lavalier microphone could not explain the wound, as it remained attached to an unstained shirt.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1971444576268509472))
- Google Trends showed searches for "Sorensen Center" from DC and Israel IPs 12 days before the shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977705888753270987))
- The account claims LeonJakeB arrived at UVU campus at 11:22 AM, just before the assassination. Placement is asserted from open-source material and is unconfirmed.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977794500215853439))
- The account alleges that [Sameer Massey](/People/sameer-massey) gave an inaccurate account of recording the assassination video, arguing he was inside a building at the time. Massey has not been charged with anything, no record supports the allegation, and this site makes no finding that he said anything untrue.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1976147779492688092))
- The account alleges that [Blake Bednarz](/People/blake-bednarz) gave an inaccurate account of having original recordings and questions the handling of his footage. Bednarz has not been charged with anything, the allegation is unproven, and this site makes no finding that he misstated anything or altered any evidence.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1976698947034132550))
- The wound on Charlie Kirk's neck is an exit wound, supporting a shot from behind.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1977758665185558837))
- Audio forensics indicate multiple shots, with the fatal one from behind at low velocity.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1976294654589964352))
- No hospital surgeon name was released, and the coroner's report may be controlled or inaccurate.  ([Source](https://x.com/JG_CSTT/status/1976645593020936471))
