# Grok — X Replies

[@grok](https://x.com/grok) is **xAI's AI assistant on X**, not a human investigator. Its automated
replies and summaries are cited across this site chiefly as the **counter-claim and
official-narrative-adjacent voice** in citizen-investigator threads. AI replies are not primary
evidence; they show which arguments circulate and how the mainstream framing answers them.

## Claims from X posts

- **ATF ballistics (July 7, 2026)** — summarized that the ATF exam on fragments from Kirk was
  **inconclusive** (could neither confirm nor exclude the .30-06 rifle) while a spent cartridge case
  did match — see [Assassination Investigation](/Legal/Assassination-Investigation) and
  [.30-06 Did Not Kill Charlie](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/thirty-06-did-not-kill-charlie).
- **Prelim recaps (July 2026)** — noted **GSR was not presented** as evidence, named
  **David Hull** as the Utah DPS investigative sergeant reported to have led the state's presentation
  ([Utah DPS / Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason)), and recapped Davis-testimony
  phone-seizure timing ([custody vs Discord timeline](/Timeline/custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline)).
- **Surrender footage** — replied across threads that no surrender footage has been publicly
  released, with "no records"/30-day-overwrite explanations —
  [Sheriff Video Withheld](/CoverUp/Sheriff_Video_Withheld).
- **Mic-theory counters** — stated the device was a RØDE Wireless PRO (July 4, 2026), argued
  battery energy is insufficient for the neck wound (July 6, 2026), and stated crack-bang delay is
  consistent with a distant rifle — [RØDE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic),
  [Canon XA55 Audio](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio).
- **Early witness summaries (Sept 11, 2025)** — flagged the Fox News "shot in the heart" witness
  wording ([post](https://x.com/grok/status/1966248546732224913),
  [related](https://x.com/grok/status/1966214396847468859)) — see
  [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart).
- **Debunk attempts** — called the Goat Island / Valhalla wallpaper stack unverified
  ([Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island)) and defended the SUV hospital run as reasonable given no
  planned ambulance ([Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital)).

## Investigative value

Grok replies function as a **circulating counter-argument archive**: when citizen claims spread,
the AI's answers show the official-adjacent rebuttal in the same thread. This site quotes them for
balance, never as adjudication — verify every summary against primary filings and lab reports.
