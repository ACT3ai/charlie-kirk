# SamParkerSenate — X Posts

[@SamParkerSenate](https://x.com/SamParkerSenate) is a handle of researcher **Sam Parker**, whose
main compiled-claims page lives at [Based Sam Parker](./based-sam-parker). The @SamParkerSenate
handle appears (usually alongside @BasedSamParker) as the **watermark on Parker's evidence
graphics** — the Google Trends composites preserved in the site's Photos section. All items below
are Parker's attributed correlation claims, not findings.

## Claims from watermarked graphics

- **Utah autopsy law** — a graphic claiming Utah's 2025 law criminalizing the sharing of autopsy
  photos was drafted by attorney **Greg Gunn**, whose name shows Google-search spikes **filtered to
  Israel** in June 2024 (months before the bill) and May 2025 (the week it took effect).
  Preserved at [Utah Changed Its Autopsy Law](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/Img_Law_Autopsy_dd7e13); written
  treatment at [Utah Autopsy Law Changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes).
- **Judge vacancy timeline** — a saved Hoodline article (April 13, 2025) showing the Fourth District
  shortlist including **Tony Graf**, five months before the assassination — a documented timeline
  fact, nothing more. See [the court-legal capture](/Photos/Court_Legal/Img_Photo_81af02) and
  [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).
- **Graf / Lunnen searches** — a Trends panel showing single isolated Israel-region spikes for both
  **Tony Graf** and retiring judge **Robert Lunnen** in a mid-2025 window.
  ([Capture](/Photos/Court_Legal/Img_Photo_f3a6f5))
- **Skyler Baird searches** — a four-panel collage showing "skyler Baird" peaking in **Tel Aviv,
  Haifa, and DC** before September 10 while **Utah** returned no data.
  ([Cluster](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/overview))

## Methodological caution

Every one of these is a **Google Trends correlation claim**. Trends reports relative, normalized
interest; a peak of 100 in a low-volume region can rest on a handful of searches, and the tool does
not identify who searched or why. The site records the claims as the poster makes them and states
the caution alongside. No wrongdoing by any named person is alleged or implied.
