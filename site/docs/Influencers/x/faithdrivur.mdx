# FaithDrivUr — X Posts

[@FaithDrivUr](https://x.com/FaithDrivUr) is the source of one of July 2026's most-tracked open
questions: whether the person police identify as the assassin made contact with a
[Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) representative on the UVU quad hours before
[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot. The claim is a **request for identification and
footage**, not an allegation that TPUSA or any staffer participated in the shooting.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 19, 2026** — Posted a ~27-second clip asking: "WHO was this Turning Point USA employee
  who was TALKING with the ASSASSIN a few HOURS BEFORE Charlie Kirk was murdered???", tagging
  @FinanceWolves, @TPUSA, @AndrewKolvet, and @RealCandaceO.
  ([Primary post](https://x.com/FaithDrivUr/status/2078917940993311136)) — full transcript on
  [the TPUSA–Tyler Robinson chat page](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat).
- The clip characterizes police as saying the assassin **arrived on campus at 9:24 AM** and went
  to the quad, where he "makes contact with some representatives from TPUSA" — a time that
  **conflicts** with the law-enforcement-referenced **8:29 AM** Dodge Challenger arrival; see
  [the pre-shot campus timeline](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus) and
  [the September 2025 Tyler Robinson timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept).
- No TPUSA employee has been named, and no primary law-enforcement document with a 9:24 AM time
  has been located. The baseline explanation the site records first: TPUSA staff were tabling on
  the quad all morning, so a brief exchange would be unremarkable — context on
  [the TPUSA organizational timeline](/Timeline/tpusa-organizational-timeline).

## Investigative value

The post crystallizes a checkable question: quad-camera footage from the 9:00–10:30 AM window
would settle whether any meeting occurred and with whom. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)
is **charged, not convicted**, and this site does not assert any TPUSA staffer had foreknowledge.
