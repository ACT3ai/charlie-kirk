# malteselove67 — X Posts

[@malteselove67](https://x.com/malteselove67) is one of the amplifying accounts in the
**pre-event search-pattern lane** — the claim that Google activity from foreign and DC IP ranges
targeted the medical figures who would later handle Charlie Kirk. All items below are
**attributed claims** built on Google Trends-style analysis that the site treats as leads, never
as identification of any searcher.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified the **Baron Coleman**-attributed analysis that pre-event searches for the hospital,
  its **seven Timpanogos surgeons**, the emergency doctor, and the medical examiner originated
  from **both Israeli and Washington, DC IP ranges** in the weeks before the shooting
  ([Israel Google Searches — Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people)).
- Listed (with @MZakkiel, @TheCKFiles1, @johnmcvicar16) among the accounts describing **DC-area
  searches** for the specific hospital, surgeon, and medical examiner — the strand that feeds
  speculation about the physician-parking pickup
  ([Doctor DC Phone Call](/Medical/doctor_dc_phone_call)).
- The broader assertion amplified in these threads: foreign IPs allegedly "scouted EVERY angle
  pre-assassination," with medical staff one cluster among many — a claim the site pairs with the
  skeptic position that Trends data is sampled noise and cannot identify searchers.

## Investigative value

The account's role is amplification within the medical-searches cluster — one of the
investigation's most cited but most methodologically contested data sets. The site's rule
applies: Trends anomalies are **leads**, not proof of foreign-state involvement.
