# SeeRacists — X Posts

[@SeeRacists](https://x.com/SeeRacists) appears in the site's Tyler Robinson pages as one of the
**earliest viral broadcasters** of the surrender story. The bullets below are **claims attributed to
their X posts** and match mainstream turn-in reporting of the time — not a unique exclusive.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 12, 2025** — was among the accounts broadcasting that a white male identified as
  [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) had been **turned in by his own father** after a
  nationwide manhunt. See [Tyler Robinson: Family](/Tyler_Robinson/Family).
- The early posts helped fix the **father-assisted turn-in** as the dominant public chronology of
  the surrender — the baseline that later custody-timeline skeptics measure against. See
  [Tyler Robinson: Surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender).
- Later skeptic commentary (documented on the same pages) questions **Miranda timing**, the
  confession-after-custody sequence, and whether surrender CCTV has been fully released — questions
  raised by other accounts against the narrative these posts established.

## Investigative value

The account's value is chronological: it marks what the public was told, and when, in the first
48 hours. Whether family cooperation was civic duty, pressure, or part of a managed intake is
speculation debated by others — not something these posts establish. Robinson is charged and
presumed innocent.
