# Headline USA — X Posts

[@HeadlineUSA](https://x.com/HeadlineUSA) (**Headline USA**) is a news outlet account cited in the
Security Team section for reporting on **which private firm is said to have been guarding Charlie Kirk**
on September 10, 2025. Its reporting is a media interview claim about a business dispute — not
proof of criminal intent by anyone named.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 28, 2025** — reported that a **since-deleted post from Shaffer Security Group**
  identified **Integrity Security Solutions** (Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC, founded by
  [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole)) as the firm allegedly guarding Kirk when he was
  killed — see [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security).
- **The "stabbed in the back" account** — SSG founder **Greg Shaffer** told the outlet he was
  "stabbed in the back" over how Integrity came to the detail, after SSG had guarded earlier tour
  stops. The site records this as a **vendor-transition / business-dispute narrative**, documented
  on the [Security Team overview](/Security_Team/overview) alongside AP reporting that the firm was
  rehired at the last minute with a setup lighter than Kirk's prior events.

## Investigative value

The report matters because the **identity of the contracted security firm** anchors every
downstream question — rooftop responsibility, staffing levels, bodycam absence, and the SUV
evacuation roster. It is one of the few named-source media artifacts in the private-security
thread; the deleted SSG post it describes has not been independently archived here.
