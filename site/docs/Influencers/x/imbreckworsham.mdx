# ThePatrioticBlonde — X Posts

[@ImBreckWorsham](https://x.com/ImBreckWorsham) (**ThePatrioticBlonde**) is a citizen commentator
whose court-watching posts are cited across the discovery and cover-up sections. The bullets below
are claims attributed to their X posts and to their quotations of open-court statements — not
findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **Raw DNA data withheld (April 2026)** — reported defense counsel telling the court that neither
  **ATF nor FBI** had turned over raw **DNA analysis data files** despite September 2025 summary
  reports citing that data: "We know these data files exist because we have summary ATF and FBI
  reports summarizing the analysis…" — the case's clearest **Brady** framing, tracked on
  [Ballistics / ATF / CBLA](/court/ballistics-atf-cbla),
  [Discovery Brady Disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes),
  [Discovery Document Dump](/CoverUp/Discovery_Document_Dump) and the
  [discovery-Brady evidence timeline](/Timeline/discovery-brady-evidence-timeline).
- **White House intel-halt post (Oct 29, 2025)** — summarized the Daily Mail account of a reported
  White House roundtable (Patel, Kent, Gabbard, Vance, Wiles, DOJ officials) touching the halt of
  intelligence-community inquiry, arguing it "should tell you everything you need to know" — see
  [White House Halt IC](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC).
- **34-second timeline critique** — posted that under the FBI's story, Robinson took off his
  clothing, changed, disassembled his firearm, loaded the backpack, and jumped off the roof "in 34
  seconds" — preserved in the [photo archive](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Img_Photo_691edf).
- **Intake-clock argument** — argued that under ordinary Washington County intake practice a phone
  would be booked into evidence by ~6:50 PM Sept 11, making a handset-origin 7:57 PM Discord post
  impossible under an early-custody reading — with the standard counter that Discord is reachable
  from any device; see the
  [custody / surrender / Mirandizing timeline](/Timeline/custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline).
- **Israel message screenshots** — among accounts circulating alleged extracts of Kirk's private
  messages about leaving the pro-Israel cause — unauthenticated; see
  [Charlie's Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).

## Investigative value

The account's core contribution is **Brady-discipline framing**: distinguishing summary reports
from raw underlying data, and volume of production from access to contested items. Its open-court
quotations should be checked against docket transcripts as they become available.
