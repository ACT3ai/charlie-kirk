# Sunny61120 — X Posts

[@Sunny61120](https://x.com/Sunny61120) is a citizen OSINT account focused on the **UVU surveillance record** — specifically alleged inconsistencies in the exported stairwell and parking-garage frames shown around the July 2026 preliminary hearing. Everything below is an **attributed allegation from their X posts**, not an established fact.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 9, 2026** — Stairwell camera-location labels allegedly **flip between "level 2 NE" and "level 1 NE"** on what appears to be the **same physical stairwell** with the **same parked white car** — framed by the poster as a possible **manual edit** of exported frames ([Tyler On Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras), [UVU Surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance)).
- The label discrepancy is logged on this site as an **open question**, with the mundane counter-explanation that **clerical labeling slips are common** ([Tyler Robinson pre-shot campus timeline](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus), [UVU HD Footage Withheld](/CoverUp/UVU_HD_Footage_Withheld)).
- Alongside **@Cousin_Fat_Fat**, alleged **start-time gaps** and Challenger **exhaust/bumper mismatches** in garage footage offered as showing Tyler Robinson — **OSINT disputes**, not dispositions ([FBI Official Narrative](/FBI/Official_Narrative)).
- The alteration reading supports (if true) the broader **"[placeholder](/cameras/Tyler_Placeholder)" interpretation** that the released clips identify the rooftop figure by caption and inference rather than by face.

## Investigative value

The camera-label claim is a small, checkable assertion: releasing **original DVR exports with embedded timestamps** would resolve it instantly. That makes @Sunny61120's posts a recurring citation in the site's arguments for full raw-footage disclosure. No court has found any footage was edited, and this site does not assert it was.
