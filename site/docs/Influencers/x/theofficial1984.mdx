# 1984 — X Posts

[@TheOfficial1984](https://x.com/TheOfficial1984) (**1984**) is a technically focused account whose threads on **automated content moderation** are preserved in this site's [Censorship photo cluster](/Photos/Censorship_Evidence/overview). The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts**; the technical descriptions of published research systems are plausible on their face, but the operational claims about who acts on the outputs are the poster's and are **not adopted here**.

## Claims from X posts

- The **ADL** allegedly assigns a "social credit score" on X using machine-learning models built at **UC Berkeley's D-Lab** — the **Online Hate Index V3 (OHI V3)** ([extended thread capture](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Img_Photo_56ab30)).
- Technical slides describe transcribed text fed into a **fine-tuned transformer-based neural network** (BERT / RoBERTa architectures), with **semantic analysis** for implicit bias and dog-whistles, **contextual scoring**, and **thresholding** at 0.9 confidence triggering alerts.
- The thread further alleges the system can **identify users by voice** from X Spaces audio, that it **flags content**, and that "Israeli intelligence removes it" — claims about voice ID and foreign-service action that are **unverified and not adopted by this site**.
- Related posts allege X moderation teams working with **CHEQ** and **AU10TIX** (described in the posts as Israeli-intelligence-linked firms) and academic labs to monitor, label, and suppress content in real time, and discuss **"labeling bias"** in hate-speech classification models ([thread captures](/Photos/Censorship_Evidence/Img_Photo_91fee5)).

## Investigative value

Automated flagging is directly relevant to **how quickly investigation material disappeared from platforms** — the reason these screenshots are preserved under [Censorship](/Censorship/overview). The checkable core (published classifier research) should be separated from the unverifiable claims layered on top of it.
