# TallyHallAlbum — X Posts

**@TallyHallAlbum** is different from the other accounts in this section: it is not a citizen investigator but a **since-removed X account** whose screenshots circulated widely in the days after September 10, 2025. The account holder has **not been publicly identified**, no charge has been reported, and nothing on this page asserts that any person had advance knowledge of anything.

## Claims from X posts

- A post attributed to the account, timestamped **2:47 AM on September 3, 2025** (169 views displayed), read in lowercase that it would be *"funny"* if someone like **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** got shot **on September 10th** — one week before the assassination ([photo cluster](/Photos/FBI/TallyHallAlbum/overview)).
- A second post attributed to the same account names **[Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump)** and a December date.
- Replies captured in the collages show users **tagging federal accounts** and asking the FBI director to identify whoever ran the account because, in one reply's words, they *"knew a day ahead."*
- The screenshots show the posts and the **account were later deleted or suspended**; a composite of the deleted posts was recirculated by **@MJTruthUltra** with commentary and replies ([Collage of the Deleted Posts](/Photos/FBI/TallyHallAlbum/Img_Photo_42c839)).

Screenshots can be edited and everything here is a screenshot of a social-media post, not a verified record. What the images document is that the posts existed publicly, that users escalated them to federal accounts, and that the account disappeared.

## Investigative value

The cluster sits under the [FBI photo section](/Photos/FBI/overview) because the entire thread is a public demand that the bureau examine something — and an open question about whether that examination ever happened. It belongs with the other pre-event warning material at [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).
