# TriggerSmart — X Posts

[@triggersmarthq](https://x.com/triggersmarthq) (**TriggerSmart**) is one of the primary **hearing-notes
accounts** for the July 2026 preliminary hearing in *State v. Tyler Robinson*. Their live summaries —
**hearing notes, not transcripts** — are cited across the site's law-enforcement, legal, and timeline
pages as the public's fastest window into testimony.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 8, 2026** — Day 3 notes on **Officer Davis** (SBI cold-case / case agent with Hull): a
  **~8:30 PM September 11** message that someone would surrender, arrival at **St. George Airport
  ~11:30 PM**, viewing Robinson on a room TV at the Washington County office, and official detention
  logged **September 12 ~4:00 AM**. ([Post](https://x.com/triggersmarthq/status/2074941273048502756)) —
  see [Sept 11 Surrender and Custody](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody).
- Listed the **multi-agency roster**: SBI primary, plus **FBI, ATF, UVU police, and Orem police** —
  a list that tensions with the "Orem PD not involved" public line, per
  [Orem Police Response](/Law_Enforcement/orem-police-response) and [FBI Role](/Law_Enforcement/fbi-role).
- Reported the **mother interviewed with one FBI agent present** while Utah officers handled other
  family interviews — see [FBI–Local Coordination](/Legal/FBI-Local-Coordination).
- **July 8–9, 2026** — Summarized ballistics testimony: **one spent cartridge case and three
  additional cartridges**, all four with engravings; firearm, ammo, and towel sent to the **FBI**
  and later the **ATF lab** — see [Assassination Investigation](/Legal/Assassination-Investigation).
- Day 3 notes that defense argued FBI interviewer **McBride used leading questions** in a prerecorded
  limited-immunity Twiggs interview — a defense argument in court, recorded on the
  [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026), not a finding of misconduct.
- Cited in [audio-acoustic debate context](/Timeline/audio-acoustic-mic-timeline) alongside
  @jonaaronbray in the dual-event mic-theory refinement discussion.

## Investigative value

TriggerSmart's notes are the connective tissue between the courtroom and the citizen-investigator
ecosystem: custody-clock arguments, agency-jurisdiction questions, and ballistics language disputes
all trace back to these summaries. The site's standing caution applies — compare every note against
the [court transcripts](/court/overview) when published.
