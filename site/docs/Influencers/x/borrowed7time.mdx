# Johnny Gemini (Borrowed7Time) — X Posts

[@Borrowed7Time](https://x.com/Borrowed7Time) (**Johnny Gemini**) is an aviation-OSINT tracker
whose logs are among the few **contemporaneous primary sources** in the Egyptian-aircraft
thread. Unlike later compilations, the account's posts predate any Kirk-assassination narrative
— which is exactly why the site leans on them to check the compilations.

## Claims from X posts

- Logged on **July 20, 2025** — the same calendar day — that Egyptian Falcon **SU-BTT (#0101D3)**
  was en route **Paris → Omaha Eppley (KOMA)** after a July 18 Cairo → Paris staging leg. The
  post names a **civil airfield**, not a military ramp, and makes no Kirk-case connection — the
  primary anchor on [Nebraska Army Base July 20](/Timeline/nebraska-army-base-july-20) and
  [July 20 Military Meeting](/Timeline/military-meeting-july-20), where it directly conflicts
  with later "U.S. Army base" compilations.
- Reported continuing SU-BTT **Wilmington–Cairo rotations into March 2026**, read by trackers as
  evidence the September 2025 legs were part of a standing pattern — logged on
  [SU-BTT plane movements](/Timeline/su-btt-plane-movements-timeline), where the two camps
  dispute whether a standing pattern makes September more or less significant.

## Investigative value

This account illustrates the difference between **primary tracker posts and downstream
narrative**: its same-day KOMA log is what forces the site to label the "Army base" and
"first-ever U.S. trip" framings as compilation-dependent. Readers evaluating the
[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) thread should start from these contemporaneous logs before weighing
the meeting and foreknowledge claims layered on later.
