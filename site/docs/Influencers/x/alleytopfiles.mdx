# alleytopfiles — X Posts

[@alleytopfiles](https://x.com/alleytopfiles) is an archive-style investigator account — its specialty is preserving **screenshots of posts that later disappear**, from a deleted Tyler Bowyer security post to a deleted Shaffer Security Group statement. Everything below is **attributed reposts and screenshots**; authenticity and interpretation remain the poster's, and no living person is accused of wrongdoing here.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 26, 2026** — Reposted @iluminomoly's set of photos reportedly released by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) showing the interior of the **SUV that transported Charlie Kirk**: rear-seat stain, pale fragments, debris, a small PCB fragment, and shattered tempered glass some tied to a RØDE mic housing. Unauthenticated; no chain of custody; nothing lab-tested. ([Post](https://x.com/alleytopfiles/status/2070606777691816338)) See [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie) and [SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed).
- **July 11, 2026** — Circulated a screenshot of a **Tyler Bowyer** post dated Sept 18, 2025: "We run everything like a military op, all of our people are from that background" — asking whether Bowyer later deleted it. The quote is Bowyer's reported description of TPUSA security and **not, by itself, evidence of wrongdoing**. ([Post](https://x.com/alleytopfiles/status/2076075338409398330)) See [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer).
- **July 18, 2026** — Published a screenshot of a since-deleted **@ShafferGroup** post (9/26/25) stating SSG "was not &amp; had not been affiliated or contracted to do security for TPUSA since 2022," pointing instead to [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security); also shared the Headline USA excerpt of Greg Shaffer's phone statements. ([Post](https://x.com/alleytopfiles/status/2078362407508812262)) See [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group).
- Posed the open question of whether **[Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis)** is currently with SSG or with Integrity — the vendor-accountability question at the center of the security-failure thread.

## Investigative value

The account's preservation work drives three separate site threads: the SUV evidence dispute, the TPUSA security-vendor transition (SSG vs Integrity), and the "military backgrounds" framing cited in the [stand-down whistleblower claim](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/stand-down-whistleblower-claim). Screenshots of deleted posts cannot be independently verified — the site records them with that caveat.
