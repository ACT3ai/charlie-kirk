# universehop00 — Posts

**@universehop00** is the anonymous account (originating on TikTok, with frames circulated across X)
that posted the alleged **leaked aerial imagery** attributed to the **N59906** survey flight over the
UVU courtyard on September 10, 2025. The images are watermarked **@universehop00** and are
**unverified** — their authenticity, source, and metadata have not been independently confirmed.

## Claims from X posts

- **October 13, 2025** — Posted images claimed to originate from the **[N59906](/Planes/N59906)**
  flight, reportedly one day after the account's first-ever post; the account had no other content,
  which some commenters read as a targeted leak from a contractor or insider.
- The images are described as **high-resolution orthomosaic views** of the UVU quad and stage area,
  with metadata said to align with the **11:35 a.m.** pass; one post alleged a **figure near a rooftop
  access point** about 45 minutes before the shooting — an unproven claim.
- The watermarked frames preserved on this site are among the only genuinely **top-down views** of the
  event: roof surfaces, parapets, canopy-to-terrace geometry, and crowd density — see
  [Aerial of the Courtyard](/Photos/Maps/Img_Photo_cf0c99) and the
  [Aircraft photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_a4ff5f).

## Investigative value

Whatever their origin, the frames are the best publicly circulated basis for **site-geometry
arguments** — what a person on a roof could see or be seen from, how the canopy sits relative to the
terraces, and how quickly the courtyard could clear. The site's standing rule: use the imagery for
geometry, not as a documented product of the N59906 flight, which remains unverified.
