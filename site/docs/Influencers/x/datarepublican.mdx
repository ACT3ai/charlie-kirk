# DataRepublican — X Posts

[@DataRepublican](https://x.com/DataRepublican) is documented on this site as a leading **rebuttal
voice** against the Owens-franchise theories — a data-research account that defends the state's
charged narrative and condemns attacks on **Erika Kirk**. The site preserves this side of the
argument alongside the skeptic threads; all items are public commentary, not findings.

  character attacks on Erika Kirk**, and cited **DNA and medical-examiner testimony under oath** at
  the preliminary hearing as supporting the state's charged narrative. Logged on the
  [Erika Kirk timeline](/Timeline/erika-kirk-timeline), which stresses these are commentary, not

  [Tyler Robinson Not Assassin overview](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/overview), where
