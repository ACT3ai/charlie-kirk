# KUTV2News — X Posts

[@KUTV2News](https://x.com/KUTV2News) (**KUTV 2News**, the CBS affiliate in Salt Lake City) is a
**local mainstream news source** cited on this site for public-record aftermath reporting rather
than investigative theories. Its posts anchor several dates in the memorial and political
timeline.

## Claims from X posts

- **October 14, 2025** — Reported President Trump posthumously awarding Charlie Kirk the
  **Presidential Medal of Freedom** on what would have been his **32nd birthday**, with
  [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) accepting on his behalf.
  ([Source](https://x.com/KUTV2News/status/1978261512562090336)) — cited on
  [Presidential Medal of Freedom](/Media/Presidential-Medal-of-Freedom) and
  [Trump Support After Death](/TPUSA/Trump-Support-After-Death).
- **National Day of Remembrance / memorial reporting** — cited for the memorial and TPUSA
  succession coverage window ([Memorial and TPUSA Succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession)).
- Its Medal of Freedom post is reused as a **public-record item** in the political-aftermath
  analysis, distinguished on those pages from theories
  ([Political Aftermath](/Topic-Analyses/Political_Aftermath), [Public Response](/Other/Public_Response)).

## Investigative value

KUTV is one of the few **verifiable local-broadcast anchors** in the compiled record — dates and
ceremonies that both mainstream and citizen investigators accept. The site uses it to separate
established public events from the contested claims that surround them.
