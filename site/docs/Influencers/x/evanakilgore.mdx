# Evan Kilgore — X Posts

[@EvanAKilgore](https://x.com/EvanAKilgore) (**Evan Kilgore**) is a high-engagement commentator
whose posts anchor several 2026 clusters in the investigation record — most notably the
March 31, 2026 pairing of a sheriff's resignation with ballistics doubts. All items below are
attributed claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **March 31, 2026** — Wrote that Sheriff **Nate Brooksby**, "who helped Tyler Robinson to turn
  himself in, just resigned… after 30 years," adding that "the ATF was also allegedly unable to
  conclusively match the bullet" and calling it sarcastically "surely a coincidence" — the
  temporal-clustering argument tracked on [official resignations](/Law_Enforcement/official-resignations)
  and [rifle chain of custody](/Law_Enforcement/rifle-chain-of-custody). Temporal clustering is
  **not proof** of causation; Brooksby's resignation is a public personnel fact, and motives are
  not established — see [Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff).
- **July 10, 2026** — Reported that after a **leaked exhibit** (described as Robinson's note to
  Lance Twiggs), Judge **Graf** ruled remaining exhibits would **not** be publicly viewable and
  might **privatize oral arguments** from September 1 — logged on the [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026).
- **December 12, 2025** — Amplified (~13k likes) [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)' claim that **Andrew Kolvet** told
  her off the record about a night-before "They are going to kill me" text — part of the
  public-entry chain on [the night-before text page](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text).

## Investigative value

Kilgore's posts are entry points for two of the site's most-tracked disputes: the
**inconclusive ATF fragment** language versus the DNA/casing narrative, and the
**exhibit-secrecy turn** after the July 2026 preliminary hearing. Both are contested-forensics
and process stories, not verdicts; [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted.
