# Recklesstruth00 — X Posts

[@Recklesstruth00](https://x.com/Recklesstruth00) posts citizen re-tellings of the July 2026
[preliminary hearing](/court/Preliminary_Hearing/overview) and commentary skeptical of the
lone-actor case against [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), who remains charged, not
convicted, and is presumed innocent.

## Claims from X posts

- Re-tellings of investigator testimony placing a figure identified by the State as the charged
  man **walking on the Losee roof around ~12:15 PM MDT** before later prone positioning — logged
  with cautions on the [12:23 Shooting Time](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) page as **not a court
  finding of guilt**.
- The claim that the roommate communication channel is **compromised because of an alleged
  informant** — recorded on the
  [Tyler Robinson Not Assassin overview](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/overview) as an
  **attributed social claim, not a site finding**, in the same mid-July 2026 discourse in which
  [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) said she felt "confident" Robinson did not kill Kirk.

## Investigative value

The account is a minor but cited voice in the **hearing-retelling and lone-actor-skeptic lane**.
Its roof-timeline detail feeds the shot-clock reconstruction; its informant claim is unverified
commentary the site attributes without adopting.
