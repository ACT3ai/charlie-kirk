# SeanMcCarthyCom — X Posts

[@SeanMcCarthyCom](https://x.com/SeanMcCarthyCom) is cited on the site's distraction-people and FBI
timeline pages for early **taxonomy posts** — attempts in September 2025 to catalogue the on-scene
detentions as coordinated misdirection. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts**;
such lists are citizen taxonomies, not charging documents.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 2025** — listed **four on-site "distraction" actors**: [George
  Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn), [Zachariah
  Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi), a figure labeled **"David"**, and an unnamed
  **bicyclist with an airsoft/pellet rifle** arrested by local PD
  ([Pellet Gun Man](/distraction_people/Pellet_Gun_Man)).
- The pellet-gun figure has never been publicly named or adjudicated; the site treats that item as
  an unidentified process event — a detention involving a lookalike weapon near a mass-casualty
  scene — rather than a named assassin.
- Described Salt Lake City FBI head **Mehtab Syed** as **"fired"** rather than reassigned in the
  weeks before the assassination — the site records the leadership change without adjudicating the
  label. See the [FBI involvement timeline](/Timeline/fbi-involvement-timeline).

## Investigative value

The four-actor roster became a template other accounts reused, so it is a useful index of which
detentions entered the "multi-decoy" narrative and when. None of the listed individuals has been
found by any court to have aided the shooter; presence in discourse is not guilt.
