# Stew Peters Network — X Posts

[@StewPetersNet](https://x.com/StewPetersNet) (**Stew Peters Network**) is the network account tied to broadcaster Stew Peters, whose presentations (with Ana Escobar / @AnaEscobarShow materials) are cited on this site for the **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) image-search chronology** claims. Everything below is an **attributed claim from their broadcasts and X posts**, not a confirmed finding.

## Claims from X posts

- A **mid-July through September 9, 2025** dated list of geo-associated **Google Image searches from the [Fort Huachuca](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) area** for security, TPUSA-communications, and intelligence-community figures — including **Johnnie Moore, [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), Adm. Brad Cooper, LTG James R. Adams ([DIA](/GoogleSearches/dia-search-patterns))**, and **[NSWC Crane](/GoogleSearches/crane-search-patterns)** personnel.
- The search sequence allegedly **ramped from mid-July into a Sept 6–9 cluster** — a pattern the network presents as possible pre-meeting coordination ([Fort Huachuca image searches](/GoogleSearches/fort-huachuca-image-searches)).
- A broader **July–September 2025 "search ladder"** of geo-associated image searches for security and intelligence-community names, treated on this site as **unverified search-trend claims** ([Timeline: years](/Timeline/years)).

Geo image-search data **cannot identify who searched** and the dataset has not been independently verified on this site. Living named persons are not accused of any crime; local residents near the base have publicly dismissed grand-plot narratives.

## Investigative value

The @StewPetersNet chronology is the main public source for the **Fort Huachuca search-pattern lane** feeding [the Fort Huachuca military timeline](/Timeline/fort-huachuca-military-timeline) and [2025 timeline](/Timeline/2025). It complements — and should be checked against — the separate @realstewpeters broadcast claims collected at [Stew Peters (podcasts)](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters).
