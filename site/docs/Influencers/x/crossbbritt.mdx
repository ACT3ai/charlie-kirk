# CrossbBritt — X Posts

[@CrossbBritt](https://x.com/CrossbBritt) is cited on this site as a **July 2026 preliminary-hearing
watcher** whose summaries of sworn testimony supply the main **rebuttal** to the
custody-before-Discord confession timeline. The site presents both sides; these are observer
summaries of testimony, not transcripts.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026** — Stated that Agents **[Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis)** and
  **[David Hull](/People/david-hull)** testified under oath that [Tyler
  Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) turned himself in just before **9:00 p.m. (2100)** on Sept 11 at
  the [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff).
- Per the same summaries: Robinson was **not Mirandized at that moment** by WCSO (lack of
  jurisdiction to interrogate); he was held for SBI / Utah County investigators; and **interior
  hold / interview-room video exists**.
- The implication drawn: the Discord messages were **not post-custody** — directly opposing the
  **6:25 PM custody-first** school built from the defense Bates filing. Both readings are held open
  on [Mirandizing](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Mirandizing) and the
  [Sept 8–13 timeline](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13).

began versus when the Discord "confession" was typed**. Readers should compare against the
faked-confession pages and the missing-surrender-footage thread before drawing conclusions.
