# MZakkiel — X Posts

[@MZakkiel](https://x.com/MZakkiel) is one of the amplifier accounts behind the **pre-event Google
search** thread — the claim, attributed primarily to
[Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)'s search-data analysis, that Utah medical figures were
searched from unusual IP ranges **before** September 10, 2025. Everything here is an attributed,
unverified research claim.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified the analysis that pre-shooting Google searches for the **hospital, its
  doctors/surgeons, and the medical examiner** reportedly originated from **both Israeli and
  Washington, DC IP ranges** — the DC angle being part of the claim, not just the Israeli one. See
  [Israel Google Searches — Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people).
- Listed (with @malteselove67, @TheCKFiles1, and @johnmcvicar16) among the accounts circulating the
  **DC-origin search** claims that frame the
  [Doctor DC Phone Call](/Medical/doctor_dc_phone_call) page — where the site labels the resulting
  "DC doctor" speculation as **pure speculation** layered on unverified claims.
- The amplified thread places the medical searches inside a larger reported search set that
  allegedly included [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), judge
  [Tony F. Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), and event questioners —
  covered in the site's broader Israel and Google-searches sections.

## Investigative value

An amplifier rather than an originator: the account's role is spreading the Coleman-attributed
search-pattern analysis to wider audiences. The underlying data has not been independently produced
or verified, which is why every destination page carries that caveat.
