# richtidwell — X Posts

[@richtidwell](https://x.com/richtidwell) is one of the highest-engagement amplifiers of the
**exploding-microphone / AES supply-chain** theory. A widely shared **March 20, 2026** post
compressed the whole stack into one sequence, and the site's mic pages track it as attributed
citizen commentary — not as a finding that any device was used at UVU.

## Claims from X posts

- The fatal mechanism was **"the mic under [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s shirt"** — the under-shirt RØDE
  transmitter, not a rooftop rifle. See [RØDE mic](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic).
- **[Accurate Energetic Systems (AES)](/Mic/AES)** is named in the theory as the device manufacturer,
  linked to a **DoD procurement of roughly $425k in May 2025** for miniaturized charges — figures as
  posted; primary award records are on USASpending.gov. See [DoD Contract](/Mic/DoD_Contract).
- The **October 2025 AES plant explosion** in McEwen, Tennessee (posts claim roughly **16 lives lost**,
  ~3 weeks to a month after the assassination) is framed as suspicious timing — a motive question, per
  the posts, **not a completed proof**. See [AES factory explosion](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory).
- The post ties the chain to a motive question about Kirk's public posture on **Israel and Iran war
  policy** — attributed framing only.
- The full claimed sequence: contract → mic under shirt → assassination → plant disaster. Skeptics
  reply that explosives factories explode without conspiracy; the site records both sides on the
  [AES Tennessee Explosion](/CoverUp/AES_Tennessee_Explosion) page.

## Investigative value

The account functions as a **compression node**: it took the longer @jonaaronbray / Stew Peters
analyses and turned them into a single viral timeline that the site's [Mic](/Mic/overview) section
now maps claim-by-claim. Everything here is attributed X commentary; the site makes no claim that
AES or any living person committed a crime.
