# Real Truth Real Justice (RealTruthJustic) — X Posts

[@RealTruthJustic](https://x.com/RealTruthJustic) (**LindsMac — Real Truth Real Justice**) is
cited for reposts and speculation focused on the SUV that transported
[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) to the hospital and on person-of-interest
[Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler). A caution applies throughout: the original captions on some
reposted material made serious accusations that the investigation file's compiler flagged as
**unverified and refused to publish** — this site does not repeat them.

## Claims from X posts

- On [June 26, 2026](https://x.com/RealTruthJustic/status/2070597252133818558), quote-reposted
  (with the comment *"Could this be it"*) an **annotated SUV-interior image** originally shared
  by @DanCampbel44604 labeling seating positions with names of people the post identified as
  being inside the vehicle — see [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie). The
  names are unverified, and presence in a transport vehicle indicates nothing by itself.
- Suggested a **Pero gesture-control wearable** (a smart-mouse band) might resemble an object
  near Cutler's hand in footage — recorded as **unverified** on
  [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) and in the
  [Cutler photo cluster](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Rick_Cutler/overview); no official finding
  ties any device to the shooting ([Cutler Hand Device](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/cutler-hand-device)).
- A June 2026 post comparing Cutler's appearances across images, logged on the
  [Rick Cutler distraction-people record](/distraction_people/Rick_Cutler).

## Investigative value

The account's reposts put the **SUV-occupant and hand-device questions** into circulation, but
the site's treatment of them is a case study in editorial restraint: the compiler cropped the
accusatory caption text before publishing anything, and the extreme accusations against named
living people are neither repeated nor endorsed anywhere on this site.
