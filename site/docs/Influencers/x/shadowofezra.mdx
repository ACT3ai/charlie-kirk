# Shadow of Ezra — X Posts

[@ShadowofEzra](https://x.com/ShadowofEzra) amplified **first-person paving testimony** and
**media-framing** clips relevant to the Kirk case. Claims below are attributed to their posts and
videos — not established facts.

## Claims from X posts

- Published **Dan Merrell on-camera account** of receiving an emergency call to pave the UVU courtyard;
  Merrell quotes an on-site coordinator: **"The governor and the FBI said they want this done by
  Monday"** and instructed the crew not to speak to media.
  ([Source](https://x.com/ShadowofEzra/status/2057560145379708956) — video transcribed in `Charlie_Kirk.txt`)
- Amplified clip of **[Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro)** telling Megyn Kelly that [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) is doing something
  **"evil"** by questioning the FBI version of the assassination.
  ([Source](https://x.com/ShadowofEzra/status/1986914626991198713))

## Investigative value

Shadow of Ezra's Merrell video gave a **citable primary-source voice** to the paving timeline that
[UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) and [Hardscape Specialties](/UVU/Hardscape-Specialties) pages build on.
The Shapiro clip documents **intra-conservative media conflict** over whether citizen investigation
is legitimate.

## Roommate ID & Security Coordination Clips (Attributed)

July 2026 posts include: Lance Twiggs interview clip saying campus images of Robinson are not ID'd at 100% certainty ("looks like him" via shoes/sunglasses); Jeremy Boreing meltdown clip over Shapiro security coordinating with Kirk security after the death (Boreing: safety coordination, not "IDF firm"); Trump Jr. clip declaring the case closed / Robinson guilty / no room for conspiracy theories; Frank Turek livestream reversing prior drone claims toward iPhone-from-above explanation.

These clips are **media primary sources for what was said**, not proof of the underlying theories.
