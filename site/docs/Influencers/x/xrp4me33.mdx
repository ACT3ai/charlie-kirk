# TruthIsFreedom — X Posts

[@XRP4Me33](https://x.com/XRP4Me33) (**TruthIsFreedom**) posts **crowd-photo analyses** — annotated
stills singling out individuals at the UVU event whom the poster argues were misidentified, planted,
or overlooked. Every identification below is the **poster's claim**, not a verified match; none of
the people highlighted has been charged with anything connected to the shooting.

## Claims from X posts

- Presented four stills of a young man in a **maroon shirt** at the event, carrying a skateboard and
  wearing wired earbuds, noting a wedding band and arguing he was filming the scene — tied by the
  poster to the claim that the accused "did a dry run." The maroon shirt matters because the charging
  affidavit says [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) arrived that morning in a plain maroon
  T-shirt. Preserved at [Not Tyler photo cluster](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Not_Tyle/overview).
- Posted a crowd analysis highlighting an individual in a green cap behind a speaker, circled with a
  red pointer line — filed in [Patsy Candidates — Photo 51](/Photos/Patsy/Img_Photo_2b0784).
- Posted a cropped photo of a man in a BYU cap with a superimposed red arrow pointing behind his
  shoulder — filed at [Patsy Candidates — Photo 92](/Photos/Patsy/Img_Photo_592f9f).

## Investigative value

Look-alike and crowd-position claims bear directly on the identification chain the state's case
depends on — morning arrival, stairwell figure, and rooftop runner must all be the same person. The
site preserves these annotated stills as claims to be tested against the official
[suspect description](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Img_Description_Law_Enforcement_a66b04), not as
identifications.
