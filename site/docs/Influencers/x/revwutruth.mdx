# REVWUTRUTH — X Posts

[@REVWUTRUTH](https://x.com/REVWUTRUTH) is cited on the site's Tyler Robinson message-evidence
pages for July 2026 posts circulating images of the texts at the center of the State's case.
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) remains charged, not convicted, and is presumed
innocent; the authenticity and framing of circulated message images are contested questions the
site tracks rather than resolves.

## Claims from X posts

- On [July 9, 2026](https://x.com/REVWUTRUTH/status/2075285066947719633), posted multi-image
  threads presenting **texts framed as Robinson confessing the murder to Lance Twiggs** —
  documented on [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), which examines what the
  circulated images do and do not establish.
- Cited (with @ginamilan_ and @dailymindvirus) in coverage of the roommate/girlfriend message
  threads and **rifle-drop discussion** — see the
  [Girlfriend overview](/Tyler_Robinson/Girlfriend/overview).

## Investigative value

The account's image threads are among the site's working links to the **message evidence as it
circulated publicly** — useful for comparing the public versions against what was presented in
court, where the [Twiggs phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone) testimony raised its own questions.
