# JohnMcCloy — X Posts

[@JohnMcCloy](https://x.com/JohnMcCloy) is a citizen-investigator account whose July 2026 posts on
the **rifle recovery narrative** and **Tyler Robinson timeline tensions** are cited across the
site's weapon and suspect pages. All items are attributed open questions from X posts, not proof
of a multi-person plot.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 9, 2026** — Recirculated photos described as the **Mauser** recovered wrapped in a towel
  in a cardboard box in woods across from UVU — assembled, with a spent **.30-06** and etched
  bullets — tagging @JackPosobiec
  ([post](https://x.com/JohnMcCloy/status/2075310616789573862),
  [Weapon / Rifle](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle)).
- **July 10, 2026** — Highlighted a timeline tension: Lance Twiggs reportedly said
  [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) talked about a hunting/camping trip for **months**, while
  the official UVU appearance announcement came only about **14 days** before Sept 10, and a
  neighbor account described out-of-state plates ~two weeks prior — attributed open questions.
- His rifle-photo recirculation is logged as one of the competing shot models on the
  [Shooting overview](/Tyler_Robinson/Shooting/overview) — the lone rooftop Mauser path versus
  acoustic multi-event and mic-based models.
- Was a named recipient (with @RealCandaceO) of an attendee's October 2025 claim to hold
  **11 minutes of 8K footage** from inside the crowd that "no one's gotten back to me" about
  ([the patsy photo cluster](/Photos/Patsy/Img_HCcE_31bbee)).

## Investigative value

The account keeps the **physical-evidence record** (rifle condition, packaging, ammunition) and the
**preparation-timeline paradox** in circulation — both central to the
[Tyler Robinson Investigation Index](/Tyler_Robinson/Investigation_Index) debates over whether the
charged narrative fits a solo shooter.
