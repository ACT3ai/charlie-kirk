# WeAreNotGoingToMars — X Posts

**@WeAreNotGoingToMars** is known in the investigation record for a single influential artifact: a
researcher-made **colour-keyed site map** of the UVU venue compressing the entire multi-actor theory
into one graphic. It is one poster's published hypothesis — unverified, unsourced on the graphic
itself, and supported by no agency filing or court record. No named individual is attached to any
position, and nothing about it should be read as an accusation against any person.

## Claims from X posts

- Published a stylized overhead site diagram of the venue with lettered building badges and coloured
  marker dots, and a legend reading **"3 Patsies, 2 Shooters, 2 Distractions"** — signed
  @WeAreNotGoingToMars. Preserved at
  [Colour-Keyed Map of Patsies, Shooters and Distractions](/Photos/Maps/Img_Photo_7df589_x) and in
  the [Patsy Candidates cluster](/Photos/Patsy/Img_Photo_7df589).
- The graphic asserts multiple shooters, multiple people positioned to take blame, and staged
  distractions — committing to **specific ground positions** for each role.

## Investigative value

The map is the clearest single statement of the **multi-actor theory** a large part of the
independent research community argues — and it is **falsifiable**: each marked position can be
checked against footage. The site's own sourced treatment of these threads lives at
[Patsies and Distraction Actors](/Killer/Patsies_Distraction_Actors) and
[Distraction People](/distraction_people/overview); the graphic is the poster's conclusion, not
this site's.
