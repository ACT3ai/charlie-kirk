# Furkan Gözükara — X Posts

focused on AI tutorials and tech that periodically amplifies Charlie Kirk case commentary. He is
one of the most-cited **amplifiers** in the site record — a distributor of third-party clips, not
a subject of the investigation. He has a profile at [Furkan Gozukara](/People/furkan-gozukara).
**Status: Alive.** The claims within amplified clips belong to their original speakers and are unverified.

## Investigative value

Gözükara's account is a case study in **cross-audience amplification**: a large tech audience
receiving assassination commentary packaged as "BOMBSHELL" framing. Every substantive claim
above originates with other speakers (Peters, Carlson, Owens-adjacent analysts, Kent) and
remains unverified; amplification does not make any of it a finding.
