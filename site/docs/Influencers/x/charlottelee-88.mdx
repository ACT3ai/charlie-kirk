# CharlotteLee_88 — X Posts

[@CharlotteLee_88](https://x.com/CharlotteLee_88) compiled the on-camera **witness-interview
reversal** that anchors the site's wound-narrative discrepancy pages: an early witness who described
a **chest** shot repeatedly, then gave a **neck** account on a different station within about an
hour. The compilation video is IPFS-pinned on this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 23, 2026** ([post](https://x.com/CharlotteLee_88/status/2069566053353463951)) — Posted a
  compilation of on-camera witness interviews near UVU claiming *"one of the earliest witnesses
  first described Charlie Kirk as being shot in the chest, AND SHE DOUBLES DOWN 3 TIMES, then
  appeared on another station less than an hour later with a different account."* Full treatment:
  [Shot in Chest Reversal](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal).
- Directed viewers to a **fourth woman** in the same witness lineup whose account, per the poster,
  "directly contradicts" the first witness — a contemporaneous **neck** account.
- The compilation is the featured video on the [Witnesses overview](/Witnesses/overview) and is
  credited by **@BasedSamParker's** June 2026 synthesis arguing the chest-to-neck flip reflects a
  narrative version conflict — Parker explicitly disclaims formal accusations.
- Cited on [witness testimony discrepancies](/Timeline/witness-testimony-discrepancies) alongside
  discussion on @RealCandaceO; the site notes trauma, camera angles, and medical language can
  produce conflicting impressions, and the neck account is the settled official description.

## Investigative value

The primary compilation source for the **first-hour wound-location cluster** — the site records the
accounts as-given and treats the apparent contradiction as an open documentation question, not
proof of anything. The witnesses are private individuals and are not accused of wrongdoing.
