# michael_vina27 — X Posts

[@michael_vina27](https://x.com/michael_vina27) is credited as the photographer behind one of the
most-debated images in the case: the clearer photograph of the **burnt object** in the back of
Charlie Kirk's transport SUV, reportedly taken while the vehicle sat at an **auto auction**. The
photo itself — not any theory — is this account's contribution; the interpretations built on it are
attributed to other posters and are speculative.

## Claims from X posts

- Took the **auto-auction photograph** of the charred mass in the SUV that
  [@ProjectConstitu](./project-constitution) used (July 16, 2026) to argue the object contains a
  **melted female audio jack** matching the RØDE Wireless transmitter Kirk wore, plus a fragment
  said to match **battery-pack warning symbols**.
  ([ProjectConstitu post](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/2077847223082103278))
- The photo is known to the investigation only as a **phone screenshot of a video frame**
  (`IMG_6641.jpg`) — visibly re-compressed, with a caption bar across it. The site's evidence pages
  repeatedly list obtaining the **original, uncompressed photo at full resolution** as the first
  step to settle the transmitter claim. See
  [Burnt Object — RØDE Transmitter](/Mic/burnt-object-rode-transmitter).
- The same imagery underpins the SUV seating and equipment questions on
  [Rick Cutler Hand Device](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/rick-cutler-hand-device) and the cause-of-death
  alternative examined at
  [Burnt Object as Transmitter](/Suspicious/Cause_of_Death/burnt-object-transmitter) — rated
  **THIN/SPECULATIVE** by the site pending chain of custody and lab examination.

## Investigative value

This account matters as a **primary-image source** rather than a theorist. Every downstream claim —
audio jack, battery symbol, shaped-charge speculation — depends on the resolution and provenance of
this one photograph, which is why the site's "what would settle it" lists start with the original
file. No living person is accused of any crime on this page.
