# Diligent Denizen (2) — X Posts

@DiligentDenizen is among the citizen investigators posting flight data, ballistics questions, drone sightings, and agency-involvement theories about the UVU shooting. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings. Each line links to the cited post where available.

## Claims from X posts

- No hard evidence that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s alleged assassin was political, neither left nor right; engravings on bullet casings reference an online 3rd-person shooter video game.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1966900477339333105))
- Utah dispatcher states there was no autopsy done on Charlie Kirk; death certificate signed by hospital doctor.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1971463312799039549))
- [Russell Kennington](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington), 38, was reported arrested and charged with criminal trespass and felony obstruction of justice in connection with entering Charlie Kirk's UVU crime scene hours after the assassination. Charges are allegations; he is presumed innocent.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1970945787371868256))
- Questions raised about items moving between UVU Police and Charlie's security moments after Charlie Kirk was shot.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1970901838582149313))
- Man who removed SD cards from Charlie Kirk's homicide crime scene also recorded Kirk being carried out to SUV; was waiting seconds after shooting.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1970219759728730579))
- The account alleges that more than 9 minutes after Charlie Kirk was shot, staff interfered with the closest camera facing him, noting the perimeter was 50 yards back and disputing that civilians were taking items. No evidence-handling record supports the tampering characterization, no staff member is named, and nothing here is a finding that anyone destroyed or altered evidence.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1971285016094310705))
- Utah dispatch audio reveals FBI directed a person of interest not to be interviewed moments after Charlie Kirk was shot.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1971650033964745068))
- Charlie Kirk's shooter wore one set of clothes, changed on roof without being seen, changed at home, then changed again before surrender.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1967730716357300632))
- WAPO reports [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) confessed in Discord chat before surrendering.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1967677540404957421))
- Man tampering with camera directly behind Charlie Kirk after shooting, possibly removing SD card.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1968724854263206046))
- Shooter spotted on camera but escaped off roof; [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) falsely claimed capture then retracted; FBI ignored X posts about Kirk fearing Israel.  ([Source](https://x.com/DiligentDenizen/status/1965973799012081700))
