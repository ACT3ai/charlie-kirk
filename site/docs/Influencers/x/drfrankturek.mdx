# Frank Turek — X Posts

[@DrFrankTurek](https://x.com/DrFrankTurek) is the X account of **Dr. Frank Turek**, the
apologist and event speaker who was on stage with [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) on
September 10, 2025. Unlike most accounts in this section, Turek is cited less for his own
claims than because **his phone footage** became a primary artifact for citizen analysts.
He has a full profile at [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek). **Status: Alive** — nothing
here alleges any wrongdoing by him.

## Claims tied to the account and its footage

- **Infrared laser reflection** — Analysts including [@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu)
  and @stevendenoon claim an **IR laser reflection** is briefly visible on Turek's phone screen at
  the moment of the shot, and argue the beam traces back to a **grey van** parked behind and to
  the right of the stage — the [Grey Van Shooter](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter) person-of-interest theory.
- **Trajectory reconstruction (claims)** — Cross-referencing the laser angle, a frame said to show
  the **bullet in flight**, and a reported casing location, these analysts argue the line of fire
  points to the van's cargo area rather than the rooftop — covered on
  [Close Range Theories](/Killer/Close_Range_Theories) and [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations).
- **Drone footage review** — Turek reportedly told commentators he and Charlie **reviewed drone
  footage en route** to UVU and that he warned there were **"too many buildings"** for sniper
  exposure — see [the TPUSA production drone](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone).

## Investigative value

The Turek phone video is one of the few high-value close-range recordings from the stage area,
which is why third-party frame-by-frame analyses of it anchor the grey-van lane in the
[Suspects Investigation Index](/Suspects/Suspects_Investigation_Index). All laser and trajectory
readings are contested interpretations of compressed footage; skeptics cite reflections and
compression artifacts. This site does not assert any occupant of the van committed a crime.
