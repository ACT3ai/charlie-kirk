# TheDibsterX — X Posts

[@TheDibsterX](https://x.com/TheDibsterX) is a citizen commentary account cited on this site for **evidence-disposal allegations** around the SUV that transported Charlie Kirk, and for aftermath reporting on the Robinson family. Everything below is an **attributed claim from their X posts**, not a court finding.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 30, 2026** — Alleged (with [@TPV_John](./tpv-john)) that the transport SUV's **carpet** — claimed to hold mic particles or explosive residue — was **removed**, and the vehicle **sold or shipped to a "random car lot in California,"** analogized by posters to rapid 9/11 steel removal ([Signs of Cover-Up](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/signs-of-cover-up)). Vehicle chain of custody remains an open FOIA target; no court has found evidence destruction.
- **October 2025** — Reported the **Robinson family** was in hiding with armed security after threats ([Public Response](/Other/Public_Response)).  ([Source](https://x.com/TheDibsterX/status/1978143754537058689))

## Investigative value

The SUV-disposal thread is part of the **aftermath pattern** the [Signs of Cover-Up](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/signs-of-cover-up) page inventories — scene paving, soil excavation claims, and vehicle handling. These are process allegations that disclosure of chain-of-custody records would resolve; this site records them as circulating claims, not findings.
