# alexboge — X Posts

[@alexboge](https://x.com/alexboge) is the site's most-cited **physics skeptic** of the exploding-microphone theory — the account whose July 2026 threads formalized the rifle-camp counterargument across the Mic and Medical sections. The bullets below are **attributed technical commentary**, preserved because the assessment standard requires both sides on contested mechanism pages.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 9, 2026** — Detailed debunk: a shaped charge needs **standoff distance** and **rigid fixation**; a ~**32 g** RØDE Wireless Pro clipped to fabric would rocket away under Newton's third law rather than drive a stable jet. PETN-class events should leave **flash, smoke, burns, and torn fabric** — absent from multi-angle 4K footage. See [Explosive Mic Killed Charlie — the skeptic counterargument](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/explosive-mic-killed-charlie).
- Steelmanned and rejected the proponent "**primer charge**" fix (a second explosion lifting the mic to create standoff) as an "epicycle" — an invisible event invented to rescue the theory.
- Argued that if a contact blast puffed cotton **without tearing cotton**, it lacked the energy to destroy a neck; pager-style kills (Lebanon) leave burns and fragmentation signatures absent from the UVU shirt. See [Demonstration of a Shaped Charge](/Mic/Demo_of_shape_charge).
- Noted that preliminary-hearing material entered around **July 6–11, 2026** includes autopsy findings of **cause of death: gunshot wound to the neck, with bullet fragments recovered** — so any mic-only theory must also explain projectile evidence. See [trial and autopsy report](/Medical/trial_and_autopsy_report).
- **July 12, 2026** — On a circulating TDoA audio report: its own supersonic-ordering argument "correctly kills the exploding microphone theory," even while rejecting the report's courtyard-shooter conclusion.
- Elaborated Monroe-effect geometry requirements (liner, standoff, rigid fixation) against both the pendant and transmitter-housing variants — see [exploding pendant shaped charge](/Mic/exploding-pendant-shaped-charge) and [mic explosion video analysis](/Mic/mic-explosion-video-analysis).
