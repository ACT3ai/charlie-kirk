# GoldenOne1980 — X Posts

[@GoldenOne1980](https://x.com/GoldenOne1980) is one of the July 2026 proponents of the
**microphone-device hypothesis** — the contested minority theory that a device rather than a
rifle round caused [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s fatal wound. Every item below is an
attributed, unproven mechanism claim; the official prosecution remains a rifle / gunshot case.

## Claims from X posts

- **Lapel mic, not pendant** — Argues the relevant device was the **lapel / body microphone**,
  not the necklace or pendant — the device-location argument tracked on
  [Mic vs Pendant](/Mic/Mic_vs_Pendant) and [lapel mic and audio theories](/Medical/lapel_mic_and_audio_theories).
- **Dual-microphone rarity** — States September 10 was "the **only time Charlie has used two
  microphones**," treating the redundant lav setup as unusual for his outdoor events — the
  access-and-pre-staging question developed on [the RØDE mic page](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic).
- **AES explosion timing (July 7, 2026)** — Points to the **AES factory explosion** roughly three
  weeks after the assassination — the company named in device-theory posts — as suspicious
  timing, restated as the company being "conveniently blown up" with production personnel killed.
  Recorded as **circumstantial timing, not causation**, on [Mic / AES](/Mic/AES) and
  [the AES factory explosion page](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion).

## Investigative value

The account appears in the site's proponent/counter table on the
[Mic Investigation Index](/Mic/Investigation_Index) (AES / DoD timing cluster, opposite the
industrial-accident baseline). Skeptics — including physics rebuttals citing the July 2026
