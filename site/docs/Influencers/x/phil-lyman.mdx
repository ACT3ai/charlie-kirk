# Phil Lyman — X Posts

[@PHIL_LYMAN](https://x.com/PHIL_LYMAN) (**Phil Lyman**) is a former Utah state legislator who
attended the September 10, 2025 UVU event and has posted repeatedly about the case. **Status:
Alive.** He has not been accused by any authority of anything; the site records his posts,
photographs credited to his account, and reported coincidences involving him without asserting
wrongdoing. His full written record is at [Phil Lyman](/distraction_people/Phil_Lyman).

## Contributions cited from his X account

- Wide-angle **crowd photographs credited to @PHIL_LYMAN** are among the day's key records: the
  ["1776 man" crowd photo](/Photos/Mic/1776_Man/Img_Photo_b354c5) and the still used in the
  [Table 1776 Man](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Table_1776_Man/overview) mic-placement claim both
  carry his source credit.
- He was reportedly inside the Losee Center and became the **first person on camera afterward to
  state a direction for the shot** — tracked on
  [Ten Minutes Before the Shot](/Timeline/ten-minutes-before-shot) and in his
  [Photos cluster](/Photos/Patsy/Distraction_Phil_Lyman/overview).
- He reportedly commented on an X post about "Alice in Wonderland" CIA conditioning about two
  weeks before the assassination — noted on the [MK-Ultra Program](/Gov_Mind_Control/mkultra-program)
  page as a recorded coincidence.
- After formally attempting to access the investigation, he has **reported systematic
  obstruction** — cited on [US Intelligence](/Killer/us-intelligence).
- Separately, citizen researchers note a car reportedly belonging to him was parked at 11:30 AM
  on the route the charged man walked at 11:49 AM
  ([vehicle-movements timeline](/Timeline/rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline)) — the site
  logs this as a coincidence and asserts nothing further.

## Investigative value

Lyman is unusual among accounts on this index: a **political figure and eyewitness-adjacent
source** whose photos are primary records and whose public statements are tracked on the
[Utah officials timeline](/Timeline/utah-officials-cox-timeline). Every claim about him is
