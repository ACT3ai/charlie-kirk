# kangminlee — X Posts

[@kangminlee](https://x.com/kangminlee) is an X account whose late-October 2025 posts on the
**intelligence-scope fight** are cited throughout the site's
[U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview) section. These are attributed political claims about
investigative scope — not court findings, and not proof of foreign involvement.

## Claims from X posts

- **Oct 29, 2025** — Posted that **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)**, then tied to NCTC
  leadership, reviewed FBI files for possible **foreign assistance** to the alleged killer, and
  that FBI Director **Kash Patel** shut that review down.
- Tied the scope fight to DNI **[Tulsi Gabbard's](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard)** circle —
  claims amplified in parallel by **@RealAlexJones** that Patel blocked ODNI-adjacent accomplice
  investigation ([NCTC](/US_Intelligence/nctc)).
- The claims are used by citizen investigators to argue **campus-adjacent intel questions were
  never fully tasked** ([CIA-UVU Profiles](/UVU/CIA-UVU-Profiles)) and are montaged with the
  Valhalla press-conference critique
  ([Buckley Carlson / Kash Patel video](/Videos/buckley-carlson-kash-patel-valhalla)).
- The thread has an on-record anchor: Kent himself later said his parallel foreign-nexus review
  was ordered to stop once a suspect was in custody, before leads were exhausted
  ([Cabot alibi context](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi)).

## Investigative value

Among the earliest accounts to package the **Patel / Gabbard / Kent** scope controversy for X
audiences — the frame the [US Intelligence Investigation Index](/US_Intelligence/US_Intelligence_Investigation_Index)
lists as a high-traffic thread. Readers should seek primary ODNI/FBI statements and congressional
records rather than treating the thread as a finding.
