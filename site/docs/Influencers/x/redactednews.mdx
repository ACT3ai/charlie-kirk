# Redacted News — X Posts

[@RedactedNews](https://x.com/RedactedNews) (**Redacted**, hosted by Clayton and Natali Morris)
is an independent media channel whose broadcasts are cited on two of the site's
intelligence-adjacent pages. The claims below are the channel's media allegations, attributed and
unverified.

## Claims from X posts

- A **September 16, 2025 broadcast promo** headlined that "they knew about Charlie Kirk's
  shooting BEFOREHAND" and cast early FBI messaging as damage control for Director Kash Patel.
  The site lists this on [Narrative Control](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/narrative-control) as an
  early, high-visibility **prior-knowledge narrative** — a media allegation requiring primary
  sourcing.
- A [post](https://x.com/RedactedNews/status/2035851766970122341) cited in the site's coverage of
  **LRAD and voice-to-skull (V2K/Frey effect)** claims —
  [Frey Effect / V2K](/Gov_Mind_Control/frey-effect-v2k) — where the site notes LRAD is a real
  technology while the case-specific claims remain unproven.

## Investigative value

Redacted's reach made the prior-knowledge framing part of the mainstream of the citizen
investigation within a week of the assassination. The site logs it as a narrative marker: what
was being alleged, how early, and by whom — not as corroborated reporting.
