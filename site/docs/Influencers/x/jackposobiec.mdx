# Jack Posobiec — X Posts

[@JackPosobiec](https://x.com/JackPosobiec) (**Jack Posobiec**) is a major-reach commentator whose
posts recur across the Tyler Robinson evidence pages — generally on the **prosecution-leaning**
side of the citizen debate. He also has a [person profile](/People/jack-posobiec) on this site. All
items below are attributed claims from X posts; Robinson is charged, not convicted.

## Claims from X posts

- **Discord / text log amplification (Oct 31, 2025 / Feb 26, 2026)** — amplified Substack material
  (anonbirdd.substack.com) described as Discord and text logs from Robinson and partner
  [Lance Twiggs](/Tyler_Robinson/Girlfriend/overview), claiming drug use, extreme online content,
  ideology themes, and heavy ChatGPT use — **leaked-material claims** whose authenticity and
  chain of custody remain disputed; see [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages),
  [Before](/Tyler_Robinson/Before), [Discord evidence in court](/court/discord-evidence-court) and
  [Twiggs' phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone).
- **Fingerprint warrant report (April 10, 2026)** — reported that an unsealed Tyler Robinson search
  warrant shows **fingerprints and a palm print** found on the Losee Center roof at the jump site —
  prosecution-side association evidence, treated as a claim pending full exhibit release on
  [More Reasons](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/more-reasons).
- **Preliminary-hearing commentary (July 2026)** — described the surveillance presentation as
  showing roughly four campus visits / multi-loop scouting including a Chick-fil-A stop
  ([pre-shot campus timeline](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus)), and argued the State
  presented substantial video/DNA/text evidence for bind-over — see
  [Sept 8–13 timeline](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13) and the
  [probable-cause affidavit page](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause).
- **Rifle threads** — tagged in circulating posts about the Mauser/.30-06 questions tracked on
  [Weapon / Rifle](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle); independent threads addressed to him argue the
  released Robinson–Twiggs messages contain internal contradictions.

## Investigative value

Posobiec's feed is the strongest **counterweight** to the not-the-assassin material on this site:
where skeptics stress inconclusive ballistics, he stresses prints, DNA, and surveillance loops.
Reading both sides against the primary docket is the method this site recommends.
