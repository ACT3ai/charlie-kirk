## Investigative value

Defense-oriented commentators urge treating screenshot dumps as **chain-of-custody problems**
until authenticated in court; prosecution-oriented commentators treat them as corroboration of
the charging theory. The site records @ginamilan_'s summary as one node in that dispute. No
court has authenticated the leaked images as presented on X, and claims that Twiggs was a
co-conspirator are **unverified public commentary**.
