# The Vigilant Fox — X Posts

[@VigilantFox](https://x.com/VigilantFox) (**The Vigilant Fox**) is a large aggregator account whose
posts supplied two load-bearing artifacts to this investigation: the earliest widely shared
**neck-wound eyewitness account** on the day of the shooting, and the March 2026 **Joe Kent**
Iran-war clips in the motive lane.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 10, 2025** — Shared an eyewitness clip that became the **contrast account** to the
  early "shot in the heart" wave: *"I heard just one pop... his head kind of fell back and blood was
  just gushing out of his neck."* The witness also reported the sound felt like it came from
  **uphill and behind** him. ([Post](https://x.com/VigilantFox/status/1965871960702828885)) — filed at
  [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart) and the
  [photo cluster](/Photos/Official_Narrative/Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart/overview).
- The clip's **one pop** and directionality details feed the acoustics and
  [wound-trajectory](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle) analyses, and it is the account most
  consistent with later official neck-wound reporting.
- **March 2026** — Amplified **Joe Kent** clips: that Charlie was in the Oval Office in the lead-up
  to the 12-day war, was vocal against the first strike on Iran, and said in a hallway "Stop us from
  getting into war with Iran" — three months before he was killed. Attributed paraphrase, developed
  at [Iran War — Charlie vs Israel](/Israel_Main_Suspect/iran-war-charlie-vs-israel).

## Investigative value

Unusual among aggregators, both of this account's contributions cut **against** the loudest early
narratives: the neck-wound clip undercut the heart-shot framing, and the Kent package moved the
motive discussion from social-media speculation to a named former official's on-record account.
Both remain attributed claims, not adjudicated findings.
