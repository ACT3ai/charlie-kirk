# PatriotVerity — X Posts

[@PatriotVerity](https://x.com/PatriotVerity) enters the site's record as an **amplifier** of one
specific claim family: that other prominent commentators were warned they might be next. The item
below is an attributed, **unverified** repost claim.

## Claims from X posts

- Reposted the claim that **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** and
  **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** received warnings on **September 10, 2025** of being
  on an **assassination-related target list**.
  ([Repost source](https://x.com/josettecaruso/status/1968339454248337918)) The site marks this
  **unverified** on both [Public Response](/Other/Public_Response) and
  [UVU Social Media Response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response).
- The claim circulates alongside related aftermath items — heightened security concerns for campus
  speakers and reports of the Robinson family in hiding — which the site records as attributed
  public-response material, not confirmed facts.

## Investigative value

The target-list warning is one of the recurring aftermath claims that, if ever documented (call
records, security advisories), would bear directly on premeditation and scope. Until then it stays
in the unverified column, and this page exists to track its attribution chain.
