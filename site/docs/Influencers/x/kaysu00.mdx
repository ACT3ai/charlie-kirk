# KaySu00 — X Posts

[@KaySu00](https://x.com/KaySu00) is one of the accounts that amplified and questioned the
**SUV-interior photo sets** attributed to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) in June 2026.
Everything below is a **claim attributed to their X posts** — interpretations of unauthenticated
screenshots, not confirmed forensic findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 26, 2026** — Shared a screenshot from a Candace Owens broadcast, watermarked "CANDACE,"
  showing an inset close-up of a **pale, irregular fragment with scorched dark-brown and black
  edges** on a seat surface, asking: *"Why is there burned/charred material in the SUV…..?
  Cmon now…"* ([Source](https://x.com/KaySu00/status/2070601198848049263)) — see
  [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).
- The charring question is used by proponents of the [exploding-mic theory](/Mic/overview) to argue
  a detonating device — not a rifle round — would produce heat and combustion. The site notes the
  fragment was **never independently identified or tested** and lighting or compression artifacts
  cannot be ruled out ([Charred Material Fragments](/Suspicious/Medical/charred-material-fragments)).
- Listed among the accounts (with @alleytopfiles, @iluminomoly, @DiligentDenizen, @FurkanGozukara)
  amplifying the two four-image photo sets said to show the interior of the **black GMC Yukon
  Denali** — rear-seat stain, pale floor-sill fragment, debris, a blue-solder-mask PCB fragment,
  and shattered tempered glass ([SUV Destroyed](/CoverUp/SUV_Destroyed)).
- The same material feeds the [blurred-windows question](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/suv-blurred-windows)
  and the claim that the SUV was later destroyed rather than preserved — a claim attributed to
  "they" with **no agency, company, or individual named** ([SUV Destroyed / ADL Floor](/Suspicious/FBI/suv-destroyed-adl-floor)).

## Investigative value

@KaySu00's charred-material post became the most cited **observational hook** for the device
side of the cause-of-death debate — the site rates it EMERGING precisely because nothing has been
physically recovered or lab-tested. The photos remain unauthenticated primary-source material.
