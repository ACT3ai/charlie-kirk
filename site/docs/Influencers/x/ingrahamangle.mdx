# Laura Ingraham — X Posts

[@IngrahamAngle](https://x.com/IngrahamAngle) (**Laura Ingraham**, Fox News host) appears in this
investigation's coverage as a **mainstream amplifier** of the private-security side of the rooftop
dispute. Her posts carried a coordination-failure claim to a national audience; the site treats it
as a public dispute between parties, not a court finding.

## Claims from X posts

- **November 20, 2025** — amplified [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole)'s public position
  that police failed to cover the rooftops as promised: *"We were told it would be handled."*
  Investigators treat this as a **coordination dispute** between the private detail and campus /
  local law enforcement — documented on [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security),
  [No Counter-Snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) and
  [Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures).
- The same amplification is quoted on the [Security Team overview](/Security_Team/overview) beside
  the staffing picture (about six UVU officers, no rooftop control) — mainstream outlets report
  **lapses**; the intentional-enablement extensions some citizen threads add are **not** adopted as
  fact by this site.

## Investigative value

The post matters for **provenance**: it shows the rooftop-responsibility dispute was not a fringe
theory but a claim carried by a major broadcast figure, sourced to the security contractor himself.
The underlying question — who owned the high ground on September 10 — remains the Security Team
section's central unresolved item.
