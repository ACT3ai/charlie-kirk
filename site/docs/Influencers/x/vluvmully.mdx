# Vicki Donahue — X Posts

[@VLuvMully](https://x.com/VLuvMully) (**Vicki Donahue**) is the central documentary source for the
**custody-and-Mirandizing** thread — the argument that [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) was in
custody far earlier than the public narrative implied. Her posts circulate images of an actual
**defense filing**, making this one of the better-documented citizen threads. Robinson is charged,
not convicted; the timeline conclusions below remain contested in court.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 11, 2026** — Posted images of a defense filing quoting Bates-stamped Washington County video
  exhibit **003996-R2**: at **6:25 PM September 11** an officer reads Robinson his rights; at
  **6:26 PM** he asks for legal counsel ("a fellow in Utah named Doug Terry").
  ([Post](https://x.com/VLuvMully/status/2076019571127935010)) — the pivot document of
  [Custody Timeline vs Fake Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession) and
  [Miranda Defense Filing](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/miranda-defense-filing).
- Framed the stakes: if Robinson was Mirandized at 6:25 PM without his phone, the BOLO photos,
  rooftop video, and **~7:57 PM Discord confession** were released **while the suspect was already
  held** — and the Discord messages "could not have been sent by him." Whether that holds depends on
  the phone-seizure time and Discord authentication, still contested in court.
- Circulated the **booking record**: arrest 09/11/2025 10:00 PM, Date In 09/12/2025 1:58 AM — the gap
  investigators read against the 6:25 PM Miranda reading, per the
  [Mirandize hub](/court/mirandize/overview).
- Shared the **Baron Coleman** WDYFW clip on why the exact turn-in time is "such a definitive way" to
  test the government's account — see [Baron Coleman Early Turn-In](/court/mirandize/baron-coleman-early-turn-in).
- Publicized the Washington County **GRAMA response** that **no bodycam footage of the surrender
  exists** — see [Bodycam GRAMA — No Footage](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage) and
  [Missing Surrender Discovery](/Suspicious/Defense_Attorneys/missing-surrender-discovery).
- Her material anchors the [custody-surrender-mirandizing timeline](/Timeline/custody-surrender-mirandizing-timeline),
  the [Miranda vs Brooksby account](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/miranda-vs-brooksby-account), and the
  [Cowboys Smokehouse receipt](/Tyler_Robinson/cowboys-smokehouse-receipt) thread.
