# MsJaneDoeUKnow — X Posts

[@MsJaneDoeUKnow](https://x.com/MsJaneDoeUKnow) compiles **warrant-content and dashcam research**
into minute-level timelines of September 10, 2025 — filling gaps the public probable-cause
affidavit leaves open. The site treats these as **research compilations of alleged warrant
content**, not court-admitted facts.

## Claims from X posts

- **~8:28–9:24 AM MDT** — Warrant-timeline compilations (including **WARRANT 3215391** references)
  describe a **reconnaissance walk** window after the grey Dodge Challenger parks on campus. See
  [Rental Cars and Vehicle Movements](/Timeline/rental-cars-vehicle-movements-timeline).
- **~9:32 AM MDT** — The same compilations place the Challenger **leaving campus** heading
  northeast — a candidate answer to the affidavit's conspicuously **missing departure time**, if the
  underlying warrant materials are ever produced and authenticated.
- **~11:55 AM MDT** — Frame-analysis claims of a **waistband bulge** as the figure ascends
  parking-structure stairs toward the Losee Center — presented as consistent with a concealed long
  object. The site notes a bulge in stills is **not** proof of a rifle in pants, and pairs it with
  the feasibility critique in the viral @I_Am_JohnCullen thread. See the
  [roof-jumping timeline](/Timeline/roof-jumping-timeline).
- **July 2026** — Hearing note that the [defense team](/court/defense-team) asked **no
  cross-examination questions** of Agent [Brian Davis](/People/brian-davis) at the preliminary
  hearing — strategic silence, not proof either way. Recorded on
  [Affidavit of Probable Cause](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/Affidavit_Probable_Cause).

## Investigative value

The account's compilations give the site its clearest picture of what the sealed warrant materials
allegedly contain — including the departure clock the public affidavit never states. Their value is
conditional: everything hinges on the underlying warrants being produced and authenticated.
