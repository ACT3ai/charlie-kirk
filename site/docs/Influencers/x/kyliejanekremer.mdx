# KylieJaneKremer — X Posts

[@KylieJaneKremer](https://x.com/KylieJaneKremer) appears in the site's record for circulating
**early bystander video** from the September 10, 2025 UVU shooting. The material below is an
**attributed claim from an X post**, treated as unverified unless matched to chain-of-custody
metadata.

## Claims from X posts

- **September 2025** — Circulated bystander video **alleged to show the shooter (a figure) on a
  building before the shot** at Utah Valley University.
  ([Source](https://x.com/KylieJaneKremer/status/1965894858624974975)) — referenced on
  [UVU Social Media Response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response) and
  [UVU Student Reactions](/UVU/UVU-Student-Reactions).
- The clip circulates alongside other early eyewitness materials — acoustic theories about shot
  origin (@ProjectConstitu) and the [George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn) diversion
  claims — all listed by the site as **theories and allegations, not UVU statements**.

## Investigative value

Early bystander footage is central to the rooftop-figure debate: who was on which building, and
when. The site's caution applies here — student-captured media from the chaotic first minutes has
repeatedly been misdated or misattributed, so this clip is preserved as a lead pending metadata
verification, not as an identification of any person.
