# whokilledck — X Posts

[@whokilledck](https://x.com/whokilledck) works the **archived scanner-audio lane**, publishing and
narrating stretches of Utah dispatch radio from September 10, 2025. Their characterizations are a
citizen researcher's transcriptions of clips they published — quoted fragments, not official
transcripts — cited alongside [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)'s scanner work.

## Claims from X posts

- Summarized the scanner stretch naming **Zachariah Qureshi** as a person of interest in tactical
  gear / black mask "going **hospital to hospital** looking for the victim" — with two names
  circulating on air: Zachariah (red vehicle) and "Andrew" (green Nissan Armada, the "one-armed
  bandit"). Qureshi was detained, released, and **never charged as the shooter** — the documented arc
  is exonerating. See [Dispatch / Radio Scanner Timeline](/Timeline/dispatch-radio-scanner-timeline).
- The same narrations capture the **hospital denial**: officers relay that Provo checked with the
  hospital and were told none of it happened — no armed man, no confrontation — yet the vehicle hunt
  continued through changing colors, makes, and models.
- Their Qureshi-stretch summaries are cross-referenced on the
  [distraction-events timeline](/Timeline/distraction-events-timeline), where scanner echoes fed the
  false "suspect in custody" campus alert environment.

## Investigative value

Scanner audio is one of the few **contemporaneous, un-curated** records of the response. This
account's narrations preserve the churn — phantom vehicles, a plate that returned "no records found,"
a hospital chase the hospital denied — that official summaries omit. The site's caution: scanner
traffic is raw and error-prone by nature, and no name heard on air is a finding about any person.
