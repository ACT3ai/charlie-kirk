# PolitickleAi — X Posts

[@PolitickleAi](https://x.com/PolitickleAi) (**Politickle AI**) produces political cartoons and
slide-format video essays. Two works signed or credited to this account are archived in the
site's photo evidence clusters.

## Contributions cited on the site

- A hand-drawn **political cartoon** — a speaker at a campus podium throwing his arms up at a
  quadcopter overhead, captioned "NEXT, IT'LL DELIVER THE TRUTH!" — archived at
  [Drone Political Cartoon](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Political_Cartoon_Drone_54249a) as commentary on
  the [Drones](/Drones/overview) reporting.
- A **slide video on drones, CIA, and Israel** questions, archived in the
  [CIA/Intelligence slides cluster](/Photos/CIA_Intelligence/Slides/VIDEO_Drones_CIA_Israel/Img_Slide25_e62507).
  The final slide carries the author's own on-screen disclaimer: **"No claim is made that drone
  assassinated Charlie Kirk. Or that flight crew knew about assassination."** The site treats
  that sentence as governing how the whole slide set should be read.

## Investigative value

This account contributes **editorial framing rather than primary evidence** — its value is in
showing how the drone and intelligence questions circulated visually, and in modeling the kind of
explicit disclaimer the site itself applies: raising questions without asserting that any person
or crew committed a crime.
