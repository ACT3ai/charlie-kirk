# The SCIF — X Posts

[@TheSCIF](https://x.com/TheSCIF) (**The SCIF**) is a high-engagement aggregation account best known in this investigation for summarizing the **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) military-police incident report** presented by [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens). (A separate account, @TheIntelSCIF, has its own page at [Intel SCIF](./intel-scif).) The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X posts**, not authenticated documents.

## Claims from X posts

- **December 23, 2025** — Summarized what it called a "verified military police incident report" from Fort Huachuca: an **11:17 AM, September 9, 2025** tip from **CW2 Daniel Head** at Southwest Border Mission Headquarters **Building #70525**; witness **Michael ("Mitch") Snow** in a gray Chevy Equinox (CA plate **9MIN66**); a **negative K-9 explosives sniff**; named MPs and investigators; and Snow's claim of being first questioned by a **"Captain Neff"** ([US Intelligence Assisted — Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca), [Fort Huachuca military timeline](/Timeline/fort-huachuca-military-timeline)). The report, if authentic, **places Snow on base** — it does **not** identify meeting attendees. Names, plates, and authenticity remain claims pending primary PDFs.
- Amplified the **Sept 9 "Flood variant"** of the text claims — that Charlie texted security lead **[Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood)** wording like *"They are going to kill me tomorrow"* — for which **no public screenshot or Flood confirmation** has been produced ([the "they are going to kill me" text](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text)).
- **December 12, 2025** — Circulated (with @EvanAKilgore) that Owens said **Andrew Kolvet told her off the record** about a night-before text, and that she went public after Erika Kirk publicly implied no such messages existed.
- Amplified the **foreign-IP medical search** analysis attributed to [Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) — Israeli and Washington-DC IP ranges allegedly searching Timpanogos surgeons and the medical examiner pre-event ([Israel Google searches — medical people](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people)).

## Investigative value

@TheSCIF functions as a **force multiplier** for the Owens-adjacent document drops — the MP report summary is the main public version of that paper trail. Every item needs primary authentication; no living person named in these threads is accused of a crime by this site.
