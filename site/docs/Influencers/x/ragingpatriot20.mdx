# Joshua Peterson (RagingPatriot20) — X Posts

[@RagingPatriot20](https://x.com/RagingPatriot20) (**Joshua Peterson**) is described as a former
[Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) representative who came forward on camera in a video
published by [Project Constitution](./project-constitution) (@ProjectConstitu) on **June 26,
2026**. His account is the single source for the recruitment allegations below; the site
publishes it **only so it can be properly investigated** and does not claim it is true or
accurate.

## Claims from his testimony and posts

- During TPUSA training, new hires were allegedly taken out by former representatives in rented
  vans to Arizona bars, plied with alcohol, and pulled aside one by one — when it was his turn,
  the reps allegedly asked his views on Israel and "[Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)," then said
  **"we're part of it"** and claimed to have worked at Turning Point for four or five years. Full
  transcript: [Joshua Peterson](/Israel/Mossad_TPUSA/Joshua_Peterson).
- The account is treated by the site as alleged **foreign-intelligence recruitment behavior**
  inside TPUSA's new-hire pipeline —
  [TPUSA Foreign Recruitment](/intelligence/TPUSA_Foreign_Recruitment).
- Peterson is also the sole cited source for a claimed **secret stock fund**
  ([Secret Stock Fund](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/secret-stock-fund)) — the site notes the
  underlying video is not linked in the investigation file.
- His allegations feed the attributed **handler-network** discussion
  ([Handler Network Claims](/Gov_Mind_Control/handler-network-claims)) and the
  [Recruited](/Tyler_Robinson/Recruited) and
  [Israelis at UVU](/CoverUp/Israelis_At_UVU) analyses.

## Investigative value

Peterson is the closest thing the Mossad-TPUSA thread has to a named, on-camera insider source —
which is exactly why the site treats it carefully: it is **uncorroborated single-witness
testimony**, relayed via Project Constitution, with no supporting documents yet on record. See
[Peterson Whistleblower](/Suspicious/Israel/peterson-whistleblower) for the source assessment.
