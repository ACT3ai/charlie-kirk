# Angela Beth Moore — X Posts

[@AngelaBethMoore](https://x.com/AngelaBethMoore) is a citizen video-and-scheduling analyst whose work appears in the site's second-by-second reconstruction of September 10. Both contributions are **attributed observations**, not allegations of wrongdoing against any living person.

## Claims from X posts

- **The Turek pan stills** — Posted stills claiming **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** executed a **360° phone pan of the crowd at 12:22:08–12:22:12 PM MDT**, less than a minute before impact. The site logs this as an open question about recording orientation — significance disputed, and expressly **not an allegation of wrongdoing**. See [the 12:23 shooting-time page](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time).
- **Traveler scheduling analysis** — Noted that several named TPUSA travelers ([Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), [Danny Phillip](/People/danny-phillip), Nate Walker in that analysis) landing at **SLC ~9:23 AM** could not yet have been on campus during the claimed 8:29–9:25 AM window — so any genuine early-morning contact between the suspect and TPUSA staff would have involved **different** staff, if the contact happened at all. A scheduling observation, not an accusation. See [Tyler Robinson pre-shot campus movements](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).

## Investigative value

Moore's lane is **cross-checking claims against clocks** — her traveler analysis is one of the few posts that pushes back on the viral 9:24 AM TPUSA-contact claim from inside the skeptic community, which is exactly the discipline the timeline pages try to preserve. Her Turek pan observation remains an open item in the disputed final minute.
