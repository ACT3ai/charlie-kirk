# Brandon Taylor Moore — X Posts

[@LetsGoBrando45](https://x.com/LetsGoBrando45) (**Brandon Taylor Moore**) posts in the
Israel-motive and Epstein-intelligence lanes of the case. The items below are **claims attributed
to his X posts** — amplifications and summaries, not confirmed findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **October 7, 2025** — In a multi-thousand-like post after [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)'s October disclosures,
  summarized Owens as saying Charlie **openly communicated that he thought Israel was going to
  kill him ~24 hours before he died**, and that **three separate people close to Charlie** can
  confirm that statement. The site stresses no sworn affidavits from those three have been
  produced; "three" is a media claim pending identification
  ([Charlie Said Israel Would Kill Him](/Israel_Main_Suspect/charlie-said-israel-would-kill-him)).
- **Epstein–Mossad framing** — Amplified threads linking Jeffrey Epstein to intelligence
  operations, cited on [Epstein Files Push](/Charlie/Epstein_Files_Push) as an example of the
  "Epstein is Mossad" framing attributed to clips of Kirk.
  ([Source](https://x.com/LetsGoBrando45/status/1985896546706342049))
- Same threads appear in [Charlie Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel) among the
  accounts extrapolating Kirk's alleged Epstein-intelligence remarks into wider narratives.
- A separate historical-ideology graphic post by the account (Bolshevik Revolution framing) is
  preserved in the photo record as context for the feeds where the case was discussed — the site
  notes the framing is the poster's and is **not evidence** about the shooting
  ([Photo record](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Img_Photo_bbdb91)).

## Investigative value

The "three separate people" post is one of the most-shared **witness-count claims** in the
Israel-suspect discourse: if true, those witnesses would be subpoena-grade; if overstated, the
claim collapses to a paraphrase of Owens. The site treats it as the test case for converting
viral summary into verifiable testimony.
