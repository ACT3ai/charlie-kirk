# realtoriabrooke — X Posts

[@realtoriabrooke](https://x.com/realtoriabrooke) provided **July 6, 2026 preliminary-hearing
coverage** in the Tyler Robinson case, cited across the site's court and law-enforcement pages
alongside @JOKAQARMY1 and @TPostMillennial. This is court-reporting commentary, not an official
transcript substitute.

## Claims from X posts

- Summarized testimony that former UVU officer **[Chris Bagley](/People/chris-bagley)**'s
  body-cam footage **reportedly ended on the roof before crime-scene tape went down**, with
  testimony that the camera **died** at that moment — the single 27:35 recording being his only
  body-cam footage that day. See [Bagley Bodycam Gap](/CoverUp/Bagley_Bodycam_Gap) and
  [Officer Bagley Bodycam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam). The site stresses this is
  **not a finding of intentional spoliation or perjury**.
- Coverage of Bagley's testimony about finding a **red-and-black screwdriver** on a rooftop, with
  a photo entered into evidence.
- Summarized exhibit descriptions of **marks consistent with elbows, legs, and a weapon rest** in
  the Low C rooftop gravel — the "sniper pad" language noted on the
  [Photos overview](/Photos/overview).
- General hearing-witness and testimony summaries cited on
  [Hearing Witnesses](/court/hearing-witnesses) and [Testimony](/court/testimony).

## Investigative value
