# NihiloX — X Posts

[@NihiloX](https://x.com/NihiloX) (also cited as @nihilox) is the **flight-data compiler** behind
much of the site's Egyptian-jet coverage — credited with assembling the raw ADS-B logs that
[Project Constitution](./project-constitution) and others amplified. The bullets below are the
trackers' compiled claims and the account's stated conclusions, **not confirmed flights or
findings**.

## Claims from X posts

- Compiled the year-long **date lists** alleging that [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT),
  [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), and [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV) repeatedly flew from Cairo into **Israel**
  while public logs read Cairo-to-Cairo — the jets allegedly going **transponder-dark** mid-leg.
  Posted what the account called *"irrefutable evidence SU-BTT regularly flies into Israel."* The
  site notes the same logs are equally consistent with routine VIP flying; only the **October 21,
  2025** Cairo–Israel leg has mainstream corroboration. See
  [Israel Planes](/Planes/Israel-Planes) and the
  [SU-BTT movements timeline](/Timeline/su-btt-plane-movements-timeline).
- Stated conclusion: **Egypt is misrepresenting its relationship with Israel**, pointing to a
  possible Israel connection to the jets on the ground in Provo the day of the assassination — the
  account's inference, not a verified fact. Credited in the
  [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) data compilation.
- Credited in the **December 15, 2025** @ProjectConstitu thread claiming SU-BTT launched on Sept 10
  not from Provo but from **Dugway Proving Ground's Michael Army Airfield** with transponder off —
  an alternative point-of-origin theory.
- **May 2026** — Video compilation naming donor **Robert Shillman** as having gone on record about
  pulling ~$2M from TPUSA amid Israel friction — the site stresses nothing asserts any donor
  ordered or funded a killing. See
  [Extreme Motivation](/Israel_Main_Suspect/extreme-motivation).
- **June 2026** — Argued there is "no innocent explanation" for the FBI shutting down
  [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)'s foreign-nexus inquiry if the case were truly closed. See
  [Leads to Foreign Involvement](/Israel_Main_Suspect/leads-to-foreign-involvement).
- **July 2026** — Claimed **Curt Kolvet** trained at **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)** with a cousin at NETCOM;
  Kolvet **publicly denied** working there, describing a three-day TDY. The site records claim and
  denial together on the [Fort Huachuca timeline](/Timeline/fort-huachuca-military-timeline).
- Circulated a viral video **mocking "shot with a 30-06" certainty** language — an example of
  platform culture shaping ballistics belief, noted on
  [Media Narratives After](/After/Media_Narratives_And_Censorship_After).

## Investigative value

NihiloX is the **data layer** under the Egyptian-jet story: the date compilations others cite start
here. The strength is volume and specificity; the weakness is that transponder-gap inference cannot
distinguish covert Israel legs from ordinary in-country VIP flying, and the account's absolute
framing ("irrefutable") exceeds what the data shows. Read with the skeptic audits preserved on each
plane page.
