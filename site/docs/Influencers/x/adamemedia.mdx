# AdameMedia — X Posts

[@AdameMedia](https://x.com/AdameMedia) is one of the highest-reach compilation accounts in the case. Its **72-hour Sept 8–10 timeline** — donor-loss texts, an emergency Zoom, and the "they are going to kill me" message — is described on this site as the dominant social template for the pre-assassination story. Everything below is **attributed viral compilation**, not authenticated documents or court findings; living persons named are not accused of any crime.

## Claims from X posts

- **Sept 8–9, 2025 timeline (widely circulated)** — Kirk texted a group including [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) and [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki) about losing a "big donor" and "leaving the pro-Israel cause"; an **emergency Zoom** followed on Sept 9; Kirk then allegedly texted [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) "**they are going to kill me**." Screenshots remain unauthenticated in any public court file. See [the text page](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) and [pre-event political pressure](/Timeline/pre-event-political-pressure).
- Amplified the **"AIPAC treason"** quote attributed to Kirk — "Israel didn't like that" framing. ([Post](https://x.com/AdameMedia/status/1999306381623709957))
- **Oct 25–26, 2025** — Echoed the clip alleging the FBI asked a UVU witness to **delete video** ("FBI DEMANDS CHARLIE KIRK WITNESS DELETE EVIDENCE… This is a cover up") — repost commentary, not independent confirmation. See [FBI Asked to Delete Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video).
- **Nov 2025** — Amplified Candace Owens-network claims that the FBI **seized all hospital surveillance** inside and outside [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) — attributed and unverified. See [Hospital Footage and NDAs](/CoverUp/Hospital_Footage_And_NDAs).
- **Sept 11–13, 2025** — Circulated screenshots asserting the **Jerusalem Post was first** to confirm Kirk's death, feeding the OR-timeline dispute at [media vs citizen timeline](/Timeline/media-vs-citizen-investigation-timeline).
- Published multi-part **"every connection" Israel mega-threads** stacking biographical, speech, and money-pressure claims — cataloged with caveats at [More Israel](/Israel_Main_Suspect/more-israel).
- **Jan 20, 2026** — Summarized [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) arguing Erika Kirk shifted TPUSA away from Charlie's positioning on AIPAC — Owens's commentary, relayed. See [Candace Owens Israel controversies](/Media/Candace-Owens-Israel-Controversies).
- Cited by [@ProjectConstitu](./project-constitution) as the source of an alleged "FBI whistleblower" claim that leadership huddled on "Twitter strategy" after UVU — whistleblower status unverified. See [narrative control](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/narrative-control).

## Investigative value

AdameMedia functions as the case's **narrative compressor**: it packages scattered claims into timelines that mainstream aggregators then repeat. That reach is exactly why the site attributes rather than adopts — the compilations bundle authenticated public records with unverified screenshots, and bundling is not corroboration.
