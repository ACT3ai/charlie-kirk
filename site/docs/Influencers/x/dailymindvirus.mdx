# dailymindvirus — X Posts

[@dailymindvirus](https://x.com/dailymindvirus) appears in the record in the **leaked-messages
lane** — the July 2026 circulation of alleged Discord/text screenshots in the
[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) case. Items below are attributed claims about who
circulated what, not authenticated evidence.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026** ([post](https://x.com/dailymindvirus/status/2075294270068609495)) — Attributed the
  circulation of the leaked message images to **TPUSA spokesman Andrew Kolvet**. That is this
  account's attribution claim, recorded on [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages);
  the site urges treating screenshot dumps as **chain-of-custody problems** until authenticated in
  court.
- Appears (with @ginamilan_ and @REVWUTRUTH) in the leaked-text threads placing **Lance Twiggs** as
  the recipient of alleged confession messages and rifle-drop discussion — see
  [Girlfriend / Twiggs overview](/Tyler_Robinson/Girlfriend/overview). No court has found Twiggs
  guilty of anything; claims that Twiggs was the "real shooter" or a co-conspirator are unverified
  public commentary, and Kolvet is not accused of wrongdoing.

## Investigative value

A provenance-tracking data point: in the authentication fight over the alleged confession
screenshots, **who introduced the images into circulation** matters as much as their content.
This account's Kolvet attribution is one of the claims that fight will have to resolve against
primary records.
