# KanekoaTheGreat — X Posts

[@KanekoaTheGreat](https://x.com/KanekoaTheGreat) is a high-engagement research account whose
**April 2026** publication of what it described as unsealed search-warrant texts became a central
artifact in the court-evidence debate. Until raw forensic reports are public, the site treats these
message screenshots as **attributed reporting, not verified forensics**.

## Claims from X posts

- **April 2026** — Published what it described as **unsealed search-warrant texts** between
  [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) and **Lance Twiggs**: "I am fucked," a coworker campus
  still, intent to surrender "on own terms," and family-cover discussion
  ([Discord Evidence in Court](/court/discord-evidence-court)).
- If authentic warrant extracts, the texts are **powerful prosecution evidence**; if partial or
  miscaptioned, the defense will attack **authentication** — the open question the site's court
  pages track ([Twiggs' Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone)).
- The publication raises the extraction-chain question: which messages come from **Cellebrite
  dumps** versus **cloud exports** versus **Discord server production**
  ([Phone Extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction)).
- Adjacent amplifications by @JackPosobiec and @GuntherEagleman of alleged **Discord logs** remain
  contested in citizen threads that note Miranda timing and phone-custody windows.

## Investigative value

The account moved sealed-case material into public debate — whichever way authentication resolves,
its April 2026 thread defined the terms of the **texts-versus-timeline** fight, including the
claimed conflict between prosecution text times (~4:30 p.m.) and Twiggs' reported testimony of an
11 p.m. contact.
