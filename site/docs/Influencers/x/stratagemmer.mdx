# stratagemmer — X Posts

[@stratagemmer](https://x.com/stratagemmer) is one of the most-cited **court-watch accounts** for the July 2026 Tyler Robinson preliminary hearing, posting live notes on autopsy testimony, evidence handling, and defense objections. The bullets below are **claims attributed to their X notes** — not official transcripts or court findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 6, 2026** — State witness **Agent David Hull** reportedly testified an autopsy was performed the **evening of September 10** by a male pathologist Hull spelled as **"Dr. Guajardo"**; cause of death listed as **homicide by gunshot wound to the neck**; the judge reportedly ruled the autopsy report **not public** ([Ballistics & Wound Analysis](/Medical/ballistics_and_wound_analysis), [Trial & Autopsy Report](/Medical/trial_and_autopsy_report)).
- Hull reportedly **could not recall exact dates or methods** of receiving the ME report (email vs CD); defense hearsay and reliability objections were described as **overruled** ([Investigation Challenges](/Other/Investigation-Challenges)).
- The autopsy was **entered into evidence** with **bullet fragments recovered** — a point bullet-claim debaters must reconcile with no-exit rhetoric and the ATF's **inconclusive** rifle-match filings ([Hospital People & Bullet Claims](/Medical/hospital_people_and_bullet_claims)).
- Hull reportedly testified that AV figure **[Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth)** delivered a **flash drive** and Google links rather than original SD cards, while Farnsworth's sworn statement allegedly mentions only links — framed by posters as a **chain-of-custody gap** ([TPUSA Production Footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage)).
- The hands-on pathologist name attribution ("Dr. Guajardo," via Hull's spelling) is **unconfirmed** in the public court record ([Autopsy Sealed / Examiner](/CoverUp/Autopsy_Sealed_Examiner), [Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner)).
