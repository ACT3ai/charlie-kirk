# ArtifexMemor — X Posts

[@ArtifexMemor](https://x.com/ArtifexMemor) is an X account whose bio reportedly lists prior
work with **@tpusa** and **@charliekirk11**. In July 2026 the account became a key circulation
node for two threads on this site: the **Joe Kent NCTC shutdown** clip and the resurfaced
**Posobiec-Bowyer Fort Huachuca** exchange. The bullets below are **claims and materials
attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings.

## Claims from X posts

- Quote-tweeted the Julian Dorey Podcast clip in which former NCTC director **Joe Kent** says
  his center's parallel review of potential **foreign ties** to the Kirk assassination was halted
  early ("stop what you're doing now because we have Tyler"), adding: "This is what an American
  patriot looks like." See [Joe Kent on the Dorey Podcast](/intelligence/Joe_Kent_Dorey_Interview).
  ([Source](https://x.com/ArtifexMemor/status/2079560806690324910))
- Resurfaced (July 7, 2026) a **February 4, 2022 screenshot** in which [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)
  described military training trips to **Fort Huachuca** — home of the U.S. Army Intelligence Center
  of Excellence — and [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) replied "jack stop going to huachuca."
  The site records the exchange as banter circulated as a pointer to Posobiec's documented
  intelligence background, not as evidence of a role in the events of September 10, 2025.
  ([Source](https://x.com/ArtifexMemor/status/2074570091673649274))
- Circulated (July 20, 2026) a clip relating to [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet) used on
  this site's Kolvet profile. ([Source](https://x.com/ArtifexMemor/status/2079275082648809861))

## Investigative value

The account functions as an **amplifier with insider framing**: its claimed TPUSA/Kirk work history
gives its reposts reach inside the movement's audience. Its Kent quote-tweet is the site's cited
entry point to the [intelligence-services theme](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview), and the Huachuca
screenshot appears on both the Posobiec and Bowyer profiles. Everything remains attributed
circulation, not independent documentation.
