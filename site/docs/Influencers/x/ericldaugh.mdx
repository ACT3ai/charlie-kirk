# Eric Daugherty — X Posts

[@EricLDaugh](https://x.com/EricLDaugh) (**Eric Daugherty**) is a verified breaking-news account
whose posts appear at three distinct points in the investigation record: the chaotic first hours,
the 2026 court-transparency fight, and July 2026 courtroom reporting. All items below are
attributed claims from X posts, not findings of this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **First hours, Sept 10, 2025** — Posted "BREAKING: Subject in custody... reported as Zachariah
  [Ahmed Qureshi](/People/ahmed-qureshi)" with a portrait photo, minutes into the story. **The man
  named was released without charge and was not the shooter.** The site preserves the screenshot
  ([People Photo 40](/Photos/People/Img_Photo_23324a)) as a record of how a name and face spread
  before verification — see [Zach Qureshi](/People/zach-qureshi).
- **June 2026** — High-engagement posts (with [@GigaBeers](./gigabeers)) celebrated Judge **Tony
  Graf** denying defense efforts to **close/seal** preliminary-hearing material and **allowing
  cameras** — the "keep it all public" framing tracked on [gag orders and sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing)
  and [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).
- **July 9–10, 2026** — Courtroom reporting of [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)'s description
  of enhanced Losee roof video: timestamp progression into position at **~12:21–12:22** before the
  12:23 shot. This is reporting of a video exhibit, not a conviction — cross-clock detail on
  [12:23 shooting time](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) and the [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026).
- Cited on the [legal investigation overview](/legal_investigation/overview) among accounts
  amplifying rulings affirming a **presumptive public right** to court records.

## Investigative value

Daugherty's feed shows both edges of fast aggregation: the Qureshi misidentification is a
case study in premature naming, while his 2026 court reporting is a widely used public record
of hearing-day developments. Treat courtroom summaries as commentary pending transcripts.
