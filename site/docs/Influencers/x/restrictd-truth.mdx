# Restrictd_Truth — X Posts

[@Restrictd_Truth](https://x.com/Restrictd_Truth) is cited on the site for one theory post about
the scene chaos immediately after the shot. It is recorded as an unverified X theory.

## Claims from X posts

- In an [October 2025 post](https://x.com/Restrictd_Truth/status/1978097973415289253), theorized
  that **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** — the man who shouted "I shot him — now
  shoot me!" — was performing a **shouted diversion to aid the shooter's escape**. The site logs
  this on [UVU Social Media Response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response) and in the
  [Miscellaneous Evidence Leads](/Other/Miscellaneous_Evidence_Leads) table as **unverified**.

## Investigative value

The Zinn-diversion framing is one of several competing readings of the immediate post-shot
confusion, in which Zinn was detained and later cleared of the shooting itself. The site records
the theory with attribution; it asserts no coordination by Zinn or anyone else.
