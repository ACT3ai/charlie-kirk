# Tony Seruga — X Posts

[@TonySeruga](https://x.com/TonySeruga) (**Tony Seruga**) is a self-described data and intelligence analyst whose "raw GPS data" claims are the **strongest foreign-presence data points in open-source threads** — and among the least verifiable, since no dataset, methodology, or provenance has been published. Everything below is **his reported assertion**, rated **SPECULATIVE** by this site. He has a full profile at [Tony Seruga (People)](/People/tony-seruga).

## Claims from X posts

- **44 foreign-registered cellphones** were allegedly in Charlie Kirk's immediate area at the shot: **16 Israeli** (some posts say 12), **13 Chinese**, **12 Russian**, with Iranian devices also named ([Foreign Phones at UVU](/intelligence/Foreign_Phones_UVU), [Israelis At UVU](/CoverUp/Israelis_At_UVU)).
- **April 2026** — **Four** of the 16 Israeli-registered phones were allegedly later traced to the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** in Pakistan during high-stakes US–Iran talks ([Islamabad Serena Link](/intelligence/Islamabad_Serena_Link)).
- The **NSA Utah Data Center ("Bumblehive")**, 40 minutes from UVU, allegedly has "cross-pollination" algorithms that can link a **burner phone to its real owner after roughly 30–60 days** of use — and, per the claim, the government has not used that capability to identify the phones ([NSA Bumblehive Cellphones](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive), [NSA Bumblehive phones](/Suspicious/US_Intel/nsa-bumblehive-phones)).
- The accompanying phone-map image was labeled by Seruga himself as a **"recreation and NOT real"** — a caveat this site keeps attached to every version of the claim.

## Investigative value

The phone claims were amplified by [Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) and drive the [Israeli phones](/Suspicious/Israel/israeli-phones-at-uvu) and [NSA](/US_Intelligence/nsa) lanes — yet counts conflict across posts (11 / 12 / 16) and **no raw data, carrier record, or court filing** has been produced for any version. Proximity of phones, even if real, would prove presence, not participation. No phone owner is accused of anything by this site.
