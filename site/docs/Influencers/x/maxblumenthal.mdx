# Max Blumenthal — X Posts

[@MaxBlumenthal](https://x.com/MaxBlumenthal) (**Max Blumenthal**, editor of The Grayzone) is a
journalist whose reporting-style allegations about TPUSA's post-assassination direction are
documented on this site. Items below are **his journalistic allegations and framings**, not site
findings. Nothing here asserts that Erika Kirk or anyone at TPUSA planned, knew of, or
participated in the killing.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 2026 — distribution-control claim** — Posted a high-engagement claim that **about one
  week after Kirk's killing, a registered foreign agent of Israel was given control over his
  show's distribution**, and that Erika Kirk and TPUSA thereafter brought the organization "back
  into line with the Israeli agenda," including "deliberately burying his opposition to war on
  Iran." The site notes this should be checked against **FARA registrations and distribution
  contracts** ([Control Over TPUSA](/Israel_Main_Suspect/control-over-tpusa)).
- **The Grayzone framing (September 2025)** — With @anyaparampil, coverage summarized in a widely
  shared post: "Charlie Kirk rejected Netanyahu's bid to flood TPUSA with Zionist money, warned
  Trump against bombing Iran, and started calling out Israel's stranglehold on DC — then he was
  assassinated" — presented as their reported framing, bundling three separate claims
  ([Iran Funding Rejection Claims](/Iran/funding-rejection-claims)).

## Investigative value

Blumenthal supplies the **journalist-grade version** of the post-death organizational-capture
theory — *cui bono* arguments about what happened to TPUSA's direction after September 10, which
motive theorists cite as circumstantial context rather than proof of prior conspiracy. His claims
are among the most checkable in this lane because they point at registries and contracts.
