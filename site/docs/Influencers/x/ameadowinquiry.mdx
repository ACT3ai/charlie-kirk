# AMeadowInquiry — X Posts

[@AMeadowInquiry](https://x.com/AMeadowInquiry) is a citizen investigator working the **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) lane** — visual-comparison claims connecting people near the case to the Army intelligence base in Arizona. Both of the account's signature claims are **unverified visual comparisons**; the living persons named have not been accused of any offense, and this site does not assert either identification.

## Claims from X posts

- **Jan 15, 2026** — Reshared a video **Cabot Phillips** had posted and argued the **carpet, dark base trim, and yellow wall** he walks past match Fort Huachuca interiors: "We know Fort Huachuca has the same carpet on its base that Cabot is seen walking on in his Alibi video. What is his video supposed to prove?" ([Post](https://x.com/AMeadowInquiry/status/2011947043674034660)) The site's caveat: government-contract finishes repeat across enormous numbers of federal buildings, and no metadata or geolocation has been published. See [Cabot alibi and Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi) and the [photo cluster](/Photos/CIA_Intelligence/US_Intelligence/US_Intelligence_Cabot_alibi/overview).
- **July 13, 2026** — Posted that the man many call "**[Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown)**" — reportedly responsible for the microphone and positioned directly in front of Kirk at UVU — "appears to be the same individual photographed at Fort Huachuca with **[Jeb Jacobi](/People/jeb-jacobi)**." ([Post](https://x.com/AMeadowInquiry/status/2076765947151393053)) The referenced image is a Fort Huachuca MWR car-dealership promo photo open to the public; the comparison is logged as unresolved.
- The same post flagged **two large identical rectangular objects** in the man's pant pockets, "too large to be cell phones" — the poster's interpretation; ordinary explanations (battery packs, belt-pack transmitters, radios) are recorded alongside it.

## Investigative value

Both claims feed the [Fort Huachuca Sept 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) thread — the reported gathering of lieutenant colonels at the base 48 hours before the shooting. Visual matches are the weakest class of evidence the site tracks; they are preserved because they circulated widely and because they generate checkable questions (metadata, building numbers, personnel records) that disclosure could resolve.
