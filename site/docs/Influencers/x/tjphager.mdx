# TJPHager — X Posts

[@TJPHager](https://x.com/TJPHager) is the most-cited **skeptical voice** inside the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) aircraft debate — by his own account he set out to refute [John Cullen](/Influencers/john-cullen)'s drone-deployment claim, and his airframe-configuration arguments are quoted on nearly every Planes page as the counterweight to the surveillance theories. The bullets below are **claims attributed to his X posts**.

## Claims from X posts

- N1098L **was not built out to launch drones** — the airframe carried only **interior equipment with no visible exterior modifications** (radomes, antennas, sensor blisters) matching the full [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) **ME-11B** contracted specification a true drone-launch/ISR collection platform would show ([N1098L overview](/Planes/N1098L/overview), [ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations)).
- Cited an **alternate crew roster** ([Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown), Michael Reynolds, Sarah Kline) against the air-launched-drone reading — no FOIA release has settled the manifest or mission orders ([N1098L Air-Launched Drones](/Drones/N1098L_Air_Launched_Drones)).
- Disputes the low-pass surveillance reading of the September 10 Orem maneuvers, consistent with the published **drone-retrieval training** explanation ([N1098L HADES low passes](/Suspicious/US_Intel/n1098l-hades-low-passes), [low-pass timing](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/n1098l-low-pass-timing)).
- Cited alongside @BasedSamParker on the narrower **[N2100L](/Planes/N2100L)** question — the reported September 11 St. George pass overlapping Tyler Robinson's surrender window — framed as an open timing question, not a finding.

## Investigative value

The site presents the aircraft lane **even-handedly** because of accounts like this one: @TJPHager's configuration argument is the principal reason the aggressive drone-launch theory is not presented as settled. His photo threads also anchor several [Aircraft photo clusters](/Photos/Aircraft/Drop_Off/overview).
