# Turning Point USA (@TPUSA) — X Account

[@TPUSA](https://x.com/TPUSA) is the **official X account of Turning Point USA**, the organization [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) founded. Unlike the citizen-investigator accounts in this section, @TPUSA appears in the coverage mostly as the **tagged subject** of public questions, not as a poster of investigation claims. Nothing on this page asserts wrongdoing by TPUSA or any of its staff; the full organizational record is at [TPUSA overview](/TPUSA/overview).

## How the account appears in the coverage

- **Tagged in public questions** — e.g. [@FaithDrivUr](https://x.com/FaithDrivUr/status/2078917940993311136) (July 19, 2026) tagged @TPUSA, @AndrewKolvet, and @RealCandaceO asking which TPUSA employee was reportedly in contact with the accused hours before the shooting ([TPUSA–Tyler Robinson chat claim](/TPUSA/tpusa-tyler-robinson-chat)) — an unverified question, not a finding.
- **In bios as a credential** — accounts such as @ArtifexMemor list prior work with @tpusa and @charliekirk11, context that shaped how their resurfaced screenshots (the [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec) / [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) Fort Huachuca banter exchange) were received.
- **In profile screenshots** — the "Frmr @TPUSA Director" line in Jared Fleming's profile is the identification card for a Google Trends dispute in which Fleming publicly rejected the framing ([Google Search photo cluster](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_5d5539)).
- **In suppression allegations** — Candace Owens is cited claiming "@TPUSA tried to hide" Charlie's private messages about leaving the pro-Israel cause ([coverup-foreign-involvement](/Israel_Main_Suspect/coverup-foreign-involvement)) — an **attributed allegation** that TPUSA disputes; TPUSA reportedly confirmed the screenshots' authenticity while framing them as private venting.

## Investigative value

Tracking the official account matters because organizational statements (and silences) are part of the public record around footage handling, drone admissions, and messaging disputes documented elsewhere. All contested claims about the organization live on their own pages with TPUSA's rebuttals attached; no court has found that TPUSA or any named staff member committed any crime.
