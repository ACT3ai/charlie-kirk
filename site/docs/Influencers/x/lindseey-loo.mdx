# lindseey_loo — X Posts

[@lindseey_loo](https://x.com/lindseey_loo) is the origin point of the **"signed off before
finished" autopsy allegation** and a contributor to the device-model debate in the mic theories.
Everything below is **secondhand or attributed claim material** — uncorroborated hearsay in the
site's own rating — and no living person is accused of wrongdoing here.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 3–4, 2026** — Relayed a **personal DM from a longtime acquaintance** alleging that a
  "**new, young, blonde**" woman the medical examiner "had never met before" **"signed off"** on
  Charlie Kirk's autopsy report before the examiner finished — with the reasoning that "a report
  is a draft until it is signed," so changes could be made in that window. The site treats this
  as **uncorroborated hearsay passed through two layers**
  ([Mystery Woman Who Signed Off](/People/mystery-woman-signed-off),
  [Autopsy Sealed](/CoverUp/Autopsy_Sealed_Examiner)).
- The poster **speculated, without confirming**, whether the woman was
  [Dr. Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro) — speculation the site does not adopt, noting the
  "unqualified" description is inconsistent with Amaro's board certification
  ([Medical Examiner & Surgeons](/Medical/Autopsy/medical_examiner_surgeons)).
- Weighed against it: DPS agent **David Hull** testified (~July 6–7, 2026) that the Utah OME
  conducted the autopsy, ruled homicide by gunshot wound to the neck, and recovered a bullet
  fragment ([Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner)).
- **Sennheiser G4 variant (June 2026)** — Added an alternate hardware candidate to the mic-device
  debate: a **Sennheiser G4 + PETN** model (a receive-capable transmitter that could receive a
  detonation signal, versus a RØDE that only sends audio)
  ([Lapel Mic and Audio Theories](/Medical/lapel_mic_and_audio_theories),
  [Mic vs Pendant](/Mic/Mic_vs_Pendant)).

## Investigative value

The signed-off allegation is a case study in how the site handles **hearsay**: recorded because
it spread widely and shaped the autopsy-transparency debate, but rated unverified and weighed
against sworn testimony. The Sennheiser thread keeps the device-model question — RØDE PRO, ME,
GO II, or Sennheiser — visibly **unsettled** in the mic sections.
