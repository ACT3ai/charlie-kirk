# Blake Neff (BlakeSNeff) — X Posts

[@BlakeSNeff](https://x.com/BlakeSNeff) is the X account of **Blake Neff**, a TPUSA-adjacent
media figure who has his own [People profile](/People/blake-neff) on this site. In the July 2026
preliminary hearing cycle his posts served as one of the **hearing-coverage summaries** the site
cites for what prosecutors presented. His summaries are attributed reporting of state-side
evidence, not independent verification of it.

## Claims from X posts

- Summarized **Agent [David Hull](/People/david-hull)** testimony describing a longer **text
  thread with roommate [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)** in which the account attributed to
  Robinson allegedly admitted the shooting and said the rifle was left wrapped in a towel in a
  bush — cited on the [September 10 timeline](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), where authenticity and
  authorship remain contested by researchers who date custody earlier.
- His hearing clips (with @JLRINVESTIGATES) carried the testimony placing a **gray Dodge
  Challenger** near the gun-recovery neighborhood around **12:30–12:38 AM Sept 11**, with an
  officer reportedly logging the plate to Robinson — against a homeowner report of a bald driver
  and three passengers.
- Described (with @Liz_Wheeler and @JackPosobiec) the surveillance presentation of the charged
  defendant's **multi-loop campus scouting** and Chick-fil-A stop the morning of September 10 —
  cited on [pre-shot campus movements](/Timeline/tyler-robinson-pre-shot-campus).
- Separately, citizen scheduling analysis notes Neff himself was among named TPUSA travelers
  landing SLC ~9:23 AM that day — a scheduling observation on the same page, not an accusation.

## Investigative value

Neff's posts are among the closest things to a public record of **what the state showed in
court** during hearing days without full transcripts. The site uses them as hearing-coverage
attribution while flagging that they present the prosecution framing; defense-side and citizen
counter-readings are preserved alongside on every page that cites him.
