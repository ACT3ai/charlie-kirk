# Matt Tardio — X Posts

[@angertab](https://x.com/angertab) (**Matt Tardio**) is a veteran/analyst voice cited on both of the site's central mechanism disputes — the .30-06 exit-wound argument and the exploding-mic physics fight. His posts are **attributed technical commentary**, not sworn testimony or lab findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **.30-06 exit behavior** — Ammunition commentary from the July 2026 hearing week (amplified by @IkeWingate) arguing that a .30-06 **can fail to leave an exit wound** under the facts presented — a counter to the "no exit means no rifle" argument. Recorded on [.30-06 Did Not Kill Charlie](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/thirty-06-did-not-kill-charlie), where the soft-point debate is laid out with both camps.
- **Mic-theory physics critique** — Argued the potential charge diameter is **limited by the mic's width**, that suboptimal standoff to the neck would **dissipate a shaped jet**, and that any real PETN detonation would have been **visually obvious** on the footage. Cited alongside [@alexboge](./alexboge) on [the RØDE mic page](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/rode-mic).

## Investigative value

Tardio occupies an unusual position: his ammunition commentary **supports** the state's rifle narrative on the exit-wound question while his physics critique **undercuts** the leading alternative mechanism. That cross-cutting profile makes him a useful calibration point — the site cites him where technical experience, not team loyalty, drives the argument.
