# Villgecrazylady (Mel) — X Posts

[@Villgecrazylady](https://x.com/Villgecrazylady) (posting as **Mel**) built some of the most reused
**citizen timelines** in the case — her morning camera-chain map is the reference sequence many later
posts simply repeat. Her items are attributed timeline claims, not court findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **October 15, 2025** — Published the widely shared **morning map** of the suspect camera chain
  (Ring → WYZE → UVU → outfit change → Ring return), the sequence that kept citizen timelines
  synchronized with the affidavit narrative — see [Tyler on Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras)
  and [WYZE](/cameras/WYZE).
- **December 17, 2025** — Citing Washington Post reporting, noted alleged **Wordle chat activity at
  ~11:28 a.m.**, about sixteen minutes before the first post-outfit-change Ring sighting at
  **11:44 a.m.** — a soft timing seam in the "continuous person" story, per
  [Tyler as Placeholder](/cameras/Tyler_Placeholder).
- **December 18, 2025** — Restated the **screwdriver discrepancy**: first associated with the woods
  near the rifle, later placed **on the roof** by [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) on Fox News, and
  reportedly absent from charging documents — see
  [Officer Bagley Bodycam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam).
- **December 2025** — Timeline collage placing an after-Zoom **"they are going to kill me"** text to
  [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), clarifying Kolvet was not on the Zoom itself — see
  [the fear-text page](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text); also circulated the disputed
  **Frank Turek** "they want me dead" variant.
- **October 29, 2025** — Shared the Daily Mail article on [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) shutting
  down the foreign-intelligence probe, commenting that some stones would stay unturned — a reader
  reaction preserved at [the FBI photo cluster](/Photos/FBI/Img_Photo_a45434).
- Fear-text and donor-loss posts cited on [TPUSA Founder Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA-Founder-Charlie) as
  evidence the founder was under personal political threat.

## Investigative value

Mel's map of the morning chain remains **the common reference point** for the camera timeline — most
subsequent posts reuse her sequence rather than re-analyzing raw files. Her later timing seams
(Wordle vs Ring) and record-gap notes (screwdriver, charging documents) define several of the
disclosure targets in the [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview).
