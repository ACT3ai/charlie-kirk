# Partisangirl (Maram Susli) — X Posts

[@Partisangirl](https://x.com/Partisangirl) (**Dr. Maram Susli**, "Syrian Girl") is a
high-follower geopolitics account whose posts supplied several load-bearing clips and document
questions to the investigation record — including the most-cited Tucker Carlson quote on the site's
home page. All items are attributed claims from her X posts.

## Claims from X posts

- **June 20, 2026** — Circulated the video in which **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)**
  says: *"All of us who knew Charlie Kirk, believe he was murdered because his views on Israel were
  changing."* Carlson's attributed opinion, not a finding.
  ([Source](https://x.com/Partisangirl/status/2068463186668605858)) Featured on the
  [home page](/), [Tucker Carlson — Israel](/Israel/Tucker_Carlson_Israel), and
  [Tucker Carlson coverage](../podcasts-tucker-carlson).
- **September 2025** — Among the accounts compiling the reported **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"**
  texts allegedly sent during Charlie's combative September 9 meeting.
  ([Source](https://x.com/Partisangirl/status/1965873642547589214)) See
  [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) and
  [Political Pressure and Meetings 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).
- Raised the **arrest-time discrepancy**: two official records reportedly give arrest times **six
  hours apart**, with the earlier one predating the affidavit's own arrival time — rated
  **MODERATE** by the site. See
  [Arrest Time Discrepancy](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/arrest-time-discrepancy) and
  [Missing Surrender Footage](/Suspicious/Law_Enforcement/missing-surrender-footage).
- Recirculated alleged Kirk positions on Iran — that war with Iran would be worse than Iranian
  nukes, and that Iranian nukes threaten Israel more than America — in the
  [Iran War — Charlie vs Israel](/Israel_Main_Suspect/iran-war-charlie-vs-israel) motive thread.
