# The Post Millennial — X Posts

[@TPostMillennial](https://x.com/TPostMillennial) (**The Post Millennial**) is a news outlet whose court-window posts are cited repeatedly in this site's hearing and FBI pages. It is media coverage rather than citizen theorizing; the bullets below summarize **what the outlet reported**, as attributed on the referencing pages.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 2026 hearing** — Reported (with @SarahisCensored) that enhanced **rooftop/surveillance video was shown to the Kirk family in court but not broadcast**, with **Judge Tony Graf** limiting enhanced playback ([FBI Gag Orders](/FBI/Gag_Orders), [FBI Investigation](/FBI/FBI_Investigation)).
- **July 6, 2026** — Coverage of former UVU officer **[Chris Bagley](/People/chris-bagley)** describing a **red-and-black screwdriver** found on the rooftop, with a photo entered into evidence, and body-cam footage that reportedly **ended while he was still on the roof** ([Officer Bagley bodycam](/Law_Enforcement/officer-bagley-bodycam), [court testimony](/court/testimony), [hearing witnesses](/court/hearing-witnesses)).
- **April 17, 2026** — Court-video summary of defense counsel arguing to delay the preliminary hearing because **DNA data files from the FBI and ATF had not been produced** ([Timeline 2026](/Timeline/2026)).
- Hearing coverage (with [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec)) that prosecutors **played surveillance of a prone rooftop position and shot sequence** — with dueling summaries about whether any clip shows the shot itself versus only approach and flight ([roof-jumping timeline](/Timeline/roof-jumping-timeline)).

## Investigative value

The outlet's clips are among the few **contemporaneous video summaries** of hearing sessions the public could not fully watch, which is why both prosecution-leaning and skeptic pages on this site cite them. Coverage claims still need checking against official transcripts when released.
