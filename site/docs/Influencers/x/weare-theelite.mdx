# WeAre_TheElite — X Posts

[@WeAre_TheElite](https://x.com/WeAre_TheElite) is the source of the **raw flight-log screenshots**
that expanded the "two Israel/Egyptian planes" story into the **five-plane armada** thread. The
watermarked log tables — amplified by [@ProjectConstitu](./project-constitution) and
[Sam Parker](./based-sam-parker) — establish presence and routing only; no passenger manifest or
purpose has been publicly established for any leg.

## Claims from X posts

- Published flight-history tables for **[SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU)**, **[SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV)**, and
  **[SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM)** — expanding the Candace Owens-era SU-BTT / SU-BND story into a
  five-tail fleet with repeated Cairo–Paris–Minot/Omaha–Provo–Wilmington circuits across
  February–October 2025.
- **SU-BGM legs**: an April 10, 2025 **Provo local flight** (~55 minutes) and an April 16
  **Goose Bay → Paris → Provo** arrival — characterized in the thread as a "spring swarm during
  Kirk's Midwest push."
- **SU-BTV legs**: Provo activity March 2, July 2, and October 2, 2025 (the last speculatively
  labeled "post-assassination cleanup?" — rhetorical speculation, not evidence), plus a Cairo local
  flight October 29.
- The screenshot tables, with relevant legs highlighted and the **@WeAre_TheElite watermark**, are
  preserved in the [5 Planes photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/5_Planes/overview) and cited on the
  [other-planes activity timeline](/Timeline/other-planes-activity-timeline).
- Skeptic counterpoints recorded alongside: John Cullen's Provo **maintenance-contract** history and
  Liz Wheeler's argument that loose date/region criteria inflate the overlap counts.

## Investigative value

This account supplied the **primary-source layer** of the armada thread — raw tracking-site tables
rather than commentary. The site preserves the screenshots precisely because logs are checkable:
each leg can be confirmed or refuted against ADS-B archives, which is more than most theory lanes
can offer. What the logs cannot do is say who flew or why.
