# Iwendtster — X Posts

[@Iwendtster](https://x.com/Iwendtster) is a citizen-investigator account cited in the
photo-analysis and hospital-transport threads. Its material circulates mostly through
amplification by larger accounts; everything below is an attributed claim or question, not a
finding of coordination or wrongdoing by anyone named.

## Claims from X posts

- **Last-photo glance analysis (July 2026)** — produced the analysis of the **last photo** of
  Charlie Kirk that [Based Sam Parker](./based-sam-parker) amplified, asking about the timing of
  glances between **Mikey McCoy** and **Dan Flood** seconds before the shot — framed as
  probability/oddity questions, explicitly **not** proven coordination between living persons.
- **SUV-ride commentary** — posts quoting @Iwendtster circulate hostile **questions** about the SUV
  ride to the hospital — routing and care decisions — recorded on
  [Hospital Footage Seizure](/FBI/Hospital_Footage_Seizure) with the site's caveat that no court
  has found any EMS, security, or hospital staff intentionally denied care.

## Investigative value

The account contributes **frame-level scrutiny** of the final seconds and the evacuation — the kind
of observational material that only becomes evidence if primary video, seizure inventories, and
witness statements are released. Until then its questions stay open questions.
