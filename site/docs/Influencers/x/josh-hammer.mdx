# Josh Hammer — X Posts

[@josh_hammer](https://x.com/josh_hammer) (**Josh Hammer**, Status: Alive) is a conservative
commentator and colleague of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) whose own X posts — rather than
investigative threads — became discussion objects after September 10, 2025. **Nothing on this page
suggests that Josh Hammer had foreknowledge of any attack, and no such claim is made.** He has not
been accused of any offence by any authority. Full profile: [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer).

## Posts that circulated in investigation commentary

- A screenshot documents two posts from the verified account: the single word **"based"** quoting a
  2013 Donald Trump post reading "Should be public execution for all to see" (timestamped 18:05 on
  **9/9/25**), and the words **"Public execution."** (timestamped 15:57 on **9/10/25** — the day of
  the shooting). Researchers pointed to the phrasing and proximity; the site records this as a
  **juxtaposition of timestamps and nothing more** — the quoted 2013 post concerned an unrelated
  subject ([the screenshot page](/Photos/TPUSA/Img_Photo_9dc5d4)).
- Commentary attributed to other posters explains the "public execution" post as referring to the
  **Iryna Zarutska murder** — a widely reported unrelated case that week.
- **Dave Smith** (@ComicDaveSmith) publicly questioned Hammer's and the Daily Mail's use of the word
  **"neutralized"** in reference to Tucker Carlson — a criticism of rhetoric, not an allegation of
  anything ([the exchange screenshot](/Photos/Israel/Img_Tucker_Neutralize_b76210)).
- Separate attributed claims about Hammer's role in Kirk's final weeks (Zoom calls, Israel-pressure
  disputes) come from **other accounts'** posts, chiefly [@BasedSamParker](./based-sam-parker), and
  are recorded on that page and [Donors, Israel and Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy).

## Why this page exists

Hammer's timeline became evidence-adjacent because researchers screenshot and debate it — this page
documents **what circulated**, with the site's clear statement that timestamp coincidences prove
nothing and that no authority has accused Hammer of any wrongdoing.
