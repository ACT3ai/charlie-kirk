# leahfiles — X Posts

[@leahfiles](https://x.com/leahfiles) (also referenced as **@theleahfiles**) is a researcher the
site describes as having an intelligence background, best known for a **TPUSA financial-filings
series** and for domain and aircraft-ownership threads. Every item below is an **attributed
claim** from their posts — filings analysis and OSINT, not verdicts. No person named is accused
of a crime by this site.

## Claims from X posts

- **TPUSA financial series, Part 3 (July 20, 2026)** — Focused on **Stacy Sheridan**, described
  as Senior Advancement Director and owner of successive outside fundraising entities (GSM
  Strategy → Lionrock → Cloverstone) that took custody of tens of millions in donations, framing
  Schedule L / private-inurement questions from public filings while stating **no public
  government charge** exists ([TPUSA organizational timeline](/Timeline/tpusa-organizational-timeline),
  [insider payments and 990s](/Suspicious/TPUSA_Peripheral/insider-payments-990s)).
- **Insider-payment compilation** — Roughly ten years of alleged flows, including $9M+ to a
  direct-mail group, $999K to Clocktower LLC for a "research project," and $2.7M+ routed through
  shell entities — contested financial allegations, not findings.
- **vancekirk2028.com domain chain** — Traced the March 8, 2026 site launch back to April 2025
  Arizona searches for "Erika Kirk Vice President," an alleged June 2025 Israeli-IP search for the
  domain string, and a **September 12, 2025 domain purchase** — two days after the shooting. The
  site notes domain and search records are not evidence of intent ([2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026)).
- **N59906 ownership thread** — Among the accounts amplifying the claim that the MARC Inc. plane
  circling UVU ties to a company where **Alexis Wilkins** is registered agent — a claim disputed
  in the same threads, with rebuttals stating no public evidence connects the LLCs to the flight
  ([N59906](/Planes/N59906)).
- **Dual-mechanism view (July 10, 2026)** — Stated a personal belief that an explosive (citing the
  AES contract and plant blast) **and** a simultaneous shot could both be true, asserting it was
  "not Robinson" ([AES factory explosion](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/aes-factory-explosion)).
  Robinson is charged, not convicted; dual cause-of-death is an online hypothesis.

## Investigative value

@leahfiles is one of the few accounts working the **financial-records lane** — 990s, LLC
registries, and domain histories — rather than video analysis. Those documents are checkable,
which makes the account's threads a recurring source for the TPUSA money pages even where the
inferences drawn remain contested.
