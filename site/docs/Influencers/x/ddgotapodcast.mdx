# DDGotAPodcast — X Posts

[@DDGotAPodcast](https://x.com/DDGotAPodcast) is a podcast-host account that amplified one of the
stranger witness-side allegations in the record: a UVU student's account of a **"free trauma
counseling" session** that allegedly turned into an intimidation encounter. The account's framing is
that commentator's interpretation, not a proven conclusion.

## Claims from X posts

- Amplified the account of a **UVU student** who says he was about **20 feet away** when Kirk was
  shot and accepted a "free trauma counseling" session **advertised exclusively to witnesses**. Per
  the reported account, the session ran roughly **two hours with no real therapy**, the door was
  **locked from the outside**, and he was told *"You're the next target. You went on national TV,
  they're coming for you."* No police report, therapist name, or agency name appears in the record.
  Full treatment: [Therapy Intimidation](/CoverUp/Therapy_Intimidation).
- Quoted framing the episode as possible **"intimidation theater"** — an operation allegedly staged
  to make people believe there were real civilian witnesses and to scare genuine witnesses into
  silence. The site does **not** endorse the "psyop" framing; it records the attribution. See
  [UVU student reactions](/UVU/UVU-Student-Reactions), where every alleged detail is marked
  **unverified**.

## Investigative value

The single documented amplifier of the witness-intimidation allegation — a claim that, if a
verifiable program name, therapist, or police report ever surfaces, would move from unverified
student account to checkable record. Until then it stays in the attributed-claims column.
