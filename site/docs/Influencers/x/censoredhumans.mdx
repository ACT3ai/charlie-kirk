# CensoredHumans — X Posts

[@CensoredHumans](https://x.com/CensoredHumans) is one of the aggregator accounts that amplified the
**Israeli-phones-at-UVU** allegation through late 2025 and 2026. The core claim originates with
**[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)**; @CensoredHumans is documented on this site as a repeater and amplifier. All items
below are attributed claims, not verified records.

## Claims from X posts

- Posted clips repeating the core line that **12 Israeli-purchased / Israeli-registered phones**
  were on the UVU campus on September 10, that "all the devices were registered in Israel," with
  **NSA** involvement and administration sources described as "freaked out" — alongside
  **@ShadowofEzra** and **@Ryanmatta**. Documented on
  [Israelis present at UVU](/Israel_Main_Suspect/israelis-present-at-uvu).
- Amplified summaries under headlines such as *"Israeli Cell Phones … Islamabad Serena Hotel"* and
  *"12 Israeli cell phones on UVU grounds"* — logged in the
  [Israelis at UVU cover-up page](/CoverUp/Israelis_At_UVU).
- The site notes the claim remains **single-stream / second-hand**: no documents, data dumps, or
  official confirmation have been produced, and court filings in the Robinson case have not
  introduced Israeli-phone exhibits. That absence is what FOIA efforts and the
  [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) target — but the claim is unverified.

## Investigative value

Primarily an **amplification node**: the account shows how the Owens phones claim propagated and how
numbers drift (11 vs 12 vs 16) in secondary retellings. Use the primary pages for the full
treatment, including benign explanations and deanonymization limits.
