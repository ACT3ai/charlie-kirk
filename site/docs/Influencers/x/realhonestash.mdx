# Honest Ash (realhonestash) — X Posts

[@realhonestash](https://x.com/realhonestash) (**Honest Ash**) is one of the main amplifiers of
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)'s segments and of resurfaced scene video. The site cites
this account for reposted and captioned clips; the claims are attributed to the posts and the
underlying segments, not asserted as findings.

## Claims from X posts

- A [June 22, 2026 repost](https://x.com/realhonestash/status/2069185145555255370) of an Owens
  segment reviewing **on-camera witness statements describing a chest wound** and the
  shaped-charge question — the source for [Wrong Witnesses](/Witnesses/wrong_witnesses),
  [Chest Shot Witnesses](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses), and
  [Chest vs Neck Accounts](/Suspicious/Medical/chest-versus-neck-accounts).
- The same segment relays the attributed claim that a hospital executive's **SD card was
  removed** — logged with cautions on [SD Card Removed](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/sd-card-removed).
- A [resurfaced video](https://x.com/realhonestash/status/2075740274379112646) of a **man in all
  black running from the UVU scene**, amplified with the caption "It just keeps getting
  better…" — see [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) and
  [All-Black Construction Worker](/Suspicious/UVU_9_10/all-black-construction-worker) (with
  Dylan Hope's reported interview).
- Amplification of the **shirt-pull-at-the-shot** observation alongside @DiligentDenizen and Jon
  Bray ([Shirt Pull at the Shot](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/shirt-pull-at-the-shot)).
- Commentary threads (with @DiligentDenizen) interpreting **SUV interior debris photos** as
  consistent with RODE-transmitter fragment hypotheses —
  [Mic Explosive](/CoverUp/Mic_Explosive) and the [Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll) page.

## Investigative value

Honest Ash functions as a **clip librarian for the exploding-mic and second-actor lanes**: the
account's reposts are the site's working links to Owens segments and witness footage. The site
presents this material as reported by @realhonestash rather than asserted, and none of it
establishes that any named person committed a crime.
