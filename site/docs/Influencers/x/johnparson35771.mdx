# JohnParson35771 — X Posts

[@JohnParson35771](https://x.com/JohnParson35771) is a citizen-investigator account cited in the
site's **event-security failure** record for the drone-denial claim. The items below are attributed
public claims about staffing and decisions — they do not establish that any UVU officer
intentionally enabled a killing.

## Claims from X posts

- **July 9, 2026** — Stated that Charlie's security team **requested drone surveillance** for the
  UVU event and that **UVU Campus Police Chief Jeff Long denied** the request, adding "I want to
  hear Long testify" ([UVU Campus Police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police)).
- Cited (with **@BDPelton**) in the under-staffed elevated-security picture: **six UVU PD
  officers**, a private detail of ~**12**, and **rooftops unsecured** at the speaking site
  ([Public Speaking Event at UVU](/Locations/Public-Speaking-Event-UVU)).
- The drone-denial claim is why citizen investigators treat **rooftop jurisdiction** and aerial
  coverage as first-order law-enforcement questions rather than private-security-only failures.

## Investigative value

The account supplies one of the sharpest single claims in the security-gap record: that elevated
surveillance was **requested and refused**. If borne out in testimony or records, it would move the
rooftop question from hindsight critique to a documented pre-event decision. See
[Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [Drones](/Drones/overview) for the wider picture.
