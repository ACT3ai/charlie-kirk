# misfitpatriot_ — X Posts

[@misfitpatriot_](https://x.com/misfitpatriot_) is one of the **counter-voices** in the ballistics
debate — an account that pushes back on skeptic overclaims rather than amplifying them. The site
records their arguments across the [Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Bullet_Investigation_Index)
cluster as attributed claims from X posts, alongside the skeptic positions they answer.

## Claims from X posts

- Argues that **soft-point .30-06 ammunition *should* fragment** on impact, and that the "bullet
  doesn't match the rifle" reading of the ATF's inconclusive finding is being **overclaimed** by
  skeptics. This counter appears across
  [ATF Fragment Inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive),
  [Mauser .30-06 Rifle](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle),
  [Rifle Fingerprints Mismatch](/Gun_Bullet/Rifle_Fingerprints_Mismatch), and
  [Thirty-06 Energy Mismatch](/Gun_Bullet/Thirty_06_Energy_Mismatch).
- Their position pairs with **@OfVoice35353**'s citation of hearing testimony that the recovered
  fragments measure roughly **.286–.301 inches** and fit the .30-caliber family — the
  official-narrative-leaning side of the fragment debate.
- **July 2026** — Posted video (amplified by @JustJenRX, July 8, 2026) claiming a person **can bend
  a leg with a rifle down the pant leg**, answering the "physically impossible" logistics challenge,
  and adding the claim that Robinson allegedly **spoke with TPUSA staff before the stage** —
  prosecution-leaning premeditation framing. Recorded on
  [More Reasons](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/more-reasons) with the counters it answers.

## Investigative value

The site's ballistics pages deliberately preserve **both sides** of every fragment, energy, and
logistics argument. This account supplies the strongest recurring rebuttals to the non-match and
impossible-carry claims, which keeps those pages from becoming one-sided. Nothing here is a
forensic finding; all of it is attributed commentary awaiting full public exhibits.
