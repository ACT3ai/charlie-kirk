# Bizarro_Videos — X Posts

[@Bizarro_Videos](https://x.com/Bizarro_Videos) posted some of the earliest **day-of frame
analysis** (September 10, 2025) in the wound-location debate, plus one of the case's most
cautionary examples of overreach: the "planted witness" allegation. Both are recorded on this
site as the poster's claims only.

## Claims from X posts

- Claimed frame-by-frame that *"the bullet appears to hit a bulletproof vest on his chest and
  ricochet up into the neck"* — part of the vest/ricochet strand of the
  [Shot in the Heart narrative catalog](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart).
  ([Source](https://x.com/Bizarro_Videos/status/1965892000185725129))
- Alleged an elderly man interviewed on Fox News saying Kirk was "shot in the heart" was
  **"planted"** — asking "Who told him to say that?" The site explicitly does **not** adopt this:
  no evidence has been produced for it, and an unidentified person on a live broadcast is a
  bystander. See the analysis on
  [the Narrative photo page](/Photos/Official_Narrative/Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart/Img_Photo_Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart_7).
  ([Source](https://x.com/Bizarro_Videos/status/1965913654840406034))

## Investigative value

The account matters as a record of **how fast the narrative moved** — from "what did witnesses
see" to "were the witnesses real" within hours of the shot. Its vest-ricochet reading sits inside
a larger cluster of impact analyses (@aducarrabh, @CheriJacobus, @themarketswork) that the site
catalogs together; its planted-witness claim is preserved as an example of the parts of the arc
that do not withstand scrutiny.
