# Jeannin55385803 — X Posts

[@Jeannin55385803](https://x.com/Jeannin55385803) is one of the X accounts that circulated
**screenshots and text extracts** claimed to come from [Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) private
messages in the weeks before September 10, 2025. The underlying message logs have **not been
authenticated** in publicly available court records — everything below is an alleged, attributed
claim, not a confirmed statement.

## Claims from X posts

- Circulated alleged donor-frustration texts, including the widely shared paraphrase that Charlie
  reportedly wrote **"Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes. I cannot and will not be bullied
  like this. Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause"**
  ([Charlie's Reported Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel)).
- Shared, alongside **@RealCandaceO**, **@Rahul_EIWF**, and **@JustTheTweets17**, screenshots
  alleging Charlie wrote in early September 2025 that he **"just lost another huge Jewish donor"**
  over refusing to cancel **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)**
  ([Donors, Israel and Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy)).
- These extracts are used by posters to argue Charlie was making a **significant break** from prior
  pro-Israel positions in private — a reading the site records as an allegation requiring primary
  documents.

## Investigative value

The account is part of the **donor-text screenshot ecosystem** the site's motive pages track. Its
value is documentary — showing which alleged quotes circulated, when, and through whom — rather
than evidentiary, since no underlying message log has been authenticated publicly.
