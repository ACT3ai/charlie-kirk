# GreatUnwokening — X Posts

[@GreatUnwokening](https://x.com/GreatUnwokening) is a citizen-investigator account cited for
packaging the major open disputes in the case into shareable threads. The bullets below are
**claims attributed to their X posts** — not confirmed findings, and not accusations adopted by
this site against any living person.

## Claims from X posts

- **Kent shutdown package (July 2026)** — bundled the reported block on **Joe Kent**'s foreign
  entanglements inquiry together with the courtyard paving, .30-06 energy doubts, mic-case
  fragments, and chain-of-custody disputes — explicitly leaving guilt to a jury. See
  [FBI Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads).
- **Camera-card removal allegation (July 2026)** — alleged that **Terryl Farnsworth** and
  **Andrew Kolvet** removed camera memory cards before police secured them. This is an
  **attributed allegation requiring contemporaneous video**, recorded on
  [FBI Witness Coordination](/FBI/Witness_Coordination) and cited as a transparency gap in the
  [Digital Evidence policy proposal](/Fix/Digital_Evidence). No court has found any such removal
  occurred or was improper.

## Investigative value

The account's threads are useful as an **index of unresolved questions** — they show which
disputes (foreign-lead shutdown, media chain of custody, ballistics) citizen researchers treat as
open. Corroboration for the media-handling claims means a named agent, date, channel, and preserved
original media hashes; until then, everything above stays attributed.
