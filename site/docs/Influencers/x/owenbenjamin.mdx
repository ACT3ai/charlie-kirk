# Owen Benjamin — X Posts

[@OwenBenjamin](https://x.com/OwenBenjamin) (**Owen Benjamin**, comedian) contributes two distinct
strands to the site's record: sober commentary on a public lab document, and viral satire about the
quality of released surveillance footage. Both are attributed commentary, not findings.

## Claims from X posts

- **April 18, 2026** — Commentary on the public forensic lab report: emphasized that casing
  comparisons support **Remington** branding and that **function-testing showed the recovered rifle
  can fire** — narrowing, in his reading, the "frangible-round" explanations for the absence of an
  exit wound. The site labels this **commentary on a public lab document**, not a court finding.
  See the [autopsy and forensic timeline](/Timeline/autopsy-forensic-timeline).
- **July 12, 2026** — Argued sarcastically that the **1967 Bigfoot film is clearer** than any public
  UVU sequence of the roof figure, and that the rooftop silhouette "could literally be a bird" — a
  pointed way of saying the public has been **denied identification-quality surveillance** despite a
  modern camera-saturated campus. Recorded on
  [Signs of Cover-Up](/Tyler_Robinson_Not_Assassin/signs-of-cover-up) alongside @TheTexasWiseGuy's
  Zapruder comparison.

## Investigative value

The Bigfoot line became shorthand for the footage-quality complaint that runs through the entire
Proof-Not-Tyler debate: every released frame of the roof figure is low-resolution while
higher-quality sources remain withheld. The lab-report commentary shows the same account engaging
the official record on its own terms.
