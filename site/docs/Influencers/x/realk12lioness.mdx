# RealK12Lioness — X Posts

[@RealK12Lioness](https://x.com/RealK12Lioness) is cited on the site for a single but widely
viewed contribution: an **annotated screenshot** archived in the
[Brown Shirt Puller](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brown_Shirt_Puller/overview) photo cluster. The
annotation is the poster's argument, not a finding.

## Claims from X posts

- An X post (about **15.1 thousand views, 177 replies** in the archived capture) showing a man in
  a plaid shirt being escorted across pavement, with two hand-drawn annotations: a **white circle
  around his upper torso** and a **red circle around his lower back and rear pocket area**, where
  a small bulge is visible.
- The implied claim is that the bulge is significant. The site's assessment is explicit: **"a
  rear-pocket bulge in a compressed screenshot is not evidence of anything specific."** The
  uncropped capture is archived at
  [Img_Photo_f569fe](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brian_Harpole/Img_Photo_f569fe) and the annotated
  copy at [Img_copy_f569fe](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brown_Shirt_Puller/Img_copy_f569fe).

## Investigative value

The annotation is part of the wider crowd-identification effort mapping who was near the table
area — useful as a record of what circulated, but the site does not adopt the implication, and no
person shown in the image is accused of anything.
