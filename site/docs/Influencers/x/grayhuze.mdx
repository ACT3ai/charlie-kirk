# Gray Hughes Investigates — X Posts

[@GrayHuze](https://x.com/GrayHuze) (**Gray Hughes Investigates**) is a citizen investigator whose
open-source surveillance-timing analysis of the September 10, 2025 UVU shooting scene is cited
across this site. He is referenced as an independent analyst, not a witness or participant, and his
reconstructions remain **attributed claims pending independent reproduction**.

## Claims from X posts

- **25-second egress reconstruction** — using timestamped surveillance stills (12:23:29, 12:23:34,
  12:23:45, 12:23:54), he mapped movement "from shooting position to North East corner of Losee
  Center building in 25 seconds" — the analysis anchoring
  [Tyler Robinson on 9/10/2025](/Tyler_Robinson/Tyler_9_10_2025).
  ([Primary thread](https://x.com/GrayHuze/status/2044131420956963292))
- **Manhunt-duration discrepancy (Apr 14, 2026)** — asked why officials claim a "33-hour manhunt"
  when other statements suggest 30: "So which is it? 30 hours… or 33?" — arithmetic citizen
  researchers argue is hard to square with a 6:25 PM September 11 custody time. See the
  [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026) and
  [custody vs Discord confession timeline](/Timeline/custody-vs-discord-confession-timeline).
- His stills are also cited **against** the anonymous "runner started before the shot" theory: the
  released material includes a 12:23:29 frame, which undercuts the claim that trimming concealed
  the run start — see [Runner Sprint Before Shot](/Suspicious/Five_Minutes/runner-sprint-before-shot).

## Investigative value

His posts are treated as **reproducible challenges**: if the Losee Center egress is ~25 seconds in
timestamped stills, the manhunt and "who was where" narratives must reconcile that window. Skeptics
note possible timestamp skew and frame-selection bias; best practice is to republish the stills with
source URLs and alternate sync methods.
