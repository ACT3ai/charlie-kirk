# BDPelton — X Posts

[@BDPelton](https://x.com/BDPelton) is a citizen commentator whose **July 2026 security-staffing
posts** are cited across the site's security-failure pages. The account's core contribution is
keeping the staffing arithmetic in public view: how few officers covered the event, and who — if
anyone — owned the rooftops. All items below are attributed public claims about coordination and
staffing, not findings that any named person intentionally enabled a killing.

## Claims from X posts

- Argued (reply thread, **July 10, 2026**) that despite known death threats, campus police
  fielded only **six UVU officers**, **rooftops were not secured**, and the hired firm
  **Integrity Solutions** (associated in posts with [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole))
  also failed to control elevated positions — cited on
  [UVU Campus Police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police).
- Restated the same staffing picture against the death-threat context on
  [Integrity Security](/Security_Team/integrity-security) and
  [No Counter-Snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) — six officers plus private staff,
  with the high ground left an obvious unowned zone
  ([Perimeter and Rooftop Failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures)).
- Pressed the question of whether Harpole **verified** the campus rooftop commitment after the
  reported "I got you covered" exchange with Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long) — recorded as
  commentary on the [Brian Harpole profile](/People/brian-harpole).
- Included among the accounts framing the event footprint — six UVU PD, ~12 private detail,
  rooftops unsecured, alleged drone denial — on
  [the UVU speaking event location page](/Locations/Public-Speaking-Event-UVU).

## Investigative value

The account adds little new primary evidence but performs a durable function: it keeps
**rooftop jurisdiction and staffing** framed as first-order law-enforcement questions rather
than private-security-only failures. Its numbers match the figures other researchers track,
making it a useful index of the consensus citizen-side staffing claims.
