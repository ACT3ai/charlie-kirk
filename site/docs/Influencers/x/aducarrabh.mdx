# aducarrabh — X Posts

[@aducarrabh](https://x.com/aducarrabh) posted one of the earliest high-engagement frame analyses of the shooting — on **September 10, 2025** itself, reaching roughly **238k views**. The claims below are **the poster's own frame reading**, which this site neither endorses nor rejects.

## Claims from X posts

- "Something **whizzes by** Charlie Kirk a split second before impact" — an observation about the footage that anyone can attempt to check against the clip. ([Primary post](https://x.com/aducarrabh/status/1965884012565766172))
- "The bullet also appears to have, indeed, **ricocheted off of his vest and through his neck**" — an interpretation of wound mechanics that cannot be established from video; whether body armor was worn has not been established, and no ballistic report supports a deflection.
- The site's write-up separates the two claims deliberately: the moving-object observation is checkable, the ricochet mechanism is not. See [the frame's evidence page](/Photos/Official_Narrative/Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart/Img_Photo_Narrative_Shot_in_the_Heart_8).

## Investigative value

This post sits in the site's catalog of day-one impact analyses on [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart), alongside @whipwesur's stiffening-on-impact angle and @CheriJacobus's vest close-up.
