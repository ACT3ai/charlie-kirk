# Andrew Kolvet — X Posts

[@AndrewKolvet](https://x.com/AndrewKolvet) (**Andrew Kolvet**) is the **TPUSA spokesman** — unlike most accounts in this section, he is an organizational insider whose public statements the site tracks as primary-source commentary. He has a full profile at [People — Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet). Status: Alive. He is not accused of any crime by this site; the items below are his **attributed public statements** and how citizen investigators use them.

## Claims from X posts

- **Donor-message confirmation** — Kolvet has been widely reported as confirming the authenticity of the leaked WhatsApp/group-chat frustration texts in which Kirk discussed losing a donor and leaving the pro-Israel cause — while other commentators stress those texts were private vents, not a public renunciation. See [pre-event political pressure](/Timeline/pre-event-political-pressure).
- **Succession statement** — Stated on Fox/NewsNation that Charlie had told him **Erika Kirk could lead TPUSA** — the statement at the center of the succession dispute documented at [Memorial and TPUSA Succession](/After/Memorial_And_TPUSA_Succession).
- **"They are going to kill me" recipient** — Viral timelines identify Kolvet as the "Andrew" who received Kirk's September 9 text after the emergency Zoom — an attributed claim about a message not produced in any public filing. See [the text page](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text). Kolvet has not been accused in any court of anything.
- **June 2026 contempt ruling** — Summarized Judge Graf's civil-contempt finding against prosecutor Christopher Ballard as having "no effect" on case trajectory and a practical "win for the prosecution." See [2026 timeline](/Timeline/2026).
- **Wound descriptions** — Kolvet-attributed wound characterizations are quoted by trajectory analysts (e.g. [@_DaniFesto](./danifesto)) in the [Wound Trajectory Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle) argument.
- Citizen threads also contrast Kolvet/TPUSA-aligned accounts of whether [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy) was on the phone with Erika Kirk against her "called me within seconds" Fox account — competing recollections only. See the [Erika Kirk timeline](/Timeline/erika-kirk-timeline).

## Investigative value

Kolvet sits at the junction of nearly every TPUSA-side dispute: text authenticity, succession, the call to Erika, and courtroom messaging. Because he is both a source and a subject of claims, the site quotes him precisely and attributes every characterization — his statements are evidence of what TPUSA said publicly, not adjudicated facts about September 10.
