# AF Post — X Posts

[@AFpost](https://x.com/AFpost) (**AF Post**) is a high-volume news-aggregation account whose screenshots are preserved in the site's photo evidence clusters. The items below are **claims contained in posts the site archived**, not verified facts.

## Claims from X posts

- A post alleging that Israeli Prime Minister [Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) offered [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) **$150 million in cash** to adopt a more pro-Israel stance, which Kirk allegedly refused — archived in the [Israel and Mossad photo cluster](/Photos/Israel/Img_Photo_6c6fa7). The claim circulates widely in the donor-pressure lane and has not been documented by any public filing.
- A post discussing **leaked text messages** allegedly exchanged between Charlie Kirk and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) concerning [Ben Shapiro](/People/ben-shapiro), with a collage of message bubbles — archived in the [Text Msgs photo cluster](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/Text_Msgs/Img_Photo_607284). Authenticity of the messages is not established.

## Investigative value

AF Post is an amplifier rather than an original researcher: its screenshots matter because they mark when high-reach aggregation accounts pushed the $150M-offer and leaked-texts claims into mass circulation. Both claims feed the motive threads at [Donors, Israel &amp; Foreign Policy](/Motive/Donors_Israel_ForeignPolicy) and should be checked against primary sources there.
