# Adam (bayonerd09) — X Posts

[@bayonerd09](https://x.com/bayonerd09) (**Adam**) is a data-checking account whose thread is
the site's principal **rebuttal exhibit** in the planes-following-Charlie-and-Erika debate. Where
most accounts in this section advance suspicion, this one argues the flight-tailing claims fail
against public flight-tracking records. Their analysis is preserved as an attributed
counter-argument, not as a settled finding.

## Claims from X posts

- Posted a data-analysis thread **disputing claims by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)**
  (@RealCandaceO) that Egyptian aircraft tailed [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk),
  [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) — archived as
  [Planes Following Charlie or Erika — Photo 2](/Photos/Aircraft/Planes_Following_Charlie_Erika/Img_Photo_b771ff).
- Presented **color-coded spreadsheet screenshots** (red, green, blue, yellow rows) arguing that
  the vast majority of the alleged tailing flights either **did not exist** or occurred when the
  aircraft in question was **on a different continent**.
- The thread anchors the counter-argument side of the
  [Planes Following Charlie or Erika photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/Planes_Following_Charlie_Erika/overview),
  which deliberately keeps the original Owens-side assertion and the data check side by side.

## Investigative value

This account demonstrates the site's both-sides standard on the aircraft question: the
[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) and overlap-count claims are serious enough to document, and so is the
strongest public attempt to falsify them. Readers comparing the two should work from primary
flight-tracking exports rather than either side's screenshots.
