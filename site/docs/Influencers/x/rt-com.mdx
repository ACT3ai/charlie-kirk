# RT (@RT_com) — X Posts

[@RT_com](https://x.com/RT_com) is the X account of **RT**, the Russian state-controlled media
outlet. Unlike the citizen investigators in this section, RT is a foreign state broadcaster; its
posts are tracked here because they amplified case-relevant material at scale. Readers should weigh
the source's institutional interests when evaluating anything it circulates.

## Claims from X posts

- Circulated video clips of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s Israel-critical remarks —
  including the attributed line *"I have got to be careful how I say this — they are going to
  ethnically cleanse Gaza"* — alongside accounts such as @hippyygoat, @5149jamesli, and
  @MarioNawfal. See [Charlie Kirk Quotes on Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel).
- These clips are used by commentators to argue Kirk was **increasingly uneasy about Israeli
  policy** in his final months — context for the motive theories collected under
  [Israel](/Israel/overview).
- In **February 2026**, high-engagement @RT_com posts (with @dom_lucre and others) described the
  backlash over the trailer for [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' **"Bride of Charlie"**
  series examining [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) and TPUSA succession — media framing, not a
  judicial finding.

## Investigative value

RT's involvement is itself a data point: a state outlet with an interest in U.S. discord chose to
amplify the Israel-quotes thread and the Owens-Erika conflict. The site uses its posts only as
evidence of **what circulated and when**, and verifies quote context against the primary videos
rather than the outlet's framing.
