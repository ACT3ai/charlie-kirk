# X (Twitter) Coverage

[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and Egyptian jets, drone sightings over UVU, ballistics and
crack-thump analysis, **UVU GRAMA** threads, and questions about FBI coordination. The pages below
compile **claims attributed to specific accounts** — not confirmed findings.

## How X Claim-Compilation Pages Fit the Hearing (Attributed)

The 277 X researcher pages under this index supply **primary-claim inventories** that podcast monologues often lag. July 2026 hearing coverage (Project Constitution, danksterintel, [Sam Parker](/Influencers/sam-parker) timeline work, Shadow of Ezra roommate-ID clips) should be folded back into each researcher's page when their specialty appears in court (rifle photos, Twiggs statements, 911 call inventories, GRAMA records).
