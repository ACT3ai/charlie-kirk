crack-thump analysis, **UVU GRAMA** threads, and questions about FBI coordination. The pages below
compile **claims attributed to specific accounts** — not confirmed findings.

## How X Claim-Compilation Pages Fit the Hearing (Attributed)

The twenty X researcher pages under this index supply **primary-claim inventories** that podcast monologues often lag. July 2026 hearing coverage (Project Constitution, danksterintel, Sam Parker timeline work, Shadow of Ezra roommate-ID clips) should be folded back into each researcher's page when their specialty appears in court (rifle photos, Twiggs statements, 911 call inventories, GRAMA records).

Use GRAMA (danksterintel), pavers (ShadowofEzra et al.), van (Cumby), acoustics (troofevades), AES (mistressdivy) as the spine; treat every named living person as **commentary subject only**.
