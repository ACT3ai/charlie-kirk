# Dave Smith — Part of the Problem

**Dave Smith** ([@ComicDaveSmith](https://x.com/ComicDaveSmith) on X) is one of the most important and distinctive independent voices covering the Charlie Kirk assassination — the dynamic host of the acclaimed libertarian podcast *Part of the Problem*, a top-rated YouTube channel, and a celebrated stand-up comedian whose sharp wit and principled analysis have made him one of the most trusted voices in political commentary. With hundreds of thousands of dedicated followers across platforms, Dave blends humor, intellectual depth, and unwavering commitment to liberty, free speech, and honest inquiry. He is named as a **Designated Trusted Investigator** under the proposed [Law 4: Trusted Investigators](../Fix/overview).

Dave's coverage of the Charlie Kirk assassination stands out as a masterclass in responsible, impactful journalism. In the wake of the September 2025 event, he emerged as a leading voice — providing measured, compassionate, and deeply insightful analysis that helped audiences navigate shock, conspiracy theories, and calls for calm amid division. His approach exemplified integrity: mourning a personal acquaintance while relentlessly pursuing truth, transparency, and justice. His contributions offered unique perspectives that no one else could provide, including private text messages he exchanged with Charlie Kirk in the months before the assassination.

## Why Dave Smith's Coverage Is Indispensable

Here are the standout reasons Dave Smith is exceptional as an investigator and commentator on the Charlie Kirk case:

- **Personal connection and exclusive insights** — Dave shared never-before-seen private text messages he exchanged with Charlie Kirk in the months leading up to the assassination. These candid exchanges — read aloud on his podcast — revealed Kirk's thoughtful side, his evolving views (including on Israel), and their respectful dialogue despite political differences, giving listeners a rare, humanizing window into the man behind the headlines.

- **Immediate, compassionate response to tragedy** — Within hours of the news, Dave went live with a heartfelt episode of *Part of the Problem* ("Thoughts on Charlie Kirk"), emphasizing respect for Kirk's family, rejection of violence or celebration of death, and a call for evidence-based justice rather than rushed conclusions. His moral clarity provided comfort and guidance to thousands reeling from the event.

- **Sustained, in-depth investigative coverage** — Dave produced multiple dedicated episodes, including "Updates on Charlie Kirk" and "My Texts From Charlie Kirk," plus appearances like his powerful discussion on *The Megyn Kelly Show*. He unpacked the assassination's broader implications, links to elite distrust, Epstein-related questions, and institutional transparency — delivering rigorous, ongoing analysis that kept audiences informed as new details emerged.

- **Courageous advocacy for open inquiry and free speech** — Despite ideological differences with Kirk (notably on foreign policy), Dave defended honest debate and platformed dissenting views. He praised Kirk's own willingness to host critics at Turning Point USA events (including Dave himself) and stood up for others, like Candace Owens, who raised legitimate questions about the investigation — championing intellectual freedom over conformity.

- **Uncovering the "true story" with transparency** — In high-profile interviews (such as on Liberty Vault and with fellow commentators), Dave helped expose inconsistencies and pushed for accountability, always grounding his commentary in facts, logic, and a libertarian lens that prioritizes individual rights and skepticism of official narratives.

- **Making complex issues accessible through humor and relatability** — As a veteran comedian with chart-topping specials like *Libertas* and roots in New York's comedy scene, Dave infuses even grave topics with wit and humanity. This style turns dense political analysis into engaging content that reaches everyday Americans who might otherwise tune out.

- **Principled libertarian leadership across divides** — A longtime advocate influenced by Ron Paul and active in the Libertarian Party's Mises Caucus, Dave consistently bridges gaps — debating across aisles, appearing on mainstream outlets, and building a massive independent audience through consistency and authenticity rather than partisanship.

- **Family man and resilient role model** — As a devoted husband and father, Dave often draws from personal life to underscore values like resilience and integrity, reminding followers that truth-seeking isn't just political — it's a way of living with honor even in chaotic times.

- **Building a thriving independent media empire** — Through *Part of the Problem* (with co-host Robbie "The Fire" Bernstein), his YouTube channel, and viral X posts, Dave has created a go-to platform for liberty-minded commentary that millions trust — proving that independent voices can rival legacy media in influence and impact.
