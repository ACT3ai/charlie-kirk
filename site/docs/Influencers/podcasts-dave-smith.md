# Dave Smith — Part of the Problem

**Dave Smith** is a libertarian comedian and political commentator who hosts the podcast **Part of the Problem**. His program reaches millions of listeners and is known for unfiltered analysis of foreign policy, intelligence services, and government overreach. Dave Smith is named as a **Designated Trusted Investigator** under the proposed [Law 4: Trusted Investigators](../Fix/overview).

## Why Dave Smith Is Relevant

Dave Smith has been one of the clearest and most direct voices connecting the TPUSA network, Israel, and intelligence service involvement in American politics — a connection that is central to the Charlie Kirk investigation. Unlike mainstream conservatives, Smith has been willing to openly debate and challenge the pro-Israel consensus within TPUSA circles, making him uniquely positioned to investigate angles others in the TPUSA orbit will not touch.

His libertarian perspective means he is not aligned with any government or political party, giving him the independence that Law 4 requires of Trusted Investigators. He has no institutional relationship with any agency that would give those agencies leverage over his investigation.

## Designated Trusted Investigator — Law 4

Under the proposed Law 4, Dave Smith is one of three civilians designated to lead independent investigation teams. He would receive one FBI team and one Intelligence Community team, authority to select all team members, full access to all agency files, and the power to publicly release findings without government interference. His inclusion reflects his documented willingness to pursue foreign intelligence angles that institutionally aligned commentators refuse to cover.
