# Patrick Bet-David — PBD Podcast

**Patrick Bet-David** hosts the **PBD Podcast**, one of the highest-production-value independent political interview programs in America. Bet-David is the founder of Valuetainment Media and brings a business entrepreneur's analytical framework to political investigations. His program is known for hosting panels that actively debate alternative theories, government overreach, and contested explanations for major political events.

- **YouTube:** [PBD Podcast on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@PBDPodcast)

## Coverage Approach

The PBD Podcast, according to its published content, hosts panels that actively debate conspiracy theories, government overreach, and alternative motives behind major political events. For the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation, this format is valuable — Bet-David tends to bring together guests with different perspectives and let them argue their cases, which can surface evidence and counter-evidence more effectively than single-perspective commentary.

Bet-David appeared at [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)'s AmericaFest 2024 (December 19–24, 2024), giving him direct familiarity with the TPUSA network and its key personnel in the months before Kirk's death.

## Why This Program Matters

Bet-David's business audience skews toward professionals and entrepreneurs who may be skeptical of both mainstream media and partisan political commentary. The PBD Podcast can reach an audience that other programs in this list cannot — people who would dismiss InfoWars or Steve Bannon but will sit through a 3-hour structured debate format that presents evidence systematically.

For investigators, any PBD Podcast episode featuring guests with direct knowledge of the Kirk case, the TPUSA organization, or the intelligence angles of the investigation would be high-value source material.

## Origin of Charlie Kirk's "Stand-Down" Question

The PBD Podcast holds a specific place in the investigation's backstory: it is where **Charlie Kirk first
publicly raised the possibility of a stand-down order** during the October 7, 2023 Hamas attacks on
Israel. In an episode that aired around **October 13–18, 2023**, Kirk questioned the delayed Israeli
military response and asked, **"Was there a stand-down order?"** — tying it to the intelligence failures
and the pre-attack protests against Netanyahu. He stopped short of calling the delay intentional but said
"some questions need to be asked." Per the master file, this PBD appearance is the **earliest known
instance** of Kirk voicing that view publicly, and clips from it have circulated widely as the origin of
his October 7 skepticism — a thread the investigation links to the later souring of his relationships with
pro-Israel donors. See [Motive](/Motive/overview) and [Israel](/Israel/overview) for how that thread
develops.

Patrick Bet-David's panel format is cited by citizen investigators as a way to put **opposing** Kirk-case positions (lone actor vs. intel/foreign-lead theories) on the same stage for business and immigrant-entrepreneur audiences who do not live on X OSINT accounts.
