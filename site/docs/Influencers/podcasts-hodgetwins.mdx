# Hodgetwins

The **Hodgetwins** (Keith and Kevin Hodge) host the **Hodgetwins Podcast**, a conservative commentary program with a large social media following across YouTube, X, and other platforms. Their program is known for direct, unfiltered political commentary and willingness to discuss alternative theories about major political events.

- **YouTube Channel:** [Hodgetwins on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCX8lCshQmMN0dUc0JmQYDdg)

## Key Episode: Israel Theories with Stew Peters

The Hodgetwins hosted a notable episode in which they discussed Israeli theories about the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination alongside **[Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)**, opening up exploration of non-traditional motives beyond the official law enforcement narrative.

- **Episode:** "Hodgetwins Discuss Israel Theories with Stew Peters on Kirk Murder"
- **YouTube:** [Watch the episode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J2DKRfwSt94)

This episode is significant because it brought two independent media programs together to discuss the foreign-intelligence angle of the Kirk assassination — an angle the mainstream media has not covered and that the FBI has reportedly not pursued through its official investigation.

## Ian Carroll Collaboration

The Hodgetwins have also featured research from **[Ian Carroll](/People/ian-carroll)**, whose analysis of the FBI's narrative inconsistencies was discussed in the context of the Kirk assassination. This cross-pollination between independent researchers and the Hodgetwins' platform helped spread detailed investigative analysis to a broader audience.

## Investigative Value

The Hodgetwins provide cultural commentary and audience reach that complements the more analytically dense coverage from investigative podcasters like Ian Carroll, Dave Smith, and Glenn Greenwald. Their program is a useful amplification channel for key findings that need to reach a wider audience.
