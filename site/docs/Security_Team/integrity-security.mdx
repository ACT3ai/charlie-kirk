# Integrity Security Solutions

Charlie Kirk's private security at Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025** was reportedly provided by **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC**, doing business as **Integrity Security Solutions** — founded by former police officer **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)**.

## Who the firm is

| Entity | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Legal name | Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC |
| DBA | Integrity Security Solutions |
| Founder | Brian Harpole (reported former police officer) |
| Names linked online | Justin Davis, Pierce Beck — on-site status **uncertain** |

The investigation file notes additional security-adjacent names in crowd speculation ([Dan Flood](./dan-flood), Frank Turek, Laine Schoneberger, Butch Hibbs). Those identifications are **unverified** photo matches, not firm rosters.

## Not Shaffer Security Group that day

A critical vendor switch appears in the master file:

- Prior Kirk events reportedly used **Shaffer Security Group (SSG)**, associated with twenty-year FBI veteran **Greg Schaffer** and Harpole.
- **September 10** security was **not** SSG per file summary — **[Greg Shaffer](./greg-shaffer)** stated SSG had not worked with TPUSA since **2022**.
- Eyewitness footage nonetheless shows familiar **"white-shirt"** agents from earlier stops, raising transparency questions about who was actually on the detail.
- The detail reportedly had **no bodycams**.

The FBI and Utah AG probe referenced in the file includes SSG **subcontractors** in obstruction questions (scene cleanup, chain of custody). That is investigative scope, not a finding against Integrity Security Solutions.

## Hybrid security model

Protection combined:

1. **TPUSA-funded** private close-protection detail (Integrity Security Solutions).
2. **Six UVU campus police officers** (~25% of department) under Chief Jeff Long — crowd control and broader perimeter, detailed on [UVU campus police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police).

Local **Orem police** were not described as on-site before the shot; they responded afterward. Commentators characterize the hybrid as **lighter than Kirk's prior events**.

## Last-minute rehiring

A **September 2025 Associated Press** report and Greg Shaffer's interview suggest Integrity was rehired at the last minute for **"specialized security."** Shaffer said he was **"stabbed in the back"** in the dispute over how the firm came to guard Kirk. Researchers raise this as a **process transparency** question, not proven wrongdoing.

## Speculative crowd identifications (unverified)

- **Laine Schoneberger** — Yrefy sponsor rep (square sunglasses / dark blue shirt theories)
- **Dan Flood** — Ray-Ban glasses, dark blue button-up (confirmed role as head of security in other sources)
- **Frank Turek** — white shirt / hat; Kirk mentor, SUV occupant

None of these crowd-sourced IDs is verified by the firm or law enforcement. They are listed to document speculation, not to assert roster fact.

## Key questions

* Who signed the contract, and what was the written threat assessment?
* Were rooftop inspections assigned to private detail, campus police, or both?
