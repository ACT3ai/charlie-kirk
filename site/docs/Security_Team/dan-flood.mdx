# Dan Flood — Head of Security

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Full Name | Dan Flood |
| Described role | Charlie Kirk's head of security / "security chief" |
| Status | **Alive** |
| Evidence Rating | MODERATE (SUV role corroborated in file; some claims unverified) |

**Dan Flood** is described in the investigation file and social posts as Charlie Kirk's **head of security** for the period surrounding the **September 10, 2025** UVU event. This page documents **attributed claims** about his role. We do **not** assert Flood knew of any attack in advance or acted with criminal intent.

## SUV immediate response team

According to the master file witness list, Flood was one of **five people in the SUV** with Kirk immediately after the shooting:

| SUV occupant | Reported role |
|--------------|---------------|
| Dan Flood | TPUSA security director; shielded Kirk with Harpole |
| Brian Harpole | Security chief; heard "two distinct sounds" |
| Rick Cutler | Private security; held Harpole's shirt during transport |
| Justin Davis | Emergency driver |
| Frank Turek | Kirk mentor |

Flood reportedly **jumped on Kirk** to shield him from further harm per Brian Harpole's public accounts. See [Rick Cutler POI](/Suspects/Rick_Cutler_POI) for separate "arm puller" theories — we do not merge unverified accusations here.

## Appearance claims (unverified)

Crowd posts describe Flood wearing **Ray-Ban glasses** (said to record video) and a **dark blue button-up shirt**. These are crowd-sourced descriptions, not confirmed by official roster releases.

## Charlie's death-threat messages to Flood

The investigation file records Candace Owens reporting that Charlie Kirk sent messages via **Signal** and **Telegram** (not iMessage) to close contacts including Flood the night before the event:

- Charlie reportedly told donors, TPUSA members, and Flood he thought he would be **"executed tomorrow."**
- A related thread references Erika Kirk and a message that **"The Left will kill me."**

These are **reported statements about Charlie's fears**, not evidence about Flood's conduct. Frank Turek has also publicly stated Charlie told him he believed "they're going to take me out."

## Cattle Barons Ball — open question (not allegation)

An anonymous post described as a **childhood friend** of Flood claimed the last time they saw him, he was "running security for some Dallas billionaires at the **Cattle Barons Ball**" — a Texas charity gala drawing wealthy donors.

The post raises, strictly as a **question**, whether client conflicts could exist. **No conflict of interest has been established.** We document the question because it circulated publicly; we do not accuse Flood of divided loyalties.

## Next event — no security plan claim

The same commentary thread links Flood to claims that his team had **no security plan** for **[Therese Bible College on September 11](/Security_Team/next-event-no-plan)** — the day after the assassination. That claim is **unverified** and documented on the dedicated page.

## Prior vendor work (heavily attributed)

Some X posts allege Flood previously worked for Shaffer Security or Echelon with Israel-related networking claims and even "commence firing" hand signals. Those are **unverified social-media allegations** with serious defamation risk. They are **not** stated as fact on this page. If readers encounter them elsewhere, treat them as uncorroborated commentary unless sworn testimony or documents emerge.

## Defense-witness commentary

Investigation-file commentary argues Tyler Robinson's defense should call Flood for SUV timeline testimony. That is opinion, not a court filing.
