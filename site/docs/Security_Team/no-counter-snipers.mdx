# No Counter-Snipers or Secret Service

Coverage of **September 10, 2025** repeatedly notes **capabilities the detail did not have** — and how that intersected with the **elevated-threat** geometry of the UVU courtyard.

## Counter-snipers — the missing overwatch

**Counter-snipers** (overwatch teams scanning surrounding high ground) are standard at high-profile outdoor events with rooftop sightlines. According to posts and commentary:

- TPUSA's private detail at UVU had **no counter-snipers**.
- No one was tasked with controlling the **Losee Center** and adjacent rooftops overlooking the stage.

Critics argue this is the capability most directly matched to the **official rooftop shooter narrative**. Whether a counter-sniper team would have prevented an **inside-tent** or **van/tunnel** shot — alternative theories on [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) — is a separate debate.

## No Secret Service layer

Charlie Kirk was a **private citizen** without Secret Service protection (reserved for designated protectees). Posts cite that absence to emphasize how much rested on:

- A **last-minute** [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security) detail, and
- **Six** UVU campus police officers.

This is **context**, not a claim that federal protection was legally required.

## Thin staffing vs. prior events

The investigation file and [@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) eyewitness commentary describe the UVU event as **low-key** relative to earlier stops — e.g., only **2–3 black-shirt guards** and a **sketchy audio setup** (tinny Q&A mics vs. clear feed for Kirk). The broader file also states the hybrid setup was **lighter than Kirk's prior events**.

## Airspace and drones — "beyond the bubble"

UVU campus police reportedly engaged **no drones**. Posts describe **airspace and rooftops** as outside the private detail's jurisdiction — **"beyond Charlie's bubble"** — leaving a gap between close protection and perimeter monitoring. See [Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](./perimeter-rooftop-failures) and [Drones](/Drones/overview).

## N1098L / rehearsal theories (contested)

Some commentary links **N1098L** HADES ISR passes near UVU to rehearsal for chaos rather than routine training. That claim is **contested** and documented under [Planes/N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview). It does not establish what the protective detail knew or should have planned for.

## No bodycams on private detail

The master file notes the September 10 detail had **no bodycams** — limiting after-action review of positioning and response. Washington County's separate dispute over **Tyler Robinson surrender bodycam** footage is covered under [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview).
