# Screening & Credential Gaps

Separate from rooftop failures, some posts focus on **who was admitted** to the Q&A line at UVU on **September 10, 2025**.

## Hunter Kozak — last Q&A question

**[Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak)** reportedly asked [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) the **last question** before the fatal shot. Investigation-file commentary notes:

- Kozak is described as active in the **"UnF*ck America" / "UN-Eff America"** tour positioned as a counter-narrative to [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
- A **September 12, 2025** Channel 5 interview with **Andrew Callaghan** is cited in the file.
- Some X posts claim Kozak was seen **"practicing being surprised"** before the event — **unverified** footage interpretation.

We document these as **reported claims**. They are **not** accusations that Kozak committed a crime.

## Revoked credentials claim

A post in the master file states there was **"almost no security at UVU"** because Kozak and affiliates of **"UN-Eff America"** had reportedly had their credentials **revoked** from Charlie Kirk's events — yet Kozak was present and asked a question.

The post asks: **"Who let him in line?"**

| Claim element | Status |
|---------------|--------|
| Prior credential revocation | **Unverified** in public official records cited here |
| Kozak present at UVU Q&A | **Confirmed** in widespread reporting of last question |
| Screening failure inference | Commentator framing only |

## Why screening matters to investigators

If credentialing rules were not enforced at the door, critics argue that indicates a **front-of-house** failure distinct from rooftop overwatch:

- Controlled access is part of a protective plan even when the threat is elevated-position shooting.
- A barred affiliate reaching the mic line raises process questions for TPUSA and the detail.

Whether the revocation claim is accurate awaits document release — a target of [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure pressure.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Screening posts on X tend to merge **access control** with **threat-context** rather than produce a public credential log:

- Recurring commentary argues that if **UN-Eff America** affiliates truly had **revoked credentials**, Kozak’s mic-line access is the sharpest simple process failure at front-of-house — distinct from rooftop overwatch. The **“Who let him in line?”** question remains unanswered in public records cited here.
- July 2026 posts (e.g. **@TriciaThom40938**) place screening inside a Butler-style frame: cameras and known threats existed, yet suspicious pre-shot behavior (in the official Robinson narrative) was not stopped. That analogy does **not** prove Kozak or any Q&A participant was part of a plot.
- Crowd-sourced “security-adjacent” photo IDs (Dan Flood, Frank Turek, Laine Schoneberger, and others — see [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)) are sometimes treated as **credential roster proxies**. They are **not** verified vendor manifests.

## Open Questions Raised by Investigators

1. Does TPUSA or the event vendor retain a **credential revocation list** for the Utah stop, and does Kozak appear on it?
2. Was Q&A line access controlled by TPUSA staff, private security, campus PD, or student volunteers?
3. Were bags / metal detection used at any chokepoint into the courtyard?
4. What will defense or civil discovery compel about guest lists, wristbands, or radio traffic at the mic line?
5. How should readers separate **legitimate screening critique** from unverified attempts to criminalize a questioner?

No living person named on this page is accused by this site of participating in the assassination. Screening gaps, if proven, would be **process failures** unless a court finds otherwise.
