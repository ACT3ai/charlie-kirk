# Screening & Credential Gaps

Separate from the rooftop and perimeter questions, some posts focus on who was let through the front of the event. The concern they raise is about screening and credentialing at UVU on **September 10, 2025**.

## The claim about revoked credentials

A post claims there was "almost no security at UVU" and points to a specific example. It states that **Hunter Kozak** and affiliates of a group it calls "UN-Eff America" had reportedly had their credentials revoked from Charlie Kirk's events.

According to the post, Kozak was nonetheless present and asked a question during the event. The post asks, "Who let him in line?"

## Framing it as an open question

This is presented strictly as an open question about screening gaps, attributed to the post that raised it. It is not an accusation that Kozak committed any crime, and nothing here suggests he did.

The claim about revoked credentials is itself unverified. It is documented because it speaks to the broader screening concern, not because it has been confirmed.

## Why screening matters here

If credentialing decisions were not enforced at the door, critics argue, it would point to a gap distinct from the rooftop failure: a breakdown in controlling who entered the secured area. That is the angle the post pushes.

Whether the underlying credential claim holds up is unresolved on the public record. The takeaway offered here is the open question, not a conclusion about any individual.
