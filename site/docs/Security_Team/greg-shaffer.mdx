# Greg Shaffer

**Greg Shaffer** is a figure connected to **[Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)** and to the prior **Shaffer Security Group (SSG)** vendor that guarded [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) on earlier tour stops. He has spoken publicly about how Integrity came to take the UVU assignment.

## "Stabbed in the back" interview

According to the investigation file, Shaffer gave a public interview providing **"new details about how Integrity Security Solutions came to guard Kirk in the first place."** In that interview he said he was **"stabbed in the back."**

That phrase is **his characterization** of a business dispute — not a legal finding about [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), Integrity, or any other party.

## Last-minute rehiring — AP report

Shaffer's account aligns with a **September 2025 Associated Press** report describing **last-minute rehiring** for **"specialized security."** Researchers cite this timing when asking why the UVU setup was described as **lighter than prior events** and why **no bodycams** were on the detail.

Open questions about process are not proof of intentional sabotage.

## Shaffer vs. Schaffer — disambiguation

The master file distinguishes:

| Name | Role discussed |
|------|----------------|
| **Greg Shaffer** | Interview subject tied to Integrity coming to guard Kirk |
| **Greg Schaffer** | Twenty-year FBI agent; SSG founder with [Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole); [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gregoryschaffer/) |

Shaffer stated **SSG had not worked with TPUSA since 2022**, yet eyewitness footage reportedly shows familiar **white-shirt** agents from earlier Kirk events. That tension is a **transparency** question documented on [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security).

## SSG background (context)

The file describes SSG as recruiting **U.S. Tier 1 operators** with FBI-aligned training. It notes **no foreign nationals** documented on SSG rosters — countering some online [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)-link theories, which remain **unproven** regardless.

Post-incident, the [FBI](/FBI/overview) / Utah AG probe reference includes SSG **subcontractors** in obstruction questions (scene cleanup, chain of custody). That scope is investigative, not an adjudicated charge.
