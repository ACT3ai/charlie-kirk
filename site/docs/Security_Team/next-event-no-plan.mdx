# The Next Event Had No Security Plan

One claim circulating after the assassination concerns **the stop after UVU** — not the Orem campus itself.

## The claim

Social posts allege **[Dan Flood](./dan-flood)'s** team had **no security plan** prepared for [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s next scheduled appearance:

| Detail | Attribution |
|--------|-------------|
| Venue | **Therese Bible College** |
| Date | **September 11, 2025** — day after the assassination |
| Responsible party named | Dan Flood / [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) security leadership |

This is **attributed to anonymous online posts**, not confirmed by TPUSA releases, contracts, or law-enforcement records cited in the file.

## Link to Cattle Barons Ball thread

The same commentary ecosystem ties the "no plan" claim to an anonymous post about Flood allegedly running security for **Dallas billionaires** at the **Cattle Barons Ball** — framing both as signs of a **thin, divided-attention** protective operation.

We document that **framing as commentary**. It does **not** establish that Flood or any colleague knew Kirk would not reach September 11.

## Why critics raise it

Commentators who push the claim use it to argue:

- The protective operation was **under-prepared** across consecutive tour dates.
- A missing plan for the **very next day** fits a broader "lighter than usual" pattern on [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security).

Even if the plan were truly absent, that would speak to **preparedness**, not intent — and the underlying claim remains **unverified**.

## How to weigh it

Treat this as an **uncorroborated allegation** until TPUSA schedules, vendor invoices, or sworn testimony surface. The page exists so readers can see what has been claimed — with explicit limits.

## Who Said What (Attributed)

Citizen-investigator discussion of Flood’s dual focus usually clusters three claims together. **None** of the following is a court finding:

1. **Next-day plan gap** — Anonymous posts allege Flood’s team had **no written security plan** for **Therese Bible College** on **September 11, 2025**.
2. **Cattle Barons Ball moonlighting** — A purported childhood-friend anecdote (circulated in the master investigation file’s Cattle Barons section) says Flood was seen running security for **Dallas billionaires** at the **Cattle Barons Ball**, used to argue **divided attention**.
3. **Night-before fear messages** — Separate commentary (including claims amplified by **[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)**) alleges Kirk messaged **Dan Flood** and others the night of **September 9** about being killed / “executed.” That message thread is documented as **attributed Candace/file material** on the [“They Are Going to Kill Me” text](/Timeline/they-are-going-to-kill-me-text) page — it does **not** prove Flood withheld a Sept 11 plan.

Linking (1)+(2)+(3) into a single “he knew” narrative is a **commentator inference**, not established fact. This site does **not** adopt any claim that Flood or TPUSA leadership knew Kirk would die on September 10.

## Timeline Claims (As Reported)

|------|----------------------|--------|
| Pre-Sept 10 | Flood allegedly moonlighting at elite Dallas events (Cattle Barons) | Anecdotal / anonymous source chain |

| Sept 11 | “No plan” for Therese Bible College | Unverified social claim; event cancelled/overtaken by assassination |

## Open Questions

* Can TPUSA produce a schedule packet or vendor SOW for Therese Bible College dated before Sept 10?
* Is “no plan” a missing document, a cancelled stop, or a rumor that grew after the fact?
* How do advance-work norms for multi-stop campus tours usually allocate prep days — and did UVU consume resources meant for Sept 11?
* What, if anything, will discovery in related civil/criminal matters force into the open about Flood’s dual assignments?

Until primary documents appear, the responsible reading is: **preparedness questions are fair; foreknowledge or intentional abandonment is not established.**
