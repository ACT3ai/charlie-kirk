# The Next Event Had No Security Plan

One claim circulating after the assassination concerns not UVU but the event that was supposed to follow it. According to posts, the security team had no security plan in place for Charlie Kirk's next scheduled appearance.

## The claim

The posts identify the next event as **Therese Bible College on September 11**, the day after the assassination. They claim that **Dan Flood's** team had not prepared a security plan for it.

This is attributed as a claim made in social posts, not a confirmed fact. It has not been independently verified, and no official record is cited in the posts to support it.

## Why critics raise it

For those making the claim, the alleged lack of a plan for the very next day fits a broader theme they push: that the protective operation around Kirk was thin and under-prepared. They use it to question how seriously the upcoming schedule was being secured.

That inference is the posters' framing. An absent plan, even if confirmed, would speak to preparedness rather than intent, and nothing here establishes either.

## How to weigh it

Because this rests on anonymous social posts, it should be treated as an unverified allegation. It is documented here so readers can see what has been claimed, with the clear caveat that it is uncorroborated.

If reliable records about the September 11 event later surface, the claim could be checked against them. Until then it remains a reported assertion and nothing more.
