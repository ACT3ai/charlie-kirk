**Bobby Harpole** is described in open-source posts as the **father of [Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)**, the founder of Integrity Security Solutions — the firm reported to have provided Charlie Kirk's private protective detail at Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025**. This page documents the biographical background that has circulated about him, with attribution. It does not assert that Bobby Harpole was involved in the events of that day.

## Reported background

According to an X post by **@ProjectConstitu** (Project Constitution), Bobby Harpole is a Texan with a long career in law enforcement. The post describes him as a former **Erath County Sheriff's Captain** and a retired **Texas DPS Highway Patrolman**, and states that after retiring he took a role as a **funeral director / Funeral Associate at Lacy Funeral Home** in the same county.

The same post describes him as a **Past Master Mason at Stephenville Masonic Lodge #267**. These are the biographical details as stated by the account. They have not been independently confirmed here, and are presented as reported claims rather than established fact.

## Why these details are noted — and their limits

Independent researchers have pointed to the Harpole family's overlapping backgrounds — law enforcement, Freemasonry, and funeral services — as a reason for further questions about the private security around Charlie Kirk. This page records that these observations have circulated publicly.

It is important to be clear about what these details do and do not show. Serving in law enforcement, holding a Masonic membership, and later working in funeral services are all lawful, common occupations and affiliations. None of them, individually or together, is evidence that any person did anything wrong. No court has made any finding against Bobby Harpole, and this page accuses him of nothing. The details are documented only because they have been raised in public discussion of the security detail; readers should treat them as background, not as proof of any involvement.

## Family connection

Bobby Harpole is described as the father of **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)**, who founded the firm reported to have supplied Kirk's protective detail. See Brian Harpole's page for the security-firm role and for the separate, unverified online allegation raised about him.
