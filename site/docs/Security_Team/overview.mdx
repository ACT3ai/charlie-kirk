# Security Team

[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s UVU detail on **September 10, 2025** was a **hybrid** arrangement: [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)-funded private close protection plus a limited UVU campus-police contingent. For government police and agencies, see [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview).

The contracted firm that day was reportedly **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC** (DBA **Integrity Security Solutions**), founded by **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)** — not the prior **[Shaffer Security Group](./shaffer-security-group)** vendor that had guarded earlier tour stops. According to a **September 2025 AP** report and **[Greg Shaffer](./greg-shaffer)**'s public interview, the firm was rehired at the last minute for "specialized security," and the setup was described as **lighter than Kirk's prior events**, with **no bodycams** on the detail. High-engagement X coverage (including **[@HeadlineUSA](/Influencers/x/headlineusa)**, September 28, 2025) amplified Shaffer's **"stabbed in the back"** account of how Integrity came to guard Kirk — a **business-dispute narrative**, not a court finding of criminal setup. **[Dan Flood](./dan-flood)** is described in posts as Kirk's head of security; he was in the **SUV** with Harpole immediately after the shot alongside [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek). Citizen investigators treat the SUV roster as a **must-call witness list** for positioning, screening, and immediate medical response — not as proof of collective guilt.

The most visible physical gap was **perimeter overwatch**: six UVU officers (~25% of the department) under Chief **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)** handled crowd control but reportedly did **not** thoroughly inspect nearby rooftops or engage drones, while posts describe rooftops and airspace as **"beyond Charlie's bubble."** Harpole gave interviews (Shawn Ryan Show, *Salt Lake Tribune*) criticizing UVU police over what he described as a failure to secure rooftops as promised, and said he had texted Long pre-event; that characterization is Harpole's and has not been adjudicated; **[@IngrahamAngle](/Influencers/x/ingrahamangle)** (November 20, 2025) restated Harpole's line that security was **"told it would be handled."** Mainstream outlets have reported **coordination lapses**; some citizen threads extend that into intentional-enablement theories, which this section does **not** adopt as fact. The shooter allegedly reached a **prone position on the Losee Center roof** overlooking the courtyard — the failure documented on [Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](./perimeter-rooftop-failures) and [No Counter-Snipers](./no-counter-snipers). Parallel suspect-location debates (grey van, acoustic placement) live under [Suspects](/Suspects/overview) and do not erase the rooftop-control question under the official narrative.

Parallel questions concern **preparedness and screening**: anonymous posts claim Flood's team had **no plan** for **Therese Bible College** on **September 11**, and that **[Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak)** — who asked the last Q&A question — should not have been credentialed if prior revocations for "UN-Eff America" affiliates were enforced. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reporting alleges Charlie messaged Flood via **Signal** the night before that he feared being **"executed tomorrow."** X commentary continues to demand subpoenas for private-security phones, recording glasses, and contracts — advocacy that only court process can test. None of this establishes intent by any security person; gaps in protection are documented here with attribution. Start with **[Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](./perimeter-rooftop-failures)** → **[Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)** → **[Dan Flood](./dan-flood)**, then [Screening & Credential Gaps](./screening-credentials) if front-of-house access is your focus.
