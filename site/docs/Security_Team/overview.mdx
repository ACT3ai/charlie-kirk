# Security Team

[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s UVU detail on **September 10, 2025** was a **hybrid** arrangement: [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)-funded private close protection plus a limited UVU campus-police contingent. For government police and agencies, see [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview).

The contracted firm that day was reportedly **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC** (DBA **Integrity Security Solutions**), founded by **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)** — not the prior **Shaffer Security Group** vendor that had guarded earlier tour stops. According to a **September 2025 AP** report and **[Greg Shaffer](./greg-shaffer)**'s public interview, the firm was rehired at the last minute for "specialized security," and the setup was described as **lighter than Kirk's prior events**, with **no bodycams** on the detail. **[Dan Flood](./dan-flood)** is described in posts as Kirk's head of security; he was in the **SUV** with Harpole immediately after the shot alongside [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek).

The most visible physical gap was **perimeter overwatch**: six UVU officers (~25% of the department) under Chief **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)** handled crowd control but reportedly did **not** thoroughly inspect nearby rooftops or engage drones, while posts describe rooftops and airspace as **"beyond Charlie's bubble."** Harpole gave interviews (Shawn Ryan Show, *Salt Lake Tribune*) criticizing UVU police for failing to secure rooftops as promised after he had texted Long pre-event. The shooter allegedly reached a **prone position on the Losee Center roof** overlooking the courtyard — the failure documented on [Perimeter & Rooftop Failures](./perimeter-rooftop-failures) and [No Counter-Snipers](./no-counter-snipers).

Parallel questions concern **preparedness and screening**: anonymous posts claim Flood's team had **no plan** for **Therese Bible College** on **September 11**, and that **[Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak)** — who asked the last Q&A question — should not have been credentialed if prior revocations for "UN-Eff America" affiliates were enforced. [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reporting alleges Charlie messaged Flood via **Signal** the night before that he feared being **"executed tomorrow."** None of this establishes intent by any security person; gaps in protection are documented here with attribution.
