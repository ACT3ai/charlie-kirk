# Perimeter & Rooftop Failures

The clearest physical security failure on **September 10, 2025** — under either the official or alternative theories — is that an attacker reportedly used an **overlooking elevated position** without being stopped. The charging narrative places the shooter on the **Losee Center** rooftop in a **prone firing position** ~150 yards from the courtyard stage.

## Affidavit rooftop timeline (official narrative)

| Time (MT) | Alleged action |
|-----------|----------------|
| 11:52 | Movement toward Losee |
| 12:15 | Roof access via southeast stairs / walkway |
| 12:23 | Shot fired |
| 12:24 | Flight from roof toward wooded area |

Surveillance stills from **12:23:29**–**12:23:54** map movement to the northeast corner in ~**25 seconds**. Pre-shooting video (~**one hour before**) shows a **prone all-black figure** on the same roof — see [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect).

## UVU campus police gap

The investigation file states UVU provided **six campus police officers** — about **one quarter** of the department — for crowd control and event oversight under Chief **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)**. That contingent reportedly:

- Did **not** conduct thorough inspections of nearby **rooftops or buildings**
- Engaged **no drones** for airspace monitoring

Full campus-police context: [UVU campus police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police).

## Brian Harpole's pre-event warnings

**[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)** publicly criticized UVU police for failing to secure rooftops **as promised**. The file states he **texted Jeff Long about rooftop concerns before the event**. His interviews (Shawn Ryan Show, *Salt Lake Tribune*) are primary sources for that dispute — distinct from unverified conspiracy claims about Harpole himself.

## "Beyond Charlie's bubble"

Posts report a **jurisdictional split**: campus police treated **drones, airspace, and rooftops** as outside the private detail's close-protection perimeter — **"beyond Charlie's bubble."** Critics argue no single party owned the high ground between:

1. Integrity Security Solutions close protection, and
2. UVU's limited perimeter monitoring.

That characterization comes from social posts and the visible outcome, **not** a formal after-action review released publicly.

## Lighter hybrid vs. prior events

The arrangement combined a **last-minute** Integrity detail with six campus officers and reportedly **no Orem police** on-site beforehand. Multiple sources describe it as **lighter than Kirk's prior tour stops** — see [No Counter-Snipers](./no-counter-snipers).

## Ballistics angle tension

If the shot truly came from Losee, ballistics commentary argues a **5–9°** downward angle would likely strike face/jaw rather than the observed neck/chest path — feeding alternative location theories on [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations). That does not by itself prove rooftop security should have stopped a different weapon type.

## Key questions

* Who was contractually responsible for rooftop clearance — [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), Integrity, or UVU?
* What did Harpole's texts to Long say, and what was Long's written response?
* Was the pre-shooting prone figure ever flagged to security leadership?
* Why were drones not deployed for overwatch at a high-profile outdoor event?

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Rooftop-gap posts usually restate three attributed points rather than producing new primary video:

1. **Promise vs delivery** — **[@IngrahamAngle](/Influencers/x/ingrahamangle)** (November 20, 2025) and Harpole’s own interviews claim campus/local police said rooftops **would be handled** and then were not. That is a **public coordination dispute**.
2. **Staffing arithmetic** — Accounts such as **[@BDPelton](/Influencers/x/bdpelton)** (July 2026) note ~**six UVU officers**, no rooftop detail from Integrity, and known death threats — arguing the high ground was an obvious unowned zone.
3. **Pre-shot roof figure** — **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** and related threads emphasize the **all-black prone figure** on the roof roughly an hour before the shot (cross-link [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect)), asking why security leadership never flagged that presence in real time.

Separately, the master investigation file tracks **Officer Bagley’s** bodycam reportedly **dying on the roof** when LE later processed the scene — an after-the-fact documentation gap about screwdriver / “sniper pad” discovery, not the same as pre-shot perimeter failure, but often cited in the same “rooftop opacity” bundle.
