# Shaffer Security Group (SSG)

**Shaffer Security Group (SSG)** — X handle **[@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup)**, a security firm founded by **[Greg Shaffer](./greg-shaffer)**, described in reporting as a **former (twenty-year) FBI agent** — is the vendor that reportedly provided private protection for [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) on **earlier tour stops**. SSG has publicly stated it was **not** the firm on the [detail at UVU on September 10, 2025](/Security_Team/overview); the firm on stage that day was reportedly **[Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)** (Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC), founded by former SSG employee **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)**.

## The deleted @ShafferGroup post

According to **[@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles)** (July 18, 2026), who published the screenshot from their files, a since-deleted post from **[@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup)** (timestamped 11:33&nbsp;PM, 9/26/25) stated, in SSG's own words:

> "This is the security firm contracted by TPUSA for security when Charlie Kirk was killed. They want to remain in the shadows while my firm takes blame. **SSG was not & had not been affiliated or contracted to do security for TPUSA since 2022!**"

The post displayed a **Texas business record** for the firm SSG was pointing to:

* **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC** (DBA [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security))
* Texas Tax ID **32089791597**; File Number **0805052578**
* Incorporated **May 9, 2023** in Texas as a Texas Limited Liability Company; status **Active**
* Registered Agent / Manager: **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)** (registered address in Lantana, Texas; street address withheld here — it is a private residence)
* Governing Agency: Texas Secretary of State

This is SSG's public **characterization** of who was contracted — an attributed claim in a business dispute, not a court finding.

After the [@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup) post was deleted, **Headline USA** reported that SSG's owner, described as **former FBI agent Greg Shaffer**, reiterated the same information by phone. The excerpt shared by [@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles) attributes these statements to Shaffer:

* "The individuals who work for that company [[Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)] were the ones on the stage."
* Integrity founder **[Brian Harpole](./brian-harpole)** and **TPUSA security director [Dan Flood](./dan-flood)** both "used to work with him until they 'stabbed [him] in the back.'"
* "Dan Flood headed up security for [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview), and Brian Harpole is his best friend. Brian worked for me for seven years, and then Brian started his own company..."

These are **Shaffer's characterizations of a business dispute** reported by a media outlet. They are not adjudicated findings, and nothing here establishes criminal intent by Harpole, Flood, TPUSA, or SSG.

## Why researchers track SSG

The [@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles) post frames an open question that citizen investigators keep returning to: *is [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis) — a name linked online to the detail — currently with SSG or with Integrity?* More broadly, the SSG-vs-Integrity split matters because:

1. It defines **who was actually accountable** for the [perimeter and rooftop plan](./perimeter-rooftop-failures) at UVU.
2. It documents a **vendor transition** (SSG era through ~2022, then Harpole/Flood spinning out Integrity) that shapes the "who controlled the bubble?" debate.
3. Shaffer's **former-FBI** background is cited both by those alleging deeper agency ties and by those countering foreign-intelligence theories — the master file notes **no foreign nationals** documented on SSG rosters, which cuts against some [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)-link claims that remain **unproven** regardless.

## Disambiguation: Shaffer vs. Schaffer

The master file distinguishes two similar names — see [Greg Shaffer](./greg-shaffer) for the full note. In short: **Greg Shaffer** is the interview subject and [@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup) owner; **Greg Schaffer** is referenced as a twenty-year FBI agent tied to SSG's founding with Brian Harpole.

## Open questions

1. Does [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis) currently work for SSG or [Integrity Security Solutions](./integrity-security)? (the direct question raised by [@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles))
2. If SSG last worked with TPUSA in 2022, why did eyewitness footage reportedly show familiar "white-shirt" agents at the 2025 event?
3. What records (contracts, filings) would confirm the SSG → Integrity transition timeline?
