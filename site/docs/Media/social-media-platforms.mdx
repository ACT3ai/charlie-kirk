# Social Media Platforms

Platforms are not neutral pipes in this case — they are **distribution**, **moderation battlegrounds**,
and **evidence channels** (especially **Discord**, treated on [court Discord page](/court/discord-evidence-court)).

## X (Twitter)

**Primary venue** for citizen investigators after September 10:

| Account cluster | Role |
|-----------------|------|
| **@RealCandaceO** | Text releases, witness profiling, donor theories |
| **@ian_carroll** | Ballistics database, court-filing analysis |
| **@ShadowofEzra** | Paving / crime-scene alteration amplification |
| **@healthranger**, **@george_webb**, **@zeb_boykin** | Censorship, intel, legal threads |

**Moderation claims:** UVU research notes allege **thousands suspended** for engaging [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)
Kirk posts ([UVU Social Media Response](/UVU/UVU_Social_Media_Response)). These are **reported claims**,
not verified platform transparency reports.

See also [Influencers](/Influencers/overview) and [social_media_analysis](/social_media_analysis/overview).

## YouTube

Long-form video replaced TV segments for contested forensics:

* **Baron Coleman** — Miranda / Bates **003996-R2** breakdowns (citizen citing).
* **@stevendenoon** — ballistics collaborations cited in X threads.
* **"Charlie Kirk 30-06"** search cluster — gel tests and caliber education videos referenced in CK_FILE.

YouTube is also where **[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)**, **InfoWars**, and **podcast** ecosystems rebroadcast theories
([Influencers podcasts](/Influencers/overview)).

## TikTok

**Short-form outrage loops:**

* Fox News covered a **dancer at the assassination site** TikTok — meta-story about disrespect and
  virality ([Fox](https://www.foxnews.com/media/viral-video-shows-young-woman-dancing-near-charlie-kirk-assassination-site)).
* **Reuters** documented **Libs of TikTok** driving workplace retaliation visibility in the **600+
  firings** investigation ([Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/investigations/charlie-kirk-purge-how-600-americans-were-punished-pro-trump-crackdown-2025-11-19/)).

## Discord

Not a "press outlet" but the **prosecution's alleged confession channel**. Platform policies matter
less than **custody and authentication** in court ([Discord evidence](/court/discord-evidence-court)).

## Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

Less central in the master file than X, but **witness phone footage** and **local community groups**
still appear in anecdotal delete-video claims ([Media Censorship](./Censorship)).

## Dual restriction: platforms + gag orders

**December 2025 gag order** limits what witnesses and lawyers can say to **reporters**; platform
moderation limits what users can post **online** — stacked chilling-effect debate on
[Media Censorship](./Censorship).

We do **not** claim platform employees or executives knew of the assassination in advance.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Platform roles: **X** for rapid claim discovery and hearing notes (**@triggersmarthq** style); **YouTube** for multi-hour forensics; **TikTok** for emotional virality; **Discord** as both **evidence narrative** and contested confession medium. Users argue moderation + gag orders create a **double bind** against public fact-finding.
