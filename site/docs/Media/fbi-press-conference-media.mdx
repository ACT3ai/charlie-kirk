# FBI Press Conference & Official Releases

Official FBI channels function as **the citation layer** beneath AP/Reuters sentences. When that
layer wobbles — e.g., a **premature custody announcement** — the whole media stack wobbles with it.

## September 11, 2025 — Salt Lake City field office

**[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)**, Special Agent in Charge of the FBI's Salt Lake City field office, led a **press
conference on the shooting** the day after the UVU event per research notes — the public **law-
enforcement anchor** for national desks still in manhunt mode.

Bohls arrived amid reported **FBI leadership turnover** (predecessor **[Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed)** out, Bohls in
August 2025). Commentary links timing to broader personnel changes; that is **not** a court finding.

## Utah Valley Shooting Updates page

The FBI maintains a **[Utah Valley Shooting Updates](https://www.fbi.gov/news/press-releases/utah-valley-shooting-updates)** press-release hub — the canonical **official statement** index cited on
[Media Articles](./Media_Articles).

## Week-two motive framing

**ABC** and other outlets broadcast FBI-characterized **"obsession with the influencer"** language
about [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) ([ABC](https://abcnews.com/US/charlie-kirk-killing-tyler-robinson-obsession-influencer/story?id=125582085)). Robinson is **charged, not convicted**; FBI statements are **agency
characterizations**, not jury findings.

## Premature custody story

An official **suspect-in-custody** signal that was **later corrected** became its own media-trust
thread — documented on [Legal — Official Statements](/Legal/Official-Statements) and echoed in
[Sept 10 Breaking News](./september-10-breaking-news).

## What official media did not release

Investigators note ongoing absence of:

* **FBI Form 302** interview reports (site-wide citation)
* Full **device / Discord** custody logs ([discovery](/court/discovery-brady-disputes))
* Unedited **Miranda** video beyond defense exhibits ([mirandize](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview))

Citizen media filled the gap; mainstream desks mostly **did not** rebroadcast those threads in
primetime.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

FBI press conferences are treated as **narrative-setting events**. Citizen clips re-cut **enhanced photo** moments, **custody timing**, and the **Valhalla** line. Media that transcribes without probing **lab language** is criticized as stenography. See [FBI](/FBI/overview).
