# Fox News Coverage

**Fox News** (national) and **Fox 13** (Utah) occupy different lanes: national security and campus-
accountability storytelling versus **local courthouse** reporting. Tyler Robinson is **charged, not
convicted** throughout.

## Phase 1 — Event and security accountability

Fox published **exclusive photos** of the alleged sniper path and rooftop access weeks after the
shooting, framing **UVU security failures**:

* [Campus rooftop access scrutiny](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized)
* [Security chief: rooftops not covered as promised](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-security-chief-says-police-promise-cover-rooftops-failed-before-assassination)

These pieces kept **physical-site questions** in mainstream view — overlapping [UVU security](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps) and [Tent/paving](/Tent/overview) threads.

## Succession interview (research note)

The master investigation file flags strange event **#7**: a **TPUSA replacement figure** appeared on
**Fox within one to four hours** of Kirk's death. See dedicated page
[TPUSA Replacement Fox Interview](./tpusa-replacement-fox-interview). We document the **media timing**
claim; we do not assert the interviewee knew of the killing in advance.

## Fox 13 Utah — gag order and courthouse

Local reporting described **heavy courthouse security**, lawyers **declining comment**, and scheduling
hearings under the **December 2025 gag order** ([court gag](/court/gag-orders-sealing)). Cross-link
[Utah Local Press](./utah-local-press).

## Prosecutor contempt connection

Deputy County Attorney **Christopher Ballard** reportedly made case-strength statements to **Fox**
(among TMZ and USA Today) that contributed to a **June 2026 contempt** finding — documented court
action, not an accusation of prosecutor criminality ([AP](https://apnews.com/article/7b3e65b0c7b12a06828e4f8f0337cb29)).
