broadcast, local Utah desks, tabloids, platforms, and citizen investigators on X. This memo routes
readers without rating outlet guilt.

## Coverage phases

1. **Sept 10–11** — manhunt live blogs, premature FBI custody correction, political-violence framing.
2. **Suspect week** — Robinson texts/Discord, family-tip arrest, obsession motive ([WaPo interactive](https://www.washingtonpost.com/investigations/interactive/2025/tyler-robinson-charlie-kirk-shooting/)).
3. **Society backlash** — Reuters **600+** workplace actions; NYT **145+** firings vignettes.
4. **Memorial politics** — Medal of Freedom, National Day, [Trump](/People/donald-trump)/[Vance](/People/jd-vance) legacy speeches.
5. **2026 court press** — camera access, ATF unsealing, **Ballard contempt** (TMZ/Fox/USA Today), seal-fight coalition.

## Highest-signal media disputes

* **Inconclusive ballistics** — PBS explainer vs prosecutor "ample evidence" media tour → June contempt ([trial press](./tyler-robinson-trial-press)).
* **March 2026 sealing** — Deseret News, Tribune, AP, NYT, Fox, CBS coalition vs defense seal ([court gag](/court/gag-orders-sealing)).
* **Succession optics** — TPUSA replacement on **Fox within hours** of death ([replacement interview](./tpusa-replacement-fox-interview)).
* **Platform + gag** — dual speech restrictions ([Censorship](./Censorship), [social platforms](./social-media-platforms)).
