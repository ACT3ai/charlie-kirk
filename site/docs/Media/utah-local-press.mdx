# Utah Local Press

National wires rebroadcast Utah facts; **local desks** own **courthouse geography**, **Governor Cox**
quotes, and **UVU institutional** follow-ups. Tyler Robinson is **charged, not convicted**.

## Deseret News

**Deseret News** (LDS Church–owned, Salt Lake City) appears in two high-signal roles:

1. **Cartridge engraving reporting** — quoted Governor **Spencer Cox** on markings on recovered
   ammunition, including phrases reported as *"Notices, bulges, OwO what's this?"*, *"Hey fascist!
   CATCH!"*, and *"O Bella ciao…"* lyrics on an unfired casing (per research file summary).

2. **March 2026 transparency litigation** — lead member of the **news coalition** opposing Tyler
   Robinson's motion to seal filings discussing the **inconclusive ATF** report (Case **251403576**).
   Docket references include defense response to **Deseret News, et al.** intervention (**Dkt 197**,
   Dec 2025).

## Salt Lake Tribune

**The Salt Lake Tribune** joined the same **anti-sealing coalition** per research notes — arguing
that if defense characterizes ATF material as **exculpatory**, public access should follow
([gag & sealing](/court/gag-orders-sealing)).

## Fox 13 Salt Lake City

**Fox 13** supplied recurring **Fourth District courthouse** color:

* Heavy security even when Robinson appeared remotely.
* Lawyers **declining comment** under gag restrictions.
* Scheduling and gag-order context for national readers who will never visit Provo.

## KSL and broadcast affiliates

Surrender-timeline reporting cites **local TV** alongside ABC, AP, and Fox national when describing
Washington County Sheriff **Nate Brooksby** statements and the Sept 11–12 arrest sequence
([Timeline](/Timeline/Tyler_Robinson/2025_Sept)).

## Utah News Dispatch

**Utah News Dispatch** reported a **formal review** of the shooting and **UVU campus security**
expected to start in **2026** ([article](https://utahnewsdispatch.com/2025/12/01/review-of-charlie-kirk-shooting-uvu-campus-security-expected-to-start-in-2026/)) — policy-process coverage national desks skipped.

## Why local press matters to investigators

* Closer to **GRAMA** and **state executive** threads ([UVU GRAMA](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)).
* First to print **Utah County Attorney** courtroom culture details.
* Coalition litigation ties **local + national** brands on **sealing** — rare cross-tier alignment.
