Media coverage is where most of the public first encountered this case, and how it was framed early on still drives what most people believe happened. Mainstream outlets supplied the factual backbone — the UVU shooting, Charlie's death, the law-enforcement response, and the charging of Tyler Robinson — alongside legal-process updates, workplace-retaliation stories, and memorial politics including the posthumous Presidential Medal of Freedom. Independent journalists, podcasters, and citizen investigators pushed further into ballistics disputes, flight paths, leaked-text motive theories, and allegations that footage was deleted or discussions were gag-ordered.

The substance of this section spans both what was published and what may have been kept from publication. [Media Articles](./Media_Articles) is the curated index tracing claims back to wire services, major papers, and broadcast pieces. [Media Censorship](./Censorship) collects gag-order reporting, platform moderation claims, and witness delete-video allegations. [Media Response](./Media_Response) tracks the press arc from September 10 live blogs through 2026 contempt coverage. Topic pages on [Guardian Kirk Beliefs](./Guardian-Kirk-Beliefs), [Candace Owens Israel Controversies](./Candace-Owens-Israel-Controversies), [Israeli Agenda Criticism](./Israeli-Agenda-Criticism), and [Wikipedia Coverage](./Wikipedia-Coverage) show how reference desks, international papers, and influencer disclosures framed the same events differently. Memorial-politics coverage — [Presidential Medal of Freedom](./Presidential-Medal-of-Freedom), [National Day Remembrance](./National-Day-Remembrance), [Trump Votes Speech](./Trump-Votes-Speech), and [Vance Mission Continuation](./Vance-Mission-Continuation) — sits alongside criminal-process reporting, not in place of it.

Start with [Media Articles](./Media_Articles) if you want a fast map of who reported what, then [News Sources Analysis](./News-Sources-Analysis) and [Mainstream vs Alternative Media](./Mainstream-vs-Alternative-Media) to see which questions each ecosystem emphasizes. Use [Media Timeline Compilation](./Media-Timeline-Compilation) when comparing timestamps across official, mainstream, and citizen chronologies. Move to [Media Censorship](./Censorship) and [Leaked Kirk Texts](./Leaked-Kirk-Texts) when evaluating whether the public record may be incomplete. Because verification standards vary widely, the goal here is not to rate outlets but to point toward primary footage, court filings, and official releases wherever possible.
