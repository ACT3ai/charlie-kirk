Media is where the government's **bullet-killed-Charlie-Kirk** story became **common knowledge** before
ballistics, audio, and flight data had adversarial testing. September 10–11 **live blogs** fixed a
template — political assassination, rooftop shooter, manhunt — while a **premature FBI custody**
correction showed how official channels can mislead rebroadcasters ([breaking news](./september-10-breaking-news),
[FBI press](./fbi-press-conference-media)). [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **charged, not convicted** in all trial
coverage referenced here.

The **2026 press arc** shifted to courtroom ethics: a **December 2025 gag order**, a **March 2026**
**Deseret News / Tribune / AP / NYT / Fox / CBS** coalition opposing defense **sealing** of an
**inconclusive ATF** report, and **June 2026 contempt** over prosecutor comments to **TMZ, Fox, and
USA Today** ([trial press](./tyler-robinson-trial-press)). **PBS** supplied rare **forensic literacy**
on what inconclusive ballistics does and does not mean — while **citizen investigators on X and YouTube**
pushed Miranda timing, discovery dumps, and alternate-shooter threads mainstream desks deferred
([social platforms](./social-media-platforms), [Mainstream vs Alternative](./Mainstream-vs-Alternative-Media)).

Parallel tracks matter: **memorial politics** (Medal of Freedom, National Day, [Trump](/People/donald-trump)/[Vance](/People/jd-vance) legacy
speeches) and **society backlash** (Reuters **600+** workplace actions over Kirk-related posts) ran
alongside criminal-process reporting without replacing it. A flagged **TPUSA replacement Fox interview
within hours** of death sits in the succession-media bucket ([TPUSA Fox timing](./tpusa-replacement-fox-interview))
— documented as optics research, not proof anyone knew of the killing in advance.
