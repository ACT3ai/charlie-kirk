# Foreign Leads Blocked

The [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation reportedly had two contradictory federal postures on
**foreign nexus** leads: public statements that foreign intelligence was assisting,
and on-record claims that the FBI **shut down** a counterterrorism inquiry pursuing
those same threads. This page documents both sides. We do not assert that any
official blocked a legitimate investigation or acted illegally.

## The Joe Kent Statement

**Joe Kent**, then director of the [National Counterterrorism Center (NCTC)](/US_Intelligence/nctc),
stated publicly:

> "Multiple people had pre-knowledge of Charlie Kirk's assassination and the FBI
> shut down my investigation before I was able to run down all of my leads."

See the dedicated [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) page and
[Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt) for full attribution and context.

Kent stepped down from NCTC to speak publicly — an unusual move that investigators
treat as significant because his account is **on the record** and **attributable**,
not anonymous X speculation.

His seniority is the point. The reported chain of command runs directly to the
top:

- **Joe Kent** (Director) → National Counterterrorism Center (NCTC)
- **NCTC** → Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI)
- **ODNI** → President of the United States

A shutdown at that level, if documented, would not be a local field-office
decision. The record also ties Tulsi Gabbard (as DNI) to the same friction, and
points to [New York Times reporting](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/10/28/us/politics/fbi-files-charlie-kirk-case.html)
and a [Candace Owens post](https://x.com/RealCandaceO/status/1983328053519688164)
framing Patel as wanting to be "the sole point person on this."

## Benny Johnson: "State Actors"

Podcaster **Benny Johnson**, who describes himself as very close to Kirk and his
team, stated on camera:

> "There is some considerable evidence that there were state actors involved here.
> That's it. I don't want to go any further than that. I can't go any further than
> that."

The research record notes that the day after these remarks, Johnson reportedly met
with [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) and [Pam Bondi](/People/pam-bondi) over death threats. Source: quote-tweet by
[@CatQuestionsAll](https://x.com/CatQuestionsAll/status/2071736376215920842),
June 29, 2026. Johnson's phrasing — "considerable evidence," then a hard stop —
sits directly next to the Joe Kent shutdown thread. It is attributed testimony,
not a finding.

## Kash Patel Counter-Narrative

Commentary attributed to **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** and others claims FBI Director
**Kash Patel** was upset that Kent "went through FBI material" on this case and
needed the parallel inquiry shut down. Posts also claim the FBI told **local law
enforcement** not to pursue certain lines. These are allegations, not court findings.

## "Foreign Intel Assisting" Public Messaging

The research record notes FBI public messaging that **foreign intelligence was
assisting** the investigation — while Kent's account says foreign-nexus leads
were not fully run down. The tension between those two public postures is the core
question: assistance versus shutdown.

subpoenas, and 302 interviews — and which were closed without public explanation.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Attributed)

**@LucyRiles** (July 2026) asked why the FBI would shut down **Joe Kent**'s look into foreign entanglements. **@GreatUnwokening** packaged the Kent foreign-investigation block with paving, .30-06 energy doubts, mic-case fragments, and chain-of-custody disputes—explicitly leaving guilt to a jury.

High-engagement **@FurkanGozukara** posts present Kent saying multiple people had pre-knowledge and that FBI stopped the inquiry before leads were exhausted. **Tucker Carlson** podcast coverage has hosted Kent-class guests questioning whether the lone-actor story is complete (as discussed in secondary X posts).

A minority **counter-thread** (**@CogitoInGa**) argues Kent improperly amplified foreign/Israel theories while in office—also unproven; recorded for balance.

None of these posts establish that any foreign service or living person ordered Kirk's death. They show investigators treat the Kent shutdown as **unresolved** and demand lead lists and shutdown orders via [Fix laws](/Fix/overview).
