# Hospital Footage Seizure

Commentary in the investigation record alleges the FBI **seized interior hospital
surveillance footage** from [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Medical/Hospital/overview)
while the manhunt was still active outside — and while questions remained about
Kirk's transport, the cleared emergency room, and staff NDAs. This page documents
those reported claims. We do not assert the seizure was improper or that any
official acted illegally.

## Why Hospital Footage Is Federal-Stewardship Evidence

If Kirk was transported to Timpanogos Regional (a disputed point covered under
[Medical](/Medical/overview)), hospital cameras would capture:

- Arrival time and condition of the patient
- Who entered treatment areas and when
- Whether the ER lobby was cleared and by whose authority
- Movement of evidence-like materials (blood, fragments, clothing)

Federal seizure of that footage places the FBI in the chain of custody for some of
the only interior video of the post-shooting medical response.

## Reported Claims

Sources in the record include:

- **[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)** — FBI seizure of hospital footage
- [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed) —
  whether footage exists or was released
- [Hospital Scene & EMS](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems) — cleared ER, NDAs, EMS blocked at UVU

The master file asks: *Who has authority to lock down an ER in that manner, and
why was the FBI allegedly so quick to seize interior hospital footage while an
active manhunt was still underway outside?*

## Standard Protocol vs. Reported Conduct

Hospitals routinely cooperate with law enforcement on preservation requests after
violent crimes. The investigatory question is whether a **federal seizure** occurred
**before** local medical examiners and defense counsel could request copies, and
whether any public inventory of seized media exists.

## Open Questions

- What legal instrument authorized FBI seizure of hospital surveillance media?
- What date/time range was seized, and has any portion been released?
- Does seized footage show the transport patient was Charlie Kirk?
- Were hospital staff asked to sign NDAs before or after the seizure?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The FBI seizure inventory for Timpanogos hospital cameras, the legal instrument authorizing it, and any unreleased interior footage are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
