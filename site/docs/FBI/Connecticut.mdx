# FBI Connecticut

A recurring thread in the research record holds that the **FBI's Connecticut
office** — not the Utah field office alone — played a role in the Charlie Kirk
investigation. Commentators tie this to the reported co-location of FBI and **ADL**
(Anti-Defamation League) facilities in Connecticut and to questions about why an
eastern field office would deploy to a Utah homicide. This page documents those
reported claims. We do not assert improper motive or illegal conduct by any person
or organization.

## Why Connecticut Appears in the Record

The master investigation file's strange-events list includes:

> FBI Connecticut building and ADL on the same floor.

[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) commentary, relayed in the record, reportedly asked which state FBI
office was involved and cited the Connecticut bureau investigating the UVU
assassination while noting FBI and ADL sharing office space in Connecticut.

## Reported Deployment Questions

If Connecticut FBI personnel traveled to Utah for this case, investigators want:

- Travel orders and TDY authorizations
- Case number assignment and which SAC owned the file
- Whether Connecticut's role was primary, advisory, or parallel to
  [Salt Lake City Field Office](./SLC_Field_Office) under **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)**

Utah is normally within SLC field-office jurisdiction. An eastern office taking a
visible role would require a documented reason — joint terrorism task force,
digital forensics specialty, or national-security overlay.

## ADL Co-Location Note

The ADL co-location claim is cited in citizen-investigator commentary exploring
[Israel](/Israel/overview) and [censorship](/Censorship/overview) angles. Co-location
of federal and nonprofit offices in a shared building is not itself evidence of
coordination on a criminal case; it is a lead that warrants FOIA and lease-record
verification, not inference.

## How to Verify

- FBI field-office org charts and press releases naming personnel deployed to Utah
- GSA or commercial lease records for the cited Connecticut address
- Travel vouchers and JTTF rosters for September 2025
- FBI Form 302 header pages showing interviewing office of record

- What case file number did Connecticut use versus SLC?
