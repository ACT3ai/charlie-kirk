# Crime Scene Paving

Contractor **Dan Merrell** gave an on-camera account, amplified on X, that he was
called to pave over the UVU courtyard area where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed — and that
on-site coordinators cited orders from the **Utah governor** and **[Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)'s
FBI** requiring the work be done by Monday. This page documents that reported
account. We do not assert that paving destroyed evidence or that any official
ordered obstruction.

## Why This Is an FBI Thread

Physical alteration of a homicide scene within days of the killing is rare in
high-profile cases. When the contractor's account names the **FBI** alongside the
governor as the chain-of-authority, the question lands in the federal bucket: who
tasked the work, under what case number, and what forensic sweep occurred first?

The dedicated [UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) and [Tent](/Tent/overview) pages cover
the contractor narrative in more site-alteration context. This page focuses on the
**FBI attribution** in Merrell's account.

## Merrell's Reported Statements

From the research record (ShadowofEzra X video and related posts):

- Merrell (of **Hardscape Utah**) reportedly says he **installed the pavers on
  September 14, 2025** — four days after the killing — after receiving "the call"
  under an emergency order.
- He reportedly says he was called by "men who work for the state of Utah."
- Merrell was told: *"This is above our pay grade... the governor and the FBI
  said they want this by Monday."*
- On-site coordinator: *"We're not talking to anybody. We're not saying anything
  to the media."*
- The urgency was reportedly framed as coming from both **Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox)**
  and the **FBI under Kash Patel**.

Merrell's first-person video account adds operational specifics investigators can
test against employment records. He reportedly says he corresponded with **two
coordinators** — *"one of the guys was in charge of a state-owned facility in
American Fork, and the other guy was the maintenance groundskeeper on a facility in
Provo"* — describing them as running "a ragtag crew." On proper procedure he notes
that "to do pavers correctly you over-excavate, you get rid of the top" for a good
base — the same soil-removal step that, under the [exploding-microphone](/Mic/overview)
and shaped-charge theories, would strip explosive residue that is highly
soil-absorbed. Commentators also stress that the work was done on a **Sunday in Utah
County**, where "even gas stations are closed, gas pumps are turned off and vending
machines have been known to be deactivated" — framing weekend paving as unusual
enough to warrant explanation.

These are contractor statements relayed through social media, not sworn testimony.

## Reported Israeli-IP Search Claim

Consistent with the pattern alleged for FBI and judicial names elsewhere in the
record, commentators claim **Daniel Merrell** and "Hardscape Utah" drew unusual
**Google searches from Israeli IP addresses** in early-to-late **July 2025** —
weeks before the September 10 killing and before Merrell was publicly tied to the
UVU paving. As with the [SLC Field Office](./SLC_Field_Office) and
[Gag Orders](./Gag_Orders) search claims, this is an unverified open-source assertion
that needs search-log and IP-attribution corroboration before it means anything. See
[Israel](/Israel/overview).

## The Second Contractor Question

The record separately names **[Hardscape Specialties](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties) LLC** in connection with
paving the UVU tent area, noting the company was reportedly **dormant for ~20
years and revived in February 2025** — months before the incident — and that its
owner, **[Michael Powell](/People/michael-powell)**, is described as a **Utah Attorney General special
agent**. If a state law-enforcement officer's revived company was involved in
altering the scene, that would raise the same authorization and chain-of-custody
questions in the state bucket rather than the federal one. This is a reported
open-source claim; business-registration and employment records would confirm or
refute it. See [Lanscaping cement under Tent](/Tent/overview).

## Historical Comparison

Commentary compares rapid UVU paving to other cases where crime scenes were
altered quickly — including the reported hosing of the Thomas Crooks scene after
the Trump assassination attempt. Comparisons are illustrative, not proof of the
same intent here.
