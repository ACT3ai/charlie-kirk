# Israeli Phones & Bumblehive

The [NSA](/US_Intelligence/nsa)'s **Utah Data Center** ("**Bumblehive**") sits minutes from UVU. Reporting and insider-tip
threads allege **Israeli-registered cellphones** (and other foreign handsets) were present at the
courtyard when [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot — with some devices later traced to **diplomatic hotels**
during Iran peace talks. This page holds the **Proof Intel Services** framing; technical NSA agency
detail lives on [U.S. Intelligence — NSA](/US_Intelligence/nsa) and
[Israel — NSA phones](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive).

## Core claims (unverified until subscriber release)

| Source thread | Claim |
|---------------|--------|
| Admin insider tip (CK) | **12 Israeli-registered** phones at scene; data reached **White House** |
| [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) | Government keeping **12 Israeli phones** secret; public disclosure |
| **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** posts | **16 Israeli** + **44 foreign** phones (Israeli, Chinese, Russian, Iranian) near Kirk at assassination |
| Seruga follow-up | **4 of 16** Israeli phones later at **Islamabad Serena Hotel** during **US–Israel–Iran** talks |

Seruga argues Bumblehive **"cross-pollination"** deanonymizes **burner phones** within **30–60 days** —
so foreign handsets at UVU are not permanently anonymous to NSA. The facility he names is the **NSA Utah
Data Center in Bluffdale, Utah**, minutes north of UVU; per his posts, once a device is used **~30–60 days**,
proprietary algorithms link a "burner" to **every prior phone** its user owned "with almost 100% certainty."
His underlying claim: **raw GPS data** showed foreign-registered phones **right near Charlie Kirk** at the
moment of the assassination.

## Why this implicates intelligence services

If NSA collections show **foreign-government-linked devices** at the homicide window, then:

- **Foreign services** had personnel or assets on site (allegation), **or**
- NSA **knew** and did not publicly disrupt the event (cover allegation), **or**
- Claims are **wrong or exaggerated** (needs subscriber data release)

Any of those outcomes is an **intelligence story**, not a street-crime-only story.

## Serena Hotel thread

CK_FILE amplifies Seruga: four Israeli-linked devices from UVU reappear at the **Serena Hotel** in
**Islamabad** during active **Iran negotiations** — framed as "*accident narrative is dead*" in posts.
**No official confirmation** ties those handsets to [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad), UVU shooters, or diplomats; treat as
**open-source geolocation allegation**.
