# Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting

**Fort Huachuca, Arizona** is the U.S. Army's **intelligence and counterintelligence** school. CK_FILE
places **~12 lieutenant colonels** there **Sept 8**, a **SAM-702 VIP flight** on **Sept 9 morning**,
and **UVU/TPUSA-linked individuals** allegedly photographed on base the same week Charlie Kirk was
killed **Sept 10**. This page summarizes **timing indicators**; deep dive: [U.S. Intelligence — Cabot](/US_Intelligence/Cabot_alibi).

## SAM-702 VIP flight — Sept 9

[Candace Owens](https://x.com/RealCandaceO) threads name **Bradley D. Hansell** — Trump's **Under Secretary
of War for Intelligence and Security** — as one of **four passengers** on **SAM-702** leaving Fort Huachuca
**morning of Sept 9, 2025**.

Hansell's resume (public): Army Special Forces, **Boston Consulting Group**, NSC, defense intelligence
oversight — including **HADES** per CK notes linking to [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview). CK_FILE states
Hansell **"is in charge of the HADES program,"** which is **run out of Biggs AFB — where personnel went
after Fort Huachuca."** That is the same **Biggs Army Airfield (KBIF)** from which N1098L departed on Sept 10,
tying the intelligence-school meeting to the ISR jet over UVU.
