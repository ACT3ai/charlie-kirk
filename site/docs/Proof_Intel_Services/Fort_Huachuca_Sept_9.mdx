# Fort Huachuca — Sept 9 Meeting

**[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview), Arizona** is the U.S. Army's **intelligence and counterintelligence** school. CK_FILE
places **~12 lieutenant colonels** there **Sept 8**, a **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) VIP flight** on **Sept 9 morning**,
and **UVU/TPUSA-linked individuals** allegedly photographed on base the same week [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was
killed **Sept 10**. This page summarizes **timing indicators**; deep dive: [U.S. Intelligence — Cabot](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi).

## SAM-702 VIP flight — Sept 9

[Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) threads allege that one of the **four passengers** on
**SAM-702** leaving Fort Huachuca the **morning of Sept 9, 2025** was
**[Bradley D. Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** — Trump's **Under Secretary of War for Intelligence and
Security**. That passenger identification is Owens's allegation. **No released flight manifest, passenger
list, or base visitor log confirms who was aboard**, and this site does not assert it as fact.

Hansell's resume (public): Army Special Forces, **Boston Consulting Group**, NSC, defense intelligence
oversight — including **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)** per CK notes linking to [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview). CK_FILE asserts
Hansell **"is in charge of the HADES program,"** which is **run out of Biggs AFB — where personnel went
after Fort Huachuca."** That is the same **Biggs Army Airfield (KBIF)** from which N1098L departed on Sept 10 —
a link investigators cite to connect the intelligence-school meeting to the ISR jet over UVU. It is an
**organizational** overlap, not evidence that any tasking existed.

## Sept 8 LTC meeting

CK timeline asserts **~12 Lieutenant Colonels** at Fort Huachuca; **SAM702** departs **Sept 9 @ 0:49**
(timestamp in notes). No released visitor log or attendee list confirms who, if anyone, met there.

## Strange event #5 — administration visitors

A master-file "strange event" entry — an anonymous, unverified account — describes a
**Fort Huachuca "JTF"** visit:

- A **"rich woman"** "dressed down" — the account's author speculates this was **Karoline Leavitt** (unconfirmed; no record places her on the base)
- A **Secret Service** woman (Hispanic) who allegedly cared about **"the next 24 hours"**
- Allegedly departed with **Rep. Mark Amodei** (R-NV)

Treat every name in this entry as **unverified speculation by an anonymous source**. Nothing here places
any named person on the installation as a matter of record.

## Cabot Phillips — the carpet-comparison claim

[@AMeadowInquiry](/Influencers/x/ameadowinquiry) posted a side-by-side comparing **Cabot Phillips**'s alibi
video flooring/trim to photos the poster identifies as **Fort Huachuca interiors** — arguing the video was
shot **on or near** the intelligence base. [Cabot page](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi) holds the
images. Government-contract interior finishes repeat across countless federal buildings; a **visual
resemblance is not** a geolocation, and this site makes no assertion that Phillips was at Fort Huachuca.

## TPUSA figures at Huachuca?

Posts allege **[Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown)** (standing mic) and **[Jeb Jacobi](/People/jeb-jacobi)** were photographed at Fort Huachuca after UVU —
and question why **"Come Back America"** volunteers would appear at an **intel installation**. The photo
identifications are **unverified**; presence in a photograph on a public Army post would not, in any event,
establish wrongdoing.
