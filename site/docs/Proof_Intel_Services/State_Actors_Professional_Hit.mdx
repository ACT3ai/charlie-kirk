# State Actors & Professional Hit

Some observers argue [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s killing displays **professional execution** and **state-level**
aftermath management — not a spontaneous campus shooting. The most cited **on-camera** U.S. source
in this bucket is commentator **[Benny Johnson](/Influencers/podcasts-benny-johnson)**, who says he is **"very, very close to Charlie and his team."**
We present **reported statements** only; we do **not** identify which state or agency.

## Benny Johnson transcript (attributed)

From video shared June 29, 2026:

> "Yes, there's reason for people to believe that this is a professional hit job. John Solomon is an
> excellent reporter, and [Steven Crowder](/People/steven-crowder) has some leaked information… I'm very, very close to Charlie
> and his team… there is some considerable evidence that there were **state actors involved** here.
> That's it. I don't want to go any further than that. I can't go any further than that."

[@CatQuestionsAll](https://x.com/CatQuestionsAll/status/2071736376215920842) paired the clip with:
*"Remember our government stopped Joe Kent from investigating foreign involvement."*

Unverified follow-on claim: Johnson reportedly met **[Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)** and **[Pam Bondi](/People/pam-bondi)** the next day over
death threats — **reported**, not confirmed here.
