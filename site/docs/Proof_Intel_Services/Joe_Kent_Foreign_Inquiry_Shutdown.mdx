# Joe Kent — Foreign Inquiry Shutdown

The strongest **on-record U.S. intelligence thread** in this investigation is not anonymous X
analysis — it is **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)** stating that as **NCTC director** he
opened an inquiry into **foreign involvement** in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death and the **FBI halted** it
before foreign-nexus leads were exhausted. We document **Kent's public claims** and reported
administration reactions. We do **not** assert any agency committed murder.

## What Kent says happened

Per Kent's interviews and the dedicated [NCTC page](/US_Intelligence/nctc):

1. **NCTC** under [ODNI](/US_Intelligence/odni) began tracing **foreign-government** angles in the killing.
2. The **FBI** stopped the inquiry — Kent says he was not allowed to finish.
3. Reporting alleges **[Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)** was upset Kent **"went through FBI material"** on this case and moved to **shut** the effort.

The reporting chain, per CK_FILE, runs: **Joe Kent (Director) → NCTC → Office of the Director of National
Intelligence (ODNI) → President of the United States**. CK cites a **New York Times**-type article framing
Kash Patel as upset that **Kent and [Tulsi Gabbard](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard)** were **trying to do
their jobs** — with commentators asking why Patel **"wants to be the sole point person on this."**

Kent's account is **first-person from a serving intelligence official** (at the time), not citizen
speculation.

## Why the White House reportedly cared

CK_FILE cites reporting that **Trump administration officials** worried Kent's probe could give
**[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s defense** ammunition to argue **more than one suspect** or **foreign orchestration**
— i.e. the foreign thread was politically dangerous to the **lone-gunman** frame.

That incentive structure — if accurate — is itself an **intel-adjacent cover indicator**: stopping
foreign-lead exhaustion while prosecuting capital charges.

## Benny Johnson "state actors" remark

Commentator **[Benny Johnson](/Influencers/podcasts-benny-johnson)** (close to Charlie's team per his statement) said in a June 2026 video
there is **"considerable evidence that there were state actors involved"** — see
[State Actors page](./State_Actors_Professional_Hit). The account sharing the clip tied it to the
government **stopping Joe Kent** from investigating foreign involvement.

Johnson did not name agencies or countries on camera; treat as **reported opinion** from a commentator.

## What shutdown does not prove

Halting NCTC does **not** prove [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad), Egypt, or [CIA](/US_Intelligence/cia) killed Kirk. It proves — if Kent is accurate —
that **the federal government refused to complete** a foreign-nexus investigation publicly. That gap
is what **Proof Intel Services** catalogs.
