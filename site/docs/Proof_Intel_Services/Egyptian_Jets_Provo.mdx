# Egyptian Jets — Provo Sept 4–10

**Egyptian-government** aircraft — especially tail **SU-BTT** (Dassault Falcon 7X) and companion **SU-BND** —
appear in **ADS-B** and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) tracker threads as **on the ground in Provo**
the week Charlie Kirk was killed. This is among the strongest **foreign-government presence** indicators
in **Proof Intel Services**. We document **flight facts** and **intent theories** separately.

## Timeline at Provo (reported)

| Date | SU-BTT activity (citizen logs / planes.yaml) |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **July 20, 2025** | First documented **U.S. visit** — Nebraska **army base** context |
| **Sept 4, 2025** | **Inbound** to Provo (~6 days before shooting) |
| **Sept 10, 2025 (morning)** | **Departure** from Provo area — assassination **day** |
| **Sept 10–11** | Continued routing (e.g. **Wilmington**, **Egypt**) per tracker posts |

**SU-BND** ("blue plane"): transponder **ON** but **did not take off** at a key moment per CK strange-event **#37**.

## Erika/Charlie overlap claims

Plane trackers allege **68–73 location overlaps** between **SU-BTT/SU-BND** and **Erika Kirk** travel
2022–Sept 2025 (numbers vary by post). Proponents call overlap **"statistically near impossible without intent."**
Critics note **routine maintenance** stops (e.g. **Wichita** aviation hubs). See [Planes — SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT).

## Dugway stealth departure allegation

[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) video claims SU-BTT actually launched from **Dugway Proving Ground**
with transponder **OFF** — mislabeled as Provo departure. **Unverified**; would imply **military-restricted**
airspace use.

## Why Egypt matters to intel narrative

Egyptian military jets on a **civilian campus assassination week** raise:

- **Foreign intelligence liaison** or **surveillance** (theory),
- Joint **Israel–Egypt** routing (Cairo–Israel flight logs cited for intelligence chiefs),
- **Coincidental diplomatic maintenance** (counter-theory)

Egyptian government has **not** confirmed operational purpose at UVU.

## Strange event #31 — contractors stayed

CK lists: Egyptian flight got **gov clearance to skip security** at Provo; **4–6 "contractors"**;
**did NOT fly away**; **Counter-UAS** testing **Sept 4–10**; badges **"US Department of Defense Liaison."**
See [Provo Airport Counter-UAS](./Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS).
