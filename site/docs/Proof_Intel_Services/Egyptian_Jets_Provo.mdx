# Egyptian Jets — Provo Sept 4–10

**Egyptian-government** aircraft — especially tail **SU-BTT** (Dassault Falcon 7X) and companion **SU-BND** —
appear in **ADS-B** and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) tracker threads as **on the ground in Provo**
the week [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed. This is among the strongest **foreign-government presence** indicators
in **Proof Intel Services**. We document **flight facts** and **intent theories** separately.

## Timeline at Provo (reported)

| Date | SU-BTT activity (citizen logs / planes.yaml) |
|------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **May 27, 2025** | **SU-BTU** flies into Provo — **[Walid Mahmoud](/People/walid-mahmoud)** aboard (departs June 2) |
| **July 20, 2025** | First **U.S. visit EVER** — to an **army base in Nebraska** (Omaha→Lincoln) |
| **Sept 4, 2025** | **Inbound** to Provo — routed **Paris → Minot ND → Provo** (~6 days before shooting) |
| **Sept 10, 2025 @ ~7:08 MT** | **Departure** from Utah to **Wilmington, DE** — ~5 hours before the assassination |
| **Sept 11, 2025** | Leaves the U.S. for **Egypt (Cairo)**, again via **Wilmington** |

**SU-BND** ("blue plane"): transponder **ON** but **did not take off** at a key moment per CK strange-event **#37**.
CK also logs companion Egyptian tails **SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM** in the flight-overlap discussions.

## Other suspicious flights that week

CK_FILE flags two more aircraft that Candace Owens' trackers surfaced:

- **N560TW** — **Sept 10** from **Scottsdale** to **Provo**, then turned around to **Santa Barbara** and back to Scottsdale (strange event #39)
- **N888KG** — a white private jet (owner **N888KG LLC**, registered **Lehi, UT**; **[Derek Maxfield](/People/derek-maxfield)** listed in some databases) that **departed Provo ~7:12 PM MDT**, roughly **one hour after** the shooting. The tail number **n888kg** was **searched on Google Trends in Utah on Sept 9** — the day *before* the killing.

## Erika/Charlie overlap claims

Plane trackers allege **68–73 location overlaps** between **SU-BTT/SU-BND** and **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** travel
2022–Sept 2025 (numbers vary by post). Proponents call overlap **"statistically near impossible without intent."**
Critics note **routine maintenance** stops (e.g. **Wichita** aviation hubs). See [Planes — SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT).

## Dugway stealth departure allegation

[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) video claims SU-BTT actually launched from **Dugway Proving Ground**
with transponder **OFF** — mislabeled as Provo departure. **Unverified**; would imply **military-restricted**
airspace use.

## Why Egypt matters to intel narrative

Egyptian military jets on a **civilian campus assassination week** raise:

- **Foreign intelligence liaison** or **surveillance** (theory),
- Joint **Israel–Egypt** routing (Cairo–Israel flight logs cited for intelligence chiefs),
- **Coincidental diplomatic maintenance** (counter-theory)

Egyptian government has **not** confirmed operational purpose at UVU.

## Strange event #31 — contractors stayed

CK lists: Egyptian flight got **gov clearance to skip security** at Provo; **4–6 "contractors"**;
**did NOT fly away**; **Counter-UAS** testing **Sept 4–10**; badges **"US Department of Defense Liaison."**
Sources at **Duncan Aviation** reportedly confirmed the contractors boarded with **Egyptian passports**,
and two possible passenger names surfaced — **Maj. [Ahmed Khalil](/People/ahmed-khalil)** and **Eng. [Omar Nassar](/People/omar-nassar)** (Egyptian Army
Intel, per an uncorroborated witness). Provo Tower (PVU) logs allegedly show a **"no-scan priority clearance"**
via **federal override** on Sept 4 with **no passenger manifest recorded**. All of this remains
**unconfirmed witness testimony**. See [Provo Airport Counter-UAS](./Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS).
