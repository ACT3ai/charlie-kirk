# UVU Sept 9 Foreknowledge

**Proof Intel Services** includes **institutional foreknowledge** indicators — not only planes and phones.
A **GRAMA document release** (amplified by [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305))
describes **two separate Sept 9, 2025 warnings** before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot on Sept 10 — with **names
redacted** and **no documented follow-through**.

## Warning #1 — "a plan" (10:35 PM, Sept 9)

Per released chat logs (UVU 25-330:0023):

- Event Services staff reported **two people** arrived asking to speak with **police about "a plan"**
- Initially described as tied to **"two senators"** coming tomorrow
- **11:19 PM walk-back:** identified as **"GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did"**
- **Nobody documented what the plan was**

## Warning #2 — message for Charlie (2:23 PM, Sept 9)

Separate email through **UVU Police Chief [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)'s** chain:

- Subject: **"FYI"**
- Body: someone **"wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk"** — **name and phone redacted**
- **Undocumented** whether message was delivered

## Other GRAMA red flags (same release)

- **Orem PD** sent Charlie Kirk **ticket link Sept 3** — later said **"not involved"** despite joint Public Order Unit
- **Run-Hide-Fight** alert **88 minutes** after shooting still had placeholder **"Shooter in [INSERT location]"**
- **First alert 19 min** after shooting: **"suspect in custody"** — **false**; ~**10,000 students** remained on campus
- **No ambulance** on site; **6 campus cops** for ~**3,000** crowd; faculty **no active-shooter training**
