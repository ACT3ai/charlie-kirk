The strongest **attributable U.S. thread** is [Joe Kent](./Joe_Kent_Foreign_Inquiry_Shutdown): NCTC opened a
**foreign-nexus** inquiry and the **FBI halted** it while the administration reportedly feared defense
ammunition for Robinson. Parallel **signals** threads cite **Israeli and foreign handsets** at the courtyard
([Bumblehive](./Israeli_Phones_Bumblehive)) and **Army HADES** jet **N1098L** at **204–600 ft** over Orem
**before and after** the shot ([N1098L](./N1098L_HADES_Over_UVU)). **Foreign government** aircraft (**SU-BTT**)
sat in **Provo Sept 4–10** with **Counter-UAS** contractors ([Egyptian jets](./Egyptian_Jets_Provo),
[Counter-UAS](./Provo_Airport_Counter_UAS)).

Cover signatures cluster in the **[FBI](./FBI_Intel_Cover_Signatures)** bucket — witness gag, bomb-dog routing,
gun-discovery sequence, paving, video issues — while **[UVU Sept 9 warnings](./UVU_Sept_9_Foreknowledge)** suggest
institutional **foreknowledge**. **[Benny Johnson](./State_Actors_Professional_Hit)** publicly cites **"state actors"**
without naming them; **[Israel motive](./Israel_Motive_Timeline)** and **[foreign hardware](./Foreign_Weapons_And_Hardware)**
threads supply **why** and **how** intel services might operate. **[Fort Huachuca](./Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9)** ties
**cabinet DOD intel** to the **day before** the killing.
