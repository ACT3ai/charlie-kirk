# Egyptian Crew Hotel (Claims)

[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reported receiving a tip from someone **directly
involved in the booking process** that occupants of the **Egyptian government private jets**
in Provo lodged at:

**La Quinta Inn & Suites — 521 W. University Parkway, Orem, UT 84058** (University Parkway location)

A second La Quinta exists at 1100 W 780 N, Orem; the tip specifies the **University Parkway**
property. This page documents the **allegation and investigative next steps** — not confirmed
guest identities or wrongdoing by any hotel employee or guest.

## Alleged stay window

Per Owens's public posts (summarized in CK_FILE):

- **Arrival context:** Egyptian jets including **[SU-BTT](./SU-BTT)** flew into Provo **September 4, 2025** — six days before the assassination.
- **Departure context:** Fleet activity continued through **September 13, 2025** (e.g., [SU-BND](./SU-BND) Sept 13 outbound leg).
- **Requested evidence:** Hotel lobby or exterior camera footage; employee memories of unusual guests.

Owens invited tips to **moretips@candaceowens.com** from nearby businesses or La Quinta staff.

## Why investigators care

If corroborated, hotel records would:

1. **Name passengers** linked to tail numbers already in ADS-B logs.
2. Connect **ground transport** ([Duncan Aviation](https://www.duncanaviation.aero/) rental-car claims on [Sept 10 timeline](./Sept10-Flight-Timeline)) to specific individuals.
3. Test the **maintenance-only** counter-narrative ([John Cullen](/Influencers/john-cullen)) vs. **multi-week staging** reading.

Proximity to UVU (~minutes from campus) makes the location **geographically salient**; proximity alone is not proof of participation in violence.

## Evaluation notes

- Single-source booking tip — not independently verified on this page.
- Hotel guest lists are **private**; lawful process or voluntary release required.
- Egyptian military or diplomatic travelers may have **routine** reasons to lodge near Provo airport.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

La Quinta guest registers, booking-system logs, and Egyptian manifest cross-matches are among
the records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may surface.
