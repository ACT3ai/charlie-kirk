# SU-BTT Egyptian Jet

An Egyptian-registered **Dassault Falcon 7X** (tail number SU-BTT) — referred to on Candace Owens's podcast as "the yellow plane" — is reported to have been on the ground in or near Provo, Utah from September 4 through the morning of September 10, 2025, departing the United States in the hours immediately before and after Charlie Kirk's assassination.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** SU-BTT
* **Type:** Dassault Falcon 7X (VIP/Gulfstream-class)
* **Registry:** Egyptian-registered (Egyptian Air Force / government use)
* **Color label in podcast coverage:** "Yellow plane"

## Flight history relevant to the case

* **July 20, 2025:** First trip ever made to the United States — to an Army base in Nebraska (Omaha → Lincoln corridor).
* **September 4, 2025:** Arrived Provo, UT via France (Paris routing).
* **September 10, 2025, 7:08 AM MT:** Departed Utah for Wilmington, DE — hours before the UVU shooting.
* **September 11, 2025:** Departed the United States for Egypt via Wilmington, DE.

## Long-term tracking pattern (2022–2025)

Beyond the September 2025 window, Candace Owens and independent plane trackers allege that SU-BTT showed a multi-year pattern of overlapping with the Kirk family's movements:

* According to Owens and the trackers, the jet allegedly tracked Erika Kirk's — and at times Charlie Kirk's — movements extensively from roughly 2022 through September 2025, with dozens of reported location overlaps.
* The reported overlaps are said to include international flights and legs connected to Israel.
* Trackers point to public ADS-B data that they say shows the aircraft appearing near the family's locations with unusual frequency.

These claims rest on public ADS-B records circulated by independent researchers and have not been independently confirmed. They are reported here as allegations made by the named trackers, not as established fact.

## Theories about the aircraft's role

Researchers covering SU-BTT have floated several interpretations of the pattern above, framed as open theories rather than conclusions:

* **Foreign surveillance** — that a foreign-government aircraft may have been monitoring Charlie Kirk, Erika Kirk, or TPUSA events.
* **A possible joint operation** — tied to the Wilmington routing discussed below.
* **Monitoring of Kirk/TPUSA events** — based on the reported overlap between the jet's flight history and TPUSA event geography.

Separately, while public flight logs cited above show a morning departure on September 10 (see the Dugway discussion below), at least one OSINT report instead claims SU-BTT departed the Provo area shortly *after* the assassination. The two accounts conflict, and neither has been independently verified.

## The Dugway departure claim

According to public reporting that builds on flight-tracker analysis, SU-BTT did not actually take off from Provo Municipal Airport on September 10:

* Trackers showed SU-BTT "materializing" mid-air near Utah with no recorded takeoff from PVU.
* The reported origin was **Dugway Proving Ground's Michael Army Airfield** — a restricted US military test site west of the Great Salt Lake.
* The aircraft is reported to have flown with its transponder OFF until clearing the R-6404 restricted airspace, then re-acquired tracking, with public trackers defaulting to the nearest public airport (Provo) as the displayed origin.

If accurate, this places an Egyptian Air Force jet on a restricted US military airfield in the days before a high-profile political assassination.

## The Wilmington destination

SU-BTT's September 10 destination — Wilmington, DE — has been linked in public reporting to **920 North King Street, One Rodney Square, Suite 404**, an address publicly associated over the years with the DEA, the Department of Justice, and the Secret Service. Researchers connect this to claims that a foreign dignitary may have been shuttled from Provo to Wilmington for a joint US/foreign-intelligence operation on the day of the assassination.

## Open questions

1. Who authorized an Egyptian Air Force jet to operate from Dugway Proving Ground?
2. Who were the passengers on SU-BTT during the September 4-11 US visit?
3. What was the purpose of the September 10 morning departure, hours before the UVU shooting?
4. What occurred at the Wilmington destination on the day of the assassination?

## Track This Aircraft

Research this aircraft's flight history yourself on both major flight-tracking sites. They differ in one important way:

* [FlightRadar24 — SU-BTT](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/su-btt) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list, so flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing.
* [ADS-B Exchange — SU-BTT](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?reg=SU-BTT) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD, so it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out.
