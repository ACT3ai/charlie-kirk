# September 10 Flight Timeline

This page correlates **public flight-tracking claims** with **witness timing statements**
on the day [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot at UVU. It does not assert that any aircraft participated
in the killing or that any living person lied — it documents **reported conflicts** that
investigators use to test the official timeline.

## Shooting anchor

- **~12:23 PM MDT** — Shot fired during Charlie's UVU courtyard event (see [Timeline](/Timeline/overview)).
- **[N1098L](./N1098L/overview)** — Reported low-altitude passes before and ~20–24 minutes after the shot.

## SU-BTT transponder window (claims)

Candace Owens's **"nuclear flight logs"** presentation (summarized in CK_FILE and
[Airport & Planes research notes](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)) alleges:

| Time (MT, claimed) | Event (claimed) |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| ~7:08 AM | Some logs show departure for Wilmington — **conflicts** with later window |
| ~9:05 AM | Power-up at Provo Municipal |
| ~9:05–10:34 AM | Transponder **dark** |
| ~12:40 PM + ~17 min | Transponder back on; idles on tarmac until ~1:29 PM |
| Morning (alternate) | [Dugway origin claim](./SU-BTT) — jet never departed Provo per OSINT thread |

These three departure narratives **cannot all be true**; see [SU-BTT](./SU-BTT) for detail.
We do **not** claim Egyptian personnel committed a crime.

## Hospital timing conflict (claims)

**[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)** (former Navy officer) reportedly stated on the record that **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)
arrived at Timpanogos Regional Hospital around 4:00 PM**. Commentary from
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) highlights a **logistical tension**:

- If Erika met **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** on the runway when **N40JD landed at ~4:40 PM**, then
  deplaning, an emotional tarmac meeting, and a ~20-minute drive (with manhunt traffic)
  would place hospital arrival **no earlier than ~5:15 PM** — not 4:00 PM.
- Owens argues both Turek and Kolvet may be telling the truth only if Erika arrived on an
  **earlier flight** — and **[N102DZ at 3:31 PM](./N102DZ)** is the tail that fits that math.

This is **citizen-investigator reasoning**, not a court finding. Erika Kirk is a living
person; we document the **timeline question** attributed to Owens and Turek, not a conclusion
of dishonesty.

## N102DZ / SU-BND ramp allegation

X thread attributed to **@AnaEscobarShow** claims N102DZ linked up with **[SU-BND](./SU-BND)**
at **Duncan Aviation** after the 3:30 PM landing, with SU-BND "still there on and running."
Co-location at a busy MRO field is **not** proof of coordination.

## Rental cars (claim)

Research notes allege **rental cars issued to unidentified passengers** from Egyptian jets
via Duncan Aviation on Sept 10. No manifests published; [Egyptian Crew Hotel](./Egyptian-Crew-Hotel)
tracks the parallel hotel tip.
