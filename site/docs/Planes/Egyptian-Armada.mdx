# Israel/Egyptian Plane Armada

Five Egyptian-registered VIP aircraft — **SU-BTT, SU-BND, SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM** — appear in 2025 flight logs operating in and out of **Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)**, about seven miles from Utah Valley University. Independent researchers describe the pattern as a **"five-plane armada"** of Israel/Egyptian Air Force Falcons and Gulfstreams routing from Cairo via Paris into Utah, with overlapping presence in the days around [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s September 10, 2025 assassination. This page is the hub for the fleet pattern; each tail has its own page linked below.

The framing comes from [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) (who color-coded the lead jets) and X/OSINT researchers including **@ProjectConstitu** (Project Constitution), **Sam Parker**, [**Ian Carroll**](/People/ian-carroll), **Ryan Matta**, and **John Cullen**. It is contested. The "armada" reading rests on public ADS-B records circulated by amateur trackers; several of the claimed overlaps with the Kirk family have been challenged as inaccurate, and at least one investigator close to the story — John Cullen — has pointed to ordinary maintenance contracts as the explanation. Both the surveillance theory and the innocent explanations are laid out below.

## Pattern of presence

* The aircraft are described in public reporting as **VIP Falcons and Gulfstreams from Cairo via Paris**, with multiple stops at Provo Municipal Airport throughout 2025.
* Researchers report that **SU-BTT and SU-BND** have been observed near **TPUSA events for years** before September 10, 2025, with the claimed overlaps spanning roughly **2022 through 2025**.
* Candace Owens's team claims a cumulative **73 documented occurrences near Erika Kirk's travel** and about **23 near Charlie Kirk** across the United States — Omaha, Wichita, Provo, plus domestic and international legs. These tallies are disputed (see fact-checks below) and are reported here as the trackers' allegations, not as established fact.
* The overlap count varies by source. Different X posts cite **73, 72, 70+, or 68** Erika-Kirk overlaps for the lead jets (SU-BTT and SU-BND). One widely shared post claimed: *"72 times those exact planes followed Erika Kirk… That is statistically near impossible without intent."* The inconsistency in the tallies is itself part of the dispute.
* **Candace Owens has clarified that no one is alleging the Kirks flew *on* these planes.** Per Owens, the claim is only that the aircraft were in the same cities or regions as Erika Kirk, Charlie Kirk, or a TPUSA event — not that either Kirk was a passenger. She has said she shared the screenshots and spreadsheets on her show, called the pattern suspicious, and **stated she sent the information to the FBI**.
* One independent analysis cited by researchers describes the US overlaps as concentrating in **five states — Missouri, Delaware, Utah, Nebraska, and Kansas.** This geographic clustering is offered by the trackers as supporting their theory; it has not been independently verified.
* Researchers credit **@nihilox** with compiling much of the raw flight-log data, which was then amplified by Project Constitution and others.
* A separate tracker tally circulated on X claims that the **blue plane (SU-BND) and yellow plane (SU-BTT)** together showed **68 overlaps with Erika Kirk's locations from 2022 through September 2025** — including overseas trips — of which **29 also coincided with Charlie Kirk**. Like the Owens figures, these are unverified researcher counts, not confirmed records.
* Some posters note the claimed overlaps begin around **2022**, which they say **aligns with the Kirk marriage timeline**, and that several overlaps involve **international/overseas legs** alongside the Kirks rather than only domestic stops.
* Researchers also report that the jets **routinely fly in and out of Israel**, a detail some threads fold into the broader "foreign-intel" framing. This is reported as a pattern observation, not as proof of any purpose.
* Some threads further allege that the lead jet **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** logged legs near **Dugway Proving Ground** — the U.S. Army chemical/biological test range in Utah — and cite recent stops at **Wichita, Kansas** as part of the same pattern. The Dugway claim is an unverified OSINT reading of public logs; the Wichita stops, by contrast, are explained by skeptics as routine Dassault servicing (see fact-checks below). Both are reported here as claims, not findings.

## Assassination-day transponder activity

Reported as a claim from the tracking threads, not a confirmed record:

* On the day of the assassination, researchers say one of the jets' **transponder came on around 9:05 AM and switched off around 10:34 AM**. They claim it **came back on roughly 17 minutes after the 12:40 PM shooting** and **stayed on until about 1:29 PM** while the aircraft sat on the tarmac/runway rather than flying.
* Posters have raised questions about whether the aircraft had **encrypted communications or satellite capability**, and whether it was **"waiting for someone."** These are open questions advanced by trackers, not established facts; transponder on/off behavior is also consistent with the mundane data and tracking artifacts described in the fact-checks below.
* A related claim, attributed on X to **@AnaEscobarShow**, alleges that **[N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ) — a jet associated in coverage with the Kirk party — "linked up" with SU-BND at Duncan Aviation** after N102DZ's own ~**3:30 PM** Provo landing, with SU-BND described as **"still there on and running."** This is an unverified reading of two aircraft parked at the same maintenance ramp; co-location at a busy MRO field is not, by itself, evidence of any coordination. See the [SU-BND page](/Planes/SU-BND) for detail.

## Theories about the fleet's role

Framed as open theories, each attributed to the people advancing it:

* **Foreign surveillance / intel-vacuum** — Candace Owens and Project Constitution argue the jets were "Deep State vacuums" allegedly harvesting phones, faces, and plates at TPUSA rallies, not maintenance stops. Owens has cited a Provo hangar she says shut in 2021 and a 2024 French lease as evidence she believes the "maintenance" account is a cover.
* **A possible joint operation** — tied to SU-BTT's Sept 10 routing to Wilmington, DE and unconfirmed "DoD Liaison" contractor claims at Duncan Aviation. See the [SU-BTT page](/Planes/SU-BTT) for that detail, which remains uncorroborated. As part of this framing, **Candace Owens posted a claim** that an Israel/Egyptian jet landed in Delaware and stayed the night of Sept 10, and that *"a foreign dignitary was picked up after arriving from Provo the morning of Charlie's assassination on the Egyptian plane, and then transferred to 920 King for some joint operation"* — a reference to **920 N. King St., Wilmington**, which she said houses a joint **DHS/FBI/DEA** office. This is presented strictly as Owens's posted allegation; it is unverified, names no individual, and asserts no proven wrongdoing.
* **Counter-drone or drone role** — John Cullen has speculated at points that one of the jets was associated with drones; this sits awkwardly beside his maintenance-contract explanation, and the disagreement shows the researchers themselves do not have a single account.
* **Israel / broader-plot links** — because researchers say the fleet routinely transits Israel, some threads extend the theory to alleged Israeli or other foreign-intelligence involvement, or to links with broader plots to monitor Erika Kirk, the wider family, and TPUSA. Some posters have **urged TPUSA to publicly address the flight-tracking story.** No evidence ties any specific tail to such a plot; this is reported framing, not fact.
* **Fringe / unverified variants (flagged as such).** A minority of X posts push the theory further, alleging **[Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad) ties** or other foreign-intel control of the fleet, and a few even allege **Erika Kirk's own involvement** or claim that **"files" have been released.** These are **unverified fringe conspiracy claims** with no substantiation, recorded here only to document what is circulating online. Erika Kirk is a living person; the defamation caution applies in full, and no claim of wrongdoing by her or any named individual is established.

## Innocent explanations and fact-checks

Balance is essential here. Several facts cut against the "armada" reading, and some come from the same investigative circle:

* **John Cullen's maintenance-contract dismissal.** Cullen has pointed to ordinary **maintenance contracts** as the explanation for the Israel/Egyptian jets at Provo — Falcon servicing through **Duncan Aviation**, whose Provo relationship for this kind of work is described as long-standing (reportedly dating to **1999**). Under this reading, a foreign VIP Falcon parked at a full-service MRO field is routine, not operational.
* **Duncan Aviation is a legitimate jet hub.** Duncan runs a full-service maintenance, repair, and overhaul facility at Provo Municipal Airport — airframe, engine, avionics, paint, and interior work. A foreign VIP Falcon or Gulfstream stopping there for service is not inherently suspicious.
* **A skeptic's audit of the overlaps.** At least one reviewer who pulled a year of FlightRadar history reported that **"5 of the 8"** overlaps Owens cited were wrong — claiming the plane had not even left Egypt on some of the cited dates — and disputed the "73 overlaps" framing entirely.
* **Erika Kirk's own account.** According to that audit, **Erika Kirk reportedly said she stayed home in Arizona** on September 10 rather than being in the Nebraska/Delaware locations some overlap claims placed her near, and she has **reportedly disputed some of the overlap claims in later interviews.** Erika Kirk is a living person; all claims about tracking her movements are allegations by third parties, not established fact, and her stated denials are included here in fairness.
* **SU-BND stayed for months.** The "blue plane" reportedly sat in Provo for nearly four months, which fits a long maintenance or storage stay more than a surgical operation.
* **Tracking artifacts.** Transponder gaps and "materializing" tracks can result from terrain shadowing, receiver coverage gaps, and trackers snapping an origin to the nearest public field — mundane data artifacts, not proof of covert flights.
* **[Liz Wheeler](/People/liz-wheeler)'s "dwindles to one" rebuttal.** Commentator **Liz Wheeler** argued the overlap spreadsheets use loose criteria — counting a match when an Israel/Egyptian jet was within roughly **50–100 miles ("the same region") and within plus-or-minus three days**, rather than at the same airport on the same day. Per Wheeler, *"when you narrow the parameters to Egyptian planes landing on the same day in the same location, the overlap dwindles to… one. On 9/10 in Utah."* On her reading, the headline counts collapse once the criteria are tightened.
* **Misattribution and time-zone errors.** Independent reviewers claim a large share of the cited overlaps — by some accounts roughly **60 to 66 percent** — are simply wrong: **UTC-versus-local-time mistakes** (for example in the Provo departure timing), aircraft placed on the wrong continent, and routine maintenance legs misread as surveillance.
* **Wichita explained as a maintenance stop.** SU-BTT (a Dassault Falcon) is reported to have made **"no fewer than 8 stops" in Wichita, Kansas** — but Wichita is the **"Air Capital of the World,"** home to aviation maintenance hubs including **Yingling Aviation**, an FAA Part 145 facility that services Dassault jets and has had a Dassault relationship since 2015. Critics say this explains repeated Wichita stops as ordinary servicing rather than tracking of any person.
* **Grok / ADS-B caveat.** Asked about the theory, the AI assistant **Grok** reportedly noted that public ADS-B data shows the aircraft were active but **does not prove any surveillance or assassination link**, and that the official investigation centers on a lone shooter. The flight data is publicly viewable on FlightRadar24 and ADS-B Exchange, subject to coverage limits for certain aircraft.

## Why this pattern still draws scrutiny

A single foreign-government aircraft at a US municipal airport is unremarkable, and the maintenance explanation may well account for most of it. What keeps researchers asking questions is the combination they describe — **multiple aircraft from one foreign air force, recurrent visits over months, claimed overlap with TPUSA event geography, and presence in Provo in the days preceding a high-profile assassination**. That combination, if the underlying logs hold up, is the basis for calls to investigate the operators, passengers, and tasking of all five tails. Whether it survives a documentary check is exactly what the open records below would settle. Foreign-nexus questions of this kind fall squarely within the review that former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his center was conducting in parallel with the FBI — a review he states was ordered to stop once Tyler Robinson was in custody, before such leads were exhausted.

3. Do Duncan Aviation's maintenance records account for the full set of Provo stops, as John Cullen suggests?
4. Which of the claimed Erika/Charlie Kirk overlaps are accurate once cross-checked against full flight history — the trackers' tallies or the skeptic's "5 of 8 wrong" audit?
5. What is the operational relationship, if any, between SU-BTT, SU-BND, SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM?

## Track These Aircraft

Each jet in the armada can be researched on both major flight-tracking sites. FlightRadar24 honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list (flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing); ADS-B Exchange is volunteer-fed and unfiltered (it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out).

## Note on Registration & Control

These five aircraft carry Egyptian registrations — the **SU-** prefix on each tail number is Egypt's national civil registry, so the Egyptian registrations themselves are real and not in dispute. What is *not* settled is **who ultimately controlled the jets.** If Egyptian front companies were registered as their operators, those registrations — Egyptian on paper — may have been arranged on behalf of an intelligence service from another country. That open possibility is why these pages label the fleet **"Israel/Egyptian"**: the label flags the unresolved question of control. It is **not** a claim that the aircraft were Israeli-registered or Israeli-owned — the public record does not establish that, and nothing here asserts as fact that any government or person controlled them.
