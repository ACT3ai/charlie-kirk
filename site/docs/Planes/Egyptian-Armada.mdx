# Egyptian Plane Armada

Five Egyptian-registered VIP aircraft — **SU-BTT, SU-BND, SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM** — appear in 2025 flight logs operating in and out of Provo, Utah. Public researchers describe the pattern as a "five-plane armada" of Falcon and Gulfstream VIP jets routing from Cairo via Paris into Utah, with overlapping presence in the days surrounding Charlie Kirk's September 10 assassination.

## The five tail numbers

| Tail Number | Type | Color label | Notes |
|-------------|------|-------------|-------|
| **SU-BTT** | Dassault Falcon 7X | "Yellow plane" | Departed Provo morning of Sept 10; left US after the assassination |
| **SU-BND** | Gulfstream G550 | "Blue plane" | Transponder ON but did not take off at the key moment |
| **SU-BTU** | Egyptian-registered | (none stated) | Listed in 2025 Provo flight logs |
| **SU-BTV** | Egyptian-registered | (none stated) | Listed in 2025 Provo flight logs |
| **SU-BGM** | Egyptian-registered | (none stated) | Appears in flight logs near the same window |

## Pattern of presence

* The aircraft are described in public reporting as **VIP Falcons and Gulfstreams from Cairo via Paris**, with multiple stops at Provo Municipal Airport (PVU) throughout 2025.
* Public researchers report that SU-BTT and SU-BND have been observed shadowing **TPUSA events for years** prior to September 10, 2025.
* Cumulative documented presence near Erika Kirk's travel: reportedly 73 occurrences across the United States — including Omaha, Wichita, Provo, and various international legs.

## Why this pattern matters

A single foreign-government aircraft appearing at a US municipal airport is unremarkable. The reported pattern here — **five distinct aircraft from one foreign air force, recurrent visits over months, overlap with TPUSA event locations, and presence in Provo in the days preceding a high-profile political assassination** — is the basis for the "Egyptian armada" framing in public commentary. Researchers have called for federal investigation into the operators, passengers, and tasking of all five tails.

## Open questions

1. Who in the US authorized recurring access for an Egyptian Air Force VIP fleet to a non-international US municipal airport?
2. Who were the passengers on each aircraft during the 2025 visits?
3. What is the operational relationship between SU-BTT, SU-BND, SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM?
4. Why does the reported presence overlap with TPUSA event geography over multiple years?
