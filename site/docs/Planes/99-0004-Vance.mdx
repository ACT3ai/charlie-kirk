# 99-0004 Vance Flight

A US Air Force **C-32A** (the military Boeing 757-200 used for executive transport), tail number **99-0004**, was logged on public ADS-B data flying to **Las Vegas on May 27, 2025** under the call sign **SAM912** and reportedly carrying the "AF2" / Air Force Two designation used for the Vice President's aircraft. Investigators tracking aircraft around the case flagged the flight and asked an open question — what business took Vice President JD Vance to Las Vegas that day, roughly three and a half months before the September 10, 2025 assassination. This page records the tail number and the question; nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any person.

## Why this aircraft is in the catalog

This jet is **not** placed at UVU or over Orem. It surfaces in the investigation's aviation tracking as a separate VIP-transport data point — a C-32A operating as Air Force Two months before the assassination. It is logged here for completeness alongside the other government and VIP aircraft (the Hegseth-linked [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) C-37A and the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) Army ISR jet) so the full set of flagged tail numbers is preserved in one place.

It is also worth keeping straight because a C-32A in the Air Force Two role appears again in the case timeline the day after the killing — the September 11, 2025 flight that carried Charlie Kirk's casket. That is a different flight on a different date and is documented separately on the [Air Force Two Casket Flight](/Planes/Casket-Flight) page. This page is only about the **May 27, 2025 Las Vegas** entry.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** 99-0004
* **Type:** US Air Force C-32A (Boeing 757-200 / "B752" in tracker shorthand) VIP transport
* **ICAO hex:** ADFEBA (per ADS-B Exchange)
* **Call sign:** SAM912 — one of the rotating "SAM" (Special Air Mission) call signs this airframe flies under; the role-based "AF2" / Air Force Two designation applies when the Vice President is aboard
* **Reported designation:** "AF2" — Air Force Two, the call/role used when the Vice President is aboard
* **Operator/owner:** US Air Force (VPOTUS / government VIP use)
* **Home station:** The C-32A Special Air Mission fleet is based at [Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews), Maryland — the staging point for VIP and Vice-Presidential movements such as memorial-related departures

Note: tail **99-0004** (C-32A, this page) is a distinct aircraft from tail **99-0404** (a C-37A Gulfstream V), which is covered separately on the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page. The two tail numbers differ by a single digit and are easy to confuse.

## Flight history relevant to the case

* **May 27, 2025 — Las Vegas, NV.** Tail 99-0004 (hex ADFEBA), call sign **SAM912**, logged on ADS-B Exchange as a B752 / C-32A flying to Las Vegas, reportedly under the Air Force Two role. An X post circulated among case watchers noted "May 27th 2025 was a busy day" and asked what Vice President JD Vance was doing in Vegas that day. This is the only logged movement of 99-0004 tied to the case material.
* **September 11, 2025 — casket transport (separate entry).** A C-32A in the Air Force Two role transported Charlie Kirk's casket the day after the assassination, with Vice President Vance accompanying and reportedly helping carry the casket on the tarmac. That respectful, openly reported flight is documented on the [Air Force Two Casket Flight](/Planes/Casket-Flight) page and is not the same flight as the May 27 Las Vegas record above.

## Post-assassination memorial transport

Beyond the May 27 Las Vegas record, the **99-0004** airframe (and the C-32A / Air Force Two role it flies) appears in the case material again as part of the official government aviation response to the assassination. The facts below are drawn from **X posts and public coverage** of the memorial period and are recorded here so this tail number's full memorial role is preserved on one page. They are factual logistics of an openly reported, respectful event — **nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any person**.

* **Coffin transport to Phoenix.** According to X posts circulated during the memorial period, **Air Force Two / 99-0004** was used in post-assassination logistics to carry **Charlie Kirk's coffin** from the event area to **Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport** for the **memorial service**. Posts noted the aircraft **wheels-down** in Phoenix.
* **JD Vance carried the coffin aboard.** Public coverage and X posts reported that **Vice President JD Vance** personally helped **carry the coffin onto the aircraft** — a widely photographed moment during the loading on the tarmac.
* **Departures from Joint Base Andrews.** The aircraft was also involved in **departures from [Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews)** for the memorial event, consistent with its role as the Special Air Mission fleet's staging base for Vice-Presidential movements.
* **The trip.** The memorial leg recorded in the case material runs from the **JB Andrews / Hagerstown, Maryland area** to **Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport** for the Charlie Kirk memorial.
* **Relevant dates.** The memorial flights fall in the window of **September 11, 2025 onward**, with some reports placing related VIP movements around the **September 21, 2025** memorial-period context.
* **Not tied to the shooting day.** This memorial transport is **not** directly connected to the **September 10, 2025** shooting in X discussions. It is logged here as part of the high-profile government aviation response and memorial transport — scrutinized only in the context of how officials handled the aftermath.

## Associated identifiers in the same record

The tracking note that surfaced the May 27 flight listed several identifiers together in one block. They are recorded here as-found, without asserting any connection between them:

* **N885LS** — a separate tail number appearing in the same data block; no further public detail has been established for it in the case material.
* **[SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV)** — written "SUBTV" in the source note; one of the Egyptian-registered jets covered on the [Israel/Egyptian Plane Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) page.

## Theories about the aircraft's role

These are **open questions and unverified observations**, not conclusions. There is no evidence in the case material connecting this aircraft to the September 10 assassination.

* **OSINT flight-watchers** raised the May 27 Las Vegas flight simply as an unexplained VIP movement worth a public answer — what the trip was for. No claim of wrongdoing accompanies it.
* **A separate strand of commentary** notes that Vice President Vance was later, in the case period, in Islamabad for US–Iran peace talks (a context discussed elsewhere on the site). That is unrelated to the May 27 Vegas flight and is mentioned only to keep the two from being conflated.
* The most defensible reading is the simplest: this is a routine government VIP movement that an investigator flagged for transparency, and the open question is benign until records say otherwise.

## Track This Aircraft

Research this aircraft's flight history yourself on both major flight-tracking sites. They differ in one important way:

* [FlightRadar24 — 99-0004](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/99-0004) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list, so flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing.
* [ADS-B Exchange — 99-0004 (hex ADFEBA)](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?icao=adfeba) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD, so it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out.
* [ADS-B Exchange — by ICAO hex ADFEBA](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?icao=adfeba) — the Mode-S hex is this jet's permanent transponder ID; use it when the registration lookup comes up empty (common for military aircraft).

**Aircraft ID:** USAF C-32A (Boeing 757-200) VIP transport · tail **99-0004** · ICAO hex **ADFEBA** · callsign SAM912, reported "Air Force Two." Military VIP flights are often limited on FlightRadar24, so the ADS-B Exchange hex link above is usually the most reliable.
