# Utah Cox Foreign Meetings (Claims)

Thirteen days after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death, commentary alleges Utah Governor [**Spencer Cox**](/People/spencer-cox)
hosted **back-to-back closed-door meetings** in the **Utah Capitol Gold Room** with
**Egyptian** and **French** representatives — the same foreign threads tied to
[Egyptian jets in Provo](./Egyptian-Armada). This page documents those **claims** from
CK_FILE and social posts. We do **not** assert Governor Cox or any diplomat committed
wrongdoing or knew of the killing in advance.

## Alleged Sept 23, 2025 schedule (claims)

| Time (claimed) | Meeting (claimed) | Figure (claimed) |
|----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| 11:30 AM | Closed door | **Egyptian Ambassador Motaz Zahran** — described as D.C. intel bridge (Egypt–Israel–US) |
| 3:30 PM | Closed door | **French Consul General Florian Cardinaux** — San Francisco-based; DGSE pipeline alleged in commentary |

Posts claim both entries appeared on **internal governor.utah.gov calendars** but were
**erased from public press and social channels** — no photos, no statements.

## Why aircraft investigators link this

- **[Egyptian planes](./SU-BTT)** were on the ground in Provo Sept 4–13 per ADS-B research.
- **[Egyptian crew hotel](./Egyptian-Crew-Hotel)** tip places jet occupants in Orem.
- CK_FILE frames Sept 23 meetings as possible **"damage control"** after the assassination —
  an allegation, not a finding.

French angle: separate research threads allege French intel interest in
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) coverage; those belong primarily to
[Israel/foreign leads](/Israel/foreign-leads), not this page.

## Sourcing caution

- Calendar screenshots and ambassador identities need **primary verification**.
- Governors routinely hold **undisclosed diplomatic meetings**; secrecy alone is not evidence of a cover-up.
- Spencer Cox is a **living person**; attribute all claims to cited posts, not as established fact.

## What corroboration would look like

- Governor's office readouts or FOIA for Sept 23 Gold Room bookings
- Ambassador travel records into Salt Lake City
- Overlap with [Egyptian armada](./Egyptian-Armada) passenger names from manifests

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

the records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may compel.
