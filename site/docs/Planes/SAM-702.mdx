# SAM702 Flight

**SAM702** is the primary **Special Air Mission (SAM)** callsign reported in the investigation notes for the US government VIP flight tied to tail number **99-0404** — the aircraft associated with the September 8-9, 2025 **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca), Arizona** window two days before the UVU shooting. The source material lists this flight as *"SAM-702 (or SAM-000 or SAM-112)"*, so SAM702 is the leading callsign reading for the same airframe covered on the [SAM 99-0404 Flight](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page, with [SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000) and [SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112) as the two alternate readings. SAM702 is the callsign most heavily discussed in **Candace Owens'** podcast coverage of the case. Nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any person.

## Why this callsign is the focal point

Of the three candidate callsigns for tail 99-0404, **SAM702** is the one that recurs most often in citizen-investigator discussion and in Candace Owens' broadcasts. The interest is twofold: the Special Air Mission designation means it was a senior-official transport, and its position at Fort Huachuca — a US Army intelligence base — one day before the assassination places a high-level government flight at a sensitive node in the timeline. Until the official flight log is produced, SAM702 remains an open identifier that researchers want matched to a confirmed route, callsign, and passenger manifest.

## Aircraft identification

* **Callsign (as reported):** SAM702 — the primary of three callsigns (SAM-702 / SAM-000 / SAM-112) listed in the source for this flight
* **Tail number:** 99-0404
* **Type:** Possibly a US Air Force C-37A (the Gulfstream V VIP transport used for Special Air Mission flights)
* **Window:** Around September 8-9, 2025 (the Fort Huachuca timeframe)
* **Reported associated official:** Discussed in commentary alongside senior defense officials; see the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page for the Hegseth-linked framing

Note: tail 99-0404 (this flight) is a distinct aircraft from tail **99-0004**, the C-32A covered on the [99-0004 Vance Flight](/Planes/99-0004-Vance) page. The two tail numbers differ by a single digit and are easy to confuse.

## The reported Fort Huachuca meeting

According to coverage circulated by Candace Owens, a high-level meeting took place at **Fort Huachuca on or around September 9, 2025** — the day before Charlie Kirk's assassination. The full account of the reported 12-lieutenant-colonel session, the whistleblower material, and the call-sign change is on the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page. Some citizen-investigator posts have additionally speculated about who else may have been connected to a Fort Huachuca meeting in that window — names raised in discussion include figures such as Charlie Kirk's wife **Erika Kirk** and his security chief **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)**. These are reported as unverified online speculation, not established fact; no record has placed any named living person at such a meeting, and nothing here alleges any wrongdoing by them.

## The Las Vegas staging theory

A separate strand of online discussion ties SAM702 — or the broader cluster of government VIP flights around this window — to **Las Vegas** flights and possible "staging" the day before a meeting. This overlaps with the separately documented Las Vegas VIP-flight question on the [99-0004 Vance Flight](/Planes/99-0004-Vance) page. The Las Vegas angle is presented here as a reported theory raised in citizen tracking, not as a verified connection.

## Companion SAM flights and the reported itinerary

A set of X/OSINT threads discusses **SAM702** alongside a companion Special Air Mission callsign, **[SAM650](/Planes/SAM-650)**, treating the two as part of the same cluster of government VIP movements in the days around the assassination. According to these posts, the SAM flights showed *"suspicious itineraries"* — one route described as **Wilmington (Delaware) → [Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews) → Nashville** — and what users called timeline *"syncs"* with **Colorado Springs**, Colorado, a military and intelligence hub. Posters tie that Colorado Springs activity back to the alleged **Fort Huachuca, Arizona** meeting window of **September 8-9, 2025**.

These are unverified citizen-tracking claims circulated on X, not confirmed flight records, and no manifest, route, or meeting has been independently verified. The Wilmington → Andrews → Nashville reading appears in the same threads that critics say rely on loose overlap criteria. As **Liz Wheeler** argued of the broader plane-overlap analysis, narrowing the parameters to same-day, same-location matches collapses most of the claimed overlaps, and reviewers have flagged UTC-versus-local time errors and misattributed locations in the underlying spreadsheets. Public ADS-B data, per Grok responses cited in the threads, shows aircraft activity but no proven surveillance or assassination link; the official finding remains a lone shooter.

## The AES Tennessee flyover claim

The same notes report that this flight reduced altitude — flying low and slow — over the **Accurate Energetic Systems (AES)** plant in Tennessee. According to **Stew Peters**, who presents this as his own allegation, the Defense Secretary's plane (tail 99-0404, which he cites under callsign SAM702) flew over the AES facility that later detonated on **October 10, 2025**. These are presented as reported allegations for verification, not established facts. See [AES Factory Explosion](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) and [Stew Peters Broke the AES / Mic Connection](/Mic/Stew_Peters_Broke_AES_Mic) for the fuller account.

## Command-and-control coordination theory

Beyond the route-overlap discussion, a strand of the **X OSINT / SAM-flight threads** frames **SAM702** — bundled with **[SAM650](/Planes/SAM-650)** and **[SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)** — as evidence of an alleged **command-and-control / high-level coordination** posture in the days around the assassination. Per these theories, clusters of Special Air Mission (government VIP) movements syncing with **Colorado Springs** and the alleged **Fort Huachuca** meetings would, if confirmed, indicate senior officials gathering and coordinating in the **September 8-9, 2025** window, one day before the September 10 shooting.

This is **user theory only**. There is **no official link** connecting SAM702 to any meeting, coordination effort, or the assassination — no flight log, manifest, route, or attendee roster has been produced or independently verified, and nothing here asserts wrongdoing by any person or agency. The "command-and-control" reading is the interpretation offered in citizen-tracking posts, not a finding of any investigation.

## Relevant dates

* **Reported tracking window:** around **September 8-11, 2025** — covering both the pre-event activity (the alleged Fort Huachuca window of September 8-9) and the event/aftermath days surrounding the September 10 shooting.
* All dates are **per X OSINT / SAM-flight threads** and remain unverified against official ADS-B or flight-log records.

## Track This Aircraft

SAM702 is a **callsign** for tail **99-0404** (USAF C-37A). Research the airframe on both major flight-tracking sites — they differ in one important way, and for a military jet the unfiltered site and the ICAO hex matter most:

* [FlightRadar24 — 99-0404](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/99-0404) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list; US military flights are frequently limited or absent here.
* [ADS-B Exchange — by registration 99-0404](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?reg=99-0404) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD; the most reliable place to see military movements.
* [ADS-B Exchange — by ICAO hex AE04F9](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?icao=ae04f9) — the Mode-S hex is the aircraft's permanent transponder ID; use it when registration or callsign lookups come up empty.

**Tip:** SAM flights broadcast under rotating callsigns (this airframe appears as SAM-702 / SAM-000 / SAM-112), so search by the **hex AE04F9**, not the callsign. See also [SAM 99-0404 Flight](/Planes/SAM-99-0404).
