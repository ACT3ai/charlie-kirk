# T7ELL & EJM36 (Flagged Tails)

When researchers expanded the Egypt story from **two** jets ([SU-BTT](./SU-BTT),
[SU-BND](./SU-BND)) to a **[five-plane armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)**, Project Constitution's post also flagged
**two additional foreign tails**: **T7ELL** and **EJM36**. Public route detail for these
two is **thin** compared to the SU-registered fleet — they appear in source notes as
*"more planes"* grouped for disclosure purposes.

Source: [@ProjectConstitu on X](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1999347020142710872) amplifying the five-plane Provo swarm narrative.

## What is documented publicly

| Tail | Public detail level |
|------|---------------------|
| **T7ELL** | Named in CK_FILE and [Egyptian Armada](./Egyptian-Armada) fleet list; no standalone ADS-B timeline published on this site yet |
| **EJM36** | Same — flagged for manifest review under proposed [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) |

Unlike [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)/SU-BND, these tails do not yet have **independent per-tail pages** with
verified Provo legs on September 10. They are catalogued so the fleet picture matches the
research record and law drafts.

## Investigative status

**Open questions:**

- Aircraft type and registry state for each tail
- Any Provo Municipal (PVU) appearances in 2025 flight logs
- Passenger overlap with SU-BTT Sept 4 drop-off claims
- Whether either tail routes through Israel per [Israel Planes](./Israel-Planes) methodology

Until ADS-B screenshots or FOIA manifests surface, treat T7ELL and EJM36 as **placeholder
flags** — not established participants in the assassination.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Full flight records, crew, and passengers for **T7ELL** and **EJM36** are explicitly
requested alongside the SU- armada in the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview).
