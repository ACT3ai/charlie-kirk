# N40JD Provo Arrival

A **Beechcraft Premier 1** (6-seat light business jet) registered **N40JD** is listed in flight records circulated by independent investigators as one of several private aircraft arriving at **Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)** on September 10, 2025. It is included on the investigation's aircraft list as part of building a complete picture of who flew into Provo around the day of the assassination.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** N40JD
* **Type:** Beechcraft Premier 1 (reported 6 seats)
* **Role on the list:** A private aircraft logged arriving at Provo on September 10, 2025

## Reported flight record

As reproduced from flight-record screenshots circulated by investigators:

* **Departed** Scottsdale, Arizona at **~12:27 p.m. local**.
* **Arrived** into Provo (PVU) at **~2:29 p.m. local**.

This is an afternoon arrival, after the shooting. No public reporting ties the aircraft, its crew, or its passengers to the event; it is on the list only so its manifest can be checked alongside the other Provo arrivals.

## Why it is on the list

The investigation seeks the passenger manifests of every private aircraft that arrived at Provo in the window around September 10 so that no relevant traveler is missed. N40JD is one of those Provo arrivals and is grouped with the other same-day inbound private jets ([N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ) and [N872RA](/Planes/N872RA)) for that reason.

The flight's public tracking has been **referenced in podcasts and independent investigations** as part of broader arrival/departure pattern analysis around UVU and Provo. Some of those investigator discussions have reportedly connected the flight to figures such as **Tyler Bowyer**. That association is **reported by commentators and unproven** — there is no evidence in the public record tying Mr. Bowyer, the aircraft, its crew, or its passengers to the event, and it is noted here only to explain why the flight has drawn discussion.

## Open questions

1. Who was aboard N40JD on the September 10 arrival into Provo?
2. Who is the registered owner and operator?
3. What was the stated purpose of the trip, and where did the occupants go afterward?
