# Planes Following Charlie or Erika

For the **September 10 minute-by-minute** correlation (hospital timing, N102DZ 3:31 PM, SU-BTT transponder window), see **[Sept 10 Flight Timeline](./Sept10-Flight-Timeline)**.

This page is the hub for the single biggest allegation in the aircraft angle of the case: that one or more aircraft did not merely happen to be near Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025**, but had been **shadowing [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), and [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) (TPUSA) events for years** — and that the pattern tightened in the **two months before the assassination**. The headline number, attributed to [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) and amplified by independent flight trackers, is that Israel/Egyptian government jets logged **up to 73 overlaps with Erika Kirk's travel** and roughly **23 with Charlie Kirk**. Every figure on this page is a **researcher claim built on public ADS-B data**, not a confirmed record, and the most prominent counterarguments are laid out in full below.

## The core claim

According to **Candace Owens**, two Israel/Egyptian Air Force VIP jets — the **"yellow plane" [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** (a Dassault Falcon 7X) and the **"blue plane" [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** (a Gulfstream) — repeatedly appeared near the Kirks' locations and near TPUSA events over a period researchers describe as **roughly 2022 through September 2025**. One widely shared post framed it as *"72 times those exact planes followed Erika Kirk… that is statistically near impossible without intent."* The investigation file records the same allegation tersely: *"Egyptian Planes had 73 overlaps with Erika. Only ~23 with Charlie."*

The claim is that this is not coincidence but **tasking** — that foreign aircraft were positioned to track the Kirk family and TPUSA's movements, and that this surveillance ran right up to the day Charlie Kirk was killed. Some posters note the overlaps **begin around 2022**, which they say **aligns with the Kirk marriage timeline**, and that several overlaps involve **international / overseas legs** alongside the Kirks rather than only domestic stops. The same researchers argue the Israel/Egyptian jets **regularly flew in and out of Israel** between these overlaps — the thread followed in detail on the [Israel Planes](/Planes/Israel-Planes) page.

## The overlap counts — and why they disagree

The "tracking" tally is the most cited and most disputed figure in the whole aircraft investigation. The exact number changes depending on which researcher and which post you read:

* **73 / ~23** — The headline split: roughly **73 documented overlaps near Erika Kirk** and about **23 near Charlie Kirk**, attributed to **Candace Owens**'s team, across U.S. cities including **Omaha, Wichita, and Provo**, plus domestic and international legs.
* **72, 70+, 68** — Different X posts cite **72**, **"70+"**, or **68** Erika-Kirk overlaps for the lead jets. The inconsistency between counts is itself part of the dispute.
* **68 / 29 (dual-plane count)** — A separate tally claims the blue ([SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)) and yellow ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)) jets *together* overlapped Erika Kirk's locations **68 times between 2022 and September 2025** — including overseas trips — of which **29 also coincided with Charlie Kirk's location.** This 68/29 figure is not reconciled with the 73/23 tally; both are independent researchers' numbers.
* **Geographic clustering** — One analysis cited by researchers concentrates the U.S. overlaps in **five states: Missouri, Delaware, Utah, Nebraska, and Kansas.** This clustering is offered as supporting evidence and has not been independently verified.

These are **trackers' allegations, not established fact.** They rest on amateur readings of public flight-radar history, and — as the counterarguments below show — several specific dates have been challenged.

## The TPUSA-event tie and the two-month window

Researchers connect the overlaps specifically to **TPUSA programming**, not just to the Kirks' personal travel. The flight-log thread that introduced **[SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU)** summarized its 2025 movements as **"multiple NE/UT hops tied to TPUSA Faith tours"** (the researcher's characterization, not a confirmed itinerary). The **[Israel/Egyptian Plane Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)** reporting likewise ties the spring-2025 Provo legs to **Kirk's Midwest push** and the recurring Northeast-to-Utah hops to **TPUSA Faith-tour stops**.

The two months before September 10, 2025 are the tightest part of the timeline. The lead jets are reported around Provo and TPUSA stops through the summer, and the fleet's presence peaks in early September: **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** landed at Provo on **September 4, 2025 — six days before the assassination** — and departed the **morning of September 10**, while **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** sat at Provo with its transponder **on but no takeoff** at the moment of the shooting before leaving on **September 13**. Because TPUSA's calendar of campus and faith-tour events ran continuously through that window, any flight overlap "near a TPUSA event" in the two months prior falls inside this same period.

The Kirk-party aircraft (N102DZ, N560TW, the casket flight) appear here because they fix where Charlie and Erika actually were, which is what the overlap counts are measured against.

## Counterarguments and fairness

The shadowing claim is **contested**, and several of the people closest to the flight data dispute it. These rebuttals are reported here for balance:

* **Erika Kirk's own denial.** Erika Kirk has reportedly pushed back on the narrative that the Israel/Egyptian jets shadowed her movements, including stating that she **stayed home in Arizona on September 10** rather than traveling. Her denial directly undercuts any "overlap" counted for that date and is reported here as her stated position.
* **[Liz Wheeler](/People/liz-wheeler)'s methodology challenge.** Commentator **Liz Wheeler** argues the tallies use **loose criteria** — counting a "match" when an Israel/Egyptian jet was within a broad region (tens of miles) and within a few days of an Erika Kirk location, rather than the same airport on the same day. Per Wheeler, *"when you narrow the parameters to Egyptian planes landing on the same day in the same location, the overlap dwindles to one — on 9/10 in Utah."*
* **The "5 of 8 are wrong" audit.** A reviewer who pulled a year of FlightRadar history said **five of the eight overlaps** Owens cited were inaccurate — claiming a plane "hadn't even left Egypt" on some listed dates. That reviewer found some entries accurate but could not source others.
* **[John Cullen](/Influencers/john-cullen)'s maintenance-contract explanation.** Open-source investigator **John Cullen** — himself a major figure in the plane investigation — has **dismissed** a strong assassination link, citing **long-standing Falcon and VIP-jet maintenance contracts at Provo dating back to roughly 1999.** In his reading, a foreign fleet routing through Provo's **Duncan Aviation** MRO facility is a routine, decades-old arrangement, not an operation.

In short: the surveillance theory rests on a striking pattern in public data, while the skeptics argue the pattern shrinks dramatically under stricter parameters and is explained by ordinary aircraft maintenance. Both readings are testable only against records that are not yet public.

## Open questions

1. Do the **"73 overlaps"** with Erika Kirk survive a strict, same-day / same-location documentary audit?
2. How are the competing tallies — **73/23**, **72**, **68/29** — reconciled, and which underlying date list is correct?
3. If Erika Kirk **stayed in Arizona on September 10**, which overlap counts depend on her presence in Utah that day?
4. Does **John Cullen's maintenance-contract** explanation account for every Israel/Egyptian-jet leg, or are there legs it does not explain?
5. Who was aboard the lead jets ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)) on the TPUSA-overlap dates, and do passenger manifests support or refute the tracking claim?
