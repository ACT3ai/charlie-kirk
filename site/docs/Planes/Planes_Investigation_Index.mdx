out the killing. Nothing here asserts wrongdoing by any pilot, owner, or passenger.

## What more can we learn? (per tail)

For each aircraft, the investigative question is the same: **what records would settle the dispute?**

* **[N1098L](./N1098L/overview)** — Crew, mission tasking, sensor data from Sept 10 passes; routine training vs. targeted ISR.
* **[SU-BTT](./SU-BTT)** — Manifests, Dugway vs. Provo departure truth, Wilmington routing; passengers dropped Sept 4.
* **[SU-BND](./SU-BND)** — Four-month Provo stay work order; why transponder on but no departure at shot time.
* **[SU-BTU](./SU-BTU)** / **[SU-BTV](./SU-BTV)** / **[SU-BGM](./SU-BGM)** — Passenger purpose on each Provo hop; Faith-tour shadowing vs. Duncan Aviation service.
* **[Egyptian Armada](./Egyptian-Armada)** — Shared operational control across five tails; Delaware 920 N King St transfer claim.
* **[Israel Planes](./Israel-Planes)** — Cairo→Israel legs with transponders dark; Oct 21 Hassan Rashad corroborated flight.
* **[SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404)** — Sept 8–9 Fort Huachuca manifest; 12-lieutenant-colonel meeting claim.
* **[N888KG](./N888KG)** — Page, AZ stop passengers; ADS-B gap vs. rural coverage explanation.
* **[N59906](./N59906)** — Survey imagery over campus that morning; operator contract scope.
* **[N102DZ](./N102DZ)** — Full Sept 10 itinerary; 3:30 PM Duncan link-up with SU-BND allegation.
