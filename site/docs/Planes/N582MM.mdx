# N582MM TPUSA Aircraft

Tail number **N582MM** is an aircraft that public commentary describes as frequently used by **Turning Point USA (TPUSA)** insiders. It is named in the proposed Charlie Kirk disclosure laws over a reported **October 2025 movement from [Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca), Arizona to Kalispell, Montana**. Standalone sourcing on this tail number is thin — the TPUSA-insider association is a **reported characterization**, not an established fact, and naming the aircraft is not an accusation against any person or organization. The page exists because citizen investigators have flagged the tail in OSINT threads and because the disclosure-law draft lists it among the aircraft for which it seeks records.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** N582MM
* **Type:** Not confirmed in public reporting; described in commentary as a likely business jet
* **Registry / operator / owner:** Not independently confirmed on this page; described in public commentary as an aircraft frequently used by TPUSA insiders
* **Program / callsign:** None known; this is treated as a civil aircraft, not a military or government tail
* **Why it draws attention:** The reported Fort Huachuca stop and immediate Kalispell leg overlap two locations that recur across the case's intelligence angle

## Flight history relevant to the case

The flight history below is drawn from the disclosure-law draft and OSINT commentary, not from a flight-tracking record reproduced in the master investigation file. Treat the legs as **reported** rather than independently verified here.

* **Reported October 4–5, 2025:** N582MM is described as present at **Fort Huachuca, Arizona** — the U.S. Army Intelligence Center and Counterintelligence school in Sierra Vista. Fort Huachuca recurs throughout this investigation: roughly a dozen lieutenant colonels are reported to have met there on September 8–9, 2025, and the **[SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)** and **[SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702)** government flights are tied to the same post in the days before the assassination.
* **Reported immediate onward leg to Kalispell, Montana:** The aircraft is reported to have flown directly to **Kalispell** (Glacier Park International Airport area), said to coincide with a TPUSA security-team gathering nearby. Kalispell is notable because the **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** Bombardier Global 6500 — analyzed elsewhere on this site — also landed at Glacier Park International (KGPI) the morning of September 10, 2025. The recurrence of the same small Montana airport across two separately flagged aircraft is what investigators ask the disclosure laws to explain.
* **Reported broader pattern:** Citizen investigators say they have spotted the tail in patterns around Kirk events and locations, framing those sightings in the context of pre- and post-September 10, 2025 timelines.

## Theories about the aircraft's role

Each item below is an **open theory** raised in public commentary, not a finding. None is established, and several have innocent explanations.

* **Organizational-travel theory.** The simplest reading is that N582MM is ordinary TPUSA-associated business-jet travel and that any overlap with Fort Huachuca or Kalispell is coincidental — a routine logistics movement that happens to touch locations the investigation is already watching. This is the null hypothesis and should be stated first.
* **Fort Huachuca overlap theory.** Some commentators ask whether an aircraft linked to TPUSA travel touching the Army's counterintelligence post — the same post tied to the September 8–9 lieutenant-colonel meeting and the SAM flights — points to a coordination angle. This is posed as a **question**, not a conclusion; presence at an airfield is not evidence of any operation.
* **Kalispell convergence theory.** Because N1098L also flew to the Kalispell area around September 10, a few investigators ask whether the two aircraft and a reported TPUSA security gathering converged there for a reason. The counterpoint is that Glacier Park International is a normal destination and that two flights to one airport, weeks apart, can be unrelated.

## TPUSA event-location patterns

Citizen investigators describe N582MM, **per X OSINT discussions**, as a TPUSA-associated jet whose reported movements trace **various TPUSA event locations** and form alleged **patterns around Utah and other Kirk event sites**. Commentators frame these reported sightings in the context of the pre- and post-**September 10, 2025** timelines. None of this is independently verified here — it reflects how OSINT commentators on X read the tail's reported movements, not an established flight record. The simplest explanation remains routine organizational travel to and from TPUSA events.

## Questions about organizational aviation and surveillance overlap

A recurring theme **per X discussions** is the question of whether Turning Point USA's organizational aviation resources overlap in any way with the surveillance or operational flights flagged elsewhere in this case. This is posed as an **open question**, not a conclusion. The mere presence of an aircraft, or its appearance near a flagged location or event, is not evidence of any such overlap, and TPUSA has not been shown to have done anything wrong. These are exactly the kinds of records the disclosure laws would clarify.

## Broader TPUSA-asset scrutiny

N582MM is one of several **TPUSA assets** that citizen investigators have placed under scrutiny in **X OSINT threads** following the assassination. That scrutiny extends to the organization's fleet and aviation logistics generally, with commentators **alleging** patterns they say warrant explanation. This page treats that scrutiny as **reported public commentary**, not as a substantiated claim against TPUSA. Naming the aircraft is not an accusation against the organization or any individual.

## Open questions

1. Who was aboard N582MM on the reported Fort Huachuca and Kalispell legs, and who chartered or owns the aircraft?
2. What was the purpose of the reported Fort Huachuca stop and the immediate move to Kalispell?
3. What is the registered owner and operator, and what is the documentary basis for the TPUSA-insider characterization?
4. Does the reported Kalispell leg connect in any way to N1098L's separate Kalispell arrival, or is the shared airport coincidental?
5. Does the timing connect to any other Fort Huachuca activity already flagged in the case?

## Track This Aircraft

Research this aircraft's flight history yourself on both major flight-tracking sites. They differ in one important way:

* [FlightRadar24 — N582MM](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/n582mm) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list, so flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing.
* [ADS-B Exchange — N582MM](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?reg=N582MM) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD, so it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out.
