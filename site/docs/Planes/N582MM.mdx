# N582MM TPUSA Aircraft

Tail number **N582MM** is an aircraft that public commentary describes as frequently used by **Turning Point USA** insiders. It is named in the proposed Charlie Kirk disclosure laws over a reported **October 2025 movement from Fort Huachuca, Arizona to Kalispell, Montana**. The TPUSA-insider association is a **reported characterization**, not an established fact, and naming the aircraft is not an accusation against any individual.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** N582MM
* **Type:** Not confirmed in public reporting; described in commentary as likely a business jet
* **Reported association:** Described in public commentary as an aircraft frequently used by TPUSA insiders

## Why it is on the list

The proposed disclosure laws ask each covered agency to produce all records relating to N582MM, specifically citing:

* Its reported **October 4–5, 2025 presence at Fort Huachuca, Arizona** — the same Army intelligence post tied elsewhere in the investigation to a pre-assassination meeting.
* An **immediate onward flight to Kalispell, Montana**, reported to coincide with a TPUSA security-team gathering in the same area.
* A request for **all records of who was aboard** this aircraft for any flight to or from Fort Huachuca at any time.

The interest is in the overlap between an aircraft linked to TPUSA travel and the Fort Huachuca location that recurs across the case's intelligence angle. The movement is presented as a publicly flagged pattern that the disclosure laws seek to explain — not as proof of any wrongdoing.

Citizen investigators report having spotted the aircraft in patterns around Kirk events and locations, and they flag its movements in the context of pre- and post-assassination timelines. These OSINT threads reportedly include multiple sightings of the tail number. The questions raised concern the scale of the organization's aviation resources and whether any of its flights overlap with surveillance or operational activity — framed by commentators as open questions rather than established findings.

## Open questions

1. Who was aboard N582MM on the reported Fort Huachuca and Kalispell legs?
2. What was the purpose of the Fort Huachuca stop and the immediate move to Kalispell?
3. Who is the registered owner and operator, and what is the basis for the TPUSA-insider characterization?
4. Does the timing connect to any other Fort Huachuca activity in the case?

## Track This Aircraft

Research this aircraft's flight history yourself on both major flight-tracking sites. They differ in one important way:

* [FlightRadar24 — N582MM](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/n582mm) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list, so flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing.
* [ADS-B Exchange — N582MM](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?reg=N582MM) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD, so it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out.
