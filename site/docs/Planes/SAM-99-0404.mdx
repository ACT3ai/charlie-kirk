# SAM 99-0404 Flight

A US Air Force VIP transport — **Special Air Mission (SAM) 99-0404**, a C-37A Gulfstream V — was tracked into **Fort Huachuca, AZ** on September 8-9, 2025, two days before the UVU shooting. Whistleblower reporting routed through Candace Owens links the flight to a 12-lieutenant-colonel meeting at Fort Huachuca on September 9, with one attendee said to be a "Captain Neff" — a name that overlaps with Charlie Kirk's longtime producer Blake Neff (a former US Marine Corps War College wargames specialist).

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** 99-0404
* **Type:** US Air Force C-37A Gulfstream V VIP jet
* **Base:** Joint Base Andrews (Camp Springs, ADW)
* **Mission designator:** SAM (Special Air Mission) — used to ferry senior executive-branch and Congressional officials
* **Capacity:** Approximately 12 passengers
* **Booking authority for the trip:** Reported as Bradley Hansell's office

## Flight pattern leading up to September 10

According to a tipster's email (December 10, 2025) circulated by Candace Owens:

* **September 7, 2025:** Andrews → Colorado Springs.
* **September 8, 2025:** Colorado Springs → Tucson, then 5 hours later Tucson → (transponder off).
* **September 9, 2025, 12:31 PM:** Reappears flying **Fort Huachuca → El Paso**, then El Paso → Andrews.
* The call sign reportedly changed from **RCH to SAM** as the aircraft entered Fort Huachuca on September 8.

This was reported as the only time the tipster had observed this aircraft at Fort Huachuca.

## Connection to the September 9 lieutenant-colonel meeting

Candace Owens has reported that a military whistleblower (referred to as "Harry Myers") walked into a high-clearance meeting at Fort Huachuca on September 9 attended by approximately 12 lieutenant colonels — Pentagon-level brass — described by the source as a "final op planning session — isolated, high-clearance, no walk-ins allowed." The seven-hour interrogation that followed was reportedly led by a "Captain Neff." The combination of the SAM-class aircraft, the call-sign change, and the meeting timing has been flagged for federal investigation.

## Other reported users of this aircraft

According to the same tipster, **Pam Bondi** flew on SAM 99-0404 approximately five months prior, from the Tampa area back to DC.

## Open questions

4. What was the relationship between "Captain Neff" and Blake Neff?
