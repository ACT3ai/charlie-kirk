# SAM 99-0404 Flight

A US Air Force VIP transport — **Special Air Mission (SAM) 99-0404**, a C-37A Gulfstream V — was tracked into **Fort Huachuca, AZ** on September 8-9, 2025, two days before the UVU shooting. Whistleblower reporting routed through Candace Owens links the flight to a reported meeting of roughly 12 lieutenant colonels at Fort Huachuca on September 9. The account below comes from anonymous tipster material; it is presented as a documented public claim, not as established fact, and no named individual has been shown to have committed any crime.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** 99-0404
* **Type:** US Air Force C-37A Gulfstream V VIP jet
* **Base:** Joint Base Andrews (Camp Springs, ADW)
* **Mission designator:** SAM (Special Air Mission) — used to ferry senior executive-branch and Congressional officials
* **Reported government/VIP use:** Operated by the US Air Force for government and VIP transport; aircraft of this Special Air Mission class are reported to carry senior officials, with commentary noting figures such as Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth among the kinds of officials such flights serve
* **Capacity:** Approximately 12 passengers
* **Booking authority for the trip:** Reported as Bradley Hansell's office
* **Reported callsigns:** Investigation notes list this flight as *"SAM-702 (or SAM-000 or SAM-112)"* — three candidate Special Air Mission callsign readings for the same airframe. Each is tracked on its own page so the identifier is searchable: [SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702) (the primary reading), [SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000), and [SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112).

## Flight pattern leading up to September 10

According to a tipster's email (December 10, 2025) circulated by Candace Owens:

* **September 7, 2025:** Andrews → Colorado Springs.
* **September 8, 2025:** Colorado Springs → Tucson, then 5 hours later Tucson → (transponder off).
* **September 9, 2025, 12:31 PM:** Reappears flying **Fort Huachuca → El Paso**, then El Paso → Andrews.
* The call sign reportedly changed from **RCH to SAM** as the aircraft entered Fort Huachuca on September 8.

This was reported as the only time the tipster had observed this aircraft at Fort Huachuca.

## Connection to the September 9 lieutenant-colonel meeting

Candace Owens has reported that a military whistleblower (referred to as "Harry Myers") walked into a high-clearance meeting at Fort Huachuca on September 9 attended by approximately 12 lieutenant colonels — Pentagon-level brass — described by the source as a "final op planning session — isolated, high-clearance, no walk-ins allowed." The seven-hour interrogation that followed was reportedly led by a "Captain Neff." The combination of the SAM-class aircraft, the call-sign change, and the meeting timing has been flagged for federal investigation.

Commentators have advanced various theories about what the flight could mean — reportedly ranging from a command-and-control role, to high-level official involvement, to cover-up elements — and have placed it within the broader scrutiny of government aviation in the investigation. These are presented as questions and reported theories about pre-event military and intelligence activity, not as established facts, and no named individual has been shown to have committed any crime.

## Other reported users of this aircraft

According to the same tipster, **Pam Bondi** flew on SAM 99-0404 approximately five months prior, from the Tampa area back to DC.

## The AES Tennessee flyover claim

A separate strand of reporting ties this same airframe to the **Accurate Energetic Systems (AES)** plant in Tennessee. According to **Stew Peters**, who presents this as his own allegation, the Defense Secretary's plane (tail 99-0404, cited under callsign SAM702) reduced altitude — flying low and slow — over the AES facility that later detonated on **October 10, 2025**, killing 16 workers. He ties this to the "exploding-mic" theory and a reported Department of Defense order to AES. These are presented as reported allegations for verification, not established facts. See [AES Factory Explosion](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) and [Stew Peters Broke the AES / Mic Connection](/Mic/Stew_Peters_Broke_AES_Mic) for the fuller account.

3. Who attended the Fort Huachuca meeting on September 9?
4. Why did Bradley Hansell's office reportedly book this trip?

## Track This Aircraft

Research this aircraft's flight history yourself on both major flight-tracking sites. They differ in one important way:

* [FlightRadar24 — 99-0404](https://www.flightradar24.com/data/aircraft/99-0404) — large commercial tracker that honors the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list, so flights an owner or the government has asked to hide can be missing.
* [ADS-B Exchange — 99-0404](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?reg=99-0404) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor LADD, so it often shows blocked aircraft and tracks the filtered sites leave out.
