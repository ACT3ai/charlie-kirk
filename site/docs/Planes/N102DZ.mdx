# N102DZ Kirk's Plane

Tail number **N102DZ** is reported in public flight records as the aircraft associated with **Charlie Kirk's** travel into Utah on the morning of September 10, 2025. Reporting describes it as a larger private aircraft (15–16 passenger capacity) that flew from **Scottsdale, Arizona** into the Salt Lake City / Provo area ahead of his Utah Valley University appearance.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** N102DZ
* **Type:** Private aircraft, reported 15–16 passenger capacity
* **Association:** Reported as Charlie Kirk's plane for the September 10 travel into Utah

## Reported flight record

Public flight records and reporting describe the morning travel as:

* Kirk's plane (tail **N102DZ**) is reported to have landed at **Salt Lake City International (SLC) around 9:30 a.m. MDT**, after departing **Scottsdale, Arizona**, for a scheduled interview before the UVU event.
* A separate set of records circulated by investigators lists N102DZ arriving into **Scottsdale at ~1:13 p.m. local**, departing again immediately, and **landing into Provo (PVU) at ~3:31 p.m. local** — i.e., later movements on the same day after the shooting.

The two record sets describe different legs of the day and are reproduced here as reported; the exact reconciliation of the full day's itinerary is one of the open questions below.

## Why it is on the list

N102DZ is included in the investigation's aircraft list because it is the inbound aircraft tied to Charlie Kirk's own arrival, which anchors the day's timeline and the movements of his immediate party. Establishing its full manifest and itinerary helps fix who traveled with Kirk and what aircraft were co-located at Provo in the hours around the event.

## Open questions

1. What is the complete, reconciled itinerary for N102DZ on September 10, 2025?
2. Who was aboard on the inbound leg with Kirk, and who handled the return legs?
3. Who is the registered owner and operator of the aircraft?
4. Where did the aircraft and its crew go after the Provo arrival?
