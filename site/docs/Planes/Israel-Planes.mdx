# Israel Planes

This page is the hub for the **Israel thread** running through the aircraft angle of the case. The core allegation, attributed to **Candace Owens** and amplified by independent flight trackers, is that the Israel/Egyptian government jets reported near Charlie Kirk and Turning Point USA (TPUSA) events **regularly flew in and out of Israel** — frequently with their **transponders switched off** so the legs read as "Cairo-to-Cairo" or showed no destination — and that this Israel routing is what makes the fleet, in Owens's words, the source of so much "panic." From there the theory branches: that the Israel/Egyptian aircraft functioned as **proxies or cover for Israeli intelligence**, and that the assassination itself was a foreign-intelligence operation. Every figure and route on this page is a **researcher claim built on public ADS-B data, an unverified witness account, or a contested ownership association** — not a confirmed record — and the most important corroborated fact, the counterarguments, and the defamation cautions are all laid out in full below.

## The core Israel claim

The Israel angle grew directly out of the [shadowing investigation](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika). After researchers tallied the Israel/Egyptian jets' alleged overlaps with the Kirks' travel, **Candace Owens** told her audience that the reason the planes had "caused such panic" was not the overlaps themselves but where the same jets went the rest of the time. In her framing: **"there is overwhelming evidence that these planes regularly fly in and out of Israel."** Independent trackers echoed the point — that the **[Israel/Egyptian Plane Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)** of at least five tails ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND), [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV), [SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM)) is not just an Israel/Egyptian fleet but one that routinely transits Israel.

The interpretation that posters attach to that pattern is that the Egyptian-registered aircraft were acting as **proxies, cutouts, or cover for Israeli or "deep-state" operations** — a foreign air force's tail numbers providing diplomatic cover for intelligence work that, the theory goes, served other principals. This is the connective tissue between the **planes** evidence and the broader **[Israel](/Israel/overview)** angle of the investigation. It is a reading of public flight data, not an established finding, and the same data has an innocent explanation — ordinary Egypt–Israel security and diplomatic contact — laid out near the end of this page.

## SU-BTT: the documented Israel-flight pattern

The single most detailed Israel claim attaches to the lead jet, the **"yellow plane" [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** (an Israel/Egyptian Dassault Falcon 7X). Independent compilers — credited on X to **@nihilox** and amplified by **@ProjectConstitu** (Project Constitution) — assembled a year-long list of dates on which they say SU-BTT flew toward Israel. Their claim is that the public logs read **Cairo-to-Cairo (CAI → CAI)** or show no destination because the jet flew with its **transponder off** and actually landed in Israel. The dates they list (presented here as the trackers' compiled claims, not confirmed flights) are:

* **Jan:** 05, 12, 26 — **Feb:** 11, 16, 18, 23 — **Mar:** 18 — **Apr:** 05, 08 — **May:** 27
* **Jun:** 14, 18, 21, 22 — **Jul:** 05, 08 (two flights), 13 — **Aug:** 05
* **Sep:** 20 (two), 21, 30 (two) — **Oct:** 13, 21, 22, 26 — **Nov:** 18, 30 — **Dec:** 02, 07

@nihilox summarized the pattern in absolute terms, posting what the account called **"irrefutable evidence SU-BTT regularly flies into Israel."** The alleged ADS-B signature, as these trackers describe it, is consistent: the jet departs Cairo, **flies south or east, then goes dark** — dropping off public tracking — and reappears only after the leg into Israel would have been completed. One thread also cites an **eyewitness** said to place the **head of Egyptian intelligence landing in Israel shortly after** one such blackout. That witness account is unverified.

### The one corroborated leg

Most of the SU-BTT Israel dates rest only on ADS-B screenshots, but **one leg is corroborated by mainstream reporting**. Per **Israeli Channel 12 News**, SU-BTT flew **Cairo to Israel on October 21, 2025**, reportedly carrying **Hassan Rashad**, the head of Egyptian intelligence. Rashad is a foreign public official, and an Egyptian intelligence chief flying to Israel is consistent with the two countries' long-standing diplomatic and security contacts. This single corroborated trip is important in both directions: it shows the Cairo–Israel routing is real and happens, **and** it shows the obvious innocent context for it. It does not establish that the other listed legs occurred as described, nor that any of them connect to the Charlie Kirk case.

## SU-BND and the broader fleet's Israel routing

The companion **"blue plane" [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** (an Israel/Egyptian Gulfstream) carries the same allegation in a softer form. Owens and the trackers group SU-BND with SU-BTT as **"two Egyptian Air Force intelligence jets"** and note that the Israel/Egyptian jets **"routinely fly in and out of Israel,"** folding that detail into theories of the fleet monitoring Erika Kirk, the wider family, or TPUSA events. The [Israel/Egyptian Plane Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) page extends the same observation to the fleet as a whole: because researchers say the aircraft routinely transit Israel, some threads stretch the theory to alleged **Israeli or other foreign-intelligence involvement** in the broader plot.

The remaining armada tails — [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV), and [SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM) — are named in the same fleet pattern, with the Israel-routing claim applied to the group rather than documented leg-by-leg for each. Two further foreign-registered tails, **T7ELL** and **EJM36**, are flagged alongside the SU- jets in the disclosure list; public reporting on their routes is minimal, and no Israel leg has been specifically established for either. They appear here so the fleet picture is complete.

## Transponder dark, then Israel — the recurring signature

The thread that ties the Israel claim together across aircraft is **transponders going dark**. The pattern posters describe is the same each time: a tracked aircraft turns off its ADS-B transponder, vanishes from public radar for a stretch, and (in the theory) uses that gap to fly a leg that does not appear in the logs — sometimes characterized generally as planes that **"turn off their transponders and then head toward Israel."** The signature shows up in several entries in this catalog:

* **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** — the Cairo-to-Cairo "go dark over the Mediterranean" pattern described above, the centerpiece of the Israel-flights compilation.
* **[SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND)** — the "transponder ON but no takeoff" detail at Provo on September 10, and the broader routine-Israel-routing claim.
* **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** — a private Bombardier Challenger 300 that departed Provo about an hour after the shooting and **lost its transponder mid-flight**; its ownership is separately tied by commentators to alleged Israel connections (see below).

A standing caution applies to every item on this list: transponder gaps are also produced by **terrain shadowing, receiver-coverage holes, and trackers snapping an aircraft's origin to the nearest public airport.** A dark stretch on a public feed is not, by itself, evidence of a hidden flight to anywhere — let alone to Israel. The "dark then Israel" reading is an inference layered on top of the gap, not a recorded destination.

## N888KG and the reported Israel ownership ties

The private jet **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** enters the Israel thread through ownership rather than flight path. Its registered operator is reported as **N888KG LLC**, with **Derek Maxfield** listed as an associated owner in some public databases, and commentators further associate the entity with the companies and foundations **Komigo** and **Saprea**. Some of those same commentators note **reported Israel connections** to the ownership, and a separate set of X posts claims the owners made **Chabad Lubavitch donations**.

Every one of those ownership associations is **unverified and reported strictly as an allegation**. Derek Maxfield is a living private individual who has **publicly denied any connection** between the aircraft and either Charlie Kirk or the shooting, explaining the September 10 flight as a prescheduled trip whose tracking gap was routine rural-coverage loss near a non-towered airport. Naming the entity here is not an accusation against any person, and no wrongdoing by any named individual is established or implied. The N888KG Israel angle is the most speculative thread in this catalog: it rests entirely on contested ownership claims, not on any flight to or from Israel.

## The wider "Israel killed Charlie Kirk" framing

The planes feed a larger theory promoted on X and in some podcast coverage: that the assassination was a **"Mossad hit"** or a warning to Charlie Kirk. Posters point to messages they say show Kirk **feared Israel would "kill him if he stepped out of line,"** to his having questioned the response to October 7 and hosted anti-Zionist guests, and to his comments tying Jeffrey Epstein to Israeli intelligence. Some threads blend the plane claims with other alleged methods (a "rigged microphone" or exploding device). On the other side, **Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu** is reported to have quickly and publicly stated that **Israel "didn't do it."**

This framing is the **[Israel section](/Israel/overview)** of the investigation, not the aircraft section, and it is contested on every point. It is summarized here only because it is the destination the plane-Israel claims are pointing toward — the reason trackers treat "these jets fly to Israel" as significant. The motive analysis, Kirk's own Israel statements, and Netanyahu's denial are documented in full on the dedicated Israel pages. Netanyahu and the other living people named in that theory are entitled to the same attribution and defamation cautions applied throughout this site; no claim of wrongdoing by any of them is established here.

## Counterarguments and fairness

The Israel thread is **contested**, and several plain readings cut against it:

* **Egypt–Israel contact is routine.** The one corroborated leg — an Egyptian intelligence chief flying Cairo to Israel — is exactly what decades of formal Egypt–Israel security and diplomatic relations produce. A pattern of Israel/Egyptian government flights toward Israel is consistent with normal statecraft, not necessarily with any operation.
* **A single corroborated flight does not validate the list.** Channel 12 confirmed one October 21 leg. The dozens of other dates remain **ADS-B-screenshot claims** that no outlet has confirmed, and the "transponder off therefore Israel" inference is unprovable from a public feed.
* **The overlap math behind the whole fleet theory is disputed.** Commentator **Liz Wheeler** argues the tracking tallies use loose criteria and that under strict same-day, same-location parameters the overlap "dwindles to one." A separate reviewer's audit claimed **five of eight** cited overlaps were inaccurate. The Israel claim sits on top of that same contested dataset.
* **John Cullen's maintenance explanation.** Open-source investigator **John Cullen** — himself central to the plane investigation — has **dismissed** a strong assassination link, pointing to long-standing Falcon and VIP-jet maintenance contracts at Provo's Duncan Aviation facility dating back to roughly 1999. Under that reading, the Israel/Egyptian jets are at a US MRO for service, and where they fly between visits is a separate matter.
* **N888KG's owner denies everything.** The Israel-ownership claim against N888KG is unverified, and the owner has publicly rejected any tie to the case.
* **Netanyahu's denial.** The head of the government the larger theory accuses has flatly denied involvement.

In short: the Israel thread rests on a real, partially corroborated pattern of Israel/Egyptian flights toward Israel, wrapped in inferences the available public data cannot prove. Both the surveillance/operation reading and the routine-diplomacy-plus-maintenance reading are live, and only records that are not yet public could settle which is right.

## Open questions

1. Of the SU-BTT dates compiled as "flights to Israel," how many survive a documentary audit — and how many are Cairo-to-Cairo legs with mundane explanations?
2. Who was aboard SU-BTT and SU-BND on the alleged Israel legs, beyond the one corroborated October 21 flight?
3. Does the "transponder off, then Israel" inference hold for any specific leg where landing records could be checked, or is it always inferred from a coverage gap?
4. What, concretely, are N888KG's ownership ties, and do any of the reported Israel associations hold up?
5. Does any evidence connect a documented Israel flight to the events of September 10, 2025 — or does the Israel thread stay at the level of pattern and association?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The passenger manifests for the Israel/Egyptian jets' alleged Israel legs, the ADS-B and landing records that would confirm or refute the "transponder off, then Israel" pattern, and the true ownership of N888KG are exactly the kinds of records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are designed to force into the open — so the Israel theory and the routine-diplomacy explanation can both be tested against documents rather than screenshots.

## Note on Registration & Control

The SU-registered jets discussed on this page carry Egyptian registrations — the **SU-** prefix is Egypt's national civil registry, so those registrations are real and not in dispute. What is *not* settled is **who ultimately controlled the jets.** If Egyptian front companies were registered as their operators, those registrations — Egyptian on paper — may have been arranged on behalf of an intelligence service from another country. That open possibility is why this site labels them **"Israel/Egyptian"**: the label flags the unresolved question of control. It is **not** a claim that any aircraft was Israeli-registered or Israeli-owned, which the public record does not establish.
