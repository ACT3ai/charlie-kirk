# N872RA Provo Arrival

A **Hawker Beechcraft** (reported 7-seat business jet) registered **N872RA** is listed in flight records circulated by independent investigators as one of several private aircraft arriving at **Provo Municipal Airport (PVU)** on September 10, 2025. It is included on the investigation's aircraft list so its manifest can be reviewed alongside the other Provo arrivals around the day of the assassination.

## Aircraft identification

* **Tail number:** N872RA
* **Type:** Hawker Beechcraft (reported 7 seats)
* **Role on the list:** A private aircraft logged arriving at Provo on September 10, 2025

## Reported flight record

As reproduced from flight-record screenshots circulated by investigators:

* **Departed** Santa Barbara, California at **~2:19 p.m. local**.
* **Arrived** into Provo (PVU) at **~4:36 p.m. local**.

This is a late-afternoon arrival, after the shooting. The Santa Barbara origin is noted by researchers because another flagged aircraft ([N560TW](/Planes/N560TW)) also routed through Santa Barbara earlier that day. No public reporting ties N872RA, its crew, or its passengers to the event.

## Why it is on the list

Like the other Provo arrivals, N872RA is on the list so its passenger manifest can be checked. The shared Santa Barbara connection with N560TW is the specific reason researchers asked for it to be pulled rather than treated as routine.

## Open questions

1. Who was aboard N872RA on the September 10 arrival into Provo?
2. Is there any connection between this Santa Barbara departure and the Santa Barbara leg of N560TW?
3. Who is the registered owner and operator?
