# Fort Huachuca / Libby Army Airfield

**[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)**, near **Sierra Vista, Arizona**, is the home of the **US Army Intelligence Center** and the Army's counterintelligence school. Its airfield is **Libby Army Airfield (KFHU)**. The base draws scrutiny in this investigation because a US Air Force VIP jet — **Special Air Mission (SAM) [99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404)**, a C-37A Gulfstream V — was tracked through Fort Huachuca on **September 8-9, 2025**, roughly 36 hours before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at Utah Valley University. Whistleblower reporting amplified by [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) alleges a connection between that stop and a reported high-clearance meeting of about a dozen lieutenant colonels on September 9. No released passenger manifest, visitor log, or official record confirms who was aboard the aircraft or who attended that meeting. Everything below is assembled from public claims, a tipster's email, and named-source reporting; it is presented as documented allegations, not as established fact, and no named individual has been shown to have committed any crime.

The detailed flight narrative lives on the [SAM 99-0404 Flight](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) page and the callsign pages. This page is about the **airport** — what Fort Huachuca is, which planes used it, and why an Army intelligence base appears in the timeline of the assassination.

## About the airfield

* **Base:** Fort Huachuca, Sierra Vista, Cochise County, Arizona.
* **Airfield:** Libby Army Airfield (ICAO KFHU), the installation's military airport.
* **Type:** US Army intelligence base airfield — not a civil/commercial airport.
* **Role on base:** Fort Huachuca is the US Army Intelligence Center and the Army's counterintelligence and signals-intelligence training hub. According to investigation notes circulated on X, the post trains thousands of counterintelligence personnel each year.
* **Why a stop matters:** A Special Air Mission VIP jet does not routinely land at an Army intelligence base. The investigation notes flag this stop because it was reported as the **only** time the tipster had ever observed tail 99-0404 at Fort Huachuca, and because it lands one day before the killing.

Investigation notes also reference Fort Huachuca's "signals" department in the context of **Candace Owens'** "exploding-mic" theory — she has reported receiving emails claiming the base has a department capable of assembling rigged microphone hardware. This is presented as her reported claim from correspondents, not as a verified capability or finding.

## Which planes used it

**SAM 99-0404** is the aircraft tied to this airfield in the investigation:

* **Aircraft:** US Air Force C-37A Gulfstream V VIP transport, callsign **SAM** (Special Air Mission). Reported callsign readings: [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) (primary), [SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000), and [SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112).
* **Operator:** US Air Force, 89th Airlift Wing, based at **[Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews)**.
* **Role of this airport:** **Stopover**, September 8-9, 2025 (pre-event).
* **September 7, 2025:** Andrews → Colorado Springs.
* **September 8, 2025:** Colorado Springs → Tucson, then Tucson → (transponder reportedly off).
* **September 9, 2025:** Reappears flying **Fort Huachuca → El Paso**, then El Paso → Andrews. The tipster notes the aircraft shown leaving the Fort Huachuca area around **11:43 AM** on the 9th.
* The callsign reportedly changed from **RCH** (Reach, the standard Air Mobility Command transport prefix) to **SAM** on the Fort Huachuca leg.

Separately, investigation notes report that after Fort Huachuca the activity moved to **[Biggs Army Airfield](/Planes/Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield)** (El Paso, TX) — the home of the **[HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES)** ISR program that [**Bradley Hansell**](/People/bradley-hansell) is reported to oversee, and the field from which the [N1098L spy plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview) departed on September 10. The El Paso leg of the 99-0404 flight overlaps geographically with Biggs.

**Unconfirmed speculation — N582MM:** Some X discussions have raised a possible link between the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)-associated jet **[N582MM](/Planes/N582MM)** and Fort Huachuca. This claim is **unconfirmed** and rests on pattern-spotting in OSINT threads, not on documented flight records presented here. It is noted only so readers know it has been raised; treat it as speculation, not evidence.

## What trip it was on

Per a tipster's email forwarded to **Candace Owens** (dated December 10, 2025), the trip ran from **Joint Base Andrews** westward and back: Andrews → Colorado Springs → Tucson → (transponder off) → reappears at **Fort Huachuca** → El Paso → Andrews. According to the investigation notes, **Bradley Hansell's** office reportedly booked the outbound legs (Andrews → Colorado Springs → Tucson) before the callsign turned to SAM. A separate report cited by Owens places Hansell visiting **JTF South Border** leadership in Colorado Springs — the same unit the whistleblower says he encountered at Fort Huachuca the next day. These are reported attributions, not confirmed facts.

## The reported September 9 meeting

According to **Candace Owens**, a military whistleblower — the source named in later reporting as [**Mitch Snow**](/People/mitch-snow) (also called "Harry Myers") — walked into a high-clearance meeting at Fort Huachuca attended by roughly **12 lieutenant colonels**, described by the source as a "final op planning session — isolated, high-clearance, no walk-ins allowed." Investigation notes add detail attributed to this reporting:

* A roughly seven-hour interrogation reportedly followed, led by a **Captain [Adam Neff](/People/adam-neff)** of Cookeville, TN.
* The meeting reportedly concluded around **7:30 AM** local.
* Owens has stated she received an **incident report** indicating bomb dogs were called at **11:17 AM**, with the visit logged as a possible bomb threat.
* Commentary on X has noted that a **Lt. Col. [Daniel Neff](/People/daniel-neff)** is described as a cousin of [**Blake Neff**](/People/blake-neff), a longtime Charlie Kirk producer and TPUSA figure — a connection presented as a question raised by commentators, not as a proven link.

All of the above is reported as documented public claims and attributed allegations. Captain Adam Neff, Blake Neff, Bradley Hansell, Mitch Snow, Candace Owens, and [Pete Hegseth](/People/pete-hegseth) are living persons; nothing here establishes that any of them committed any crime, and the meeting's purpose has not been confirmed by any official source.

## Why it matters to the investigation

The core fact this page documents is narrow and verifiable in the public record: a SAM-class VIP jet was reported at an Army intelligence base roughly 36 hours before the assassination, with a mid-trip callsign change and a transponder gap. The interest follows from the base itself — Fort Huachuca is the Army's intelligence and counterintelligence hub, not a routine VIP destination. Commentators have advanced theories ranging from a command-and-control role, to high-level official involvement, to cover-up elements; these are presented as questions and reported theories about pre-event military and intelligence activity, not as established facts. Fort Huachuca also recurs elsewhere in the investigation: the reported targeting of whistleblower Mitch Snow by a Fort Huachuca-trained counterintelligence figure, and a visual-comparison claim that interior footage in an alibi video resembles Fort Huachuca rooms — both treated here as documented public claims, not findings.

## Open questions

1. Who was aboard SAM 99-0404 when it landed at Fort Huachuca, and who were the other passengers on a roughly 12-seat aircraft?
2. What was the purpose of the reported September 9 meeting of lieutenant colonels, and who attended?
3. Why did the callsign change from RCH to SAM on the Fort Huachuca leg, and who was reportedly picked up in Colorado Springs?
4. Why was the transponder reportedly off between Tucson on September 8 and Fort Huachuca on September 9?
5. What is the documented relationship, if any, between the Fort Huachuca stop and the subsequent Biggs Army Airfield / HADES activity tied to the N1098L flight?
