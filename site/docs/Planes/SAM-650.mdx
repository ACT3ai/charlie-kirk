# SAM650 Flight

**SAM650** is a **Special Air Mission (SAM)** callsign that surfaces in the broader citizen tracking of US government VIP aircraft in the days surrounding the September 10, 2025 assassination. Unlike the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) / [SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702) cluster, SAM650 is not tied to a single confirmed tail number in the case notes — it appears in route-tracking of pre-event government flights that touched **[Joint Base Andrews](/Planes/Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews)**, **Nashville**, and **Colorado Springs**, among other nodes. It is catalogued here so the callsign is searchable and its open questions are recorded. Nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any person.

## Why this callsign is on the list

Citizen investigators compiling the movements of Special Air Mission aircraft in early September 2025 logged SAM650 among the government VIP flights crossing the same corridors that recur elsewhere in the case — the Andrews hub, the Nashville/Tennessee corridor (also central to the [AES](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) thread), and Colorado Springs. The flight is included for completeness in the survey of government aviation around the event, not because any specific link to the assassination has been established. Its value is as one more government tail/callsign whose route and manifest a disclosure process could confirm or rule out.

## What is reported

* **Callsign:** SAM650 (Special Air Mission)
* **Reported route nodes:** Joint Base Andrews (ADW), Nashville, Colorado Springs, and — per OSINT compilations — **Wilmington, Delaware**, among others
* **Window:** The pre-event days surrounding September 10, 2025
* **Status in the case:** A broader-context government VIP flight, not tied to a confirmed single tail number in the notes

The Nashville and Colorado Springs nodes are the reason SAM650 draws attention: Nashville is the nearest major airport to the [AES](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) facility in Tennessee, and Colorado Springs recurs in the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) route (Andrews → Colorado Springs on September 7). Researchers flagged SAM650 alongside those routes for cross-checking, without asserting a connection.

## Wilmington itinerary and the Fort Huachuca timeline (OSINT claims)

In the same X/OSINT threads that compiled the Israel/Egyptian-plane data, citizen researchers describe SAM650 (grouped with [SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702)) as flying an itinerary they call suspicious: **Wilmington → Andrews → Nashville**, with what they label "Colorado Springs syncs." These are unverified online claims drawn from public ADS-B/flight-tracking screenshots, not confirmed flight records, and no source establishes a link to the assassination. The official investigation describes a lone shooter.

The Wilmington (Delaware) node is the new element here. Separate threads tie Delaware to the event — one claim, attributed to **Candace Owens**, alleges that "a foreign dignitary was picked up after arriving from Provo the morning of Charlie's assassination on the Egyptian plane, and then transferred to 920 King" in Wilmington (described in those posts as a joint DHS/FBI/DEA office). That allegation concerns the Israel/Egyptian aircraft, not SAM650; it is noted only because both threads place activity at the same Delaware node and a disclosure process could confirm or rule out any overlap.

Researchers also link the Colorado Springs node to **[Fort Huachuca](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca), Arizona** — a military intelligence hub — and to alleged meetings said to have occurred around **September 8–9, 2025**, just before the event. This is presented in those threads as a timeline theory only. No manifest, attendee list, or flight record corroborating such meetings has been made public, and the claim is unproven.

## Open questions

1. What tail number flew under the SAM650 callsign in this window?
2. What was the full route and who was aboard?
3. Is the overlap with the Andrews / Nashville / Colorado Springs nodes meaningful or coincidental?
4. Does the reported Wilmington → Andrews → Nashville itinerary hold up against actual flight records, or is it a misread of public ADS-B data?
5. Is there any record substantiating the alleged September 8–9, 2025 Colorado Springs / Fort Huachuca meetings, or is that timeline theory unsupported?

## Track This Aircraft

SAM650 is a **callsign**, not a confirmed tail number, so there is no single-aircraft page to deep-link until it is matched to an airframe. Research it by callsign on the unfiltered tracker:

* [ADS-B Exchange — filter callsign SAM650](https://globe.adsbexchange.com/?filterCallSign=^SAM650$) — volunteer-fed, unfiltered tracker that does **not** honor the FAA's [LADD](https://www.faa.gov/pilots/ladd) block list. The callsign filter shows matching aircraft currently broadcasting; resolving a past Special Air Mission flight may require ADS-B Exchange's historical data.
* [FlightRadar24](https://www.flightradar24.com/) — search "SAM650" in the tracker; note FlightRadar24 honors LADD, so government flights are often limited or absent.

**Tip:** Special Air Mission callsigns are reused across different airframes, so SAM650 may refer to more than one jet on different days. Once it is matched to a tail number, switch to that tail's **registration** and **ICAO hex** for a durable link.
