# SAM650 Flight

**SAM650** is a **Special Air Mission (SAM)** callsign that surfaces in the broader citizen tracking of US government VIP aircraft in the days surrounding the September 10, 2025 assassination. Unlike the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) / [SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702) cluster, SAM650 is not tied to a single confirmed tail number in the case notes — it appears in route-tracking of pre-event government flights that touched **Joint Base Andrews**, **Nashville**, and **Colorado Springs**, among other nodes. It is catalogued here so the callsign is searchable and its open questions are recorded. Nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any person.

## Why this callsign is on the list

Citizen investigators compiling the movements of Special Air Mission aircraft in early September 2025 logged SAM650 among the government VIP flights crossing the same corridors that recur elsewhere in the case — the Andrews hub, the Nashville/Tennessee corridor (also central to the [AES](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) thread), and Colorado Springs. The flight is included for completeness in the survey of government aviation around the event, not because any specific link to the assassination has been established. Its value is as one more government tail/callsign whose route and manifest a disclosure process could confirm or rule out.

## What is reported

* **Callsign:** SAM650 (Special Air Mission)
* **Reported route nodes:** Joint Base Andrews (ADW), Nashville, Colorado Springs, among others
* **Window:** The pre-event days surrounding September 10, 2025
* **Status in the case:** A broader-context government VIP flight, not tied to a confirmed single tail number in the notes

The Nashville and Colorado Springs nodes are the reason SAM650 draws attention: Nashville is the nearest major airport to the [AES](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory) facility in Tennessee, and Colorado Springs recurs in the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) route (Andrews → Colorado Springs on September 7). Researchers flagged SAM650 alongside those routes for cross-checking, without asserting a connection.

## Open questions

1. What tail number flew under the SAM650 callsign in this window?
2. What was the full route and who was aboard?
3. Is the overlap with the Andrews / Nashville / Colorado Springs nodes meaningful or coincidental?
