This section catalogs aircraft whose movements deviate from routine civilian patterns near
UVU in September 2025: a US Army [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) ISR jet making low passes, five Egypt-registered
VIP jets cycling through Provo, US government SAM flights touching [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview) the day
before the shooting, and private jets with transponder gaps or tight timeline conflicts.
The area matters because flight data is **primary-source-adjacent** — ADS-B is public — yet
manifests and mission tasking remain sealed.

Several patterns recur. **[N1098L](./N1098L/overview)** looped at low altitude the day of the
event; **[SU-BTT](./SU-BTT)** was on the ground Sept 4–10 with competing departure narratives;
the **[armada](./Egyptian-Armada)** allegedly shadowed Kirk travel for years per
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens); **[SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404)** stopped at Fort
Huachuca Sept 8–9. The [Sept 10 timeline](./Sept10-Flight-Timeline) page merges transponder
windows with hospital-timing claims. Each thread has innocent explanations (training, MRO
contracts, VIP logistics) documented on the per-tail pages.

New readers should start with **[Planes Investigation Index](./Planes_Investigation_Index)** for
the full map. For foreign-intel framing go **[SU-BTT](./SU-BTT)** → **[Israel Planes](./Israel-Planes)**;
for US military ISR go **[N1098L](./N1098L/overview)**; for command-and-control go
**[SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404)**. Ground truth requires manifests the
[Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) seek to compel. The foreign-aircraft threads cataloged here are
also the kind of leads former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his
center's parallel foreign-nexus review was ordered to abandon once Tyler Robinson was in custody.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

*(Aircraft OSINT and attributed claims — not findings that any pilot, passenger, or official ordered or carried out the assassination.)*

- **HADES / N1098L:** X users (e.g. [@Josh89220](/Influencers/x/josh89220) and amplifiers) cite FAA/transponder logs of an Army HADES-class ISR jet making low-altitude passes near UVU/Orem before and after the shooting — correlation claims documented under [N1098L](./N1098L/overview), not proven operational culpability.
- **Egyptian VIP tails:** Candace Owens and citizen trackers map [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) / [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND) (and related armada entries) overlapping Kirk/TPUSA travel and a ~Sept 4 Provo window; critics argue the theory was debunked or re-aimed at Erika-tracking tallies. See [Egyptian Armada](./Egyptian-Armada) and [Following Charlie or Erika](./Following-Charlie-Erika).
- **SAM / Fort Huachuca:** Pre-event VIP traffic ([SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) / [99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) cluster) fuels meeting-day narratives and defamation litigation over Huachuca claims — distinguish carefully from the Sept 11 [casket flight](./Casket-Flight) on [99-0004](/Planes/99-0004-Vance).
- Competing tallies and “debunk” cycles continue; **flight logs ≠ intent**. Demand manifests via FOIA/GRAMA and the [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) rather than treating X threads as verdicts.
