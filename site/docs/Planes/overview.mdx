## What this area covers

This section catalogs the aircraft activity around UVU and the broader Utah corridor in the days surrounding Charlie Kirk's assassination. The focus is on aircraft whose movements deviate from routine civilian or commercial patterns — military reconnaissance jets, foreign-registered government aircraft, US government VIP transports, and private planes that disabled their transponders mid-flight. Each plane covered here has been independently surfaced in public investigation by Candace Owens, Ian Carroll, Sam Parker, and other researchers tracking the case.

## Why — What More Can We Learn?

For each aircraft below we ask the same question: what more can we learn? Each line names what we want to uncover, the information that would help, and what it could ultimately tell us.

* [N1098L](./N1098L/overview) (callsign AXLE10, a US Army HADES/ATHENA ISR Bombardier Global 6500 that made low-altitude loops near UVU) — We care about who operated it and what its sensors captured that day, and if we can obtain the crew, mission tasking, and flight-data records, that could help show whether it was routine training or actively surveilling the area around the assassination.
* [SU-BTT](./SU-BTT) (an Israel/Egyptian Air Force jet present around Provo whose September 10 departure shows tracking inconsistencies suggesting a Dugway origin) — We care about who was aboard and its pattern of position overlaps with Kirk-family locations, and if we can obtain passenger manifests, communications, or tasking, that could help show whether foreign intelligence services were tracking people connected to the events.
* [SU-BND](./SU-BND) (the Israel/Egyptian "blue plane" Gulfstream reported parked at Provo for nearly four months in 2025, transponder on but not taking off at the moment of the shooting, before its September 13 Provo → Goose Bay → Paris → Cairo departure) — We care about the work order that covered the multi-month stay and the crew and passengers tied to it, and if we can obtain the Provo maintenance records and ADS-B history, that could help settle whether it was a routine service stay or part of a surveillance pattern.
* [SU-BTU](./SU-BTU) (an Israel/Egyptian Falcon 7X logging multiple Northeast-to-Utah hops — Feb 14 Provo → Wilmington, Mar 2 Detroit → Provo, Aug 8 Minot → Provo — that researchers tie to TPUSA Faith tour stops) — We care about who was aboard each Provo visit and whether the named-passenger claims hold up, and if we can obtain manifests and the maintenance-contract paperwork, that could help show whether the hops were event-shadowing or ordinary VIP service runs.
* [SU-BTV](./SU-BTV) (an Israel/Egyptian Falcon 7X whose 2025 Provo legs — Mar 2, Jul 2 local, and an Oct 2 visit one thread labeled "post-assassination cleanup?" — cluster pre-September) — We care about the passengers and purpose of those recurring Provo stops, and if we can obtain its flight records and any Duncan Aviation service log, that could help show whether the pattern is surveillance or maintenance.
* [SU-BGM](./SU-BGM) (an Israel/Egyptian Gulfstream IV, the fifth armada tail, with only spring-2025 legs on record — an April 10 Provo local and an April 16 Goose Bay → Paris → Provo arrival during Kirk's Midwest push) — We care about its crew and passengers and whether it has any September-window presence at all, and if we can obtain its records, that could help show whether it belongs in the assassination timeline or only in the broader fleet pattern.
* [Israel/Egyptian Armada](./Egyptian-Armada) (a coordinated group of Egyptian military-registered jets including SU-BTT and SU-BND, in and out of Provo) — We care about the full scope of their flight patterns relative to key individuals, and if we can obtain shared operational control, manifests, or coordination records, that could help show whether this reflected foreign intelligence interest around the assassination.
* [Israel Planes](./Israel-Planes) (the hub for the claim that the Israel/Egyptian jets regularly flew in and out of Israel — often with transponders off — and may have acted as proxies or cover for Israeli intelligence, plus N888KG's reported Israel ownership ties) — We care about whether the alleged Israel legs are real and who was aboard, and if we can obtain ADS-B and landing records and passenger manifests, that could help show whether the Israel routing connects to the assassination or is ordinary Egypt–Israel diplomacy.
* [SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404) (a USAF C-37A government Special Air Mission whose September 8–9 Fort Huachuca stop whistleblowers tie to a 12-colonel meeting) — We care about its passengers and purpose, and if we can obtain full flight records and manifests, that could help show whether US government personnel were positioned in ways relating to the day's events.
* [SAM702](./SAM-702) (the primary of three reported callsigns for that same tail 99-0404 government VIP flight, the one most discussed on Candace Owens' podcast and tied in citizen theories to a high-level Fort Huachuca meeting and a Las Vegas staging angle) — We care about who was aboard and whether any September 9 meeting occurred, and if we can obtain the official SAM flight log and manifest, that could help show whether the Fort Huachuca timeline is benign or significant.
* [SAM-000](./SAM-000) (one of three Special Air Mission callsigns — SAM-702 / SAM-000 / SAM-112 — reported for the same government VIP jet, tail 99-0404, associated with Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth) — We care about which callsign this flight actually used and who was aboard, and if we can obtain the official SAM flight log and manifest, that could help show whether the callsign ambiguity is benign or hides a flight that warrants scrutiny.
* [SAM-112](./SAM-112) (a second alternate callsign reported for that same tail 99-0404 Hegseth Special Air Mission flight, also tied to a reported low-and-slow pass over the AES plant in Tennessee) — We care about resolving the three candidate callsigns to one confirmed record and verifying the AES flyover, and if we can obtain the route and altitude logs, that could help show whether the reported flyover claim holds up.
* [SAM650](./SAM-650) (a separate Special Air Mission callsign that appears in broader citizen tracking of pre-event government VIP routes touching Joint Base Andrews, Nashville, and Colorado Springs) — We care about which tail flew it and who was aboard, and if we can obtain the route and manifest, that could help show whether its overlap with the Nashville/AES and Colorado Springs nodes is meaningful or coincidental.
* [N888KG](./N888KG) (a Bombardier Challenger 300 private jet that left Utah after the shooting, routed toward Wendover, and lost transponder tracking) — We care about who was aboard, its precise route, and the reason for the tracking gap, and if we can obtain the flight plan, passengers, or communications, that could help show whether it was a pre-scheduled trip or moved people or items relevant to the investigation.
* [N59906](./N59906) (a Piper survey aircraft operated by MARC Inc. reported to have flown four low-altitude camera-and-LiDAR passes over campus that morning) — We care about its mission parameters and what it documented near UVU, and if we can obtain its operator records, flight logs, or collected imagery, that could help show whether its presence was coincidental or tied to documentation efforts around the assassination.
* [N102DZ](./N102DZ) (the plane associated with Charlie Kirk, and reportedly with Erika Kirk, that anchors the day's timeline) — We care about its flight history and any reported changes to its records, and if we can obtain its full operational records and who authorized any changes, that could help show whether its movements add context on activities before, during, or after the assassination.
* [ISR Program Context](./ISR-Operations) (the US military Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance programs — HADES/ATHENA — behind platforms like N1098L) — We care about the standard procedures, sensor capabilities, and tasking of these jets when flying near civilian events, and if we can obtain mission tasking and data-sharing protocols, that could help show whether their presence was routine or part of heightened surveillance connected to the assassination.
* [N560TW](./N560TW) (a private jet from the Scottsdale area routed through Provo) — We care about its passengers, purpose, and exact timing around September 10, 2025, and if we can obtain its flight plan or occupants, that could help show whether it transported key individuals or equipment, or played a logistical role around the events.
* [N40JD](./N40JD) (a private aircraft that arrived into Provo around the same window) — We care about who was aboard, its origin, and activities right before and after landing, and if we can obtain manifests or ground-transport records, that could help show whether it brought personnel or items relevant to security, the event, or the investigation.
* [N872RA](./N872RA) (another Provo arrival in the same window) — We care about its passengers and schedule and any discrepancies with reported movements of people connected to TPUSA or the event, and if we can obtain full tracking data or related communications, that could help show whether it supports or challenges certain timelines in the investigation.
* [N55906](./N55906) (a tail flagged by independent investigator John Cullen in citizen flight-data analysis) — We care about its operators, purpose, and any patterns it revealed in Provo-area activity, and if we can obtain its full records or links to other tracked aircraft, that could help show whether it offers key insights into movements or surveillance related to the assassination.
* [N582MM](./N582MM) (an aircraft reported to be associated with Turning Point USA) — We care about its flight history, passengers, and purpose on or near September 10, 2025, and if we can obtain its operational logs or funding, that could help show whether it was used for routine organizational travel or carried individuals or items connected to the timeline.
* [99-0004](./99-0004-Vance) (a USAF C-32A / Boeing 757 VIP transport, callsign SAM912, reportedly operating as Air Force Two and logged to Las Vegas on May 27, 2025) — We care about its passengers and purpose, and if we can obtain its full flight records and manifests, that could help show whether this VIP flight has any bearing on the broader timeline. This entry alleges no wrongdoing by any person.
* [Casket Flight](./Casket-Flight) (Air Force Two, a USAF C-32A, which transported Charlie Kirk's casket from Salt Lake City to Phoenix on September 11, 2025, with Vice President JD Vance accompanying the remains alongside Erika Kirk and family) — We care about the full manifest and official records of the flight that carried his body, and if we can obtain them, that could help complete the immediate post-assassination timeline. This entry documents a respectful official transport and alleges no wrongdoing by any person.
* [N2100L](./N2100L) (a Bombardier Global 6500 in the same LASAI Aviation reconnaissance fleet as N1098L) — We care about its role and tasking as a fleet sister ship, and if we can obtain operator filings and crew details, that could help show whether N1098L was one airframe in a fleet of Army ISR-capable jets rather than a one-off charter.
* [Pilatus PC-12](./Pilatus-PC-12) (an unconfirmed report of a single-engine turboprop "circling" near UVU, with no tail number and no flight track) — We care about whether any specific PC-12 can actually be tied to a time and place near the campus, and if a tail number and ADS-B track surface, that could either substantiate the sighting or finally retire it as the lowest-confidence aircraft claim in the case.

## The substance of the evidence

Several flight patterns recur across the data. A US Army ISR-capable Bombardier Global 6500 (N1098L, callsign AXLE10) made low-altitude passes within a few miles of the UVU campus on the day of the shooting. A fleet of at least five Israel/Egyptian Air Force jets ([SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND), [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV), [SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM)) was in and out of Provo Municipal Airport in the days surrounding the event, with SU-BTT's morning departure on September 10 showing tracking inconsistencies suggesting it actually originated at Dugway Proving Ground rather than Provo. Researchers further allege these same Israel/Egyptian jets [routinely flew in and out of Israel](/Planes/Israel-Planes) — often with transponders off — the thread that ties the aircraft evidence to the foreign-intelligence theory of the case. A Special Air Mission (SAM 99-0404, a USAF C-37A) made a Fort Huachuca stop on September 8-9 that whistleblower reporting links to a 12-lieutenant-colonel meeting one day before the assassination. A private plane with tail number N888KG departed Utah after the shooting, landed in Wendover, and disabled its transponder. A separate aerial-survey contractor aircraft (Piper N59906, operated by MARC Inc.) is reported to have flown four low-altitude camera-and-LiDAR passes over the campus on the morning of the event. Charlie Kirk's own inbound plane (N102DZ) anchors the day's timeline, while several private jets — N560TW from Scottsdale, plus the Provo arrivals N40JD and N872RA — landed at or routed through Provo around the same window. Independent investigator John Cullen has flagged a further tail (N55906), and the proposed disclosure laws add N582MM, an aircraft reported to be used by TPUSA insiders. The SAM 99-0404 flight itself surfaces under three candidate callsigns in the notes — [SAM702](/Planes/SAM-702) (the primary reading, heavily discussed on Candace Owens' podcast), [SAM-000](/Planes/SAM-000), and [SAM-112](/Planes/SAM-112) — and a separate callsign, [SAM650](/Planes/SAM-650), appears in broader tracking of pre-event government routes through Andrews, Nashville, and Colorado Springs. Finally, the day after the shooting, [Air Force Two carried Charlie Kirk's casket](/Planes/Casket-Flight) from Salt Lake City to Phoenix on September 11, 2025, with Vice President JD Vance accompanying the remains — the one flight in this catalog whose purpose is openly and undisputedly reported.

## Military Airports

Several of the aircraft in this section did not start, stop, or end at civilian fields — they used active U.S. military airfields. Each military airport below has its own page covering which planes used it, on what dates, what trip they were on, and what is interesting about that airfield for the investigation.

* [Biggs Army Airfield (KBIF)](./Airport-Biggs-Army-Airfield) — Fort Bliss, TX. The U.S. Army airfield from which the [N1098L](./N1098L/overview) HADES ISR jet departed on the morning of September 10, 2025.
* [Fort Huachuca / Libby Army Airfield](./Airport-Fort-Huachuca) — Sierra Vista, AZ. The U.S. Army Intelligence Center airfield where [SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404) stopped on September 8–9, tied by whistleblower reporting to a pre-event meeting.
* [Joint Base Andrews (KADW)](./Airport-Joint-Base-Andrews) — Maryland. The Special Air Mission / VIP home base for [SAM 99-0404](./SAM-99-0404) and the [Air Force Two casket flight](./Casket-Flight).
* [Roland R. Wright Air National Guard Base](./Airport-Roland-Wright-ANGB) — Salt Lake City, UT. The Utah Air National Guard base where [Air Force Two](./Casket-Flight) received Charlie Kirk's casket on September 11, 2025.
