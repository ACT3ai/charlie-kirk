# Tent Investigation Index

The **tent and courtyard directly under Charlie Kirk** are where the killing occurred — and where
**grass was removed and pavers installed within days**. This memo routes the contractor names,
government-worker accounts, forensic-preservation questions, and proximity-shot theories without
treating any allegation as proven fact.

## Paving timeline at a glance

- **Sept 10** — Charlie Kirk shot at the UVU courtyard tent
- **~Sept 12** — work order issued (~two days post-event, per Hardscape Specialties notes)
- **Sept 14 (Sunday)** — **Dan Merrell** installs pavers; over-excavation strips topsoil
- **By Monday** — coordinator tells Merrell **"the governor and the FBI"** want it done; UVU later calls the work **"pre-scheduled"** (CK: "evidence contradicts")
