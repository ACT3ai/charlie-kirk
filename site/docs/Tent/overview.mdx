The tent zone is not a side detail — it is the **primary blood and trace surface** of the homicide.
Contractor **Dan Merrell** says he paved the courtyard on **Sunday, September 14, 2025**, after
**state facility workers** from **American Fork** and **Provo** coordinated the job and cited a
**governor and FBI Monday deadline** (his account, not confirmed by those offices). Parallel research
names **Hardscape Specialties LLC** — revived after ~20 years dormant — with ties reported to a
**Utah AG special agent** and **former UVU operations staff**. Strange-event **#15** in the master
file asks bluntly: **why pave the tent area?**

The substance splits into **who moved earth**, **what was destroyed**, and **whether the shot came
from inside the canopy**. **Two phases, not one:** an unidentified state crew reportedly stripped
[roughly ten inches of soil](./Dirt_Removed_Before_Pavers) before Merrell's hardscape crew ever
arrived. **Contractors:** [Merrell](./Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah) vs
[Hardscape Specialties](./Hardscape_Specialties) vs [unverified dad-company rumors](./Other_Contractors).
**Timeline:** work order ~two days post-event, install ~four days ([Sept 14](./Paving_Timeline_Sept_14)).
**Forensics:** excavation, pavers, and alleged **ceiling mudding** ([site alteration](./Site_Alteration_Evidence)).
**Mechanism:** [Canon XA55](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) places a **tent detonation** separate from a
**distant rifle**; [inside-tent theories](./Tent_Shooter_Theories) cite shell casings and fabric
penetration claims. Cross-read [FBI Crime Scene Paving](/FBI/Crime_Scene_Paving) and [UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers).

Prioritize [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index) → [Merrell](./Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah) +
[State Workers](./State_Workers_Groundskeepers) for authorization threads, then [Paving Timeline](./Paving_Timeline_Sept_14)
and [Site Alteration](./Site_Alteration_Evidence) for forensic preservation, then [Tent Structure](./Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie)
and [Shooter Theories](./Tent_Shooter_Theories) for proximity mechanism. [Mic](/Mic/overview) and
[Medical site changes](/Medical/construction_and_site_changes) cover the same ground from other angles.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Who authorized paving over the crime scene, paving and landscaping contracts and scheduling, and
explosive-residue testing of the tent and tunnel surfaces are things the
[Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that
aren't out yet.

On X, the highest-engagement 2026 refresh for this section is the Daniel Merrell hardscape package ([@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra), May 2026): the contractor is quoted claiming the Governor and FBI wanted paving done by Monday, clashing with routine-beautification messaging. Citizen investigators treat rapid courtyard alteration as a scene-preservation emergency whether or not any court later finds spoliation. Pair Merrell claims with the Sept 14 paving timeline, site-alteration evidence, and tent-shooter theories that depend on the original substrate and canopy geometry. Document contractor statements as attributed claims — not automatic proof of criminal intent by named officials.
