# Hardscape Specialties LLC

Research notes in the master investigation file and citizen threads allege that
**Hardscape Specialties LLC** — distinct from contractor **Dan Merrell's Hardscape Utah** firm —
is the **corporate shell** behind UVU courtyard hardscape work after September 10, 2025. This
page documents **reported ownership and timeline claims**. We do **not** assert that any owner
or employee knew of the assassination in advance, obstructed justice, or committed any crime.

## Company profile (reported claims)

| Field | Claimed detail |
|-------|----------------|
| Entity | **Hardscape Specialties LLC** |
| Status | **Dormant ~20 years**, revived **February 2025** |
| Owner | **[Michael Powell](/People/michael-powell)** — described online as **Utah Attorney General special agent** |
| Co-owner | **[Burton Romrell](/People/burton-romrell)** — described as **former UVU operations staff** |
| Scope | UVU **tent/courtyard** hardscape after the shooting |
| Work order | Issued **~two days** after September 10, 2025 |
| Start | Paving reportedly began **~four days** after the event (**Sunday, Sept 14**) |
| Process | **No public bidding** cited in notes; **overnight crew**; **Monday deadline** |

See also the fuller [UVU — Hardscape Specialties](/UVU/Hardscape-Specialties) page for UVU-campus
context and media posture.

## Two-firm puzzle

Citizen investigators flag **two names**:

1. **Hardscape Specialties LLC** — corporate entity with Powell/Romrell ties (research record).
2. **Hardscape Utah / Daniel Merrell** — on-camera paver installer ([Merrell account](./Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah)).

Merrell's video does not name Hardscape Specialties LLC. Open questions include whether the LLC
issued the work order, subcontracted Merrell, or represents a parallel reporting thread. Business
filings and invoices would resolve the relationship.

Neither firm appears to be responsible for the **earth removal that came first**. Merrell says the
courtyard was already dug out roughly **ten inches** by an unidentified state crew before any
hardscape work started — see [10 Inches of Dirt Removed Before the Pavers](./Dirt_Removed_Before_Pavers).
Whether Hardscape Specialties' work order covered that excavation, or only the pavers laid on top of
it, is unresolved.

## Why investigators care

If a **revived dormant LLC** with ties to **state law enforcement** and **former UVU operations**
altered the homicide scene within days, authorization and chain-of-custody questions follow —
without needing to prove criminal intent. UVU reportedly cited **pre-scheduled** work in some
postures; CK notes claim **evidence contradicts** that framing. Neither UVU nor the Utah AG has
released a full contract package as of public research compilations.

## UVU and media silence

Per the research record:

- UVU spokesperson reportedly **declined detailed questions** after the shooting.
- AP reporting: UVU president cited **pending external review** when declining specifics.
- KUTV/KATV noted courtyard changes without always naming contractor or authorizer.

## Defamation note

**Michael Powell**, **Burton Romrell**, and related officials are living persons. Allegations
about revived companies and scene alteration are **reported claims** from open-source research,
not adjudicated findings. We do **not** state that Powell, Romrell, or any employee participated
in a cover-up or had foreknowledge of the killing.
