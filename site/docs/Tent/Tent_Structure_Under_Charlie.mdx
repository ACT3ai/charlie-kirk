# Tent Structure Under Charlie

Charlie Kirk was at the **UVU courtyard tent/stage** when he was killed on September 10, 2025.
Everything under and immediately around that canopy — **decking, grass, mic wiring, tent poles,
backdrop vinyl, tunnel adjacency** — is the **core homicide surface** later covered by pavers. This
page describes **physical layout claims** from video, photos, and witness descriptions.

## Canopy and stage (reported)

According to compilations of event footage and witness accounts:

- A **fabric canopy** with **support poles** and **side panels / vinyl backdrops** (TPUSA branding).
- Partial shelter for the **stage** and some audience; open air and buildings visible beyond.
- Placement adjacent to a **tunnel or passageway** and **landscaped courtyard** — later central to
  line-of-sight and [paving disputes](./Paving_Timeline_Sept_14).

CK_FILE emphasizes: *"Right at the tent. Right where Charlie was seated."*

## Professional audio distance

The **Canon XA55** broadcast camera recorded **uncompressed four-channel audio** at 48 kHz,
positioned roughly **46 meters** from the tent per CK analysis. That distance provided an acoustic
buffer that citizen analysts say clarifies:

- **Stage/tent detonation** signature at ~**+321 ms**.
- Separate **distant rifle** signature ~**120 m** away.

See [Mic — Canon XA55](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) and [Tent Shooter Theories](./Tent_Shooter_Theories).

## Microphone placement under the shirt

CK_FILE flags **Phillip Goldsberry Jr.** placing a **wireless mic under Charlie Kirk's shirt** when
outdoor debates typically clip the mic **on the outside** with a windscreen. That detail matters for
[exploding-mic theories](/UVU/Tent) placing a device at the **chest/neck** interface. We do **not**
allege Goldsberry knew of any device or acted with criminal intent.

## Concealment features cited in proximity-shot threads

Citizen reconstructions ([ProjectConstitu thread](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1973304018572071043)) cite:

- **Concrete pillar** and **AC vent** near tent edges.
- **Boxes stacked behind the tent**.
- **Security positioning** limiting sight lines from crowd angles.

These are **hypothesis scaffolding** for inside-tent or corner-shot claims — not confirmed security
plans.

## Post-event surface under the tent

The **grass and soil directly under the stage** were reportedly **excavated and paved** within days
([Site Alteration](./Site_Alteration_Evidence)). Any **bullet strike on decking**, **blood soak into
soil**, **blast pit**, or **fragment scatter** on that surface is what investigators say was **lost
or buried** under paver base — independent of which contractor performed the work.
