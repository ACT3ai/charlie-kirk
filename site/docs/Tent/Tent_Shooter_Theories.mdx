# Tent Shooter Theories

Some analysts argue the killing mechanism originated **inside or immediately behind the event tent**
where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) stood — not from [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s alleged rooftop position with a .30-06. This
page documents **reported video, audio, and ballistics claims** from citizen threads (notably
[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1973304018572071043)). These are **contested
hypotheses**, not established facts. **Tyler Robinson** is charged but **not convicted**; we do
**not** assert any living person carried out a hit from inside the tent.

## Why the tent matters acoustically

The [Canon XA55](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) professional camera sat roughly **46 meters** from the tent.
CK_FILE audio analysis distinguishes:

- A **detonation/stage event at the tent** (~+321 ms on professional channels).
- A **distant rifle signature** ~**120 meters** away.

That **two-location acoustic split** is central to tent-proximity theories: a single rooftop .30-06
kill scenario struggles to explain both signatures on uncompressed multichannel audio.

## Shell casing and fabric penetration claims

Per the ProjectConstitu thread summarized in CK_FILE:

- A **cartridge** from a semi-auto weapon allegedly **ejects inside the tent**, rolls down tarp,
  and **falls out** — visible in slowed footage (citizen claim).
- Analysts debated a **scope or barrel** protruding through **tent fabric** for roughly an hour
  in the thread (their description).
- **Camera operator** allegedly spun **right toward the tent**, not left toward the Losee rooftop,
  immediately after the shot (frame analysis claim).

None of these interpretations have been confirmed by law-enforcement ballistics releases.

## CornerShot and Israeli-hardware framing

The same thread alleges use of an **Israeli-made CornerShot** system — a hinged mount allowing
aiming around barriers with a screen feed, often discussed with **9mm** platforms and **downward
shell ejection**. Proponents tie that to the **casing-roll** observation.

**Dr. [Chris Martenson](/People/chris-martenson)** ([@peakprosperity](/Influencers/x/peakprosperity)) is cited for **cavitation / overpressure** neck injury,
**necklace snap**, and **pugilist pose** — argued as more consistent with a **9mm +P** fragmenting
round dumping energy locally than a full-power .30-06 (citizen interpretation).

An **IR laser** allegedly reflected off **Dr. [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s** phone screen before impact (video
claim in thread). Treat as unverified frame analysis.

## Concealment setup (alleged)

Thread claims describe:

- **Security positioning** blocking sight lines.
- **Concrete pillar** and **AC vent** as cover.
- **Boxes stacked behind the tent** for concealed access.

These are **reconstruction hypotheses** from open-source video, not confirmed security diagrams.

## Proximity sound signature

Citizen **sound-signature analysis** in the thread places a shooter within roughly **36 feet** of
one recorder — far closer than the rooftop distance. Compare [witness Nick](/Witnesses/overview)
(MuppetMasher interview) describing **pops/fireworks** rather than .30-06 booms.

## Relationship to exploding-mic theory

[Tent Structure Under Charlie](./Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie) and [Mic section](/Mic/overview)
overlap here: shaped-charge or **rigged lapel mic** theories also place the fatal event **at the
chest/tent**, explaining rapid [site paving](./Paving_Timeline_Sept_14) as trace removal. Tent
shooter and exploding-mic threads are **compatible in location** but differ on **weapon type**.

## Defamation note

Living persons — event staff, security, vendors, foreign-state actors named in threads — must not
be stated as assassins here. Present claims with **attribution** and note **no court finding**
on inside-tent shooting.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

**Under-tent / tunnel shooter** theories remain tied to grey-van acoustics and IR-laser geometry (see [Grey Van Shooter](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter)).

- X continues to contrast **Losee rooftop** official path vs **cargo hatch / tunnel** POIs.
- Tent teardown and paving are argued to destroy the physical reference frame those models need — a **forensic-preservation** argument, not automatic proof of an under-tent gunman.
- No court has adopted a tent-tunnel shooter as the official cause of death.
