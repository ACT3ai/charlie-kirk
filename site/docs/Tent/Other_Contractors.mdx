# Other Contractors & Dad Rumor

Beyond **Hardscape Specialties LLC** and **Dan Merrell's Hardscape Utah**, social-media threads
name **other construction firms** and allege a **family tie** between suspect **Tyler Robinson's
father** and the company that paved the tent grass. CK_FILE marks this section **[Needs Confirming]**.
This page documents **named entities and rumors** only. We do **not** claim any owner knew of the
assassination, destroyed evidence, or committed a crime. **Tyler Robinson** is charged, not convicted.

## Viral framing

A recurring X post theme asks:

> "Who TF was the construction company that raced to UVU days after Charlie Kirk's assassination?
> They ripped up the bloody grass where he was shot & slapped down a concrete pad—PLUS re-mudded
> the tunnel roof behind it."

Official **contractor silence** in some media reports fueled speculation. That gap is real; the
**specific company identities** below are **not** equally verified.

CK paving section separately states: *"Rumor blamed suspect's father's company; reporting found
**no credible link**."* Readers should hold both the **viral dad-company claim** and the **no credible
link** note simultaneously until records release.

## How this differs from Merrell / Hardscape Specialties

The **on-camera contractor account** ([Daniel Merrell](./Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah)) names
**Hardscape Utah** and **state facility coordinators** — not Precision Granite or Crew GC. The
**Hardscape Specialties LLC** thread ([corporate page](./Hardscape_Specialties)) names **Michael
Powell** and **Burton Romrell**. The dad-company rumor is a **parallel social-media track** that
may be wrong, partially right, or refer to a different job phase (tunnel mudding vs courtyard pavers).

## Tunnel re-mudding claim

Some posts bundle **courtyard paving** with **tunnel roof re-mudding** behind the tent. Merrell's
public video centers on **paver over-excavation**, not tunnel work. If tunnel alteration occurred,
it may involve **different trades** than Merrell's crew — another reason to demand **scoped work
orders** rather than assuming one contractor did everything.

**Matt Robinson**, **Lori Robinson**, and employees of named firms are living persons. Present all
claims as **unverified allegations** from online research. No contractor here has been charged in
connection with scene alteration in public court records reviewed for this page.
