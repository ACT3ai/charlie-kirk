# DOJ Prosecution and Evidence Secrecy

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding that the Department of Justice, the FBI, or any individual government employee committed crimes, engaged in evidence tampering, or participated in a cover-up. Questions raised here are drawn from public commentary and must be treated as unproven allegations.
:::

## Overview

The Department of Justice's response to the Charlie Kirk assassination centers on the prosecution of **Tyler Robinson**. This page documents the public record on charges and evidence handling, plus transparency questions raised by commentators — not adjudicated findings about agency misconduct.

## Prosecution of Tyler Robinson (As Reported)

According to charging documents and public reporting:

- **Capital murder** and related charges against Robinson
- **Digital forensics** from devices cited in the case
- **Ballistics** — reported confirmation that a recovered rifle matched bullet fragments
- **Potential sentence** includes capital punishment under Utah law

## Sealed Evidence and Access Questions (Claims)

Commentators and citizen researchers have raised questions about how evidence is being handled:

- **Digital forensics warrants** reportedly sealed until March 2026, limiting public review of device data
- **Classification claims** — some commentators allege over-classification of information relevant to the case
- **International dimensions** — questions about whether foreign-involvement theories received adequate investigation
- **Discovery under protective orders** — court reports describe a large evidence volume shared under gag and protective orders

These items are **open transparency questions**, not proof of intentional suppression.

## Government Secrecy Themes (Allegations)

Recurring allegations in public commentary include:

- Excessive classification of investigation materials
- Limited public disclosure of international or intelligence-adjacent leads
- Gag orders constraining what participants and attorneys can say publicly

For a numbered index of specific cover-up allegations, see [(Possible) Cover-up](/CoverUp/overview). For FBI conduct specifically, see [FBI Investigation](/FBI/FBI_Investigation).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The sealed digital forensics on Robinson's devices and FBI 302 reports withheld by DOJ and the over-classified foreign-involvement records are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
