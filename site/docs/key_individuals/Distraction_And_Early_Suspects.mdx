# Distraction & Early Suspect Figures

Sophisticated-kill theories often include **decoy suspects** — people who absorb scanner traffic, press conferences, and public attention while the real exit completes. The dedicated section is [distraction_people](/distraction_people/overview); this page summarizes the **highest-signal names**.

## George Zinn — documented distraction admission

**George Hodgeson Zinn** ([distraction_people/George_Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)):

- Shouted that he shot Charlie Kirk to **draw attention from the real shooter** (police account)
- **Cleared within hours** — [Beau Mason](/key_individuals/Officials_And_Law_Enforcement) / DPS narrative
- Commentary links him to **Boston Marathon** and **9/11** "witness hotspot" claims — **unverified patterns**

We do **not** claim Zinn committed the assassination; police cleared him as gunman.

## Zachariah Qureshi — early custody, released

Per [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) and [FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi):

- **Heritage Foundation intern**; Arabic speaker; within **~25 feet** of Kirk
- **Kash Patel** reportedly announced suspect **in custody** — released **~90 minutes** later
- Citizen threads: did announcement **slow** the manhunt?
- **BYU / Romney Henderson** classmate tangents — speculative

**Zachariah Qureshi** was **not charged** as shooter. No crime allegation here.
