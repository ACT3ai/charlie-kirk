## The Hamptons "intervention" (claims)

CK_FILE dates the meeting to roughly **August 4, 2025** (some accounts say Aug 5–6, others conflate a later **Sept 9** contact). It is described as an **"intervention"** where Charlie Kirk was reportedly confronted by pro-Israel figures — named in commentary as **Rabbi Pesach Wolicki, Josh Hammer, Seth Dillon, Bill Ackman, and Natasha Hausdorff**. One thread claims Ackman pushed Charlie to "take the cash and fall in line... or else" — **advocacy framing, not a verified quote**.

Under later civil questioning, [**Carrie Prejean Boller**](/People/carrie-prejean-boller) reportedly asked **Seth Dillon** about the Hamptons meeting. Per CK_FILE, Dillon's **September 16, 2025** posts had characterized the weekend as "no angry confrontation" and "a perfectly enjoyable summer retreat," while critics maintain **Charlie left describing it as an intervention**. These are **competing characterizations**, not adjudicated findings.

## Why investigators list them

- Kirk's **late 2025 Israel criticism** coincided with **donor defunding threats** (open-source lists in CK_FILE)
- **July 20, 2025** Google search spikes overlap **first Tucker + Charlie AMFEST** week ([intelligence/Foreign_Search_IP](/intelligence/Foreign_Search_IP_Patterns))
- The meeting **preceded Kirk's public Israel shift** by roughly four days per CK_FILE timeline notes
- Meeting claims explain **motive** — not **day-of logistics**

We do **not** claim any attendee ordered or knew of the assassination. All named individuals are living and **not accused** of any crime.
