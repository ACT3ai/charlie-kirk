# Eyewitnesses & On-Site Workers

These individuals are peripheral because they claim **direct sensory contact** with the shot or the shooter — often **conflicting** with the rooftop Tyler Robinson narrative.

## Construction worker — [Dylan Hope](/People/dylan-hope)

At **[785 College Dr](/Locations/Construction_Site_785_College)** ([Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/)):

- **Electrician / construction worker** on site Sept 10
- Described man in **black trench coat, cargo pants, greasy hair** — matching early dispatch "all black tactical" language
- Crew shown **photo by LE** that **did not match** Tyler Robinson

We do **not** claim Hope's sighting identifies the shooter — it is **one data point** in [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview).
