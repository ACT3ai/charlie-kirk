# TPUSA Staff — September 10 Timeline

**TPUSA leadership** was on-site when Charlie Kirk was shot. Citizen timelines focus on **who called whom** in the first minutes — especially **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)** (Chief of Staff) and **[Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff)**.

## The 12:23–12:24 question

From [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) timeline reconstruction:

| Claim | Source |
|-------|--------|
| **~12:23 PM** shooting | Affidavit / widespread reporting |
| Erika says **Mikey called her** at the shooting moment (12:23 Utah / 11:23 Arizona) | Erika account |
| Blake says he **witnessed** Mikey's call to Erika **and** call to **[Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** | Blake account |
| **12:24:02** footage shows **Mikey alone** — Blake **not in frame** | Video still cited in CK_FILE |

Investigators note the gap: **how did Blake witness calls he may not have been beside?** The project records this as an **open timeline question**, not an accusation.

**Mikey McCoy, Blake Neff, Rob McCoy, and Erika Kirk** are living persons. We do **not** claim they concealed the shooting, had foreknowledge, or acted improperly — only that **public accounts diverge** on the call timeline.
