The **official ring** is **[Kash Patel](/key_individuals/Officials_And_Law_Enforcement)**, **[Spencer Cox](/key_individuals/Officials_And_Law_Enforcement)**, **[Tony Graf](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo)**, and **[Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent)'s [halted NCTC thread](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC)** — people who controlled press conferences, custody timing, gag orders, and foreign-lead follow-up.

The **peripheral ring** is where citizen investigation lives: **[Dylan Hope](/Locations/Construction_Site_785_College)** (all-black eyewitness), **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** and **[Zachariah Qureshi](/FBI/Zachariah_Qureshi)** (distraction / early custody), the **[Hamptons donor names](./Donor_Meeting_Circle)**, and **[Candace / MuppetMasher / Seruga](./Investigators_And_Media)** who keep surfacing new documents. **[Mikey McCoy vs Blake Neff](/People/mikey-mccoy)** call-timing at **12:23–12:24** is a staff-level puzzle that may never require guilt — only **consistent timelines**.

We **do not** claim any living person committed a crime unless court-proven.
