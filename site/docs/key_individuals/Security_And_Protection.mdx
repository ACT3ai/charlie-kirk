# Security & Protection Personnel

The **SUV detail** and **UVU campus police** were the primary protection layer when Charlie Kirk was shot. Investigators ask what they saw, what cameras they controlled, and whether **perimeter/rooftop** gaps enabled the kill shot.

## Open questions

- Who authorized **lighter** security vs prior Kirk events?

- Defense witness list reportedly includes Flood, Harpole, Cutler, Davis, Turek, Long ([Security_Team](/Security_Team/overview))

We do **not** claim any security member shot Charlie Kirk or knew of the assassination in advance.

## Security roster — process, not guilt

Failures of protection, if any, are **questions for discovery and after-action review**, not proven intentional enabling. See [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).
