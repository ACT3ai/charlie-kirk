## Role in the investigation

Citizen investigators are **not suspects** — but their **source networks** (French official, NSA tip, witness footage) define which **key individuals** stay in public focus.

Every figure here is a **living private individual or public commentator**, discussed only for their **published work** — none is accused of a crime. Where an account frames a claim as opinion (e.g. Stew Peters on foreknowledge), we **preserve that framing** and do not restate it as fact.
