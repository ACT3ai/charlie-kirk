### Andrew Zenger (attributed timing)

Per CK_FILE compilation, **Andrew James Zenger** was appointed CEO of [Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital) on **August 19, 2025**, started **August 25**, and reportedly **joined the hospital's surgical team on Aug 28 — the day after his name was searched from Israeli IPs on Aug 27** (Google Trends claim, unverified). He began as an administrative intern at the same hospital in 2014 and holds a Trinity University degree. Timing is **coincidence-flagging by citizen investigators**, not proof of anything.

### Deirdre Amaro (attributed timing)

**Dr. Deirdre Amaro** reportedly started full-time as **Utah's chief medical examiner on July 1, 2025**, weeks before the shooting. OSINT threads (h/t [@Ammermazing](/Influencers/x/ammermazing)) claim her name saw **sudden Israel-origin search interest** while barely being searched in DC or Utah, and question autopsy handling. See [Autopsy overview](/Medical/Autopsy/overview). Medical analysis: [Medical/Hospital](/Medical/Hospital/overview) — not duplicated here.
