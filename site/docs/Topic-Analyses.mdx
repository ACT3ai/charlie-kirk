These pages extract substantive material from former `analysis_documentation` trash files (`comprehensive_examination`, `detailed_analysis`, `10_page_write_up`, `charlie_kirk_assassination_overview`, `heinous_act_description`, `shockwaves_political_landscape`) and link outward to specialized sections (Planes, FBI, UVU, Israel) instead of duplicating them.

The week before September 10 matters because donor-pressure meetings, Kirk's reported policy shifts, and UVU warning failures all appear in the same narrow window. The day-of timeline anchors flight data and shooting timestamps that motive theories rely on. Ballistics and surveillance claims remain the technical battleground between the prosecution's rooftop narrative and independent tent, acoustic, and drone analyses.

Start with [Pre-Assassination Context](./Topic-Analyses/Pre_Assassination_Context) for motive background, then [September 10 Event Timeline](./Topic-Analyses/September_10_Event_Timeline) for chronology. If your question is technical, read [Ballistics & Surveillance Claims](./Topic-Analyses/Ballistics_And_Surveillance_Claims). For societal impact, see [Political Aftermath](./Topic-Analyses/Political_Aftermath).
