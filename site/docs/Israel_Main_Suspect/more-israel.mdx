# More on Israel & 9/10

This page is a roundup of additional Israel-angle threads circulating in connection with Charlie Kirk's killing on **September 10, 2025**. Each entry summarizes what proponents **allege** and links to a fuller page. Nothing here is a proven conclusion; the official case treats **Tyler Robinson** as a charged, not convicted, lone suspect, and each thread below rests on reporting and social-media claims rather than adjudicated evidence. **This site does not claim that Israel or any named living person ordered the killing.**

## Control Motive as Reported (How These Threads Fit)

The control-motive package in this section argues that after Kirk's reported Israel friction, the **platforms he built** (campuses, short-form video, podcast, Amfest, churches) were the strategic prize — and that extreme post-9/10 censorship and narrative control protected that prize. The threads below are **supporting strands** often cited alongside that package:

* **Capability** (Grim Beeper / PETN) — "could a service do this?"
* **Money pressure** ($150M / $1B offers) — "was leverage applied?"
* **Social pressure** (Hamptons) — "was he cornered before death?"
* **Presence claims** (phones / flights) — "was a foreign footprint near UVU?"
* **Analogy rhetoric** (pagers / "same energy") — tone-setting, not proof

## Mossad Supply-Chain Capability (Grim Beeper)

Proponents cite **Operation Grim Beeper** — the documented September 17–18, 2024 operation in which thousands of pagers and walkie-talkies intended for [Hezbollah](/Israel/Hezbollah) exploded across Lebanon and Syria after **Mossad** reportedly infiltrated the supply chain of Taiwanese manufacturer Gold Apollo. The historical operation is well-reported in mainstream outlets. Theory proponents use it not as direct evidence but as a **capability** argument: if a state intelligence service can pull off a mass, precision, supply-chain assassination, then a single targeted killing is, in their words, "operationally trivial." The capability claim says nothing about who acted in Utah.

## PETN and the Explosive-Device Theory

A related thread argues Charlie may not have been struck by a **.30-06** rifle round at all, but by a small directional charge using **PETN**, a high-yield, low-smoke explosive. Proponents point to PETN's relative effectiveness (~1.66 vs TNT), its low visible signature in tiny amounts, and its high soil absorption — which they tie to claims that the scene was rapidly paved over and soil excavated. This is a contested forensic hypothesis, not a finding; it depends on the disputed wound and ballistics analysis covered elsewhere on the site. Shared research also captures **physics-based debunks** of mic shaped-charge claims.

## The Alleged $1 Billion Offer

Commentator **[Stew Peters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** has alleged — amplified on X — that Charlie Kirk rejected an alleged **$1 billion** offer from an Israeli source, naming a figure "called Pierre," and that funds were reportedly accepted by his organization after his death. Peters also references a **$150 million** rejected figure in the same orbit of claims. Peters explicitly labels parts of his statement as opinion, and none of it has been verified or adjudicated. Where the allegation touches living people (including [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)), they are described only as the subject of Peters' reported claims, not as proven wrongdoers. **This site does not claim they were involved in any crime.**

## The Hamptons Pressure Meeting

Multiple posts describe an "infamous" **Hamptons** meeting in early August 2025 at which Charlie Kirk was, according to investigators, pressed over his shifting positions on Israel. Named figures in circulation include [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff) — all **living persons**, discussed only as subjects of attributed reporting about a heated political weekend, **not** as accused killers. The source material reports that **Seth Dillon**, asked under oath, later acknowledged a "heated and at times spirited debate" after initially describing the weekend as an ordinary retreat. Proponents say Charlie left describing it as an "intervention" and vented about it afterward. What was said, and by whom, remains contested.

## The Hezbollah Pager Analogy

Separate from the operational-capability point, commentators have drawn a rhetorical analogy between the **Hezbollah** pager operation and the Charlie Kirk case. In a widely circulated segment, [Russell Brand](/Israel/Russell_Brand_Cabinet_Claims) references a claim that an ADL figure invoked "the same energy we had with Pagers" as a model "against the American people." Investigators cite this as tone-setting context; the ADL statement itself should be independently sourced, and the analogy is argumentative, not evidentiary.

## Foreign Phones and NSA "Bumblehive"

Investigator **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** has claimed that the **[NSA's](/US_Intelligence/nsa)** Utah Data Center in Bluffdale (nicknamed "Bumblehive") could identify foreign phones present at UVU, alleging that raw GPS data showed foreign-registered phones — including a reported 16 Israeli-registered devices — near the scene, and that some later surfaced in Pakistan near high-level talks. A separate reported "scoop" claims the NSA detected **12 Israeli-registered cellphones** at the site and passed the data to federal officials ([Candace Owens](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens) disclosure path in master notes). These are attributed claims from social-media sources; no official confirmation is documented here. Presence ≠ authorship.

## Netanyahu Denial Messaging as Its Own Data Point

High-engagement clips (amplified by **[@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra)** and others) show **Netanyahu** publicly rejecting any Israeli role, framing believers as Nazis or Qatar-funded, and asserting Kirk wanted him to tell Americans how vital Israel is to U.S. security. Separately, X users claim Netanyahu had requested a Kirk visit to Israel and that financiers offered more money in the weeks before the death, with a message ~two days prior withdrawing support.

These items matter for the **more-Israel** roundup because:

* Head-of-government messaging is a **primary-source narrative** to archive (what was said, when, to whom).
* Denial intensity is **not** proof of guilt; leaders deny false charges too.
* Visit-offer and funding-offer claims still require documents (Grayzone and others have published related reporting — attributed journalism only).
