# Israel Intel Flights & UVU

This page collects **aircraft and flight-tracking claims** that citizen investigators use to tie Israeli or intelligence-linked flights to the **Utah Valley University (UVU)** timeframe around Charlie Kirk's assassination on **September 10, 2025**. Everything below is a reported allegation drawn from **ADS-B**/Flightradar24 screenshots, YouTube breakdowns, and X threads. No court has found that any flight was connected to the killing, and prosecutors officially present a domestic lone-suspect case against [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) (charged, not convicted).

**Scope note:** This page does **not invent flight data**. It summarizes claims already circulating in the investigation file and links to the [Planes](/Planes/overview) section for primary flight write-ups. "Israel" appears in the title because citizen investigators **frame** some of these flights inside an Israel/foreign-intel theory — not because any court has labeled these aircraft Israeli operational assets in the Kirk case.

## The SU-BTT "Egyptian plane" timeline (claims)

The most-discussed foreign aircraft in this material is a plane tracked under tail number [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT), an Egyptian registration. According to timeline notes and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)' reported commentary, its movements bracket the event:

- **July 20, 2025:** SU-BTT allegedly made its **first-ever trip to America**, flying to an Army base in Nebraska, coinciding with a reported military meeting involving foreign leaders on U.S. soil.
- **September 4, 2025:** SU-BTT reportedly flew **from France to Provo, Utah**.
- **September 10, 2025, ~7:08 MT:** SU-BTT allegedly **left Utah for Wilmington** just hours before the assassination at 12:23 MT.
- **September 11, 2025:** SU-BTT reportedly **left the US bound for Egypt**.

Investigators also claim the so-called "Egyptian flight" received **government clearance to skip security at Provo**, carried **four to six "contractors"** who **did not fly away** with the plane, and that some allegedly wore **"US Department of Defense Liaison"** badges. Owens' reported claim is that these contractors were dropped off "in a highly secret way." A companion aircraft, [**SU-BND**](/Planes/SU-BND) (a "blue plane"), reportedly **did not take off but had its transponder switched on**.

None of this is verified as connected to the crime; these are **flight-log interpretations** shared by commentators. Deeper Egyptian-aircraft material lives under [Planes — Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) and related pages.

## The SAM-702 flight from Fort Huachuca (claims)

A second thread centers on a military VIP flight tracked as **SAM-702**:

- Notes describe roughly **12 lieutenant colonels at Fort Huachuca, Arizona** around **September 8, 2025**, with [SAM-702](/Planes/SAM-702) reportedly departing **the morning of September 9**, the day before the assassination.
- Candace Owens is credited with **identifying [Bradley D. Hansell](/People/bradley-hansell)** as one of four passengers on that flight, described as Trump's Under Secretary of War for Intelligence and Security.
- Commentators note Hansell's reported prior ties to **Boston Consulting Group**, which they link to Israeli-connected consulting work, presenting this as circumstantial rather than proven.

These are **attributed allegations** about who was aboard and why; they do not establish any operational link to the shooting. See [Fort Huachuca, September 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9) and [Planes — Fort Huachuca airport](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) for the broader treatment.

## N1098L and the HADES surveillance jet (claims)

The domestic aircraft claim most cited alongside the foreign flights is **N1098L**, a Bombardier Global 6500 that investigators link to the U.S. Army's **HADES** (High Accuracy Detection and Exploitation System) ISR program, operated via Leidos:

- Trackers report ICAO hex **A0299E**, callsign **AXEL10 / AXLE10**, sourced to the **ADS-B Exchange** and Flightradar24.
- Posts allege the jet cruised near **41,000 feet** but **descended to a few hundred feet** (cited variously as 203, 335, and 600 feet AGL) and slowed near stall speed roughly one mile from UVU, which some interpret as **drone drop-off and recovery** windows around **9:16 AM** and **12:48 PM MT** relative to the 12:23 PM shooting.
- The tail number was reportedly **N911PV before being changed to N1098L**.

Officials and some analysts describe the low passes as **drone-retrieval training**, and at least one commenter stated the jet "wasn't from Ukraine" and "wasn't from Israel." That rebuttal is part of the record: N1098L is discussed here because it appears in the same flight-tracking discourse, not because it is Israeli.

Primary write-up: [Planes — N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and [ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations).

## How citizen investigators connect flights to an "Israel" frame

Honest mapping of the discourse requires stating how the leap is made — and why it is weak without more:

1. **Timing clusters:** Foreign or military aircraft near Utah in the event window.
2. **Phone SIGINT claims:** Israeli-registered devices at UVU (see [Israelis Present at UVU](/Israel_Main_Suspect/israelis-present-at-uvu)).
3. **Motive narratives:** Donor pressure, Hamptons, Iran-war friction (see [Extreme Motivation](/Israel_Main_Suspect/extreme-motivation)).
4. **Capability arguments:** Grim Beeper / state tradecraft (commentary only).

Steps 1–4 stacked still do **not** equal "Israel flew the shooters in." Citizen investigators allege patterns; this site records the allegations and demands manifests.

## Cautions on flight-tracking evidence

Flight data is powerful but easy to over-read:

- **ADS-B shows aircraft, not intent.** Tail numbers, altitudes, and routes are objective; the **motive assigned to them** is interpretive.
- **Coincidence of timing is not causation.** Business jets, military ISR flights, and foreign aircraft transit U.S. airspace constantly; overlap with an event window is not proof of coordination.
- **Provenance matters.** Much of this rests on screenshots and secondhand summaries rather than official manifests or court exhibits.
- **Nationality of registration ≠ nationality of mission.** An Egyptian tail is not automatically an Israeli mission; a U.S. Army ISR jet is not automatically a foreign hit team.

## What would settle it

To move these flight claims from allegation toward evidence, the record would need:

- Passenger and cargo manifests for **SU-BTT**, **SU-BND**, and **SAM-702**.
- The identities and roles of the "contractors" reportedly dropped at Provo.
- Official mission logs for **N1098L / AXEL10** on September 10.
- Confirmation of whether any device from the alleged UVU phone set matches devices later tracked abroad.
- Airport security exception paperwork for Provo if the "skip security" claim is real.

The [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are proposed to compel exactly these aviation and intelligence records, which remain undisclosed.

## July 20 dual event (meeting + SU-BTT)

Citizen timelines treat **July 20, 2025** as a hinge: foreign leaders military meeting on U.S. soil **and** SU-BTT's first America trip to a Nebraska Army base. That dual timing is **ambient geopolitics**, not a UVU ops plan. It becomes relevant to this page only when investigators claim the same aircraft later stages into Provo. Even then, multi-week aircraft itineraries have many innocent explanations (diplomacy, logistics, maintenance).

## Training vs. ops: the official-sounding rebuttals

For N1098L, the **drone-retrieval training** explanation is the primary non-hostile reading. For foreign aircraft, **diplomatic logistics** and **contractor transport** are the non-hostile readings. Hostile readings assign intent from motive theory. Scientific flight analysis can confirm **where a plane was**; it cannot alone confirm **why**.

## Reader caution on composite graphics

Many viral "flight + phone + timeline" images are **composites**. The Islamabad graphic was labeled recreation. Treat composite posters as **argument slides**, not ADS-B exports. Demand original FR24/ADS-B Exchange queries with timestamps.

## ADS-B literacy for readers

When evaluating flight claims:

1. Confirm **tail, date, UTC vs local** time conversion (MT vs UTC mistakes are common).
2. Prefer raw **ADS-B Exchange** exports over screenshots with arrows.
3. Remember Mode-S/ADS-B can be **spoofed or gapped**; absence of track is not absence of aircraft.
4. Passenger identity almost never appears on public ADS-B — manifests are the missing piece.

## SU-BTT contractor claim as the highest-value sub-lead

If contractors were dropped in Provo and did not reboard, **who housed them, who paid them, and where cameras show them** are higher-value questions than the plane's Egypt return alone. That sub-lead needs hotel, FBO, and CBP work — exactly the sort of foreign-nexus tasking Kent's quotes imply was incomplete. Former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that his center's foreign-nexus review was ordered to stop once a suspect was in custody, before leads like these were run to ground.

## Wilmington and Egypt legs (claims)

The alleged **Sept 10 morning leave to Wilmington** and **Sept 11 exit toward Egypt** are used by theorists to suggest a clean exfiltration window. Innocent reading: preplanned itinerary independent of the noon shooting. Settling requires FBO logs and passenger lists, not vibes about direction of flight.

## Intervention-style caveat for flight pages

No aircraft on this page is declared an **Israeli hit platform**. Egyptian registrations, U.S. Army ISR, and VIP SAM flights are discussed because citizen investigators **embed them in foreign-involvement narratives**. Official explanations (training, diplomacy, logistics) are first-class counter-hypotheses. The site finds **neither** "flights prove Israel killed Charlie" nor "flights are fully explained and irrelevant" — both require manifests.

## Coordination hypothesis vs. coincidence hypothesis

**Coordination hypothesis (citizen investigators):** multiple air assets timed to UVU window for ISR, insertion, or exfiltration related to the killing.

**Coincidence hypothesis (skeptics):** busy national airspace + military training + foreign itineraries produce spurious clusters when researchers start from a motive conclusion.

Scientific approach: pre-register the time window, measure baseline traffic, then test whether Sept 10 is an outlier. Few public analyses do that rigorously. Until they do, both hypotheses remain live.

The highest-volume "intel flight" claim on X is not always an Israeli-registered jet — it is the **U.S. Army HADES ISR** platform **N1098L** (callsign **AXLE10**) that citizen investigators place near UVU on September 10, 2025. - Two low-altitude passes (~pre-event morning and ~post-event recovery window).
- Origin narrative tying Biggs AAF / **Fort Huachuca** UAS ecosystem.
- **Alleged crew names** (publicly circulated: Michael Harlan; Elena Vasquez; J. "Jax" E. Donovan) and purported **NSCC name overlaps** after a list was "scrubbed" online — all **unverified in court**, and **naming living people as flight crew is not proof of criminal acts**.
- Israeli-IP **Google Trends** spikes for uncommon names in alleged planning windows (aggregate analytics claim; highly speculative).

**This site does not conclude that N1098L, its crew, the CIA, or any foreign service killed Charlie Kirk.** Flight tracks, if confirmed via raw ADS-B, establish **platform presence and timing questions** only.
