## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

Baker Tilly matters for two reasons in the [Wolves And Finance](https://youtu.be/_QwGo0LyZ3I) analysis:

- **Timeline:** Zach of Wolves And Finance states a **Baker Tilly audit concluded June 20, 2025**, and speculates it "would have revealed all this information to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shortly before his murder." This is an **inference about timing**, not evidence of motive.
- **The dispute:** Baker Tilly (and TPUSA's attorney [Robert Barnes](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-usa)) reportedly maintain there is **"nothing wrong" with the financials.** Wolves And Finance disputes that, asking what specific audit procedures were used to test for prohibited political activity by the 501(c)(3)s. His challenge is opinion; the firm's clean opinion is the official position.

## Why investigators track it

An independent auditor's conclusions are exactly what a competing [DOGE-style audit](../doge-audit) would be checked against. This page fixes the auditor's identity and the disputed June 20, 2025 date so both sides of the record are visible.

## Open questions

2. Did it test for political-activity compliance across the c3 entities?
