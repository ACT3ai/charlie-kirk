## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

Salem Media had an **existing partnership** carrying [The Charlie Kirk Show](/Influencers/podcasts-charlie-kirk-show). Investigation notes report that **Brad Parscale's [Clock Tower X LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clock-tower-x-llc)** filed a **FARA registration dated September 18, 2025 — eight days after** the assassination — covering **Israeli-government work, including messaging integration into Salem Media properties.**

The investigative interest is the **overlap**: a foreign-government messaging contract touching the same media pipeline that carried Kirk's program, filed days after his death. A FARA filing is a **lawful disclosure**; this page records the adjacency, not a proven connection to the assassination.

## Why investigators track it

Salem is a **media node** linking the [Israel](/Israel/overview) messaging thread to the Kirk-show distribution channel. The substantive analysis lives on the Israel and [Clock Tower X LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clock-tower-x-llc) pages. Foreign-government adjacencies of this kind are the category of lead former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) says his center's parallel foreign-nexus review was ordered to stop pursuing before it could run them to ground.
