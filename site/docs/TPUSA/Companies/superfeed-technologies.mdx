## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

A cited claim states: *"[Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve) has recently been put on the board of **Superfeed Technologies** alongside Turning Point Action COO [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)… Money from TP Action has been routed into Superfeed. These two are… business partners."* The interest is the **overlap of a TPUSA-family member and a TPUSA executive** on a private company's board, with an unverified allegation of fund flow from the nonprofit arm.

**This is an attributed claim, not a proven fact.** Board service and business partnerships are lawful; the question raised is whether any nonprofit funds moved improperly — which no filing or court has established.

## Why investigators track it

The entity sits at the intersection of the **Kirk family, TPUSA leadership, and money flow** — the same nexus examined by the reported [DOGE audit](../doge-audit). It is one of the "insider-controlled companies" that citizen investigators want an audit to examine.
