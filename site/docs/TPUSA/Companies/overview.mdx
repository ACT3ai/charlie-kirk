## What this section covers

These companies span the money trail, the media pipeline, and the intelligence backdrop. [Moore DM Group](./moore-dm-group) is the alleged real entity behind TPUSA's "Resource One" printing line — the single largest dollar figure in the citizen-investigator compilations. [Superfeed Technologies](./superfeed-technologies) is a private company whose board reportedly overlaps TPUSA's leadership and the Kirk family. [Baker Tilly](./baker-tilly) is the outside auditor whose clean opinion the whole fraud debate is argued against.

The other two are context nodes rather than finance entities. [Salem Media](./salem-media) carried [The Charlie Kirk Show](/Influencers/podcasts-charlie-kirk-show) and appears in a Brad Parscale FARA filing about Israeli-government messaging. [Boston Consulting Group](./boston-consulting-group) surfaces through the resume of an intelligence official on the scrutinized SAM-702 flight and reported Gaza-related work. Both connect the TPUSA story to the broader [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [intelligence-services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview) threads.

Readers following the finances should start with Moore DM Group and Baker Tilly and then read [Results of the DOGE audit](../doge-audit-results). Readers following the geopolitical angle should follow Salem Media and BCG into the [Israel](/Israel/overview) pages. As with every entity in this part of the site, doing business with a nonprofit — as a printer, an auditor, a broadcaster, or a consultancy — is lawful, and inclusion here is not an accusation.
