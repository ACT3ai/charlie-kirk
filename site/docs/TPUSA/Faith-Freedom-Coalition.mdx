# Faith & Freedom Coalition (July 31 Context)

The **Faith & Freedom Coalition (FFC)** is a major Christian-conservative political organization chaired by **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed)**. Project timelines cite FFC as a **co-organizer** of the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House meeting on Israel-related politics within the MAGA movement.

## Reported meeting role

According to [Timeline/2025](/Timeline/2025), [Before/Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025), and [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA):

| Element | Reported detail |
|---|---|
| **Date** | July 31, 2025 |
| **Co-organizers** | FFC, [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel), [Philos Project](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding) |
| **Themes** | "Woke-right defamation" of Israel; antisemitism in MAGA |
| **Reported attendees** | Reed, [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu) |
| **Absent** | Charlie Kirk; TPUSA representatives |

Sources are **secondary reporting and commentary** — not a public White House transcript.

## Why FFC appears in the investigation

Researchers highlight FFC because:

- Kirk remained a leading **youth-facing pro-Israel voice** while reportedly reassessing donor pressure
- Four days later (~**August 4**), a **Hamptons intervention** reportedly confronted Kirk ([Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons))
- Post-death **TPUSA policy shifts** are compared to goals stated at July 31 sessions ([TPUSA Opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie))

**Correlation is not coordination or ill intent.** FFC is documented here as part of the **pre-event political map**, not as an accused party in the shooting.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

Investigators cite the **Faith & Freedom Coalition** as a reported organizer / co-host environment around the **July 31, 2025** White House–adjacent **Israel / MAGA antisemitism** strategy conversation that **excluded** Charlie Kirk and TPUSA (see [July 31 Meeting](./July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA)).

- X threads on donor alignment often name Faith & Freedom as part of the **institutional conservative** stack that kept meeting even when Kirk was sidelined — a **process observation**, not proof of violent intent.
- No public court finding ties Faith & Freedom Coalition to the UVU assassination. Presence in pro-Israel advocacy networks is **documented political context**, not a crime.
