## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

**Cloverstone** appears in the same [@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles) compilation as [Lionrock](./lionrock-llc): both are described as companies through which TPUSA advancement director [Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan) allegedly received money **on top of a salary for the same job**. Files reports a combined **$2.7M+** flowing to Sheridan-linked entities and asks whether this represents "double-dipping." The claim is unverified and TPUSA has not been shown in any court record to have paid this entity improperly.

## Why investigators track it

Like the other entities in this section, Cloverstone is of interest because [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly launched a [DOGE-style internal audit](../doge-audit) of TPUSA spending just before his death — an audit that, per Files and [Wolves And Finance](https://youtu.be/_QwGo0LyZ3I), [was never carried out](../doge-audit-results) after [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) took over.

## Open questions

1. Where is Cloverstone LLC registered, and does it share an address or agent with other Sheridan-linked entities?
2. Do TPUSA filings list Cloverstone by name, or only under a vendor category?
3. Are there deliverables that would explain the payments?
