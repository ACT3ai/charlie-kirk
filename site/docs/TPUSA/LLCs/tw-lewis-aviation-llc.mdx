## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

**T.W. Lewis Aviation LLC** owns a Cessna Citation XLS (**N560TW**) tied to the **[T.W. Lewis Foundation](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/tw-lewis-foundation)**, described in the notes as a large conservative donor that **publicly lists Turning Point USA as a key partner** and has "funneled millions" into TPUSA, the Heritage Foundation, and similar organizations. The aircraft appears in flight-tracking discussion around the case timeline.

The relevance is the **donor relationship** and an aircraft on the tracking boards — not any established operational role in the events. The philanthropic analysis lives on the [foundation page](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/tw-lewis-foundation).
