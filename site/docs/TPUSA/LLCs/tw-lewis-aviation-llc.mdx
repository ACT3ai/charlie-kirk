## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

Lewis Foundation](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/tw-lewis-foundation)**, described in the notes as a large conservative donor that **publicly lists Turning Point USA as a key partner** and has "funneled millions" into TPUSA, the Heritage Foundation, and similar organizations.
