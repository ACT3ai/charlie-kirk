## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

An investigator identified a white-liveried private jet, **N888KG**, owned by **N888KG LLC** (registered in Lehi, Utah), which flight-tracking platforms such as ADS-B Exchange reportedly show departing **Provo Municipal Airport** — roughly a 10–12 minute drive from UVU — about an hour after the September 10 shooting. The flight path was described as "anomalous."

This entity's relevance is entirely to the **[aviation/timeline](/Planes/overview)** thread, not to TPUSA's corporate structure. It is listed here so the corporate ownership (an LLC) is captured. **No connection to the assassination is established.**

## Why investigators track it

Aircraft departing the area shortly after the event are logged for completeness in the [Planes](/Planes/overview) analysis. Ownership through an LLC is common and not itself suspicious.

## Open questions

1. Who were the passengers, and what was the flight's destination?
2. Is there any documented link to TPUSA, the event, or its principals? (None established.)
