## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

**Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC**, doing business as **Integrity Security Solutions**, appears in the investigation's discussion of the **security arrangements** for [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and the UVU event. Questions raised by citizen investigators concern the **security posture** at the September 10 event — perimeter coverage, rooftop/line-of-sight gaps, and the interplay between TPUSA-funded close protection and campus/local law enforcement. Those questions are documented in detail on the [Day-of Security](/Charlie/Day_Of_Security) and Security Team pages.

The company is listed here as the corporate entity behind that vendor discussion. **No finding of negligence or complicity has been established.**

## Why investigators track it

Security-vendor competence and contracts are a recurring theme in the case. The substantive analysis lives under the security pages; this page fixes the corporate identity (LLC name and DBA) so records requests can be precise.
