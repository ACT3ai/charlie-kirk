## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

Per notes compiled in the investigation, the **FARA filing for Brad Parscale's Clock Tower X LLC** was dated **September 18, 2025 — eight days after** Kirk's assassination. The filing reportedly covered **Israeli-government messaging work**, including integration into **[Salem Media](/TPUSA/Companies/salem-media)** properties. Because Salem already had a partnership carrying [The Charlie Kirk Show](/Influencers/podcasts-charlie-kirk-show), investigators note the overlap between a foreign-government messaging contract and the media channel that carried Kirk's program.

A FARA registration is a **legal transparency requirement** for agents of foreign principals; the filing itself is not evidence of any crime. The point investigators raise is one of **timing and adjacency**, not proven causation.
