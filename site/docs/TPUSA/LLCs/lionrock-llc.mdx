## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

According to citizen investigator **Leah Files** ([@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles)), who has described a background working with U.S. intelligence agencies, **Lionrock** is one of several companies through which TPUSA advancement director [Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan) allegedly received payments **while also being salaried for the same fundraising role**. Files' compilation reportedly totals **$2.7M+** across Sheridan-linked entities such as Lionrock and [Cloverstone](./cloverstone-llc).

These figures come from Files' reading of TPUSA's public 990 filings and are presented as **questions for an audit**, not established fraud. TPUSA has not, in public filings or court records, been shown to have made improper payments to this entity.

## Why investigators track it

The entity matters to the investigation only because of **timing**: [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly ordered an internal [DOGE-style audit](../doge-audit) of TPUSA spending in the **September 2, 2025** memo, days before his death. Investigators ask whether entities like Lionrock were among the line items that audit would have examined — and why, per Files and others, [the audit was never completed](../doge-audit-results).

## Open questions

1. Is Lionrock LLC a genuine vendor with deliverables, or a pass-through?
2. What state is it registered in, and who is the registered agent?
3. Do TPUSA 990 schedules corroborate the payment totals Files cites?
