## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

In her TPUSA financial thread, [@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles) links **1Ten LLC** to the same direct-mail money trail as **"Resource One"** — which she reports is not a printer but a **DBA for [Moore DM Group](/TPUSA/Companies/moore-dm-group)**, a large political direct-mail company. Files reports TPUSA paid **$9M+** to "Resource One" while its 990 lists only **$1.35M for printing**, and she names Arizona Republican figure **Jake Hoffman** in connection with 1Ten. These are reported allegations; no court has established improper payments.

## Why investigators track it

The direct-mail chain is the largest single dollar figure in Files' compilation, which is why it recurs in discussion of the [DOGE-style audit](../doge-audit) Kirk reportedly wanted. Investigators want the audit's underlying vendor ledger to determine whether "Resource One," Moore DM Group, and 1Ten describe the same payments or separate ones.

## Open questions

1. Is 1Ten LLC a vendor, an intermediary, or unrelated to the TPUSA payments?
2. What is the documented relationship, if any, between 1Ten and Resource One / Moore DM Group?
3. Do TPUSA filings reconcile the $9M+ figure Files cites?
