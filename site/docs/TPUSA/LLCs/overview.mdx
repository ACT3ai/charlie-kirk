## What this section covers

The LLCs here fall into three loose groups. First are the **finance-adjacent entities** — Lionrock, Cloverstone, 1Ten, Clocktower, GGLF 2023 — named by investigators like [@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles) in connection with TPUSA payments, shell structures, or a life-insurance policy. Second are the **event-and-aviation entities** — LASAI Aviation II, [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG), T.W. Lewis Aviation, Rockhouse Integrity Group, Hardscape Specialties — that appear in the security, flight-tracking, and crime-scene threads. Third are **thin, rumor-level family claims** — Precision Granite & Marble and RobinsonSTG — flagged as unverified precisely so they can be checked and, if false, retired.

The substance here is uneven on purpose. Some entities have documented public filings (GGLF 2023 LLC's Wyoming registration; LASAI's FAA records). Others rest on a single citizen-investigator post. Each page states its **evidence rating** up front so readers can weigh the claim, and each links to the person or thread where the underlying analysis lives.

Readers who want the financial story should start with the finance-adjacent entities and then read [Results of the DOGE audit](../doge-audit-results), which ties the LLC payments to the audit [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly ordered. Readers focused on the day of the event should follow the aviation and crime-scene entities into the [Planes](/Planes/overview) and [Cover-Up Indicators](/Killer/Coverup_Indicators) pages. Throughout, remember that forming an LLC, owning an aircraft, or performing contracted work are all lawful; being listed here is not an accusation.
