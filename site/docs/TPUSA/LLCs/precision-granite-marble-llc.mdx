Investigation notes record a **speculative, AI-assisted claim** that **Precision Granite & Marble LLC** is owned by **Matt Robinson**, father of the accused, and a further speculation that the company "may have been the one that did the construction over the tent's grass" at UVU. The notes themselves flag this as unverified ("If true? BOMBSHELL"), meaning it is **rumor, not evidence**.

Because [Tyler Robinson](/Killer/overview) has not been convicted and his family members are not accused of any crime, this page exists only to **log and contextualize a circulating claim** — and to make clear how thin it is. There is **no established link** between this company and any tent construction or the assassination.

## Why investigators track it
