## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

According to [@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles), TPUSA paid roughly **$999,000** to **Clocktower LLC** for a "research project" — an entity she reports was **dissolved in 2022** — with **no public deliverables**, which she characterizes as resembling a "slush fund." This is a reported allegation. No filing or court record has established that the payment was improper.

## Why investigators track it

A near-million-dollar payment to a dissolved entity is exactly the kind of line item that a genuine [DOGE-style audit](../doge-audit) would flag. It is cited among the reasons commentators find it significant that [the audit Kirk reportedly ordered was never completed](../doge-audit-results).

## Open questions

1. What was the "research project," and does any deliverable exist?
2. If Clocktower dissolved in 2022, when were the payments actually made?
3. Who controlled the entity?
