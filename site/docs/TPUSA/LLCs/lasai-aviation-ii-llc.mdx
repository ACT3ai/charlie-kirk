## Connection to the Charlie Kirk case

**LASAI Aviation II LLC** operates **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)**, a Bombardier Global 6500 that flight-trackers logged flying on the timeline investigators scrutinize (departing Biggs Army Airfield, El Paso, ~07:48 MDT, arriving Kalispell, MT ~10:38 MDT). The company is described in the notes as a **defense contractor specializing in ISR missions for the U.S. Army**, which is offered as an explanation for limited public disclosure about the aircraft.

This entity's relevance is to the **[Planes](/Planes/overview)** aviation thread rather than to TPUSA's corporate structure; it is catalogued here as a corporate node so the investigation's company list is complete.

## Why investigators track it

An ISR defense contractor's aircraft appearing on the timeline feeds the [intelligence-services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview) angle of the case. The detailed flight analysis lives on the Planes pages.
