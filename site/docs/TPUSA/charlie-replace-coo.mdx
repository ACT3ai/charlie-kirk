## The memo

Per accounts compiled in the investigation, on **September 2, 2025**, [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) sent an **internal memo** that named **[Justin Streiff](/People/justin-streiff) as the new Chief Operating Officer** of TPUSA. The same memo reportedly announced the [DOGE-style internal audit](./doge-audit). Some investigator accounts (e.g., [George Webb](https://x.com/RealGeorgeWebb1)) go further and frame the change as Kirk **moving long-time COO [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) out** of that operational role — Webb wrote that Kirk was "killed eight days after he moved Bowyer out for not doing the DOGE outside audit."

The precise nature of the change — a straightforward appointment versus a removal — is **not settled** in public records. What the accounts agree on is the **date (Sept 2)** and the pairing of a new COO with an audit announcement.

## Why the timing draws scrutiny

The memo lands inside a tight, much-discussed window:

- **September 2, 2025** — reported memo: new COO + DOGE-style audit.
- **September 10, 2025** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is assassinated at UVU.
- **~One week later** — [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) is named CEO of Turning Point.

Investigators note that a founder reorganizing operations and launching a financial review, then dying eight days later, is a sequence worth examining. **Sequence is not causation** — no evidence establishes that the leadership change was connected to the assassination.

## Who's who

- **[Justin Streiff](/People/justin-streiff)** — named new COO in the reported memo. Separately, [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) has alleged Streiff had a phone call shortly after the shooting; that claim is covered on his profile and is unproven.
- **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)** — the prior COO of Turning Point Action / TPUSA, reportedly affected by the change.
