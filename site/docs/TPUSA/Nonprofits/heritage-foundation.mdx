# Heritage Foundation (Context)

:::caution Context only
The Heritage Foundation is a major, lawful think tank. It appears here only as **context** where its name intersects the case. Nothing below implies the organization had any role in the assassination.
:::

## Where it intersects the case

The Heritage Foundation surfaces at three unrelated edges of the investigation:

- **Early-named individual:** The man briefly detained and named on the day of the shooting, **Zachariah/Ahmed Qureshi**, was reported to have **interned at the Heritage Foundation** before the event. He was released; this is background, not accusation.
- **The Tucker Carlson dispute:** Notes report that **Laurie Cardoza-Moore** "rage-quit the Heritage Foundation when they refused to cancel [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)" — an episode tied to the same pro-Israel-donor pressure theme that recurs around [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).
- **Shared donor networks:** Large conservative funders such as the [T.W. Lewis Foundation](./tw-lewis-foundation) reportedly support **both** Heritage and TPUSA, which is why Heritage appears on the donor map.

## Why investigators track it

The think tank is a **node in the donor and personnel network** around TPUSA and the [Israel](/Israel/overview) angle. It is included so the ecosystem is mapped, with a clear line that presence in the network is not participation in any wrongdoing.
