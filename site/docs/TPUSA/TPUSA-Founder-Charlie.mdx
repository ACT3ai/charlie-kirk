# Charlie Kirk as TPUSA Founder

**Charlie Kirk** co-founded **Turning Point USA (TPUSA)** and served as its primary public leader until his death on **September 10, 2025**. Understanding Kirk as **founder** clarifies why organizational succession, donor networks, and security decisions are central to the investigation.

## Founding mission (public record)

TPUSA publicly positioned itself as:

- A **conservative youth organization** focused on campus activism and voter engagement
- A **speaker and event platform** that brought national figures to universities
- A **nonprofit** with a large donor base and chapter network nationwide

For biographical detail see [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and [Charlie Overview](/Charlie/overview).

## Kirk's dual role at UVU

On September 10, Kirk was simultaneously:

- The **featured speaker** at the outdoor courtyard event
- The **organizational principal** whose team arranged logistics and security ([TPUSA Organization](/TPUSA/TPUSA))

That overlap is why investigators scrutinize **TPUSA cameras, drones, and private security contracts** — not because TPUSA is accused of the shooting.

## Late-2025 evolution (claims)

Commentary describes Kirk in his final months as:

- Platforming **Israel policy skeptics** alongside traditional allies ([TPUSA Opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie))
- Launching a reported **DOGE-style internal audit** ([DOGE-TPUSA Connection](/TPUSA/DOGE-TPUSA-Connection))
- Publicly signaling a break from strict **pro-Israel donor alignment** ([Israel Quotes](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel))

## After the founder's death

Documented post-death developments include:

- **Leadership transition** to Erika Kirk ([TPUSA Replacement Timeline](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Replacement_Timeline))
- Alleged **policy reversals** relative to Kirk's late positions
- Continued operation of TPUSA media and campus programs under new stewardship

Debates over **institutional fidelity to Kirk's legacy** are political and organizational — not, by themselves, criminal allegations.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* TPUSA board succession records, Kirk's internal audit materials, and unreleased TPUSA security footage from UVU are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
