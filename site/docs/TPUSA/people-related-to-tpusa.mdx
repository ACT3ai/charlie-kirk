## How these people connect to the investigation

The people above sit at three points of interest. **Leadership** — [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), [Justin Streiff](/People/justin-streiff), and [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) — anchors the succession and [DOGE-audit](./doge-audit) questions: who ran the organization before and after September 10, and who controlled the money. The reported September 2 [COO change](./charlie-replace-coo) puts Streiff and Bowyer at the center of the timeline.

**Advancement and finance figures** — [Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan) and [Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve) — are the individuals named in the [@theleahfiles](https://x.com/theleahfiles) and [Wolves And Finance](https://youtu.be/_QwGo0LyZ3I) reporting about vendor payments and insider-controlled companies (see [LLCs](./LLCs/overview) and [Companies](./Companies/overview)). Those pages present the allegations and TPUSA's rebuttal side by side.

**Event and media staff** — [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth), [Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet), [Andrew Feraci](/People/andrew-feraci), [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood), and others — appear in the day-of and evidence-handling threads. For the complete, role-sorted roster across the whole investigation, see the [People Investigation Index](/People/Investigation_Index) and the main [People overview](/People/overview).
