# Latino Coalition for Israel (July 31 Context)

The **Latino Coalition for Israel (LCI)** is a pro-Israel advocacy organization focused on Hispanic evangelical and conservative communities. Investigators cite LCI because project timelines list it as a **co-organizer** of the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House meeting held **without [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) or [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) present**.

## Reported July 31 role

Compiled sources ([Paul Singer Funding](/TPUSA/Paul_Singer_Funding), [Philos Project Funding](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding), [Timeline/Charlie_Kirk](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk)) describe:

- **Joint sponsorship** with the [Faith & Freedom Coalition](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition) and Philos Project
- Agenda themes: combating **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel and **antisemitism within MAGA**
- Named attendees overlapping other pro-Israel strategy threads (Reed, Moon, Bramnick, Kaploun, [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu))

No public transcript independently verifies every attendee or talking point.

## Investigative significance (context only)

Commentators connect LCI's July 31 role to:

- Kirk's **reported donor friction** in August–September 2025 ([2025 Political Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025))
- Questions about **who spoke for "MAGA youth"** on Israel after Kirk began platforming skeptics
- Whether post-assassination TPUSA messaging aligned more with **July 31 attendees' stated goals** than with Kirk's late-2025 public evolution

These are **interpretive timelines**, not findings of wrongdoing by LCI or any individual.
