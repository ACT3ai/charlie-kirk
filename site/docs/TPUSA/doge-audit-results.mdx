## The short version: the audit never happened

The [DOGE-style internal audit](./doge-audit) that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reportedly ordered in his **September 2, 2025** memo was, according to investigators, **never completed** after his September 10 death. A week later, [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) was named CEO. In place of that internal audit, independent commentators — principally **Leah Files** ([@theleahfiles](/Influencers/x/theleahfiles)), who describes a background with U.S. intelligence agencies, and **Zach of [Wolves And Finance](https://youtu.be/_QwGo0LyZ3I)** — went through TPUSA's **public 990 filings** and published what they say they found.

## What the reporting alleges (attributed)

These are the investigators' claims, drawn from public filings and presented as questions for a real audit:

### The four-entity structure
- Turning Point runs four nonprofits — [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-usa) (c3), [Turning Point Action](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-action) (c4), [Turning Point Endowment](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-endowment) (c3), and [America's Turning Point](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/americas-turning-point) (c3) — plus [Turning Point PAC](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-pac).
- The reporting alleges partisan "get out the vote" / ballot-chasing work may have run through the **non-political c3 entities** and been under-reported. [Turning Point Action](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/turning-point-action)'s Schedule C reportedly listed only **$637,500 (~3%)** as political, even as its budget rose **$16.5M** year-over-year — an increase TPUSA's own letter reportedly attributed to *"lobbying and political activity during election years."*
- [America's Turning Point](/TPUSA/Nonprofits/americas-turning-point), run by [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer), reportedly received **$8.6M** transferred from TPUSA and had **~80 employees not reported** on its 990s.

### The vendors and insiders
- **[Stacy Sheridan](/People/stacy-sheridan)** reportedly received **$2.7M+** through entities like [Lionrock](/TPUSA/LLCs/lionrock-llc) and [Cloverstone](/TPUSA/LLCs/cloverstone-llc) while salaried for the same role.
- **"Resource One"** — allegedly a DBA for [Moore DM Group](/TPUSA/Companies/moore-dm-group) — reportedly received **$9M+** vs **$1.35M** listed for printing; linked to [1Ten LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/1ten-llc).
- **[Clocktower LLC](/TPUSA/LLCs/clocktower-llc)** (dissolved 2022) reportedly got **$999K** for a "research project" with no public deliverables.
- **[Lori Frantzve](/People/lori-frantzve)** (Erika Kirk's mother) reportedly sat on boards that the investigators allege steered money to insider-controlled companies, including [Superfeed Technologies](/TPUSA/Companies/superfeed-technologies).

### The post-death picture
- The donation "hat" complaints: donors reported paying for a promised hat and receiving a **rubber bracelet** instead.
- [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) claimed TPUSA raised roughly **$250M** since the assassination.
- A reported **Treasury letter** to [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), signed by Treasury Secretary **Scott Bessent**, states there is **no IRS investigation** and that all four 990s were filed by May 15, 2025 — a letter Wolves And Finance controversially claims bears handwriting he believes resembles President Trump's. **That handwriting claim is pure speculation by the commentator and is not established.**

## TPUSA's rebuttal (for balance)

The same video includes **TPUSA's side**, via attorney **Robert Barnes** on the Paramount Tactical podcast: *"There was nothing wrong at all with any aspect of TPUSA's finances. They clearly complied with tax law. There was no fraud there… no evidence of any fraud… all the accusations are false. There's no proof to them whatsoever. If you look at the 990 forms, they completely comply with existing tax law."* Barnes notes that no left-leaning group has challenged the returns, which he argues they would have if there were an obvious problem. The reported Treasury letter likewise states there is **no IRS investigation**.

Readers should weigh both: the investigators read the public filings as showing large, mis-categorized political spending; TPUSA's counsel says the filings are fully compliant and the accusations baseless.

## Why this connects to the death

Investigators tie the audit to [motive](/Motive/overview) through the timeline: Kirk reportedly ordered an aggressive review, a [Baker Tilly](/TPUSA/Companies/baker-tilly) audit reportedly **concluded June 20, 2025**, he [changed the COO](./charlie-replace-coo) on September 2, and he was killed on September 10. **This is a sequence, not a proven causal chain.** The purpose of documenting it is to argue for a **trustworthy, independent investigation** — not to declare anyone guilty.
