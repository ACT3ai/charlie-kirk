# Paul Singer and Conservative Funding Networks

**Paul Singer** is a billionaire hedge-fund manager (Elliott Management) and major Republican donor. Public reporting places him at the center of **pro-Israel advocacy funding** that intersects with the Charlie Kirk story through **organizational meetings and donor-pressure narratives** — not through any verified link to the shooting itself.

## Documented Public Role

According to articles and timelines compiled in this project:

- Singer has long funded **conservative and pro-Israel institutions**, including networks tied to the **Philos Project**
- He is described in public materials as a **major donor class figure** whose preferences influence which organizations receive sustained support
- **July 31, 2025** White House off-the-record meeting organizers included groups Singer is publicly associated with funding ([Before/Political_Pressure](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025), [Timeline/2025](/Timeline/2025))

This page does **not** allege Singer knew of or participated in violence. It documents **financial and political context**.

## July 31, 2025 White House Meeting

Reported details:

- **Organizers**: Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, **Philos Project**
- **Stated purpose**: "Confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people" and combating "growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement"
- **Reported attendees**: Ralph Reed, Luke Moon, Apostle Mario Bramnick, Yehuda Kaploun, **Yair Netanyahu**
- **Notable absence**: Charlie Kirk and **TPUSA representatives** were not present despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel alignment

Investigators debate whether the absence signals **donor-class alignment against Kirk's drift** or routine meeting attendance. See [July 31 Meeting Without TPUSA](./July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA).

## Connection to Kirk Pressure Narratives

In the weeks after July 31, project timelines document:

- **Hamptons "intervention"** (~August 4) with pro-Israel figures
- **September 9** confrontational meeting and Kirk's reported fear-for-life texts
- Public claims (unverified) that **donors threatened to pull funding** if Kirk continued platforming Israel critics

Singer's name appears in these narratives as part of the **funding ecosystem**, not as a named participant in every meeting.

## Why Researchers Track This

Understanding TPUSA's donor environment helps explain:

- Why Kirk's **Israel-policy shift** was politically explosive inside the conservative movement
- How **post-death TPUSA direction** may reflect donor preferences ([TPUSA Opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie))
- What documents the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) seek — wire transfers, board communications, donor letters

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* Donor communications to TPUSA and Charlie Kirk between July 1 and September 10, 2025, and Philos Project funding flows tied to the July 31 meeting are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
