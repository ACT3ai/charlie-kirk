## About This Cluster

The images here are screenshots that online researchers have flagged as things that, in their reading, **do not add up**. Several are annotated compilations of posts from X: one highlights what posters claim are odd timestamps, with red and green underlines pointing to future 2025 dates on posts about a "public execution." Another pairs an alleged timeline of events with a screenshot of commentator [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) replying "based" to a 2013 [Trump](/People/donald-trump) tweet. A third sets a September 9 post beside a September 10 post to argue a discrepancy, and a fourth captures an AI search response that appears to reference an assassination date before the day itself.

None of this is settled. The open question these images raise is whether the apparent oddities reflect genuine foreknowledge, ordinary coincidence, editing or display quirks, or misread metadata. Some researchers propose the timestamps hint at advance planning; others argue caching, time zones, and screenshot artifacts explain them entirely. The claims written into the images are the posters' own, reported here with attribution and treated as unproven. They are collected so a reader can weigh the competing explanations rather than accept any single one.
