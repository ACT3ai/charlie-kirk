## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of a post from the social media platform X (formerly Twitter) by user [Sam Parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker). The post features a compiled graphic showing screenshots of other posts on X, specifically focusing on posts by conservative commentator [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) quoting or repeating the phrase "public execution." The graphic includes colored underline annotations in red and green to highlight perceived anomalies in the timestamps of the posts, pointing to future dates in the year 2025.

The annotations and the inference drawn from them belong to the poster. Displayed timestamps on screenshotted social posts are notoriously unreliable — they shift with the reader's time zone, with caching, with archive and quote-repost rendering, and with the device the screenshot was taken on — so an apparently out-of-order date is not, on its own, evidence of anything. Nothing here establishes that [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer) or any other commentator quoted in the graphic knew anything in advance of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and no such allegation is made on this page. Using a phrase in commentary is not foreknowledge. The screenshot is preserved because the claim circulated, and it is left unproven.
