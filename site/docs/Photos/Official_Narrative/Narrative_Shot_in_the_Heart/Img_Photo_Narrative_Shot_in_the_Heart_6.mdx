## What This Image Shows

A clip shared on X by [@its_The_Dr (Johnny Midnight)](https://x.com/its_The_Dr/status/1966293765158560167) on **September 12, 2025**, in which the speaker advances a theory: that the shot was aimed at the heart, struck body armor, and fragmented — producing the neck injury visible in the footage while leaving no bullet hole in the shirt front. The original uses strong language throughout.

This is where the thread turns from testimony to interpretation. The theory is an attempt to reconcile the two incompatible early accounts — the witnesses who said heart and chest, and the footage that shows neck bleeding — by proposing a mechanism that would produce both. A related version of the same reconciliation appears in the slow-motion clip elsewhere in this cluster, whose poster argues the round "ricocheted off of his vest and through his neck."

It is recorded here as the speaker's opinion and not as a finding. No ballistic report supports it, no confirmation that body armor was worn has been established, and the physical questions it raises are examined properly on this site's ballistics pages rather than settled by a video. A competing physical theory — that something on his body rather than a ricochet produced the visible effects — is covered in the microphone thread.
