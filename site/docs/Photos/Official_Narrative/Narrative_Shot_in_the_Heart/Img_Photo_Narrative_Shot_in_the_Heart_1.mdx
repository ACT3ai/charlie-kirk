## What This Image Shows

This is the featured broadcast clip of the wound-location thread — a live television segment in which an on-camera witness describes what he saw at Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025**. It was shared on X by [@charlie_ward77](https://x.com/charlie_ward77/status/2076465128329846991) and is pinned to IPFS so the recording survives if the post is removed.

From the roughly fifty-second transcript, the witness says: "I turn and look, and they are arresting a guy by the name of **George Z[inn]**. Yeah, I know the guy. I don't think he did it. I think they arrested the wrong guy. I think whoever did it was a good shooter, a **sharp shooter. It was one shot, right directly to the heart.** And we need to find out who did this." He adds that in his opinion Zinn was "poor, broke... maybe a perfect person to pick and say that did it. But I don't think he did it at all." The segment then cuts away with the anchor saying "we're gonna go to Jason Chaffetz", confirming it ran inside a live news program.

Two things put this clip at the head of the cluster. The witness independently uses the exact "one shot, right directly to the heart" phrasing that recurs across the earliest television interviews — before any footage was public and before any medical information was released. And he names the man taken into custody as **George Zinn** while stating his own belief that the wrong person had been detained; the public record is that Zinn was not the shooter and faced unrelated charges. Mr. Zinn is a living person and nothing here asserts he committed any crime, nor is the witness accused of anything.
