## What This Image Shows

The contrast account. This eyewitness clip was shared on X by [@VigilantFox](https://x.com/VigilantFox/status/1965871960702828885) on **September 10, 2025**, and describes the same moment in different terms: "All of a sudden I heard just one pop. Saw Charlie all of a sudden like fall. His head kind of fell back. And blood was just gushing out of his **neck**."

The witness continues: "at that point I kind of turned my head to look uphill because it felt like it came from up behind me. And then I saw everybody dropping, so I dropped down. And then the next thing I know, they said run, so we all took off running." He notes the exchange underway was "about gun control" and a transgender-rights question.

Two details in this account are load-bearing beyond the wound location. The witness reports **one pop**, not multiple, which bears on the acoustics analysis elsewhere on this site. And he says the sound felt like it came from **uphill and behind** him, which bears on shot-direction analysis. This clip is filed inside the heart-shot cluster precisely because it is the counterweight — the earliest accounts were not uniform, and preserving the neck-wound testimony alongside the heart-wound testimony is what keeps the comparison honest.
