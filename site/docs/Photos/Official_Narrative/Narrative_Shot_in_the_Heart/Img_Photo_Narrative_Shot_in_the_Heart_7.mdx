## What This Image Shows

A frame-analysis clip shared on X by [@Bizarro_Videos](https://x.com/Bizarro_Videos/status/1965892000185725129) on **September 10, 2025**. The same account separately advanced a claim that an older man interviewed on Fox News had been "planted" — that is, that the witness was not a genuine bystander.

The clip itself is frame-by-frame footage analysis of the moment of the shot, of the kind that circulated heavily in the first days. The reason it sits in this cluster is the pairing: it shows how quickly the wound-location question moved from "what did witnesses see" to "were the witnesses real," which is a much larger and much less supportable claim.

The "planted" framing is that poster's allegation and nothing more. No evidence has been produced for it, the witness is not named or identified here, and this site does not adopt or repeat the allegation as a finding — an unidentified person appearing on a live news broadcast is not thereby anything other than a bystander. It is recorded because the catalog aims to show the full arc of how this narrative developed, including the parts that do not withstand scrutiny.
