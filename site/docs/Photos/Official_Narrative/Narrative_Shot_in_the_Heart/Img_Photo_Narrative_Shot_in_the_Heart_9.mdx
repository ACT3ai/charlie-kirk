## What This Image Shows

A media post shared on X by [@MarioNawfal](https://x.com/MarioNawfal/status/1969832388823003519) on **September 21, 2025**, reporting that **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** had asked Charlie to wear a vest before the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) event.

This item circulated inside the vest and heart-area threads for an obvious reason: several of the interpretive clips in this cluster depend on body armor having been worn, and this post is the closest thing to sourcing for that premise that the compilation contains. It is a media report of a private conversation, published eleven days after the event.

It should be weighed accordingly. A reported request that a vest be worn does not show that one was worn, and even if it was, that would not by itself support the ricochet or fragmentation readings advanced elsewhere in this cluster. Mrs. Kirk is a living person; the post is reproduced as a reported claim with attribution, nothing about it suggests she or anyone else knew anything in advance, and no wrongdoing by anyone is asserted or implied. Full catalog: [Shot in the Heart](/Narrative/Shot_in_the_heart).
