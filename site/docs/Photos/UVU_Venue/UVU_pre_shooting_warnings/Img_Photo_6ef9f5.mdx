## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of redacted **UVU GRAMA** records released to the **Daily Caller News Foundation** as part of a set of 50 documents and 112 pages, tagged with the Utah exemption code **63G-2-305(11)**.

The record of **9/9/2025 10:35 PM**, document **UVU 25-330:0023**, reads: **Everyone from Event Services who reports to me just came to my office letting me know that two senators are coming tomorrow. He said that two people showed up at their offices asking to talk to police about a plan. Said they got them to police. If this is indeed true we do need to notify. Does anyone have any additional information?** The follow-up at **11:19 PM**, document **UVU 25-330:0024**, reads: **The two gentlemen who showed up at office are NOT from a senator's office. They are GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did!**

According to the reviewer who published the records, nobody in the chain asked what the plan was, nobody documented what the two men told police, and their names remain redacted. A separate email that afternoon reportedly read **He wants us to get a message to Charlie Kirk. His phone number is REDACTED**, and whether it was ever delivered is not documented anywhere in the 112 pages. Fourteen hours after the 10:35 PM contact, [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot.

None of this establishes that anyone at the university knew a shooting would occur, and nothing here suggests it. What it establishes is that two warning lanes existed on paper and that the record goes quiet on both, and that names are still withheld. The full transcription, the exemption codes and the emergency-alert failures are set out on [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings); see also [Campus Security Gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps) and [the disclosure laws](/Fix/overview).
