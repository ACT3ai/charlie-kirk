## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide view of the densely packed courtyard. The white canopy at the left carries **THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR** and the slogan **PROVE ME WRONG**. The crowd is predominantly college-aged, in casual clothes, caps and backpacks.

This is one of the standard press frames of the event, and it does the job those frames do well: it shows the size and density of the audience and the position of the tent against the terraces.

Crowd density is directly relevant to two disputed questions - how many recording devices were present, and how freely anyone could have moved through or away from the space. Both recur throughout the case. See [Media](/Media/overview) and [Campus Security Gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps).
