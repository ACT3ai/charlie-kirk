## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide view of a crowd, mostly young adults, gathered on the concrete plaza overlooking the sunken multi-tiered grass amphitheater. A large American flag hangs from the concrete railing of the upper bridge structure in the midground and is the visual focal point. Arid brown mountains rise behind under a clear sky.

The flag on the bridge rail appears in several frames in this sub-cluster and is a useful marker: it dates a group of photographs to the memorial period after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) rather than to the event itself.

Distinguishing before-frames from after-frames is not pedantry. Photographs of the same courtyard taken days apart get shared interchangeably, and a memorial-period frame presented as a scene photograph will mislead anyone trying to read the site as it was. See [After](/After/overview) and [UVU Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward).
