## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide frame of a large, densely packed crowd on the concrete plaza and the adjoining steps, all facing the white pop-up event tent. The prominent branding on the building behind identifies the campus as [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview). Bright midday light, clear sky.

The filename carries a date of [**September 12, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13), two days after the shooting, which if accurate makes the crowd here a gathering at the site rather than the event audience.

Dating these wide frames correctly is the difference between a photograph of the event and a photograph of its aftermath, and they are routinely confused. Compare it against the timestamped event frames in the parent cluster before drawing anything from it.
