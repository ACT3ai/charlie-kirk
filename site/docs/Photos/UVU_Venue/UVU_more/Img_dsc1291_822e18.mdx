## What This Image Shows

A wide daytime frame of a gathering on the campus plaza. Onlookers and media personnel stand behind silver metal crowd-control barriers, facing the brutalist concrete academic building. A large American flag is draped over the second-storey balcony at the left, and clear sky fills the upper third.

The presence of a press pool behind barriers, facing the building rather than a stage, marks this as a post-event media gathering at the site.

It is a record of who was permitted where in the days after, which is the same category of question the event-day frames raise - and the barrier line here is directly comparable to the one in the event photographs. See [Media](/Media/overview) and [After](/After/overview).
