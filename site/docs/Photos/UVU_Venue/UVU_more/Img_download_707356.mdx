## What This Image Shows

An elevated wide-angle view of the campus plaza: geometric multi-tiered grass terraces held by concrete retaining walls, a concrete pavilion framing a shallow water feature, and a large contemporary academic building with dark glass behind. A mountain range rises under a partly cloudy sky.

There is no event here, no crowd and no annotation. That is why it is useful. It is a clean architectural record of the space that everything else in this section is argued over.

Read the terraces and the pavilion carefully - the step heights, the width of the channel, the position of the covered structure - and the crowd frames elsewhere in this cluster become readable as geometry rather than as a scene.
