## What This Image Shows

An elevated wide-angle view of the landscaped plaza, centred on the water feature: a cascading fountain of stepped pools with a concrete trellis structure and sheet waterfall behind it, flanked by geometric terraced planters of manicured lawn and a line of deciduous trees, under a dramatic cloudy sky.

Water features are more than scenery in this case. Running water is a continuous broadband sound source, and it sat between the audience and the covered passage.

That bears on the acoustic arguments directly, since several competing analyses depend on how the report of the shot and its echoes were captured by phones and cameras at different points around the bowl. Ambient noise from the fountains is part of what those recordings contain. See [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories) and [Mic](/Mic/overview).
