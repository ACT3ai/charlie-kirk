## What This Image Shows

A wide landscape photograph of the terraced plaza. Geometric concrete steps filled with manicured grass flank a central cascading water feature that runs down into a small pool; beyond it a concrete pavilion canopy shelters a larger pool of pale blue water, ringed by paved walkways, lawns, mature trees and low academic buildings. A dark overcast sky hangs over silhouetted mountain peaks behind.

Another reference view, shot in different light and from a slightly different angle to the other plaza frames.

Two of these clean views taken from different positions are worth more than either alone, because together they let a reader triangulate where any given event photograph was taken from. That is the whole purpose of this sub-cluster. See [Campus Plaza and Terraces From Above](/Photos/UVU_Venue/UVU_more/Img_download_707356) and [Campus and University](/campus_university/overview).
