## What This Image Shows

An aerial photograph of the campus courtyard with its geometric terraced grass amphitheater. A handful of students and visitors walk the paved paths and gather near the central lawn. The multi-level academic building with its pedestrian bridges borders the courtyard, and a small pond and parking areas are visible behind.

An agency frame of the site with almost nobody in it. Together with the other empty-courtyard views it establishes the baseline condition of the ground surface before the works that followed the shooting.

The surface itself is the thing to look at here - the lawn, the paved margins, the channel - because that is what was excavated and replaced. Comparing this against the paving frames is the most direct way to see the alteration.
