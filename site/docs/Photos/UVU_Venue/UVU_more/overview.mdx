## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster is the baseline reference set for the venue: press photographs, campus marketing images and stock frames of the **Utah Valley University** courtyard, most of them showing the site either before **September 10, 2025** or in the days immediately around it. Several are ordinary architectural shots of the terraced grass amphitheater, the cascading water feature, the concrete pavilion and the Brutalist academic buildings that ring the plaza. They exist in this file for one reason: almost every geometric argument in the case - sightlines, distances, elevations, what was visible from where - depends on knowing what the courtyard actually looked like.

Mixed in are event frames from the day itself and from the days after. Some show the white canopy printed **THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR** and **PROVE ME WRONG** with a dense college-age crowd filling the terraces and packing the upper balconies. Others show the enormous American flag draped over the concrete balustrade of the elevated walkway, and one shows construction workers paving a patio area next to the terraced grass seating under banners reading **UVU** and **A PLACE FOR YOU**. Note what a photograph like that can and cannot settle: it establishes that paving work occurred and roughly where, but the date, the work order and who authorized it are documentary questions the image itself cannot answer.

Use this set as the map before reading anything geometric. The elevation of the walkway, the depth of the sunken amphitheater, the position of the tent against the buildings and the roofline of the **Losee Center** are all legible here. Then move to [Roof](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Roof/overview) for the annotated rooftop frames, [Construction](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Construction/overview) for the paving work, and the written analysis in [UVU](/UVU/overview) and [Campus and University](/campus_university/overview).
