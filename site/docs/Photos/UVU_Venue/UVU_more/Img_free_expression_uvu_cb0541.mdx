## What This Image Shows

A wide exterior view from the red-brick terrace on an upper level, looking out over the terraced green courtyard below - manicured grass steps, rectangular concrete stepping stones and integrated water features. Students sit on the grass steps and walk near the central fountain, with contemporary campus buildings and a mountain range behind.

The file name marks this as a campus free-expression image, the sort of photograph a university publishes to illustrate open-air speech on its grounds. That framing is worth noting given what the courtyard later became known for.

More practically, it is another elevated vantage point recorded - a railed brick terrace with a clear view down into the bowl. The number of such positions around this courtyard is exactly what the sightline questions turn on.
