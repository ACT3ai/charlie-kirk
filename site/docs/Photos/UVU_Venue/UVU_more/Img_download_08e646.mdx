## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level view of a large American flag draped over the side of the brutalist concrete building. Above it runs the elevated walkway with its metal railings; beneath it is the open-air driveway or tunnel passage. To the left, the terraced grass amphitheater climbs to the main plaza levels under a clear sky.

This frame shows the two things this section keeps coming back to in a single view: the covered passage where the vehicles were staged, and the deck above it. The flag places the photograph in the memorial period following **September 10, 2025**.

The passage is visible here as what it is - a through-route open at both ends, directly under a pedestrian deck, adjoining the amphitheater.
