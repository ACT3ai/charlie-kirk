## What This Image Shows

A heavily cropped, low-resolution section of a modern building facade. On a lower level, a small blurred group of people is gathered inside a recessed dark alcove beside a large window. An upper balcony level is partly visible above, with a plant and a wooden slatted screen.

The crop is extreme, which is itself informative about what is being claimed: someone enlarged a small region of a much wider frame because they believed the figures in the alcove mattered. The file was saved under a name referring to camouflage, indicating the annotator thought the clothing of one of the figures was notable.

At this resolution nothing about those figures can be established - not clothing, not number, not identity. It is logged as a question raised, not an observation. The building interiors and the rooms overlooking the courtyard, including the teachers lounge, are covered in [UVU](/UVU/overview) and the rooftop and balcony questions in [Roof](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Roof/overview).
