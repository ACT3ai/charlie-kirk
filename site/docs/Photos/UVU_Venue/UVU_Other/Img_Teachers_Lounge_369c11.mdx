## What This Image Shows

A high-angle agency photograph of a massive crowd of college-age attendees in the concrete and glass courtyard. Two tents stand out in the foreground - a blue-and-pink gradient canopy carrying **SAVE AMERICA - GET INVOLVED** and a white canopy branded **THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR**. Hundreds of onlookers fill the plaza and pack the multi-level balconies behind. Visible credit lines read **gettyimages** and **Credit: The Salt Lake Tribune**.

Professionally shot and properly credited, which makes this one of the more reliable wide records of the crowd. The credit line matters: it means an agency holds the full frame and the surrounding images from the same photographer.

Press archives are an under-used avenue in this case. Agency photographers shoot continuously, so a single published frame usually sits inside a sequence that was never released. Who was shooting from where, and what else is in their take, is an answerable question. See [Media](/Media/overview) and [Photos](/Photos/overview).
