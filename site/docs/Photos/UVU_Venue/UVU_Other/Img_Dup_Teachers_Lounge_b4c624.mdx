## What This Image Shows

An eye-level, medium-wide exterior view of the campus on a bright day. The modern concrete and glass building dominates the frame; in front of it the multi-tiered grass amphitheater is filled with metal crowd barriers and a promotional tent. Students and attendees are scattered across the plaza, the stairwells, the elevated walkways and the grass slopes. Signage fragments read **A PLACE FOR YOU**, **UVU**, **AMERICAN COMEBACK** and **PROVE ME WRONG**.

The barrier layout is the useful detail. This frame shows how the amphitheater was divided before the crowd filled it - which sections were fenced, where the gaps were, and how the tent sat inside that arrangement.

Barrier placement determined who could stand where, and by extension who had close access to the tent and the covered passage behind it. That is a security-posture question with a documentary answer. See [Campus Security Gaps](/UVU/Campus_Security_Gaps) and [UVU Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward).
