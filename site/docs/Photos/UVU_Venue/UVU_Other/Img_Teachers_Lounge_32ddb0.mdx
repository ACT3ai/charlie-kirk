## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide shot of a dense crowd of hundreds, predominantly young adults, in the paved plaza flanked by the modern multi-storey buildings with their extensive glass walls. Two pop-up tents, one bright pink and blue and one white, stand among the attendees in the lower middle of the frame. Legible signage includes **SAVE AMERICA - GET INVOLVED** and **AMERICAN COMEBACK**.

Note the glass. The buildings around this plaza are largely glazed, which means the interiors overlooking the courtyard had a clear view out, and that reflections and interior positions are both in play in any sightline analysis.

Glass frontage cuts both ways for an investigation: it multiplies the number of vantage points overlooking the tent, and it means interior security cameras behind those windows may have recorded the courtyard. Whether any such footage was collected is a records question. See [Cameras](/cameras/overview) and [GRAMA Request for Campus Camera Footage Denied](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Img_Photo_10e148).
