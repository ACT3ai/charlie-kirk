## What This Image Shows

A wide-angle view of the concrete courtyard and terraced amphitheater lawn under a clear sky. The brutalist building complex frames the space, with the elevated skybridge carrying green campus banners reading **A PLACE FOR YOU** and **UVU**. Silver metal crowd-control barricades run across the concrete walkway in the immediate foreground, and a few distant figures are working in the lower courtyard.

An almost-empty frame, with the barricades still in place and work under way below. It sits between the event photographs and the paving photographs, and shows the site in the condition it was left in.

Use it to check the barricade line against the crowd frames, and the ground surface against the later paving frames. Those two comparisons are what let a reader judge the site-alteration claims for themselves rather than taking anyone's word.
