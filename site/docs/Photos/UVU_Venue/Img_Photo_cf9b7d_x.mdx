## What This Image Shows

A phone screenshot of the published daily schedule on **governor.utah.gov**. The page lists a run of budget hearings at the Utah State Capitol - Economic Opportunity, Trust Lands, State Treasurer, Workforce Services, National Guard, State Auditor. One line has been highlighted in yellow by the poster and marked with an arrow: **11:30 a.m. Meeting with the Ambassador of Egypt**, in the Gold Room.

The highlighting is the claim. Researchers who circulate this screenshot are drawing a line to the separate Egypt thread in the investigation - the Egyptian-registered aircraft tracked around the relevant period and the questions raised about foreign contacts. A published diary entry showing a routine diplomatic meeting is not evidence of any connection to the events at **Utah Valley University**, and none is asserted here.

It is logged because it is a primary public record and because the question it prompts is answerable from documents rather than speculation. See [Planes](/Planes/overview) for the aircraft threads and [Israel](/Israel/overview) for the wider foreign-contact analysis.
