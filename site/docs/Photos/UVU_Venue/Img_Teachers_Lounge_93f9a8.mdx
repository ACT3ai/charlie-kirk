## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level view of the campus on a bright day. The white pop-up canopy branded **AMERICAN COMEBACK** sits on the terraced lawn ringed by metal barricades, with people scattered through the midground walking the concrete paths, climbing the outdoor staircases and gathering near the booth. Signage reading **A PLACE FOR YOU** and **UVU** is visible, along with an administration building sign.

This is a setup or early-arrival frame, before the courtyard filled. It shows the barricade line as originally laid out and the routes into the space - the staircases, the walkways and the terraces.

Access routes are not incidental to this case. How people entered and left the courtyard, and which of those routes were watched, is at the heart of both the security-failure questions and the disputes about who was seen leaving.
