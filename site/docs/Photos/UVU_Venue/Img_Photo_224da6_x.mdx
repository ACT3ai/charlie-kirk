## What This Image Shows

The companion screenshot to the Egypt one. The same published Utah schedule page lists budget hearings for Higher Education, Wildlife Resources, Veterans and Military Affairs and the State Board of Education, plus a **5:30 p.m.** speaking engagement at the Kem C. Gardner Policy Institute. The line highlighted in yellow with an arrow reads **3:30 p.m. Meeting with Consul General of France**, in the Gold Room.

Taken as a pair, these two screenshots show a researcher combing the [governor's](/People/spencer-cox) published diary for [foreign diplomatic contacts](/Planes/Cox-Foreign-Meetings) and marking each one found. That is a legitimate open-source exercise, but it produces a list of routine engagements, not a finding.

Nothing here connects any diplomatic meeting to [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and nothing on this page suggests one. The value is that the diary is public and checkable against the timeline. See [Before](/Before/overview) and [Planes](/Planes/overview).
