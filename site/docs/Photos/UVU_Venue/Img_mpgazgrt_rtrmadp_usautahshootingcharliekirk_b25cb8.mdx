## What This Image Shows

An elevated, high-angle view of the **Utah Valley University** campus: concrete architecture, terraced grass plazas, ornamental basins with active fountains, and beyond them a large double-tiered pond, athletic fields and roads. The terraced amphitheater lawn in the foreground still carries the promotional tent and banners. Campus signage reading **UVU** and **A PLACE FOR YOU** is legible on the central building.

The file name identifies it as a wire-service frame filed on **September 11, 2025**, the day after the shooting. That timing makes it one of the few professionally shot overhead views of the site as it stood before the ground was altered.

Everything the geometry arguments in this case rest on is visible here in one frame: the elevation of the terraces, the position of the tent on the lawn, the skybridge, and the distance across the courtyard to the surrounding rooflines.
