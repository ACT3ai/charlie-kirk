## What This Image Shows

A close-up frame, slightly off-level, showing a white dome security camera mounted on a metal arm under a concrete structure. Beside it, set into the light brown concrete ceiling, is a dark rectangular utility hatch or opening. A video player interface with a timecode of **1:16 / 1:36** is overlaid across the bottom.

Two features, both of interest for different reasons. The dome camera is fixed campus surveillance covering the covered passage - which means footage of that area on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) existed. The hatch is an opening in the ceiling directly above the same area.

Both lead to records requests rather than conclusions. Campus camera footage has been the subject of a **GRAMA** request that was denied, and the ceiling above the passage was reportedly worked on in the days after the shooting. See [Cameras](/cameras/overview), [Security Cameras GRAMA Denial](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Img_Photo_10e148) and [Site Alteration](/Tent/Site_Alteration_Evidence).
