## What This Image Shows

A high-angle architectural view of the **Sorensen** courtyard on a bright day. Geometric stepped lawns are separated by concrete retaining walls and a narrow water channel. A large concrete pedestrian bridge spans the scene in the background, carrying colourful window banners, with golf carts parked underneath it.

This is the clean reference view - the site with no crowd, no tent and no barricades. The golf carts parked under the bridge show that the covered passage was a routine service and staging area long before [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

Use it as the base map. The stepped lawns are where the audience sat, the passage under the bridge is where the vehicles were staged, and the surface in front of the bridge is the ground that was excavated and paved four days after the shooting. See [Site Alteration](/Tent/Site_Alteration_Evidence) and [UVU Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward).
