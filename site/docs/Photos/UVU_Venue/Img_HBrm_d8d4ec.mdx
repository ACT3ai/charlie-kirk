## What This Image Shows

A vertical frame from the moments after the shot. The crowd behind the metal barricade is reacting with visible shock. In the foreground the black mesh-backed folding chair is overturned, leaning against the platform draped in black fabric. A small black utility fan sits on a folding table nearby, and several people, including a man in a white cap, are close by in the immediate area.

This is a scene photograph rather than an annotated exhibit, and it records several things at once: the position and orientation of the chair after the shot, the height and drape of the platform, the fan and table on the deck, and how close the barricade line was.

The chair, the platform and the objects on the deck are the physical detail that the surface alteration removed within days. Anything recorded on or around that platform on **September 10, 2025** now exists only in photographs like this one.
