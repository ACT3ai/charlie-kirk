## What This Image Shows

A high-angle frame of a large, disorganised crowd across the amphitheater plaza, with modern buildings and hazy mountains behind. Red markup has been drawn over it by hand: a large circle, two arrows, and the caption **Object Gone**. A player timecode reads **1:09:45**.

This is the second half of a before-and-after pair. Its companion frame, taken from the same playback moments earlier, is captioned **Object there**. The annotator is arguing that something visible in one frame is absent in the next.

The claim cannot be tested from the image. At this distance and resolution the marked area resolves into a handful of indistinct shapes, and the arrows and circle are drawn on rather than photographic. The grass in the frame is littered with dropped items, which is consistent with a crowd that has just scattered. It is a question a researcher raised, and it is logged as that.
