## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide view of the campus: a grassy hillside cut into terraced concrete amphitheater seating on the left, a standard grass slope with shrubs and stairs on the right, and in the lower centre a white pop-up canopy with red lettering standing on a paved landing, ringed by white-and-red branded barriers. Behind rises the horizontal brutalist concrete building, and beyond that the large turquoise pond with its fountain.

This is the before-frame for the construction sub-cluster: the paved landing and the surrounding ground as they were, with the event infrastructure in place.

Its whole purpose is comparison. Set it beside the paving photographs and the change to the ground surface is legible without anyone having to characterise it.
