## What This Image Shows

A blurry screenshot from the tunnel clip. A dark vehicle is partly visible in the midground, a person in a red shirt is blurred at the left edge and another figure stands at the right. Three hand-drawn red circles have been added, all marking areas of the concrete ceiling. The overlay reads **In the tunnel right after!**, watermarked **@rynestimetwo**, with a view counter showing **1.3M**.

The circles are the claim: the annotator is asserting that marks or damage are visible on the underside of the deck above the vehicle area. At this resolution, on a compressed social video frame, that cannot be verified from the image, and nothing here establishes what the marked areas are.

It is kept because of what happened next. Workers were photographed on scaffolding carrying out overhead repair on that same underside, which means whatever condition the ceiling was in on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) is no longer inspectable. That sequence - marks alleged, surface repaired, no independent examination recorded - is the shape of the argument, and it is a records question rather than a proven one.
