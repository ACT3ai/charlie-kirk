## What This Image Shows

An indoor frame showing an overturned black metal-framed office chair lying on its side on patterned carpet, with a thin wire or string strung through the frame and a small black adapter or weight attached to it. The background is an ordinary office interior - beige walls, wooden baseboards, the corner of a desk.

This is not the venue. The carpet, walls and furniture are an office room, which means the picture is almost certainly a demonstration or reconstruction someone set up indoors to test an idea about how the chair on the platform ended up in the position it did.

Read it that way and nothing more. A staged indoor arrangement proves nothing about [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025); it records what one researcher was trying to model. The actual scene photograph of the overturned chair against the platform is catalogued separately as [Overturned Chair at the Platform](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Img_HBrm_d8d4ec), and the platform structure is examined in [Tent Structure Under Charlie](/Tent/Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie).
