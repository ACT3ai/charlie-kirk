## About This Cluster

This cluster is the geography of the crime scene: the **Sorensen Center** courtyard at **Utah Valley University**, where **Charlie Kirk** was shot at **12:23 p.m. on September 10, 2025**. The terraced amphitheater lawns, the fountain channel, the elevated skybridge with its "UVU — A PLACE FOR YOU" banners, the tunnel beneath it, and the surrounding rooflines all appear in the analysis threads, so clean reference images of the venue matter as much as the annotated evidence frames. Every trajectory argument, camera-position debate, and crowd-flow question ultimately gets checked against this ground.

The images mix plain venue photography with contested material: red-circled figures on an upper balcony, a dome security camera mounted beside a dark utility hatch, the overturned chair with a thin wire threaded through its frame, paving crews reworking the courtyard days after the shooting, and GRAMA response letters citing Utah Code 63G-2-305(11) to withhold names and camera records. Researchers also circulate stranger finds filed here — the Utah governor's public schedule showing meetings with the Ambassador of Egypt and the Consul General of France, a Perplexity query about UVU's Center for National Security Studies, and a Utah registry page for a company called ROBINSONSTG, LLC. Each of these is a poster's claim or an open question, reported with attribution, not a conclusion.

Walk the sub-clusters in order of weight: [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/Photos/UVU_Venue/UVU_pre_shooting_warnings/overview) for the September 9 GRAMA records, [Construction](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Construction/overview) for the courtyard pave-over, [Roof](/Photos/UVU_Venue/Roof/overview) for the annotated object-on-the-roof frames, then [more](/Photos/UVU_Venue/UVU_more/overview) for baseline venue geography. The written analysis lives in [UVU](/UVU/overview), [Campus &amp; University](/campus_university/overview), and the paving thread under [Tent](/Tent/overview).
