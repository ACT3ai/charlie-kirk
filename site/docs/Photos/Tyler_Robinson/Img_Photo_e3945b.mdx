## What This Image Shows

This is a news-graphic treatment of a portrait photograph. A young man faces the camera in a plush brown-and-white sloth-themed hooded onesie against a plain blurred indoor background, with a small credit mark reading TikTok in the corner of the photo. Beneath it a white caption bar reads that [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs), seen here, the roommate of main suspect [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), 22, made the remark in a Reddit post.

The image matters to the investigation because Twiggs is the single most load-bearing witness in the state's public case. Prosecutors confirmed in open court that a [**Cellebrite**](/court/Cellebrite) forensic extraction was performed on his phone and that it recovered text messages exchanged with Robinson on September 10 and 11, 2025, with the first message timed at 11 p.m. on September 10. The alleged confession the public knows came off the roommate's device rather than the defendant's, which puts a third party's phone at the center of the chain of custody.

This picture is a media portrait, and the caption's reference to a social media remark is the outlet's characterization, not a finding. Twiggs has not been charged with anything, and nothing on this page alleges any conduct by him. For what the record does contain, read [Twiggs Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone), [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite), [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) and the image cluster at [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview).
