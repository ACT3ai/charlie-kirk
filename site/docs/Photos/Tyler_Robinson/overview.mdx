## About This Cluster

**Tyler Robinson** is the 22-year-old from Washington City, Utah, whom the State of Utah has charged with the **September 10, 2025** assassination of **Charlie Kirk** at Utah Valley University. He has pleaded not guilty, and no court has convicted him of anything. This cluster is the photographic backbone of that thread of the investigation: the surveillance stills, the charging documents, the recovered rifle, the clothing record, the message evidence, and the timeline images that researchers measure the official account against.

The charging affidavit describes a rooftop suspect in a black long-sleeve shirt with an American flag and eagle, dark jeans, and Converse-style shoes — while it places Robinson arriving on campus at 8:29 a.m. in a maroon T-shirt and light shorts. The state calls that a change of clothes; researchers argue it may be two different people, pointing to backpack and build differences in the stairwell stills. Court documents cited by attorney **Baron Coleman** put a Miranda reading at 6:25 p.m. on **September 11** — roughly ninety minutes before the 7:57 p.m. Discord "confession" allegedly posted from Robinson's account — and the booking sheet records a 10:00 p.m. September 11 arrest even though officials described a "33-hour manhunt" ending September 12. Robinson's family has said publicly they do not believe he did it.

The [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview) sub-cluster holds roommate **Lance Twiggs** and the message evidence.
