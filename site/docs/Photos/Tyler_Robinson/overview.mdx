## About This Cluster

[**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) is the 22-year-old from Washington City, Utah, whom the State of Utah has charged with the [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) assassination of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). He has pleaded not guilty, and no court has convicted him of anything. This cluster is the photographic backbone of that thread of the investigation: the surveillance stills, the charging documents, the recovered rifle, the clothing record, the message record, and the timeline images that researchers measure the official account against.

The charging affidavit describes a rooftop suspect in a black long-sleeve shirt with an American flag and eagle, dark jeans, and Converse-style shoes — while it places Robinson arriving on campus at 8:29 a.m. in a maroon T-shirt and light shorts. The state calls that a change of clothes; researchers argue it may be two different people, pointing to backpack and build differences in the stairwell stills. Court documents cited by attorney [**Baron Coleman**](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) put a Miranda reading at 6:25 p.m. on **September 11** — roughly ninety minutes before the 7:57 p.m. [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) "confession" allegedly posted from Robinson's account — and the booking sheet records a 10:00 p.m. September 11 arrest even though officials described a "33-hour manhunt" ending September 12. Robinson's family has said publicly they do not believe he did it.

Start with [Description Law Enforcement](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Img_Description_Law_Enforcement_a66b04) for the official suspect description, then work through the [Walking](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Walking/overview), [Clothing](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Clothing/overview), and [Gun Found](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Gun_Found/overview) sub-clusters for the surveillance and weapon threads. The [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview) sub-cluster holds roommate [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs) and the message record. For the written analysis behind these images, see [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview).
