## What This Image Shows

This is a second circulated copy of the counter security still, posted by a different account and without the decorative border applied to the other version. A young man in a dark red T-shirt, dark pants and a baseball cap stands at a curved service counter on diagonally laid tan tile, a small drink in one hand and the other touching a red box on the counter, looking off to his left.

The claim attached to both copies is that this is the **6:38 p.m.** sighting on the evening of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), at a [**Dairy Queen**](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) in Orem about 15 to 17 minutes from the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus. The dark red shirt is the point of connection to the charging timeline, which describes a maroon T-shirt in the morning frames and an apparent return to that attire after the shooting.

The identification is the claim of the people circulating the image; readers responding on X questioned the lighting and shadows, and no agency has confirmed the still or the time. It is included so both copies of the same frame can be compared. See the route maps in this cluster, then [Travel](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel) and [Timeline](/Timeline/overview).
