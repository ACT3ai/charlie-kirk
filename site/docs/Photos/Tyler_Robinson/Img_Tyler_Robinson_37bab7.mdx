## What This Image Shows

This is the booking photograph of [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), taken after he was placed in custody. He faces the camera with a neutral expression against a plain wall, in a grey shirt with a dark padded garment visible over the shoulders — a safety smock of the type used in restrictive custody housing.

The timing of this photograph is itself contested. The public account describes a voluntary surrender at the [Washington County Sheriff's Office](/Locations/Washington_County_Sheriff) in Hurricane, Utah on the evening of [**September 11, 2025**](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody), with transport north and booking into the [Utah County Jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail) on **September 12**. Attorney [**Baron Coleman**](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) has argued from the inmate booking sheet and probable cause statement that the arrest date is recorded as September 11 at 10 p.m. by an officer from northern Utah, which he says is impossible to square with a manhunt that officials described as ending the following day. That is his reading of the documents; an investigative-hold explanation has been offered in response, and no court has resolved it.

Robinson has pleaded not guilty and remains in custody pending trial; nothing on this page asserts guilt. Read the custody-timing material at [Mirandize](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview), [Probable Cause Affidavit](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit) and [Utah County Jail Custody](/court/utah-county-jail-custody), and see [Arrest](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Arrest/overview) for the related document images.
