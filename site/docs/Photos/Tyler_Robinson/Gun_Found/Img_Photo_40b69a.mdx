## What This Image Shows

An overhead 3D rendering of the campus buildings with red marker dots and three labels: **Shooting Position**, **Jump Down** and **Hid Gun**, with the Technology Building identified alongside.

Those three labels compress the most physically demanding part of the official account. The affidavit describes a single shot fired at about **12:23 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025**, then the suspect standing and running north to the roof edge, placing a dark item — described as a rifle wrapped in a dark towel — on the rooftop at **12:24**, lowering himself to the grass below and running north toward the parking lot and on into wooded ground where the **Mauser Model 98** was later recovered. A researcher circulating stills of the same window has argued that the movement from the firing position to the northeast corner of the **Losee Center** took twenty-five seconds.

The map is a reconstruction and the Hid Gun marker is the compiler's placement, not a surveyed evidence location.
