## What This Image Shows

A high-angle aerial photograph, apparently from a drone or light aircraft, looking down on a suburban street where it runs beside a heavily wooded greenbelt buffer and backs onto residential properties. Two vehicles are moving on the roadway and several are parked in a lot at lower right.

The wooded strip is the point. The affidavit describes the suspect fleeing north across Campus Drive, crossing a grassy area with trees at the edge of campus and going into a wooded area north of the campus, where a **[Mauser Model 98](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle)** in .30-06 with a scope was later recovered. An overhead view shows how narrow that band of cover actually is and how close it sits to homes and traffic, which is the basis for the question researchers keep asking: who else could see that ground, and what cameras covered it.

This is a mapping photograph rather than forensic imagery, and no find location is marked on it.
