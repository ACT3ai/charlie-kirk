## What This Image Shows

A high-angle daytime frame across a full asphalt parking lot, with a grassy slope rising to a line of dense trees behind it and a lone dark figure apparently running on the grass at the upper right. A date and running-time marker appear in the corner, indicating this is a still from a recorded review of footage.

A figure running toward the tree line is exactly the movement the charging documents describe after the shot: standing, running north across the roof, dropping to the grass, running toward the parking lot east of the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)**, crossing Campus Drive and a grassy area with trees, and entering the wooded ground north of campus where the rifle was later reported found. Whether this frame shows that movement or a different moment entirely cannot be determined from the still.

No identification is made here of the running figure and no time on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** is established for this frame.
