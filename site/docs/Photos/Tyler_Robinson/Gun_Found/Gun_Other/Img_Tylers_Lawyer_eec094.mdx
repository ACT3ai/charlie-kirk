## What This Image Shows

A vertical crop of a dense crowd of mostly young adults seated in outdoor bleachers under a clear sky, all looking toward the lower left.

Crowd crops circulate in this investigation because people search them for faces they believe they recognize from elsewhere in the case. That practice produces a great many claims and almost no verified findings, and it regularly sweeps in bystanders. No one in this frame is identified here, no one pictured has been connected by any authority to the events of **September 10, 2025**, and nothing on this page alleges any conduct by anyone visible.
