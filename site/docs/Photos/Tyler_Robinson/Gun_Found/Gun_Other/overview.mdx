## About This Cluster

This catch-all under **Gun Found** holds the weapon-related images that do not show the wooded recovery area itself — court and exhibit material, comparison shots circulated by researchers, and two photographs filed under **Tyler Robinson's** defense attorney. Together they round out the record around the **Mauser Model 98** the state says was used on **September 10, 2025**.

The rifle is contested from every direction: how it got onto campus, when it was found, who handled it, and what forensics actually tie it to the fired round. The defense-attorney images matter because Robinson has pleaded not guilty and his lawyers have challenged the state's evidence chain, including in the discovery disputes documented elsewhere on this site. As always, claims inside the images are the posters' claims, reported with attribution.

Start with the six exhibit photos, then the two [Tylers Lawyer](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Gun_Found/Gun_Other/Img_Tylers_Lawyer_817c45) images. For the recovery-scene photos go back to [Gun Found](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Gun_Found/overview); for the written analysis see [Tyler Robinson Weapon and Rifle](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle) and the trial coverage at [Trial](/Tyler_Robinson/Trial/overview).
