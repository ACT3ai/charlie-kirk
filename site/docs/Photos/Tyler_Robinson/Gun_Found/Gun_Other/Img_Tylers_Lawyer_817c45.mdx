## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot from a mobile social media app showing two photographs of women placed side by side, with the app's reply bar still visible at the bottom. The person who posted it is inviting readers to conclude that the two subjects resemble each other closely.

Side-by-side resemblance posts are among the least reliable material that circulates around this case, and they carry the highest risk of harming people who have nothing to do with it. Neither individual in this comparison is identified here, neither has been named in any filing or connected to the events of **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** by any investigator or court, and nothing on this page suggests that either of them did anything improper or unlawful. The archived filename attaches a role to one of the images; that attachment is the archiver's label and is not established.
