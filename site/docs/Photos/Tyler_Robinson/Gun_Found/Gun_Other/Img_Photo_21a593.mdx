## What This Image Shows

A drone-height aerial looking down on the interface between residential streets and an institutional parking lot: houses, mature trees, an asphalt roadway, one white vehicle in a driveway and a row of parked cars at the top of the frame. A running time marker in the corner shows this is a still pulled from a video walk-through rather than a standalone photograph.

The area covered is the stretch between the north edge of the Utah Valley University campus and the neighborhoods beyond it — the ground the affidavit describes the suspect crossing after **12:24 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025**. Aerial passes like this are how researchers establish what lies between the campus and the reported rifle recovery point, and which properties would have had a view.

No location on this frame is marked as evidentiary and no claim is made about any property or vehicle shown.
