## What This Image Shows

A wider, higher aerial of the same residential area — winding streets, large detached homes, heavy tree cover and a private tennis court near the center right.

The value of the wide view is scale. Route reconstructions in this case are argued in minutes, and minutes only mean something once a reader can see how far apart the campus edge, the neighborhood streets and the wooded ground actually are. The official sequence gives **11:49 a.m.** in the neighborhood, **11:50 a.m.** on the grass north of Campus Drive, and **12:02 p.m.** entering the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** — a set of intervals that only makes sense against this geography.
