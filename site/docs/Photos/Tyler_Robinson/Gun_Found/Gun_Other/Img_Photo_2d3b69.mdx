## What This Image Shows

A high-resolution aerial of the transition zone: institutional buildings and large parking lots on one side, single-family homes, lawns and cul-de-sacs on the other, with a curved multi-lane road running between them as the physical divider.

This is the geography every route argument in this section depends on. The affidavit's account has the suspect leaving the campus at **12:24 p.m.** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** heading north across Campus Drive and into wooded ground, while the neighborhood cameras on the residential side produced the **11:49 a.m.** sighting and several of the most-shared frames. Seeing both zones in one image makes clear how short the distances are and how many private cameras face that boundary.

This is mapping imagery, not forensic photography, and nothing on it is marked. See the annotated versions in [Gun Found](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Gun_Found/overview), the street frames at [Walking](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Walking/overview), and read [Cameras](/cameras/overview).
