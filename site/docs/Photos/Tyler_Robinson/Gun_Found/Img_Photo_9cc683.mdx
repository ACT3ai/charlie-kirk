## What This Image Shows

An annotated 3D aerial rendering looking north-northwest across the boundary between the residential streets and the Utah Valley University campus, with red dots tracing a route down a central street. The white labels read **Shooting Position**, **UVU Army ROTC**, **Limping**, **Route Back** and **Car Parked** with a question mark.

The question mark is the honest part. The charging documents record the gray Dodge Challenger arriving on campus at **8:29 a.m.** on **September 10, 2025** but do not state when it left the lot, and researchers have pressed that gap repeatedly — asking why a timeline built with camera and cell data leaves the vehicle's departure unrecorded. The Limping label refers to the affidavit's own description of a stiff right leg at a slow pace in the late-morning frames, a detail described as no longer noticeable by the time of the rooftop sequence.

Every label on this map is a citizen researcher's reconstruction, not an official exhibit.
