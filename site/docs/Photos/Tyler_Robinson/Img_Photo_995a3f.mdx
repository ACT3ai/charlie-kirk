## What This Image Shows

This is a navigation map of **Orem, Utah**, presented as a graphic on a pale green background with a stylized silhouette and the handwritten name **Candace**. It shows three driving routes between a point labeled Marked Location in the southwest, near the Utah Valley University area, and a **Dairy Queen Grill and Chill** in the northeast, with travel times of 15 and 17 minutes and the fastest route highlighted.

The map exists to make one claim checkable. Investigation records note a reported sighting on CCTV at a Dairy Queen in Orem at **6:38 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025**, roughly six hours after the shooting. The distance shown here is the basis for asking a further question that researchers have raised repeatedly: if someone was in Orem at 6:38 p.m., what vehicle carried him the 250-plus miles south to Washington County, and how does that fit the surrender on the evening of September 11.

The map is a graphic prepared by a commentator, and the identification of the Marked Location is that commentator's assertion. The still frame the claim rests on is filed separately in this cluster, along with the X post that discusses it.
