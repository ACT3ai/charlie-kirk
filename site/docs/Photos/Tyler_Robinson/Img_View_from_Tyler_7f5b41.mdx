## What This Image Shows

This is the wide version of the rooftop view, looking down from the gravel roof across the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) plaza toward the paved walkways, lawns and the distant pond with fountains, with a highway visible far beyond. Campus signage reading UVU and the name of the **Sorensen** building are legible in the frame. Two red circles have been drawn onto the image: one around a small white object lying on the gravel in the foreground, and one around the white tent in the plaza below.

The pairing is the argument in a single picture. The charging documents place the firing position on the roof of the [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center), roughly 430 feet from the courtyard where [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was speaking under that tent, with the shot fired at about [**12:23 p.m.**](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). Circling both ends lets a reader judge the distance, the angle of depression and what stood between them without taking anyone's word for it.

The circles and the identification of the object on the gravel are annotations by whoever prepared the image, not official markings.
