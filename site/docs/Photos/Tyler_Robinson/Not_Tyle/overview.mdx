## About This Cluster

This small cluster holds imagery circulated by researchers who argue that people photographed at the **September 10, 2025** UVU event have been wrongly matched to **Tyler Robinson**. Identification is the load-bearing wall of the state's case — the morning arrival, the stairwell figure, and the rooftop runner all have to be the same person — so every credible look-alike in the crowd matters to the analysis.

The single image here is an X post by @XRP4Me33 presenting four stills of a young man in a maroon shirt at the event, carrying a skateboard and wearing wired earbuds. The poster notes the man is wearing a wedding band, argues he was filming the scene, and ties him to the claim that the accused "did a dry run" — all of which are the poster's claims, not verified identifications. The maroon shirt matters because the charging affidavit says Robinson arrived on campus that morning in a plain maroon T-shirt.

Compare this against the stairwell stills in [Stairs](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs/overview) and the official suspect description in [Description Law Enforcement](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Img_Description_Law_Enforcement_a66b04). The identity dispute is developed in full at [Stairs Guy vs Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy) and across the [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) section.
