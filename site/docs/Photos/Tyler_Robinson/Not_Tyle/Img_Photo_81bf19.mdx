## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot of a post on X presenting four video stills from the crowd at the Utah Valley University event on **September 10, 2025**. The panels focus on a young man in a maroon shirt carrying a skateboard, wearing wired earbuds and holding a smartphone. The **PROVE ME WRONG** tent banner is partly visible behind the crowd.

The post text is written in alert style and makes several assertions about this bystander, tagging commentators and attaching a hashtag naming him only by his shirt color. None of those assertions has any support: the person is not identified by any agency, has not been named in any filing, has not been connected to the events by any investigator, and is not accused of anything on this page. Holding a phone in a crowd of hundreds where nearly everyone was filming is not evidence of anything, and the maroon shirt described in the affidavit's morning timeline is a mass-market garment.

The reason to preserve the screenshot is that this kind of post is how bystanders get turned into suspects in public, and the record of it belongs in the file with the correction attached.
