## What This Image Shows

This is a video thumbnail circulated online, not proof. Three portraits of young men are placed side by side in vertical panels with white captions beneath them, and a large red circle is drawn by hand over the panel on the right. One caption reads [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) with the qualifier alleged shooter; the others carry a personal name and a note describing that person as a friend of someone else.

Graphics of this shape are how face-comparison claims spread, and they need to be read for what they are. Circling a face is an assertion by whoever made the thumbnail, and the private individuals named in the other panels have not been identified by any investigator, agency or court as having any connection to the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). No one pictured other than Robinson has been charged with anything, and nothing on this page suggests that any of them did anything improper or unlawful.

It is filed here because the graphic is in circulation and readers who encounter it should be able to see how thin its basis is. The re-photography of the surveillance locations that produced some of these portraits is described at [Rob Hild](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Rob_Hild/overview), and the serious version of the identification question is at [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy) and [Stairs Guy Identity Gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap).
