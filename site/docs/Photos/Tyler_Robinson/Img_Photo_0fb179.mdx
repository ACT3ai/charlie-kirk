## What This Image Shows

A second frame from the same residential porch camera, taken from a slightly wider angle. A single person carrying a backpack walks the sidewalk on the left side of the street, and the red and silver dump truck is parked on the right. The porch pillar and white railing frame the shot as before.

This is the neighborhood segment of the timeline, where the charging documents place a sighting at **11:49 a.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shortly before the campus frames begin. The backpack is the detail worth attention, because its apparent fullness is the pivot of the argument set out at [Backpack](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Backpack/overview) — a light blue JanSport described by researchers as roughly half empty in the parking-lot frames, against a dark pack that reads as nearly full in the stairwell sequence.

At this distance the frame supports observations about direction, clothing and load, and no identification is asserted here.
