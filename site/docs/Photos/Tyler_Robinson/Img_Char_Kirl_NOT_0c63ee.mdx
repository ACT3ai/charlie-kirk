## What This Image Shows

This is an argument graphic rather than a photograph. The left half is a blurry video still of the outdoor event at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview), showing the white canopy tent printed with **PROVE ME WRONG**. The right half is a text panel in red and black claiming that no blood and no bullet hole are visible 21 seconds after the shot, asserting that a .30-06 did not fire the round that struck [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk), and reasoning that so powerful a cartridge fired from the front would have produced massive bleeding and a visible hole. It closes by quoting the phrase evil doers, plural, attributed to Kirk's widow.

The underlying question is real and is treated seriously elsewhere on this site. The recovered weapon is described in the charging documents as a [**Mauser Model 98**](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) in .30-06, and investigation notes record independent threads on the wound path, on the vertebra involved, on acoustics suggesting a direction inconsistent with the rooftop, and on whether the ATF's ballistics comparison methods can support the state's conclusion. The plural phrasing in the widow's remark has also been noted by many people as worth asking about.

What this particular graphic offers is inference from a low-resolution video still, which cannot carry the weight the text puts on it, and it is not a forensic finding.
