## What This Image Shows

A casual full-length portrait of a smiling teenager standing on a concrete path in front of a light brick building and a row of hedges, in daylight — the kind of photograph taken for a school or family occasion.

Full-length images have one specific use in this file. Every comparison researchers make against the surveillance frames is a comparison of build, height and proportion rather than of face, because the campus and doorbell cameras do not resolve features. A clear standing photograph in ordinary clothing is what those comparisons are anchored to.

Nothing is asserted here about the subject, and this page makes no identification. Set it beside the stairwell and street frames in this cluster, then read [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy) and [Stairs Guy Identity Gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap).
