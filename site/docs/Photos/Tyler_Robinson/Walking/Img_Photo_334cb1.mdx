## What This Image Shows

A stairwell frame annotated by a citizen researcher, with a bright green circle and arrow drawn around the grey baseball cap worn by the descending figure.

The cap is circled because it is one of the specific mismatches researchers raise. The morning frames from the Utah Valley University parking lot on **September 10, 2025** show a black hat with a white logo, per the affidavit's own description of the 8:29 a.m. arrival, while the stairwell sequence shows a lighter cap. Investigation notes on the stairwell figure record the hat as a blue mesh baseball cap and argue that a 22-year-old would not swap hats and backpacks mid-morning.

The annotation is the researcher's, and the mismatch argument is contested by the state's clothing-change account.
