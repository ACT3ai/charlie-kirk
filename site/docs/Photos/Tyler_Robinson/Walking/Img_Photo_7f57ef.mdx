## What This Image Shows

A high overhead camera looks down onto a concrete landing as a person descends the steps in a dark long-sleeved shirt with a graphic print, dark jeans, a baseball cap and a black backpack.

This is the outfit the charging affidavit describes for the rooftop suspect: a long sleeve black shirt with a picture of an American flag and an eagle printed on it, dark colored jeans, a dark colored hat, sunglasses, white or gray shoes consistent with Converse styles, and a dark colored backpack. The frames from this stairwell between about **11:52 a.m.** and **12:15 p.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) are the connective tissue between the neighborhood sightings and the [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof.

The backpack is what researchers examine hardest in this angle, because the top-down view shows how full it sits. Set it beside the light blue pack seen earlier the same day and the arguments at [Backpack](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Backpack/overview) and [Backpack and Rifle Impossibility](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Backpack_Rifle_Impossibility), then read [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy).
