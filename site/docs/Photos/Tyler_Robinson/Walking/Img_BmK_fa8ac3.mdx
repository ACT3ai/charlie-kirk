## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution security-style frame taken from an elevated vantage point behind a white railing, showing a single pedestrian walking away from the camera down the middle of a curved asphalt road. He wears a T-shirt, shorts, a cap and a backpack.

The shorts are the significant detail. The charging affidavit describes the person who arrived on the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus at **8:29 a.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) wearing a plain maroon T-shirt, light shorts, light shoes and a black hat, and describes the rooftop suspect in dark jeans and a black long-sleeve shirt. Researchers arguing that the state's account requires an implausible sequence point to exactly this: a person in shorts carrying a pack that would also have to hold a rifle section, then jeans and a fuller pack an hour later.

No identification is asserted here; at this resolution the frame supports observations about clothing, load and direction.
