## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an old social media post showing two boys standing side by side in Halloween costumes on a path outside a suburban house. The caption expresses that it feels strange not to be trick-or-treating with the boys this year, and carries hashtags dating it to **2017**.

Old family photographs surface in cases like this because reporters and researchers assemble a biography from whatever is public, and there is very little else. The result here is a picture of a child roughly eight years before the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), which tells a reader nothing about the case and a great deal about how these files get built.

It is filed for completeness rather than for evidentiary value, and the children pictured are not identified on this page.
