## What This Image Shows

A video still from a residential gravel drive, looking past two parked vehicles toward manicured lawns, sculpted hedges and a dry grassy hillside beyond, at a playback position of about 6:03 of 6:56 — near the end of the walk-through.

By this point in the route the surroundings are private property: driveways, hedges, houses with a clear view of the ground being crossed. That is the practical basis for the question this whole cluster is asking. The affidavit's account requires a person to have moved from the campus roof through open residential ground carrying or disposing of a rifle, in daylight, within minutes, and the public record contains no footage of that leg despite the density of private cameras visible in these frames.
