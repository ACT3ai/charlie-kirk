## What This Image Shows

A still labeled **WOODED AREA 2**, showing tall dry grass, trees and a dirt path, with a small aerial map inset in the corner and the same investigation overlay, at a playback position of about 5:28 of 6:56.

The woods are where the case's physical exhibits are said to have ended up. The affidavit describes the suspect fleeing into a wooded area north of the campus, and a [**Mauser Model 98**](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) in .30-06 with a mounted scope being recovered there. The transcript attributed to the alleged confession thread separately describes leaving the rifle in a bush where the writer changed outfits, being unable to retrieve it because of a squad car parked nearby, and worrying about fingerprints. Seeing the actual ground — dry grass, thin cover, a walking path — is what lets a reader judge how concealed anything left there would have been.

The area labels are the video maker's numbering, not official designations, and no recovery point is marked as verified.
