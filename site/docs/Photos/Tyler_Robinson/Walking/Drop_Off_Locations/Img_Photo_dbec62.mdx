## What This Image Shows

A 3D aerial map still from the same walk-through, showing large houses with lawns, mature trees and driveways along a curved roadway. The overlaid labels include **700 W**, **W 925 S** and a **UVU Army ROTC** marker, alongside the investigation graphics and a small map inset.

The street numbering here matches the annotated camera map filed in the parent [Tyler Robinson](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/overview) cluster, which marks a private camera position on **W 925 S St** near **S 700 W St** and **S 635 W St**. Those are the blocks where the neighborhood sighting recorded in the charging documents at **11:49 a.m.** on **September 10, 2025** was captured, and where several of the most-circulated porch and doorbell frames come from. Being able to name the streets is what turns a set of anonymous stills into a sequence.

Aerial map graphics are approximations produced for a video, not surveyed exhibits. Read the porch frames in [Walking](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Walking/overview), the camera-location map in the parent cluster, and [Maps](/maps/overview).
