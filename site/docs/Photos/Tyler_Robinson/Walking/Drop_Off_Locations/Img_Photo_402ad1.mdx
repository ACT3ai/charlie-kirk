## What This Image Shows

A wide elevated shot framed from above a gravel-covered rooftop, looking out over the Utah Valley University campus — concrete and brick buildings, a central plaza with stairs, and the decorative pond with fountains beyond.

This frame anchors the walk-through to its starting point. The charging documents place the firing position on the roof of the **Losee Center**, roughly 430 feet from the courtyard where **Charlie Kirk** was speaking, with the shot at about **12:23 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025** and the descent to the grass a minute later. Everything in the rest of this cluster is the ground the route crosses after this vantage.

This is the video maker's establishing shot rather than an evidence photograph, and nothing on it is marked.
