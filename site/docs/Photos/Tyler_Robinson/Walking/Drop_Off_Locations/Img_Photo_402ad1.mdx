## What This Image Shows

A wide elevated shot framed from above a gravel-covered rooftop, looking out over the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus — concrete and brick buildings, a central plaza with stairs, and the decorative pond with fountains beyond.

This frame anchors the walk-through to its starting point. The charging documents place the firing position on the roof of the [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center), roughly 430 feet from the courtyard where [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was speaking, with the shot at about [**12:23 p.m.**](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) and the descent to the grass a minute later. Everything in the rest of this cluster is the ground the route crosses after this vantage.

This is the video maker's establishing shot rather than a forensic photograph, and nothing on it is marked. Compare with the two annotated rooftop views in the parent [Tyler Robinson](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/overview) cluster, then read [Tyler Robinson on September 10, 2025](/Tyler_Robinson/Tyler_9_10_2025) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
