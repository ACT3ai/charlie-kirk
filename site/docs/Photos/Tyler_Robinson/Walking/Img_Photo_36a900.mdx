## What This Image Shows

A second frame from the same residential camera run, catching the pedestrian in mid-stride moving right to left across the street with a backpack on. The image has the washed-out, high-contrast look of consumer outdoor surveillance in bright daylight.

Sequences of frames matter more than any single one, because direction of travel and pace are what let researchers test the affidavit's minute-by-minute account against the map. The relevant window is the **11:49 a.m.** neighborhood sighting on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), running into the **11:50 a.m.** entry that describes a walk across the grassy area north of Campus Drive with a stiff right leg at a slow pace.
