## What This Image Shows

This is an aerial graphic produced by **CBS News Confirmed**, overlaying numbered callouts on a 3D satellite view of the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus in Orem. The labels read suspect seen before shooting, suspect entered garage before shooting, suspect fired from [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center), Kirk shot in courtyard, suspect jumped from roof and fled, and police search area after shooting.

This is the mainstream visual version of the charging timeline, and it is the most convenient single reference for the campus geography. The underlying document sequence runs from stairwell and parking-garage frames at about **11:52 and 11:53 a.m.**, entry to the Losee Center at **12:02**, the roof at **12:15**, a firing position taken at **12:22**, a single shot at about [**12:23 p.m.**](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time) from roughly 430 feet, and a descent to the grass and flight north at **12:24** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

A news graphic is a summary of an official account, not an independent verification of it, and the numbered positions are approximations drawn for readers.
