## What This Image Shows

Four stairwell stills placed side by side, showing the same descent from different camera angles, with a hand-drawn orange oval added to one panel to draw the eye to a specific detail.

Composites are how the identification argument is actually conducted, because no single frame from a campus camera carries enough resolution to settle anything. Placing four angles together lets a reader check whether the pack, the cap, the shirt graphic and the shoes stay consistent across the sequence, which is the test both sides need. The frames sit in the window between about **11:52 a.m.** and **12:15 p.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

The highlight is the compiler's, and no identification is asserted on this page. Compare with the two composites in [Stairs](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs/overview) and the three-panel version in the parent cluster, then read [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy).
