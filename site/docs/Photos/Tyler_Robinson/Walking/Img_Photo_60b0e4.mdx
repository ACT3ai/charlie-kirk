## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an article page from the entertainment news site **TMZ**, showing a snippet of text, two side-by-side portraits, and a red banner reading TYLER ROBINSON PHOTOS above a gallery link.

The visible text carries two claims worth isolating. One is that [**Discord**](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) removed the account attributed to [Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), which matters because the alleged confession is a Discord post and platform deletion affects what can later be independently verified. Investigation records show the Utah State Bureau of Investigation subsequently sought Discord records by warrant. The other is the line reporting that Utah authorities said they believed Robinson acted alone while the investigation continued — the official position that the arguments collected across this section are pressing against.

A celebrity news site is not a primary source, and the portraits were gathered from social media rather than released by any agency.
