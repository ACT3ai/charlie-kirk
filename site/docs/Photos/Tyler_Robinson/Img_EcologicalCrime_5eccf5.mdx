## What This Image Shows

This is the first page of an **AFFIDAVIT FOR SEARCH WARRANT** filed in the **Fourth District Court** in and for **Utah County, State of Utah**. The header block establishes the court and jurisdiction, and the body opens the affiant's sworn request for a warrant directed at specified **Apple Inc.** accounts.

Warrant affidavits are among the few primary documents the public gets to read in a case that is otherwise pre-trial and partly sealed. They matter here because the digital evidence is the case: investigation records show the Utah State Bureau of Investigation also sought records from **Discord** relating to usernames including Craftopia, Gamin and The Corruption, and prosecutors confirmed in court that a **Cellebrite** extraction was performed on **Lance Twiggs**' phone. Set against that is a prosecution filing stating the state did not intend to offer evidence obtained through forensic extractions of the seized electronics, a tension researchers have repeatedly flagged.

Read this page for the scope of what was requested rather than for conclusions, and continue with the companion affidavit pages elsewhere in this cluster that itemize the electronics seized.
