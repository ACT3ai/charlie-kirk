## What This Image Shows

A composite graphic reproducing an **Instagram** post. The post interface on the right shows a username, a location tag reading [**UVU**](/UVU/overview), and caption text stating that the poster took friends to a grandmother's house that night, that they retraced his steps and took pictures in the exact places the security cameras caught him, that the poster attended the event to support [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk), and that the last picture shows **Converse brand footprints**. Two inset photographs sit on the left, one a selfie of three young men with one circled in red, the other a building exterior. A personal name is overlaid at the lower left by the graphic maker.

The caption is the valuable part. It indicates that private residential property near the campus is where some of the surveillance angles come from, which explains why porch and doorbell frames are so central to this file, and the poster says the last photograph shows Converse-style footprints at the route — the affidavit describes a shoe impression consistent with Converse or Chuck Taylor sneakers, and the BBC graphic at [Clothing](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Clothing/overview) repeats the point.

The red circle and the overlaid name are annotations added on top of the post, not part of it. No one shown has been connected by any authority to the events, and nothing here alleges any conduct by the poster or by anyone in the photographs.
