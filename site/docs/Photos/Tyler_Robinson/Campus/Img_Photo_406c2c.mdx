## What This Image Shows

A phone screenshot of a paused **YouTube** video titled Raw Footage of Charlie Kirk TPUSA Shooting at Utah Valley University, published by an attendee. The frame shows a crowded outdoor campus scene with students in front of a modern academic building, and a hand-drawn yellow arrow points at a young man in a light blue polo shirt. Player timestamps are visible at the bottom of the frame.

Raw attendee footage matters because it is unedited and continuous, and because it covers the courtyard and walkways in the minutes before the shot at about **[12:23 p.m.](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time)** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**. The playback position shown is the key detail on the screenshot: it is what lets anyone find the same moment in the full recording and see it in motion rather than as a crop.

The arrow is the poster's annotation. The person it points at is not identified here, has not been connected to the events by any investigator or court, and nothing on this page suggests any conduct by him.
