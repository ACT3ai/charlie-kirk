## About This Cluster

These four frames come from a single long piece of raw bystander video of the [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) event at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**, published to YouTube as a roughly 69-minute recording. Raw crowd footage matters here for a specific reason: it is unedited, it was posted by an attendee rather than an agency, and it shows the courtyard, the ramp and the surrounding walkways continuously in the minutes before the shot. Researchers use it to test claims about who was standing where, and to look for individuals visible in the crowd who are also visible elsewhere in the case record.

The screenshots are annotated by the person circulating them. One carries a hand-drawn yellow arrow pointing at a young man in a light blue polo standing in front of a campus building; another carries a hand-drawn red circle around a small group at the lower right of the frame. The annotations are the poster's assertions about who is worth looking at, not identifications supported by any filing, and none of the circled people are named here. What the frames establish on their own is more modest and still useful: the density and layout of the crowd, the sightlines from the elevated walkway, and the playback positions in the source video where each moment can be checked.

Open each frame and note the playback timestamp shown in the player, then use those to locate the same moments in the full recording rather than relying on the crop. Compare what you see against the wider campus geometry in [On Campus](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/On_Campus/overview) and the route frames in [Walking](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Walking/overview). For the surrounding written analysis see [UVU](/UVU/overview), [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Tyler Robinson on September 10, 2025](/Tyler_Robinson/Tyler_9_10_2025).
