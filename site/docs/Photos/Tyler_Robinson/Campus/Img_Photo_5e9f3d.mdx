## What This Image Shows

A horizontal playback screenshot of the same raw attendee recording, black bars top and bottom, showing the crowd on the paved campus walkway under a partly cloudy sky, with the player's controls and progress bar overlaid. The position reads about 6:55 of 1:09:43.

The playback metadata is the useful content here. A screenshot with a visible position and total duration is a citation — it tells another researcher exactly where in a public recording to look. Investigation notes on this case repeatedly return to the problem that claims circulate as crops with no way to check them against the source, so frames that carry their own reference are worth more than sharper ones that do not.
