## What This Image Shows

A screenshot from the raw attendee recording with a thick red circle drawn by hand around a small group of people in the lower right quadrant of the frame, over a dense crowd on the plaza in front of the campus buildings. The player position reads about 6:55 of 1:09:43.

Circling a group in a crowd is an assertion that they are worth looking at, and it is only as good as the reasoning behind it — which a screenshot does not carry. Nobody inside the circle is identified here, none of them has been named in any filing or connected to the events of **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** by any investigator or court, and nothing on this page suggests that anyone pictured did anything improper. What the frame does usefully preserve is a checkable playback position in a public recording.

Use the timestamp to review the moment in motion rather than relying on the still. Then read [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview), [Suspects](/Suspects/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
