## What This Image Shows

This is a satellite map of the residential streets immediately west of the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus, annotated by a citizen researcher. Street labels visible in the frame include **W 925 S St**, **S 700 W St** and **S 635 W St**. A red circle with an arrow is drawn onto one property and labeled CAMERA HERE, marking the position of a private security camera whose footage entered the public record.

The neighborhood matters because the charging timeline places a sighting at **11:49 a.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) in a nearby neighborhood, roughly a minute before the walk across the grass north of Campus Drive. Doorbell and porch cameras on these streets are the source of several of the most-shared stills in the case, and identifying exactly which house each camera sits on is what allows a viewer to work out the direction of travel rather than guessing from a cropped frame.

Annotations like the circle and label are the researcher's own work and have not been verified against any filing.
