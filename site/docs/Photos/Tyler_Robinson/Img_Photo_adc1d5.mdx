## What This Image Shows

This screenshot captures a post on X dated **September 18, 2025** reporting that [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) had released a previously unseen photograph of [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) at a [**Dairy Queen**](/Locations/Dairy_Queen_Orem) in Orem at **6:38 p.m.**, after the shooting. The post text describes him as appearing unbothered and calm in a maroon shirt, blue jeans, a hat and sunglasses, and notes that the Dairy Queen is about 17 minutes from campus. The route map is embedded alongside, and the visible replies question the image, with one reader writing that the lighting looks off and to pay attention to the shadows, and the poster replying that the shadows look inconsistent to him as well.

The clothing description is the part that connects to the wider case. The charging timeline has the person arriving on campus that morning in a maroon T-shirt and cap, changing into a black long-sleeve shirt with an American flag and eagle for the late-morning sequence, and apparently returning to the morning attire afterward. A 6:38 p.m. sighting in the maroon shirt is consistent with that account and is also, on the other reading, one more thing that has to be reconciled with a drive to southern Utah the same night.

Everything in this screenshot is claim and commentary, including the doubts about lighting, which are impressions offered by readers rather than any forensic finding. Nothing here establishes that the photograph is genuine or that it is not.
