## What This Image Shows

A digital document page headed **The Roommate**, running to two paragraphs and a transitional line, with the closing sentence of the second paragraph — beginning with the words Police found — highlighted in light blue by the person circulating it.

The note is the earliest link in the state's public chain. The account is that a note was left under a keyboard at the apartment [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) shared with [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs), that the roommate found and read it, and that his reaction is preserved in the message thread later recovered from his phone. The transcript filed at [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview) opens with exactly that: a narrative line stating that after reading the note the roommate responded, followed by a disbelieving reply. Everything downstream — the messages, the alleged Discord post, the confessions described to family — is built on top of it.

Highlighting is one reader's emphasis, and the page states an account rather than proving it; the note itself has reached the public only as photographs.
