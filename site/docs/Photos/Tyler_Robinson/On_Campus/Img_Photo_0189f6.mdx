## What This Image Shows

A wide, high-angle photograph taken from an elevated walkway, its metal railing cutting diagonally across the foreground, looking out over a large crowd of students on the campus plaza and lawn, with concrete campus buildings and a distant mountain range behind.

This is the vantage that makes the case's central physical claim legible. The charging documents place the firing position on the roof of the **Losee Center**, roughly 430 feet from the courtyard, and describe the suspect crossing a railing from a public walkway onto the roof, running across it and crawling into position between **12:15 and 12:22 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025**. A view like this shows how much of that geometry was in the open, overlooked by hundreds of people and by the walkway itself.
