## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot of a post on X by a user posting as Morgan. The text argues that there is no verifiable public record trail for **Lance Twiggs** — no addresses, licenses or history — and that his name appears only in press coverage of the shooting. It then points to a records entry for a similarly named southern Utah physician tied to a pediatric practice and a large regional health system, notes that the system runs pediatrics, psychiatry and crisis programs, and suggests the family is positioned in clinical services and medical supply chains where behavioral-health funding would flow after a high-profile incident. The attached composite pairs the news portrait of Twiggs with the cropped records page showing birth information and known aliases.

This is a theory, and it should be read as one. The absence of a data-broker footprint for a 20-something is unremarkable, shared surnames in a small state are common, and a hospital system offering psychiatric services is not evidence of a motive for anyone. No filing, agency statement or court document supports any part of the chain the post proposes, and nothing here alleges that any named individual did anything improper or unlawful.

It is included because the post circulated widely and its component screenshots are filed separately in this cluster, so readers can see the full claim in one place rather than encountering the fragments without context.
