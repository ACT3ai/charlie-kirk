## What This Image Shows

An annotated satellite map of the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus, overlaid with colored markers and path lines. The legend at lower right reads, in order: **Suspect's arrival**, **Rooftop access**, **Rifle found**, **Sniper's perch**, **Charlie Kirk**, **Bullet's path** and **Suspect's movements**.

That legend is the official account compressed into seven labels, and it is the most efficient orientation available for everything else in this section. It fixes the arrival point where the gray Dodge Challenger was parked at **8:29 a.m.**, the roof of the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** where the affidavit places the firing position at about **12:23 p.m.** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**, the courtyard where **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** was speaking roughly 430 feet away, and the wooded ground beyond campus where the **[Mauser Model 98](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle)** was reported recovered.

A published map is a summary of an official narrative, and the bullet path line in particular is a depiction rather than a survey.
