## What This Image Shows

Four panels from elevated cameras capture the same descent of an outdoor concrete staircase in sequence, showing a figure in a black long-sleeve graphic shirt, dark pants, sneakers, a cap and a backpack.

This is the most complete angular coverage available of the stairwell segment, which the affidavit places between about **11:52 a.m.** and **12:15 p.m.** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** as the figure moved through a pedestrian tunnel, a parking garage stairwell and on toward the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)**. Each panel is a chance to check whether the pack fill, cap shape, shirt graphic and shoe colors hold constant.

Nothing here identifies the person; the affidavit itself calls him the unidentified individual.
