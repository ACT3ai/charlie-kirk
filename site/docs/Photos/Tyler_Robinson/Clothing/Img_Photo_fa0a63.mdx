## What This Image Shows

A composite pairing a blurry, low-resolution surveillance still of a figure walking outdoors with a clear indoor portrait inset in the upper right, ringed in a thick red circle.

The format is the standard identification graphic used across news and social media in the days after **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**. It is also the format that does the most to shape a reader's conclusion before the reader has examined anything: placing a sharp face beside a blurred one and circling it invites the eye to complete the match. The affidavit itself does not make that leap, describing the person on camera only as the unidentified individual referred to as Suspect.

No identification is asserted on this page and the compiler of the graphic is not stated.
