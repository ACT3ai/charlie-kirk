## What This Image Shows

A low-angle camera captures the figure from behind, standing near a flight of stairs in a utilitarian stairwell of metal railings and concrete. It is the clearest available view of the black backpack.

This frame is the one researchers reach for when arguing about volume. The claim set out in the investigation record is that the pack seen here reads as roughly 90 to 95 percent full while the light blue **JanSport** carried from the campus parking lot that morning reads as roughly half empty, and that the transaction the state describes — shorts and light shoes in, jeans and dark clothing out — should have left the pack emptier, not fuller. The further argument is that a 22-year-old would not swap one backpack for another mid-morning.

These are inferences from image interpretation, not measurements, and the state's position is that this is one person who changed clothes.
