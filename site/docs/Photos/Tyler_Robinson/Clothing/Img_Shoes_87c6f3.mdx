## What This Image Shows

A surveillance still of the same descent, framed so the footwear is visible against weathered concrete steps beside a light grey metal railing with square balusters. The figure wears a black long-sleeved graphic shirt, dark pants, a grey cap and a black backpack, and is looking toward the rail.

Footwear is a specific evidentiary thread in this case. The affidavit describes white or gray shoes with white shoelaces appearing consistent with Chuck Taylor or Converse styles, and describes a shoe impression consistent with Converse or Chuck Taylor sneakers left in the ground. Separately, an attendee who went out to re-photograph the surveillance locations posted that the last picture in their set showed Converse-brand footprints, which is filed at [Rob Hild](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Rob_Hild/overview).

A low-resolution still cannot establish a shoe brand, and an impression consistent with a mass-market sneaker sole is a weak individuating detail on its own.
