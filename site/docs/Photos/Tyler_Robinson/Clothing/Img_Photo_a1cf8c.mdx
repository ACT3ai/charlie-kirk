## What This Image Shows

A second circulated version of the same two-frame comparison, showing the identical street location at **8:07 AM** and **11:49 AM** with the automated green tracking box drawn on the earlier frame.

The value of having both versions is that the crops and directions differ slightly, and readers can see how much of the apparent argument depends on how the panels were assembled. In one version the earlier figure moves left to right and in the other right to left, which is the kind of inconsistency that creeps in when graphics are rebuilt and reshared rather than exported once from the source footage.

Both frames sit within the neighborhood segment of the **September 10, 2025** timeline, and neither supports a facial identification at this resolution.
