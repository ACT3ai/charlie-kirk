## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a **Steam** profile comments section showing eight comments from different accounts, with timestamps running from late April through [**September 10 at 11:01 a.m.**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) The content is joke posting and in-group references, including several users swapping Discord handles.

The September 10 timestamp is why this was captured, since it falls on the day of the shooting, roughly an hour and twenty minutes before the shot described in the charging documents. What the comment itself says is unrelated to anything in the case. The rest of the thread is ordinary gaming-community joking that reads badly when quoted out of that context, and it has been used online to argue about a person's character rather than about any fact.

No account in this thread is identified here with any person, and nothing on this page alleges conduct by any commenter. If the account-identity question interests you, the documented version is at [Discord Records in Court](/court/discord-evidence-court) and [Robinson Phone and Devices](/court/Robinson_Phone_Devices).
