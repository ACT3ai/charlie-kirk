## What This Image Shows

A composite graphic built to make an identity argument. Snippets of legal documents occupy one band and a chat log screenshot the other, against a black background, with the compiler's explanatory text along a white footer. The subject is which online usernames the filings attach to which person in connection with the **September 2025** events at Utah Valley University.

Username attribution is the hinge of the digital case and it is genuinely contestable. The account credited with the alleged confession post is rendered in circulating material as **Zealous Monkey 55095**; investigation records show the Utah State Bureau of Investigation sought **Discord** records for usernames including Craftopia, Gamin and The Corruption; and separate affidavits sought Apple account records and an internet subscriber account tied to a Gmail address. Binding all of those to one person is an evidentiary chain, and none of the underlying records has been made public.

The composite is one researcher's reading, its document snippets are cropped, and its conclusions are not adopted here.
