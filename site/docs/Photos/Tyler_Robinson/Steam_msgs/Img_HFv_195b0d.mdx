## What This Image Shows

A transcript excerpt of the exchange between the speakers labeled [**Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) and **Roommate**, opening with a narrative sentence and continuing in script format through the passages about a hidden rifle and engraved bullets. Horizontal ellipses mark where blocks have been cut out of a longer exchange.

Those ellipses are worth pausing on. The full transcript filed at [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview) contains the sequence about being stuck in Orem, needing to grab the rifle, a squad car parked near the drop point, changing outfits and leaving the weapon in a bush, and worrying about fingerprints. An excerpt that keeps the rifle and the engraving while dropping the surrounding material is a curated version, whoever curated it and for whatever reason.

The engraved-cartridge detail was among the earliest specifics released to media and did a great deal of work in setting the public narrative; the underlying documentation for it has not been produced publicly.
