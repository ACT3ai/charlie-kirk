## About This Cluster

Online accounts are a large part of how the public narrative about [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) was assembled in the days after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and this cluster holds the account-identity material built from the **Steam** gaming platform. Steam profile pages, comment threads and the handles attached to them were circulated as windows into the defendant's online life, and warrant paragraphs and filings in this cluster show investigators pursuing the same identifiers. Because identity attribution online is easy to assert and hard to prove, this material sits at the center of a genuine evidentiary question rather than settling one.

The screenshots include a Steam profile using an Animal Crossing character avatar with a Community Ambassador badge and a Discord handle listed in the bio, comment threads timestamped around September 10, and document excerpts that name Discord usernames investigators sought records for. [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation notes record that the Utah State Bureau of Investigation sought [Discord](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages) records tied to usernames including Craftopia, Gamin and The Corruption. Researchers reading these screenshots have questioned whether the joke-heavy comment threads support any inference at all, and separately have flagged filings in which prosecutors said they did not intend to offer material obtained through forensic extractions of seized electronics — a statement in tension with the extracted material that has since been reported publicly. Every attribution of an account to a named person in these images is the poster's claim unless a filing says otherwise.

Read the profile and comment screenshots first, then the document excerpts that show which handles law enforcement actually named. Pair them with [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), [Phone Extraction](/court/Phone_Extraction) and [Discord Records in Court](/court/discord-evidence-court). The related message record pulled from the roommate's device is in [Lance](/Photos/Tyler_Robinson/Lance/overview) and [Twiggs Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone).
