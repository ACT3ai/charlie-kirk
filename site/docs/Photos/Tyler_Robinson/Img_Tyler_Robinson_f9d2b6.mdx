## What This Image Shows

This is a second frame from the same bedroom sequence: **Tyler Robinson** standing with his hands in his pockets, smiling, in a faded maroon crew-neck T-shirt, medium-wash blue jeans and a dark baseball cap with sunglasses resting upside down on the brim.

The maroon shirt is why this photograph gets used. The charging timeline describes the person who arrived at Utah Valley University at **8:29 a.m.** on **September 10, 2025** as wearing a plain maroon T-shirt, light shorts, light shoes and a black hat with a white logo, and describes an apparent return to that morning attire after the shooting. The X post about the reported **6:38 p.m.** sighting at an Orem restaurant that evening also describes a maroon shirt. A casual photograph showing the same combination — maroon shirt, cap, sunglasses on the brim — is what reporters and researchers reach for when making that comparison.

Robinson has pleaded not guilty; this image is a reference likeness and asserts nothing.
