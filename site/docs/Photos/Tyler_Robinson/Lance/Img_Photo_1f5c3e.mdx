## What This Image Shows

A wide banner-shaped crop of analysis text, describing a sequence that involves an arrestee, individuals identified in the text as Fox and [Beau Mason](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason), [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), and the discovery of the gun.

How and when the rifle was found is one of the least settled parts of the public record. The charging account has a dark item placed on the rooftop at about 12:24 p.m. on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), a flight north into woods beyond the campus, and a [**Mauser Model 98**](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) in .30-06 recovered there later. Separate threads in this investigation concern informant-related court orders and the order in which locations were searched, and this screenshot is one commentator's attempt to line those up.

The named individuals have not been accused of anything by any authority and nothing here alleges wrongdoing by any of them; the reconstruction is unverified.
