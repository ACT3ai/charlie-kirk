## What This Image Shows

This analysis screenshot lays out a timeline in compressed form, moving between **UVU**, the **River Road** address in St. George where **Tyler Robinson**'s apartment was, the message described as left under a keyboard, and the rifle.

The geography matters because it is roughly 250 miles between those two points. The affidavit-derived account has the shooting at Utah Valley University at about **12:23 p.m.** on **September 10, 2025**, has Robinson driving south overnight with a documented gas stop in Cedar City at about 7:15 a.m. on September 11, and has him surrendering in Washington County that evening. A note found at a southern Utah apartment and a rifle reported recovered in woods north of the Orem campus sit at opposite ends of that drive, and the sequence in which each was found is part of what researchers are testing.

The timeline in this screenshot is the author's reconstruction, unverified, and it should be checked against filings rather than accepted.
