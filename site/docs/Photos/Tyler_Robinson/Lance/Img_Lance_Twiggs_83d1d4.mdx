## What This Image Shows

A portrait of a smiling young man in formal attire, photographed outdoors in late afternoon light against blurred foliage and stonework, captured as a screenshot from a mobile social media app with the interface bars still visible.

This is one of the reference images that circulated once [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs) was named in coverage as [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson)'s roommate. It carries no evidentiary content of its own; its role in the record is that it is what people were shown while the story about the roommate's phone and the recovered messages was being formed.

Twiggs has not been charged with anything and no conduct is alleged against him here. The material that does sit in the record is at [Twiggs Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone), [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite) and [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs), and the message record itself is transcribed elsewhere in this cluster.
