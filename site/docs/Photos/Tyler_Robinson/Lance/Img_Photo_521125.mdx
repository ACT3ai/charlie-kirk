## What This Image Shows

A block of analysis prose examining the order in which the **FBI** and the Utah **State Bureau of Investigation** appear in the case record, alongside [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs), [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) and an individual identified as Mike Mitchell.

Which agency did what and when is a live question in this investigation. The affidavits in the record are signed by a State Bureau of Investigation investigator, the arrest recorded on the booking sheet is credited to a State Bureau of Investigation officer, and the FBI released the suspect video on [**September 11, 2025**](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody) and has been the subject of separate reporting about blocking other law enforcement from parts of the investigation. Sorting the sequence out from public documents is what this screenshot attempts.

It is one researcher's reading, unverified here, and it names private individuals against whom nothing is alleged. See [FBI](/FBI/overview), [Case Overview](/court/case-overview) and [Mirandize](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview).
