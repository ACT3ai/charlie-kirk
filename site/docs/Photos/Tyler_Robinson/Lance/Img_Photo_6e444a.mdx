## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot of analysis text, not a filing. It works through what the search warrants in the case actually name, focusing on **Discord** usernames — including the handle rendered as **Zealous Monkey 55095**, the account the alleged confession message is attributed to, and a variant spelling of another handle. It also references reporting by the journalist **Ken Klippenstein** and discusses **Tyler Robinson** and **Lance Twiggs** by name.

Handle attribution is the weak joint in the digital case and this is why researchers keep pulling at it. Investigation records show the Utah State Bureau of Investigation sought Discord records for usernames including Craftopia, Gamin and The Corruption, and separate affidavits sought Apple account records and internet service subscriber records. Each of those requests is an attempt to bind an account to a person, and none of the resulting records has been made public.

The author's conclusions are that author's own, unverified here, and the exact spellings and capitalizations that the analysis leans on cannot be checked without the underlying documents.
