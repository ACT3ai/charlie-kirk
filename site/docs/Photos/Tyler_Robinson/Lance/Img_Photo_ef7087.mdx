## What This Image Shows

A cropped block of analysis text examining a search warrant, a social media post and an indictment together, and naming several individuals discussed in that context, including a commentator whose reporting on the case has been widely shared.

The point of setting those three document types beside each other is that they are produced at different stages and by different parties. A warrant affidavit records what an investigator asserted in order to obtain access; an indictment records what a prosecutor is prepared to charge; a post records what a member of the public claims. Treating all three as equally authoritative is the error this kind of analysis is usually trying to correct, and sometimes commits.

The author's reading is unverified here, and the individuals named in it have not been accused of anything by any authority. See [Case Overview](/court/case-overview), [Discord Evidence in Court](/court/discord-evidence-court) and the other analysis screenshots in this cluster.
