## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot of a **Facebook** profile picture update dated **November 15, 2021**, showing a young man in a dark puffer jacket and baseball cap, photographed outdoors against evergreens and a soft sunset sky. The account name shown is a family account rather than the individual's own.

Personal social media of this kind entered the record only because [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs) became the state's central witness. His phone produced the message record, prosecutors confirmed a [**Cellebrite**](/court/Cellebrite) extraction of it in open court, and the messages recovered from it are the confession material the public knows. Photographs like this circulate as identification references rather than as proof of anything.

Twiggs has not been charged with any offense and nothing on this page suggests any conduct by him or by the account holder who posted the picture. For the record material, see [Twiggs Phone](/court/Twiggs_Phone), [Cellebrite](/court/Cellebrite) and [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs).
