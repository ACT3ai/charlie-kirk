## What This Image Shows

A short text graphic with a bolded heading above a single narrative paragraph, summarizing the case as it runs through the messages recovered from [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs)' phone and connecting [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) and [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk).

The heading the author chose characterizes the relationship between the two roommates in personal terms. That framing is the writer's and is not adopted here; what is in the record is narrower and more consequential. Prosecutors confirmed in open court that a [**Cellebrite**](/court/Cellebrite) extraction was performed on the roommate's phone and that it recovered messages exchanged on September 10 and 11, 2025, with the first timed at 11 p.m. on September 10. That means the most quotable material in the case came off a third party's device.

Twiggs has not been charged with any offense, and nothing here alleges conduct by him or characterizes his private life as established fact.
