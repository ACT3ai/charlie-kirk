## What This Image Shows

A cropped excerpt of analysis text working through who communicated with whom across the events of [**September 10 and 11, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13), and where the reported communications do not line up.

The communication record is unusually central in this case because it is nearly the whole of the state's public evidence. Prosecutors confirmed in court that a [**Cellebrite**](/court/Cellebrite) extraction of [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs)' phone recovered messages beginning at 11 p.m. on September 10; a note is said to have been left at the apartment; a Discord post is attributed to an account on the evening of September 11; and separate accounts describe confessions to family, to a retired detective and to a church leader. Arranging all of those into one consistent sequence is what this screenshot attempts and what critics say cannot be done.

The conclusions are the author's own and are not adopted here. Read the transcript in this cluster, then [Discord Messages](/Tyler_Robinson/Discord_Messages), [Discord Custody Timeline](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline) and [Surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender).
