## What This Image Shows

This is a plain directory listing, screen-captured and circulated on X. The text describes **Dixie Regional Medical Center** as a full-service regional referral center serving the medical needs of Washington County and the tri-state area. It lists general inpatient and outpatient medical, surgical and obstetrical care, heart surgery and trauma services, home health and hospice, a regional cancer center, a rehabilitation center, a wound clinic, a NICU and a community health and information center, followed by department phone numbers including behavioral medicine for inpatient psychiatric care.

The geography is why it was pulled. Washington County is where [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) lived, with [an apartment](/Locations/Tyler_Apartment) on South River Road in St. George and his parents' home in nearby Washington City, and it is where he surrendered at the sheriff's office in Hurricane on the evening of [**September 11, 2025**](/After/Sept_11_Surrender_And_Custody). The screenshot travels as one panel of a larger X post arguing a connection between the Twiggs surname, southern Utah pediatric medicine and behavioral-health funding streams.

Standing alone the listing is public hospital information and establishes nothing about this case. The theory it is attached to is unverified and is described on the X-post image elsewhere in this cluster; no agency filing supports it, and no wrongdoing is alleged here against any institution or individual named on the page. For the parts of the southern Utah timeline that are documented, see [Surrender](/Tyler_Robinson/Surrender) and [Travel](/Tyler_Robinson/Travel).
