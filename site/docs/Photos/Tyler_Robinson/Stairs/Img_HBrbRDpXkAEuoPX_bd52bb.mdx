## What This Image Shows

Four security camera frames of the same stairwell descent, assembled into a widescreen layout with blurred, stretched copies of the frames filling the empty space at the left and right edges — a formatting convention borrowed from video, not an artifact of the original footage.

What the four angles allow is a consistency check across the sequence: whether the cap, the chest graphic, the pack fill and the shoe colors hold steady as the figure moves. The affidavit places this stretch between about **11:52 a.m.** and **12:15 p.m.** on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), running from a pedestrian tunnel through a parking garage stairwell and on to the [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center). It also describes the person as walking with a distinctive gait, a stiff right leg at a slow pace, which stills cannot show but which multiple frames of a stair descent can hint at.

The affidavit itself calls this figure the unidentified individual; no identification is asserted here.
