## About This Cluster

These are screenshots of X posts in which commentators lay out what they allege is an attempted cover-up around [Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) death. One reproduces claims attributed to [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) that several key officials tied to the case — a coroner, a surgeon, a judge, and an FBI figure — were newly appointed, which the poster reads as suggesting coordination. Another captures a post asserting that phones were hacked and campus footage edited at the Utah venue. A third is a "Post Analytics" panel over a graphic comparing "before" and "after" media layouts, framed by its author as evidence of a **cover-up**. All are presented here as attributed allegations, not proven facts.

Why the topic matters: much of the investigation's cover-up thread centers on records that reportedly were withheld or lost. Researchers point to GRAMA public-records disputes over surrender bodycam footage that Washington County has said it no longer has, medical and ballistics details kept private during the active case, and the crime scene being paved over. These screenshots gather the online claims that first raised those questions, so readers can weigh them against the official account.
