## What This Image Shows

The twelve-month companion to the ninety-day capture of the same term. This mobile **Google Trends** screen runs "George Hodgeson Zinn" against **Israel**, **Past 12 months**, with the axis spanning October 6, 2024 to July 27, 2025. The tooltip marks the peak at **April 6-12, 2025** with a reading of **100**.

Widening the window changes the reading, and that is the point of keeping both captures. On the ninety-day view the strongest point falls in August 2025; on the twelve-month view the maximum sits in **April 2025** — five months before [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) — because the index rescales to whatever the highest point in the selected range happens to be. Anyone citing a Trends peak has to state the window, or the number means nothing.

**George Zinn** was detained at the scene at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) and, per the public record, was not the shooter and faced unrelated charges; nothing here asserts otherwise.
