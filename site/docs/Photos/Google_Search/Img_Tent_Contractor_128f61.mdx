## What This Image Shows

A mobile **Google Trends** capture for the search term "Daniel Merrell", filtered to **Israel**, **Past 12 months**. The axis runs October 13, 2024 to July 20, 2025 and the tooltip marks the peak at **June 29 - July 5, 2025** with a reading of **100**. An "Interest by subregion" panel is partly visible below.

The filename this image was archived under identifies the subject as a tent contractor, which places it in the thread examining who built and staffed the stage and canopy structure at the Utah Valley University event. That thread matters because the tent, its anchoring, and the ground work beneath it are examined elsewhere on this site for what they may show about sightlines and about who had site access in the days before September 10, 2025.

The week of June 29 to July 5, 2025 is the same window in which the "Elena Vasquez" search peaks in the sibling [Google Quotes](/Photos/Google_Search/Google_Quotes/overview) cluster, which is the kind of coincidence researchers log rather than interpret. For the site work see [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [construction and site changes](/Medical/construction_and_site_changes). Nothing here suggests Mr. Merrell did anything wrong; Trends identifies no searcher and a name with no baseline can reach 100 on very few queries.
