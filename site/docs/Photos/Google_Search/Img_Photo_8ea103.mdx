## What This Image Shows

A mobile browser capture of **Google Trends** at trends.google.com, showing the search term "George Hodgeson Zinn" filtered to **Israel** over the **Past 90 days**. The interest-over-time chart carries a peak the tooltip dates to **August 13, 2025**, and an "Interest by subregion" panel is partly visible below the graph.

**George Zinn** is the man taken into custody at the scene at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. The public record is that he was not the shooter and faced unrelated charges. That is what makes the date on this capture the point of the screenshot: August 13, 2025 is roughly four weeks before the event, and the search string uses his full middle name rather than the short form that circulated afterward. Researchers raise that as a question. It is not evidence that anyone knew anything in advance, and Trends cannot show who ran the query or why.
