## What This Image Shows

Three **Google Trends** panels in one capture with different region filters. "**Utah Valley University**" is set to **Israel**, **Past 90 days**, peaking **July 14, 2025** at **100**. "**Benjamin Netanyahu**" is set to the **District of Columbia**, peaking **July 16, 2025** at **38**. "**helldivers**" is also set to the District of Columbia, peaking **July 15, 2025** at **68**.

The third panel is a control, and it is the most useful thing in the capture. **Helldivers** is a video game with no connection to this case; including it shows the reader what an ordinary consumer term's ninety-day series looks like in the same bucket over the same days. A researcher who publishes a control alongside his claims is doing the right thing, and the control here reads 68 — comfortably in the same range as the terms being flagged, which cuts against reading much into a single mid-range number.

The three peaks fall within seventy-two hours of each other in mid-July 2025, inside the window the wider thread anchors on. Nothing here identifies a searcher, and no claim is made that anyone knew anything in advance.
