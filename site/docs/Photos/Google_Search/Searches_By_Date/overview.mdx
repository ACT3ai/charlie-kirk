## About This Cluster

These captures are the date-driven half of the Google Trends work. Instead of running one term and reading its geography, the researcher stacks several **Google Trends** panels into a single screenshot — usually three or six at a time — all set to the same region and the same **Past 90 days** window, so the peak dates line up side by side and can be compared at a glance. Nearly every panel here is filtered to the **District of Columbia** bucket, with a handful set to **Israel**. The point of the layout is the calendar: if unrelated terms all crest within the same few days, the researcher argues that something drove attention to all of them at once.

The stacked terms cut across every part of the case. One panel set pairs "[**brian harpole**](/People/brian-harpole)" peaking around **August 30, 2025** with "**charlie kirk hasbara**" and "**charlie kirk family**" both peaking **September 2, 2025** — that is eight days before the shooting. Another set groups "**distance uvu timpanagos**," "**orem ut coroner**," and "[**Valhalla Danish House**](/Locations/Valhalla)" all cresting on **September 5, 2025**, five days out, mixing a hospital-distance query, a coroner query, and the Frantzve family property that appears elsewhere in this investigation. A third panel places "[**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview)" at **July 14, 2025** and "[**Benjamin Netanyahu**](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)" at **July 16, 2025** against an Israel filter, and a fourth pairs "**33rd parallel north**" with "**Justin Streiff**." The captures also include deliberate controls — a "**Dairy Queen Grill and Chill**" panel for a fast-food restaurant in Orem, Utah, and a "**helldivers**" video-game panel — which the poster appears to include to show what ordinary background interest looks like next to the terms being questioned. Nothing in a Trends panel identifies a searcher; the index is relative within its own bucket, so a spike to 100 can rest on a very small number of queries.

Read these captures against the [July 20, 2025 anchor](/GoogleSearches/july-20-2025-anchor) page and the [DC metro search patterns](/GoogleSearches/dc-metro-search-patterns) analysis, which is where the District of Columbia bucket is examined term by term. The [Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns) page covers the Israel-filtered panels. When a panel names a person, the profile pages under [People](/People/overview) give the background, and the medical and hospital terms are worked through on [Timpanogos and medical searches](/GoogleSearches/timpanogos-medical-searches).
