## What This Image Shows

The final panel of the compiled research table, listing dated queries in three groups with the compiler's interpretation beside them.

Firearms: "vintage Mauser" DC **8/5**, "Mauser" DC **7/20**, "Mauser 98" DC **8/27**, "30-06" DC **8/5**, "mauser 98 velocity" DC **8/6** and **9/2**, "mauser 98 bullets" DC **8/28** — annotated "exact antique rifle model, caliber, and ballistics suddenly searched in perfect sequence". Symbol: "bella ciao" DC **9/2**, noted as rising from 75 to 100 after the event and annotated "possible left-wing or operational signal", with "sharp spike the week of execution". Family and background: "matt robinson" DC **7/20**, "amber robinson" DC **8/5**, "tyler robinson" DC **8/20** and **8/24**, "tyler robinson facebook" DC **8/20**, "tyler robinson high school" DC **8/10**, and "Pine View" — annotated "shooter's father, relatives, social media, and high school" and "full patsy background built months early".

The phrases in quotation marks are the compiler's, reported with attribution and not adopted here; the word "patsy" in particular is his framing of a contested theory, not a finding of this site. What the table usefully documents is the claimed sequence — weapon model, then caliber, then ballistics, then the accused's family and school — as a progression rather than a scatter. It remains a hand-transcribed secondary document that no search chart can corroborate on its own, and it establishes nothing about who ran any query.
