## What This Image Shows

Five **Google Trends** panels in one capture, all filtered to the **District of Columbia**, **Past 90 days**, axes running July 11 to October 3. The terms are "**33rd parallel north**" (classified by Google as a Topic), "**Justin Streiff**" (also a Topic), "**deirdre amaro**", "**Harrison Smith**", and "**bella ciao**".

Two of these carry meaning only inside the case. **Deirdre Amaro** is the medical examiner associated with the death certificate; her name had no public connection to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) before [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). "**bella ciao**" is the Italian anti-fascist song that appears in this investigation because of the markings reported on ammunition — a term the compiler elsewhere dates to a spike on September 2, 2025. "**33rd parallel north**" is a latitude line that recurs in conspiracy literature generally and has no established bearing on this case.

That mix is the honest picture of this material: two case-specific terms, one that is symbolically loaded, and one that proves nothing. No tooltips are visible, so no dates can be read from this capture. Trends identifies no searcher, and no wrongdoing by any named person is asserted.
