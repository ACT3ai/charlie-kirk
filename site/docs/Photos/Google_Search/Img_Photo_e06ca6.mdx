## What This Image Shows

The image is a digital collage arranged in a 4x4 grid of sixteen screenshots from a search engine analytics tool (resembling Google Trends). Each panel displays a trend graph for a specific search term, tracking "Interest over time." Almost all of these graphs depict a flatlined search history at zero, punctuated by a single, highly anomalous spike reaching a value of 100 during a specific date range in mid-2025.
