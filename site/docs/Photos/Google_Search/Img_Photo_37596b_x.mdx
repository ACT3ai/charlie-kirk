## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of the **Wayback Machine** displaying an archived page from utcourts.gov. The archive bar reads "**7 captures** / 3 Oct 2023 - 5 Aug 2025", with the calendar strip showing 2023, 2025, and 2026. The archived page itself is the **Utah State Courts** site — "The Judicial Branch of Utah" — on the path District Courts, Fourth District Court, and the heading **JUDGE ROBERT C. LUNNEN**.

The archive metadata is the substance of this capture. Seven snapshots is a thin record, and the final one falls on **5 August 2025** — four days after Judge Lunnen's retirement took effect on August 1 and one day after his successor was sworn in. Researchers preserve the archive view because an official court biography page is normally taken down when a judge leaves the bench, and a Wayback capture is then the only remaining public copy.

That is the practical value here rather than any claim of wrongdoing: it establishes that the page existed, what it said, and when it was last captured.
