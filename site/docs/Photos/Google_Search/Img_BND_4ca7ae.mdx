## What This Image Shows

This is a **Google Trends** screen for the quoted search term "SU BND", filtered to the **Huntsville-Decatur** media market over the **Past 90 days**. The chart is flat at zero across the whole window except for one vertical spike to the maximum index of 100, and the tooltip labels it "Sep 12, 2025".

**SU-BND** is the registration of an Egyptian aircraft tracked in this investigation. September 12, 2025 is two days after the shooting at Utah Valley University, so the spike sits immediately after the event rather than before it — this capture documents post-event interest, not foreknowledge. What researchers find worth noting is the location: Huntsville-Decatur, Alabama is a defense and aerospace corridor, and a six-character foreign tail number is not a string that ordinary web traffic produces by accident.
