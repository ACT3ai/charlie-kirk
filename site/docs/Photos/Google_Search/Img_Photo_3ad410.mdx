## What This Image Shows

A desktop **Google Trends** capture for the search term "Candace owens", filtered to **Israel**, **Past 90 days**, All categories, Web Search. The axis runs from roughly **July 14** through **September 30**, so the window straddles September 10, 2025.

**Candace Owens** is central to this investigation for reasons that have nothing to do with search data: she has said publicly that she was told "it was supposed to be you," she pursued her own reporting on the case, and she is the subject of separate threads on this site involving foreign travel and reported threats. A ninety-day Israel-bucket view of her name is therefore expected to move sharply after September 10 simply because the story broke — the question researchers put to a chart like this is whether anything happens on the left side of the window, before the event.

Because this capture does not carry a tooltip fixing the peak date, it should be read as context rather than as a finding. Her profile is at [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), and the surrounding threads are at [UVU, Utah, France, Israel and Candace](/Israel/overview) and [Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns). Trends measures relative interest inside one bucket and identifies no one.
