## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a ChatGPT response summarizing the judicial calendar. The answer states that **Robert C. Lunnen** of Utah's Fourth District Court had a retirement "listed as effective **August 1, 2025**"; that **Tony F. Graf, Jr.** was "appointed **May 2, 2025**" with a swearing-in date "reported as **August 4, 2025**"; and that, "given the swearing-in August 4, 2025 and a court appearance on **September 16, 2025**, it's reasonable to assume his active service began in early August 2025." Small source chips beside each line read "Judicial Vacancy", "Utah State Bar", and "WEAR".

This capture is a working note rather than evidence. It shows a researcher using an AI assistant to assemble the date sequence quickly, and the assistant's own answer flags that the last step is an inference, not a record. Anything drawn from it needs to be checked against the primary documents, which this cluster also holds.
