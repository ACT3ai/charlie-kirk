## What This Image Shows

Two **Google Trends** panels stacked in one image, both running the quoted term "Jared Fleming" across the custom range **3/1/25 - 9/9/25**. The upper panel is filtered to the **Tel Aviv District** and carries tooltips at **June 16, 2025** (reading 74) and **September 2, 2025** (reading 100). The lower panel is filtered to the **Cayman Islands** with tooltips at **May 22, 2025** and **August 24, 2025** (reading 87).

The choice of buckets is the argument. Tel Aviv District and the Cayman Islands are two of the smallest geographies Trends will report, and the range ends **September 9** — the day before the shooting — so every reading is pre-event by construction. A quoted full name registering repeatedly in two such buckets is the anomaly the poster is pointing at.

The limits are equally clear and cut hard here. Very small geographies produce very noisy indices, a handful of queries can reach 100, and a common personal name may belong to someone else entirely. **Jared Fleming** publicly disputed the framing when these charts were posted; that exchange is captured at [Sam Parker and Jared Fleming Trade Posts on X](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_b56f73), and his X profile is at [Jared Fleming X Profile](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_5d5539). No wrongdoing by Mr. Fleming is established or alleged. See [Israel IP search patterns](/GoogleSearches/israel-ip-search-patterns).
