## What This Image Shows

A second mobile **Google Trends** capture of the search term "Hunter kozak" filtered to **Israel**, **Past 90 days**, taken minutes apart from its companion — the phone clock reads 5:17 here against 5:21 on the other. The axis spans July 11 to October 3 and an "Interest by subregion" panel sits below the graph.

Duplicate captures like this one are worth keeping rather than pruning. Trends is a live service that recomputes its index as new data arrives, and pages have been reported to change or disappear after they go viral. Two captures of the same query minutes apart establish what the tool actually returned at a specific moment, which is the only defense against a series that later reads differently.

**Hunter Kozak** was mid-question with Charlie Kirk when the shot was fired at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025. His profile is at [Hunter Kozak](/distraction_people/Hunter_Kozak).
