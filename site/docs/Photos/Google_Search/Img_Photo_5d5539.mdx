## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of the X profile for **JARED FLEMING**, handle **@JaredFlemingAZ**. The bio reads: "Christian Husband Dad x4 | @ericmetaxas | Frmr Pastor | **Frmr @TPUSA Director** | MAHA | 'Speak softly and carry a big stick' — Teddy Roosevelt". The profile lists the location **Goodyear, AZ**, the link LettertotheAmericanChurch.com, and "Joined February 2018", with 5,015 following and 3,517 followers.

This is the identification card for the Google Trends captures elsewhere in this cluster. It is included because the search-pattern claim only means anything if the name being charted belongs to a specific person with a specific connection to the case — here, a self-declared former director at **Turning Point USA**, the organization Charlie Kirk founded and was speaking for at Utah Valley University.

Publishing a public profile establishes association and nothing more. Mr. Fleming publicly rejected the framing when the charts were posted, replying "You're a clown dude. Shame on you." No wrongdoing by him is established or alleged on this site, and a Google Trends chart cannot show who searched a name or why.
