## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** comparison of the search terms "lance twigs" and "tyler robinson", filtered to **Israel** across the custom range **1/1/25 to 9/5/25**, All categories, Web Search. The window is drawn deliberately: it stops on September 5, five days before the shooting at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview), so nothing on the chart can be explained by post-event news interest.

[**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) is the man charged in the case and [**Lance Twiggs**](/People/lance-twiggs) is his former roommate. Neither name was publicly connected to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) before [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), which is the entire basis for treating pre-event search interest in them as an anomaly worth documenting. The counterargument is that "lance twigs" is a misspelling and both are common personal names, so the series may be picking up unrelated people entirely.

Companion captures comparing the same two names in the **Washington D.C.** bucket, where the Twiggs series reads 100 against Robinson at 0 on June 15, 2025, are at [Lance Twiggs Searched in DC While Robinson Sits at Zero](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_bd1ac0) and [second capture](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_bf3535). See [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs), [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), and the written analysis at [Tyler and Lance Twiggs searches](/GoogleSearches/tyler-robinson-lance-twiggs-searches).
