## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of the Utah State Bar press release dated **Friday, May 2, 2025**: "SALT LAKE CITY (May 2, 2025) - Utah Gov. Spencer J. Cox has appointed **Tony F. Graf** to the Fourth District Court, filling a vacancy left by the retirement of **Judge Robert Lunnen**. Judicial appointments are subject to confirmation by the Utah Senate."

The release quotes Governor **Spencer Cox**: "Tony Graf brings a steady hand, strong ethics, and the kind of judgment we count on in Utah's judiciary. From prosecuting difficult cases to training new attorneys — his experience reflects a commitment to justice and public service." It records that Graf was then a Deputy County Attorney for Davis County prosecuting physical and sexual abuse cases, had previously led the Special Victims Unit for the Utah County Attorney's Office, and had served as a Deputy District Attorney in Salt Lake County, an Assistant Attorney General in American Samoa, a Deputy District Attorney in Lincoln County, Nevada, and a Special Assistant United States Attorney in Washington, D.C. The capture is watermarked with the X handles that circulated it.
