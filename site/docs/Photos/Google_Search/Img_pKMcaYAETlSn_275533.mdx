## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** comparison running four quoted search terms at once — "Utah License Plate Z923DA", "tail number N560tw", "Utah License Plate T092ZS", and an Arizona plate number — filtered to **Israel** across the custom range **4/1/25 to 9/10/25**. The tooltip is parked on **August 12, 2025**, where the four series read **0**, **63**, **0**, and **0** respectively, with only the tail-number term registering.

Quoted-string searches on license plates and tail numbers are the strongest form of this test. Nobody types an exact seven-character plate or a five-character aircraft registration casually; these strings have effectively no organic search demand, so any reading above zero is a real query rather than noise. The custom end date of September 10, 2025 is chosen so that everything on the chart is pre-event by construction.

**N560TW** is one of the aircraft tracked in this investigation; see [N560TW](/Planes/N560TW).
