## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture for the search term "Spencer Cox", filtered to **Israel**, **Past 90 days**, All categories, and the **News Search** property rather than Web Search. The axis runs July 20 to October 6 and the tooltip marks the peak at **August 22, 2025** with a reading of **100**.

The News Search filter narrows the measurement to queries run against Google News, which is a much smaller pool than web search and therefore both easier to move and more specific in what it captures — someone looking for coverage of a person rather than for the person. **Spencer Cox** is the Governor of Utah; he appointed Judge Tony Graf in May 2025, appointed a new Department of Public Safety commissioner in July 2025, and became the public face of the state's response after September 10.

August 22, 2025 falls about two and a half weeks before the event. As a sitting governor of a U.S. state he is a routine subject of foreign news interest, which is the ordinary explanation, and nothing here suggests he did anything wrong.
