## What This Image Shows

The same twelve-month **Google Trends** series for "Jax Donovan" filtered to **Israel**, captured with the tooltip open. It reads **December 1-7, 2024** at a value of **82**. The axis spans October 20, 2024 to September 21, 2025, and the line is flat at zero from early 2025 onward.

The name comes from the disputed crew list for **N1098L**, the Bombardier Global 6500 that dropped to roughly 203 feet near the Utah Valley University campus on the morning of September 10, 2025 and repeated the maneuver about twenty-three minutes after the shooting. Crew names for private ISR flights are not published by the FAA, the operator, or any flight-tracking service, so the lists circulating on X are unverified and mutually contradictory. **J. "Jax" E. Donovan** appears on one of the two competing lists as second pilot.

Putting a date on the earlier capture's unlabeled spikes is the whole purpose of this screenshot, and the date does not help the pre-event argument: the first week of **December 2024** is more than nine months before September 10, 2025, and nothing registers in the summer or after the event. Researchers who cite it point to the same December window that recurs elsewhere in this thread — the [Kathryn Nester](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches) claim also lands in early December 2024 — but coincidence of week across unrelated low-volume terms is weak ground.
