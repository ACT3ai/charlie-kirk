## What This Image Shows

The twelve-month **Google Trends** series for "Michael Harlan" in the **Washington DC (Hagerstown)** bucket, captured with the tooltip on the **earlier** of the two spikes: **December 1-7, 2024**, value **100**. The axis runs October 20, 2024 to September 21, 2025.

The name comes from the disputed crew list for [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview), the Bombardier Global 6500 that dropped to roughly 203 feet near the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus on the morning of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) and repeated the maneuver about twenty-three minutes after the shooting. Crew names for private ISR flights are not published by the FAA, the operator, or any flight-tracking service, so the lists circulating on X are unverified and mutually contradictory.

Dating the first spike is what this capture contributes, and the date lands in a week that recurs across this whole investigation's search thread. The first week of **December 2024** is also where researchers place the claimed surge for defense attorney [Kathryn Nester](/GoogleSearches/kathryn-nester-searches), and the sibling capture for "Jax Donovan" in the Israel bucket peaks in that same week. Three unrelated names cresting in one week nine months before the event is either a meaningful cluster or an artifact of how a small number of researchers selected and cropped their windows.

Nothing here resolves that question. Trends identifies no searcher, no volume, and no purpose, and no person is accused of anything.
