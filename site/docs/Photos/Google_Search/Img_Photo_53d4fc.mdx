## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** Explore page for the search term "Kathryn Nester", filtered to **Israel**, **Past 12 months**, All categories, Web Search. The interest-over-time chart is flat and then carries a single sharp spike to the maximum index of 100 before dropping back to zero. The axis is marked at October 13, 2024 and February 2, 2025.

**Kathryn Nester** is the defense attorney in the case against Tyler Robinson. This is the capture that anchors one of the most-cited claims in the whole search thread: researchers report a one-week Israel-bucket surge for her name in **December 8-14, 2024** — roughly nine months before the shooting and long before any defense appointment could have been anticipated. The commentary around it notes that she was proposed through the prosecution's process and approved by the court, which the poster treats as an additional reason to ask about the timing.

The caution is standard and applies with force.
