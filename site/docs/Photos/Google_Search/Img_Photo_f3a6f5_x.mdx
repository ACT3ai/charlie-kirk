## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** comparison running "Tony graf" against "Robert lunnen", filtered to **Israel** over the custom range **5/1/25 - 10/19/25**, All categories, Web Search. The axis is marked at May 1, July 12, and September 22, and the capture carries the watermark of the X account that circulated it.

This is the single-geography version of the stacked Israel-versus-Utah capture in the same cluster. Isolating one bucket makes the shape of the series readable without the visual distortion that comes from comparing two panels drawn at different scales. **Tony Graf** was appointed to Utah's [Fourth District Court](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo) on May 2, 2025 and sworn in August 4; **Robert Lunnen** retired effective August 1. The range starts the day before the appointment, so the whole chart covers the transition period.

What the researcher is pointing at is presence rather than shape: measurable interest in two Utah county-level judges inside an Israel bucket at all. What the chart cannot establish is who searched, how many did, or whether the terms are catching other people with the same names. See [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf), [Judge Robert Lunnen](/court/judge-robert-lunnen), the stacked version at [Israel Versus Utah](/Photos/Google_Search/Img_Photo_db4f1a_x), and the written analysis at [judges Graf and Lunnen searches](/GoogleSearches/judges-graf-lunnen-searches).
