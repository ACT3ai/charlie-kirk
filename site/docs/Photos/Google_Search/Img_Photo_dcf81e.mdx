## What This Image Shows

This **Google Trends** capture runs "ZACHARIAH QURESHI" against **Israel**, **Past 12 months**, on **Web Search**. Unlike most charts in this cluster it shows two peaks rather than one: the tooltip marks **December 15-21, 2024** at an index of **86**, and a second peak at **August 3-9, 2025** at the maximum of **100**. The axis spans October 13, 2024 to September 14, 2025. The capture carries a "THE FOUNDERS' PRESS" watermark.

A two-peak pattern is harder to dismiss as a one-off artifact than a single needle, which is why researchers highlight it. The August 3-9, 2025 window sits roughly five weeks before September 10; the December 2024 peak sits about nine months before. Neither reading identifies a searcher, establishes intent, or implies that Mr. Qureshi did anything wrong.
