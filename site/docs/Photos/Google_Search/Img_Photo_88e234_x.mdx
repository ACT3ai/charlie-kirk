## What This Image Shows

The same **Google Trends** comparison of "Tony graf" against "Robert lunnen" filtered to **Utah** over **1/1/25 - 10/19/25**, but with the tooltip moved forward to the week of **October 12-18, 2025**, where the reading is **10**. The capture is watermarked with the X handles that circulated it.

Placed next to its companion — the same chart with the tooltip on September 21-27, 2025, reading 100 — this screenshot documents the decay. Utah interest in the two judges peaked when the case assignment became news and had fallen to a tenth of that within three weeks. That is the ordinary life cycle of a local news story.

Preserving the trough alongside the peak is what makes the comparison honest. A reader can see the full arc of a normal series and judge for themselves whether the foreign-bucket panels for the same names behave differently.
