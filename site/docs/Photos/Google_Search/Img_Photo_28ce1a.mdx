## What This Image Shows

The last capture in the four-term plate-and-tail-number sequence. The **Google Trends** comparison runs "Utah License Plate Z923DA", "tail number N560tw", "Utah License Plate T092ZS", and "Arizona License Plate number CWN9872" against **Israel** across the custom range **4/1/25 - 9/10/25**. The tooltip sits on **September 7, 2025**, where the first three series read **0**, **0**, and **0** and the **Arizona plate** series reads **100**.

The handoff is the story. Across the sequence the tail-number series carries the readings through mid-August and then goes to zero, while the Arizona plate — whose full string, CWN9872, is visible in this capture — takes the maximum on **September 7**, three days before the shooting. A researcher reads that as attention moving from an aircraft to a vehicle in the final week.

The same structural caution applies as to every panel in the set: a normalized comparison pins a series at zero relative to the group maximum without telling you its absolute volume, and an exact seven-character plate string has effectively no organic demand, so the index is easy to move.
