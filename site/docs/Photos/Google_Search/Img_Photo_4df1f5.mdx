## What This Image Shows

This image is a clean, direct digital screenshot of the Google Trends web interface displaying comparative search volume data over time. The graph tracks four specific, highly niche search queries containing vehicle license plates and an aircraft tail number over a period from April 1, 2025, to September 10, 2025, specifically filtered for searches originating in Israel. The visualization displays a flat baseline of zero interest for most of the period, punctuated by massive, isolated spikes in search activity in mid-to-late August and early September 2025.
