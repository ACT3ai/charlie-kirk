## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a WEAR News article about **Judge Tony Graf** and his appointment to Utah's [**Fourth District Court**](/Locations/Fourth_District_Court_Provo). The article quotes Governor [**Spencer Cox**](/People/spencer-cox), who praised Graf as someone who brings "a steady hand, strong ethics, and the kind of judgment we count on in Utah's judiciary." It goes on: "Still, no one expected his first major assignment would come so quickly after he was sworn in on Aug. 4, 2025." A source named Evershed is quoted saying "It's not something he asked for... It's not something anyone could have anticipated."

The piece also summarizes Graf's background — prosecuting sex abuse cases in Davis County, leading the Special Victims Unit for the Utah County Attorney's Office, serving as an assistant attorney general in American Samoa, and working as a special assistant U.S. attorney in Washington, D.C.
