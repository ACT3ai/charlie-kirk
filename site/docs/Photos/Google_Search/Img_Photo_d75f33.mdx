## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture for the search term "tyler robinson", filtered to **Israel**, **Past 90 days**, All categories, Web Search. The axis runs July 12 to September 28 and the tooltip marks **September 7, 2025** with a reading of **7**.

The low number is what makes this capture useful. Seven, on a scale where 100 is the busiest point in the window, means the term barely registered on that date — and the busiest point in a ninety-day window straddling [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) will inevitably be the days after the shooting, when the name became global news. So this chart shows a small pre-event reading dwarfed by the post-event surge, which is exactly what an unremarkable series should look like.

Including it matters. A cluster that only preserved dramatic spikes would be selecting its own evidence; keeping the modest readings is what lets a reader judge the rest. **September 7, 2025** is three days before the event.
