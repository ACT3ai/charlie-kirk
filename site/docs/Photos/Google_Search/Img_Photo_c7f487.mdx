## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of the Google Trends "Explore" web page showing a search comparison between four specific terms related to vehicle license plates and an aircraft tail number. The search parameters are set to the country of Israel for a future date range spanning from April 1, 2025, to September 10, 2025. Below the search terms, a line graph displays "Interest over time," showing flatlined activity for most of the period punctuated by sharp spikes in late August and early September.
