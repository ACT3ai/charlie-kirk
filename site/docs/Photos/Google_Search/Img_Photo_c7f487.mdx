## What This Image Shows

The same four-term **Google Trends** comparison as its companions — "Utah License Plate Z923DA", "tail number N560tw", "Utah License Plate T092ZS", and an Arizona plate number — filtered to **Israel** over the custom range **4/1/25 to 9/10/25**. Here the tooltip sits on **August 16, 2025**, where the tail-number series reads **91** and the plate series read **0**.

Read across the set, these captures trace one series moving through August while the others stay flat. That internal contrast is what gives the sequence whatever weight it has: the same chart, the same bucket, the same window, with only one of four quoted strings registering. It also exposes the limit — a comparison chart scales every series to the single highest reading across all of them, so a term that never moves is pinned at zero by construction and tells you nothing about its own absolute volume.
