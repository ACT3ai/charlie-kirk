## What This Image Shows

The axis runs October 27, 2024 to September 28, 2025. This name belongs to the **second** of the two competing crew lists for [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview). Where one account names Michael Harlan as pilot in command with Jax Donovan and [Elena Vasquez](/People/elena-vasquez), the rival account — advanced by a commentator who disputes that the aircraft was even configured to launch drones — names **Cooper Brown** as pilot in command, **Michael Reynolds** as co-pilot, and **Sarah Kline** as relief and systems operator. Neither list has been confirmed by the operator, the FAA, or any flight-tracking service.

That the two lists contradict each other is the honest starting point for every name in this cluster. Running them through Google Trends cannot resolve which, if either, is accurate — it can only show whether a string registered somewhere, and "Michael Reynolds" is a common enough name that a D.C.-bucket spike in late **November 2024** may have nothing to do with any aircraft.
