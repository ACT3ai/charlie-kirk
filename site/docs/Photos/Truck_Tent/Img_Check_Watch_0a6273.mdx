## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide, slightly low-angle frame from inside a dense crowd. Most of the foreground is the backs and profiles of onlookers facing a black vehicle in the midground with its trunk standing open, several figures around it who appear to be security personnel or event organizers. The concrete overhang runs across the top of the frame and the campus water feature with active fountains sits behind. Fragments of **PROVE ME WRONG** and the **YREFY** sponsor banners are legible.

The open trunk again. Across this cluster the same configuration recurs - a dark vehicle, tailgate up, staff around it, crowd pressed in close - and it is the configuration the disputed rear-hatch timing claims are built on.

Read what the photograph settles and what it does not. It settles that a trunk was open, that the vehicle was reachable from the public side, and roughly where it sat relative to the water feature and the overhang. It settles nothing about timing.
