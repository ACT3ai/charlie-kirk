## What This Image Shows

A blurry vertical capture from a mobile video taken under the concrete underpass. The focal subject is a muscular man in a black baseball cap and dark short-sleeved shirt holding a large professional camera fitted with a long white telephoto lens. People are packed around him.

The caption fragment and watermark place it in the same **In the tunnel right after!** clip as the rest of the tunnel frames in this cluster.

A long white telephoto on a body of that size is a stills or broadcast rig, not a phone. It records who among the press pool was in the covered area at the relevant moment and what focal length they were shooting - both of which bear on the recurring question of what professional footage exists from behind the tent and where it went.
