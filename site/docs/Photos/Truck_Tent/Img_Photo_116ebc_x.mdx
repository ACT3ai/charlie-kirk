## What This Image Shows

A cropped, low-resolution frame of a man caught mid-movement, head tilted sharply up and to his left, standing against a white step-and-repeat backdrop covered in sponsor logos. Legible fragments of the backdrop include **YREFY.COM** and **NT USA**, the Yrefy and Turning Point USA branding used on the tour.

Two hand-drawn red arrows have been added over his white shirt, both pointing toward the collar. The arrows are the researcher's annotation, not part of the original photograph. They point at the area where a lapel or collar-mounted microphone and its transmitter would sit, which is the subject of a separate and heavily disputed line of analysis.

The claims built on collar-area detail are covered on their own pages rather than here, and none of them has been validated by any published forensic examination. See [Mic](/Mic/overview) for the microphone and transmitter thread and [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview) for the competing accounts of the wound. This frame is catalogued as an annotated researcher exhibit, not as a finding.
