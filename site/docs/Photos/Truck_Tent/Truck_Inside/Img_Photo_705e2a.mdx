## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution screenshot of a zoomed video frame: the profile of a young man in a two-toned baseball cap and glasses, facing left, in an outdoor crowd. Video player interface elements, graphics and text intrude at the edges of the frame.

The heavy digital zoom is the tell. A researcher has magnified a small region of an ordinary crowd video, which is why the pixels are visible and the edges are soft.
