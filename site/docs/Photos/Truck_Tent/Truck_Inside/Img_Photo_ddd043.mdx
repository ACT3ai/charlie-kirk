## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution, eye-level frame of four men standing in the concrete underpass. On the right a man in a white shirt gestures with his arm raised toward a large yellow pointing emoji that the poster has added over another man behind him. The men on the left, one of them a police officer, stand watching. A faint watermark runs across the whole frame.

The emoji is the annotation and the annotation is the claim. Somebody wanted viewers to look at one particular man in the passage, and nothing in the frame itself explains why.

Nobody in this image is identified here, and no authority has connected any person in it to anything. What it records is that a uniformed officer and several other men were standing together in the covered passage - which is the kind of presence detail this sub-cluster exists to document.
