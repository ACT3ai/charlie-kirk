## What This Image Shows

A blurry vertical screenshot, itself photographed off a screen, showing a young man partly obscured by a white vertical element at the left as he walks or stands. He wears a dark graphic t-shirt, a baseball cap and glasses, and is looking toward the camera.

The second of the three same-figure frames. Of the three, this is the one where the face is most nearly visible, which is why it recurs in the identification composites elsewhere in this cluster.

It still will not carry an identification. A cap, glasses and a dark shirt at this resolution describe a very large number of people, and the site names nobody on the strength of such a frame.
