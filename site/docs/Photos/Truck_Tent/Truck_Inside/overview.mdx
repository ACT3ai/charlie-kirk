## About This Cluster

This is the most contested sub-cluster behind the tent. It collects frames of a person researchers say was seated inside or standing immediately beside a vehicle in the service tunnel behind the stage at **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)** on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** - the same vehicle that threads by **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** and others claim a rear hatch opened on roughly four seconds before the shot and closed immediately after. Because the official account places the shot on the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** roof, any figure inside a vehicle only feet behind **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** becomes a competing line of inquiry that researchers argue was never publicly resolved.

Several of these frames are attempts to put a name on that figure, and they need reading with care. One image is a phone screenshot of a **familytreenow.com** search for the surname **Sipes**; another is a three-panel composite pairing that search with a highlighted result reading **Taylor M Sipes, Age 33, Born Jan 1993** and a video still of a man in a baseball cap beside a vehicle, with emoji arrows added by the poster. The master investigation file logs at least three competing names floated for the same figure and marks all of them as possibilities rather than identifications. No authority has identified this person, no court has adjudicated anything about him, and the researchers circulating the composites have themselves posted disclaimers asking people not to contact or harass anyone shown. This site names no one here as a suspect.

The frames with real evidentiary value are the plain ones - the blurry side-on stills where a figure is visible through a door frame, and the cropped shot of someone seated in a vehicle with light reflecting off glasses. Those establish presence and rough position, which is all a photograph can establish. For the claimed laser and audio work that made this vehicle a focus, see [MonoPod Camera](/Photos/Truck_Tent/MonoPod_Camera/overview) and the write-ups in [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories); for the surrounding people, see [Assistant](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Assistant/overview) and [Truck In Charge](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Truck_In_Charge/overview).
