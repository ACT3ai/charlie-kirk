## What This Image Shows

A heavily cropped, low-resolution frame of a person sitting inside a vehicle at the left of the shot, partly hidden by the vehicle body. The face is in deep shadow; light reflects off what appear to be glasses. Beside them the white rear pillar and taillight of a vehicle are visible, with another dark vehicle or structure behind.

This is the frame the whole sub-cluster is named for. Researchers argue that someone was seated inside a vehicle in the covered passage behind the stage, and this is the image offered in support.

What it supports is limited and should be stated exactly: a person was inside a vehicle, in shadow, near the rear pillar. The crop is severe, the resolution is poor, and the frame carries no timestamp of its own. It cannot show who the person was or when they were there. The claims built around the vehicle - the rear hatch timing, the audio triangulation, the claimed laser reflection - are separate arguments and are unproven.
