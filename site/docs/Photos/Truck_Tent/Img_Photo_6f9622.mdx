## What This Image Shows

A horizontal capture from a video of the crowded event area. The rear of a black luxury vehicle sits behind metal crowd-control barricades in the midground while people in the foreground raise hands and phones. Pink handwritten annotations have been added: the words **Right veh** and a circle drawn around the licence plate. A video timecode reads **16:19 / 20:11**.

This is a vehicle identification frame. The annotator is asserting that this is the vehicle their analysis concerns and is directing attention to the plate so that others can attempt to trace it. Whether the plate was ever traced, and by whom, is not established by the image.

Vehicle identity is one of the genuinely answerable questions in this section. Registration, ownership and the assignment of each vehicle in the tunnel that day are matters of record that have not been publicly disclosed - exactly the category the proposed [disclosure laws](/Fix/overview) are written to force out.
