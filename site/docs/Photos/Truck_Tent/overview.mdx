## About This Cluster

The strip of ground directly behind the stage tent at **Utah Valley University** — a service tunnel under the Sorensen Center skybridge where black SUVs, a **GMC Denali**, and event vehicles sat only feet from **Charlie Kirk's** back on **September 10, 2025** — is one of the most contested locations in the case. The official account places the shooter on the Losee Center roof roughly 142 yards away, but a persistent body of citizen analysis argues this area deserves equal scrutiny. The images here document those vehicles, the people working around them, and the choreography behind Charlie in the minutes surrounding the shot.

Researchers including **@ProjectConstitu** and **@stevendenoon** claim that an infrared laser which flashed across **Dr. Frank Turek's** phone screen at the instant of the shot traces back to a vehicle parked behind and to the right of Charlie's position, and audio analysis circulated by **Jon Bray** describes a 4940Hz impact signature triangulated toward the same vehicle using six separate camera recordings. Footage cited in these threads reportedly shows a rear hatch opening about four seconds before the shot and closing immediately after, a shell casing lodged where the tent's rear vinyl panel meets the canopy, and a monopod-mounted camera behind Charlie that was taken down before the event ended. All of these are reported claims under active dispute, not established findings, and no person shown in these images has been accused of any crime by authorities.

Start with [Back Hatch](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Img_Back_Hatch_0de48d) and the Denali plate frames, then work through the sub-clusters: [Truck Inside](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Truck_Inside/overview) for the disputed figure seen inside a vehicle, [MonoPod Camera](/Photos/Truck_Tent/MonoPod_Camera/overview) for the CornerShot comparison graphic, and [Platform Water Bottles Under Charlie's Feet](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Tent_Water_Bottles/overview) for the platform detail. The written analysis behind these images lives in the [Tent](/Tent/overview) section, especially [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories) and [Tent Structure Under Charlie](/Tent/Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie).
