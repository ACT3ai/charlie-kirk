## What This Image Shows

A blurry, sunlit frame of the roadway under the overpass. Several vehicles - SUVs and pickup trucks - are parked or idling in a congested layout, with people standing and walking around them. Metal barricades run across the foreground.

There is no annotation on this one, which is part of its value. It is a plain record of how many vehicles were staged in the covered strip and how tightly they were packed together, without an analyst's arrow telling the viewer what to conclude.

The count and arrangement of vehicles is the baseline every vehicle-related claim in this section has to be measured against.
