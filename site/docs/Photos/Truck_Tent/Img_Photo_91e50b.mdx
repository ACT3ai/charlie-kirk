## What This Image Shows

A blurry action frame from a chaotic moment. Several people stand inside a cordoned area behind metal crowd-control barricades. In the foreground a woman wearing **PRESS** credentials looks back over her shoulder with a tense expression. Several people in the background wear red shirts and caps.

Credentialing is a small detail that carries real weight. Press badges determine who was allowed inside the barrier line, and by extension whose cameras had an unobstructed angle on the tent, the vehicles and the ground behind them.

The recurring question across this section is what the credentialed cameras behind the barricade recorded and whether any of it was ever collected or released. That is a records question rather than a theory. See [Media](/Media/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).
