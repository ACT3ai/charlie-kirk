## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide shot of the packed courtyard. A large crowd is seen mostly from behind, looking down toward the white **The American Comeback Tour** canopy on the lower platform, with the university building's large promotional window graphics rising directly behind it. Many attendees hold phones up to record. Legible signage includes **A PLACE FOR YOU** and **UVU**, and a clock overlay reads **12:07**.

The visible time matters. It places the frame within minutes of the shooting on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**, which makes it one of the establishing views of the site as it stood immediately beforehand.

Two things are legible here that arguments depend on: the elevation difference between the upper terraces and the tent platform, and the sheer number of phones pointed at the stage from every level. Both bear on how much footage of the moment exists and from how many angles. See [UVU](/UVU/overview), [Campus and University](/campus_university/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
