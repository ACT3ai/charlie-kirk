## What This Image Shows

A blurry outdoor frame centred on a white pop-up canopy. Several people stand under and around it - some appearing to be organisers, security or participants - with a table in the foreground holding assorted items. The banner across the front of the canopy reads **PROVE ME WRONG**. Large semi-transparent yellow watermarks for the **greatest1command** analysis account run across the middle of the image.

The file name refers to a person in a white shirt with a logo on it, which is what the analyst who saved it was tracking. The frame itself does not single anyone out, and no person in it is identified here.

What it does record is the working layout under the canopy: the table, the merchandise, the staff positions and the banner line. Those fixtures are the reference points for locating anything else claimed to have happened under the tent. See [Tent Structure Under Charlie](/Tent/Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie).
