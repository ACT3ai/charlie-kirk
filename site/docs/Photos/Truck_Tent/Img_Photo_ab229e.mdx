## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide shot taken from above the covered area. A dense crowd fills the foreground, many with hands raised or phones held up, all looking toward the centre. Black SUVs are parked toward the waterfront and fountain in the background, and a promotional canopy stands at the right. Sponsor lettering including **YREFY.COM** and **AMERICAN** is legible across the banners.

The elevated angle is the useful part. It shows the relationship between the crowd, the vehicles and the canopy in one frame, which frames taken from inside the crowd cannot do.

This is one of the reference views researchers work back to when they need to check where a ground-level frame was shot from.
