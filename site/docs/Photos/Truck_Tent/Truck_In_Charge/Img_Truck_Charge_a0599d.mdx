## What This Image Shows

An eye-level medium shot on light-grey asphalt. The same man - dark hair, sunglasses, navy graphic shirt with white lettering and a lightning bolt, faded jeans, black watch, white keycard on the belt loop - stands between two sheriff's deputies in matte-black tactical helmets and olive-drab and tan uniforms. Behind them, blurred, a line of police vehicles with emergency lights active runs down the road. Legible lettering includes **AMERICAN COMEBACK** and **SHERIFF**.

The setting is not the courtyard. Tactical helmets, a road, and a line of marked vehicles with lights running indicate a police operation somewhere else, which places this frame at a different time and location from the event photographs.

Stating that plainly matters, because a frame that looks dramatic is easily read as showing more than it does. What it shows is a man in tour merchandise standing among deputies during a police deployment. It does not show what the deployment was, when it happened, or what his role in it was, and it accuses nobody of anything. See [Truck In Charge](/Photos/Truck_Tent/Truck_In_Charge/overview).
