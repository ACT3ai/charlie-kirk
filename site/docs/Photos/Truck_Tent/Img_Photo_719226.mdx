## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical screenshot of people moving through the concrete tunnel. Several individuals are clustered tightly in the foreground, one in a red cap; a cameraman with a professional video rig is visible at the left, and others look around with hurried expressions under the flat concrete ceiling.

Same clip, same **In the tunnel right after!** caption and **@rynestimetwo** watermark as the other tunnel frames in this cluster.

Read as a group rather than singly, these frames document the evacuation path out of the event area and who took it. That matters because the covered passage was both the vehicle staging area and the exit route, so anyone leaving through it passed the vehicles that later became the subject of dispute.
