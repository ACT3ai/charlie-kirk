## What This Image Shows

A vertical frame of the crowded gathering, layered front to back. Several blurred heads and shoulders fill the extreme foreground; behind them stand people seen from the back - one in a navy t-shirt with a light horizontal stripe, one in light grey, one in a dark shirt with a dark backpack - near media equipment. Beyond them a dense, multi-layered crowd stands at the metal barricade. Watermarks read **BYU** and **@caseypbryson**.

Same clip and same vantage as its companion frame. This one is wider and shows the arrangement rather than any individual.

Its value is that it shows the layering: public crowd, then a working strip with media equipment, then the barrier. Establishing who was permitted into that middle strip, and what their equipment recorded, is a documentary question rather than a speculative one.
