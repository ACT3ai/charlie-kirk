## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical video screenshot of the crowded event, shot from within the audience. Blurred heads fill the foreground. In the midground, near the metal crowd-control barricades, personnel dressed in black stand among the barriers; one man in a black t-shirt, dark jeans, a black baseball cap and a large black backpack faces to the left. The TikTok watermark reads **@caseypbryson**.

The man with the backpack is what the researcher saved this frame for. Descriptions logged in the investigation file - from a construction worker who reportedly encountered a man in black immediately afterward, and from police dispatch traffic that day - both describe a person in dark clothing carrying a bag.

That is a clothing resemblance drawn from a blurred phone video, and it identifies nobody. Black shirts, caps and backpacks were common in that crowd, and no authority has connected any person in this frame to the shooting.
