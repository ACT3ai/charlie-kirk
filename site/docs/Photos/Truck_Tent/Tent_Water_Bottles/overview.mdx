## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster holds the three images behind the [Platform Water Bottles](/Tent/Platform_Water_Bottles) page - a detail of the tent platform at **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)** that published coverage of **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** passed over entirely. The composite posted by **[@SelinaAnneXX](/Influencers/x/selinaannexx)** in **July 2026** marks the foot area of the seated figure on the low metal riser and zooms in on what appears to be a cluster of crushed plastic water bottles wedged under the white sneakers. Nothing about the bottles is claimed here as evidence of anything; they may be ordinary event litter or an improvised shim.

The reason the frame is catalogued at all is that the platform surface directly under **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** sits in the zone that was **excavated and paved over on September 14, 2025**, four days after the shooting. Any object recorded on that surface beforehand exists now only in photographs. The same post raises two further questions worth keeping separate from the bottles: what the man in the dark polo standing to the right of the canopy was standing on, described in the thread as his own separate riser set on a hard-shell equipment case, and who held the low close-range vantage that the tight crop was shot from. The third image in the cluster is a four-panel composite from the parent post advancing a speculative device theory that this site does not endorse and that no published forensic examination has validated.

The answerable questions are evidentiary rather than theoretical - whether the platform and riser surfaces were photographed and inventoried before removal, and whether any evidence log lists the objects on them. Read the full write-up at [Platform Water Bottles](/Tent/Platform_Water_Bottles), then [Tent Structure Under Charlie](/Tent/Tent_Structure_Under_Charlie), [Paving Timeline September 14](/Tent/Paving_Timeline_Sept_14) and [Site Alteration](/Tent/Site_Alteration_Evidence).
