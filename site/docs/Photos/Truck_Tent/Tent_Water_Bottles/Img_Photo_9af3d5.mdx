## What This Image Shows

A composite posted to X in **July 2026** by **[@SelinaAnneXX](/Influencers/x/selinaannexx)**. The wide panel is a shot inside the tent at **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)**: a black chair set on a low metal riser, a seated figure in a white shirt and white sneakers, a blue-skirted table, a boom mic and camera rig at the left, and the crowd behind the barricade. A red box and arrow mark the foot area. Two zoomed crops at the right show a cluster of crushed plastic water bottles, **Kirkland** branding visible, wedged under the sneakers on the riser deck.

The post asks a question rather than making an accusation. Reproduced from the thread: **I am VERY curious as to your opinion on the water bottles, and what Flood is standing on... PS: WHO...took the photo on the right...who was standing in the perfect spot to take it?**

Nothing here establishes that the bottles mean anything. They may be event litter, a discarded case pushed under a riser, or an improvised shim. What makes the frame worth keeping is that this exact surface was **excavated and paved over on September 14, 2025**, four days after the shooting - so anything resting on it beforehand now survives only in photographs. The answerable questions are whether the platform and riser surfaces were photographed and inventoried before removal, and whether any property log lists the objects on them.
