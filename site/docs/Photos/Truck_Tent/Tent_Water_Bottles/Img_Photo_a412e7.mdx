## What This Image Shows

A four-panel composite attached to the parent post in the thread by **[@VLuvMully](/Influencers/x/vluvmully)**: a wireless audio receiver, a laboratory shaped-charge demonstration, a high-speed frame of a shaped-charge jet, and a skin close-up offered as the comparison.

The theory it illustrates is that a custom device concealed in a wireless receiver was on **[Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk)** chest and functioned there, and that a square pale mark, localised burning and a pin-point central wound are its signature.

This site does not endorse that theory. It is an unproven claim by a private individual on social media, no published forensic examination has validated the physical readings offered, and no independent expert has publicly confirmed that any device was present. It appears here only because the water-bottle post is a reply within this thread. Weigh it against the official ballistic account in [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and see the related device claims, with their caveats, in [Mic](/Mic/overview) and [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview).
