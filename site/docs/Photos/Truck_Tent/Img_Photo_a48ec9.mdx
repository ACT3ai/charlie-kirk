## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide frame under the concrete portico. A group of people, some in event merchandise and red caps, stand and walk near a dark SUV, and at least two hold smartphones up to record. Banner text is legible: **[TURNING POINT USA](/TPUSA/overview)**, **TPUSA.COM/WEARECHARLIEKIRK** and a call to action reading **TEXT FREEDOM TO 71776**.

The banner wording places this frame after **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** - the WeAreCharlieKirk campaign address was created following the assassination - so this is a later tour stop rather than the day itself. That is worth stating plainly, because frames from later events circulate alongside frames from the day and are easily conflated.

Its value is comparative. It shows how vehicles were routinely staged in covered areas at these events, which is the baseline against which the arrangement at **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)** should be judged.
