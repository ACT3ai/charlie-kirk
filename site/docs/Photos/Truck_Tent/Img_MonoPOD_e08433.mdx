## What This Image Shows

A low-quality vertical frame of a crowd packed tightly into the concrete underpass. Several people look agitated; a woman in sunglasses is shouting in the midground and a cameraman holds a rig at the left edge. A red cap is visible in the lower midground.

The overlay is the same **In the tunnel right after!** caption, watermarked **@rynestimetwo**. Taken together the four tunnel frames give a rough sequence of the covered passage filling with people in the minutes after the shot.

The evidentiary value is crowd flow, not identification. These frames show that the area behind the stage went from controlled to uncontrolled very quickly, which matters for any argument about scene preservation and about who could have left the area unrecorded. That thread runs through [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) and [Site Alteration](/Tent/Site_Alteration_Evidence).
