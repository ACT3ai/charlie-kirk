## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide exterior shot of the courtyard at **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)**. A large crowd of young adults is densely packed through the foreground and midground, all facing the white **The American Comeback Tour** canopy in the centre-left. Behind it stands the modern academic building with its large glass windows and campus banners. A clock overlay reads **12:05** and the signage fragments **A PLACE FOR YOU** and **UVU** are legible.

At **12:05** this is one of the last wide views of the site before the shooting. It is the frame to hold in mind when reading any claim about what was visible from where.

Everything the case turns on is in this one image: the crowd depth, the height of the surrounding terraces and walkways, the position of the canopy against the building, and the rooflines beyond it.
