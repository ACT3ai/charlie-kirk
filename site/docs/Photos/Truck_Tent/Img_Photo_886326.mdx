## What This Image Shows

A cropped, low-resolution screenshot of the rear of a black **GMC Denali**. The licence plate is circled by hand in pink with a letter **R** written above it. A man in a white cap points toward the centre of the frame from the left, and a blurred hand holding a thin metal rod occupies the right foreground. The timecode overlay again reads **16:19 / 20:11**.

The pink circle and the letter are the annotator's, matching the marking system used across this small set of frames to sort which vehicle is which. The Denali is one of the vehicles researchers place in the covered passage behind the stage at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025**.

What the frame supports is narrow and worth stating plainly: it shows a particular model of vehicle at the scene and preserves a view of its plate. It does not show anything about who owned it, who was in it, or what it was doing there. Those are record questions.
