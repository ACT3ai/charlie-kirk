## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution outdoor frame of a crowded gathering. A dense crowd fills the foreground, seen mostly from behind, looking toward several vehicles and what appear to be security personnel in the midground and background.

The file was saved under a name referring to a man in a truck, which is the thread this cluster keeps returning to. The frame itself is unannotated and singles out nobody.

Its use is as context rather than proof: it shows the crowd density on the public side of the barrier and how much of the vehicle area was visible from there. That bears directly on whether anything happening around the vehicles could have gone unnoticed by the hundreds of phones pointed in that direction.
