## What This Image Shows

A pixelated, slightly overexposed video still of people standing on the raised platform in front of the large event banners, with part of the crowd visible along the bottom of the frame. A tilted black overlay box in the lower right carries website text for the **Greatest-1-Command** analysis account, and a hand-drawn red curve has been added at the left.

The OCR picks up a source filename in the frame, **Big_Great_Truck Shooter.mp4** - the analysis video this still was captured from. That name states the argument plainly: the analyst was working the theory that the shot originated from the vehicle area behind the stage rather than from the roof position in the official account.

That theory is contested and unproven. It rests on annotated low-resolution stills, a claimed laser reflection and an audio triangulation, none of which has been validated by any forensic authority, and it has been publicly disputed. Read it against [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and see the fuller argument in [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories).
