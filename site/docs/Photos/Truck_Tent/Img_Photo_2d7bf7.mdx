## What This Image Shows

A blurry vertical screengrab of press and camera operators gathered under the concrete walkway. In the foreground a photographer bends forward with her back to the lens; behind her a cameraman stands holding a professional video camera rigged on a monopod, looking off to his left.

The overlay reads **In the tunnel right after!** and the clip is watermarked to the TikTok handle **@rynestimetwo**. That caption is the provenance of the whole tunnel set in this cluster - the frames were pulled from one bystander clip filmed in the covered passage behind the stage in the minutes following the shot on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**.

Monopod-mounted rigs recur across these frames and are tracked deliberately. The [MonoPod Camera](/Photos/Truck_Tent/MonoPod_Camera/overview) sub-cluster covers the argument researchers have built around camera positions behind the canopy, and the wider vehicle and tunnel analysis sits in [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories).
