## What This Image Shows

A blurry vertical screenshot from the tunnel clip. A crowd has swarmed around a black vehicle as it moves through the covered passage; security personnel in the foreground try to hold people back while onlookers press against the car, some holding out sheets of paper.

The overlay is the familiar **In the tunnel right after!** caption with the **@rynestimetwo** watermark.

The frame records something specific and checkable: a vehicle moving out through the passage with an uncontrolled crowd around it. Any vehicle that left the area in those minutes is a vehicle whose movement, occupants and destination are matters of record - the sort of detail a full release of scene logs would settle.
