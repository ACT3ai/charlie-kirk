## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution capture from a video showing the working area under the white event canopy. Several men stand around the long table, one bald man prominent in the foreground and others behind him. The navy banner reading **PROVE ME WRONG** is mounted along the top edge of the canopy frame.

The browser tabs and stray interface text visible along the top edge show this was screenshotted from a desktop while another analyst reviewed the footage, which is typical of how these frames reached the file. The canopy, the table and the banner are the fixed landmarks investigators use to orient every other frame from under the tent.

Geometry under the canopy is not a trivial detail. The heights of the platform, the table and the banner line determine what could be seen from where, and those measurements underpin both the official account and the competing close-range arguments.
