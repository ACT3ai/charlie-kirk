## What This Image Shows

A blurry vertical frame of people moving fast under the concrete overhang. A woman in jeans and a black shirt is bent forward with her back to the lens; behind her a camera operator holds a professional rig, and a large man in a dark brown uniform walks past. The impression is of urgency in a concrete corridor.

The overlay again reads **In the tunnel right after!**, watermarked **@rynestimetwo**. This is one of four frames in the cluster filed under the monopod label because of the camera rig visible in each.

Why the rigs are tracked: a monopod-mounted camera positioned behind the canopy would have had a continuous view of the area researchers dispute, and the question of what those cameras recorded and who holds the footage has never been publicly answered. The claim built around that position is set out in [MonoPod Camera](/Photos/Truck_Tent/MonoPod_Camera/overview).
