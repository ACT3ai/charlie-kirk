## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical frame taken from inside the crowd, looking slightly upward at the people packed under the wide concrete overpass. A person holding a professional camera rig is visible, along with several onlookers, one in a red cap. A dark banner across the top of the frame reads **In the tunnel right after!** with a TikTok watermark in the lower right.

The upward angle is useful in a way the poster probably did not intend: it records the underside of the skybridge, the ceiling height of the passage and the structural columns, all of which are the physical constraints any close-range firing-position argument has to satisfy.

The same ceiling was reportedly re-mudded during the works that followed the shooting, which is one of the alterations researchers have flagged.
