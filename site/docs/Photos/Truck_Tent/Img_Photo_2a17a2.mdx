## What This Image Shows

An eye-level, medium-wide frame centred on a black vehicle with its rear hatch standing open, parked in the middle of a crowd. Raised hands and phones fill the foreground. A young man in a white graphic shirt stands close to the open hatch, and bystanders in event merchandise watch from around him.

The open rear hatch is the single detail that makes this strip of ground contested. Threads published by **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/x/project-constitution)** claim that footage shows a rear hatch opening roughly four seconds before the shot on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** and closing immediately afterward, and that an individual near the vehicle reacted before anyone else in frame. Those are reported claims from citizen analysis, not findings by any investigating body, and no authority has connected any vehicle behind the tent to the shooting.

This frame does not show that sequence - it shows an open hatch in a crowded scene, which by itself proves only that the vehicle was accessible and that people were close to it.
