## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution frame of onlookers gathered below the elevated concrete and glass pedestrian skywalk. Beyond them several black security SUVs and utility vehicles are parked, and a uniformed officer stands near an open vehicle trunk. A marked police car sits in the background. Legible signage fragments read **FOR YOU**, part of the **A PLACE FOR YOU** campus banners, and **POLIC**.

This is the clearest plain view in the cluster of how the covered passage was actually laid out on the day: crowd on one side of the barrier, staged vehicles on the other, uniformed presence among them, and the skywalk overhead.

The layout is what makes the area argued over. It put a line of vehicles, with people working around them, within a short distance of the back of the stage, under a solid concrete deck that shaped how sound travelled. Both the sightline and the acoustic questions in [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories) depend on this geometry, and the venue is mapped in [Campus and University](/campus_university/overview).
