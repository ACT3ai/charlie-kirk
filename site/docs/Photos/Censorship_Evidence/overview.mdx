## About This Cluster

These are mostly screenshots capturing what researchers allege is the suppression of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) material online. Several show posts on X where footage has been replaced by blank grey placeholders reading "This video has been deleted," reproduced by accounts that say the clips were removed after they began circulating. Others document users who report that a years-old account of theirs was taken down, or preserve a since-deleted post before it disappeared. The common thread, according to the posters, is that material tied to the case keeps vanishing from public platforms.

A second group concerns automated content moderation. Images reproduce posts describing systems named the **Online Hate Index** (OHI V3), **Au10tix**, and **Cheq** — tools that posters allege link the Anti-Defamation League and Israeli cybersecurity firms to how speech on X is monitored, scored, and filtered. These are the posters' claims, shown with attribution, not established facts.

Why this matters to the investigation: witnesses reportedly said footage on their phones was edited or deleted before they reached home. If material is removed from the record, the public's ability to reconstruct events erodes, which is exactly why researchers archive these screenshots.
