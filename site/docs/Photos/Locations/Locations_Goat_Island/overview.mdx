## About This Cluster

**Goat Island** entered the investigation through a single reported detail: that a photo of it was found as the desktop background on **[Tyler Robinson's](/People/tyler-robinson)** computer. What kept it in the file is geography - Goat Island sits down the road from the **Valhalla Danish House**, and at the post-assassination press conference **[Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)** reportedly closed his remarks on **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** with "rest now brother, we have the watch, and I'll see you in Valhalla." Researchers noticed the coincidence of the word and the place and started asking whether it was one.

The three images here are thumbnail frames from the video set posted by **@MJTruthUltra** in **April 2026**: the Patel statement clip, the location-analysis clip mapping the relationship between Goat Island and the Valhalla Danish House, and a broader investigative-analysis clip covering the desktop photo. The poster who assembled the thread said plainly that there is no documented proof of any connection, and this site repeats that. A desktop wallpaper of a well-known Utah landmark has an obvious innocent explanation, and nothing has been presented showing that Kash Patel had any awareness of Goat Island or of a building called Valhalla near it. This is a coincidence being logged so it can be checked, not a claim being made.

The checkable questions are narrow and ordinary: whether either location appears anywhere in the accused's documented movements before or after **September 10, 2025**, and whether any records tie either place to anyone else in the case. Read [Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island) and [Valhalla Danish House](/Locations/Valhalla) for the full write-ups, and [Locations](/Locations/overview) for the rest of the geography.
