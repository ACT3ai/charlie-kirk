## What This Image Shows

A widescreen video thumbnail, one of three saved from the thread posted by **@MJTruthUltra** in **April 2026**. This one fronts the clip of the post-assassination press conference remark.

At that press conference **Kash Patel** reportedly said of **Charlie Kirk**: **To my friend Charlie Kirk, rest now brother, we have the watch, and I'll see you in Valhalla.** Researchers noticed that a building called the **Valhalla Danish House** stands near **Goat Island**, the Utah landmark reportedly found as the desktop background on **Tyler Robinson's** computer, and asked whether the coincidence was one.

The poster who assembled the thread stated plainly that there is no documented proof of any connection, and nothing has been presented showing that Patel had any awareness of Goat Island or of a building called Valhalla near it. It is logged as a coincidence to be checked, not a claim. See [Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island) and [Valhalla Danish House](/Locations/Valhalla).
