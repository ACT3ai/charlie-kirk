## What This Image Shows

A tall-format video thumbnail from the same **@MJTruthUltra** thread. It fronts the clip that maps the geographic relationship between **Goat Island** and the **Valhalla Danish House**.

The geography is the whole of the claim: the two places are described as being a short distance apart. That is a checkable fact about a map, and it is separate from any question of significance.

The questions that would actually settle the matter are narrow and documentary - whether either location appears anywhere in the accused's recorded movements before or after **September 10, 2025**, and whether any record ties either place to anyone else in the case. Neither has been shown. See [Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island), [Valhalla Danish House](/Locations/Valhalla) and [Locations](/Locations/overview).
