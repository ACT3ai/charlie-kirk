## About This Cluster

The investigation tracks a map of physical places beyond the UVU campus — the wooded area where the rifle was recovered, **Tyler Robinson's** apartment in St. George, the Provo airport, the hospitals and courthouses, and stranger entries like **Goat Island** in Utah Lake. This photo cluster is the visual index for those places. It currently holds one sub-cluster, but the [Property &amp; Locations](/Locations/overview) section it mirrors covers more than a dozen sites, each with its own written page.

The one sub-cluster here, [Goat Island](/Photos/Locations/Locations_Goat_Island/overview), exists because of a convergence researchers find hard to ignore: a photo of Goat Island was reportedly found as the desktop background on Tyler Robinson's computer, the island sits just down the road from a property called the **Valhalla Danish House**, and FBI Director **Kash Patel** closed his press conference with "I'll see you in Valhalla." According to **@MJTruthUltra**, who assembled the thread, there is no documented proof of any connection — the images preserve the geographic claim so readers can weigh it themselves.

Open the [Goat Island](/Photos/Locations/Locations_Goat_Island/overview) images first, then read the full written treatments at [Goat Island](/Locations/Goat_Island) and [Valhalla Danish House](/Locations/Valhalla). For the rest of the investigation's mapped sites — the gun recovery site, the Robinson apartment, the courthouse — start at [Property &amp; Locations](/Locations/overview).
