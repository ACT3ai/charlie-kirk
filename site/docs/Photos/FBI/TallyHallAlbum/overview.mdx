## About This Cluster

This cluster holds one composite screenshot documenting a since-removed X account, [@TallyHallAlbum](/Influencers/x/tallyhallalbum), that circulated widely in the days after September 10, 2025. The collage assembles several posts attributed to that account alongside the platform notices showing the posts and the account were later deleted or suspended, plus replies from other X users tagging federal accounts and asking for a look. It sits under the [FBI](/Photos/FBI/overview) cluster because the entire thread is about a public demand that the bureau examine something, and about whether that examination ever happened.

The screenshots show a post attributed to the account reading, in lowercase, that it would be funny if someone like [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) got shot on September 10th, with a displayed timestamp of 2:47 AM on September 3, 2025 and 169 views. A second post attributed to the same account names [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) and a December date. The collage also captures a reply reading that the FBI director needed to track down whoever had the account because, in that user's words, they knew a day ahead. Everything here is a screenshot of a social-media post, not a verified record: the account holder has not been publicly identified, no charge has been reported, screenshots can be edited, and nothing on this page asserts that any person had advance knowledge of anything. What the images do document is that the posts existed publicly, that users escalated them to federal accounts, and that the account disappeared.

Open the single image for the full collage and the readable post text. The wider question of what the bureau chose to pursue and what it shut down is covered at [FBI](/FBI/overview), and specifically at [Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads) and [Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt).
