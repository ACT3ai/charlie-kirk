## What This Image Shows

A composite collage assembled from screenshots of X, watermarked to the account [@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra). It gathers several posts attributed to an account displaying as Mushy, [@TallyHallAlbum](/Influencers/x/tallyhallalbum), along with platform notices reading This Post was deleted by the Post author and Something went wrong, and replies from other users.

The post text captured in the collage reads, in lowercase, that it would be funny if someone like charlie kirk got shot on september 10th, with a displayed timestamp of 2:47 AM on September 3, 2025 and 169 views. A second post attributed to the same account names [Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump) and a December date, timestamped 3:50 PM on September 13. Below, a user posting as Panican Finder tags the FBI and the bureau's director and deputy director, writing that they may want to take a look at the account, timestamped 12:30 PM on 9/14/25 with 1.9K views. A further reply states that the account holder knew a day ahead and had called out a date for Trump; another suggests notifying the Secret Service.

Everything here is a screenshot of social media, and screenshots can be edited. The account holder has not been publicly identified, no charge has been reported, and nothing on this page asserts that any person had advance knowledge of anything. What is documented is that the posts were publicly visible, that users escalated them to federal accounts, and that the account and its posts subsequently disappeared. Whether any of it was examined has not been made public. See [FBI Investigation](/FBI/FBI_Investigation), [Foreign Leads](/FBI/Foreign_Leads), and [UVU GRAMA Records](/CoverUp/UVU_GRAMA_Records).
