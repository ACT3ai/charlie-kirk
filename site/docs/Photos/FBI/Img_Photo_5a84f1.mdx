## What This Image Shows

A two-panel collage on a solid red background, assembled from broadcast footage. The top panel shows a group walking through a public building, with yellow overlay text reading 7:58 PM Utah Time (MDT) and September 11, 2025, and white labels naming **Henderson**, **Lieutenant Governor Cox**, **FBI SAIC Bohls**, **Bongino**, and **Kash Patel**. The bottom panel is labeled SEPT 12, 2025 and OREM, UTAH and shows the formal press conference at the podium. An abc4.com station logo appears at the lower right of the top panel.

The collage is an identification aid rather than an argument: someone went through the broadcast footage and put names to the people who walked in together on the evening of September 11 and who stood at the podium the next morning. That matters because so much of the dispute about this case turns on who was in the room and when.

The September 11 evening timing lines up with the other annotated collage in this cluster, [the 7:58 PM press conference frames](/Photos/FBI/Img_HFaOtMybsAAiSoh_e13dfc), and with the custody sequence documented at [Custody and Confession Timeline](/CoverUp/Custody_Confession_Timeline).
