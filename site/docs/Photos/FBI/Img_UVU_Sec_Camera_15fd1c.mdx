## What This Image Shows

This is a screenshot of a formal records-response letter, addressed to a requester identified as Camelot Jack, answering a **Utah Government Records Access and Management Act** request dated **November 1, 2025**. The letter restates the request before answering it, which is why the page is worth reading closely: it preserves exactly what was asked for.

The quoted request seeks all surveillance video footage, including raw unedited files in their highest available resolution, captured by any and all security cameras located at or near the pedestrian tunnel under Campus Drive — the tunnel connecting off-campus housing such as The Green to central campus — on **September 10, 2025**, from 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM MDT. It also asks for entry and exit point footage, adjacent pathways, overlapping campus cameras, and any associated metadata, timestamps, camera activation records, motion detection alerts, or export histories for that window. The letter then states that the request is denied.

The time window is the point. The shooting happened just after 12:20 PM, and the tunnel is on the pedestrian route between campus and the housing areas north of it. A denial covering that exact six-hour window means the public record contains no independent camera view of who moved through the tunnel in the hours around the event.
