## What This Image Shows

Three heavily armed men in tactical gear stand in the dry, grassy yard of a stucco house in bright daylight. One is close to the concrete front steps; two stand further out in the yard. They carry rifles fitted with optics, and one weapon has a long suppressor. Plate carriers, chest rigs, magazine pouches, and a green ballistic helmet are visible. The photograph is watermarked to trent.photo.

This is the same residential search as the perimeter frame in this cluster, photographed from ground level at the front of the property. The suppressor and the plate carriers indicate a tactical team rather than a forensics unit.

The photograph records that armed officers were at this address. It says nothing about who lives there or what was found, and no inference about any resident is drawn or intended. The wider question of how the search of the corridor was conducted and documented is at [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence), [FBI Investigation](/FBI/FBI_Investigation), and [Discovery Delays](/FBI/Discovery_Delays).
