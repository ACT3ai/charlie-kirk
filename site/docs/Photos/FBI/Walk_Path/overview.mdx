## About This Cluster

This cluster covers the ground between the [**Losee Center**](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop and the wooded area north of the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus where the [**Mauser .30-06**](/Gun_Bullet/Mauser_30_06_Rifle) was reportedly recovered — the so-called walk path. Two kinds of images sit here. The first is a set of oblique 3D aerial map renders with location pins dropped on them, labeling the **LDS Institute**, the **UVU Army ROTC** building, and individual house numbers on the residential streets north of campus. The second is a set of daylight press photographs, watermarked to trent.photo, showing heavily armed tactical officers moving across a grass slope, walking a road below a terraced retaining wall, and surrounding a single-story stucco house. The route matters because the official account of September 10, 2025 depends on one person covering that distance on foot, unseen, while carrying or ditching a rifle.

The aerial renders let a reader do what the official releases have not made easy: measure the actual distances between the rooftop, the labeled buildings, the numbered houses, and the recovery area. Researchers have pressed a set of open questions against that geometry. According to a June 2026 post by [@MuppetMasher](/Influencers/x/muppetmasher), the official statement places the suspect's car in a UVU parking lot at 8:29 AM but never states when he was seen leaving it, and no cell-tower tracking has been publicly released even though the technology to reconstruct a footstep-by-footstep timeline exists. Others have questioned whether the figure in the released stairwell frames matches [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) at all, which would make the whole walk path a reconstruction of someone else's movements. None of these questions has an answer on the public record, and the tactical search photographs are simply what a search of a residence looked like on the day, not evidence of what was found inside.

Start with the two aerial renders, which orient you to the campus edge and the numbered houses, then move through the tactical search frames in sequence. For the written analysis of where the rifle turned up and why the recovery path is disputed, read [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence), then [Rifle Found After Feds](/CoverUp/Rifle_Found_After_Feds). The parent cluster [FBI](/Photos/FBI/overview) holds the bureau-conduct imagery this search sits inside, and the full written FBI record is at [FBI](/FBI/overview).
