## What This Image Shows

A high-angle photograph taken through a chain-link fence, whose out-of-focus diagonals cross the whole frame. Beyond it, tactical officers in SWAT gear move across a grassy slope beside a road that has been fenced off. Two civilian cars sit in a paved lot at the top of the frame, and a blank green and white directory sign stands on a concrete base at the lower left. The word SWAT is legible on one officer's shirt and POLICE on another. The photograph is watermarked to trent.photo.

Shooting through the fence is itself informative: the photographer could not get closer, which means the area was already closed off. This is documentation from the public side of a cordon rather than from inside an operation.
