## What This Image Shows

Armed officers, at least one with SWAT lettering on the back of his shirt, are positioned around the perimeter of a single-story beige stucco house on a dry, semi-rural residential lot. One stands directly at the front door and others cover the sides. Ordinary domestic detail fills the frame — a blue municipal bin, two mesh patio chairs, a window air-conditioning unit, a green backyard slide, a basketball hoop. The photograph is watermarked to trent.photo.

The children's play equipment is worth registering, because it makes plain that this is a family home in a lived-in neighbourhood rather than an isolated site.

The photograph documents that a search took place at a residence. It does not establish whose house it is, what was sought, or what was found, and nothing here suggests any occupant did anything. The public record of what searches were conducted, under what authority, and what came of them is thin — which is one of the recurring complaints in this case.
