## What This Image Shows

An oblique aerial render of the suburban neighbourhood beside the campus, with dense patches of trees and dry dirt fields between the houses. Three individual homes carry overlay labels — **875**, **895**, and **918** — and a map pin marks the **UVU Army ROTC** building.

Labelling individual addresses rather than streets is what makes this map a working document. Whoever built it was tracking specific properties along the route, which is how a reconstruction gets tested: identify the houses a person would pass, then ask what each one's occupants or cameras would have recorded.

That is the check the public record does not contain. No doorbell-camera or witness reconstruction of the walk has been released, and one of the properties along the route has drawn separate attention in the investigation notes for an unrelated reason. Nothing on this page asserts anything about any resident. The route material is at [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location), [Ring](/cameras/Ring), and [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence).
