## What This Image Shows

An oblique three-dimensional aerial map showing part of the **Utah Valley University** campus and the residential neighbourhood beside it — winding roads, full parking lots, single-family homes with lawns and mature trees. Overlay labels identify the **LDS Institute** and the **UVU Army ROTC** building, and the mapping interface controls are visible at the corner.

The map is a working reference for the route the official account says the suspect took on foot after leaving the **Losee Center** rooftop. Rendering it obliquely rather than straight down matters, because it shows building heights and the sight lines between the campus edge and the houses — what a person on that route could see and be seen from.

The route is contested at every point. According to a June 2026 post by @MuppetMasher, the official statement puts the suspect's car in a campus lot at 8:29 AM without ever stating when it left, and no cell-tower reconstruction has been released. The written record is at [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [Gun Discovery Sequence](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Gun_Discovery_Sequence); the search photography along the same corridor is at [Drop Off Locations](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Ballistics_Drop_Off_Locations/overview).
