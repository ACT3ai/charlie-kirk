## What This Image Shows

This is a phone screenshot of a post on X from the account Polly St George. The attached video thumbnail shows a man in a white shirt with heavy red staining across the chest, holding a dark handheld microphone in front of a backdrop reading TURNING POINT USA, with YREFY.COM printed above it. The visible interface shows a 1:14 video length and 235 replies.

The post text reads: it enters near his right ear, there is video but I cannot post it here, this is a still from that video, see the blood on his right ear and temple. The claim being advanced is about the entry point — that the wound is higher and further right than the official account describes.

That claim sits inside one of the most contested parts of the case. The state's position is a **.30-06** round fired from the **Losee Center** rooftop; researchers have argued from the autopsy description and the visible wound that the geometry does not fit a downward shot from that roof. The competing readings are laid out at [Trajectory and Wound Mismatch](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Trajectory_Wound_Mismatch) and [Ballistics and Wound Analysis](/Medical/ballistics_and_wound_analysis), with the alternative mechanisms discussed at [Alternative Kill Mechanisms](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Alternative_Kill_Mechanisms). This image records what one researcher posted publicly, not a medical finding; the autopsy report itself is still not public, as covered at [Autopsy Report Not Public](/Medical/autopsy_report_not_public).
