## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X by an account posting as Mel, @Villgecrazylady, sharing the **Daily Mail** article about the foreign intelligence probe. The user's comment reads: turns out there are a few stones Kash has no intention of unturning.

The embedded article shows the headline [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) shuts down [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) foreign intelligence probe in explosive feud with Trump's counterterror chief, bylined to Phillip Nieto, US political reporter, and timestamped 09:38 EDT on 29 October 2025 with an update at 10:38 EDT. The same three-photo collage from the article is visible below the headline.

This is a reader reaction rather than new information, and the comment is the poster's opinion about a public official. It is kept because it dates the spread of the story precisely and shows the framing that carried it: the article as evidence of deliberate incuriosity rather than of ordinary jurisdictional friction.
