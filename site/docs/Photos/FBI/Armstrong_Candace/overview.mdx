## About This Cluster

This cluster holds two images tied to material that surfaced through the Armstrong Williams broadcast and Project Veritas channels concerning [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens), who has been one of the loudest public voices questioning the official account of [Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) death. One image is a promotional graphic for The Armstrong Williams Show, showing the program on a laptop, a monitor, and a phone under a lower-third reading violence in America. The other is a screenshot of an iPhone message thread carrying a repeating Project Veritas watermark. It sits in the [FBI](/Photos/FBI/overview) cluster because the material raises the question of what federal authorities did, or did not do, with reported threats against a person publicly challenging the investigation.

The message screenshot shows incoming texts from a partly hidden Washington, D.C. area-code number, with portions highlighted. According to the text visible in the screenshot, the sender accuses the recipient of leaking messages about Candace to Veritas, tells the recipient to remember they are only alive and were never extradited because of the sender, and then asks whether Candace is paying for the recipient's security, adding that things will not end well for either of them if the nonsense keeps up and to take a good hard look at what happened with Kirk. The sender is not identified anywhere in the image, the exchange has not been independently authenticated on the public record, and nothing here asserts that any identified person sent these messages, made a threat, or committed any offense. It is recorded as a circulated screenshot and a reported claim, no more than that.
