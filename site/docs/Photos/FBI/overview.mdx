## About This Cluster

This cluster collects the images tied to how the **FBI** has handled the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation — the released suspect photos, the UVU security cameras above the tunnel, screenshots of bureau statements and press coverage, and posts documenting what witnesses say federal agents asked of them. The FBI's conduct is one of the investigation's central threads because so many of the case's disputed facts — the stairwell photos, the camera footage, the foreign-lead reporting — run through the bureau.

The pattern the images document is contested but consistent in the claims: witnesses have said on camera that FBI callers asked them to delete footage and then called back to confirm it was gone; reporting by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) and others alleges bomb dogs were kept away from specific areas; former special-operations veteran [**Mike Glover**](/People/mike-glover) has said a senior CIA contact told him FBI officials physically blocked agency personnel from assisting; and [**Kash Patel**](/People/kash-patel) acknowledged on Fox News that the stairwell images had been "enhanced," while researchers note the second photo set was released after [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) was already in custody. Each of these is a reported claim with attribution, not a court finding — the bureau maintains it is conducting a lawful investigation.

Start with the UVU security-camera frames and the released suspect photos, then the press-coverage screenshots. The sub-clusters hold the [Walk Path](/Photos/FBI/Walk_Path/overview) search imagery, the [TallyHallAlbum](/Photos/FBI/TallyHallAlbum/overview) foreknowledge post, and the [Armstrong Candace](/Photos/FBI/Armstrong_Candace/overview) alleged-threat screenshots. The written record is at [FBI](/FBI/overview), including [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/FBI/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos), [Evidence &amp; Footage](/FBI/Evidence_Footage), and [CIA Block](/FBI/CIA_Block).
