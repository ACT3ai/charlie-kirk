## About This Cluster

The three images here are screenshots of public X posts asking the same question: were drones flying over the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) event at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on the day [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot. One post by the account "Project Constitution" argues the possibility should not be dismissed; a second, from the same account, circles what it calls a "mysterious man" holding a controller-like device in the crowd; a third, by [John Cullen](/Influencers/x/john-cullen), quotes an embedded video clip shared by [Blake Bednarz](/People/blake-bednarz) showing the outdoor crowd under the canopy tent. None of these establishes that a drone was present. They document that the drone question became an active thread in the online discussion of the case.

The interest is not idle. The investigation's written record tracks a business jet, tail number [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview), whose low, slow passes near the campus that morning some researchers read as consistent with launching and recovering small aircraft, a reading others reportedly dispute. Whether any of that connects to the event remains unproven and contested. These screenshots are useful mainly as a record of who raised the drone angle and what they claimed, not as confirmation of it.
