## What This Image Shows

The most detailed timed reconstruction in this cluster. A satellite view spanning the residential neighborhood at the top and the campus at the bottom, with coloured pins, a yellow path line and numbered stops laying out a full sequence.

The annotations read: **Ring Camera (11:44)**, **1. Ring Camera (8:07) and (11:49)**, **WYZE Camera (8:23) and (9:57)**, **2. Grassy Area (11:50)**, **3. Pedestrian tunnel (11:53)**, **4. N. side of Losee (12:02)**, **5. Enters Losee Center SE (12:15)**, **6. Enters roof on northside (12:17)**, **7. Shots fired from (12:22)**, **8. Exit (12:23)**, **9. Crosses Campus Drive (12:24)**, plus **Gun found**, **UVU Army ROTC**, **Center for National Security Studies** and **Stage**. Two things stand out. The times are drawn from private doorbell and security cameras rather than from institutional sources. And the **Center for National Security Studies** sits directly on the marked route, which the site treats separately.

Every timestamp here is a researcher's compilation from footage of varying quality, and the identity of the person in that footage is contested.
