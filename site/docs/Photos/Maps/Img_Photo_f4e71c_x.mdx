## What This Image Shows

A satellite map of the campus and the housing immediately north of it, with a continuous looping red path drawn on by hand and text annotations pinned along it.

The annotations read **Car Parked ?**, **Limping**, **Seen Later**, **Mission house**, **UVU Parking Garage**, **Army ROTC**, **Center for National Security Studies**, **Gun**, **Way Down**, **Way Up ?**, **Shooter** and **Charlie**, over printed map labels including the Computer Science Building, the Noorda Center for the Performing Arts, the Environmental Technology Building and the Office of Admissions. Two annotations carry question marks - the parking place and the route up - and those are the honest markers of what is not known.

The **Center for National Security Studies** label is why this map gets attention. The site's research file records public commentary about a university programme with intelligence-community connections, and the fact that the marked route runs past it has been treated by some researchers as significant. Proximity on a campus map is not a connection, and the site records that claim as unverified commentary rather than as a finding.
