## What This Image Shows

A satellite street map of Orem, Utah, covering the grid between Mountain View High School, Orem Community Park, SCERA Park and the **UVU** campus, with custom white text labels, arrows and icons added on top by a researcher tracking vehicle and foot movement.

The added labels include **Tyler Car seen at 12.4**, **Tyler Car Thought to be Parked Here**, **Tyler Running 12.303** and **Charlie Kirk Shot at 12.23**, alongside ordinary map furniture such as **Campus Dr**, **UCCU Center** and **Westmore Elementary School**. Note the phrasing the annotator chose: thought to be parked here is explicitly a guess, and the site carries it as one. Vehicle location and parking place remain among the least documented parts of the public movement account.

What this map contributes is scale on the street grid rather than on campus. It shows how far the annotated parking candidates sit from the courtyard, which is the input to any argument about how long the trip on foot would take. Independent analysts have used exactly this measurement to question the published timeline.
