## What This Image Shows

A satellite map covering the neighborhood streets above and the campus below, layered with red tracking dots, direction arrows and hand-added text tracing a movement account.

The labels read **Seen Here 11.49**, **Seen Walking Here**, **Car Seen Passing Here 08.23**, **Probable Parking Place**, **Hide Gun**, **Down Stairs**, **Shot at 12.23** and **Charlie**, with street references including 800 S, Cherry Dr, W 850 S and Campus Dr. It is the same reconstruction family as the numbered route map in this cluster but built by a different researcher, which makes the two worth comparing directly.

Where independent reconstructions agree, the underlying footage is probably being read the same way by different people. Where they diverge - and the parking candidates and the descent route are the usual points of divergence - the disagreement marks exactly what the released material does not settle. The word probable on the parking label is the annotator's own hedge.
