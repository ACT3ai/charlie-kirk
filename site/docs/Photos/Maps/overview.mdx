## About This Cluster

Maps and Site Geometry is where the physical argument of this investigation is settled or lost. Almost every disputed question about **September 10, 2025** reduces to geometry: how far the **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** roof is from the courtyard stage, what a shooter on that roof could actually see, whether the sound on the recordings arrives when a shot from roughly 150 yards would arrive, and what route a person could take from the roof to the wooded area where a rifle was recovered. These twenty-five aerial views, satellite captures, annotated diagrams and campus photographs are the raw material for all of it.

The set includes a CBS News graphic numbering five stages from suspect seen before shooting through to jumped from roof and fled; a Fox News Digital map keyed for suspect arrival, rooftop access, rifle found, sniper's perch, Charlie Kirk, bullet's path and suspect movements, captioned as an approximation based on court documents, scene video and law-enforcement sources; a timed route map annotated with a Ring camera at 8:07 and 11:49, a WYZE camera at 8:23 and 9:57, a pedestrian tunnel at 11:53, north side of Losee at 12:02, entering the roof on the north side at 12:17, shots fired at 12:22, exit at 12:23 and crossing Campus Drive at 12:24; a camera-position overlay naming individual filmers and mounted campus domes; and a ranging diagram marking a rooftop, balcony, stage-left and livestream point of view with distance rings, one label reading 75 yards from stage right and another 78 yards. Independent analysts have used exactly this geometry to argue that the audible report and the visible impact coincide too closely for a 150-yard shot, which would imply a much closer origin. That is a contested technical claim, and the maps let a reader test it rather than take it on trust.

Start with the labelled route maps and the ranging diagram, then open the child cluster [Angles](/Photos/Maps/Angles/overview) for the trajectory work. The written analysis lives in [Maps](/maps/overview), [UVU Maps](/UVU/maps), [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center), [Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward), [Wound Trajectory and Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle), [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview).
