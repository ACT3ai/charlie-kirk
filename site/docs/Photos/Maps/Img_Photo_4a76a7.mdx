## What This Image Shows

A satellite map of the north Orem street grid, bisected diagonally by Route 89, with large white text labels and directional brackets added on top of the standard map furniture.

The added labels read **Charlie Kirk Hospital** pointing at **Timpanogos Regional Hospital**, and **Tyler Dairy Queen**, alongside printed map features such as **Intermountain Health Orem**, **Central Orem Clinic** and **Orem Imaging Center**. Both labels mark contested threads. The hospital choice is one the site tracks in detail, because researchers have asked why the transport went to the facility it did when another hospital was closer to the courtyard. The Dairy Queen marker refers to a location that appears in the movement account attributed to the accused.

Questions about hospital routing are questions about decisions made under extreme pressure by people trying to save a life, and the site does not treat distance alone as evidence of anything improper. What it does support is a request for the transport record, the dispatch audio and the receiving-facility log.
