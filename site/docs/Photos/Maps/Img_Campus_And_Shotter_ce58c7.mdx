## What This Image Shows

A 3D **Google Earth** render of the campus with red callout boxes and a large red arrow drawn across it to show a line of sight. The underlying imagery captures the brick-paved plaza, the light grey concrete buildings with their flat roofs and angular massing, and the open green spaces between them.

The three labels read **LOCATION OF CHARLIE KIRK**, **POSSIBLE LOCATION OF SHOOTER** and **LOSEE CENTER**. The word possible is the annotator's, and it is doing real work: this is a proposed sightline, drawn on public imagery by an independent researcher, not a surveyed line from an official reconstruction.

What the graphic does establish is that the geometry is at least plausible in the crude sense - there is an unobstructed path between those two points. That is not the same as establishing that a shot came from there, which is the question the acoustic work and the wound-path work in this investigation are trying to answer from other directions.
