This is a composite reference image consisting of a split screen that correlates a ground-level photograph of a college campus with an overhead, satellite-view map of the same location. The left panel shows a modern concrete and glass academic building overlooking a terraced grassy amphitheater courtyard, while the right panel shows a 2D digital map with icons detailing the "UVU Courtyard" and surrounding buildings.
