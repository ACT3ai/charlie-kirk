## What This Image Shows

A 3D aerial render of the **[Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview)** campus, built from satellite mapping imagery, with the roof geometry, the parking lot, the lawns and an access road all visible from above. Small red marker dots have been dropped in a line across the render to trace a route, and white text labels with black outlines have been added by the researcher who published it.

The labels read **Shooting Position**, **Technology Building**, **Enology Building**, **Jump Down** and **Hid Gun**. Read together they are a compressed version of the official movement account: a firing point on a roof, a place where the person came down off that roof, and a location where the rifle was left. The map is one researcher's reconstruction drawn on public imagery, not an official exhibit, and the label positions are that researcher's reading of the released material.

This is the frame to hold next to the news-organisation maps in this cluster, because it uses the same landmarks but places them by eye rather than from court filings. Where the labels agree with [the CBS News timeline graphic](/Photos/Maps/Img_kirk_shooting_map8_939f1b_x) and the Fox News Digital map, the geometry is at least consistently reported; where they disagree, the disagreement is itself the finding. For the buildings named here see [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) and [UVU Maps](/UVU/maps), and for the recovery location see [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [Wooded Area Gun Recovery](/Locations/Wooded_Area_Gun_Recovery).
