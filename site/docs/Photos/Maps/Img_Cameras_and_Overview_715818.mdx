## What This Image Shows

A high-angle photograph of the **UVU** courtyard - the tiered grass amphitheater, the white event tent, the surrounding concrete buildings and the water feature behind - overlaid with red pointer lines and black text naming who or what was recording from each position. Source URLs are printed along the bottom, including a YouTube link and a Guardian piece on the shooting maps, videos and images.

The annotator's labels name individual filmers and fixed cameras: **Tripod Boy/breaking911?**, **[Jeremy King](/People/jeremy-king)**, **Laura Lo**, **Tiana Lo**, two **4K Videographer** positions marked mounted and manned, **[Sameer Massey](/People/sameer-massey)** with the TikTok handle sameermassey64, **Adam Bartholomew** with the note X [@lifeisdriving](/Influencers/x/paramounttactcl) sons down there in front and a reference to SD card removal, **[Henry Dells](/People/henry-dells)**, **Beccers.g.hales instagram**, a person described as inside the library catching the head on the roof, an unidentified woman recording in the Sameer video, and three **campus dome** or **dome/PTZ** positions marked not shown.

The names on this map are the researcher's own attributions and none of them has been independently confirmed. The people identified appear here as attendees who were filming, which is what nearly everyone in the courtyard was doing; nothing on this page should be read as suggesting that any of them did anything improper, and the SD card note is the annotator's shorthand for a question the site follows elsewhere rather than an allegation that anyone destroyed or withheld material.

This is the single most useful map in the cluster for anyone asking what footage should exist. Every not shown label is a camera the annotator marks as having had line of sight and whose output has not been made public.
