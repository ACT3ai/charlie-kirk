## What This Image Shows

A high-angle photograph of the **UVU** courtyard - the tiered grass amphitheater, the white event tent, the surrounding concrete buildings and the water feature behind - overlaid with red pointer lines and black text naming who or what was recording from each position. Source URLs are printed along the bottom, including a YouTube link and a Guardian piece on the shooting maps, videos and images.

The labels name individual filmers and fixed cameras: **Tripod Boy/breaking911?**, **Jeremy King**, **Laura Lo**, **Tiana Lo**, two **4K Videographer** positions marked mounted and manned, **Sameer Massey** with the TikTok handle sameermassey64, **Adam Bartholomew** with the note X @lifeisdriving sons down there in front and SD card removal, **Henry Dells**, **Beccers.g.hales instagram**, a person described as inside the library catching the head on the roof, an unidentified woman recording in the Sameer video, and three **campus dome** or **dome/PTZ** positions marked not shown.

This is the single most useful map in the cluster for anyone asking what footage should exist. Every not shown label is a camera that had line of sight and whose output has not been made public, and the SD card note is the thread the site tracks separately.
