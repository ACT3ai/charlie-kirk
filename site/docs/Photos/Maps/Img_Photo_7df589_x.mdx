## What This Image Shows

A stylized overhead site map, drawn in flat greys, black and white rather than taken from satellite imagery, with lettered badges marking buildings and zones - **PS**, **HF**, **EN**, **B**, **WB**, **FG**, **BA** - and coloured dots placed at specific positions.

The legend reads **3 Patsies**, **2 Shooters** and **2 Distractions**, watermarked **@WeAreNotGoingToMars**. This is a whole theory of the event compressed into one graphic: it asserts multiple shooters, multiple people set up to take blame, and staged distractions, and it commits to specific positions for each. The site records it as one researcher's published hypothesis. It is an unverified claim, no named individual is attached to any dot on this page, and nothing here should be read as an accusation against any person.

It is included because it is the clearest single statement of the multi-actor theory that a large part of the independent research community has been arguing, and because it is falsifiable - each marked position can be checked against footage. The site's own treatment of these threads is at [Patsies and Distraction Actors](/Killer/Patsies_Distraction_Actors), [Distraction People](/distraction_people/overview), [Theories](/Theories/overview) and [The Real Killer](/Killer/overview).
