## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level frame taken from behind the stage. The lower third shows the edge of the setup - a speaker seated on a black swivel stool with his back to the camera, facing the crowd under the blue canopy, one hand raised mid-gesture. Beyond, a dense crowd stands behind metal security barriers.

A bright yellow arrow has been digitally added, running from a man in a plaid shirt standing in the crowd down to a group of water bottles sitting on the table in the foreground. There is no readable on-screen text.

The annotator is asserting a relationship between a person in the crowd and the bottles on the table. What that relationship is meant to be is not written on the image, and the site does not supply one. The objects on the table are separately of interest simply as an inventory: knowing exactly what was on that surface before the shot is one of the few physical facts the public footage can establish.
