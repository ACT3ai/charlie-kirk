## About This Cluster

Brown Shirt Puller is the label online researchers attached to a man in a brown or plaid patterned shirt seen moving in the crowd near the table, and this sub-cluster collects both the event frames and the background photographs gathered while trying to identify him. Public commentary has connected the label to **Rick Cutler**, and several of the images here are personal photographs associated with that name. The identification and everything built on it remain unverified claims; the site asserts no wrongdoing by anyone shown.

The most substantive document in the set is a smartphone screenshot of a **May 1, 2019** Facebook post by the page **Iris Relief**, showing a man at a podium in front of an Irving Police Department backdrop. The post reads that Rick Cutler just retired from twenty-nine years of service with Irving PD and would be moving to Guatemala to serve as Director of Training and Logistics for Iris Relief, training and equipping first responders in prevention, sustainability and preparedness. Alongside it sit ordinary personal photographs - a man on a mossy rock beside a creek in a Spartan Race shirt, a man on a boat holding a red drum, a man in a **HEX** cap holding a baby - plus an annotated X screenshot by **@RealK12Lioness** in which a hand-drawn white circle marks the upper torso of a man in plaid being escorted across pavement and a red circle marks a bulge at his rear pocket. That annotation is the poster's argument, not a finding, and a rear-pocket bulge in a compressed screenshot is not evidence of anything specific.

Read [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) and [Rick Cutler - Person of Interest](/Suspects/Rick_Cutler_POI) for the written analysis, and [Rick Cutler](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Rick_Cutler/overview) for the remaining images. The escort frame also appears in [Brian Harpole](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole/overview).
