## About This Cluster

Item is a one-image page, and the image is the tightest crop in the whole section. It is a heavily pixelated close-up of a pale hand with the fingers extended, angled downward over a dark blue surface that could be clothing or a lap, with a small multi-coloured object - yellowish underneath, blue and white on top - resting in or under the hand. Two bright red arrows have been drawn on by whoever published it, one pointing at the object and one at faint white lettering nearby.

The reason a single blurry crop gets its own page is that this frame is the closest thing circulating publicly to a picture of an actual object changing hands at the table. Everything else in this section shows people, positions and movement; this shows a thing. What the object is has not been established. Suggestions in public commentary have ranged from an ordinary item of event merchandise to a piece of audio equipment, and no analysis has resolved it. The arrows are the poster's annotation, not a finding, and no claim is made here that anyone concealed, removed or passed anything improperly.

The substantive question this feeds is set out in [Table Jumpers and the Missing Mic Device](/Mic/Table_Jumpers_Device), with the equipment itself described in [RODE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic) and the recovered-fragment thread in [Burnt Object and the RODE Transmitter](/Mic/burnt-object-rode-transmitter). For the surrounding frames, see [Hand Off](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Hand_Off/overview).
