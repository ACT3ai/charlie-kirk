## What This Image Shows

A blurred, pixelated frame from a video player showing a moment of close physical contact. At the left of frame a person in a dark short-sleeved polo and khaki trousers is bent forward, right arm extended downward, hand gripping another person whose head is partly visible in the lower-left corner. A black camera tripod stands in the midground at the right, and a video progress bar runs along the bottom.

A large solid red arrow has been drawn pointing at the first person's left wrist, where a light-coloured, possibly metallic wristwatch is visible. There is no readable on-screen text.

The arrow is the annotator's claim that something about that wrist matters. What can actually be seen is a wristwatch. Claims have circulated in social-media threads that wrist-worn devices at the table were something other than watches; those are unverified assertions by anonymous accounts, and the site does not adopt them or repeat their accusations. No person shown is identified and none is accused of anything.
