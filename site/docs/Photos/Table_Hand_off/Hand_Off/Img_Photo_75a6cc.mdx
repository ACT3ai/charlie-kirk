## What This Image Shows

A frame from the short clip that makes up this sub-cluster. A silver crowd-control barricade runs horizontally across the foreground over bright, worn grass. Beyond it, on the stage area, several men in plainclothes security or production roles are crouched low behind the blue-draped table, alert and scanning outward. One stands with his back to the camera in a blue and grey plaid short-sleeved shirt and dark grey trousers. Behind them the crowd is seated on the grassy hillside in hard overexposed sun.

The on-screen text is the recorder's **HDR** indicator, a fragment reading **THE**, and the player position **0:16/0:36**.

Crouching behind cover immediately after a shot is the trained and the instinctive response, and it is what most of the people in this frame are doing. The frame's value is positional: it records who was behind the table at the sixteen-second mark and which way each of them was facing. No conduct is alleged.
