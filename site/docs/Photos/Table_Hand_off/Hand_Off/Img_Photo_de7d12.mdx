## What This Image Shows

An overexposed high-angle frame from the same clip. A white metal crowd barrier crosses the lower third; beyond it, on the grass, a group of men are clustered in evident commotion. One in a grey polo and khaki trousers kneels facing right, looking back over his shoulder toward the camera with a tense expression. Beside him another in a dark blue polo and brown trousers is bent over, apparently reacting to or assisting with something on the ground. A third in a plaid shirt stands behind them leaning forward. A collapsed chair lies nearby and the dark blue table edge is at the right.
