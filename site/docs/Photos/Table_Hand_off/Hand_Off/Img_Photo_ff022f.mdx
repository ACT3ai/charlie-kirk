## What This Image Shows

Another frame from the same clip at the same **0:16/0:36** mark, taken a moment apart from its neighbour. The silver crowd barricade runs across the lower frame over bright green grass. Behind it sits a large dark grey plastic equipment transport case, flat on the grass, with two moulded compartments and a white rectangular plate resting on top. To its right is the stage structure draped in blue and white fabric, with the crowd in the bleachers and on the hillside behind.

The on-screen text is **HDR**, fragments reading **THE** and **HOLCON**, and the timestamp.

The automated description generated for this file misidentifies the scene as the July 2024 rally in Butler, Pennsylvania. It is not; the canopy text, the hillside seating and the surrounding frames place it at the **UVU** courtyard. The site does not adopt that misidentification. What the frame usefully records is the equipment inventory on the grass beside the stage - cases, plates and drapes - which is part of establishing what audio and production kit was physically present and where.
