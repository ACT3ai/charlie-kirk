## What This Image Shows

A vertical, slightly high-angle frame from the same short clip. In the foreground the grass is overexposed and a white barricade runs along the bottom edge. Just beyond it, a heavily built man in a blue T-shirt and khaki trousers is caught mid-motion, leaning forward with his right hand flat on the white top of a rectangular platform and his left leg extended back. The platform rests on two large dark heavy-duty plastic equipment cases with visible latches and a small white tag. Behind, the crowd sits on the grassy hill and television cameras stand on tripods.

The on-screen text is **HDR**, fragments **THE**, **AM** and **CO**, and the player position.

The hand flat on the platform top is the detail. It is what somebody vaulting a barrier looks like, and it is equally what somebody bracing to steady themselves looks like. The site records the posture without choosing between those readings and identifies no one.
