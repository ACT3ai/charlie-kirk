## About This Cluster

The Table Hand Off is the investigation's file for everything that happened at and around the merchandise table under the **Prove Me Wrong** canopy at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025**. That table sat directly in front of Charlie Kirk, it was the closest fixed object to him when the shot was fired, and it is the surface across which hats, water bottles, microphones and other items moved all afternoon. The reason this area matters is the open question the site tracks on [Table Jumpers and the Missing Mic Device](/Mic/Table_Jumpers_Device): several people reached Kirk within seconds, and researchers have asked what, if anything, was carried away from the table area before the scene was secured.

The images collected here are almost entirely frame grabs from event livestreams, bystander phone video and social-media reposts, so they are low resolution and heavily compressed. What they do preserve is position and sequence: who was standing behind the blue-draped table, who was in front of the barricade, who moved toward the stage after the shot, and what each person was carrying. Several sub-clusters are organized around a single unidentified or partially identified figure, using the shorthand labels that circulated online rather than confirmed identifications. Those labels are working handles for research, not conclusions, and the site does not assert that any person shown here did anything wrong or knew anything in advance.

Start with [Hand Off](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Hand_Off/overview) and [Item](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Item/overview) for the moment-of-transfer frames, then move to the people sub-clusters: [Brian Harpole](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole/overview), [Rick Cutler](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Rick_Cutler/overview), [Blake Harruff Plaid Light Blue](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Blake_Harruff_Plaid_Light/overview) and [Butch Hibbs](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Butch_Hibbs/overview). The wider written analysis lives in [Mic - Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview), [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview), [Persons of Interest](/Suspects/overview) and [Witnesses at UVU](/Witnesses/overview).
