## What This Image Shows

A full-screen mobile screenshot of a Facebook post by **Shaffer Security Group**, dated **Oct 8, 2020**, captured through a DuckDuckGo browser session at 21:21.

The post reads that SSG is proud and honored to call **Dan Flood** one of our Senior Security Specialists, that Dan is a leader on SSG and is a former United States Marine and a professional model, that he is one of the original SSG team members and brings experience, professionalism, leadership and customer service to valued clients, and that Dan is one of the reasons Shaffer Security Group is the best in the industry, closing that he truly is one of the best in the business. The hashtags include Respect, Marines, brothersforlife and ExecutiveProtection. Below the text is a grid of four photographs of a sharply dressed man in professional and security settings, with a plus-six indicator and text fragments reading **TURNIN** and **POINT US**.

This is a primary source rather than a compilation, published by the firm itself, and it is the clearest public documentation of the professional relationship between **Dan Flood** and **Shaffer Security Group**. That relationship matters because the same firm recurs across the security personnel identified around **Charlie Kirk**. A firm praising its own employee alleges nothing about anyone.
