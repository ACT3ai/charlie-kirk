## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide frame under the white canopy with its **PROVE ME WRONG** valance and the red and blue lightning-bolt device. **Charlie Kirk** is seated in the midground centre-right talking with an older man standing in front of him, and rows of metal barricades separate the working area from the onlookers behind. A **kTok** watermark fragment is visible.

A large red circle has been superimposed on the frame to highlight the interaction between the seated host and the standing visitor. That is the annotator's emphasis. What it marks is the ordinary mechanic of the event format: attendees came up one at a time to put a question, which is why so many people are seen close to the table across this whole section.

Establishing that baseline is important, because it is what any claim of unusual movement has to be measured against. A person at the table is not, by itself, anything. The site makes no claim about the man standing here.
