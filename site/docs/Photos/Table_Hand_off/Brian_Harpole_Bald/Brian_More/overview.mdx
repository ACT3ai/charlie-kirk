## About This Cluster

Two press photographs are held here as reference plates for the [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview) comparison set. Both show **Charlie Kirk** seated and speaking into a handheld dynamic microphone at the outdoor event, wearing the white T-shirt with FREEDOM in black block lettering, one from a wire service and one carrying a Getty Images filename.

Their purpose in this section is the background, not the subject. Both were shot on professional equipment at short range, so the people standing behind Kirk - security, production crew, photographers, media rigs and the merchandise table itself - resolve far more clearly than they do in the compressed video stills that make up most of this cluster. When a researcher wants to test whether a blurred figure in a livestream grab is the same man in the blue shirt with the earpiece, these are the frames used as the control. They also fix the seating geometry: where Kirk sat relative to the table, the barricade and the canopy uprights.

Go back to [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview) for the annotated set, or across to [Blake Harruff Plaid Light Blue](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Blake_Harruff_Plaid_Light/overview) for the other identification thread that depends on high-resolution reference frames.
