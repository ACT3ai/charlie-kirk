## What This Image Shows

An agency photograph, filename marked as a Getty Images edit, taken from within the crowd. **Charlie Kirk** is captured mid-speech with an open-mouthed expression and slightly furrowed brow, holding a black dynamic microphone, seated or leaning near a table laden with merchandise and coffee cups. Spectators and professional camera equipment are heavily blurred in the foreground, framing him sharply in the midground.

There is no readable on-screen text in the frame itself.

The merchandise table in the same shot as the speaker is the reason this frame is filed in the Table Hand Off section: it fixes the distance between where Kirk sat and the surface that objects were moving across all afternoon. Every question about who could reach the table, and from where, is a question about that distance.

The coffee cups and merchandise are also part of the physical inventory that a full accounting of the table would need.
