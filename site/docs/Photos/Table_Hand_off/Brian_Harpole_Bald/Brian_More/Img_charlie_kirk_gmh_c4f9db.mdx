## What This Image Shows

A wire-service photograph of **Charlie Kirk** seated and angled toward frame left, speaking into a black dynamic microphone, in the plain white crew-neck T-shirt with the black **FRE** and **M** of FREEDOM visible across the chest. His expression is animated, mouth open mid-speech, and behind him spectators, photographers and media equipment are thrown out of focus in bright daylight.

The legible text is the shirt lettering and a small fragment above.

This frame is held in the working set because of what is behind Kirk rather than Kirk himself. At professional resolution, the media rigs, the crew and the people standing inside the working area resolve clearly enough to be compared against the compressed livestream grabs that make up most of this section. Identification work needs at least one high-quality plate to anchor to, and this is one of very few available.

It also records the handheld microphone, which is separate from the body-worn wireless system at the centre of the mic thread. See [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview), [RODE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic) and [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).
