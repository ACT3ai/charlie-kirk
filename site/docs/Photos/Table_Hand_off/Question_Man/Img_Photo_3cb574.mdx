## What This Image Shows

A wide frame of the plaza as it clears. People are running across the concrete, a camera operator with voluminous dark curly hair carries a black tripod at the left of frame, and several utility carts are parked in the midground against a gravel-covered slope.

A hand-drawn red circle marks a middle-aged man in a dark polo shirt and khaki trousers walking briskly at the right of the frame. There is no on-screen text. The contrast the annotator is pointing at is between running and walking - most of the frame is in flight, and the circled man is not.

That is a thin basis for any inference. Trained protective and medical personnel are taught not to run in a crowd, people react to emergencies at different speeds, and a still frame cannot show what someone was doing a second earlier. The site reproduces the circle as the poster's emphasis and makes no claim about the person marked.
