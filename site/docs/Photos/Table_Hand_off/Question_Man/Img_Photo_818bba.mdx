## What This Image Shows

A tight crop of the figure who recurs through this sub-cluster: a man with dark hair slicked straight back and gathered into a small knot behind his right ear, wearing thick-framed dark sunglasses and a black short-sleeved collared shirt. He is looking off camera to the right in hard daylight, with the background blurred out.

There is no on-screen text. Crops like this are made so that a distinctive appearance - here the bun and the black collared shirt - can be matched across other frames from other cameras, which is how a person is tracked through a scene when nobody knows their name.

No name is attached to this figure on the site, and none is asserted here. The frames in which he appears show him near the vehicle, on the walkway and, in one frame, with a hand marked with what appears to be blood, all of which is consistent with somebody who helped during the emergency. Nothing here implies otherwise. See [Question Man](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Question_Man/overview) and [Witnesses at UVU](/Witnesses/overview).
