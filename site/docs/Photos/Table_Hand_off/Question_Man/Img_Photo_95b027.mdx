## What This Image Shows

A frame on the concrete path outside the courtyard. An older bald man in a blue short-sleeved button-down, light grey trousers and brown shoes is pitching forward at the right of the frame, while several younger men move around him, one stepping forward in what reads as a defensive or catching posture. A dark utility vehicle is parked in front of a gravel embankment behind, and onlookers watch from an elevated platform behind a railing.

There is no on-screen text. Whether the man is falling, being pushed, or being pulled out of the way cannot be determined from a single frame, and the site does not assert any of those readings.

The blue button-down shirt is the same garment type worn by the figure tracked in [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview), and researchers have used frames like this one to argue about whether the same person appears in both places. That comparison is unresolved. No conduct by any person is alleged. See [Question Man](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Question_Man/overview) and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).
