## What This Image Shows

A frame from the seconds of chaos on the stage platform. A table draped in dark blue cloth carries white banners and rows of white baseball caps with red embroidery, a PA speaker stands on the far left, and a man in a blue shirt is falling backward over the edge of the stage with his legs in the air.

A thick hand-drawn red circle has been placed around a different man - in a black polo shirt and sunglasses - crouching in a low, reactive posture in the midground. The visible text reads **YREFY.COM** on the sponsor banner, fragments of **TURNING POINT USA**, and the watermark **X @MJTruthUltra**, which identifies the account that added the circle.

The circle is that poster's emphasis and carries no evidentiary weight of its own; crouching when a shot is fired is what almost everyone in the frame is doing. The site reproduces the annotation with attribution and asserts nothing about the person marked.
