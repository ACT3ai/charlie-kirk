## What This Image Shows

A split-screen product or demonstration image. On the left, a hand rises vertically from the bottom edge, palm away from the camera with the fingers extended, wearing a black wearable band around the palm and back of the hand just below the knuckles. On the right is a computer desktop showing the default macOS Mojave desert wallpaper with a single blue folder icon labelled **PERO** and a cursor beneath it.

There is no other on-screen text.

This is a commercial product image, not an image from the event. It is filed here because of a specific reported claim: the investigation notes record a post by **@RealTruthJustic**, quote-reposting **@DanCampbel44604** on **2026-06-26**, suggesting that a device resembling this brand of gesture-control wearable was visible on a hand in footage from the day, and asking what gesture was being made. That is an unverified social-media claim about a low-resolution image, and the accusatory captions that travelled with the original post are not reproduced here.

The site records the claim with attribution, does not adopt it, and asserts that no person has been shown to have worn any such device or to have done anything improper. Wrist and hand wearables in footage are far more readily explained as watches and fitness bands.
