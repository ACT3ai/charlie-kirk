## What This Image Shows

A wide, slightly high-angle video still of the moment the courtyard breaks. The white pop-up canopy dominates the frame with **PROVE ME WRONG** on the valance and **THE AMERICAN COMEBACK** on the sloped roof, and beneath it the long blue-draped table with red and white caps laid out. Multiple people are running, ducking and scrambling for cover in every part of the frame.

The legible on-screen text includes the doubled banner lettering, the sponsor mark **yrefy**, the watermark **X @THEINTELSCIF**, and the player timestamp **0:13 / 2:06**.

A thirteen-second mark on a two-minute clip means everything visible here happened almost immediately after the shot. This is the single most informative frame in the sub-cluster for reconstructing crowd direction: who moved toward the table and who moved away from it. That distinction is what the whole table jumpers thread turns on, and it can only be settled by frame-by-frame work on the original video, not by stills.
