## About This Cluster

Other is the holding area for four table-adjacent images that belong to no single person cluster. Two are press photographs from a Charlie Kirk vigil in Ohio, showing a middle-aged man in a light blue plaid button-down speaking into a microphone with a red windscreen under stage lighting at night. Two are labeled as Consolidated Training Group employee photographs: one a profile shot of a bearded man in a brown button-down inside the UVU crowd under the white canopy, and one of the same man outdoors in a field in cold-weather tactical gear holding a rifle with a mounted optic.

The plaid shirt is the thread that ties the vigil photographs to this section. A light blue plaid button-down is worn by one of the men repeatedly visible behind the table at UVU, and researchers have used the vigil imagery to argue about whether the two are the same person. That comparison is unresolved on this site. The Consolidated Training Group pair is filed here because researchers have asked, without confirmation, whether private training contractors were present in the crowd; no employment record has been produced publicly to establish that, and no claim is made that any person shown was acting improperly.

For the plaid-shirt identification thread, go to [Blake Harruff Plaid Light Blue](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Blake_Harruff_Plaid_Light/overview). For the contractor and security question, read [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [Companies and Organizations](/Companies_Organizations/overview) if you are tracing corporate links, and [Persons of Interest](/Suspects/overview) for how the site handles unconfirmed identifications.
