## What This Image Shows

A four-panel composite built from an event livestream. The large bottom panel shows the whole setup: the white pop-up canopy with blue and red printing, the long table under it covered in a blue cloth with red and white caps lined up along the left, a metal barricade separating the stage area from the packed crowd, and a vertical backdrop printed with sponsor logos. The three smaller panels at the top isolate individuals, including **Charlie Kirk** in the white FREEDOM T-shirt and another man ringed in red.
