## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a **Hoodline** article dated **April 13, 2025**, headlined about five potential nominees vying for a Fourth District Court vacancy ahead of Judge Lunnen's retirement in Utah. Red digital underlines mark the five names and the sentence about the retirement. A vertical purple watermark carrying the handles **[@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** and **[@SamParkerSenate](/Influencers/x/samparkersenate)** runs down the centre.

The article states that Judge [**Robert Lunnen's**](/court/judge-robert-lunnen) retirement was "set, effective August 1," and that the Fourth District Judicial Nominating Commission had narrowed the field to five: **Von Christiansen**, County Attorney of Beaver County; **Joshua Esplin**, Assistant Director and Trial Attorney with the Utah County Public Defenders Association; **Tony Graf**, then Deputy County Attorney for Davis County and Volunteer Judge Pro Tempore at Salt Lake City Justice Court; **Samuel Pead**, an Assistant United States Attorney; and **Ryan Stack**, Deputy County Attorney in Summit County.

The researcher saved this to establish a date. As of **April 13, 2025**, five months before the assassination, the vacancy that would eventually receive this case was public, named, and had a shortlist. That is a documented timeline fact and nothing more; a judicial vacancy being reported in April says nothing about anything that happened in September. See [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf) and [Judge Robert Lunnen](/court/judge-robert-lunnen).
