## What This Image Shows

A clean digital rendering of a formal Utah state court order. The title is **Order to Hold Without Bail**. The defendant is named as [**Tyler James Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), charged with a capital felony. It is digitally signed by District Court Judge **Shawn R. Howell** and dated **September 12, 2025**.

The date is the reason this page is in the archive. **September 12** is the date the official narrative gives for the formal arrest, with a Utah County affidavit listing a 4:00 AM arrest time after transport. This order issues the same day, holding the defendant without bail on a capital charge.

Set against the argument documented in the [Mirandize](/Photos/Court_Legal/Mirandize/overview) cluster — that a Miranda reading is logged at 6:25 PM on **September 11** in Washington County — the paperwork chain runs a full day behind that timestamp. Researchers argue the gap needs explaining; the counterargument is that a lawful investigative hold permits it. Note also that the judge signing here is not the judge who later presided over the case. Robinson is charged, not convicted. See [Utah County Jail Custody](/court/utah-county-jail-custody) and [Custody Timeline and Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession).
