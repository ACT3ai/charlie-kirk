## What This Image Shows

A composite graphic inside a thick blue border, pairing two screenshots. The top is a **KUTV 2News** article by Jim Spiewak, dated **October 15, 2025** and updated **October 16**, headlined "New Utah judge takes on accused killer's death penalty case in Charlie Kirk murder," with a mugshot and inset photographs. Its lead reads: "Judge Tony Graf Jr. has found himself at the center of one of Utah's most closely watched murder cases less than two months after taking the bench." The bottom is a **Utah State Bar** press release dated **Friday, May 2, 2025**: "Gov. Spencer Cox appoints Tony F. Graf to the Fourth District Court, filling a vacancy left by the retirement of Judge Robert Lunnen. Judicial appointments are subject to confirmation by the Utah Senate." Red underlines mark names and dates in both. The graphic carries the **[@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** handles.

The composite is an argument made by juxtaposition: appointment in **May**, sworn in **August**, capital case in **October**. Both halves are official or mainstream sources and both dates are accurate.

What the pairing does not supply is any mechanism. Judges are assigned cases by court administration, the vacancy predates the shooting by months, and the KUTV story frames the assignment as happenstance. Nothing here shows that any person arranged anything, and no claim of wrongdoing is made against [**Judge Graf**](/court/judge-tony-graf), Governor [**Cox**](/People/spencer-cox), or [**Judge Lunnen**](/court/judge-robert-lunnen). See [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf) and [Case Overview](/court/case-overview).
