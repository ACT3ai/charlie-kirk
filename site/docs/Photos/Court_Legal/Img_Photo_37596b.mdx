## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of the **Utah State Courts** website showing the page for **Judge Robert C. Lunnen**, with a formal medium close-up portrait against a mottled brown background. Across the top sits the **Wayback Machine** archival navigation bar, showing 7 captures between **3 Oct 2023** and **5 Aug 2025**, with markers for 2023, 2025 and 2026. The site header reads "UTAH STATE COURTS — The Judicial Branch of Utah," and the breadcrumb runs District Courts, Fourth District Court.

The reason this is an archive capture rather than a live page is the retirement. Lunnen's retirement took effect **August 1, 2025**, and the last capture in the toolbar is **5 Aug 2025** — the page came down shortly after he left the bench, which is normal practice.

It is preserved here because he is the judge whose departure created the vacancy that **Judge Graf** filled, weeks before the case arrived. Establishing who held the seat, and until when, is basic timeline work. Nothing about either judge's conduct is alleged.
