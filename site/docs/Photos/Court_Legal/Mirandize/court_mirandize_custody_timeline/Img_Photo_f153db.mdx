## What This Image Shows

A summary panel assembling the custody sequence from the filings and public records, shared by **[@VLuvMully](/Influencers/x/vluvmully)** on **July 11, 2026**. The image is cropped to the factual portion.

The sequence it sets out runs: **around 6:00 to 9:00 PM on 9/11**, [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) reportedly arrives at the Washington County Sheriff's Office with his parents and a family friend, choosing Washington County rather than Utah County three and a half hours north; **6:25 PM 9/11**, Miranda rights read and the custody log recorded at the WCSO, per exhibit **Bates 003996-R2**; **around 7:57 PM 9/11**, the Discord messages attributed to Robinson appear; **8:02 PM 9/11**, Sheriff [**Nate Brooksby**](/People/nate-brooksby) says he received a call from a retired deputy about the suspect; **10:00 PM 9/11**, a Utah County booking-sheet entry lists this as the arrest date and time; **around 04:00 AM 9/12**, a Utah County affidavit lists a formal arrest time after transport.

The argument is that the 6:25 PM Washington County reading predates every later booking and affidavit time, and predates the Discord messages by roughly ninety minutes. If that is right, the person-of-interest photos and rooftop video released as a public bulletin around **7:55 PM** went out after the suspect was already in custody. Whether it is right depends on facts still contested in court, including the exact phone-seizure time and the authentication of the Discord account. Robinson is charged, not convicted. See [Custody Timeline and Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession) and [Bodycam and GRAMA No-Footage](/court/mirandize/bodycam-grama-no-footage).
