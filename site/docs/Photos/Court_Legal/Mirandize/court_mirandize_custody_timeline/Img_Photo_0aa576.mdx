## What This Image Shows

The circulated inmate booking record, with the driver-licence number redacted, shared by **[@VLuvMully](/Influencers/x/vluvmully)** on **July 11, 2026**.

The fields read: **ROBINSON, TYLER JAMES**; a Washington, UT address; booking **#460956**; **Arrest Date 09/11/2025 10:00 PM**; Arrest Agency "Other"; **Date In 09/12/2025 01:58 AM**; charges including **76-5-202 Aggravated Murder**, **76-8-306 Obstruction of Justice**, and **76-11-210 Felony Discharge of a firearm causing serious bodily injury**.

Investigators read the gap between the **6:25 PM** Miranda reading and the **10:00 PM** and **01:58 AM** entries as showing that the formal arrest paperwork was generated hours after the suspect was already being held and questioned. The roughly four-hour interval between the listed arrest time and the listed arrival is consistent with the drive from southern to northern Utah, which is the basis of attorney [**Baron Coleman's**](/Influencers/podcasts-baron) argument that the arresting officer must have departed hours earlier. The counterargument, documented on this site, is that a person may lawfully be held on an investigative hold for up to 24 hours before rights are read, so a delay alone establishes nothing. [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) is charged, not convicted. See [Probable Cause Affidavit](/court/mirandize/probable-cause-affidavit) and [Investigative Hold Counterpoint](/court/mirandize/investigative-hold-counterpoint).
