## About This Cluster

These three images are the exhibits behind the single most consequential timing question in the case against [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson). They were circulated by **[@VLuvMully](/Influencers/x/vluvmully)** on **July 11, 2026** and they are the basis for the argument that Robinson was already in custody, and already without his phone, when the Discord messages the state treats as a confession were posted at roughly **7:57 PM** on **September 11, 2025**.

Taken together they form a chain. The first is a defense filing quoting the discovery video **Bates 003996-R2**: at internal timestamp **03:36:53**, logged as **06:25 PM**, an officer reads the Miranda warning, and at **06:26 PM / 03:37:38** Robinson asks for counsel by name. The second is a summary of the assembled custody timeline — Miranda at the Washington County Sheriff's Office at 6:25 PM on 9/11, a Utah County booking entry at 10:00 PM on 9/11, a formal arrest time of 04:00 on 9/12 after transport. The third is the inmate booking sheet itself, listing booking **#460956**, an arrest date of **09/11/2025 10:00 PM**, a Date In of **09/12/2025 01:58 AM**, and charges including aggravated murder. Investigators read the gap between the 6:25 PM reading and the 10:00 PM paperwork as showing that the formal arrest record was generated hours after Robinson was already being held. That reading is contested, and Robinson is charged, not convicted.

Open all three in order — filing, timeline, booking sheet — because the argument only works as a sequence. The full written version, including the counterargument that a lawful investigative hold permits a delay of up to 24 hours, is at [Custody Timeline and Confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession) and the [investigative hold counterpoint](/court/mirandize/investigative-hold-counterpoint). The peer cluster [Image: Mirandized Sept 11](/Photos/Court_Legal/Mirandize/court_sept_11_image/overview) holds the Bates-reference explainer, and the parent [Mirandize](/Photos/Court_Legal/Mirandize/overview) cluster holds the wider set of booking records and filing pages.
