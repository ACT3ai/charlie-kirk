## About This Cluster

This one-image cluster exists to isolate a single artifact: a Google **AI Overview** panel summarizing what public sources say about the discovery exhibit **Bates 003996-R2** and the custody of [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) in September 2025. It sits on its own because it is the clearest illustration of why the Mirandize thread will not settle — the summary contains the contradiction inside itself.

The panel reports that a video shows an encounter involving Robinson on the evening of **September 11, 2025**, referencing timestamp **03:36:53** in the document identified as Bates 003996-R2. In the next bullet it states that the video at that same timestamp shows an in-person encounter with investigators on **September 12, 2025**, and that records indicate Robinson was arrested for the offense on **September 12, 2025, at 4:00 AM**. Same exhibit, same timestamp, two different dates one line apart. Investigators point to this as evidence that the public record itself is inconsistent about when Robinson entered custody; skeptics note that an AI summary is a summary of sources, not a source, and proves nothing about what the video contains. Both readings are worth holding.
