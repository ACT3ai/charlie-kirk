## What This Image Shows

A vertical phone screenshot of an AI search tool in light mode. The panel summarizes a press conference dated **September 12, 2025** at which the arrest of [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) in connection with the assassination of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was announced. Standard mobile interface elements — status bar, navigation tabs, text input — frame the summary.

The file this was saved under records what the researcher took from it: an argument that the suspect was in hand earlier than the official announcement implied. The press conference is the moment the public account was set, and this cluster exists because several documents appear to predate it.

As with the other AI-generated panels in this cluster, the caution is real. A generated summary is a restatement of whatever sources it drew on, it can compress or misdate events, and it is not a record. Its use here is as a marker of what the public narrative was understood to be, to be checked against the booking sheet and the filing transcripts filed alongside it — not as proof in its own right. Robinson is charged, not convicted. See [Mirandize Record](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview) and [Official Statements](/Legal/Official-Statements).
