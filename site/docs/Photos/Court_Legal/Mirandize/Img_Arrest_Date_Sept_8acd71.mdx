## What This Image Shows

A flat scan of the **Utah County Sheriff Inmate Booking Sheet and PC Statement** for [**Tyler James Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson), carrying demographic details, arrest details, the charge list and a probable cause narrative. A large faint blue circular watermark lies over the page, and selected text is picked out in yellow highlighter.

The highlighting marks the arrest-date fields. That is the whole reason this version exists alongside the unmarked copy in this cluster: a researcher wanted the date and time entries readable at a glance, because those entries are the fixed points every version of the timeline has to accommodate.

Circulated readings of this record give an arrest date of **09/11/2025 at 10:00 PM** and a date in of **09/12/2025 at 01:58 AM**, with charges including aggravated murder, obstruction of justice and felony discharge of a firearm. The **6:25 PM** Miranda reading documented in the defense filings precedes both. Whether that gap reflects a lawful investigative hold or something else is the argument, and it is not resolved by this page. Robinson is charged, not convicted.
