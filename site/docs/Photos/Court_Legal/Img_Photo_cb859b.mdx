## What This Image Shows

A mobile browser screenshot of **Google Trends** at trends.google.com, comparing **Tony graf** and **Robert lunnen** in **Utah** from **1/1/25 to 10/19/25**. A tooltip is open on the week of **Sep 21 - 27, 2025**, showing **Tony graf** at 100 and **Robert lunnen** at 0. The **[@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** watermark runs across the chart.

This is the in-state control for the Israel charts filed beside it. Inside Utah, interest in the new judge's name is flat all year and then goes to maximum in late September 2025 — two weeks after the assassination, as the case moved through its early hearings.

That is what news-driven search looks like, and saying so is not a concession that weakens the archive. The value of this panel is precisely that it shows a normal pattern, giving readers a baseline to compare against the pre-event spikes the researcher flags in the Israel panels. The retiring judge's name never moves at all. Neither judge is accused of anything. See [Google Searches](/GoogleSearches/overview) and [Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf).
