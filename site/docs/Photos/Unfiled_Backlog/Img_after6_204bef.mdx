## What This Image Shows

A vertical frame of the outdoor preparation area at the event. In the foreground, a grey staging platform sits on two black molded transport cases on the grass; a warning tag reading HEAVY hangs from one case handle. Behind them are a collapsible metal table frame and a larger table under a fitted dark blue cloth with the tour's red and white lightning-bolt logo.

Further back, concrete bleachers or retaining walls run across the frame, and staff and security personnel move near a metal crowd-control barrier in direct sunlight. Only fragments of text resolve in the image.

The value of this frame is spatial: it fixes the relationship between the staging area, the barrier line and the concrete terracing behind. Those sightlines and access routes are the raw material for the reconstruction work collected in [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
