## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Chris Menahan**, handle @infolibnews, posted an hour before capture. The embedded video player at 2:23 shows a journalist speaking against bookshelves, captioned with a Grayzone lower third.

The post reads: "Zionist billionaire Bill Ackman 'hammered' Charlie Kirk during an August 'intervention' in the Hamptons for platforming critics of Israel at TPUSA events, Max Blumenthal reports." The quoted remarks in the clip read: "Ackman, according to multiple sources, had this meeting to basically bully Charlie Kirk into submission," "Charlie Kirk walked away, according to one person, feeling like there was an attempt to blackmail him," and "He refused any offers of funding, and also refused the offer to travel to Israel and meet with Netanyahu."

The **Hamptons meeting** is documented in this archive as one of the load-bearing episodes in the motive argument, because it places a reported confrontation over Kirk's guest list roughly a month before **September 10, 2025**. Every element of it is sourced to anonymous accounts relayed by one journalist, is characterized in that journalist's own words, and is denied or unaddressed by those named; it is reported here as a claim.
