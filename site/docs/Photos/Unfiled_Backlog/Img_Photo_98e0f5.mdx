## What This Image Shows

A broadcast graphic under a bold black header. The left side shows a shared screen displaying a detailed spreadsheet timeline; the right side shows a video feed of a man with a dark moustache in a black hoodie speaking into a microphone.

The header reads: "WHO WAS ON THE PHONE WITH Mikey McCoy." The spreadsheet columns cover location, online status and event description, with rows including search-trend entries for terms such as the **Losee Center**, a regional hospital, and several personal names, tagged to locations including Israel, DC and Florida, interleaved with dated events reading "Infamous Hamptons meeting takes place" and "Charlie on Megyn Kelly, lashes out about donors pressuring him."

The sheet is an attempt to place search-interest data and known events on a single timeline, which is a legitimate structure and rests on a data source with real limits, since regional search interest identifies no searcher. The dated events themselves, the **Hamptons meeting** and the broadcast remarks about donor pressure, are tracked separately in this archive. The header poses a question about a phone call; making calls during an emergency is ordinary, and no wrongdoing by anyone named is established.
