## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an AI-powered search interface showing a question and its generated answer, with two bright red rounded annotation boxes added over the image.

The question reads: "Did TPUsa rent out the State Farm Stadium for September 21 in May 2025." The generated answer reads: "Yes, Turning Point USA (TPUSA) rented State Farm Stadium for September 21, 2025, with the rental agreement secured in May 2025. The venue hosted a large public memorial service for TPUSA founder Charlie Kirk, who was assassinated on September 10, 2025." The overlaid caption reads: "GUESS WHEN TPUSA RENTED THE STADIUM FOR CHARLIE'S 'FUNERAL!'"

The implication being drawn is obvious, and it does not survive scrutiny in this form. The source is a chatbot, not a contract or a venue record, and language models routinely conflate dates and invent specifics. Large organizations also book major stadium dates months ahead for reasons that have nothing to do with any later event. Nothing here establishes that anyone knew anything in advance. If a booking date matters, the underlying rental agreement is what would have to be produced. See [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and [Before the Shooting](/Before/overview).
