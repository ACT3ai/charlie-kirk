## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, posted 47 minutes before capture. The embedded video player shows a medium-wide view of the **Woodbury Building** with several people, described in the post as police officers, walking across its tiered flat roof. The player is at 0:55 and the building label WOODBURY BUILDING is overlaid on the frame.

The post reads: "MYSTERIOUS: If Kirk Was REALLY SHOT From the Losee Bldg. Then... Why Were There Cops ALL OVER The Roof of the WOODBURY Bldg? Check this: Video and image show cops avoiding the edge/corner while leaning across Kirk's 4 for a better view. They carefully circle around that spot,"

The official account places the shot on the **Losee Center** roof, so where responders concentrated afterward is a fair question to put to the record. Movement on an adjacent roof has ordinary explanations as well as the one the post proposes, and the poster's reading of the officers' path is an interpretation of a low-resolution video.
