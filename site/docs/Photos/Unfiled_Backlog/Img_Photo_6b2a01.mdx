## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Candace Owens**, handle @RealCandaceO, dated **Oct 11**, replying to her own earlier post and carrying a repost marker from an account shown as Project Constitution.

The post reads: "With all we have crowdsourced, it looks like a decision to murder Charlie Kirk in Utah was made around July 18th. A military meeting with foreign leaders took place on July 20th on U.S soil. The plane flew into Provo direct from France on September 4th and stayed until just after Charlie was assassinated- flying out to Wilmington on that day. The plane returned home to Egypt the following day on September 11th."

The aircraft described matches the Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT** thread this archive tracks in detail, including its arrival window at Provo and its departure on **September 10, 2025**. The flight movements can be checked against public records; the inference that a decision was taken on a particular date is the commentator's reading of that pattern, not an established fact, and no person is accused of a crime here.
