## What This Image Shows

A cropped screenshot of a single post from an account shown as John Wade, handle @LtJohnWade, dated **Oct 18**.

The post reads: "You also can't talk about the assassination of Charlie Kirk without talking about MOSSAD; we have had this fully confirmed by trusted intel sources. The REAL assassins stood to Kirk's right and were assisted by one and possibly more of Kirk's own security team. Sad and alarming."

This is an accusation of participation in a killing, made against unnamed members of a small identifiable group, sourced entirely to unnamed "trusted intel sources" with nothing produced. No evidence for it appears in the frame or elsewhere in this archive, no such finding has been made, and it is not adopted here. It is recorded because the claim circulated widely and the archive documents what circulated. The documented questions about the protective arrangements that day are handled in the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) and [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).
