## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **James Li**, handle @5149jamesli, posted seven hours before capture. Attached is a cropped list of state names, legislator names, party affiliations and citation numbers, plus a quoted post carrying a group photograph.

The post reads: "This is a list of the 250 United States state legislators who took part in the '50 States 1 Israel' conference - to sell out America in exchange for foreign cash. SHAME!!" The visible list runs by state from Delaware through Maryland, naming senators and representatives from both parties, including several marked as delegation co-leads.

The attendance list is the checkable part; the characterization of attendees' motives is the poster's, is unsupported by anything in the frame, and is not adopted here. Attending a policy conference is lawful and ordinary. The relevance to this archive is the broader lobbying and foreign-influence material it tracks, including the funding threads documented under [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [Political Context](/political_context/overview).
