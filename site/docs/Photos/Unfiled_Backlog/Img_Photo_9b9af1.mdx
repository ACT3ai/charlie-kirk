## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Alex Barnicoat, handle @AlexBarnicoat_, posted four hours before capture, with an embedded video thumbnail of a young man with bleached hair speaking to camera indoors at 1:51.

The post reads: "The Israeli Government has sent me a letter, and I could be arrested for my videos... I'm accused of being a 'national security threat to an ally' and 'exposing state secrets'. They just banned my tiktok. Help me fight them, and consider donating."

The letter itself is not shown in the frame, so the characterization of its contents rests on the poster's account. The pattern it describes, a legal threat paired with a platform removal, is the same pairing documented against people covering this investigation, which is why it sits in the backlog.
