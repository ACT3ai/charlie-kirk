## What This Image Shows

A split-screen frame. The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the document is not quoted here.

The statement itself is held in full elsewhere in this cluster, where the substance and its limits are set out: grave allegations against named living people, sourced to a single anonymous informant, publicly rejected by those they concern, unverified and not adopted here. This frame records that the written version was presented on air as well as posted.
