## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post from an account shown as ThePatrioticBlonde, handle [@ImBreckWorsham](/Influencers/x/imbreckworsham), posted ten hours before capture. The layout is the standard light-mode X post with profile image, verification badge, timestamp and engagement counts.

The post reads: "According to the FBI's story, in order to make the timeline fit based on the alleged video of him on the roof after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot, Tyler Robinson took off the clothing he was wearing when he shot Kirk, changed into a second set of clothes, disassembled his firearm, loaded it into his backpack, and jumped off the roof....in 34 seconds."

The compressed timeline is one of the most repeated objections raised against the official account. The investigation record separately tracks reported clothing changes, the backpack, and the interval between the shot and the rooftop video. Nothing in this post is established fact, and **Tyler Robinson** has not been convicted; the claim is presented here as the poster's argument.
