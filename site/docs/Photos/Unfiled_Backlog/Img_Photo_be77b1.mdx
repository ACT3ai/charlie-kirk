## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **MJTruthUltra**, posted an hour before capture, with several embedded screenshots of earlier posts and account states.

The post reads: "Reminder... it's December now. An X user by the name of TallyHallAlbum allegedly said 'it'd be funny if someone like Charlie Kirk got shot on September 10'. After Charlie was shot.. This same user then wrote on Sept 13 - 'Donald Trump. December 14th.' The users account was then deactivated... I just checked and it is now active again. Those tweets appear to have been deleted. Care to share anything more @TallyHallAlbum now that you've reactivated your account?" The embedded panels show the two quoted posts with their timestamps and view counts, and an error state reading "Something went wrong. Try again."

This is the follow-up to the earlier capture of the same claim held in this cluster, and it adds the account lifecycle: deactivated, reactivated, posts gone. The poster's own wording is careful, using "allegedly" and reporting only what he could check. Nothing here establishes that anyone had advance knowledge of anything, and screenshots of deleted posts from anonymous accounts cannot be verified after the fact.
