## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Robin Basil, handle @RealRobinBasil, timestamped 10:36 AM, **Sep 24, 2025**. The embedded photograph shows six people posing indoors around a round table with a film premiere poster; a blurry video still sits in the lower-right corner.

The post reads: "This is not UVU Police or Charlie's security. This was a TPUSA sponsor rep from Yrefy. Looks a lot like Laine Shoneberger, Chief Investment Officer and Managing Partner but difficult to know for sure. Photo below is from his FB where he is dressed in company attire, pictured with Larry Elder, Brandon Tatum and Chad Jackson for the film premiere of Uncle Tom in August 2022." A follow-up reads: "Here's the screen grab from the video you just shared where you can see the logo on the shirt is Yre[fy]."

The method here is careful by the standards of this material: the poster reasons from a shirt logo rather than a face, names a candidate, and states plainly that it is "difficult to know for sure." The identification is unconfirmed. Attending a company event and wearing company attire is ordinary work, and nothing here indicates wrongdoing by anyone.
