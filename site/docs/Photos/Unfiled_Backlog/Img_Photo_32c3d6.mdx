A screenshot of a post from an account shown as 1984, handle @TheOfficial1984, posted four hours before capture, with an attached black table headed "Confirmed Data Recipients (2025)."

The post reads: "The ADL is controlling the Charlie Kirk narrative on X through its OHI V3 neural network and in coordination with Israeli intelligence. The ADL feeds flagged content directly to CHEQ (israeli intelligence) and they remove it." The table's structure is plausible in form and its contents are unverified; labelling a commercial security vendor as an intelligence service is the poster's characterization, not a fact.
