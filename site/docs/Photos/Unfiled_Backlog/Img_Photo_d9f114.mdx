## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Peter Bernegger**, handle @PeterBernegger, posted 21 minutes before capture, with an embedded composite of a text block and a photograph of a man speaking, plus a reposted video.

The post opens with a quotation: "In 4 hours I can invent 100 million [election] records for you." It then sets out biographical claims about the speaker and his family, describes him as "the mastermind behind stealing the 2020 election," and states that the quotation comes from a confession that officials "print false electoral records."

This is election material rather than case material. The quotation's context is not shown in the frame, the biographical and criminal claims made about the named living individuals are unverified and unproven, and the sourcing given is a single third party.
