## What This Image Shows

A vertical mobile screenshot of a public social media profile page, with the employer line picked out in yellow highlight.

The page reads: "Duane Burt, 1.1K friends," and under Details: "Engineer at Microchip Technology, Went to Preston High School / Joseph City high school, Lives in Mesa, Arizona, From Mesa, Arizona."

This is the person named as registered agent on the Arizona corporate filing held elsewhere in this cluster, with the same Mesa location. Matching a registry agent to a real person is a standard step in corporate tracing, and everything shown here is publicly posted biography. Nothing on this page indicates any wrongdoing by anyone, and no connection to the events of **September 10, 2025** is established by it.
