## About This Cluster

The **Unfiled Backlog** is the intake queue of the photo archive: images gathered during the investigation of the [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) at [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview) that have not yet been filed into a named concept cluster. Most are screenshots of X posts by independent researchers, stills pulled from event video, maps, flight-tracking captures, and document photographs. Because this is the raw feed of the investigation, the backlog touches nearly every thread of the case at once, from the months before the shooting to the court case of [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson).

Recurring subjects in these images track the investigation's open questions. Researchers have raised acoustics and ballistics arguments about the true direction of the shot, questions about the bolt-action rifle reported found in a wooded area only after federal agents directed a re-search, and doubts about the official single-shooter account. Other images concern aircraft the investigation follows, including the Egyptian-registered [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) and the [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview) surveillance plane, and the reported donor pressure on Kirk over his shifting [Israel](/Israel/overview) positions in his final weeks. Every claim that appears inside a screenshot is the poster's claim, reported here with attribution rather than adopted as a conclusion.

Start with the small filed sub-clusters, which each hold one tight question: [Fake Policeman](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Fake_Policeman/overview) and [Fake Cop](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Fake_Cop/overview) on the badge-wearing man filmed at UVU, [Post Before Assas](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Post_Before_Assas/overview) on an alleged threatening post made the day before, and [Black Vehicles](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Black_Vehicles/overview) on the SUV motorcade footage. Then browse the numbered images and follow their links out to the site sections they support, especially [Before the Shooting](/Before/overview), [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview), and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview).
