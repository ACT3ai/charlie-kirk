## What This Image Shows

A wide still from the event recording. At frame left a speaker sits under the white canopy at a table draped in a dark blue cloth and a white branded runner; the canopy valance carries the tour logo with its stylized flag lightning bolt. Event staff and security stand on the grass behind metal barricades, and a large crowd fills the tiered grass slope beyond.

On-screen text includes the PROVE ME WRONG lettering and a player timestamp of 6:25 / 13:58.

The seated-speaker arrangement, low and central with the crowd banked above and behind, recurs across every frame of this recording. It is the same geometry that made the elevated positions around the plaza consequential. See [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview) and [Tent](/Tent/overview).
