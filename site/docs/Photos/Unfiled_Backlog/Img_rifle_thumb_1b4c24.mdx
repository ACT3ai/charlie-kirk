## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution overhead frame of a bolt-action rifle with a telescopic scope, lying in a plain open cardboard box set on the ground beside grass. The lighting is a harsh direct source, consistent with a flashlight or camera flash at night. The rifle has a dark synthetic stock with textured grip surfaces and a silver-finished receiver and barrel.

The frame carries no on-screen text, caption or agency marking.

This is a smaller companion to the fuller rifle frame elsewhere in this cluster, and it is kept because the archive prefers to hold every version of an image rather than only the best copy.
