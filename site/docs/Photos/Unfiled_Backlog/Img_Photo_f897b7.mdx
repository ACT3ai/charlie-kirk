## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a single post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, posted an hour before capture.

The post reads: "Who TF was the construction company that raced to UVU days after Charlie Kirk's assassination? They ripped up the bloody grass where he was shot and slapped down a concrete pad - PLUS re-mudded the tunnel roof behind it. No-one knows who they are. Official silence = sus AF." It then names a company and an owner sourced only to a chatbot, and immediately undercuts itself: "If true? BOMBSHELL cover-up. I've scoured the net for facts - CRICKETS. No names, nada. I have not been able to disprove this yet unfortunately... Know anything? Company? Owner? Pics? DM or, email, or reply NOW."

The underlying question is documented and serious: this archive tracks the speed of the landscaping and concrete work over the spot where **Charlie Kirk** was shot, and who authorized it. The company and ownership claim in the post is not. It is sourced to an AI chatbot, the poster states he found no facts to support it, and "I have not been able to disprove this" is not evidence of anything. No family member of any suspect is shown here to have done anything. See [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview), [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
