## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Publius, handle @OcrazioCornPop, posted three hours before capture. The attached graphic places two encyclopedia-style election infoboxes side by side.

The post reads: "NJ Governor Election: Democrat votes JUMPED from 1.34M in 2021 to 1.81M in 2025. +465,000 VOTES, over 35% INCREASE! HOW IS THIS POSSIBLE?" The infoboxes show 1,339,471 against 1,255,185 for the earlier race, and 1,805,244 against 1,384,601 for the later one, with reporting at 95 percent as of **Nov 6, 2025**.

This is election material rather than case material. Turnout varies substantially between cycles for ordinary reasons, and the Republican total in the same comparison also rose by more than 100,000; the framing is the poster's. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
