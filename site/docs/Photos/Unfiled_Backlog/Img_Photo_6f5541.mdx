## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of a thread from an account shown as 1984, handle @TheOfficial1984, with a large dark text card and replies below.

The post reads: "Why would a company, founded by members israeli intelligence, funded by Peter Thiel, be moderating content for US election integrity on X?" The card reads: "Cyabra's Role with X (Formerly Twitter): A Detailed Overview. Cyabra, a Tel Aviv-based cybersecurity firm founded in 2020 by former IDF Unit 8200 alumni (Ofer Ravid, Avi Koren, and Eran Lederman), specializes in AI-driven detection of disinformation, bot networks, and inauthentic online behavior. The company uses machine learning to analyze social media profiles, behavioral patterns, and engagement clusters, identifying coordinated campaigns. With over 40 employees (many IDF reservists), Cyabra has been pivotal in X's efforts to combat bots and fake accounts since Elon Musk's 2022 acquisition. Its role evolved from a pre-acquisition consultant to an ongoing analytical partner, particularly for election integrity and content moderation."

The firm, its founders' backgrounds and its platform relationship are matters of public record and the card describes them in ordinary commercial terms. The question posed is a legitimate one about vendor selection for content analysis; it is not an allegation of wrongdoing, and none is established here.
