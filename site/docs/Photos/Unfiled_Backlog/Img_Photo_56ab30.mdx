## What This Image Shows

A long vertical screenshot of a thread from an account shown as 1984, handle @TheOfficial1984, each post carrying a black-and-white technical slide.

The opening post reads: "Did you know the ADL is assigning your social credit score on X using machine learning and neural networks created at Uc Berkley D-Lab? Online Hate Index V3 (OHI V3). It can identity you by your voice from analyzing spaces. It flags the content. Israeli intelligence removes it." The slides describe: "The transcribed text is fed into OHI V3's core model, a fine-tuned transformer-based neural network (built on architectures like BERT or RoBERTa)," with sections on "Semantic Analysis: Detects implicit bias, dog-whistles," "Contextual Scoring," and "Thresholding: Content scoring above 0.9 (high confidence) triggers alerts."

This is the extended version of the material held elsewhere in this cluster. The classifier described is a published research system and the technical account is plausible on its face; the claims about voice identification from audio spaces and about a foreign intelligence service acting on the output are the poster's, unverified, and not adopted here. Automated flagging is directly relevant to how quickly material from this investigation disappeared from platforms.
