## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by an account shown as **John Cullen**, marked BREAKING and posted an hour before capture. Below the text sits an embedded Flightradar24 playback screen: a map of the Provo, Utah area with the track of an aircraft registered **N59906**, and telemetry panels reading barometric altitude 19,000, ground speed 212 KTS, track 44 degrees, squawk 4323, aircraft type Piper Navajo Chieftain, serial number 31-7552027, departing and returning to Provo with a 5:35 PM UTC time.

The post reads: "A 2nd 'highly-suspicious aircraft' in the vicinity of Charlie Kirk's murder? What was N59906 doing, flying this pattern above the Charlie Kirk murder scene, one hour prior to his shooting? This plane isn't supposed to be airborne, at all.. Its registration is 'In Question.'"

Aircraft over the venue are one of the investigation's longest-running threads, and the record here also tracks a Piper N59906 associated with MARC Inc. The claims in this post are the poster's, including the assertion about the registration status, and they have not been independently confirmed here.
