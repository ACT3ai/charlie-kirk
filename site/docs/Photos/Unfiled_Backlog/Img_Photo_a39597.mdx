## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Shadowbanned, handle @GaRmOnBoZiA79, posted 39 minutes before capture, above a low-resolution video still of a man holding a microphone on stage in front of the Yrefy and Turning Point USA backdrop.

The post reads: "One other piece of evidence that shows that this is impossible, is that if the bullet hit Charlie's vertebrae and bounced around in his neck, it would have resulted in INSTANT MUSCLE failure and collapse given the severing of his nerves. He would NOT have kept holding the mic."

The argument is a physiological one and it is testable in principle: a cervical spinal cord injury at the level described would be expected to produce immediate loss of motor control below the lesion. This archive tracks the **C2 vertebra** and bullet-angle material, the wound-location dispute and the shirt-movement analysis as connected threads. The premise, that the bullet struck the vertebrae as described, is itself part of the disputed official account rather than an agreed fact.
