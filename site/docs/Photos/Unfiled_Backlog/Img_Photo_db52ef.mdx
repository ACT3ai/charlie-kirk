## What This Image Shows

A close capture of a printed document page in black text on white paper, partly obscured by a translucent green bar across the bottom and a small pop-up box at the middle left.

The text sets out a timeline involving **YREFY**, **Charlie Kirk** and **Turning Point**. The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here.

This is a companion to the other Yrefy timeline capture held in this cluster. The sponsor's branding appears on the tent valance, the barrier banners and the table runners in nearly every event photograph in this archive, which makes the commercial arrangements behind the tour a documented part of the venue rather than a side issue.
