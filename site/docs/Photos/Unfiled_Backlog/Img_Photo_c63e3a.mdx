## What This Image Shows

A frame from a live stream broadcast. The main image shows a large crowd gathered behind metal barricades in front of a modern grey-panelled building on a bright day. Two small picture-in-picture commentator feeds sit in the upper left, and a live chat panel overlays the lower right.

The only legible extracted text is a single fragment from the chat panel.

Live streams are among the most useful sources in this archive because they were broadcast in real time, which makes later alteration harder and preserves the timing of what was said as it was said. The barricade line and the building face in this frame are the same fixed references the position arguments rely on.
