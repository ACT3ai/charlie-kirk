## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide frame of a large crowd packed onto the terraced grass plaza of the campus, multi-story buildings on all sides and distant mountains beyond under clear sky. To the right, onlookers line an elevated balcony rail above the plaza.

The overlays are the important part: a burned-in timecode reading 12:23:18:28 in the upper right, a CROWDSOURCE THE TRUTH watermark at lower left, and a player position of 1:26 / 3:11.

A burned-in timecode is worth more than almost anything else in this archive, because it lets a frame be placed on the timeline independently of anyone's account. **12:23** is the minute the shot is generally placed at, which makes this frame a reference point that other footage can be aligned against. The balcony line of spectators also marks another elevated position with a clear view.
