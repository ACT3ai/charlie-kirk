## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as TPV Sean, handle @tpvsean, posted 32 minutes before capture. The embedded video thumbnail shows a man speaking in a studio with a photograph of **Charlie Kirk** holding a microphone on the screen behind him, running to 19:38.

The post reads: "TPUSA Donor Reveals Charlie Kirk Was Granted Access to Epstein Client List Before He Was Killed. A powerful TPUSA donor has just gone on the record - and what they're saying changes everything. According to the whistleblower, Charlie Kirk wasn't just asking for the Epstein client list - he was granted access to it. Insiders within the government, fed up with the cover-ups and sealed files, trusted him to reveal what no one else dared to touch."

The source is unnamed, the claim is uncorroborated, and "gone on the record" is doing a lot of work in a post where no record is shown. What is documented is that Kirk was publicly pressing on the Epstein files before **September 10, 2025**, and his own post on the subject is held elsewhere in this cluster. The step from public advocacy to private access is unsupported here. See [Motive](/Motive/overview), [Charlie Kirk](/Charlie/overview) and [Intelligence](/intelligence/overview).
