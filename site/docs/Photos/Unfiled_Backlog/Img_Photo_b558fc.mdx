## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Jon Bray**, handle @jonaaronbray, dated **Sep 20**. Below the text is a product illustration of a semi-transparent **RODE Wireless PRO** transmitter with its black rectangular magnetic clip assembly floating above it.

The post reads: "This is the murder weapon. The only projectile that hit Charlie was the magnetic clasp used to hold his RODE Wireless PRO microphone under his shirt. Rectangular wound.. Rectangular projectile."

This is the sharpest single statement of the exploding-microphone hypothesis in the cluster: a named hardware component, a specific geometry, and a claimed match to the wound shape. This archive tracks the **RODE Wireless PRO** transmitter, the reported burnt object, the shirt movement and the wound description as connected threads, including the separate question of why the microphone was worn under the shirt rather than over it. The independent ballistics presentation held elsewhere in this cluster argues for a supersonic rifle round instead. This is a hypothesis, not a finding.
