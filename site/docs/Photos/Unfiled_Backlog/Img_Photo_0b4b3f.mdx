## What This Image Shows

A clean white document page of typed black text listing private aircraft tail numbers and flight itineraries for dates in late 2022 and early 2023. Several passages are highlighted in yellow and one line in light blue.

The first two lines read: "TAIL#: SU-BND (Gulfstream 4) 12-16 PASSENGERS" and "TAIL#: SU-BTT (Falcon 7X) 12-16 PASSENGERS." The text extraction sidecar returned no further usable lines, so the itineraries themselves are not quoted here.

Both registrations are central to this investigation. The Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT** is the aircraft tracked into Provo in the days before **September 10, 2025**, and **SU-BND** is documented alongside it in the record's Egyptian aircraft material. A working sheet that establishes airframe type and passenger capacity going back years is exactly what is needed to test claims about who those aircraft carry.
