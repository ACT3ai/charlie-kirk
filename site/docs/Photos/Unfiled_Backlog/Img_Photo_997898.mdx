## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Holly Kesler, handle @hmkesler, with an embedded video of a government meeting room, board members seated around a U-shaped table.

The post reads: "EXPLOSIVE TESTIMONY AND GROUND-STANDING BY HERO ELECTION BOARD MEMBER JULIE ADAMS! Georgia - In Fulton County's Board of Elections meeting yesterday (Nov 7, 2025), Julie Adams was the ONLY one pushing for transparency on the DOJ's demand for 2020 ballots and records. While Chair Sherri Allen tried to shove it into secret 'executive session' and even ordered the director NOT to answer basic questions - claiming bogus 'litigation' - Adams stood firm: 'I'm a board member, and I'm allowed to ask.'" It quotes her further: "I think with this letter from the DOJ that we should comply... I am not fighting at all... I'm not obstructing anything from the SEB or the Department of Justice."

This is election material rather than case material. The quoted remarks are from a public meeting; the characterizations of other board members' motives are the poster's. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Legal](/Legal/overview).
