## What This Image Shows

A split-screen broadcast frame. **Candace Owens** is seated behind a laptop and microphone in a studio at frame left; at frame right, a shared Google Docs page shows a timeline of events running from May to July, with some lines highlighted in light purple.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the timeline entries are not quoted here.

The summer months matter to the motive argument, because that is the window in which the reported donor conflict, the **Hamptons meeting** and the shifting positions on Israel are all placed. Reading a working document on air is a form of sourcing, and the underlying entries would need checking against the primary records this archive holds.
