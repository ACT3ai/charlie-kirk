## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, posted three hours before capture, with a video paused at 0:08 of 23 seconds showing a man in a light-colored shirt walking away along a suburban sidewalk beside a white SUV.

The post reads: "EXCLUSIVE: NEW FOOTAGE Shows FBI Agents Spotted ON 925 S ST in Orem Just Hours After Kirk Assassination... Location: 670 925 Street, Orem - agents sweeping the area, going yard to yard, clearly looking for something they don't want the public to see. Remember - officials have released only a couple blurry stills and one sketchy 'roof jump' video... yet here's proof they were all over the neighborhood that SAME DAY. Why have we NOT seen any more video from the neighborhood that day, nor videos of the Challenger."

The closing question is the strongest part: a neighbourhood searched door to door is a neighbourhood full of doorbell and security cameras, and very little of that footage has surfaced. This is the fuller version of the post held in shorter form elsewhere in this cluster. The identification of the man as an agent and the characterization of intent are the poster's.
