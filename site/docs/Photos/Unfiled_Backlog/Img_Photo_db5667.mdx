## What This Image Shows

An outdoor frame of the **American Comeback Tour** setup on a grass lawn beside a concrete building. A white pop-up canopy carries THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR across the peak and repeats PROVE ME WRONG along the valance, with matching banners on the metal barriers. On-screen text also shows the Yrefy.com sponsor panel.

A uniformed officer in dark clothing stands in the center foreground. To the right, a man in a blue t-shirt stands with a holstered handgun on his hip. The grass around them is scattered with paper scraps and bottles, indicating the event was already well underway or over.

Who was armed, who was uniformed, and which of them answered to the university, local police or the private detail are unresolved questions across the record. The investigation has logged accounts of security being restricted or blocked, and no single roster of the people posted around the tent has been published.
