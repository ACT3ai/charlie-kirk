## What This Image Shows

A four-panel visual study. Each panel steps closer to the same target: a small illuminated rectangular fixture mounted on a concrete wall structure overlooking the crowd, isolated with red circles and arrows.

The overlay text reads "THE LIGHT OVER THE GRASSY KNOLL.." with a credit to an account shown as [@THEINTELSCIF](/Influencers/x/intel-scif).

This is the extended version of the composite held elsewhere in this cluster, and the extra panels are the point: a progressive zoom is how a researcher shows that the object in the wide shot and the object in the close-up are the same thing rather than two different features. What the fixture actually is remains unestablished. See [Technology and Surveillance](/technology_surveillance/overview), [Cameras](/cameras/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
