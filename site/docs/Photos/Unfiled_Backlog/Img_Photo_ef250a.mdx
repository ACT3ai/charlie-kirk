## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as @HustleBitch_, posted ten minutes before capture, summarizing remarks it attributes to **Candace Owens**.

The post reads: "CANDACE OWENS: 'THE CORONER WAS BRAND SPANKING NEW'... AND SO WAS THE SURGEON. Candace reveals everyone connected to Charlie Kirk's case was new." It then lists: "A new FBI director and new FBI members in Utah after 'cleaning house.' A new CEO and a new lead surgeon at the hospital, ranked extremely low in treating traumatic neck wounds, and not even the closest hospital to UVU. A new judge, appointed May 2025 by Gov. Cox. And the coroner? 'BRAND. SPANKING. NEW.'" It closes with the quoted line "9/10 was everybody's first day on the job" and the question of whether that is coincidence.

Several of the underlying facts are separately documented in this archive: the hospital chosen was not the closest to campus, the medical examiner paperwork has been questioned, and the judge assigned to the case took the bench in 2025. Personnel turnover is not evidence of a plan, and the framing here is the commentator's argument rather than a finding.
