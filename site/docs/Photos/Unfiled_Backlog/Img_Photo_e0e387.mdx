## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as 1984, handle @TheOfficial1984, posted an hour before capture, with an embedded graphic headed "Insights and Implications." A quoted repost from an account shown as **Project Constitution** sits below with a video thumbnail.

The post reads: "~80% of Israel's cybersecurity exports trace to Israeli intelligence shin bet Unit 8200. Anything on the internet is infested and spied on with machine learning neural networks and a pipeline between the adl and israeli intelligence aka x content moderation." The graphic is more measured than the caption: "Prevalence: ~80% of Israel's cybersecurity exports trace to Unit 8200, per 2025 Drop Site News database (1,400+ veterans in U.S. tech, including X partners)," then "Concerns: Ties raise bias fears," citing content flagged as disinformation during 2024, and then "Gaps: Opaque contracts (e.g., NDAs) limit full disclosure; no 2025 X transparency report lists all."

The panel's own "Gaps" section is the honest part: it states plainly that the contracts are not public and the claim cannot be fully checked. Veterans of a military technical unit founding security companies is documented and unremarkable on its own; the pipeline asserted in the caption is not established.
