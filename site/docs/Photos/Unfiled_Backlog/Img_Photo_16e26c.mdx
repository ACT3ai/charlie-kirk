## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of two posts from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, the upper dated **Nov 26, 2025**. It carries a cropped image of a published daily schedule with highlighted sections and blue indicator arrows.

The post reads: "BOMBSHELL: Utah Gov's Secret Meetings with Egypt and France Just EXPOSED - 13 Days After Kirk's Assassination! WHAT ARE THEY HIDING? On Sept 10, 2025, Charlie Kirk was gunned down at UVU. The nation mourned. The manhunt raged. But 13 days later-Sept 23-Cox turned Utah's Capitol" The schedule itself lists ordinary state business alongside the highlighted entry: "11:30 a.m. Meeting with the Ambassador of Egypt, Location: Gold Room, Utah State Capitol," surrounded by budget hearings for the Utah Trust Lands Administration, the State Treasurer, Workforce Services and Veterans and Military Affairs.

The schedule is a public document, which is the point worth holding: a meeting listed on a published calendar is not a secret meeting, and state governors meet foreign ambassadors routinely. The Egyptian thread in this investigation rests on flight records and the **SU-BTT** movements rather than on this calendar entry.
