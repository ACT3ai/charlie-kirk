## What This Image Shows

A composite image. Three inset panels on the left show wide and medium-wide views of the dense crowd outside the multi-level concrete academic building, red circles and arrows marking one fixture on the facade. The right side is a large high-resolution close-up of that same fixture, which at this magnification resolves as a small grey vent with a dark aperture.

On-screen text includes a partial UVU marking and the overlay "THE LIGHT OVER THE GRASSY KNOLL.." credited to an account shown as [@THEINTELSCIF](/Influencers/x/intel-scif).

This is the most informative of the several versions of this study held in the cluster, because the close-up largely answers the question the wide shots pose: the object reads as a vent housing rather than a lamp or a camera. Working an anomaly until it resolves, in either direction, is the standard the rest of this material should be held to.
