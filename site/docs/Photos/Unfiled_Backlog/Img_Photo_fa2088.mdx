## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an email presented on a stylized background with a digital signature overlay. The email is dated **November 20, 2025** and addressed to a commentator; the sender is described as an anonymous military member.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the message is not quoted here. Per the visual description, its subject is a flight stopover at **Minot**, North Dakota.

Minot appears in the aircraft record this archive tracks: the **SU-BTT** inbound leg to Provo on **September 4, 2025** is logged as coming from Minot. An anonymous tip offering an interpretation of that stopover is a lead rather than evidence, and it is unverified as captured.
