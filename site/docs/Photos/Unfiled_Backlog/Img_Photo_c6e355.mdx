## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Lauren Witzke**, handle @LaurenWitzkeDE, posted eight hours before capture. The attached image shows side-by-side screenshots of message exchanges with passages highlighted, overlaid with a large Project Veritas watermark.

The post relays a reported story alleging that a named former Justice Department official was involved in a plan to set up and pressure [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens). The specific allegations, including those in the message screenshots, describe serious criminal conduct by named living people. They are unverified, no court has found any of them, and they are not reproduced here.

The frame is retained because the general theme, pressure and leverage applied to public commentators, recurs throughout this investigation's own material, including the reported threats against journalists covering the case. Those documented threats are handled in [Media](/Media/overview) and [Influencers](/Influencers/overview).
