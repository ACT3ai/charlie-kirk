## What This Image Shows

A still frame from a video recording of the outdoor event. A white pop-up canopy carrying THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR at the peak and PROVE ME WRONG along the valance stands on the grass at center-left. Staff, camera operators and organizers stand under and around it near the stage setup, and a dense crowd fills the amphitheater-style grass bank behind under clear sky.

The player timestamp reads 7:53 / 13:58, confirming this is a still lifted from a longer recording rather than a photograph.

The frame is useful for counting: how many crew, how many with credentials, how many with a clear view of the stage from the bank. Those counts feed the reconstruction of who was where, which underlies both the sightline arguments and the questions about staffing.
