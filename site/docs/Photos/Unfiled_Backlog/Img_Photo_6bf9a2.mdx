## What This Image Shows

A vertical mobile screenshot of a working database, laid out as twenty stacked cards. Each card carries a name or handle, a role label, a status flag and in some cases a numeric badge.

The visible entries include board members and officers of **TPUSA** with the label "Board Member" or "Organizational Leadership," a "Student Speaker," a "Mentors" entry, two security entries labelled "Security Guard" and "Unnamed Officer," **George Zinn** labelled "Attendee/Distraction Creator," **Charlie Kirk** labelled "TPUSA Founder/CEO" and marked Deceased, several **Kirk** family members labelled "Family Member," a church leader marked Deceased, and three researcher handles labelled "Primary Investigator," "Security Team IDs" and "LDS-CIA Research."

This is a researcher's index, and every role label on it is that researcher's working classification rather than an established fact. Appearing on such a list means only that someone is being tracked; it is not an accusation, and none of the living people listed has been charged in connection with the shooting.
