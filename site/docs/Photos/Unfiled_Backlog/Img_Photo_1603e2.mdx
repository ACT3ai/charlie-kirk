## What This Image Shows

A second, taller capture of the same X post by an account shown as **John Cullen** about aircraft **N59906**. This version shows more of the Flightradar24 panel: the map around Provo with waypoints at Highland, Faust, Genola and Goshen, an inset photograph of the aircraft, and the telemetry fields for barometric altitude 19,000, ground speed 212 KTS, track 44 degrees, squawk 4323, and the type and registration lines reading Piper Navajo Chieftain, N59906, serial number 31-7552027.

The post text repeats the claim and adds a credit line, reading "(h/t: Fritz, @JG_CSTT)" beneath the assertion that the aircraft "isn't supposed to be airborne, at all" and that its registration is "In Question."

The two captures are kept together because the second preserves telemetry the first crops away, which is what makes the claim checkable against public flight data rather than only readable. The aircraft argument, including the separate **N1098L** and **SU-BTT** threads, is collected in [Planes](/Planes/overview) and the [Aircraft photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/overview).
