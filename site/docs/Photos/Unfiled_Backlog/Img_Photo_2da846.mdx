## What This Image Shows

A composite screenshot. The left side is a large security camera still showing a young man inside a fast food restaurant carrying a drink and a red box. The right side is a post thread from an account shown as **George Webb**, handle @RealGeorgeWebb1, dated **Sep 18** with 1.5M views, plus replies.

The post reads: "It just got worse. Tyler is at a Dairy Queen north of UVU at 6:38 PM. You'd better hurry and drive your grey Dodge Challenger to get to St. George unless you are flying on a plane like N888 or the other spy plane!" A reply from the same account reads: "Wouldn't the license plate readers pick up the grey Dodge Challenger?"

The argument is about elapsed time and distance: if a person was at a location north of the campus at a given hour, the drive to St George becomes the constraint on the rest of the timeline. This connects to the impossible-timeline material this archive tracks in detail, and to the aircraft threads including **N888KG**. The identification in the still is the poster's, **Tyler Robinson** has not been convicted, and the license plate reader question is exactly the kind of record that could settle it.
