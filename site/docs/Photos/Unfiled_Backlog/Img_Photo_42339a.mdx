## What This Image Shows

A structured infographic titled **BLUEPRINT FOR REGIME CHANGE**, laid out as a flowchart of logos, boxes and directional arrows on a light grey background.

The visible labels include USAID under "STRATEGIC DIRECTION"; the National Endowment for Democracy under "COORDINATION"; CIPE, IRI and the National Democratic Institute under "FUNDING FOR PROGRAMS IN GOVERNANCE, CIVIL SOCIETY, AND ECONOMIC REFORMS"; a reporting box reading "REPORTS AND NARRATIVES ON POLITICAL FREEDOM AND HUMAN RIGHTS TO JUSTIFY POLICY INTERVENTIONS AND FUNDING"; boxes for "TRAINING," "FINANCE" and "LOGISTICS"; the Open Society Foundations and a Solidarity Center; a MEDIA COVERAGE row of outlet logos; and a lower band naming movements: "OTPOR RESISTANCE, 2000 BULLDOZER REV. SERBIA," "PORA IT'S TIME, 2004 ORANGE REV. UKRAINE," "KMARA ENOUGH, 2003 ROSE REV. GEORGIA," and "KELKEL, 2005 TULIP REV. KYRGYZSTAN."

This is an argument diagram about foreign policy, not evidence about the **September 10, 2025** shooting. The organizations, the funding relationships and the named movements are real and documented; the causal coordination the arrows assert is the maker's thesis. It sits in the backlog because the same structural reasoning, follow the funding and the messaging, runs through this investigation's own material.
