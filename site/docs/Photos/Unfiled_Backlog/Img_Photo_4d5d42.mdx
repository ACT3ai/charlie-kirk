## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post from an account shown as **Sam Parker**, handle @BasedSamParker, posted two hours before capture. The attached composite has bright red borders and splits into four panels: a commentator at upper left, a crowded outdoor scene under a PROVE ME WRONG banner at upper right, and two blurry zoomed crops along the bottom showing a young man in a dark maroon t-shirt and a baseball cap.

The post reads: "CALL FOR HELP: @RealCandaceO wants to contact the guy in the maroon shirt. He might have useful information about the bush, which is now an area of interest. NO BLAME, GUILT OR ACCUSATIONS are implied at all. Please don't attack or doxx this guy. Just pass the info to Candace."

The post's own disclaimer is the right frame for this page as well: the man is a potential witness, not a suspect, and he is not identified here. The "bush" referenced is a landscaping feature near the tent area that researchers have examined in connection with the ground-level evidence questions.
