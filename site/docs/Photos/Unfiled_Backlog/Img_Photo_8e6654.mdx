## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide still from the event recording. A man in a white t-shirt sits at the banner-draped table at center; a man in a black shirt and grey trousers stands at the left edge of the stage, and a man in a black cap and shirt is partly visible at the right. Only the lower metal frame and white fabric edge of the canopy appear at the top of the frame. A dense crowd watches from stadium-style seating behind.

The legible text is the partial banner reading THE AMERICAN and the player timestamp 8:45 / 13:58.

Stage-level frames like this one are how the investigation builds its list of who had physical access to the table, the microphone and the equipment during the broadcast. That access list is central to the questions raised in [The Mic](/Mic/overview) and the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview).
