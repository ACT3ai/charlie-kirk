## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level daylight frame of the event. The white canopy stands on the grass carrying PROVE ME WRONG banners and housing a table signed for THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR. Staff and security move around the perimeter, and a large crowd is packed on the hillside behind.

The player timestamp reads 4:20 / 13:58, marking this as another still from the long event recording.

Wide frames from a continuous recording are the reference plates the annotated versions elsewhere in this archive are drawn on top of. Holding the clean original alongside the annotated derivative is how a reader can check what was actually in the frame before anyone added a circle or an arrow.
