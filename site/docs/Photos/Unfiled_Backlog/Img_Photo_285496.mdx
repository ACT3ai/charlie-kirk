## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **DC_Draino**, posted two hours before capture, quoting a post from an account shown as RealRobert. The quoted post carries a paused low-resolution news clip of **Brad Raffensperger**, the Georgia Secretary of State, standing outdoors with an NBC News bug in the corner.

The outer post reads: "'Why do you keep saying Trump won Georgia in 2020?' Because the GA Secretary of State said it on national tv. Then days later everything flipped and we caught election workers pulling out secret ballot boxes when Republicans weren't in the room. And it's common sense for anyone who's actually visited Georgia - it's MAGA country." The quoted post frames the clip as being from the morning of **Nov 4th 2020**.

This screenshot is not about the **September 10, 2025** shooting. It sits in the backlog among the election-related material collected alongside the investigation. The claims in it are contested and were rejected in litigation and official reviews at the time; they are recorded here as the poster's assertions, not as findings.
