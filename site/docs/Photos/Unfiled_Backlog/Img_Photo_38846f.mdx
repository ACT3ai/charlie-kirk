## What This Image Shows

A frame from the outdoor event. A man in a blue button-down shirt stands in the foreground facing slightly right. To the left, a production desk sits under a white pop-up canopy whose valance reads PROVE ME WRONG in bold white on black. Event crew, cameras and security personnel fill the middle ground, and a large crowd covers the terraced grass slope behind under clear sky.

On-screen text includes the tour banner lettering and a player timestamp of 6:31 / 13:58.

This is another still from the same recording, and it is kept because it shows the production desk position relative to the stage and the crowd. Where the audio desk sat, who had hands on the microphone feed, and how the signal ran are questions the investigation pursues in detail.
