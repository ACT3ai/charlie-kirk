## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a reposted item from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, dated **Oct 16, 2025**. The embedded player shows a wide daytime view from a residential front porch camera looking out onto a suburban street.

The post reads: "Breaking: Newly Uncovered Footage Appears to Show Tyler Robinson's Grey Dodge Challenger Near UVU Neighborhood - Video Raises Many Questions. Brand-new video surfaced from a neighborhood on West 925th St, showing a grey Dodge Challenger pulling up, parking, and the driver who looks like Tyler Robinson getting out to meet another individual in a second car. PROOF OF AN ACCOMPLICE!!! The Person was wearing a reddish T-shirt and shorts and very clearly did not walk with any kind of limp or unusual gait unlike the person in the other video released by TMZ which had a noticeable limp and completely different clothing."

The gait comparison is the substantive point, and it connects to the clothing-change and movement questions this archive tracks around **Tyler Robinson**. Everything else is inference: the identification is by resemblance in doorbell footage, the second person is unidentified, and "PROOF OF AN ACCOMPLICE" is the poster's conclusion, not a finding.
