## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post asserting that documents obtained under freedom of information law confirm a 2020 ballot fraud operation.

The post reads: "Documents obtained by investigative journalist @yehuda_miller through the Freedom of Information Act CONFIRMS, BEYOND THE SHADOW OF A DOUBT. The Department of Justice and the FBI uncovered a massive 2020 ballot fraud operation based in Michigan and operated in multiple swing states funded by Joe Biden's 2020 presidential campaign," and continues: "GBI Strategies GOT CAUGHT for submitting fraudulent voter registrations during the 2020 election. Following a raid, Michigan authorities discovered caches of pre-paid gift cards, firearms equipped with silencers, and disposable burner phones." It then itemizes payments: "Biden for President: $450,000. Democratic Senatorial Campaign: $2,117,605. DNC Services Corp: $1,031,856. Democratic Party of Iowa: $493,100."

This is election material rather than case material. The contractor and the Michigan registration matter are real and were the subject of state action; the leap from that to a campaign-funded fraud operation, and the personal allegations attached to named officials, are the poster's and are not adopted here.
