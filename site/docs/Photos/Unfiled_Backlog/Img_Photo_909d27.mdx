## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a reposted self-reply from an account shown as John Wade, handle @LtJohnWade, posted six hours before capture.

The post reads: "The FBI isn't stupid. The FBI FALSE NARRATIVE is ridiculously false. Just a little of what the Yes, STILL CORRUPT FBI must have deliberately ignored... friends, this was almost certainly a MOSSAD assassination. We have (1) initial confirmation via CIA contact."

The post's own wording sets its evidentiary weight: "almost certainly" and "initial confirmation" from a single unnamed contact. Nothing is shown, no document is produced, and the conclusion is the poster's assertion. This archive tracks the intelligence-service hypothesis on documentary grounds elsewhere, and a post like this one is a data point about what was being said, not about what happened.
