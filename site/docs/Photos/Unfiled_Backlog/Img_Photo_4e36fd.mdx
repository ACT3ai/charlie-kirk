## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of an X thread. The opening post comes from an account shown as **John Cullen**, handle @I_Am_JohnCullen, and carries a link card to an article from The War Zone with a photograph of a grey military surveillance jet in flight. Below it a reply from an account shown as Fritz, handle @diddlezs, includes a small Flightradar24 map with aircraft tracking data.

The text extraction sidecar for this file returned no usable lines, so the post wording is not quoted here; the description above is drawn from the visual record.

Aircraft in the airspace around **Utah Valley University** on and around **September 10, 2025** are among the most heavily documented threads in this investigation, covering **N1098L**, the Piper **N59906**, the Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT**, and the SAM flight tracked separately in the record.
