## What This Image Shows

A composite image. The left panel is a vertical video frame showing officers in dark uniforms handling an individual on the ground beside a dark vehicle. The right panel is a Facebook post from an account shown as Wienerdog Wifi, dated **September 10 at 4:31PM**, with its comment thread.

The post reads: "The alleged shooter of Charlie Kirk has been arrested. Authorities also recovered a rifle among his belongings. Justice is moving - the killer is off the streets." Comments below include ordinary condolences, and the counter is marked "2 of 31."

The post was wrong within hours: everyone detained on **September 10, 2025** was released, and no one was in custody that night. Preserving the early claims, timestamped, is how the archive documents the drift between what was announced and what held. The same account's Instagram version, with sharper corrections in the comments, is held elsewhere in this cluster. See [After the Shooting](/After/overview) and [Narrative](/Narrative/overview).
