## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of a business filing from the State of Wyoming Secretary of State, Business Division, Herschler Building East, Cheyenne.

The record reads: "Name: Firebird Electronics. Filing ID: 2013-000649286. Type: Profit Corporation. Status: Inactive - Administratively Dissolved (Tax). Old Name: Continental Electric Co. Formed in Wyoming. Term of Duration Perpetual. Standing - Tax: Delinquent. Standing - RA: Delinquent. Filing Date 08/22/2013. Inactive Date 10/09/2025. Common Shares 1,000,000. Principal Address: 625 W. Deer Valley Road #103-234, Phoenix, AZ 85027. Registered Agent Address: No Agent, No Office, Cheyenne, WY 82001."

Corporate filings are among the few genuinely primary documents in this archive: they are state records, dated, and checkable by anyone. A Wyoming shell with no registered agent, a mail-drop style Phoenix address and a dissolution dated **October 2025** is the kind of entity researchers trace when following money or ownership.
