## What This Image Shows

A split composite. The left half shows a thread of two posts from an account shown as Omar, handle @NajraGalvz, both marked as posted one day earlier. The right half is a close side-profile video still of a person in a red baseball cap with an American flag patch, standing at the outdoor event with Yrefy and [Turning Point](/TPUSA/overview) branding visible behind.

The first post reads: "[Charlie kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) is coming to my college tomorrow i rlly hope someone evaporates him literally," logged at 92K views. The second reads: "Lets just say something big will happen tomorrow," logged at 239K views.

Foreknowledge is one of the investigation's central tests, and posts made the day before an attack are exactly the kind of item that has to be examined rather than ignored. It also has to be examined carefully: the account behind these posts has not been publicly identified, the screenshot is unverified, hostile posts about a visiting speaker are common on campuses, and "something big will happen tomorrow" is the kind of line that reads very differently before and after an event. Nothing here establishes any connection between the person pictured, the account, and the shooting. See [Before the Shooting](/Before/overview) and [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).
