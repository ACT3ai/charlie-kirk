## About This Cluster

This cluster preserves a screenshot researchers circulated as possible evidence of foreknowledge of the [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk). The composite pairs an X thread from an account named "Omar" with a video still of a person in a red American-flag cap at an outdoor event. According to the posts shown in the screenshot, the account wished for Kirk to be "evaporated" during his college visit and then hinted that "something big will happen tomorrow."

Foreknowledge is one of the investigation's central tests: if anyone signaled the attack before it happened, the lone-shooter narrative has to explain how. This screenshot is unverified, the account behind it has not been publicly identified, and hostile posts before a public event do not by themselves prove any connection to the crime. It is filed here so the claim can be examined rather than repeated blind, and any link between the pictured person and the posts is the poster's suggestion, not a finding.

Open the image page to read the full on-screen text, then weigh it against the documented pre-event record in [Before the Shooting](/Before/overview), including the reported warnings and threat chatter cataloged there. The [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) section covers how fast this kind of material disappeared from platforms after the shooting.
