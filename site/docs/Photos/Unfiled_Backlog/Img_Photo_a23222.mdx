## What This Image Shows

A still from the event recording showing the stage area from the front. The white canopy carries the tour logo on its pitched roof and the PROVE ME WRONG banner on the valance. Organizers, security personnel and camera operators are active in the foreground around the stage, and a densely packed crowd is seated on the terraced grass hillside behind.

The player timestamp reads 7:47 / 13:58.

This frame sits one second away from another still held in this cluster, and the pair is kept because small differences between adjacent frames are how researchers establish who moved where and when. That frame-by-frame method underpins much of the reconstruction work in [Cameras](/cameras/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
