## What This Image Shows

A split-screen graphic. On the left is a video insert of **Candace Owens** seated at a studio desk with a laptop and microphone. On the right is a dark-mode messaging thread presented as an exchange between **Charlie Kirk** and an unnamed contact, timestamped between 3:48 PM and 3:54 PM.

The messages attributed to Kirk read: "Just lost another huge Jewish donor. $2 million a year because we won't cancel Tucker," then "I'm thinking of inviting Candace," then "Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes. I cannot and will not be bullied like this," and "Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro Israel cause." The other party replies: "Please don't invite Candace. That might feel good short term but it's not good long term, in my opinion," and adds that any group will hold a wide variety of opinions.

The authenticity and provenance of these messages rest on the party who released them; they are reproduced here as a circulated screenshot, not as an authenticated record. If genuine, they document donor pressure on Kirk in the period before **September 10, 2025**, which is why the messages sit at the center of the motive argument.
