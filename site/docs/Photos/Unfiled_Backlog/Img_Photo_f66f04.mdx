## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a Facebook post from a local television station account, dated **September 11**, reporting on the investigation into the shooting near the university. The attached photograph shows a makeshift memorial on the ground: miniature American flags, flowers, a handwritten tag and a framed photograph.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the post is not quoted here. The framed photograph in the memorial shows the image of a former president raising his fist after the July 2024 attempt on his life.

That detail is the one worth noting. Within a day, mourners were placing an image of a previous political shooting at the memorial for this one, which records how the event was immediately understood by the public. Mainstream local coverage from [**September 11, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13) is also part of the baseline this archive measures later accounts against.
