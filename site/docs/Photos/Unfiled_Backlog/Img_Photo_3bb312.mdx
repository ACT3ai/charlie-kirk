## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a long post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, with small thumbnails of flight log screenshots beneath the text.

The post reads: "BOMBSHELL EVIDENCE: CONFIRMED NOT 2 BUT 5 EGYPTIAN MILITARY PLANES SWARMED PROVO in 2025 - Direct Flights to Charlie Kirk's Assassination Site," and continues: "@RealCandaceO ignited the fire with her timeline exposing SU-BTT and SU-BND shadowing TPUSA events for years-landing in Provo Sep 4, 2025, just 6 days before Charlie's murder on Sep 10. But now? Raw flight logs (screenshots attached) reveal THREE MORE Egyptian Air Force jets in Provo throughout 2025: SU-BTU, SU-BTV, and SU-BGM." It itemizes: "SU-BTT (Falcon 7X): Sep 4 Minot to Provo (full inbound); Sep 10 Provo to Wilmington (lighter outbound post-hit)," and "SU-BND (Gulfstream G550): Sep 13 Provo to Goose Bay/Paris/Cairo."

The tail numbers and the arrival and departure legs are the checkable part, and **SU-BTT** and **SU-BND** are both tracked independently in this archive. The characterizations, from "swarmed" to "lighter outbound post-hit," are the poster's, and the detailed flight-data rebuttal held elsewhere in this cluster argues that a majority of the claimed tailing flights did not occur.
