## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution outdoor frame looking slightly upward at a modern low-rise building complex under hazy blue sky. The top of a bystander's head is visible at the bottom centre of the frame, cut off by the edge. The image is washed out and soft.

There is no on-screen text.

The frame belongs to the same sequence of handheld crowd captures held elsewhere in this cluster, all shot from within the crowd looking up at the surrounding structures. Their value is cumulative rather than individual: enough of them, from enough positions, reconstruct what the buildings looked like from the ground on that day.
