## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **End Wokeness**, handle @EndWokeness, posted five hours before capture. The video thumbnail at 0:11 shows a large open interior with people working at tables while others mount large white boards over a curved multi-paned glass partition.

The post reads: "5 years ago tonight, Detroit boarded up their windows before counting mail-in ballots."

This is election material rather than case material. The footage of the boards is real and the episode was widely covered at the time; the interpretation placed on it is contested, and it is recorded here as the poster's framing rather than an established account. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
