## What This Image Shows

A broadcast frame. **Candace Owens** sits at a studio desk behind a microphone and laptop at frame left; a large beige panel at frame right carries a numbered list headed "TPUSA Verified Lies," running from item 8 to item 13.

The listed statements, each attributed on the panel to a named individual, are: that a staff member "was told to remove the SD cards by police"; that she "removed the SD cards because people were stealing hats"; that "Charlie establishing DOGE was not unusual" because the organization "financially audited the company every year since its inception"; that "Charlie Kirk's team did not fly drones at UVU"; that "Security communicated with UVU police to secure the rooftops"; and item 13, headed "Inexplicable events: Egyptian Planes tracking Charlie and Erika."

The heading and the attributions are the presenter's characterizations of other people's public statements. Those named dispute her account, no finding has been made against any of them, and nothing here establishes that any statement was knowingly false. What the list does usefully mark is where the public accounts conflict, and each of those points, the camera SD cards, the drones, the rooftop coordination and the aircraft, is documented separately here.
