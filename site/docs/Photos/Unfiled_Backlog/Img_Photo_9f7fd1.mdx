## What This Image Shows

A clean screenshot of a single short post from an account shown as Trisha Hope, handle @JustTheTweets17, timestamped 6:31 PM, **Oct 20, 2025**, with 6,318 views.

The post reads: "Have all of @RealCandaceO podcasts since Charlie's assassination disappeared off of YouTube?"

The question is posed rather than answered, and a separate capture in this cluster shows those episodes present under the channel's Live tab with view counts in the millions, which is a partial answer: material filed under a different tab can look missing without having been removed. That distinction matters, because genuine removals in this case are documented elsewhere in this cluster, including a video about the security detail marked Removed on a creator dashboard.
