## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a single post from an account shown as In2ThinAir, on a plain white background.

The post reads: "Charlie Kirk UPDATE! Watch Close for the EDIT! CELL phones at the Utah campus were HACKED and footage EDITED in real time!? Multiple people who were present for the shooting are claiming that FOOTAGE on their phones was EDITED or deleted before they even got home!! This footage was shared by account StephenGardnerX."

The witness reports underlying this are documented in this archive independently: multiple people present at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025** have said footage was missing from their own devices by the time they got home. That is a serious and repeated claim. The explanation offered here, real-time hacking and editing, is the poster's hypothesis and one of several possible causes, alongside platform-side removal, app-level sync behaviour and ordinary device failure.
