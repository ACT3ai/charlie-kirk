## What This Image Shows

A high, wide photograph taken from a gravel-surfaced rooftop at **Utah Valley University** in Orem. The near third of the frame is the roof itself, with small orange marker flags and a scrap of white paper on the gravel and dark metal flashing along the edge. Below, a paved courtyard of reddish-brown pavers runs between concrete and glass campus buildings, with a turquoise pond and running fountains, a highway and a distant lake beyond.

Signage caught in the frame reads BROWNING ADMINISTRATION, A PLACE FOR YOU UVU, and a partial SORENSE, which places the vantage point in relation to the Browning Administration Building and the Sorenson Molecular Biotechnology Building.

Rooftop lines of sight are one of the most argued technical questions in the case. Which roof a shot could have come from, what a person on that roof could actually see, and how those angles square with the wound description are all open.
