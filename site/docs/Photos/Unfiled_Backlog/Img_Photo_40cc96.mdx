## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **RyanMatta**, handle @RyanMattaMedia, posted two hours before capture and timestamped **Sep 20 9:17 AM**. The video still below shows a man gesturing toward a computer monitor displaying the tour's schedule webpage, with the campus stops listed including **UTAH VALLEY UNIVERSITY**, Colorado State, Virginia Tech and Utah State.

The post reads: "A source at Utah Valley claims that 2 weeks prior to the shooting a man was seen on the rooftops on two separate occasions. The man was seen on or coming off the Losee building or the Computer Science building. Updates on the men on the rooftop!"

Prior rooftop access is directly relevant, because the official account places the shot on the **Losee Center** roof, and this archive separately documents pre-event warnings and records requests to the university about exactly this. The claim is single-sourced, anonymous and relayed second-hand, and no rooftop sighting weeks earlier has been confirmed.
