## What This Image Shows

A high-angle wide photograph of several hundred people, mostly young adults in summer clothing, packed in front of a multi-story concrete building complex. More spectators watch from an elevated concrete walkway that runs horizontally across the upper third of the frame. A promotional canopy stands at the right, flanked by large advertising graphics on the building windows.

The frame carries no on-screen text.

The elevated walkway is the detail that matters. It gave dozens of people a raised, unobstructed view over the crowd, which means it also gave dozens of cameras an angle no one at ground level had. Recovering what was shot from that walkway is part of the footage-gathering problem this investigation keeps running into.
