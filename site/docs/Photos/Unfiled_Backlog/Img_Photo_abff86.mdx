## What This Image Shows

A full browser capture of an AI chat interface on X, showing a long multi-part prompt and the system's response.

The prompt reads: "Generate a list of quotes Charlie Kirk made. Focus on ones that may be related to his assassination. Or anyone (person, country, donors) who may have contention with him. Anything that may show he worried about safety to his life, content with Israel, or zionism. Don't leave any quotes out. Quote how he said it (Text message, podcast, interview, etc.) if that information is available... Quote when, if that information is available." Below it the interface reads: "Grok was unable to reply. Something went wrong."

The failure is what makes this worth keeping. A carefully specified research request on this subject returned an error rather than an answer, and researchers working this case have repeatedly reported similar refusals and disappearances. Whether this instance was a routine service failure or something else is not determinable from the frame. This archive maintains its own quote collections compiled by hand for exactly this reason.
