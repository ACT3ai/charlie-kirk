## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Sean Davis**, handle @seanmdav, posted two hours before capture, with an attached two-panel press release.

The post reads: "Fascinating. Dominion Voting was just bought out." The press release is headed: "LIBERTY VOTE ACQUIRES DOMINION VOTING SYSTEMS IN HISTORIC DEAL; WILL NOW BE 100% AMERICAN OWNED," and reads: "ST. LOUIS, MO - As of today, Dominion is gone. Liberty Vote assumes full ownership and operational control." It quotes the founder: "Liberty Vote signals a new chapter for American elections - one where trust is rebuilt from the ground up," and lists commitments to paper-based transparency, hand-marked paper ballots and "Third-Party Auditing: Incorporating rigorous, industry-leading third-party auditing standards."

This is election material rather than case material, and it is the underlying announcement behind the rebrand posts held elsewhere in this cluster. It is a corporate press release quoted as such. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Companies and Organizations](/Companies_Organizations/overview).
