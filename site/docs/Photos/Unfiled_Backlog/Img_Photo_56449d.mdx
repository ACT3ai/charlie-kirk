## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **John Cullen**, with an attached screenshot of an email.

The email is headed "GRAMA Request - Denial" and comes from the City Recorder for **Provo City**, stating that the requested records are protected under Utah Code because of an ongoing investigation. The text extraction sidecar returned no further usable lines, so the full wording is not quoted here.

This is a primary document and a significant one: it confirms that communication records relating to **Provo Airport** exist and were withheld, in the same window in which this archive's aircraft threads, including **N1098L** and the Egyptian registrations, all pass through that airport. A denial establishes the existence of records and a legal basis for withholding them; it does not establish their contents.
