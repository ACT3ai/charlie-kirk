## What This Image Shows

A clean screenshot of a long post from the verified account shown as **Candace Owens**, handle @RealCandaceO.

The post reads: "URGENT. Two days ago I was contacted by a high-ranking employee of the French Government. After determining this person's position and proximity to the French couple, I have deemed the information they gave me to be credible enough to share publicly in the event that something happens. In short, this person claims that the Macrons have executed upon and paid for my assassination... that the green light was given to a small team in National Gendarmerie Intervention Group. I am told there is one Israeli that is on this assassination squad and the plans were formalized. Again, this person provided concrete proof that they are well placed within the French government apparatus. Further to this point, this person claims that Charlie Kirk's assassin trained with the French legion 13th brigade with multi-state involvement."

These are grave allegations against named living people, sourced to a single anonymous informant, with no evidence shown. They are unverified, have been publicly rejected by those they concern, and are not adopted here; they are recorded because the statement was made publicly by a prominent figure and became part of the record.
