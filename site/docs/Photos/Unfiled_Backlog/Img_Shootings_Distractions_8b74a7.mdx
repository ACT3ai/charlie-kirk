## What This Image Shows

A close capture of a spreadsheet table. The columns hold incident names, locations, dates in late 2025 and a short summary of each. Eight rows are listed under the working heading "Distraction Shootings," grouped by the maker under an unnamed intelligence service.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual rows are not quoted here.

This is a researcher's working hypothesis in tabular form, not a finding. The underlying proposition, that unrelated violent incidents cluster in time in a way that displaces coverage of a given story, is testable in principle and unproven here; grouping events in a spreadsheet does not establish a connection between them. The distraction material in this archive is collected in [Distraction People](/distraction_people/overview) and [Theories](/Theories/overview).
