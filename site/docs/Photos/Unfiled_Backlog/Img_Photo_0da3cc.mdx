## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as TPV Sean, handle @tpvsean, posted thirteen hours before capture. The embedded video thumbnail shows a heavily shadowed silhouette of a person wearing headphones in front of a blurred flag overlaid with a digital node-network pattern, running 1:55.

The post promotes a video built on claims attributed to an unnamed "high-level TPUSA insider," including that the organization was infiltrated during **Charlie Kirk's** final years leading it. The post also makes grave criminal allegations against a named living person. Those allegations are anonymous, unverified, unsupported by any court finding or document in this archive, and they are not reproduced here.

The frame is retained for one reason: to record what circulated in the weeks after **September 10, 2025**, and how quickly anonymous sourcing produced accusations against named individuals. Treating that material as evidence would be a mistake. The documented organizational questions about **TPUSA** are handled on the record in [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and [Companies and Organizations](/Companies_Organizations/overview).
