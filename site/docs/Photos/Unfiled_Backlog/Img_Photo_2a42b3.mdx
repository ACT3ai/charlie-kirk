## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a single post from an account shown as YourFavoriteGuy, handle @guychristensen_, posted 22 hours before capture with 260K views logged.

The post reads: "X has erased most videos of Gaza over the last few weeks. Some people noticed, but most people just forgot. Don't let it be lost on you. Elon Musk belongs in the Hague and should have his assets stripped."

The allegation of systematic removal is the poster's and is unverified, as is the call for prosecution; nothing in the frame documents any takedown. It is kept alongside the other platform-removal captures in this cluster because the pattern they describe, quiet deletion without notice, is the same pattern documented against material from this investigation.
