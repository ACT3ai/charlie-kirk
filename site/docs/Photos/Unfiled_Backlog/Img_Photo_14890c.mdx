## What This Image Shows

A front-on view of the **American Comeback Tour** stage area, empty of people. Bright yellow POLICE LINE DO NOT CROSS tape runs horizontally across the frame. Metal crowd-control barriers in front of the stage are draped in vinyl banners reading THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR and PROVE ME WRONG, with **Turning Point USA** branding and a Yrefy.com sponsor panel visible in the on-screen text.

Still sitting on the stage are a red Make America Great Again cap, several white caps, and a professional video camera on a tripod. That combination is what makes the frame useful: it captures the setup after the area was sealed but before it was struck, so equipment positions are preserved as they were.

The tent, the barrier layout and the camera positions are all contested points in the investigation. Researchers have asked which cameras were rolling, where the audio feed ran, and who had access inside the barrier line.
