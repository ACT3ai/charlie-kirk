## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **War Reporter**, handle @tangentsofwar. The embedded video player is paused at 1:06 / 1:37 on several officers gathered on a sidewalk beside a police vehicle, one kneeling and handling something in a green bag.

The post reads: "BREAKING: The alleged shooter of Charlie Kirk has been arrested. The weapon reportedly used appears to be a rifle."

This is one of the earliest public claims of an arrest, and it did not hold: the people detained on **September 10, 2025** were released, and no one was in custody that night. Preserving the early breaking posts alongside what followed is how the archive documents how the public account changed hour by hour.
