## What This Image Shows

A browser capture of the official English-language schedule page of the Elysee Palace, the office of the President of the French Republic. The layout uses a dark navy header over a light grey page listing a sequence of diplomatic meetings and telephone interviews for **Wednesday, September 10**.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual entries are not quoted here.

France recurs in this investigation's record through the aircraft movements, the reported meetings involving French and Egyptian officials, and the material on [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) and the [**Macrons**](/People/emmanuel-macron). An official published schedule for the day of the shooting is a primary document and one of the few in this area that can be checked against an institutional source rather than a screenshot.
