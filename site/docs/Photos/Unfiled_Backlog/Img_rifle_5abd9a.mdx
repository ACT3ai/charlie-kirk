An overhead night photograph of a bolt-action rifle lying inside an open cardboard box set on grass, lit by a harsh direct light. The rifle has a matte black synthetic stock with a textured grip and forend, a black rubber recoil pad, a telescopic scope in mounting rings, and a silver-grey bolt handle and barrel.
