## What This Image Shows

An eye-level shot of **Charlie Kirk** moving through a crowd at an outdoor campus event. He wears a white t-shirt with FREEDOM printed across the chest and is looking slightly up and to his right. The frame splits between a sunlit lawn on the left and a shaded concrete overhang on the right, with bystanders, supporters and staff on both sides.

The only readable on-screen text is a partial PROVE ME WRONG banner and the word FREEDOM on the shirt, tying the image to the **American Comeback Tour** campus format Kirk used through 2025.

Images of Kirk moving through open crowds document how little separation existed between him and the public at these events, which is the backdrop to every question the investigation raises about the protective arrangements on **September 10, 2025**.
