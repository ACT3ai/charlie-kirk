## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X from an account shown as Destiny Rezendes, handle @dezzie_rezzie, posted eleven hours before capture. The attached four-panel grid holds screenshots of documents, a book cover reading "Sourcebook: Where to find the best, most current information on industries, companies, products, government, markets, the economy, statistics, and more. First Edition" credited to Dr. Peter Vincent Pry, an Internet Archive profile page, and an Arizona business registration record.

The registration panel reads: "Company Age: 29 Years, 3 Months. Registered Agent: C T Corporation System, 3225 N Central Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85012. Governing Agency: Arizona Corporation Commission," and lists a company contact as "LORI A FRANTZVE, Chief Executive Officer." The post's caption reads: "Nothing strange at all. Erika Kirk has a totally normal family..."

The caption is sarcasm, and it is worth naming that plainly: corporate filings, an archive profile and a book cover establish that public records exist, not that anyone did anything wrong. The **Frantzve** family records are separately documented in this archive on their own terms. No allegation against any living person is made or repeated here.
