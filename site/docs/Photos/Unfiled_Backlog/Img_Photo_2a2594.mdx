## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of an X post by an account shown as **Shaun King**, timestamped 3:19 PM on **Aug 18, 2025** with 1.3M views. The post embeds a quote of his own earlier post from the same day.

The post reads: "My lawyers were told today that my tweets about the Israeli pedophile that was arrested for sex crimes in Vegas were all removed after a GOVERNMENT DEMAND. They were not told which government. Has this ever been done before? I got zero notice, explanation, nothing." The quoted earlier post says every one of his posts on the same subject was deleted overnight. The characterization of the person arrested is the poster's own wording, made about a matter that had not been tried; it is quoted here as the text on screen rather than adopted.

What makes the screenshot relevant to this investigation is not the underlying case but the mechanism it alleges: bulk removal of a user's posts, with no notice, following a demand the platform would not attribute. The same pattern of sudden takedowns is documented after **September 10, 2025**. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
