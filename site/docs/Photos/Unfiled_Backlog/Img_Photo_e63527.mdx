## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu. The embedded video frame shows the outdoor crowd gathered under the canopy carrying the PROVE ME WRONG banner.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the post is not quoted; its subject, per the visual description, is whether drones were flying at the event and whether the answer given publicly was accurate.

The drone question is well documented in this archive on independent grounds, including accounts that **TPUSA** had drones in the air that day and that **Charlie Kirk** saw them. Whether any drone footage exists, who held it, and whether it was produced to investigators are open questions.
