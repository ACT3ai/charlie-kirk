## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post from an account shown as **Sam Parker**, handle @BasedSamParker. Inside it sits a compiled graphic of two other posts, with red and green underline annotations drawn under the timestamps to draw the eye to the dates.

The compiled posts are attributed to **Josh Hammer**. One is a one-word reply, "based," to a **Apr 19, 2013** post by an account shown as Donald J. Trump reading "Should be public execution for all to see-you will end this bullshit fast!", captured at 6:05 PM on **Sep 9, 2025**. The second is a standalone post reading "Public execution.", captured at 3:57 PM on **Sep 10, 2025**. Parker's caption reads: "Whoops. Did Josh Hammer spill the Charlie Kirk assassination beans?"

The screenshot is a juxtaposition, and it should be read as one. A commentator reacting to a twelve-year-old post about criminal sentencing, and then using the same phrase the next day, is not evidence that anyone knew anything in advance, and no such claim is established here. The material is filed because the posts circulated widely and the archive records what circulated.
