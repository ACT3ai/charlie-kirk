## What This Image Shows

A side-by-side composite of two video stills of the same man standing on a grass lawn. He wears a white baseball cap carrying political text, a dark button-down shirt with embroidery at the shoulders, and a white undershirt. In both panels a bright red arrow is superimposed, pointing at a small dark object at his shirt pocket in one frame and near his waistline in the other.

The only readable on-screen text is the cap lettering, which resolves as MAKE AMERICA GREAT AGAIN.

This is the single image in the [Fake Policeman](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Fake_Policeman/overview) cluster, and it should be read alongside the three-panel sequence in [Fake Cop](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Fake_Cop/overview), which appears to show the same man with a gold star badge. The arrows and the badge reading are the posters' interpretation of a small dark shape at this resolution. The man has never been publicly identified, no agency has claimed him, and nothing in these frames shows anyone doing anything wrong.
