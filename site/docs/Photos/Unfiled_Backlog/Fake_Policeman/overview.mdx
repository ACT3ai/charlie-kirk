## About This Cluster

This cluster holds comparison stills of an unidentified mustached man filmed on the **Utah Valley University** lawn around the **September 10, 2025** event, wearing a white Make America Great Again cap and a dark embroidered shirt. Researchers circulating the frames added red arrows pointing to a small dark object at his chest pocket and waistline, and have questioned whether the object is a law-enforcement style badge. The question matters because the identity and affiliation of everyone moving through the venue that day remains a core open issue for the investigation.

The frames show the man raising his hand to his left breast pocket, where a metallic rectangular object sits partly under his fingers. The posts that spread these stills claim he was presenting himself as some kind of officer or security figure; the man has never been publicly identified, and no official account has explained who he was. Nothing in these images establishes wrongdoing by anyone, and the badge reading is the posters' interpretation, not a confirmed fact.

Open the single image in this cluster, then compare it with the closely related sequence in [Fake Cop](/Photos/Unfiled_Backlog/Fake_Cop/overview), which appears to show the same man removing a gold star badge. For the wider question of who controlled the ground that day, read [Suspects](/Suspects/overview) and the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview).
