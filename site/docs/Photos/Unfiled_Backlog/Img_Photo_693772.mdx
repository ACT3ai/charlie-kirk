## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as @amuse, posted nine hours before capture, with an embedded video at 1:09 showing a woman in a green suit seated for an interview.

The post reads: "ELECTION INTEGRITY: Assistant Attorney General Harmeet Dhillon confirmed that 75,000 independent voters in Chester County, Pennsylvania, were missing from the Election Day poll books, an error that benefited Democrats."

This is the follow-up to the earlier Chester County post held in this cluster, and it differs in kind: the earlier one relayed an allegation, this one reports a senior official confirming a specific figure. The characterization of who the error benefited is the poster's addition. This remains election material rather than case material. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Legal](/Legal/overview).
