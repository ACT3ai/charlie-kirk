## What This Image Shows

A capture of a text document in black sans-serif on off-white, showing several short chronological paragraphs covering figures and travel dates. One line is highlighted in light blue and another in bright yellow.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here.

The two-color highlighting convention recurs across the working chronology captures in this intake queue, and in the aircraft sheets held here blue marks one registration and yellow another. Read as part of that set, this page is another slice of the same effort to line up people's movements against flight records. See [Timeline](/Timeline/overview) and [Planes](/Planes/overview).
