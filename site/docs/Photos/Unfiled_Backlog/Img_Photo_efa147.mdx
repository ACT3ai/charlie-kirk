## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **The SCIF**, handle @TheSCIF, posted fourteen minutes before capture. The attached collage has three panels: two stills of a commentator from different clips, and an outdoor public scene with a highlighted inset of a young man in a maroon shirt.

The post reads: "Military operational tactics exposed during the Charlie Kirk assassination, aka, 'The Maroon Boys.' This is just another layer of the active color revolution against the United States. Charlie Kirk's assassination appears as a professional, multinational military intelligence..." A caption on one panel reads: "that this is a deep state assassination, I do, okay."

The man in the maroon shirt is the same figure a separate post in this cluster asked the public to help contact as a possible witness, with an explicit note that no blame was implied. Here he is folded into a group label and an operational theory. He remains unidentified, is not accused of anything by this archive, and clothing colour is not evidence.
