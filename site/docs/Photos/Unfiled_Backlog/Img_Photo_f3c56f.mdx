## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X dated **Sep 12** from an account whose display name is a satirical handle, showing a booking-style photograph of a young man against a plain blue background with a news outlet watermark in the corner.

The post reads: "So has there been any reason given for *why* the FBI arrested Zachariah Qureshi? At the event within 25 feet of Kirk. Arabic speaker and Heritage foundation intern. He's the guy Kash Patel rushed on here to announce was the shooter and was in custody. Distraction? Original patsy?"

The detention and release of a man in the hours after the shooting, announced publicly and then reversed, is documented in this archive alongside the other early detentions. Being detained and released is not evidence of involvement in anything, and no charge against him is recorded here; the questions in the post are the poster's. The **Qureshi** material is tracked in [Suspects](/Suspects/overview), [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [After the Shooting](/After/overview).
