## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **AF Post**, handle @AFpost, posted seven hours before capture. Below the text is a side-by-side composite of two portraits, identified in the visual description as **Charlie Kirk** on the left and **Benjamin Netanyahu** on the right.

The post reads: "According to multiple reports, Israeli PM Benjamin Netanyahu offered Charlie Kirk's TPUSA $150 million in cash to adopt an even more ardently pro-Israel stance. Kirk refused the money."

The reported offer, tracked in this archive under the Israel funding material, is one of the load-bearing claims in the motive argument, because it would place a very large sum and a refusal in the months before **September 10, 2025**. The post itself sources the figure only to "multiple reports" and names none of them, so it is recorded here as a widely circulated claim rather than a documented transaction. Read it against [Israel](/Israel/overview), [Motive](/Motive/overview) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
