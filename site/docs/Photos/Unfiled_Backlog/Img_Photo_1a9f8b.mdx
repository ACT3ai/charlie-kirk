## What This Image Shows

An eye-level, medium-wide still from the event recording. The white commercial canopy carries THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR on the peak and PROVE ME WRONG on the front valance. Crew members, photographers and security personnel work in the foreground and midground on the grass, and a dense crowd fills the concrete bleachers behind under clear sky.

The player timestamp reads 7:48 / 13:58.

What this frame documents is the working population inside the barrier: how many people with cameras, how many with credentials and how many with a security role were within a few feet of the table at any moment. Reconstructing that roster is one of the investigation's open tasks. See the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) and [Cameras](/cameras/overview).
