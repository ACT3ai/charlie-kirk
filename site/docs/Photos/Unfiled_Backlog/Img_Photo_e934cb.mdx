## What This Image Shows

A vertical frame from the event, annotated after the fact with a bright red circle drawn around a professional video camera on a tripod in the foreground. A female crew member works beside it. Behind them a white pop-up canopy stands with its printed underside visible in reverse, a dark blue vertical banner reading PROV hangs at the front left, and a large crowd fills a grassy slope in front of a modern building under clear sky.

The legible on-screen text is limited to fragments of the banner lettering.

The circle is the point of the image: someone marking which camera was where. Which cameras rolled, who operated them, where the recordings went and what was later handed over are recurring questions in this record, including the separate thread on a camera SD card.
