## What This Image Shows

A wide, high-angle photograph of the **Utah Valley University** campus taken in daylight. The foreground is the tiered amphitheater: light-grey concrete steps doubling as retaining walls between strips of mown grass. Behind it a long modern concrete building spans the frame, its glass fronted with green and white university banners. Beyond that lie a turquoise ornamental pond with running fountains and landscaped paths.

Signage in the frame reads BROWNING ADMINISTRATION and the university's A PLACE FOR YOU banners, fixing the vantage point relative to the Browning Administration Building.

This is the geometry of the venue: how the amphitheater steps down, what stands behind it, and what lines of sight the surrounding buildings command.
