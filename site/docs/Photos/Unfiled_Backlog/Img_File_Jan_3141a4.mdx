## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a public booking record database, laid out as a grid of white rows.

The entries read: "Arrested/Held For: New Charge. Bail $0.00, Not Bailable. 76-8-306(2)+(3A) OBSTRUCTION OF JUSTICE - CAPITAL/FIRST DEGREE FELONY CONDUCT. Court: 4th District Court." Then: "Arrested/Held For: New Charge. 76-5B-201 SEXUAL EXPLOITATION OF A MINOR. Case # 251403630. Bail $0.00, Not Bailable." Then: "Court: Provo Justice Court. Case # 251001224. Bail $0.00, Not Holding On This Charge. 76-8-703(2)+(3A) CRIMINAL TRESPASS - INSTITUTION OF HIGHER EDUCATION."

No name appears in the visible portion of the record, and none is supplied here. Booking records list charges as filed by prosecutors; a charge is an accusation, not a conviction, and anyone facing these charges is entitled to the presumption of innocence. The trespass charge at an institution of higher education and the obstruction charge both fall within the range of matters this archive tracks around the detentions on and after **September 10, 2025**.
