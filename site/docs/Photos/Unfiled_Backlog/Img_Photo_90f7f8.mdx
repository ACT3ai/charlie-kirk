## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **George Webb**, handle @RealGeorgeWebb1, with an attached table headed "Flight Activity Log."

The post reads: "Charlie went to Savory Group meeting Andrew and Shauna Smith in Salt Lake City the morning of his death. We had the Toyotas from 19th Mountain National Guard team going down University Parkway to converge on Savor's Swig near BYU stadium for the escape. The DQ next to Swig could have been the place where some of the Toyotas stopped." The table lists: "N102DZ, Scottsdale, AZ to Salt Lake City, UT, 8:12 AM - 9:23 AM; N560TW, Scottsdale, AZ to Provo, UT, 9:00 AM - 10:06 AM; SU-BND, Transponder Active, 10:05 AM - 11:35 AM; N560TW, Provo, UT to Santa Barbara, CA, 10:24 AM - 12:12 PM; N560TW, Santa Barbara, CA to Scottsdale, AZ, 12:23 PM - 1:44 PM."

The table is checkable and the tail numbers **N560TW** and **SU-BND** are both tracked separately in this archive. The narrative wrapped around it is not: the vehicle movements, the unit designation and the escape route are asserted without any source shown, and no person named is shown to have done anything.
