## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Hurley27, handle @Hurleys3d27, posted ten hours before capture. The attached grid holds four low-resolution zoomed stills: a reflective phone screen, a white car, a person's legs, and a wider outdoor view showing the tent and an overpass. Red circles mark details in two panels.

The post reads: "White car Shows up same time blue jean brown boots with yellow stripe wearing guy shows back up behind charlie seconds before the tragedy." One panel is labelled "white car."

The method is coincidence-in-time: two things appearing in the same window and being read together. The reflective phone screen panel is the more interesting one, since a screen caught in frame can preserve what a bystander was looking at. Nobody is identified here, boots and a car are not evidence of anything, and the reading is the poster's.
