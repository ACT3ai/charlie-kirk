## What This Image Shows

A high-angle frame of the **American Comeback Tour** setup on the grass at **Utah Valley University**. A white branded canopy stands at center, ringed by metal crowd-control barriers carrying matching tour banners. The brutalist concrete facade of a campus building runs across the top of the frame, its windows covered in green university decals reading A PLACE FOR YOU and UVU WOLVERINES. The barrier banners repeat AMERICAN COMEBACK and PROVE ME WRONG.

A single officer stands in the background near the concrete underpass beneath the building.

The barrier ring and the underpass behind it define who could approach the tent and from which direction. That perimeter, and how thin the staffing around it was, is a recurring subject in the record of the day.
