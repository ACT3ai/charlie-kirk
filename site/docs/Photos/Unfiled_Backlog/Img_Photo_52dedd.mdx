## What This Image Shows

A mobile capture of an X feed at 9:44. A reply from an account shown as Public Bureau of Investigation appears beneath a post attributed to **Candace Owens**, with the top of a further post visible below. A LinkedIn profile card is shown in the frame.

The reply reads: "According to LinkedIn, Robert Flood *trained* at the Fort Huachuca Center/School for 2 years in 1982-84. Still interesting info. But let's keep each other honest... He did NOT 'run army intelligence' there. If it helps sell the story, though, I guess you could technically say you now have evidence Dan Flood was there (just happened to be a ba..." The profile card lists "Robert Flood 3rd, Chief Deputy Sheriff (Ret)," membership of the FBI National Academy Associates, graduate work at the University of Virginia in criminal justice administration, the U.S. Army Command and General Staff College 1989-1990, and the U.S. Army Intelligence Center and School at **Fort Huachuca**, AZ, 1982-1984.

The value of this page is the correction itself. A widely shared claim is walked back to what the public record actually supports: training at an installation, not command of anything. **Fort Huachuca** appears repeatedly in this investigation on separate documentary grounds, and holding claims about it to the record is how that thread stays usable.
