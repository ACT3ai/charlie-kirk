## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Murray, handle @Rothbard1776, quoting a post from [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk), handle [@charliekirk11](/Influencers/x/charliekirk11), dated **Sep 3**. The quoted post carries a live news broadcast still showing a woman with long curly hair speaking at a press microphone.

The commentary reads: "In the months leading up to his assassination, Charlie Kirk was publicly and privately working on the complete disclosure of the Epstein files and sharing the testimony of survivors working to compose a list of Epstein's clients. He had many enemies, both foreign AND domestic." The quoted Kirk post reads: "BREAKING: Epstein survivors announce that they will compile their own list of abusers who frequented his island and plane. 'We know the names. Many of us were abused by them. We will compile the names we all know were regularly in the Epstein world.'" The broadcast lower third reads: "EPSTEIN VICTIMS SPEAK TO PRESS, LIVE Washington, D.C."

The underlying artifact is Kirk's own post, dated one week before [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), amplifying a survivors' press conference. That is a documented fact about what he was publicly doing in his final week, and it is one of the threads the motive argument rests on. The inference about enemies is the commentator's.
