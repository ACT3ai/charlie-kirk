## What This Image Shows

A multi-panel composite. The left half stacks three panels: a wide shot of the crowd, a zoom onto a concrete building facade with people standing on top of it, and a close-up inset with a red circle around a small glowing fixture. The right half is a high-resolution photograph of the same concrete structure and the small grey, down-facing vent-like fixture.

The overlay text reads "THE LIGHT OVER THE GRASSY KNOLL.." with a credit to an account shown as [@THEINTELSCIF](/Influencers/x/intel-scif).

This is the sharpest of the three versions of this study held in the cluster, and the extra detail changes what can be said: at this resolution the object reads as a small grey down-facing housing rather than a lamp. The crowd standing on top of the concrete structure in the second panel is worth noting on its own, since it establishes that the structure was accessible.
