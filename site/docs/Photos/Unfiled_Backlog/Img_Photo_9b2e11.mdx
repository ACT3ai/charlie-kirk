## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of an X thread. The upper post, from an account shown as **Sword Truth**, embeds a generated aerial visualization overlaid on a diagram of the campus plaza, titled "Acoustic TDOA Analysis: Charlie Kirk Assassination," with a "Create your own with Grok" credit at 0:05. Below it sits a quoted post from an account shown as **troofevades**, dated **Jan 21**.

The upper post reads: "Hey @troofevades great job! I asked @Grok imagine to make a visualization video after summarizing your post. What improvements or changes would be needed to make this more accurate?" The quoted post reads: "I analyzed smartphone recordings from the Charlie Kirk/UVU shooting using acoustic triangulation - the same tech used to locate artillery fire in war zones. Across multiple audio files and analysis runs, one location kept emerging. Within 2 meters."

Time-difference-of-arrival work is one of the strongest technical lines in this investigation, because it derives a firing position from the audio itself rather than from any witness account, and the record separately documents acoustic analysis pointing away from the official position. The claimed two-meter precision is the analyst's, and the visualization on top of it was machine-generated for illustration rather than measurement.
