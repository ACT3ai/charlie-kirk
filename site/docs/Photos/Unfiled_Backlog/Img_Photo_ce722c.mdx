## What This Image Shows

A still from the event recording. The white canopy stands on the grass where it meets the concrete plaza, its valance reading PROVE ME WRONG and its table wrapped in the tour banner. Event staff, security personnel and a speaker occupy the foreground and midground, and a large crowd fills the concrete amphitheater seating behind under clear sky.

The player timestamp reads 6:29 / 13:58.

The transition point between grass and concrete plaza in this frame is a useful marker, because it fixes the table position relative to the fixed architecture rather than to the temporary furniture. Position fixes of that kind are what the sightline arguments depend on. See [UVU](/UVU/overview) and [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview).
