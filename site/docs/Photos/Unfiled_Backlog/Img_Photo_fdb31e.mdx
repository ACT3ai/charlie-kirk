## What This Image Shows

A square-cropped screenshot of a social media post. The caption line at the top reads "Who is this guy?" Below it is a slightly pixelated view of a modern building facade on two levels: a crowd gathered behind a dark wall on the lower level, and a single figure standing on an upper balcony, partly obscured by a potted plant.

That caption is the only on-screen text.

The frame was captured on **September 10, 2025**, the day itself, which makes it one of the earliest of the many unidentified-figure items in this archive. Standing on a balcony behind a planter to watch an event below is ordinary behaviour; the frame identifies nobody and establishes nothing about anyone. It is preserved because the elevated positions around the plaza are a live question.
