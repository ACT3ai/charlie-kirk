## What This Image Shows

A clean screenshot of a post from an account shown as Cynthia, handle @CynthiaDempse19, timestamped 1:22 PM, **Oct 18, 2025**, with 781 views.

The post reads: "There had to have been almost no security at UVU because Hunter Kozak and all of his affiliates of 'UN-Eff America' had their credentials revoked to ever enter Charlie's events. He shouldn't even have been there let alone ask a question. He knew that. Who let him in line? His question, I believe was the trigger to set the plan into motion. It had to be a trans/gun violence question because that's the package they had tied up in a bow and ready for delivery to Steven Crowder the next morning. It's all choreographed. Some of these people are useful idiots."

The first part is a legitimate question about venue access control, which is a documented weak point in this case. The rest is speculation: the poster offers no evidence that any credential was revoked or that any question was coordinated, and says plainly "I believe." **Hunter Kozak** asked a question at a public event, which is not wrongdoing, and no evidence connects him to the shooting.
