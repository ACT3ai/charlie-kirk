## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of a post from an account shown as RealRobert, handle @Real_RobN, posted six minutes before capture, with an embedded low-resolution video of a man speaking at a podium in front of a crowd holding signs.

The post reads: "Eight minutes and forty-four seconds of Intentional Human Intervention, the Predicate for Election Fraud: The epicenter of the 2020 election fraud, Fulton County, Georgia," and lists: "17,852 votes counted with no ballot images. 20,713 original votes from tabulators out of thin air. 3,930 were duplicates counted and inserted into the CERTIFIED count." It closes: "Yet, Operation Arctic Frost unleashed thousands of FBI and DOJ agents to hunt down anyone, including members of Congress who questioned the overthrow. Now: Decertify the overthrow."

This is election material rather than case material. The figures and the conclusion are the poster's; the Fulton County claims of that period were examined and rejected in official reviews and litigation, and the individuals the post names in connection with ballot handling have prevailed in court against those who repeated similar claims about them. Their names are not repeated here. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
