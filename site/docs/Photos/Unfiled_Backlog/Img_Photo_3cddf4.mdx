## What This Image Shows

A capture of a text document listing an April chronology of dates, locations and flight details, interleaving the movements of **Charlie** with those of the aircraft referred to as **BND** and **BTT**. Several lines are highlighted in yellow and blue.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual entries are not quoted here.

This belongs to the same working set as the other highlighted chronology pages in this intake queue, where the two highlight colours separate the two registrations. Read together they are the source material behind the aircraft-tailing claim, and the rebuttal held elsewhere in this cluster attacks that claim by disputing the flights themselves.
