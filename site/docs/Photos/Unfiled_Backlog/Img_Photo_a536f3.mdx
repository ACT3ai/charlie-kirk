## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Grass, handle @DewyGrassBlades, posted an hour before capture, with an embedded news article excerpt.

The post reads: "Their own article admits the FBI was complicit from day one so why on earth would they not release everything now instead of giving the FBI time to bury the truth? I'll review all the evidence with an open mind, but this stinks of corruption from the start!" The excerpt reads: "Former FBI Special Agent Kyle Seraphin realized Friday that he was doing surveillance next door to the woman now suspected of being the Jan. 6 pipe bomber. 'The FBI put us one door away from the pipe bomber within days of January 6, and we were deliberately pulled away for no logical or logically investigative reason,' Seraphin told Blaze News Friday. 'And everything about that tells me that they were involved in a cover-up and have been since day one.'"

This concerns the pipe bomb investigation rather than the events at **Utah Valley University**, and it pairs with the annotated route map held elsewhere in this cluster. The former agent's account is his own recollection and his conclusion is his inference; the person referred to has not been convicted. The reason it sits here is the parallel to this case's own record of federal handling and reported obstruction of other agencies. See [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).
