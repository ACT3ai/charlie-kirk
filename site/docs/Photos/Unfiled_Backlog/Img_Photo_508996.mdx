## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Sam Parker**, with a long block of text and, below it, a paused video showing a Google Trends results page with a "Watch again" overlay.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file. Per the visual description, the post sets out claims about search activity originating from the Cayman Islands, Washington D.C. and Israel in relation to specific individuals.

This is the same Google Trends method used repeatedly across this cluster, and its limits apply here as they do everywhere else: Trends reports normalized relative interest by broad region, identifies no searcher, and cannot distinguish anticipation from early reporting.
