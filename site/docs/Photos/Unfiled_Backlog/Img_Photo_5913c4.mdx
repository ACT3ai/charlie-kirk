## What This Image Shows

A still from the event recording. A white pop-up canopy printed with AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR stands on the grass at frame left, with a table beside it. Event crew, security and organizers stand along a line of metal crowd-control barricades, and a dense crowd covers the grassy hillside behind. A modern multi-story academic building and trees close the background under clear sky.

On-screen text includes the PROVE ME WRONG valance and a player timestamp of 4:23 / 13:58.

The barricade line is the detail worth holding onto: it is a single row of light metal fencing between the crowd and the table, with the crew standing inside it. The thinness of that separation is part of what the investigation examines when it asks how the venue was secured.
