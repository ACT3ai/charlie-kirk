## What This Image Shows

A close crop of a single row from a flight tracking interface, highlighted in pale blue.

The row reads: "10 Sep 2025, Kalispell (FCA), El Paso (BIF), (AXEL10), 3:04, 11:27 AM, 11:27 AM, 2:31 PM, Landed 2:31 PM."

The date is the day of the shooting, the callsign is a military-style block callsign, and the destination airfield code BIF is Biggs Army Airfield at Fort Bliss.
