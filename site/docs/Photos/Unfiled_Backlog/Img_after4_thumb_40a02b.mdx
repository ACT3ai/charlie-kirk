## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical frame of the production area on the grass at the tour setup. A grey platform panel rests directly on two heavy hard-shell transport cases, forming a low riser. Behind it stand folding tables draped in dark blue and black cloths carrying the tour's lightning-bolt logo, and two people are visible out of focus near a metal crowd barrier.

The frame contains no readable on-screen text.

Production-area frames like this one matter for the reconstruction because they show where equipment sat, how the barrier line ran, and who could reach the staging area without passing a checkpoint. Those details feed the questions gathered in [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [Cameras](/cameras/overview).
