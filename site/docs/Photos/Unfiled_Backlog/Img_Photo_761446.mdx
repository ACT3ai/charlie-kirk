## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of an X post by an account shown as @amuse, quoting a post by an account shown as **Brandi Kruse**. The embedded card carries a police body-camera still stamped 2025-09-24 18:50:18 -0700 and AXON BODY 3, showing two young men standing in an asphalt loading area behind a large retail building.

The quoted post reads: "EXCLUSIVE: A @Walmart employee in Covington, WA, stole and destroyed voter signatures. When deputies responded, they told the suspect they were going to put him in handcuffs 'just for show' and then release him." The post above it argues that in some states "the police routinely work WITH far-left activists."

This screenshot is not about the [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting. It sits in the backlog as an example of the wider argument about selective law-enforcement conduct that runs through the investigation's commentary, and the claims in it are the posters' claims about a separate matter, reported here without adoption. For the material that is actually about policing in this case, see [Law Enforcement](/Law_Enforcement/overview) and [Political Context](/political_context/overview).
