## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **GoingStrategic**, timestamped 3:51 AM, **Sep 25, 2025**, with 54 views. The attached four-panel collage pairs bystander or security footage of a detention with a product image of a wooden-stock air rifle.

The post reads: "'Pellet Gun man' wrt Charlie Kirk. Anyone know his name or any more info? I'm investigating. What I know so far: He put a silencer on it. Why that is done for an Pellet gun makes no sense... unless to appear like the assassin. Pellet gun model: Ruger Yukon Magnum .177 Pellet Break Barrel Air Rifle."

The pellet gun episode is documented separately in this archive as one of the distraction threads. What makes it worth tracking is not the weapon's capability, which is negligible against the wound described, but the possibility that something was made to look like a rifle in the confusion. The man is unidentified, was not charged in connection with the shooting, and the poster's inference about intent is speculation.
