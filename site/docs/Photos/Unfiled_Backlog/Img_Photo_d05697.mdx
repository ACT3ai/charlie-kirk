## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post from an account shown as **Red Panda Koala**. Below the post text is a composite graphic containing a flight path map, an aircraft details panel, a photograph of a man speaking into a microphone and a photograph of an outdoor monument with a statue and obelisk.

The post reads: "An SAIC CIA plane was flying above Charlie Kirk during the assassination. Aircraft N1098L flew from New Mexico to the US Canada border the day of the Kirk assassination and stopped to do a loitering circle over Provo, Utah during the assassination. SAIC is long connected to the CIA, defense contracting, drones, UFOs, gangstalking." The graphic panel adds: "MILITARY PLANE ENTERS PROVO AREA 14 MINUTES BEFORE KIRK SHOT - LOITER LOOPS, SLOWS DOWN, LOWERS ALTITUDE 5000FT DEPARTS PROVO AIRSPACE 24 MINUTES AFTER." A row of handles credits several accounts including Diligent Denizen and John Cullen.

**N1098L** is one of the aircraft this investigation tracks most closely, alongside the Egyptian-registered **SU-BTT** and the Piper **N59906**, and its registration history and ownership chain are documented separately in the record. The characterization of the operator and the inference about intent are the poster's; the flight track itself can be checked against public data.
