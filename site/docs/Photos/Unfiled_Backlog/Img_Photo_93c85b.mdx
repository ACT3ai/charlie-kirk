## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Tony Seruga, handle @TonySeruga, posted three hours before capture, with an attached photograph of I VOTED stickers still on their white backing paper.

The post reads: "US Attorney for Nevada Starts Sweeping Formal Investigation into 2020 Stolen Presidential Election. Tick tock!" and links a Reuters article.

The linked report is mainstream wire coverage; the characterization of the 2020 result as stolen is the poster's addition and not something the linked outlet asserts. This is election material rather than case material, filed in the backlog with the rest. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Legal](/Legal/overview).
