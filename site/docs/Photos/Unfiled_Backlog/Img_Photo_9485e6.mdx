## What This Image Shows

A close screenshot of a Google Docs page, the toolbar visible at the top, showing a chronological list of April and May dates covering travel and tour events. Several lines carry yellow highlight and the bottom two are selected in light blue.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here.

This is one of a set of working chronology captures in the intake queue, all built for the same purpose: to establish where a subject was on given dates so that other records, chiefly flight data, can be tested against it.
