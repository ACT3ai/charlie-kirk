## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution, slightly blurred still from a video shot outdoors in bright sun. The lower-left foreground is filled by an out-of-focus hand and the top of someone's dark hair, as though the person filming was shading the lens or holding the camera close to their face. Beyond them a crowd is gathered outside a modern light-colored building under clear sky.

There is no on-screen text in the frame.

Obstructed handheld frames like this one are typical of what actually survives from a crowd: shaky, partly blocked, and shot by someone not trying to document anything. They still carry usable information, since the building edge and the crowd density fix a position and a moment. The larger problem of what crowd footage survived at all is documented in [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Videos](/Videos/overview).
