## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a long, text-heavy post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, posted seventeen hours before capture, ending in a preview strip of several Google Trends line charts.

The post opens: "BREAKING: The Charlie Kirk Assassination Plot Deepens - Israel and DC IPs Were STALKING His ENTIRE CIRCLE for MONTHS! This is NO 'Lone Gunman' BS," then continues that searches concentrated "Around JULY 20, 2025 - exactly 2 months before they took him out," that this was "the SAME WEEK Charlie platformed TUCKER CARLSON at AMFEST in Florida for the FIRST TIME," and that it has "SCREENSHOTS FROM GOOGLE TRENDS" for terms including an address given as "3419 S River Road, St. George UT."

The underlying artifacts are checkable in part: this archive separately documents the **3419 S River Road** address and maintains its own Google Searches material. What the post cannot support is the leap from regional search interest to intent, because Trends reports normalized volume by broad region and identifies no searcher. The framing and the conclusions are the poster's alone and are recorded here as claims.
