## What This Image Shows

A medium shot of two flat-screen monitors mounted vertically on a light grey interior wall. The upper monitor shows a multi-camera security grid covering exterior and interior angles of a property, including a white pickup truck in a driveway, with a channel label reading Front Entry and a burned-in date stamp. The lower monitor shows a split-screen feed of an outdoor media event, with a man in a white cap gesturing near a PA speaker.

The legible on-screen text includes the camera channel label, a partial timestamp, and a fragment of the Yrefy sponsor lettering on the event feed.

The frame is a record of a monitoring setup rather than of the event itself, and it shows two categories of footage in one shot: fixed premises cameras with burned-in timestamps, and the broadcast feed. Which recordings of each type exist, and which were preserved and produced, is a recurring question in this case, including the disputed handling of camera SD cards.
