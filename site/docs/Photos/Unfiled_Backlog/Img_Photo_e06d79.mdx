## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Charlie's Voice Rising, handle @CharlieK_news and labelled a fan account, posted an hour before capture, with a low-resolution surveillance video at 1:41 and a link to an outside site.

The post reads: "TWO MINUTES OF SURVEILLANCE FOOTAGE THEY HOPED YOU'D NEVER SEE. Conspiracy Day - Nov 3-4, 2020. Detroit, Michigan, TCF Center. Roughly 7 hours after polls closed, security cameras capture something wild," and then narrates named state officials unloading and re-entering with boxes at specific overnight times. It closes with turnout figures: "2020 turnout: 155,508,985. 2024 turnout: 140,306,875. 15,202,110 voters... vanished."

This is election material rather than case material. The narrative identifies named living officials as personally handling ballots on camera; that identification is the poster's, unverified, contradicted by the reviews and litigation that followed the 2020 count in Michigan, and it is not adopted here. Turnout differing between a presidential cycle and the next is ordinary. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
