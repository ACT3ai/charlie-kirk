## What This Image Shows

A browser capture of a healthcare provider directory page from a Utah hospital group.

The listing reads: "Lee Trotter, DO. Phone (435) 216-3590. Gender Male. Specialty General Surgery. Languages English. Address: Pediatrix Medical Group, Inc., 700 West 800 North Ste 220, Orem, UT 84057."

The medical response is one of the most examined parts of this case: which hospital **Charlie Kirk** was taken to and why it was not the closest, who was on the surgical team, and how the death certificate and medical examiner paperwork were handled. Public directory listings are how researchers establish who practised where and in what specialty. A directory entry is ordinary professional information and indicates nothing about any individual's conduct. See [Medical](/Medical/overview) and [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview).
