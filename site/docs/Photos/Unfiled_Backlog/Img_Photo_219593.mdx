## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a typed document in black sans-serif text on white, setting out a 2023 chronology that interleaves appearances by political figures, **Turning Point USA** events and flight records. One line is highlighted in blue and one in yellow.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here.

The reason the researchers went back to 2023 is that the aircraft-tailing claim is a claim about a pattern over years, not about a single week. Establishing whether a pattern exists requires exactly this kind of long-baseline sheet, and the rebuttal held elsewhere in this cluster attacks the same claim on the same ground.
