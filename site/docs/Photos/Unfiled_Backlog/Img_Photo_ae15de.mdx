## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as 1984, handle @TheOfficial1984, posted seven hours before capture. Beneath the post text sits a black quote card in white sans-serif type.

The post reads: "This is the @adl colluding with Israeli intelligence, and X, to track, target, flag and violate your RIGHT TO FREE SPEECH." The quote card reads: "Au10tix closes the loop: OHI detects, Cheq validates, Au10tix identifies-turning algorithmic flags into real-world traceability."

The named companies are real vendors in the identity verification and bot detection market, and the card describes a plausible technical chain: detection, validation and identity resolution. The allegation of collusion between an advocacy organization, a foreign intelligence service and a platform is the poster's, is unverified, and is not adopted here. The documented takedowns and account actions against people covering this case are collected in [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) and [Technology and Surveillance](/technology_surveillance/overview).
