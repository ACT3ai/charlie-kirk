## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an Instagram post from an account shown as tpusa_rockies, tagged at the Phoenix Convention Center. Seven young adults, five men and two women, pose standing and kneeling in front of a dark blue backdrop carrying **Turning Point USA** and **AmericaFest** branding.

The caption reads: "Best territory, change my mind #amfest2022," dated **19 December 2022**.

Organizational event photographs are how researchers establish who was involved with a group and when, particularly for people who later appear in the record of **September 10, 2025**. **AmFest** recurs in this archive, including in the reported plan to invite a guest to the 2025 edition. Everyone in this frame is attending a political conference, which is ordinary public activity, and nobody here is identified or accused of anything.
