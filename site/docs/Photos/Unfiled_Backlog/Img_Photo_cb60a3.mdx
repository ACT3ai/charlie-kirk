## What This Image Shows

A dark-mode screenshot of a single X post from an account shown as **Michael MacConnell**, posted three hours before capture. There is no attached media; the frame is the post text alone.

The post reads: "Reminder that the FBI Special Agent in Charge of Salt Lake City who was fired a few weeks before Kirk's assassination is a Pakistani Muslim counter-terrorism expert who wouldn't have been inclined to play ball with Zionists running a false-flag operation designed to implicate American liberals."

The verifiable part of that statement is the personnel change: leadership turnover in the Salt Lake City field office shortly before **September 10, 2025** is tracked in the investigation record alongside the broader pattern of new appointments across the agencies and offices that handled the case. Everything after the personnel fact is the poster's own theory, offered without supporting evidence, and it is reproduced here as a claim rather than a finding.
