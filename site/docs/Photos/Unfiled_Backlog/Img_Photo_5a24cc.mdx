## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **George Webb**, handle @RealGeorgeWe, posted 23 hours before capture. Below the text is a captured Wikipedia entry for **Nevatim Airbase**.

The post reads: "Erika Kirk's parents Golden Dome jets - 'Egyptian military planes' covering secret flights to Israel Negev desert - Mossad HQ by turning transponder off. We have covered the Negev air base since the Braverman Prophecy in September 2016!" The encyclopedia entry reads: "Nevatim Airbase, also Air Force Base 28, is an Israeli Air Force (IAF) base, located 15 km east-southeast of Beersheba, near moshav Nevatim in the northern Negev desert. It is one of the largest in Israel and has three runways of different lengths. Stealth fighter jets, transport aircraft, tanker aircraft and machines for electronic reconnaissance/surveillance, as well as the Wing of Zion, are stationed there."

The encyclopedia entry is background, not evidence. The claims in the post text, about transponder use, about the aircraft's purpose and about the family named, are unverified assertions by the poster, and the flight-data rebuttal held elsewhere in this cluster disputes the underlying tailing claim directly. No wrongdoing by any living person is established or alleged here. See [Planes](/Planes/overview), the [Aircraft photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/overview) and [Israel](/Israel/overview).
