## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a business lookup interface headed "COMPANY CONTACTS," showing two listings under a blue header bar.

The listings read: "DUANE BURT, Director, 943 E. Lockwood St, Mesa, AZ 85203," and "DUANE BURT, President/Ceo, 943 E Lockwood St, Mesa, AZ 85203."

This is the third registry capture in the Firebird set held in this cluster, and together they show the standard shape of a small closely held company: one person occupying every officer role, a residential-style address doing duty as principal address, registered agent address and mailing address. That structure is entirely lawful and extremely common. What any of these entities connects to is not established on this page. See [Companies and Organizations](/Companies_Organizations/overview).
