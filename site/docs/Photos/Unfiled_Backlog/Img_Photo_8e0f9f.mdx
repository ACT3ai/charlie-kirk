## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a short post from an account shown as CCG BRYSON, handle @RealBrysonGray, posted sixteen hours before capture.

The post reads: "Objective facts. 1. Tyler Robinson confessed to shooting Charlie kirk in discord messages. Evidence: The literal discord logs. 2. Tyler Robinson confessed to the shooting Charlie to his parents. Evidence: His parents said it."

This is the case for the official account stated in its most compressed form, and it belongs in the archive for exactly that reason. Both cited items are contested here on their own terms: the Discord material and its provenance, and the account of what the family said and when, are tracked as separate open questions, alongside the disputed timing of any statement relative to arrest and Miranda warnings. **Tyler Robinson** has not been convicted, and a claimed confession is not a proven one.
