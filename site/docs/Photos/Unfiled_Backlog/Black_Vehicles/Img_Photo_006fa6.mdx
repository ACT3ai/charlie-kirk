## What This Image Shows

A video playback frame in a picture-in-picture layout. The main image is a bright, sunlit outdoor scene at the event: a crowd gathered around black full-size SUVs, waving American flags and holding up smartphones to record. A smaller inset in the upper right shows a young man reacting to the footage indoors against a yellow door.

The visible on-screen text is the Yrefy sponsor lettering on the event branding behind the crowd.

The SUV detail matters because a vehicle from this arrival group carried **Charlie Kirk** to hospital minutes after the shot, and this archive logs unresolved questions about that vehicle: fragments reported on its floor, a photograph in which the windows appear blurred, and reports that it was later destroyed. This frame documents the before state, with the crowd standing directly alongside the vehicles.
