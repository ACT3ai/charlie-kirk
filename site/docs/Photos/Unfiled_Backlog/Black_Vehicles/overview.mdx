## About This Cluster

This cluster captures the black SUV presence around **Charlie Kirk's** arrival at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025**. Both stills come from a commentary video reviewing raw footage titled "Charlie Kirk's Last American Comeback Tour at UVU - Raw Footage," and show multiple black full-size SUVs, a uniformed security guard, and a crowd waving American flags and filming with phones. One SUV sits with its rear hatch open while people move around it.

The vehicles matter because an SUV from this same detail carried Kirk to the hospital minutes after the shot, and the investigation has logged unresolved questions about that vehicle: glass or plastic fragments reported on its floor, a photo in which the windows appear deliberately blurred, and reports that the SUV was later destroyed. Establishing which vehicles were present, who staffed them, and what happened to them afterward is groundwork for those questions. These frames document the before state, staging and crowd access, not any wrongdoing by anyone pictured.

Step through the two stills in order, then follow the SUV thread into [After the Shooting](/After/overview) and the destroyed-vehicle and blurred-window claims in [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview). For who was working the detail itself, see the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview).
