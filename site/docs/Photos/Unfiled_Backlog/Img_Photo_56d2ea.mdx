## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Blake Bednarz**, handle @BlakeBednarz. The embedded video player is paused on a close, slightly blurred view of a man in a white t-shirt seated on stage behind a PA speaker.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file. Per the visual description, the post questions the official shooting account and proposes instead that a small explosive device detonated under the shirt.

The **Bednarz** name carries weight in this archive because the original UVU video and its metadata report, published through this investigation and pinned to IPFS, come from that source. The device hypothesis is the same one argued in more technical form elsewhere in this cluster, and the independent ballistics presentation held here argues against it. It remains a hypothesis.
