## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a quoted X thread. The outer post comes from an account shown as Shadowed News, handle @shadowed_news; it quotes a post from an account shown as Israel Exposed, handle @xIsraelExposedx, dated **Aug 18**. Inside the quote sits an earlier capture timestamped 11:55 AM, **13 Jul 25**, showing three dark grey media containers each carrying the placeholder "This video has been deleted."

The quoted post reads: "X has been quietly deleting war crimes videos without notification or flagging the posts. Like this:" The outer caption asserts a motive for the removals, which is the poster's own characterization and is not established by anything in the frame.

What the image itself documents is the mechanism rather than the motive: media removed in place, leaving the post standing with empty slots and no notice to the author. The same failure mode is what makes archival of this investigation's material urgent, and comparable takedowns after **September 10, 2025** are catalogued in [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
