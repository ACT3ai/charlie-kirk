## What This Image Shows

A broadcast frame companion to the later panel in this cluster. **Candace Owens** is at the studio desk at frame left, with a tan overlay at frame right carrying items 1 to 7 of a numbered list headed "TPUSA Verified Lies."

The listed statements, each attributed on the panel to a named individual, are: that a young staff member "had blood all over him"; that he "called his father first"; that he "was a hero that day"; that "Charlie's neck stopped a 30-06 bullet because he ate salad and had strong bones. It was a modern day Christian miracle"; that "Charlie never wavered in his support for Israel"; that "Charlie was a committed Evangelical, who only liked Catholic architecture"; and that "Ben Shapiro and Charlie Kirk never had any bad blood between them."

As with the companion panel, the heading and the attributions are the presenter's characterizations. Those named dispute her account and no finding has been made against any of them. The items themselves point at real evidentiary disputes in this archive: the wound and ballistics description, the reported shift in Kirk's Israel position, and the documented friction with other movement figures.
