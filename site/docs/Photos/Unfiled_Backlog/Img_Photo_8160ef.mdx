## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a long post from an account shown as **The SCIF**, handle @TheIntelSCIF, with a composite graphic below carrying a news headline about foreign interference and two photographs of seized identification documents laid out on tables.

The post reads: "FBI HAS INTEL and EVIDENCE THE 2020 ELECTION WAS STOLEN through a global election fraud cartel with the help and interference of Democrats and foreign nations in conjunction with USAID and AWEB," and continues that the scheme is "linked to over 109 countries including South Korea, Canada, Brazil, Australia, Iraq." It adds: "FBI Director Kash Patel has declassified and shared documents with senators that he says 'detail alarming allegations' about potential Democrats and Chinese interference in the 2020 election using fake IDs, fake ballots, and electronically tampering with voting machines," and then notes the denial: "China has denied any nefarious involvement in swaying the election results in 2020 and again in 2024."

This is election material rather than case material. The post's own text distinguishes between documents said to contain allegations and the allegations being established, and it carries the denial; both distinctions are preserved here.
