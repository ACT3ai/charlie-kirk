## What This Image Shows

A composite of two aerial photographs of the same campus plaza, stacked vertically and shot from different high angles. Both show the tiered chevron-shaped lawns, concrete pathways and the raised brick walkway. Red arrows and the white caption "SHOOTER WAS HERE" mark one position in each frame.

The visible credits read "gettyimages, Credit: Chet Strange," identifying the underlying photographs as agency press images.

Working from professionally shot press aerials is a strength, because they are higher resolution and better documented than most of the material here. The annotation is the annotator's assertion of a position, not a finding, and different researchers in this archive mark different roofs and different spots. The acoustic and echolocation work is the independent check on all of them. See [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview), [Maps](/maps/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
