## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Peter Bernegger**, handle @PeterBernegger, posted 47 minutes before capture, with a press photograph of a state official at a podium and an embedded earlier post from the same account carrying a spreadsheet snippet.

The post reads: "Its seeming more and more Colorado and Dominion voting machines got caught again - Nov 4th 2025 election! 2nd tweet on this: the Sec. of State took down the El Paso County election results from their state website. Those results were up on the site a few hours ago! They are claiming there is a software bug pertaining to ballots cast." The embedded earlier post reads: "El Paso County Colorado Nov 4th 2025 election results... being investigated. Canvasser(s) refusing to sign off on certification: numbers are not matching up. Attorneys being sent in from Sec. of State's office tomorrow morning."

This is election material rather than case material. The observable facts, a results page taken down and a stated software issue, are separable from the conclusion drawn about them, which is the poster's. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
