## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of an X post from an account shown as **Shaun King**. The post carries a link card to a Substack article whose headline repeats the claim in the post, illustrated with a photograph of two men seated together indoors, identified in the visual description as **Benjamin Netanyahu** and **Elon Musk**.

The text extraction sidecar for this file returned no usable lines, so the post is summarized from the visual description rather than quoted. Its substance is the assertion that the platform removed the author's posts at the request of a foreign government, an allegation the author attributes to what his lawyers were told and which no platform statement in the frame confirms.

This is the companion capture to the other King post held in this cluster, and the pair is kept because they document a claimed removal mechanism rather than any fact about the **September 10, 2025** shooting.
