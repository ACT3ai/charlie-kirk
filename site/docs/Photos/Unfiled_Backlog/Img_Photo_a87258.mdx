## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a flight tracking log for tail number **N102DZ**, listing September 2025 movements with origin, destination, duration and status columns. The row dated **10 Sep 2025** is highlighted in pale blue.

The visible legs read, in reverse date order: 19 Sep Bentonville to Morristown; 19 Sep Scottsdale to Bentonville; 14 Sep New Braunfels to Scottsdale; 14 Sep Hailey to New Braunfels; 14 Sep Chicago to Hailey; 14 Sep Scottsdale to Chicago; 14 Sep Phoenix to Scottsdale; 11 Sep Provo to Phoenix; 10 Sep Camarillo to Provo; 10 Sep Provo to Camarillo; 10 Sep Scottsdale to Provo; 10 Sep Salt Lake City to Scottsdale; 07 Sep Los Angeles to Scottsdale; and 04 Sep Dallas to Los Angeles, with durations from 0:07 to 2:53.

**N102DZ** is described in the flight documents held elsewhere in this cluster as the aircraft associated with **Charlie Kirk**, and this log is the primary record behind those summaries. It shows four legs on **September 10, 2025** beginning from Salt Lake City, and a departure from Provo the following day. Cross-checking a summary against the raw log is exactly the discipline the disputed aircraft claims in this archive require.
