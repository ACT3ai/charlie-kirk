## What This Image Shows

A composite image. The background is a wide, high-angle photograph of the terraced campus plaza after the event, its geometric lawns strewn with litter. In the upper-left corner sits a smaller inset of the same event from close range, annotated with red and green circles and an explanatory text box.

The annotation text box reads: "BLUE SHIRT IS BRIAN HARPOLE. MANAGING DIRECTOR OF INTEGRITY SECUITY SOLUTIONS. AKA THE LEADER. BLACK SHIRT IS DAN FLOOD WITH META SUNGLASSES. THE TWO GREEN CIRCLES ARE TPUSA'S TERRYL AND '1776' HAT GUY WHO PLACED MIC ON CHARLIE." Banner text for the tour and the Yrefy sponsor panel is visible in the inset behind the circles.

These labels are the annotator's identifications, not confirmed ones, and being present at an event and doing a job at it is all that any circle in this frame shows. The people named worked in security and event roles that the investigation documents separately, including the question of who attached the microphone and how.
