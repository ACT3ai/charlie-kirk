## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, wrapping a mobile browser capture of a Steam community profile page.

The post reads: "ALERT: Somebody Named Ankha Is Also In Tyler Robinson's Steam Group. Notably They Claim To be From Egypt. Hmm... Where else have we heard Egypt before? Oh Yeah That's right, @RealCandaceO Exposed There Were Egyptian Government Planes and Egyptian Military Contractors In Provo, UT On Sept 10th. Same Day Charlie Kirk was assassinated." The profile shows a display name, a location of Egypt, a game-related tagline including the phrase "Death Runner AKA Sniper GOD," a Discord handle and a level badge.

The connection asserted here is thin by any standard: a self-declared country field on a gaming profile, set against a separate aircraft thread. Location fields on gaming platforms are unverified and freely chosen, gaming taglines about snipers are ubiquitous, and belonging to a group is not a relationship. The Egyptian aircraft material stands or falls on the flight records, not on this. See [Planes](/Planes/overview) and [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview).
