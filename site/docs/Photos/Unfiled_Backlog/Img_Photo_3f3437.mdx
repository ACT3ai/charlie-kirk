## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Rasmussen Reports**, handle @Rasmussen_Poll, posted 52 seconds before capture. An embedded data table shows 2020 Arizona results by county, and the post quotes an earlier item from the same account dated **June 11, 2024**.

The post reads: "Arizona 2020: Maricopa County - 1 legal election ballot paper type, 10 types found - Over 200,000 'non-conforming' ballots found - 1 legal software version, 2 versions and a compiler found - Biden 2020 Arizona 'win' margin - 10,457 'votes'." The table beneath it shows the Maricopa County line as 1,040,774 to 995,665.

This is election material rather than case material, and the claims listed are contested and were not sustained in the reviews and litigation that followed. It is recorded here as the poster's assertion. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Political Context](/political_context/overview).
