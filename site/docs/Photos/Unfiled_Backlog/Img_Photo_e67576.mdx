## What This Image Shows

A split-screen composite. The left half stacks three lower-resolution panels of the crowded outdoor event, each marked with red circles and arrows pointing at one location on a concrete wall. The right half is a high-resolution close-up of that spot: a black rectangular fixture set into a recessed wall niche, glowing bright yellow-green.

The overlay text reads "THE LIGHT OVER THE GRASSY KNOLL.." with a credit to an account shown as @THEINTELSCIF.

The title is a deliberate borrowing from the Kennedy case, and the argument is the same shape: an object in the built environment, close to the target, that no official account has explained. Whether the fixture is ordinary architectural lighting or something else is exactly what the composite invites the viewer to ask, and nothing here answers it.
