## What This Image Shows

A browser capture of an X thread. The main panel is a heavily cropped, low-resolution photograph of a flat rooftop against hazy sky and distant mountains, overlaid with a digital clock graphic reading 12:06pm and a thick red arrow pointing at the roof. The right column shows the post text, engagement counts and a reply.

The post, from an account shown as @HustleBitch_, reads: "WHO IS THIS? - September 10, 2025 at 12:06 PM - The Woodbury Building roof, directly next to the Losee Center at UVU. - 17 minutes before Charlie Kirk was assassinated. LOOK CLOSER." A reply from an account shown as @PaladinR describes a hypothesis it labels "OPERATION KIRK," auto-translated by the platform from Portuguese.

Which roofs at **Utah Valley University** had people on them, and when, is one of the case's central factual disputes, and the Losee Center and the buildings around it recur throughout the record. This frame is a low-resolution crop with a hand-placed arrow and a hand-placed clock; it identifies no one and establishes no time of its own.
