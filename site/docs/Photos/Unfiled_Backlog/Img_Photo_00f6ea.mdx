## What This Image Shows

A plain screen capture of a university testing services agreement, set as two bulleted lists on white.

The text reads: "To take my exams using DAC services, I agree to: Submit my DAC accommodations requests, via myDAC, for each course that I intend to utilize my implemented DAC accommodations within. Schedule each individual quiz or exam of mine a minimum of 3 business days in advance to ensure sufficient staffing levels. Schedule each individual Final Exam no later than the last day to withdraw from a class... Arrive on time at the DAC Testing Center on the day and exact time I scheduled the exam/quiz. Conduct myself professionally and respectfully at all times." A second list is headed: "When taking exams, I agree to: Monitor my own testing time and submit my quizzes/exams before the calculated..."

This is an ordinary campus administrative document and it has not been placed within the investigation. It carries no names and no connection to the events of **September 10, 2025** is established by it. It is described here so the archive's record of the intake queue is accurate.
