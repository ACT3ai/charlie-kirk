## What This Image Shows

An annotated satellite map covering central Washington, D.C., from the United States Capitol south to Virginia Ave SE, with text overlays, arrows and colored dots plotting a timed route.

The annotations read: "White Hyundai Turned 7.16," "Bomber First Seen 7.34," "Bomber Looking Around 7.40," "Plants Bomb 7.52," near the DNC building; then "Back A: 8:00," "Cole Nissan? seen at 8.03," "Plants Bomb 8:14" near the RNC building; plus "White Hyundai U Turn hits Kerb," "Waves to Cop?," "Bounce luggage Stor No Cameras," "Lockers in 3 Taps" and "3 Parks Car?".

This concerns the still-unidentified person who placed pipe bombs at the two party headquarters in Washington on the night of **January 5, 2021**, not the events at **Utah Valley University**. It sits in this backlog because the analytical method is the same one applied throughout this archive: build a minute-by-minute route from surveillance frames, then ask which cameras should have covered the gaps and why nothing was produced from them. Every annotation is the mapmaker's reading. See [Technology and Surveillance](/technology_surveillance/overview), [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [Maps](/maps/overview).
