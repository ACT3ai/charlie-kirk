## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as TRILLION, handle @Trillion0x, posted fifteen hours before capture, with an embedded card for a live audio Space and a reply below from an account shown as RealBaronPodcast.

The post reads: "X deleted the first space @BasedSamParker spoke on after CK. That's super unfortunate bc I download all of my spaces. I'll upload the recording shortly." The embedded card reads: "Justice for Charlie 01. CHARLIE KIRK AFTERSPACE + 9/11 Pilot Whistleblowers Dan and Doug. Sep 11, 2025," over a panel reading "Space Unavailable," with the annotation "nice try Elon" and a timestamp of 4:43 PM, **9/26/25**. The reply reads: "If they're going to use AI to make me go after @BasedSamParker, why did they give me Ben Shapiro's voice?"

This documents the removal of a recorded live discussion held on **September 11, 2025**, the day after the shooting, along with its title and participants. The poster's practice of downloading his own recordings is the practical answer to that failure mode, and it is the same reasoning behind this archive's own preservation work. The reply raises a separate concern about synthesized voice audio circulating in this space. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
