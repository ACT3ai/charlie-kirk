## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as [**Project Constitution**](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution), handle [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution), posted five hours before capture. The multi-panel image shows the profile page of an account and the platform's suspension notice.

The post reads: "ALERT: Very Strange that this man's X account just so happened to be suspended the same day i put out my story on his and Charlie's relationship and Charlie knowing he was in danger back in December of 2024. I'm sure that's just a coincidence though right?" The profile panel shows "Arizona, USA, Born June 4, 1970, Joined March 2025" and the pinned post about lawfare; the notice reads: "Your account (@MFrye85295) is currently suspended... you can't post, Repost, or Like content. You won't be able to create new accounts."

The same profile appears earlier in this cluster in an unsuspended state, which is what makes the pair useful: the archive holds a before and an after. The timing may be coincidence and the post says as much sarcastically; suspensions have many causes and no cause is shown here. The claim that [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) knew he was in danger as early as [**December 2024**](/Timeline/Year_2024) is tracked separately in this archive on other grounds. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview), [Before the Shooting](/Before/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
