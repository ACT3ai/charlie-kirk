## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of the same interactive relationship-mapping application held elsewhere in this cluster, this time with a different dossier card open. The left canvas shows a node-link diagram of colored circles arranged in a ring; the right sidebar holds the card and the people database.

The open card reads: "INVESTIGATION DOSSIER. [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy). 10/10 - TPUSA. TPUSA Chief of Staff. ACTIVE," with a note fragment referring to a family relationship and to "Observed making calls," a suspicion rating, and connections listed to [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk), [**Blake Neff**](/People/blake-neff) and [**Rob McCoy**](/People/rob-mccoy). The database below repeats the roster held elsewhere in this cluster, with role labels, status flags and scores out of ten.

As with the companion capture, every label, score and status on this screen is the tool builder's working classification rather than an established fact, and the observational notes are one researcher's reading of video. Making phone calls during an emergency is ordinary behaviour. Being listed here is not an accusation, and except for the person charged, none of the living people shown has been charged in connection with the shooting.
