## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Johnny Midnight, handle @its_The_Dr, posted eleven hours before capture, with an embedded clip at 1:49 showing a woman speaking at a committee hearing, audience members seated in red chairs behind her.

The post reads: "The Arizona Senate says Katie Hobbs was involved in election rigging. Investigators found 'more than a 100,000 filled in ballot and more than $30 million dollars were identified by our investigators in unmarked rental cars'."

This is the same hearing clip circulated in another post held in this cluster. It is election material rather than case material. Testimony before a committee is a witness assertion, not a legislative finding, and the claim that a named living official was involved in rigging an election is unproven, was rejected in litigation, and is not adopted here.
