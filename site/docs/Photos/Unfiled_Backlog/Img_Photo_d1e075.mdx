## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as Charlie's Voice Rising, handle @CharlieK_news and labelled a fan account, posted three hours before capture. The embedded clip is a low-resolution broadcast of a woman speaking at a microphone during a hearing, with closed captions reading "the one point nine million envelopes from the" and a slide counter of 23/23.

The post reads: "LOOK AT THIS: Mail-in ballots in Democrat-run states and cities are a total disaster - and Arizona just proved it. The Arizona Senate audit of Katie Hobbs and Adrian Fontes found something insane: 25% of all 2020 mail-in ballots - 420,987 out of 1.9 million - had zero signature match. And that's just Maricopa County. How was this ever certified?"

This is election material rather than case material, and the figure quoted is contested; signature-matching methodology disputes turn on what counts as a match and which images were compared. It is recorded here as a claim attributed to its poster. See [Vote](/Vote/overview) and [Political Context](/political_context/overview).
