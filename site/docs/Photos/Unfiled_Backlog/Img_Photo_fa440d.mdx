## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, posted three hours before capture. The video player is paused at 0:08 of a 23-second clip showing a man in a light blue short-sleeved shirt and dark trousers walking away from the camera.

The post reads: "EXCLUSIVE: NEW FOOTAGE Shows FBI Agents Spotted ON 925 S ST in Orem Just Hours After Kirk Assassination. I just obtained footage showing FBI agents combing for evidence in Orem just hours after Charlie Kirk was assassinated at UVU. Location: 670 925 Street, Orem."

The address is the useful part, because it can be checked against the other locations this archive tracks in Orem, including the doorbell footage claim on a nearby street held elsewhere in this cluster. What the clip actually shows is one man walking; the identification of him as a federal agent is the poster's. Federal agents searching a neighbourhood after a shooting is ordinary investigative work. See [FBI](/FBI/overview), [Locations](/Locations/overview) and [After the Shooting](/After/overview).
