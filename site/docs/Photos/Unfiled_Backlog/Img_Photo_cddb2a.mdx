## What This Image Shows

A wide, high-angle frame from a video player showing a large outdoor gathering on the campus plaza, mountains visible beyond, under a bright clear sky. A red circle has been drawn onto the upper-left quadrant, marking an upper-level balcony area on a modern building.

The video title bar reads: "Raw Footage of Charlie Kirk TPUSA Shooting at Utah Valley University."

Balconies matter for the same reason roofs do: they are elevated positions with a clear line to the tent, and each one has to be accounted for before any firing-position claim can be settled. Circling one is a way of asking the question, not answering it, and the frame supports no identification of anything on the balcony.
