## What This Image Shows

A close screenshot of a typed document listing, side by side, speaking engagements for **Charlie** and flight itineraries for the aircraft referred to as **BTT** and **BND**, running from **August 17** to **September 13**. Passages are picked out in bright yellow and light blue highlight.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual legs and dates are not quoted here.

This is the working sheet behind the aircraft-tailing argument: the whole claim depends on placing a person's public schedule and an aircraft's movements on the same timeline and looking for coincidence. Building that comparison is legitimate method; whether the coincidences survive checking is contested, and the flight-data rebuttal held elsewhere in this cluster argues that many of the claimed legs did not happen.
