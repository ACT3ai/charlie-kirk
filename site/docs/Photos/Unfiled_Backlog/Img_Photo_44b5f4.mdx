## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as The General, handle @GeneralMCNews, posted an hour before capture. The composite below it shows direct message exchanges on either side of a profile preview panel and a cropped photograph.

The post reads: "BREAKING: Kash Patel's FBI says it cannot locate Israeli sniper Aaron Levitsky, who threatened to take out a Christian influencer on X, claiming his country of origin can't be disclosed." The message panel shows an exchange timestamped between 12:17 AM and 12:36 AM containing hostile lines including "There'll be deadly consequences," alongside a profile bio reading "Israeli-American, Jewish, sculptor, Sniper, working on something greater than myself."

The messages are presented by a third party and are unverified as captured; no charge is recorded against the person named, and nothing here establishes that he sent them. The reason the frame is retained is the pattern rather than the individual: threats reported by people covering this investigation, and reported difficulty getting them acted on, are documented across the record.
