## What This Image Shows

An upward-angled frame captured from a video player, with the play controls and progress bar still visible across the bottom. It shows a poured concrete ceiling with a thick horizontal seam running across the lower third, a rectangular cutout housing a ventilation unit near the top center, and a dome-style security camera mounted on a metal conduit pipe directly in front of that vent.

No usable on-screen text appears in the frame beyond the player furniture.

Camera positions at **Utah Valley University** are a documented gap in the case. The investigation record tracks which fixed cameras covered which approaches, what footage was requested, what was produced, and what witnesses say vanished from their own phones. A frame that pins one camera to one ceiling is a small piece of that map. See [Cameras](/cameras/overview), [Technology and Surveillance](/technology_surveillance/overview) and [UVU](/UVU/overview).
