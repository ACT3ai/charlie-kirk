## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an email received by a tip line, dated **Wednesday, December 10, 2025**, from a sender whose details are redacted in the capture. The message is presented on a dark grey background with a thin decorative border.

The email lays out flight tracking for a U.S. Government **C-37A Gulfstream V** VIP jet, listing movements to and from Andrews Air Force Base, Colorado Springs, Tucson, **Fort Huachuca** and El Paso. The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines, so the individual legs are not quoted here.

**Fort Huachuca** is one of the recurring locations in this investigation, and this archive separately documents a SAM flight to Fort Huachuca carrying a named VIP passenger in the days before **September 10, 2025**. Tip emails of this kind are raw leads, unverified and unattributed as captured, but the underlying flight records are checkable.
