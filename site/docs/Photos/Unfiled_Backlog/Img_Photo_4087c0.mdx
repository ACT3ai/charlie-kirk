## What This Image Shows

A split-screen frame. **Candace Owens** is at a studio desk at frame left; at frame right, a spreadsheet of short entries is grouped under column headings including "Liberty University" and "Eastern Europe."

The legible entries are largely biographical: several named individuals "attended and graduated" from the university; one "was accepted, but chose not to attend and began working for TPUSA instead"; one "met Charlie Kirk via Jerry Falwell Jr."; another "came out of Johnnie Moore's Kairos Company." A separate group of entries concerns Romania: "N888KG formerly registered in Romania as YR-NVY April 26th, 2013 - October 29th, 2015. Owned by SC International Charter"; "Late 2012/early 2013: Erika Kirk begins Romanian Angels project"; "2011: Tyler Bowyer goes to Romania to work with the Romanian government"; and a 2013 photograph entry. Other cells in the grid carry serious criminal allegations against organizations and individuals; those are unverified, unproven, and are not reproduced here.

The checkable element is the aircraft registration history, since **N888KG** is tracked in this archive on flight and corporate records and a prior foreign registration is the kind of fact that can be confirmed. Attending a university, doing charitable work abroad or working for an organization is not evidence of anything, and no living person named on this screen has been charged in connection with the shooting.
