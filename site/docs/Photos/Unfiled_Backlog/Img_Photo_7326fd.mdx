## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Red Panda Koala**, handle @RedPandaKoala, posted an hour before capture. The attached three-panel collage places surveillance stills of a young man walking outdoors beside a photograph of a scoped bolt-action rifle.

The post reads: "How did 'Tyler Robinson' fit the gun in his skinny jeans? Why can we see his phone in his pocket but not the gun?"

The observation is simple and physical, which is what makes it one of the more durable objections in the record: a full-length bolt-action rifle with a mounted scope is a large object, and the surveillance frames show a slim silhouette with an outlined phone in a pocket. This archive tracks the disassembly-and-backpack account, the compressed timeline and the clothing changes as connected disputes. **Tyler Robinson** has not been convicted, and the identification in the surveillance stills is contested.
