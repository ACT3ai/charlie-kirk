## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of an X post from an account shown as **Clint Russell**, handle @LibertyLockPod, posted two hours before capture. Embedded beneath the text is a dark-mode capture of a post by an account shown as **Josh Hammer** replying "based" to an **Apr 19, 2013** post by an account shown as Donald J. Trump reading "Should be public execution for all to see-you will end this bullshit fast!"

Russell's post reads: "9/8 Charlie Kirk sends a group text to Josh Hammer and others in which he says he refuses to be bullied by his zionist donors and he has no choice but to leave the pro-Israel cause / 9/9 Josh praises a 12-year-old tweet from Trump on public executions / 9/10 Charlie publicly executed."

This is a three-line juxtaposition of dates, and it should be treated as exactly that. The **September 8, 2025** group text about donor pressure is separately documented in this archive; the reaction to a 2013 post about criminal sentencing is a matter of public record; connecting them is the poster's inference and nothing here establishes that anyone had advance knowledge of anything. The underlying donor-pressure material is in [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [Motive](/Motive/overview).
