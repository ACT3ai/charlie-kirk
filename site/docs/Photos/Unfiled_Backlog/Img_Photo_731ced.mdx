## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a social media feed. The upper portion shows notification lines summarizing the user's own posts, including one beginning "BIG SECRET: She says the Quiet Part out Loud... 'evil Do-ERs' (Plural). She only said it once but I think it was intentional," referencing a speech on **9/12/2025**, and another beginning "IMPORTANT: Charlie Kirk. I have a critical contribution beyond @TheLFLounge's great post about state level (instead of UVU) doing the 'Landscaping' with breakneck speed."

Below the notifications sits a reply from an account shown as WILLIAM CUMBY, posted sixteen minutes before capture, with an attached photograph of law enforcement officers at an outdoor scene, one wearing an FBI-labelled shirt. The reply reads: "Wonder what he took pictures of and what he's looking at on the ground..."

Two separate threads surface in this one frame: the speed of the landscaping work done around the tent area afterward, which this archive tracks in detail, and what agents were photographing on the ground at the scene. Both bear on how the site was handled in the hours and days after. See [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview), [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [FBI](/FBI/overview).
