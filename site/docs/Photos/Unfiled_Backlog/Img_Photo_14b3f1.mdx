## What This Image Shows

A split-screen frame. **Candace Owens** is at a studio desk at frame left; at frame right, a shared document lists private flight records for three tail numbers.

The document reads: "Tail# N560TW Cessna Citation XLS (7-8 passengers). Departed: from Scottsdale at 8:00am (local). Arrived: in Provo at 10:06am (local). Departed: 10:24am for Santa Barbara, Landed: into SB at 11:12am. Departed: Santa Barbara at 11:37am. Arrived: into Scottsdale, landing at 12:44pm (local). Passengers: ???" Then: "Tail# N40JD Beechcraft Premiere 1 (6 seats). Departed: Scottsdale at 12:27pm Local. Arrived: 2:29pm (local) into Provo. Passengers: ???" And: "N102DZ G5 (15-16 Passengers) **Charlie's Plane in SLC. Arrived: in Scottsdale at 1:13pm (local) time. Immediately Departed: 1:31pm from Scottsdale. Arrived: Lands into Provo at 3:31PM (local). Passengers: ???"

This is one of the most specific aircraft documents in the cluster: three airframes, sizes, and same-day legs converging on **Provo** on **September 10, 2025**, with the passenger field left open on each. **N560TW** and **N102DZ** appear in the flight activity log held elsewhere here, and the times are broadly consistent between the two documents, which is the kind of cross-check that matters. Who was aboard is the unanswered question. See [Planes](/Planes/overview) and the [Aircraft photo cluster](/Photos/Aircraft/overview).
