## What This Image Shows

A wide broadcast capture. **Candace Owens** is seated at a desk with a laptop and microphone at frame left, looking toward the document; the right half of the frame holds a word processor page of chronological entries, some highlighted in yellow and some in light blue.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here.

The same two-color highlighting convention runs through the aircraft working documents held elsewhere in this cluster, where blue and yellow separate two registrations. Reading working documents on air is how much of this material entered public circulation, and it also means the underlying sheets, rather than the broadcast, are what should be checked.
