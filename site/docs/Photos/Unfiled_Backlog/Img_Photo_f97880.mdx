## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide still from a video of the event under harsh midday sun. A yellow pointer line is drawn across the frame to the seated speaker in a white t-shirt under the white canopy. Several people stand around him under the tent, and a dense crowd presses against the metal barriers around the perimeter.

On-screen text includes the AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR and PROVE ME WRONG banner lettering, a watermark reading CROWDSOURCE THE TRUTH, and a player timestamp of 0:22 / 0:35.

The watermark is worth noting on its own: much of the surviving footage of this event reaches the archive through independent channels that stamp their own branding onto it, which adds a layer between the viewer and the original recording.
