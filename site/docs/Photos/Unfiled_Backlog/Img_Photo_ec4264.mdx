## What This Image Shows

A high-speed frame capturing a fluid-filled object bursting in mid-air in a starburst of reddish-brown liquid, centred over a wooden workbench in a grassy, wooded backyard. A boardwalk crosses a small body of water in the background.

The only on-screen text is a player position reading 1:15 / 2:31.

Backyard demonstrations of this kind are how independent analysts show what a projectile does to a fluid-filled target, and they are the amateur cousin of the gel-block testing referenced in the ballistics presentation held elsewhere in this cluster. Such demonstrations illustrate a principle; they do not reconstruct a specific wound, and nothing here bears directly on the medical record.
