## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Project Constitution**, handle @ProjectConstitu, with an attached collage placing photographs of a security professional from 2017 beside photographs from the **September 10, 2025** event.

The post states that **Brian Harpole**, a former police officer working in private security, was part of **Charlie Kirk's** detail at the event and was part of a foreign head of government's personal security team in 2017, and asserts that the side-by-side photographs "leave no room for doubt: It's the same man, in the same elite role." The post's headline goes considerably further and poses a question implying involvement in Kirk's death.

That implication is not supported by anything in the post or in this archive, and it is not adopted here. Working a protective detail, at either event, is lawful professional work and is not evidence of wrongdoing by anyone. The identification itself is the poster's reading of two photographs and has not been independently confirmed. Harpole has publicly disputed claims made about him and has pursued legal action over statements in this area. For what is documented about the detail on the day, see the [Security Team photo cluster](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) and [Security](/security_law_enforcement/overview).
