## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Kevin Sorbo**, handle @ksorbs, posted three hours before capture. The attached graphic tiles three photographs of young men with a black headline banner across the lower left.

The post reads: "Tyler Robinson's supposed boyfriend is gone." The banner headline reads: "Boyfriend of Kirk Suspect Disappears - Alarming Thing Left Behind."

The person referred to is **Lance Twiggs**, who shared an address with **Tyler Robinson** and appears throughout the record in the message material and the timeline of the days around **September 10, 2025**. He has not been charged with anything and there is no indication he was involved in the shooting; being someone's roommate or partner is not evidence of anything. "Disappears" is the headline's framing, not an established fact. See [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) and [People](/People/overview).
