## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Chris Martenson**, handle @chrismartenson, posted two hours before capture, quoting a post from an account shown as EXX ALERTS. The embedded article image shows a man standing beside an electronic voting machine.

The outer post reads: "Once is an accident. Twice is coincidence. But three times is enemy action." The quoted alert reads: "Voting machines DOWN in three CONSERVATIVE districts in New Jersey," with the article headline: "PRESLER: VOTING MACHINES DOWN IN THREE RED DISTRICTS OF NEW JERSEY AS ELECTION DAY HEATS UP."

This is election material rather than case material. Equipment failures on election day are common and the inference drawn from a count of three is the poster's, offered as an aphorism rather than an analysis. It is recorded without adoption. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
