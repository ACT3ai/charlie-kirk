## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a full-width post from an account shown as **Patrick Colbeck**, handle @pjcolbeck, posted three hours before capture.

The post reads: "BREAKING: Dominion sold and renamed as 'Liberty Vote'. Re-branding doesn't hide the following facts: Electronic voting systems are NOT secure. Dominion Voting Systems feature Smartmatic DNA. Whistleblowers responsible for running Venezuelan elections confirm that Smartmatic has been used to steal Venezuelan elections. 48% of Dominion ImageCast Precinct tabulators are manufactured by CCP-aligned businesses in China. Dominion Election Management System (EMS) software is developed in Belgrade, Serbia on networks compromised by the CCP." It closes with an allegation of perjury against a named company executive.

This is election material rather than case material. The listed claims are contested; several were the subject of major litigation that the voting systems company won, and the allegation against the named executive is unproven and not adopted here. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
