## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as Propaganda & co, handle @propandco, posted eighteen hours before capture. It carries a cropped excerpt with passages highlighted in yellow and underlined in red, plus a link preview from **The Grayzone**.

The excerpt reads: "According to Kirk's longtime friend, Kirk's resentment of Netanyahu and the Israel lobby was spreading within Trump's inner circle. In fact, they said, the president himself was terrified of Netanyahu's wrath, and feared the consequences of defying him. During the past year, the Trump insider was told by contacts in the White House that the Secret Service had caught Israeli government personnel placing electronic devices on its emergency response vehicles on two separate occasions. While The Grayzone was unable to confirm the story with..." The link preview headline reads: "Charlie Kirk refused Netanyahu funding offer, was 'frightened' by pro-Israel forces before death."

The excerpt is unusual in this archive for containing its own limitation: the outlet states plainly that it could not confirm the electronic-device account. That is the standard the rest of this material should be held to. The reported refused funding offer and the pressure described are tracked here through several independent routes. The caption above the excerpt is the poster's own rhetoric. See [Israel](/Israel/overview), [Motive](/Motive/overview) and [Charlie Kirk](/Charlie/overview).
