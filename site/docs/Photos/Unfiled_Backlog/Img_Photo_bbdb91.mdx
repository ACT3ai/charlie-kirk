## What This Image Shows

A light-mode screenshot of a post from an account shown as Brandon Taylor Moore, handle [@LetsGoBrando45](/Influencers/x/letsgobrando45), posted eight hours before capture, with an attached black-and-white graphic carrying overlaid explanatory text.

The post reads: "The Bolshevik Revolution was the first successful communist coup. It's how Russia became the Soviet Union. The first law they passed was outlawing anti-Semitism. They burned over 54,000 churches and killed over 60,000,000 Russian Christians. They made Hitler, Hitler." The graphic text reads: "Hitler portrayed Bolshevism as an imminent danger to Germany's sovereignty and racial purity, warning that 'the danger to which Russia succumbed is always present for Germany.'"

The historical figures cited are heavily contested by mainstream scholarship, and the framing is the poster's. The frame does not bear on the [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting; it is retained as a record of the ideological argument circulating in the same feeds where the case was discussed.
