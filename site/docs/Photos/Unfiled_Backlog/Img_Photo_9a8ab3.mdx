## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post from an account shown as **Rasmussen Reports**, handle @Rasmussen_Poll, posted two hours before capture, with an embedded table of 2020 Arizona results by county and a further partial post below carrying a linked article preview.

The post reads: "AZ Background 2020: Maricopa County had 1 legal election ballot paper type, 10 types were found by voter volunteers in the Senate audit. It also had 1 legal software version, 2 versions and a compiler were found by top forensic experts. Over 200,000 counted ballots were on..." The table lists every Arizona county with vote totals and percentages, including Maricopa at 1,040,774 to 995,665.

This is the fuller version of a claim held in shorter form elsewhere in this cluster, and it is election material rather than case material. The audit claims were disputed at the time by county officials and subsequent reviews, and are recorded here without adoption. See [Vote](/Vote/overview).
