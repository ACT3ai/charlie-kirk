## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a working spreadsheet organized into a grid, with four themed column headings and short sentence-length assertions filling the cells. The headings group the material by institution, region, alleged conduct and military connection.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual cells are not quoted here. Several of them assert serious criminal conduct involving named organizations and individuals; those assertions are unverified, no court has found any of them, and they are not reproduced.

What the frame documents is a method rather than a conclusion: a researcher laying out proposed links in a grid so that each one can be checked or discarded. A cell in a spreadsheet is a hypothesis, not evidence, and this one has not been substantiated. For the organizational material that is documented, see [Companies and Organizations](/Companies_Organizations/overview) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
