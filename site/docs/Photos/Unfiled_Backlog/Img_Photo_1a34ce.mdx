## What This Image Shows

A capture of a word processor document showing a chronological timeline for 2023, interleaving political and travel milestones involving several public figures with specific flight itineraries. Portions of the text are highlighted in light blue and yellow.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the entries are not quoted here. Per the visual description, the figures covered include **Charlie Kirk**, **Rob McCoy**, **James O'Keefe** and **Sean Feucht**.

This is another slice of the multi-year working chronology set held in this intake queue, and it is the one that most clearly shows the method: public figures' movements and aircraft itineraries laid on one timeline so that coincidences can be looked for. Coincidence is not connection, which is the point the flight-data rebuttal in this cluster presses. See [Timeline](/Timeline/overview), [Planes](/Planes/overview) and [People](/People/overview).
