## What This Image Shows

A capture of an online news broadcast in split-screen: a commentator speaking at frame right, rally footage at frame left, with headline banners across the top and bottom.

The on-screen text reads: "TPUSA Ditches Charlie Kirk's Stance On Iran!" and, in the lower banner: "WARHAWK REVIVAL - TPUSA SEEMS TO DITCH CHARLIE KIRK'S OPPOSITION TO WAR W/ IRAN."

This is a substantive point and it comes from an outlet outside this investigation's usual sources, which is worth noting. **Charlie Kirk's** documented positions on foreign intervention, and how the organization's line moved after **September 10, 2025**, sit at the centre of the motive argument. The banner is a broadcaster's characterization of an organization's positioning, not a finding. See [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), [Iran](/Iran/overview) and [Motive](/Motive/overview).
