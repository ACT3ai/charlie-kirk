## What This Image Shows

A split-screen video still. **Candace Owens** is at a studio desk with a microphone and laptop at frame left, caught mid-reaction; the right half carries a graphic of bulleted chronological events running from **September 10** to **September 22**, with some lines highlighted in yellow and blue.

The text extraction sidecar returned no usable lines for this file, so the individual entries are not quoted.

The twelve days after the shooting are where much of the dispute in this case sits: the detentions and releases, the shifting public account, the handling of the body and the paperwork, and the work done at the site. Any timeline covering that window is asserting an order of events that can be checked against the records this archive holds.
