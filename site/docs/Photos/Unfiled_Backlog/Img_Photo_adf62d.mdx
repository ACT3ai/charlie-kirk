## What This Image Shows

A clean screenshot of a single post from a verified account shown as Alan Watson, handle @DietHeartNews, posted 43 minutes before capture.

The post reads: "Out of 535 members of Congress, only 10 of them reject AIPAC money. 525 members (98.2%) of Congress are paid to support Israel's interests - no matter what they do!"

The figure is asserted without a source and the inference, that campaign contributions purchase positions, is the poster's characterization rather than a demonstrated fact. Lobbying and its influence on American foreign policy is a documented thread in this archive, and it is the backdrop against which the reported donor pressure on **Charlie Kirk** is read.
