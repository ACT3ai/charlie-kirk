## About This Cluster

Iran had nothing to do with killing **Charlie Kirk**. The Iran thread in this investigation runs the other way: Charlie publicly opposed a U.S. war with Iran, and researchers argue that opposition put him in the path of the war's most powerful advocates. The **June 2025** twelve-day war and **Operation Midnight Hammer** sit roughly three months before **September 10, 2025**, and Charlie was reportedly lobbying against regime change in that window. This cluster holds the visual material for that argument — a single reference graphic that lays the sequence out on one timeline.

The timeline image is the anchor for a motive claim rather than a physical-evidence claim, and it should be read that way. Nothing on it establishes that any government, official, or organization ordered violence against anyone; it puts publicly reported dates next to each other and lets the reader judge the proximity. The supporting threads elsewhere on the site cover reported Israeli pressure for the war, a reported final phone call, claims about rejected funding, and the shift in **TPUSA**'s posture on Iran after Charlie's death. All of those are reported claims from public commentary, presented with attribution and not adopted as conclusions here.

Open the **[Iran Timeline](/Photos/Iran/Img_Iran_Timeline_fb93ab)** graphic, then read the section it belongs to at [Iran War](/Iran/overview). The pages that carry the most weight are [Charlie's Stand Against the Iran War](/Iran/charlie-anti-iran-war), [Kirk's Iran Warnings: The Record](/Iran/kirk-warnings-quotes), [The 12-Day War and Midnight Hammer](/Iran/midnight-hammer-12-day-war), and [How the Iran War Raised His Risk](/Iran/iran-war-motive). For the counterarguments, see [The X Discourse and Counter-Narratives](/Iran/x-discourse-and-counter-narratives).
