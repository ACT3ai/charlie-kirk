## What This Image Shows

A digital timeline graphic headed "Charlie Kirk Assassination &amp; Iran Regime Change: — The Timeline That Raises Eyebrows... —". Eight colour-coded columns run left to right, each pairing an event involving [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) with a parallel development in the Iranian opposition, under a red banner reading "Pressure to Silence".

The columns read: **Early 2025 (Jan-Mar)** — "Kirk Pro-Israel TPUSA Tours" and "Iran Quiet, Pahlavi Low Profile", with an Israeli flag icon. **Spring 2025 (Apr-May)** — "Kirk Criticizes Iran Escalation" and "Pahlavi Renewal Plan", megaphone icon. **June 2025** — "12-Day Iran-Israel War" and "Pahlavi Calls for Uprising", fighter jets and explosion. **July 2025** — "Kirk Rejects Israel Funding" and "Donor Bullying Peaks", money-sack icon. **Aug 2025** — "Kirk's Letter to Netanyahu" and "Pahlavi: West Must Back My Plan", envelope icon. **Sep 2025** — "Kirk Assassinated Sep 10", "Pahlavi Regime Push", "USC Speech Sep 17", with a headstone reading "RIP CHARLIE KIRK 1983-2025". **Oct 2025** — "[Owens](/People/candace-owens) Leaks Donor Info" and "Iran Economic Crisis", crowd with pre-revolutionary flags. **Jan 2026** — "Massive Uprising", "Javid Shah Will Return", "Pahlavi Calls for Strike".

The graphic is an argument about proximity, and it labels itself as such: "the timeline that raises eyebrows" claims a pattern, not a proof. Iran had nothing to do with Charlie Kirk's death — the thread this site follows is the reverse, that his public opposition to a U.S. war with Iran placed him against that war's most powerful advocates and, researchers argue, raised the motive to remove him. Several entries here are contested reported claims rather than documented facts, in particular the rejected-funding and Netanyahu-letter items, and nothing on the graphic establishes that any government, official, or organization ordered or knew of any violence.
