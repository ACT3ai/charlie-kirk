## What This Image Shows

An editorial cartoon in the style of a roadside warning sign posted on a university campus. Under a red "CAUTION" header, a silhouette shows a caricatured figure running wildly; the text beneath the graphic reads "UNIVERSITY" and "RADICAL LEFT LUNATICS IN AREA." The signature and syndication marks read Stiglich and "2025 CREATORS.COM," with the artist's website "WWW.TOMSTIGLICH.COM."

This is commercial editorial cartooning, syndicated through Creators, rather than anonymous social-media material — which makes it a useful marker of how mainstream conservative commentary framed campus politics in 2025.

It is commentary, not evidence. The cartoon makes no claim about **September 10, 2025**, names nobody, and takes no position on who was responsible for anything. It belongs to the blame contest that opened within hours of the shooting, which the site treats separately and with sourcing.
