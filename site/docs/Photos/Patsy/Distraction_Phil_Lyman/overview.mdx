## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a social-media post about Phil Lyman, a Utah politician, shared by an account named "Project Constitution." The post is a layered quote-repost that ties Lyman to an online theory about the shooting, and the nested graphics reference "Alice in Wonderland" imagery that other posters have linked to conditioning claims.

Some online posts have questioned why Lyman was reportedly among the early voices commenting on where a shot came from. This cluster gathers images that circulated in that discussion, and it is presented as a **reported question**, not an accusation. It is an unproven online theory, and appearing in it does not indicate wrongdoing. Lyman is a living public figure who has not been charged in connection with [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death, and authorities named a different person as the suspect. His documented actions here amount to public commentary.

The claims written into this screenshot are the posters' claims, reproduced here with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions.
