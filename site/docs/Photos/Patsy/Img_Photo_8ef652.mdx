## What This Image Shows

This is a long, vertical screenshot of a Facebook post and its subsequent comment thread. The original post is by a user named Kristine Leclaire, setting out a theory about an arrest and describing the person detained as a "FALL GUY." The post includes self-replies with cropped photographs of law enforcement officers handling what appears to be a long gun, followed by various comments from other users discussing a potential shooting incident, campus rooftops, and comparisons to political figures like [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and Martin Luther King Jr.

"Fall guy" is the poster's characterisation and carries a double allegation: that the man detained was innocent and that somebody else arranged for him to be blamed. Neither claim is sourced in the thread, and neither is supported by any agency finding, filing or court record. The man detained has never been publicly identified and no charging outcome has been published; nothing on this page accuses him, or anyone else named or pictured in the thread, of any conduct. The screenshot is filed as an example of how the theory circulated in September 2025, not as a finding.
