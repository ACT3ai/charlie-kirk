## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of a social media post on X (formerly Twitter) by the user "Shadow of Ezra." The post contains three paragraphs of text claiming that [FBI](/FBI/overview) Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) was questioned about a conspiracy involving George Zinn and the "real killer of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)." Below the text, there is an embedded image consisting of a split-screen broadcast graphic: the left side displays a Fox News studio panel with three anchors/commentators, while the right side shows a split-screen video of an older man being detained by a police officer outdoors on a sunny day.

The account's characterisation of what was asked and answered is its own, and a screenshot of a broadcast panel does not establish the exchange. No transcript, filing or agency statement reproduced here supports the claim that any conspiracy involving Zinn was put to the FBI Director or confirmed by him. Zinn was cleared as the shooter the same day and has not been charged in connection with the killing; nothing on this page finds that he acted with anyone, and no conduct is alleged here against Patel or against any commentator visible in the broadcast frame.
