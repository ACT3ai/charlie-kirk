## About This Cluster

The images here are screenshots of social-media posts that circulated about George Zinn, an older man who was reportedly detained near the venue on the day [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot. According to these posts, Zinn drew attention by allegedly yelling "shoot me" during the chaos. The pictures show him being escorted by uniformed officers in a crowd, alongside commentary from accounts such as "Shadow of Ezra" and "National Conservative."

Some online researchers have questioned whether attention paid to figures like Zinn functioned as a **distraction** from the actual shooter. It is important to be clear that this is an unproven online theory, not an established fact. Being talked about in that discussion is not evidence of any wrongdoing. Reporting indicates the [FBI](/FBI/overview) stated that Zinn was not the shooter, and authorities charged a different person in connection with the assassination; any counts Zinn reportedly faced were described as unrelated. He has not been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death.

The claims written into these screenshots are the posters' claims, reproduced here with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions.
