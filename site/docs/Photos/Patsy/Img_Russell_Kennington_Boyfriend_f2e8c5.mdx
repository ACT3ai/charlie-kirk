## What This Image Shows

An outdoor selfie with meme-style white text and red hand-drawn arrows added on top. The man wears a white lab coat, dark round sunglasses, a black baseball cap, and a large pentagram pendant on a chain. Blue sky and light cloud fill the left of the frame; green park foliage and a lamppost fill the right. The arrows point at the coat and at the pendant.

This was circulated during online attempts to match the white lab coat seen in the [UVU](/UVU/overview) detention photographs to a coat worn elsewhere. Posts sharing it carried competing captions about who is pictured — some said the man detained at UVU, others said an associate of his. Neither identification has been confirmed by any reliable on-the-record source, and this site adopts neither.

It belongs in this cluster as an example of how the "patsy" threads get built: a garment in a news photograph, a social-media photograph with a similar garment, arrows drawn between them, and an inference. Wearing a lab coat, or any pendant, proves nothing.
