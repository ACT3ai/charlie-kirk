## About This Cluster

These are the lower-priority frames from the **pellet-gun arrest** in Provo, Utah on **September 10, 2025** — blurrier or more redundant captures kept for completeness rather than for new information. They show the same scene as the main set: Provo police officers detaining a man on a sidewalk near a black-and-white Provo Police SUV, a wooden rifle stock visible in one gloved officer's hands, and a bystander text overlay reading "I think they just found the guy right outside of carry-on."

Even low-quality frames have a use here: they confirm the sequence of the arrest, the number of officers present, and the wooden-stocked long gun that was recovered — the detail that let researchers later identify the weapon as a break-barrel air rifle rather than a firearm capable of the UVU shot. The "found the guy" caption also preserves how bystanders and early posts framed this stop, in the first minutes, as the capture of the shooter.

Start with the sharper versions of these scenes in the parent [Patsy Candidates](/Photos/Patsy/overview) cluster and in [More](/Photos/Patsy/More/overview), then come back here only if you are tracing frame-by-frame details. The maps of the arrest corner are under [Location](/Photos/Patsy/Location/overview), and the analysis is at [Pellet Gun Man](/distraction_people/Pellet_Gun_Man).
