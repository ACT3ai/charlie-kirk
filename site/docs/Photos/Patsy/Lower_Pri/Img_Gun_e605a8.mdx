## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical crop. A wooden rifle stock is visible in the foreground, held or slung vertically. An individual in a black uniform and tactical vest stands in the midground. A bright neon-green arrow has been drawn on the right side, pointing down toward the lower-right corner.

The green arrow matches the annotation style used on the frames in the parent cluster, so this crop comes from the same researcher's working set — the additional angles kept as supporting rather than headline material.

What it adds is another independent view of the wooden stock at the **Provo** scene. Multiple angles of the same feature from different bystander cameras is what makes the identification of a wood-stocked long gun solid, even while the identification of the specific model as a **.177 break-barrel air rifle** remains a visual match against a product listing rather than a published evidence record.
