This image is a horizontal, close-up digital screenshot of a structured spreadsheet table containing text in English. The table lists eight incidents under the heading "Distraction Shootings" and, in a further column, attributes them to an unnamed "intel service." The document is organized into columns detailing the names of the incidents, their locations, dates in late 2025, and a short summary of each event.

The attribution is the compiler's, written into a spreadsheet the compiler made. Grouping separate incidents under one heading is an assertion of a pattern, not a demonstration of one, and naming an intelligence service as the cause of any of them is an allegation that no agency finding, filing or court record supports. Nothing on this page adopts the grouping or the attribution, and no individual connected to any of the listed incidents is accused of anything here.
