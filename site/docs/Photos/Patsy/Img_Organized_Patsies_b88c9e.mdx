## What This Image Shows

This image is a digital graphic collage presenting a conspiracy theory about an "Organized Murder." It features several distinct photographic inserts of men labeled with numbers and titles like "Distraction Patsy," alongside bold red and blue text blocks on a white background. The image is structured to suggest a coordinated effort involving multiple individuals rather than a "Lone Wolf" action.

The labels are the graphic-maker's, and they are accusations. Assigning a man in a photograph the caption "Distraction Patsy" asserts that he played a role in a murder; nothing on the collage sources that claim, and no agency, filing or court record supports the roles it assigns. None of the men pictured other than the charged defendant has been accused by any authority of any involvement in the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and nothing on this page suggests any of them did anything improper or unlawful. The graphic is filed because it circulated and shows how the distraction theory is assembled, not because its assignments are established.
