## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of the [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) profile on X, account **[@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)**, showing 13.3K posts. The timeline below shows what appears to be the same post repeated several times within about twenty minutes, each with slightly different engagement counts.

The repeated text reads: "I am told that payments for assassinations are running through the Club des Cent in France. This means there is a paper trail and I encourage the patriots of France to do what you can to uncover it. I would again like to stress that there was a French female assassin but also" — the post is truncated by a "Show more" link in the interface.

That is an unverified claim, attributed by Owens to unnamed sources and reported here as her claim rather than as fact. No authority has confirmed payments of any kind through any Paris club, no French institution has been found responsible for anything in this case, and named individuals are not accused here. The repetition visible in the screenshot is itself part of why researchers saved it — whether the duplicates are a posting error or deliberate emphasis is unresolved. For the full thread with its pushback, see [French Foreign Legion Claims](/intelligence/French_Foreign_Legion_Claims) and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens).
