## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a social-media post, dated September 25, 2025, discussing an unidentified man that online researchers nicknamed the "pellet gun man." The post is a four-panel collage that pairs bystander or security footage of a detention near the venue with a product photo of a wooden-stock air rifle, and it questions who the man was and what he was carrying.

Some posts have speculated that a man briefly pulled aside with a pellet or air rifle may have served as a **distraction** on the day [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot. This is an unproven online theory offered as a question, not a statement of fact. The man in these images is not publicly identified, and nothing here establishes that he did anything wrong. Reporting indicates authorities named and charged a different person in connection with the shooting; the person shown here has not been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death.

The claims written into this screenshot are the poster's claims, reproduced here with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions.
