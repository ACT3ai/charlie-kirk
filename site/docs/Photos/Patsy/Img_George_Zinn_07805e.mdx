## What This Image Shows

A medium close-up in bright, harsh daylight. The elderly man in the blue button-down shirt and wire-rimmed glasses stands with his hands secured behind his back, surrounded by other people. Concrete steps and terraced landscaping fill the background.

This is the frame that shows the restraint most plainly. Taken with the composite of him being walked away and the wider plaza shots in this cluster, the set documents a continuous sequence: detained on the spot, restrained, escorted out.

The sequence matters because of how quickly it ended. He was cleared as the shooter the same day, per Utah Department of Public Safety Commissioner **Beau Mason**, while the actual suspect was still at large. The minutes and hours consumed by this detention are the measurable cost that makes "distraction" a substantive question in this case rather than a rhetorical one.
