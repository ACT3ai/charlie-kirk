## What This Image Shows

A desktop Google Maps screenshot, overhead 2D view, centred on the street grid of downtown **Provo, Utah**. Business names, street labels and parcel outlines are visible; a search sidebar sits on the left with recent searches and a light-traffic note.

The labelled block is **W Center St** between roughly **100 W** and **300 W**. Businesses named on the map include a Korean barbecue restaurant at **198 W Center St**, **Provo Fire Dept. Station #1**, **The Compass Gallery**, **Arlington Hall**, the **Cabrini Parking Lot**, a dance studio, a law firm, and — importantly for the next frame — a business named **Carry On**.

That last name is the reason this map is filed. A bystander video in the [Lower Pri](/Photos/Patsy/Lower_Pri/overview) sub-cluster carries the spoken line "I think they just found the guy right outside of carry-on," and this map is what turns that offhand remark into a fixed address. Geolocating from a business name spoken on camera is ordinary open-source method, and it is how the detention frames were pinned to this block. The addresses circulated with this thread include the area of **44 S 200 W** and a point on **Freedom Boulevard** just south of West Center Street.
