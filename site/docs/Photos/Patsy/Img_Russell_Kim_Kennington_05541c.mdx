## What This Image Shows

A two-panel news graphic. Both panels show the same man in a white lab coat with a red hood over green clothing, detained outdoors by escorts. Across the bottom sits a bold caption block with the **New York Post** logo.

The caption reads: "Prowler wearing lab coat arrested after sneaking into Charlie Kirk assassination crime scene to snap selfie, photos: report." That is a national outlet reporting an arrest inside the taped scene, and characterizing the purpose as photographing it. The wording is the paper's, framed as a report rather than as a finding.

This image is filed here because it is the second mainstream confirmation that something happened at the scene on **September 11, 2025**, alongside the FOX 13 broadcast still in this cluster — and because it sits directly against the **GRAMA** records response that reportedly said nobody was arrested.
