## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a reply on X from **Mr. Mcgraw**, handle **@proudparentusa**, dated **October 13, 2025**, addressed to **[@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)** and **[@JohnMcCloy](/Influencers/x/johnmccloy)**. The attached photograph is a close-up selfie of two smiling men in the foreground with a dense crowd behind them, a white promotional tent, and a modern building facade — the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) event as it looked before the shot.

The reply reads: "I know who he is I've reached out to you so many times this is my best friend. I'm in the red hat and gray shirt I have 11 minutes of 8K footage that I've been wanting to give you but no one's gotten back to me."

Two separate things are in that sentence. One is a claim to know the identity of a man researchers were trying to name — an unverified claim like the others in this thread. The other is a claim that an attendee holds **11 minutes of 8K video** from inside the crowd and has been unable to get it to anyone. That second claim is the reason this image is filed. High-resolution attendee footage is exactly the material that would settle several disputes in this case, and this cluster documents more than one report of such footage going nowhere.
