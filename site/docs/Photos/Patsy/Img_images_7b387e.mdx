## What This Image Shows

A television broadcast still, low resolution and captured off a screen. A man is walked between two escorts on an outdoor campus path, hands behind his back, wearing a white and red cape-like garment over green clothing.

The chyron is the value here. It reads **FOX 13**, **UVU ARREST**, and then: "Video shows man being arrested on UVU campus today after entering shooting scene and fleeing police." That is a mainstream local newsroom stating, on air, that an arrest occurred on the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus in connection with someone entering the shooting scene.

That single line is why this frame is filed here. A **GRAMA** public-records response reportedly told requesters that nobody was arrested. The broadcast chyron and the records response cannot both be complete, and reconciling them is one of the open questions in this area.
