## What This Image Shows

A composite screenshot of a post on X. The left half is the annotated escort photograph — the man in green scrubs and a white red-lined coat walked by two officers, with a red arrow pointing at him and a red circle around an escort's waist. The right half is the post text and the replies under it.

The post, from **@Mia_Stretch** on **October 7, 2025**, reads in part: "Meet Russell Kennington, 38 who was arrested for criminal trespass and FELONY Obstruction of Justice for tampering with Charlie Kirk's crime scene at UVU in the hours after his assassination. You know When he was BOOKED ON 9/11/25 at 12:33 PM." It then asks, of the circled object, "Is that a rifle the officer is carrying next to him." The booking time matches the booking record filed elsewhere in this cluster, which is the only part of the post that is independently checkable.

The replies are worth reading as a caution about the source material. One is a hostile rant dismissing the whole subject; another simply asks "Is this the guy?" Numerology claims about the booking time appear in the post itself. This site carries the checkable facts — the **12:33 PM** booking, the charges as described in circulated records — and does not adopt the poster's inferences.
