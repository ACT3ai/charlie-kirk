## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a social-media post, shared by an account named "Project Constitution," about Russell Kim Kennington. According to the post, Kennington was reportedly arrested near the venue in the hours after the shooting, and the screenshot includes an official-looking message about a GRAMA public-records request seeking more information from Utah Valley University police.

Some online posters have raised questions about the circumstances of that reported arrest and have speculated about a **distraction** angle. This is an unproven online theory presented as a question, not an accusation. Being discussed here is not evidence that Kennington did anything wrong. Reporting indicates that any charges tied to him were later dropped, and authorities named and charged a different person in connection with the assassination. Kennington is a living person who has not been convicted of anything related to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death.

The claims written into this screenshot are the poster's claims, reproduced here with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions.
