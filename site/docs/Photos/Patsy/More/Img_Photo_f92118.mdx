## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution daytime frame on a city sidewalk. A man in a white shirt and dark trousers stands with his hands behind his back while an officer secures him. Two other officers stand close by. Dark police vehicles are parked along the curb and the black lamppost stands in the foreground.

This is the moment of the cuffing itself, earlier in the sequence than the frames showing him placed in the car. The white shirt matches across the whole set, which is how the angles are stitched together into one timeline.

It closes the sub-cluster where it should close: with a man, unidentified to this day, detained in daylight on a public street in **Provo** on the afternoon of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), while the shooter at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) was still at large. No name, no charge in connection with the shooting, and no published disposition have followed. That absence is the open question, and it is a question for the agencies rather than about him.
