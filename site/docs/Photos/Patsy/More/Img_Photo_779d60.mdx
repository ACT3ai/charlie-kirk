## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical frame. An officer in blue nitrile gloves crouches on a paved sidewalk holding a bright green bag beside scattered debris. Another officer stands in the immediate foreground on the left, partly blocking the view. A black classical-style lamppost frames the right edge.

The same lamppost appears in several frames across this set and in the parent cluster, which is how the separate bystander videos are confirmed to be the same location. The gloved crouch and the green bag repeat the evidence-collection sequence.

What is scattered on the pavement cannot be resolved at this resolution. That is worth stating plainly rather than guessing at: the frames establish that officers collected something on a **Provo** sidewalk on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and nothing more. The identification of the recovered item as an air rifle comes from other frames and from a product-listing match, not from this one. See [Pellet Gun Man](/distraction_people/Pellet_Gun_Man).
