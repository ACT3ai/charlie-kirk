## What This Image Shows

An outdoor frame on a brick and concrete sidewalk. Several officers are gathered around an active scene response. One kneels on the pavement in blue medical gloves, holding a bright green bag. The others stand around him, partly blocking the background.

The green bag is the detail that recurs across this sub-cluster. Its repeated appearance in the hands of a gloved, kneeling officer is consistent with exhibit collection or packaging at the point of the stop.

That is what makes these frames worth keeping. Something was recovered on this sidewalk in **Provo** within about an hour of the shooting, handled in gloves and bagged in the open, and the item circulated afterward as a **.177 break-barrel air rifle**. No public record of what was booked, or under what case number, has been produced. Nothing here identifies anyone or establishes what the bag contained.
