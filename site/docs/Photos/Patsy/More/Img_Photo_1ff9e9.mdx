## What This Image Shows

A close, slightly low-angle vertical frame. The lower body and duty gear of an officer walking to the right fill the foreground. Behind him, other officers in tactical and utility uniforms stand on the paved surface, and one of them holds a long gun with a wooden stock.

The wooden stock in the background is the reason this frame was kept. It is the same item feature that the annotated close-ups in the parent cluster mark with green arrows, seen here without annotation and from a different position.

Having an unannotated frame matters. Arrows and circles direct a reader's eye and can manufacture certainty that the pixels do not support; a plain frame lets a reader judge the shape without help. What is visible is a wood-stocked long gun in an officer's hands at the scene of the **Provo** detention. What it was is the argument, and the identification as an air rifle is a visual match by researchers rather than a published property log.
