## What This Image Shows

A researcher-made graphic: a stylized overhead site diagram of the venue rendered in high-contrast white, light grey, dark grey and black, resembling a property site plan. Coloured marker dots are placed across it with short labels. A black legend box in the upper left reads **3 Patsies**, **2 Shooters**, **2 Distractions**. A tiny circular photo of a face sits near the centre of the diagram. The graphic is signed **@WeAreNotGoingToMars**.

This is one poster's theory drawn as a map. The legend asserts a specific count of roles and places them at specific positions on the ground. None of it is sourced on the graphic itself, and no agency, filing, or court record supports the counts.

It is filed in this cluster because it is a clean summary of the shape of the distraction theory — the argument that more than one person was positioned at the venue and that some drew attention while others acted. That argument is why this cluster exists. The graphic is the poster's conclusion, not this site's, and it is not evidence that any individual did anything.
