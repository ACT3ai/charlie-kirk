## What This Image Shows

A close-up selfie taken under a covered outdoor walkway with a vine-covered stone wall and wooden beams overhead. The man has a brown beard, blue eyes, and partly blue-dyed hair, and is smiling at the camera. He wears a light blue dress shirt, a patterned tie, a green scarf, and a large circular geometric pendant.

Nothing in the photograph relates to [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) or to **September 2025**. It circulated because posts about the lab-coat detention needed a clear face reference, and this was the image people attached. It is filed in this cluster for that reason and no other.

Treat it as an identification aid whose identification is itself unconfirmed. The booking screenshot elsewhere in this cluster lists physical descriptors — height 5 foot 11, weight 204, blue eyes, brown hair, year of birth 1987 — that readers can compare against it, which is exactly the kind of check that has to be done before any of this is taken as established.
