## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a social-media post about [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak), reported to be the audience member who asked [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) the final question moments before the shooting. The post, from an account named Cynthia, raises concerns about event security and speculates about the framing of that last question and about Kozak's on-camera reaction.

Some online posters have asked whether Kozak's presence and timing were significant. This is presented strictly as a **reported question**, not an accusation. Appearing in this online discussion does not show that Kozak did anything wrong or had any advance knowledge. According to reporting, Kozak was questioned by the FBI and cleared of involvement, and law enforcement and major outlets have treated his reaction as a genuine startle response to a sudden gunshot — an involuntary reaction that is common and expected. He has not been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death, and authorities named a different person as the suspect.

The claims written into this screenshot are the poster's claims, reproduced here with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions.
