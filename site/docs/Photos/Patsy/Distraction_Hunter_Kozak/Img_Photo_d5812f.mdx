## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of a post on the social media platform X (formerly Twitter) by a user named Cynthia. The post expresses concern about security at a "UVU" event, alleging that an individual named [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak) and his affiliates should not have been allowed entry. It includes a text commentary setting out the poster's theory about a planned question involving trans issues and gun violence, and provides a YouTube link.

Those are the poster's allegations and this page does not adopt them. Kozak is reported to have been the audience member who asked [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) the final question, and asking a question at a public Q&A is not evidence of anything. According to reporting he was questioned by the FBI and cleared of involvement, and his on-camera reaction has been treated by law enforcement and major outlets as an ordinary startle response to a gunshot. No investigator, agency or court has connected him to the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025); he has not been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death, and authorities named and charged a different person. Nothing on this page suggests he did anything improper or unlawful. See [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak).
