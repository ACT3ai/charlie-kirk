## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of the official Utah governor's website, **governor.utah.gov**, showing a published daily schedule. The entries are almost all budget hearings in the Rampton Boardroom at the Utah State Capitol: the Governor's Office of Economic Opportunity at 10:30 a.m., Utah Trust Lands Administration at 11:00 a.m., the State Treasurer, Workforce Services, the Utah National Guard, the State Auditor. One entry is highlighted in yellow: **11:30 a.m. Meeting with the Ambassador of Egypt**, in the Gold Room. The date is not visible in this crop.

A researcher highlighted that line because of a separate thread in this investigation about Egyptian-registered aircraft moving through Utah around **September 2025**. A state governor meeting a foreign ambassador is an ordinary diplomatic courtesy, published openly on a public calendar precisely because there is nothing hidden about it.

That is the honest reading, and it is worth stating alongside the reason the screenshot exists. Nothing here suggests that any official knew anything or did anything improper, and the highlight is a researcher's mark, not an official one.
