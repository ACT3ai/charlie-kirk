## About This Cluster

This cluster is the visual record of the exploding-mic question — whether the fatal wound on **September 10, 2025** at Utah Valley University came from a rooftop rifle at all, or from the **RØDE Wireless PRO** transmitter **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** wore under his shirt. The thread matters because it attacks the physical premise of the official case head-on: if researchers are right that a stage-origin detonation caused the neck wound, the rooftop .30-06 attributed to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) no longer explains the death. The images here — hardware close-ups, SUV debris photos, shaped-charge timelines, and broadcast frames — are the exhibits that argument is built from.

Several independent lines feed the theory, and each is represented in this set. **[Jon Bray's](/People/jon-bray)** frame-by-frame video work argues the shirt fabric expanded outward before blood appeared; a **Canon XA55** professional recording reportedly separates a muzzle blast about 120 meters out from a louder low-frequency event at the tent itself; and photos attributed to **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** show stains, fragments, and what posters describe as shattered tempered glass on the floor of the GMC Yukon Denali that carried Kirk from the scene. Other images model the physics — one graphic charts the theoretical effects of a 2g RDX shaped charge with a 2cm standoff, another lays out a microsecond-scale detonation timeline — while a defense-styled CLAHE analysis measures the RØDE Wireless PRO housing against pre-event photos. All of these are researchers' claims presented with attribution; the official finding remains a single rifle shot.

Start with the SUV interior photos and the RØDE hardware close-ups on this page, then open the sub-clusters: [Burnt Object in the SUV](/Photos/Mic/Mic_Burnt_Object_Transmitter/overview), [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Photos/Mic/Mic_SUV_Transporting/overview), [Accurate Energetic Systems](/Photos/Mic/Accurate_Energetic_Systems/overview), and [The Exploding Pendant](/Photos/Mic/Mic_Exploding_Pendant/overview). For the full written case, see [Microphone — The Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview), the [RØDE Wireless PRO hardware page](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic), and the [Canon XA55 audio analysis](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio).
