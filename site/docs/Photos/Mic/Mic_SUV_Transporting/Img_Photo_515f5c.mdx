## What This Image Shows

A close-up of the floor sill of the SUV. A pale fragment rests on the sill, with a small black object carrying a visible blue label nearby.

This is the single most-cited frame in the set. Some commentators describe the pale fragment as possible bone or tissue, and some describe the small black object with the blue label as resembling a microphone-style windscreen or a transmitter component. Both readings are what make this image the load-bearing exhibit for the argument that device wreckage ended up in the cabin. A wider-context version of the same sill appears elsewhere in this cluster.

Neither identification has been confirmed. Nothing recovered from this vehicle has been publicly identified by any examiner, and at this resolution a fragment, a food remnant, and a plastic component are difficult to separate.
