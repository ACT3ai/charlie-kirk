## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster covers the hospital-transport leg of the microphone thread. **Charlie Kirk** was driven from UVU by private SUV — not ambulance — and taken to **Timpanogos Regional Hospital**, a Level IV trauma center 5.8 miles away, rather than **Utah Valley Hospital**, the closer (4.2 miles) and more capable Level II center that Utah County protocol designates for life-threatening gunshot wounds. Posts by @MJTruthUltra and others have asked why the transport deviated from protocol, and a separate claim reports a bomb scare at the closer hospital at the same time. What happened inside the vehicle during that drive is one of the investigation's open questions.

The single image here is a screenshot of an X post whose author speculates, using a cutaway SUV seating diagram with colored silhouettes, about where each occupant sat and what may have occurred inside the vehicle. The post's narrative is the author's speculation alone — it is preserved as an example of the questions circulating about the transport, not as evidence that any of it happened. What is documented separately is the vehicle's interior condition afterward: the stains, fragments, and debris photographed in the [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Photos/Mic/Mic_SUV_Transporting/overview) set.
