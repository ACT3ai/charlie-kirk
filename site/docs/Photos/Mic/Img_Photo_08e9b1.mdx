## What This Image Shows

A close, high-angle photograph of the lower interior threshold of the vehicle. Black leather seat upholstery with a double-stitched seam sits at the top of the frame; below it are two manual seat adjustment levers on the matte black seat base molding, then the mounting bracket and a bright exposed metal seat track. The floor carpet shows light dust and debris. Set into the textured black door sill is a rectangular brushed-metal scuff plate embossed DENALI. The image carries a repeating CANDACE watermark across it.

The Denali plate is what fixes the vehicle model in this photograph set. Identifying the transport vehicle as a GMC Yukon Denali matters because it lets researchers cross-reference the interior frames against the exterior shots of the same vehicle, including the auction-lot photographs that appear elsewhere in this cluster, and against claims about what later happened to the vehicle.

The CANDACE watermark identifies the broadcast these frames were pulled from rather than the photographer. For the full set with sourcing and the competing readings of the debris in these frames, see [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).
