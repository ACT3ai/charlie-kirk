## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution screen grab with heavy pixelation and soft focus. In the foreground, seen from behind, a person wears a red structured baseball cap backwards with the snapback strap visible above the neck, over a white t-shirt with a small dark logo below the collar, with red straps across both shoulders. A second person in a grey and white mesh-back trucker cap is partly visible at left, and a third in sunglasses at the far edge. Between them a raised hand holds a small dark object. The background is obscured, showing a large dark blue or grey structure with vertical slats and a thin vertical pole.

Crowd frames like this are collected because the position of individual attendees relative to the stage is used in reconstructions, and because the red-cap figure recurs across multiple frames from this angle. The image quality here is a genuine limit: at this pixelation, clothing color and body position are readable but faces, objects in hands, and background structures are not.

Presented without identification of anyone shown. For the reconstruction work these frames feed, see [Timeline](/Timeline/overview), [Maps](/maps/overview), and [The Tent](/Tent/overview). For the site-wide caution about reading detail out of low-resolution sources, see [Burnt Object in the SUV — RODE Transmitter Claim](/Mic/burnt-object-rode-transmitter).
