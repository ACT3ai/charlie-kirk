## About This Cluster

**Accurate Energetic Systems (AES)** is the Tennessee munitions maker at 5891 TN-230, McEwen, whose factory building exploded on **October 10, 2025** — one month after the assassination — killing 16 people in what has been described as the deadliest U.S. munitions blast in over a century. Researchers connect it to the microphone thread through a procurement record: on **May 1, 2025** the Department of Defense awarded AES a $440,494 purchase order (PIID N0016425PJ538) for "MINIATURIZED-XS DEMOLITION CHARGES, ANTI PERSONNEL-XS," with delivery due **August 25, 2025** — reportedly the only "XS" device contract on USAspending.gov since records began, and awarded to the exact building later leveled.

The four images carry the documentary spine of that claim. A USAspending.gov screenshot shows the contract summary with the award amount and AES's McEwen address highlighted; a labeled satellite view locates the intact facility in its wooded Tennessee hills; an aerial photo shows the aftermath, a smoke column rising over pulverized debris; and a before-and-after composite puts the two side by side. A former DoD procurement worker posting as [@mistressdivy](/Influencers/x/mistressdivy) flagged the contract's anomalies — a short procurement window, simplified acquisition, single bidder, and no parent IDV — while adding her own caveat that "correlation doesn't mean causality." The timing and the contract are verifiable public records; any link to the Kirk case is an open question, not a finding.

Read the full written treatment at [AES — The Company](/Mic/AES), [AES Factory Explosion](/Mic/aes-explosion-factory), and [DoD Anti-Personnel Contract](/Mic/DoD_Contract), then the [Heber/Nashville flight question](/Mic/aes-heber-nashville-flight) that ties the delivery date to Utah. The pocket-object photos that flight claim leans on are in [Pocket](/Photos/Mic/Pocket/overview).
