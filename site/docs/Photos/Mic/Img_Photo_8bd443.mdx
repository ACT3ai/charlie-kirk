## What This Image Shows

A high-angle photograph looking down into the vehicle footwell. A large crumpled brown paper bag with a faint white grid print fills the upper left, creased, folded in on itself and torn in several places, with dark reddish-brown staining smeared across its upper-left and right sections and thick dark crusting on the top-right fold. A cluster of ten to twelve small yellow twisted-wrapper candies sits in the center of the dark carpet, with a single matching candy separated near a black plastic trim piece. A grey upholstered seat edge and its mounting bracket are visible at the upper right.

This frame belongs to the same vehicle-interior set as the other footwell images in this cluster and shows the same two recurring objects: the stained bag and the scattered candies. Their positions differ slightly between frames, which researchers use to argue about the order in which the photographs were taken and how much the cabin was disturbed between them.

The staining is described by commentators as blood; it is unidentified in any published record, and the site does not adopt that characterization. Sourcing and the competing readings are documented on [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie). For the medical timeline the vehicle sits inside, see [Charlie Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital).
