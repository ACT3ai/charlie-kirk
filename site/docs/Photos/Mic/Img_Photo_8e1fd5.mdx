## What This Image Shows

A low-light, heavily pixelated video freeze-frame of several people in a physical struggle. A central figure is being held or pulled, with the waist and lower stomach exposed under a harsh patch of white light and a dark belt partly visible. Above the exposed midriff is a light-colored shirt carrying a large dark reddish-purple stain. An arm stretches across the upper frame, gripped by another person dark-clothed arm; a figure in a black t-shirt is partly visible at right, and more shapes are lost in shadow at left. Two orange arrows have been drawn in, both pointing toward the exposed midsection. A small watermark reads alleytopfiles.

The arrows are the poster annotation. This frame belongs to the same annotated set as the circled shorts and inset-zoom images in the After cluster, and the annotations across that set consistently direct attention to clothing and to the torso rather than to faces. What is being argued is location: whether marks and staining appear where a projectile path would put them.

The image quality does not support fine conclusions. At this level of pixelation and motion blur, a stain, a shadow, and a graphic print are difficult to distinguish, which the source description itself flags.
