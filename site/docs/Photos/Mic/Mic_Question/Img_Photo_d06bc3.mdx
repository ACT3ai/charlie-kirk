## What This Image Shows

A dark-mode screenshot of a reply on X by the account Andrea Elliott, handle @DreaLeePower, replying to @RealCandaceO. The post text runs to three paragraphs and comments on a debate participant, stating he is part of a comedy tour associated with the streamer Steven Bonnell. Below the text is an embedded card showing a tour website profile page with a circular headshot of a man resting his chin on his hand, his name Hunter Kozak in bold red, and a short biography beneath it.

The person named here is the individual who was asking the question at the microphone at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025 when Charlie Kirk was hit. His presence and his affiliations have been examined by researchers since, and this capture is one of the affiliation claims that circulated.

The post asserts an association with a tour website. It does not allege any wrongdoing, and neither does this page: being on stage and asking a question at a public event, and being listed on a comedy tour site, are not allegations of anything. No conduct is attributed to any person here.
