## What This Image Shows

A digital collage on a flat slate blue-grey background, headed with a speculative operation codename. At the center is a translucent light-grey render of a human head and shoulders, the cerebral hemispheres shown as a glowing white web, with bilateral curved orange-red structures inside the cranial cavity connected by a bright yellow line running toward the nasal cavity and ear canals. On the viewer left, a cutaway of the ear canal shows a purple-and-blue marbled earbud shell inserted, with a highlighted yellow pathway leading into a gold-rendered inner ear and cochlea. Running diagonally down and right is an exploded component sequence linked by red indicator lines: a metallic outer casing, a small silver cylinder resembling a battery or canister, a second cylindrical component with gold contacts, a small green and gold printed circuit board, and a light-grey silicone dome tip. One of the mock-up in-ear monitors shown around the render carries a printed ribbon in the colors of the Israeli flag.

The collage is an assertion made entirely in graphics: it names an operation, illustrates a mechanism, and attributes it by flag, without presenting any evidence for any of the three. That is worth saying plainly rather than paraphrasing the imagery as though it were analysis.

No such device has been recovered, no operation of that name has been documented, and no state or agency has been shown to have any role in the events of September 10, 2025. The graphic is preserved because it circulated and because this cluster documents what was published, not because any part of it is established. The state-actor allegations that circulate around this case, and the evidentiary status of each, are handled on [Israel](/Israel/overview) and [Intelligence](/intelligence/overview).
