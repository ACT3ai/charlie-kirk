## What This Image Shows

Two Facebook panels side by side. On the left, two posts from an account under the name **Skyler Baird**: one dated Sep 24 quoting 1 Nephi 14:7 from the Book of Mormon in full, and one dated Jul 10 — a plain black graphic reading "Epstein Files — Is there any form of protest that makes it so our government isn't beholden to Mossad anymore?"

On the right, a comment thread of mostly warm replies, with one comment boxed in heavy red and marked by a large red arrow. It reads: **"Sadly I was there when Charlie was shot. I have to wonder how high up the idea came to install the pavers."**

That comment is a first-person statement of presence, published by the account holder himself, which is a different and stronger category of evidence than the inference-based material elsewhere in this cluster. The reference to pavers ties into the separate question about hardscaping installed in the plaza before the event. Holding and posting political opinions is lawful, and nothing in these posts indicates knowledge of or involvement in anything.
