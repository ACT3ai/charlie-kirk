## What This Image Shows

A tilted phone photograph of a screen showing a split-screen news broadcast, replayed inside a video player — the black **"Watch again"** pill overlays the middle banner. Above, a studio anchor in a tan jacket sits against a blue set with a "BREAKING NEWS" graphic; the divider banner names him as **JOSH BRESLOW**, handle [@JOSHBRESLO](/Influencers/x/joshbreslo). Below, a fair-haired man in a plain white t-shirt speaks from an outdoor patio.

The caption across the bottom is the point of the whole image: **SKYLER BAIRD / WITNESS.** This is the frame that establishes, from a broadcaster's own graphic, that the man in the lower pane was presented to viewers as an eyewitness to the [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview).

Every other image in this cluster hangs off that fact. The interviews are real, they are on the record, and they are the starting point rather than the conclusion. What commentators built on top of them — questions about his position in the crowd and his later attendance at the memorial — are questions posed by posters, not findings. He has not been charged with anything and no court has found he did anything wrong. See [Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird), [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and [Media](/Media/overview).
