## What This Image Shows

The PBS player paused on a split-screen moment — two white-bordered video frames side by side inside a blue bokeh background, host on one side and guest on the other. The PBS navigation bar, the Cascade PBS station notice and the search icon run along the top of the page.

The metadata below the player is unchanged from the other captures: *Think Tank with Steve Adubato*, February 22, 2025, 25 minutes 8 seconds, and the description of a conversation about the aftermath of the Sandy Hook shooting and the abuse the family endured.

Five near-identical captures of one page tell you what the researcher was doing: stepping through a public-television interview frame by frame, looking for something. What they were looking for was support for a resemblance claim that the interview itself contradicts.
