## What This Image Shows

A vertical TikTok screenshot built around a recorded CNN Situation Room segment. The white text across the top reads **"The Sandy Hook father accused of being a 'crisis actor'."** Inside the video, a man in a dark suit and red tie speaks in front of a brick wall; the corner box reads "Newtown, Connecticut, 5:22 PM" and the ticker reads "FAMILY OF 6-YEAR-OLD VICTIM SPEAKS."

Overlaid captions add "This man lost his daughter one day befor this interview" and "So my name is Robbie Parker." The posting account is credited as **False Flag Files**, dated 2024-8-21, tagged "#fyp #conspiracy #warmode #conspiracytiktok."
