## What This Image Shows

A top-down photograph of a bright blue semi-translucent plastic storage clipboard resting on a dark surface, with a grey flecked carpet visible at the left edge and a yellow pencil nearby. Clipped under the silver spring clip is a sheet of white printer paper carrying a hand-drawn genealogy in black ballpoint.

The chart repeats three surnames — **Bednarz**, **Pennell** and **Sipes** — alongside dates running from **1958 to 1997**, connected by hand-drawn boxes, brackets and arrows labelled **"siblings," "married," "Parents," "daughter," "Brother," "uncle"** and **"nephew."** Several entries have been scribbled out and corrected.

Those corrections are the most useful thing on the page. They show a researcher revising guesses in real time rather than presenting a settled answer, which is the honest state of this whole family thread. **Nothing on the chart has been verified against public records by this site, and appearing on it is not an accusation of anything.** The photographs it was assembled from are in [Dustin Bednarz](/Photos/People/Dustin_Bednarz/overview), [Blake Bednarz](/Photos/People/Blake_Bednarz/overview) and [Shawn Sipes](/Photos/People/Shawn_Sipes/overview).
