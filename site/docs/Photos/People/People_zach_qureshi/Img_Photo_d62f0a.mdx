## What This Image Shows

The image published alongside a **July 18, 2026** post by **[@MuppetMasher](/Influencers/x/muppetmasher)** on X naming the Qureshi brothers. The post text asserts, in the poster's own words: **"Zach and Zane Qureshi will become relevant again very soon. They were always relevant but it's coming around again. National guard just won't go away."**

No specifics accompany that claim. The assertion of future relevance and the reference to a lingering National Guard thread are the poster's commentary, not established fact, and this site reports them as such.

The context that matters most is the plainest fact in the file: **Zach Qureshi was briefly named in public reporting on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), detained, and released roughly ninety minutes later without charge. He was not the shooter.** He is a living person who was publicly misidentified, and no wrongdoing by him or by his brother is alleged here. The investigation's genuine open question concerns the effect that public naming and retraction had on the search that was still under way. See [Zach Qureshi](/People/zach-qureshi), [Zane Qureshi](/People/zane-qureshi) and [Suspects](/Suspects/overview).
