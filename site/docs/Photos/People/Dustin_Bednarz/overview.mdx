## About This Cluster

This cluster grew out of a single online claim: that a man named [Blake Bednarz](/People/blake-bednarz) was at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). Researchers responded the way they usually do, by pulling public social-media photographs of the extended Bednarz family to work out who is who. Dustin Bednarz appears in that material as a family member, not as a person accused of anything. Nothing in this set places him at the event, and no allegation of wrongdoing attaches to him or to anyone else pictured.

The six images are ordinary family and social photographs harvested from public Facebook posts: a heavily pixelated close crop of a face behind glass, a couple posing in a dimly lit restaurant, a selfie in a vehicle, a group of seven adults and children at what looks like a Fourth of July gathering, two young men in front of a pickup truck, and a wide shot of a black MCI charter bus loading outside a modern church-style building. That last frame is the one with investigative weight — its Facebook caption reads "Jennifer Sipes Bednarz at Turning Point Community" and is dated July 5, 2025, which is the sort of date-and-place anchor identification work depends on. The rest are useful mainly for face-matching against crowd footage.

Read this cluster as raw identification material. The family-relationship claims that motivated it are laid out in the investigation file and remain unconfirmed, and the pixelation on several frames means any visual match should be treated as tentative at best. Continue to [Blake Bednarz](/Photos/People/Blake_Bednarz/overview) and the [Sipes Family](/Photos/People/Sipes_Family/overview) chart for the rest of the family thread, and see [Blake Bednarz](/People/blake-bednarz) for the written profile.
