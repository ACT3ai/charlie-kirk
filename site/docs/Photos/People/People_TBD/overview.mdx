## About This Cluster

TBD is exactly what it sounds like — the parking bay for a person-identification graphic whose subject the investigation has not settled on a filing name for. Keeping it visible rather than burying it matters, because the citizen-research method that produced it depends on other readers spotting a face they recognise. The page will be renamed and re-filed the moment the identification firms up.

The single image is a composite graphic assembled on a black background. It is headed "Jeremy Gallman" with the subtitle "TPUSA camera operator," and it layers a tilted Instagram-style photograph of four people standing in front of a Turning Point Action backdrop in Phoenix, Arizona, with a green box drawn around one man in a black polo and lanyard, a large golden arrow, red pointer arrows, and a red-bordered frame containing a bright outdoor shot of a camera operator working an event. The argument the graphic makes is a visual one: that the man in the posed group photograph and the man behind the camera at the event are the same person.

Being a camera operator at an event is a job, not an accusation, and this site makes no claim that anyone pictured had knowledge of or involvement in what happened on September 10, 2025. The same graphic type recurs across the [Jeremy Gallman](/Photos/People/Jeremy_Gallman/overview) cluster, which holds the version published on the site, and the profile itself is at [Jeremy Gallman](/People/jeremy-gallman). For the organisational context see [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
