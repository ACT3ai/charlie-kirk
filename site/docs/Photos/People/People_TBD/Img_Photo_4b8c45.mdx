## What This Image Shows

A composite graphic on a black background. Across the top, large white text reads **"Jeremy Gallman"**, with **"TPUSA camera operator"** beneath it. The layers below include a tilted Instagram-style photograph of four people in front of a blue **Turning Point Action** backdrop, captioned "jeremyfilm" and "Phoenix, Arizona"; a **bright green rectangle** drawn around a smiling bearded man in a black polo and lanyard at the left of that group; a large golden arrow; and a red-bordered panel holding a high-contrast outdoor frame of a camera operator working an event.

The graphic's argument is that the boxed man in the posed group photograph and the operator behind the camera at the event are the same person.

Operating a camera at a political event is a job. Nothing in this graphic indicates that anyone pictured had knowledge of, or involvement in, what happened on September 10, 2025, and no such claim is made here. The identification itself rests on visual resemblance and has not been independently verified.
