## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an **anonymous email** sent to the [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) tips line, with the sender's details redacted. The message describes the recording behaviour of an **Axon Body Cam 4** — how the device buffers, records and stops.

It was shared by **[@tsorama](/Influencers/x/tsorama)** on X on July 17, 2026, quoting **[@TheCKFiles1](/Influencers/x/theckfiles1)**. The author is unknown and nothing in the email has been independently verified.

The reason it is preserved is that its central claim is testable: statements about how a specific camera model behaves can be checked against the manufacturer's own documentation and against the reported recording length in this case, a single file of roughly twenty-seven and a half minutes ending on the roof. **Chris Bagley is a witness, not a suspect. He has not been charged with anything, is presumed innocent, and body-worn cameras genuinely do fail** — nothing here asserts that he destroyed, tampered with or withheld material. See [Chris Bagley](/People/chris-bagley), [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) and [Court](/court/overview).
