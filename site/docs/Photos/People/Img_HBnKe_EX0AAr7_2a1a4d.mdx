## What This Image Shows

A dark red and black banner graphic with a pulp-thriller design: a featureless black silhouette at the centre, faint temple spires in the background, scattered red-tinted document textures, and red arrows fanning out to portraits on either side. The heading reads **UTAH MORMON MAFIA EXPOSED**, with the subtitle **HENDERSON • QURESHI TIES**.

The names printed beside the portraits are, as rendered on the graphic, **NATHAN HENDERSON, AMHED QURESHI, GINA ROMNEY HENDERSON, ZACHARAH QURESHE, DEIDRE HENDERSON** and **TOM QURESHI** — several of them misspelled. A small circular logo in the corner reads "PROJECT CON..." and a faint silhouette of two military aircraft sits opposite it.

This is an assertion of a network, drawn as a diagram. It corresponds to a thread in the investigation file about family and business links between the Qureshi and Henderson families, a Department of Defense contract awarded in June 2025, and a hospital connection — all of which are reported claims compiled by commentators, not findings. Being related to someone, or holding a government contract, is not evidence of wrongdoing, and none of the living people named here has been charged with anything in connection with this case.
