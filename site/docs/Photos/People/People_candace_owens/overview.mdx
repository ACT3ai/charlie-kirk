## About This Cluster

[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) is a conservative political commentator who has covered the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case extensively on her own program. In the investigation's public record she appears as a source and reporter of others' claims — for example, releasing photographs said to show the interior of Charlie Kirk's vehicle and identifying, in her broadcasts, an individual connected to a reported funding offer that Kirk was said to have declined. These are matters she has discussed publicly; they are presented here as her reporting, with attribution.

The single image on this page is a mobile screenshot of the X app showing a post by the account "[@Kahlissee](/Influencers/x/kahlissee)" that features a video of Owens. The capture includes the app's status bar, navigation tabs, and timeline, so what it primarily documents is that a clip of Owens was shared and discussed on the platform.

The claims that appear in or around the post are the poster's, reproduced with attribution rather than adopted as conclusions. Nothing here alleges wrongdoing by Owens.
