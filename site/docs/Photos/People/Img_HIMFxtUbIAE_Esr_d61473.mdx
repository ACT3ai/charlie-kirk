## What This Image Shows

A four-quadrant citizen-research collage built from photographs, inset crops, red arrows and bold white captions. It names [**Butch Hibbs**](/People/butch-hibbs), described on the graphic as the brother of **Pastor [Jack Hibbs**](/People/jack-hibbs), and **Teddy Ritter**, described as a nephew-in-law, and draws lines from them to Pastor Jack Hibbs and to the conservative media organisation **PragerU**. The photographs include three men standing outdoors, one in a t-shirt reading FREEDOM and another with a long white beard in a plaid shirt and cap, plus several smaller crops of the same bearded man highlighted in green.

The structure is a family-and-affiliation diagram assembled from public social-media photographs. It belongs to the same identification effort as the "Contracted May 2025" graphic filed alongside it in this cluster, and to the broader attempt to work out who among the people photographed near the venue is related to whom.

Family relationships and media affiliations are not evidence of anything. The graphic asserts connections; it does not demonstrate that any of the named people were at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) or that any of them did anything wrong, and no such claim is made here. See [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs), [Jack Hibbs](/People/jack-hibbs), and the [related graphic](/Photos/People/Img_TQb_cc135c).
