## What This Image Shows

The same PBS episode page, captured with the player showing promotional key art instead of video: the cover of the book **A Father's Fight: Taking on Alex Jones and Reclaiming the Truth About Sandy Hook** by Robbie Parker. The cover blurb visible in the capture praises it as a tribute written with courage and determination.

Everything else on the page is unchanged — the show branding, the February 22, 2025 date, the episode description naming Emilie Parker and the impact of hate speech on the family.

The book itself is the strongest available answer to the claim that pulled these pages into this file. A man who spent years being publicly accused of faking his daughter's death wrote a book about that experience, and a public broadcaster interviewed him about it. That is the documented record. The resemblance-based claim circulating alongside it is not. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/overview) and [Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird).
