## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X by the verified account [**Cabot Phillips](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi) ([@cabot_phillips](/Influencers/x/cabot-phillips))**. The text is a public response denying an allegation that he was at **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)** on September 8 or 9, and stating that he was at home in **Nashville, Tennessee**.

Beneath the text, the media attachment is split into two panels. The left is a wide-angle security-camera frame timestamped **"09-09-2025 Tue 09:17:36"**, showing a brightly lit indoor hallway with light-coloured carpet and plain walls, two figures with heavily blurred faces standing near a doorway, a dark trash can in the foreground, and a playback bar reading "09:11." The right panel is a composite of photographs.

Fort Huachuca is the U.S. Army Intelligence Center in Sierra Vista, Arizona, and it recurs across several strands of this investigation, including a reported September 8 to 9, 2025 gathering of officers and the [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) aircraft movements. An independent open-source investigator has since claimed the carpet pattern, dark trim and wall colour in this footage resemble Fort Huachuca interiors — **one account's visual reading, contested, unverified, and not a forensic match or a court finding.** Both the allegation and the denial are reproduced without adjudication. See [Cabot Phillips alibi video](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi) and [U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview).
