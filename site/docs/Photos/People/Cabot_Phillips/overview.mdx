## About This Cluster

Cabot Phillips published a video answering an online allegation that he had been at Fort Huachuca, the U.S. Army Intelligence Center in Sierra Vista, Arizona, on September 8 or 9, 2025. His answer was that he was at home in Nashville, Tennessee. He posted it himself, in public, and this cluster preserves the primary document. The base matters to the investigation because it recurs across several unrelated strands of the file, including the movements of an aircraft and a reported gathering of officers in that same window.

The single image is a screenshot of his post on X. Underneath the text are two side-by-side media panels offered as verification: a wide-angle security-camera frame timestamped "09-09-2025 Tue 09:17:36," showing a brightly lit interior hallway with light-coloured carpet and two figures with heavily blurred faces standing near a doorway, and a composite panel of photographs. An independent open-source investigator has since claimed that the carpet pattern, dark wall trim and yellow wall colour in that footage resemble interiors at Fort Huachuca. That is one account's visual reading, contested and unverified, and it is not a forensic match or a court finding.

Both sides of this are on the record and both are reproduced here without adjudication: an allegation made by an anonymous account, and a public denial with supporting footage from the person accused. That is the correct way to read it. The full analysis is at [Cabot Phillips alibi video](/US_Intelligence/Cabot_alibi), the surrounding intelligence thread is at [U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview), and the aircraft strand is at [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).
