## What This Image Shows

A **Texas Secretary of State** entity record for an organisation named the **Faith and Family Foundation Charity**. The document is the state's own registration listing — the standard record generated when a nonprofit entity is formed in Texas.

It was published on X by the account **[@1MrsWhistledown](/Influencers/x/1mrswhistledown)** on June 17, 2026, in a post whose framing was: "Lance Twiggs flees to Texas post-shooting, while his parents suddenly acquire two new homes and start a 'Faith and Family' charity. The timing of these events is interesting." The words "flees" and "suddenly acquire" are that poster's characterisation. The filing itself says only what a filing says: an entity was formed, on a date, under a name.

**Forming a charity is entirely lawful**, and no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of anything in connection with the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case. The record is reproduced as a primary document with the question left open.
