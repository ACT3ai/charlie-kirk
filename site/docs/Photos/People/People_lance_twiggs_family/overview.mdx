## About This Cluster

[Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) is the partner and roommate of [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson), the man charged in the [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) shooting of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). This cluster is not about him — it is about public records concerning his family that were surfaced on X by the account [@1MrsWhistledown](/Influencers/x/1mrswhistledown) in a post dated June 17, 2026. Forming a charitable organisation and buying property are entirely lawful, no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of anything in connection with this case, and the documents are reproduced here as primary records with the questions they raise left open.

The five images are the documents themselves. Two are Texas Secretary of State records for an entity named the Faith and Family Foundation Charity — an entity record and a company overview describing its purpose. Three are county property ownership histories referencing the 13 Branches Family Trust and the Twiggs surname, one of which shows the "Sold by" field naming [Twiggs Amy R](/People/amy-r-twiggs) and [Twiggs Mikayla R](/People/mikayla-r-twiggs). The poster's framing was that "Lance Twiggs flees to Texas post-shooting, while his parents suddenly acquire two new homes and start a 'Faith and Family' charity. The timing of these events is interesting." The words "flees" and "suddenly acquire" are that poster's characterisation, not a finding, and the underlying filings say only what they say.

What makes the set worth keeping is that it is documentary. Corporate filings and county deed histories can be checked by anyone, which is more than can be said for most of what circulates around this case. The full write-up, including the exact filing details, is at [The Lance Twiggs Family](/People/Lance_Twiggs_Family); the individual profiles are [Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs) and [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson); and the court thread is at [Court](/court/overview).
