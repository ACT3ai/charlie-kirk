## What This Image Shows

A **county ownership history** with the **"Sold by"** field visible — the detail that makes this the most substantive of the three property records in this cluster. It records the property as sold by **Twiggs Amy R** and **Twiggs Mikayla R** to the **13 Branches Family Trust** and Twiggs.

It was published by **[@1MrsWhistledown](/Influencers/x/1mrswhistledown)** on X on June 17, 2026, in a companion post to the one carrying the charity filing and the other two deed histories.

Read carefully, the sold-by line reframes the transaction: this is a transfer within a family, from named individuals into a family trust, rather than an outside acquisition. Intra-family transfers into trusts are routine estate planning and entirely lawful. No member of the family has been charged with or accused in court of anything in connection with this case, and none is accused here.
