## What This Image Shows

A second **county property ownership history** referencing the **13 Branches Family Trust** and the **Twiggs** surname. Together with the first record in this cluster, it accounts for the two homes referred to in the post that surfaced these documents.

Both were published by **[@1MrsWhistledown](/Influencers/x/1mrswhistledown)** on X on June 17, 2026, framed around the timing of the purchases relative to [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). That framing is the poster's; the deed history is the county's.

Buying a house is lawful, and no member of the Twiggs family has been charged with or accused in court of any offence connected to this case. What the records permit is a check — anyone can compare the recorded dates against the timeline rather than taking a caption's word for it.
