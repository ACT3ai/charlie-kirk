## What This Image Shows

A circulated screenshot of a social-media exchange dated **February 2022** — more than three years before the events at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). In it, **Jack Posobiec** describes military training trips to [**Fort Huachuca**](/Planes/Airport-Fort-Huachuca) and border crossings to Nogales and Juarez. **Tyler Bowyer** of [Turning Point](/TPUSA/overview) replies, as transcribed in the screenshot: **"jack stop going to huachuca."**

The reply reads as a joke between colleagues. Nothing in the exchange refers to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), to Utah, or to anything that happened in 2025, and the screenshot's provenance rests entirely on the account that published it.

It is in the file for one reason: [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview), the U.S. Army Intelligence Center and home of the Army's counterintelligence school, recurs across several unrelated strands of this investigation, and Jack Posobiec's U.S. Navy intelligence background is a matter of public record. **Neither man is accused of anything here**, and neither has been charged with or accused of an offence in connection with this case by any authority. This is a 2022 conversation that acquired attention in retrospect. See [Jack Posobiec](/People/jack-posobiec), [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer) and [U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview).
