## What This Image Shows

A circulated screenshot of a social-media exchange dated **February 2022** — more than three years before the events at Utah Valley University. In it, **Jack Posobiec** describes military training trips to **Fort Huachuca** and border crossings to Nogales and Juarez. **Tyler Bowyer** of Turning Point replies, as transcribed in the screenshot: **"jack stop going to huachuca."**

The reply reads as a joke between colleagues. Nothing in the exchange refers to Charlie Kirk, to Utah, or to anything that happened in 2025, and the screenshot's provenance rests entirely on the account that published it.

It is in the file for one reason: Fort Huachuca, the U.S. Army Intelligence Center and home of the Army's counterintelligence school, recurs across several unrelated strands of this investigation, and Jack Posobiec's U.S. Navy intelligence background is a matter of public record. **Neither man is accused of anything here**, and neither has been charged with or accused of an offence in connection with this case by any authority. This is a 2022 conversation that acquired attention in retrospect.
