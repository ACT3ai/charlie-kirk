## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of the PBS website playing an episode of *Think Tank with Steve Adubato*. The video shows a man in his forties in a dark blue polo, speaking with his hands raised mid-gesture. The lower-third graphic reads **ROBBIE PARKER — Author, A Father's Fight: Taking on Alex Jones and Reclaiming the Truth About Sandy Hook.**

The page metadata below the player is the substance here. It reads: "Steve Adubato welcomes Robbie Parker, Author of 'A Father's Fight: Taking on Alex Jones and Reclaiming the Truth About Sandy Hook,' for a powerful conversation about the impact of hate speech on Robbie and his family after the loss of their daughter, Emilie Parker, in the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting." The episode is dated 2/22/2025 and runs 25 minutes 8 seconds.

This is a mainstream public-television record of who Robbie Parker is and what happened to him. It was collected by researchers as part of the online comparison between him and a witness who appeared on television after September 10, 2025 — a comparison based on facial resemblance that has never been substantiated and that this site does not adopt.
