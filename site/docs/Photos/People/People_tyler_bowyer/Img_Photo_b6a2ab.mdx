## What This Image Shows

A circulated screenshot of a post attributed to **Tyler Bowyer** and dated **18 Sep 25** — eight days after the shooting — describing how Turning Point handled security. The quoted text reads: **"We do have a team... We run everything like a military op, all of our people are from that background."** It was shared by **[@alleytopfiles](/Influencers/x/alleytopfiles)** on X on July 11, 2026.

Researchers flagged the phrasing because responsibility for security at the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) event, and how it was divided between the organisation, campus police and contract personnel, is one of the least settled parts of the record. A senior executive describing an in-house team staffed from military backgrounds speaks directly to that division.

A statement about staffing an internal security team is not an admission of anything, and the screenshot's provenance rests on the account that published it. **Tyler Bowyer is the chief operating officer of Turning Point Action and TPUSA; no wrongdoing by him in connection with the events of September 10, 2025 is alleged or established anywhere on this site**, and he has not been accused of any offence by any authority. See [Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer), [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
