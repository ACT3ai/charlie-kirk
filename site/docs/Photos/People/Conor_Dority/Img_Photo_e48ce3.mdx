## What This Image Shows

A vertical compilation of three U.S. Army group portraits stacked top to bottom and separated by thin white borders. Each panel shows an artillery team in Operational Camouflage Pattern uniforms posed in front of a bright red fabric backdrop patterned with white lightning-bolt lines and a grey and black shield logo carrying crossed cannons and the words **"BEST REDLEG."** The top panel holds eight soldiers in two rows of four. The panels are labelled with team designations and home bases for the **3rd Infantry Division**, the **82nd Airborne Division** and the **11th Airborne Division**.

These are publicity photographs from a U.S. Army field-artillery competition. The same backdrop and event branding appear in the [Sanchez](/Photos/People/Sanchez/overview) cluster, and cropped versions turn up in the researcher desktop screenshot filed under the parent People cluster.

They are in this file because faces from them are being held against faces in crowd footage from [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). No such match has been established. Every soldier in these frames is an identifiable living person photographed at a public military event, **none is named here and none is accused of anything**, and nothing connects any of them to [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
