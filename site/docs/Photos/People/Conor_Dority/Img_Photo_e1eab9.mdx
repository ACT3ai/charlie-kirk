## What This Image Shows

A citizen-research collage on a black background. Across the top, large white text reads **"Conor Dority"**, with **"EMT"** beneath it in a stylised serif and **"Vascular access technician"** below that. The largest panel is an outdoor campus daytime scene in which a young man in a grey t-shirt with a black strap across his shoulder is caught mid-movement, a **bright green circle** drawn around his head. Around it sit a side profile of a first responder in uniform, a frame from a public simulation exercise, and various logos, arrows and callout boxes.

The green circle is the claim: that the man in the crowd is the named EMT. Everything else in the graphic is supporting material for that assertion.

The underlying account in the investigation file is that EMS crew members, reportedly including Connor Dority and a partner, were on scene in full gear with a trauma kit and asked to be let through to render aid. That account describes someone trying to help, and **no wrongdoing is alleged against him** — he has not been charged with or accused of anything by any authority. Whether the circled face is his remains unverified. See [Connor Dority](/People/connor-dority) and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
