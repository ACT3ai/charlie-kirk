## What This Image Shows

A photograph of a young man in a red **"American Comeback"** shirt — the tour branding for the event — standing near the barricade at the September 10, 2025 gathering at Utah Valley University. It was published with the caption **"My POV before I spoke to Charlie."**

That caption is a self-statement of proximity, posted by the account holder. It places him at the barricade before the shooting and indicates he expected to speak with Charlie Kirk, which is what makes him a close-range witness worth locating.

Researchers have set this frame beside the Fort Huachuca community photograph filed with it and asked whether the same face appears in both, and separately whether the man in this frame is the person publicly identified as Cooper Brown or the person identified as Jeb Jacobi. Both questions are open and contested. Online commentary about who he is and what he heard has shifted over time; this site reports the attributed claims only and **does not assert that anyone pictured knew of any attack in advance or acted to conceal anything**.
