## About This Cluster

Mikey McCoy is described in [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) tributes as the organization's chief of staff and one of [Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) closest friends. In the public record of the day of the shooting he is placed near Kirk and is reported to have made several phone calls in the immediate aftermath. Some commentators have raised questions about his movements and those calls; those questions are unresolved and are presented as reported observations, not as findings of wrongdoing. Nothing here alleges that McCoy did anything improper.

The single image on this page is a graphic built around the headline "WHO WAS ON THE PHONE WITH Mikey McCoy." It shows a shared Google Sheets spreadsheet on the left and, on the right, a video feed of a man in a black hoodie speaking into a microphone — the format of a livestream or commentary segment discussing the timeline.

What the image documents is the framing and wording chosen by whoever produced the graphic. The question it poses is the creator's, reproduced here with attribution rather than answered or endorsed.
