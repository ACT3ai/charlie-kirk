## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution, heavily cropped vertical frame of an outdoor crowd on a grassy slope or tiered seating in bright sun. In the foreground at the right, a middle-aged man with a short white beard, a dark grey t-shirt printed with a Native American face in light grey line art, and a two-tone cap looks upward and to his right with his mouth slightly open. At the left edge, cropped by the frame, is a blonde woman in dark sunglasses and a blue t-shirt with a red, white and blue **USA** flag graphic. Behind them a young man in a light pink polo, denim shorts and white socks stands among other onlookers.

The compression artefacts show this has been digitally zoomed and re-cropped from a much wider photograph — somebody enlarging a section of an audience.
