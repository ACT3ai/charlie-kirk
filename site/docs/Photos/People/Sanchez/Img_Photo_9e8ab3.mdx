## What This Image Shows

A digital banner graphic in brushed-metal silver, sitting over a blurred camouflage background. At the left is a shield-shaped metallic badge: curved text reading **"GEN RAYMOND T. ODIERNO"** across the top, **"BEST"** beneath it, **"REDLEG"** in large blocky letters at the centre flanked by two columns of four-pointed stars, the year **"2026"** split by crossed cannon barrels, and **"FORT SILL"** at the tip.
