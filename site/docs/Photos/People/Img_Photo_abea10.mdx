## What This Image Shows

This is a phone capture of a split-screen television news segment, replayed inside a social-media video player — the small black "Watch again" pill sits over the middle banner. The top pane shows a studio anchor in a tan jacket against a blue set with a "BREAKING NEWS" graphic behind him. The dividing banner reads **JOSH BRESLOW** with the handle @JOSHBRESLO. The lower pane is an outdoor interview with a fair-haired man in a plain white t-shirt, speaking from what looks like a porch.

The on-screen text at the bottom of the frame is the reason this image is filed: it reads **SKYLER BAIRD / WITNESS**. That single chyron is the documentary anchor for everything else in this cluster — it establishes that the man in the interview was presented to a national audience as an eyewitness to what happened at Utah Valley University on September 10, 2025.

Skyler Baird gave several televised interviews in the days after the shooting, and a section of online commentary later questioned how he came to be positioned where he was and how he was subsequently admitted to Charlie Kirk's memorial. Those are questions raised by posters, not findings. He has not been charged with anything and no court has found that he did anything wrong.
