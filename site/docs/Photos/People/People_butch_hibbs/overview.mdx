## About This Cluster

Butch Hibbs, also reported as Burton Hibbs, is a Heber City, Utah resident and, according to public reporting, a brother of California pastor [Jack Hibbs](/People/jack-hibbs). He is described in social-media discussion as an older man who was photographed near [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) as Kirk arrived at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). Some commentators have focused on an object visible in his shirt pocket in those photos; that discussion is unproven speculation, and nothing here establishes that Hibbs was involved in any wrongdoing.

The single image on this page is a screenshot of a Facebook post — written by a user named Charles Huyck in a Heber City church group and reposted with commentary by an account called "Alley Files" — that describes a long-standing friendship with Hibbs. Several lines are underlined in red by the poster, and the account's watermark is overlaid across the image.

What the image documents is the wording of that reposted personal account. Its highlighting and framing are the posters' choices, reported here with attribution rather than treated as findings.
