## About This Cluster

This is the holding area for a person-related image that has not yet been attached to a name. It exists because the investigation would rather keep an unassigned frame visible than let it disappear into an unsorted pile, and because faces that look unimportant in week one have repeatedly turned out to matter in week ten. When the person in the frame is identified, the image moves into a named cluster and this page shrinks.

The one image here is a candid, annotated frame from the plaza: a woman in a black sleeveless top reaching out to touch the arm of a man in a black t-shirt, while behind them a man in dark sunglasses and a black button-up shirt looks on with what appears to be red fluid on his right hand. Someone has added yellow text labels and a red arrow to the picture, which means it was circulated as an investigative post rather than as a plain photograph. The annotations are the poster's own reading of the scene; this site does not adopt them, and no claim is made here about who any of these people are or what they were doing.

Because the labelling is unverified, treat the frame as a scene rather than an identification. If you are working on the people visible in the immediate aftermath, the more developed threads are [Direct Crowd Black Shirts](/Photos/People/Img_Direct_Crowd_Black_110952) on the parent page and the [Security Team](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) cluster. For firsthand accounts from that part of the plaza, see [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
