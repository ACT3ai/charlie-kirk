## What This Image Shows

An older man in a bright blue long-sleeved button-down, balding with thin grey hair and silver wire spectacles, is bent forward at roughly forty-five degrees with his mouth wide open in a shout or grimace. A young man in a grey Lightning McQueen t-shirt, jeans and a charcoal cap grips his upper arm. Behind him a **Utah Valley University police officer** in a dark navy short-sleeved uniform shirt, with a short grey-flecked goatee, black-framed sunglasses and a gold name plate, moves in. A second officer in a black polo and khaki tactical trousers stands adjacent, holding a radio and speaking.

This is a documentary frame of a physical detention on campus, and it captures three distinct categories of person in one shot: a uniformed campus officer, an officer or contractor in a black polo, and a civilian participating in the restraint.
