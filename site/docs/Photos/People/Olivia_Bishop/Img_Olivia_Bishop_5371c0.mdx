## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a Facebook post from the verified **Orem Police Department** page, dated **August 19, 2025**, announcing the retirement of three Records Service Specialists identified as Chris, Kim and CJ. The post shows a side-by-side photo comparison of a woman at two points in her career. Beneath it, a comment from an account under the name [**Olivia Bishop**](/People/olivia-bishop) has been boxed in yellow by whoever captured the screenshot.

The yellow box is the researcher's emphasis, not part of the post. What the capture establishes is a link between the named account and the Orem Police Department's public page — the same employer shown circled in the LinkedIn capture elsewhere in this cluster.

That employment link is the verified part. The claim that drew this name into the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) file — an assertion about Ring-camera footage and contact with [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) — is a single unsupported online assertion, and no wrongdoing by anyone is alleged here. Commenting on a colleague's retirement post proves nothing.
