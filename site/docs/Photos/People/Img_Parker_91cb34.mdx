## What This Image Shows

Another capture of the same PBS episode page, this time paused on a split-screen moment: two bordered video frames side by side inside the player, host on one side and guest on the other, against the show's blue bokeh background. The PBS navigation bar, the Cascade PBS station notice and the search icon are all visible along the top.

The metadata below is identical to the other captures — *Think Tank with Steve Adubato*, February 22, 2025, 25 minutes 8 seconds, and the description of the interview about the aftermath of the Sandy Hook shooting and the harassment that followed the family.

Multiple near-identical screenshots of one web page usually mean a researcher was working through a video, pausing and capturing as they went. That is what this set is: someone building a case from a public-television interview. The case being built — that the interviewee is a man who appeared on television after [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) — is unsupported by anything beyond appearance. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/overview) for how that is handled here.
