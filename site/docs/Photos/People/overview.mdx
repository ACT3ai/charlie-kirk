## About This Cluster

This is the people layer of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation — the faces, the badges, the family charts, and the social-media profiles of everyone whose name has surfaced around the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). The cluster spans four very different groups: witnesses who spoke to cameras that afternoon, first responders and campus officers who were on the plaza, relatives and in-laws of people already named in the file, and men whose names briefly appeared in early misidentification reporting. Nobody pictured here has been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death, and the images are filed for identification and timeline work only. Every person shown is presumed innocent, and the descriptions on the child pages report what commentators have claimed rather than adopting those claims.

The material in this area is mostly identification material — a screenshot of a broadcast chyron, a name tape on a uniform, a hand-drawn family tree, a LinkedIn employer field circled in yellow. That is the whole point: independent researchers have been trying to put names to the people visible in crowd footage, and to work out how those people relate to each other. Several threads run through the set. One is the early Wednesday-afternoon misidentification, when multiple outlets briefly named a man later released without charge. Another is the Bednarz and Sipes family material, which grew out of a claim that a relative was on campus that day. A third is the set of images of a repeatedly interviewed witness whose broadcast appearances drew online scrutiny. All of these are open questions, not findings.

Start with the sub-clusters if you already know which name you are following — each one collects everything filed for that person in one place. If you are browsing cold, the images on this page are the loose set that has not yet been assigned to a person, and they include crowd frames, first-responder photos, and screenshots of early news reporting. For the written profiles behind these faces, see [People](/People/overview) and the [key individuals](/key_individuals/overview) index; for the people whose names surfaced in early suspect reporting, see [Suspects](/Suspects/overview); and for firsthand accounts from the plaza, see [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).
