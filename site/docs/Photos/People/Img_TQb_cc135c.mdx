## What This Image Shows

A citizen-research collage on a black background. The left side is a mirror selfie of a bearded, muscular man in jeans holding a phone. The right side carries a cropped **Facebook profile card for "Burton Hibbs III"** and a close-up photograph of a high-visibility safety vest showing a corporate logo. Bold text runs across the top and bottom of the graphic identifying the subject and stating **"Contracted May 2025."**

The claim being made is a contracting one: that a named individual was working under a contract from May 2025, months before the September 10 event. That matters to researchers because the construction and hardscaping work carried out around the venue is a live thread in this investigation, and because the file separately records material about a man referred to as [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs) and his family connections.

Holding a construction contract is entirely lawful and ordinary. Nothing in this graphic establishes that the contract relates to the event site, that the man pictured was present on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), or that he did anything wrong, and no such allegation is made here. See [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs) for the profile, the [related family graphic](/Photos/People/Img_HIMFxtUbIAE_Esr_d61473) in this cluster, and [UVU](/UVU/overview) for the construction thread.
