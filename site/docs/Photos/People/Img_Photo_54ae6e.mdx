## What This Image Shows

A tight selfie-style video still: a fair-skinned man with light brown hair fills the lower left of the frame, mouth open mid-sentence, a white wireless earbud in his right ear, wearing a white athletic-cut collared shirt. Directly behind him a tall black expanded-metal mesh fence runs across the frame, and beyond it a flat green athletic field stretches away toward blurred trees, red structures and distant grey buildings under a partly cloudy sky.

There is no on-screen text on this frame, which makes the background the only usable detail in it. A mesh perimeter fence, a maintained playing surface and what may be benches or equipment on the far side are consistent with a school or university athletic facility. Researchers use frames like this to place a person at a location and time when no caption is available.
