## What This Image Shows

A close, slightly low-angle outdoor shot of a light-skinned man in a crisp white dress shirt and a light blue and white striped tie, looking off to the right of frame with a squinting, tense expression. His hair is short on the sides with a distinctive thin gathered strand hanging at the nape of his neck, and a white wireless earbud sits in his right ear. There is no on-screen text on this frame.

Two details carry weight for identification work. The earbud is the standard sign that someone is on a live remote feed, taking direction from a control room, which fits the broadcast interviews recorded elsewhere in this cluster. Behind him, below a bright cloud-filled sky, are the tops of large white temporary structures with black metal framing — the kind of event tents and crowd barriers used at outdoor venues.

The clothing here is the same white shirt and striped tie visible in the memorial selfie later in this set, which is how researchers have been chaining these frames together into a single day-by-day account. Nothing in the picture itself establishes where or when it was taken.
