## What This Image Shows

A vertical TikTok screenshot wrapped around a recorded CNN broadcast. The white caption across the top reads: **"The Sandy Hook father accused of being a 'crisis actor'."** Inside the video frame, a man in a dark suit and red tie stands in front of a brick wall, with the location box "Newtown, Connecticut" and the time "5:22 PM" in the corner. The CNN Situation Room ticker along the bottom reads "FAMILY OF 6-YEAR-OLD VICTIM SPEAKS."

The TikTok overlay adds "This man lost his daughter one day befor this interview" and the caption line "So my name is Robbie Parker." The account is credited as **False Flag Files**, dated 2024-8-21, with the hashtags "#fyp #conspiracy #warmode #conspiracytiktok." Those hashtags are the honest label for what this clip is: content produced for a conspiracy audience, not reporting.

This image sits in the Charlie Kirk file only because posters have claimed the man in it is the same person as the witness who spoke on air after September 10, 2025. That claim rests on visual resemblance and nothing else. **Robbie Parker** is a real, living father whose daughter Emilie died at Sandy Hook Elementary; his loss, and the harassment campaign that followed him for years, are documented in public record and in litigation, and he has written a book about it. This site does not adopt the accusation reproduced in this clip.
