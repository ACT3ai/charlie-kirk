## What This Image Shows

A composite identification graphic on a black background, headed **"[Jeremy Gallman](/People/jeremy-gallman)"** with the subtitle **"[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) camera operator."** It layers an Instagram-style group photograph taken in front of a blue **Turning Point Action** backdrop in Phoenix, Arizona — with one man in a black polo and lanyard boxed in bright green — beside a high-contrast outdoor frame of a camera operator working an event, connected by a large golden arrow and red pointers. It was published by **[@MuppetMasher](/Influencers/x/muppetmasher)** on X on **April 20, 2026**.

The graphic asserts that the boxed man in the posed photograph and the operator behind the camera are the same person. That assertion rests on visual resemblance and has not been independently verified here.

The question underneath it is a real one and does not depend on this particular match: the people holding cameras at the event controlled a large share of the footage, and researchers want the videographer roster and the recordings. **Operating a camera is a job. No allegation of wrongdoing is made against Jeremy Gallman, and he has not been accused of any offence by any authority.** See [Jeremy Gallman](/People/jeremy-gallman), [Videos](/Videos/overview) and [Cameras](/cameras/overview).
