## About This Cluster

Jeremy Gallman is identified in citizen research as a [Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview) camera operator and video editor, and as part of the videographer roster working the [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) event at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). That matters to the investigation for one reason: the people holding cameras that afternoon control a large share of the footage, and researchers want to know who they were and what they recorded. Operating a camera is a job. No allegation of wrongdoing is made against him here, and he has not been charged with anything.

The single image is the composite graphic that made the identification. It sets an Instagram-style group photograph taken in front of a Turning Point Action backdrop in Phoenix, Arizona, with one man boxed in green, beside a bright outdoor frame of a camera operator working an event, and adds a large golden arrow and red pointers to link the two. It was published by @MuppetMasher on X on April 20, 2026. The graphic's own claim is that the boxed man and the camera operator are the same person; nothing in it verifies that beyond visual resemblance.

Read it as an unconfirmed identification of a working videographer. The value is in the underlying question — what did the event's own camera crews capture, and where is that footage now — rather than in the match itself. The written profile is at [Jeremy Gallman](/People/jeremy-gallman), a second copy of the same graphic sits under [TBD](/Photos/People/People_TBD/overview), and the footage question is developed at [Videos](/Videos/overview) and [Cameras](/cameras/overview).
