## About This Cluster

Brian Davis is named in the booking paperwork surrounding the arrest and transport of the accused. That is his entire connection to this case: he is a law-enforcement officer whose name appears on a form. He has not been accused of wrongdoing by this site or by any court, and the document is filed here because arrest timing is one of the load-bearing disputes in the whole investigation.

The single image is the Utah County Sheriff Inmate Booking Sheet and Probable Cause Statement for Tyler James Robinson. The line commentators point to reads "arrest date 09/11/2025 10:00 PM" with the arresting officer listed as DAVIS. That timestamp matters because it sits against a separate document, an affidavit of probable cause, whose timing the file records as roughly eighteen hours later — a gap that researchers have asked questions about. The booking sheet was published by @BasedSamParker on X on July 17, 2026. Elsewhere in the file, an "Agent Davis" of the Utah State Bureau of Investigation is described in a government document as handling the accused's driver licence at the Washington County Sheriff's Office.

The document is the evidence; the interpretation is the open question. Read the timestamps for yourself before reading anyone's argument about them.
