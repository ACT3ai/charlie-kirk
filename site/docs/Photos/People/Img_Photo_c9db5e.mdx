## What This Image Shows

A two-panel collage of Facebook screenshots. The left panel holds two posts from an account under the name **Skyler Baird**: one dated Sep 24 quoting 1 Nephi 14:7 from the Book of Mormon at length, and one dated Jul 10 that is a plain black graphic reading "Epstein Files — Is there any form of protest that makes it so our government isn't beholden to [Mossad](/Israel/Mossad) anymore?"

The right panel is a comment thread, mostly supportive replies from named accounts. One comment is boxed in thick red and pointed at by a large red arrow. It reads: **"Sadly I was there when Charlie was shot. I have to wonder how high up the idea came to install the pavers."** The reference to pavers connects to the separate question elsewhere in this investigation about the hardscaping installed in the plaza area before the event.

This is a self-statement of presence, posted publicly by the account holder, and that is what makes it evidence — it is his own account of where he was, not somebody else's claim about him. Expressing political opinions on social media is lawful and unremarkable, and nothing in these posts indicates knowledge of or involvement in anything. See [Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird) for the profile, [Pavers](/UVU/Pavers) for the hardscaping question, and [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) for the other accounts from the plaza.
