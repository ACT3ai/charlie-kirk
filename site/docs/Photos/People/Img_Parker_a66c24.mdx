## What This Image Shows

A capture of the PBS player with the interview running and a different lower-third graphic on screen. Where the other frames in this set carry the book credit, this one reads simply **ROBBIE PARKER — Father of Emilie Parker.** The guest is shown chest-up against a neutral domestic background, captured mid-speech.

The rest of the page is the familiar PBS interface and the same episode description below the player, naming the daughter lost at Sandy Hook Elementary and the years of harassment that followed.

Of all the frames in this group, this is the one that states the relationship most plainly, on a public broadcaster's own graphic. It is reproduced here because the claim that drew these screenshots into the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) file asks a reader to disbelieve exactly this caption, on the basis of nothing more than facial resemblance. This site does not ask that. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/People/Skyler_Baird/overview) for the full framing.
