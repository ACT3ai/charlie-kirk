## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution meme built to look like a wanted poster: a heavy black border, the word **"WANTED!"** in large red type across the top, and a square cropped photograph beneath it. The photograph shows a fair-skinned middle-aged man in thin aviator sunglasses and a light tan short-sleeved button-down shirt, turned three-quarters to camera with a slight relaxed smile. Other onlookers are partly visible around him, including someone in a light cap. The compression is severe — visible pixelation and colour banding.

There is no name on the poster, no source, no date, and no explanation of why this face was selected. It is a crowd frame with red text added.

This is the most legally dangerous format in the entire image corpus, and it is filed here as an example of what circulated rather than as an identification. **This site does not assert that the man pictured is a suspect, did anything unlawful, or has any connection to the death of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).** He is an unidentified member of a crowd.
