## What This Image Shows

A full desktop browser screenshot of the PBS episode page. The address bar shows the URL for the page titled "robbie-parker-and-the-aftermath-of-the-sandy-hook-shooting." The video player fills the upper half, and its thumbnail is a photograph of a smiling young blonde girl, credited on screen as **"Courtesy Robbie Parker."**

Below the player, the same episode metadata appears as on the other PBS captures in this set: the February 22, 2025 air date, the 25-minute runtime, and the description of a conversation "about the impact of hate speech on Robbie and his family after the loss of their daughter, Emilie Parker, in the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting."

The child in the thumbnail is the reason this material demands care. The online comparison that pulled these PBS pages into the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) file is a resemblance argument between two adult men, and it has never been substantiated by any document, record or authority. It is reproduced here in full context precisely so a reader can see what the underlying source actually says.
