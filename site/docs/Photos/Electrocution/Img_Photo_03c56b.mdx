## What This Image Shows

This is the diagram labeled Step 3, illustrating the claimed compression phase. It depicts **Lorentz forces** and a **magnetic pinch** acting on the chest and neck, following from the claim that touching the corded microphone with both hands completed a conductive path through body tissue, a metal necklace, and the mic cord, and that a rapidly changing magnetic field then induced strong currents by Faraday's law.

The physics named here is real physics used in roughly its conventional sense. Faraday induction, the Lorentz force, and magnetic pinch are all genuine phenomena with genuine equations. Naming a mechanism correctly is not the same as showing it operated: the diagram contains no field strength, no current, no timing, and no generator specification, which are precisely the numbers that would let anyone test whether the claimed effects are physically achievable.
