## What This Image Shows

This is an advocacy graphic, not a document. Its headline text reads: "FOUND IN THE EPSTEIN FILES / PROOF OF ORGANIZED, COVERT OPERATIONS to target individuals with technology / What happens without oversight." Behind the headline is a document image so degraded that the optical character reader returns only garbled fragments. Two campaign web addresses appear at the bottom: MindNexusLive.com and Stop3024.com.

The graphic makes a strong claim and supplies nothing a reader can check. No document identifier, page number, release batch, or date is given, and the underlying page is not legible in the image. "Proof" is asserted in the headline and demonstrated nowhere in the frame.

The **Epstein files** are a genuine and large body of released material, and specific documents in them can be cited by anyone. A graphic that says a proof exists without pointing to it is a campaign asset, not evidence. Nothing here references Charlie Kirk, Utah Valley University, or September 10, 2025.
