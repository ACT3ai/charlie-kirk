## About This Cluster

This overflow cluster holds material filed alongside the MKUltra and mind-control research that does not fit a named sub-thread. The Charlie Kirk investigation tracks this genre of material because the patsy framework — the argument that **Tyler Robinson** may have been steered or handled rather than acting alone — draws on a long public record of claims about covert behavioral-influence technology, and readers should be able to see the actual graphics that circulate rather than a summary of them.

The single image here is a campaign banner claiming the "Epstein files" contain proof of organized covert operations targeting individuals with technology, pointing viewers to two advocacy websites. It is advocacy art, not a document: the investigation logs it as an example of the targeted-individual genre that surrounds the mind-control thread, and none of its claims have been verified or tied to the September 10, 2025 assassination.
