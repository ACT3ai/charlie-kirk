## About This Cluster

This container holds the investigation's presentation decks on the CIA and intelligence threads. Its one sub-cluster is a 28-slide deck built for a video walk-through connecting three lines of inquiry: the reported CIA link to UVU's Center for National Security Studies, the pre-assassination Google-search spikes researchers flagged for names tied to that center, and the [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview) [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) surveillance jet's low passes near Orem on **September 10, 2025**.

The deck matters because it is the investigation's own synthesis of the intelligence threads rather than a third-party post — each slide states its sourcing (Flightradar24 playback, Google Trends charts, LinkedIn profiles) and repeats its own caution that the named individuals are not accused of anything, only that the linkage patterns are worth explaining. The core open questions the deck poses: who was on the N1098L flight, were drones launched, and why did seven UVU-linked profiles come down on September 28.

Open the [VIDEO Drones CIA Israel](/Photos/CIA_Intelligence/Slides/VIDEO_Drones_CIA_Israel/overview) sub-cluster to walk the deck slide by slide. The underlying threads are written up at [U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview), [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview), and the Israel search-pattern analysis at [Israeli Search Patterns](/Israel/Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims).
