## What This Image Shows

This is the single chart the deck relies on most. It tracks the search term **Mark Harlan** filtered to **Israel** over a 90-day window, and the tooltip on the graph reads "Sep 9, 2025 — Mark Harlan — 100." That is the day before the assassination. The slide places the chart beside the CIA thread, where Mark Harlan is listed first among the seven names and described elsewhere in the deck as a counterintelligence ethics expert.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
