## What This Image Shows

The charts on this slide track the search term [**Elena Vasquez**](/People/elena-vasquez) filtered to **Israel**, shown in both a 12-month and a 90-day window. The peaks legible on the graphs fall at **November 3-9, 2024** with a value of 100, and again in the 90-day view around **August 23, 2025**. The slide pairs these charts with the CIA thread's description of the name as a "national security policy" figure among the seven profiles the deck says came down on September 28.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
