## What This Image Shows

The chart here tracks **Jax Donovan** filtered to **Israel** over 12 months, with its labeled peak at **December 1-7, 2024** at a value of 82 — notably not a maximum-value spike, and nine months before the assassination. The slide repeats the same unsourced crew list, naming Michael Harlan as pilot in command, Elena Vasquez as mission engineer or observer avionics specialist, and J. 'Jax' E. Donovan as second pilot, each marked with a question mark.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
