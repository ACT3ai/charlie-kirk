## What This Image Shows

The slide's left column is the deck's CIA thread. It reads "CIA is suspected linked to NSCC (or UVU Center for National Security Studies)" and states that seven profiles connected to that campus body "appear to have profiles removed" on **September 28**, roughly two weeks after the assassination. The seven names listed are [**Mark Harlan**](/People/mark-harlan), [**Dr. Elena Vasquez**](/People/elena-vasquez), **Jamal Reid**, [**Rusty Needs**](/People/rusty-needs), [**Sarah Klein**](/People/sarah-klein), **T Ramirez**, and **L Patel**.

The seven names are lettered A through G on this version of the slide: A Mark Harlan, B Dr. Elena Vasquez, C Jamal Reid, D Rusty Needs, E Sarah Klein, F T Ramirez, G L Patel. The slide also captures the profile of the account the deck was circulating through, **@realjesseonfire** / Laissez Faire Lounge, described on screen as "Daily commentary" with roughly 11.2K followers, plus a fragment reading "TRACK COACH WITH" that belongs to the annotation on Jamal Reid.

The deck states its own limit in writing: "This doesn't mean these people did the assassination." That caveat should travel with every reproduction of this slide. A removed or updated web profile proves nothing, universities revise staff pages constantly, and none of these individuals has been charged with or accused of any offense by any authority.
