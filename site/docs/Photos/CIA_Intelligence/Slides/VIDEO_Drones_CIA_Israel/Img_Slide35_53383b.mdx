## What This Image Shows

This slide pairs two **Jamal Reid** charts: one filtered to the **Washington DC (Hagerstown)** market over 90 days, and one filtered to **Israel** over 12 months with axis labels running from October 20, 2024 through September 21, 2025. The CIA thread beside them describes the name as a "track coach with security ties," with the deck noting elsewhere that the spelling may be Jarmal Reid.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
