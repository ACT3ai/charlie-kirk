## What This Image Shows

This slide carries two **Michael Harlan** charts filtered to the **Washington DC (Hagerstown)** market over 12 months, with legible peaks at **December 1-7, 2024** and **August 24-30, 2025**, both reaching 100. Alongside them the deck repeats its unsourced crew claim: "One internet researcher said these were on the Sept 10th [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) Flight — Michael Harlan, Pilot in Command; Elena Vasquez, Mission Eng/Observer Avionics Specialist; J. 'Jax' E. Donovan, Second Pilot." Each name carries a question mark on the slide.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
