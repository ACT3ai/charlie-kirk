## What This Image Shows

The recurring caption block on this slide is the deck's core flight claim, repeated on every N1098L slide: "The Flight N1098L is designed to launch military drones. Normally flies at 41,000' altitude. Dropped to 203' altitude 116 knots stall speed @ 9:16am MT 1-mile from UVU. Then looped back to 1-mile from UVU and 203' altitude @ stall speed 25 minutes after assassination." The header calls the aircraft a "Drone Spy Plane" associated with the callsign **AXEL10** and the Army's **HADES** program.

This is the closing slide and the most useful one in the deck, because it converts the argument into questions rather than assertions. The nine items read: release video footage from university cameras and witnesses; publish who was on the N1098L flight; were drones launched; were military drones at UVU at 12:23 MT; who authorized the flight; who communicated with the flight; if drones, who watched the video footage; was foreign intelligence in communication with, or wanting, the N1098L flight on September 10; and was any of the N1098L flight crew associated with the campus national security body.

The slide ends with an explicit disclaimer from the author, credited on screen to **@PolitickleAi**: "No claim is made that drone assassinated Charlie Kirk. Or that flight crew knew about assassination." That sentence governs how every other slide in this cluster should be read.
