## What This Image Shows

The slide's left column is the deck's CIA thread. It reads "CIA is suspected linked to NSCC (or UVU Center for National Security Studies)" and states that seven profiles connected to that campus body "appear to have profiles removed" on **September 28**, roughly two weeks after the assassination. The seven names listed are **Mark Harlan**, **Dr. Elena Vasquez**, **Jamal Reid**, **Rusty Needs**, **Sarah Klein**, **T Ramirez**, and **L Patel**.

This is the deck's master slide, the one that shows all four numbered threads at once. Thread 2 lists the names the author says "Internet researchers said Israel before assassination searched for" — Michael Harlan, Elena Vasquez, Rusty Needs, Jax Donovan, Sarah Klein, Mark Harlan, and Jamal Reid. Thread 3 is Charlie Kirk himself, marked "Sept 10th, 2025. Assassinated 12:23 MT," with the note "These 4 may have NO connection to each other." Thread 4 is the N1098L flight claim.

The deck states its own limit in writing: "This doesn't mean these people did the assassination." That caveat should travel with every reproduction of this slide. A removed or updated web profile is not evidence of anything, universities revise staff pages constantly, and none of these individuals has been charged with or accused of any offense by any authority. The slide's own hedge — that the four threads may have no connection to each other — is the honest reading of it.
