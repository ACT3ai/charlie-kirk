## What This Image Shows

This chart tracks **Rusty Needs** filtered to the **Washington DC (Hagerstown)** market over 90 days, with the tooltip reading "Aug 15, 2025 — Rusty Needs — 100." The baseline is flat at zero across the rest of the window. The deck elsewhere lists a uvu.edu email address for this name and associates it with the campus national security body it writes as NSCC or NCSS. The heading on this slide reads "DC Google Searches" even though the body text underneath still says Israel, an inconsistency the author did not clean up.

Google Trends normalizes every chart to a 0-100 scale, so a spike built from a handful of searches is drawn exactly like one built from thousands. The tool reports no searcher identity and no reason. Common names also collect unrelated traffic. No person named on this slide has been accused of anything, and no agency has confirmed any link between these names and any intelligence service.
