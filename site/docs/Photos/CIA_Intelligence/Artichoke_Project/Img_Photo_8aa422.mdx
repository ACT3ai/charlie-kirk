## What This Image Shows

This image is a digital scan of a declassified United States Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) document, dated January 31, 1975. The document is a "MEMORANDUM FOR THE RECORD" regarding "Project ARTICHOKE," which discusses the agency's study and use of "special" interrogation methods, including drugs, hypnosis, and chemical substances. The scan shows aging, slight skewing, and various official stamps and markings, including a modern green QR code added in the top-right corner.
