## What This Image Shows

This is the first of the two photographs the post presents as showing a **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)** interior. The features it is offered for are the yellow wall color and the dark base trim running between the floor and the wall — two of the three elements the comparison rests on.

The comparison comes from an X post of **January 15, 2026** by **[@AMeadowInquiry](/Influencers/x/ameadowinquiry)**, which reshared a video **Cabot Phillips** had posted and wrote: "We know Fort Huachuca has the same carpet on its base that Cabot is seen walking on in his Alibi video. What is his video supposed to prove? All the Ft. Huachuca rooms have the dark trim between the floor and wall and there's that yellow wall again..."

This is a visual-similarity claim by one account, not a forensic identification. No metadata, geolocation, or building number has been published for any of the four images, and government-contract carpet, base molding, and institutional wall paint repeat across enormous numbers of federal and contractor facilities. Cabot Phillips is a living person who has not been accused of any offense, and no agency has said his video was recorded anywhere other than where he said it was.

See the full write-up with the mirrored video at [Cabot alibi and Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/Cabot_alibi), and the Fort Huachuca thread at [Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence_Assisted/fort-huachuca), [Fort Huachuca September 9](/Proof_Intel_Services/Fort_Huachuca_Sept_9), and [SAM 99-0404](/Planes/SAM-99-0404).
