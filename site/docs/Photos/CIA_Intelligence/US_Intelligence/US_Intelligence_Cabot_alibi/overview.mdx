## About This Cluster

These three photographs come from a single X post published on **January 15, 2026** by the account **[@AMeadowInquiry](/Influencers/x/ameadowinquiry)**, which reshared a video **Cabot Phillips** had posted showing himself walking through an interior hallway. The account's argument is a room-matching claim: that the carpet under Phillips's feet, the dark base trim between floor and wall, and a particular yellow wall color all resemble interior rooms at **Fort Huachuca** — the U.S. Army Intelligence Center and home of the Army's counterintelligence school in Sierra Vista, Arizona. Fort Huachuca matters to this investigation because of a separate thread: reported movements of the aircraft [**SAM 99-0404**](/Planes/SAM-99-0404) and a claimed gathering of roughly twelve lieutenant colonels at the base on **September 8-9, 2025**, two days before [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview).

The account wrote: "We know Fort Huachuca has the same carpet on its base that Cabot is seen walking on in his Alibi video. What is his video supposed to prove? All the Ft. Huachuca rooms have the dark trim between the floor and wall and there's that yellow wall again..." That is the entire evidentiary basis — three visual features compared by eye. It is worth stating clearly what this is not: it is not a forensic match, no metadata or geolocation has been published, and government-contract carpet, institutional base molding, and painted wall colors are standardized across enormous numbers of buildings. Cabot Phillips has not been charged with anything and no agency has said his video was recorded anywhere other than where he said it was. The claim is reproduced here because it circulated widely, not because it has been substantiated.
