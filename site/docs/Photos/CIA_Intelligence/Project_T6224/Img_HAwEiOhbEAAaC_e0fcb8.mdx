## What This Image Shows

This is the central chart of the set: a flow diagram headed "Targeted Individual Diagram," credited to RLighthouse.com, Rev 4.2, October 2019.

Its on-screen text lays out a claimed pipeline. A "Standard Nomination Form" submitted by the FBI, CIA, DHS, or another agency places a person on a watchlist as a "Non-Investigative Subject (NIS)." From there the chart draws arrows to blocks reading "Directs Program," "provides Funding and PsyOps," "hacks phones and electronics," "Lockheed GPS Satellite Tracking," "Vircator Attacks (3920-3935 MHz)," "Cell Tower Attacks (digital beam forming at 2300-2900 MHz)," "V2K Implants," "Remote Neural Monitoring," "Block Legal Help," "Block Medical Help," and "Gangstalking, House break-ins, Chemical Attacks, Poisoning," ending at "Family Relations Destroyed. Isolation."

These diagrams are the published opinions of one author, distributed by **Targeted Justice** and **RLighthouse.com** under a Creative Commons license and labeled on their face as "Opinions." They name serving and former U.S. military officers and assign each of them a role in the alleged program. This site does not reproduce those name-to-allegation pairings. The officers shown hold or held real, publicly listed commands; none has been accused of any offense by any authority, and nothing is offered anywhere in the diagrams that any of them directed anything against any civilian.

What the diagrams do establish is what the targeted-individual literature looks like and how it is organized, which matters because the mind-control thread of this investigation draws on it.
