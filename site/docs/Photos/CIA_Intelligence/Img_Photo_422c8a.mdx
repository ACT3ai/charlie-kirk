## What This Image Shows

This is a composite image showing a social media post on the left and a web browser screenshot of a government media database on the right. The post, from an account belonging to Candace Owens, details a theory about military flight patterns and government appointments, using an image of military officials inside an aircraft as supporting evidence. The right side of the image displays the original source of that photo on the DVIDS (Defense Visual Information Distribution Service) website, complete with its official caption, metadata, and licensing information, which contradicts the claims made in the social media post.
