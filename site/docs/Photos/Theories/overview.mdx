## About This Cluster

These images are the **explanatory diagrams** that circulated as people tried to make sense of the killing. They are infographics and annotated spreadsheets, not scene photographs. One is a flowchart titled "Chain of Evil" that maps proposed relationships between what its author labels puppet masters, [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview), church leaders, influencers, and citizens. Another, "Blueprint for Regime Change," uses arrows and logos to argue that government agencies, NGOs, and media coordinate foreign political transitions. Two more are spreadsheet grids, one shown beside commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), that assert links across headings like Liberty University, Eastern Europe, trafficking, and the military.

Each of these is a hypothesis, not a finding. Different posters advance very different frameworks, and the frameworks contradict one another as often as they overlap. Some researchers propose coordinated networks; others read the same names as loose association or coincidence. The connections drawn inside these graphics are the creators' claims, reproduced here with attribution and left explicitly unproven. They are gathered so a reader can see how the competing theories are built and judge the reasoning for themselves, rather than to endorse any one of them.
