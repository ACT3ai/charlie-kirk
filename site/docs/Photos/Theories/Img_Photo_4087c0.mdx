## What This Image Shows

This is a split-screen digital image or video still featuring political commentator [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) on the left and a detailed spreadsheet document on the right. Owens is shown from the chest up, speaking into a professional microphone behind a laptop, with a blurred office background. The right side displays a structured grid of black-and-white text columns in which a compiler has drawn lines between several individuals, organizations, and military connections under headings like "Liberty University," "Eastern Europe," "Trafficking/Sex Abuse," and "Military."

Every connection drawn on that grid is the compiler's own assertion. None of it is sourced on the graphic, no agency, filing or court record supports the groupings, and placing a name in a column headed "Trafficking/Sex Abuse" is an allegation by whoever built the spreadsheet — not a finding, and not one this site adopts. No living person named or pictured in the grid has been charged with any offence in connection with the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and nothing on this page suggests any of them did anything improper or unlawful. The image is filed because the graphic circulated widely, not because its contents are established. See [Theories](/Theories/overview).
