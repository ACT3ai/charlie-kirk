## What This Image Shows

The graphic uses colored, text-filled rectangular boxes organized in columns and rows to illustrate relationships between various entities, such as "Puppet Masters," "[TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview)," "Good Church Leaders," "NOT Sellout Influencers," and "Citizens." The hierarchy in the chart is the graphic-maker's argument, not a documented structure. No agency, filing or court record supports the chain of control the graphic depicts, no organisation shown is alleged on this page to have directed or concealed anything, and no living person placed in it has been charged in connection with the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
