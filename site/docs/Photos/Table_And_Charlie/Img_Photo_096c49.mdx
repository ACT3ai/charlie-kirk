## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical frame on the concrete plaza in bright overhead sun, the pavers casting hard elongated shadows toward the bottom left. A man in a dark T-shirt and light shorts leans forward in the foreground with both arms extended, taking hold of the arm of a person mostly hidden at the left edge. A third man in a graphic T-shirt and sunglasses stands behind, watching. A heavy dark band of motion blur runs down the far-left edge.

The only OCR text is the fragment **RELO**, probably part of signage rather than anything meaningful.

A hand on an arm can be a restraint, an escort, a person being steadied, or someone being pulled out of the way, and a single frame cannot say which. The site describes what is visible and stops there: no one in this frame is identified, and no conduct is alleged. The same moment appears from another angle in [Woman at the Table](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Woman_At_Table/overview), and the wider plaza sequence is in [Question Man](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Question_Man/overview).
