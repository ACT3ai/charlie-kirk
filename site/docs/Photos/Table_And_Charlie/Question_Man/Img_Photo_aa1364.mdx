## What This Image Shows

An exterior frame on damp light-grey concrete in the service area, with a white paper detailing mat printed with a boot design lying on the ground near the foreground man's feet. One man walks briskly past the camera at the left; a second stands in the midground beside the open door of the silver SUV, looking off to the right. Industrial-style buildings close the background.

There is no on-screen text. The detailing mat is worth noticing: it suggests the area was in use for vehicle work, which fits the appearance of a back-of-house service lane rather than a public route.
