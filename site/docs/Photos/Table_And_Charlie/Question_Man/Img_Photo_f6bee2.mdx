## What This Image Shows

A candid frame taken in the paved service area behind the courtyard. A silver SUV is parked at an angle with its rear passenger door standing open, a tan retaining wall runs behind it, and hard shadows put the time at the middle of the day.

A woman with a large black shoulder bag walks away from the camera toward a man in sunglasses and a black polo who is gesturing with both hands near the open door. There is no on-screen text. This is the same vehicle and the same stretch of pavement that recurs across several frames in this sub-cluster and in [Woman at the Table](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Woman_At_Table/overview).

A vehicle with an open door in the minutes after a shooting is unremarkable on its own, and the site draws no conclusion from it. What the frame contributes is a fixed point: this vehicle, in this position, with these people around it, in a sequence that can be ordered against the other frames.
