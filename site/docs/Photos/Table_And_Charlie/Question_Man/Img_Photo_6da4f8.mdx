## What This Image Shows

A sunlit frame on the paved walkway in front of the brick and concrete building. Several men in dark T-shirts and cargo-style trousers are moving alongside a man whose hand is covered in a red, blood-like fluid, guiding him away from the courtyard. Bystanders in a navy T-shirt, tan sweatpants and white high-tops stand in the foreground, and more people watch from an elevated balcony.

No on-screen text is present. This is the same person and the same walkway as the closer frame elsewhere in this sub-cluster, a few seconds later or earlier, and together the two give a short sequence: a man with blood on his hand being moved along the path by others.

The most natural reading is a person who assisted a gunshot casualty being escorted clear of the scene. Escorting someone away from a shooting is what security and medical response look like. The site states no other reading and accuses no one. See [Medical](/Medical/overview), [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [Witnesses at UVU](/Witnesses/overview).
