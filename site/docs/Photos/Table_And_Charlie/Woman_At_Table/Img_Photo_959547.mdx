## What This Image Shows

A close daylight frame of an exchange at the event. The woman in the midground has her brown hair pulled back with a thick strand over her right shoulder, wears white-framed sunglasses with dark lenses and a navy crew-neck T-shirt printed with white block text, and is caught mid-speech with a serious expression. The man she is addressing is positioned with his back mostly to the camera in a blue button-down shirt. A black loudspeaker on a vertical stand stands between them at the right of frame.

There is no readable on-screen text.

The speaker on its stand fixes the location: this is at the edge of the audio setup, close to the tent, rather than out on the open plaza. The blue button-down is the same garment type worn by the figure tracked through [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_Hand_off/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview), which is why this frame is kept in the identification working set. It shows a conversation and nothing more; nobody is identified and no conduct is alleged.
