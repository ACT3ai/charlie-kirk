## About This Cluster

This small sub-cluster holds two frames that did not fit any single named-person folder but that both bear directly on the table itself. One is an annotated wide shot in which a bright yellow arrow has been drawn from a man in a plaid shirt standing in the crowd down to a group of water bottles sitting on the table in front of the speaker. The other is a vertical photograph taken from behind a kneeling bystander, showing security and crew in frantic motion around the low stage platform, with one figure appearing to vault or tumble over a barrier.

The water-bottle arrow matters because the objects on the table are one of the few physical inventories the public footage provides. The site tracks the same question in [Platform and Water Bottles](/Tent/Platform_Water_Bottles): what was on that surface before the shot, and what was still there afterward. The second frame is one of the clearest public views of a person crossing the barrier line, which is the physical act that gave the table jumpers question its name. Neither image identifies anyone, and neither supports a claim that any person removed anything.

Compare with [At the Table](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/At_The_Table/overview) for the same seconds from the opposite side, and with [More](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Table_More/overview) for the longer video sequence.
