## What This Image Shows

A press-quality frame of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) moving through a dense crowd at the outdoor event, wearing the white T-shirt with **FREEDOM** in black block lettering, mouth open in a focused expression, reaching out toward the people around him. A red baseball cap hangs frozen in mid-air to his left, showing its green under-brim, caught as it was thrown or dropped. Hands, phones and red caps fill the frame around him.

The legible text is **FREEDOM** on the shirt and a fragment of the **Yrefy** sponsor mark.

The filename records the working label - Husky Sunglasses - that researchers gave to a figure standing near Kirk in this set, tied to the sponsor-representative identification thread this sub-cluster is built around. The reason a press frame is used for that work is resolution: at this quality the faces of people around Kirk are legible in a way that livestream grabs never are. No identification is asserted here, and no conduct by any person is alleged.
