## What This Image Shows

A sun-drenched, high-resolution frame of **Charlie Kirk** moving through the crowd, slightly right of centre, pointing forward with a red baseball cap frozen in mid-air just to his left. Around him the crowd layers from the immediate foreground back - a raised hand with gold jewellery, a partly visible red cap, and rows of people holding up smartphones to record.

The legible text is **FREEDOM** on the shirt and a fragment of the **Yrefy** sponsor mark.

The filename carries the working label used in the identification thread this sub-cluster supports - a man in a blue shirt and square sunglasses standing near Kirk. That comparison is unresolved and the site asserts no identification.

The frame's other value is documentary: it records how many people in the immediate crowd were recording on phones. Separate reporting the site tracks says some witnesses found their footage missing by the time they got home. Establishing how much footage existed in the first place is the necessary first half of that question.
