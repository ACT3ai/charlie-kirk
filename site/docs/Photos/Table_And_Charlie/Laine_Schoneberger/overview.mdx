## About This Cluster

The investigation file records that a man behind the table wearing a dark blue shirt and square sunglasses, referred to in the notes as the Husky Square Sunglass Dark Blue Shirt guy, was thought by researchers to resemble [**Laine Schoneberger**](/People/laine-schoneberger), Chief Investment Officer and Managing Partner at **Yrefy**, which was a sponsor represented at the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) event. The Yrefy logo is visible on the backdrop banner in several frames from this cluster, which is what made the sponsor-representative theory worth checking.

The images here support that comparison rather than settle it. One is a posed group portrait of six people behind a table holding promotional signing boards for the documentary **Uncle Tom II**, with silver marker pens piled on the table. One is a professional outdoor headshot of a bearded man in glasses, a black suit jacket, a blue dress shirt and a striped tie, of the kind used on a corporate or LinkedIn profile. The remaining frames show [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) in a white FREEDOM T-shirt moving through the crowd holding red MAKE AMERICA GREAT AGAIN caps, with the man in question somewhere in the surrounding group. The resemblance is a researcher's visual read, reported here with attribution and not asserted as an identification; no claim of wrongdoing attaches to anyone in these frames.

Read [Laine Schoneberger](/People/laine-schoneberger) for the profile, then look at the child page [More](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Laine_Schoneberger/Laine_More/overview) for the additional crowd frames. For sponsors, staffing and who had table access, see [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).
