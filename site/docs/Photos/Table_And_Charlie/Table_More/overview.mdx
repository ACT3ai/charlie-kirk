## About This Cluster

More holds five screen grabs taken from a single longer clip - the player clock runs to 2:06 and one frame reads 0:47 - watermarked **@THEINTELSCIF**. This is the sequence that gave the Table and Charlie section its shape, because it follows the table area continuously from ordinary event activity through the moment of panic rather than freezing one instant.

The frames show a videographer standing behind a professional camera on a tripod with the **YREFY.COM** banner behind him, a man with a shaved head and sunglasses leaning forward over the table or barrier with a gold watch on his left wrist and both arms extended downward, the white and blue **PROVE ME WRONG** and **THE AMERICAN COMEBACK** canopy with multiple people running, ducking and scrambling underneath it, and a two-part collage in which red circles and a red arrow have been drawn onto a hand movement and onto a man in sunglasses. The annotations are the poster's, added to argue that a specific hand motion is significant; this site reproduces them as a reported reading of the footage and does not adopt the conclusion or suggest that any person shown acted improperly.

Watch these against [At the Table](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/At_The_Table/overview) and [Images](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Images/overview) for the same seconds from other cameras, and against [Item](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Item/overview) for the closest annotated crop. The camera positions themselves are catalogued in [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview) and [TPUSA Production Footage](/cameras/TPUSA_Production_Footage).
