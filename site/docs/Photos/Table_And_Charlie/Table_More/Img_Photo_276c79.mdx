## What This Image Shows

A compressed video still. On the left, a man with a shaved head and sunglasses in a dark short-sleeved polo with a small gold emblem at the chest leans forward over a barrier or the table, both arms extended downward toward something out of focus in the foreground. A gold watch is visible on his left wrist. A professional broadcast camera on a tripod stands behind him and a white banner with blue text is partly in frame.

The legible text reads **EFY.COM** and the watermark [**@THEINTELSCIF**](/Influencers/x/intel-scif).

The reaching-down posture is why this frame is filed here. It is the physical action at the centre of the table jumpers question - a person leaning over the table line toward the ground on the other side. It is also exactly what somebody reaching to help a person who has collapsed looks like, and the frame alone cannot distinguish the two. The site records the posture and draws no conclusion, and accuses no one.
