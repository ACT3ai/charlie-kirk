## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide frame of the media position behind the table. A videographer stands at a professional camera on a tripod in the centre, mostly in shadow, with a man in a light blue plaid short-sleeved shirt, dark sunglasses and a trimmed goatee leaning slightly forward at the left. The table in the foreground carries rows of white baseball caps with red striping.

The legible banner text reads **EFY.** and **G USA**, fragments of the **YREFY.COM** sponsor banner and the Turning Point USA logo.

This is the calm frame in the sequence, before anything happens, and that is its use: it fixes the normal state of the table area - who was standing where, which camera was on which tripod, and what was laid out on the surface. Every claim about something changing has to start from a picture of what it changed from. The man in light blue plaid is the figure tracked in [Blake Harruff Plaid Light Blue](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Blake_Harruff_Plaid_Light/overview).
