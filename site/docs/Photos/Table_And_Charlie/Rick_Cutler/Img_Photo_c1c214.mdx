## What This Image Shows

A composite split vertically down the middle. On the left is a screenshot of a **Jews for Jesus** web page headed **Why Was Jesus' Original Name Yeshua?**, subtitled the story behind Jesus' Hebrew name and why many Israelis have heard it wrong, next to a video still of a man in a **YESHUA** T-shirt captioned **Rick Cutler**. On the right is a Facebook video post from **United Rescue Alliance** dated **Dec 9, 2019**, captioned that Rick Cutler, Director of Training and Logistics, URA Guatemala, gives an update from the Natio... with the text truncated. The **UNITED HATZALAH** and **United Rescue Alliance** logos are placed together at the top right.

The collage is constructed to draw a visual line between three organisations. That is the compiler's argument, made by juxtaposition rather than by proof: placing logos next to each other does not establish any relationship between the organisations or any significance to a person's involvement with them.

The site reproduces the collage with attribution and rejects the inference. Volunteering with faith-based emergency and humanitarian organisations is ordinary and lawful, and nothing here supports any claim that **Rick Cutler** did anything improper, was involved in the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), or had any knowledge of them.
