## About This Cluster

**Rick Cutler** is one of the named people who was near Charlie Kirk on **September 10, 2025**, and social-media analysts have built a large body of claims around him. This sub-cluster holds the three images those claims are usually attached to. The site treats all of it as reported allegation. Nothing here has been established, no charge has been brought, and the site does not assert that Rick Cutler committed any crime, acted improperly or had any advance knowledge.

One image is a side-by-side collage joining a **Jews for Jesus** article page headed why Jesus was originally named Yeshua, showing a man in a Yeshua T-shirt captioned Rick Cutler, to a **United Rescue Alliance** Facebook video post dated **Dec 9, 2019**, captioned that Rick Cutler, Director of Training and Logistics for URA Guatemala, gives an update, with the **United Hatzalah** logo placed alongside. The second is a split-screen product image for a wearable gesture-control band, showing the band worn on a hand next to a desktop with a folder labeled **PERO**; the investigation file records the claim, from @RealTruthJustic quoting @DanCampbel44604, that a device resembling this brand was visible on his hand, and asks what gesture was being made. The third is a dark, low-resolution interior frame of a cluttered workspace. A separate and far more sensational social-media thread alleging a concealed weapon is recorded in the private research file and is not repeated here; it is an unverified claim by anonymous accounts.
