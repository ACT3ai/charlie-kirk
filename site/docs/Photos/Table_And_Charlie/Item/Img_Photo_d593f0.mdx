## What This Image Shows

The tightest crop in this section, and the only one that shows an object rather than a person. It is heavily pixelated and blurred, consistent with a crop from a surveillance-quality or low-bitrate recording. In the upper middle of the frame a pale hand is angled downward with the fingers extended, over a dark blue surface that reads as clothing or a lap. Below the fingers is a small object with a yellowish base and a blue and white upper portion.

Two bright red arrows have been drawn on: one at the object, one at faint white lettering nearby. There is no readable text in the frame.

What the object is has never been established. Public commentary has proposed everything from event merchandise to a piece of audio equipment, and the resolution does not support any of it. The site publishes the frame because it is the closest public imagery to the question of whether anything physically changed hands at the table, and states clearly that it does not show a transfer, does not identify an object, and does not implicate any person in any conduct.
