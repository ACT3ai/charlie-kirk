## About This Cluster

This is the background set for **Brian Harpole**, gathered separately from the event frames in [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview). It exists because the identification of the man in the blue shirt with the earpiece rests on face and build comparison, which needs clear reference photographs of the named person to work against. Harpole is a living person; nothing on this page asserts wrongdoing, foreknowledge or criminal conduct.

The most useful documents are the two that carry text. One is a mobile screenshot of a Google image-search result whose caption reads that this is SSG's Director of Training, Brian Harpole, at SHOT Show, showing a man in an olive button-down with a lanyard badge holding a handgun at a trade-show display. The other is a photograph of two men behind an outdoor bar under a caption banner reading SSG's Greg Shaffer and Brian Harpole. Together they place the name inside **Shaffer Security Group**, which is the same firm the [Dan Flood](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Dan_Flood_Raybands/overview) material points to. The rest are ordinary personal photographs - a family portrait on a blanket in the woods, a smiling man behind the same outdoor bar with pink annotation marks added by a researcher pointing to his temple and to sunglasses hanging from his collar - plus a tight crop of a blue collared shirt and khaki cargo pants with a utility tool in the pocket, and the annotated X screenshot showing a man in plaid being escorted across pavement.

Read [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole), [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole), [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer) and [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group). For the escorted-man frame in its other filing, see [Brown Shirt Puller](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brown_Shirt_Puller/overview).
