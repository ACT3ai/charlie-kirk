## What This Image Shows

A dossier-style graphic assembled on a plain white background. It is headed **Dan Flood** in large bold type with the subtitle **Head of Risk Strategy - Executive Protection and Cybersecurity**, and combines several cropped portraits of the same man's face, a screenshot of a digital assistant's answer about his role at **Turning Point USA**, a screenshot of a LinkedIn experience panel, and a block of summary text about his background.

Compilations like this circulate widely in open-source investigation threads. They are convenient and they are also the point at which sourcing is most often lost, because the underlying screenshots get flattened into one image and the original links disappear.

What the graphic establishes, to the extent its component screenshots are accurate, is a professional profile: a security professional in executive protection and risk work, associated with TPUSA. That is background. The site's questions about the security arrangements at **UVU** are about planning, staffing and the absence of specific protective measures, not about any individual's conduct, and no wrongdoing is alleged here.
