## About This Cluster

Two images sit here, and the second is one of the more directly evidentiary photographs in the whole Table and Charlie section. **Butch Hibbs** is the older man with a full white beard, sunglasses and a cap, photographed standing beside **Charlie Kirk** in the white FREEDOM T-shirt at the event. Investigation notes record public commentary about the Hibbs family and about backstage access, all of it unverified claim by online accounts; the site makes no assertion that Butch Hibbs did anything improper or knew anything in advance.

The second image is a close-up of a black **RODE Wireless GO II** receiver clipped to the chest pocket of a green, grey and white plaid button-up shirt, its LCD lit and showing active channel levels. The shirt matches the one worn by the older man in the first photograph. That matters because the wireless audio chain is at the centre of the [exploding mic theory](/Mic/overview): if a receiver was worn on a person at the table, then the corresponding transmitter and lavalier were on Kirk, and the question becomes who fitted it, who handled it and where the unit went afterwards. A receiver on a chest pocket is a completely normal way to run event audio, and the photograph is offered as documentation of the rig, not as an accusation.

Read [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs) for the profile and [Jack Hibbs](/People/jack-hibbs) for the family thread that appears in public commentary. For the equipment, go to [RODE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic), [AV Team and Mic Setup](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup) and [Table Jumpers and the Missing Mic Device](/Mic/Table_Jumpers_Device).
