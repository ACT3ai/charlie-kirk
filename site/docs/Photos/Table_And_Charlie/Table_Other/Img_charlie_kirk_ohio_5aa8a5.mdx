## What This Image Shows

A close profile press photograph from the same Ohio vigil, lit by a cool blue spotlight at night. The man faces left, holding the red-windscreened microphone, with his left hand resting on his chest in a gesture of address. He has short brownish-grey hair, visible stubble along the jaw, and wears the same light blue and white plaid long-sleeved button-down with the collar unbuttoned.

There is no on-screen text.

A near-perfect profile at press resolution is a useful comparison plate, because profile geometry - nose line, brow, chin, ear placement - survives lighting changes better than a three-quarter view does. That is why both vigil frames were kept: they are the highest-quality reference images available of a man in a light blue plaid shirt connected to this circle of events.

Whether he is the same person as the plaid-shirted figure at [**UVU**](/UVU/overview) has not been established, and this site does not assert it.
