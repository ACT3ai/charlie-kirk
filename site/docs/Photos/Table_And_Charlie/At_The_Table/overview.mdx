## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster holds four frames pulled from the same short clip - the player clock reads 0:16 of 0:36 in three of them - showing the grass, barricade and stage furniture immediately after the shot at [**UVU**](/UVU/overview). These are the closest thing the public record has to a continuous view of the table area in the first seconds, before responders converged and before the scene was cleared. That window is exactly the period the [Table Jumpers](/Mic/Table_Jumpers_Device) question covers.

The frames show men in plainclothes security and production roles crouching low behind the blue-draped table, a large dark equipment transport case sitting flat on the grass, a white-topped platform resting on two heavy-duty cases, and a silver crowd barricade in the foreground with the seated hillside crowd behind. One man in a blue shirt is caught leaning hard on the platform with one hand flat on its top. Researchers reviewing these frames have asked which of these objects were part of the audio-video rig and whether anything was lifted off the platform in the seconds that follow. Note that the automated description generated for one of these frames misidentifies the setting as a 2024 rally in Butler, Pennsylvania; the surrounding frames, the canopy text and the hillside seating all place it at the UVU courtyard.

Compare these against the wider shots in [More](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Table_More/overview) and the annotated close-ups in [Item](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Item/overview), then read [AV Team and Mic Setup](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup) for who had equipment responsibility that day. The [UVU Tent](/UVU/Tent) page covers the structure the table sat under, and [Surveillance Cameras](/cameras/overview) lists which camera positions were pointed at this angle.
