## What This Image Shows

The sharpest frame in this set. A tightly cropped view into the crowd in hard sunlight, with a middle-aged bearded man in dark thick-framed sunglasses and a light blue, white and dark plaid short-sleeved button-up looking toward the right of frame. Several other people stand in front with their backs to the camera, and a table stacked with red campaign caps is visible at the left edge.

The only text fragment is on clothing in the crowd. Because this frame resolves beard texture, hairline and the exact plaid grid, it is the reference plate the rest of the comparisons in this sub-cluster are built on. Everything else here is a lower-quality frame being matched back to this one.

The cap table at the edge is the same merchandise surface that gives this whole section its name, so the frame also fixes proximity. That proximity is a fact about position, not about conduct, and the site asserts nothing about the person shown.
