## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level frame of the audience packed onto the rising concrete terraces, arms raised and phones up, with a grey-covered metal crowd barrier running across the lower third and several staff and security personnel standing behind it facing the crowd. A PA speaker on a tripod rises near the centre. One man in a plain white T-shirt has his back to the camera.

The only legible text is a fragment of FREEDOM lettering on a shirt. What this frame documents is the staffing line - how many people were positioned between the crowd and the stage, where they stood, and which way they were facing.

That is directly relevant to the security questions this site tracks: the perimeter, the barricade line and the absence of certain protective measures. Standing at a barrier facing a crowd is the normal job, and no inference about any individual is drawn from it here.
