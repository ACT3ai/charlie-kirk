## About This Cluster

A bearded man in a light blue and white plaid short-sleeved button-down shirt appears repeatedly in the footage from behind the merchandise table at [**UVU**](/UVU/overview), and researchers working from the public video assigned that figure the working name **Blake Harruff**. This is the largest sub-cluster in the Table and Charlie section because the plaid shirt is one of the few reliably trackable visual markers in low-resolution frames, which lets analysts follow one person across several separate camera positions.

The set mixes three kinds of material. There are event frames from the day itself, including a wire-service crop carrying the credit line for [**Charles McClintock Wilson**](/People/charles-mcclintock-wilson) / ZUMA, showing the bearded man in profile in front of a blurred crowd. There are background images gathered from social media, including a memorial Facebook post dated **11-Jun-2025** signed Blake Harruff that reads that fifteen years have passed, that not a day goes by without thinking about the price paid, above a photograph of a young Marine in desert MARPAT camouflage. And there are personal photographs showing a bearded man in tactical gear with a rifle in a field and at what appears to be a night range. The identification is an open-source inference drawn from visual comparison and has not been confirmed by any official record; nothing here indicates that the person shown did anything wrong or knew anything in advance.

Use the [Blake Haruff](/People/blake-haruff) profile for the written biography and the state of the identification, then compare against [Brian Harpole Bald](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Brian_Harpole_Bald/overview) and [Other](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Table_Other/overview), which contains the Ohio vigil photographs of another man in a light blue plaid shirt. Wider context is in [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [Persons of Interest](/Suspects/overview).
