## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution crop of the crowd behind the metal crowd-control barricades. A dark banner spans the upper frame with only the word **WRONG** fully legible. A man in a white T-shirt sits or leans in the foreground with his head bowed, a woman in a white cap stands at the far left, and a bright orange circle has been drawn over the head of a short-haired blond man standing in profile in the midground.

No other text is present. The circle is the publisher's marker, added to single out one attendee from the crowd. Why is not stated on the face of the image, and the site does not repeat any of the unsourced captions that have accompanied circulating copies.

At this resolution the marked person cannot be identified, and the site names no one and alleges nothing. The frame is retained so that the annotation itself is on the record and can be checked if better footage of the same crowd position ever surfaces.
