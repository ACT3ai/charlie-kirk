## What This Image Shows

An event photograph of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) standing under the white pop-up tent, holding a baseball cap and pointing to the left of frame. Behind the metal barricade a large crowd of mostly young people watches, many holding phones up to record.

The blue vinyl banner across the top of the frame reads **WRONG** and **PROVE ME** either side of a stylized lightning bolt split into stars and stripes; a FREEDOM shirt fragment is also legible. The date and event are the [**UVU**](/UVU/overview) stop of the tour, and the frame is one of the clearer views of the front-of-stage geometry: how close the barricade sat to the tent, and how many phones were already recording before anything happened.

That second point matters for the whole evidentiary picture. A large fraction of the crowd was filming, which is why so much footage exists - and why the site tracks reports that some witnesses found their recordings missing later.
