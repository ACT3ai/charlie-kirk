## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of the X web interface showing a post by **EddieSpaghetti** (@PnutGalaree). Attached is a paused video still of a sunlit crowd in front of a light two-storey building with an angled canopy reading **AMERICA COMEBACK**, with one person in the crowd ringed in bright green. The still is credited **SOURCE: [@PHIL_LYMAN](/Photos/Patsy/Distraction_Phil_Lyman/overview)**.

The post text reads that if an actual autopsy was performed, as required by law, it would or will show evidence of that, and that if so the [FBI](/FBI/overview) needs to investigate or interrogate the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) individual, described as Mr. 1776, that was recorded and pictured here as the one placing the wireless lavalier mic onto [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk). It is timestamped **2:20 AM, Oct 8, 2025** with 54 views, and a reply from the same account appears below.

This is one anonymous account's unverified claim about an unidentified person, reproduced here with attribution because it is one of the few public statements that names a specific role at the table. The site does not adopt it, does not identify the circled person, and asserts no conduct, involvement or knowledge by anyone. The underlying questions - who fitted the transmitter and what the autopsy record shows - are handled at [AV Team and Mic Setup](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup), [RODE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic), [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview) and [Medical](/Medical/overview).
