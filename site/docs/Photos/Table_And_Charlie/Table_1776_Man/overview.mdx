## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster holds a single screenshot, but it carries one of the most specific public claims about the table area. It is a post on X by the account **EddieSpaghetti** ([@PnutGalaree](/Influencers/x/pnutgalaree)), timestamped **2:20 AM on October 8, 2025**, with an attached video still in which one man in the crowd is ringed in bright green. The nickname **Mr. 1776** comes from lettering visible on that person in the footage.

The post reads that if an actual autopsy was performed, as required by law, it would or will show signs of that, and that if so the [FBI](/FBI/overview) needs to investigate or interrogate the [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) individual, labeled Mr. 1776, who was recorded and pictured as the one placing the wireless lavalier mic onto [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk). The attached still credits [**@PHIL_LYMAN**](/Photos/Patsy/Distraction_Phil_Lyman/overview) as the source and shows the American Comeback canopy behind the crowd. This is one poster's unverified claim about an unidentified person, reproduced here with attribution and not adopted by this site; no one has been shown to have done anything improper, and the site makes no assertion about the identity or conduct of the person circled.

The claim connects to two live threads: who physically fitted the lavalier transmitter, covered in [AV Team and Mic Setup](/Mic/AV_Team_Mic_Setup) and [RODE Wireless Mic](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic), and what the autopsy record shows, covered in [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview) and [Medical](/Medical/overview). For the mic-transfer imagery itself, see [Butch Hibbs](/Photos/Table_And_Charlie/Butch_Hibbs/overview).
