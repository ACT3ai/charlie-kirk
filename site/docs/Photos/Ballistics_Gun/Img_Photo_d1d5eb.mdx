## What This Image Shows

An aerial satellite photograph of the campus, overlaid with a red circle, a larger blue circle, and yellow and blue measurement lines running out to labelled points. The buildings are named on the map — Engineering and Transportation Technologies, the Woodbury School of Business, the Department of Information Systems — with the Hall of Flags marked at the bottom.

The measurements are the content: 143 yards to one pin, 137 yards out to another, and 78 yards repeated at three points around the central red circle, with a purple pin labelled Bartholomew. Those distances are the inputs every acoustic and trajectory argument in this cluster depends on, and putting them on a single sheet is what makes those arguments checkable.

The named buildings matter too, because the dispute over where the shot originated is a dispute about which of these structures had a line of sight to the stage and which of them anyone was seen on or near. The candidate positions are examined at [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) and [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center); the acoustic use of these distances is at [Acoustic Two-Event Split](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Acoustic_Two_Event_Split), and the plaza itself at [Courtyard](/UVU/Courtyward).
