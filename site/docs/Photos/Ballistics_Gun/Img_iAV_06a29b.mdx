## What This Image Shows

This image is a digital composite split vertically into two equal halves: an annotated satellite map on the left and handwritten physics calculations on a plain white background on the right. The map shows an aerial view of a building plaza with overlaid geometric lines, circles, and concentric wave propagation arcs. The right side contains mathematical equations written in red digital ink, calculating sound propagation time and distance based on temperature and relative humidity.
