## About This Cluster

This cluster holds the physical-evidence images at the center of the case: the wound photographs, trajectory diagrams, bullet and caliber comparisons, casing engravings, acoustic analyses, and pictures of the recovered **Mauser Model 98** chambered in **.30-06 Springfield**. The official narrative says a single .30-06 round fired from the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** killed **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). Every image here bears on whether that account fits the physics — the entry point, the exit, the energy, the angle, and the sound.

The evidentiary record these images document is contested. The **ATF lab report of September 17, 2025** called the recovered jacket fragment ".30-caliber class" but reached an inconclusive result, writing that rifling like this appears on "numerous makes and models" — and a January 2026 defense motion objected when the State moved the fragile fragment to the FBI for further testing that could alter it, with no defense expert present. Researchers such as @_DaniFesto have argued that a 5-9 degree downward shot from the Losee roof should have struck the face or jaw rather than entering at the throat, and independent acoustic analyses shared by @troofevades and Project Constitution reportedly place the sound source far from the official position. These are reported claims and open questions, not conclusions — but they are the reason this cluster exists.

Start with the entry and exit wound frames, the casing-engraving images, and the trajectory diagrams, then walk the sub-clusters: [Acoustics Expert](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Acoustics_Expert/overview) for the 3D sound reconstruction, [Mauser Model 98](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Mauser_Model_98/overview) for the alleged weapon, [Drop Off Locations](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Ballistics_Drop_Off_Locations/overview) for where the rifle was reportedly found, and [.30-06 Energy Mismatch](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Gun_Thirty_06_Energy/overview) for the energy math. For the written analysis behind these images, see [Gun &amp; Bullet Analysis](/Gun_Bullet/overview), [ATF Fragment Inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive), and [Wound Trajectory Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle).
