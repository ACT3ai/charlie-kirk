## What This Image Shows

A livestream frame showing the shared **SOLIDWORKS** workspace with a 3D architectural model and the full assembly tree expanded down the side. The tree lists a detailed human model broken into named components — eyeball, eyebrow, iris, eyelid, and mouth parts, left and right — alongside a Gazebo assembly, multiple 3D sketches, and construction planes. The sidebar shows [**Project Constitution**](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) with **Gideon** and **Tai (PenPub)**.

The level of detail in the tree is the point worth registering. This is not a sketch over a photograph; it is a dimensioned assembly with a scaled human figure placed in a modelled structure, which means angles and sight lines can be measured rather than estimated.

Whether the inputs are right is a separate question from whether the method is sound, and the inputs here — the seated position, the head orientation, the structure dimensions — are reconstructed from public footage rather than from survey data. The conclusions the presenters draw remain their own analysis.
