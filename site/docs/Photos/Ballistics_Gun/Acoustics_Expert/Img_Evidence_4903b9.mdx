## What This Image Shows

A livestream frame showing the source footage itself under review in the shared window: a large crowd around the white canopy printed THE AMERICAN COMEBACK with the valance reading PROVE ME WRONG twice, metal crowd-control barricades, speakers on tripods, and a red arrow overlay added by the presenters. The sidebar carries the **Project Constitution** branding and the two participants.

This frame is the raw input to everything else in the sub-cluster. The models, the sightline diagrams, and the angle measurements are all reconstructions built from footage like this, and the resolution of the source is the ceiling on how precise any of them can be.

That constraint is not the analysts' fault. The high-resolution campus and production footage has not been released, so independent reconstruction depends on publicly posted video.
