## About This Cluster

These seven screenshots come from a [**Project Constitution**](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) livestream in which an acoustics analyst walks through a 3D reconstruction of the [UVU](/UVU/overview) courtyard where [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot on **September 10, 2025**. The frames show the SOLIDWORKS CAD model of the Hall of Flags area — the canopy tent, the terraced steps, the surrounding buildings — with sightlines and camera-pointing vectors drawn through it, alongside annotated satellite views measuring angles and distances. Acoustics matter because the sound of the shot, captured on dozens of phone recordings, can be triangulated the way artillery fire is located in war zones.

According to the analysis presented in the stream, the acoustic signature points to the shot arriving from the south, above the Teachers balcony area, rather than from the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) roof named in the official account. A separate triangulation effort shared by [@troofevades](/Influencers/x/troofevades) reportedly kept converging on a location in the courtyard within about two meters, near Kirk himself — which the analysts note would fit the "firecracker" description many witnesses gave. These are the analysts' reported findings, not established fact, and they remain unreconciled with the official narrative.
