## What This Image Shows

A livestream frame showing the shared screen running **SOLIDWORKS**, the engineering CAD package, with an assembly file open named Assem2.SLDASM. The model on screen is a simple canopy shelter with a seated mannequin figure inside it. The system clock in the capture reads 21 October 2025. The sidebar shows the **Project Constitution** branding and the two participants.

Building the venue in CAD rather than annotating photographs is what separates this work from most of the material in this cluster. A dimensioned model lets you place a canopy, a seat, a figure, and a rooftop in true relation to one another and then read angles and sight lines off the geometry instead of estimating them from a perspective image.

The seated figure is positioned because the head and neck orientation at the moment of impact is the variable everything else depends on — the same variable the annotated anatomy diagrams elsewhere in this cluster argue about.
