## What This Image Shows

This image is a composite digital screen capture of a social media post on the platform X (formerly Twitter) by the user John Cullen (@I_Am_JohnCullen). The left side of the frame features a large lightbox overlay showing a screenshot of audio software displaying a stereo waveform with several distinct sharp peaks. On the right side, the original social media post and its metadata are visible, containing text arguing that two shots were fired 0.07 seconds apart during a shooting incident involving Charlie Kirk.
