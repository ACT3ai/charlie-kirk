## What This Image Shows

This image is a digital medical infographic illustrating a ballistic trajectory through a human neck and head, using three 3D digital model renderings of a young man's bust. The models, set against a solid black background, demonstrate the entry and exit points of a projectile from three angles: frontal, right profile, and left profile. Red and yellow markers, accompanied by green arrows and white labels, map out the simulated path of the wound.
