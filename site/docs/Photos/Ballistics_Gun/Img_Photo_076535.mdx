## What This Image Shows

A highly pixelated vertical frame from the same rally footage. A person in a white shirt is ducking or leaning sharply downward behind a blue-rimmed stage barrier, with red and white striped bunting beneath the ledge. In the crowd behind the metal barricades, a man in a grey hoodie is holding a phone up to record. The TruthUltra watermark sits in the lower left.

The detail that gets pointed out in this frame is the bystander filming. In this case as in others, the surviving visual record depends almost entirely on what members of the public captured on their phones, because the professional and institutional footage has largely not been released.

That dependency runs through the whole investigation. Witnesses have said publicly that they were contacted and asked to delete their recordings, and campus footage requests have been denied.
