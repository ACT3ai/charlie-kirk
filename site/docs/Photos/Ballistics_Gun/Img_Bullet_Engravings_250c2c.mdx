## What This Image Shows

This image is a 3x2 grid of six forensic laboratory photographs documenting two specimens of rifle ammunition. The top row shows three different angles of a fired, empty brass rifle casing, while the bottom row shows three angles of an unfired, complete cartridge. Each panel includes a yellow metric forensic ruler and a handwritten white evidence label, suggesting a detailed documentation of markings etched into the ammunition's metal bodies.
