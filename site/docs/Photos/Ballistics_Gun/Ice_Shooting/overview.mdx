## About This Cluster

This small cluster holds comparison material from the **Dallas ICE facility shooting**, a separate 2025 attack in which authorities reported rifle cartridges marked with handwritten anti-ICE slogans. The single image shows five rifle cartridges on a stripper clip with "ANTI-ICE" written on one casing in marker. It sits inside the Ballistics section because it is the closest public parallel to the inscribed casings reported in the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) case.

The parallel is what gives it weight. In the Kirk case, Governor [**Spencer Cox**](/People/spencer-cox) and multiple outlets described casings engraved with meme phrases — "Hey fascist! CATCH!", "O Bella ciao...", and others — and researchers have questioned how plausible it is that two high-profile 2025 shootings both featured message-inscribed ammunition. Whether the repetition reflects genuine copycat behavior or, as some posters allege, a staged signature, is an open question; both readings circulate online and neither is established.

Compare this image against the Kirk-case engraving photos in the parent cluster, especially the Bullet Engravings frames and the NY Times etchings image. For the written record of the Kirk casing inscriptions and the rotary-tool detail from the ATF report, see [Gun &amp; Bullet Analysis](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and [ATF Fragment Inconclusive](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive).
