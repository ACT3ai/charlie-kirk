## What This Image Shows

An oblique aerial view of the suburban neighbourhood taken from digital mapping imagery, integrated into the route video at five minutes forty-two. Houses with green lawns surround a central unkempt vacant lot of dense trees and dry golden grass. A large white arrow points down into those trees. The road is labelled 800 West, and the inset route map with its compass sits at the lower left.

The arrow identifies the wooded pocket itself rather than a street position, and the aerial view shows what a street-level photograph cannot: that this is an enclosed patch of overgrowth in the middle of a residential block, overlooked on several sides by houses, a shed, and a garden trampoline.

That is the geometry researchers keep returning to. A wooded area in this corridor is not remote ground; it is surrounded by homes. Who saw what, and what any nearby private camera recorded, is not in the public record.
