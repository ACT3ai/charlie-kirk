## What This Image Shows

A wide sunny frame from early in the route video, thirty-four seconds in. It shows a dusty, grassy transition lot with a flatbed utility trailer, a slatted livestock trailer, and a white pickup truck, bordered by a treeline with houses on a low hill behind. A post carries a red-on-black KEEP OUT sign. The overlay label reads WOODED AREA 3 with an arrow pointing to the trees.

This is the third distinct wooded area the video identifies along the route, and cataloguing them is the whole purpose of the exercise. The official summary refers to a wooded area near the campus as though it were a single unambiguous place; the reconstruction shows that the corridor contains several, on ground with different owners and different access.

Which of them the rifle was recovered from, and how the search moved between them, has not been published. That is the gap the sub-cluster documents.
