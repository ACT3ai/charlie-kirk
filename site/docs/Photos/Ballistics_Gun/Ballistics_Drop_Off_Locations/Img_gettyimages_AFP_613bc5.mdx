## What This Image Shows

A wire photograph of four investigators gathered at the edge of a grassy area bordering a dark treeline in low, warm sunlight. Three wear [**FBI**](/FBI/overview) tactical clothing, one back reading FBI EVIDENCE RESPONSE TEAM. The fourth wears a black polo reading **DPS FORENSICS** across the shoulders and carries a ruggedized black tablet.

The mix of agencies is the detail. [Utah Department of Public Safety](/Law_Enforcement/utah-dps-beau-mason) forensics personnel are working the same ground as the federal evidence team, which is what a joint scene looks like in practice.

That is worth recording because the division of labour between federal and state investigators became a live issue in this case. Local and state investigators have been described as sidelined, and the intelligence-side inquiry into possible foreign links was reportedly shut down. This photograph documents at least one point at which state forensics and the bureau were working the scene together.
