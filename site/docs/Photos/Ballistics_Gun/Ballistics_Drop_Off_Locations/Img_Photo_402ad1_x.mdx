## What This Image Shows

A high-angle daytime view over the [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview) campus plaza in autumn. The frame takes in modern institutional buildings, paved walkways, a sunken concrete amphitheatre, and a large ornamental pond with fountains running. Signage reads BROWNING ADMINISTRATION at the left and the partial word SORENS at the right, for the Sorensen Student Center. Banners on the pedestrian bridge read A PLACE FOR YOU and UVU. A white event canopy stands in the plaza.

The frame is the establishing shot of the route reconstruction: the campus as it looks from above, before the video follows the claimed movements outward through the neighbourhood to the wooded areas.

The buildings named here recur throughout the investigation. The Sorensen Student Center and the plaza are where the event and the immediate aftermath took place, and the surrounding rooftops are the positions argued over in the trajectory material.
