## What This Image Shows

An oblique aerial satellite render of the suburban neighbourhood, overlaid with the same broadcast graphics — EXCLUSIVE INVESTIGATION, TRACKING THE SHOOTER'S MOVEMENTS, and the We the People [Project Constitution](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) logo. Map labels read [**UVU Army ROTC**](/UVU/overview), West 925 South Street, South 700 West Street, and 925 South. An inset at the lower left shows the route as a red line with a compass rose.

The inset is the thing to read. It draws the claimed path as a continuous line rather than as a set of points, which is more than the public record does — the official account gives a rooftop, a wooded area, and a car park, without a published reconstruction between them.

The ROTC building appears again in the [Walk Path](/Photos/FBI/Walk_Path/overview) aerials and in the [gun drop area map](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/Img_Gun_Dropped_RED_6f7c85), where it is boxed alongside the Center for National Security Studies. Whether the route running past those facilities means anything is not established. See [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and [CIA and UVU Profiles](/UVU/CIA-UVU-Profiles).
