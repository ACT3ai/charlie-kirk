## What This Image Shows

A still from an independently produced video investigation, watermarked to the We the People [Project Constitution](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) channel. The frame shows a clean suburban roadside — paved street with double yellow centrelines, concrete sidewalk, manicured lawn, a landscaped strip with river stones, boulders and ornamental grasses, a white slat fence and a guardrail behind. A long white arrow points down to a specific spot on the road shoulder, an inset route map sits at the lower left, and the header reads EXCLUSIVE INVESTIGATION and TRACKING THE SHOOTER'S MOVEMENTS.

The video this comes from walks the claimed route from the campus out through the neighbourhood, stopping at each point of interest and pointing to it. The arrow here marks one such point on the shoulder.

That kind of reconstruction is being done by citizens because the official version has not been published in a form that can be checked: according to a post by [@MuppetMasher](/Influencers/x/muppetmasher), the official statement places the suspect's car in a campus lot at 8:29 AM without stating when it left, and no cell-site reconstruction of the walk has been released. The route material is at [Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location) and the search imagery at [Walk Path](/Photos/FBI/Walk_Path/overview).
