## What This Image Shows

A second crop of the same wire coverage, framed to show the lettering clearly: [FBI](/FBI/overview), EVIDENCE, RESPONSE, TEAM stacked in yellow on the back of the centre officer's navy shirt. Four officials stand beside the asphalt road at the edge of the wooded area, one with a circular agency patch on her sleeve, a black tactical bag on the grass by their feet and a concrete utility post at the far left.

Both crops of this photograph circulated separately in news coverage, which is why both are kept. The wide version emphasises the setting; this one emphasises which agency was working the scene.

That attribution is not incidental. Which agency controlled the search of the recovery corridor bears on who holds the documentation now, and on the complaint that local investigators were sidelined.
