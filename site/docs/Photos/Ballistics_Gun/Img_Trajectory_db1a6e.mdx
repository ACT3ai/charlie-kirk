## What This Image Shows

A heavily cropped, low-resolution frame of a man at the event with his eyes closed, the face soft from enlargement. A hand-drawn red oval marks a point behind the ear, and a long white arrow runs diagonally down toward the shoulder. Fragments of the event backdrop are readable at the edges.

The two annotations make one claim between them: an entry or impact point at the circled position, and a direction of travel indicated by the arrow. That direction runs downward and forward across the neck rather than steeply down from above, which is the same argument the diagrams in this cluster make.

The frame itself cannot support the identification. At this level of enlargement from compressed footage, the circled area is a handful of pixels, and hair, shadow, an earpiece, and a microphone cable all read the same. It is recorded as an annotated claim. The substantive treatment of entry position and direction is at [Wound Trajectory Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle), [Trajectory and Wound Mismatch](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Trajectory_Wound_Mismatch), and [Ballistics and Wound Analysis](/Medical/ballistics_and_wound_analysis).
