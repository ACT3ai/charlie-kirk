## About This Cluster

This cluster is the image side of the witness problem at Utah Valley University on **September 10, 2025**. Hundreds of people were in the plaza, dozens were interviewed on camera within the hour, and their accounts do not agree on the two things that matter most: where the wound was and where the sound came from. A witness named **Sara** told one station she saw Kirk hit in the chest and repeated it three times, then described a neck wound in a second interview at a different station less than an hour later; the very next witness in the same lineup described "a fountain of blood… out of the side of his neck" from the start. Another witness, **Nick**, described "a loud pop" and a trajectory from his left through the neck — one of many accounts that do not describe a heavy rifle report.

The images filed here document who was present and where they were sitting, which is the other half of the witness question. Reserved front-row seating at these events is allocated rather than first-come, so identifying who occupied it is a legitimate line of inquiry into how the event was organized. The sub-cluster below preserves the photographs attached to a post by **@MuppetMasher** asking why a particular attendee held a reserved front-row seat. That post asks a question and makes no allegation, and this site carries it the same way: presence is recorded, no conduct is attributed to anyone, and benign explanations — a ticketed guest, a local invitation, a sponsor allocation — remain fully available.
