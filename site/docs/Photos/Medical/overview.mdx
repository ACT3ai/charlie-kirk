## About This Cluster

This cluster holds images tied to the medical and forensic questions surrounding [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s death. The single image on this page is a screenshot of a post on X from an account called "Project Constitution," which claims that leaked police dispatch audio indicates no autopsy was performed. That claim is contested and is presented here as an allegation, not a finding. It sits alongside broader medical questions the investigation tracks: the nature of the neck wound, whether it was an entry or exit, and what a full examination would have shown.

The medical thread matters because forensic detail bears directly on which account of the shooting is credible. According to court filings discussed elsewhere on this site, an [ATF comparison](/Gun_Bullet/ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive) of a bullet jacket fragment reportedly recovered during an autopsy to a recovered rifle was described as inconclusive, and Utah enacted a 2025 law restricting the sharing of autopsy photographs. This site does not assert a definitive cause of death, which remains a subject of dispute. Images in this area are described clinically and treated as records of claims to be weighed, not conclusions. Open the image above for its full write-up. For the written analysis, see the Medical section.
