## About This Cluster

**Justin Davis** appears in the investigation not as a suspect but as a link in an employment chain. The private firms around Charlie Kirk's protection detail share personnel history: **Shaffer Security Group**, founded by **Greg Shaffer**, and **Integrity Security Solutions** (Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC), founded by former SSG employee **Brian Harpole**, drew from the same small pool of operators. Davis shows up in that pool, and the open question researchers have asked in public — most directly in a July 2026 post by @alleytopfiles on X — is simply which firm he was working for and when. He is a living private citizen against whom no allegation of wrongdoing is made here.

Three images document the association. The first is a photograph at what appears to be an outdoor range or quarry, with a red arrow and text overlay identifying one of two men in tactical clothing as "Justin Davis." The second is a screen capture of a Facebook post from the Shaffer Security Group page dated April 8, 2017, showing four men in tactical gear and naming them in the caption as **Brian Harpole**, **Greg Shaffer**, Justin Davis and Dutch Moyer — a straightforward, dated, first-party record of who worked together at that time. The third is a side-by-side comparison of two men in dark clothing and sunglasses, offered by the poster as a likeness match; that comparison is the poster's own and this site draws no conclusion from it. Nothing in these images places Davis at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025**.

For the firms themselves see [Shaffer Security Group (SSG)](/Photos/Security_Team/Security_shaffer_security_group/overview) here in the photo hierarchy and the written pages at [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group), [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer) and [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security). The detail as a whole is covered at [Security Team](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).
