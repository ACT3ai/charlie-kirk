## What This Image Shows

A screen capture of a Facebook post from the **Shaffer Security Group** page, dated April 8, 2017. The photograph shows four men in tactical and military-style clothing standing side by side under a covered structure at what looks like an outdoor range or quarry.

The caption names them: "Brian Harpole; Greg Shaffer; Justin Davis; Dutch Moyer."

This is the strongest document in the sub-cluster, because it is first-party and dated. The company itself published the photograph and identified the people in it, which establishes that these four men worked together in 2017 without relying on anyone's visual guesswork. That matters to the case only in a narrow, factual way: **Brian Harpole** later founded the firm reported to have provided protection at **Utah Valley University**, and **Greg Shaffer** founded SSG, which has publicly stated it was not there.

An eight-year-old company photograph is not evidence about **September 10, 2025**, and no allegation is made against any of the four men named. Working together in the past is not wrongdoing.
