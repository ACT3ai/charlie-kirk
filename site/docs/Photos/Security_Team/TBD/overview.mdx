## About This Cluster

This is a holding area, not a finding. It exists because the security-team material includes footage of people who have not been identified, and the investigation's rule is that unidentified is not the same as unimportant — an unnamed figure filed away quietly is how evidence gets lost. The single item here is parked under **Security Team** because that is where it was filed, and it stays labelled "TBD" until someone can say with attribution who is in it.

The image is a vertical screen capture of a social-media video player, timestamp reading 0:12 of 0:34, showing two young men outdoors in bright daylight. One walks briskly across the foreground from right to left; the other stands in the midground beside a silver SUV, looking off to the right. Behind them is a pile of gravel and a modern concrete building. Nothing in the frame names either man, dates the footage, or places it at [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview) on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) — and this site makes no claim that either person is connected to the security detail or to any part of the events of that day.
