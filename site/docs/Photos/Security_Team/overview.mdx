## About This Cluster

Charlie Kirk was surrounded by people whose job was to keep him alive, and on **September 10, 2025** that did not happen. Private protection at **Utah Valley University** was reportedly provided by Integrity Security Solutions, trading under **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC**, founded by former police officer **Brian Harpole**; **TPUSA** security was headed by **Dan Flood**; UVU supplied roughly six campus police officers under Chief Jeff Long for perimeter and crowd duties. This cluster collects the photographs of that detail — who stood where, which firms were involved, what the personnel histories are, and what was said afterwards about responsibility. None of it establishes wrongdoing by anyone; all of the individuals named here are living people and no criminal conduct has been alleged against them in any court.

The images raise questions that reporting has left open rather than closed. A text-message screenshot shows an exchange with a campus chief flagging that "there is student roof access pretty close to where CK will be set up" at the Sorensen Student Center, with a request to control that access or to post a guard there and the reply "I got you covered" — which makes the rooftop question a matter of prior notice rather than hindsight. A since-deleted post from **@ShafferGroup** states that **Shaffer Security Group** was not in Utah on 9/10/2025 and had not been affiliated with TPUSA since 2022, naming Rockhouse Integrity Group instead; founder **Greg Shaffer**, described in reporting as a former FBI agent, repeated the substance of that to Headline USA in what reads as a business dispute between former colleagues. Other images in the cluster reproduce third-party claims about **Dan Flood** and Israeli connections, and a poster's "palm gun" theory about **Rick Cutler** — both of which are the posters' assertions only, unsupported by any evidence this investigation has verified, and reproduced here solely because they circulated widely.

Start with the position notes and the roof-access text thread on this page, then open the sub-areas: [Dan Flood](/Photos/Security_Team/Dan_Flood/overview), [Justin Davis](/Photos/Security_Team/Justin_Davis/overview), [Shaffer Security Group (SSG)](/Photos/Security_Team/Security_shaffer_security_group/overview), [lsraeI connection](/Photos/Security_Team/lsraeI_connection/overview) and [TBD](/Photos/Security_Team/TBD/overview). The written profiles and the failure analysis sit at [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview), with specific pages on [perimeter and rooftop failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures), [no counter-snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) and [screening and credentials](/Security_Team/screening-credentials); the rooftop geometry itself is at [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview).
