The legible text is the article's own summary: that the Herzog family is a Jewish family that includes the sixth and eleventh Presidents of Israel and a Chief Rabbi of Israel, with an information sidebar classifying it as a political family, listing the current region as Israel and the place of origin as Eastern Europe.

The image is a reference lookup, not proof.
