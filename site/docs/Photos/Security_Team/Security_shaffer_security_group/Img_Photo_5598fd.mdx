## What This Image Shows

An excerpt from a Headline USA report, captured as an image and preserved on IPFS.

The text states that Shaffer Security Group deleted its post hours later, but that its owner, former FBI agent **Greg Shaffer**, reiterated the information by phone on the Saturday night. It quotes him: "The individuals who work for that company [Integrity Security Solutions] were the ones on the stage." It goes on to report Shaffer saying that Integrity Security Solutions founder **Brian Harpole** and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) security director **Dan Flood** both used to work with him until, in his words, they "stabbed [him] in the back," and that Harpole "worked for me for seven years, and then Brian started his own company" before a financial disagreement, the sentence trailing off mid-quote in the excerpt.

This is the corroborating half of the deleted post: the same claim, made on the record to a publication rather than anonymously online. What it establishes is that Shaffer said these things, not that they are true. His account describes a commercial and personal falling-out between former colleagues, and it should be read as one party's characterisation of that dispute. No wrongdoing by Harpole, by Flood or by anyone else named is established by it, and all are living private individuals.

See [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group), [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer), [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security), [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood).
