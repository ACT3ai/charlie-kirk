## About This Cluster

In the days after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), the public assumed the security firm that had guarded [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) on earlier tour stops was the firm that guarded him at [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview). **Shaffer Security Group (SSG)** — X handle [@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup), founded by **Greg Shaffer**, described in reporting as a former FBI agent of some twenty years — publicly said otherwise, and that correction is why this cluster exists. Which company held the contract on the day matters for every question that follows about advance work, rooftop coverage and who answers for what.

The three images here support the written page on the firm. They reproduce a since-deleted [@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup) post stating that the firm was not in Utah on 9/10/2025 and had not been affiliated with [**TPUSA**](/TPUSA/overview) since 2022, naming instead **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC**, trading as **Integrity Security Solutions**; the attached Texas business record shows that company incorporated 05/09/2023, file number 0805052578, with **Brian Harpole** as registered agent at a Lantana, Texas address. A second image captures the [@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup) account itself, joined April 8, 2017. Shaffer subsequently repeated the substance to Headline USA by phone, saying the individuals on the stage worked for the other company and describing a prior falling-out with former colleagues. That account is one party's characterisation of a business dispute; it is reported here with attribution and is not a finding of wrongdoing by anyone named.

The full write-up, including the company snapshot table, is at [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group), with related pages at [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer), [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security) and [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole). For the personnel overlap between the two firms see [Justin Davis](/Photos/Security_Team/Justin_Davis/overview); for the detail as it stood on the day see [Security Team](/Photos/Security_Team/overview) and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).
