## What This Image Shows

A capture of the **Shaffer Security Group** account on X, handle **[@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup)**, showing the account header and a join date of April 8, 2017.

On its own this is a small thing. Its function is authentication: when a post is deleted, the first response is usually to question whether the account was real, whether the handle was genuine, or whether the screenshot was fabricated. A capture of the account itself, with an eight-year history predating the case entirely, answers that.

The same April 2017 date appears on the company Facebook post reproduced in the [Justin Davis](/Photos/Security_Team/Justin_Davis/overview) sub-cluster, which is a small consistency check across two independent platforms.

Nothing on this frame makes any claim about [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) or about any individual. The substantive statement from this account is reproduced at [Deleted SSG Tweet With the Texas Business Record](/Photos/Security_Team/Security_shaffer_security_group/Img_Photo_31b790); the write-up is at [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group).
