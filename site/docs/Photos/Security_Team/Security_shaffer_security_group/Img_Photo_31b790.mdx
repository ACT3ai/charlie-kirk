## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a since-deleted post from the **[@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup)** account, timestamped 11:33 PM on 26 September 2025 with only 13 views at capture, shown alongside a Texas business registration record.

The post reads: "This is the security firm contracted by TPUSA for security when Charlie Kirk was killed. They want to remain in the shadows while my firm takes blame. SSG was not and had not been affiliated or contracted to do security for TPUSA since 2022!" The attached record shows **ROCKHOUSE INTEGRITY GROUP LLC**, Texas Tax ID 32089791597, incorporated 05/09/2023 as a Texas limited liability company, status Active, file number 0805052578, with **Brian Harpole** as registered agent and manager at a Lantana, TX address (street address withheld here — it is a private residence), governing agency the Texas Secretary of State.

That the post was deleted within hours is itself part of the record, which is why the screenshot was preserved and pinned to IPFS. The statement is one firm's account of which company held the contract; the registration details are a matter of public record and can be checked independently. Neither establishes wrongdoing by **Brian Harpole**, by Integrity Security Solutions or by anyone else named.

The full write-up is at [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group), with related pages at [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security) and [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole).
