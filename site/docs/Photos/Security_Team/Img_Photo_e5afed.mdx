## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X by ShafferSecurityGroup ([@ShafferGroup](/Influencers/x/shaffergroup)), dated September 27, 2025, with a collage of corporate records attached.

The post reads: "My firm was NOT in Utah on 9/10/2025. SSG has not been affiliated with TPUSA since 2022. The private security firm, hiding in the shadows, not taking responsibility for their actions is Rockhouse Integrity Group, dba: Integrity Security Solutions, (Open Source). #IntegrityMatters" The attached record states that **ROCKHOUSE INTEGRITY GROUP LLC**, Texas Tax ID 32089791597, was incorporated on 05/09/2023 as a Texas limited liability company, filing status In Existence, file number 0805052578, with the registered agent given as **Brian Harpole** at a Lantana, TX address (withheld here — it is a private residence) — also listed as the company's principal address. A HeadlineUSA.com watermark appears on part of the collage.

This is a first-party statement by one security firm about another, published two weeks after the shooting and later deleted. Its factual core — the corporate registration — is a public record and independently checkable. Its characterisation of the other firm is one party's view in what reporting has described as a longstanding business dispute between former colleagues, and it is reported here with attribution rather than adopted. No wrongdoing by **Brian Harpole**, by Integrity Security Solutions or by any other named person is established by this document.

See [Shaffer Security Group](/Security_Team/shaffer-security-group), [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security), [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole) and [Greg Shaffer](/Security_Team/greg-shaffer).
