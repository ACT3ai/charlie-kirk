## About This Cluster

Brian Harpole is a former police officer who founded Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC, operating as Integrity Security Solutions, the private firm reported to have provided Charlie Kirk's security on September 10. The single image in this cluster is not a photograph of him at the venue. It is a screenshot of an X post from an account called Project Constitution, presented here as an example of the online commentary that has attached to his name.

That post pairs a 2017 image with a 2025 one and speculates that Harpole was previously attached to a foreign leader's protective detail. This is unproven online speculation, not an established fact. The comparison photos have not been independently authenticated, and presence on or near a security team is not evidence of any wrongdoing. Some posts have questioned the security arrangements that day, but no such claim about Harpole has been substantiated.

For balance, Harpole has publicly disputed narratives of this kind, appearing on the Sean Ryan Show to respond, and has pursued a court case against commentator Candace Owens over statements made about him. That context is noted here only to show that the claims circulating online are contested and denied, not accepted.
