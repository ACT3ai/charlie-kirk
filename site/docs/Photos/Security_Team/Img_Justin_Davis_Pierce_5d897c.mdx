## What This Image Shows

A low-angle group photograph of four men standing in pairs on and around a large stone structure engraved "FREEDOM PLAZA." All four are smiling toward the camera. Bare branches and a pale sky behind them place it in late autumn or winter. The engraved words are the only text in frame.

The file is named for two people the collector believes are present, **Justin Davis** and Pierce Beck, both of whom have been described in reporting and social-media discussion as having worked in the same private-security circle as the firms connected to **TPUSA** protection. The photograph itself carries no caption identifying anyone, so the naming is the collector's, not the image's.

What this is, at most, is an association record: four men who know each other well enough to pose together. It is not dated, not located beyond the monument name, and shows nothing connected to **September 10, 2025**. All four are living private individuals and no allegation of any kind is made against any of them here.
