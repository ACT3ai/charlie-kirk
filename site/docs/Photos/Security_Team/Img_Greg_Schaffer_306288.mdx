## What This Image Shows

A square studio headshot: a smiling middle-aged man with dark, greying wavy hair, in a black blazer over a black t-shirt, against a plain grey background. There is no text on the image. It is a professional publicity portrait of the kind used on a company website or a speaker profile.

The picture is filed here as the reference likeness for **Greg Shaffer**, founder of **Shaffer Security Group**, described in reporting as a former FBI agent of around twenty years. Shaffer's relevance to the case is that his firm publicly stated it was not the security provider at **Utah Valley University** on **September 10, 2025**, and had not worked with **TPUSA** since 2022 — naming Rockhouse Integrity Group, trading as Integrity Security Solutions, instead.

A clean reference portrait matters in a case saturated with face-comparison graphics. When someone circulates a low-resolution crop and asserts a match, the sourced likeness is what makes the claim checkable. Shaffer is a living private citizen and nothing here alleges any wrongdoing by him.
