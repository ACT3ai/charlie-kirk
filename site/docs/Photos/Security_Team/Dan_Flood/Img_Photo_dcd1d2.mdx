## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of a Facebook post consisting of a single image: a circular embroidered military-style patch on a plain white background, with a blue field, a central five-pointed star, flanking olive-branch wreaths and a red-and-white striped bar at the base.

The interface text identifies the poster and position in an album: "18 of 30," the account name "Bob Flood," a date of "Jan 28, 2024," and the standard Share control.

This is one of a run of album images saved by a researcher going through the public posts of an account bearing the surname Flood. A shared surname is the only connection the file itself asserts, and the image contains nothing about [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), nothing about [**TPUSA**](/TPUSA/overview), and nothing about the security detail. The account holder is a private individual; no allegation of any kind is made against him here, and readers should not read a family resemblance in a name as a finding.
