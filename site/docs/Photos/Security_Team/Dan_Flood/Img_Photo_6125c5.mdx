## What This Image Shows

A vertical phone screenshot of a public Facebook profile belonging to an account named **Guy Hood**, showing the cover photo, profile picture, friend count and a Details section.

The readable text gives the bio, "I'm a veteran and patriot living in the beautiful Texas Hill Country and CEO of Good Hood Wood Inc," and lists: "Owner and CEO of Good Hood Wood at Tow, Texas"; "Worked at Central Intelligence Agency (CIA)"; "Worked at Ft. Bragg, N.C"; and "Former Infantry/Military Intelligence."

The self-described agency and military-intelligence background is why a researcher saved this page, since **CIA** involvement is one of the standing questions in this investigation. But the connection here is entirely indirect: this is a public profile of a private individual who appears in the comment threads of another account with the surname Flood. Employment history posted on a personal profile is unverified by definition, and nothing in it links this person to [**TPUSA**](/TPUSA/overview), to the security detail, or to anything that happened on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). No allegation is made against him.

For the sourced CIA material see [CIA and Intelligence](/Photos/CIA_Intelligence/overview) and [US Intelligence](/Killer/us-intelligence); for the cluster's framing see [Dan Flood](/Photos/Security_Team/Dan_Flood/overview).
