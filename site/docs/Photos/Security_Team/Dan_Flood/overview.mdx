## About This Cluster

**Dan Flood** was the head of security for [**TPUSA**](/TPUSA/overview) and, according to archived copies of the organisation's website, was listed as its Chief Asset Management Officer with responsibility for risk mitigation and executive protection as recently as September 1, 2025 — nine days before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview). He is a living private citizen who has not been charged with anything, and nothing on this page or its image pages asserts otherwise. He is of interest to researchers for the ordinary reason that the person responsible for protecting a principal is always of interest when the protection fails.

The images gathered here are almost entirely open social-media screenshots collected by researchers looking into Flood's family and professional circle rather than at Flood himself. They include Facebook posts by accounts using the names Bob Flood, Guy Hood and Tom Flood — military unit emblems including a US Army Special Forces insignia and a US Pacific Command crest, a profile listing prior work at Fort Bragg and at the Central Intelligence Agency, comment threads discussing US Army Military Intelligence branch symbolism, and a post about the Israel-Hamas conflict — plus a comment thread under a profile-picture update by an account named Rick Cutler. Readers should be clear about what this is and is not: it is an association map built from public profiles, and a shared surname, a service emblem or a comment on a friend's photograph establishes nothing about the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). The open question researchers pose is whether any of these relationships are relevant at all; that question has not been answered by anyone, and this cluster does not answer it.

Read these against the sourced written profile at [Dan Flood](/Security_Team/dan-flood) and the surrounding detail at [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview), including [Brian Harpole](/Security_Team/brian-harpole), [Bobby Harpole](/Security_Team/bobby-harpole) and [Integrity Security Solutions](/Security_Team/integrity-security). Third-party claims linking Flood to Israeli security work are collected, with attribution and without endorsement, at [lsraeI connection](/Photos/Security_Team/lsraeI_connection/overview) and on the parent page, [Security Team](/Photos/Security_Team/overview).
