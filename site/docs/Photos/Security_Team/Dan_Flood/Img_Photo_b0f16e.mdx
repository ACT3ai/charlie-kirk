## What This Image Shows

A social-media screenshot of a single graphic: the United States Army Special Forces distinctive unit insignia set on a 5th Special Forces Group flash, rendered in detail with metallic and embroidered textures, on a white background.

The interface text gives the album position "12 of 30," the account name "Bob Flood," a date of "Jun 10, 2024," and the Share control — the same album run as the service patch earlier in this cluster.

Unit insignia posted to a personal album is a common way for veterans and their families to mark service history, and that is all this image records. It is not a record of anyone's service, it is not connected on its face to [**TPUSA**](/TPUSA/overview) or to the security detail, and it says nothing about [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). No allegation attaches to the account holder, who is a private individual.
