## What This Image Shows

A phone screenshot of a Facebook comment overlay: a white rounded panel over a black background, with a sliver of the original post visible at the top and a position marker reading "26 of 60."

The comments, all timestamped two years old, read: **Guy Hood** — "They will be very busy soon"; **Bob Flood** replying to Guy Hood — "Yep."; **Al Thompson** — "PATRIOTS". Reaction and share counts are visible beside them.

The line "They will be very busy soon" is presumably what caused this thread to be saved. Read in isolation a fragment like that can seem loaded, but it sits under a post about a military command emblem, dates from around two years before the screenshot was taken, and has no stated subject. There is no basis whatever for reading it as foreknowledge of anything, and this site does not suggest that any of these three private individuals knew about or had any connection to the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
