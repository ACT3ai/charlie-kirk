The interface text reads "NORAD AND USNORTHCOM," the account name "Guy Hood," a date of "Mar 29," a position marker of "4 of 6," and the Share control. NORAD and US Northern Command are the joint commands for North American aerospace and homeland defence, and the reference appears to be to the polo shirt's insignia.

This is an ordinary personal social-media photograph, saved as part of a researcher's sweep through the public albums of accounts connected by comments and friendship to an account with the surname Flood.
