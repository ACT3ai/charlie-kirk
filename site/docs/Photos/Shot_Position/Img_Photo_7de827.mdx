## What This Image Shows

A view taken from within the crowd, over the heads of spectators in red and white caps, toward the low stage under the white and blue **Turning Point USA** canopy. One man is standing, another is seated at the table. Branding across the tent and the barricade skirts reads "PROVE ME WRONG," "THE AMERICAN COMEBACK TOUR," and the sponsor mark "YREFY.COM," repeated several times.

The frame is unannotated and carries no claim. Its value to this cluster is that it shows the target's position exactly as the audience saw it: the seat under the canopy, its height above the crowd, the depth of the tent, and how much of the stage the canopy roof cuts off from anything above.

That last point is why researchers keep returning to images from this angle. Any proposed elevated firing position has to have had a line to a person sitting under a solid canopy roof, and the canopy geometry visible here is what constrains that.
