## What This Image Shows

A slightly blurry, high-angle photograph taken from inside the building, shooting out through a large glass window at the crowd below. Two flags hang from the ceiling or window frame in the foreground, one of them a pride flag. Two red circles with white labels have been added, marking distant figures on rooftops: "Figure in white, Sorensen" and "Figure in black, Losee."

This is the frame that puts two people on two different roofs at the same moment. The official account concerns one roof only. If both labels are correct, then whatever the figure on the **Losee** building was doing, a second person in white was simultaneously on the **Sorensen** roof — which is either an innocent presence that should be identifiable and accounted for, or a second unexplained rooftop presence. The investigation's position is that both should be identified on the record; neither has been publicly.

The caution is obvious from the picture itself. Both figures are a few pixels across, seen through window glass at distance, in a compressed frame. The labels are the annotator's identification of which building is which, not a confirmation of what either figure is. No identity is claimed for either person here.
