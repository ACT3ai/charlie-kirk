## What This Image Shows

A slightly blurry, zoomed frame of the crowd behind the white pyramid canopy. The visible branding reads "THE AMERICAN COMEBACK" and "PROVE ME WRONG" — the standard livery of the tour stop. At the very top edge of the picture, centre-left, a thin hand-drawn red circle has been added around a small dark rectangular object sitting on an upper architectural level of the building behind.

The circle is unlabelled. The object is a handful of pixels across and could be a camera, a light, a vent cowl, a piece of railing hardware or an artefact of compression; the frame does not resolve it and no caption states a claim.

It is filed here, in the material named for the doorway behind [Charlie Kirk's](/People/charlie-kirk) seat, because that upper level is the one repeatedly marked in this cluster — the same band of the facade circled in [Object Circled on the Overhang](/Photos/Shot_Position/Img_Charlie_Tent_Door_614529) and examined in [Gun Port](/Photos/Shot_Position/Gun_Port/overview). Whether all these marks point at the same feature is one of the open questions this hierarchy exists to make checkable.
