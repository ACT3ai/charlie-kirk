## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical crop of a group of people standing behind a railing on a balcony of a modern building with flat grey cladding panels, viewed from a considerable distance. A black bar across the bottom carries the caption "WHENEVER YOU ZOOM," with a further fragment reading "WOOZ."

The caption is a sentence fragment cut off by the crop, and whatever claim it introduced is not present in the image. Without it, all that remains is a distant, degraded picture of people standing on a balcony watching something.

The file is kept in the Questionable area for that reason. Magnifying a compressed image past the point where detail survives produces shapes that the eye will readily organise into whatever the viewer has been told to expect, and material of this kind should be treated as illustrating that problem rather than as evidence of anything on **September 10, 2025**.
