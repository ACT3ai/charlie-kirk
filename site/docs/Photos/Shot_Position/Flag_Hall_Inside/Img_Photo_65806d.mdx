## What This Image Shows

An eye-level wide shot of the same concourse in active use: international flags hanging from the multi-coloured pipe-lined ceiling, a row of information booths set up along the glass window wall, representatives at the tables and students walking through in the daylight. Partial signage reading "MOUNTAIN AMERICA" is visible.

This is the space as it normally functions — a busy public thoroughfare, not a controlled or restricted area. That is directly relevant to any theory that treats the interior side of this glass as a plausible concealed position: the concourse is open, well used, and overlooked from inside as well as out.

The photograph is undated, unannotated and unconnected to the day of the event. It is included as architectural and occupancy reference.
