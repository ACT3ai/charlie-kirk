## What This Image Shows

A four-quadrant collage. On the left, [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) at top and [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) below. On the right, a security-style frame of a young man in a red shirt and a closer photograph of what the maker presents as the same man in a grey shirt. Two small yellow-bordered insets sit at the centre: a map of **Orem, Utah**, and a grainy security image of a walking silhouette circled in red. The only legible text is the fragment "FREEDOM."

The layout links the two commentators to the security material by adjacency alone. Owens has been one of the most prominent public voices questioning the official account, and her presence here reflects that role in the story rather than any claim about her.

No identification made in this graphic is adopted by this site. The young man in the security frames is not named, the walking silhouette is not identified, and no allegation is made against anyone shown.
