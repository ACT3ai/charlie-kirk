## What This Image Shows

A grid collage of three scenes. The left half is a stylised, plainly synthetic image of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) on a hospital gurney in a neck brace with a nurse beside him — an illustration, not a photograph, and there is no reason to treat it as documentation of anything. The upper right is a photograph of Kirk speaking at the outdoor rally. The lower right is a grainy security-style frame of a person running across a grassy lawn, circled in red.

The filename, Full Video From North, points at what matters here. The running figure is the same subject as the timing analysis elsewhere in this hierarchy, and the claim implicit in the file name is that a camera to the north captured the full run rather than the trimmed segment researchers say was released. If such a recording exists, it would settle the timing question outright.

That is the checkable part; the rest is packaging. Nobody in the running frame is identified, and no allegation is made against the person shown.
