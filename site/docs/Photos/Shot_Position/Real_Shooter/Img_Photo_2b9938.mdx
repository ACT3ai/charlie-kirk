## What This Image Shows

A seven-segment collage laid out like a physical case board: three portraits, security-camera frames showing two men, an indoor event setting, and a street map. Yellow borders and a red annotation circle tie the segments together. The scattered text is largely unreadable, but the word "Orem" is legible on the map segment, along with fragments of distance or scale markings such as "17 mm" and "12 man."

**Orem** is the Utah city where [**Utah Valley University**](/UVU/overview) sits, so the map anchors the graphic to the real geography of the case. Beyond that anchor, nothing on the frame is sourced: no segment is dated or credited, none of the three people is named, and the security frames are not attributed to any camera or agency.

The site records the graphic without adopting it. No person shown here has been identified by this investigation, and no claim is made that anyone depicted was involved in the events of [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
