## About This Cluster

A large body of social-media graphics has grown up around the proposition that the person who fired on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) was not the man the state has charged. This cluster archives sixteen of those graphics as artefacts of the public conversation. Nothing here identifies anyone, and this site does not adopt any identification made inside these images: [**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) has not been tried, no alternative shooter has been named by any court or agency, and every person appearing in these collages is entitled to the presumption of innocence. What the cluster documents is what was circulated, by whom, and on what visual basis.

The graphics share a house style and, read together, a set of recurring claims — all of them the posters' own. They are multi-panel collages built from event photographs, low-resolution security-style frames, portraits and screen grabs, stitched together with red circles, yellow borders and arrows drawn to imply a relationship between panels. Captions in the set include "NEW FOOTAGE CHALLENGES LONE GUNMAN NARRATIVE" and a claim that a guard "freezes 42 seconds." One graphic draws a comparison to the July 13, 2024 rooftop shooting in Butler, Pennsylvania. Several are visibly digital composites or AI-generated renderings rather than photographs — a sniper figure on a roof, a stylised hospital scene — and the descriptions on the individual pages say so where that is the case. The evidentiary weight of an arrow drawn between two faces is zero; the sociological weight of thousands of shares is not, which is why the material is kept and labelled rather than deleted.

Read each image page for what is actually visible in it before accepting any caption. The site's own reasoning about who was and was not in a position to fire is set out at [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) and [Real Killer](/Killer/overview), with the specific identification gap covered at [Stairs Guy Identity Gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap) and the enhanced-image question at [Enhanced Suspect Photos](/CoverUp/Enhanced_Suspect_Photos). For the physical geometry these graphics assert, go back to [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview); for the charged defendant's own page see [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview).
