## What This Image Shows

A four-quadrant collage in true-crime style: a man in an orange prison jumpsuit held by a police officer, a mugshot-style portrait of another young man, a young woman signing a document at a desk, and a circular inset at the centre highlighting a scoped rifle. The only legible text fragments are garbled — "DEPANTVENT" and "COOT ESTONG."

Those mangled words are the tell. Together with the airbrushed skin textures and the too-smooth uniform detailing, they mark this as AI-generated or heavily manipulated imagery rather than photography. Nothing in it is a record of a real moment: not the arrest scene, not the mugshot, not the document signing.

The image is kept as documentation of the synthetic material circulating around this case, and it should never be cited as proof. Nobody depicted here corresponds to a verified person, and no allegation attaches to any real individual from it.
