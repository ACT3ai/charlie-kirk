## What This Image Shows

A four-quadrant social-media graphic that sets the **July 13, 2024** rooftop shooting at a rally in **Butler, Pennsylvania** beside the **September 10, 2025** event at **Utah Valley University**. The panels show the building the Butler shooter used, a photograph of Charlie Kirk at the outdoor event, a close-up of a tactical marksman, and a portrait of Kirk speaking. No text caption states the argument; the comparison is made by layout.

The implied point is about pattern: two political events, two low rooftops within range, two failures to cover an obvious elevated position. As a question about protective advance work that is a legitimate one, and it is asked in the site's own words at [No Counter-Snipers](/Security_Team/no-counter-snipers) and [Perimeter and Rooftop Failures](/Security_Team/perimeter-rooftop-failures).

What the graphic does not establish is any connection between the two incidents beyond the visual analogy. Similar building profiles are not evidence of a common cause, and nobody is identified or accused anywhere on the frame.
