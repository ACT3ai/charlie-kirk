## What This Image Shows

This is a digital image featuring a crop of a social media post asking "Who is this guy?". The image shows a close-up, slightly pixelated view of a modern building facade with two distinct levels: a lower level where a crowd of people is gathered behind a dark wall, and an upper balcony level carrying an indistinct shape beside a potted plant, which the post reads as a figure standing partly concealed.

The reading is the poster's. At this crop the shape does not resolve into a person, and the site makes no identification from it and no suggestion that anyone on that balcony was doing anything other than watching the event. The same balcony claim, and why the imagery does not support it, is set out at [Forum Post Claiming a Figure Behind a Plant](/Photos/Shot_Position/Questionable/Img_Photo_f20a0b).
