## What This Image Shows

A wide, high-angle photograph of the terraced grassy plaza at **Utah Valley University** with students scattered across the lawns and a denser group gathered around the white promotional tent marked "PROVE ME WRONG." The brutalist buildings behind carry graphic window wraps, and campus signage reading "A PLACE FOR YOU" and "UVU" is visible.

Compared with the packed frames elsewhere in this hierarchy, the crowd here is thin and spread out, which suggests a point earlier in the event before it filled. That makes the picture unusually useful: with fewer people in the way, the ground itself is visible — the terrace edges, the paths, the position of the tent relative to the buildings.

Nothing is annotated and no claim is made on the frame. It is a clean reference view of the site as it was set up on the day.
