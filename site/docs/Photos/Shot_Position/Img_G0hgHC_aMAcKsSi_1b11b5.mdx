## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution vertical crop of the upper tiers of the concrete building against a pale, washed-out sky: beige stucco, dark window panes and reddish-brown rectangular panels in a stepped, geometric arrangement. On the highest roofline there is a small dark silhouette.

The image carries no text, no arrow and no caption, which is unusual for this part of the corpus and is the reason it is worth reading carefully. Nothing has been asserted about the silhouette. It may be a bird, a vent, an antenna, a parapet fitting or a person; at this resolution the shape cannot be resolved, and honest description stops there.

Frames like this are how the rooftop thread began — someone noticed a shape on a roofline and asked what it was. That is a legitimate question and it deserves a checkable answer rather than an assumption in either direction. What would settle it is the original full-resolution source frame with its timestamp, which has not been published alongside this crop.
