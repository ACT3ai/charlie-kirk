## What This Image Shows

A heavily cropped, low-resolution frame consisting mostly of pale blue sky, with a blurred mountain and distant trees along the lower-left horizon and the flat roofline of a building at the lower right. Two elements have been added digitally: a pixelated timestamp reading "12:06pm" and a thick red arrow running from the timestamp down toward the lower right.

The file is kept under the name Guy 2, and the frame comes from the roof material rather than the plaza material — the arrow points at the roof edge, not at the crowd. What the arrow is meant to indicate is not stated, and at this crop nothing on that roofline is resolvable.

The timestamp is the substance. If the source video is authentic and its clock is right, 12:06pm places this roughly seventeen minutes before [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was hit — inside the window in which researchers have asked who had access to the roofs and when. Both conditions are unverified here: the clock is an overlay, and no source is credited on the frame.
