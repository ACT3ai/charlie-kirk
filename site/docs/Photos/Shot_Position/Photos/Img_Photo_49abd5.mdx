## What This Image Shows

A close, high-angle walkthrough still of the brutalist facade, framed on a single large window that mirrors the landscaped courtyard and concrete pathways below. The presenter's inset is in the top right, with the metallic "SOURCE THE TRUTH" overlay at lower left.

What a window reflects tells you what that window overlooks, and this is a more precise instrument than it first appears: the reflection shows the specific stretch of ground visible from this pane, which is exactly the question that separates a viable elevated position from an impossible one.

It also raises the recurring camera question. Glazing that mirrors the plaza is glazing that a camera behind it could have shot through, which connects to the withheld-footage material elsewhere on the site.
