## About This Cluster

Almost a month after the shooting, an independent researcher walked the [**UVU**](/UVU/overview) courtyard on camera and narrated what he was looking at. This cluster is a run of eight stills pulled from that broadcast, all captured within about five minutes of each other on **6 October 2025**, and all carrying the same picture-in-picture layout: the walker's phone camera filling the frame, a commentator inset in a corner, and a gold "SOURCE THE TRUTH" overlay in the lower left. The value of a walkthrough is that it is continuous — it shows the relationship between one vantage point and the next in a way that isolated annotated crops never can.

What the sequence records is the site in its post-event state rather than as it was on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). The frames move across the brutalist concrete faces and their full-height reflective glass, down the terraced lawn and concrete pathways, past an American flag hung vertically from the building, and across an area still closed off with metal barricades and yellow caution tape. Because the glazing reflects the courtyard back at the camera, several of these stills capture both the building face and the plaza in the same frame, which is useful for checking what any given window actually overlooks. No claim is asserted on the images themselves beyond the presenter's running commentary, and no timestamp from the day of the event appears in them.

Walk these in order first to build a mental model of the space, then take that model back to the annotated overlays on [The Shot and the Shooter Position](/Photos/Shot_Position/overview) and to the specific openings circled in [Gun Port](/Photos/Shot_Position/Gun_Port/overview). Undated clean views of the same plaza are in [Other](/Photos/Shot_Position/Other/overview); the site's discussion of ground alteration after the event is at [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).
