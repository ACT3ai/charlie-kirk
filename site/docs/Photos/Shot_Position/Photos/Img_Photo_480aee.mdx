## What This Image Shows

A broadcast screenshot: the presenter recording himself in first-person on the paved plaza, clean modernist architecture behind him, clear blue sky. The studio host is inset in the upper left, with the "SOURCE THE TRUTH" overlay partly visible at the bottom.

The value of a frame like this is that it shows the paving itself, close and unobstructed. The condition and extent of the hard surfacing around the tent position is a live question in this investigation, because researchers have documented paving work carried out in the days after **September 10, 2025** and asked what ground was covered over in the process.

The still makes no claim about that. It is one frame of a continuous walk across the plaza, roughly four weeks after the event.
