## What This Image Shows

A video screenshot of a man filming himself outdoors on the campus, with a small inset in the upper left showing another man speaking from an indoor setting, and a social-media watermark in the lower left. The visible handle fragment reads @jg_cstt. Bright direct sunlight; contemporary campus architecture behind him.

This is the frame that identifies the format of the whole sequence: someone physically walking the site with a phone, in conversation with a studio host who is watching along. That structure matters for evaluating the other stills — the camera path is continuous and narrated, so the relationship between viewpoints can be followed rather than guessed.

It also marks the limits of the material. This is one citizen's tour of a public plaza roughly four weeks after **September 10, 2025**, not a controlled forensic survey, and nothing on the frame is dated or measured.
