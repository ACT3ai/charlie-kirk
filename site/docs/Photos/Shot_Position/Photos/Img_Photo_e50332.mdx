## What This Image Shows

A wide, eye-level walkthrough still of the plaza: the concrete amphitheatre, the heavily glazed building behind it, an American flag hanging from a concrete overpass at the right, and the presenter's inset in the upper left. The overlay text reads "SOURCE THE TRUTH."

The barricades and yellow caution tape across the main area are the point. Roughly four weeks after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), part of the plaza was still closed to the public. That is a plain, dateable observation, and it bears directly on the site-alteration questions the investigation has raised — what was done to this ground after the event, when, and under whose direction.

The still asserts nothing beyond what it shows. It does not establish why the area was closed or what work, if any, was carried out inside the taped zone.
