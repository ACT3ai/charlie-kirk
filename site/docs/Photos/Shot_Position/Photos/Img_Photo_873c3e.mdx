## What This Image Shows

A walkthrough video frame of a building entrance: brutalist concrete, large dark-framed reflective windows, and in those windows the mirrored image of the sunlit courtyard opposite with its grass panels and concrete paths. The presenter's inset sits in the upper right, and the gold overlay in the lower left reads "SOURCE THE TRUTH."

Entrances matter to this cluster for two separate reasons. They are the points at which someone enters or leaves a building, which is the concern behind the frame captioned "EASY ESCAPE" in the parent cluster; and the glass above and beside them reflects the plaza, which is what makes these facades useful for checking sight lines.

The still carries no annotation and asserts nothing. It records the entrance as it stood roughly four weeks after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
