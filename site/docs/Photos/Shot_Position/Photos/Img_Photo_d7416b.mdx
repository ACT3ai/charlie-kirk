## What This Image Shows

The last of the eight walkthrough stills: a wide view of the brutalist concrete entrance with large glass windows, here reflecting blue sky and white cloud rather than the courtyard. The studio host is inset in the upper right and the "SOURCE THE TRUTH" overlay is partly visible.

The difference between this frame and the others in the run is instructive. Reflections depend entirely on the camera's angle to the glass, so the same window can mirror the plaza from one position and the empty sky from another. Anyone reasoning from a reflection has to know where the camera stood, which is precisely why a continuous walkthrough is more useful than isolated crops.

Nothing is annotated or claimed on the frame. It records the entrance as it stood roughly four weeks after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).
