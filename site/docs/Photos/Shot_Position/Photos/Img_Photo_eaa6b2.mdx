## What This Image Shows

A screen capture from an online broadcast in picture-in-picture layout. The main frame is an exterior view of the building's large glass facade, reflecting the plaza and green lawns behind the camera; a small inset in the top right shows the presenter speaking from an indoor setting. The gold overlay in the lower area reads "SOURCE THE TRUTH."

The reflection is the useful content. Because the glazing mirrors the courtyard, this single frame captures both the building elevation and the ground opposite it, which lets a viewer confirm what faces what without needing a second photograph.

The frame is one of eight stills from a site walkthrough recorded on **6 October 2025**, roughly four weeks after the shooting. It shows the plaza in its later state and carries no annotation or claim of its own.
