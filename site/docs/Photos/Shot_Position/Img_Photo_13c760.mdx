## What This Image Shows

A two-panel composite. Both panels show the same building roof and landscaped courtyard from overhead, at different angles and zoom levels, with arrows and circles added to point at one spot. The caption "SHOOTER WAS HERE" appears on both panels, marking the same position at wide and close range.

Other text on the frame identifies the underlying material: a Getty Images watermark with the credit "Chet Strange" and asset number 2235088153, plus a video timeline reading 1:30 of 1:33. So the graphic is built from licensed press imagery and a short video, re-annotated by whoever made it. The phrase "SHOOTER WAS HERE" is that maker's assertion; no agency or court has published a diagram in these terms, and this site does not adopt the placement.

Its usefulness is as a fixed reference point for the argument. Because the marked spot is stated plainly, it can be checked against the angle work in the Justin Ruh diagrams, against the roof-access frames, and against the runner-timing calculation — all of which either support or contradict this exact position. Naming a location precisely is what makes it falsifiable.
