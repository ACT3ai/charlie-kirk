## What This Image Shows

A medium-wide outdoor frame. On the left, several young people stand on a metal balcony looking toward the concrete structure on the right, which carries a prominent metallic exhaust duct system. A hand-drawn black oval has been added around the central wall detail, focusing on a square metallic vent cap. No caption text.

The vent cap is a piece of building services plant, and the honest reading of the picture is that a researcher circled a mechanical fitting and asked what it was. That is a reasonable first step; it is not a finding. Nothing in the frame indicates that the cap opens, that anything is behind it, or that it has any connection to [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

The onlookers on the balcony are worth noticing too. They are in the open, close to the circled fitting, watching the event — which is a constraint on any theory that treats that part of the structure as concealed.
