## What This Image Shows

A high-angle photograph of the quad at full capacity: hundreds of young adults packed across the lower courtyard, with more filling the elevated walkway, the stairwell and the balcony behind them. Bright direct sun, no annotation, no text.

The file name records its function. This is the uncropped frame from which the tighter details in this cluster are taken, and it is filed here so the crops can be traced back to their source. That matters more in this sub-cluster than anywhere else in the hierarchy, because everything in the gun-port argument depends on heavy magnification of a small area — and a crop with no parent frame cannot be checked at all.

The same image also appears in the parent cluster as a general establishing view. Read together, the two uses show the difference clearly: the wide frame is unambiguous, the crops are where interpretation begins.
