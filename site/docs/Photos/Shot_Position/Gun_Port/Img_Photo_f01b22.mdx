## What This Image Shows

A heavily magnified, low-resolution crop of the concrete facade, with a thick green circle drawn over a run of narrow rectangular openings. A person stands on a balcony at the left of the background. Inside one of the circled openings there is a small indistinct yellowish shape. There is no caption and no text.

Whether that yellowish shape is an object inside the opening, a reflection, a patch of interior light or a compression artefact cannot be determined at this magnification, and the maker does not say what he believes it is. Blowing up a small region of an already-compressed photograph introduces exactly this kind of ambiguity, which is why claims built on magnified crops need the parent frame and, ideally, the original file.

The reason the openings themselves are of interest is set out on the cluster page: researchers have asked whether any recess in these facades could conceal a muzzle. That remains a question, not a finding, and nothing in this frame answers it.
