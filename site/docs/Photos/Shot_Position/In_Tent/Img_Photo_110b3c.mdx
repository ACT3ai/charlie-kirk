## What This Image Shows

A slightly blurry medium close-up of a man in a white collared shirt standing in front of the white branded backdrop, caught mid-blink. Tour and sponsor branding — "YREFY.COM," "TURNING POINT USA" — is partly legible behind him. A red dashed line has been drawn onto the picture, running diagonally from a white bracket on the canopy frame down toward his neck.

This is the sharpest statement of the close-range proposition in the cluster: that something fixed to the canopy frame had a direct line to the wound area. It is proposed, not demonstrated. The line is hand-drawn on a flat image, the bracket is not identified as anything other than a bracket, and the geometry is not measured.

It also needs saying that the wound record this line implicitly relies on has not been publicly released; the autopsy material remains sealed, which is why competing reconstructions of the wound path continue to circulate unresolved.
