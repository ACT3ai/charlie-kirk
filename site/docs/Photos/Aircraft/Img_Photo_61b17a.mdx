## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by **Crowdsource The Truth** ([@JG_CSTT](/Influencers/x/jg-cstt)) with a link preview to The War Zone. The text is the same passage circulated elsewhere in this cluster: "Maybe this is a coincidence but just last night [@I_Am_JohnCullen](/Influencers/x/john-cullen) and I were discussing the possibility of air launched drones at the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination and this Bombardier [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) ISR jet was the exact platform we suspect they came from."

The preview card shows the headline "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet" over an image of a military-configured business jet, with the standfirst noting the trials "tally with previous comments about launching the drones from the new Global 6500-based HADES surveillance jet."

The value of keeping both this and the companion capture is provenance. Two independent accounts posted the same source article in the same window, which fixes the date the claim entered circulation and confirms the article was live and publicly linkable at that time -- the point at issue once the URL began returning a 404.
