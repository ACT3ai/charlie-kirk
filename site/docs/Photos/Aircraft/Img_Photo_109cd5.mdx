## What This Image Shows

The Flightradar24 aircraft record for **N1098L**. The header reads "Flight history for aircraft - N1098L," with AIRCRAFT Bombardier Global 6500, AIRLINE Private owner, TYPE CODE GLEX, MODE S **A0299E**, SERIAL NUMBER (MSN) **60098**, and AGE 2 years with a build date of Feb 2023. The flight-history table below is empty of rows in this capture.

This is the identity card for the aircraft the entire spy-plane thread rests on. The Mode S hex code A0299E and serial 60098 are the two fields that let researchers confirm they are all discussing the same airframe across different tracking services, and serial 60098 is the same number that appears on the AeroAmerica sales brochure preserved elsewhere in this cluster.

One detail worth noting: the record lists the operator as "Private owner" rather than naming a company. Public reporting associates the aircraft with **LASAI Aviation II LLC**, described as an aviation arm of the defense contractor **Leidos**, which is the corporate research documented in the [Nwq](/Photos/Aircraft/Nwq/overview) sub-cluster.
