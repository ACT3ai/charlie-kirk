## What This Image Shows

A split-screen still from a video broadcast: a speaker at a laptop with a microphone on the left, an annotated aerial street map of downtown **Wilmington, Delaware** on the right.

The map labels read, among others: "500 Delaware Ave - FBI Suite #300 - MG+M Law Firm," "919 N Market St - Brigitte Macron's Lawyers - Stacy Sheridan's Registered Agent," "Wilmington Trust founded by T. Coleman du Pont," "100 N King St - Bracebridge Complex - Incyte HQ," and "**920 N King St** - Rodney Square - DEA, FBI, and USSS."

Wilmington enters this investigation because it is the reported destination of the Egyptian jet **SU-BTT** on **September 10, 2025**, and the annotations here are an attempt to show what sits within a few blocks of that arrival. Some of the labels are verifiable street facts -- building addresses and tenants -- while others assert relationships between people and addresses that this site has not confirmed. Treat the annotations as the broadcaster's research notes rather than as established connections, and note that a federal office being near an airport-bound destination establishes proximity and nothing more.
