## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by [**John Cullen**](/Influencers/x/john-cullen) ([@I_Am_JohnCullen](/Influencers/x/john-cullen)) with a four-panel crop of an aircraft sales listing attached. The post reads: "Did you know the HADES spy plane we flying around the scene of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s murder is for sale? When the CIA can't fit all their crap in a plane, they sell it, and get a bigger plane.. $50 million.. Should we set up a GoFundMe? Or, should @elonmusk buy it..."

The listing panels show a **2023 BOMBARDIER GLOBAL 6500**, "Call for price," "Aircraft Location: Wilmington, Delaware," seller AeroAmerica Group, with an operating-costs tab and a financing link. This is the same listing captured in the companion image on [Photo 2](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_6f2a4f).

The CIA attribution in the post is the poster's assertion; the aircraft researchers have tied to the Utah flights, N1098L, is publicly associated with **LASAI Aviation II LLC** and US Army work rather than with any intelligence agency, and the operator has not commented on this site's record. What the listing itself shows is that a special-mission-configured Global 6500 was on the open market in Wilmington.
