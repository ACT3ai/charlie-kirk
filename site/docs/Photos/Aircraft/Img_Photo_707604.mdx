## What This Image Shows

An infographic timeline running from **January 2023 to September 2025**, with parallel tracks labelled **Egypt - SU-BTT**, **Egypt - SU-BND**, **Turning Point Event**, **Other Events** and **Flight Overlaps**.

Visible entries pair aircraft movements with organizational milestones: St. Louis, MO arrivals and departures in early 2023 against "TPUSA Faith kicks off Tour headed by Sean Feucht" and "Rob McCoy Hosts 1st TPUSA"; Provo / SLC, Utah arrivals in April 2025 against "Charlie at Salt Lake City University"; Omaha, Nebraska arrivals in July and August 2025 against "Mikey McCoy promoted to Chief"; and a **9/4** arrival at the end of the sequence.

This is the correlation argument laid out visually, and it is the strongest form the Egyptian thread takes -- not "a plane was there" but "these arrivals track these events across three years." It is also where the greatest care is needed. Correlation charts are easy to build and hard to falsify unless someone counts the misses as well as the hits, and the audit sheets elsewhere in this cluster show that several of the flight entries in circulation were duplicates or wrong. A chart is only as good as the rows underneath it.
