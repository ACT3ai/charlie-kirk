## What This Image Shows

A wide outdoor shot of a large gathering beside a modern concrete building under bright, partly clouded sky. People line an elevated concrete walkway behind a railing, several holding signs and a flag, while a second dense crowd stands on a sloped hillside below.

The signs and flag visible here indicate a demonstration rather than a speaking event, which suggests this frame and its companions in the same group document a different gathering from the [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) event -- possibly a counter-protest, possibly an unrelated campus occasion.

It is preserved because it was filed with the drone research, and because crowd-position frames are the raw material for sightline questions regardless of which day they show. But nothing in the image dates it or ties it to the assassination, and it should not be read as doing so.

For the documented crowd and vantage-point material, see [Security](/security_law_enforcement/overview), [Cameras](/cameras/overview) and [Photos](/Photos/overview).
