## What This Image Shows

A mobile capture of a document viewer showing a tabular roster. The organization column lists repeated entries for **Pilot Makers**, **Provo Fire Department**, **Simco Electric**, **Signature Flight**, **Signature Flight/Hangar Tenant**, **Smith Detection** and **Stone Security**, each paired with individual surnames and given names and a set of checkmark columns.

This reads as an airport access, badging or training roster for [**Provo Municipal Airport**](/Locations/Provo_Airport) -- the field where the Egyptian aircraft parked, where **[N888KG](/Planes/N888KG)** departed from, and where the **[N59906](/Planes/N59906)** survey flight originated. Documents of this kind list who is authorized to be on the ramp and in the hangars.

The individuals named are private people doing ordinary jobs -- flight training, fire and rescue, electrical contracting, ground handling, security screening and detection equipment. Their names are not reproduced here, and nothing about appearing on an airport roster implies any involvement in or knowledge of the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). The document is relevant only as a record of which organizations operate at the field.
