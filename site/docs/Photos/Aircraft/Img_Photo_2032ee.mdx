## What This Image Shows

A close-up capture of a printed page from the 2019 Romanian complaint, containing the writer's account of workplace conduct and pressure she says she experienced in her employment.

One of nine pages from a single foreign legal document filed in this folder by mistake. It has no connection to the Charlie Kirk investigation, no overlap with any aircraft, person or date in the case, and no evidentiary relevance to anything on this site.

The page makes serious allegations about identified individuals that have not been proven in any proceeding on this record, and neither the names nor the allegations are reproduced here.
