## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a public social-media post from the owner of **N888KG**, framed by an olive-green border.

The post reads: "Shelaine and I join our community, our state and our nation in mourning the death of Charlie Kirk and we pray for his family and loved ones during this difficult and tragic time. His death and the way it occurred was both horrifying and senseless. We pray that the extensive efforts by law enforcement to locate Mr. Kirk's killer will be swift and successful. As often happens, unfortunately, in the wake of such terrifying and public events, a variety of baseless theories and suspicions around Mr. Kirk's murder immediatel..."

This is the companion to the detailed flight statement, and it belongs in the record for a straightforward reason: when private people are named in circulating theories, their response is part of the evidence and not an afterthought. The owner describes the theories about his aircraft as baseless and expresses condolences.

No authority has alleged any wrongdoing in connection with this aircraft or its owners, no evidence has been produced connecting the flight to the events at [UVU](/UVU/overview), and this site does not suggest otherwise. The unanswered questions in this thread concern tracking data and flight-plan records, not the conduct of any person.
