## What This Image Shows

A scanned page of a printed English-language legal complaint dated **20 November 2019**, addressed to Romanian prosecutors and concerning a case file from that country.

This page belongs to a nine-page document filed in the same folder as the aircraft screenshots. It is part of an entirely separate matter -- a Romanian legal complaint from 2019 -- and has no established connection to the events of September 10, 2025, to any aircraft in this investigation, or to any person in this case. It appears to have been imported into the folder alongside unrelated research.

The document contains serious criminal allegations against named individuals, none of which have been adjudicated in any court on this record. This site does not reproduce those allegations or those names. Publishing untested accusations about identifiable people is not something this investigation does, and the page is described rather than transcribed for that reason.
