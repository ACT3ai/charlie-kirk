## About This Cluster

Aircraft tracking is one of the deepest threads in the Charlie Kirk investigation, and this cluster gathers the screenshots that document it. The images cover the **N1098L** surveillance jet tied to the Army's **HADES** program, the Egyptian-registered jets led by **SU-BTT**, the private jet **N888KG** that left the Provo area after the shooting, the MARC Inc. survey plane **N59906**, and the small drones researchers report seeing on video near the stage. Nearly all of them are flight-tracker captures, FAA registry lookups, and X posts by independent researchers working from public ADS-B data.

The recurring pattern, according to those researchers, is aircraft behaving unusually around **September 10, 2025**: N1098L reportedly descended to a few hundred feet near stall speed over Utah Lake both before and after the 12:23 PM shooting, SU-BTT departed Provo the morning of the attack after arriving September 4, and N888KG's transponder reportedly went dark mid-flight that evening. Flight logs shared by **Candace Owens** and others claim the Egyptian jets crossed paths with TPUSA event cities for years. These remain reported claims built on public tracking data, and the core questions — who flew, who authorized, and why — are unresolved.

Start with the [N1098L NCSS CIA](/Photos/Aircraft/N1098L_NCSS_CIA/overview) sub-cluster for the disputed crew identifications, then [Trip History](/Photos/Aircraft/Trip_History/overview) and [5 Planes](/Photos/Aircraft/5_Planes/overview) for the Egyptian flight logs. For the written analysis behind these images, see [Planes](/Planes/overview), the [N1098L Spy Plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview) page, and the [Drones](/Drones/overview) section.
