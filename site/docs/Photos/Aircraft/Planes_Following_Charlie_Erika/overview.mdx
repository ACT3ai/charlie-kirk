## About This Cluster

These four screenshots capture both sides of the claim that aircraft tracked [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)'s movements. One is a post by [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) ([@RealCandaceO](/Influencers/podcasts-candace-owens)), reposted by "Project Constitution," describing an alleged flight path running France to Provo to Wilmington and back to Egypt. Another is a flight-data spreadsheet mapping dates, routes and events against the names "Charlie" and "Erika." The remaining images are a rebuttal thread by a user posting as "Adam" ([@bayonerd09](/Influencers/x/bayonerd09)).

Keeping the claim and the counter-argument together is the point of this cluster. The rebuttal presents color-coded spreadsheets arguing that most of the alleged tailing flights either did not exist or occurred while the aircraft in question was on a different continent - the same debunking result seen across the Egyptian-flight threads. Readers can compare the original assertion against the data check side by side.

None of these images establishes as fact that any aircraft was following anyone. They are competing interpretations of public flight-tracking records, reported here with attribution to the people who posted them.
