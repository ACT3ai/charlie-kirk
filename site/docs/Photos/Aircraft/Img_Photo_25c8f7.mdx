## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a research-tracking interface, each person rendered as a white card carrying a name, a role label and a status marker. Visible roles include Wife, CEO and Chair of the Board; Security Guard; Guard/Tech; Q and A Questioner; Person of Interest; Mentors; Executive Producer/Co-host; Founder and Board Member; [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) Ally; COO and Board Member; Board Member; and Student Speaker.

This is an investigator's working index, not a finding. The labels are categories somebody assigned while organizing research -- who spoke, who worked security, who sat on the board -- and appearing in such a list carries no implication of wrongdoing by anyone named. The status markers track whether a person has been interviewed or accounted for in public reporting, not any legal status.

The screenshot is filed in the aircraft folder rather than with the people material, which appears to be a filing accident. The site's maintained index of individuals is the [People](/People/overview) section, which applies attribution rules and states each person's status.

For the aircraft material this cluster is actually about, see [Planes](/Planes/overview).
