## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a mobile **NPR** article page. The section is Middle East conflict, the headline reads "**Eyewitnesses in Gaza say Israel is using sniper drones to shoot Palestinians**," dated **NOVEMBER 26, 2024, 11:48 AM ET**, bylined **Kat Lonsdorf**.

This is filed with the aircraft and drone material because of a specific claim raised in the investigation: that small armed quadcopters -- commercial airframes modified to carry a rifle or a small munition -- are an existing, documented capability rather than a hypothetical one. The NPR report is cited as third-party evidence that such systems have been described in use, with the headline itself framing the account as eyewitness testimony rather than confirmed fact.

The reach from there to Utah is long and should be stated as such. Nothing in this article concerns **September 10, 2025**, Utah Valley University, or any aircraft in this investigation. It establishes that a capability has been reported to exist in a war zone. It does not establish that any such system was present in Orem, and no evidence has been produced that one was.
