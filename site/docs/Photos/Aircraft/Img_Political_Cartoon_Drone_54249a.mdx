## What This Image Shows

A black-and-white hand-drawn political cartoon, signed [@PolitickleAi](/Influencers/x/politickleai). A man at a podium on a campus lawn throws his arms up at a quadcopter hovering overhead while a dense crowd looks on in surprise. The caption reads: "NEXT, IT'LL DELIVER THE TRUTH!"

This is commentary, not proof. It is filed in the aircraft cluster because it is part of how the drone question moved from flight data into public argument -- once researchers started posting tracker screenshots, the drone-over-the-rally image became a shorthand that cartoonists and meme accounts picked up.

It is worth keeping distinct from the material it references. Nothing in a cartoon establishes that a drone was present at [UVU](/UVU/overview) on **September 10, 2025**. The competing accounts of that question -- [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and UVU initially saying no drones flew, against video frames and [**Frank Turek**](/People/frank-turek)'s statement that a TPUSA drone was up -- are documented at [UVU Security No Drones](/Drones/UVU_Security_No_Drones), [TPUSA Production Drone](/Drones/TPUSA_Production_Drone) and [Military Drones On Video](/Drones/Military_Drones_On_Video).
