## What This Image Shows

A high-angle daytime aerial of the campus courtyard, the crowd gathered in structured curved rows on the central lawn and spilling onto the surrounding walkways between large geometric concrete buildings. Bright direct sunlight. Watermarked **@universehop00**.

What this frame adds to the others in the set is the perimeter. It shows how the walkways feed into the courtyard and where the crowd thins toward the edges -- the routes by which people entered and, in the minutes after the shot, left. Movement questions in this case have mostly been argued from ground-level phone video, where the geometry is impossible to reconstruct.

The origin claim for the set -- leaked imagery from the **N59906** contractor overflight -- has not been verified, and nothing in this frame dates or attributes it. Use it for geometry, not as a documented product of that flight.
