## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a foundation website's board-of-advisors page. The entry reads: "Ambassador / [**Walid Mahmoud Abdel Nasser**](/People/walid-mahmoud), Member of the Board of advisory. Dr. Abdelnasser currently serves as Director of the Regional Bureau for Arab Countries at the **World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO)** in Geneva. As a career Egyptian diplomat since 1984, Dr. Abdelnasser served as a member of the Cabinet of the First Under-Secretary of the Foreign Ministry and t..." The site navigation shows Home, About, Boutros Boutros Ghali, Newsroom, Events, Founders and Members, Publications and Executive Director.

This is a published professional biography from an organization's own website -- an entirely public record of a long diplomatic career, including postings in Geneva and Washington and a current international civil service role.

The reason it sits in the aircraft folder is a speculative claim circulating online that this diplomat might have been a passenger on the Egyptian jet **SU-BTT**. That claim rests on no manifest, no flight record, no witness, and no official statement, and nothing on this site should be read as asserting it. The individual is a living person with a documented public career and no established connection to the events of September 10, 2025.
