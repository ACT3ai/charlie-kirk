## About This Cluster

The single image here is a screenshot of a social media post from an account called "Project Constitution," reproduced so readers can see exactly what was circulated rather than a paraphrase of it. The post alleges that Utah Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) held back-to-back closed-door meetings with representatives of Egypt and France on September 23, 2025 - thirteen days after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was killed at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) - and pairs that claim with a cropped image of a daily schedule marked with highlighter and arrows. A second post visible below it advances a separate theory tying French President [Emmanuel Macron](/People/emmanuel-macron) and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) together.

These are the poster's assertions, not established facts. The word "(Claims)" in the cluster title is deliberate: the alleged meetings are sourced to an anonymous account's reading of an online calendar, and no independent confirmation, official statement, or primary record is shown in the image itself. Nothing here demonstrates any wrongdoing by any named person.

The reason the material is kept at all is that the timing and the foreign-meeting angle recur elsewhere in the investigation's aircraft threads, so researchers wanting to trace where the claim originated can see the primary post and weigh it for themselves.
