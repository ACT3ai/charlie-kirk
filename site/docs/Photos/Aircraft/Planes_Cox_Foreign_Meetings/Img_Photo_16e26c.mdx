## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of a social media feed, specifically showing two posts from an account named "Project Constitution" on a platform resembling X (formerly Twitter). The upper post discusses alleged secret meetings of the Utah Governor with representatives from Egypt and France, accompanied by a cropped image of a daily schedule with highlighted sections and blue indicator arrows. Below it, a partial view of another post by the same user discusses a conspiracy theory involving French President [Emmanuel Macron](/People/emmanuel-macron) and [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), featuring composite thumbnail graphics.

Everything in these posts is the poster's assertion. The alleged meetings rest on an anonymous account's reading of a published online calendar, no independent confirmation or official statement is shown, and nothing here indicates wrongdoing by [Governor Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox) or by anyone else named. The screenshot is reproduced so readers can see the original claim rather than a paraphrase of it.
