## What This Image Shows

A two-column flight log for **SU-BTT** running from late 2024 into May 2025.

Entries include **27 May 2025** Cairo local; **02 May** Medina (MED) to Cairo and Cairo to Medina; **26 Apr** Jeddah to Cairo and Cairo to Jeddah; **08 Apr** El Arish (AAC) to Cairo; **05 Apr, 18 Mar, 23 Feb, 18 Feb, 16 Feb, 11 Feb, 26 Jan, 12 Jan** and **05 Jan** Cairo legs; **22 Dec 2024** and **20 Dec** Cairo; **11 Dec 2024** Muscat (MCT) to Cairo; and **26 Nov 2024**.

The Muscat leg in December 2024 is the furthest afield in this window, and it is still within the Gulf. Everything else is Egypt, Saudi Arabia and the Sinai. That regional footprint, sustained across six months, is the necessary comparison for the trans-Atlantic runs that draw all the attention.

These logs are the source material behind the "Incorrect - in Cairo" verdicts on the audit sheets. They are unglamorous and they do more to discipline the flight-overlap claim than any single dramatic capture in this cluster.
