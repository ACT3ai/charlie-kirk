## What This Image Shows

A full-page capture of an error page at **The War Zone** (TWZ). The site navigation runs across the top -- LATEST, NEWS &amp; FEATURES, AIR, SEA, LAND, SPACE, NUCLEAR, EVENTS, MORE -- above a centred message reading "**404. We couldn't find that page. Try searching for something or explore our categories below.**" A search box and a grid of category buttons sit beneath it, with a sidebar of recent articles including "Bunker Talk: Let's Talk About All The Things We Did And Didn't Cover This Week" by **Tyler Rogoway**.

This is the page returned at the URL researchers had been citing for the article "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet." It documents a state, and only a state: at the moment of capture, that address did not resolve to an article.

The inference drawn from it -- that the piece was pulled because it connected to **N1098L** -- is a claim, not a finding. URLs change for editorial and technical reasons all the time, the publisher has not commented on this site's record, and a second outlet published its own report on the same Army program, which survives.
