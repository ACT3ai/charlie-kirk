## What This Image Shows

One cut of a thirteen-slide deck built to argue that a cited defence-trade article was taken down after researchers linked it to the aircraft thread.

The banner text on this slide reads: "**Cover Up in Charlie Kirk.** The Classified Drone launching plane at Charlie Kirk's assassination just had a cover up. TWZ article documented the program. The article was removed, because it was proof of the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) flight to Charlie Kirk's event. Assassination Spy program of planes to drop off drones is Project [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) (High, Accuracy, Detection &amp; Exploitation, System). Same plane model at assassination." The cited link is the TWZ story "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet," filed under AIR / AIRBORNE SENSORS.

This version foregrounds the split-screen comparison, with the red accusatory header above and the two screenshots below. It is worth separating the observable from the inferred: a defence-trade article about planned Army drone-launch trials was published and later stopped resolving at the linked address. Everything after "because" in the caption is the poster's reasoning, not a documented fact.
