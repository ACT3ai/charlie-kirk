## What This Image Shows

One cut of a thirteen-slide deck built to argue that a cited defence-trade article was taken down after researchers linked it to the aircraft thread.

The banner text on this slide reads: "**Cover Up in Charlie Kirk.** The Classified Drone launching plane at Charlie Kirk's assassination just had a cover up. TWZ article documented the program. The article was removed, because it was proof of the N1098L flight to Charlie Kirk's event. Assassination Spy program of planes to drop off drones is Project HADES (High, Accuracy, Detection &amp; Exploitation, System). Same plane model at assassination." The cited link is the TWZ story "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet," filed under AIR / AIRBORNE SENSORS.

This cut compresses the two browser states side by side under the red header. The deck's value in the record is as an artifact of how the claim spread -- a single graphic, reposted in many crops, is how a link failure became a widely repeated assertion about suppression.
