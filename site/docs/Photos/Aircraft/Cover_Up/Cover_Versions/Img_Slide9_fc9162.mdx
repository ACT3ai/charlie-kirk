## What This Image Shows

One cut of a thirteen-slide deck built to argue that a cited defence-trade article was taken down after researchers linked it to the aircraft thread.

The banner text on this slide reads: "**Cover Up in Charlie Kirk.** The Classified Drone launching plane at Charlie Kirk's assassination just had a cover up. TWZ article documented the program. The article was removed, because it was proof of the [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) flight to Charlie Kirk's event. Assassination Spy program of planes to drop off drones is Project HADES (High, Accuracy, Detection &amp; Exploitation, System). Same plane model at assassination." The cited link is the TWZ story "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet," filed under AIR / AIRBORNE SENSORS.

A version emphasising the Before panel's article headline and the After panel's error state. Worth noting: the phrase "Classified Drone launching plane" overstates what any source says. The HADES program and its airframe are publicly reported, the trials were publicly announced, and nothing about the aircraft type is classified.
