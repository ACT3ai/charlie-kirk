## What This Image Shows

This is a digital graphic collage designed to propagate a conspiracy theory about an alleged "cover-up" involving an assassination attempt on political commentator Charlie Kirk. The image compares two screenshots of a military aviation news website, "The War Zone" (TWZ): one showing a published article about the US Army testing air-launched drones from a Global Express business jet, and the other showing a 404 error page indicating the article has been removed. The collage uses red arrows, bold text headers, and explanatory paragraphs to assert a connection between this deleted article, a "Project HADES" spy program, and a specific aircraft flight (N1098L).
