## About This Cluster

These are the shareable slides built to make the disappearing-article point in a single frame. Each one puts the live **The War Zone** article on the left, labelled "Before," and the 404 error page on the right, labelled "After," under a red banner reading "Cover Up in Charlie Kirk." They were produced as a numbered deck so the same argument could be posted in different crops and aspect ratios, which is why thirteen near-identical versions sit here rather than one.

The body text is the same on every slide and reads: "The Classified Drone launching plane at Charlie Kirk's assassination just had a cover up. TWZ article documented the program. The article was removed, because it was proof of the N1098L flight to Charlie Kirk's event. Assassination Spy program of planes to drop off drones is Project HADES (High, Accuracy, Detection &amp; Exploitation, System). Same plane model at assassination." The cited URL is the TWZ story "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet," filed under AIR / AIRBORNE SENSORS. That wording states the removal as proof; the more careful reading is narrower — the article existed, it is now returning a 404, and no explanation for the change has been published. The slides record the claim as it was made and circulated, with the reasoning left visible so a reader can judge it.

Because the slides repeat, one page is enough to read the argument; the rest are alternate cuts of the same deck. Go up to [Cover Up](/Photos/Aircraft/Cover_Up/overview) for the raw 404 capture, then to [N1098L Air-Launched Drones](/Drones/N1098L_Air_Launched_Drones) and [N1098L Spy Plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview) for the substance of the drone-launch claim. Surviving HADES coverage is in [Articles](/Photos/Aircraft/Articles/overview), and the wider removal pattern is tracked in [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview) and [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).
