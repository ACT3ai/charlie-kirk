## What This Image Shows

A meme-format image: a headshot of a man against a plain light-grey background, with bold white block text above and below reading "**WALEED MAHMOUD**" and "**THIS YOUR GUY CANDACE?**"

The caption is addressed to **Candace Owens**, who has reported on the Egyptian aircraft thread, and asks whether the person shown is the figure she has described. It is a question posed as a meme, not a claim supported by anything.

That needs saying plainly, because the format invites a reader to assume the identification is settled. It is not. No passenger manifest for **SU-BTT** has been released, no witness has placed any named person aboard, and no official source has identified anyone on that aircraft. The man shown is a living private individual with a public diplomatic career and no established connection to the events of September 10, 2025. Circulating a face beside a suggestive caption is not evidence, and this site does not treat it as such.

The published biography behind the name is on [Photo 89](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Walid_Mahmoud_Abdel_4761b0). For what the flight records document, see [SU-BTT Israel/Egyptian Jet](/Planes/SU-BTT).
