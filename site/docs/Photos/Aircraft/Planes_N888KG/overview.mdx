## About This Cluster

These two images document the claim built around tail number [N888KG](/Planes/N888KG), a white private jet registered to N888KG LLC in Utah. One is a social-media thread discussing the aircraft's flight path, transponder behavior and possible link to the September 10 incident; the other pairs commentary with a cropped business-registration lookup connecting the registration to a named individual and to [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). The circulating theory holds that the jet left [Provo Municipal](/Locations/Provo_Airport) shortly after the shooting, went quiet, and turned up in the Page, Arizona area.

The important counterweight, noted in the investigation's own findings, is that the aircraft owner's public statements are exculpatory. According to those statements, the flight was a pre-scheduled flight plan, the outbound leg carried two pilots and no passengers, and the trip was a pickup at Page, Arizona. That account, if accurate, undercuts the "vanishing plane" reading.

Because a business-registration screenshot names a living person, the material is presented as reported claims only. Being listed as an aircraft's registrant does not establish involvement in any event, and nothing here should be read as an allegation of wrongdoing against anyone named.
