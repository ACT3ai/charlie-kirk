## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture. Search term **Michael Harlan**, region **Washington DC (Hagerstown)**, window **Past 12 months**, with the tooltip reading "**Dec 1 - 7, 2024. Michael Harlan. 100**."

This is the second of the two spikes visible in the other captures of this term, and it lands in the first week of **December 2024** -- nine months before the events this investigation concerns.

Put the two labelled peaks together and the pattern is a term with two unrelated bumps, one in December 2024 and one in late August 2025, on an otherwise empty line. Neither falls on or after September 10, 2025. Whatever drove them, the data does not support a story about lookups tied to the shooting, and presenting these charts as if it did would be reading the graph backwards.
