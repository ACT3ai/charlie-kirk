## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture. Search term **Elena Vasquez**, region **Israel**, window **Past 12 months**. The graph spans **Oct 20, 2024** to **Sep 21, 2025**, showing one sharp spike in **late October 2024** and near-zero interest through the rest of the period.

This is the twelve-month view of the same term captured over ninety days elsewhere in this sub-cluster, and the longer window changes the picture. The only significant activity is in **October 2024**, eleven months before the shooting. September 2025 is flat.

That is a useful demonstration of how easily these charts mislead. A ninety-day window starting in July 2025 will normalize a small August blip up to 100 and make it look dramatic; the twelve-month view shows that same blip as negligible against a genuinely larger event the previous autumn. Interest indices are relative to the window chosen, and changing the window changes the story.
