## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture. Search term **Jax Donovan**, region **Israel**, window **Past 12 months**. The graph spans **Oct 20, 2024** to **Sep 21, 2025**, with a tooltip reading "**Dec 1 - 7, 2024. Jax Donovan. 82**."

The labelled value makes this the most precise chart in the sub-cluster and, read carefully, one of the most deflating. The peak week is the first week of **December 2024** -- more than nine months before September 10, 2025 -- and it reaches 82 rather than the maximum, meaning some other week in the window scored higher still.

So whatever drove searches for this name in [Israel](/Israel/overview), it happened long before the events this investigation concerns and is unrelated to them on the face of the data. Presenting these charts as support for the crew-identification theory requires ignoring where the peaks actually fall.
