## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture. Search term **Jax Donovan**, region **Israel**, window **Past 12 months**, All categories, Web Search. The graph spans **Oct 20, 2024** to **Sep 21, 2025** and shows a sharp rise in late 2024 followed by a flat line into late 2025.

The name appears in the disputed crew list for [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview) as J. "Jax" E. Donovan, second pilot. The chart was run to test whether interest in the name spiked around the September 10, 2025 date.

It does not. The visible activity is in **late 2024**, nearly a year before the shooting, and the line is flat through September 2025 -- which is the opposite of what the theory would predict. That is worth stating plainly, because a chart offered as support here actually argues against the reading it was made to test.

The underlying crew list came from an AI exchange in which the model characterized the details as fabricated, documented on [Photo 26](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_95fe57). No verified crew list for this flight exists in public. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/Aircraft/N1098L_NCSS_CIA/overview).
