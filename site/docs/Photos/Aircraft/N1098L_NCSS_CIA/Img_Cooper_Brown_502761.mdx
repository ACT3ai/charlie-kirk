## What This Image Shows

A **Google Trends** capture. Search term **cooper brown** in lower case, region **Israel**, window **Past 12 months**. The graph runs **Oct 27, 2024** to **Sep 28, 2025**, flat until a sharp spike to 100 labelled "**Aug 17 - 23, 2025**."

The name belongs to the second, competing crew list -- the one attributed to **[@tjphager](/Influencers/x/tjphager)**, who has argued the first list was wrong and that [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) was not configured for drone launch at all. It is also the name of a person who appears elsewhere in the investigation's material in a security or technical role at the event, which means a Trends result for it cannot be attributed to the aircraft thread even in principle.

The spike falls in **mid-August 2025**, three weeks before September 10. Reading that as meaningful again requires assuming advance knowledge, which this site does not assume and which nothing supports. "[Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown)" is also a common combination of two common names.

No verified crew list for the flight exists, and nothing here indicates that any person by this name was aboard or did anything improper. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/Aircraft/N1098L_NCSS_CIA/overview).
