## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a working spreadsheet -- the source data behind the correlation charts elsewhere in this cluster. Columns run Index and Date, with rows numbered from 28 upward and columns tracking routes, locations and events, including headings for [**Erika**](/People/erika-kirk) and [**Charlie**](/People/charlie-kirk).

The visible date column reads: **2024-07-20, 2024-08-17, 2024-12-08, 2025-01-12, 2025-01-17, 2025-03-15, 2025-03-22, 2025-05-23, 2025-06-18, 2025-07-07, 2025-07-20, 2025-08-17, 2025-09-04, 2025-09-10, 2025-09-13**, then an earlier block from **2022-11-25** through **2023-06-25** and a 2024 block.

This is worth more than the polished infographics built on top of it, because it shows the workings. The two dates at the centre of the case are right there in sequence -- **2025-09-04** and **2025-09-10** -- alongside dozens of others, and a reader can see how many entries the pattern is drawn from.

The same index numbers appear on the audit sheets in this cluster, where several rows were checked and marked incorrect. Any figure quoted from this workbook should be quoted with that correction attached.

See the audits on [Photo 101](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_4a64aa) and [Photo 96](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_15a6be), and [Following Charlie and Erika](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika).
