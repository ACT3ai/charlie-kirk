## What This Image Shows

A scanned page from the same 2019 complaint, referencing a numbered file at a Romanian military prosecutor's office attached to that country's Military Court of Appeal, and stating that the case was closed without certain matters being investigated.

This is procedural history from a foreign legal matter. It has no bearing on the Charlie Kirk investigation, no connection to any aircraft in this cluster, and no relationship to the events of September 10, 2025. The document set was filed in the aircraft folder in error.

The pages name individuals in connection with unproven allegations, and this site does not reproduce either the names or the allegations. That restraint is deliberate: unadjudicated accusations about identifiable people are not published here regardless of where the document came from.
