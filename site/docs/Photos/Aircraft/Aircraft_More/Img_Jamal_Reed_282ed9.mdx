## What This Image Shows

A professional studio athletic headshot: a young basketball player in a green jersey with white and yellow trim, centred against a plain grey background, looking directly at the camera.

This has nothing to do with aircraft. It is here because the filename matches a name that appears in the material around the disputed **N1098L** crew lists, and a researcher searching that name collected whatever the search returned.

That is exactly the hazard the crew-name thread runs into repeatedly. Common names return unrelated people -- athletes, academics, local news subjects -- and once an image is saved into a folder it acquires an implied relevance it never had. The same problem appears with the academic profile captured in the main [Aircraft](/Photos/Aircraft/overview) cluster and with the Google Trends charts in [N1098L NCSS CIA](/Photos/Aircraft/N1098L_NCSS_CIA/overview).

The person shown is an unidentified athlete with no connection to this investigation, and none is suggested. The crew of N1098L on September 10, 2025 remains unidentified in public.
