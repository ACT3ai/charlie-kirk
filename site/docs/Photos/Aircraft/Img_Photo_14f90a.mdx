## What This Image Shows

A scanned page from the 2019 Romanian complaint, arguing that investigative bodies have a duty to examine any hypothesis "whether it seems plausible or not," and referring to press coverage of a separate criminal case in that country.

The reasoning in that opening line is the only thing on the page that touches this site's subject matter even in principle, and it does so as a general proposition about how investigations should be conducted rather than as anything about the Charlie Kirk case. Nothing here concerns the aircraft in this cluster or the events of September 10, 2025.

The rest of the page contains untested allegations involving named individuals, which are not reproduced. The document reached this folder through an unsorted import of unrelated research.
