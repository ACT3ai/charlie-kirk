## What This Image Shows

A Flightradar24 playback card for **SU-BTT**. The header reads "Playback of flight SUBTT," with FROM Provo (PVU) and TO Wilmington (ILG), GREAT CIRCLE DISTANCE 3,065 km, ACTUAL FLIGHT TIME 3:37, and TIME 1:08 PM UTC. Aircraft is listed as **Dassault Falcon 7X**, registration SU-BTT, serial number (MSN) 239. Live telemetry reads barometric altitude 0 FT, ground speed 4 KTS, track 185 degrees, squawk 0000 -- the aircraft is on the ground at the moment captured.

This is the primary-source version of the departure that **Candace Owens** described on her podcast. Read against the 12:23 PM MDT shooting, 1:08 PM UTC is early that morning in Utah, and the 3 hour 37 minute leg carries the aircraft to Delaware the same day.

Wilmington recurs across this thread as the destination for several Egyptian-registered jets, and the investigation's notes point at a specific downtown address there as a federal office nexus. That connection is an open research thread rather than a documented finding -- no manifest, tasking record, or official statement has been produced.
