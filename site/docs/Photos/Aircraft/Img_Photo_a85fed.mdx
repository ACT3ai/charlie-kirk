## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by **Penned Patriot** (@pennedpatriot) showing a three-dimensional aerial map, rendered in a Google Earth style, with tracked flight positions plotted over Orem, Utah and the shoreline of Utah Lake.

Every plotted point carries a UTC timestamp, and they cluster tightly: 2025-09-10 15:06:05, 15:07:04, 15:15:34, 15:15:47, 15:16:01, 15:16:14, 15:16:28, 15:16:37, 15:16:44, 15:16:51, 15:22:54, 15:22:57, 15:23:55, 15:24:03. Named landmarks visible on the map include the **UVU Museum**, Provo River Delta, Powell Slough Waterfowl Management Area, Lakemount, Westmore, and EoS Fitness. Converting to local time places that whole window in the 9 AM hour on the morning of the [shooting](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

This is the kind of capture the drop-off theory is built from -- the position points show an aircraft holding over the water and the campus edge rather than transiting through. The map shows where and when; it does not show what the aircraft was doing, and the operator has been reported as describing low passes of this type as drone retrieval training.
