## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a flight-tracking page for tail number [N102DZ](/Planes/N102DZ), the aircraft associated with [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s own travel. It lists September 2025 flights with columns for date, origin and destination, duration, scheduled and actual times, and status, with the September 10, 2025 row highlighted and a tooltip near the destination airport code.

N102DZ matters because it anchors the timeline that the rest of this aircraft section debates. Public flight records place Kirk's plane arriving at Salt Lake City International around 9:30 a.m. MDT on September 10 after departing Scottsdale, Arizona, ahead of his [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) event. A recurring question raised by commentators, including [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens), is why he was routed to Salt Lake City - roughly 45 miles from UVU - rather than [Provo Municipal](/Locations/Provo_Airport), only about seven miles away. That routing observation is the substance of the discussion; the screenshot is the primary record it rests on.

The tracking data itself is neutral. It shows where an aircraft went and when, and readers can check the highlighted entry directly rather than relying on any single interpretation of it.
