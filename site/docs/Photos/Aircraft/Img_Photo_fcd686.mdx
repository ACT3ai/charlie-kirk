## What This Image Shows

An oblique high-angle aerial of the campus, showing multi-storey academic buildings, the rooftops between them, connecting plazas and walkways, and parking lots filled with cars. A player scrubber reading **1:13 / 1:48** is visible, indicating this frame was pulled from a video, and the **@universehop00** watermark is present.

The rooftops are the reason this frame matters. Much of the dispute in this case turns on elevated positions -- which roofs were accessible, what each overlooked, and where a person on one could and could not be seen from. An oblique aerial shows roof geometry and parapet heights in a way a straight-down view flattens away.

The scrubber is a useful caution about provenance: this is a still lifted from a video posted by the same account behind the leaked-photograph claim, not a raw survey image. Whether any of that material originated with the **N59906** flight has not been verified, and no contractor or agency has confirmed a release.
