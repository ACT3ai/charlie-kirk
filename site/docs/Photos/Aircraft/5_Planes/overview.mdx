## About This Cluster

[**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) put two Egyptian-registered jets, [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) and [**SU-BND**](/Planes/SU-BND), at the center of the flight thread. Researchers working from the same public tracking sources then reported three more — **SU-BTU**, **SU-BTV** and **SU-BGM** — flying similar Cairo-to-Paris-to-United-States routes through 2025, with Provo among the recurring stops. That expansion from two aircraft to five is what this cluster documents, and it is the difference between a coincidence and a pattern worth explaining, which is why the raw flight histories are preserved as screenshots rather than summarized.

The four captures are flight-history tables lifted straight from a tracking site, with the relevant legs highlighted in yellow. The **SU-BTT** log reads 03 Sep 2025 Cairo to Paris, 04 Sep Paris to Minot, 04 Sep Minot to Provo, then 10 Sep Provo to Wilmington and 11 Sep Wilmington to Cairo — the six-day Provo stay that brackets the shooting. The **SU-BND** log shows 05 Sep 2025 into Provo, 13 Sep Provo to Goose Bay, and 15 Sep on to Paris. **SU-BTU** and **SU-BTV**, both Dassault Falcon 7X airframes listed as Egypt Air Force, show repeated Cairo-Paris-Minot/Omaha/Lincoln-Provo-Wilmington circuits across February through October 2025, and **SU-BGM**, a Gulfstream IV listed as Egypt Government, shows Goose Bay to Provo on 16 Apr 2025 alongside a 55-minute local Provo flight on 10 April. The screenshots carry a **[@WeAre_TheElite](/Influencers/x/weare-theelite)** watermark. What the logs establish is presence and routing; they say nothing about passengers or purpose, and no manifest has been released.

Read these next to [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada), which sets out the fleet-wide pattern, and the individual pages for [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND), [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV) and [SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM). For the claim that these aircraft tracked [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events over years, see [Following Charlie and Erika](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika); for the ground side in Provo, see [Egyptian Crew Hotel](/Planes/Egyptian-Crew-Hotel). More log captures are in [Trip History](/Photos/Aircraft/Trip_History/overview).
