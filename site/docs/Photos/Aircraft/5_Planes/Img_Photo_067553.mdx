## What This Image Shows

A flight-history table headed "**Flight history for aircraft - SU-BTV, Dassault Falcon 7X, Egypt - Air Force**," with several rows highlighted in yellow.

The log runs backward from **29 Oct 2025** Cairo, through **12 Oct** Wilmington (ILG), **11 Oct** Lincoln (LNK), **05 Oct** Omaha (OMA) and Paris (LBG), **04 Oct** Cairo; **06 Aug** Wilmington, **03 Aug** Wichita (ICT), **30 Jul** Paris, **29 Jul** Cairo; **03 Jun** Wilmington, **02 Jun** **Provo (PVU)**, **27 May** Minot (MOT) and Paris, **26 May** Cairo; **06 Apr** Wilmington, **03 Apr** Wichita, **30 Mar** Detroit and Paris, **29 Mar** Cairo; **24 Mar** Wilmington, **23 Mar** Lincoln, **17 Mar** Omaha and Paris, **16 Mar** Cairo; **10 Feb** Wilmington, **03 Feb** Detroit.

This is the third Egyptian jet, and its log shows the same repeating shape as the two better-known aircraft: Cairo, Paris, a mid-continent United States stop, then Wilmington, then home. The **02 Jun 2025** Provo entry is the one that puts this tail number into the Utah thread.

The structure is real and documented. The purpose is not, and no manifests have been released for any of these flights.
