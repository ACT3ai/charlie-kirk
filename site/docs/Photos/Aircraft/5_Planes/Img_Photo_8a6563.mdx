## What This Image Shows

A flight-history page headed "**Flight history for aircraft - SU-BTU, Dassault Falcon 7X, Egypt - Air Force**," with two thumbnail photographs of the jet in flight and several legs highlighted.

The log reads: **01 May 2025** Wilmington (ILG) to Cairo; **30 Apr** Lincoln (LNK) to Wilmington; **24 Apr** Omaha (OMA) to Lincoln and Paris (LBG) to Omaha; **23 Apr** Cairo to Paris; **15 Apr** Wilmington to Cairo; **14 Apr** **Provo (PVU) to Wilmington**; **08 Apr** Minot (MOT) to Provo and Paris to Minot; **07 Apr** Cairo to Paris. Earlier: **23 Feb** Wilmington to Cairo; **22 Feb** St. Louis (CPS) to Wilmington; **16 Feb** St. Louis (STL) and Paris; **15 Feb** Cairo; **24 Jan** Wilmington; **23 Jan** Lincoln; **17 Jan** Omaha and Paris.

The fourth aircraft, and the same template again: Cairo, Paris, a northern-plains or midwest stop, Provo or St. Louis, Wilmington, Cairo. The **14 April 2025** [Provo](/Locations/Provo_Airport) departure and the **08 April** Minot-to-Provo arrival put this tail number in Utah five months before the shooting.

Wilmington closes almost every circuit, which is the recurrence researchers find hardest to explain innocently. No official account of these movements has been published.
