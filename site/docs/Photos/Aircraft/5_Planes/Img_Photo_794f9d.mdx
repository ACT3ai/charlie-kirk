## What This Image Shows

Two flight-history tables stacked vertically, with key rows highlighted in yellow.

The upper table, **SU-BND, Gulfstream 4SP**, reads: **15 Sep 2025** Paris (LBG); **13 Sep** Goose Bay (YYR); **13 Sep** **Provo (PVU)**; **05 Sep** Provo (PVU); **10 Jun** Provo (PVU); **23 May** Minot (MOT), Goose Bay, Paris; **22 May** Cairo (CAI).

The lower table, **SU-BTT, Dassault Falcon 7X, Egypt Government / Egypt - Air Force**, reads: **11 Sep 2025** Wilmington (ILG) to Cairo; **10 Sep 2025** **Provo (PVU) to Wilmington (ILG)**; **04 Sep** Minot (MOT) to Provo (PVU); **04 Sep** Paris (LBG) to Minot; **03 Sep** Cairo to Paris; **24 Aug** Wilmington to Cairo; **23 Aug** Lincoln (LNK) to Wilmington; **17 Aug**...

This single image contains the entire documented core of the Egyptian aircraft claim. SU-BTT arrives at Provo on **4 September**, departs for Wilmington on **10 September**, and returns to Cairo on **11 September**. SU-BND is at Provo from **5 September** and leaves on **13 September**.

Those dates survived the audit that found sixty-six percent of the wider claims inaccurate. They are the part of this thread that has not been explained away, and no manifest or official statement addresses them.
