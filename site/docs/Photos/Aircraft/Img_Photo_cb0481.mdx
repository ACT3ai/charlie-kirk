## What This Image Shows

A full-page capture of the article page at **The War Zone**, section AIR / AIRBORNE SENSORS. The headline reads "Army Wants To Test Air-Launched Drones From A Global Express Business Jet," the standfirst "The planned trials tally with previous comments about launching the drones from the new Global 6500-based [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) surveillance jet," and the byline **THOMAS NEWDICK / PUBLISHED JAN 9, 2025 4:03 PM EST**. A large photograph of a matte-grey military business jet sits below, credited US ARMY, alongside the site's advertising rail.

This is the most complete capture of the source article held in the investigation -- page furniture, byline, date and all -- which is what gives it evidentiary weight once the live URL began returning a 404. A screenshot with visible chrome and a publication timestamp is much harder to dispute than a quoted sentence.

Hold on to the actual claim in the text while reading the argument built on it. The article reports planned Army trials of a capability. It does not report that any drone was launched from any aircraft over Utah, and it predates the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) by eight months.
