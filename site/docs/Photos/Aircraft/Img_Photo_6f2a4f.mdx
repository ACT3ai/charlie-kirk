## What This Image Shows

A composite screenshot of an X post built around a four-panel crop of an aircraft-for-sale listing. The listing header reads "AIRCRAFT FOR SALE &gt; BOMBARDIER &gt; GLOBAL 6500 &gt; 2023," with "Call for price," "Aircraft Location: Wilmington, Delaware," and seller **AeroAmerica Group** of Manassas, Virginia. One panel is captioned "FILE PHOTO / SAMPLE OF SPECIAL MISSION INTERIOR."

The post text reads: "Did you know the [HADES](/US_Intelligence/US_Army_HADES) spy plane we flying around the scene of Charlie Kirk's murder is for sale? When the CIA can't fit all their crap in a plane, they sell it, and get a bigger plane." That framing is the poster's characterization. The listing itself documents something narrower but still relevant: a 2023 Global 6500 offered by AeroAmerica Group with a special-mission interior, located in Wilmington, Delaware.

Two details connect outward. The airframe type matches the one at the center of the flight thread, and the brochure for serial number 60098 registered as **N1098L** carries the same AeroAmerica branding, preserved in the [Nwq](/Photos/Aircraft/Nwq/overview) and [More](/Photos/Aircraft/Aircraft_More/overview) sub-clusters. Wilmington is also the destination researchers report for the Egyptian jet [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) on [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

See [N1098L Spy Plane](/Planes/N1098L/overview) for the flight-data analysis and [ISR Operations](/Planes/ISR-Operations) for how these platforms are tasked.
