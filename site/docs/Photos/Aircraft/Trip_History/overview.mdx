## About This Cluster

These four screenshots reproduce the flight-history spreadsheet — the "Owens Sheet" — that researchers compiled for the Egyptian-registered jets [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) and [**SU-BND**](/Planes/SU-BND). The tables list dated flights from October 2022 into 2025 on routes such as Cairo to Paris to New York, Chicago, Boston, St. Louis, Wichita, and repeatedly **Wilmington, Delaware**. This sheet underpins the claim, first aired by [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens), that these jets crossed the United States for years before the assassination.

According to the researchers who built the sheet, the routes repeatedly touched cities near [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events, and SU-BTT's final U.S. sequence ran Paris to Minot to **Provo on September 4, 2025**, then Provo to Wilmington on September 10 — the day [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot — and out of the country afterward. Wilmington matters to this thread because researchers tie the stop to One Rodney Square, an address shared with federal offices. Whether the pattern reflects surveillance, routine diplomacy, or coincidence is an open question the logs alone cannot answer.

Read these tables alongside [Says Wrong](/Photos/Aircraft/Says_Wrong/overview), where posters dispute entries in the same sheet, and [5 Planes](/Photos/Aircraft/5_Planes/overview), which extends the claim to three more Egyptian tail numbers. The written analysis lives at [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) and [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada).
