## What This Image Shows

Audit rows numbered 58 through 67, nine shaded red and one green, covering **6/18/2024** through **7/20/2025**.

The notes column is where the work is: "**In Egypt**," "**Still in Egypt**," "**Yeppers egypt**," "**CAIRO. DUPLICATE REF INDEX NUMBER 31**," "**Duplicate Ref INDEX NUMBER 33**," "**DOUBLE ENTRY ON OWENS SHEET, REF INDEX # 35**," "**PROVO. REF INDEX # 35. ONLY LOCAL FLIGHTS WERE FLOWN IN AND AROUND PROVO FROM 10 JUNE - 13 SEPT**," and "**ACCURATE-ISH. FLIGHT BEGAN ON 19 JUL IN CAIRO AND TERMINATED 20 JUL IN LINCOLN.. RETURN LEG WAS 6 DAYS LATER 26 JUL**."

Two failure modes are documented here. Some entries are simply wrong -- the aircraft was in Egypt. Others are the same real flight counted more than once under different index numbers, which inflates the total without adding any actual trip.

The Provo note cuts the other way and is worth flagging: it confirms that **SU-BND** was present around Provo flying only local legs from **10 June to 13 September 2025**, a three-month stay that spans the shooting. That is a documented fact the audit does not dispute.
