## What This Image Shows

A flight-log table for **SU-BTT** covering **October and November 2025**, listing dates, origin and destination airports and durations.

Rows include **13 Nov** Cairo to Antalya (AYT), flown twice that day; **06 Nov** Cairo local, Jeddah (JED) to Cairo, and Cairo to Jeddah; **04 Nov** two Cairo local legs; **03 Nov** Cairo local; **01 Nov** Medina (MED) to Cairo, Cairo to Medina, and a Cairo local; then a long run of Cairo local flights through **29, 28, 26, 25** and **22 October**.

The density here is worth noting -- this is a busy aircraft flying multiple legs most weeks, overwhelmingly within Egypt and the surrounding region. Read against the multi-year itinerary elsewhere in this cluster, it puts the United States trips in proportion: a handful of episodes inside hundreds of flights.

It also documents that the aircraft continued flying openly with a public transponder trail after September 10, which is the opposite of what one would expect from an airframe involved in something requiring concealment. That observation does not resolve the Provo questions, but it belongs in any fair reading of the record.
