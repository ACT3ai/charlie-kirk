## What This Image Shows

A high-angle aerial of the crowded plaza, with dozens of people on the grassy lawn and along the paved walkways beside the modern buildings. A sharp diagonal shadow cast by one of the structures runs across the lawn. Watermarked **@universehop00**.

The shadow is the most analytically useful feature in the frame. Shadow direction and length let a reader estimate the time of day independently of any caption -- which, for a set whose provenance rests entirely on an anonymous account's word, is one of the few internal checks available. A shadow angle consistent with late morning would fit the claimed **11:35 AM** survey pass; one that does not would undercut the whole origin story.

That check has not been published, and this site does not assert a result. The set is described by its poster as leaked imagery from the **N59906** flight, and that claim remains unverified.
