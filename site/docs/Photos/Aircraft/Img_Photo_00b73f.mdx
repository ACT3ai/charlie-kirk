## What This Image Shows

The last of the nine scanned pages from the 2019 Romanian complaint held in this folder, continuing the writer's account of personnel and events at the facility where she worked.

Taken together, the nine pages form one foreign legal document from 2019. None of it relates to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) investigation, to the aircraft tracked in this cluster, or to the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). The group is published here only so the image corpus on this site matches the corpus the investigation holds, with its filing errors visible rather than quietly removed.

The document names individuals against whom serious allegations are made, and none of those allegations has been established in any proceeding on this record. This site reproduces neither.
