## What This Image Shows

A low-angle Google Street View capture of a tall, modern red-brick office building on a street corner, labelled **920 N King St**, with a black sedan parked in the foreground. A large soft-edged blur covers part of the building facade, and the imagery is dated 2021 in one credit and 2025 in another.

This is the address labelled "DEA, FBI, and USSS" on the annotated Wilmington map elsewhere in this cluster. Researchers photographed it because Wilmington is the reported destination of the Egyptian jet [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) on [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and because the investigation's private notes flag this specific building as a federal office nexus.

The blur is the detail most likely to be over-read. Google routinely blurs faces, plates and, at the request of building owners or occupants, whole facades -- a common practice at government and secure sites and not, by itself, evidence of anything. What this image documents is the building's appearance and location, nothing more, and no connection between the address and any flight has been established.
