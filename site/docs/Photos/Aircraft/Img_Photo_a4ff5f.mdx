## What This Image Shows

A high-angle aerial of the plaza with hundreds of people seated in rows along the terraced, V-shaped green lawns and moving along the concrete paths around them. The stage area under a white canopy tent is visible at the focal point of the terracing. Watermarked **[@universehop00](/Influencers/x/universehop00)**.

This is the clearest frame in the set for the single most contested relationship in the case: the position of the canopy relative to the terraces, the crowd, and the surrounding buildings. Every argument about angles -- from the roof, from a stairwell, from above -- is ultimately an argument about this geometry, and most have been made without a scaled overhead view to work from.

The caveat stands with this frame as with the rest. The set is presented by an anonymous account as leaked imagery from the **N59906** survey flight of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025); that has not been confirmed by metadata, by the contractor, or by any agency, and no independent verification has been published.

See [N59906](/Planes/N59906), [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) for the trajectory arguments, and [Maps](/maps/overview).
