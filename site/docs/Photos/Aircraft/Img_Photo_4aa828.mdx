## What This Image Shows

A more detailed version of the correlation chart, with columns for **Flight Overlaps**, **DATE** and **Other Events** across **2023, 2024 and 2025**, and tracks labelled **Egypt - SU-BTT** and **Egypt - SU-BND**.

Visible pairings include St. Louis, MO arrivals and departures in early 2023 against "TPUSA Faith kicks off Tour headed by Sean Feucht"; a February 2024 sequence at **Omaha, Nebraska** -- arrives 2/9, departs 2/14 -- against "TP New Chapter Opening Event"; "Sean Feucht Announced as TP Faith Contributor / Head Kingdom to the Capitol tour"; "Mikey McCoy promoted to Chief" and "TPUSA Campus tour" against **8/17** and **8/24** Omaha entries; and a **9/4** arrival at the end.

The method is visible in the layout, and so is its weakness. Placing two independent calendars side by side will always produce apparent overlaps if both are dense enough; what would make the argument stand up is a base rate -- how often the aircraft visited a city with no TPUSA activity, and how many TPUSA events had no corresponding flight. Neither is shown.

The underlying dates are also the ones the audit sheets found partly unreliable. Treat the chart as a hypothesis to be tested rather than as a result.
