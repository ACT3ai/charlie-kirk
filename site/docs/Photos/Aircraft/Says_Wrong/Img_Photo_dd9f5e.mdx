## What This Image Shows

The continuation of the audit spreadsheet, covering entries numbered roughly 31 through 66 with colour-coded verification status in red, green, yellow and blue.

The 2025 rows include **1/12, 1/17, 3/15, 3/22, 5/23, 6/18, 7/7, 7/20, 8/17** and then the three dates at the centre of the case -- **9/4/2025**, **9/10/2025** and **9/13/2025** -- against registrations [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) and [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND). Routes visible include "Paris to Wichita to Cairo," "Wichita to Wilmington to Cairo," "LANDS IN PROVO, UTAH," "Paris to Omaha to Lincoln to Wilmington to Cairo," and "Goose Bay to Paris to Cairo."

The September rows are the ones that matter most and they are also the ones that survive checking: an arrival at Provo on September 4, a departure on September 10, and the second aircraft leaving on September 13. Those are documented in the raw tracking logs preserved elsewhere in this cluster.

What the sheet strips out is the surrounding volume -- dozens of earlier claimed United States trips that the aircraft's own logs place in Cairo. The core is real; the inflation around it is not.
