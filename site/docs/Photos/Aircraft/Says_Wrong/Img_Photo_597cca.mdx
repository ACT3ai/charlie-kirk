## What This Image Shows

A colour-coded audit spreadsheet, with columns for Index, Date, Flight, Route, actual location, event reason, whether Charlie Kirk was present, and notes.

The visible rows run from **2022-10-05** through **2025-05-23**, alternating between [**SU-BTT**](/Planes/SU-BTT) and [**SU-BND**](/Planes/SU-BND), with routes including "Cairo to Paris to New York," "New York to Paris to Chicago," "Chicago to Paris to Boston," and the later Wichita, Wilmington and Provo legs. Row colours mark each claim as verified, partly verified or wrong.

This is the working sheet behind the most valuable exercise in the entire aircraft file: somebody took the widely shared list of Egyptian flights and checked every entry against the aircraft's own recorded position. That is what the sub-cluster name refers to -- it identifies where the popular version of the story says the wrong thing.

The results, summarised on the companion page in this cluster, are not favourable to the version in circulation. Anyone citing the flight-overlap timeline should read this sheet first.
