## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by [**Project Constitution**](/Influencers/x/project-constitution) (@ProjectConstitu) with flight-log rows attached below the text. The post opens: "BOMBSHELL EVIDENCE: CONFIRMED NOT 2 BUT 5 EGYPTIAN MILITARY PLANES SWARMED PROVO in 2025 - Direct Flights to Charlie Kirk's Assassination Site. This Is No Coincidence." It credits [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) with "exposing SU-BTT &amp; SU-BND shadowing [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) events."

The attached log fragments show Provo and Minot legs with dates including 02 Jun 2025 and 27 May 2025 and landing times in the 11 AM to 1 PM range. The full versions of these logs, covering **SU-BTU**, **SU-BTV** and **SU-BGM** alongside the original two tail numbers, are preserved in the [5 Planes](/Photos/Aircraft/5_Planes/overview) sub-cluster.

The logs are public tracking records and can be checked. What they establish is routing and presence: Egyptian-registered aircraft repeatedly flying Cairo to Paris to a US Midwest stop and on to Provo across 2025. They do not establish purpose, and no passenger manifests have been released. The word "swarmed" and the coincidence claim are the poster's characterization.

Read [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) for the assembled pattern, and the individual pages [SU-BTU](/Planes/SU-BTU), [SU-BTV](/Planes/SU-BTV) and [SU-BGM](/Planes/SU-BGM).
