## What This Image Shows

A Google Maps mobile capture showing a walking route in downtown **Wilmington, Delaware**, from **919 N Market St** to **920 N King St**, marked as a **2 min** walk along a dotted blue path. Rodney Square, the Hotel du Pont, the DE.CO Food Hall, M&amp;T Bank and a DoubleTree by Hilton are all visible on the surrounding grid.

The point of the capture is proximity. Two addresses that appear separately in the investigation's Wilmington notes turn out to be a two-minute walk apart, which is the kind of geographic detail researchers use to argue that separately reported entities occupy the same small downtown block.

It is worth being clear about how weak that inference is on its own. Downtown Wilmington is a dense, compact grid where a very large share of American corporate registrations are addressed, so almost any two addresses in that area will be minutes apart. Proximity here is a starting point for a question, not an answer to one, and no connection between the entities at these addresses and the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) has been established.
