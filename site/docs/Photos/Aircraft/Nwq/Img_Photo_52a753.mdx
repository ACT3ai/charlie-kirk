## What This Image Shows

A mobile screenshot of an AI chat answer. The user asked "**How is Laisi and Leidos connected**," and the reply reads: "LAISI (often stylized as LASAI Aviation LLC) is the aviation division of **Leidos**, a major U.S.-based defense, aviation, and technology contractor. Leidos, headquartered in **Reston, Virginia**, provides services in areas like national security, health, and civil sectors, including extensive work with federal agencies such as the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the Department of Defense." Under "Key Connections" it adds that LAISI "operates as a subsidiary or specialized arm of Leidos, focusing on aviation-related operations... including managing flight services, aircraft operations, and support for government contracts."

**LASAI Aviation II LLC** is the registered operator publicly associated with [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview). Establishing what stands behind that name is the first step in the operator research, because a single-purpose aviation LLC tells you nothing while a major defense contractor tells you a great deal about what kind of flying the aircraft does.

The caution is that this is an AI-generated answer, not a corporate filing. Elsewhere in this cluster the same class of tool produced crew names it described as fabricated. The Leidos connection is corroborated by other sources here, but the ideal record would be the FAA registration and corporate filings themselves.
