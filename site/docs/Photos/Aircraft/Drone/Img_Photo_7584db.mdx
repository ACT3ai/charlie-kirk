## What This Image Shows

A wide, high-angle frame of the sunny campus plaza filled with people, the pink-to-blue gradient event tent in the lower left surrounded by students. A yellow circular callout with a magnified inset draws attention to a small dark object high in the sky against the mountains behind.

The only text burned into the frame is a timecode reading **12;24;00;05** and a **CROWDSOURCE THE TRUTH** watermark. That timecode places the frame roughly a minute after the [12:23 PM shot](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time).

The object is small, dark and unresolved. Researchers have offered frames like this as evidence of an aircraft overhead; the counter-readings are a bird, an insect close to the lens, or a compression artifact -- and the same account that circulated this frame has acknowledged that some objects in the same footage are "obviously birds and insects." Nothing at this resolution settles it.

What can be said is that the question is live and unanswered: [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) and UVU initially said no drones were flying, while [**Frank Turek**](/People/frank-turek) has said publicly that a TPUSA drone was up and that he and [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) reviewed its footage.
