## What This Image Shows

An annotated still from video: an outdoor daytime gathering on a campus plaza with a concrete pavilion structure, trees, a multi-storey building and mountains behind. A yellow circle and a magnified inset pick out a small blurry object in the sky.

Unlike its companion frame, this one carries no legible burned-in text -- the sidecar text extraction returns nothing. So there is no timecode, no watermark and no caption to date or source it from the image alone.

That absence matters when weighing it. A frame with a visible timecode can be placed in the sequence of events; a frame without one cannot, and it could come from any point in the footage or from a different day entirely. The magnification also does not help: enlarging a few pixels of a compressed video adds no information, it only makes the ambiguity larger on screen.

Held as one of several unresolved sky-object frames rather than as an identification of anything. See [Military Drones On Video](/Drones/Military_Drones_On_Video) and the [cluster overview](/Photos/Aircraft/Drone/overview).
