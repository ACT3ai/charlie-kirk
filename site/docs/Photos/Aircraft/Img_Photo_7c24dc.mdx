## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of two posts from X. The first, by **Donato Yaakov Secchi** (@doyaksec) and dated **Oct 21, 2024**, reads: "#Egypt #Intelligence. Al #Sisi has removed the head of intelligence, Abbas #Kamel, appointed advisor to the pres and replaced by his deputy, General Hassan Mahmoud #Rashad. #CIA and #Mossad have leaked #disturbance for the removal of Kamel. Come on! Al Sissi changes his strategies?"

The second, by **@Dinlas3** and dated **Aug 29, 2024**, reads: "#EGY Bombardier BD-700 w/ reg. T7-EGY utilized by The head of the Egyptian General Intelligence, Abbas Kamel from Cairo, Egypt to Port Sudan on 8/23 and went to Brazzaville, Co..."

Both predate September 10, 2025 and neither mentions it. They are filed here as background: aircraft-tracking accounts routinely link specific tail numbers to specific Egyptian officials, and the second post is an example of the method the Charlie Kirk flight thread later borrowed. The tail number named, **T7-EGY**, is a San Marino registration on a Bombardier BD-700 and is not one of the Egyptian-registered aircraft in this investigation.

Both posts are unverified third-party claims about foreign officials and their travel. See [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada) and [T7ELL and EJM36](/Planes/T7ELL-EJM36) for the related tail numbers.
