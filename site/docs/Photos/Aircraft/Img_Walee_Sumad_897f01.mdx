## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a **Google Scholar** academic profile. The entry reads "**Waleed E. Mahmoud**, Departement of Physics, Faculty of Science, **Suez Canal University**, Egypt. Verified email at science.suez.edu.eg. Homepage. Natural science / Physics." A profile photograph of a man in a white shirt, dark tie and dark hat appears at the left.

This is a name-search result, and it is included precisely because it illustrates a hazard in this thread. The investigation's notes carry several similar names -- Waleed Mahmoud, Walid Mahmoud Abdel Nasser, Waleed Mamood -- and searching them turns up unconnected people who happen to share common Arabic given names and patronymics.

The person shown here is an academic physicist at an Egyptian university with a public scholarly record. There is no indication he has any connection to any aircraft, to any diplomat, or to the events of **September 10, 2025**, and none is suggested. Publishing this capture is a caution about identification, not an identification: a matching name in a search engine is not evidence about anybody.

The separate diplomat's published biography is on [Photo 89](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Walid_Mahmoud_Abdel_4761b0). For the flight records, see [SU-BTT Israel/Egyptian Jet](/Planes/SU-BTT).
