## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of an X post by **Rebecca Ramaley** (@RebeccaRamaley), dated Sep 20, with a cropped FlightAware map attached. The post asks what the same plane was doing circling over Fallon / Pyramid Lake, Nevada later the same day, and offers two speculative possibilities as a question: a data dump, or an equipment transfer.

The FlightAware panel shows a green track across the southwest with Los Angeles, San Diego, Phoenix and New Mexico labelled, an origin marker at **BIF** -- Biggs Army Airfield, El Paso -- and flight times reading Takeoff 05:52PM PDT, Landing 09:44PM MDT. The Biggs origin matches the departure airfield researchers report for [**N1098L**](/Planes/N1098L/overview) on [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025).

The two suggested explanations in the post, a data dump or an equipment transfer, are speculation offered as questions rather than conclusions, and the poster frames them that way. Fallon hosts a US Navy air station, so military aircraft operating in that airspace is not by itself unusual. What the post adds to the record is a documented continuation of the day's track after the Utah passes.
