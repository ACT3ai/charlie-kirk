## What This Image Shows

A three-column flight log covering **December 2024 into January 2025**. Almost every row is Cairo (CAI) to Cairo (CAI): **22, 19, 18, 15, 14** and **04 January**; **30, 29, 24, 22, 21, 14, 11, 10, 07, 03** and **01 December**. The exceptions are **21 Dec** Giza (SPX) to Cairo and **14 Dec** Hurghada (HRG) to Cairo.

This is the aircraft's baseline. Weeks of short local flights out of Cairo, with occasional domestic hops to Giza and the Red Sea coast -- the profile of a government transport aircraft doing crew currency, maintenance checks and short official movements.

Its value in this investigation is corrective. Several claims in circulation placed **SU-BTT** in Wichita, Paris or Wilmington on dates in this window; the audit sheets in this cluster mark those entries "Incorrect - in Cairo," and this log is the record that supports those corrections. Anyone weighing the flight-overlap timeline should read this page first.
