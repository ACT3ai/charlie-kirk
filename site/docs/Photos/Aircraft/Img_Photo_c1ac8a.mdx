## What This Image Shows

A high-angle aerial across the campus plaza -- large concrete buildings, paved walkways, landscaped lawns, and the crowd gathered in the small semi-circular grass amphitheatre near the bottom of the frame. Watermarked **@universehop00**.

This frame gives the fullest sense of scale in the set: the amphitheatre occupies a small part of a large complex, ringed on several sides by multi-storey structures. That relationship is the central fact of the venue and the origin of the security criticism -- **Frank Turek** has said publicly that he warned Charlie Kirk there were "too many buildings" at the site.

As with the rest of this group, the origin claim -- that these are leaked images from the **N59906** survey flight -- is unverified. No metadata has been published, and no agency or contractor has confirmed a release. The frames are useful for geometry regardless of where they came from, which is why they are kept, but their provenance should not be assumed.
