## What This Image Shows

A Flightradar24 mobile flight list headed AIRCRAFT **SU-BND**, Year 2025.

Reading down: **Sep 15** Goose Bay (YYR) to Paris (LBG); **Sep 13** Provo (PVU) to Goose Bay (YYR); **Sep 05** Provo (PVU) to Provo (PVU); **Jun 10** Provo (PVU) to Provo (PVU); **May 23** Minot (MOT) to Provo (PVU); **May 23** Goose Bay (YYR) to Minot (MOT); **May 23** Paris (LBG) to Goose Bay (YYR); **May 22** Cairo (CAI) to Paris (LBG).

Two features stand out. The May 22 to May 23 sequence is a single continuous ferry -- Cairo, Paris, Goose Bay, Minot, Provo -- flown across two calendar days, which is the standard trans-Atlantic routing for a Gulfstream-class aircraft. And the entries showing Provo to Provo on June 10 and September 5 are local circuits from the same field, the pattern also visible on the playback card elsewhere in this cluster.

The September 5 local flight falls the day after the Egyptian Falcon **SU-BTT** arrived at Provo, and the September 13 departure follows the shooting by three days. Those are documented dates from public tracking. What the aircraft was doing, and who was aboard, is not documented anywhere.
