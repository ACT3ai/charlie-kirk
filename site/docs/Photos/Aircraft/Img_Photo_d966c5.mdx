## What This Image Shows

A high-angle aerial photograph of the crowd concentrated in the terraced amphitheatre between the interconnected academic buildings at the campus. The image carries a **[@universehop00](/Influencers/x/universehop00)** watermark.

That account is the origin of the leaked-photograph claim. Public reporting describes a TikTok user posting a set of high-resolution aerial images on **October 13, 2025**, said to come from the **N59906** survey flight of [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), with the account holding no other content -- a pattern researchers read as a deliberate, targeted release from someone with access.

The claim is unverified in both directions. No metadata has been published tying these frames to that aircraft, no contractor or agency has confirmed a release, and the images could equally be from a drone, a helicopter, or a different survey pass. What can be said is that they show the venue populated, from overhead, in daylight -- which is why they matter for reconstructing where people and equipment were.

See [N59906](/Planes/N59906) for the survey-flight analysis, the ADS-B capture on [Photo 64](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_d7340e), and the rest of the watermarked set in this cluster.
