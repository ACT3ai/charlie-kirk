## About This Cluster

**SU-BTT** is an Egyptian-registered Dassault Falcon 7X that researchers have placed at Provo Municipal Airport (PVU) during the week of the **September 10, 2025** shooting at Utah Valley University. **Candace Owens** called it "the yellow plane" on her podcast, and independent trackers say it flew Paris to Minot to Provo on September 4, sat on the ground for six days, then departed Provo for Wilmington, Delaware on September 10 before continuing to Cairo the next day. Provo airport sits roughly four miles from the UVU campus. The tail number matters because it is one of only a handful of foreign government aircraft researchers have been able to place near the event on public ADS-B data, which is why this cluster exists as its own page.

This cluster holds the flight-tracker capture that anchors the SU-BTT timeline. The screenshot shows the aircraft's detail panel with the origin, the destination, and a playback clock set to September 10, 2025, so the departure can be read directly off the tracking tool rather than taken on assertion. Researchers have raised questions about the aircraft's earlier movements — its reported first-ever US arrival in July 2025, a second visit in August, and the Wilmington destination shared with other Egyptian-registered jets — but the passenger manifests have never been released, and no official statement has confirmed who was aboard or why the aircraft was in Utah. Everything beyond the raw tracking data remains a reported claim.
