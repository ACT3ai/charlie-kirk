## What This Image Shows

A medium close-up of an older man with a full white beard, wearing dark sunglasses and a white baseball cap with a gold **47** on the front, holding a smartphone in both hands. A utility vehicle enclosure is beside him and the grille of a large white truck fills the background.

The image sits in the drone folder because of a claim that circulated after the event -- that an eyewitness photograph showed a man in the crowd operating a drone -- and researchers assembled frames of individuals seen holding devices while comparing them. Whether this frame belongs to that comparison, and who the man is, is not established by anything visible in it.

So the honest reading is limited: a person is holding a phone. Holding a phone at a public gathering is what nearly everyone present was doing, and this site does not identify the individual or suggest that he did anything improper. Any identification circulating elsewhere should be treated as an unverified claim about a living person.
