## What This Image Shows

A second Google Maps capture of **Tuba City Airport**, this one on satellite view with the terrain and runway visible, alongside the same information card -- Arizona 86045, rated 3.4 from nine reviews, popular-times chart, phone number.

The satellite view adds what the road map cannot: the field is a single strip in open high desert with almost no supporting infrastructure. That is the physical fact underneath the "super-remote" characterization in the claim about where **N888KG** may have been heading.

It cuts both ways as proof. A strip like this would be unobserved, which is the poster's point. It would also be unlit, unstaffed and unattractive for a jet arrival, which is the counter-point. Nothing in the tracking record shows the aircraft landing here or anywhere else during the coverage gap, and no arrival at Tuba City has been documented.
