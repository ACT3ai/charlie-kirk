## What This Image Shows

A composite capture of the post by **Stellar Man** (@stellarman22), screenshotted on the morning of [**September 11, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13), roughly fifteen hours after it was published.

The text and overlay match the version on [Photo 23](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_c132eb): the aircraft "left Provo airport an hour after the shooting," "drops off of ADS-B Illegally," and "reappears headed back to Provo approx. an hour later," with the UTC timeline giving Assassination 18:10, Take off 19:12, transponder loss 19:44, reappearance 20:30, and the FlightAware note that the tail number "is no[t available for public tracking]."

The capture time is what makes this copy worth keeping separately. It fixes the claim to within a day of the event, before the owner's public statement was issued, which lets a reader see the sequence: the tracking observation came first, and the owner's account of a pre-scheduled round trip to Page came afterward. Whether the two can be reconciled -- particularly the difference between the posted 19:12 UTC departure and the roughly 1:20 PM MT departure the owner describes -- is an open question nobody has resolved publicly.
