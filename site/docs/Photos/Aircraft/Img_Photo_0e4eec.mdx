## What This Image Shows

A low-resolution outdoor photograph of a small white four-legged robot -- a quadruped of the type sold for research and demonstration use -- caught mid-jump on a building balcony, with onlookers watching from behind a railing. Trees, a distant mountain and blue sky fill the background.

This is filed in the drone folder because it belongs to the same general question researchers were chasing: what unmanned systems were on or near the [UVU campus](/UVU/overview), and who brought them. A quadruped robot is not a drone and has no bearing on anything in the air, but a demonstration of robotics equipment on campus is the kind of ordinary activity that has to be identified and set aside before an unexplained sighting means anything.

Nothing in the frame ties it to [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). There is no timestamp, no event signage, and nothing that fixes the date. Treat it as unverified context rather than proof.
