## About This Cluster

The image here is a screenshot of a post from the "Project Constitution" account laying out the "Egyptian armada" theory: the idea that a fleet of Egyptian-registered private jets - the tail numbers [SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT), [SU-BND](/Planes/SU-BND) and others discussed elsewhere in this section - shadowed [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) in the days around September 10, 2025. The post bundles flight-log text with small thumbnails of spreadsheets and a private jet, tagging multiple accounts.

This is one of the most contested threads in the whole investigation, and the honest state of it is that much of the specific claim has been debunked. An independent data review of the circulating flight claims found that a large majority - reported by one analyst as roughly two-thirds - were inaccurate, with most of the alleged flights showing the aircraft on a different continent at the stated time. Publicly, Duncan Aviation has had an announced Egyptian Air Force maintenance contract since 1999, and U.S. Central Command's BRIGHT STAR 25 exercise release is dated September 8, 2025 - both of which offer innocent explanations for Egyptian-aircraft activity.

The screenshot is preserved as a record of what was asserted, not as confirmation of it. Only a small number of Provo-area rows survived the scrutiny; the sweeping "armada" framing did not.
