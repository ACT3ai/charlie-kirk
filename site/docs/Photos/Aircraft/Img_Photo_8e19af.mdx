## What This Image Shows

A two-page magazine layout headed "**Special Forces - Egypt**," described as "Part Five of a series that takes a look at Special Forces units around the world." The text summarizes the Egyptian Armed Forces -- Army, Navy, Air Force and Air Defence Command -- and their involvement in conflicts from the 1952 revolution through the Suez Crisis, the North Yemen Civil War, the war against ISIL and the Sinai insurgency. Photographs of troops accompany the columns.

This is published reference material, not evidence. It sits in the Egyptian aircraft folder because of a claim that circulated after the flight logs emerged: that Egypt's elite **El Sa'ka** unit, trained near Cairo, could be relevant given the aircraft's origin. That connection is speculation built on geography, and the article itself makes no claim about anything in this case.

Stating it plainly: nothing here links any Egyptian military unit to the events of **September 10, 2025**. Egypt is a long-standing United States security partner whose forces train jointly with American units, which is documented separately in this cluster by the BRIGHT STAR exercise release.
