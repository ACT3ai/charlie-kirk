## What This Image Shows

A slightly blurred wide shot of a large crowd on and below a multi-level concrete structure -- a long line of people along an elevated walkway behind metal railing, some holding signs, with more gathered on the level below.

This is one of four near-identical frames filed together in the drone folder, all of the same gathering from marginally different angles. Sequences like this are how researchers work out who was standing where and what each vantage point could see, which is the necessary groundwork before any claim about a camera position or a sightline can be tested.

As with its companions, nothing in the frame dates it or identifies the occasion, and the signs visible in the group suggest a demonstration rather than the TPUSA event. Treat it as unverified context.
