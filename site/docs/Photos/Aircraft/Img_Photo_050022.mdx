## What This Image Shows

A low-angle exterior photograph of a multi-level concrete building on a bright day. People stand along an upper elevated walkway behind metal railing, some holding a rainbow flag and signs, with a dense crowd at lower right.

The fourth of the near-identical frames in this group. Taken together they cover the same gathering from ground level, from a distance, and at an angle, which is what makes them useful for reconstructing positions -- who occupied the upper walkway, how full the lower level was, what a person at either level could see.

The flag and signs again indicate a demonstration rather than the [September 10](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) speaking event, and no timestamp or signage in any of the four frames fixes the date. They are held here as unverified context filed with the drone research rather than as a finding about the assassination.
