## What This Image Shows

A flight-log table for **SU-BTT** covering the weeks after the shooting, in descending date order.

Representative rows: **18 Nov 2025** Cairo to Cairo, 0:19; **26 Oct 2025** Cairo to Cairo, 2:24; **22 Oct** Cairo to Cairo, 2:14; **21 Oct** Cairo to Cairo, 2:43; **16 Oct** Medina (MED) to Cairo, 1:31 and Cairo to Medina, 1:28; **12 Oct** Cairo to Sharm el-Sheikh (SSH), 0:38; **11 Oct** Jeddah to Cairo and Cairo to Jeddah; **30 Sep** Cairo to Cairo.

The pattern after September is entirely regional -- Egypt, Saudi Arabia, the Red Sea coast -- with no further United States legs in the visible window. That is the aircraft returning to ordinary government-transport work, and it is the natural comparison set for the trans-Atlantic runs earlier in the year.

It is worth noting what this log does for the strongest versions of the claim. An aircraft that resumed routine regional flying within weeks, and kept a public transponder trail while doing so, is not behaving like one being concealed. The unanswered questions about the September 4 to September 10 [Provo](/Locations/Provo_Airport) stay are not resolved by that, but they should be weighed alongside it.
