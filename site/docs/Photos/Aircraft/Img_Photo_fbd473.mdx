## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a post on X by [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) (@RealCandaceO), dated October 11. Two images are attached to the post: a cropped flight-tracker detail panel on the left, and a scrolling column of flight-history rows on the right.

The post reads, in part: "BREAKING NEWS! On September 10th, after Charlie's assasination, an Egyptian Air Force plane flew out of Provo, Utah. Tail number: SU-BTT... It's first trip to America EVER was made on July 20t-to an army base in Nebraska. Tyler Robinson must have had some powerful friends!" The attached tracker panel shows a Dassault Falcon 7X, registration **SU-BTT**, listed as Egypt Government, routed to ILG Wilmington, with a playback clock reading Wed, Sep 10, 2025, 13:08 UTC and barometric altitude 0 ft. The history column below shows repeated Cairo legs through the year.

This is the post that put the Egyptian aircraft thread into wide circulation. The closing line about [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is rhetorical framing rather than a claim of any established link between the aircraft and the accused; no manifest, passenger list, or official statement has connected the two. What the tracker data supports on its own is the aircraft's presence at Provo and its departure that day.

The written analysis is on [SU-BTT Israel/Egyptian Jet](/Planes/SU-BTT), the fleet pattern is at [Egyptian Armada](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada), and the day-of sequence is at [Sept 10 Flight Timeline](/Planes/Sept10-Flight-Timeline).
