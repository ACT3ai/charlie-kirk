## What This Image Shows

The cover page of the document set: a formal complaint dated **20 November 2019**, addressed to a Romanian prosecutorial body, submitted by a former translator who states she was employed at a base in that country between 2008 and 2014. Several passages on the page have been highlighted.

This identifies the whole nine-page group in this folder as a single 2019 Romanian legal filing. It is not connected to the Charlie Kirk investigation. No aircraft, person, date, or location in this document overlaps with anything in the flight evidence, and the pages appear to have been swept into the folder during an unsorted import of research material.

The complaint names individuals and levels serious allegations against them. None of that is reproduced on this site. The description here is limited to the document's type, date and origin, which is what a reader needs in order to understand why the image is in the corpus and to set it aside.
