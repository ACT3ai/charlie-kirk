## What This Image Shows

A three-column compiled timeline in black, blue and yellow text, pairing aircraft itineraries with public events across **2022, 2023, 2024 and 2025**. The scan quality is poor and parts of the text are garbled.

Legible fragments include: "**BTT departs for Wilmington** at 8:54am. Leaves for Cairo on November..."; "**December 20th: BTT Cairo to Paris to St Louis** arriving at 11:37 am"; "**February 20th: Sean Feucht announced as heading up the Kingdom to the Capitol**" tour; "**March 14th: Charlie speaks at UC Davis**"; "**March 21st: Charlie and Candace speak in Baton Rouge, LA**."

This is the narrative version of the correlation argument -- aircraft movements and speaking engagements interleaved in prose rather than plotted on a chart. Read alongside the spreadsheet and the audit sheets in this cluster, it shows how the case was assembled: raw logs into a workbook, workbook into a timeline, timeline into an infographic.

Each step compounds any error in the layer below. Since the audit found several of the underlying flight entries to be duplicates or wrong, this document should be read with the corrections in hand rather than on its own.

See the workbook on [Photo 108](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_e0b9e3), the audits on [Photo 96](/Photos/Aircraft/Img_Photo_15a6be), and [Following Charlie and Erika](/Planes/Following-Charlie-Erika).
