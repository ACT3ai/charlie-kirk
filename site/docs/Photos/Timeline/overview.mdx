## About This Cluster

The images here are not photographs of the event itself. They are broadcast stills and split-screen video frames in which an on-screen chronology is being read to an audience. Most are captures from commentator **Candace Owens** shows, where the left of the frame holds the host at a desk and the right holds a scrolling document - a Google Docs or word-processor timeline of dated entries, lines highlighted in yellow, blue, purple, and red as each point is walked through. The spans range from a May-to-July run of events to the tight September 10 to September 22 window around and after the shooting.

An image-based chronology matters because the core dispute in this case is about **sequence and timing**. The shot was fired at roughly **12:23 PM Mountain Time on September 10, 2025**, and much of the investigation turns on the seconds and minutes on either side of that mark - who was where, which calls were placed when, and whether the released timestamps line up. Freezing these on-air timelines as stills preserves which dates and claims were presented publicly, so a viewer can weigh them against other records rather than trust memory of a livestream.

Every entry inside these images is the presenter's assertion, reported here with attribution rather than adopted as settled fact.
