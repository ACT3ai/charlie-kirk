## What This Image Shows

The final slide in the olive-green series, and the one that states the series' thesis outright. The top half carries the same **Pastor Rob McCoy** bullets and the same red-boxed line about his father's naval psychological-warfare role, watermarked **X @THEINTELSCIF**.

The bottom half is an organisational chart headed **"September 10th Personnel."** The names on it are **Frank Turek, Rob McCoy, Andrew Kolvet, Mikey McCoy** and **Tyler Bowyer**, arranged as a linked network.

That heading is doing a great deal of work. It groups five named living people under a date and invites the reader to infer significance from the grouping alone, without stating any allegation that could be tested. **This site makes no claim that any of these men had knowledge of, or involvement in, what happened on September 10, 2025.** None has been charged with or accused of an offence by any authority. The graphic is reproduced so the claim can be seen and weighed rather than repeated secondhand.
