## What This Image Shows

A fourth slide from the same sage-green series. The upper half is a cropped composition — the studio video still, the square portrait, the bullet block and the red box, with the cursive "Candace" watermark. The lower half repeats the identical bullet text, larger and cleaner, beside a bigger portrait of the same smiling man in a white button-down.

Both blocks read the same: **"Pastor Rob McCoy — Father was Naval Captain Roy Edgar McCoy. Father was also the assistant chief of staff for psychological warfare in the Navy."** The graphic is watermarked **X @THEINTELSCIF**.

Printing one claim twice on a single slide is a presentation choice, not new evidence. The underlying assertion concerns a man's late father's Navy assignment, which — if accurate — is a matter of historical service record and says nothing about his son. No wrongdoing by Rob McCoy is alleged here and he has not been charged with anything in connection with this case.
