## What This Image Shows

A second slide in the same olive-green series, watermarked **X @THEINTELSCIF** and carrying the faint "Candace" signature mark. The top half repeats the **Pastor Rob McCoy** bullets — "Father was Naval Captain Roy Edgar McCoy" and the red-boxed line "Father was also the assistant chief of staff for psychological warfare in the Navy," with the words "psychological warfare" underlined in red.

The bottom half introduces **Dr. Lee Trotter**, beneath a studio portrait, with two bullets: "Completed a residency at Keesler Air Force Medical Center in Mississippi" and "He is also a pilot, likely in the Air Force." Note the hedge in the second bullet — the word "likely" is the graphic's own, and marks it as inference rather than verified fact.

The series is building a cumulative impression rather than a specific allegation. A medical residency at a military hospital is a normal career path for a physician, and holding a pilot's licence is not a security matter. No wrongdoing by either man is alleged here, and neither has been charged with anything in connection with this case.
