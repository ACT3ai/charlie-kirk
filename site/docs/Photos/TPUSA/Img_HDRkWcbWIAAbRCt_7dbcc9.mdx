## What This Image Shows

A layered outdoor event photograph. In the immediate foreground, in right profile, is a middle-aged man in a grey collared shirt and thin rectangular glasses with a **clear coiled acoustic-tube earpiece** running down behind his right ear — the standard security or staff communications rig. At the bottom right, red and white baseball caps are stacked in neat rows on a table under dark blue cloth. Behind him a woman with long hair works a professional video camera mounted on a monopod. Beyond the metal crowd barrier stands a dense crowd wearing branded merchandise.

Three of the recurring questions in this investigation are visible in a single frame: who was on the security communications net, who was operating cameras, and where the physical barrier line sat between staff and public.

Nobody in the photograph is identified and no wrongdoing by anyone visible is alleged. The value is documentary — it shows the operational layout of a Turning Point event rather than any individual.
