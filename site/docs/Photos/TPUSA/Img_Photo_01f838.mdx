## What This Image Shows

A third slide in the olive-green series, again watermarked **X @THEINTELSCIF** with the faint "Candace" signature. The top half carries the by-now familiar **Pastor [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy)** bullets, with the red box around "Father was also the assistant chief of staff for psychological warfare in the Navy."

The bottom half is a single line under a portrait: **"Dr. Deirdre Smith Amaro — Is a Major in the Army."** That is the entire assertion. No date, no unit, no source and no explanation of relevance travels with it.

Holding a commission in the U.S. Army Reserve or National Guard while practising medicine is common and lawful, and this graphic offers no argument connecting it to anything. It is filed as part of the series so the pattern of the series is visible: repeat one red-boxed claim across every slide and pair it with a different name each time. No wrongdoing is alleged against anyone named. See [Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro), [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy) and [Medical](/Medical/overview).
