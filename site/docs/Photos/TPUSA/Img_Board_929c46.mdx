## What This Image Shows

A clean screenshot of an organisation's **Board of Directors** page, with the "Governance" tab selected in dark red and "Staff" beside it in grey. Six circular headshots are arranged in two rows against an off-white background.

The names printed beneath them are **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk), Erika Kirk, David Engelhardt, [Doug DeGroote](/People/doug-degroote), Mike Miller** and **Tom Sodeika**. That is the full published board.

This is a primary source published by the organisation itself, and it is the reference point for the governance strand of this investigation — who sat on the board before September 10, 2025, and how leadership changed afterward. Board membership is a matter of public disclosure and carries no implication of wrongdoing by any of the six. See [Doug DeGroote](/Photos/TPUSA/Doug_DeGroote/overview) for the post-event internal message, [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk), [David Engelhardt](/People/david-engelhardt), [Mike Miller](/People/mike-miller), [Tom Sodeika](/People/tom-sodeika), and [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview).
