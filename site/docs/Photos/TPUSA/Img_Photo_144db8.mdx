## What This Image Shows

A split-screen broadcast still. On the left, a presenter in a white blazer speaks into a boom microphone in a studio with a dark blue wall behind her. On the right, a large beige graphic panel watermarked with a faint portrait and the cursive word **"Candace"**, headed **"Mikey McCoy Call Logs."**

The panel lists a minute-by-minute sequence: "12:02 Danny McCoy calls Mikey McCoy. 12:23 [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) shot. 12:24 Charlie brought to the SUV by his security detail. 12:24 Mikey McCoy calls [Elizabeth McCoy](/People/elizabeth-mccoy). They will stay on the phone for 10 mins. 12:24 Blake Neff calls his mother. Call will last 45 seconds. 12:25 Blake Neff's phone call ends with his mother. 12:26 Mikey McCoy adds Erika to the Call. 12:28 Mikey McCoy drops Erika from the phone call. 12:30 Mikey McCoy then adds his father Rob to the phone call with him and Elizabeth. 12:32 Mikey McCoy ends his phone call with his Rob McCoy. 12:34 Mikey McCoy ends his phone call with Elizabeth McCoy. 12:35 Danny McCoy calls Mike McCoy again."

This is a presenter's reconstruction shown on a programme, not a court exhibit or a carrier record produced in court. Its provenance and accuracy have not been independently established here. People call their families in a crisis, and doing so implies nothing. Nobody named on the panel has been charged with or accused of a crime in any court, and no such claim is made here. See [Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy), [Rob McCoy](/People/rob-mccoy), [Blake Neff](/People/blake-neff), [Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk) and [Timeline](/Timeline/overview).
