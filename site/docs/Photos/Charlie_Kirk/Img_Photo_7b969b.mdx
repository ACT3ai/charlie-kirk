## What This Image Shows

A phone screenshot of a post on X by the account Polly St George, captured four hours after posting. The post text reads: It enters near his right ear. There is video but I can not post it here. This is a still from that video. See the blood on his right ear/ temple? Below the text sits an embedded video player showing a low-resolution still: a person in a white t-shirt with a dark red streak running down the front, holding a microphone, standing before a step-and-repeat backdrop carrying TURNING POINT USA and YREFY.COM branding. The engagement counter reads 235.

The claim is a wound-location claim, and it matters because wound location is the fault line of the whole case. The published account describes a projectile recovered in the neck with no exit wound. A separate group of researchers argues the fatal injury originated at the stage rather than from a rooftop. An entry point near the right ear or temple, if that is what the still shows, would sit awkwardly with more than one of those accounts, which is why the poster is asking rather than asserting.

The still is low-resolution and heavily compressed; at that quality a shadow, a strand of hair, and a blood trace are difficult to separate, and the poster frames the observation as a question. The same screenshot appears in the After cluster.
