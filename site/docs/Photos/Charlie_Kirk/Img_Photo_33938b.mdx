## What This Image Shows

A three-quarter rear exterior photograph of a gloss-black GMC Yukon Denali, its length consistent with the extended XL variant. The paint reflects the surrounding lot and adjacent vehicles; the windows are heavily tinted. Black roof crossbars sit on silver rails, with chrome along the lower window line, side steps, liftgate garnish, and rear bumper. The wheels are large multi-spoke alloys with machined faces. The rear shows wraparound C-shaped LED lamps, an embossed DENALI strip above the plate recess, a YUKON badge at lower left, and quad exhaust tips. A white utility van is parked close at right, a silver sedan at left on gravel, and the rear corner of a dark red car is cropped in the foreground.

This is a commercial-style vehicle reference photograph, the kind produced for storage, insurance, or auction listing.
