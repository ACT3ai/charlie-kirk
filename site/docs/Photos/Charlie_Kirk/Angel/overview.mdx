## About This Cluster

This cluster holds the memorial imagery made for **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** after **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** — tribute graphics that supporters composited over the [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview) campus where he was killed. It belongs in the photo record for two reasons. The obvious one is that these images are how a large part of the public processed the event, and they circulated widely enough to become part of the story. The less obvious one is that the underlying photograph is itself documentary: it shows the UVU plaza, its terraced lawn steps, and the state of the grounds.

The image in this cluster overlays a translucent figure with wings above the UVU building, with the university's own window banners reading "UVU" and "A PLACE FOR YOU" visible behind it. In the middle ground, a work crew is laying stone pavers on a freshly prepped dirt and gravel bed, with a compact excavator and pickup trucks parked in the covered bypass below. That construction detail is worth noting on its own: questions about who performed the landscaping and hardscape work around the event area, and when, are an active thread in the investigation. Nothing here establishes any link between that work and the shooting — the point is only that the photograph records the site's condition.

Look at the underlying campus detail as carefully as the overlay, then follow the site's written coverage of the venue and the ground work: [The Tent](/Tent/overview), [UVU](/UVU/overview), and [Locations](/Locations/overview). Peer clusters worth opening next are [Charlie Kirk Himself](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/overview) for the stage frames and [After](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/After/overview) for what the same area looked like in the minutes following the shot.
