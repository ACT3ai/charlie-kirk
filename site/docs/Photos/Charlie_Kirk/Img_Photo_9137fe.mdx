## What This Image Shows

A wide frame of the plaza. In the foreground a dense crowd is huddled to the ground: a young man in a white t-shirt looking right with his mouth open, another crouched in a beige cap, a man in grey lying flat on his side, more bodies pressed together including one bent double and one in a yellow graphic shirt, and a woman in sunglasses and a floral top seated and looking toward the commotion. A line of silver interlocking bike-rack barricades separates them from the security zone. Behind the barricades an officer in a dark blue short-sleeved uniform with a shoulder badge faces right, grimacing, while several men in black shirts tackle or hold down an unseen person; a single white sneaker is kicked up in the scuffle. Black SUVs are parked beyond, under the shadow of a concrete overpass structure.

This frame captures a detention at the barricade line in the immediate aftermath, with the crowd already on the ground. The identity of the person being restrained, and whether this is the individual later released or one of the others detained and released that day, is not established by the image.

Several people were reportedly detained and let go at the scene on [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and the handling of those detentions is itself part of the investigation. No person is identified here and no conduct is attributed to anyone.
