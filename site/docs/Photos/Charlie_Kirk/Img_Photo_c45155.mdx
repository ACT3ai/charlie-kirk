## What This Image Shows

A still frame from video, shot across an open light-grey concrete plaza. A group of men in semi-tactical casual dress — polo shirts, dark t-shirts, khaki and olive cargo pants — are moving fast while physically supporting a man in a light-colored shirt whose head is slumped forward and whose body is bent low. One man in a black shirt and sunglasses leads; another in a plaid shirt guides the path toward a large glossy black SUV whose chrome grille is visible at frame right. Behind them rises the concrete structure of the campus building with its dark window band and rooftop railing. A dense crowd stands behind a barrier at frame left, one woman running forward in visible distress. Overlay text on the frame reads Charlie being rushed to the hospital, with a fragment of the underlying audio caption reading gang violence.

That caption fragment is significant on its own. Multiple witnesses have said [Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was speaking about gang violence at the moment he was hit, and the words surface in the broadcast captions of the earliest video. This frame is also the clearest available record of the transfer to the vehicle — who was carrying, in what formation, and toward which SUV.

The man in the plaid shirt appears in several frames from this sequence and is a recurring subject of identification efforts; no claim about any individual is made here. For the transport, see [Charlie Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital) and [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie).
