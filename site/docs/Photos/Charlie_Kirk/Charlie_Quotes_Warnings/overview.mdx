## About This Cluster

This cluster gathers imagery around the statements attributed to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) that, in hindsight, read as warnings about his own safety. The single image here is a screenshot of the Grok AI interface on X, capturing a user's request for a list of Kirk quotes touching on threats to his life. Below the prompt sits a system error message: the AI returned nothing. Whether by design or by ordinary failure, the empty result became part of the story people were telling about how hard these quotes were to surface.

The reported quotes themselves are second-hand. The most circulated versions have Kirk allegedly saying, around mid-August 2025, some form of "if I go against Israel, I think they will kill me" or "they will kill me if I break away from being pro-Israel." According to the accounts, these were told to friends or associates and later posted online. No verified first-hand audio or video of Kirk speaking these exact words has been established.

Because these are attributed statements rather than confirmed recordings, they are reported here with that caveat rather than treated as settled fact. Open the image for its full description, and see the written record linked below for how these reported warnings fit the wider timeline.
