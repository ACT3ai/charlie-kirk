## About This Cluster

This cluster is the visual record of **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** as a person and as a body of material — what he wore, what he said, what he wrote, and what happened to him on **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)** at [Utah Valley University](/UVU/overview). It sits at the center of the case because almost every disputed question runs through his own words and his own body: the reported warnings he sent friends in the weeks before, the microphone and lanyard visible on his collar, the wound location that witnesses described inconsistently, and the vehicle he was carried to. The images here are the primary exhibits behind those questions, pulled from broadcast frames, social-media posts, legislative-tracking screenshots, and photos researchers attribute to **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)**.

Four threads dominate this set. The first is the **stage frames** — cropped, low-resolution stills of Kirk in the white "FREEDOM" shirt with a lavalier mic clipped at his collar, several annotated with red arrows pointing at the neck and shirt placket, and one posted by researcher **Polly St George** with the caption "It enters near his right ear. There is video but I can't post it here… See the blood on his right ear/temple?" The second is the **SUV interior set** — third-row seats, footwells, stained upholstery, a punctured plastic panel, and a charred fragment on a dashboard-textured surface, alongside a seating diagram labeling positions for Dan, Justin, Brian, CK, Rick, and Frank; these photos also appear on [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie). The third is the **autopsy-law thread**: screenshots of Utah **S.B. 82 "Autopsy Photo Amendments"**, signed **March 26, 2025**, which the accompanying graphic states "criminalizes the sharing of autopsy photos," plus a Google Trends panel that researcher **[Sam Parker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker)** presents as showing searches for the bill's drafting attorney spiking from Israeli IP addresses in June 2024 and May 2025 — an inference the poster draws, not an established fact. The fourth is a screenshot of an AI assistant summarizing the official finding: "exsanguination and cardiorespiratory failure, with the manner ruled homicide… the bullet was recovered lodged in the neck with no exit wound."

Start with the stage frames and the annotated neck images on this page, then work outward into the sub-clusters: [Text Msgs](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/Text_Msgs/overview) for the reported warnings and donor conflicts, [Quotes](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/Quotes/overview) for the posts recording what people say he told them, [After](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/After/overview) for the tent aftermath, and [Mystery Woman](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/Mystery_Woman/overview) for the medical-examiner sign-off question. For the written case behind these images, see [Charlie Kirk](/Charlie/overview), [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings), [Utah Autopsy Law Changes](/Medical/utah_autopsy_law_changes), and [The Medical Examiner](/Medical/medical_examiner).
