## About This Cluster

These images are screenshots of social media posts tied to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and the Jeffrey Epstein files. One is a post from Kirk's own X account laying out allegations about Epstein's calendar and his meetings with prominent political and public figures. Another, from the account TPV Sean, carries a claim that Kirk was granted a look at Epstein's client list before Epstein's death. A third continues the same thread of posts and commentary.

The cluster matters because Kirk was one of the louder public voices pressing for the release of the Epstein files, and because that stance sits inside several motive theories the investigation tracks. The images do not prove any of the underlying claims; they document what was being posted and circulated about Kirk and Epstein, including material that surfaced or spread in the period around his death.

The statements inside these screenshots are the posters' assertions, reported here with attribution rather than adopted as fact.
