## What This Image Shows

A high-angle interior view of the third row. A torn, crumpled, heavily grease-stained brown paper bag with a light tan grid print lies across the split bench, dark brown splotches concentrated around its edges and folds. A white thermal order receipt is attached to its left side; the visible customer field reads a first name and a last initial. A white flyer with blue and black text is tucked into the folds at right, and white string handles lie limp across the black leather cushions. The seats are dark charcoal leather with light contrast stitching and adjustable headrests. The interior sidewall at left carries a moulded armrest with cup holders, a small compartment, a vertical vent, and a seatbelt anchor with the strap hanging down. A clear water bottle sits partly in frame at lower left.
