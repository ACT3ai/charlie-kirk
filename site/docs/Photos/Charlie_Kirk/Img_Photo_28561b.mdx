## What This Image Shows

A chaotic, motion-blurred frame captured from a camera at or near ground level. Deep blue canvas fabric dominates the left foreground in soft folds, with a dark indistinct smudge along its upper curve. A rigid light-grey plastic panel with moulded structural ridges lies flat on the grass across the lower frame, carrying a dark reddish-brown stain near its left hinge. A folding utility table with a laminate top is tilted sharply upward at left, a small dark rectangular object resting on its surface, its white scissor-legs crossing beneath. A small bright blue sphere sits in the grass by the legs. A round object with a white outer edge and a reddish-pink center is caught mid-flight and heavily blurred. At right, a black folding lawn chair tumbles upside-down in mid-air.

Frames like this record the physical disturbance of the tent area in the seconds after the shot, when the crowd and staff moved. What is on the ground, what is knocked over, and what is stained are the details researchers use to argue about where people were and what was displaced.

Nothing here is interpreted. The blur and the ground-level angle make identification of individual objects unreliable, and the dark marks are not characterized.
