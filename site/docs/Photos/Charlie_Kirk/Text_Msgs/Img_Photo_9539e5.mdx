## What This Image Shows

A vertical iMessage screenshot. The contact header reads Charlie with the same suit-and-white-shirt avatar and a blue badge reading 30. Eight consecutive grey incoming bubbles are followed by three blue outgoing replies and one final grey bubble. The image carries visible compression halos around the text, indicating it has been saved and reshared repeatedly.

The exchange, read from the capture: Anyway I am not sure if I will live to see the end of this revolution. I believe you were the piece god meant me to meet that will finish the fight. Since the beginning of tpusa I knew in my gut that I might get wiped out at any time. I cannot explain it. But I dream about it all the time. Like all the time. Anyway thats depressing convo for another rite elmao. time**. The replies read: That is so weird. More a fear manifestation than an actualizacion. Actualization*. The closing grey bubble reads: Not really afraid of it but I am just telling you what I know to be true.

This is the second premonition exchange, and it is the more specific of the two: it ties the feeling to the founding of TPUSA and describes it as recurring. The counterparty response, framing it as a fear manifestation rather than a prediction, is preserved here because it is part of the same record and cuts against the strongest reading of the messages.

The capture is unverified, the counterparty is unnamed, and the compression artifacts confirm it has passed through multiple hands. The competing accounts of whether [Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) warned people are documented on [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).
