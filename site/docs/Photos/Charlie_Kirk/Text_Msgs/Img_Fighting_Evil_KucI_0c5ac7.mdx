## What This Image Shows

An iMessage screenshot of a two-person group thread, headed 2 People with overlapping avatars — one a headshot of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) in a white collared shirt and dark suit jacket, the other a generic grey silhouette — and a blue badge reading 39. Three message blocks appear, each preceded by a centered timestamp, each attributed to Charlie Kirk, and two carrying heart tapback reactions from the other participant.

The messages, with their dates: Oct 4, 2018 at 6:31 AM — For we are not fighting against flesh-and-blood enemies, but against evil rulers and authorities of the unseen world, against mighty powers in this dark world, and against evil spirits in the heavenly places. Ephesians 6:12. Oct 5, 2018 at 9:31 AM — For the LORD watches over the path of the godly, but the path of the wicked leads to destruction. Psalms 1:6. Oct 8, 2018 at 5:13 AM — Trust in the Lord forever, for the Lord, the Lord himself, is the Rock eternal. Isaiah 26:4.

These are from 2018, seven years before [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and they are ordinary devotional messages. They are filed with the case material because Ephesians 6:12 recurs in commentary about the investigation, and because the messages establish the register in which the later premonition exchanges were written — scriptural rather than operational. Reading them as foreknowledge of anything would be a considerable stretch, and no such reading is adopted here.

The capture is unverified and the second participant is not identified. See [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings) and the section overview [Charlie Kirk](/Charlie/overview).
