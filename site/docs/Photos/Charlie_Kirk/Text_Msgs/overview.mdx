## About This Cluster

This cluster holds the leaked and circulated message screenshots attributed to **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** — chat threads that researchers point to as the closest thing the public has to his private state of mind before **[September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**. They matter because the motive question in this case turns on two things: whether Kirk was in a funding and political fight serious enough to make him a target, and whether he told people he expected to be killed. These screenshots are cited as proof for both. None of them has been authenticated in court, and the identity of the person on the other side of most threads is blacked out in the images themselves.

Three distinct threads recur across the set. The donor thread, captured repeatedly in dark-mode chat form, shows a bubble labeled "Charlie Kirk" reading: "Just lost another huge Jewish donor. $2 million a year because we won't cancel Tucker. I'm thinking of inviting Candace," followed by "Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes. I cannot and will not be bullied like this" and "Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro Israel cause." The counterparty replies "Ugghhh" and then "Please don't invite Candace. That might feel good short term but it's not good long term." The second thread is the premonition exchange, in grey-and-blue iMessage bubbles: "Anyway I am not sure if I will live to see the end of this revolution… Since the beginning of tpusa I knew in my gut that I might get wiped out at any time. I cannot explain it. But I dream about it all the time." A companion capture reads "i might be Moses tho / I might not see this whole thing through lmao," answered with "If that is the case, be my David!" The third is a 2018 group thread of scripture messages — Ephesians 6:12 on "evil rulers and authorities of the unseen world," Psalms 1:6, and Isaiah 26:4. Also present is a repost of the **[Harrison H. Smith](/People/harrison-smith)** claim of **8/13/25** about what someone close to Kirk said he believed. Every one of these is presented as the posters present it: unverified screenshots, not adjudicated fact, and no living person is accused of wrongdoing here.
