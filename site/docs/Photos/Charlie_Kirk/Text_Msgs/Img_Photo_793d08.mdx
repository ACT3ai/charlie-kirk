## What This Image Shows

A vertical screenshot of the same donor-loss exchange, rendered here over the doodle-pattern wallpaper associated with WhatsApp dark mode. Five bubbles are visible: the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) message at 3:49 PM about losing a donor and thinking of inviting [Candace](/People/candace-owens), the redacted counterparty Ugghhh at 3:50 PM, the Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes message at 3:54 PM, Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro Israel cause at 3:54 PM, and the counterparty reply beginning Please do not invite Candace. The word donor is again highlighted in translucent orange from an active search. Along the far left edge runs a thin vertical strip showing a sliver of another interface — an olive-green border over a dark blurry background with hints of purple and blue.

That sliver is the useful detail. It indicates this capture was taken from a screen showing something else alongside the chat — a video call, a broadcast, or a second window — rather than from a phone in ordinary use. Several of the versions of this exchange circulating publicly carry the same artifact, which suggests they were captured from a presentation of the messages rather than from the original device.

These screenshots are unverified. The counterparty name is blacked out in every published version, no forensic authentication has been performed, and neither the messages nor the search highlight has been tested in any proceeding. They are recorded here as published, with attribution, and nothing in them establishes wrongdoing by any person.
