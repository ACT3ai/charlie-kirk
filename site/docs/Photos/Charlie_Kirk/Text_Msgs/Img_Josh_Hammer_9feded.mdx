## What This Image Shows

A screenshot of a mobile messaging thread in dark mode, the wallpaper carrying the faint repeating icon pattern used by encrypted messaging apps. Incoming messages sit in dark grey bubbles on the left, headed Charlie Kirk in blue with a circular blue CK avatar. Outgoing replies sit on the right, and the sender name above them has been completely covered by a solid black redaction bar. The word donor in the first message and donors in the third are highlighted with a translucent orange overlay, indicating an active in-app search when the capture was taken.
