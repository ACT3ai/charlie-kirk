## What This Image Shows

An iPhone iMessage capture, clock reading 10:13, from the same thread. A banner below the contact name offers to update the saved name from Charlie Kirk New to Charlie Kirk. The avatar shows the same headshot against a Turning Point Action backdrop, and the blue badge reads 27. The exchange closes with a date stamp reading Nov 17, 2023 at 1:15 PM.

The exchange, read from the capture: Exactly, in grey. Then: Can not wait to see you in person and tell you what is going on behind the scenes. I just cannot let what happened fly. I have taken the high road too many times on Ben public emotions. It is stunning to me they he is 40 and behaves like this. The reply: I can not wait to hear. He is losing a lot of support. Then: Yeah. Unbelievable bad for him from a PR stance. I have expressed that I think he needs to take a week off. I think he is in some sort of a psychosis. Genuinely. And: He has fewer and fewer allies. Also I wish he got this worked up about America. Weird. The closing message: The Israel First thing is something I cannot comprehend. AIPAC going after Thomas Massie was a major wake up call for me. Cannot have foreign interest groups going after our congressmen.

The Massie line is the substantive one, because it names a specific, checkable public event as the turning point being described: AIPAC electoral spending against Representative Thomas Massie. Massie appears elsewhere in this investigation as the legislator to whom the proposed disclosure laws are directed.

The person referred to only as Ben is not identified here, and the remarks about him are one participant private opinion, published by the other. Nothing in this capture alleges wrongdoing by anyone.
