## What This Image Shows

A vertical iMessage screenshot showing the same premonition exchange as its companion in this cluster. The header reads Charlie with the suit-and-white-shirt avatar and a blue badge reading 30. Nine grey incoming bubbles appear — the first eight in a tight stack sharing a single tail, the ninth standing alone at the bottom — with three blue outgoing bubbles grouped on the right.

The text matches the companion capture: Anyway I am not sure if I will live to see the end of this revolution. I believe you were the piece god meant me to meet that will finish the fight. Since the beginning of tpusa I knew in my gut that I might get wiped out at any time. I cannot explain it. But I dream about it all the time. Like all the time. Anyway thats depressing convo for another rite elmao. time**. The replies: That is so weird. More a fear manifestation than an actualizacion. Actualization*. And the closing bubble: Not really afraid of it but I am just telling you what I know to be true.

Having two independent captures of the same thread with matching text and matching bubble structure is worth recording, because it at least rules out one version having been altered relative to the other. It does not authenticate the underlying messages, which remain unverified and whose counterparty is unnamed.
