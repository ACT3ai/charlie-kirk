## What This Image Shows

A dark-mode screenshot of a post on X by Tyler Bowyer, verified, dated 12/5/25. The full text reads: Do you know how many times we discussed the threats that were out there? I probably have seen hundreds of significant threats and we regularly discussed the belief that the deep state and radical regimes were after him. I have been asked zero questions directly by anyone. I have seen plenty of people make a whole lot of stuff up for clicks. That has been interesting. Hence why I am thrilled for anyone to come down to our HQ and talk to us. A translucent yellow highlight has been drawn across the phrase and we regularly discussed the belief that the deep state and radical regimes were after him. The engagement row shows 37 replies, 177 reposts, 43 likes and 24K views.

The post is significant on both sides of the central dispute. Bowyer is a TPUSA figure, and he states directly that threats were discussed regularly and that the belief about who was behind them was a live topic. That supports the researchers who say Kirk spoke about expecting to be targeted. At the same time he states that no one has asked him any questions directly, and he criticizes people inventing material for engagement.

The yellow highlight is the annotator emphasis, not part of the original post. This is a recollection published nearly three months after September 10, 2025, and it names no individual and alleges no crime. The competing accounts about whether Kirk warned people, including Erika Kirk statement that no such messages exist on his phone, are documented on [Reported Quotes and Warnings](/Charlie/Reported_Quotes_and_Warnings).
