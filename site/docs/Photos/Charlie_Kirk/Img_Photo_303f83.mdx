## What This Image Shows

A composite on a black background. Three photographic panels run across the top: a close view of a dark leather seat cushion with a crushed clear water bottle and a red and yellow wrapper on the carpet behind it; a wider shot between the front and middle rows showing the same bottle and wrapper plus a crumpled brown bag; and a vertical shot of the floor in front of the third-row bench. Beneath them is a large stylized cutaway diagram of a multi-row SUV interior, overlaid with green silhouettes and yellow labels placing named individuals in specific seats. The labels read Dan, Justin, Brian, CK, Rick and Frank.
