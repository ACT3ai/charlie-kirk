## What This Image Shows

A phone screenshot of a post on X by the account Polly St George, captured four hours after posting with the device battery at 50 percent. The post text reads: It enters near his right ear. There is video but I can not post it here. This is a still from that video. See the blood on his right ear/ temple? Below it is an embedded video still showing a person in a white t-shirt leaning backward on stage in front of a Turning Point USA and YREFY.COM backdrop, with a visible red stain on the collar and chest. The engagement counter reads 235.

The claim is about entry location, and entry location is the central factual dispute in the case. The published account describes a projectile recovered in the neck with no exit wound. A separate body of research argues the injury originated at the stage rather than from a rooftop. An entry near the right ear or temple, if that is what the still shows, sits awkwardly with more than one account, which is why the poster frames it as a question.

The underlying still is low-resolution and heavily compressed, and at that quality shadow, hair, and blood trace are hard to separate. The same screenshot also appears in the parent [Charlie Kirk Himself](/Photos/Charlie_Kirk/overview) cluster. For the competing accounts see [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview), [Ballistics and Wound Analysis](/Medical/ballistics_and_wound_analysis), and [Shot in Chest Reversal](/Witnesses/Shot_in_Chest_Reversal).
