## What This Image Shows

An eye-level interior view of the third-row seats. The upholstery is dark synthetic leather with vertical stitching and cross-stitch detailing along the outer seams. Across the double seat lies a crumpled light-brown paper shopping bag with twisted-cord handles, heavily stained with dark brown wet-looking splotches. A white paper receipt or label is affixed to its left side. Resting on the right portion of the bag is a folded white paper document or pamphlet showing inverted black text and a blue graphic banner. The interior trim panel at left carries a molded cup holder, a seatbelt slot near the roofline, a vertical air vent, and a small cargo hook, with a grey seatbelt strap hanging down. A clear water bottle with a blue and white label sits in the dark footwell below. The frame is bordered by faded repeating vertical text.

This is one of the wider-context frames in the vehicle set, published on [SUV Transporting Charlie](/Mic/suv-transporting-charlie). Its evidentiary value is that it establishes the general condition of the cabin: food waste, paper, water bottles, and staining spread across multiple rows, which is the backdrop every close-up in the set is cropped from.

The staining is described by commentators as blood and has not been publicly identified. The order in which these photographs were taken, and by whom, has not been established either. For the transport itself, see [Charlie Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital); for the vehicle-disposal claims, see [Cover Up](/CoverUp/overview).
