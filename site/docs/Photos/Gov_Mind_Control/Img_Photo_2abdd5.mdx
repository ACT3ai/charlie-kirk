## What This Image Shows

This is a digital screenshot of a post on the social media platform X (formerly Twitter) by the user "Died Suddenly" (@DiedSuddenly_). The post displays a quote-reposted tweet from "MJTruthUltra" ([@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra)) that contains an image of a CNN US article headline alongside two mugshot-style photographs of young men and a snippet of text discussing "[ARTICHOKE techniques](/Gov_Mind_Control/project-artichoke-memo)." The overall post is structured to suggest a connection between a news event involving a New Mexico compound and psychological manipulation techniques.

That connection is the poster's suggestion and nothing in the screenshot supports it. The 2018 New Mexico compound case is a separate prosecution in a different state, and the men in the photographs are defendants in that matter — **they are not accused here of anything, and neither they nor any intelligence programme has been shown to have any connection to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) or to the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025)**.
