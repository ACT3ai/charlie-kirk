## What This Image Shows

This is a clean text crop on a plain grey background. It reads in full: "During the past year, the Trump insider was told by contacts in the White House that the Secret Service had caught Israeli government personnel placing electronic devices on its emergency response vehicles on two separate occasions."

Count the layers of attribution in that one sentence, because they are what determine its weight. An unnamed "Trump insider" was told something by unnamed "contacts in the White House" about what the Secret Service allegedly found. The screenshot shows no publication name, no author, no date, and no incident reference.

Neither the **U.S. Secret Service** nor any Israeli official has confirmed the account, and no incident report has been published. The passage makes no reference to **Charlie Kirk**, to Utah Valley University, or to **September 10, 2025**; it is filed in this investigation because it speaks to the general question of foreign technical access to American protective infrastructure.
