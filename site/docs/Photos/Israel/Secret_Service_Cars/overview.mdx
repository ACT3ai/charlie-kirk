## About This Cluster

This small sub-cluster carries one reported claim and one campus photograph. The claim concerns the **U.S. Secret Service** and its emergency response vehicles, and researchers keep it inside the [Israel and Mossad](/Photos/Israel/overview) area because it goes to a question that runs through this whole investigation: whether a friendly foreign service has the access and the willingness to operate against American protective infrastructure. That question is the hinge of the entire foreign-involvement line of inquiry in the **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** case, and this is one of the few published items that speaks to it directly.

The text image reproduces a passage that reads, according to the source, "During the past year, the Trump insider was told by contacts in the White House that the Secret Service had caught Israeli government personnel placing electronic devices on its emergency response vehicles on two separate occasions." Every layer of that sentence should be visible to the reader: it is an anonymous "Trump insider" relaying what unnamed White House contacts allegedly said, reproduced without a named publication in the screenshot itself. Neither the Secret Service nor any Israeli official has confirmed it, no incident report has been published, and no connection to **September 10, 2025** or to Utah Valley University is claimed by the passage. The second image is a low-resolution campus street scene showing a white sedan passing a parked dark SUV; nothing in the frame identifies either vehicle as a government car, and the filing is a researcher's tentative label rather than an identification.
