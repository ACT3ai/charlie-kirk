## What This Image Shows

This image is a composite screenshot of a social media thread from an X (formerly Twitter) account named "Khalissee" ([@Kahlissee](/Influencers/x/kahlissee)). The posts make a serious and unverified allegation: the account asserts that an Israeli-born U.S. Attorney appointee, **Sigal Chattah**, acted to protect alleged child predators. The post includes two primary visual elements: a split image featuring a portrait of Chattah alongside what appears on its face to be a mock news article from "The Rule of Law," and a screenshot of a LinkedIn profile for **Tom Alexandrovich**, labeled as the Executive Director of Israel's National Cyber Directorate, set against a mountain landscape background.

That allegation is the poster's, and this site does not adopt it. **Sigal Chattah and Tom Alexandrovich are living people who have not been convicted of any offence, and nothing on this page states or implies as fact that either protected, shielded, or was involved with any offender.** No court has made such a finding, no charging document naming either in those terms has been published in this file, and neither has been shown to have any connection to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) or to the events of [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025). The screenshot is preserved to document what circulated. See the [cluster overview](/Photos/Israel/overview).
