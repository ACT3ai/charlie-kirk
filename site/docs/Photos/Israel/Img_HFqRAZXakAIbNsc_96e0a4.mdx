## What This Image Shows

This image shows a monitor displaying a satellite map interface with a dark, military-style heads-up display overlay: coordinate readouts, text panels, and glowing red radar-style markers indicating four tracked cell phone devices at the **Serena Hotel in Islamabad, Pakistan**.

It illustrates a claim made by the data analyst **Tony Seruga**, who posted that raw GPS data placed 44 foreign-registered cellphones near Charlie Kirk on **September 10, 2025** — 16 Israeli, 13 Chinese, 12 Russian, and 3 Iranian — and that four of the 16 Israeli-registered devices later appeared at the Islamabad Serena Hotel on **April 10-11, 2026**, during US-Iran talks hosted by Pakistan. The hotel was cleared of regular guests and locked down for those delegations, which is a matter of public record.

Read the image itself with care. Its rendering style is presentational rather than forensic — a HUD-style visualization of the sort used in film and simulation, not an export from any named analytic system. No raw data, provider, methodology, or device identifier has been published to support the underlying claim, and nothing about a phone's registration establishes who carried it. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
