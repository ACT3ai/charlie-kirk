## What This Image Shows

This is the copy of the **"The Last 60 Days of Charlie Kirk"** infographic that the site itself publishes, which is why it carries host-page references while the near-identical copy elsewhere in this cluster does not. It is a horizontal timeline running from **July 11** to [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), over a faded high-contrast portrait of Kirk, in red and dark blue, tracking public actions, statements, and reported financial consequences connected to his position on Israel.

It appears on [the Israel section overview](/Israel/overview) and on [the Timeline overview](/Timeline/overview), where it functions as a visual index to the friction narrative rather than as proof for it. Readers arriving from either page should treat it as a map of claims to check, not a set of findings.

The graphic has no citations and no named author. Its entries are traceable to public reporting, but the causal arc implied by putting them on one arrow is the designer's, not any investigator's. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
