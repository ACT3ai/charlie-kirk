## What This Image Shows

This is a two-panel meme built on the unmasking scene from the cartoon **Scooby-Doo**. In the top panel, a character whose head has been replaced with a photograph of **Candace Owens** reaches to pull the hood from a bound figure. In the bottom panel the hood is off, and the unmasked figure's face has been replaced by a graphic of the flag of **Israel**.

The joke encodes an argument in one image: that the actor responsible is being concealed and that Owens is the one exposing it. That is the argument Owens has advanced publicly in her own commentary, but this meme is an anonymous creation. It is not a statement by her, and there is no indication she made or endorsed it.

It asserts nothing checkable. It names no individual, cites no source, and offers no evidence about **Charlie Kirk**, Utah Valley University, or **September 10, 2025**. It is preserved so the visual rhetoric of this period is part of the record.
