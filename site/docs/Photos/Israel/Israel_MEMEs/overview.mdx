## About This Cluster

This sub-cluster holds internet memes rather than evidence. They are filed under [Israel and Mossad](/Photos/Israel/overview) because they show how the Israel question in the [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) case was being argued in image form on social media in the months after [**September 10, 2025**](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) — which is itself a fact about the case worth recording, since the online argument shaped what mainstream outlets did and did not cover. A meme is a rhetorical device. It compresses a contested claim into a punchline and strips out the sourcing, and none of these images should be read as documenting anything about the assassination.

The single image here is a **Scooby-Doo** unmasking meme: the character pulling off the mask has been given **Candace Owens'** face, and the figure being unmasked turns out to be the Israeli flag. The joke encodes an argument Owens has made publicly — that the real actor behind events is being concealed — but the meme itself is an anonymous creation, not a statement by Owens and not a claim she has been shown to endorse. It makes no factual assertion capable of being checked, names no one, and proves nothing. It is preserved here so the visual rhetoric of this period is part of the record rather than lost.

For the sourced analysis behind the argument this meme gestures at, see [Israel motivation](/Israel/motivation), [Charlie and Israel friction](/Israel/Charlie_Israel_Friction), and the [Killer: Israel/Mossad theory](/Killer/israel-mossad).
