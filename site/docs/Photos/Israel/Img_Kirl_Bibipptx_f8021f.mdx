## What This Image Shows

This is a second edit of the same meme. The composition is unchanged — [**Benjamin Netanyahu**](/People/benjamin-netanyahu) left, [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) right, speech bubbles between — but one caption has been dropped. The text now reads only: "Earlier in 2025," "Take $150 million CASH From Israel. To your Turning Point TPUSA," "NO!", and "Assassinated Sept 10th, 2025." The line calling Kirk "the man with a mountain of integrity" is gone.

Both versions are preserved because the edit history is itself informative: it shows a graphic being tightened for circulation, with the editorializing line stripped so the remaining text reads more like a factual sequence. That is a common pattern in how claims harden as they spread.

The dialogue remains invented. The underlying allegation of a refused offer comes from **Stew Peters** and from Grayzone reporting based on unnamed sources, and neither has been verified or adjudicated. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
