## What This Image Shows

This is a circulated quote graphic: a grayscale photograph of [**Charlie Kirk**](/People/charlie-kirk) seated on an outdoor bench, overlaid with white text reading "Jewish donors play into all the stereotypes. I cannot and will not be bullied like this. Leaving me no choice but to leave the pro Israel cause." It is attributed on the image to Kirk.

The words come from the leaked private messages published by [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) and shown in their original form on [the previous page in this cluster](/Photos/Israel/Img_pXwAASJgN_aa36a3). This graphic strips away everything around them — the conversation, the reply, the timestamps, the reporting on the withdrawn pledge — and presents them as a standalone public statement. That transformation matters, because these were private messages in a specific exchange about a specific donor, not a manifesto Kirk published.

The underlying screenshots have not been forensically authenticated, and this derivative graphic carries no source attribution at all.

Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
