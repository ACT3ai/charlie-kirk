## What This Image Shows

This is a social-media preview of a video or article, built around a network infographic headed **"ISRAELI-EVANGELICAL INFLUENCE AND INTERFERENCE CAMPAIGN"** on a dark blue field. The chart maps Israeli government bodies — the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Tourism, and the Ministry of Strategic Affairs — to organizations including the Israel Allies Foundation and Friends of Zion, and from there to a column of named American evangelical figures: Mike Evans, James Dobson, Greg Laurie, Francis Chan, Mike Huckabee, Jack Graham, Jentezen Franklin, George Barna, John Hagee, Robert Jeffress, Jonathan Cahn, David Platt, and others. The headline beneath reads "Intel Memo: Exposing Israel's Sophisticated Evangelical Foreign Influence Campaign."

The graphic is filed in this cluster because the argument it makes — that a foreign government invests systematically in American religious constituencies — is the background against which the Kirk donor dispute is read by researchers.

Everything about how to weigh it should be stated plainly. This is an anonymous chart with no citations, no methodology, and no indication of what a connecting line means: funding, a speaking invitation, a shared board seat, or nothing at all. The living people named on it have not been accused of any offense, none of them is connected on this chart to [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) or to [September 10, 2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025), and lawful advocacy and travel programs are not wrongdoing. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it.
