## What This Image Shows

This is a research worksheet rather than a social-media post, and it is one of the more useful documents in the cluster because it tries to source its claims. The table has columns for date, media source, transcribed quote, a hyperlink, and a verification column marked "Yes." Visible rows include **2025-08-06**, The Megyn Kelly Show on SiriusXM, logged as "Megyn Kelly and Charlie Kirk Push Back Against Israel Demanding Their 100% Israel," with a partial quote reading "It's all of the sudden... no longer with us.' Wait a second, what does 'with us' mean, exactly?" A second row logs **2025-08-14**, an X Spaces discussion on Gaza and U.S. policy, carrying a [**Candace Owens**](/People/candace-owens) quote: "The way Israel is handling Gaza is not just a military operation; it's a humanitarian disaster. And the U.S. is complicit by funding it." A note on that row states it counters the claim that Kirk distanced himself.

The table's value is that it dates and locates each statement, which most items in this cluster do not. Its limits are equally clear: the transcription is fragmentary, some quotes are Owens rather than Kirk, and the "Yes" verification column is the compiler's own assessment.

Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
