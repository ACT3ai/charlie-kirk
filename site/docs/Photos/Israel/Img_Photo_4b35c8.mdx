## What This Image Shows

This is a clean screenshot of an article header from **The Grayzone**. The headline reads: "Charlie Kirk refused Netanyahu funding offer, was 'frightened' by pro-Israel forces before death, friend reveals." The byline shows **Max Blumenthal** and **Anya Parampil**, dated **September 12, 2025** — two days after the assassination at Utah Valley University.

This single headline is the source of a claim that propagates through much of this cluster and much of the site: that money was offered, that it was refused, and that a named friend described Kirk as frightened beforehand. The article's sourcing is people close to Kirk speaking without attribution. It is reporting, and reporting of this kind can be right, but it has not been confirmed by any agency and the individuals it describes are not named in the headline.

Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
