## About This Cluster

These two images do not come from Utah. They are screenshots of reporting out of **Gaza**, and they were filed here because of a single point of comparison researchers raised: **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** was struck in the neck on **September 10, 2025**, and a body of medical reporting from Gaza describes children arriving at hospitals with precise head and neck wounds. The comparison being drawn is about wound placement and marksmanship, not about any operational link. It is the weakest kind of inference in the case file — pattern similarity across two unrelated conflicts — and it is presented here so readers can see exactly what the argument rests on rather than only hearing it summarized.

The first screenshot captures an X post from journalist **Ben Goggin** referenced as "44 doctors, nurses, and medical workers say..." with an excerpt in which an emergency medicine physician who had worked in a Gaza hospital says, "I couldn't believe the number of kids I saw shot in the head," and a colleague answers, "Yeah, me, too. Every single day." Alongside it is a lateral X-ray. The second is a **UK-Med** and MedAid item captioned "Meet Razan, the 3-year-old child shot in the neck in Gaza," showing a radiograph with a dense object lodged near the cervical spine. These are published accounts by named medical charities and journalists about a different conflict. They document nothing about Utah Valley University, no shooter, and no chain of command, and no investigating body has drawn a connection between them and the Kirk case.
