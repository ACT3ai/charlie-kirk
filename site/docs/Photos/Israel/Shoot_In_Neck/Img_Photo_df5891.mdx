## What This Image Shows

This screenshot shows a mobile browser displaying a lateral radiograph of a young child's head, neck, and upper chest. A dense, bright, bullet-shaped object is lodged at the back of the neck near the cervical spine. The interface identifies the source as **UK-Med** with a **MEDAID** credit, and the caption reads: "Meet Razan, the 3-year-old child shot in the neck in Gaza."

Like its companion in this sub-cluster, this is published material from a named medical charity about a different conflict. It was filed here because of the neck-wound comparison researchers raised with the **Charlie Kirk** case.

It should be said plainly that this image documents a child's injury in Gaza and nothing about Utah. It has no evidentiary bearing on **September 10, 2025**, and no investigating body has connected the two.
