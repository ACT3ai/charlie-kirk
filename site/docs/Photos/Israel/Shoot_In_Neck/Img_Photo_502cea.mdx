## What This Image Shows

This is a mobile browser screenshot of a post on X. The visible excerpt reads: "...emergency medicine physician who had worked in a different hospital in Gaza two months before me. 'I couldn't believe the number of kids I saw shot in the head,' I told him. To my surprise, he responded: 'Yeah, me, too. Every single day.'" A link preview beneath identifies the source as journalist **Ben Goggin** on X, headed "44 doctors, nurses, and medical workers say..." Beside the text is a lateral X-ray showing a child's skull and neck with a metallic fragment in the neck area.

This is published reporting about a different conflict, in a different part of the world, involving named medical professionals and a named journalist. It is filed in the Charlie Kirk investigation for one narrow reason: **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** was struck in the neck on **September 10, 2025**, and researchers raised the comparison of wound placement.

That comparison is pattern similarity across unrelated events, which is the weakest form of inference available. Nothing in this material concerns Utah Valley University, and no investigating body has drawn any connection.

The forensic questions about Kirk's wound are handled with sourced material at [Cause of Death](/Cause_of_Death/overview), [Gun and Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview), and in the [Ballistics and Gun](/Photos/Ballistics_Gun/overview) image cluster.
