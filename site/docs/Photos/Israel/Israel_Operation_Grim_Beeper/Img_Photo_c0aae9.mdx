## What This Image Shows

This is the infographic timeline **"The Last 60 Days of Charlie Kirk,"** as it was attached to the **July 10, 2026** post by **@SwordTruth** that invoked Operation Grim Beeper. It runs from **July 11** to **September 10, 2025**, marking reported statements, donor losses, and [Israel](/Israel/overview)-related friction across [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s final two months.

The post it accompanied made the capability argument in one line: "Maybe Charlie Kirk was killed by a bullet but it sure as hell wasn't a 30-06. One man killed by a bullet? That's child's play for [Mossad](/Israel/Mossad)." The timeline is the motive half of that pairing — capability from the 2024 pager operation, motive from these sixty days.

The individual entries trace to public reporting, much of it reproduced in the parent [Israel and Mossad](/Photos/Israel/overview) cluster. The graphic itself carries no citations and no named author, and arranging real events along one arrow implies a causal sequence that no investigating body has established. No Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any role in Kirk's death, and prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted.
