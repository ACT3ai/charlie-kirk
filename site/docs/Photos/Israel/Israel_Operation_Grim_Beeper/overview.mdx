## About This Cluster

These two graphics came from a single X post published on **July 10, 2026** by the account **[@SwordTruth](/Influencers/x/swordtruth)**, which invoked the September 2024 pager operation nicknamed **Operation Grim Beeper** to argue about the **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** case. The post's own words were: "Maybe Charlie Kirk was killed by a bullet but it sure as hell wasn't a 30-06. One man killed by a bullet? That's child's play for [Mossad](/Israel/Mossad)." The graphics attached to it are what this sub-cluster preserves — not the 2024 operation itself, which is documented in the peer sub-cluster [Beepers](/Photos/Israel/Israel_Beepers/overview), but the way the argument was packaged for circulation.

The first graphic is the widely shared infographic timeline **"The Last 60 Days of Charlie Kirk,"** running from **July 11** to **September 10, 2025** and marking out reported statements, donor losses, and Israel-related controversies across Kirk's final two months. Its individual entries are drawn from public reporting, but the graphic carries no citations, has an anonymous author, and arranges those entries into an implied causal sequence that no investigating body has established. The second is a satirical meme whose captions are invented — the quotes in it were never said by anyone depicted, and it is reproduced here labeled as satire precisely so no reader mistakes it for a record of a real exchange. Neither image is evidence. Both are commentary, and both are included because they circulated widely enough to shape how the case was discussed.
