## What This Image Shows

This is a political meme, not a document. It places **Benjamin Netanyahu** on the left and **Charlie Kirk** on the right with speech bubbles between them. The captions read: "Earlier in 2025," "Take $150 million CASH From Israel. To your Turning Point TPUSA," then Kirk's "NO!", then "The man with a mountain of integrity said," closing with "Assassinated Sept 10th, 2025."

The dialogue is invented. No such exchange between Netanyahu and Kirk is on record, and the meme should not be read as depicting a real conversation. What it dramatizes is a claim that does exist in the record: a reported refused funding offer, described by **Stew Peters** as an initial $150 million and later a $1 billion figure, and reported separately by The Grayzone as a Netanyahu funding offer Kirk turned down.

Those reports are unverified allegations from named commentators and unnamed sources. The meme converts them into a scene with quotation marks around them, which is the opposite of sourcing. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
