## What This Image Shows

This third version of the meme adds an annotation in the upper corner: "Bill Ackman's meeting with Charlie Kirk was Aug 2025." The rest is unchanged — "Earlier in 2025," "Take $150 million CASH From Israel. To your Turning Point TPUSA," "NO!", and "Assassinated Sept 10th, 2025."

The annotation is where a meme starts trying to become an argument. It stitches the invented Netanyahu exchange to a real event: the early-August 2025 Hamptons gathering reported by The Grayzone, which five unnamed sources described as an "intervention" over Kirk's views on Israel. Placing the two side by side implies they were parts of one pressure campaign, which no source establishes.

**Bill Ackman** has not been accused of any offense, and no reporting connects him to any funding offer of the kind the meme depicts. The dialogue in the panels remains fabricated. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
