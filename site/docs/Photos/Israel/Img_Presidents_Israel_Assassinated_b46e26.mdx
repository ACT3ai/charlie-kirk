## What This Image Shows

This five-panel meme is captioned "ALL STOOD UP TO DEMOCRATS" at the top and "ALL WERE SHOT" at the bottom. The panels show **Abraham Lincoln**, **Donald Trump**, **John F. Kennedy**, **Ronald Reagan**, and **Charlie Kirk**, arranged around a central image of Trump after the July 2024 attempt on his life.

The grouping is political rather than historical. The men shown were shot by individuals with widely different and in several cases well-documented motives, and the framing does not survive examination — Kennedy was a Democrat, and the assailants in these cases were not acting on behalf of a party. The meme's method is juxtaposition, which produces a feeling of pattern without producing a pattern.

It is preserved here as a record of how the case was argued visually in the weeks after September 10, 2025, not as evidence about it. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
