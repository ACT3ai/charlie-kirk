## What This Image Shows

This screenshot shows a social-media post, apparently from Telegram, with a professional headshot of a man above a block of text. The post is dated **September 2** and reads: "Based on all that I know, September is likely to bring what can best be described as a political tsunami. October will, in turn, mark the military one. Without intending to sound overly sensational, these 60 days will profoundly shape the world." Reaction counts and a view count of 185.6K appear beneath, with a 4:00 AM timestamp.

It is filed alongside the condolence letter because both carry a September 2 date, eight days before the assassination, and the folder name states the inference researchers drew from that.

What the post actually says is worth reading closely before that inference is accepted. It names no person, no country, no city, and no event. It refers to political and military developments in general terms of the kind that geopolitical commentators publish constantly. There is no reference to **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)**, to the United States, or to Utah, and nothing in it indicates advance knowledge of anything.
