## What This Image Shows

This is a researcher-built table headed **"Distraction Shootings by intel service"** with the sentence completed beneath the grid: "that assassinated Charlie Kirk." The columns are incident, city and state, date, and summary. The eight rows are: Israeli Staff Museum Attack, Washington, DC, Sept 15, 2025; Ambush Kills 3 Officers, Spring Grove, PA, Sept 17, 2025; Harris Office Gunshots, Tempe, AZ, Sept 23, 2025; Dallas ICE Facility Attack, Dallas, TX, Sept 24, 2025; Mormon Church Attack, Grand Blanc, MI, Sept 28, 2025; CDC Headquarters Shooting, Atlanta, GA, Oct 5, 2025; Katy Coach Shot, Katy, TX, Nov 1, 2025; National Guard Ambushed, Washington, DC, Nov 26, 2025.

The incidents themselves are real events with their own investigations, victims, and in several cases prosecutions. What the table adds is a theory about them — that they were staged or timed to divert attention — and that theory is asserted in the title and nowhere supported in the grid. There is no column for evidence, no source citation, and no stated method for selecting these eight incidents out of the far larger number that occurred in the same period.

No agency has connected any of these events to the [Kirk case](/People/charlie-kirk) or to each other, and no intelligence service has been shown to have any role in any of them. Nothing here shows that any person or government had advance knowledge of the assassination or any role in it. Prosecutors have charged a domestic suspect who has not been convicted, and no Israeli individual or institution has been shown to have any connection to Charlie Kirk's death.
