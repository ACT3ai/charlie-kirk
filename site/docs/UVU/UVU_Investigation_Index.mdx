# UVU Investigation Index

Utah Valley University is both **the physical kill zone** and an **institution** whose
GRAMA records show pre-event warnings buried, emergency alerts with placeholder text, and
post-shooting courtyard paving within days. This memo maps the threads without asserting
that any UVU employee committed a crime.

## Before September 10

- **10:35 PM Sept 9** — Two men ask to speak with police about **"a plan"**; cabinet notified; later dismissed as "GOP reps" ([GRAMA](./pre-shooting-warnings))
- **2:23 PM Sept 9** — Email chain: someone wants a **message delivered to Charlie Kirk** — delivery undocumented
- **Sept 3** — Orem PD sends ticket link; joint Public Order Unit **never activated**
- **Six campus police** for ~3,000 attendees; **no ambulance** on site; **no active-shooter faculty training**
