# UVU Social Media and Public Statements

This page extracts **documented public claims** from UVU's official communications and closely related sources compiled in project research notes (`Research/raw/uvu_posts.md`). It excludes generic boilerplate about "community healing."

## UVU.edu Statement

UVU published a statement grieving the loss, noting the tragedy shook the community, and expressing condolences to Kirk's family. [Source: uvu.edu — "Welcoming you back to campus with care"](https://www.uvu.edu/news/2025/welcoming-you-back-to-campus-with-care.html)

UVU's first post cites **~12:10 PM**; FBI/prosecutor timelines often use **12:23 PM**. Researchers should reconcile against released security-camera metadata when available.

## Alleged Mass Suspensions on X (Candace Owens engagement)

Research notes and X commentary document claims that **thousands of accounts** were hit with **suspensions, 12-hour locks, shadowbans, and "limited visibility"** flags after liking, replying to, or reposting **[@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO)** content about the assassination — while official **@UVU** posts reportedly remained visible ([Censorship — X Deboosting](/Censorship/X_Deboosting_Investigators)).

| Alleged pattern | Status |
|---|---|
| Coordinated violation reports (not organic) | **Unverified** — no platform logs published |
| Repeated labels: violent speech / harassment | **Reported** in investigator compilations |
| Scale: **thousands** of accounts | **Unverified** headcount |

These are **unverified allegations** of platform censorship, not confirmed policy actions. They sit beside UVU's own public communications on this page as a **reach asymmetry** question — not proof @UVU coordinated takedowns. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).

## Reported FBI Request to Delete a Witness Video

Separately, witness **Ryne Simmons** reportedly said he sent his **4K / 60 fps** footage to the FBI and was **instructed to delete it**, saving a copy anyway. This is an **allegation** compiled in project research notes and is not adjudicated in public court filings. Ryne sits on the Grok Q8 people-at-UVU roster ([Investigation Index](./UVU_Investigation_Index)); whether his footage enters **State v. Tyler Robinson** discovery is an open [court](/court/overview) question.

## Trial & institutional messaging cross-check (Grok Q10)

UVU's public posts frame campus closure and FBI tip lines while **State v. Tyler Robinson** (Case **251403576**) treats UVU surveillance as core prosecution evidence. Commentary contrasts UVU's **false "suspect in custody"** alert (~19 min post-shot) with the **~7:58 PM** rooftop clip release timing — after Robinson was reportedly already in custody. For trial depth see [Tyler Robinson Trial](/Tyler_Robinson/Trial/overview) and [Court Investigation Index](/court/investigation-index). GRAMA files UVU withheld may overlap trial discovery ([Fix Laws](/Fix/overview)).
