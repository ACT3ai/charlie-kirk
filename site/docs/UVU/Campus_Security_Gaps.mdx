# UVU Campus Security Gaps on September 10

The Kirk assassination exposed **layered security failures** at Utah Valley University — some documented in **GRAMA records** ([@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) / Daily Caller News Foundation), others alleged in reporting, post-event photography, and public interviews. This page catalogs gaps **without asserting** that any named official intended harm or committed wrongdoing.

For the **September 9 warning threads** (two men and "a plan," undelivered Kirk message), see [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

## Hybrid security model

The event used a **hybrid model**: **TPUSA private security** for close protection alongside a **lighter UVU campus police footprint** than some prior Kirk appearances reportedly received.

| Layer | Role (as reported) |
|-------|---------------------|
| **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)** / Integrity Security | Kirk's contracted protective detail — in SUV with Kirk; no body-worn cameras per public accounts |
| **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** | TPUSA Head of Security — coordination with campus police questioned in [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) threads |
| **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)** / UVU Police | ~**6 officers** (~**25%** of department) for perimeter and event oversight |

Commentators debate which layer owned **rooftop overwatch** and **building envelope** security. Contracts and after-action reports are **not public**.

## Pre-event measures (what existed)

According to compilations from GRAMA review and mainstream reporting:

- **Metal detectors** and **bag checks** at the outdoor amphitheater entrance
- **TPUSA private security** inside the "bubble" around Kirk
- **Limited perimeter** around the speaking area — **not** a full rooftop or building envelope

That configuration left elevated positions — especially the [Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center) rooftop ~150 yards from the tent — outside the screened entrance zone.

## Documented institutional failures (GRAMA)

From [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) review of **112 pages** (also on [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)):

| Failure | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Understaffing** | ~**6 campus police** for ~**3,000** attendees (~**25%** of UVU PD) |
| **Joint Public Order Unit** | UVU-funded UVU–Orem unit **not activated** despite Sept 3 ticket link |
| **No ambulance on site** | Medical response delayed |
| **No outside LE on scene** | Orem PD reportedly not present during event |
| **No active-shooter faculty training** | Documented gap in records |
| **False first alert** | "Suspect in custody" **19 min** post-shooting — not true |
| **Placeholder alert text** | "Shooter in [INSERT location]" **88 min** post-shooting |

## Orem PD joint unit failure

According to the GRAMA thread, UVU pays Orem Police Department annually for a **joint Public Order Unit** designed for large campus events. On **September 3**, Orem PD emailed UVU the Charlie Kirk **ticket link** (subject: "Charlie Kirk Tickets") — correspondence researchers treat as part of the same coordination track.

According to [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305):

- The joint unit was **never activated** for the September 10 event
- **No Orem officers** were on site during the speaking program
- After the shooting, Orem PD reportedly said it was **"not involved"** — a statement commentators contrast with the September 3 email and the standing paid unit

This is a **documented coordination gap** in released records, not a judicial finding of negligence. See [UVU Campus Police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police).

## Rooftop and perimeter vulnerabilities

### Losee Center rooftop

Prosecutors allege the shot came from the **Losee Center rooftop**. Security criticisms include:

- **No publicly released proof** that UVU police conducted a **pre-event rooftop sweep** of Losee Center despite reported pre-event assurances
- **Unmonitored building access** — charging documents describe suspect entry via southeast doors and roof climb ~12:17 p.m.
- **Surveillance gaps** on surrounding structures
- Weeks later, [Fox News published photos](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized) reportedly showing **rooftop access paths still obtainable**, renewing scrutiny

### Jeff Long and the staffing ceiling

**[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)**, UVU Police Chief, led the campus contingent described as ~**six officers** — roughly **one quarter** of the entire department. Researchers note that force size alone cannot explain every gap, but it frames why **full-building envelope** coverage (rooftops, tunnels, adjacent structures) may have been deprioritized relative to amphitheater entrance screening.

### Brian Harpole's pre-event warnings (attributed)

According to public interviews **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)** gave — including accounts circulated from the **Shawn Ryan Show** and **Salt Lake Tribune** — he:

- **Texted Chief Long about rooftop concerns before the event**
- Criticized UVU afterward for **failing to secure rooftops "as promised"**
- Stated he heard **"two distinct sounds"** at the moment of the shot (his perceptual account, not a ballistics finding)

Harpole and Long are believed **alive**. All statements here are **attributed to Harpole's public accounts**, not asserted as established fact. Inclusion does not imply Harpole or Long committed any illegal act.

### Dan Flood and TPUSA coordination (attributed)

**[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)**, TPUSA Head of Security, was reportedly in the immediate post-shot group with Harpole. [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) commentary questions whether TPUSA security and UVU police **coordinated rooftop responsibility** in writing before the event — contracts are not public. The September 9 **undelivered Kirk message** ([Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)) adds a separate GRAMA-documented coordination question; researchers note no released record showing Flood's team was notified.

## Private security detail — body cameras and "lighter" coverage

According to the CK research file and [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) threads:

- Kirk's detail was **Rockhouse Integrity Group LLC** (dba Integrity Security Solutions), founded by **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)**
- The arrangement was reportedly **lighter than Kirk's prior events**
- Campus officers reportedly wore **no body cameras**; the private detail also reportedly had **no bodycams** — limiting independent review of perimeter disputes

At the shot, Harpole and **[Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood)** reportedly shielded Kirk in the SUV alongside [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler), [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis), and [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) (per circulated rosters).

## Law-enforcement coordination beyond Orem

- **FBI** assumed lead role; local agencies coordinated manhunt
- **UVU surveillance** tracked the suspect path per charging documents ([UVU Campus Police](/Law_Enforcement/uvu-campus-police))
- Allegations (project note) that **FBI told locals not to interview witnesses** — unverified; see [Government Agency Claims](/Theories/Government_Agencies_Claims)

## Post-event security narrative

UVU's public messaging emphasized **campus closure, counseling, and policy reviews**. Critics contrast that with:

- **Rapid courtyard paving** ([Pavers](/UVU/Pavers), [Hardscape Specialties](/UVU/Hardscape-Specialties))
- **Withheld GRAMA pages** beyond the initial 112-page release — redactions citing **Utah Code 63G-2-305(11)** (security of public property) and privacy exemptions ([Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings))
- **Russell Kennington** — circulated reports of a **trespass/obstruction arrest ~24 hours post-event** at the UVU scene ([Russell Kennington](/distraction_people/Russell_Kennington)); public-records accounts **conflict** — treated as a separate distraction thread from the [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) capital case, not proof of scene tampering

## Key open questions

- Who owned **rooftop clearance** — UVU PD, Orem joint unit, TPUSA, or some combination?
- Why was the **joint Public Order Unit** not activated after the September 3 Orem ticket email?
- Were **pre-event promises** to secure Losee Center documented in unreleased contracts or GRAMA pages?
- Did anyone deliver the September 9 **Kirk warning** to Flood or Harpole — or was escalation limited to internal UVU email?

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

UVU security staffing rosters for September 10, TPUSA private-security contracts, unreleased rooftop camera footage, and the full GRAMA set UVU acknowledges withholding are among disclosures the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may force into public view.

## Security-Gap Claims Amplified After GRAMA Releases

GRAMA-driven X coverage (@danksterintel / Daily Caller chain) remains the backbone of security
critique:

- Only **~six campus officers** for a crowd later described as ~3,000; UVU–Orem joint Public Order
  Unit **not activated**.
- False **"suspect in custody"** alert minutes after the shot; later **Run-Hide-Fight** text still
  containing **"[INSERT location]"** placeholder.
- Faculty active-shooter training gaps and no on-site ambulance coordination noted in the same
  records discussion.

Citizen investigators treat these as either catastrophic negligence or worse; official defenders
call them resource and communication failures under surprise attack. Document both readings.
