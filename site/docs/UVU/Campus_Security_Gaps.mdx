# UVU Campus Security Gaps on September 10

The Kirk assassination exposed **layered security failures** at Utah Valley University — some documented in **GRAMA records**, others alleged in reporting and post-event photography.

## Pre-Event Measures (What Existed)

According to compilations from the trash `campus_security` analysis and mainstream reporting:

- **Metal detectors** and **bag checks** at the outdoor amphitheater entrance
- **TPUSA private security** for close protection (hybrid model with campus police)
- **Limited perimeter** around the speaking area — not a full rooftop or building envelope

## Documented Institutional Failures (GRAMA)

From [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) review of **112 pages** of UVU GRAMA records (also on [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)):

| Failure | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Understaffing** | ~**6 campus police** for ~**3,000** attendees |
| **Joint Public Order Unit** | UVU-funded UVU–Orem unit **not activated** |
| **No ambulance on site** | Medical response delayed |
| **No outside LE coordination** | Despite Orem PD sending ticket link Sept 3 |
| **No active-shooter faculty training** | Documented gap in records |
| **False first alert** | "Suspect in custody" **19 min** post-shooting — not true |
| **Placeholder alert text** | "Shooter in [INSERT location]" **88 min** post-shooting |

## Rooftop and Perimeter Vulnerabilities

### Losee Center Rooftop

Prosecutors allege the shot came from the **Losee Center rooftop** (~150 yards). Security criticisms include:

- **Unmonitored building access** — Fox News published photos weeks later showing a path to the rooftop still obtainable ([Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized))
- **Surveillance gaps** on surrounding structures
- No publicly released proof that UVU police conducted **pre-event rooftop clearance**

### TPUSA vs. Campus Roles

Commentators debate whether **TPUSA production/security** or **UVU police** owned rooftop/overwatch responsibility. Contracts and after-action reports are **not public**.

## Law-Enforcement Coordination

- **Orem PD** sent UVU a Charlie Kirk ticket link **September 3**; post-shooting statements reportedly said Orem was "not involved"
- **FBI** assumed lead role; local agencies coordinated manhunt
- Allegations (project note #1) that **FBI told locals not to interview witnesses** — unverified; see [Government Agency Claims](/Theories/Government_Agencies_Claims)

## Post-Event Security Narrative

UVU's public messaging emphasized **campus closure, counseling, and policy reviews**. Critics contrast that with **rapid courtyard paving** ([Pavers](/UVU/Pavers)) and **withheld GRAMA pages** beyond the initial 112-page release.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* UVU security staffing rosters for September 10, TPUSA private-security contracts, unreleased rooftop camera footage, and the full GRAMA set UVU acknowledges withholding are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.
