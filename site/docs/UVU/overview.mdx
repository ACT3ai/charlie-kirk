UVU matters as both **kill zone** and **institution**. GRAMA records (Daily Caller / @danksterintel)
show two parties flagged concerns on September 9 — men asking police about **"a plan"** and a
separate undelivered **message to Charlie** — while six campus officers covered ~3,000 attendees
and the UVU–Orem joint unit never activated.

The post-shooting record is equally contested: false **"suspect in custody"** alerts, placeholder
**"[INSERT location]"** text, courtyard **paving within days** ([Hardscape Specialties](./Hardscape-Specialties)),
and withheld GRAMA beyond the released 112 pages. Physical-site threads include [Losee rooftop](./Losee_Center)
claims vs. [tent/mic](./Tent) theories and [witness trajectories](/Witnesses/Nick).
