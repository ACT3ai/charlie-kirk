UVU is both **kill zone** and **institution**. GRAMA records ([@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) / Daily Caller) show two parties flagged concerns on September 9 — men asking police about **"a plan"** and a separate undelivered **message to Charlie** — while **six campus police** covered ~3,000 attendees and the UVU–Orem joint unit never activated. [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long) and [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) sit at the center of the **rooftop failure** debate that matters because charging documents place the shooter on [Losee Center](./Losee_Center) for nearly an hour.

The **courtyard** is where people and physics converge: [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak) asked the final question, [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) appears in arm-puller footage, and the **Canon XA55** at ~46 m recorded multi-boom audio that shaped-charge researchers cite against the lone-rifle narrative. Post-shot, false **"suspect in custody"** alerts, placeholder **"[INSERT location]"** text, and **courtyard paving within days** ([Pavers](./Pavers), [Hardscape](./Hardscape-Specialties)) frame the cover-up thread — while the [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview) capital case treats UVU surveillance as core evidence ([trial press](/Media/tyler-robinson-trial-press)).
