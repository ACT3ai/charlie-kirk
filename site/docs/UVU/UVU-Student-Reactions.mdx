# UVU Student Reactions

This page documents **student-visible effects** of the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) assassination at UVU — what official channels reported, what GRAMA records show about emergency messaging, and what researchers have flagged in public eyewitness media. It does not generalize the political views of the student body.

## Immediate On-Campus Experience (September 10)

### Event Attendees vs. Broader Campus

The TPUSA "American Comeback Tour" stop drew an outdoor crowd estimated at **~3,000** in GRAMA summaries, while UVU's broader enrollment meant **thousands more** students were on or near campus during the lockdown. Official [@UVU](/Influencers/x/uvu) posts ([compiled](./UVU_Social_Media_Response)) cite the shooting at **~12:10 PM**; prosecutor/FBI timelines often use **12:23 PM** — a discrepancy researchers should reconcile against released camera metadata.

### Lockdown and Shelter-in-Place

UVU instructed students to:

- Remain in place until **police escort**
- Treat the campus as **closed** with classes canceled

Students therefore experienced the shooting aftermath through **UVU alert texts/apps** — the same channel that GRAMA records show sent:

| Timing | Alert problem |
|---|---|
| ~19 minutes post-shooting | **False** "suspect in custody" language |
| ~88 minutes post-shooting | **Placeholder** "Shooter in [INSERT location]" |

An estimated **~10,000 students** may have believed the campus was secure during the false-custody window ([Pre-Shooting Warnings](./pre-shooting-warnings), [Campus Security Gaps](./Campus_Security_Gaps)).

## Documented Support and Community Events

### Counseling and Return to Campus

UVU's uvu.edu statement emphasized grief support and a careful return to instruction — ["Welcoming you back to campus with care"](https://www.uvu.edu/news/2025/welcoming-you-back-to-campus-with-care.html).

### Vigil for Unity

[@UVU](/Influencers/x/uvu) announced a **Vigil for Unity** — **September 19, 2025**, UCCU Center, doors **2:30 PM** ([@UVU post](https://x.com/UVU/status/1968004319350841422)). Details and adjacent public claims are on [UVU Social Media Response](./UVU_Social_Media_Response).

## Eyewitness and Student-Captured Media

Research notes compiled alongside official posts reference **bystander video** alleging a figure on a building before the shot ([@KylieJaneKremer](https://x.com/KylieJaneKremer/status/1965894858624974975)) and acoustic theories about shot origin ([@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880)). Treat these as **unverified** unless matched to chain-of-custody metadata.

The Laws section below flags interest in whether students were asked to delete footage or faced platform takedowns — **allegations not adjudicated** in public court filings reviewed for this page.

## Security Concerns Students Inherit from Institutional Gaps

Students attending large outdoor events at [UVU Courtyard](./Courtyward) inherited a security model documented in GRAMA as:

- **Metal detectors** and bag checks at amphitheater entrance
- **Limited perimeter** — not a full rooftop/building envelope ([Campus Security Gaps](./Campus_Security_Gaps))
- **Hybrid security** — campus police plus TPUSA private detail ([Security Team](/Security_Team/overview))
- **No on-site ambulance**

Post-shooting, the courtyard was rapidly paved ([Pavers](./Pavers)), affecting any student-documented ground evidence.

## What This Page Does Not Claim

Trash-era drafts attributed specific reactions to **College Republicans**, **TPUSA chapters**, and **liberal student groups** without primary citations. Those narratives are **omitted** here pending verifiable sources (student-government minutes, dated organization posts, named interviews).

## A Student Witness in Her Own Words

A UVU student who gave her name as **Sarah** told interviewers she had **classes at UVU that day** and went to watch Charlie Kirk between classes. Across two on-camera interviews her description of the wound location **shifted from chest/heart to neck** — a discrepancy documented in the site's witness-analysis notes. On the aftermath she said: *"I'm definitely gonna be seeing my counselor, I will have a lot of therapy ... I just couldn't stop crying."* The wound-location reversal is treated here as an **unresolved discrepancy**, not proof of anything.

## Allegation: "Trauma Counseling" Aimed at Witnesses

A separate account amplified by [@DDGotAPodcast](/Influencers/x/ddgotapodcast) and compiled in research notes (cross-checked in grok query threads against [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305)-era GRAMA work) describes a UVU student who says he was **~20 feet away** accepting a **"free trauma counseling" session advertised exclusively to witnesses**. He alleges the session ran roughly **two hours with no real therapy**, that the door was **locked from outside**, and that he was told *"you're the next target."*

| Alleged detail | Status |
|---|---|
| Session offered only to witnesses | **Unverified** — no UVU program name in primary record |
| **~2 hours**, door locked | **Unverified** — student account only |
| *"You're the next target"* quote | **Unverified** — attributed, not corroborated |

These are **unverified allegations** — no police report, therapist name, or agency name has surfaced — and are flagged here as reported claims of possible **witness intimidation**, not established fact. UVU's public counseling messaging ([uvu.edu return-to-campus statement](https://www.uvu.edu/news/2025/welcoming-you-back-to-campus-with-care.html)) is separate from this anecdote.

## Instruction to Delete Footage

Witness **Ryne Simmons** reportedly said he filmed the assassination in **4K at 60 fps**, sent it to the FBI to assist investigators, and was **instructed to delete** his video; he says he kept a copy. Reported alongside broader takedown claims, this account is **not adjudicated** in public filings and is presented as an allegation. See [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* UVU eyewitness camera footage of the courtyard, records of students asked to delete their videos, and platform takedown logs for removed student posts are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.

## Student-Facing Impacts Discussed Publicly

X and local media preserved student accounts of:

- Lockdown confusion after the **false "suspect in custody"** alert.
- Trauma of reopening a courtyard many believed still held forensic meaning.
- Vigil and counseling responses — often cited by UVU as care, and by skeptics as narrative
  management.

Eyewitness mobile video (see [Eyewitness Mobile](/cameras/Eyewitness_Mobile_Video)) is the other
student-facing evidence stream.
