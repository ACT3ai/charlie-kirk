# UVU Campus Maps

Utah Valley University sits in **Orem, Utah** (~45 miles south of Salt Lake City). The
September 10, 2025 Charlie Kirk event used the **outdoor courtyard** west of the **Losee Center** —
the geometric anchor for shooter-location debates, security-gap reviews, and witness trajectory
accounts.

## Event core (courtyard complex)

| Feature | Investigative role |
|---------|-------------------|
| **White canopy / tent** | Stage where Charlie spoke; [exploding mic theory](./Tent) |
| **Courtyard lawn** | Later **paved over** — [Pavers](./Pavers), [Hardscape](./Hardscape-Specialties) |
| **Audience bowl** | Witness videos; [Nick](/Witnesses/Nick) trajectory (shot from Kirk's **right**) |
| **Walkways / tunnels** | Alternative-position theories; background movement in footage |
| **Ingress gates** | Metal detectors and bag checks per [security gaps](./Campus_Security_Gaps) |

Detail page: [UVU Courtyard](./Courtyward).

## Losee Center (east of courtyard)

Charging documents allege the fatal shot originated from the **Losee Center rooftop**
(~150 yards east). Investigators debate whether line-of-sight, audio, and witness accounts
support that distance and angle — see [Losee Center](./Losee_Center) and
[Gun/Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview).

Weeks after the shooting, [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized)
published photos suggesting **rooftop access paths** remained obtainable.

## 3D and trajectory work

Citizen researchers built **3D reconstructions** testing multiple shooter positions and
synchronizing audio/video (CK_FILE: sound-arrival-order analyses). Results are contested;
use as hypothesis tools, not court findings.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

Campus CAD drawings, rooftop-access logs, geofence warrant maps, and withheld surveillance
feeds are among the records the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may compel.
