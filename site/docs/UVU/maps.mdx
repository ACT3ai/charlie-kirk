# UVU Campus Maps

Utah Valley University sits in **Orem, Utah** (~45 miles south of Salt Lake City). The
September 10, 2025 Charlie Kirk event used the **outdoor courtyard** west of the **Losee Center** —
the geometric anchor for shooter-location debates, security-gap reviews, and witness trajectory
accounts.

| Feature | Investigative role |
|---------|-------------------|

## Losee Center (east of courtyard)

Charging documents allege the fatal shot originated from the **Losee Center rooftop**
(~150 yards east). Investigators debate whether line-of-sight, audio, and witness accounts
support that distance and angle — see [Losee Center](./Losee_Center) and
[Gun/Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview).

Weeks after the shooting, [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized)
published photos suggesting **rooftop access paths** remained obtainable.

Per the charging documents, the courtyard sits roughly **150 yards** (about **430 feet / 130 meters**) west of the Losee rooftop position, and one CK_FILE ballistics note argues a shot from that roof toward a crouching target would carry only a **5–9 degree** downward angle.

## Suspect's alleged route across campus

The probable‑cause affidavit (summarized in CK_FILE) traces a path a reader can follow on a campus map. The individual described as "Suspect" was first seen around **11:50 a.m.** crossing a **grassy area north of Campus Drive near 800 West**, then walking south through a **parking lot** to a **pedestrian tunnel under Campus Drive**, heading toward the Losee Center. He was recorded on the north side of the building around **12:02 p.m.**, entered through **southeast‑corner doors**, and reached the rooftop via an interior stairwell and a short concrete wall. After the shot he is said to have sprinted to the **northeast corner**, dropped to the grass, and fled **north across Campus Drive** into a wooded area where a rifle was later reported recovered. These are allegations from the public filing, not proven facts; **Tyler Robinson** is presumed innocent.

## Construction site adjacent to campus

A separate geographic thread points to a **construction site next to the university** at **785 College Dr, Orem, UT 84058**. Electrician **Dylan Hope** — a witness reportedly working there — described seeing a man in all‑black tactical clothing, and CK_FILE notes he told officers the photo shown to the crew did not match the FBI's later public image. This location matters to the map because it places a working eyewitness immediately adjacent to the campus perimeter. The account is a reported witness statement, not an adjudicated finding.

## Sound-analysis anchor point

For audio geolocation, CK_FILE identifies a **Canon XA55** broadcast camera positioned roughly **46 meters** from the tent, recording **four uncompressed audio channels at 48 kHz**. Its fixed distance makes it a useful anchor for researchers attempting to time‑align booms across the courtyard; the analyses built on it are contested hypothesis tools, not court findings.

use as hypothesis tools, not court findings.
