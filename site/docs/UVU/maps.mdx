# UVU Campus Maps

Utah Valley University sits in **Orem, Utah** (~45 miles south of Salt Lake City). The **September 10, 2025** Charlie Kirk event occupied the **outdoor courtyard west of the Losee Center** — roughly **3,000 attendees** under a white canopy whose opening faced **east** toward the building. This page maps the geometric relationships investigators use for shooter-location debates, affidavit movement paths, acoustic anchors, and perimeter witnesses. **Tyler Robinson** is the **accused** shooter in charging documents and is **presumed innocent**.

## Losee Center (east of courtyard)

Charging documents allege the fatal shot originated from the **Losee Center rooftop**
(~150 yards east). Investigators debate whether line-of-sight, audio, and witness accounts
support that distance and angle — see [Losee Center](./Losee_Center),
[Gun/Bullet — trajectory](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle), and
[Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Trajectory_Wound_Mismatch).

Weeks after the shooting, [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized)
published photos suggesting **rooftop access paths** remained obtainable.

Per the charging documents, the courtyard sits roughly **150 yards** (about **430 feet / 130 meters**) west of the Losee rooftop position, and one CK_FILE ballistics note argues a shot from that roof toward a crouching target would carry only a **5–9 degree** downward angle.

## Suspect's alleged route across campus (map-readable timeline)

The probable‑cause affidavit traces a path a reader can follow on a campus map. The individual described as "Suspect" — charged as **Tyler Robinson**, not convicted — appears in [UVU surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance) summaries as follows:

| Time | Map landmark | Alleged movement |
|------|--------------|------------------|
| **08:29 a.m.** | Campus entry | Gray **Dodge Challenger** arrival; maroon tee, light shorts, black hat — no backpack |
| **~11:50 a.m.** | Grass north of **Campus Drive / ~800 West** | Southbound crossing; **limp gait**; black flag shirt in later frames |
| **~11:50+ a.m.** | **Parking lot → pedestrian tunnel** under Campus Drive | South toward Losee — same tunnel cited in rear-of-tent theories |
| **~12:02 p.m.** | **North side, Losee Center** | Enter **southeast-corner** doors |
| **12:15–12:22 p.m.** | Interior stairs → rooftop | Wall climb; prone **west edge** facing courtyard |
| **~12:23 p.m.** | Courtyard line-of-sight | Shot fired at tent stage |
| **~12:23+ p.m.** | **Northeast corner** → grass → north across Campus Drive | Escape toward wooded recovery area of alleged Mauser rifle |

These are **allegations** from the public filing, not proven facts. Compare [Tyler Robinson — Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy) for clothing and backpack mismatch threads.

## Construction site adjacent to campus

A separate geographic thread points to a **construction site next to the university** at **785 College Dr, Orem, UT 84058**. Electrician **Dylan Hope** — a witness reportedly working there — described seeing a man in all‑black tactical clothing, and CK_FILE notes he told officers the photo shown to the crew did not match the FBI's later public image. This location places a working eyewitness immediately adjacent to the campus perimeter. The account is a **reported witness statement**, not an adjudicated finding — see [black-clothing suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect).

## Tunnel, grey van, and acoustic map points

The **pedestrian tunnel under Campus Drive** links the north parking zone to the courtyard's east side and appears in both affidavit movement charts and citizen maps placing a **[grey van](/Suspects/Van_Man_Bednarz)** behind the tent. [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) summarizes a **~4940 Hz** left-channel signature near the courtyard edge in some audio analyses — a near-field hypothesis distinct from a 150-yard rooftop source. Treat as contested research, not court findings.

## Sound-analysis anchor point

For audio geolocation, CK_FILE identifies a **[Canon XA55](/cameras/Canon_XA55_Broadcast)** broadcast camera positioned roughly **46 meters** from the tent, recording **four uncompressed audio channels at 48 kHz**. Its fixed distance makes it a useful anchor for researchers attempting to time‑align booms across the courtyard; see [Mic overview](/Mic/overview) and [Courtyard](./Courtyward). Analyses built on it are hypothesis tools, not court findings.
