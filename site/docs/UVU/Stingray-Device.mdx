# Stingray Device at UVU (Claims)

Commentary alleges that **Turning Point USA** brought an **IMSI catcher** ("Stingray")
to UVU on September 10, 2025 — the same day Charlie Kirk was killed — as part of a broader
**mass surveillance** pattern at TPUSA tour stops. This page documents the **claim and its
limits**. We do **not** assert that TPUSA or any employee operated an illegal device or
knew of the killing in advance.

## The allegation

From CK_FILE / X research threads:

- TPUSA allegedly deploys Stingray-class devices at **"Make Heaven Crowded"** tour locations.
- Poster claims **photo proof** of a device at **UVU on Sept 10**.
- Framed as supporting the theory that TPUSA functions as a **mass surveillance operation**.

## What a Stingray does (technical context)

**IMSI catchers** mimic cell towers, forcing nearby phones to connect. Agencies use them to:

- Collect **subscriber identifiers** (IMSI) in bulk
- Log **movement paths** during an event
- **Fingerprint devices** for later matching even after SIM swaps

If present at UVU, such a device could theoretically log phones at the rally — including
foreign-registered devices discussed on [Israel/NSA cellphone](/Israel/NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive) pages. Presence would not, by itself, prove operational involvement in violence.

## Foreign-Registered Phones Allegedly Pinged at UVU

The Stingray claim is cited alongside a separate surveillance allegation in the CK research file. According to posts attributed to **Tony Seruga**, raw GPS data allegedly showed **44 foreign-registered cellphones** in the immediate area of Charlie Kirk when he was shot — reportedly **16 Israeli, 13 Chinese, 12 Russian, and 3 Iranian**. A separate, earlier report cited in the file claims the **NSA detected 12 Israeli-registered cellphones** at the site, handed the data to federal law enforcement, and passed it to the White House.

According to the same posts, **4 of the 16 Israeli-registered phones** later allegedly surfaced at the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** in Pakistan, where high-level US–Israel–Iran talks were reportedly taking place. These are unverified claims; presence of a device near an event is not proof of operational involvement.

## Bumblehive / NSA Utah Data Center (Cited Context)

The research file ties the surveillance theory to the **NSA's Utah Data Center** in **Bluffdale, Utah**, nicknamed **"Bumblehive."** According to Tony Seruga's posts, "cross-pollination" of call, text, location, app, and Wi-Fi metadata allegedly lets the system re-identify even a **"burner" phone** to its real owner after roughly **30–60 days of use**. An IMSI catcher operating on-site would, in theory, feed the same identifier-collection pattern. A noted alternate explanation in the file: UVU hosts the region's largest **Center for National Security Studies** program, so foreign nationals may legitimately be present.

## Geofencing Pattern: Show Faith By Works

The research file cites **"Show Faith By Works"** — described in posts as a company allegedly hired to **geotrack** Charlie Kirk's fans after his memorial and churchgoers across America. According to those posts, **FARA filings** reportedly reveal **523 US churches geo-fenced** for targeted advertising aimed at worshippers' phones. This is presented as part of the broader alleged mass-surveillance pattern, not as proof of any role in the shooting.
