# UVU Administration Response

This page tracks **documented administrative actions** at Utah Valley University on and after September 10, 2025 — drawn from GRAMA releases, official @UVU posts, and uvu.edu statements. It does not attribute motives or wrongdoing to named officials.

## Pre-Event Cabinet Communications (September 9)

GRAMA document **UVU 25-330:0023** shows a UVU staff member escalated a **10:35 PM** report — two visitors asking to speak with police about **"a plan"** — to the **university cabinet** (president's office chain). A follow-up at **11:19 PM** reportedly dismissed the visitors as **"GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did."**

According to [@danksterintel's](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) review of 112 released pages:

- No one in the chain asked what **"the plan"** was
- No follow-up documented what the men told police
- Identities remain **redacted**

A separate **2:23 PM** email (documents **0079/0080**) sought to relay a message to Charlie Kirk through Chief **Jeff Long's** chain; delivery is **not documented** in the release. See [Pre-Shooting Warnings](./pre-shooting-warnings).

## GRAMA vs. @UVU official posts (cross-check)

[@danksterintel's](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305) review of the **112-page** Daily Caller GRAMA release is the backbone for administrative facts on this page. Cross-checked against official [@UVU](https://x.com/UVU) posts compiled on [UVU Social Media Response](./UVU_Social_Media_Response):

| Topic | GRAMA / danksterintel record | @UVU public line |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-event warnings | Sept 9 **"plan"** escalated to cabinet; **11:19 PM** dismissal as **"GOP reps"** | No contemporaneous public warning |
| First custody language | False **"suspect in custody"** ~**19 min** post-shot | Initial post cites shooting + custody framing |
| Location alert | **"Shooter in [INSERT location]"** ~**88 min** post-shot | Shelter-in-place without corrected location text in compiled posts |
| Post-event site | Courtyard paved ~**Sept 14** per contractor account | No public engineering/preservation rationale located |
| Records | **Additional GRAMA beyond 112 pages** acknowledged but withheld | Grief/counseling messaging on uvu.edu |

Discrepancies are **documented messaging gaps**, not proof any official acted with criminal intent.

## Day-of-Event Actions (September 10)

### Campus Operations

Official @UVU posts ([compiled here](./UVU_Social_Media_Response)) report:

| Action | Source |
|---|---|
| Shooting during visiting-speaker event; initial custody language | [@UVU](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965856814320300326) |
| **Campus closed**, classes canceled, shelter-in-place | [@UVU](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965868138228068793) |
| FBI tip line shared | [@UVU](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965969976184988136) |

### Emergency Alert Failures (GRAMA)

Released records cited on [Campus Security Gaps](./Campus_Security_Gaps) and [Pre-Shooting Warnings](./pre-shooting-warnings) document administrative messaging problems:

| Alert | Issue |
|---|---|
| First alert (**~19 min** post-shooting) | Reportedly stated suspect **in custody** when he was not |
| Run-Hide-Fight alert (**~88 min** post-shooting) | Placeholder text: **"Shooter in [INSERT location]"** |

Researchers estimate **~10,000 students** remained on campus during the false-custody window.

### Security Posture Decisions

GRAMA compilations document pre-event administrative/security choices later questioned in reporting:

- **~6 campus police** for a crowd later described as **~3,000**
- UVU–Orem **Joint Public Order Unit** reportedly **not activated** despite UVU funding it for large events
- **No ambulance** staged on site
- **No outside law-enforcement coordination** despite Orem PD sending a ticket link **September 3**
- Faculty reportedly had **no active-shooter training** on record

For perimeter and rooftop gaps, see [Campus Security Gaps](./Campus_Security_Gaps), [Losee Center](./Losee_Center), and [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview).

## Coordination gaps: Orem PD, FBI, and TPUSA

GRAMA summaries document **documented gaps** in how UVU administration aligned campus police, outside agencies, and the event host:

| Partner | Documented gap |
|---|---|
| **Orem PD** | Ticket link emailed **Sept 3**; **Joint Public Order Unit** (UVU-funded for large events) **not activated** |
| **FBI** | Tip line promoted post-shooting; **no documented pre-event joint threat desk** in released GRAMA |
| **TPUSA** | Outdoor amphitheater layout approved; **hybrid security** (campus + private detail) without full rooftop envelope |

Private close-protection disputes (e.g., [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) rooftop texts to [Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)) sit in [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview) and [Law Enforcement at UVU](/Law_Enforcement/overview). This page records **institutional choices** visible in GRAMA; it does **not** claim any Orem officer, FBI agent, or TPUSA staffer knew of the killing in advance.

## Post-Event Administrative Messaging

### Public Statements

UVU published a grief-and-condolences statement on uvu.edu — ["Welcoming you back to campus with care"](https://www.uvu.edu/news/2025/welcoming-you-back-to-campus-with-care.html) — emphasizing counseling and return-to-campus support.

@UVU later announced a **Vigil for Unity** (Sept 19, 2025, UCCU Center). Details are on [UVU Social Media Response](./UVU_Social_Media_Response).

### Records Transparency

UVU has acknowledged **additional GRAMA documents exist** beyond the initial **112-page** DCNF release and has **not released** them. Names in September 9 warning chains remain redacted under state exemptions.

### Physical Site Changes

Contractor **Dan Merrell** reports being told the courtyard pave-over was ordered for completion by **Monday** — see [Pavers](./Pavers). UVU has not publicly released an after-action security report or TPUSA contract terms.

## Open Administrative Questions

## Leadership Transition After the Event

According to project research notes citing [@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305)-era commentary, **UVU President Astrid S. Tuminez** reportedly resigned in **2026** — the same year Washington County **Sheriff Nate Brooksby**, who handled the suspect's surrender, also resigned. Both are noted here only as documented leadership changes; no court finding ties either resignation to wrongdoing in the case.

- **Status (Astrid S. Tuminez):** Alive
- Post-shooting, UVU's president **declined to give specifics**, citing a **pending external review** (per AP); a UVU spokesperson **declined detailed questions** after the shooting.

## GRAMA Records: Exact Documents and Redaction Basis

The pre-event chain released by the **Daily Caller News Foundation** (50 documents, 112 pages) maps to specific record numbers: **UVU 25-330:0023, 0024, 0079, 0080**. The **10:35 PM** cabinet message (0023) reads in part: *"two people showed up at their offices asking to talk to police about a plan ... If this is indeed true we do need to notify."* The **11:19 PM** reply states the visitors were *"NOT from a senator's office. They are GOP reps who played like they knew more than they did!"*

Every name in the September 9 communications is redacted under **Utah Code 63G-2-305(11)** — state exemptions for *"security of public property"* and *"privacy."* Researchers question why the identities remain withheld given the suspect's reported custody and the concluded event. See [Pre-Shooting Warnings](./pre-shooting-warnings).
