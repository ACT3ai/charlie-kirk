The **highest-stakes camera gap** investigators cite is the contrast between **low-quality public suspect clips** and the **HD institutional and production systems** that existed on campus — plus witness **Ryne Simmons'** reported **4K close-up** that the FBI allegedly asked him to delete ([Censorship](/Censorship/Ryne_Simmons_FBI_Video)). The morning Tyler timeline is a **chain of captioned sightings** on [Ring](./Ring), [WYZE](./WYZE), and [UVU surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance), not eyewitness IDs; the [Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Broadcast) broadcast track adds a separate **four-channel acoustic** record ~46 m from the tent that citizen analysts use in bullet-versus-device debates.

The **substance** is timestamps, missing files, and contested enhancement. Doorbell and WYZE cameras place a figure in maroon and light shorts between **8:07 and 9:57 a.m.**; UVU CCTV picks up a stiff-gait figure in a black flag shirt from **~11:50 a.m.** through the **12:23 p.m.** shot. Evening [Dairy Queen CCTV](./Merchant_And_Travel_CCTV) and missing **Washington County lobby** footage test whether the same person fits the full day. [Crime-scene camera actions](./Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions) — SD card removal, camera takedown — ask whether rear tent angles ever entered evidence. [TPUSA production](./TPUSA_Production_Footage) birthday clips released **selective 4K** while red-zone and roof angles stay withheld, per public commentary.
