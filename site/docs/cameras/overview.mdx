# Surveillance Cameras

Nearly the entire public case for [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s movements — and much of the debate over **what happened at the tent** — is built on **camera footage**: neighborhood doorbells, campus CCTV, crowd phones, TPUSA production rigs, and travel-stop CCTV. The **highest-stakes camera gap** investigators cite is the contrast between **low-quality public suspect clips** and the **HD institutional and production systems** that existed on campus — plus witness **Ryne Simmons'** reported **4K close-up** that the FBI allegedly asked him to delete ([Censorship](/Censorship/Ryne_Simmons_FBI_Video)). The morning Tyler timeline is a **chain of captioned sightings** on [Ring](./Ring), [WYZE](./WYZE), and [UVU surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance), not eyewitness IDs; the [Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Broadcast) broadcast track adds a separate **four-channel acoustic** record ~46 m from the tent that citizen analysts use in bullet-versus-device debates. On X, **[@Villgecrazylady](/Influencers/x/villgecrazylady)**'s map of that morning chain remains a common reference point; competing threads push **Wordle 11:28 vs Ring 11:44** timing notes and **courtyard-vs-rooftop 12:23** dual-location claims.

The **substance** is timestamps, missing files, and contested enhancement. Doorbell and WYZE cameras place a figure in maroon and light shorts between **8:07 and 9:57 a.m.**; UVU CCTV picks up a stiff-gait figure in a black flag shirt from **~11:50 a.m.** through the **12:23 p.m.** shot. Evening [Dairy Queen CCTV](./Merchant_And_Travel_CCTV) and missing **Washington County lobby** footage test whether the same person fits the full day. [Crime-scene camera actions](./Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions) — alleged SD card removal, alleged camera takedown — ask whether rear tent angles ever entered evidence; July 2026 X commentary revived **Terryl Farnsworth / flash-drive vs original SD card** chain-of-custody questions. [TPUSA production](./TPUSA_Production_Footage) birthday clips released **selective 4K** while red-zone and roof angles stay withheld; the same commentary cites a cease-and-desist **media-drone B-roll admission** while security was denied drones. Preliminary-hearing coverage describes UVU video of **rooftop positioning and flight**, not a verified public trigger-pull frame, and defense-aligned posts claim Agent **Hull** acknowledged under cross-examination that the roof figure lacks **identifiable features**.

Readers tracing the **official narrative** should start with [UVU Campus Surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance) and [Tyler on Cameras](./Tyler_On_Cameras). Readers testing **identification** should read [Tyler as Placeholder](./Tyler_Placeholder) and [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview). Readers focused on **what the tent cameras show** should pair [Eyewitness Mobile Video](./Eyewitness_Mobile_Video) with [Canon XA55](./Canon_XA55_Broadcast) and [Videos](/Videos/overview) — without duplicating the full censorship deep dive in [Censorship](/Censorship/overview).
