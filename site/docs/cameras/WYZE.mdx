# WYZE Cameras

**WYZE** is a low-cost home security camera brand commonly mounted on houses, garages, and porches. Two of the September 10 morning sightings in the released timeline come from a WYZE camera on **W 880 S**, a residential street near the [UVU](/UVU/overview) campus. Like [Ring](./Ring) doorbell footage, these are private neighborhood cameras — identification of the figure is an interpretation, not a device output.

## WYZE sightings on September 10, 2025

Per the footage described in investigation notes, a figure first in a **maroon shirt and light shorts** was captured by a WYZE camera at these times (source: [Charlie_Kirk.txt]):

| Time (MDT) | Action on W 880 S |
|------------|-------------------|
| **8:23 a.m.** | **Driving** |
| **9:57 a.m.** | **Walking** (before late-morning Ring return) |

The 8:23 sighting shows the figure in a **vehicle**; the 9:57 sighting shows the same street on foot. Together they are the **only morning frames** that place the figure specifically on **W 880 S**, and the 8:23 clip is one of only two **vehicle** frames in the morning chain (the other is **8:29** [UVU surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance) on campus).

## Outfit change seam

A reported **outfit change** to jeans and a long-sleeve shirt falls between the **9:57 WYZE** sighting and the late-morning **Ring** sightings at **11:44** and **11:49**. That seam is what allows investigators to treat early street footage and later campus rooftop footage as one person — see [Tyler on Surveillance Cameras](./Tyler_On_Cameras).

## Why the WYZE frames matter

The two WYZE timestamps sit **between** the early-morning [Ring](./Ring) approach and the late-morning return, so they are **load-bearing** for the claim that a single person moved continuously through the neighborhood. If WYZE timestamps are wrong — as the broader doorbell-timeline dispute alleges via Baron Coleman's **shadow analysis** on Ring — the continuity of the whole morning chain weakens.

## Non-Ring house cameras, same caveat

Neighborhood cameras say "Tyler," but the cameras do not. WYZE footage shows a passerby on one stretch of street; the name is supplied afterward. For disputed identification and surrender clothing mismatch, see [Tyler as a Placeholder Figure](./Tyler_Placeholder).
