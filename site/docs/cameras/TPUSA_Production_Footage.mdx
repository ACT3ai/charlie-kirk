# TPUSA Production Footage

**[Turning Point USA](/TPUSA/overview)** ran professional video production at the September 10 [UVU](/UVU/overview) event — tent cameras, stage feeds, and (per later admissions) **drone B-roll**. This page tracks what TPUSA has **released selectively**, what investigators and researchers say remains **withheld**, and who operated the rigs. We do **not** claim any named staff member knew of or participated in illegal acts.

## Official production cameras

Investigation notes list TPUSA AV staff who controlled audio and video that day, including **[Andrew Feraci](/People/andrew-feraci)** and **[Charles McClintock Wilson](/People/charles-mcclintock-wilson)** (see [TPUSA Staff Sept 10](/key_individuals/TPUSA_Staff_Sept_10)). **[Jeremy Gallman](/People/jeremy-gallman)** is identified in social posts as a TPUSA camera operator and video editor (Phoenix-based; LinkedIn cited in [Charlie_Kirk.txt]).

These feeds differ from [UVU campus surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance): they are **event-production** angles on Charlie, the crowd, and the tent — often higher resolution than the low-quality suspect clips later released to the public.

## Birthday tribute — selective 4K release

Citizen-investigator commentary (source: [Charlie_Kirk.txt], TPUSA drone section) describes TPUSA's **October 14** [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) birthday tribute video as containing **4K clips from campus cameras** researchers had been asking for to identify shooters, aircraft, and cover-up indicators. The same commentary alleges:

- FBI had **warned** against releasing footage to "protect the investigation."
- The tribute **cherry-picks** flattering angles while **red-zone cameras**, **roof sightlines**, and **security logs** stay hidden.
- If a gag existed, a birthday post using "untouchable" cam material is framed as **selective transparency**.

These are **reported allegations** in public posts, not court findings.

## Drone footage contradiction

Posts credit **[Tyler Bowyer](/People/tyler-bowyer)** and TPUSA's own birthday video with exposing a contradiction: leadership had claimed **drones were banned** by university police, yet the tribute includes a **crystal-clear overhead drone shot** with crowds an hour before the hit. Charlie reportedly reviewed drone feed with **[Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)**, who warned **"too many buildings."**

In a cease-and-desist to [@FinaceWolves](/Influencers/x/financewolves) (Zach), TPUSA allegedly **acknowledged** that a **"media production drone briefly flew for B-roll BEFORE the event"** while **security was denied** drone use — a contradiction researchers treat as central. Full drone analysis: [Drones](/Drones/overview).

## Camera behind Charlie — removal thread

Separate from drone B-roll, investigation notes ask **who removed the camera behind Charlie's head** before the shot. **[Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler)** is named in the file as having **taken down the camera** on the left while a mechanical issue was staged (see [Crime-Scene Camera Actions](./Crime_Scene_Camera_Actions)). Cutler is a **living person**; this page reports the allegation only and does not assert he committed a crime.

## Low-quality suspect video vs HD production

Commentary on [Videos/buckley-carlson-kash-patel-valhalla](/Videos/buckley-carlson-kash-patel-valhalla) notes **low-quality video of Tyler Robinson** was released despite **high-quality campus cameras** existing — implying institutional capacity to show clearer imagery than the public received.

## Citizen investigator claims on X (drone, SD cards, court)

Public commentary since the shooting has focused on **what TPUSA cameras captured and what left the chain of custody**.

- According to **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** on X (November 6, 2025; high-engagement thread), fresh drone stills and livestream clips allegedly sync with parking-lot and FBI-released frames, and the account names **Butch Hibbs** (described as a Utah drone expert) in connection with legal drone operations. These are **attributed claims**, not findings that any named person committed a crime.
- The same account (January 19, 2026) highlighted TPUSA's **cease-and-desist to [@FinaceWolves](/Influencers/x/financewolves)**, which investigators quote as acknowledging that a **"media production drone briefly flew for B-roll BEFORE the event"** while **security was denied** drones — a contradiction also attributed in commentary to statements by **Brian Harpool** ("no drones — university denied") and **Andrew Kolvet** (recharacterizing angles as "wide-angle from bridge, not drone").
- **[@Uncommonsince76](/Influencers/x/uncommonsince76)** (September 21, 2025) circulated video purporting to show TPUSA personnel removing an **SD card** from a camera near Charlie minutes after the shot. Later, **[@stratagemmer](/Influencers/x/stratagemmer)** (July 2026) claimed lead investigator **Hull** testified that **Terryl Farnsworth** delivered a **flash drive** and Google links rather than original SD cards, while Farnsworth's sworn statement allegedly mentions only links — a **chain-of-custody** dispute, not a court finding of spoliation against any living person on this site.
- During preliminary-hearing discussion (July 2026), some posts claim the judge asked how TPUSA handled **footage from the back of the tent**; other posts (**[@gatewood__](/Influencers/x/gatewood)**, **@DerekImproves**) assert there is still **no released footage of the trigger pull** and demand full raw multi-camera dumps.

These threads are **public commentary**. No court has found that TPUSA as an organization planned the assassination or that named staff intentionally destroyed evidence.

## What readers should prioritize

1. **Raw, unedited TPUSA feeds** from the tent and red zone with timestamps.
2. **Drone flight logs** and who authorized the pre-event B-roll flight.
3. **Chain of custody** for any clips used in the birthday tribute versus discovery — original SD cards vs later flash drives/Google links as raised in X commentary and investigator testimony discussion.
