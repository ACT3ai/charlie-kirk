# Tyler on Surveillance Cameras

This page collects every camera sighting the **released timeline** attributes to **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** on September 10–11, 2025. The figure is identified as Robinson by investigators and commentators reading footage; cameras record movement, not names.

**Status: Tyler Robinson is Alive (in custody, charged, not convicted as of this writing).** Defamation-safe framing applies throughout.

## How identification is argued

The thread tying sightings to one person is **clothing and gait**, not clear facial images. Morning street figure: maroon and light shorts. Campus rooftop figure: black American-flag shirt and stiff right leg. The **outfit change** between them is what lets investigators treat street and rooftop as one individual. Remove that assumption and the chain splits.

Commentary further alleges **morning "waif-like"** figure vs **afternoon "linebacker-shouldered"** enhanced stairwell photos look like **different people** (Baron Coleman / [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) "enhanced" thread — reported, not proven).

## The caption problem

Neighborhood cameras "say Tyler" only because someone captioned the clip. [Ring](./Ring) and [WYZE](./WYZE) show passersby; identification is supplied afterward. Competing reading: [Tyler as a Placeholder Figure](./Tyler_Placeholder).

## The clothing mismatch at surrender

The probable-cause affidavit notes Robinson turned himself in the night of September 10 **not wearing clothing consistent with surveillance during the incident** — dark hat, maroon T-shirt, jeans, white/gray Converse-style shoes (source: government document, [Charlie_Kirk.txt]). Rooftop suspect wore black American-flag shirt. Innocent explanation: he changed clothes. Placeholder reading: camera figure may not be Robinson.

## Missing surrender footage

Commentary alleges **Washington County Sheriff lobby CCTV** of Tyler arriving with parents is **unavailable**, and **no bodycam** from intake — see [Merchant & Travel CCTV](./Merchant_And_Travel_CCTV) and [Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff). If true, evening timeline gaps cannot be closed with video.

## Citizen investigator claims on X — chain, Wordle, dual location

- **[@Villgecrazylady](/Influencers/x/villgecrazylady)** (October 15, 2025) published a widely shared morning map that matches the table above (Ring → WYZE → UVU → outfit change → Ring return). That map is how many citizen timelines stayed synchronized with the affidavit narrative.
- The same account (December 17, 2025), citing **Washington Post** reporting, noted alleged **Wordle** chat activity around **11:28 a.m.** — about **sixteen minutes** before the first post-outfit-change **Ring** sighting at **11:44 a.m.** Commentators treat that as a soft timing seam; it is **not** a court-proven alibi.
- High-engagement posts by **[@HustleBitch_](/Influencers/x/hustlebitch)** and **[@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** ask how a figure could be shown **running through the UVU courtyard at 12:23 p.m.** and on the **Losee rooftop at the same second**. This is an **unresolved public contradiction claim**, not a resolved forensic finding.
- **[@zeeemedia](/Influencers/x/zeeemedia)** (July 7, 2026) reported that defense counsel **Kathryn Nester** elicited testimony from Agent **David Hull** that rooftop footage of the individual **lacks identifiable features or clothing** — supporting the [placeholder](./Tyler_Placeholder) reading if true.
- **[@Sunny61120](/Influencers/x/sunny61120)** (July 9, 2026) claimed stairwell camera labels flip between **"level 2 NE"** and **"level 1 NE"** on the same physical stairwell and parked white car — an **alleged edit**, unverified here.
- Preliminary-hearing coverage (July 2026) describes surveillance of arrival, rooftop climb, crouch near the edge, and flight. Multiple clarifications (including public AI summaries of the hearing) state the video **does not capture the trigger pull**, even when some viral posts claim it does.
