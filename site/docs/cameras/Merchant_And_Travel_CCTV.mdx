# Merchant & Travel CCTV

Not every camera in the [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) timeline is a doorbell or campus feed. **Restaurants, gas stations, and sheriff's lobbies** also appear in the released narrative — and in several cases the footage itself is disputed or reported missing.

**Status: Tyler Robinson is Alive (in custody, charged, not convicted).** Allegations about his movements are attributed to released timelines and commentary; we do not state guilt as fact.

## Dairy Queen — Orem, 6:38 p.m.

The probable-cause timeline includes a **6:38 p.m. MDT** sighting on **CCTV at a Dairy Queen** in Orem, roughly six hours after the shooting (source: investigation notes, [Charlie_Kirk.txt]).

Commentary from **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** describes an **unseen photo** of Tyler Robinson at Dairy Queen — location cited as **813 E 800 N, Orem, UT 84097** — wearing **maroon shirt, blue jeans, hat, and sunglasses**, described as calm and unbothered. The site is about **17 minutes** from campus by car.

Researchers ask:

- Was there **enough time** between rooftop exit and Dairy Queen given the official route?
- **Who drove** — and does vehicle footage exist for the trip south toward St. George that night?
- Does the **maroon shirt** at Dairy Queen align with morning [Ring](./Ring) footage but not rooftop [UVU](./UVU_Surveillance) clothing?

These are timeline questions, not proof of innocence or guilt.

## Cedar City Maverik — September 11 morning

On **September 11, 2025**, Tyler Robinson was reportedly captured on **surveillance video at a Maverik gas station in Cedar City, Utah**, around **7:15–7:17 a.m.** (source: [Charlie_Kirk.txt]). This frame sits in the **overnight drive south** narrative after the shooting and before the evening surrender in Washington County.

The Maverik clip is a **travel-corridor** camera — not neighborhood or campus — but it is load-bearing for the claim that Robinson transited southern Utah by car on September 11.

## Washington County Sheriff lobby — surrender CCTV

Government documents quoted in the investigation file state Tyler Robinson arrived at the **Washington County Sheriff's Office** with his parents at approximately **10:26 p.m.** on September 10 (with booking and transport details on [Washington County Sheriff](/Law_Enforcement/washington-county-sheriff)).

Citizen-research commentary alleges the **lobby CCTV** showing Tyler walking in with his parents is **"gone"** or no longer available — a **"double deletion"** paired with claims that **no body-worn camera** footage exists from intake (see hearing account of **Mr. Snow** in [Charlie_Kirk.txt]).

If lobby video were preserved, researchers argue it would test whether Robinson was **in custody without his phone** before the disputed **7:57 p.m. Discord** "I did it" message — a timeline thread on [court/discord-evidence-court](/court/discord-evidence-court), not resolved here.

## Why merchant CCTV matters

[Ring](./Ring), [WYZE](./WYZE), and [UVU surveillance](./UVU_Surveillance) build the **morning-of** story. Dairy Queen, Maverik, and sheriff lobby cameras test whether the **afternoon and evening** movements fit one continuous person — or whether gaps and missing files break the chain.
