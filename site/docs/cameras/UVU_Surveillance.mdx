# UVU Campus Surveillance

Unlike the neighborhood [Ring](./Ring) and [WYZE](./WYZE) cameras, **UVU campus surveillance** is an official institutional system covering walkways, parking lots, building exteriors, and some rooftops. It is the backbone of the probable-cause affidavit: investigators say they reviewed UVU's cameras and used them to track a rooftop suspect across campus in the hour before the shot.

## What the affidavit says the cameras showed

According to the **affidavit of probable cause**, investigators reviewed UVU surveillance and observed an individual on the **rooftop of the Losee Center** at the time of the shooting, referred to as "Suspect." From the footage the suspect is described as a white male in a **dark hat, sunglasses, a long-sleeve black shirt with an American flag and eagle, dark jeans, and white/gray Converse-style shoes**, carrying a dark backpack and walking with a **distinctive stiff, slow, right-leg gait** (source: probable-cause affidavit quoted in [Charlie_Kirk.txt]).

The tracked movements begin at **approximately 11:50 a.m.** on 9/10/2025:

- **~11:50 a.m.** — UVU camera first captures the suspect walking across a grassy area north of Campus Drive at about **800 West**, then south through a parking lot toward a pedestrian tunnel under Campus Drive.
- **~11:53 a.m.** — Suspect pauses at the top of the tunnel stairs and pulls what appears to be a cellphone from his right pocket, then returns it.
- **~12:02 p.m.** — Suspect recorded on the north side of the **Losee Center**, then enters through doors on the building's southeast corner.
- **12:23 p.m.** — The shot is fired during the UVU event.

## Rooftop stills mapping the exit

Citizen-research notes describe a sequence of UVU/building surveillance stills timestamped **12:23:29, 12:23:34, 12:23:45, and 12:23:54**, with red arrows tracing a figure's path from the shooting-position area toward the **northeast corner of the Losee Center** (source: image description in [Charlie_Kirk.txt]). These frames are used to argue how the suspect left the roof in the seconds after the shot.

## An earlier morning sighting

In addition to the 11:50 a.m. rooftop track, the released morning timeline includes one earlier UVU-surveillance frame:

- **8:29 a.m.** — UVU surveillance, the figure **driving on campus** (source: investigation notes, [Charlie_Kirk.txt]).

This is the only UVU-camera sighting that predates the late-morning rooftop sequence, and the only one showing a vehicle on campus.

## Open questions about UVU footage (claims)

Public commentary raises recurring questions that UVU's footage could answer:

- Were **all relevant UVU recordings preserved** and provided to investigators and defense counsel under a proper chain of custody?
- Are there **unreleased angles** — certain rooftops, the tunnel, building edges — that could test the single-rooftop-shooter narrative?
- Do the recordings clearly show the suspect's face, or only the gait and clothing used to infer identity? This bears directly on the [placeholder/decoy question](./Tyler_Placeholder).

These are transparency questions, not findings that footage was lost or withheld. A related, separate concern about institutional footage handling is covered at [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed).
