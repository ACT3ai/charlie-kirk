# Witness Footage Pressure

Beyond platforms and gag orders, **individual witnesses** report pressure on their recordings.

## Missing or altered phone video (claims)

[Media Censorship](/Media/Censorship) summarizes attendee accounts that **phone clips recorded at UVU** were **missing or appeared edited** by the time they got home — **anecdotal**, not verified here.

## Skyler Baird (claim)

Commentary in the master file alleges witness **[Skyler Baird](/People/skylar-baird)** was **asked to delete phone footage**. **Unverified** — listed because influencers cite it as part of a pattern.

## Ryne Simmons — FBI deletion

The best-documented case: [Ryne Simmons — FBI Video](./Ryne_Simmons_FBI_Video) (4K close-up, federal delete request).

## FBI "protect the investigation" vs selective release

Commentary contrasts:

- [FBI](/FBI/overview) allegedly **warned against footage release** to "protect the investigation"
- Later **selective 4K clips** dropped (e.g. birthday tribute framing) while **red-zone cams, roof angles, security logs** remain withheld

That **selectivity** argument is commentary — not proof insiders were protected.

## SD card / CCTV threads

- **[Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth)** allegedly removed **SD cards** before police secured area — evidence-handling thread crossing [Tent](/Tent/overview) and [cameras](/cameras/overview)
- **The executive said to have removed an SD card** had reportedly never attended another university event — per CK_FILE research note. Neither the removal nor the attendance point is confirmed.

## N1098L / signal jam theory (contested)

Some posts theorize **N1098L** or ISR aircraft could **jam livestreams** or **alter phone uploads** — highly contested. See [Planes/N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview). Documented as **speculation**, not fact.
