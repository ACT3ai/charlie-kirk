# Platform — YouTube & Meta

Long-form video is central to Kirk OSINT. Creators report **policy friction** on major platforms.

## YouTube

Reported experiences include:

- **Age restrictions** on graphic UVU reconstructions
- **Demonetization** / not-suitable-for-advertisers on assassination-analysis videos
- **Strikes** under **harmful misinformation** or violence policies
- Multi-hour **ballistics and timeline** breakdowns affected disproportionately vs ordinary political content (creator self-report)

**Counter-distribution:** mirrors to **Rumble**, **BitChute**, and **IPFS** — see [Videos](/Videos/overview).

### @HolonCitizen (documented elsewhere)

The most detailed **HolonCitizen** YouTube suppression thread lives on
[CoverUp — YouTube HolonCitizen](/CoverUp/YouTube_HolonCitizen_Censored) — not duplicated here.

## Meta (Facebook / Instagram)

Less central to OSINT than X, but investigation notes report:

- **Cross-posted UVU clips** triggering automatic **violence violation** removals
- Reduced reach on shared links to alternative investigation sites

## Mike Adams framing

[@HealthRanger](https://x.com/HealthRanger/status/1965918254028955789) connected Kirk-case speech climate to broader **"censorship"** themes — poster characterization, not platform admission.

## Ordinary moderation note

Graphic shooting analysis legitimately triggers safety rules. This page tracks **Kirk-specific** reports; many takedowns may be **routine enforcement**, not targeted suppression.
