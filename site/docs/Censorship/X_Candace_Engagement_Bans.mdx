# X — Candace Owens Engagement Bans

[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) published a widely shared post titled in substance:
*"They're Banning THOUSANDS of Us for Simply LIKING, REPLYING or REPOSTING CANDACE OWENS POST."*
This page documents that **reported wave** — not a proven platform policy or donor conspiracy.

## What users report

According to the post and amplifying commentary:

- **Thousands** of accounts hit with **suspensions**, **12-hour locks**, **shadowbans**, and **"limited visibility"** warnings
- Triggers allegedly include **liking, retweeting, or commenting** on [@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO) posts about the Kirk assassination
- Violation categories cited repeatedly: **"violent speech," "hateful conduct," "harassment"**

## Topics tied to enforcement (claims)

Commentary links enforcement timing to Candace threads on:

- **Maroon shirts** / Egyptian plane contractors
- **Israeli Google Trends** spikes pre-event
- **Foreign money** / donor threads inside TPUSA

We **do not** assert any donor network or TPUSA insider **illegally** operated bot-report farms. The post **characterizes** enforcement as coordinated reporting; X has not released data confirming that.

## Archive campaign

Project Constitution urged readers to **screenshot every suspension** and post ban notices publicly so the scale could be documented — a citizen counter-archive parallel to [IPFS](/IPFS/ipfs.txt) evidence pinning.
