# Ryne Simmons — FBI Video Deletion

This is **government-adjacent** censorship — distinct from X/YouTube moderation.

## The account

From the investigation file and [@ninoboxer](https://x.com/ninoboxer/status/1982164944914162103) amplification:

- Witness **Ryne Simmons** says he recorded **close-up 4K @ 60fps** video of the assassination
- He **sent the footage to the FBI** to assist investigators
- **FBI at the federal level** reportedly called **multiple times** to ensure the video was **deleted** from his device
- Simmons says he **preserved a copy anyway** (claim)

## Why investigators treat this as critical

Commentary argues close-range high-frame-rate video could:

- Resolve **wound mechanism** debates (bullet vs device)
- Show **timing** relative to audio and crowd reaction
- Contradict or support **rooftop shooter** narrative

If federal investigators prioritized **deletion over chain-of-custody preservation**, that is a **transparency** issue — not yet a court finding.
