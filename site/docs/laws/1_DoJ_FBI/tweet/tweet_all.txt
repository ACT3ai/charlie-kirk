Why four laws, not one?

This set of four is designed to close every loophole those laws left open:

Law 1 forces law enforcement to disclose its files.
Law 2 forces the Intelligence Community to release its analytical product.
Law 3 mandates a real, funded, independent investigation — not just a record dump.
Law 4 embeds named civilian investigators inside the agencies so the work cannot be buried.

Every mandate is backed by criminal penalties, automatic budget cuts, no presidential override, whistleblower protections, and a citizen private right of action.

Law 1 — Force the FBI to Open Its Files

* Forces DoJ, FBI, ATF, CBP, TSA, DHS, FinCEN to disclose
* Covers every federal, state, and local law enforcement agency
* 30-day deadline from enactment
* All 175 Schedule A items plus broad catch-all
* No presidential override allowed
* 10 years prison for withholding records
* 15 years for destruction of records
* Automatic 25% budget cut for non-compliance
* Private right of action for citizens
* Permanent congressional review board
* Whistleblower protections with treble damages
* Anti-embarrassment clause — no reputational withholding

============================================================

Law 2 — Force the Spies to Open Their Files

* Forces CIA, NSA, DIA, NRO, NGA, ODNI to release files
* Covers the entire Intelligence Community (50 U.S.C. 3003(4))
* Redaction allowed only for active human sources
* Active collection methods may stay protected
* Analytical conclusions must always be disclosed
* Same criminal penalties as Law 1
* No presidential override allowed
* Whistleblower protections inside the IC
* Records preservation triggered at bill introduction
* Closes the intel loophole Law 1 cannot reach

============================================================

Law 3 — Force Real Investigations

* Forces a real investigation — not just disclosure
* Dedicated FBI team of at least 20 agents
* $10 million independent budget
* Lead agent must have no prior case involvement
* Investigates every one of the 175 Schedule A items
* Independent external monitor appointed by Congress
* Conflict-of-interest trigger if any agency is implicated
* Public reports every six months
* No presidential override allowed

============================================================

Law 4 — Embed Citizen Investigators Inside the Agencies

* Named civilian investigators with full agency access
* Independent teams embedded in FBI and the IC
* Immediate TOP SECRET / SCI clearances
* Independent budgets and independent counsel
* Authority to publicly release findings
* Whistleblower bounty — $1,000,000 minimum for foreign intel leads
* Cannot be terminated by any government official
* 90-day public reports on each disclosure item
* Up to 15 years prison for interference
* Employees can volunteer secretly, bypassing chain of command
* No presidential override allowed
