Law 2 fixes the structure: criminal penalties, automatic budget cuts, no presidential override, an anti-embarrassment clause, whistleblower protections with treble damages, and records preservation triggered at bill introduction — not enactment.

The thesis driving these laws: evidence suggests Tyler Robinson did not act alone, and an intelligence service may have been involved. The four laws together force that question into the open.

Law 1 — Force the FBI to Open Its Files

* Forces DoJ, FBI, ATF, CBP, TSA, DHS, FinCEN to disclose
* Covers every federal, state, and local law enforcement agency
* 30-day deadline from enactment
* All 175 Schedule A items plus broad catch-all
* No presidential override allowed
* 10 years prison for withholding records
* 15 years prison for destruction of records
* Automatic 25% budget cut for non-compliance
* Private right of action — any citizen can sue
* Anti-embarrassment clause — no reputational withholding
* Whistleblower protections with treble damages
* Records preservation triggered at bill introduction

Law 2 — Force the Spies to Open Their Files

* Forces CIA, NSA, DIA, NRO, NGA, ODNI to disclose
* Covers entire Intelligence Community (50 U.S.C. 3003(4))
* Includes FBI intelligence division and DHS intelligence
* 30-day deadline from enactment
* Redaction only for active human sources
* Redaction only for active collection methods
* Analytical conclusions must always be disclosed
* 10 years prison for withholding records
* 15 years prison for destruction of records
* No presidential override allowed
* Automatic 25% budget cut for non-compliance
* Closes the intel loophole Law 1 cannot reach

Law 3 — Force Real Investigations

* Goes beyond disclosure — mandates active investigation
* Dedicated FBI team of at least 20 agents
* $10 million independent budget
* Lead agent must have no prior case involvement
* Investigates every one of the 175 Schedule A items
* Independent external monitor appointed by Congress
* Authority transfers if a covered agency is implicated
* Public reports every six months
* No presidential override allowed

Law 4 — Embed Citizen Investigators Inside the Agencies

* Named citizen investigators with full access
* Embedded inside FBI and U.S. Intelligence Community
* Independent teams covering FBI and the IC
* Immediate TOP SECRET / SCI clearances
* Independent budgets and independent legal counsel
* Authority to publicly release all findings
* $1M minimum whistleblower bounty for foreign intel leads
* No presidential override allowed
* Cannot be terminated by any government official
* 90-day public reports on each disclosure item
* Up to 15 years prison for interference
* Government employees can volunteer secretly, bypassing chain of command
