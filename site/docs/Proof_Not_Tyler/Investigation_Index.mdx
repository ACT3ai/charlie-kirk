# Proof Not Tyler — Investigation Index

The official narrative says **Tyler Robinson** fired a **.30-06** from the **Losee Center rooftop**
and killed Charlie Kirk. This section compiles **reported challenges** to that story — forensic,
physical, witness, and timeline — without asserting Robinson's innocence as a legal conclusion.
**Robinson is charged, not convicted.**
