and custody timing. Start with the [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index). **Robinson is charged,
not convicted.**

**Proof Not Tyler** is not a verdict — it is a map of **gaps in the official kill chain**. The state's
own **September 17, 2025 ATF report** reportedly **cannot match** the autopsy bullet jacket fragment
to Robinson's alleged Mauser ([ATF page](./ATF_Inconclusive_Ballistics)). **Trajectory** from the east
rooftop does not align with reported **neck entry** ([trajectory](./Trajectory_Wound_Mismatch)).
**Canon XA55** audio separates a **tent/stage event** from a **distant rifle** ([acoustic split](./Acoustic_Two_Event_Split)).
Campus video raises **Stairs Guy ≠ Tyler** ([identity gap](./Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap)).

The institutional layer matters too: the rifle was reportedly found only after **failed searches** and
**federal direction** ([gun discovery](./Gun_Discovery_Sequence)); **Discord posts** timestamp **after**
**Miranda custody** ([timeline](./Discord_Custody_Timeline)); witnesses describe **pops not .30-06**
([witness conflicts](./Witness_Audio_Conflicts)). If the rooftop rifle did not kill Kirk, [alternative
mechanisms](./Alternative_Kill_Mechanisms) route to tent, mic, and intel threads elsewhere on the site.

Prioritize [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index) → [ATF](./ATF_Inconclusive_Ballistics) +

## The Mirandize Timeline — Why It's Pivotal

If Tyler Robinson was mirandized and in custody at **6:25 PM on September 11** with his phone
seized, then he physically could not have typed the Discord "confession" posted around **7:57 PM**.
That single timing gap is one of the strongest exculpatory arguments in the entire case, because it
attacks the **authorship** of the confession, not merely its meaning. Read the
[Mirandize evidence hub](/court/mirandize/mirandize-overview) and the
[custody timeline vs. the Discord confession](/court/mirandize/custody-timeline-fake-confession).
