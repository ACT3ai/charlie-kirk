# Witness & Audio Conflicts

If Tyler Robinson fired a **.30-06** from a rooftop **~120 m** away, witnesses near the tent
should predominantly report a **heavy rifle crack**. Citizen compilations instead highlight
**pistol/fireworks-class** sound descriptions, **false all-clear alerts**, and **eyewitness photo
mismatches** — all **reported conflicts**, not proof of another shooter.

## Witness Nick — not a large-calibre sound

[@MuppetMasher](https://x.com/MuppetMasher/status/2053805778952417528) interviewed **Nick**, a UVU
witness who says it is **hard to find anyone** describing a **large-calibre rifle**:

- Many heard **pops**, **fireworks**, **smaller calibre** or **pistol** sounds
- **None** in Nick's experience said **.30-06**
- Nick's **trajectory** description is flagged as **interesting** in the thread

Video pinned IPFS in CK_FILE: `QmNjgg3Trjfiq4YEK4XjDur9YyJF1xk8nhAkeCkXMoo41Y`.

## False "suspect in custody" alert

UVU GRAMA threads ([@danksterintel](https://x.com/danksterintel/status/2044300845609824305)) document
a campus alert **19 minutes** after the shooting stating **suspect in custody** when the suspect
**was not** — leaving **~10,000 students** on campus believing it was safe. Wrong early custody
claims undermine trust in same institutions' Tyler narrative.

## Construction crew vs FBI photo

CK **NOT TYLER** section quotes **Mr. Hope**: officers showed construction workers a photo that
**did not** resemble the person they encountered post-shooting — while narrative converged on Robinson.
See [Gun Discovery](./Gun_Discovery_Sequence).

## Black-clothing descriptions

Witness text in CK describes shooter aesthetic as **black sunglasses, C-mask, trench coat, cargo
pants, small backpack** — not maroon-shirt Tyler packaging. Overlaps [Stairs Guy Gap](./Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap)
and [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect).

## Zachariah Qureshi — announced then discarded?

CK notes: **Zachariah Qureshi** was within **~25 feet** of Kirk; **Kash Patel** reportedly rushed to
announce him as shooter. Qureshi was questioned and released (with later obstruction case noted).
Commentators ask if he was **distraction patsy** before Robinson selection. **No public finding**
that Qureshi shot anyone; living person — defamation-safe framing only.

## Deleted eyewitness media

CK section on witnesses alleges **cell footage deleted by the time people got home** — if true,
independent audio/video corroboration of .30-06 vs pops is **lost**. See [Censorship](/Media/Censorship).
