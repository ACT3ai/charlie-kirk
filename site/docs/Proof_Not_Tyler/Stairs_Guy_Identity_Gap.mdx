# Stairs Guy Identity Gap

The FBI narrative collapses if the **rooftop shooter on camera** is not the same person as
[**Tyler Robinson**](/People/tyler-robinson) tracked across UVU that morning. Citizen video analysis — compiled in
Charlie_Kirk.txt and the dedicated [Tyler Robinson — Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy)
page — documents **visible mismatches** in backpack, hat, clothing, and rifle-carry plausibility.
We do **not** claim to know who "Stairs Guy" is.

## Core mismatch table

| Feature | Tyler on campus (morning / midday) | Stairs / rooftop figure |
|---------|-----------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Backpack** | Light blue, ~**half full** | Different pack, ~**90–95% full** |
| **Hat** | Black hat, white logo | Different headwear in stair clips |
| **Pants** | **Light shorts** (morning) | **Dark jeans** on rooftop suspect |
| **Shirt** | Maroon T-shirt → later black flag shirt | Black long-sleeve / flag variants |
| **Rifle carry** | Scoped Mauser **too large** for shorts/pant leg | Prone shooter on roof |

CK_FILE logic: a 22-year-old **would not swap backpacks and hats** mid-escape; outfit change
should **shrink** pack volume if a rifle were stowed — yet stairs footage shows a **fuller** pack.

## Timeline: Tyler without backpack near stairs

CK movement timeline notes Tyler **without backpack** at **11:50–11:53** approaching tunnel/stairs
— while the rooftop narrative requires him to have carried a **scoped .30-06** through the same zone.

## Independent witness claims

- **Filmmaker witness** (via [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) coverage): reportedly states **"Tyler Robinson is NOT the guy"**
- **Tyler's father** (sources in CK posts): surveillance of stairs shooter **did not look like Tyler**;
  commentary compares figure to [**Robbie Hill**](/People/robbie-hill) — unverified identification

We do **not** state as fact that Robbie Hill or any named person was the shooter.

## Black-clothing alternate suspect

Separate witness descriptions cite **black trench coat, C-mask, cargo pants, small backpack**
— a different silhouette from Tyler-in-jeans media packaging. Commentary alleges a **prone black
figure on the roof** an hour before the hit **vanished** from narrative once feds settled on Robinson.

See [Suspects — Black Clothing](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) and [Witness Conflicts](./Witness_Audio_Conflicts).

## Consequences if Stairs Guy ≠ Tyler

CK_FILE conclusions from mismatch proofs:

- **Gun IN / OUT** through Tyler's clothing **does not work**
- **Someone else** had to supply, stage, or retrieve the rifle — or it was **pre-positioned**
- FBI **lone gunman** timeline **fails** without resolving identity on camera
