# Trajectory & Wound Mismatch

Even if [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) were on the [**Losee Center rooftop**](/UVU/Losee_Center) with a **.30-06**, citizen analysts
argue the **geometry and wound morphology** do not fit a distant high-power rifle kill. Without a
public autopsy, these are **interpretations** of video, witness quotes, and physics — not ME
findings. Robinson is **charged, not convicted**.

## Rooftop angle vs entry site

CK_FILE and [@_DaniFesto](https://x.com/_DaniFesto/status/2039372441068155219) compile:

- Shooter position ~**120 m east**, downward angle roughly **5–9 degrees**
- That trajectory would likely strike **face or jaw first**
- Reported entry instead **between larynx and trachea**, **left of cervical spine** — more
  **straight-on** to the neck than a shallow downward rifle path from the east

If the neck wound were an **exit** from an internal device or close shot, rooftop Tyler becomes
**physically unnecessary** — a core [Proof Not Tyler](./Investigation_Index) argument.

## Energy and visible damage

[Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) notes **.30-06** carries roughly **8×** the muzzle energy
of **9mm**. Analysts expect:

- Catastrophic tissue destruction at rifle distances
- Prominent **exit wound** and heavy **blood on white stage/tarp**

Conflicting public claims include:

- Treating doctor quote: bullet **"did not exit"** — called an **"absolute miracle"** (CK notes)
- [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens): **no pooling near heart**; blood **sideways**; **shirt clean**
  vs pre-scripted medic descriptions
- Witness [Nick](./Witness_Audio_Conflicts) trajectory descriptions vs rooftop line

## Shirt timing on video

[Mic video analysis](/Mic/mic-explosion-video-analysis) alleges **shirt fabric movement before**
visible neck blood — argued as inconsistent with external rifle strike timing. Treat as frame-analysis
claim pending raw footage release.

## Alternative calibers fit better (hypothesis)

[ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1973304018572071043) / Dr. [Chris Martenson](/People/chris-martenson)
threads argue **9mm +P**-class **cavitation** explains necklace snap and **pugilist pose** without
launching the body as .30-06 would. [Alternative Kill Mechanisms](./Alternative_Kill_Mechanisms)
routes tent and mic hypotheses.

## Disclosure that would settle it

- Public autopsy with trajectory rods
- High-resolution wound photography
- Official ME entry/exit determination

Until then, trajectory mismatch remains a **strong citizen-investigator pillar** for doubting
the rooftop .30-06 kill story.
