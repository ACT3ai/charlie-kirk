Twenty-three seconds from the same Utah press conference as the companion clip filed beside it, capturing Governor Spencer Cox answering the same question about the suspect's clothing. This version is one second longer and carries large stylised burned-in subtitles across the bottom of the frame, tracking his words as he speaks. ## What Is Said

The transcript matches the companion clip almost word for word, with the on-screen subtitles rendering it in capitals:

"Yes, yes. So when he's first spotted on campus, he has different clothing on, and then he changes clothing on the roof, and then changed back into that clothing at some point so that when he was apprehended, when he was arrested, the clothing matched the clothing he had on before the shooting here at UVU."
