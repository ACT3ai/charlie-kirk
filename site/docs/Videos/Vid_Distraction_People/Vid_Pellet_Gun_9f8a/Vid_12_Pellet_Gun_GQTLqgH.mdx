## What Is Said

The bystander narration on this cut is the fullest version of the audio in the family. Transcribed, with the machine transcription's uncertainty left visible:

> I think they just found the guy right outside of carry on. Rifle on the [...] dash, and they just [handcuffed] them. Shit. Do you see his gun? That's his rifle.

Several things are happening in that one sentence, and separating them is the point of this page. The narrator asserts, in real time and from across a street, three things: that police have "found the guy"; that a rifle was on a dashboard; and that the object being carried away is a rifle. None of the three is established by anything in the frame. "Right outside of carry on" appears to be a transcription failure over an unclear word and should not be read as a location.

According to the investigation's notes, the weapon associated with this detention was reported as a .177 calibre break-barrel pellet air rifle rather than a firearm. On that reading, the narrator's identification and the uploader's caption both scale a modest object up into something it was not - which is exactly why the uploader's own scare quotes around "RIFLE" are the most honest thing in the frame.
