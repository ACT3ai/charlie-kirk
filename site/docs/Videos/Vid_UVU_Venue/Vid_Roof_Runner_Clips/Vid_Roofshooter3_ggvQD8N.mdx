## How It Connects

That burned-in 12:23:35 is the reason this clip is filed with the roof and runner material rather than with general campus footage. The investigation file places the shot at 12:23:30 p.m. Surveillance stills circulated by independent researchers carry timestamps of 12:23:29, 12:23:34, 12:23:45 and 12:23:54, tracing movement toward the northeast corner of the Losee Center building — a span researchers have summarised as covering the distance from the claimed firing position to that corner in roughly twenty-five seconds.

Those same researchers have argued that the publicly released rooftop view begins at 12:23:34, by which time the runner was already about sixty feet clear of the claimed perch, and that measuring the visible run backwards puts the start of the sprint at roughly 12:23:28 — before the shot. On that reading the person filmed running could not have fired it. That is an argument advanced by citizen investigators working from partial releases, not a finding of any court, and it turns entirely on footage that has not been made public in full. Investigation notes also record sharp public criticism of the Utah County District Attorney's office and of UVU for the extent of what has been withheld; those are allegations by the critics, and the offices concerned have not conceded them.
