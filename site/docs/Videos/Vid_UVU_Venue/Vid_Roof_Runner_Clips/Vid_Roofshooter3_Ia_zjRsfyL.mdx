Two cautions, because this is the kind of footage people over-read.

First, "pursuit" is an inference, not an observation. The automated visual description filed with this clip asserts that the figure is "being pursued by campus police officers" and describes a coordinated containment. What the frames themselves establish is that a person in dark clothing moved quickly across these areas and that marked police vehicles and uniformed figures were present in the same minutes. Whether any given uniformed figure is chasing this specific person, arriving for an unrelated reason, or securing a perimeter is not something a fixed camera at this distance settles.

Second, the person in dark clothing is not identified by this footage and is not identified here. The figure's identity, and whether the movement shown is the movement described in the charging documents, is exactly what is disputed elsewhere on this site.
