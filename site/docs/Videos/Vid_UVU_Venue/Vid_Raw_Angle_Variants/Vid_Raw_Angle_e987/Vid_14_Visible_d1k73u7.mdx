The twin file's pass of the identical recording renders part of that as "There's **no** shot fired right now" and adds "Hey, there was a shootout." The two machine transcripts of one audio track contradict each other on the single most consequential line. That is a processing artefact, and it means neither rendering can be quoted as a witness statement without listening to the source. This page reports both and relies on neither.

The stable content across both passes: repeated calls of the name **Isaac**, someone searching for a companion; a remark about being "a bigger target right here"; and "Where are you? I'm going." The same name is called out in [Yard 1](/Videos/Vid_Ballistics_Gun/Vid_South_Side_Shot/Vid_Yard_1_EpS4n6m) elsewhere in this corpus.

What the audio conveys, and conveys reliably, is confusion. Nobody in earshot says where the sound came from.
