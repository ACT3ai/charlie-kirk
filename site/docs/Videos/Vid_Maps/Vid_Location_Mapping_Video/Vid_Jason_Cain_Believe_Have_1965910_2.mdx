## How It Connects

The clip is worth keeping for what it demonstrates about method rather than for what it proves. Street-level archival imagery is a legitimate and powerful tool for establishing sightlines, distances and building relationships. It is also a tool with a hard limit: it dates from whenever the camera car drove past, and every conclusion drawn from it inherits that date.
