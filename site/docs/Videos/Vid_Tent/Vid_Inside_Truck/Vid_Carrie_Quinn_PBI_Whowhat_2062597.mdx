## What This Video Is

Twenty-five seconds of handheld phone video of Charlie Kirk's walk-out at the
Utah Valley University event on September 10, 2025 — from the parking structure,
past the camera, toward the stage. It was reposted with a caption asking a
question about one of the people in frame.

The clip is filed in this investigation's capture mirror under the reposter's
handle and the caption text, with no original-author credit and no upload date.
It is a repost of somebody else's capture.

**The caption is a question, not a finding, and it is not adopted here.** The
posted caption asks what or who [Jason Behunin](/People/jason-behunin) is
"intentionally covering up with his fingers," on the basis that a hand briefly
crosses the camera lens at about six seconds. Mr. Behunin is a living person.
Nothing in twenty-five seconds of shaky walk-out footage establishes that anyone
obscured the lens deliberately, and a hand crossing a phone camera in a crowded
walkway is entirely ordinary. This site records that the question was asked and
by whom, and it does not assert that anyone concealed anything.
