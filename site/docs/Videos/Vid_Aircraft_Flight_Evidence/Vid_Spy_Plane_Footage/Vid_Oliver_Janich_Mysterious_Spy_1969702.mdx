## What This Video Is

A 3 minute 37 second commentary vlog by the German commentator Oliver Janich,
opening and closing on his webcam outdoors under overcast sky and spending the
middle three minutes as a screen share through an Opera browser set to German.
It is a first-person opinion piece, not footage of anything, and Janich presents
it as a corrective to other people's work rather than as reporting of his own.
Everything he shows is a publicly reachable web page.

## What Is Said

Janich states his premise in the first twenty seconds. "Many people are
desperately searching for a link to Israel in the murder of Charlie Kirk,
because they have a motive, but motive is not a proof," he says, "but I found
one link."

He then builds a chain of ownership on screen. He credits Crowdsource the Truth
with reporting the "mysterious spy jet flying lazy circles over the Utah Lake"
and says the aircraft is registered to LASAI Aviation II LLC. He corrects that
earlier account directly: "He is speculating here that it belongs to SNC, but it
doesn't. It belongs to Leidos." He shows a filing and says the confusion is
explainable, because the entity carried a different name previously.

From there the chain widens. Janich notes that Leidos is a large defense
contractor and that among its largest institutional shareholders are Vanguard and
BlackRock. He then makes an aside he explicitly marks as unproven, connecting
Jacob Rothschild's advisory role at the Blackstone Group to BlackRock's origin as
a Blackstone spin-off, and says of the inference: "you could argue that maybe the
Rothschilds are also owners of BlackRock, but there's no proof because they
didn't have to disclose everything." That caveat is his own and it belongs in any
account of the clip.

The link he is actually after arrives at about 2:00. He displays a Leidos
corporate article about high-tech data centres in Israel and states that a Leidos
subsidiary there works with the Israel Defense Forces, partnering with Israel
Aerospace Industries on consolidating and transferring IDF computing
infrastructure. From this he draws his conclusion, and it is worth quoting
exactly because it is speculative in form: "So you could argue that all the data
which was caught by this plane directly goes to the IDF. I think this is a
missing link."

The last forty seconds change subject to the wound. Janich says that the official
account has the round entering and travelling downward, and that "everybody I
spoke to or listened to" holds that a projectile of that calibre would pass
through, and he notes the absence of a stated exit wound as strange. He closes by
arguing that in a domestic cover-up scenario no direct foreign link would be
needed at all, and refers in passing to his own view of how the Epstein files
have been handled. Those closing remarks are his characterisations of other
people's conduct, offered as opinion, and this site does not adopt them.

## What The Footage Shows

Janich to webcam from 0:00 to 0:25 and again from 2:58 to the end, centred in
frame with blurred water and hills behind him. In between, browser pages in

The gap between the argument and the frames is the thing to hold onto. What the
