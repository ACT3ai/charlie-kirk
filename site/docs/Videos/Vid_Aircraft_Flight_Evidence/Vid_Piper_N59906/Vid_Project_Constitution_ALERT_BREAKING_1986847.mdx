Now the distance between the two halves. The graphics establish that a survey
operator with government clients flies this class of aircraft and that circuits
were flown over Orem. The imagery establishes that somebody filmed the campus
from height while the crowd was seated. What the video never does is join them:
nothing on screen shows that the aerial footage came from that operator, from
that aircraft, or from any of the tracked passes. The word "apparent" in the
video's own map title is carrying the whole claim, and it is the video's own
hedge. The tail number this clip is filed against is likewise not shown anywhere

It is also worth saying plainly that aerial survey flights over a university are
ordinary. Campuses are photographed for mapping, planning and utility work
