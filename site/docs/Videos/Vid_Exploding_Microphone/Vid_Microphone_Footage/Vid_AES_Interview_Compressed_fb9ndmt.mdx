## Why This Footage Sits In The Investigation

The captioned event is the destruction of Accurate Energetic Systems Building 602 in McEwen, Tennessee on October 10, 2025 — a melt-pour explosives operation, roughly 23,000 to 24,600 pounds of energetic material, 16 workers reported killed and 7 injured, investigated by ATF, FBI, DHS, Tennessee authorities and the U.S. Chemical Safety Board. Independent researchers have connected AES to this investigation through a single Department of Defense procurement record for miniaturised anti-personnel demolition charges, about $440,494, delivered August 25, 2025, two weeks before September 10.
