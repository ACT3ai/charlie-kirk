## What This Video Is

A 1 minute 33 second clip in two halves. Aggressive upscaling of low-bitrate video reliably manufactures bright transient artefacts, which is exactly what a claimed muzzle flash looks like at that scale. The clip offers no control frames, no comparison against a shot known not to have been fired, and no camera or codec information. "Irrefutable" is the caption's word, not a description of what is visible.
