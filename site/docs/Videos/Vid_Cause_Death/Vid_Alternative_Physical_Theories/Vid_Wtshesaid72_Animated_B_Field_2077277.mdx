A supersonic projectile does generate a pressure field that can move hair and fabric, and that is the mainstream explanation for exactly these appearances. It is also true that a body struck at high energy moves, and that at this frame rate and this compression level the intermediate stages of any such motion are not actually resolved - what a viewer sees between labelled frames is interpolation by the eye. Labelling a sequence "compression phase" and "expansion phase" imports a physical model; it does not demonstrate one.
