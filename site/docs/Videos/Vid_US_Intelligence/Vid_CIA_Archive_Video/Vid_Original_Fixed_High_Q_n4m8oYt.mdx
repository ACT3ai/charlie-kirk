## What Is Said

The narration is identical to the companion copy and is set out in full there. In brief, it walks four threads and attaches an explicit disclaimer to each.

* **The campus centre.** Utah Valley University hosts a Center for National Security Studies; the narrator asks whether it has any connection to the CIA, and refers to seven people - identified only by letters - whose public profiles he says were removed roughly two weeks after the assassination. He states that this "doesn't imply any linkage to anything." No individual is named in this presentation, and none is named on this page.
* **Search records.** Searches he says originated abroad for two names before the event, immediately qualified: "that would not necessarily imply any guilt or any involvement."
* **The aircraft.** The bulk of the presentation. Tail number N1098L, an airframe of a type built to launch and recover drones in flight, tracked on public flight data across September 10, 2025: a long loop over the lake beside the university, a pass roughly three hours before 12:23 and another roughly twenty-five minutes after, and two descents to about 203 feet above the water at about 116 knots against a normal profile of about 500 knots at about 41,000 feet. The narrator characterises the low, slow passes as consistent with drone release or recovery, then declines the conclusion: "we don't know if they dropped off drones or picked up drones, but this is interesting."
* **The drone sightings.** He concedes some circulating clips show insects or birds and argues others show objects moving in ways birds cannot. He states directly that he does not allege a drone fired anything, and that his interest is whether an aerial platform recorded the event and who would have received such a feed.

The closing is a list of specific disclosure requests: who was aboard, what the flight was for, whether drones were deployed, and whether recorded footage exists. The narrator's final position is that "this all was probably coincidence" and that the questions should nonetheless be answered.
