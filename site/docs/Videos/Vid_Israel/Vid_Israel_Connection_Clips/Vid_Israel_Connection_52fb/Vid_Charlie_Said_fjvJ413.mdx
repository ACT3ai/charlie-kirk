## What Is Said

Kirk is the only voice. The reel opens on the donor-funding argument:

> "The philosophical foundation of anti-whiteness has been largely financed by Jewish donors in the country. It is true that some of the largest financiers of left-wing anti-white causes have been Jewish Americans."

He continues that "they went all in on woke, and it wasn't just ADL," naming "some of the top Jewish organizations in the country," and states that "Jewish Americans have primarily been financing cultural Marxist ideas." He notes he had said the same the previous week and that "people came after us."

At about 0:40 the cut moves to the locker-room setting and the tone drops. Kirk says "I gotta be careful the way I say this," then "They're going to try to ethnically cleanse Gaza," adding "I don't use that term lightly." The thought is cut off mid-sentence by the editor and the reel returns to Studio 1.

From roughly 0:52 the donor argument resumes and hardens into the passage that circulated most widely:

> "And Jewish donors, they have a lot of explaining to do, a lot of decoupling to do... This is a beast created by secular Jews. And it's not just the colleges. It's the nonprofits, it's the movies, it's Hollywood, it's all of it. It's like time for you guys to wake up and say no more. Draw a line in the sand. I don't care if you hate me."

He goes further here than in the standalone version of the same monologue archived separately in this cluster, adding: "I don't feel sorry for these rich Jews that have been funding this crap." The reel ends on the TCN segment with a short line — "They know that we are aware. They notice that they know that we are noticing things."

All of the above are Kirk's assertions and Kirk's characterisations of groups and institutions. The page reports what the reel contains; it does not endorse any of it and makes no claim about the accuracy of his descriptions. Readers should note that Kirk is generalising about a religious and ethnic category throughout, and that he repeatedly anticipates the backlash himself.
