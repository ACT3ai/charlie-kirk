7. "Get to the bottom of how Epstein got his money. Subpoena bank records and financial statements. Figure out the capital flows."
8. Release the names of the prisoners on the floor the night Epstein died, overriding privacy rules.
9. Recover the missing minutes of prison footage: "There's right now 2.5 missing minutes... If you want to create conspiracy theories, that is what you do."
