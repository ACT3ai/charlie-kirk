# Technology & Surveillance

## Overview

This section collects **technical and surveillance claims** around the September 10, 2025 killing of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) at UVU: drones and UAS footage, camera systems (campus, TPUSA event, municipal Flock), digital forensics (phones, Discord, SD cards), and contested "exotic weapon" theories (IR laser designation, mic-as-device). Official proceedings emphasize a **rooftop rifle** narrative; citizen investigators document **competing technical models**. Neither side is rubber-stamped here.

## Key Areas

- Drone / UAS sightings and foreign-system analogies ([Drones](/Drones/overview))
- Aircraft and ISR-adjacent flight context ([Planes](/Planes/overview), [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview))
- Campus and event camera chains; Flock / ALPR coverage gaps
- Digital forensics: Discord, phones, search history, wiped servers (as alleged)
- Contested tech theories: IR designators, loitering munitions analogies, mic explosive claims

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

*(Unverified technical claims from public commentary — not court findings.)*

- **Drone-strike model:** **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** and associated long-form videos argue the hit was a **foreign drone / laser-designated** engagement (Spike Firefly / Hero-class analogies, "Cold Sword"-style networked sniper demos, alleged IR paint visible on enhanced phone video). Some threads name an unidentified **TPUSA-adjacent spotter**. These are **high-speculation OSINT narratives** with no court adoption.
- **Multi-drone / dual-weapon packages:** Later Project Constitution / **[@WeAreNotGTM](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)** commentary claimed **multiple military-style drones**, dual wounds (distance + close), and **camera dead zones** built into a "killbox." Treat as **attributed theory**, and cross-check against ballistics pages.
- **Flock / municipal cameras:** **[@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** (July 2026) asked why a dense **Flock** camera environment around Provo/Orem produced, in the public case narrative, mainly **grainy stills** and few plate hits. That is a **records-demand question**, not proof of spoliation by itself.
- **Event-camera seizure timing:** Separate but related tech-chain claims (see [Social Media Analysis](/social_media_analysis/overview)) allege **SD cards pulled within minutes** by event AV staff — a chain-of-custody issue that belongs in both tech and legal tracks.
- **Mic / RF theories:** Recurring posts claim a **modified wireless microphone** or body-adjacent device, not a .30-06, caused the fatal injury. See [Cover-Up — Mic Explosive](/CoverUp/Mic_Explosive). Official autopsy/ballistics commentary rejects that model; the dispute remains a live citizen topic.

## How to Read Competing Models

| Model | Primary claimed sensor / tool | What would falsify it |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lone rooftop rifle | Mauser .30-06, rooftop pads, DNA/prints | Independent ballistics + multi-angle video contradicting roof origin |
| Drone / guided slug | UAS footage, IR designator frames | Authenticated flight logs empty + continuous camera coverage of sky clear |
| Mic / close device | Wound geometry, mic removal video | Medical examiner primary records showing classic rifle wound path |
| Hybrid / multi-actor | Dead zones + multiple origins | Full synchronized multi-cam release with continuous custody |

Use [Drones](/Drones/overview), [cameras](/cameras/overview), [Gun_Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview), and [US Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview) for depth.
