## How to read this section

Every page in this area separates **what a public document or filing says** from **what citizen investigators infer from it**. Agency pages (campus police, sheriffs, DPS) state the on-record account first, then flag the timestamp and jurisdiction questions raised around it. The two federal rows below are **pointers only** — the FBI's conduct and the halted NCTC review are documented in their own sections so they are not duplicated or contradicted here. Start with the on-scene response if you want the first-minutes gap, or the custody chain if you want the surrender-timeline disputes.
