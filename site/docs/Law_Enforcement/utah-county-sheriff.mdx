# Utah County Sheriff — Surrender Coordination

**Utah County Sheriff Mike Smith** and his office sit in the **northbound custody chain** after [Washington County](./washington-county-sheriff) handled Robinson's voluntary surrender in Hurricane.

## The 8:04 PM call (government document)

Per a quoted government document in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/):

| Time | Event |
|------|--------|
| **~20:04 (8:04 PM)** Sept 11 | **Sheriff Smith** receives call from **Sheriff Brooksby** (Washington County) |
| Earlier chain | Former deputy **Mike Mitchell** ← Robinson's father ← reported confession |
| **~22:26 (10:26 PM)** | Robinson arrives Washington County SO with parents |

Smith's office is the **Utah County** coordination point for transferring the case toward [Spanish Fork jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail) booking.

## Booking agency & timeline disputes

Research notes list:

- **Booking Agency:** Utah County Sheriff Office
- **Inmate booking sheet** arrest time **10:00 PM** Sept 11 vs later **4:00 AM** Sept 12 entries — citizen investigators argue the later stamp reflects affidavit prep, not first contact
- **Mirandized 6:25 PM** Sept 11 — earlier than Brooksby-to-Smith call per some documents ([Proof Not Tyler — Discord/Custody](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Discord_Custody_Timeline))

These are **document-timestamp questions**, not findings of misconduct.

## USBI Agent Davis

The surrender document references **Agent Davis** of the **Utah State Bureau of Investigation** furnishing a photo of Robinson in **maroon T-shirt, jeans, Converse-style shoes** — **not matching** clothing on earlier surveillance stills. That detail feeds [Suspect Manhunt](./suspect-manhunt) misidentification threads.

## Press conference presence

**Governor Spencer Cox** held a press conference with **Sheriff Mike Smith** present while the surrender was still being coordinated — timing questions appear in [Utah DPS & Beau Mason](./utah-dps-beau-mason) and [key_individuals/Officials](/key_individuals/Officials_And_Law_Enforcement).

**Mike Smith** is a living public official; we do **not** claim wrongdoing.
