# FBI Role (See FBI Section)

The **Law Enforcement** Level 2 area focuses on **state and local** agencies — campus police, sheriffs, DPS, and county prosecution. **FBI** conduct, [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) press conferences, foreign-lead shutdowns, and cover-up threads are maintained in the dedicated **[FBI](/FBI/overview)** section. This page exists only to hand readers off to that section and to flag the few points where FBI action **intersects** the state and local chain covered here.

## Where the FBI touches the local chain

Even though the FBI thread lives in its own section, several state-and-local pages in this area reference FBI conduct because the two chains overlap in time:

* **Custody transfer.** Per the surrender documents summarized in [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/), state actors handled Robinson's voluntary surrender at the [Washington County Sheriff's Office](./washington-county-sheriff), but investigators who took formal custody and transported him north were described as **federal and state** — the handoff point where FBI involvement begins. See [Utah County Sheriff](./utah-county-sheriff).
* **Crime-scene control.** The research file's account of the [courtyard paving](./crime-scene-handling) attributes the emergency order to clear the scene to "the Utah governor and [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)'s FBI." That is an **attributed claim** from the contractor's account, not a court finding.
* **Discovery.** The [court proceedings](./court-proceedings) page notes a defense continuance request based on **ATF and FBI** files not being produced. That is a reported procedural dispute.

## The Salt Lake City field-office change (attributed timeline)

The research notes flag a personnel change at the **FBI Salt Lake City field office** around the time the case opened. Per those notes, **[Mehtab Syed](/People/mehtab-syed)** — reported as appointed **Special Agent in Charge** in February 2025 — was **reportedly removed** in **August 2025** and replaced by **[Robert Bohls](/People/robert-bohls)**, who is described in the notes as previously liaising with the U.S. Attorney in Washington, D.C., and heading the National Cyber Investigative Joint Task Force. The notes present the timing of this switch as an **open question**, not proof of coordination. Full development of this thread belongs in the [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [CoverUp](/CoverUp/overview) sections; it is summarized here only because it overlaps the state-court timeline on the [court proceedings](./court-proceedings) page.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (FBI–Local Overlap)

The following are **attributed public claims** about where the FBI intersects the **state and local** chain. They are **not** findings of wrongdoing. Full FBI coverage remains in the [FBI](/FBI/overview) section.

According to **@triggersmarthq** on X (**July 8, 2026**), preliminary-hearing testimony described **Officer Davis** (Utah SBI cold-case / case agent with **Hull**) as working a multi-agency investigation that included **FBI, ATF, SBI, UVU police, and Orem police**. The same post timeline says Davis received a **~8:30 PM September 11** message that someone would surrender, arrived at **St. George Airport around 11:30 PM**, went to the Washington County Sheriff's Office, viewed Robinson on a room TV with audio/video, and that Robinson was **officially detained September 12 at about 4:00 AM**. One claim in that thread is that the **mother was interviewed with one FBI agent present**. These are **hearing-summary claims from social media**, not a court transcript published here.

Separately, citizen investigators continue to cite the **Dan Merrell / Merrill** paving interview — amplified by **@MJTruthUltra** (**May 22, 2026**) — that **the Governor and the FBI** asked the contractor to finish the courtyard paving **by Monday**, contrasting UVU's **pre-scheduled renovation** public framing. That claim is developed on [Crime Scene Handling](./crime-scene-handling); it is listed here only as an FBI–local control overlap.
