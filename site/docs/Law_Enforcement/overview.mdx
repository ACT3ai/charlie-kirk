Private security is [Security Team](/Security_Team/overview). Federal FBI threads: [FBI](/FBI/overview). Start with the [investigation index](./Investigation_Index).

The **on-scene gap** investigators cite most often is [UVU Campus Police](./uvu-campus-police): six officers led by **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)**, **no rooftop sweep**, while the charging narrative places the shooter on **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** for nearly an hour. **[Orem PD](./orem-police-response)** reportedly never activated a paid **joint Public Order Unit** built for exactly this event type. **State voice [Beau Mason](./utah-dps-beau-mason)** cleared **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** and released **enhanced suspect stills** at the **~7:58 PM** press conference while dispatch audio described **all-black tactical** clothing — see [Suspect Manhunt](./suspect-manhunt).

The **custody chain** runs [Washington County](./washington-county-sheriff) (**Nate Brooksby**, Mike Mitchell tip) → [Utah County Sheriff](./utah-county-sheriff) (**Mike Smith**, 8:04 PM call) → [Spanish Fork jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail). Timestamp conflicts (6:25 PM Miranda, 10:00 PM booking, 4:00 AM federal entry) are documented on those pages — not resolved here. **Physical evidence** questions: [courtyard paving](./crime-scene-handling) within days, [ATF non-match](./rifle-chain-of-custody) on the Mauser. **Prosecution**: [Utah County Attorney](/Law_Enforcement/court-proceedings) team, **Judge Tony Graf** gag order, [resignations](./official-resignations) of Brooksby and UVU President **Astrid Tuminez**.
