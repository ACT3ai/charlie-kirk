The **on-scene gap** investigators cite most often is [UVU Campus Police](./uvu-campus-police): six officers led by **[Jeff Long](/People/jeff-long)**, **no rooftop sweep**, while the charging narrative places the shooter on **[Losee Center](/UVU/Losee_Center)** for nearly an hour. **[Orem PD](./orem-police-response)** reportedly never activated a paid **joint Public Order Unit** built for exactly this event type. **State voice [Beau Mason](./utah-dps-beau-mason)** cleared **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** and released **enhanced suspect stills** at the **~7:58 PM** press conference while dispatch audio described **all-black tactical** clothing — see [Suspect Manhunt](./suspect-manhunt).

The **custody chain** runs [Washington County](./washington-county-sheriff) (**[Nate Brooksby](/People/nate-brooksby)**, [Mike Mitchell](/People/mike-mitchell) tip) → [Utah County Sheriff](./utah-county-sheriff) (**[Mike Smith](/People/mike-smith)**, 8:04 PM call) → [Spanish Fork jail](/Locations/Utah_County_Jail). Timestamp conflicts (6:25 PM Miranda, 10:00 PM booking, 4:00 AM federal entry) are documented on those pages — not resolved here. **Physical evidence** questions: [courtyard paving](./crime-scene-handling) within days, [ATF non-match](./rifle-chain-of-custody) on the Mauser. **Prosecution**: [Utah County Attorney](/Law_Enforcement/court-proceedings) team, **[Judge Tony Graf](/court/judge-tony-graf)** gag order, [resignations](./official-resignations) of Brooksby and UVU President **[Astrid Tuminez](/People/astrid-s-tuminez)**.

## What Citizen Investigators Are Watching (Attributed)

Across X in **2026**, non-FBI law-enforcement threads cluster around four claim-sets: (1) **UVU campus PD staffing and rooftop/drone decisions** (six officers; Chief Jeff Long drone-denial claims); (2) **crime-scene restoration speed** (contractor statements that the Governor and FBI wanted pavers done "by Monday"); (3) **weapon forensics** (ATF / Utah State language that fragments could not be identified or excluded as from the Mauser 98); and (4) **personnel exits** (Washington County Sheriff Nate Brooksby's March 2026 resignation clustered online with the ballistics dispute). None of these claim-sets, standing alone, proves a multi-agency conspiracy; together they explain why readers reject a simple "local LE cleaned up a lone-wolf scene" summary.

For federal FBI conduct, use the [FBI](/FBI/overview) section. For prosecution secrecy and sealing, use [Legal](/Legal/overview).
