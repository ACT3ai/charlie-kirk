# Utah DPS & Commissioner Beau Mason

**[Beau Mason](/distraction_people/Beau_Mason)** leads the **Utah Department of Public Safety** (appointed by **Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox)**, June–July 2025 per reporting). He is the **state-level** law-enforcement voice most visible in the first 48 hours after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was shot at [UVU](/UVU/overview).

## George Zinn clearance

Mason's DPS role connects to **[George Zinn](/distraction_people/George_Zinn)** — the man who shouted that he shot Kirk to **draw attention from the real shooter** and was **cleared as gunman within hours**. Mason is cited in threads as the **commissioner** who communicated that clearance and released **enhanced suspect imagery** to the public.

## Sept 10 press conference (~7:58 PM)

Per [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/) attendance lists for Utah executive / state LE press:

| Named present (state/local) | Role |

| **Spencer Cox** | Utah Governor |

Reporting describes **enhanced photos** of a suspect in a **stairwell** and low-resolution **rooftop runner** imagery released while Robinson was still at large — central to [Suspect Manhunt](./suspect-manhunt) timing debates.

FBI officials also attended; see [FBI](/FBI/overview) for federal angles — **not duplicated here**.

## The recovered-rifle statement

The research file records that **Beau Mason** publicly stated a **"high powered bolt action rifle" was recovered in a wooded area** from the assassination, and that as **Commissioner of the Utah Department of Public Safety** he "shares new, enhanced photos of Charlie Kirk's suspected killer." Notably, the same file line refers to Mason as an **"FBI agent"** in one framing and **DPS Commissioner** in another — an inconsistency in the source material worth flagging rather than resolving here. The weapon account is developed further on the [rifle chain-of-custody](./rifle-chain-of-custody) page, including the reported **ATF non-match** questions; this page carries only the DPS spokesman role.

## Dispatch vs released imagery

Citizen threads contrast Mason-era releases with early **scanner/dispatch** language: suspect in **all black, tactical helmet, mask** ([Suspect Manhunt](./suspect-manhunt)) vs later **T-shirt/jeans** Robinson imagery. Per the file, the **~7:58 PM** press conference put out **"enhanced" stairwell photos** and **video of a small figure in black running on the Losee Center roof**. One citizen commentator, quoting **[Baron Coleman](/Influencers/podcasts-baron)**'s timeline analysis, argues a second set of stairwell photos was released after Robinson was already in custody being Mirandized — raising the question of why suspect imagery would drop after a surrender was underway. These are **attributed timing arguments**, not established facts; the imagery-doctoring claims (including a reported [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings) "enhanced" admission) belong to the [FBI](/FBI/overview) and [CoverUp](/CoverUp/overview) sections, not this DPS page.

## Open questions

- Did state LE **continue searching** after enhanced stills dropped?
- Full **scanner audio** and **DPS release logs** remain disclosure targets under [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview).

**Beau Mason** and **Spencer Cox** are living public officials; we do **not** claim they obstructed justice or knew of the plot.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

X discussion of the preliminary hearing identifies **David Hull**, described as a **Utah DPS investigative sergeant** (former SBI), as a lead investigator visible in courtroom clips (**Grok [@grok](/Influencers/x/grok)** summary, **July 8, 2026**). That places **DPS** — not only FBI — at the center of the state's investigative presentation.

Citizen investigators continue to contrast early **scanner** language (**all black / tactical helmet / mask**) with later **enhanced** stills and roof-run video associated with **Beau Mason**'s public role. Timing arguments (including **Baron Coleman** analysis amplified online) ask why enhanced stairwell imagery would release while a surrender was already being coordinated. Those are **attribution and chronology questions**, not proof of image fabrication by any named official.

**Beau Mason**, **David Hull**, and **Spencer Cox** are living public officials; this page does not claim they obstructed justice or participated in any crime.
