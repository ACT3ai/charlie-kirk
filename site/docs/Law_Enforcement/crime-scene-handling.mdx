# Crime Scene Handling

This page covers the reported handling of the **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)** crime scene — specifically the claim that the UVU courtyard where he was shot was **paved over within days** of the **September 10, 2025** event. The reporting is attributed throughout, and the official explanations are included.

## The Reported Paving-Over

According to **[@ShadowofEzra](/Influencers/x/shadowofezra)** and compiled notes, the courtyard where Kirk was shot was reportedly paved over within days. A company named **[Hardscape Specialties](/Tent/Hardscape_Specialties) LLC** is named as performing the UVU tent-area paving. Per the posts, that company was reportedly dormant for about 20 years and revived in **February 2025**.

The posts name the owner as **[Michael Powell](/People/michael-powell)** (described in the post as a Utah Attorney General special agent) and a co-owner as **[Burton Romrell](/People/burton-romrell)** (described as former UVU operations staff). These descriptions are reported claims; no wrongdoing is asserted against either named person.

## The Reported Timeline of the Work

Per the same compiled notes, a work order was reportedly issued two days after the shooting, and the paving reportedly started four days after the assassination. The posts claim there was no public bidding, that the job was rushed, and that the crew reportedly worked overnight and on a Sunday.

Separately, **[Daniel Merrell](/Tent/Daniel_Merrell_Hardscape_Utah)** (Hardscape Utah) reportedly said he installed pavers on **9/14/25** and was "called by … men who work for the state of Utah." That is presented as his reported statement.

A separate online thread (attributed in the notes to **[@Ammermazing](/Influencers/x/ammermazing)** and others) claims that **Daniel Merrell was the subject of Google searches from Israeli IP addresses** in the summer before the shooting — reportedly in the same window that searches from D.C. and Israeli IPs allegedly targeted **"Timpanogos Regional Hospital"** (where Kirk was taken) and **[Deirdre Amaro](/People/deirdre-amaro)**, described as the new Utah state medical examiner who performed the autopsy. The same poster expressly states he is **"not accusing Daniel Merrell of anything nefarious"** and assumes Merrell "was just doing what was asked of him." This is an unverified online claim about search-trend data, recorded here with that caveat and asserting no wrongdoing by Merrell or any named person.

A related claim in the master file holds that **Governor [Spencer Cox](/People/spencer-cox)** ordered the crime scene torn up and paved over. That is an attributed allegation, not an established fact; Cox is a living public official and no wrongdoing is asserted here.

## The Official and Counter Explanations

UVU reportedly claimed the work was **prescheduled**. The Associated Press reported that the UVU president declined to give specifics, citing a pending external review. A separate rumor blaming the suspect's father's company reportedly found **no credible link**.

These official and counter explanations are included so the page does not present the speed and timing as proof of anything. The reported facts raise questions about why a crime scene would be resurfaced so quickly; they are not, on this record, evidence of a crime by any named person.
