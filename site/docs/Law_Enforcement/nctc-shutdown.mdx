# NCTC Inquiry (See CoverUp / US Intelligence)

The reported **halt** of **Joe Kent**'s **National Counterterrorism Center (NCTC)** foreign-nexus review is a **federal intelligence** thread — not Utah campus police or county sheriff work. This page is a pointer so the local Law Enforcement reader knows where the federal review sits and why it matters to the state timeline. Full documentation lives under CoverUp and U.S. Intelligence.

## What the notes report

Per [Charlie_Kirk.txt](https://github.com/), the reporting runs as follows, presented as **attributed claims and open questions**, not established fact:

* **Joe Kent**, described as Director of the **National Counterterrorism Center (NCTC)**, was reportedly **investigating** a possible foreign nexus to the Kirk killing.
* The notes state that **FBI Director [Kash Patel](/FBI/Kash_Patel_Briefings)** wanted that review **shut down**, and was reportedly "very mad that Joe Kent 'went through FBI material' on this case."
* A cited article is characterized as being about Patel being upset that **Joe Kent and [Tulsi Gabbard](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard)** — Gabbard as Director of National Intelligence over the **Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI)**, NCTC's parent — were pursuing the foreign-involvement question.
* A quoted post (@CatQuestionsAll, 2026-06-29) reads: "Remember our government stopped Joe Kent from investigating foreign involvement."
* The notes separately record an assertion that **NCTC believed the same group was behind the Butler attack** — "same drones, apparently. Highly sophisticated." This is an attributed claim, not a verified finding.

Why it belongs adjacent to this section: if a federal counterterrorism review was halted while the **state** prosecution proceeded on a lone-shooter theory (see [court proceedings](./court-proceedings)), the two timelines diverge — a gap the [Fix Laws](/Fix/overview) disclosure proposals are aimed at closing.

## Why Local Law Enforcement Readers Care

Citizen investigators on X treat the reported **NCTC / Joe Kent** halt as the reason the **state lone-suspect prosecution** never absorbed a **foreign-nexus** track. The claim circulating in commentary (including posts in the style of **"the government stopped Joe Kent from investigating foreign involvement"**) is that once the federal counterterrorism review was shut down, **Utah campus, county, and DPS** actors stayed inside a single-suspect frame. That is an **attributed structural theory**, not proof that any local officer participated in a cover-up.

A related unverified claim in the master notes is that NCTC personnel **believed the same sophisticated group** (including drone patterns) may have been behind **Butler** and UVU. No court has established that link. Readers who want the federal thread should leave this pointer for [CoverUp — White House Halt](/CoverUp/WhiteHouse_Halt_IC) and [U.S. Intelligence — Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent); readers who want the **state charging path** should stay in [Prosecution & Court Proceedings](./court-proceedings) and [Legal](/Legal/overview).
