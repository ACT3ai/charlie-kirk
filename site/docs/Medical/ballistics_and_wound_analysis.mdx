# Ballistics and Wound Analysis

Without a public autopsy, wound and ballistics debates rely on **video, photographs, witness
quotes, and the ATF's inconclusive fragment report**. This page organizes mechanism-of-injury
hypotheses. [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is the **accused**, not convicted.

## Official narrative

Prosecutors attribute death to a **.30-06** round from a **Mauser Model 98** fired from the
**Losee Center rooftop** (~120 m east). See [Tyler Robinson — Weapon](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle).

## ATF autopsy fragment — inconclusive

Court filings reference **Exhibit 6A**, a deformed **bullet jacket fragment** (.30-caliber class)
recovered at autopsy plus **four lead fragments**. September 17, 2025 ATF lab result:

- **Cannot identify or exclude** the seized Mauser as source
- Rifling matches **numerous makes and models**

Full detail: [Trial and Autopsy Report](./trial_and_autopsy_report).

## Morphology challenges to .30-06 at distance

Citizen analysts cite:

| Issue | Argument |
|-------|----------|
| **Neck entry path** | Rooftop ~5–9° downward would likely strike **face/jaw first**; reported entry **between larynx and trachea**, left of cervical spine, more **straight-on** ([@_DaniFesto](https://x.com/_DaniFesto/status/2039372441068155219)) |
| **Exit / back-spray** | .30-06 from east expected catastrophic neck exit and **blood on white tarp** — disputed against scene descriptions |
| **No-exit relay** | Treating doctor reportedly said bullet **did not exit** ("absolute miracle") — conflicts with through-neck witness [Nick](./Witness_Account_Conflicts) |
| **Shirt timing** | Fabric movement **before** visible neck blood per [video analysis](/Mic/mic-explosion-video-analysis) |

## Alternative mechanism hypotheses

### 1. Israeli CornerShot 9mm +P (tent)

[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) / Dr. [Chris Martenson](/People/chris-martenson) thread: **9mm +P** from
**CornerShot** inside tent — high-speed **cavitation**, necklace snap, **pugilist posture**,
energy dumped internally without Tyler-scale exit wound. Shell casing rolling from tent cited.

### 2. Shaped charge / exploding mic or pendant

[PETN micro-charge](/Mic/Mic_vs_Pendant) — inward charge emulates rifle trauma **without exit**;
outward blast explains clasp shrapnel and rectangular neck wound. [Canon XA55](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio)
reports **stage detonation** separate from distant muzzle blast.

### 3. Compressed-air / acoustic gunshot theories

Secondary audio-layer hypotheses — PA speaker injection, staged crack–thump — see
[Lapel Mic & Audio](./lapel_mic_and_audio_theories).

## Reported physical observations

- Necklace severed with **upward** displacement (explosive-force framing)
- **Rectangular** wound shape linked to [magnetic clasp](/Mic/rode-wireless-mic) projectile
- Lack of extensive back-spatter on backdrop
- Decorticate posturing / midbrain trauma in some analyses

Deeper wound catalog: [Autopsy — Wound Theories](/Medical/Autopsy/wound_analysis_theories).

## Needed forensic work

- Public autopsy: wound track, caliber, residue (gunpowder + explosive)
- Trajectory rods and 3D scene reconstruction (tent, stairwell south, rooftop east)
- Ballistic gel at reported distances through barriers
- Authenticate any tent shell casing in video


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Wound & Ballistics)

*(The following are attributed public claims and counter-claims — not findings of this site.)*

Citizen investigators continue to argue that **wound morphology and the ATF fragment record** do not cleanly support a distant .30-06 rooftop kill, while others insist autopsy and hearing evidence do.

- According to **[@HarkjJytte](/Influencers/x/harkjjytte)** on X (July 2026), the “bullet finding” in court does not settle mechanism: the user argues fragments may confirm a projectile without proving a Mauser match, and continues to favor a **RØDE wireless mic** explosion framing. This is an attributed opinion, not a court finding.
- According to **[@MarioNawfal](/Influencers/x/marionawfal)** (Sept 25, 2025), mainstream autopsy explanation is **no exit wound because the bullet lodged** under the skin; the post frames conspiracy claims about “missing exit” as misunderstanding ballistics and notes Utah ME backlog / reports withheld during trial. Source cited in that thread: Laurenthemortician / PBS framing.
- According to **[@stratagemmer](/Influencers/x/stratagemmer)** (July 6, 2026), state witness **Agent David Hull** testified that an autopsy was performed the evening of September 10 by a male pathologist named (**as Hull spelled it**) **Dr. Guajardo**; cause of death listed as **homicide by gunshot wound to the neck**; the judge reportedly ruled the autopsy report **not public**. Defense objections to reliability/hearsay were described as overruled. Unverified beyond X court-watch notes.
- According to **[@alexboge](/Influencers/x/alexboge)** (July 9, 2026), exploding-mic/shaped-charge theories fail physics (standoff, fabric not torn, no soot/flash in multi-angle video) and must now also explain **bullet fragments** entered at the July 6, 2026 preliminary hearing.
