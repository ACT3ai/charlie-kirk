# Events at Hospital

This page collects what has been reported about the scene at **Timpanogos Regional Hospital** after Charlie Kirk was transported there on **September 10, 2025**. Several of the accounts below are unverified eyewitness statements circulating on social media; they are presented with attribution and no wrongdoing is established by any of them.

## Arrival and the OR

According to a reported eyewitness account, the SUV carrying Charlie Kirk was met by hospital staff outside the **Surgery doors** rather than the main Emergency Room entrance. The same account states that Charlie was effectively **dead on arrival (DOA)**.

The account describes roughly **20–30 minutes** inside the **operating room** before a surgeon emerged.

See also [Charlie's Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital) for the transport sequence that preceded these events.

## Pronouncement of Death

Per the reported account, a surgeon came out of the OR and **pronounced Charlie Kirk deceased at approximately 1:03 PM MT**. This time is drawn from a reported eyewitness narrative and has not been independently confirmed here.

## Reported Account: The Second SUV and Physician Parking

An account circulating on X, attributed to a person describing themselves as an eyewitness/contractor on site, claims the following. It is presented strictly as a **reported observation** — the identity and purpose of the people involved are unknown and speculative, and no wrongdoing is established.

According to that reported eyewitness account, Charlie Kirk's Chief of Staff, **Mikey McCoy**, arrived in a **second SUV** that pulled in right behind the first. The account claims that, rather than going to the Emergency Room, the second SUV reportedly drove to the **physician parking** area, where an **unidentified person** got into the passenger seat, after which the vehicle left again — reportedly without the occupants visiting Charlie's body.

It must be stressed that this is an **unverified eyewitness claim only**. Who the unidentified person was, and why they were picked up, are **unknown** and remain entirely speculative. Nothing in this account establishes that **Mikey McCoy** or anyone else did anything wrong.

## The "On a List" Account

Separately, **Andrew Kolvet** reportedly said that he was told by **Candace Owens** that she and **Tucker Carlson** "were on a list." According to the reported statement, this was conveyed around the time Andrew arrived at the hospital.

This is presented as a **reported statement** attributed to those individuals; it is not verified here, and it does not establish any wrongdoing by any person named.

## Death Certificate and Aftermath — Claims

Citizen investigation notes claim that a **death certificate was "signed by a hospital doctor"** and that the body was then **"whisked away."** The same notes claim that Charlie's wife, **Erika Kirk**, later **"got a private flight out."**

These are **attributed claims** from private investigation notes and have not been independently confirmed on this page.

For a related thread on what reportedly happened to hospital surveillance footage, see [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed).

## Caveats

The majority of the material above consists of **unverified accounts** — reported eyewitness statements, social-media posts, and citizen investigation notes. They are recorded here because they are part of the public record of claims surrounding the event, not because they have been confirmed.

No content on this page should be read as asserting that any living person — including **Mikey McCoy**, **Andrew Kolvet**, **Candace Owens**, **Tucker Carlson**, or **Erika Kirk** — committed any wrongdoing or crime. Where an action is described, it is a **reported observation** awaiting verification, and any inference of guilt would be unwarranted.
