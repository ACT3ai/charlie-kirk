# Events at Hospital

This page collects what has been reported about the scene at **[Timpanogos Regional Hospital](/Locations/Timpanogos_Regional_Hospital)** after [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was transported there on **September 10, 2025**. Several of the accounts below are unverified eyewitness statements circulating on social media; they are presented with attribution and no wrongdoing is established by any of them.

## Arrival and the OR

According to a reported eyewitness account, the SUV carrying Charlie Kirk was met by hospital staff outside the **Surgery doors** rather than the main Emergency Room entrance. The same account states that Charlie was effectively **dead on arrival (DOA)**.

The account describes roughly **20–30 minutes** inside the **operating room** before a surgeon emerged.

See also [Charlie's Trip to the Hospital](/Medical/charlie_trip_to_hospital) for the transport sequence that preceded these events.

## Pronouncement of Death

Per the reported account, a surgeon came out of the OR and **pronounced Charlie Kirk deceased at approximately 1:03 PM MT**. This time is drawn from a reported eyewitness narrative and has not been independently confirmed here.

Some accounts add that Charlie reportedly **still had a pulse in the Emergency Room** on arrival but was not expected to survive (a claim attributed to a head-of-security statement shared by **[@MJTruthUltra](/Influencers/x/mjtruthultra)**). A catastrophic neck gunshot wound is widely described by clinicians as non-survivable regardless of effort.

## The ER Scene — Reported Claims

Several attributed claims describe an unusual or contested scene at the hospital:

* **[Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole)**, head of Kirk's private security detail, was reportedly present in the ER during the chaotic arrival.
* **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** referenced a claim that **"no one was in the ER"** when Kirk was brought in. Fact-checks have called this **inconclusive/speculative**.
* According to **@TziTzi183690**, the **FBI kept the treating surgeon away from the body for about two hours** — presented here as an unverified claim about evidence/scene control, not an established fact.
* Hospital staff reportedly handed out **scrubs** for people (security/witnesses) to change into amid the chaos, and Charlie's assistant later bought everyone new clothes because they were still in scrubs.

These are reported claims; some have been disputed or labeled inconclusive, and none establishes wrongdoing by the hospital, its staff, or any named individual. For the related claim that a surgeon left the room to make a phone call, see [Doctor DC Phone Call](/Medical/doctor_dc_phone_call).

## Reported Account: The Second SUV and Physician Parking

An account circulating on X, attributed to a person describing themselves as an eyewitness/contractor on site, claims the following. It is presented strictly as a **reported observation** — the identity and purpose of the people involved are unknown and speculative, and no wrongdoing is established.

According to that reported eyewitness account, Charlie Kirk's Chief of Staff, **[Mikey McCoy](/People/mikey-mccoy)**, arrived in a **second SUV** that pulled in right behind the first. The account claims that, rather than going to the Emergency Room, the second SUV reportedly drove to the **physician parking** area, where an **unidentified person** got into the passenger seat, after which the vehicle left again — reportedly without the occupants visiting Charlie's body.

It must be stressed that this is an **unverified eyewitness claim only**. Who the unidentified person was, and why they were picked up, are **unknown** and remain entirely speculative. Nothing in this account establishes that **Mikey McCoy** or anyone else did anything wrong.

## The "On a List" Account

Separately, **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** reportedly said that he was told by **Candace Owens** that she and **[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** "were on a list." According to the reported statement, this was conveyed around the time Andrew arrived at the hospital.

This is presented as a **reported statement** attributed to those individuals; it is not verified here, and it does not establish any wrongdoing by any person named.

## Death Certificate and Aftermath — Claims

Some citizen investigators have claimed that a **death certificate was "signed by a hospital doctor"** and that the body was then **"whisked away."** The same commentary claims that Charlie's wife, **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)**, later **"got a private flight out."**

These are **attributed claims** drawn from public commentary and reporting and have not been independently confirmed on this page.

## Erika Kirk and the Aftermath at the Hospital

According to widely reposted statements, **Erika Kirk** was on a flight when Charlie was pronounced dead. A sheriff reportedly met her at the hospital and advised against viewing the body, but she **insisted on seeing him**, later describing his eyes as semi-open with a "knowing, **Mona Lisa-like half-smile**." She has also said that **the doctor told her** the wound was so instant that "even if Charlie had been shot in the operating room itself, nothing could have been done." These are **reported statements** attributed to Erika Kirk; they are presented as her account and establish no wrongdoing by anyone.

President **[Donald Trump](/People/donald-trump)** announced the death on Truth Social at roughly **2:40 PM MT**. Supporters later gathered for a **vigil outside Timpanogos Regional Hospital** with flags, flowers, and candles, and a **motorcade** eventually left with Charlie's body.

For a related thread on what reportedly happened to hospital surveillance footage, see [Hospital Security Cameras Removed](/Medical/hospital_security_cameras_removed).

## Caveats

The majority of the material above consists of **unverified accounts** — reported eyewitness statements, social-media posts, and citizen investigation notes. They are recorded here because they are part of the public record of claims surrounding the event, not because they have been confirmed.

No content on this page should be read as asserting that any living person — including **Mikey McCoy**, **Andrew Kolvet**, **Candace Owens**, **Tucker Carlson**, or **Erika Kirk** — committed any wrongdoing or crime. Where an action is described, it is a **reported observation** awaiting verification, and any inference of guilt would be unwarranted.
