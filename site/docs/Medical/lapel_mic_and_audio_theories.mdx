# Lapel Mic and Audio Theories

From a **medical forensics** angle, the exploding-mic thesis asks whether [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s fatal
injury matches **internal detonation** at the lavalier pack rather than external rifle energy.
Full mic-section depth lives at [Mic — Exploding Mic Theory](/Mic/overview); this page bridges
medical and audio evidence.

## Core medical claim

Charlie_Kirk.txt [Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad)-theory point **#3**:

> His lapel mic may have also had an explosive in it… We are waiting for the autopsies to confirm.
> This blast is what cut his necklace and shot it upward.

If confirmed, cause of death shifts from **penetrating ballistics** to **blast/fragmentation** —
with implications for [Tyler Robinson exoneration](/Tyler_Robinson/Weapon_Rifle) on ballistics grounds.
