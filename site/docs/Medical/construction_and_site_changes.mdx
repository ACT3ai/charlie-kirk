# Construction and Site Changes

Medical forensics does not stop at the body — it includes **whether the scene preserved ballistic
and blast evidence**. Investigators allege the UVU tent area was **paved or hardscaped within days**
of the shooting, complicating wound-origin reconstruction and trace recovery.

This website does not assert as fact that any living person or company named here committed a crime,
did anything wrong or illegal, or knew of the assassination in advance. Names appear only in the
context of public discussion, and nothing here is a finding by this website.

## Summary

**~Four days** after September 10, 2025, landscaping at the **tent site** was reportedly removed
and replaced with **pavers/concrete**. Work allegedly occurred on a **Sunday** — described as
unusual for Utah County scheduled maintenance.

## Merrell's first-person account (Jimmy Rex interview)

Daniel Merrell of **Hardscape Utah** gave a video interview describing how he was brought in. He says he installed pavers on **9/14/25** after being **"called by men who work for the state of Utah"** to cover the courtyard grass **"where [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was gunned down."** Key first-person claims from the interview:

- He quotes the on-site coordinator: *"This is above our pay grade. The governor and the FBI said they want this done by Monday."* The **Monday** deadline aligns with the documented **Sunday 9/14 install**.
- The two men directing the work were reportedly **not** normal contractors — one ran a **state-owned facility in American Fork**, the other was a **groundskeeper at a Provo facility** — overseeing a **"ragtag crew."**
- Merrell says he was actually the **second team**: a **prior government team** had already been on the soil and had **"taken out about ten [inches]"** of dirt before he arrived. Investigators tie the deep soil removal to **PETN high soil-absorption** in the [shaped-charge/mic](/Mic/overview) hypothesis.

**"They covered up the evidence THE NEXT DAY, demolishing and repaving the crime scene"** is how some public commentary frames the sequence. This website does **not** accuse Merrell of wrongdoing — he states he was **"just doing what was asked."**

## Forensic impact

Rapid alteration may destroy or obscure:

- **Bullet strikes** on decking, tent poles, backdrop
- **Blast residue** from [shaped-charge/mic](/Mic/overview) hypotheses
- **Blood spatter** pattern mapping for entry direction
- **Shell casings** or fragments alleged near tent ([CornerShot thread](/Killer/overview))

Public reporting and commentary also note **ceiling/tunnel re-mudding** and **podium blood** claims.

## Connection to autopsy gap

Utah statute **268-8-8-205** (cited in online threads) requires autopsy in gun-violence deaths —
yet [no public autopsy report](./autopsy_report_not_public) fuels debate. **Site destruction +
sealed autopsy** compound the medical evidence vacuum.
