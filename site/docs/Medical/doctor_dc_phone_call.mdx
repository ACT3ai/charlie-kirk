# Doctor DC Phone Call

This page collects a narrow cluster of **unverified claims** that connect a *phone call* to the doctor-and-hospital scene after Charlie Kirk was taken to **Timpanogos Regional Hospital** on **September 10, 2025** — together with a recurring **Washington, DC** thread that some investigators draw through the pre-event searches and the activity in the hospital's physician-parking area. Nothing here is established fact, and no person named or referenced is accused of any wrongdoing.

## The Surgeon Who Reportedly Stepped Out to Call

According to citizen-investigation notes summarizing X claims, the **surgeon who worked on Charlie Kirk left the ER to make a phone call**, and **FBI agents initially would not let the surgeon back into the ER room** — reportedly to clean Charlie up before Erika Kirk's arrival — until agents were told to "stand down." The same notes claim Charlie still had a pulse at that point but was not expected to survive.

This is an **unverified, attributed claim.** Who the surgeon called, and why, is **unknown**. A treating physician stepping away to make a call during a chaotic mass-casualty arrival is, on its face, unremarkable; the detail is recorded here only because it became part of the public set of questions about the hospital scene. For the related scene-control claims (a security figure "commandeering" the ER, the FBI reportedly keeping the surgeon from the body), see [Events at Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital).

## The Washington, DC Thread

The "DC" in this page's title comes from a separate strand of the investigation: the claim that **pre-event Google searches for the hospital and its doctors/surgeons originated not only from Israeli IP ranges but from Washington, DC.** Investigators (citing **Baron Coleman**-style search analysis, amplified by accounts such as **@MZakkiel**, **@malteselove67**, **@TheCKFiles1**, and **@johnmcvicar16**) describe DC-area searches for the specific hospital, the emergency surgeon/doctor, and the medical examiner in the weeks before the shooting.

Because of those DC-origin search claims, some investigators speculate that the **unidentified person picked up in the hospital's physician-parking area** (see below) might have been a "doctor" with a Washington, DC connection. That is **pure speculation** built on an unverified search claim layered on an unverified eyewitness account — it is presented here only to explain the page's framing, not as a conclusion. The full treatment of the search-pattern claim is on [Israel Google Searches for Medical People](/Medical/israel_google_searches_medical_people).

## The Physician-Parking Pickup and the Chief-of-Staff Call

A reported eyewitness/contractor account (pushed heavily by **@ProjectConstitu**, with questions raised by **@Geo735329977532**, **@TheCKFiles1**, and others) claims that a **second SUV** carrying Charlie's Chief of Staff **Mikey McCoy** drove to the **physician-parking** area rather than the ER, where an **unidentified person** got into the passenger seat before the vehicle left.

The official, on-the-record account via Erika Kirk is that **Mikey McCoy called her, screaming "He's been shot."** That ordinary explanation directly answers the "who did he call" question for at least one of the calls.

This material overlaps with [Events at Hospital](/Medical/events_at_hospital), where the second-SUV account is documented in full; it is referenced here only because the "doctor / DC / phone call" framing ties these threads together. **The identity and purpose of the picked-up person are unknown and entirely speculative.** Nothing in any of these accounts establishes that **Mikey McCoy**, the treating surgeon, or anyone else did anything wrong.

## Caveats

- Every item on this page is an **unverified claim** — drawn from eyewitness accounts, timing analyses, and search-data assertions that have not been confirmed by any official source or court.
- The "DC" connection rests on the **unproven search-origin methodology** discussed on the Israel-searches page; treat it as an alleged pattern, not a fact.
- No named or referenced person — including any treating physician, **Mikey McCoy**, or any official — is accused of any crime or wrongdoing on this site. Ordinary explanations (a call to a spouse, a physician stepping out, evidence collection by investigators) may fully account for the observations.
