# Death Timeline — Shot to Pronouncement

Whether Charlie Kirk could have received meaningful emergency care — and when he was actually
pronounced dead — matters for both medical ethics and cover-up theories. This page reconstructs
the **reported minute-by-minute window** from Charlie_Kirk.txt and related timeline notes.
Figures are investigative estimates, not hospital records.

## Anchor: the shot (~12:23 PM MDT)

Most sources place the fatal event at **~12:23 PM** Utah time on September 10, 2025. Internal
video timeline notes extend to **12:24:36 PM** for immediate post-shot movement.

## Transport and ER intake (estimated 13–20 minutes)

Investigators model:

- **~1 minute** to leave scene
- **7–10 minutes** drive to [Timpanogos Regional](./Timpanogos_Facility)
- **5–10 minutes minimum** unload, admit, move to OR

Transport was by **personal SUV**, not ambulance — relevant to trauma-protocol questions on
[Hospital Choice & Transport](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_choice_transport).

## OR and pronouncement (estimated +20–30 minutes)

Notes cite **20–30 minutes in OR** before a surgeon emerged and pronounced death around **1:03:30 PM**.

**Total modeled window:** **33–50 minutes** from shot to pronouncement → estimated end **12:56 PM–1:13 PM**.

## Jerusalem Post timing conflict

Charlie_Kirk.txt flags that the **Jerusalem Post announced Kirk's death before** the modeled
pronouncement window closed. If accurate, that implies either:

- Death was known externally before official hospital pronouncement, or
- Public reporting used a different time source

This remains an open synchronization problem — not proof of any actor's wrongdoing.

## Erika / Mikey call discrepancy

Erika Kirk reportedly said **Mikey McCoy called her at 12:23 PM** (11:23 AM Arizona). Blake Neff
claimed he witnessed that call and a subsequent call to Rob McCoy — but at **12:24:02** footage
shows **Mikey alone** without Neff in frame. Project notes state: *"Something isn't adding up"* —
timeline reconstruction only, no accusation.

## Death certificate gap

Strange-events list item **#14** in Charlie_Kirk.txt: **"No death certificate"** in public record.
See [Death Certificate Questions](/Medical/Autopsy/death_certificate).

## What records would settle it

- Hospital EMR timestamps: arrival, trauma activation, OR entry, pronouncement
- SUV GPS / cell-site data for transport duration
- Dispatch and EMS logs (EMS reportedly blocked at UVU per [Hospital Scene](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems))
- Official death certificate and certifying physician identity
