# Data Manipulation and Cell Forensics

## Overview

This section examines potential data manipulation and cell phone forensics related to the Charlie Kirk assassination investigation.

## Key Areas of Investigation

### Cell Phone Data
- Analysis of cell phone records and metadata
- Location tracking data and timeline verification
- Communication logs and call patterns

### Digital Evidence
- Examination of digital devices and their data
- Potential data tampering or deletion
- Forensic analysis of electronic communications

### Technical Analysis
- Cell tower data and triangulation
- Signal analysis and coverage patterns
- Device forensics and data recovery
