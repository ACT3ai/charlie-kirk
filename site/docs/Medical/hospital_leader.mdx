# Hospital Leader

This page looks at the leadership of **Timpanogos Regional Hospital** in Orem, Utah, around the time of Charlie Kirk's death on **September 10, 2025**, and at the open questions citizen-investigators have raised about the timing of a newly appointed CEO. None of what follows establishes that any person did anything wrong.

## Andrew Zenger — New CEO

**Andrew Zenger** (full name reported as **Andrew James Zenger**) was appointed Chief Executive Officer of Timpanogos Regional Hospital on **August 19, 2025**, and reportedly began the role on **August 25, 2025** — roughly two weeks before the events of September 10. A new hospital CEO appointment is, on its face, a routine and unremarkable event in hospital administration.

## Dual Role: CEO and Surgical Team?

According to citizen-investigator notes, Zenger is described as "the doctor that treated Charlie," and the same notes report that he joined the hospital's surgical team on **August 28, 2025**. Investigators have raised the question of why a newly appointed hospital CEO would also serve on a surgical team. This is framed as an open question, not a conclusion — and the claim that the same individual both led the hospital and treated the patient has not been independently confirmed here. See also [Medical Staff Changes](/Medical/medical_staff_changes) and [Doctors](/Medical/doctors).

## Background

Zenger reportedly began his healthcare career as an administrative intern at the same hospital in **2014** and has held various leadership roles since. He is reported to hold a degree from **Trinity University** and has been described as "an experienced leader returning to his roots in Orem." This background is consistent with an ordinary internal-promotion or homecoming hire.

## Reported Search Timing

Citizen-investigator notes report that Zenger's name was searched from an **Israeli IP address on August 27, 2025** — and that he reportedly joined the surgical team the following day, **August 28**. Investigators ask whether the timing of his appointment, the reported search, and his start on the surgical team are coincidental. This page treats that strictly as an unresolved question. The reported foreign-IP search has not been independently verified, and its existence — even if accurate — would not by itself indicate any involvement by Zenger. For related context, see [Foreign Intelligence Leads](/Israel/foreign-leads) and [Google Searches Overview](/GoogleSearches/overview).

## Status & Caveats

**Status: Alive**

Andrew Zenger is presumed alive and is **not accused of any crime or wrongdoing**. A new hospital CEO appointment is routine. The only points flagged by investigators are (1) the timing of the appointment relative to September 10, and (2) a reported foreign-IP search of his name — both presented here as open questions. Nothing on this page establishes that he did anything improper, and all of the case-specific claims above are attributed to citizen-investigator notes that have not been independently confirmed.
