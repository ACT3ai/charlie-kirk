# Timpanogos Regional Hospital

[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) was transported to **Timpanogos Regional Hospital** in Orem — not to the higher-level
**Utah Valley Hospital** (Intermountain) in Provo. Investigators treat that choice as a central
medical-process question. **We do not claim** any hospital employee caused harm or knew of the attack.

## Facility basics

| Item | Reported detail |
|------|-----------------|
| Name | Timpanogos Regional Hospital (Orem, Utah) |
| Trauma designation | **Level IV** (lower capability than Level II centers) |
| Distance from UVU | ~**2.6 miles** per CK_FILE distance notes |
| Alternative | Utah Valley Hospital ~**3.4–12.3 miles** depending on routing source — **Level II**, not used |

Standard serious-trauma protocol favors the **highest-level center reachable**. A gunshot to the
neck would normally trigger Level I/II preference if available within reasonable time.

## Leadership timing — Andrew Zenger

Reported appointments before September 10:

- **August 19, 2025** — Andrew James Zenger appointed **CEO**
- **August 25, 2025** — start date
- **August 28, 2025** — joined hospital **surgical team**
- Zenger also described as the **treating surgeon** for Kirk — dual CEO/surgeon role disputed

See [Hospital Leader](./hospital_leader). August **27** Israeli-IP search spike for Zenger name
per [Israel Google Searches](./israel_google_searches_medical_people) — correlation only.

## Cleared ER and bomb-scare theories

Commentary describes the emergency department as **unusually empty** on September 10. A separate
CK_FILE thread claims a **bomb scare** at the closer Intermountain facility may have influenced
routing — unverified. [Hospital Scene & EMS](/Medical/Hospital/hospital_scene_ems) covers EMS
access and FBI footage claims.

## Surgeon search-pattern cluster

Charlie_Kirk.txt documents **seven Timpanogos surgeons** allegedly searched from Israeli IPs in
a July 2025 window — Neilman, Patterson, Trotter, Gill, Rasmussen, Pugh, and others. Listed on
[Doctors](./doctors) and [Medical Staff Changes](./medical_staff_changes). **No accusation** —
pattern documentation only.

## Romney / Bilt / ER scribe thread (reported)

[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) coverage (summarized in Charlie_Kirk.txt) tied **Timpanogos** to:

- **Bilt Inc.** June 2025 ~$8M DOD contracts (3D immersive intelligence)
- **[Connor Romney Henderson](/People/connor-henderson)** — ER scribe at Timpanogos summer 2025
