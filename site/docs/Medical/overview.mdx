Medical evidence is central because mechanism-of-injury determines whether the [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)
frame is even physically possible. The [ATF inconclusive](./trial_and_autopsy_report) bullet-jacket
match, [wound trajectory disputes](./ballistics_and_wound_analysis), and [Canon XA55 stage
detonation](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) audio all point away from a simple rooftop .30-06 kill — while
the state still pursues capital charges. Until the ME file opens, every medical page here is a
map of **questions**, not a diagnosis.

The substance splits into autopsy opacity, hospital routing, and witness narrative. **Autopsy:**
[SB0082](./utah_autopsy_law_changes) tightened photo sharing months before the event; fragments
exist per court filings yet [viral no-autopsy claims](./autopsy_report_not_public) persist.
**Hospital:** Charlie went to **Level IV** [Timpanogos](./Timpanogos_Facility) by SUV with a
[new CEO/surgeon](./hospital_leader) and reported **cleared ER** / **FBI footage seizure** threads.
**Witnesses:** [Chest vs neck](./Witness_Account_Conflicts) descriptions may match a [rigged mic
for chest](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) that hit neck instead. **Site:** [Sunday paving](./construction_and_site_changes)
may have destroyed traces that autopsy never publicly documented.

The [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index), [Autopsy](./Autopsy/overview), and
[Trial & Autopsy Report](./trial_and_autopsy_report) sections cover the fragments question; the
[Death Timeline](./Death_Timeline) and [Timpanogos Facility](./Timpanogos_Facility) sections cover
transport; and [Ballistics](./ballistics_and_wound_analysis) and the
[Mic bridge](./lapel_mic_and_audio_theories) sections cover mechanism. Related non-medical angles on
the same physics appear in the [Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and [Mic](/Mic/overview) sections.

This website does not assert as fact that any living person named here committed a crime, did anything
wrong or illegal, or had any knowledge of or involvement in the assassination. Names appear here only in
the context of public discussion. Nothing here is a finding by this website.

## Public commentary

As of mid-2026 citizen discussion on X, the most widely discussed medical disputes are:

1. **Autopsy sealed vs fragments in court** — hearing-watch accounts say COD is GSW neck / homicide, while the full ME report remains non-public (**[@stratagemmer](/Influencers/x/stratagemmer)**, **[@Joshua_the_car](/Influencers/x/joshua-the-car)** notes).
2. **SUV transport + second vehicle** — **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** hospital-contractor narrative (McCoy / physician parking) still widely shared and still unverified by released CCTV.
3. **Glass-in-SUV vs rifle** — **[@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)** / mic-theory camp vs **[@alexboge](/Influencers/x/alexboge)** / rifle-physics camp; both cite video.
4. **ATF inconclusive Mauser match** — continues to undercut a simple ballistics slam-dunk in public filings discussed on trial pages.

Readers should start at [Trial & Autopsy Report](./trial_and_autopsy_report) and [Ballistics & Wound Analysis](./ballistics_and_wound_analysis), then hospital transport pages.
