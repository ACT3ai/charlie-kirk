Medical evidence is central because mechanism-of-injury determines whether the [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)
frame is even physically possible. The [ATF inconclusive](./trial_and_autopsy_report) bullet-jacket
match, [wound trajectory disputes](./ballistics_and_wound_analysis), and [Canon XA55 stage
detonation](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) audio all point away from a simple rooftop .30-06 kill — while
the state still pursues capital charges. Until the ME file opens, every medical page here is a
map of **questions**, not a diagnosis.

The substance splits into autopsy opacity, hospital routing, and witness narrative. **Autopsy:**
[SB0082](./utah_autopsy_law_changes) tightened photo sharing months before the event; fragments
exist per court filings yet [viral no-autopsy claims](./autopsy_report_not_public) persist.
**Hospital:** Charlie went to **Level IV** [Timpanogos](./Timpanogos_Facility) by SUV with a
[new CEO/surgeon](./hospital_leader) and reported **cleared ER** / **FBI footage seizure** threads.
**Witnesses:** [Chest vs neck](./Witness_Account_Conflicts) descriptions may match a [rigged mic
for chest](/Mic/Chest_Shot_Witnesses) that hit neck instead. **Site:** [Sunday paving](./construction_and_site_changes)
may have destroyed traces that autopsy never publicly documented.

Prioritize [Investigation Index](./Investigation_Index) → [Autopsy](./Autopsy/overview) +
[Trial & Autopsy Report](./trial_and_autopsy_report) for fragments, then [Death Timeline](./Death_Timeline)
and [Timpanogos Facility](./Timpanogos_Facility) for transport, then [Ballistics](./ballistics_and_wound_analysis)
and [Mic bridge](./lapel_mic_and_audio_theories) for mechanism. Cross-read [Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview)
and [Mic](/Mic/overview) for non-medical angles on the same physics.
