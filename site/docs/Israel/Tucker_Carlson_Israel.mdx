# Tucker Carlson on Evolving Israel Views

**[Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson)** — a personal friend of Charlie Kirk who appeared on Charlie's show
**less than 24 hours before** the UVU shooting (September 9, 2025) — made an on-camera
statement tying the killing to Charlie's **evolving Israel views**, not to transgenderism
or other culture-war issues. This page documents Carlson's words as **his reported belief**,
not a court finding or proof of who acted.

## Source

- X post: [@Partisangirl, 2026-06-20](https://x.com/Partisangirl/status/2068463186668605858) — video clip of Carlson speaking
- The same clip is summarized on the [home page](/) under *Tucker Carlson: "His views on Israel were changing"*
- Metrics at capture: ~3,228 likes, 489 reposts, 33k views
- Carlson profile: [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson) — **Status: Alive**

## Carlson's statement (transcript)

> "Charlie Kirk was murdered. Yeah. And he was not murdered for his opinions on
> transgenderism obviously. He was, I believe, and most people who knew him well as I did
> and was friends with him as I was, believe he was **most likely murdered for his evolving
> views on Israel**. So I get it. And a lot's at stake. The world is at stake. But from my
> perspective, I have a duty to say what I think is true. I'm often wrong. And I always say
> that because it's a fact. I have been wrong a lot. And I may be wrong now. But I am sincere.
> I'm saying what I believe is true. And I'm gonna continue doing that no matter what."

Carlson explicitly hedges — *"I may be wrong now"* — which this page preserves. We do **not**
state as fact that Israel or any Israeli actor ordered the killing.
