# Islamabad Serena Tracking (Claims)

In April 2026, citizen-researcher **[Tony Seruga](/People/tony-seruga)** and amplified X threads claimed a
**breakthrough link** between the Charlie Kirk case and ongoing Middle East diplomacy:
**four** of the **16 Israeli-registered cellphones** allegedly detected at UVU on
September 10, 2025, reportedly pinged at the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** in Pakistan —
the same venue hosting high-stakes **US–Iran peace talks** (sometimes called the
"Islamabad Talks"). This page documents those claims. We do **not** assert that any
phone holder committed a crime or that diplomats at the hotel were involved in the killing.

## The diplomatic context (April 10–11, 2026)

Public reporting placed senior delegations at the Serena Hotel under heavy Pakistani
security (hotel requisitioned, 3 km cordon, regular guests cleared):

- **US side:** Vice President **[JD Vance](/People/jd-vance)**, with reports also naming **Jared Kushner**
  and **Steve Witkoff** in the negotiation orbit.
- **Iranian side:** Parliament Speaker **Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf**, Foreign Minister
  **Abbas Araghchi**, and related officials.
- **Host:** Pakistan PM **Shehbaz Sharif** and mediation teams.

Israel has obvious strategic interest in any US–Iran deal (nuclear program, regional
proxies). Intelligence **monitoring** of such talks would be expected; the investigative
question is whether any device allegedly at UVU is **documentably the same** as devices
at the hotel — not whether monitoring occurred.

## The tracking claim (claims)

Per Seruga's posts and viral X summaries (April 2026):

1. Raw GPS/SIGINT data allegedly showed **16 Israeli-registered phones** near Charlie Kirk
   at UVU on September 10, 2025 (see [NSA Cellphones & Bumblehive](./NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive)).
2. **Four** of those devices allegedly arrived at the **Islamabad Serena Hotel** on the
   evening of April 10, 2026 — overlapping the peace-talk lockdown.
3. The poster labeled an attached tracking graphic as a **recreation, NOT real** — so
   screenshots should not be treated as primary evidence.

Commentary frames this as: devices that were allegedly at the murder scene later moved in
the same venue as negotiators deciding Iran policy. That is **circumstantial pattern-matching**
until subscriber identities and lawful intercept logs are produced.

## Bumblehive deanonymization angle

Seruga tied the Islamabad ping to his broader **Bumblehive** argument: even burner phones
used for ~30–60 days may be linked to real identities via NSA metadata fusion. If true as
a technical matter, the investigative implication is that the government may already know
who carried the alleged UVU phones — not that we know from public posts.

## Evaluation notes

- **Coincidence vs. operation:** Diplomatic summits attract intelligence personnel,
  journalists, and security details from many countries. Presence at both a US campus event
  and a later summit is not, by itself, proof of planning or execution.
- **Data provenance:** The chain rests on Seruga's SIGINT posts, not FOIA releases or
  court exhibits.
- **Foreign-student rebuttal:** Commentators note UVU hosts international students; the
  Islamabad thread is specifically about **the same four device IDs** from the UVU set,
  not generic Israeli nationals abroad.

## Open questions

- Can device IDs from the alleged UVU set be reproduced from primary logs?
- Who were the subscribers, and what were their stated roles on September 10 and April 10?
- Did any US agency brief Congress on foreign-device counts at UVU?
- How did [FBI foreign-lead messaging](/FBI/Foreign_Leads) align with this data existing?
