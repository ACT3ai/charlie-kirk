# Mossad and TPUSA

<div style={{border:'1px solid #f0c36d', background:'#fcf8e3', borderRadius:'6px', padding:'1rem 1.25rem', marginBottom:'1.5rem'}}>

**Read this first — what this page is and is not.** This page documents an allegation made publicly by a former Turning Point USA (TPUSA) representative. It is presented as a **reported claim, not established fact**. We are not stating that the events described are true.

We are **not** claiming that Turning Point USA, its leadership, or any of its employees knew of, approved, or caused anything described here. We are **not** claiming that anyone at TPUSA was a foreign-intelligence officer, or that anyone at TPUSA was Mossad. No such thing has been proven, and nothing here should be read as a finding of guilt against any person or organization.

We publish it for one reason: to call for a **trustworthy investigation**. We want the proposed disclosure and investigation laws to be used so that U.S. intelligence services can determine whether any foreign-intelligence actor was interfacing with TPUSA personnel — and so the truth, whatever it turns out to be, can come out. See the [Fix Laws](../../Fix/overview).

</div>

A former Turning Point USA representative, **Joshua Peterson** (X: [@RagingPatriot20](https://x.com/RagingPatriot20)), has come forward with an account from his time in TPUSA training. As reported by the account **Project Constitution**, he alleges that during a training trip, former representatives took new hires out and that some of them later identified themselves as members of a foreign "intelligence agency" who said they had been working at TPUSA for several years. These are his stated allegations and remain uncorroborated.

## The reported allegation

The following summarizes the account as published by **Project Constitution**, relaying statements attributed to former TPUSA representative **Joshua Peterson**. Every item below is a reported allegation by Peterson — not an established fact — and several reference living persons and an organization whose actions and statements should be independently verified.

> Former Turning Point USA Rep BLOWS the Whistle: STRIPPER Vans, Getting New Hires Drunk, and FOREIGN Intelligence Recruitment
>
> A former Turning Point USA representative named Joshua Peterson ([@RagingPatriot20](https://x.com/RagingPatriot20)) came forward with a disturbing account from his time at TPUSA training. According to him, after the first few days of intense training, new hires were taken out by former representatives in rented stripper vans — complete with stripper poles inside — to bars in Arizona.
>
> Once there, the reps got the new hires drunk and began pulling people outside one by one. When it was Peterson's turn, they asked him about his views on "Our Greatest Ally" and "Our Greatest Allies Military." He says they then admitted they were part of "Our Greatest Allies" intelligence agency and revealed they had been working at Turning Point USA for the past four to five years.
>
> This is a serious allegation. If true, it suggests that foreign intelligence operatives were not only embedded inside Turning Point USA but were actively trying to recruit and vet new staff while engaging in highly unprofessional behavior with new hires.
>
> This needs to be investigated.
>
> — *Project Constitution, relaying the account of Joshua Peterson ([@RagingPatriot20](https://x.com/RagingPatriot20))*

The phrase **"Our Greatest Ally"** is a euphemism that, in this context, is widely understood by commentators to refer to Israel; the original account frames the alleged operatives as belonging to that ally's intelligence service. We note that framing as the source's interpretation, not as a proven fact.

## Why this is on the site

We have placed this account here, and on the dedicated [Joshua Peterson](./Joshua_Peterson) page, only because it is exactly the kind of claim that demands a credible, independent investigation. We are not asserting that it happened. We are asking that the [proposed disclosure and investigation laws](../../Fix/overview) be applied so that U.S. intelligence services formally determine whether any foreign-intelligence actor was interfacing with TPUSA employees — and so that any TPUSA employee falsely implicated is cleared, and any genuine wrongdoing is exposed.
