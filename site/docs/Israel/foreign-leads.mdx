# Israel Foreign Leads

Investigative leads pointing **outside the United States** — toward Israeli-based actors,
communications, travel, and SIGINT data — are tracked here. These are **leads to pursue**,
not verdicts. We do **not** claim any Israeli person or institution committed a crime.

## Blocked federal posture

The highest-priority foreign lead is whether the government **ran down** Israel-related data at all:

- [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) stated the FBI **shut down** his NCTC inquiry before
  leads were exhausted — see [FBI Foreign Leads Blocked](/FBI/Foreign_Leads).
- [Russell Brand](./Russell_Brand_Cabinet_Claims) alleges cabinet sources said **"no foreign
  involvement"** and **no domestic accomplices** six days after the shooting — unattributed,
  but consistent with Kent's public account.
- Public FBI messaging reportedly cited **foreign intelligence assisting** while Kent says
  foreign-nexus work was halted — the tension is the core question.

## Foreign-government contact timeline (claims)

A separate citizen-research thread (amplified by [@Ex_Nihilo_x](https://x.com/Ex_Nihilo_x)) points to
publicly listed diplomatic contacts on the **Egyptian Presidency** website clustering around September 10,
2025, and reads them as suspicious proximity — **not** proof of coordination:

- **Aug 20, 2025** — Egyptian President **El-Sisi** speaks with French President **Macron**.
- **Sept 8, 2025** — El-Sisi meets the **USCENTCOM** commander (two days before the shooting).
- **Sept 15, 2025** — El-Sisi joins a video conference at Macron's invitation (five days after).
- **Sept 20, 2025** — another El-Sisi/Macron call.

These are **official-calendar entries** given a hostile interpretation by commentators; the timing overlaps
the [Egyptian SU-BTT jet](/Planes/Israel-Planes) movements but establishes no operational link. Separately,
[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens) reported that after she raised an alleged **Macron** directive, only
[Tulsi Gabbard](/US_Intelligence/tulsi-gabbard)'s office replied among major executive branches.
