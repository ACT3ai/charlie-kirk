# Israelis at UVU

Alleged Israeli presence on or near the Utah Valley University campus on September 10, 2025, is one of the most contested claims in the investigation. This page summarizes what commentators have reported and flags what still needs primary sourcing.

## On-site presence claims

Public posts cited in the project describe alleged sightings of Israeli-linked individuals near UVU before, during, or after the shooting — including persons reportedly moving through the event area and exiting the campus shortly after.

Detail-level discussion lives on the dedicated claims page:

- [Alleged Israeli Presence and Connectivity at UVU (Claims)](./Israeli_Presence_And_Connectivity_Claims)

## Search-pattern and IP-based indicators

Separately, commentators point to Google Trends and search-data patterns involving Israeli IP ranges spiking around case-related figures (attorneys, officials, commentators). These are circumstantial signals of interest, not action.

- [Reported Israeli Search-Pattern and IP Activity (Claims)](./Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims)

## Evaluation notes

Presence near an event does not establish participation in it. UVU is a large public campus and the claims here require primary-source corroboration — video, records, or direct testimony — before they can move beyond allegation.
