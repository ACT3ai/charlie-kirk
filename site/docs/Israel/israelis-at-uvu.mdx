# Israelis at UVU

Alleged Israeli presence on or near the Utah Valley University campus on September 10, 2025, is one of the most contested claims in the investigation. This page summarizes what commentators have reported and flags what still needs primary sourcing.

## NSA cellphone count (claims)

The highest-traffic UVU presence claim is not a sighting but **SIGINT**: commentators allege
**12–16 Israeli-registered cellphones** were active at or near the event during the shooting window,
detected via NSA Utah (**Bumblehive**) metadata. See the dedicated page:

- [NSA Cellphones & Bumblehive (Claims)](./NSA_Cellphones_Bumblehive)

In April 2026, **four** of those alleged devices were claimed to have pinged at the
[Islamabad Serena Hotel](./Islamabad_Serena_Tracking) during US–Iran talks.

## On-site presence claims

Public posts also describe alleged **sightings** of Israeli-linked individuals near UVU — persons
reportedly moving through the event area and exiting shortly after. Detail-level discussion:

- [Alleged Israeli Presence and Connectivity at UVU (Claims)](./Israeli_Presence_And_Connectivity_Claims)

## Search-pattern and IP-based indicators

Separately, commentators point to Google Trends and search-data patterns involving Israeli IP ranges spiking around case-related figures (attorneys, officials, commentators). These are circumstantial signals of interest, not action.

- [Reported Israeli Search-Pattern and IP Activity (Claims)](./Israeli_Search_Patterns_Claims)

## Evaluation notes

Presence near an event does not establish participation in it. UVU is a large public campus and the claims here require primary-source corroboration — video, records, or direct testimony — before they can move beyond allegation. The federal review built to test exactly this kind of foreign-presence lead was cut short, according to former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent), who says his center was ordered to stop its foreign-nexus inquiry once a suspect was in custody.
