# Alleged $1 Billion Israeli Offer (Stew Peters Claim)

This page documents an allegation made by commentator [Stew Peters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stew_Peters), amplified on X by @FurkanGozukara, that Charlie Kirk reportedly rejected an alleged $1 billion offer from an Israeli source, and that funds were allegedly accepted by his organization after his death. Every assertion below is attributed to Peters or to others he cites. None of the claims have been adjudicated in court. Where the allegation names a living person, that person is not implicated in any criminal wrongdoing on this page — they are described only as the subject of Peters' reported allegations.

## Transcript of Peters' Segment

And I think that it's more likely that she was actually involved with the planning phases and helping to carry that."
>
> — Stew Peters, in the segment amplified by @FurkanGozukara on 2026-05-16

## Peters' Specific Claims

Each numbered item below is a claim made by Peters in the transcript above. They are presented as Peters' reported assertions — not as established facts. Several involve living persons whose statements or actions should be independently verified before reliance.

1. **Earlier "$150 million" rejected offer.** Peters states that Charlie Kirk reportedly rejected $150 million in funding at some earlier point. Peters does not identify the source, date, mechanism, or supporting documentation for this figure in the clip.

2. **The "$1 billion" figure.** Peters says he has "reported loosely that there was a billion dollar offer." His own qualifier ("loosely") signals that, by Peters' own framing, the $1 billion number is reported rumor rather than confirmed documentation as of the date of the segment.

3. **"Pierre" in Israel.** Peters attributes to Candace Owens the identification of "this gentleman called Pierre who is in Israel" as the alleged offeror. Peters does not provide a last name. The page [Israel Foreign Leads](./foreign-leads) tracks open foreign-source leads of this kind. Owens' own reporting on this individual — including any spelling, full name, or sourcing — should be reviewed directly before any further reliance.

4. **"Pierre" announcing Kirk's death.** Peters claims the same individual was "one of the first people to announce" that he was aware of Charlie Kirk's death. Peters does not identify the announcement (post, broadcast, message) or its timing in the clip.

5. **Acceptance of funds by Turning Point USA.** Peters claims that "all of that money immediately after Charlie's death was accepted" by Turning Point USA. As of this page's publication, [TPUSA](https://www.tpusa.com/) has not, to public knowledge, publicly confirmed, denied, or otherwise addressed the specific allegation. No filed financial disclosure has been pointed to in the public record that documents this acceptance under the framing Peters describes.

6. **Allegation about Erika Kirk.** Peters states, in his own words and explicitly framed as opinion: "it's my opinion that she at minimum had foreknowledge. And I think that it's more likely that she was actually involved with the planning phases and helping to carry that." [Erika Kirk](../People/erika-kirk) is a living person. She has not been charged with any crime in connection with the death of Charlie Kirk. No court has adjudicated this allegation. Peters' statement is his own personal opinion as he labels it. Readers should treat it accordingly and not as a finding of fact.

## What This Page Does and Does Not Assert

* This page **documents** that Stew Peters made the above statements on a video segment amplified to a large audience on X.
* This page **does not assert** that the $1 billion offer was real, that "Pierre" exists or has the characteristics Peters describes, that TPUSA accepted any such funds, or that Erika Kirk had foreknowledge of or involvement in her husband's death.
* This page **flags** the allegations as open lines of inquiry that, if true, would represent a significant motive and post-event financial pattern; and that, if false, are defamatory toward several living persons.
