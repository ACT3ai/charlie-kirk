# Israel Foreign Leads

Investigative leads pointing outside the United States — toward Israeli-based actors, communications, or travel — are tracked here. These are leads to pursue, not verdicts.

## Lead categories

- **Communications routed abroad:** alleged calls and outreach from prominent Israeli or pro-Israel figures to Charlie or his circle, including reported offers of funding or trips that Charlie is said to have declined.
- **Foreign media reactions:** Israeli outlets and officials who reacted unusually quickly to the event, per commentary aggregated in the project.
- **Cross-border travel patterns:** post-event movement of persons of interest in and out of Israel, to be cross-checked against flight records.

## What corroboration would look like

Each lead needs primary documents: phone logs, flight manifests, named witnesses, or verified communications. Until then, they remain lines of inquiry.
