# PETN — The Explosive in the Devices (Claims)

**PETN** (pentaerythritol tetranitrate) is a powerful plastic explosive, valued by militaries and — because a very small quantity is highly destructive — reportedly the material of choice for [Operation Grim Beeper](./Operation_Grim_Beeper). This page explains what PETN is and why its use in the September 2024 attack is cited in [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) capability arguments.

## What PETN Is

* PETN is among the most powerful conventional high explosives, stable enough to be shaped into small, concealable amounts yet destructive far out of proportion to its size.
* It is difficult to detect and can be molded into thin sheets or slivers — properties that reportedly allowed it to be hidden inside the battery compartment of a [pager](./Beepers) without altering the device's normal appearance or function.
* It has a documented history in militant and intelligence operations, which is part of why the [Mossad](./Mossad) attribution was seen as plausible by analysts.

## How It Was Reportedly Used

According to reporting on the 2024 operation and summarized by **@SwordTruth** on X (July 10, 2026), Mossad "embed[ded] PETN plastic explosives and flammable detonators inside the devices' batteries" of pagers tied to the Gold Apollo supply chain. When triggered on **September 17-18, 2024**, the concealed PETN produced thousands of small, precise, near-simultaneous blasts against [Hezbollah](./Hezbollah) users across Lebanon and Syria.

## The Reference in the Kirk Case

The material is cited to make a **precision-and-concealment** point: a service able to hide, distribute, and remotely trigger PETN across an entire hostile network has capabilities that dwarf a single conventional killing. That is the argument, not evidence — no PETN, tampered device, or explosive of any kind has been alleged in Kirk's death, and the physical case turns on the [ballistics](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and weapon questions examined elsewhere on the site. This page is background context for the [Israel / Mossad theory](/Killer/israel-mossad), not a claim of explosive involvement at UVU.
