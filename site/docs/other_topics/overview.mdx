# Other Topics

## Overview

**Other Topics** is the overflow shelf for investigation threads that matter but do not cleanly own a single Level 2 home — or that bridge multiple sections. Use it as a **routing page**, not as a dumping ground that replaces [Topics](/Topics) or dedicated sections like [CoverUp](/CoverUp/overview), [Theories](/Theories/overview), and [gov](/gov/overview).

## Key Areas

- Cross-cutting anomalies (scene alteration after release, parallel explosions, distraction figures)
- Threads that span medical + legal + political pages
- Supplementary OSINT that has not yet earned a full Level 3 home
- Pointers into deeper trees when readers arrive from search
