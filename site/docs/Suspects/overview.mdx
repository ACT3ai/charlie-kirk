do not match the official [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) narrative. Start with the
[investigation index](./Suspects_Investigation_Index).

The strongest **documented mismatch** with the FBI story is the black-clad suspect: police dispatch broadcast a
male in all black with a tactical helmet and mask while [Dylan Hope](/People/dylan-hope), a construction worker at **785 College Dr**,
Orem, independently described a trench-coated figure with greasy hair who crossed the site before sirens sounded.
Pre-shooting rooftop video shows a prone all-black figure; the sheriff photo shown to workers reportedly did not
match the FBI's public release. **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** (April 2026) repackaged that dispatch/Hope/roof-video cluster for a large audience, arguing the FBI public package abandoned the original description — an **investigation-process claim**, not a court ruling. That thread is developed on [Black Clothing Suspect](./Black_Clothing_Suspect)
and [FBI Black Clothing Photo](/FBI/Black_Clothing_Photo).

Parallel **location and vehicle** theories argue the fatal event did not require a lone rooftop sniper at 150 yards.
[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) traced an IR laser on [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s phone to a grey van under the
tent tunnel; [@jonaaronbray](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) and [@troofevades](/Influencers/x/troofevades) published
acoustic analyses placing the crack much closer to Charlie than Losee-rooftop geometry allows. Published ballistics
commentary questions whether a **5–9°** downward shot from Losee would match observed neck/chest wounds.
[Possible Shooter Locations](./Shooting_Locations) and [Grey Van Shooter POI](./Grey_Van_Shooter) hold those lanes.
Readers should keep the **official Losee rooftop charging theory** visible beside these alternatives; this section maps claims, it does not pick a winner.

**Persons of interest** near the stage and SUV — including [Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) ("arm puller" / viral "palm gun" claims on X) and social-media
allegations about grey-van occupants ([Van Man Bednarz](./Van_Man_Bednarz)) — are documented as reported allegations, with attribution and known counterpoints. Palm-gun and second-shooter threads (e.g. high-engagement October 2025 Project Constitution posts) are **unverified video interpretations**; only Robinson is charged in the public case discussed here. We attribute allegations; we
do not assert any living person committed the assassination. Whether anyone beyond Robinson was ever seriously
examined is itself contested: former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record
that his center's parallel search for other actors — specifically foreign ties — was ordered to stop once
Robinson was in custody. New readers:
**[Investigation Index](./Suspects_Investigation_Index)** → **[Black Clothing Suspect](./Black_Clothing_Suspect)** →
**[Grey Van Shooter](./Grey_Van_Shooter)** → **[Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview)**.
