### Unverified crowd reports (checklist items)

The master investigation checklist records an **unidentified man running in the crowd with a pistol** immediately
after the shot — alongside claims that police arrested a separate figure yelling "shoot me!" while the "actual
shooter" fled. No mainstream outlet has confirmed those details as of this writing; they are documented as
**reported checklist items**, not adjudicated facts. Deeper witness threading lives under [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).

## FBI pivot narrative (commentary)

Reporting compiled in the investigation file alleges:

- The black-clad lead was **abandoned** when the FBI took over ([@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/2045871393611395289), April 2026).
- [@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO): rooftop filmmaker says outfit and gun **do not match** Tyler Robinson.
- Ballistic fragments reportedly **too fragmented** to match Tyler's rifle; **multiple prints** on recovered gun.
- Tyler reportedly **did not confess** — arrest traced to father's contact with a "sheriff friend."
- "Drop off gun at neighboring house" patsy theory vs "Discord fan-fiction" script framing.
- [@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO) commentary: early witnesses who testified on camera described a **chest**
  hit — including **Tiffany Barker** (Phil Lyman campaign) — while visible wound analysis pointed elsewhere; treated
  as organized narrative suspicion, not proof.
- Master-file commentary: FBI Director **Kash Patel** allegedly withholding ATF/FBI discovery while prosecutors push
  the preliminary hearing forward — see [discovery disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes).

These are **reported allegations**, not adjudicated findings.
