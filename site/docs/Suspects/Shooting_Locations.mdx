# Possible Shooter Locations

Citizen investigators test **where** the fatal shot originated using ballistics, acoustics, maps, and affidavit
timelines. This page summarizes proposed locations; it does **not** assert which is correct.

## Official rooftop — Losee Center (affidavit timeline)

Charging documents allege Tyler Robinson fired from the **Losee Center rooftop** (~150 yards from the courtyard).
The affidavit timeline summarized in the master file includes:

| Time (MT) | Alleged action |
|-----------|----------------|
| 11:52 | Suspect tracked toward Losee; limping gait |
| 12:15 | Accessed roof via southeast stairs / walkway |
| 12:23 | Single shot; suspect runs to NE corner |
| 12:24 | Rifle placed on roof; descent to grass; flee to wooded area |

Surveillance stills from **12:23:29** through **12:23:54** map movement from the shooting position to the
northeast corner in roughly **25 seconds** (per X analysis in the investigation file).

### Losee angle problem (ballistics)

Ballistics commentary in the master file argues that if the shot came from Losee, the downward angle is roughly
**5–9 degrees** — which would likely enter through the **face or jaw**, not the neck/chest path described in
wound analysis. See [Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) and [Medical ballistics](/Medical/ballistics_and_wound_analysis).
That geometric tension is a core reason alternative locations remain active.

### Roof video clothing mismatch

Pre- and post-shot rooftop footage is widely discussed as showing **all-black** clothing inconsistent with Tyler
Robinson's reported T-shirt and jeans. The rooftop filmmaker cited by [@RealCandaceO](https://x.com/RealCandaceO)
reportedly states Tyler is **not** the person on the roof. Identity mismatch details live on
[Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) and [Stairs Guy](/Tyler_Robinson/Stairs_Guy) — not
duplicated here.

### "Ran before shot" — rooftop runner timing (commentary)

Commentary in the master file alleges the public rooftop-runner clip is **trimmed** to begin at **12:23:34 PM** —
after the runner had already covered roughly **60 feet** from the perch position. Frame analysis cited in the file
argues:

| Timestamp (MT) | Alleged event |
|----------------|---------------|
| 12:23:28 | Runner begins full sprint on roof |
| 12:23:30 | Charlie Kirk struck |
| 12:23:34 | Released clip starts — runner ~220 ft into ~15 sec run |

If accurate, a rooftop shooter could not have fired from the perch at impact and **then** begun running — the
runner would have had to move **before** the shot. Commentators tie this to claims that Utah County DA Jeffrey Gray
and UVU are withholding fuller rooftop-view files. This is **investigator arithmetic**, not a court finding; see
[court discovery disputes](/court/discovery-brady-disputes) for the official record posture.

Sections such as [Tyler Robinson](/Tyler_Robinson/overview), [Timeline](/Timeline/overview), and [Maps](/maps/overview)
hold additional affidavit context.

## Tent‑adjacent or interior shooter (claims)

One major alternative theory suggests the fatal shot came from **inside or immediately adjacent to the tent**:

- Acoustic and timing analyses are interpreted by some as pointing to a closer, lower‑angle shot.
- Video and still images are examined for possible reflections, flashes, or casing behavior near the tent fabric and equipment.
- The tent’s structure and the nearby tunnel and courtyard features are used to argue for specific concealed positions.

These ideas are developed further in the [Tent](/Tent/overview), [Killer](/Killer/overview), and [Videos](/Videos/overview) sections and remain contested.

## Vehicle‑based shooter — grey van under tent tunnel

[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) argues an **infrared laser** on [@DrFrankTurek](https://x.com/DrFrankTurek)'s
phone at impact traced to a **grey van** parked behind and right of Charlie, under the tent tunnel. Reported elements:

- Rear hatch opens ~**4 seconds** before the shot, closes immediately after ([@HustleBitch_](https://x.com/HustleBitch_)).
- Fleeting cargo-area figure: light shirt, bald/short hair, prominent ears, gloves.
- **4940 Hz** frequency with left-channel dominance on the nearest camera ([@jonaaronbray](https://x.com/jonaaronbray)).
- Mach-cone analysis allegedly places the acoustic origin closer to van-side mics than front-row mics.

Full POI framing is on [Grey Van Shooter](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter). [@CumbyWilliam](https://x.com/CumbyWilliam)
posted separate identification allegations about van-rear occupants on [Van Man Bednarz](/Suspects/Van_Man_Bednarz).

## Acoustics — same-time crack and impact

[@troofevades](https://x.com/troofevades/status/2071072788132118610) argues video-audio sync places the audible
crack and visible impact in the **same instant** — implying a source much **closer than 150 yards**, where rifle
sound delay would separate bang from reaction. Commentary places plausible origin within about **two meters** of
Charlie in the courtyard.

Tent-adjacent theories also cite **crack-thump** patterns and canopy absorption — overlapping
[Mic](/Mic/overview) and [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories).

## Other elevated positions (claims)

Additional proposed locations include:

- **Balconies or stairwells** on nearby buildings that offer alternate angles to the stage and crowd, including the South Stairs area.
- Potential **faculty or restricted‑access areas** that might provide partial concealment and lines of sight not visible from the main audience perspective.

Maps and 3D reconstructions are especially important for evaluating whether these vantage points match observed wound angles, timing, and video artifacts.

## 3D reconstruction — sound vs. distance

When investigators model the courtyard in 3D, a recurring claim is that **synchronized** audio-video on crowd phones
rules out distant rooftop origin unless secondary sound effects (speakers, staged crack) explain the match. That
logic feeds both **inside-tent device** theories and **van/tunnel** theories rather than a single conclusion.

## Construction site escape path (dual theory)

Parallel to the Losee rooftop account, [Black Clothing Suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) documents a
black-clad figure crossing the **785 College Dr** construction site immediately after the shot — the same path
the FBI later tied to the official suspect's escape. Some commentators treat rooftop and construction-site threads
as **competing** origin stories; others argue they describe **different actors** in a coordinated event.

## Drone and advanced‑weapon theories (claims)

Google Trends notes in the investigation file flag **UVU Rooftop/Gun/Parking** searches in **May–July 2025** as
possible scouting activity — contested interpretation. Drone deployment zones and advanced-weapon theories argue
rooftop geometry alone cannot explain wound path, casing behavior, or laser sightings. See [Drones](/Drones/overview)
and [Proof Intel Services](/Proof_Intel_Services/overview).

## Campus scout claims (May–July 2025)

Investigation file entries list elevated search interest for UVU layout, Losee Center, parking, and CCTV terminology
in the months before the event. Official confirmation of pre-event surveillance is **not** established here; the
pattern is documented as citizen-search evidence only.
