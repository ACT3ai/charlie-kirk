# Van Man — Dustin Bednarz Allegations

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Full Name | Dustin Brett Bednarz (per X thread) |
| Status | **Alive** |
| Connection to CK Case | Allegedly at rear of grey van 9/10/2025 per [@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) |
| Evidence Rating | SPECULATIVE (social-media identification only) |

[@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) posted a genealogy-style thread asking why
[@BlakeBednarz](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) had not addressed claims that Bednarz family members were at the
**rear of the grey van** on September 10, 2025. This page documents those **allegations** in the context of the
[Grey Van Shooter](./Grey_Van_Shooter) laser POI theory. We do **not** assert Dustin Bednarz fired a weapon,
operated a laser, or knew of any plot to harm [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).

## What the X thread alleged

From the investigation file summary of [@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)'s posts:

- **"Van man"** — the thread alleges this figure was **Dustin Brett Bednarz** (citing double earrings in compared photos). That identification is the poster's allegation, not an established fact.
- **"Van boy"** — the thread alleges this figure was the eldest son of Dustin Bednarz and Kacey Welch Bednarz (since divorced). Also unverified.
- Family photos (~7 years old in posts) used for visual comparison.
- [@BlakeBednarz](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) tagged as brother/nephew connection to the van scene.

These are **crowd-sourced identifications**. No mainstream outlet or court document in the public record has
confirmed the names as van occupants as of this writing.

## Relationship to grey van laser theory

The Bednarz thread sits downstream of Project Constitution's claim that an IR laser on [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s phone traced
to a grey van under the tent tunnel, with a bald/light-shirt POI in the cargo area at impact. The CumbyWilliam
identification **does not match** that descriptive profile (light shirt, bald, prominent ears). Readers should treat
the threads as **parallel hypotheses** until vehicle logs or law-enforcement IDs reconcile them.

See [Grey Van Shooter POI](./Grey_Van_Shooter) for laser, hatch-timing, and 4940 Hz acoustic claims.

## Blake Bednarz — separate UVU presence claims

The master file also notes online claims that **Douglas "Blake" Bednarz** was at UVU on 9/10 (distinct from the
van-rear thread). [Blake Bednarz](/People/blake-bednarz) is discussed in private research under `Details/Dustin_Bednarz/` and in
[video evidence](/Videos/overview) contexts. We do not merge Blake and Dustin allegations into a single narrative
on the public site.

## Responsible investigation framing

Project Constitution explicitly asks tipsters to email `projectconstitutionvideos@gmail.com` or DM on X — **not**
to harass or dox individuals. The public site follows the same rule:

- Allegations are **attributed** to named X accounts.
- We **do not** publish home addresses, phone numbers, or "guilty" conclusions.
- FOIA / law-enforcement tips are the appropriate channel if a reader has **verified** footage or documents.

## Key questions

* Was the grey van registered, and to whom, for UVU event parking?
* Does any official evidence place named Bednarz family members in the tunnel area at 12:23 PM MT?
* Does the cargo-area POI description match or contradict social-media photo comparisons?
* Has any named individual publicly denied presence on campus that day?
