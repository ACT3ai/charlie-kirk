# Grey Van Shooter — Laser POI

[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) and collaborators argue an **infrared laser** visible on
[@DrFrankTurek](/Influencers/x/drfrankturek)'s phone screen at the moment of impact traced back to a **grey van**
parked behind and to the right of [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) — tucked under the tent tunnel and partially obscured by the canopy.
This page documents that **person-of-interest** theory. We do **not** assert any individual in or near the van
committed a crime or had foreknowledge of the assassination.

## Laser on [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek)'s phone

According to Project Constitution's frame-by-frame analysis (with 4K footage credited to
[@JG_CSTT](/Influencers/x/jg-cstt) and [@ckllr801](https://x.com/ckllr801)):

- Infrared lasers travel in straight lines; reverse-engineering the reflection angle on Turek's phone screen
  pointed investigators toward a fixed origin.
- The enhanced reflection allegedly shows the beam emanating from inside a grey van at Charlie's rear-right,
  under the tunnel structure.
- At the instant the laser winked out, footage allegedly shows a **fleeting figure** in the rear cargo area:
  light-colored shirt, bald or very short hair, prominent ears, gloves.

Project Constitution states this is **not an accusation** — the individual is a person of interest for tips only
(`projectconstitutionvideos@gmail.com`). We reproduce that framing on the public site.

## Hatch timing sequence

[@HustleBitch_](/Influencers/x/hustlebitch) posted a breakdown alleging:

- The van's **rear hatch opens approximately four seconds before** the shot.
- The hatch **slams shut immediately after** the crack echoes.

Commentators interpret that sequence as inconsistent with ordinary parking behavior at a public event. Alternative
explanations (equipment loading, ventilation, unrelated timing) have not been ruled out in official statements.

## Acoustic triangulation — 4940 Hz

[@jonaaronbray](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) (April 2026) analyzed audio from [@ckllr801](https://x.com/ckllr801)'s
near-van camera after [@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) shared [Lookout for Charlie](https://youtube.com/@lookoutfacharlie)
footage. Reported findings include:

- A **4940 Hz** frequency peak with **left-channel dominance** on the camera closest to the van.
- A **mach cone** pattern allegedly closer to van-side microphones than front-row crowd cameras.

See [Possible Shooter Locations](./Shooting_Locations) for how acoustics interact with rooftop and tent theories.
[@troofevades](https://x.com/troofevades/status/2071072788132118610) separately argued shot placement within roughly
**two meters** of Charlie in the courtyard — a much closer origin than a 150-yard rooftop rifle would imply for sound delay.

## Suspect profile (POI only)

From phone reflection and cross-referenced event footage, Project Constitution published a **descriptive profile**
(not an identity assertion):

| Trait | Reported |
|-------|----------|
| Shirt | Light color |
| Hair | Bald or very short |
| Ears | Prominent |
| Hands | Gloves (noted in some frames) |
| Position | Rear cargo area of grey van at impact instant |

A separate man was reportedly filmed loitering beside the van in tunnel shadows. Identity remains **unconfirmed**
in mainstream reporting.

## CornerShot / IR scope context

The master investigation file links IR laser sightings to **CornerShot**-style weapon theories (hinged barrel,
screen aim, downward ejection). That overlaps [Tent shooter theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories) and
[@stevendenoon](/Influencers/x/stevendenoon)'s YouTube breakdown. We document the overlap; we do not assert a
specific weapon was deployed from the van.

## Van rear occupants — separate thread

[@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william) posted a genealogy thread alleging **Dustin Brett Bednarz** and
family members at the rear of the grey van on 9/10/2025. That identification thread is documented on
[Van Man Bednarz](./Van_Man_Bednarz) — again as **allegations requiring verification**, not adjudicated fact.

These are **OSINT acoustic opinions**; independent peer review is not established on this page.
- **[@CumbyWilliam](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)** genealogy posts naming **Dustin Brett Bednarz** at the van rear are documented on [Van Man Bednarz](./Van_Man_Bednarz) as **allegations requiring verification** — not site findings of criminal guilt.

## Official Narrative vs Competing Claims

| Element | Official-path framing | Competing grey-van framing |
|---------|----------------------|----------------------------|
| Shot origin | Losee rooftop prone sniper | Cargo / hatch / tunnel under tent |
| IR laser | Artifact / unrelated / not charging basis | Aiming or signaling device from van area |

| Occupants | Not part of charging package | Social IDs (e.g. Bednarz thread) — **unverified** |

No court has found that a grey-van occupant fired the fatal shot. The charged defendant remains **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** under the official case (charges ≠ conviction).
