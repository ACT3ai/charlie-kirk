# Rick Cutler — Claims Raised by Citizen Investigators

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page is a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or
participation in any crime by any living person. This site documents claims
and questions raised in public commentary — not findings of fact. All persons
and organizations named are presumed innocent, and the allegations referenced
here are unproven and have not been established in any court.
:::

**No law-enforcement agency has publicly named Rick Cutler a person of interest,
a suspect, or a subject in this case.** The "person of interest" framing below
originates entirely with citizen investigators and social-media commentary.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Full Name | Rick Cutler (also misspelled "Cutlery" in early posts) |
| Status | **Alive** |
| Connection to CK Case | Reported to have been private security in the SUV with Kirk; "arm puller" / setup claims circulated on social media (unverified) |
| Evidence Rating | EMERGING (presence reported; conspiracy claims unverified and unadopted here) |

[Rick Cutler](/People/rick-cutler) was reportedly on [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s security detail and in the **SUV** used for emergency transport
immediately after the shooting. Commentary in the investigation file also labels him the **"arm puller"** — a man
in a brown shirt and sunglasses who allegedly adjusted stage fabric and camera equipment on Charlie's left side
before the shot. This page documents **reported roles and POI framing only**. We do **not** claim Rick Cutler
knew of the assassination in advance, detonated any device, or committed any crime.

## Documented presence — SUV group

According to the master file and public security accounts, five people were in the SUV with Kirk after the shot:

- [Dan Flood](/People/dan-flood) ([TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) security director)
- [Brian Harpole](/People/brian-harpole) (security chief)
- **Rick Cutler** (private security operator)
- [Justin Davis](/People/justin-davis) (emergency driver)
- [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) (Kirk mentor)

Cutler reportedly **held Brian Harpole's shirt** during high-speed transport so Harpole could administer first aid
without falling from the vehicle. That account comes from Harpole's public interviews — a logistical detail, not an
accusation.

## "Arm puller" — setup and camera claims

Early investigator notes in `Charlie_Kirk.txt` describe:

- A man in a **brown shirt and sunglasses** addressed a **mechanical issue on the left side** of the setup before shooting.
- The same figure allegedly **took down a camera behind Charlie** prior to the event segment where Kirk was struck.
- The name was initially guessed as "Rick Cutlery" then corrected to **Rick Cutler**.
- A referenced YouTube clip: [Tx84ViFZkaA](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tx84ViFZkaA)

These are **citizen-investigator observations**, not charges or court findings.

## Unverified social-media accusations (not published as fact)

Some X posts — including annotated SUV stills by [@DanCampbel44604](/Influencers/x/dancampbel44604) quoted by
[@RealTruthJustic](/Influencers/x/realtruthjustic) — made extreme accusations against named SUV occupants (shaped
charge, strangulation, etc.). The master file records that text for research but **explicitly states it was NOT
published to the public website** because it is unverified. This page does **not** repeat those accusations.

Separately, posts speculated about a **Pero wearable gesture mouse** device on Cutler's hand. That remains
**unconfirmed** speculation.

## Defense-witness commentary

Commentary in the investigation file argues [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s defense should subpoena SUV occupants including
Cutler for timeline testimony — transport decisions, Kirk's condition, and observations during the immediate
aftermath. That is investigative opinion, not a filed motion.

## Distinction — Laine Schoneberger

Square-sunglasses / dark-blue-shirt theories about **the man at the table with Charlie** often name [**Laine Schoneberger**](/People/laine-schoneberger) (Yrefy sponsor).
That thread is **separate** from Cutler and is covered under [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview) and sponsor-angle
pages. We do not merge unrelated POI descriptions into a single suspect claim.

## Key questions

* What was Cutler's official assignment and sight line during the Q&A segment?
* Did any TPUSA or Integrity Security roster place him on stage left vs SUV standby?
* Has Cutler given a sworn statement matching or contradicting "arm puller" social posts?
* Will defense discovery include SUV interior video or radio logs?

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (high-defamation caution)

:::caution Legal Disclaimer
Nothing on this page constitutes a finding of wrongdoing, criminal conduct, or participation in any crime by Rick Cutler or any other living person. Claims below are attributed public commentary — unproven and not established in any court.
:::

Viral X threads have escalated Cutler from **SUV security POI** into **second-shooter** theories. This site records those claims **only as speech about him**, not as findings:

- **[@grok](/Influencers/x/grok)**-summarized background (July 2026 circulation): Cutler is a **retired Irving, TX officer** (~30 years, SWAT), former **Army MP**, later **Operation Underground Railroad** / disaster-relief work, and **volunteered** on Kirk’s private detail. After the shot he helped carry Kirk and **held Brian Harpole’s shirt** steady during first aid in the van. Only **Tyler Robinson** is charged; **no official ballistic link** to Cutler is cited in that summary.
- **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** (October 2025) posted highly charged claims of a **scrubbed digital footprint**, “CIA puppet” framing, and coordinated security-team complicity. Those are **speculative influencer claims** with no court support.
- A later **[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)** video (October 17, 2025, high engagement) alleged Cutler **concealed a barrel / “palm gun”** and timed a hand motion to the shot, citing collaboration with **[@WeAreNotGTM](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)** and viral Jimmy Dore–adjacent clip interpretations. **Unverified**; video-interpretation theories are not forensic lab results.
- Master-file research also records even more extreme unverified accusations (e.g. device-under-hand / transport theories). Those remain **research-only rumors** and are **not adopted** here as facts.
