# Ballistics and Surveillance Claims

Investigators disagree about **where the fatal shot originated**, **what weapon was used**, and **what aerial surveillance was active** at UVU on September 10, 2025. This page separates the **prosecution narrative** from **alternative technical claims**.

## Official Ballistics Narrative (Prosecution)

According to charges and mainstream reporting summarized in this project:

- **Weapon**: .308 / .30-06 class bolt-action rifle with scope (often described as a Mauser Model 98 variant)
- **Position**: Rooftop of the **Losee Center**, roughly **150 yards** from the stage
- **Wound**: Throat/neck entry; Kirk collapsed immediately
- **Evidence**: Rifle allegedly left at scene; ballistics match cited in probable-cause materials
- **Digital forensics**: Alleged searches for "how to snipe" and Kirk's schedule on suspect devices

Full autopsy and complete ballistics reports have **not** been publicly released. Warrants and forensic packets are reported **sealed until March 2026**.

## Alternative Ballistics and Acoustic Claims

Independent analysts have raised these **unverified** technical objections:

| Claim | Proponent / basis | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Shot from **south** at a **steeper down-angle** than rooftop geometry allows | Social-media ballistics threads, [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations) | Disputed; no public autopsy |
| **Suppressor/acoustic analysis** suggests shooter within ~36 feet, not 150 yards | Audio analyses shared online | Not independently validated |
| **Tent-based or close-range** shot — bang from behind crowd, no visible distant muzzle flash | [@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880), [Tent](/UVU/Tent) theories | Conflicts with FBI rooftop video release |
| **Israeli CornerShot / ARCAS** specialty-weapon claims | Conspiracy compilations | No physical evidence publicly shown |

Readers should compare these claims against the [FBI rooftop video](https://www.fbi.gov/video-repository/utah-valley-shooting-video-091025.mp4/view) and [Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview) section.

## Drone and Aerial Surveillance Claims

### Witness-Reported Drones

Some attendee and analyst posts describe **multiple drones** over or near the courtyard during the event — counts vary (commonly cited figures include **16** in older compilations). Phone-camera captures are referenced in social threads; a definitive inventory has not been released by authorities.

### N1098L (U.S. Army HADES Program)

Open-source flight data shows **[N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** — a Bombardier Global 6500 operated under the Army **HADES** contractor arrangement — making **two very low passes** near UVU on September 10:

- **9:16 a.m. MDT** — descent to ~203 ft AGL at ~113–118 knots
- **12:48 p.m. MDT** — ~335 ft AGL, ~25 minutes after the shooting

Analysts **speculate** these passes correlate with drone deploy/retrieve operations. The aircraft's mission documentation does not publicly confirm activity over the Kirk event. See [Planes/N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and [Drones](/Drones/overview).

### Egyptian Aircraft at Provo

**[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT)** departed Provo at **7:08 a.m.**; **[SU-BND](/Planes/Egyptian-Armada)** activated its transponder at **11:59 a.m.** Investigators debate whether timing is coincidental maintenance traffic or intelligence-adjacent staging. No government filing has confirmed operational intent.

## Surveillance Gaps on the Ground

Reporting on UVU GRAMA records and Fox News photography raises separate questions:

- **Six campus police officers** for ~3,000 attendees (per @danksterintel GRAMA review cited in [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings))
- **Rooftop access** to the Losee Center reportedly obtainable without detection in post-event photos ([Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/us/charlie-kirk-assassination-exclusive-photos-show-path-campus-rooftop-weeks-later-access-scrutinized))
- Questions about which **TPUSA, UVU, and FBI-held cameras** captured the courtyard and whether footage has been fully released
