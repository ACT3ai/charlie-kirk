# Pre-Assassination Context: September 3–9, 2025

In the week before the UVU event, public reporting and project timelines describe a cluster of policy disputes, donor-pressure meetings, and social-media signals that investigators treat as background — not proof of who killed Kirk.

## September 3: Epstein Files Demand

On **September 3, 2025**, Kirk reportedly called for the **unredacted release of Jeffrey Epstein's files** during a podcast appearance, criticizing what he described as elite cover-ups involving powerful figures. This shift aligned with a broader America First critique of establishment networks. [Source: project timeline compiled from public commentary and reporting; see [Charlie Kirk Timeline](/Timeline/Charlie_Kirk).]

## September 8: Public Israel-Policy Signal

On **September 8, 2025**, Kirk posted on X about a forthcoming statement on **leaving the pro-Israel movement** — a public signal that his long-standing pro-Israel alignment was changing. [Source: [Timeline/2025](/Timeline/2025), [Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).]

## September 9: High-Pressure Meeting

Multiple accounts describe a **combative meeting on September 9, 2025** with pro-Israel figures, including (as named in public materials) [Rabbi Pesach Wolicki](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), [Seth Dillon](/People/seth-dillon), [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), and [Natasha Hausdorff](/People/natasha-hausdorff). Commentators have described the session as an "intervention" over Kirk's shifting rhetoric.

According to reporting compiled in this project, Kirk **reportedly texted associates during the meeting** with language along the lines of *"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME."* This quote appears in secondary sources and leaked-text compilations; it has not been independently authenticated here. [Source: [Timeline/Sept_8_to_13](/Timeline/Sept_8_to_13), [Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025).]

## September 9: UVU Warning Attempts (Same Day)

Separate from Kirk's meetings, **UVU GRAMA records** document two pre-event warning attempts on September 9 — two men asking to speak with police about "a plan" at 10:35 PM, and an undelivered message for Kirk at 2:23 PM. See [Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings) for the documented record.

## Kirk's Reported Policy Shifts (Context)

Public materials in this project also reference these reported changes in the months before September 10:

- Mild criticism of **Israeli influence in U.S. politics** after years of pro-Israel advocacy
- A reported **refusal of a Netanyahu-linked funding offer** for [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) (claim; not verified by primary contract)
- Continued platforming of Israel-skeptic voices, including the **Dave Smith debate** at the July 2025 TPUSA Student Action Summit

These items appear frequently in motive theories; they establish **context**, not culpability.
