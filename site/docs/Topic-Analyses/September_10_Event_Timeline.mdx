### Official Suspect Narrative (Charged, Not Adjudicated)

Mainstream reporting and charging documents allege:

- **Tyler Robinson**, 22, a UVU student, fired from a **rooftop of the Losee Center** (~150 yards from the courtyard) with a **.30-06 Springfield Mauser Model 98** bolt-action rifle mounted with a scope
- Charging materials describe movement tracked **~11:52 a.m.** through stairwells and up to the Losee Center roof in dark clothing, a dark item (rifle wrapped in a **dark-colored towel**) placed on the roof at **~12:24 p.m.**, the figure lowering to the grass and fleeing **north** across Campus Drive into a wooded area, and a **shoe impression consistent with Converse/Chuck Taylor** sneakers; the Mauser was recovered in the woods
- FBI released video allegedly showing a figure climbing to a rooftop, firing, jumping down, and fleeing — leaving a rifle behind ([FBI video repository](https://www.fbi.gov/video-repository/utah-valley-shooting-video-091025.mp4/view))
- Robinson was detained in **less than 36 hours**; prosecutors have charged capital murder and related counts

Robinson has **not been convicted** at the time of this writing. Treat all prosecution allegations as pending.

## Campus and Law-Enforcement Response

- **UVU** closed campus, canceled classes, and instructed shelter-in-place ([@UVU](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965868138228068793))
- **Utah Governor Spencer Cox** described the incident as a **"political assassination"** at a press conference ([ABC News](https://abcnews.go.com/US/charlie-kirk-shot-event-utah-university-jd-vance/story?id=125451514))
- FBI offered a **$100,000 reward** during the manhunt ([CBS News live updates](https://www.cbsnews.com/live-updates/charlie-kirk-shot-utah-turning-point-usa/))
- UVU shared the FBI digital media tip line ([@UVU](https://x.com/UVU/status/1965969976184988136))

## Alternative Timing Claims

Some independent analysts dispute elements of the official sequence — for example, **eyewitness posts** describing a loud bang from behind the crowd and theories that the shot originated **inside or behind the tent** rather than a distant rooftop ([@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu/status/1977209967485546880)). These remain **unverified claims**; see [Ballistics and Surveillance Claims](./Ballistics_And_Surveillance_Claims) and [Shooting Locations](/Suspects/Shooting_Locations).
