# Suspects (List)

## Overview
This directory contains a comprehensive list and analysis of all suspects related to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) incident.

## Goal
To compile and analyze the alternative-suspect theories that citizen investigators have raised beyond [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) — theories naming [CIA](/US_Intelligence/cia) rogue factions, Israeli intelligence operatives, Egyptian intelligence agents, and people internal to [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview). These are unproven allegations advanced in public commentary, not findings, and nothing here asserts that TPUSA or any person named on this site orchestrated the assassination. The line of argument these commentators pursue is that Tyler Robinson was a patsy, and that international aircraft, drone technology, and intelligence coordination point to other perpetrators.
