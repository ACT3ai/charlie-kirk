## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* Geofence and cell-tower dumps for UVU and the residences and flight manifests for the military-linked planes and FBI Form 302 reports of the witness interviews are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.

## What Citizen Investigators Are Tracking Across These Subtopics

Topics3 is a parallel hub into the same investigation themes that dominate X discourse after
September 10, 2025. High-signal threads that map onto the subdirectories above include:

- **FBI / intel scope** — According to [@RealAlexJones](/Influencers/podcasts-alex-jones) (Oct 30, 2025) and [@kangminlee](/Influencers/x/kangminlee), public
  commentary claims FBI Director [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) limited foreign/domestic accomplice inquiries that
  DNI Tulsi Gabbard’s circle and NCTC’s Joe Kent wanted open. These are **attributed political
  claims**, not court findings. See the primary [FBI](/FBI/overview) and
  [U.S. Intelligence](/US_Intelligence/overview) sections.
- **UVU records & paving** — [@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker) and [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution) amplify GRAMA texts arguing
  courtyard paving was **on-the-fly after the hit**, not a pre-scheduled job. Counter-posts argue
  biohazard/trauma cleanup after forensics. See [UVU Pavers](/UVU/Pavers).
- **Audio / cameras** — [@jonaaronbray](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)’s Canon XA55 analysis claims three separated low-frequency
  events including a stage-area impulse distinct from a distant muzzle blast. See
  [Canon XA55](/cameras/Canon_XA55_Broadcast).
- **Witness wound narrative** — [@BasedSamParker](/Influencers/x/based-sam-parker) (Jun 2026), citing [@CharlotteLee_88](/Influencers/x/charlottelee-88), documents
  chest-then-neck account shifts and the Cooper Brown / Jeb Jacobi confrontation. See
  [Witnesses](/Witnesses/overview).

Use Topics3 as a **routing layer**; deep evidence lives in the primary Level 2 sections linked
from each card.
