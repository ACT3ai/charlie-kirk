# South Stairs

## Overview
This directory contains specific information about the South Stairs location in relation to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) incident.

## Goal
To investigate the South Stairs as a potential staging area or evidence location that may reveal the true method of Charlie Kirk's assassination, including possible drone deployment zones, advanced weapon placement, or intelligence operatives' access points. This includes analyzing physical evidence, witness accounts, and security footage to prove that the assassination involved sophisticated technology and coordination beyond [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)'s capabilities.

## Key Areas
- Location details
- Evidence found
- Witness accounts
- Security footage
- Physical analysis
