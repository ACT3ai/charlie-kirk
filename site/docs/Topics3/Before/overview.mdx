# Before

## Overview
This directory contains information about events and circumstances that occurred before the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) incident.

## Goal
To investigate pre-assassination events that citizen investigators argue show planning and intelligence coordination behind Charlie Kirk's assassination, including his criticism of Israeli influence, the arrival of Israel/Egyptian aircraft, Google searches from Israeli IP addresses, and the deployment of surveillance equipment. This includes documenting the warning signs and preparatory activities those commentators cite in arguing the assassination was orchestrated rather than a spontaneous act by [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson). These are unproven claims drawn from public commentary, not established findings.

## Key Areas

- Background information
- Preparatory activities

- Historical context
