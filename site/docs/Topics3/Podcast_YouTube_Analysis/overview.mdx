# Podcast YouTube Analysis

## Overview
This directory contains analysis of podcasts and YouTube content related to the [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) incident.

## Goal
To analyze podcasts and YouTube content and catalogue the sources, evidence, and analysis that commentators cite in support of the theory that Charlie Kirk's assassination was orchestrated by intelligence agencies rather than by [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) alone. That theory is unproven. This includes fact-checking alternative media coverage, analyzing citizen investigators' claims, and documenting material those commentators say mainstream outlets have declined to cover. Characterizations of suppression are their arguments, not findings of this site.

## Key Areas
- Podcast discussions
- YouTube videos
- Content analysis
- Fact-checking
- Commentary review
