# Witness Reliability Issues

Eyewitness accounts are central to the UVU shooting narrative — and also the source of **major timeline conflicts**. This page explains **reliability factors**, not accusations of perjury against any named witness.

## Documented discrepancy themes

See [Witness Testimony Discrepancies](/Timeline/witness-testimony-discrepancies) for:

- **12:23 vs 12:27 PM** shot-time splits
- **Suspect sighting** timestamp ambiguities
- Early **media compilations** vs later police-audio reconstructions

## Process allegations affecting testimony weight

Public commentary (not fully proven in open docket) claims:

- **FBI Form 302** summaries may not match public statements
- Agents allegedly **asked witnesses to delete video** ([FBI Asked Delete Video](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video))
- **Camera footage** removed or withheld with inconsistent timecodes ([UVU Surveillance](/cameras/UVU_Surveillance))
- A back-facing camera's **SD card** was reportedly removed at the scene by **[Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth)**;
  commentary ([Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)) alleges this specific card, rather than the more expensive camera body,
  was taken — a claim that, if accurate, would affect what footage of the shot survives for
  authentication

If true, these issues affect **authentication**, not necessarily witness honesty.

## UVU staff vs crowd witnesses

GRAMA emails ([Pre-Shooting Warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings)) document **staff-level threat assessments** — a different category from **eyewitness shot geometry**, but part of the broader "who knew what when" landscape.

## Profiled witnesses on this site

Individual witness pages under [People](/People/overview) include [Hunter Kozak](/People/hunter-kozak), [Cooper Brown](/People/cooper-brown), [Butch Hibbs](/People/butch-hibbs), and others. Each should be read with **role-specific vantage limits** (distance, angle, stress).

## Evaluation standard

Reported claims on this page are attributed to their sources, and criminal accusations are framed with words like **charged with**, **alleged**, or **reportedly**. This website does not assert as fact that any living person named here — including **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** — committed a crime or knew of the assassination in advance. Nothing here is a finding by this website.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The full FBI 302 witness interview reports, records of agents who asked witnesses to delete video, and unedited witness footage are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.


## Citizen Investigator Claims on X

- Candace Owens and others attack early **chest-shot** witness/media lines as unreliable or scripted.
- Hospital contractor witness (per [@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)) later silence is used both as credibility attack and as intimidation evidence — neither proven.
- Hearing process (July 2026) raises formal reliability/hearsay fights over video authors not present for cross — per [@stratagemmer](/Influencers/x/stratagemmer) notes.
