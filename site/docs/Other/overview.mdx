The Other section holds leads and methods that cut across FBI, UVU, Planes, and Media. Many entries started in legacy `other_topics` trash files that were mostly placeholder prose; the three pages above retain only claims with named sources or documented records.

Investigation methodology explains how this site labels facts vs. allegations and why March 2026 seal dates and gag orders matter. Miscellaneous evidence leads track threads like Provo badge updates and George Zinn diversion claims until they graduate to dedicated pages. Public response documents societal reactions without conflating them with proof about the shooter.

Use [Investigation Methodology](./Investigation_Methodology) before citing this site in external research. Scan [Miscellaneous Evidence Leads](./Miscellaneous_Evidence_Leads) when you remember a odd detail that does not fit elsewhere. [Public Response](./Public_Response) pairs with [Political Aftermath](/Topic-Analyses/Political_Aftermath) for impact analysis.
