# Evidence Contradictions in the Kirk Case

Public investigation of the UVU shooting repeatedly surfaces **conflicting accounts** across shot time, suspect movement, ballistics, and media handling. This page catalogs major contradiction **themes** with sources — not a finding that any party lied.

This website does not assert as fact that any living person named here committed a crime, did anything wrong or illegal, or had any knowledge of or involvement in the assassination. Names appear here only in the context of public discussion and reporting. Nothing here is a finding by this website.

## Shot and death times

| Claim cluster | Where documented |
|---|---|
| **12:23 PM vs 12:27 PM MDT** impact second | [Shooting Time Disputes](/Timeline/12-23-shooting-time), [Sept 10 Timeline](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025) |
| Death window **12:23–12:27** when sources disagree | [Witness Testimony Discrepancies](/Timeline/witness-testimony-discrepancies) |

Acoustic sync, police radio, and hospital records would adjudicate — most remain **partially sealed**.

## Suspect movement contradictions

- **Dairy Queen sighting** annotated **12:38 PM** vs **6:38 PM** from single photographs ([Sept_10_2025](/Timeline/Sept_10_2025))
- **Roof egress** timelines vs morning camera sightings ([Roof Jumping](/Timeline/roof-jumping-timeline), [Tyler on Cameras](/cameras/Tyler_On_Cameras))
- **Gun-drop location** gaps vs parking-lot entry time ([Gun Dropped Location](/Gun_Bullet/Gun_Dropped_Location))

## Ballistics and weapon claims

Alternate-shooter threads cite:

- **Mauser 98** vs other rifle narratives ([Gun & Bullet](/Gun_Bullet/overview))
- **Exploding microphone** theory vs single high-velocity round ([Mic](/Mic/overview))
- Autopsy and wound-trajectory disputes ([Medical Autopsy](/Medical/Autopsy/overview))
- **Caliber-messaging inconsistency** — commentary alleges **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** and **[Andrew Kolvet](/People/andrew-kolvet)** gave
  differing early accounts of the **.30-06 caliber "text,"** cited as a contradiction in how the
  weapon was first described ([Rifle Fingerprints & Mismatch](/Gun_Bullet/Rifle_Fingerprints_Mismatch))
- **Sound-vs-impact timing** — analysts argue the shot report and Kirk's injury appear **simultaneous**
  in footage, which for a 150-yard rifle should be **sequential**, implying a **closer origin**
  ([Wound Trajectory & Angle](/Gun_Bullet/Wound_Trajectory_Angle))

## Investigative-process contradictions (alleged)

Commentary alleges mismatches between **public FBI summaries** and:

- Witness claims that agents **requested video deletion** ([Cover-Up](/CoverUp/FBI_Asked_Delete_Video))
- **Withheld sheriff video** ([Sheriff Video Withheld](/CoverUp/Sheriff_Video_Withheld))
- **Sealed warrants** until March 2026 ([Investigation Methodology](/Other/Investigation_Methodology))

## How this site handles conflicts

Per [Investigation Methodology](/Other/Investigation_Methodology): present **both claims**, cite **named sources**, note what record would **resolve** the conflict, and avoid merging incompatible theories.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Contradiction Catalog)

*(Contradiction density is a research signal — not automatic proof of a single alternate theory.)*

Citizen investigators on X repeatedly pair the following **contradiction clusters** as reasons they reject “case closed” messaging:

| Cluster | Side A (examples) | Side B (examples) |
|---|---|---|
| Wound location language | Early **chest** media/witness lines (Owens critique) | Visible **neck** wound in multi-angle video |
| Transport | Ambulance expectation / nearest trauma | **SUV hasty transport** to Timpanogos (**[@ProjectConstitu](/Influencers/podcasts-project-constitution)**, Hull notes) |
| Exit wound | “Absolute miracle / no exit” relay | Through-neck / back-spray arguments |
| Rifle match | Charging narrative Mauser .30-06 | **ATF inconclusive** Exhibit 6A commentary |
| Autopsy access | Viral “no autopsy” posts | Hearing COD **GSW neck** + fragments; report sealed |
| Mechanism | Rifle crack-bang (**[@alexboge](/Influencers/x/alexboge)**) | Glass-in-SUV / mic rupture (**[@realstewpeters](/Influencers/podcasts-stew-peters)**, **[@jonaaronbray](/Influencers/x/cumby-william)**) |

Each pair has deeper pages under Medical, Mic, Gun_Bullet, and Proof_Not_Tyler. This page’s job is to keep the **matrix of disputes** visible.
