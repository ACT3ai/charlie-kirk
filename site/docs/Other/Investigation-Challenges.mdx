# Investigation Challenges

This project investigates through **OSINT and public records**, not sealed FBI files. Multiple **systemic obstacles** shape what can be verified today.

## Sealing and protective orders

| Obstacle | Impact |
|---|---|
| Warrants/digital forensics **sealed until March 2026** | Blocks ballistics affidavits and device dumps |
| **Broad gag orders** | Limits witness interviews and media supplementation ([Legal Gag Orders](/After/Legal_Process_Gag_Orders_And_Secrecy)) |
| **Protective-order discovery** | Large evidence volume exists but is not public ([Trial](/Tyler_Robinson/Trial/overview)) |

## Intelligence and classification

- **HADES** [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview) and foreign-aircraft intent largely opaque
- Reported **halt** of foreign-lead counterterror review ([Joe Kent Halt](/CoverUp/Joe_Kent_Halt))

## Platform and media environment

- **Takedowns and deboosting** of investigator content ([Censorship](/Censorship/overview))
- Competing **mainstream vs alternative** narratives ([Mainstream vs Alternative Media](/Media/Mainstream-vs-Alternative-Media))

## Physical evidence obstacles

- **Courtyard paving** and rapid site changes
- **Deleted or edited** witness phone media ([Videos Deleted](/CoverUp/Videos_Deleted_Remotely))
- **Tests not performed** — the file notes no standard **gunshot-residue (GSR) test** was reportedly
  done on [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) after a 33-hour manhunt, foreclosing one straightforward physical check
  ([Rifle Fingerprints & Mismatch](/Gun_Bullet/Rifle_Fingerprints_Mismatch))
- **Reported SD-card removal** by [Terryl Farnsworth](/People/terryl-farnsworth) from a back-facing camera at the scene, limiting
  what shot footage survives

## What citizen research still can do

[Investigation Methodology](/Other/Investigation_Methodology) lists viable methods: GRAMA releases, ADS-B flight tracking, court docket monitoring, and **contradiction mapping** ([Evidence Contradictions](/Other/Evidence-Contradictions)).

Statutory disclosure proposals are catalogued under [Fix / New Laws](/Fix/overview).

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

* The sealed evidence blocking independent review, FBI 302 reports and case files withheld, and deleted digital records and remotely-wiped phones are things that the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) may result in powerful truths coming out that aren't out yet.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Process Obstacles)

- **FOIA / GRAMA stonewalling** is a constant theme: flight manifests (SAM / DOD jet 99-0404), Fort Huachuca visitor logs, hospital video holds (**@ProjectConstitu** posts claim FOIA resistance on SAM passengers).
- **Witness silence** narratives (hospital contractor TikTok wipe after viral amplification) are framed by posters as intimidation — alternative explanations include legal counsel, privacy, or non-credible original claims.
- **Hearing process** (July 2026) raises reliability fights when video authors/report authors are not present for cross (**@stratagemmer** notes on defense hearsay objections).
- **Platform friction** (deboosting claims, account bans) is alleged by some investigators as soft censorship of non-official theories — hard to quantify; document specific post removals when available.

These are process challenges for citizen investigation quality, not proof of any one master conspiracy.
