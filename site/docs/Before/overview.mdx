The strongest **pre-event institutional** signal is UVU's own **GRAMA record**: on September 9 two men asked police about **"a plan,"** a separate party wanted a **message delivered to Charlie** (undocumented delivery), and the joint **Orem–UVU Public Order Unit** never activated despite a Sept 3 ticket link — detailed on [Free Speech Campus](./Free-Speech-Campus-Issues) and [UVU pre-shooting warnings](/UVU/pre-shooting-warnings).

**Donor and meeting pressure** dominates the political lane: a **July 31** White House session reportedly organized around **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel **without TPUSA present**; an **August Hamptons "intervention"** with [Bill Ackman](/People/bill-ackman), [Josh Hammer](/People/josh-hammer), and allies; then September texts about **leaving the pro-Israel cause** and **"THEY ARE GOING TO KILL ME"** on Sept 9. [Political Pressure 2025](./Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) holds that chronology; we do **not** assert any attendee ordered violence.

**Logistics and reconnaissance claims** include **May–July Google Trends** spikes for UVU rooftops and parking, **Dec 2024** interest in defense counsel **Kathryn Nester**, **July 20** surgeon-search frenzies, and **[SU-BTT](/Planes/SU-BTT) / [N1098L](/Planes/N1098L/overview)** aircraft around Provo in early September. [Pre-Event Searches](./PreEvent_Searches_And_Recon) and [Planes](/Planes/overview) catalog those threads as **circumstantial OSINT**, not proof of a plot.
