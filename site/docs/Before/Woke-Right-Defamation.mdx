# Woke-Right Defamation Frame (July 31 Context)

Project timelines repeatedly cite **"confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel"** as a stated theme of the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House session ([July 31 Meeting](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA), [Paul Singer Funding](/TPUSA/Paul_Singer_Funding)).

## What commentators mean by "woke-right"

In donor-aligned discourse compiled on this site, **"woke-right"** typically labels:

- MAGA figures who criticize **Gaza military operations** or **Netanyahu government** actions
- Hosts who platform **Israel-skeptical conservatives** (e.g., [Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson), Dave Smith debates on [TPUSA Opposite Charlie](/TPUSA/TPUSA_Opposite_Charlie))
- Voices opposing **anti-BDS legislation** on First Amendment grounds

Defenders of the label argue it identifies **bad-faith or antisemitic** criticism; critics argue it **suppresses legitimate foreign-policy debate** within conservatism.

## Connection to Charlie Kirk (claims)

Commentators place Kirk in mid-2025 as:

- Still rejecting **antisemitism** in public remarks
- Moving toward **more skeptical Gaza commentary** and open debate formats
- thereby attracting the **"woke-right"** label from some donors — **disputed characterization**

## Meeting organizers' stated counter-frame

July 31 co-organizers ([Faith & Freedom](/TPUSA/Faith-Freedom-Coalition), [Latino Coalition for Israel](/TPUSA/Latino-Coalition-Israel), [Philos](/TPUSA/Philos_Project_Funding)) reportedly paired the "woke-right defamation" theme with concerns about **antisemitism inside MAGA** ([MAGA Antisemitism Debate](/Before/MAGA-Antisemitism-Debate)). Commentary places the session **~four days before** the early-August **Hamptons intervention** ([Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons)) where reported attendees included [**Rabbi Pesach Wolicki**](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [**Josh Hammer**](/People/josh-hammer), [**Seth Dillon**](/People/seth-dillon), [**Bill Ackman**](/People/bill-ackman), and [**Natasha Hausdorff**](/People/natasha-hausdorff) — we document those names as **meeting reconstructions**, not proof any attendee threatened [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk).

## Investigative limits

The phrase appears in **secondary meeting reconstructions**, not a released White House transcript. Researchers should not treat it as proof that any attendee **threatened** Kirk.
