# MAGA Movement and Antisemitism Debate (2025)

A recurring **July 31, 2025** meeting theme in project sources is combating **"growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement"** while countering **"woke-right defamation"** of Israel ([July 31 Meeting](/TPUSA/July_31_Meeting_Without_TPUSA)).

## Two competing frames

### Frame A — Antisemitism inside MAGA (meeting organizers)
Faith & Freedom, Latino Coalition for Israel, and Philos reportedly argued that **America First skepticism** of Gaza policy was bleeding into **antisemitic rhetoric**, requiring elite conservative coordination.

Per Charlie_Kirk.txt, the **July 31, 2025** off-the-record White House session was organized by the **Faith & Freedom Coalition, Latino Coalition for Israel, and the Philos Project** (the notes describe Philos as **funded by Paul Singer**) to discuss "confronting the woke-right defamation of Israel and the Jewish people" and combating "growing antisemitism in the MAGA movement." Reported attendees include **[Ralph Reed](/People/ralph-reed), [Luke Moon](/People/luke-moon), [Apostle Mario Bramnick](/People/apostle-mario-bramnick), [Yehuda Kaploun](/People/yehuda-kaploun), and [Yair Netanyahu](/People/yair-netanyahu)** (Prime Minister Netanyahu's son). The notes stress that **[Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk) and TPUSA representatives were absent** despite Kirk's prior pro-Israel alignment, and that the meeting **preceded the Hamptons intervention by about four days**. These attendee details come from reporting reconstructions, not a released transcript, and imply no wrongdoing by any named person.

### Frame B — "Woke-right" label (Kirk-skeptic voices)
Kirk and allies increasingly platformed **Israel policy critics** ([Tucker Carlson](/People/tucker-carlson), Dave Smith) whom donors labeled **"woke-right"** or disloyal ([Woke-Right Defamation](/Before/Woke-Right-Defamation)).

Kirk's own statements condemned **antisemitism** while questioning **foreign-policy alignment** — commentators dispute how to parse that combination.

## Charlie Kirk's reported position

Public materials compiled on [Charlie Quotes Israel](/Israel/Charlie_Quotes_Israel) show Kirk:

- Rejecting **antisemitism** explicitly in many appearances
- Simultaneously criticizing **Gaza military scale** and **anti-BDS legislation** in 2025

No public record in this project adjudicates him as antisemitic; **living persons must not be labeled as bigots without adjudicated findings.**

## Hamptons follow-on (Aug 4–6, claims)

Grok Q3 threads place the July 31 framing **~four days before** an early-August **Hamptons "intervention"** where reported attendees included [**Rabbi Pesach Wolicki**](/People/rabbi-pesach-wolicki), [**Josh Hammer**](/People/josh-hammer), [**Seth Dillon**](/People/seth-dillon), [**Bill Ackman**](/People/bill-ackman), and [**Natasha Hausdorff**](/People/natasha-hausdorff). Commentary alleges Prime Minister **Netanyahu** offered **money** and an **Israel trip** that Charlie **rejected** — **unverified** second-hand accounts. See [Political Pressure 2025](/Before/Political_Pressure_And_Meetings_2025) and [Israel Hamptons](/Israel/israel-hamptons). We do **not** assert any attendee ordered violence.
