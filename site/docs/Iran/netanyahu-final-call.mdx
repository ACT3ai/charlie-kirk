# The Reported Final Netanyahu Call

The most-cited — and most-contested — claim in this section is that [Charlie Kirk](/People/charlie-kirk)'s **final phone
call** was an intervention by Israeli Prime Minister **[Benjamin Netanyahu](/People/benjamin-netanyahu)** pushing for war with
Iran. This page states that claim precisely, attributes it to its source, and flags what remains
unverified. It is a **reported allegation**, not a proven fact. Netanyahu is a living public
official who has not been charged in connection with Charlie Kirk's death.

## The claim (as reported by Candace Owens)

According to the investigation file, **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** publicly stated that:

> Charlie Kirk's final phone call was a "massive intervention by Benjamin Netanyahu pushing for a
> war in Iran." According to her account, right after Charlie refused, he was assassinated, and
> his organization then shifted toward a pro-war posture.

This is presented as **Owens' on-air assertion**. The reporting is a **televised retelling**, not
a court-authenticated call record. No public transcript, call log, or official confirmation of the
described call has been produced.

## Why it draws so much attention

The claim is potent because it fuses three threads this section already documents:

- **A named, direct pressure event** rather than diffuse "donor" pressure.
- **A refusal**, tying the alleged killing to Charlie saying *no* to the war — the same restraint
  posture on [Charlie's Stand Against the Iran War](./charlie-anti-iran-war).
- **A described aftermath**, an alleged pivot toward pro-war messaging, examined on
  [TPUSA's Post-Death Pivot on Iran](./post-death-pivot).

## What is unverified

Multiple things in this account are, as of now, **unproven**:

- That the specific call happened as described, or that Netanyahu was the caller.
- That any refusal on that call is connected to the events of September 10.
- The characterization of what was said, which reaches the public only through paraphrase.

Owens is describing her own understanding; the office of the person named has not publicly
addressed the specific claim in the file. Readers should treat this as a **lead to be tested in
discovery**, not a conclusion.

## Laws (Charlie Kirk)

If any such call occurred, official readouts, White House logs, and communications records are
exactly the kind of material the [Charlie Kirk Investigation Laws](/Fix/overview) are designed to
force into the public record — the only way to confirm or refute the claim.
