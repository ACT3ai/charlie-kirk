# Tyler Robinson — Patsy Framework

**[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)** is the **charged suspect** in State v. Tyler Robinson. The **patsy framework** asks whether he
is the **operational shooter** or a **secondary figure** — groomed, framed, or selected to absorb blame while
planners remain hidden. Mind-control theories are **one branch** of that tree; **physical impossibility** arguments
([Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview)) are another. **We do not assert Robinson’s legal innocence** here.

## Two ways “control” shows up

| Model | Mechanism | Evidence grade |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **Behavioral control** | Hypnosis, drugs, V2K, digital radicalization | Historical programs + speculative UVU application |
| **Narrative control** | Fabricated confession, false custody timeline, media frame | Custody/Discord timing disputes, [Discord Timing](./discord-confession-timing) |
| **Physical frame** | Different person on roof; Tyler as label | [Stairs Guy gap](/Proof_Not_Tyler/Stairs_Guy_Identity_Gap), ballistics |

Mind-control pages focus on the **first row**. Even if hypnosis/V2K never touched Robinson, **patsy framing** could
still be true via rows two and three.

## Commentator positions (attributed)

- **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** — argued no **sworn written confession** exists; Robinson **pleaded not guilty**; Washington
  County custody timing undercuts federal “turned himself in” narrative; described federal **patsy setup** (her words).
- **Baron Coleman** — claimed Robinson **in custody before ~6:30 PM** Sept 11; **Discord messages fabricated**;
  multi-agency cover-up to frame Tyler (his allegation).
- **Mainstream / prosecution** — lone actor radicalized online and by partner; confession to family and Discord;
  rooftop shooter narrative.

One institutional data point cuts against treating the lone-actor frame as settled: former NCTC director [Joe Kent](/US_Intelligence/joe-kent) has said on the record that his center's parallel review of potential foreign ties was ordered to stop once Robinson was in custody — before those leads were exhausted.

## Early “original patsy” candidates

Before Robinson dominated headlines, **[Zachariah Qureshi](/distraction_people/Zachariah_Qureshi)** was reportedly
detained and released; **[Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel)** “in custody” timing debates treat him as a possible **distraction patsy**.
That does not exonerate or implicate Robinson — it shows **multiple false-lead slots** in the first hours.

## Handler-adjacent threads (not proven on Tyler)

- **[Lance Twiggs](/People/lance-twiggs)** — partner; commentators allege **setup** via Discord environment ([Radicalization vs Psyop](./radicalization-psyop-claims)).
- **Google Trends** — claimed monitoring of `"Tyler Robinson Discord/Reddit/FBI/LDS/TPUSA/Israel"` months pre-event —
  interpreted as **recruit-or-silence** watching ([Recruited](/Tyler_Robinson/Recruited)).

**No public record** ties Robinson to a named intelligence handler or MKUltra file.

## Defamation note

**Tyler Robinson**, **Lance Twiggs**, **Candace Owens**, and law-enforcement officials are living subjects.
Allegations are **attributed**; we **do not claim** Robinson was mind-controlled or that any official ordered his
recruitment.

## Citizen Investigator Claims on X (Attributed)

Mind-control / programmed-patsy discourse on X sits at the **highest speculation tier**. Historical facts about CIA **MKUltra** and **Project Artichoke** are real research subjects; applying them to [Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson) is **unverified theory**.

July 2026 radio/X syndication claimed **Lance Twiggs** video testimony that Robinson said "I wish I hadn't done it"—used by some to argue ordinary guilt, by others as scripted confession narrative. Discord message timing versus Miranda remains a separate factual dispute.

Patsy framework on X cites skill/timeline/ballistics gaps plus confession timing. Prosecution materials argue ordinary guilt. Neither side is adopted as site fact beyond charging status.

**Hard rule:** Do not read this page as a finding that any living handler, agency, or partner programmed Robinson. Courts have not so found.
