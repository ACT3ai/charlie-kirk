# Making a Shooter — Playbook (Theoretical)

Citizen investigators sometimes stack **multiple control layers** into one model of how intelligence services **could**
manufacture an apparent **lone shooter**. This page **synthesizes** those layers for navigation — **not** as proof
any layer was used on **Tyler Robinson** or at **UVU**.

## Layer 1 — Historical behavioral programs (documented)

- **[Project Artichoke](./project-artichoke-memo)** — feasibility of **involuntary assassination** via drugs +
  hypnosis after a single social contact.
- **[MKUltra](./mkultra-program)** — broader CIA behavioral research record.
- **[Hypnosis](./hypnosis-programmed-patsy)** — Kiriakou’s reported **multi-hour** motor control under hypnosis.

**Status:** Programs **existed**. Operational success on modern U.S. political targets is **unproven**.

## Layer 2 — Directed-energy audio (science vs application)

- **[Frey Effect / V2K](./frey-effect-v2k)** — lab-proven **in-head sound**; contested **field-scale commands**.

Hypothetical use: beam **trigger phrase** at a groomed subject in crowd noise. **No UVU evidence** on public record.

## Layer 3 — Digital engineering (contemporary)

- **[MKUltra 2.0](./mkultra-2-digital-engineering)** — private groups, algorithms, **meme triggers**, sleeper-army rhetoric.

Hypothetical use: Tyler’s **Discord arc** as **platform-scale groom** whether or not Lance acted alone.

## Layer 4 — Human handlers (attributed)

- **[Handler Network](./handler-network-claims)** — recruitment environments (Peterson TPUSA allegation), fixers
  (Saltz threads), scene coordinators (unnamed cast lists).

Hypothetical use: coach **surrender timing**, **confession script**, **custody handoff** ([Discord timing](./discord-confession-timing)).

Master-file **single-source** claim: recruited patsy told **Governor + FBI** ordered urgency — **unverified primary**.

## Layer 5 — Physical frame (non-psychological)

Even perfect mind control fails if the **wrong body** is on video:

- [Proof Not Tyler](/Proof_Not_Tyler/overview) — ballistics, stairs clothing, acoustic split,
- [Black clothing suspect](/Suspects/Black_Clothing_Suspect) — eyewitness mismatch threads,
- [Drones + patsy pattern](/Drones/Butler_PA_Drone_Parallel) — kill via advanced means, blame local actor.

**Patsy framework** can be **entirely physical** — Tyler as **label** — with **zero** hypnosis.
