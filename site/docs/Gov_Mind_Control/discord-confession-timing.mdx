# Discord Confession Timing

The prosecution narrative leans heavily on **[Tyler Robinson](/People/tyler-robinson)’s Discord messages** and pre-surrender texts as
**self-incrimination**. Independent commentators instead treat the **timestamp stack** as evidence of
**narrative fabrication** — controlling what the public *believes* Tyler did, which is a different but related
thread to **hypnotic control** of Tyler himself.

## The timing dispute

Research notes compiled in the master file highlight:

| Claimed event | Approx. time (Sept 11, 2025) |
|---------------|------------------------------|
| Robinson **mirandized** at Washington County Sheriff’s Office | **~6:25 PM** (citizen investigator reconstruction) |
| **Discord confession** messages appear | **~7:57 PM** |
| Robinson arrives with parents at sheriff’s office (gov doc) | **~10:26 PM** |
| [Kash Patel](/People/kash-patel) / federal “in custody” public framing | **~9–10 PM** band (disputed) |

**Baron Coleman** and **[Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens)** argue: if Robinson was truly **in custody and mirandized at 6:25 PM**, he
**could not have authored** 7:57 PM Discord posts — implying **inserted or forged** messages. **We have not
independently authenticated** server logs or device images.

multi-agency custody transfers that may reconcile times differently than citizen spreadsheets. **Discovery** should
adjudicate; this page logs the **dispute**.
