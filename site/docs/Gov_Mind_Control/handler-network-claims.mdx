# Handler Network Claims

**Handlers** — human case officers who **recruit, coach, debrief, or silence** assets — are the low-tech complement
to hypnosis and V2K in patsy theories. This page catalogs **named handler-adjacent figures** in citizen research.
**Presence in a thread is not proof of operational role.** All living persons: **no crime allegations** unless
court-proven elsewhere.

## Kyle Saltz — Ft. Huachuca counterintelligence (attributed)

X research summarized in the master file identifies **[Kyle Saltz](/People/kyle-saltz)** as:

- **U.S. Army** veteran, **counterintelligence** training at **[Fort Huachuca](/US_Intelligence/Fort_Huachuca/overview)**,
- Described as **“handler/fixer”** in a **[Mitch Snow](/People/mitch-snow)** burglary video,
- Linked by posters to whistleblower suppression and **[Erika Kirk](/People/erika-kirk)** pre-event sighting threads.

Posters cite **Tri-City Herald** reporting about a **2008 death of an Army peer** in which Saltz is said to be
mentioned — readers should verify the primary articles; nothing here asserts that Saltz caused or was responsible for
that death, and he has not been charged in connection with it. Alleged associations with **Kathryn Ruemmler** (Epstein
attorney) appear in **unverified** social posts.

**No public document** places Saltz at UVU Sept 10 or ties him to Tyler Robinson.

## Joshua Peterson — TPUSA training whistleblower

**Joshua Peterson** ([@RagingPatriot20](/Influencers/x/ragingpatriot20)), in video distributed by **Project Constitution** (June 26, 2026), alleges:

- New [TPUSA](/TPUSA/overview) reps taken to Arizona bars in rented vans after training,
- Alcohol-heavy outings used to **probe Israel/[Mossad](/intelligence/Mossad) views**,
- Statement that senior reps said **“we’re part of it”** regarding foreign intelligence.

**Single-source, unverified.** Does **not** prove TPUSA leadership knew of or ordered illegal acts. Included because
commentators cite it when arguing **institutional recruitment environments** exist adjacent to the Kirk circle —
relevant to **how** a young man might encounter handlers, not proof about Robinson.
