The Charlie Kirk investigation repeatedly returns to a single question: could the accused shooter have been guided, conditioned, or even physically steered by something other than his own free decision? That question only makes sense against the documented record of real government behavioral-science programs. This area exists to lay out that record plainly — what these programs actually were, what was declassified, and where the evidence stops and speculation begins — so readers can judge the case theories on facts rather than on either dismissal or hype.

The substance splits into three buckets. First, **directed-energy audio**: the Frey Effect (microwave auditory effect) is lab-proven — pulsed microwaves can create sound perceived inside the head — and "Voice to Skull" (V2K) extends that toward covert voice projection. Second, **chemical and psychological conditioning**: the CIA's MKUltra program is a matter of declassified record, and figures like Mike Cernovich (author of *Chaos*) have tied that history to discussion of the Kirk case. Third, **hypnosis and behavioral control**: former CIA officer John Kiriakou has publicly described an MKUltra-era hypnosis experiment in which a subject was controlled for hours — a touchstone for the "programmed patsy" theory.

Readers new to this area should start with the [Frey Effect / V2K](./frey-effect-v2k) page, which includes a video demonstration of beamed, head-localized audio, then read the [MKUltra](./mkultra-program) overview for the documented program history, and finally the [Hypnosis](./hypnosis-programmed-patsy) page for the specific patsy-conditioning material. Throughout, the standard is the same as the rest of the site: the established science is labeled as such, and where the public record stops, that line is stated plainly.
