# MKUltra 2.0 — Digital Social Engineering

Cold War **MKUltra** used labs, drugs, and isolation. A contemporary model — labeled **“MKULTRA 2.0”** in citizen
research notes — argues **platforms and private groups** now perform similar functions: **select vulnerable minds,
reinforce narratives, and deploy them** while subjects believe they act on conviction alone.

## The claimed pipeline (summarized from CK research)

1. **Funnel** — individuals moved from public feeds into **private Discord/Telegram** groups.
2. **Reinforce** — echo chambers mold identity and grievance.
3. **Split** — some are **radicalized** (true believers); others **weaponized** (tasked actors) per commentary.
4. **Trigger** — memes, symbols, and phrases act as **activation cues** (analogized to hypnosis trigger words).
5. **Scale** — behavioral analytics pick **most susceptible** profiles for deeper grooming.

The master file states plainly: *“That’s not a conspiracy, it is digital social engineering that is happening
right now”* — presenting **commercial and state-adjacent influence** as established, while **assassination
application** remains **unproven**.
