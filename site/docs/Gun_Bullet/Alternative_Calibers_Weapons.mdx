# Alternative Calibers & Weapons

If the **Mauser .30-06 / Losee rooftop** chain fails on [ATF match](./ATF_Fragment_Inconclusive),
[energy](./Thirty_06_Energy_Mismatch), or [trajectory](./Wound_Trajectory_Angle), investigators propose
**closer, lower-energy, or non-rifle** mechanisms. This page summarizes **attributed theories** —
not adjudicated facts.

## 1. Israeli CornerShot 9mm +P (tent / van)

[@ProjectConstitu](https://x.com/ProjectConstitu) and **Dr. [Chris Martenson](/People/chris-martenson)** threads (see
[@stevendenoon](https://x.com/stevendenoon) YouTube: [zeioeWdmSIw](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zeioeWdmSIw)) propose:

| Element | Claim |
|---------|-------|
| Weapon | **CornerShot**-style hinged system with screen aim |
| Caliber | **9mm +P** (~1330 fps) |
| Origin | **Inside tent** or **grey van** tunnel (IR laser on [Frank Turek](/People/frank-turek) phone) |
| Wound signature | **Cavitation**, necklace snap, **pugilist posture**, energy dumped internally |
| Casing | Shell ejected **downward** near tent — cited in tent-shooter threads |

Full tent geometry: [Tent Shooter Theories](/Tent/Tent_Shooter_Theories) · [Grey Van POI](/Suspects/Grey_Van_Shooter).

## 2. Suppressed high-velocity small-bore

Some audio analyses argue the crack-thump pattern fits **suppressed .22-250**-class velocity more than
a distant **.30-06 boom** — overlapping [Mic acoustics](/Mic/Canon_XA55_Audio) and
[@troofevades](https://x.com/troofevades) placement claims. A breakdown cited in the investigation file
(Red Randumb Ruminations on YouTube) describes the report as a **"flack-thump" from 100–200 yards**,
consistent with a **high-velocity snap** from a suppressed small-bore rifle rather than a **"thunderous
.30-06 boom"** or **"rooftop roar."** The same thread argues the wound signature — **small, clean,
bullet-sized entry** with a **massive cavitation exit** — fits **hollow-point or Hydra-Shok** rounds
that mushroom on impact, and compares the **T1 neck fragment** to deer-hunt bullets that **embed under
the skin** after long flight. These are **attributed audio/terminal-ballistics interpretations**, not
lab conclusions.

## 3. Shaped charge — mic, pendant, or crucifix

Parallel non-bullet hypotheses (detailed on [Mic](/Mic/overview), not fully duplicated here):

- **PETN micro-charge** in **RØDE Wireless PRO** transmitter / magnetic clasp
- **Crucifix pendant** micro shaped charge — military commentator "cone charge" math in CK file
- [Candace Owens](/People/candace-owens): shaped charge **intended for chest**, malfunctioned **up into neck**

Skeptic counter: large shaped-charge demos produce visible smoke signatures — see [Mic shape-charge demo](/Mic/Demo_of_shape_charge).

## 4. "No shot from Losee" lane

Some analysts state **no credible footage** shows a shot fired from Losee because **no rifle shot occurred
there** — the rooftop figure and recovered Mauser are **patsy props** in that framing. That is maximal
skepticism of the official weapon **location**, distinct from [alternative caliber] only.

## Hollow-point / T1 fragment analogy

Deer-hunt and **Hydra-Shok** analogies in commentary argue the **T1 neck fragment** behaves like a
**mushrooming** handgun bullet embedding under skin — not a full-power .30-06 pass-through.
