# 2025 Context Timeline (Claims and Reported Events)

## Overview

This Level_3 page expands on the **2025 timeline** leading up to and immediately following the September 10 UVU event. It combines:

- **Publicly reported events** (conferences, media appearances, flights), and  
- **Additional claims** from social‑media compilations, podcasts, and long‑form articles about meetings, text messages, and flight or search‑pattern anomalies.

All items that do not come from clear, on‑the‑record statements or mainstream coverage should be treated as **claims**, not established fact. The page does **not** assert that any particular actor “decided to kill” Charlie; it simply records how various commentators have sequenced events in their own narratives.

These elements underpin motive theories explored more fully in [Motive](/Motive/overview.md) and [Charlie](/Charlie/overview.md), but remain **unproven allegations**.

These statements—if accurately reported—are central to many motive theories. At present they remain **alleged quotes**, and readers should consult original broadcasts and posts where available.

## How to read the 2025 timeline

The 2025 context timeline should be read as a **structured summary of what different sources say happened and when**, not as a verdict on who is responsible:

- **Documented events** (speeches, podcasts, public meetings, corporate filings, and flight‑tracking logs) provide hard anchor points.  
- **Alleged private meetings, decisions, and warnings** come primarily from **commentary, social‑media threads, and Google Trends screenshots** and remain unverified.  
- This page aims to keep those categories clearly separated so readers can understand **what is firmly dated**, **what is alleged**, and **how timelines are being used in various theories** across the project.
